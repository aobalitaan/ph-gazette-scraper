OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 490

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY WAYS AND MEANS FOR THE IMPROVEMENT OF PHILIPPINE HORSE RACING AND BREEDING.

There is hereby created a Committee to study ways and means for the improvement of Philippine horse racing and horse breeding. The Committee shall be composed of the following:

The Committee shall conduct studies and submit recommendations on the following aspects of horse racing and breeding:

a. Handicapping of horses;

b. Adoption of the photo-patrol system;

c. Installation of the automatic electric totalizator;

e. Establishment of drug and urine laboratory; and

d. Such other matters as may be considered vital and necessary towards the improvement of Philippine horse racing and breeding.

The Committee may call upon any department, bureau, office or instrumentality of the government for such assistance as it may need in the performance of its functions.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, August 4, 1971

Source : Presidential Management Staff
