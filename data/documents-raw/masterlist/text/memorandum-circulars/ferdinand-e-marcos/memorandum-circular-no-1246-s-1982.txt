Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1246

URGING GOVERNMENT AGENCIES CONCERNED TO GIVE MAXIMUM SUPPORT AND ASSISTANCE TO THE NATIONAL BOARD FOR TEACHERS IN THE CONDUCT OF THE 1982 PROFESSIONAL BOARD EXAMINATION FOR TEACHERS.

WHEREAS, the National Board for Teachers, through the Ministry of Education and Culture (MEC) and the Civil Service Commission (CSC), is charged with the duty of administering the Professional Board Examination for Teachers required of all persons who shall engage in teaching, whether in public or private elementary or secondary schools, to pass the said examination, as provided for under Presidential Decree No. 1006 dated September 22, 1976;

WHEREAS, the Professional Board Examination for Teachers is scheduled to be given on November 28, 1982 in at least 25 examination centers throughout the country to about 90,000 examinees;

WHEREFORE, the Ministry of National Defense, the Ministry of Finance the Armed Forces of the Philippines, the National Intelligence and Security Authority, the Integrated National Police, the Ministry of Transportation and Communications, the Ministry of Public Works and Highways, the Philippine Airlines, the Philippine National Railways, and all offices under these agencies, are hereby urged to extend maximum support and assistance to the MEC and CSC to ensure full security, availability of air, sea, and land transportation of examination materials, personnel, and smooth administration of the examination all over the country;

The Deputy Minister of National Defense, the Commanding General of the Aviation Security Command, and the Commissioner of Customs are requested to exempt from inspection all classified materials pertinent to the examination;

The Office of Media Affairs , the National Media Production Center and the National Computer Center are likewise enjoined to extend maximum support and assistance relative to the dissemination of essential information on the holding of the examination, the utilization of communication facilities throughout the country, and the computer processing of the test results, respectively.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, July 21, 1982

Source: Presidential Management Staff
