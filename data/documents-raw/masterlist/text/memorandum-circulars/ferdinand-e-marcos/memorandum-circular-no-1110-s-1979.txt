TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1110

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE 24TH CONGRESS OF THE INTERNATIONAL UNION OF LOCAL AUTHORITIES (TULA).

The Ministry of Local Government and Community Development will host the 24th Congress of the International Union of Local Authorities (IULA) on February 5-10, 1979, at the Philippine International Convention Center, Manila.

The attendance of not more than two (2) selected delegates from each Ministry or agency whose responsibilities include the administration of services and the promotion of development of local governments in the said Congress is hereby authorized on official time with reasonable expenses for transportation, per diems and a congress fee of P750.00 per participant, chargeable against “the appropriations of their respective offices, in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344 dated November 26, 1973, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 of this Office dated August 8, 1972. Participants residing or stationed within the Metro Manila Area shall be entitled to reasonable expenses for transportation and congress fee only. Meals included in the congress fee shall be deducted from per diems.

The participants shall submit to their respective office heads a complete report on their attendance or participation in the Congress as prescribed under Memorandum Circular No.987 dated June 10, 1977.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, June 17, 1979

Source: Presidential Management Staff
