OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 118

AUTHORIZING THE ATTENDANCE OF CERTAIN GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE EIGHTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY MEETING OF THE CONFEDERATION OF GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES ORGANIZATIONS.

The Confederation of Government Employees Organizations (COGEO) will held its Eighth General Assembly Meeting on June 30, 1967, at the Conference Hall of the SSS Building, Quezon City, to consider projects designed to benefit the general mass of government employees and to conduct the annual election of the numbers of the Board of Directors.

In the interest of the rank and file of government workers, all heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices of the national government are hereby authorized to allow the attendance on official time of the President and nine (9) other delegates of member-organizations in their respective jurisdictions in the meeting provided that the same shall not involve the expenditure of public funds outside of the salaries of the delegates.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, June 13, 1967

Source: Presidential Management Staff
