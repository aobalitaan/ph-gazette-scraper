TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS. (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 747

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 739 DATED 20 MAY 1974 OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Memorandum Circular No, 739 dated 20 May 1974 of this Office urging attendance in the Seminar on Supply Management to be conducted by the Bureau of Supply Coordination is hereby amended so as to change the site of the seminar from Davao City to Cagayan de Oro City and to authorize the Director of Supply, upon prior consultation with the Secretary General Services, to adjust and/or alter the schedule of the seminar as may be warranted by the exigency of the service.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, July 1, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
