TANGGAPAN NG PAGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 804

CREATING AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE PREPARATION AND ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE HOLDING OF THE 8TH ANNUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS OF THE ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK TO BE HELD IN MANILA ON APRIL 24 TO 26, 1975

There is hereby created an Executive Committee to help in the preparation and arrangement for the holding of the 8th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of the Asian Development Bank which will be held in Manila on April 24 to 26, 1975 composed of the following:

The Committee may call upon heads of departments, bureaus and offices for such assistance as it may need in the accomplishment of its mission.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, February 11, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
