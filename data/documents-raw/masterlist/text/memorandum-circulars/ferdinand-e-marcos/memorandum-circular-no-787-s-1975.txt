Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 787

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO ASSIST IN THE PREPARATION AND ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE FORTHCOMING AFRO-ASIAN WRITER’S SYMPOSIUM.

The Afro-Asian Writer’s Symposium is scheduled to be held in Manila from January 27 to February 1, 1975.

In view of the importance of the event and in order to ensure the success of the Symposium, a Committee is hereby created to assist the Philippine Writer’s Union in the preparation and arrangements for the Afro-Asian Writers’ Symposium, composed of the following:
