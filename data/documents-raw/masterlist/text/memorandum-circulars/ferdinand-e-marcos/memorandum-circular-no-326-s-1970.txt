OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 326

REQUIRING ALL OFFICES OF THE GOVERNMENT TO HOLD REGULAR OFFICE HOURS DURING JEEPNEYS, BUS OR OTHER SIMILAR TRANSPORTATION STRIKES AFFECTING THE GREATER MANILA AREA AND IMMEDIATE ENVIRONS, ENJOINING THE ORGANIZATION OF CAR POOLS BY EMPLOYEES AND PERSONNEL OF GOVERNMENT OFFICES WHO ARE CAR OWNERS TO HELP NON-CAR OWNING EMPLOYEES AND PERSONNEL TRAVEL TO AND FROM THEIR RESIDENCES TO THEIR RESPECTIVE OFFICES AND MOBILIZING AVAILABLE GOVERNMENT OWNED MOTOR VEHICLES TO FERRY GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL TO AND FROM VARIOUS POINTS ON OFFICIAL BUSINESS DURING SUCH STRIKES.

In order to insure continuity of public services during the occasion of strikes of jeepneys, bus or other transport in the Greater Manila Area and its immediate environs during which means of public transportation or a large segment thereof ceases to function, depriving the public at large of the means of movement and causing general public inconveniences, all offices of the government located in the Greater Manila area and its immediate environs affected by the strike shall hold regular office hours and all personnel of such offices are required to report for work.

Officials, employees or personnel of the government offices concerned who are owners of cars, jeeps or other motor vehicles are hereby requested to organize car pools in their respective offices and allow, for the duration of the strike, co-employees residing within their vicinity to share rides with them to and from their respective offices.

All motor vehicles of the government offices in the areas affected by the strike, and in the immediate vicinity thereof, shall be mobilized for the duration of the said strike, for the ferrying and transporting of government personnel on official business to and from various points.

Other practical means, aside from these mentioned above, should be availed of and organized by the offices concerned to obviate any absence of government employees while such a state of condition exist.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

March 6, 1970

Source : Presidential Management Staff
