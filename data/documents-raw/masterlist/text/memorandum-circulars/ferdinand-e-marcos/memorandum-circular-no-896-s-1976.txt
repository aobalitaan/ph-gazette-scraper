TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 896

ENJOINING ALL OFFICES, AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE GOVERNMENT TO COMPLY WITH MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 320 DATED FEBRUARY 23, 1970, AS AMENDED BY MEMORANDUM NO. 362 DATED JUNE 10, 1970.

Memorandum Circular No. 320 of this Office dated February 23, 1970, as amended by Memorandum Circular No, 362 dated June 10, 1970, requires that “An Action Officer shall be designated from among the key personnel of offices and instrumentalities with a personnel strength of less than two hundred (200), and an Action Committee of three (a Chairman and two members), in offices and instrumentalities with more than two hundred (200) employees, whose duty, in addition to their regular duties, shall be to follow up cases referred by the Unit to their agencies to ensure expeditious disposition thereof.”

It has come to the attention of this Office that many government offices, agencies and instrumentalities have not complied with the provisions of the said Circular. In view thereof, all concerned are hereby enjoined to comply with the said Memorandum Circular not later than March 31, 1976 , by designating the required Action Officer or Action Committee as the case may be, furnishing the Special Action Unit of the Office of the President with the names of their respective action officers, or the names of the members of their Action Committees, as the case maybe.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, March 3, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
