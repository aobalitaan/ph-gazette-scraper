TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1085

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1076 ENTITLED “AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE OF GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL IN THE 4TH NATIONAL CULTURAL CONFERENCE-WORKSHOP SPONSORED BY THE PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT CULTURAL ASSOCIATION.”

Memorandum Circular No. 1076, dated August 22, 1978, authorizing attendance in the 4th National Cultural Conference-Workshop sponsored by the Philippine Government Cultural Association, to be held in Metro Manila on October 4-6, 1978, is hereby amended so as to extend the conference to October 7, 1978.

JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, October 3, 1978

Source: House of Representatives, Legislative Library
