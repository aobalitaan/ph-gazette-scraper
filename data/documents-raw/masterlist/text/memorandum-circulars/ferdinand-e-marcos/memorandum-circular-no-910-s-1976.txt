TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 910

EXCUSING FROM OFFICE GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL IN METROPOLITAN MANILA, AND IN THE PROVINCES AND OTHER CITIES IN LUZON ON MAY 24, 1976, DUE TO TYPHOON “DIDANG.”

The announcement issued by this Office through radio, television and telephone, excusing from work officials and employees, whether regular, casual or contractual, of government offices within the Metropolitan Manila, and in the provinces and other cities in Luzon on May 24, 1976, due to typhoon “Didang,” except those engaged in relief, rehabilitation and rescue operations, as well as communications and public utility services including the necessary personnel of “PAGASA,” is hereby confirmed.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, May 24, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
