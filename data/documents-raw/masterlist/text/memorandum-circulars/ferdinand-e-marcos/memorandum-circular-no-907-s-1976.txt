TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 907

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE 8TH NATIONAL MARKETING CONFERENCE

The Philippine Marketing Association and the Development Academy of the Philippines will jointly sponsor the 8th National Marketing Conference on May 26-28, 1976, at the Hyatt Regency Hotel, on the theme, “Marketing in Transition ….. Changing Dimensions, Changing Perspectives.”

The country’s marketing specialists, practitioners, and educators, as well as economic policy-makers in government, will participate as delegates and speakers at the Conferences.

Heads of appropriate departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send their respective participants to the conference. Attendance shall be on official time, with reasonable expenses for such participation, chargeable against the funds of their respective agencies, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) J.C.TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, May 11, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
