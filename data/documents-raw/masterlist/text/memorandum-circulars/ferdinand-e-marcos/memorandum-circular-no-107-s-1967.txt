OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 107

Henceforth, no government contract involving private financing scheme shall be awarded and/or entered into, as a result of either public bidding or negotiation, without the same having first been submitted to the Investment Office of the Presidential Economic Staff headed by Dean Cesar Virata who shall, in coordination with the Secretary of Finance and the Governor of the Central Bank, study and evaluate the feasibility of the proposed private financing, including the financial capacity and credit standing of the persons and/or entities making the financing proposal, and thereafter propose the proper recommendation therefor.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, March 30, 1967
