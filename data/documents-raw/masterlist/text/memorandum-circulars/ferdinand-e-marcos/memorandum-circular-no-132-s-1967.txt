OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 132

AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES TO LEAVE THEIR OFFICES DURING OFFICE HOURS FOR THE PURPOSE OF REGISTERING WITH THE COMMISSION ON ELECTIONS.

In view of the proximity of the deadline for the registration of voters under the new registration list, all heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices including government-owned or controlled corporations,

whose offices are located in Manila and suburbs, are hereby directed to permit their employees to leave their offices anytime during office hours to register with the nearest COMELEC office.

This accordingly amends Memorandum Circular No. 125 dated July 31, 1967, which requires employees to leave their office on staggered arrangement.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, October 12, 1967

Source: Presidential Management Staff
