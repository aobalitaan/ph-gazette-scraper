TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 626

DISPENSING WITH THE FIRING OF 21-GUN SALUTE DURING THE OBSERVANCE OF RAMON MAGSAYSAY MEMORIAL DAY ON MARCH 17, 1973.

All heads of departments, bureaus, offices and instrumentalities of the government are hereby informed that the firing of 21-gun salute during the observance of Ramon Magsaysay Memorial Day on March 17, 1973, is hereby dispensed with in line with the recommendation of the Secretary of National Defense.

This accordingly modifies the schedule of commemorative features highlighting the observance of the said occasion as embodied in Proclamation No. 15 dated. March 17, 1966, declaring March 17 th of each year as “Ramon Magsaysay Memorial Day.”

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 15, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
