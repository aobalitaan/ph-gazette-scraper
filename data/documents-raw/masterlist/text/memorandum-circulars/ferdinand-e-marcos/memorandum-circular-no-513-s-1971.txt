TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 513

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE OF GOVERNMENT NURSES IN THE 14TH NATIONAL NURSES WEEK CELEBRATION

The Philippine Nurses Association will celebrate this year’s National Nurses Week on October 24-29, 1971, pursuant to Proclamation No. 885 of the President, dated August 14, 1971.

The Office of the Secretary of Health, the Bureau of Medical Services, the Provincial Health Office of Rizal and City Health Departments of cities and national government hospitals within the Greater Manila Area, are hereby authorized to send not more than two (2) of their nurses who are members of the Association to attend the celebration on official time only, subject to the condition that the exigencies of the service will permit attendance.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 14, 1971
