TANGAPAN NGG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR.NO. 1103

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE FIRST SOUTHEAST ASIAN CONVENTION ON PLANNING AND HOUSING

The Ministry of Human Settlements, National Housing Authority and the Philippine Council for Planning and Housing jointly sponsor the First Southeast Asian Convention on Planning and Housing on 10-14 December 1978. The convention aims to provide a forum for an exchange of knowledge and experiences in planning and housing and to develop mutual cooperation in these fields.

All heads of departments, chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send not more than two (2) participants each, preferably senior officials involved in the fields of planning and housing to the convention on officiaI time with reasonable expenses of transportation, per diems and conference fees, in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344 dated November 26, 1973, as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127, Series of 1973, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 of this Office dated August 8, 1972. Participants residing or stationed within the Greater Manila Area shall be entitled to reasonable expenses for transportation and conference fees only.

The participants shall submit to their respective office heads a complete report on their attendance or participation in the convention as prescribed under Memorandum Circular No. 987 dated June 10, 1977, furnishing this Office a copy thereof.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, December 1, 1978

Source: Presidential Management Staff
