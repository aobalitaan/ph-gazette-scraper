TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 615

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 598 DATED SEPTEMBER 18, 1972, CREATING AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE PREPARATION AND ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE HOLDING OF THE 6TH ANNUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS OF THE ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK TO BE HELD IN MANILA ON APRIL 26-28, 1973.

Memorandum Circular No. 598 dated September 18, 1972, creating an Executive Committee to take charge of the preparation and arrangements for the holding of the 6th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of the Asian Development Bank to be held in Manila on April 26-28, 1973, is hereby amended to read as follows:

The Committee may call upon heads of departments, bureaus and offices for such assistance as it may need in the accomplishment of its mission.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, January 11, 1973
