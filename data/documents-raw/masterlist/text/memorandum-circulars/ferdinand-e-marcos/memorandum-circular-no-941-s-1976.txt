TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 941

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 938 DATED OCTOBER 8, 1976 ENTITLED “AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE REGIONAL SEMINARS AND ANNUAL CONVENTION OF THE GOVERNMENT PROSECUTORS LEAGUE OF THE PHILIPPINES.”

Memorandum Circular No. 983 of this Office, dated October 8, 1976, authorizing the attendance of government prosecutors in the regional seminars and annual convention of the Government Prosecutors League of the Philippines (GPLP), in conjunction with the U.P. Law Center to be held at Cagayan de Oro City on October 12-14, 1976, for Mindanao government prosecutors; at Bacolod City on October 28-30, 1976, for Visayan government prosecutors; and at Baguio City on November 24-26, 1976, for Luzon government prosecutors, where the League will also hold its Annual National Convention and election of officers for 1977, is hereby amended so as to limit the expenses of any one participant to not exceeding ₱676.00.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, October 29, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
