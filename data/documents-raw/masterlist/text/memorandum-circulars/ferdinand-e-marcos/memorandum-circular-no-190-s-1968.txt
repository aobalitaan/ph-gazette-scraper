OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 190

AUTHORIZING THE ATTENDANCE OF CERTAIN FEMALE OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE OUTSTANDING CITIZENS AWARDS CEREMONIES.

The traditional awarding ceremonies to outstanding citizens of the Republic of the Philippines sponsored by the Civic Assembly of Women of the Philippines (CAMP) in conjunction with the annual observance of Independence Day will be held at Malacañang Heroes Hall on June 11, 1968, at 4:30 o’clock in the afternoon.

All female elective officials and duly designated delegates to the Assembly, as well as five ranking women officials in each department, bureau, office and government corporation, arc hereby authorized to attend the Ceremonies on official time.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, June 1, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
