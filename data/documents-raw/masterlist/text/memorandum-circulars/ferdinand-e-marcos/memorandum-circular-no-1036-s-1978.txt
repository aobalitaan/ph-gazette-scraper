TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1036

REQUIRING MEDICAL EXAMINATION OF ALL PARTICIPANTS IN THE PAGLILINGKOD: BAGONG LIPUNAN PROGRAM.

In order to prevent incidents of illness in the field, all officials and employees going on barangay service under the Paglilingkod: Bagong Lipunan shall undergo a medical check-up before departing for rural work, and the assignment of those who are weak shall be deferred.

The medical officers in the various departments, offices, bureaus and agencies of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, shall take charge of the medical examination and certification herein required. The certificate of weak health issued by the medical officer shall be understood as made under his oath as such.

For the information and guidance of all concerned.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, January 17, 1978

Source: Presidential Management Staff
