OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 20

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE WILOCI-SPONSORED SYMPOSIUM ON ANTI-SMUGGLING.

The UP Women Lawyers’ Circle (Wiloci), an association of law alumna of the state university, will held a symposium-forum on anti-smuggling at 2:00 p.m. on March 11, 1966, at the GSIS Auditorium, Manila.

This symposium-forum is timely in view of the imperative need of focusing special attention to the smuggling problem and the measures that are and still have to be taken to effectively combat it in the interest of the general welfare.

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices which are particularly concerned with combatting smuggling in all its forms are hereby urged to send not more than two officials and employees from their respective offices to this symposium-forum. Their attendance therein will be on official time.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, March 1, 1966

Source : Presidential Management Staff
