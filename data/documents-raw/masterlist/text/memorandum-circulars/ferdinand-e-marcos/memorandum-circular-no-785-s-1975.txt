TANGGAPAN NG PAGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 785

URGING LADY OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE TO JOIN THIS RALLY IN CONNECTION WITH THE OBSERVANCE OF INTERNATIONAL WOMEN’S YEAR.

The United Nations proclaimed 1975 as International Women’ Year. This is the first time it ever happened in contemporary history. To celebrate this event the Civic Assembly of Women of the Philippines is holding a rally on January 6, 1975 at 4:30 P.M. at the Cultural Center of the Philippines. The occasion will be highlighted by a ecumenical service to be officiated by Archbishop Jaime L. Sin and the Keynote Speech of the First Lady, Mrs. Imelda Romualdez Marcos.

In view of the significance of the occasion, all heads of departments, bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations in the greater Manila area are hereby authorized to allow their lady employees to join the rally to manifest their support to the important role of women in the economic, social and moral progress of our countrymen. Their attendance shall be on official time with entitlement to reasonable transportation expenses chargeable against the funds of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, January 3, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
