TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 576

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 572 DATED MAY 18, 1972, ENTITLED “URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE TENTH ANNUAL NATIONAL CONVENTION OF THE BUREAU DIRECTORS’ ASSOCIATION, INC. (BUDIRAS).”

Memorandum Circular No. 572 dated May 18, 1972, authorizing attendance in the 10 th Annual National Convention of the Bureau Directors’ Association, Inc. (BUDIRAS), is hereby amended so as to authorize the participants in the said convention expenses for transportation, subsistence and lodging, in addition to convention fees, at rates pursuant to the provisions of R.A. No. 3847, as implemented by GAO Circular No. 88-A, series of 1964, all chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, May 30, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
