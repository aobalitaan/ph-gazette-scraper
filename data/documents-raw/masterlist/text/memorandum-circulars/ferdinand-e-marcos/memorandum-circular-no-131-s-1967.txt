OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 131

URGING ATTENDANCE OF CERTAIN GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES IN THE SEMINAR ON RURAL MEDICINE.

A seminar on rural medicine will be hold in Manila on October 11-13, 1967 under the joint auspices of certain government and private entities.

In view of the importance of this activity to the Administration’s efforts toward health education and consciousness especially in the rural areas, the Department of Health is hereby authorized to allow the attendance therein of rural health officers and nurses on official tine only, subject to the limitation that there shall not be more than four (4) participants from each province or city.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, October 6, 1967

Source: Presidential Management Staff
