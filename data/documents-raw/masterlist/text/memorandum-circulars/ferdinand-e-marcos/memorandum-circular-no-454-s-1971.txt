OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 454

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 420 DATED DECEMBER 14, 1970 ENTITLED “CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY THE PROPOSAL FOR THE CUSTOMS ARRASTRE SERVICE, BUREAU OF CUSTOMS, TO TAKE OVER THE OPERATION OF THE NORTH HARBOR ARRASTRE.”

Memorandum Circular No. 420 dated December 14, 1970, entitled “Creating a Committee to Study the Proposal for the Customs Arrastre Service, Bureau of Customs, to Take over the Operation of the North Harbor Arrastre,” is hereby amended, so as to designate Mr. Vicente Guzman, Officer-in-Charge, Bureau of Labor Standards, as member of the Committee in lieu of Director Ruben F. Santos.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, April 1, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
