TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 620

The periods for the preparation and review of staffing patterns of departments, offices and agencies of the Government are hereby extended as follows:

(a) Preparation and submission of staffing patterns by agency staff – February 15, 1973; and

(b) Review of staffing patterns jointly by the representatives of the agency concerned, the Budget Commission and the Presidential Commission on Reorganization – March 1, 1973.

Government-owned or controlled corporations are not covered by this Order.

By authority if the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, January 23, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
