Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO 1188

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1180 DATED JANUARY 16, 1980 CREATING AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE PREPARATION AND ARRANGEMENT FOR THE HOLDING OF THE 13TH ANNUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS, ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK, TO BE HELD IN MANILA FROM APRIL 30 TO MAY 2, 1980.

Memorandum Circular No. 1180, as amended, creating an executive committee to take charge of the preparation and arrangement for the holding of the 13th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors, Asian Development Bank, to be held in Manila from April 30 to May 2, 1980,is hereby further amended to include the Officer-in-Charge, Ministry of Public Information, as Member of the Executive Committee.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, April 29, 1980

Source: Presidential Management Staff
