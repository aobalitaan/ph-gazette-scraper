OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 469

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINAR-WORKSHOP FOR ADMINISTRATORS ON APPLIED NUTRITION.

The National Food and Agriculture Council will conduct a 2-day Seminar Workshop for Administrators on Applied Nutrition in Manila on May 26-27, 1971, in connection with the initial implementation of a Coordinated National Food and Nutrition Program in 10 provinces in the Luzon, Visayas and Mindanao regions.

The attendance of official time of not more than two (2) representatives each, preferably the head and assistant head of the bureaus, commissions and offices mentioned in the attached list, is hereby authorized.

The participants shall submit a report on their attendance to their respective agency heads within thirty (30) days after the seminar.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, May 19, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
