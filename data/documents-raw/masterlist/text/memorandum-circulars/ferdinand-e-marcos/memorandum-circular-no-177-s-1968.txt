OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 177

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE ANNUAL NATIONAL CONFERENCE OF THE GOVERNMENT PROSECUTORS’ LEAGUE OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC.

The Government Prosecutors’ League of the Philippines, Inc. (GPLF), will hold its 17th Annual National Conference in Quezon City on April 24-28, 1968.

The Conference is primarily aimed at further improving the efficiency of the prosecuting arm of the government,

All provincial and city fiscals, as well as other accredited members of the League, are hereby authorized to attend the convention on official time with expenses for transportation, subsistence, lodging, .and conference fees, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and  auditing requirements: Provided, however, That an adequate force shall be left in their respective offices so as not to disrupt the normal course of official business thereat.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, April 17, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
