TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (Office of the President of the Philippines)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1012

REQUIRING APPROVAL BY THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF ALL NATIONAL GOVERNMENT CONTRACTS INVOLVING TWO MILLION PESOS AND ABOVE.

Effective immediately, all contracts of national government offices, agencies and instrumentalities, including government-owned or controlled corporations involving two million pesos (₱2,000,000.00) and above, awarded through public bidding or by negotiation, shall be subject to the approval of the President of the Philippines.

Any such contracts entered into without the approval required heroin shall be null and void and of no force and effect.

All orders, rules or regulations inconsistent herewith are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

By order of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, October 19, 1977

Source: Presidential Management Staff

Memorandum Circular No. 1012, October 19, 1977
