Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1047

CREATING THE INTER-AGENCY SERVICES COMMITTEE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF YOUTH AND SPORTS DEVELOPMENT

Pursuant to the Section 7 of LOI No. 662 dated January 25, 1978, there is hereby created an Inter-Agency Service Committee of the Department of Youth and Sports Development (DYSD) to be chairmanned by the representative of the DYSD and composed of the Senior Management level representatives of the following line agencies:

The Committee shall, among others:

1. Coordinate the formulation and implementation of the DYSD programs and projects to achieve a harmonized effort at the execution level.

2. To coordinate the monitoring of DYSD programs and projects and determine problem and bottleneck areas.

3. To facilitate the active involvement and participation of the different concerned agencies at the planning and execution levels.

4. To serve as coordinating point in the involved agencies and as expediter for the effective implementation and programming of physical fitness and sports development projects

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, February 22, 1978

Source: Presidential Management Staff
