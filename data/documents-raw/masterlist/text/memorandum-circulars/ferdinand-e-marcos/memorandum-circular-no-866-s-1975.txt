TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 866

ENJOINING ALL DEPARTMENTS, BUREAUS, OFFICES, AGENCIES, AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE GOVERNMENT TO OBSERVE UNITED NATIONAL WEEK.

WHEREAS, the country celebrates United Nations Week from October 24 to 30 every year;

WHEREAS, the United Nations Organization has been an instrument of peace and understanding among nations during the past three decades;

WHEREAS, there is a necessity to focus public attention on the important role played by the United Nations in the promotion of international peace and understanding;

Wherefore, all departments, bureaus, offices, agencies and Instrumentalities of the government are hereby enjoined to observe United Nations Week through the holding of various programs, exhibits, symphosia and other activities informing of the functions and activities of the United Nations.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, September 29, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
