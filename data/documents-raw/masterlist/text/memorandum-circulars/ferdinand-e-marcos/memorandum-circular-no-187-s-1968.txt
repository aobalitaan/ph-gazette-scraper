OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 187

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE OF CERTAIN GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE ANNUAL GENERAL MEETING OF THE LEGAL OFFICERS ASSOCIATION

The Legal Officers Association of the Philippines will hold its annual general meeting on June 3, 1968, at the GSIS Social Hall, Manila, to discuss, among other things, matters relating to the official functions of its members.

Heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send their officials and employees who are members of the Association to this annual general meeting provided that their attendance shall be on official time only and that they shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance in the meeting.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, May 23, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
