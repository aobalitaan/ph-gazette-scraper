OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 337

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY THE SALARY SCALES OF OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS.

There is hereby created a Committee to study the salary scales of officials and employees of government-owned or controlled corporations with a view to standardizing salaries in said Corporations, composed of the following:

All departments, bureaus, offices, agencies or instrumentalities of the government, particularly government-owned or controlled corporations are hereby required to extend full support and to provide such information as may be needed by the Committee in the accomplishment of its work.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, March 25, 1970
