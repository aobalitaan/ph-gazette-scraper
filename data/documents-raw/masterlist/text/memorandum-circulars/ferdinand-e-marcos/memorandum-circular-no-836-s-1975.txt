TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 836

REQUIRING ALL GOVERNMENT OFFICES AND AGENCIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS IN THE GREATER MANILA AREA TO SEND CONTINGENTS TO THE “HANDOG SA KABATAAN” PARADE ON SATURDAY, MAY 24, 1975.

In order to provide full government participation in the “Handog sa Kabataan” parade on Saturday, May 24, 1975, all heads of government offices and agencies, as well as government-owned or controlled corporations, in the Greater Manila Area, are hereby required to send contingents to the above-mentioned parade, including cultural groups, schedule to start at 7:00 A. M. Assembly time will be at 6:30 A. M. at the Nayong Pilipino grounds, near Pamilihang Bayan.

Compliance by all concerned are hereby enjoined.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, May 23, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
