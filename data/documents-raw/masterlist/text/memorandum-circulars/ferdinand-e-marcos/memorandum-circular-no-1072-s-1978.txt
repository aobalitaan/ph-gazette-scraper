TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1072

AUTHORIZING THE ATTENDANCE OF ALL OFFICERS AND MEMBERS OF THE LOCAL ADMINISTRATION DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM ALUMNI ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES (LADPAAP) IN MEETINGS, CONFERENCES OR CONVENTIONS CALLED BY EITHER THE NATIONAL PRESIDENT OR THE REGIONAL PRESIDENT OF THE ASSOCIATION.

All officers and members of the Local Administration Development Program Alumni Association of the Philippines (LADPAAP) when attending meetings, conferences, or conventions called by either the National President or the Regional President of the association in accordance with its Constitution and By-Laws, shall be authorized on official time with entitlement to reasonable expenses for transportation, per diems, and registration fees per participant, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344 dated November 26, 1973, as implemented by COA General Circular No.127, series of 1973, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 of this Office dated August 8, 1972. Participants residing or stationed within the site of the meeting, conference or convention shall be entitled to reasonable expenses for transportation and registration fees only.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, July 28, 1978

Source: Presidential Management Staff
