TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 928

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 903 AUTHORIZING THE SENDING OF DELEGATES TO THE TWO-DAY REGIONAL CONFERENCES OF THE GOVERNMENT ASSOCIATION OF CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANTS (GACPA).

Memorandum Circular Ho. 903 authorizing the sending of delegates to the two-day regional conferences of the Government Association of Certified Public Accountants (GACPA), is hereby amended as follows:

The regional conference for Regions I, II, III, IV & V to be held in Metro Manila is Hereby rescheduled to be conducted on September 30 to October 2, 1976.

The penultimate paragraph is likewise amended to read as follows:

“In view of the relevance of the objectives of these conferences to national and local development programs, all heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus, offices, chartered cities and provincial governments, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send not more than two (2) of their CPAS who are members of the GACPA or such members of their office staff as are involved in accounting, auditing, budgeting and fiscal administration in the region. Those attending shall be allowed reasonable expenses for transportation and other necessary expenses for subsistence and lodging in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344- dated November 26, 1973, as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127, series of 1973, including seminar fees, chargeable against the funds of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.”

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, August 25, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
