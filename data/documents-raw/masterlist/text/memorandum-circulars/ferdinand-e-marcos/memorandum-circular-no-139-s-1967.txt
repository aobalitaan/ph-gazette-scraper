OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 139

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 134 DATED OCTOBER 24, 1967.

Memorandum Circular No. 134 of this Office dated October 24, 1967, as amended by Memorandum Circular No. 137 dated December 6, 1967, is hereby amended so as to authorize the attendance of all delegates to the recently concluded 20th Annual Convention and Symposium of the Philippine Government Employees Association (PGEA) on official business, i.e., with expenses covering transportation, and subsistence and convention fees, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, December 15, 1967

Source: Presidential Management Staff
