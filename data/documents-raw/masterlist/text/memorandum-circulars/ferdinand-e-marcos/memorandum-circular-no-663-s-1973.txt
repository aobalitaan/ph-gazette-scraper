TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 663

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 326 DATED DECEMBER 15, 1972, CREATING A TASK FORCE TO PREPARE THE IMPLEMENTING DETAILS FOR THE ORGANIZATION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF LOCAL GOVERNMENT AND COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT.

Memorandum Order No. 326 dated December 15, 1972 which created a Task Force to prepare the implementing details for the organization of the Department of Local Government and Community Development, is hereby amended so as to extend the work of the Task Force up to September 30, 1973.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, September 7, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
