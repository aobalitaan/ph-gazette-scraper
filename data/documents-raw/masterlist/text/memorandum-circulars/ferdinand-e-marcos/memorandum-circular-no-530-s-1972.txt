TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 530

ANNOUNCING THE LOCATION OF THE OFFICE OF THE LEAGUE OF PROVINCIAL GOVERNORS AND CITY MAYORS OF THE PHILIPINES.

The League of Provincial Governors and City Mayors of the Philippines is now holding office at Room 301 of the Administration Building, Office of the President, Jose P. Laurel St., Manila.

Provincial Governors and City Mayors who may wish to avail themselves of the liaison work being rendered by the League to its members in connection with their numerous official transactions with the difference offices under the executive department may address their communications to this above address.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, January 10, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
