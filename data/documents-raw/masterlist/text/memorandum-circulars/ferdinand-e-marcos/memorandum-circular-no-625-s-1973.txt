TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 625

OCCUPANCY OR USE FOR ANY PURPOSE OF ANY FLOOR OR PORTION OF THE CONGRESS BUILDING.

Pending completion of the property and records inventory now being conducted in the Congress of the Philippines, and until a decision is made on the disposition of the Congress building, no official/agency of the government shall occupy or use any floor or portion thereof for any purpose, except the members of the Caretaker Forces of both Houses and the Commission on Appointments.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, February 28, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
