OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 197

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 193 OF THIS OFFICE DATED JUNE 19, 1968, URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINAR ON THE CIVIL SERVICE LAW AND RULES SPONSORED BY THE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION.

Memorandum Circular No. 193 of this Office dated June 19, 1968, urging attendance in the seminars on the Civil Service Law and Rules, is hereby amended so as to include Lanao del Norte among the places embraced in the regional seminar to be held in Cagayan de Oro City on November 27-29, 1968.

By the authority of the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, July 23, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
