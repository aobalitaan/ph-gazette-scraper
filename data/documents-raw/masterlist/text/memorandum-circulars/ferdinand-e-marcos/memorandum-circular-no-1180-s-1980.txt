TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1180

CREATING AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE PREPARATION AND ARRANGEMENT FOR THE HOLDING OF THE 13TH ANNUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS, ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK, TO BE HELD IN MANILA FROM APRIL 30 TO MAY 2, 1980.

There is hereby created an Executive Committee to assist in the preparation and arrangement for the holding of the 13th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors of the Asian Development Bank, which will be held in Manila from April 30 to May 2, 1980, and constituted as follows:

The Committee may call upon heads of departments, bureaus and other offices of the government, as well as private organizations/associations, for such assistance as it may need for the accomplishment of its mission.

This Circular takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, January 16, 1980

Source: Presidential Management Staff
