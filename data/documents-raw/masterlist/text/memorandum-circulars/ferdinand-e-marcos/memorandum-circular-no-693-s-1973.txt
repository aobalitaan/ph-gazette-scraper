TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 693

AUTHORIZING THE DETAIL/ASSIGNMENT OF CERTAIN GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE STAFF OF THE BSP GOLDEN JUBILEE JAMBOREE.

In view of the pressing need for the services of certain government officials and employees in the Staff of the Golden Jubilee Jamboree to ensure the success of the Jamboree, the detail/assignment of all government officials and employees who are members of the Staff thereof, is hereby authorized from December 15, 1973 to January 15, 1974.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, December 26, 1973
