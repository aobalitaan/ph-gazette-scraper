Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1228

AUTHORIZING THE KASALIGAN, CESDP CLASS XI, TO HOLD A THREE-DAY WORKSHOP ON DECEMBER 11-13, 1981, AT THE DEVELOPMENT ACADEMY OF THE PHILIPPINES IN TAGAYTAY CITY

In order to have ample time for thorough discussion and to finalize the recommendations of the KASALIGAN, CESDP CLASS XI, to minimize the delay in the processing of papers by government officials and employees, the members of the Class are hereby authorized to hold a three-day Workshop on December 11-13, 1981, at the Development Academy of the Philippines in Tagaytay City.

For this purpose, the members of the Class and the resource speakers indicated in the attached list, are hereby authorized reimbursement of seminar fee of FOUR HUNDRED PESOS (P400.00) each and reasonable transportation expenses pursuant to the provisions of R. A. No. 3867, as amended, and as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127, series of 1973, chargeable against the funds of their respective offices, subject to the availability thereof and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the president: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, December 9, 1981

Source: Presidential Management Staff
