TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 744

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 743 DATED MAY 31, 1974, BY REQUIRING ALL OFFICES CONCERNED TO FURNISH THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT CERTAIN DATA REGARDING RE-EMPLOYED EMPLOYEES OF THE DEFUNCT CONGRESS AND COMMISSION ON APPOINTMENTS.

Memorandum Circular No. 743 dated May 31, 1974, providing guidelines regarding the payment of gratuities of separated and/or retired personnel of the defunct Congress and the Commission on Appointments, including those re-employed in the Government service, is hereby amended by adding a new paragraph to read as follows:

“x x x

“4. It shall be mandatory upon all offices concerned to furnish the Office of the President of the name, date of employment and salary or any re-employed employees of Congress and/or the Commission on Appointments to facilitate the computation of the amount to be refunded by such re-employed employees to the Government.”

Strict compliance herewith is hereby enjoined.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, June 10, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
