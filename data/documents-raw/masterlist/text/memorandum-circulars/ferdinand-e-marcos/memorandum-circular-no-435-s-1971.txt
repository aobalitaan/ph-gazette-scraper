OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 435

URGING THE PARTICIPATION OF GOVERNMENT PHYSICIANS IN THE SIXTH ASIAN CONFERENCE ON OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH.

The Sixth Asian Conference on Occupational Health will be held in Quezon City on February 24-27, under the suspices of the Philippine Association of Occupational Health.

This activity will afford the participants the opportunity to learn the latest developments in occupational health, considering the numerous scientific papers to be presented in the Conference.

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus, and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are therefore hereby authorized to send not more than two of their physicians to the Conference on official time. It is understood that their participation shall not involve any expense of the part of the government outside of salary.

The participants shall render a report to their respective agency heads on their attendance in the conference.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, February 15, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
