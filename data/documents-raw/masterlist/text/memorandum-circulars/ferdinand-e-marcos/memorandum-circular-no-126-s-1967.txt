OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 126

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE FOURTH NATIONAL CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE ASSOCIATION FOR GOVERNMENT BUDGET ADMINISTRATION.

The Philippine Association for Government Budget Administration (PAGBA), in cooperation with the Budget Commission, will hold its Fourth Annual National Convention on September 13-15, 1967, in Quezon City, on the theme: Towards Fiscal Administration Improvement.

The convention will reassess the present status of public fiscal administration with a view to pinpointing the problem areas and seeking ways and means to improve the system of financial management in government.

Heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send not more than two (2) of their employees concerned with budgeting work to attend the convention. As a cooperating agency, the Budget Commission may send not more than fifteen (15) delegates, excluding convention officials. Their attendance, however, shall be on official time only.

The delegates shall submit to their respective agency heads a report of their attendance and participation in the convention.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, July 31, 1967

Source: Presidential Management Staff
