OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 377

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 375 URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE “EXECUTIVE SEMINAR ON LAND REFORM IN THE 70’S.”

Memorandum Circular No. 375 dated July 30, 1970 urging attendance in the “Executive Seminar on Land Reform in the 70’s” is hereby amended so as to include the following offices in the list of agencies entitled to send representatives to the said Seminar:

Bureau of Forestry U.P, College of Agriculture National Cottage Industries Development Authority National Waterworks and Sewerage Authority Bureau of Public Highways Bureau of Public Works Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office Family Planning Agency

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, July 31, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
