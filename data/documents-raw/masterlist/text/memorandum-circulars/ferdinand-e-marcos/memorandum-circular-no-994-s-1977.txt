TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (Office of the President of the Philippines)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 994

URGING GOVERNMENT AGENCIES CONCERNED TO GIVE MAXIMUM SUPPORT AND ASSISTANCE TO THE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION IN THE CONDUCT OF THE 1977 CAREER SERVICE PROFESSIONAL AND SUB-PROFESSIONAL (LOCAL GOVERNMENT) EXAMINATIONS.

WHEREAS, the Civil Service Commission is charged with the duty of administering the Career Service Professional and Sub-professional (Local Government) examination;

WHEREAS, the Career Service Professional and Subprofessional (Local Government) examination is scheduled to be given on November 20, 1977 in Regions I – XIII;

WHEREFORE, the Department of National Defense, through the Office of the Undersecretary for Civilian Relations, the Philippine Constabulary, the Philippine Air Force, the Philippine Navy, the National Police Commission, and the National Intelligence and Security Agency, are requested to extend maximum support and assistance to the Civil Service Commission to enable full security and smooth administration of the 1977 Career Service Professional and Subprofessional (Local Government) examinations.

The National Media Production Center, the Bureau of Telecommunications and the National Computer Center are likewise requested to extend maximum support and assistance relative to the dissemination of essential information on the holding of the examinations, the utilization of communication facilities throughout the country, and in the computer processing of the results, respectively.

The Commissioner of Customs is hereby requested to exempt from ordinary inspection, if legally feasible, all classified materials pertinent to the Career Service Professional and Subprofessional (Local Government) examinations.

The Administrator of Civil Aeronautics is hereby requested to give the adequate assistance and support in the transportation of personnel and materials from the Civil Service Commission to the examination centers throughout the country which are serviced by air transportation.

The Secretary of Education and Culture, the Secretary of Finance, the Chairman of the Commission on Audit, whose representatives may be designated by the Chairman or the Regional Director of the Commission, to compose the local civil service examining committee to be established in all provincial/city capitals where the examinations are going to be held, are requested to extend maximum support and assistance in all matters relative to the conduct and administration of the examinations.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, July 12, 1977
