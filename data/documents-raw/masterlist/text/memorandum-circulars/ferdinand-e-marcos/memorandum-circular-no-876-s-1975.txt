TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 876

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE ANNUAL CONVENTION OF THE CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE OFFICERS GUILD.

The Career Executive Service Officers Guild, composed of graduates of the Career Executive Service Development Program of the Development Academy of the Philippines, will hold its annual convention at the Academy’s premises in Tagaytay City on December 12-13, 1975.

The theme of the convention will bear on the new Civil Service Decree of the Philippines, Presidential Decree No. 907. Workshops and plenary sessions on the different aspects of the decree and the election of officers of the Guild will be held.

All graduates of the Career Executive Service Development Program are hereby authorized to attend the said convention on official time with convention fees, per diems and traveling expenses, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective officers, in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344 dated November 26, 1973, as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127 dated November 27, 1973, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 of this Office dated August 8, 1972.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, November 11, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
