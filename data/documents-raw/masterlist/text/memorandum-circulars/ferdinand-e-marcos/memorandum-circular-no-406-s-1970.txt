OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 406

REQUIRING PROMPT AND EARLY DISPOSITION OF ALL CASES PRESENTED BY THE PRESIDENTIAL COORDINATING COMMITTEE FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND AGRARIAN REFORMS.

All heads of departments, bureaus, and offices are hereby required to take immediate action on all cases presented by the Presidential Coordinating Committee for Social Justice and Agrarian Reforms (PCCSJAR) towards the prompt and early disposition of said cases.

When the cases involved can be decided by a particular department or agency, such cases should be disposed of within a period of thirty (30) days from the date of receipt thereof.

All other cases which require court action should be followed up with the end in view of expediting action thereon.

Periodic reports on all cases pending and/or disposed of by the offices concerned should be submitted to the Office of the President:

Strict compliance herewith is desired.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 31, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
