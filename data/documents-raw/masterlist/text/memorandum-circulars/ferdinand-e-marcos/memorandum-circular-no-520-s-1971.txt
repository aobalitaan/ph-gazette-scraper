OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 520

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO FORMULATE GUIDELINES FOR IMPLEMENTING THE RESOLUTION OF THE OIL INDUSTRY COMMISSION APPROVING IN PRINCIPLE THE PROPOSAL TO RESTORE FILIPINO OWNERSHIP OF FILOIL REFINERY CORPORATION.

There is hereby created a Committee to formulate guidelines for implementing the resolution of the Oil Industry Commission approving in principle the proposal to restore Filipino ownership of Filoil Refinery Corporation, to be composed of the following:

The Committee is enjoined to follow the conditions provided in the resolution of the Oil Industry Commission dated November 15, 1971.

It is further enjoined to submit its recommendations to the President as soon as possible.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, December 3, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
