OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 399

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE ANNUAL CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE ASSOCIATION OF MECHANICAL AND ELECTRICAL ENGINEERS.

The Philippine Association of Mechanical and Electrical Engineers (PAMEE) will hold its 40th Annual Convention in Cebu City on October 22-25, 1970, on the theme “The Total Engineer.”

The purpose of the convention are to introduce modern engineering and management tools and techniques which are useful for an engineer’s purposeful involvement in nation-building, and to achieve a higher degree of professionalism in mechanical and electrical engineering.

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send one of their mechanical or electrical engineers to the said convention on official business, that is, with official time and expenses for transportation, subsistence and lodging, and convention fees, if any, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

The delegates shall submit to their agency heads a report on their attendance in the convention within thirty (30) days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, October 7, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
