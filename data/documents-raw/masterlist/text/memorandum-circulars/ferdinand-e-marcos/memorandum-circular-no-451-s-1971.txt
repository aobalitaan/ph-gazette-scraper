OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 451

URGING ATTENDANCE OF CERTAIN EMPLOYEES IN THE TENTH NATIONAL CONFERENCE OF THE GOVERNMENT TRAINING OFFICERS ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC. (GTOAP)

The Government Training Officers Association of the Philippines, Inc., (GTOAP) will hold its tenth national conference on May 17-19, 1971, at the Amang Hall, Provincial Capitol Building, Pasig, Rizal, on the theme “THE CONTINUING CHALLENGE OF HUMAN RESOURCES DEVELOPMENT.”

The objective of the conference is to find ways and means by which the Association could help in accelerating the development of the national economy and strengthening the moral fiber in the government service.

Considering the importance of this conference, all heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, within the Greater Manila area are hereby authorized to send to the conference not more than two officials involved in training activities, preferably those who are members of the association. They shall be entitled to expenses for transportation and conference fees, all chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

The delegates shall submit to their agency heads a report on their attendance in the conference within 30 days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 31, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
