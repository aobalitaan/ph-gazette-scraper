TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 705

CONSTITUTING A COMMITTEE TO DRAFT THE RULES AND REGULATIONS TO IMPLEMENT THE MEMORANDUM OF THE PRESIDENT DATED JANUARY 22, 1974 PERTAINING TO THE MAINTENANCE OF A HIGH QUALITY OF AIRLINE AND POWER SERVICES.

There is hereby created a Committee to draft the rules and regulations to implement the Memorandum of the President dated January 22, 1974, pertaining to the maintenance of a high quality of airline and power services. The Committee shall be composed of representatives from the following government agencies:

Office of the Executive Secretary complaints and Investigation Office (CIO) Department of Trade Civil Aeronautics Board Specialized Regulatory Boards National Electrification Administration National Power Corporation

Acting Chief Quirico P. Evangelista of the CIC is hereby designated as Chairman of the Committee.

The Committee is hereby authorized to call on any department, bureau, office, and agency of the government, including government-owned and controlled corporation for such assistance as may be necessary for the early completion of its tasks. It shall submit the necessary draft of the rules and regulations within fifteen (15) days from the date hereof, for the approval of the President.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, February 20, 1974
