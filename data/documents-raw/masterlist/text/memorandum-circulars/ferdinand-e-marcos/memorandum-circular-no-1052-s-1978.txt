TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1052

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE ANNUAL CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE SOCIETY OF AGRICULTURAL ENGINEERS (PSAE)

The Philippine Society of Agricultural Engineers will hold its annual convention on April 28-29, 1978, at the Midtown Ramada Hotel, on the theme: “AGRICULTURAL ENGINEERS ON COUNTRYSIDE DEVELOPMENT.”

Heads of departments, chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, with functions or positions requiring the employment of agricultural engineers, are hereby authorized to send not more than three (3) agricultural engineers each, preferably members of the Society, to the convention on official tine with reasonable expenses for transportation and a convention fee of not more than ₱150.00 per participant, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No.344 dated November 26,1973, as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127, series of 1973, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 of this Office dated August 8, 1972. Participants residing or stationed within the Metro Manila Area shall be entitled to convention fees only.

The participants shall submit to their respective office heads a report on their attendance or participation in the convention as prescribed under Memorandum Circular No. 987 dated June 10, 1977, furnishing this Office a copy thereof.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, March 28, 1978

Source: Presidential Management Staff
