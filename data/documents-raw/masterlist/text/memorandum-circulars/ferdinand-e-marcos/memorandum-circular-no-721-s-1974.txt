TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 721

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 658 DATED AUGUST 23, 1973

Memorandum Circular No. 658 dated August 23, 1973, requiring the wearing of uniforms by lady employees in the service of the national government, is hereby amended, so as to exempt them from wearing uniforms during the later months of their period of pregnancy and during the period of mourning for the death of an immediate member of their family such as parent, child, brother or sister.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, April 15, 1974
