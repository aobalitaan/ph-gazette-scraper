TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 686

AUTHORIZING THE ATTENDANCE OF GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL WHO ARE MEMBERS OF THE BSP GOLDEN JUBILEE JAMBOREE STAFF IN THE JAMBOREE STAFF EXERCISE ON NOVEMBER 30 TO DECEMBER 2, 1973

In connection with the holding of a Jamboree Staff Exercise on November 30 to December 2, 1973, to test the Jamboree facilities and arrangement to ensure the success of the Golden Jubilee Jamboree of the Boy Scouts of the Philippines to be held next month at the Mt. Makiling National Scout Reservation, the attendance in the Jamboree Staff Exercise of all government personnel whoa re members of the Jamboree Staff is hereby authorized on official business with expenses for transportation and per diems for the duration thereof.

All departments, bureaus and offices including government-owned or controlled corporations concerned should be guided accordingly.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, November 29, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
