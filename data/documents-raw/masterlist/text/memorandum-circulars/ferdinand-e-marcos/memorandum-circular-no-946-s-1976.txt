TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 946

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 943 AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE 30TH ANNUAL CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE INSTITUTE OF CERTIFIED PUBLIC ACCOUNTANTS.

Memorandum Circular No. 943 dated November 8, 1976, authorizing attendance in the 30th Annual Convention of the Philippine Institute of Certified Public Accountants at the Philippine International Convention Center on November 18-20, 1976, is hereby amended so as to authorize government-owned or controlled corporations to send a reasonable number of CPA participants to the convention on official time, with expenses chargeable against the funds of their respective offices, subject to the same terms and conditions specified in the aforesaid Circular.

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, November 19, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
