TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 543

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINAR FOR ALL ACTION OFFICERS, THE CHAIRMAN AND MEMBERS OF SPECIAL ACTION COMMITTEES OF ALL GOVERNMENT OFFICES IN NORTHERN AND CENTRAL LUZON.

The Malacañang Special Action Unit, under the Complaints and Investigation Office (CIO formerly PARGO) will conduct a seminar for Action Officers of the various government offices in Northern Central Luzon at the Teachers Camp, Baguio City, on April 6 and 7, 1972. The Seminar has the following purposes:

1. To acquaint the participants with the objectives, organization and functions of the Special Action Unit;

2. To familiarize the participants with the policies and operating procedures of the Special Action Unit;

3. To define the role and responsibilities of Action Officers and the Chairmen and Members of the Action Committees;

4. To study representative problems and cases brought to the attention of action officers;

5. To effect better coordination among the Action Officers in all offices under the Executive Branch.

Considering the importance of this Seminar, all Heads of Departments, Chiefs of Bureaus and Offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations in Northern and Central Luzon are hereby authorized to send to the Seminar their Action Officers and the Chairmen and Members of Action Committees on official time; Superintendents of Schools and Heads of Regional Offices or their representatives are likewise enjoined to attend the Seminar on official time. They shall be entitled to expenses for transportation and per diems, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a report of their attendance and participation in the seminar within 30 days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, February 15, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
