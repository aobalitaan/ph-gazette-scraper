OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 301

URGING ATTENDANCE IN A SEMINAR TO BE SPONSORED BY THE MANAGEMENT PRACTITIONERS’ ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES (MaPAP).

The Management Practitioners’ Association of the Philippines (MaPAP), a non-profit technical and professional association of government and private management specialists, will hold a seminar on “Adjustment of International Management to Different Cultures,” on Tuesday, November 18, 1969, from 1:00-5:00 p.m. at Insular Life Auditorium. Insular Life Bldg. Ayala Avenue, Makati, Rizal.

The affair will mark the 6th anniversary of the MaPAP, an organization that has been consistently devoted to the improvement of public administration through better management. Dr. Erich Mittelsten Scheid, newly-elected President of the Council International pour d’ Organisation Scientifique (CIOS), will be the seminar leader.

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby enjoined not to send more than three of their ranking officials and all other personnel who are members of the association to the seminar on official time.

Further information on the seminar may be secured from the MaPAP Secretariat, Management Service, Budget Commission.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, November 17, 1969

Source : Presidential Management Staff
