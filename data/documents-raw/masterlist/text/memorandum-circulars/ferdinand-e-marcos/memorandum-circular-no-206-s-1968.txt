OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 206

In accordance with the crash training program of the Government for members of local police forces which is one of the measures designed to improve peace and order conditions of the country, provincial governors, city mayors and other provincial/city officials are her by enjoined to supplement national efforts by:

1. Providing training site with facilities for classroom, billeting and mess;

2. Preparing and scheduling the police members who will undergo the basic police training course to be offered under POLCOM direction.

The prospective police trainees should be, as far as practicable, limited to those who were admitted to the service without the requisite police training.

Close coordination should be maintained with the POLCOM to achieve the objectives of this circular.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

Manila, October 14, 1968

Source : Presidential Management Staff
