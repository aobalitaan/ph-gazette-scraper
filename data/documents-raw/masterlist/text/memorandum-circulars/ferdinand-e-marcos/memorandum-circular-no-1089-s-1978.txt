TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1089

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE 9TH PHILIPPINE MANAGEMENT CONGRESS.

The Philippine Council of Management (PHILCOMAN) will sponsor the 9th Philippine Management Congress to be held at the Crystal Ballroom of the Hyat Regency Manila on October 24-26, 1978. The theme of the Congress is “Strategy Management: New Applied Concepts and Techniques”.

Management experts, practitioners, educators, and government leaders from the Philippines and other countries will participate as speakers, discussants and delegates. The Management Congress will explore the role of the Management in total development in the light of the rapid political and social changes in the Asean region as well as the new priority areas, growing opportunities and expanding responsibilities of Administrators and Executives.

The Congress will enable participants to gain new insight into recent trends and developing in business management and public administration.

In view hereof, all heads departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby enjoined to authorized attendance with expenses for conference fee and reasonable transportation allowance in the congress of selected participants on official time and not exceeding three (3) from their respective office.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a report of their attendance with their observations and comments on their deliberations on the Congress.

Interested parties may contact the PHILCOMAN Secretariat with offices at Rm-311 Nos. 96-47-01, 98-60-46 and 98-96-61 local 48.

By authority of the President:

(SGD) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, October 6, 1978
