TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES) MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 569

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE MEETING AND OTHER OFFICIAL ACTIVITIES OF THE RURAL BROADCASTER’S COUNCIL

The Rural Broadcaster’s Council, an independent organization of rural broadcasters and farm writers and other public information officers, has been coordinating with the government in disseminating valuable information on modern agricultural techniques, rural development and other important projects of the government for social and economic advancement, particularly cooperative movement.

In view of the importance of this Council’s contribution to the government’s information program especially towards the establishment of cooperatives, all heads of departments, bureaus and offices indicated in the attached list are hereby authorized to send on official time their respective public information officers and/or any personnel performing similar functions to the meetings and other activities to be held at least twice a month by the Rural Broadcaster’s Council.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, April 19, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
