OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 495

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO.485 DATED JULY 26, 1971.

Memorandum Circular No. 485 dated July 26, 1971 constituting a Management Team to study the problems and deficiencies of the Bureau of Posts and to recommend solutions therefor is hereby amended so as to extend the period of time within which the Committee shall submit its recommendations from one (1) to two (2) months.

This extension is based on the recommendation of the Acting Secretary of Finance to enable the Committee to likewise conduct a complete study on the problems and deficiencies involving the Customs Postal Division, Bureau of Customs.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, August 23, 1971

Source : Presidential Management Staff
