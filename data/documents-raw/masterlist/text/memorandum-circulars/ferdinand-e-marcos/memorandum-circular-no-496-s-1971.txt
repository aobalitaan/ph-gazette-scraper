OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 496

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE EIGHTH NATIONAL CONFERENCE OF THE PHILIPPINE ASSOCIATION OF SOCIAL WORKERS

The Philippine association of Social Workers will hold its 8th National Conference at the SSS Magsaysay Hall on November 25-26, 1971, on the theme: “Social Welfare and the Constitution.”

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, as well as government-owned or controlled corporations and local subdivisions, are hereby authorized to send not more than two (2) of their personnel who are members of the Association to the Conference on official time only and subject to the condition that the exigencies of the service will permit attendance.

The participants shall submit to their, respective-office leads a report on their attendance in the conference.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, August 26, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
