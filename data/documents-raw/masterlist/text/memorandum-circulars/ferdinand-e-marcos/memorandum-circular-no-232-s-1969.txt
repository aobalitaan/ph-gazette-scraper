OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 232

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE 10TH NATIONAL CONVENTION OF THE ASSOCIATION OF PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT ARCHITECTS.

The Association of Philippine Government Architects (APGA) will hold its 10th National Convention on February 25-28, 1969 at Quezon City, on the theme “The Role of government Architects in Nation Building,”

The convention will he highlighted by an education and technical seminar intended to keep architects in the government service abreast of advanced design and construction techniques.

In view of the laudable purpose of the convention, all department heads and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send their architects or engineers, if any, to the convention on official tine provided that, in the case of offices employing several architects and/or engineers, the number of participants Shall be limited to not more than five (5), preference to be given to those who are members of the association.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, February 17, 1969

Source : Presidential Management Staff
