OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 179

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE FIFTH NATIONAL ANNUAL CONFERENCE OF THE GOVERNMENT SUPPLY OFFICERS ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC. (GSOAPI)

The Government Supply Officers Association of the Philippines, Inc. (GSOAPI) with the technical assistance of the Department of General Services, the Civil Service Commission, and the Management Service of the Budget Commission, will hold its Fifth National Annual Conference either in Manila or Quezon City on June 5-7, 1968, on the theme: “THE ROLE OP SUPPLY MANAGEMENT IN SUPPORT TO THE INFRASTRUCTURE PROGRAM OF A DEVELOPING NATION.”

The conference will discuss, among other things, how effective supply management could help in the speedy attainment of the goals of the Administrations infrastructure projects.

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, chartered cities, and provincial and municipal governments, as well as government-owned or controlled corporation, are hereby authorized to send not more than two of their supply officers or property custodians, preferably those who are members of the GSOAPI, to the conference. Their attendance shall be on official business, that is, with expenses for transportation, board and lodging, and conference fees, all chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual audit.

The delegates shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance in the conference.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, April 22, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
