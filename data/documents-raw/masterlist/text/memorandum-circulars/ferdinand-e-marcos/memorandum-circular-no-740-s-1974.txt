TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 740

AUTHORIZING DAILY ALLOWANCE FOR PARTICIPANTS IN THE CAREER-EXECUTIVE SERVICE DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM OF THE DEVELOPMENT ACADEMY OF THE PHILIPPINES.

WHEREAS, the Development Academy of the Philippines, in pursuance of Presidential Decree No. 336 dated November 14, 1973, is conducting a Carrer Executive Service Development Program in Tagaytay City for the period from April 21 to June 30, 1974;

WHEREAS, under Memorandum Circular No. 708 dated March 4, 1974, the participants in the residential executive course being conducted by the Philippine Executive Academy at Baguio City from April 16 to June 13, 1974, have been granted a daily allowance during the session as authorized by General Circular No. 127 dated November 27, 1973, of the Commission on Audit;

WHEREFORE, the participants in the aforementioned Career Executive Service Development Program of the Development Academy of the Philippines in Tagaytay City are hereby likewise granted the same daily allowance as authorized by the said COA General Circular No. 127.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, May 23, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
