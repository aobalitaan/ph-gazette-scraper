OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 294

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINAR WORKSHOP GROUP DYNAMICS AND CURRICULUM DEVELOPMENT OF THE PHILIPPINE NURSES ASSOCIATION.

The Philippine Nurses Association, with the assistance of the Department of Health, the Bureau of Private Schools and the Community Development Center of the Presidential Arm on Community Development, will hold a Seminar Workshop on Group Dynamics and Curriculum Development, as follows:

In view of the importance of the seminar-workshop, heads of government institutions/college of nursing or their representatives (only one from each institution) and not more than three officials from the assisting government agencies are hereby authorized to participate in the aforementioned activity. Their attendance shall be on official time and their expenses for transportation, subsistence, lodging and conference fees shall be chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices/institutions, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, October 24, 1969

Source : Presidential Management Staff
