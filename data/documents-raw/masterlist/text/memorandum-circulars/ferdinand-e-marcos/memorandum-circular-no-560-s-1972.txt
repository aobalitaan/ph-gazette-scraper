TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 560

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE ANNUAL SCIENTIFIC CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE ASSOCIATION OF HOSPITAL DENTISTS.

The Philippine Association of Hospital Dentists will hold its 9th Annual Scientific Convention on May 15-16, 1972, at the United Drug Laboratories, Inc., United Avenue, Mandaluyong, Rizal.

The Office of the Secretary of Health, the Bureau of Dental Services and chiefs of government hospitals, are hereby authorized to send not more than three (3) of their dentists who are members of the Association to attend the convention on official tine with expenses for transportation, subsistence and lodging, and convention fees, all chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements. Those delegates stationed in the Greater Manila Area shall be entitled to reasonable transportation expenses and convention fees only.

The participants shall submit to their respective office heads a report on their attendance in the convention within thirty (30) days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 23, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
