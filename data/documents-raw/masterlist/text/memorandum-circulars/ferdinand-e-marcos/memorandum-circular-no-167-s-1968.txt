OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 167

FILING OF INCOME TAX RETURNS BY GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES.

All officials and employees of the Government are hereby reminded to file their income tax returns on time, i.e., not later than April 15, 1968, in accordance with Commonwealth Act No. 466, as amended.

It is hereby warned that those who fail to comply herewith shall suffer dismissal from the service, in addition to the penalties imposed by law.

For the information and guidance of all concerned.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, March 19, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
