TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 647

AMENDING THE MEMBERSHIP OF THE HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT TEAM UNDER MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 643

In view of the importance and significance of the coming of a World Bank mission on Housing and Urban Development in July and October, 1973 the membership of the counterpart team created under Memorandum Circular No. 643 is hereby amended to include a Representative of the Department of Public Works, Transportation and Communication as a member of the said committee.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, 2 July 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
