TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 870

ENJOINING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES, ENTITIES, AND INSTRUMENTALITIES TO SUPPORT AND PARTICIPATE IN THE “PISTAHAN SA KAUNLARAN ‘ 75″ OR NATIONAL AGRIBUSINESS AND FOOD FAIR OF THE FOOD TERMINAL, INC.

The Food Terminal, Inc. is launching the second “PISTAHAN SA KAUNLARAN,” a national agribusiness, food and cultural fair, which will start on November 21, 1975, and to last until December 21, 1975.

The exposition will highlight the combined interrelated and mutually dependent efforts of the public and private sectors in the production of food, agricultural raw materials, export products, and other industries vital to the country’s economic development. The fair will also feature cultural, entertainment and sports events to insure maximum exposure of products and services promoted by participants. All these are aimed at encouraging and promoting greater production both in goods and services, and enhancing the socio-cultural life of our people to the end that the aims and purposes and the long-range objectives of the New Society may be fully achieved.

All heads of departments, chiefs of bureaus and offices, agencies and instrumentalities of the government, both national and local, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby enjoined to support and take part in the “PISTAHAN SA KAUNLARAN ’75” or National Agribusiness and Food Fair of the Food Terminal, Inc. For this purpose, they are authorized to disburse the necessary funds in connection with their participation, subject to the availability thereof and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 of this Office dated August 8, 1972.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, October 9, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
