OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 254

AUTHORIZING THE SENDING OF DELEGATES TO THE FIFTH NATIONAL CONVENTION AND CONFERENCE OF THE ASSOCIATION OF GOVERNMENT CIVIL ENGINEERS OF THE PHILIPPINES.

The Association of Government Civil Engineers of the Philippines (AGCEP) will hold its Fifth National Convention and Conference of Government Civil Engineers at the City Council Session Hall, Quezon City Hall, Diliman, Quezon City, on May 27-31, 1969 on the theme “The Government Civil Engineer – Instrument of Progress.”

The conference will be highlighted by a realistic assessment and a careful and thorough analysis the problems that affect the affective implementation of the Administration’s economic development and infrastructure programs.

Departments proper, bureaus, offices, chartered cities and provincial governments, as well as government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send not more than three (3) of their civil engineers who are members of the AGCEP to this convention. Various public works and public highways engineering districts nay send one representative each. The limitation as to number excludes conference officials. These attending shall be allowed reasonable expenses for transportation, subsistence, lodging and conference fees, chargeable against the appropriation of their respective offices, subject to availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirement.

The delegates shall submit a report of their attendance to their agency heads.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, April 23, 1969

Source : Presidential Management Staff
