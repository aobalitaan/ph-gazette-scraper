Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 125

AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES TO LEAVE THEIR OFFICES ON STAGGERED ARRANGEMENT FOR THE PURPOSE OF REGISTERING WITH COMMISSION ON ELECTIONS.

In order to accelerate the registration of voters currently being undertaken by the Commission on Elections, all heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including provincial, city and municipal governments, as well as government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby directed to permit their employees to leave their offices a staggered arrangement for the purpose of registering with the Commission.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, July 31, 1967

Source: Malacañang Records Office
