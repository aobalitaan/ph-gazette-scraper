OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 269

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 244 OF THIS OFFICE DATED MARCH 19, 1969, URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINARS ON THE CIVIL SERVICE LAW AND RULES SPONSORED BY THE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION.

Memorandum Circular No. 244 of this Office dated March 19, 1969, urging attendance in the seminars on the Civil Service Law and Rules, is hereby amended so as to authorize the holding of the third in the series of these seminars in Cauayan, Isabela, on July 23-25, 1969, instead of in Tuguegarao, Cagayan, on July 9-11, 1969.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, June 25, 1969

Source : Presidential Management Staff
