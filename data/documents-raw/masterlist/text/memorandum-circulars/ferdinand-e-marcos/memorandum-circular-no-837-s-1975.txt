TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

The Committee shall provide directional guidance and render coordinating assistance to a Secretariat headed by a ranking official that will be formed by the Department of Foreign Affairs to service the requirements of the meeting.

The Committees may call upon the heads of the Departments, bureaus, and offices not indicated above as may be necessary in the performance of their functions and responsibilities.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, June 2, 1975
