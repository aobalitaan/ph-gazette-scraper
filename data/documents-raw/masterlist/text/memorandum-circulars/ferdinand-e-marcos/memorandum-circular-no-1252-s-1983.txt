Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1252

CREATING AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE PREPARATION AND ARRANGEMENT FOR THE HOLDING OF THE 16TH ANNUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS, ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK, TO BE HELD IN MANILA FROM MAY 4 TO 6, 1983.

There is hereby created an Executive Committee to assist in the preparation and arrangement for the holding of the 16th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors, Asian Development Bank, which will be held in Manila from May 4 to 6, 1983.

Hon. ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Chairman, PCC-ADB
