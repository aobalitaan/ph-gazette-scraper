OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 449

URGING THE ATTENDANCE OF MUNICIPAL HEALTH OFFICERS IN THE NATIONAL CONVENTION OF THE ASSOCIATION OF MUNICIPAL HEALTH OFFICERS OF THE PHILIPPINES (AMHOP).

The Association of Municipal Health Officers of the Philippines (AMHOP) will hold its national convention on May 3-5, 1971, in Manila.

In this connection, each provincial health officer is hereby authorized to send to the convention not more than two (2) participants who shall be chosen from among the municipal health officers within the province, and whose services can be dispensed with. Their attendance shall be on official time only, that is, at no expense on the part of the government outside of salary.

The participants shall submit to their respective provincial health officers a report on their attendance within 30 days after the convention.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 31, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
