MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR No. 13

INFORMING OF THE AVAILABILITY IN THE BUREAU OF PRINTING OF THE OFFICIAL CALENDAR AND PICTURE OF THE PRESIDENT.

The Director of Printing informs that copies of the official government calendar and picture re productions in color of the oil portrait of the President are now available at the nominal prices of P1.80 and P1.50 per copy, respectively. If ordered by mail, an additional P0.45 will be required to cover postage. The size of the calendar is 20” x 31 1/2, while that of the picture is 20” x 26”.

Purchase may be made directly from the Bureau of Printing.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, February 1, 1966
