OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 346

CREATING A TASK FORCE FOR ANTI-CARNAPING CAMPAIGN

There is hereby created a Task Force to be composed of anti-carnaping specialists from the different government agencies involved in anti-carnaping drive. The Task Force shall be organized by the Chief of Constabulary and shall be under his direct supervision and control. It shall be composed of representatives from the following departments and offices:

The Philippine Constabulary Police Commission The Land Transportation Commission The Department of Justice National Bureau of Investigation Representative (s) of Police Departments, Greater Manila Area The PES-DND Computer Center

All heads of national and local government offices shall extend their fullest support and cooperation and are enjoined to authorized the detail of personnel/equipment from their respective offices to the Task Force.

The Chief of Constabulary is directed to submit a report on compliance within a week from the date of this Memorandum Circular.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, April 22, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
