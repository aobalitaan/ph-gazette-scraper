TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 845

AUTHORIZING THE ATTENDANCE OF CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE OFFICERS AT A CONFERENCE ON NEW DIRECTIONS ON DEVELOPMENT ADMINISTRATION TO BE HELD AT THE DEVELOPMENT ACADEMY OF THE PHILIPPINES, TAGAYTAY CITY.

The Working Committee of the Career Executive Service Officers in collaboration with the Executive Director of the Career Executive Development Program of the Development Academy of the Philippines will hold a conference on “New Directions on Development Administration” at the Development Academy of the Philippines, Tagaytay City, on June 27-28, 1975.

All heads of departments, and chiefs of bureaus and offices are hereby authorized to send their Career Executive Service Officers, Sessions I-IV of the CESDP, to attend the conference on official time with reasonable expenses for transportation, per diems and conference fees, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344, dated November 26, 1973, as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127, dated November 27, 1973, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 of this Office dated August 8, 1972.

The Career Executive Service Officers shall submit to their respective office heads a report on their attendance in the conference within thirty (30) days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, June 18, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
