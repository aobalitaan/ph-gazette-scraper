TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 809

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE FIFTH CONGRESS/MANILA FORUM OF THE EASTERN REGIONAL ORGANIZATION FOR PLANNING AND HOUSING (EAROPH).

The Task Force on Human Settlements, in coordination with the Philippine Institute of Environmental Planners, will hold the fifth Congress/Manila Forum of the Eastern Regional Organization for Planning and Housing (EAROPH) at the Hotel Intercontinental, Manila, on March 16-22, 1975, on the theme “PLANNING HUMAN SETTLEMENTS AND THE ENVIRONMENT FOR NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT.”

In view of the importance and timeliness of the said Congress/Manila Forum, all heads of departments, chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send their officials and employees involved in human settlements and environmental welfare to the Congress on official time with reasonable expenses for transportation, and lodging, and conference fees in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No 344 dated November 26, 1973, as implemented by Commission on Audit (COA) General Circular No. 127, series of 1973, chargeable against the funds of their respective offices, subject to the availability thereof and the usual accounting and auditing requirements. It is understood that the participants residing within the site of the conference shall be entitled to transportation expenses and conferences fees only.

The participants shall submit to their department heads/bureaus chiefs a report on their attendance in the conference within thirty (3) days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, March 13, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
