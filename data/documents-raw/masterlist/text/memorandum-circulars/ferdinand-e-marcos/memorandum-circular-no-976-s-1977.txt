TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (Office of the President of the Philippines)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 976

ALLOWING PARTICIPATION ON OFFICIAL TIME OF CERTAIN GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE REFERENDUM-PLEBISCITE IN SOUTHERN PHILIPPINES ON APRIL 17, 1977.

In order to enable all persons in the government service who are duty-bound to take active part in the April 17 referendum-plebiscite in southern Philippines, they are hereby authorized to go to their respective cities or provinces for the purpose on official time.

All heads of departments, bureaus, offices or agencies of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby directed to see to the full participations of officials and employees in their respective offices who are legal residents of any of the places where the plebiscite will be held.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, April 15, 1977

Source: Presidential Management Staff
