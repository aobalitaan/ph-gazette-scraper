MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 129

The Management Group of Sultan Moh’d Amin Baab Torres Foundation (SMABTF) is launching the search for the “Twelve Most Outstanding Muslim Nationals” to give due recognition and reward to the Filipino Muslim individuals with outstanding contributions and achievements and who have shown exemplary conduct and ethical behavior as they strive for perfection and excellence in their chosen fields of endeavor in both public and private sectors.

All heads of departments, bureaus, offices, agencies, institutions and government owned and/or controlled corporations are requested to extend appropriate assistance in the conduct of the aforesaid search by the SMABTF.

For your compliance.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, April 19, 2007
