MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 21

AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES TO ATTEND ON OFFICIAL BUSINESS THE CONFERENCE ON HOUSING NEEDS OF GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES ENTITLED “BILIS SERVISYO NG PABAHAY ALAY SA KAWANI NG PAMAHALAAN” TO BE CONDUCTED BY PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES ASSOCIATION

The Philippine Government Employees Association (PGEA) has scheduled a Conference on Housing Needs of Government Employees entitled “Bilis Serbisyo ng Pabahay alay sa Kawani ng Pamahalaan” on April 30, 2002 at the Heroes Hall, Malacañang, Manila. The Conference is specifically designed to:

In this connection, all government departments, bureaus and offices including government owned and controlled corporations, state universities, or colleges and local government units are hereby authorized to send at least two representatives from management and the associations’ rank and file employees to the Conference.

In line with the priority concern of government on housing, the Housing and Urban Development Coordinating Council, Pag-ibig, GSIS and other concerned government agencies are likewise requested to extend the necessary support for the successful conduct of the said conference.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, 24 April 2002
