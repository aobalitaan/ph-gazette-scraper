MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 135

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE TO THE 44 th FOUNDATION DAY AND ALUMNI CONVENTION AND HOMECOMING OF THE NATIONAL DEFENSE COLLEGE OF THE PHILIPPINES

The National Defense College of the Philippines will celebrate its 44 th Foundation Day Anniversary and Alumni Convention and Homecoming on August 9-10, 2007.

The NDCP Symposium and Convention will be a good opportunity for the alumni to enhance their expertise in governance and decision-making.

In this connection, alumni of NDCP who are working in the government sector are authorized to attend, on official time, the 44 th Foundation Anniversary and Alumni Convention and Homecoming to be held on August 10, 2007, from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm at the EDSA Shangri-la Hotel, Ortigas Center, Mandaluyong City, and a Symposium to be held on same date from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, July 18, 2007
