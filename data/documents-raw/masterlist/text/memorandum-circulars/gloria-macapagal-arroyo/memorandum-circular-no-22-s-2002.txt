MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 22

AUTHORIZING GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES TO ATTEND ON OFFICIAL BUSINESS THE CONFERENCE ON HOUSING NEEDS OF GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES ENTITLED “BILIS SERBISYO NG PABAHAY ALAY SA KAWANI NG PAMAHALAAN” TO BE CONDUCTED BY THE PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES ASSOCIATION

The Philippine Government Employees Association (PGEA) has scheduled a Conference on Housing Needs of Government Employees entitled “Bilis Serbisyo ng Pabahay alay sa Kawani ng Pamahalaan” on June 4, 2002 at the Holiday Plaza Hotel, Cebu City and on June 27, 2002 at the Maguindanao Hotel, Davao City. The Conference is designed to:

In this connection, all government departments, bureaus and offices including government owned and controlled corporations, state universities, or colleges and local government units are hereby authorized to send at least two representatives from management and the associations’ rank and file employees to the Conference.

In line with the priority concern of government on housing, the Housing and Urban Development Coordinating Council, Pag-IBIG, GSIS and other concerned government agencies are likewise requested to extend necessary support for the successful conduct of the said conference.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, 13 May 2002
