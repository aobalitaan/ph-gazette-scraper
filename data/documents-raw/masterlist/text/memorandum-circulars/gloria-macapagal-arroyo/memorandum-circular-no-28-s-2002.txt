MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 28

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION IN THE GENERAL LAW PRACTICE INSTITUTE

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines-Iloilo Chapter will co-sponsor with the University of the Philippines Law Center a GENERAL LAW PRACTICE INSTITUTE FOR LAWYERS both in the government and private sectors on November 6-7, 2002 at the Amigo Terrace Hotel, Iloilo City.

All lawyers under the Executive Department in Region VI (Iloilo, Guimaras, Aktan, Capiz, Antique, and Negros Occidental Provinces) may attend the said activity on official time, that is, at no cost to the government except payment of their salaries.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, 29 October 2002
