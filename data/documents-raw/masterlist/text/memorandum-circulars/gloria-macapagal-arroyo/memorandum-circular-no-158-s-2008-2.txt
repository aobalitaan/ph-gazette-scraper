MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 158

AUTHORIZING THE PARTICIPATION ON OFFICIAL TIME OF ALL GOVERNMENT LAWYERS IN GREATER MANILA REGION IN IBP’s REGIONAL CONVENTION AND MCLE SEMINAR

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP), Greater Manila Region, will be holding its Regional Convention and Mandatory Continuing Legal Education (MCLE) Seminar on August 9 and 10, 2008, at the Pearl Manila Hotel, Ermita, Manila, and on August 14 to 16, 2008 at the Manila Hotel, Roxas Boulevard, Manila.

In this connection, all government lawyers in Greater Manila Region (Manila I to IV and Quezon City Chapter) are hereby authorized to attend the above-mentioned Convention and Seminar, on official time , at no cost to the government except payment of their salaries, provided such attendance will not adversely affect the functions of their respective offices as well as the delivery of services to the public.

Manila, 21 July 2008

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary
