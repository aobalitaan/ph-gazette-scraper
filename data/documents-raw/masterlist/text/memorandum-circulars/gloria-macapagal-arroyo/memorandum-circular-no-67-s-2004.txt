MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 67

TO: ALL HEADS OF DEPARTMENTS, CHIEFS OF BUREAUS AND OFFICES/INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE NATIONAL AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTS, AND OTHER CONCERNED

RE: DIRECTING THE OPERATIONALIZATION OF THE MINERALS ACTION PLAN FOR MINERAL RESOURCES DEVELOPMENT

Pursuant to Executive Order (EO) No. 270, dated January 16, 2004, as amended, the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) was directed to take the lead in the preparation of the Minerals Action Plan (MAP) in consultation with other agencies concerned and stakeholders.

Guided by EO No. 270, as amended, and after an extensive consultation, the MAP was formulated.

In the light of the foregoing, the operationalization of the MAP is hereby ordered, and all heads of departments, chiefs of bureaus and offices-instrumentalities of the national and local governments, and others concerned shall be guided by the following directives:

For strict compliance.

By authority of the President:

Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, September 13, 2004
