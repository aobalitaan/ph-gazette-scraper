MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 155

AUTHORIZING THE PARTICIPATION ON OFFICIAL TIME OF ALL GOVERNMENT LAWYERS IN CENTRAL LUZON REGION IN IBP’s REGIONAL CONVENTION AND MCLE SEMINAR

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP), Central Luzon Region, will be holding its Regional Convention and Mandatory Continuing Legal Education (MCLE) Seminar on June 5 to 7, 2008 at the Lagenda Hotel, El Centro, Subic Bay Freeport Zone, Olongapo City.

In this connection, all government lawyers in Central Luzon Region (Bataan, Bulacan, Nueva Ecija, Pampanga, Pangasinan, Tarlac and Zambales) are hereby authorized to attend the above-mentioned Convention and Seminar on official time, at no cost to the government except payment of their salaries, provided such attendance will not adversely affect the functions of their respective offices as well as the delivery of services to the public.

By authority of the President:

Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, 14 April 2008
