MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 65

In view of the continuous heavy rains and the flooding of the streets in Metro Manila today caused by the exit of typhoon “Marce” and the probable entry of typhoon “Nina” (International name- “Chaba”), work in all government offices, including government-owned and controlled corporations, is hereby suspended the whole day today. Government agencies shall maintain skeletal work force to maintain government service. Those agencies whose function is the delivery of basic services and response to disaster and calamities shall continue to be in their place of work.

The Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, August 26, 2004
