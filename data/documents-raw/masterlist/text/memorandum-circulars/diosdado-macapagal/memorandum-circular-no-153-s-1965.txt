MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR No. 153

REQUIRING ALL HEADS OF DEPARTMENTS, BUREAUS, OFFICES, AGENCIES AND GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO SUBMIT INVENTORY AND STATUS REPORTS ON THEIR RESPECTIVE OFFICES ON OR BEFORE DECEMBER 27, 1965.

For the purpose of giving the incoming Administration a correct picture of the situation in their respective offices, it is hereby directed that all heads of departments, bureaus, offices, agencies and government-owned or controlled corporations submit to this Office an inventory of pending matters, a general outline of policies, accomplishments and other general conditions of their respective offices.

These reports must be in this Office on or before December 27, 1965.

By authority of the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary

Manila, December 22, 1965
