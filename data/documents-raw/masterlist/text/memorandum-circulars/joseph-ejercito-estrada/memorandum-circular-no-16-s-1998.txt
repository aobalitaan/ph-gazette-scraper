Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 16

In view of the continuing inclement weather brought about by Typhoon “Loleng”, work in all government offices, including government owned or controlled corporations, are hereby suspended effective 12:00 noon today, October 23, 1998.

It is understood that vital government agencies concerned with rescue operations and manning other vital public services should maintain effective skeletal forces in their respective offices until 5:00 P.M. or beyond as the exigency of the services requires.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

Manila, October 23, 1998.
