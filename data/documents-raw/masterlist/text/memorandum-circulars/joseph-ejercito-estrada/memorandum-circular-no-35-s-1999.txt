Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 35

DIRECTING ALL NON-CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE OFFICIALS OCCUPYING CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE POSITIONS TO CONTINUE, IN A HOLD-OVER CAPACITY, TO PERFORM THEIR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

Pursuant to existing laws and jurisprudence, all non-Career Executive Service Officials (non-CESO) occupying Career Executive Service (CES) positions shall continue, in a hold-over capacity, to perform their duties and discharge their responsibilities until their successors have been appointed and have qualified.

All official actions of said non-CES officials while in such hold-over capacity are deemed legitimate.

This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30 th day of June 1999.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
