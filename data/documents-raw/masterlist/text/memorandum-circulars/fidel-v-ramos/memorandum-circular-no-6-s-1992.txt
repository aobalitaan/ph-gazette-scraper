MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 6

REQUIRING ALL HEADS OF GOVERNMENT OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO SUBMIT COMPLETE INFORMATION ON THE COMPENSATION BENEFITS OF MANAGEMENT OFFICIALS AND MEMBERS OF THE BOARD IN THEIR RESPECTIVE OFFICES

For the information and guidance of the Office of the President, all heads of government owned and/or controlled corporations are required to submit to this Office within one (1) week from receipt hereof, a detailed information on total compensation, including allowances and other benefits (in cash or in kind) being received by management officials and members of the corporation.

For purposes of uniformity, the required information should be submitted in accordance with the attached form.

This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately

Manila, September 1992

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) DIONISIO C. DE LA SERNA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
