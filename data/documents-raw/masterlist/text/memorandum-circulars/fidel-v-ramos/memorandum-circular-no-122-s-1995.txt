Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 122

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION IN THE 5TH NATIONAL CONVENTION OF LAWYERS

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines will hold its 5th National Convention on the theme “Modernizing the Law Profession for the 21st Century,” which will be held on June 28-29, 1995 in Mandaluyong City.

In this connection, heads of departments, bureaus, offices or agencies, as well as local government units up to the municipality level, are hereby authorized to send one (1) representative holding a lawyer position in their respective offices in Metro Manila to attend the Convention on official time, with entitlement to reasonable expenses for transportation only, chargeable against funds of their respective offices, subject to the availability thereof and to accounting and auditing requirements.

The participants shall within fifteen (15) days thereafter, submit to their respective agency heads, copy furnished this Office, a complete report on their attendance in said activity. Likewise, within thirty (30) days after the Convention, the organizers shall submit to this Office, and a list of participants indicating their respective offices, copy furnished the Civil Service Commission.

By authority of the President. (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

Manila, June 23, 1995
