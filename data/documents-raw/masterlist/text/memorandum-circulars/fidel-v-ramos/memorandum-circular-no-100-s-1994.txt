MALACAÑANG Manila

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 100

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS, AGENCIES AND OFFICES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO EXTEND ASSISTANCE AND SUPPORT TO THE PHILIPPINE INSTITUTE OF ARCHITECTS IN THE HOLDING OF THEIR NATIONAL CONVENTION ON FEBRUARY 23-26, 1995 TO ENSURE ITS SUCCESSFUL OBSERVANCE AND URGING NON-GOVERNMENT ORGANIZATIONS TO EXTEND THEIR FULL COOPERATION THEREOF

The Philippine Institute of Architects will hold a National Convention on February 23-26, 1995, at Subic in Olongapo City.

All government departments, agencies and offices, including government-owned and controlled corporations, are directed to extend their assistance and support to the convention to ensure its successful observance.

Non-government organization and those in the private sector are also urged to extend their full cooperation to this meaningful undertaking.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 1st day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Four.

(SGD.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR Executive Secretary
