Office Of The President Of The Philippines Malañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 95

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE 25TH ANNIVERSARY AND 7TH NATIONAL CONGRESS OF THE NATIONAL SOCIAL ACTION COUNCIL

The National Social Action Council (NASAC) will hold its 25th Anniversary and 7th National Trisectoral Congress in Manila on June 30-July 1, 1994, on the theme; “Working Together Towards Sustainable Development”.

The participation in the congress of national and local government officials and employees invited by the sponsors is hereby authorized on official time only, provided there will be no disruption of normal office activities/service in their respective agencies by reason thereof.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a complete report on their attendance or participation in the congress. Likewise, the NASAC is requested to submit to this Office and the Civil Service Commission a general report and evaluation of the congress including a list by offices of participants from the government.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, June 1, 1994
