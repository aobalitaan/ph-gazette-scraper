Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 77

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 73, DATED SEPTEMBER 8, 1993, ENTITLED “AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE ANNUAL CONFERENCE OF THE PHILIPPINE PUBLIC HEALTH ASSOCIATION, INC. (PPHAI).

Memorandum Circular No. 73, dated September 8, 1993, entitled, “Authorizing Attendance in the Annual Conference of the Philippine Public Health Association, Inc., (PPHAI),” is hereby amended, so as to change the date of the holding of the conference from October 14-15 to October 21-22, 1993

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR Executive Secretary

Manila, October 21, 1993
