Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 50

DIRECTING DEPARTMENTS, BUREAUS, OFFICES AND AGENCIES OF THE GOVERNMENT TO EXTEND SUPPORT AND ASSISTANCE TO THE BOY SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES IN THE HOLDING OF THE FIRST ASEAN JAMBOREE

In order to complement the government’s efforts of fostering ASEAN regional unity and cooperation, the President has issued Memorandum dated 13 May 1993 endorsing the holding by the Boy Scouts of the Philippines of the First ASEAN Jamboree from December 28, 1993 to January 3, 1994 at Mount Makiling, Los Baños, Laguna.

Accordingly, all departments, bureaus, offices and agencies of the government are hereby directed to extend their optimum support and appropriate assistance to the Boy Scouts of the Philippines in the holding of this endeavor.

This Memorandum Circular takes effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila this 25th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Three.

Bu authority of the President: (Sgd.) ANTONIO T. CARPIO Chief Presidential Legal Counsel
