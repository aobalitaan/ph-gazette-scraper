Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 17-A

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 17 ON THE SCHEDULE OF THE OP-WIDE PLANNING WORKSHOP

Pursuant to the provisions of Memorandum Circular No. 17, the schedule of the Planning Workshop to be conducted by the Office of the President is hereby moved from November 6 to 8, 1992 to November 4, 5:00 p.m., to November 7, 1992.

In this regard, attendance to the workshop should be confirmed with the Presidential Management Staff not later than Friday, October 30, 1992.

All workshop participants are directed to assemble at the Lobby, Administration Building, Malacañang (enter Gate 7) at 3:00 p.m. on November 4, 1992.

For strict compliance.

By authority of the President (Sgd.) EDELMIRO A. AMANTE Executive Secretary

October 28, 1992
