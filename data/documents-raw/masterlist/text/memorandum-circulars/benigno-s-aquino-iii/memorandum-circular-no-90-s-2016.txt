Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 90

CONSTITUTING AN INTER-AGENCY TASK FORCE TO CRAFT A HOUSING AND RESETTLEMENT FRAMEWORK FOR THE NLEX C-5 LINK PROJECT

WHEREAS, the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) Concession Agreement consists of three (3) phases, one of which is the construction of a 7.85 kilometer road, known as the NLEX C-5 Link Project, that would link the NLEX and the C-5 Road;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Development Plan recognizes the crucial role of infrastructure in supporting the performance of the country’s economic sectors and ensuring equitable access to services, especially those affecting the people’s health, education, and housing; and

WHEREAS, the construction of the NLEX C-5 Link Project may cause the displacement of occupants within the area.

NOW THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

Section 1. Creation. An Inter-Agency Taskforce (IATF) is hereby constituted to craft a housing and resettlement framework for the occupants who will be affected by the NLEX C-5 Link Project.

The IATF is hereby directed to submit its report to the Office of the President within ninety (90) days from the issuance of this Order.

Section 2. Composition. The IATF shall be composed of representatives from the following agencies:

The DILG shall constitute the Secretariat of the IATF.

Section 3. Assistance and Cooperation. All other departments, agencies, bureaus and offices of the government are hereby ordered to coordinate and extend assistance to the IATF to carry out its functions pursuant to this Circular.

Section 4. Repeal. All orders, issuances, or parts thereof, which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Circular, are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

Section 5. Separability. If any provision of this Circular is declared invalid or unconstitutional, the other provisions not affected thereby shall remain valid and subsisting.

Section 6. Effectivity. This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 28th day of January, in the year of our Lord Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
