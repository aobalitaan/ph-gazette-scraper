MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 46

Upon the recommendation of the National Disaster Risk Reduction and
Management Council (NDRRMC), and in view of the continuing inclement weather,
work in the executive department and classes in all levels in Metro Manila, are
hereby suspended effective 3:00 PM today, 11 June 2018.

However, those agencies whose functions involve the delivery of basic and
health services, preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or the
performance of other vital services, shall continue with their operations and render
the necessary services.

The suspension of work for private companies and offices is left to the
discretion of their respective head.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.
