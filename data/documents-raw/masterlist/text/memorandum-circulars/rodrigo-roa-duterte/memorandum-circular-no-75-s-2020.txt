MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 75

Upon the recommendation of the National Disaster Risk Reduction and
Management Council (NDRRMC), and to ensure safety, work in government offices
and classes in all levels in the Province of Batangas shall continue to be suspended
on 14 January 2010 onwards until further directed by the local government units
concerned.

However, those agencies whose functions involve the delivery of basic and
health services, preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or the
performance of other vital services shall continue with their operations and render the
necessary services.

x
The private sector in the Province of Batangas is highly encouraged to suspend
work for the safety of their employees.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

Manila, 13 January 2020.
