MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 73

Upon the recommendation of the National Disaster Risk Reduction and
Management Council (NORRMC), and in view of the continuing inclement weather
brought about by Typhoon Tisoy, work in government offices and classes in schools
(public and private) at all levels in Metro Manila, are hereby suspended effective 12:00
noon today, 03 December 2019,

However, those agencies whose functions involve the delivery of basic and health
services, preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or the performance of
other vital services shall continue with their operations and render the necessary
services,

The suspension of work for private companies and offices left to the discretion of
their respective heads.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

Manila, 03 December 2019,
