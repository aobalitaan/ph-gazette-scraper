MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 39

Upon the recommendation of the Inter-agency Organizing Committee
for the Philippine hosting of the 12" Conference of the Parties to the
Convention on the Conservation of Migratory Species of Wild Animals (CMS
COP12), and to ensure the successful, secure and efficient holding of the
event, work in government agencies holding office at the Philippine
International Convention Center (PICC) is hereby suspended for the duration
of the CMS COP12 from 22-28 October 2017.

To ensure the continuous delivery of public service, the affected
agencies are hereby directed to carry out their functions in their respective
business and/or satellite offices.

The Office of the ASEAN Secretariat at the PICC main building shall
remain open and perform their normal functions during the said period.

For compliance.
