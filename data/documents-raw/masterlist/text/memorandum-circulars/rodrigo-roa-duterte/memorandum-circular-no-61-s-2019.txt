MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 61

Upon the recommendation of the National Disaster Risk Reduction and
Management Council (NDRRMC), and in order to ease up the load on the public
transport system, work in the government offices within the National Capital
Region (NCR) is hereby suspended on 23 April 2019. Governors and city mayors
of affected provinces and cities, respectively, are hereby directed to assess the
damage sustained by their respective localities and to take the necessary
measures to ensure public safety.

However, those agencies whose functions involve the delivery of basic
and health services, preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or

the performance of other vital services shall continue with their operations and
render the necessary services.

The suspension of work in private companies and offices is left to the
sound discretion of their respective employers.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

i se.
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary

Manila, 23 April 2019
