Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 2

CREATING AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE PREPARATION AND ARRANGEMENT FOR THE HOLDING OF THE 19TH ANNUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS, ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK, TO BE HELD IN MANILA FROM APRIL 30 TO MAY 2, 1986

There is hereby created an Executive Committee to assist in the preparation and arrangement for the holding of the 19th Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors, Asian Development Bank, which will be held in Manila from April 30 to May 2, 1986, composed of the following:

The Committee may call upon heads of departments, bureaus and other offices of the government, as well as private organizations associations, for such assistance as it may need for the accomplishment of its mission.

This Circular takes effect immediately.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, March 13, 1986
