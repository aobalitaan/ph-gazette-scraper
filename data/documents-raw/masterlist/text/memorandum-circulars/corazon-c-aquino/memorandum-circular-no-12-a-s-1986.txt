Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 12-A

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO PREPARE AN OFFICIAL LIST OF BARANGAYS IN REGION XII.

There is hereby created a Committee to prepare an official list of barangays in Region XII composed of the following:

The Committee shall convene immediately and submit its report not later than December 31, 1986.

The Minister of the Budget shall make available to the Committee the sum of P200,000.00 from the funds of the Commission on Elections.

The Committee is hereby authorized to call upon any Ministry, bureau, office, agency or instrumentality of the government for such assistance as it may deem necessary in the discharge of its duties and functions.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, May 27, 1986
