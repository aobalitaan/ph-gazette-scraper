MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 38

In order to provide government employees full opportunity to properly observe
All Saints’ Day on 01 November 2023, and to allow them to travel to and from the
different regions in the country, work from home arrangement in government offices
shall be implemented; and asynchronous classes in public schools shall be conducted,
on 31 October 2023.

However, those agencies whose functions involve the delivery of basic and
health services, preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or the
performance of other vital services shall continue with their usual operations and
render the necessary services in person.

A similar arrangement for work private companies and classes in private
schools is left to the discretion of their respective heads.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

 

Manila, 27 October 2023
