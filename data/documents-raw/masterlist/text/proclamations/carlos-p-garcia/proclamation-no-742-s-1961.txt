MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PHILIPPINES OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 742

DECLARING MARCH 23 OF EVERY YEAR AS WORLD METEOROLOGICAL DAY.

WHEREAS, the Executive Committee of the World Meteorological Organization has approved the establishment of an Annual World Meteorological Day;

WHEREAS, the said committee has further decided that the World Meteorological Day should be celebrated on March 23, this being the anniversary of the day in 1950 when the World Meteorological Convention formally come into force; and

WHEREAS, the Philippines is a member-state of the World Meteorological Organizations

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CARLOS P. GARCIA, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare March 23 of every year as World Meteorological Day and designate the Weather Bureau to take charge of its celebreation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-one and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

(SGD.) CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) NATALIO CASTILLO Executive Secretary
