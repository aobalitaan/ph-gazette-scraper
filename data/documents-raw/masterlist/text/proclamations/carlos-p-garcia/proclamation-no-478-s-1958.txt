MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 478

DECLARING TUESDAY, JANUARY 28, 1958, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE AND CITY OF CAVITE

In commemoration of the ninety-seventh birthday of Julian Felipe, composer of the Philippine National Anthem, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 20 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Tuesday, January 28, 1958, as a special public holiday in the province and City of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twelfth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
