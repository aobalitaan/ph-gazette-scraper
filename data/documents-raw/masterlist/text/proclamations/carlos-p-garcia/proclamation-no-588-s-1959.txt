MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 588

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 400, DATED MAY 4, 1939, ESTABLISHING A PARK AND PLAYGROUND RESERVATION SITE IN THE CITY OF DAVAO AND OPENING THE SAME TO DISPOSITION IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 400, dated May 4, 1939, which reserved a parcel of land in the City of Davao containing an area of 199,114 square meters for park and playground purposes and open the same to disposition in accordance with the provisions of the Public Land Act.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President: JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
