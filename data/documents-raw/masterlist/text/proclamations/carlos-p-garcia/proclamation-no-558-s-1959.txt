MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 558

DECLARING THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 19, 1959, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN PAMPANGA AND TARLAC

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 128, dated May 4, 1949, as amended by Proclamation No. 374, dated December 26, 1956, declared February 19 of every year as Jose Abad Santos Day to honor the memory of the great jurist, patriot, and martyr; and

WHEREAS, the people of Pampanga and Tarlac request that they be given full opportunity to celebrate the hero’s birthday with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Thursday, February 19, 1959, as a special public holiday in the provinces of Pampanga and Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
