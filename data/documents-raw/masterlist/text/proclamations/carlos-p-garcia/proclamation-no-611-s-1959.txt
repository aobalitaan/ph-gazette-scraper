MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 611

FIXING AUGUST 30, 1959, FOR THE ORGANIZATION OF THE CITY OF DAVAO

Pursuant to the authority conferred upon me by section 90 of Republic Act No. 2364, creating the city of Cotabato, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby fix August 30, 1959, for the organization of the government of the city of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fourteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
