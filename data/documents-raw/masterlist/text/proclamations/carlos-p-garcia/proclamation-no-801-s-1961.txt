MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 801

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 787, DATED SEPTEMBER 20, 1961, BY TRANSFERRING THE CELEBRATION OF BUSINESS AND PROFESSIONAL WOMEN’S WEEK TO NOVEMBER 19-25, 1961

WHEREAS, the period from October 1 to 7, 1961, has been declared as Business and Professional Women’s Week under Proclamation No. 787, dated September 20, 1961;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Federation of Business and Professional Women’s Clubs has found it impossible for lack of sufficient time to celebrate said week;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 787, dated September 20, 1961, by transferring the celebration of Business and Professional Women’s Week to November 19-25, 1961.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixteenth.

[CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President: EDILBERTO B. GALLARES Assistant Executive Secretary
