MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 474

RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES A PORTION OF THE PUBLIC, DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUBANG, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provision of section 83 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for military purposes, under the administration of the Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of the public domain situated in the municipality of Lubang, Mindoro Occidental, particularly described in A. M. S. 8712, First Edition (AMS 1) 1914, Sheet, 3152 III, Pinagdagatan Point, Mindoro Occidental, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 (Lat. 13° 48′ 00″, Long. 180° 08′ 10″), equivalent to corner 16 of Project No. 14 of Forest Map, File No. 89, province of Mindoro, on, map sheet 3152 III, northeasterly direction from Barrio Ambolong; thence N. 89° 00′ OO” JE., 700 meters to point 2, equivalent to corner 15 of Project No. 14; thence S. 82° 00′ 00″ E., 1,293 meters to point 3, equivalent to corner 14 of Project No. 14; thence, due south 1,650 meters to point 4; thence due west, 850 meters to point 5; thence N. 59° 30′ 00″ W., 1,550 meters to point 6, equivalent to corner 17 of Project No. 14; thence N. 11° 00′ 00″ E., 1,050 meters to point 1, equivalent to corner 16, the point of beginning. Containing an area of 270 hectares, more or less.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-eight and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twelfth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
