RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 475

RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES A PORTION OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PASUQUIN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provision of section 88 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for military purposes, under the administration of the Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of the public domain situated in the municipality of Pasuquin, province of Ilocos Norte, particularly described in A. M. S. 8712, First Edition (AMS 1) 1944, sheet 3266 II, Pasuquin, Ilocos Norte, to wit:
