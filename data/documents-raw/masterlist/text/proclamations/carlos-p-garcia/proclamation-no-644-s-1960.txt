MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 644

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 9 TO 15 OF EVERY YEAR AS TRAFFIC SAFETY WEEK AND MARCH 16 OF EVERY YEAR AS SAFE-DRIVING DAY (S-D DAY) AND CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THEIR CELEBRATION

WHEREAS, the loss of life, limb and property resulting from traffic accidents reaches appalling proportions;

WHEREAS, it is the government’s duty to solve the critical traffic situation with a view to minimizing, if not totally eliminating, traffic accidents and the resultant loss of life, limb, and property; and

WHEREAS, in the furtherance of this duty, it is necessary to conduct an efficacious educational campaign by the use of leaflets, posters, banners, billboards, signboards, mass media, the radio, and other appropriate means to arouse traffic-safety consciousness and impel the observance of traffic and road courtesy rules and regulations;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from March 9 to 15 of every year as Traffic Safety Week and March 16 of every year as Safe-Driving Day (S-D Day).
