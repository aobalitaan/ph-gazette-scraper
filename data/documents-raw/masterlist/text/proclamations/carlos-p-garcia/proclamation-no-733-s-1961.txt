MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PHILIPPINES OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 733

RESERVING FOR THE COMPOSTING PLANT SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF DAGUPAN.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 83 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, CARLOS P. GARCIA, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for Composting Plant site purposes under the administration of the City of Dagupan, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, situated in the City of Dagupan, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot. No. 9095-A Bad-4017

A parcel of land Lot No. 9095-A, situated in the district of Bonuan Gueset, Dagupan City. Bounded on the NW along lines 9-10; 10-11; 11-12; 12-13 by Lingayen Gulf; on the NE along line 13-1; and 1-2 by Lot No. 8901-C, Bsd-3985 or Lot No. 9094; on the SE along line 2-3 by Lot No. 8262, Dagupan Cad.; on the SW along line 304; 5-6; 6-7; 7-8 by Lot No. 9095-F of the same subdivision and on the SW along line 8-9 by a barrio road which is Lot No. 9095-C. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 37 deg. 56’ W., 68.50 meters more or less from BBM No. 12, Dagupan Cadastre.

Thence S. 50 deg. 09’ W., 230 00 m. to point 2; N. 23 deg. 50’ W., 43.79 m. to point 3; N. 23 deg. 50’ W., 6.79 m. to point 4; N. 58 deg. 06’ E., 16. 50 m. to point 5; N. 46 deg. 49’ E., 28.79 m. to point 6; N. 65 deg. 36’ E., 57.04 m. to point 7; N. 57 deg. 03’ E., 123.09 m. to point 8 S. 30 deg. 08’ E., 50.75 m. to point of beginning.

Containing an area of ELEVEN THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED NINE (11,309) square meters. Bearing True; Declination 0 deg. 40’ E., Surveyed January 12 and February 3 and 8, 1933. Original surveyed February 16, 1925 and December 24, 1926.

Note: The above technical description was submitted by the City Engineer of Dagupan City. The same is subject to future survey.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-one and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

(SGD.) CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) NATALIO CASTILLO Executive Secretary
