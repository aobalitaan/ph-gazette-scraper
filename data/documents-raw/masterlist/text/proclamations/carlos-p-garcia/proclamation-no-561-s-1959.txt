MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 561

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP, AND FUND DRIVE OF THE PHILIPPINE CANCER SOCIETY UP TO MARCH 31, 1959

WHEREAS, the Philippine Cancer Society has been authorized under Proclamation No. 546, dated November 24, 1958, to conduct a national educational, membership, and fund campaign from December 15, 1958, to February 15, 1959; and

WHEREAS, the Society needs additional time for the complete success of its campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national educational, membership, and fund drive of the Philippine Cancer Society up to March 31, 1959.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth;

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
