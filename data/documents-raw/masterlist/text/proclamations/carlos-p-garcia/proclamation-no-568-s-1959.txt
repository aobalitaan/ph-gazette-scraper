MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 568

DECLARING TUESDAY, MARCH 31, 1959, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF LIPA

WHEREAS, a historical marker in honor of the late Teodoro M. Kalaw, man of letters, scholar, journalist, historian, and stateman will be unveiled in the City of Lipa on March 31, 1959, his seventy-fifth birthday;

WHEREAS, the people of the City of Lipa desire that they be given full opportunity to witness such an important event;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Tuesday, March 31, 1959, as a special public holiday in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
