MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 711

DECLARING A PERIOD OF NATIONAL MOURNING OVER THE DEATH OF SENATOR CLARO M. RECTO

WHEREAs, Senator Claro M. Recto, former President of the Constitutional Convention, patriot, nationalist, statesman, jurist, constitutionalist, and man of letters, died in Rome, Italy, on October 2, 1960; and

WHEREAS, his death is a great loss to our people and has caused deep sorrow among them;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby proclaim the period from the day of the arrival of his remains to the day of Iris burial as a period of national mourning. During this period, flags of all government buildings and installations throughout the Philippines shall be flown at half-mast.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

NATALIO P. CASTILLO Executive Secretary
