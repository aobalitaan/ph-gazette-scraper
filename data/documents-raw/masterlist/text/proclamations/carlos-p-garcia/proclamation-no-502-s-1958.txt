MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 502

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 208, SERIES OF 1955, WHICH RELEASED A PORTION OF LAND FROM THE EXPERIMENTAL STATION SITE OF THE BUREAU OF PLANT INDUSTRY SITUATED IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO BY DECLARING THE SAID PORTION OF LAND OPEN FOR DISPOSITION UNDER THE HOMESTEAD PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I hereby amend Proclamation No. 208, series of 1955, which released a portion of the Experimental Station site of the Bureau of Plant Industry located in Baguio City by declaring the said portion of land open to disposition under the homestead provisions of the Public Land Act.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twelfth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
