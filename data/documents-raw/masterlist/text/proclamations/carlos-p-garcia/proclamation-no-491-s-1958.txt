MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 491

TRANSFERRING THIS YEAR’S CELEBRATION OF “PHILIPPINE INDUSTRY AND NEPA WEEK” TO THE SECOND WEEK OF MARCH

WHEREAS, the last seven days of February of every year is “Philippine Industry and NEPA Week” under Proclamation No. 483, dated February 4, 1958; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Chamber of Industries requests that this year’s celebration of the occasion be transferred to the second week of March;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby transfer this year’s celebration of “Philippine Industry and NEPA Week” to the second week of March.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twelfth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
