MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 517

EXTENDING THE TENTH ANNUAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST OF GREATER MANILA

WHEREAS, the period from June 1 to July 15, 1958, has been set aside for the tenth annual fund campaign of the Community Chest of Greater Manila under Proclamation No. 509, dated May 12, 1958; and

WHEREAS, said organization needs additional time for the successful prosecution of its campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the tenth annual fund cam­paign of the Community Chest of the Greater Manila up to August 15, 1958.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twelfth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President: SOFRONIO C. QUIMSON Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
