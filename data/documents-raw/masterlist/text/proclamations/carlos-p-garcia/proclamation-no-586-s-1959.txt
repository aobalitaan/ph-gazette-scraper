MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 586

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 1 TO JULY 15, 1959, FOR THE ELEVENTH ANNUAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST OF GREATER MANILA

To enable the Community Chest of Greater Manila to carry on its community welfare services, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from June 1 to July 15, 1959, for its eleventh annual fund campaign.

I call upon all residents, firms, and organizations in the City of Manila, Quezon City, Pasay City, and the municipalities of Caloocan, Makati, Malabon, Mandaluyong, Navotas, Parañaque, and San Juan to support this campaign and to give generously to the Community Chest of Greater Manila which unites into one annual appeal the fund campaigns of the following:

I authorize all government officials and employees, including school authorities and teachers, to accept, for the Community Chest of Greater Manila, fund-raising responsibilities and to give it active support and leadership in their respective communities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
