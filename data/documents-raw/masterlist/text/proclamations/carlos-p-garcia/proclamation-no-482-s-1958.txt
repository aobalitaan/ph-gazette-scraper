MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 482

RELEASING FROM THE NURSERY SITE OF THE BUREAU OF PLANT INDUSTRY A CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN SITUATED IN BAGO OSHIRO, CITY OF DAVAO, AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION IN ACCORDANCE WITH EXISTING LAW

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby release from the nursery site, which was transferred to the Bureau of Plant Industry, by virtue of the provisions of Executive Order No. 372 series of 1950, a certain portion of the land embraced therein situated in Bago Oshiro, Davao City, and declare the same open to disposition under the provision of existing law, which portion of land is more particularly described to wit:

Lot 824 — Portion, Davao Cadastre

A parcel of land (lot 824-portion of the cadastral survey of Davao), situated in the City of Davao, island of Mindanao. Bounded on the NE., along lines 1-2-3—4 by road to Tugbok; on the E., along line 4—5 by lot 824-portion; on the S., along line 5-6 by Lot 824-portion and along line 6-7 by lot 844; on the W., along line 7-8 by lot 824-portion, all of Davao Cadastre; and on the N., along line 8-1 by Guianga Cadastre. Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being S. 89° 18′ W., 531.25 meters from M.B.M. 16, Davao Cadastre, Cadastre 102; thence S. 52° 09′ E., 466.48 meters to point 2; thence N. 86° 06′ E., 265.20 meters to point 3; thence S. 78° 33′ E., 299.36 meters to point 4; thence S. 0° 06′ W., 1,261.70 meters to point 5; thence S. 89° 58′ W., 967.19 meters to point 6; thence west, 130.94 meters to point 7; thence N. 1° 33′ E., 1,588.48 meters to point 8; thence N. 89° 13′ E., 130.94 meters to the point of beginning; containing an approximate area of 1,499.765 square meters, more or less.

NOTE: Data are subject to change for future survey.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twelfth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
