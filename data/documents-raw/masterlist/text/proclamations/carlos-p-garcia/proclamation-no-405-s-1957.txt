MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 405

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 267, DATED FEBRUARY 27, 1956, WHICH RESERVED AMONG OTHERS, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF MINDORO OCCIDENTAL, ISLAND OF MINDORO, FOR PUERICULTURE CENTER SITE PURPOSES BY REDUCING THE AREA RESERVED FOR THE PURPOSE TO 1,000 SQUARE METERS AND DECLARING THE REMAINING PORTION OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisious oof sections 83 and 88 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 267, dated February 27, 1956, which reserved, among others, a certain parcel of land situated in the municipality of San Jose, province of Mindoro Occidental, island of Mindoro, for puericulture center site purposes by reducing the area reserved for the purpose, to 1,000 square meters and declaring the remaining portion open to disposition under the provisions of the; said Act and which area as reduced is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 3-H (Puericulture Center)

A parcel of land (lot 3-H of the proposed amendment plan, being a portion of lot 3 described on plan Swo-37916), situated in the municipality of San Jose, province of Mindoro Occidental, island of Mindoro. Bounded on the N., along line 1-2, by lot 3-B of the amendment plan; along lines 2-4, by lot 3-B of the amendment plan; and along lines 4-1, by Narra Street. Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being S. 58° 03′ W., 256.34 meters from B.L.L.M. 65, municipality of San Jose, Mindoro Occidental, thence N. 80° 58′ E., 45.85 meters to point 2; thence S. 9° 02′ E., 21.89 meters to point 3; thence S. 80° 58′ W., 45.51 meters to point 4; thence N. 9° 55′ W., 21.89 meters to the point of beginning; containing an approximate area of 1,000 square meters.

NOTE: Data used are subject to change for future survey.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
