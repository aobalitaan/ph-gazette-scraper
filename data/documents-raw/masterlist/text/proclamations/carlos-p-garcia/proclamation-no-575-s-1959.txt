MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 575

REVOKING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 5, SERIES OF 1917, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE SCHOOL SITE RESERVATION SITUATED IN THE (MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BULUAN) MUNICIPALITY OF BULUAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, AND DECLARING THE PARCELS OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of section 88 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Executive Order No. 5, dated January 13, 1917, which established the school site reservation situated in the (municipal district of Buluan) muncipality of Buluan, province of Cotabato and declare the land embraced therein open to disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President: JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
