MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 560

DECLARING MONDAY, MARCH 2, 1959, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABATUAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, the sixty-eighth birthday of the late Tomas Confesor, one of our most outstanding resistance leaders during the last war, falls on Monday, March 2, 1959; and

WHEREAS, the people of Cabatuan, his birthplace, desire that they be given full opportunity to celebrate his birthday with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the, Philippines, pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Monday, March 2, 1959, as a special public holiday in the municipality of Cabatuan, province of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the the thirteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
