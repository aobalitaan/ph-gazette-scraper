MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 652

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM THE FIRST MONDAY TO THE SUCCEEDING SUNDAY OF MAY OF EVERY YEAR AS NATIONAL TRAFFIC SAFETY WEEK AND THE MONDAY FOLLOWING AS SAFE DRIVING DAY (S-D DAY) AND CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THEIR CELEBRATION

WHEREAS, the loss of life, limb, and property resulting from traffic accidents has gone up to an unprecedented figure;

WHEREAS, it is the government’s duty to solve the critical traffic situation with a view to minimizing, if not totally eliminating, traffic accidents and the resulting loss of life and destruction of property;

WHEREAS, in the furtherance of this duty, it is necessary to conduct an efficacious educational campaign by the use of leaflets, posters, banners, billboards, signboards, mass media, the radio and other appropriate means to arouse traffic-safety consciousness and impel the observance of traffic and road courtesy rules and regulations; and

WHEREAS, losses due to traffic are bigger during the summer months when many people travel on their vacation;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from the first Monday up to the succeeding Sunday of May of every year as National Traffic Safety Week and the Monday following as Safe Driving Day (S-D Day).
