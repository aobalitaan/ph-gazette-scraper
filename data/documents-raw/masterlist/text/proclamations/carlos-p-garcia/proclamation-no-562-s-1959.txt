MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 562

DECLARING TUESDAY, MARCH 17, 1959, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES AND IN THE CITY OF CEBU

WHEREAS, the second anniversary of the sudden and untimely death of former President Ramon Magsaysay falls on March 17, 1959; and

WHEREAS, the people of the province of Zambales and the City of Cebu request that they be given full opportunity to observe the said anniversary with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Tuesday, March 17, 1959, as a special public holiday in the province of Zambales and in the City of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
