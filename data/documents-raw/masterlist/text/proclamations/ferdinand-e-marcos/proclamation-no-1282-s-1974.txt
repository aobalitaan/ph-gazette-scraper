MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1282

RESERVING FOR MARINE FISHERIES MULTI-CULTURE FARM PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALAMINOS, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for marine fisheries multi-culture farm purposes under the administration of the Director of Fisheries, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the municipality of Alaminos, Province of Pangasinan, Island of Luzon, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 1

(Marine Demonstration Fish Farm)

Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 1, being N. 30-54 E., 4679.80 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 1, Mun. of Alaminos, Pangasinan thru MSI-1993; thence

N. 42-23 W.,  383.39 m. to point 2; N. 42-24 W.,  369.51 m. to point 3; N. 39-45 W.,  437.35 m. to point 4; N. 58-11 E.,  202.37 m. to point 5; S. 35-52 E.,  412.44 m. to point 6; S. 43-43 E.,  358.07 m. to point 7; S. 36-40 E.,  356.16 m. to point 8; S. 35-42 W.,  148.20 m. to point 1; point of beginning.

Containing an area of TWO HUNDRED THREE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED AND EIGHTY FIVE (203,185) SQUARE METERS.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows: Points 4 and 5 by Iron Pipes; Point 8 by “X” on Conc. Wall; and the rest by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 21st day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
