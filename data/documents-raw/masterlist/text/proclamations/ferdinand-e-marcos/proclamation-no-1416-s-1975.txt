TANGGAPANG NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1416

DECLARING SUNDAY, MAY 4, 1975, AS “MONICO MERCADO DAY” IN THE PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA.

WHEREAS, Sunday, May 4, 1975, marks the 100th birth anniversary of the late Monico Mercado, former congressman, patriot and poet of the province of Pampanga; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus attention on the life of the late Monico Mercado as a fitting tribute to his exemplary life;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Sunday, May 4, 1975, as “Monico Mercado Day” in the province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
