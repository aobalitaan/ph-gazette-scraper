MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2413

DECLARING TUESDAY, APRIL 16, 1985, AS SPECIAL PUBLIC NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN MARIKINA, METROPOLITAN MANILA.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, April 16, 1985 marks the 355th anniversary of the creation of the municipality of Marikina, Metropolitan Manila; and

WHEREAS, the people of the municipality of Marikina should be afforded the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their town’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, April 16, 1985 as a special public non-working holiday in Marikina.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
