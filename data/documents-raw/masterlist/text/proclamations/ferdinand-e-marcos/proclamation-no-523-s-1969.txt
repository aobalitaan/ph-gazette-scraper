MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 523

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 19, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BAGO CITY.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, February 19, 1969, marks the foundation anniversary of Bago City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Bago City should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Wednesday, February 19, 1969, as a special public holiday in Bago City;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
