TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1371

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE PHILIPPINE MENTAL HEALTH ASSOCIATION TO MARCH 30, 1975.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Mental Health Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 1241 dated February 25, 1974, to conduct its education and fund campaign to December 31, 1974; and

WHEREAS, the association needs additional time to enable it to realize its fund campaign goal to carry on its mental health program;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the National fund campaign of the Philippine Mental Health Association up to March 30, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary
