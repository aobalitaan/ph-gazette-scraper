TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1376

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE SECOND ASIAN AND OCEANIAN CONGRESS OF RADIOLOGY.

WHEREAS, the Second Asian and Oceanian Congress of Radiology was authorized under Proclamation No. 1295 dated August 2, 1974, to conduct a fund drive from June 1 to December 31, 1974, to raise funds for the successful hosting of said Congress; and

WHEREAS, the Congress needs more time for the conduct of its fund drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the Second Asian and Oceanian Congress of Radiology up to August 31, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
