MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2414

FURTHER EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 150, DATED APRIL 17, 1937, ESTABLISHING AS SOUTHERN ILOCOS NORTE FOREST RESERVES FOR FOREST PROTECTION AND TIMBER PRODUCTION AND DECLARING AS ALIENABLE OR DISPOSABLE FOR DISPOSITION UNDER THE PUBLIC LAND ACT A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN BARANGAY 22, SAN CRISTOBAL, MUNICIPALITY OF SARRAT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon recommendation of the Director of Forest Development, concurred in by the Minister of Natural Resources, and pursuant to the provisions of Commonwealth Act No. 141, known as the Public Land Act, as amended, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 150, dated April 17, 1937, which established the Southern Ilocos Norte Forest Reserves for forest protection and timber production and do hereby declare as alienable or disposable a certain parcel of land containing approximately 7.4 hectares situated in Barangay 22, San Cristobal, Municipality of Sarrat, Province of Ilocos Norte, Island of Luzon, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Beginning from Corner 1, a Mango tree identical to FZ Corner 50, Lot 3 of the Southern Ilocos Norte Forest Reserve; thence S 20º E, 170 meters to Corner 3, identical to Corner 6, of the area under Proclamation No. 2287; thence N 79º W, 365 meters to Corner 4, identical to Corner 5 of the area under Proclamation No. 2287; thence N 15º 30’E, 190 meters to Corner 5, identical to FZ Corner 49, Lot 3, Southern Ilocos Norte Forest Reserve; thence N 24º E, 220 meters to point of beginning. Containing an approximate area of 7.4 hectares, as shown in the attached sketch.

The timber and other forest products found therein shall remain under the administrative control and jurisdiction of the Bureau of Forest Development to be disposed of in accordance with the Forest and Internal Revenue rules and regulations.

Any existing lease, license, permit or any other concession granted by the Bureau of Forest Development or by any other Office covering the area embraced by this proclamation or portions thereof is hereby terminated.

There are excluded from the operation of this proclamation a strip of 15 meters in width on each side of any public trail therein as outlet for timber and other forest products and a strip of 20 meters in width from the highest bank of any stream for stream bank protection.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
