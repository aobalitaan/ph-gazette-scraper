MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 617

RECLASSIFYING THAT PORTION OF THE FORMER COMMUNAL LAND SITUATED AT BARRIO SAN RAFAEL, MUNICIPALITY OF MONTALBAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL KNOWN AS PORTION NO. II “CN-F-1252” CONTAINING AN AREA OF 172 HECTARES MORE OR LESS AND RESERVING THE SAME TO FORM PART OF THE PATRIMONIAL PROPERTY OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONTALBAN.

Upon recommendation of the Director of Forestry duly approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, and pursuant to Section 69 of the Public Land Act, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby proclaim and grant that portion of the former communal forest, disestablishment under FAO No. 2-937, dated May 21, 1969, situated at Barrio San Rafael, Municipality of Montalban, Province of Rizal, which is more particularly described as follows:

“Beginning at a point marked “1” of Plan MR 1200, which is S. 68 deg. 44 min. E, 2,588.30 meters from B.L.L.M. 1, Municipality of Montalban, Rizal,

beginning containing an area of 144.9458 hectares, more or less subject to future survey.

as patrimonial property of the Municipality of Montalban, for the construction of Municipal Parks, Educational and Recreational Centers and other public improvements which will be beneficial to the Municipality of Montalban, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
