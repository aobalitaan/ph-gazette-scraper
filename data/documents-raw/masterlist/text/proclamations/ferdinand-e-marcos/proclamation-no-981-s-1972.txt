MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 981

DECLARING THURSDAY, MARCH 9, 1972, AS LAUREL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS AND IN THE CITIES OF BATANGAS AND LIPA.

Whereas, the eighty-first birthday of the late Jose P. Laurel, patriot, jurist and statesman, falls on March 9, 1972; and

Whereas, it is desirable to focus attention on the life of the late Jose P. Laurel as a fitting tribute to his unselfish efforts;

Now, therefore, I, Ferdinand E. Marcos, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thursday, March 9, 1972, as Laurel Day in the Province of, Batangas and in the Cities of Batangas and Lipa.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source: Malacañang Records Office
