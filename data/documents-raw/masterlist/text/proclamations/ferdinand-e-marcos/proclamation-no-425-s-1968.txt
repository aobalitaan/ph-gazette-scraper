MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 425

DECLARING MONDAY, JULY 1, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN DAVAO DEL NORTE.

WHEREAS, Monday, July 1, 1968, marks the foundation anniversary of Davao del Norte; and

WHEREAS, the people of Davao Del Norte should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their province’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, July 1, 1968, as a special public holiday in the province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Tacloban, this 1st day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
