TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1323

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 8 TO 14, 1974, AS NATIONAL HANDICAPPED WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine Foundation For The Rehabilitation Of Disabled, Inc., will hold a Conference on the Rehabilitation of the Disabled on December 12, 13 and 14, 1974, in Manila; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to awaken increased government and public interest in the economic and social uplift of the handicapped and disabled;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from December 8 to 14, 1974, as National Handicapped Week under the auspices of involved organizations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
