MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2166

PROCLAIMING THE ADOPTION OF THE PHILIPPINE DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR 1983-1987: GOALS, STRATEGIES AND POLICIES.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Development Plan for 1983-1987, upon endorsement by NEDA and the Cabinet, has been approved and adopted in the Third Regular Session of the Batasang Pambansa on January 19, 1982, through Resolution No. 374;

WHEREAS, for a comprehensive and effective implementation thereof, there is a need to direct all agencies and instrumentalities of the government to dovetail their activities thereto;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby proclaim the adoption of the Philippine Development Plan for 1983-1987: Goals, Strategies and Policies, including the supportive Regional Development Plans and Investment Programs.

The National Economic and Development Authority shall coordinate and monitor the Plan’s implementation by the various government ministries, agencies and/or instrumentalities.

The Office of the Budget and Management is hereby directed to provide the necessary budgetary support to the Plan’s implementation and its periodic assessment and updating.

The private sector is hereby enjoined to lend support to the Plan by aligning and coordinating its activities thereto, as far as may be practicable in the light of its objectives and operations.

The Prime Minister, as Vice-Chairman of the NEDA, is hereby authorized to issue such memoranda, circulars or other orders, including the creation of inter-agency committees, as he may consider necessary or advisable in the implementation of the Plan and its periodic assessment and updating by all concerned.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
