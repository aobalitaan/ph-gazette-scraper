MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1324

EXCLUDING FROM THE PASONANCA PARK RESERVATION IN ZAMBOANGA CITY, A PARCEL OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND TRANSFERRING THE SAME TO THE PHILIPPINE TOURISM AUTHORITY FOR TOURISM DEVELOPMENT PURPOSES
