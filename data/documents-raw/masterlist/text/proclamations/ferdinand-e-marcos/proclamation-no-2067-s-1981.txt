PROCLAMATION NO. 2067

DECLARING THE PROVINCE OF BATAAN AS A TOURIST ZONE

WHEREAS, the establishment of an Export Processing Zone at Mariveles, Bataan, has caused an influx of foreign technicians, as well as tourists, into the area;

WHEREAS, Mt. Samat is also the site of the Dambanang Kagitingan, and

WHEREAS, Bataan has other untapped scenic and beautiful spots with tourism potential;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the Province of Bataan, as a tourist zone under the contrl and administration of the Philippine Tourism Authority (PTA) pursuant to Section 5, of P.D. 564.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in The City of Manila, this 11th day of March, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Source: Presidential Management Staff
