TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 951

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE BADMINTON ASSOCIATION, INC.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 876 dated July 12, 1971, authorized the Philippine Badminton Association (PBA) to conduct a national fund campaign from August 1 to December 31, 1971; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Badminton Association, Inc., an affiliate of the PAAF, needs more time to realize its goal of ₱500,000 to .carry out its objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, Ferdinand E. Marcos, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Philippine Badminton Association, Inc., to extend its na­tional fund campaign to the period from January 1 to July 31, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
