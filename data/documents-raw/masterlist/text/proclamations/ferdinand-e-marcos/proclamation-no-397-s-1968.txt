MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 397

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE GERIATRICS ORGANIZATION, INC.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Geriatrics Organization, Inc. was authorized to conduct an educational and fund campaign from March 1 to May 31, 1968, under Proclamation No. 253 dated August 25, 1967; and

WHEREAS, the said organization needs additional time to prosecute its campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Geriatrics Organization, Inc. up to August 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source : Presidential Management Staff
