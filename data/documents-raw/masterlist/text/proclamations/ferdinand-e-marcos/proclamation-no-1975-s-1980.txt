MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1975

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE MEDICAL WOMEN’S ASSOCIATION, INC. QUEZON CITY CHAPTER, TO CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 1, 1980 TO DECEMBER 31, 1980.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Medical Women’s Association, Inc. is a non-profit organization engaged in various community development projects notably the Child-Youth Development; and

WHEREAS, the successful implementation of its laudable projects and programs depends to a large degree upon the continued support of the public;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Philippine Medical Women’s Association, Inc., Quezon City Chapter, to conduct a fund campaign during the period from March 1, 1980 to December 31, 1980.

I hereby enjoin all citizens and residents of the Philippines to give generously of their means and participate in this humanitarian endeavor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
