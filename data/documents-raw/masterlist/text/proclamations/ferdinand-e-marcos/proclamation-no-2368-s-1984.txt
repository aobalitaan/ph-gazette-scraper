MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2368

SETTING THE DATES FOR THE HOLDING OF PLEBISCITES TO RATIFY THE CREATION OF NEW BARANGAYS IN QUEZON CITY AND IN THE PROVINCES OF RIZAL AND SURIGAO DEL NORTE.

WHEREAS, the Batasang Pambansa has enacted several laws creating new barangays in Quezon City and in the Provinces of Rizal and Surigao del Norte; and

WHEREAS, the said laws provide that plebiscites shall be held in the areas affected to ratify the creation of the new barangays.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby direct the following:

1. The holding of plebiscites to ratify the creation of new barangays specified in the laws enumerated as follows:

1. B.P. No. 784 — AN ACT CREATING BARANGAY CAPRI IN QUEZON CITY, METRO MANILA.

2. B.P. No. 786 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS OF PAALAMAN, TATLONG PULO, CALUMPIT, SUPOT, BUKAL, AND LA MESANG BATO, ALL IN BARANGAY SPECIAL DISTRICT, MUNICIPALITY OF JALA-JALA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A NEW BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY PAALAMAN.

3. B.P. No. 787 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIO OF BEVERLY HILLS IN BARANGAY SAN ROQUE, MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A NEW BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY BEVERLY HILLS.

4. B.P. No. 788 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS OF NAYONG SILANGAN, ABA HOMES, DALIG AND TIPOLO. ALI, IN BARANGAY SAN JOSE, MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A NEW BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS DALIG.

5. B.P. No. 789 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS OF BAGONG NAYON, COGEO VILLAGE, MAKULIS, UPPER KAMIAS, LOWER KAMIAS, BULUSUKIN AND SAPANG PAPASAK, ALL IN BARANGAY DELA PAZ, MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A NEW BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY BAGONG NAYON.

6. B.P. No. 790 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS OF BUNTONG PALAY, DIRAKPAN, KAY UGAT, PALILINGAN, TINGING, BUNGKAWI, BANA, LABRAHAN, TULAKIN, BUBOM AMAK, INALSAN, INC, AND KAY SUSO BANGKO ALL IN BARANGAY DELA PAZ, SAN ISIDRO AND CUPANG, MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A NEW BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY SAN JUAN.

7. B.P. No. 791 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS OF STA. CRUZ, NAGPUSO, CAVALIERS, KAY BAIYA, MAHAL NA POON, TAWID-TAWID, UULAN, MAKATUBONG, MINI TAGISAN AND SEVEN HILLS, ALL IN BARANGAY DELA PAZ, MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A NEW BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY STA. CRUZ.

8. B.P. No. 792 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS OF MUNTINDILAW BULAO, SAN BERGA, MAHAYHAY, NAPINDAN, DAGATAN, VALLEY GOLF CLUB, AND PART OF VICTORIA VALLEY IN BARANGAY MAMBUGAN AND DELA PAZ, MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A NEW BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY MUNTINDILAW.

9. B.P. No. 793 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS OF SAN LUIS, PART OF SITIO KAY AMPAULA, PART OF SITIO KAY NAOG, BIGA, KAY BAGSIK, SUSUGIN, DAO CREEK, PART OF PINAGMINAHAN MOUNTAIN RANGE, PINAGKALIRUHAN, PART OF BUBUKAL CAVE, CULASISI, BANABA, PUTING BATO, NILULUBUGAN AND TESORERO, ALL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO, MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A NEW BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY SAN LUIS.

10. B.P. No. 794 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS OF INARAWAN, INUMAN, BUYAG, MAAGAY, BINITINAN, GALUGOD BABOY, ALL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO, MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A NEW BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY, INARAWAN.

11. B.P. No. 687 — AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY MATALAHIB OF QUEZON CITY TO BARANGAY STO. DOMINGO.

12. B.P. No. 688 — AN ACT RENAMING BARANGAY GINTONG SILAHIS OF QUEZON CITY TO BARANGAY N.S. AMORANTO.

13. B.P. No. 711 — AN ACT CONSTITUTING SITIOS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALIMONO, SURIGAO DEL NORTE INTO NEW BARANGAYS, TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAYS VILLA RIZA, CANSAYONG, AND CAN-AGA.

2. The Commission on Elections shall set the convenient dates for the plebiscites, supervise the conduct thereof and shall promulgate the rules and regulations necessary to implement this Proclamation.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-four.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
