MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2375

DECLARING THE SECOND WEEK OF SEPTEMBER, 1984, AS DEVELOPMENT BANKERS WEEK.

WHEREAS, the Development Bankers Association of the Philippines plays a vital role in the agricultural and industrial development of the country;

WHEREAS, the development banking of the Philippines, as an instrument of growth, is a vital cog in the wheel of economic progress; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting that public attention be focused on the significant role of the development bankers in accelerating countryside progress;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the second week of September, 1984, as “Development Bankers Week” under the auspices of the Development Bankers Association of the Philippines (DBAP) and other involved agencies.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of September in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-four.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
