MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 333

EXTENDING THE EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE KNIGHTS OF RIZAL UP TO APRIL 30, 1968.

WHEREAS, the Order of the Knights of Rizal was authorized to conduct an educational and fund campaign from September 1, to December 31, 1957; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs additional time to conduct its campaign in order to implement its objectives and projects;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the campaign of the Order of the Knights of Rizal up to April 30, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
