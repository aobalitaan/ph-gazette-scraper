TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1092

AUTHORIZING THE PANAMIN FOUNDATION, INC., TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND DRIVE DURING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 15, 1972 TO FEBRUARY 28, 1973.

WHEREAS, the PANAMIN Foundation, Inc., having successfully launched and spearheaded a movement towards the amelioration of our National Minorities who comprise more than twelve percent of our national population;

WHEREAS, since its establishment, the PANAMIN Foundation, Inc., under the dynamic leadership of a group of outstanding civic-spirited citizens and residents headed by Mr. Manuel Elizalde, Jr., has achieved an extraordinary record of accomplishment, bringing much needed help into the hinterlands and remotest barrios of our country;

WHEREAS, the activities of the PANAMIN Foundation, Inc., have proven to be exceptionally effective in realizing the social welfare objectives of the Government for the benefit of our unfortunate brethren in the mountains and other isolated areas;

WHEREAS, to carry on further its humanitarian mission to a greater scope of operation, the PANAMIN Foundation, Inc., needs the financial and moral support of the public;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the PANAMIN Foundation, Inc., to conduct a national, educational, and fund drive during the period from November 15, 1972 to February 28, 1973. I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality, race or creed, to assist in this humanitarian endeavor by giving generously of their means. I further call upon all national, provincial, city, and municipal government officials and employees, as well as on all school teachers and students throughout the land, to actively participate in and support his meritorious fund campaign.

This proclamation supersedes Proclamation No. 1020 dated May 29, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
