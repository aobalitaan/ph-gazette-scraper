MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2435

AUTHORIZING THE NATIONAL FEDERATION OF WOMEN’S CLUBS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE FUND DRIVE DURING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 1, 1985 TO JULY 1, 1986.

WHEREAS, the National Federation of Women’s Clubs of the Philippines since 1905 has initiated many welfare projects that were in time adopted by the Government such as primary health care programs, nutrition education, adult education, community beautification, public playgrounds and home industries, cultural and international relations;

WHEREAS, the Federation has planned to strengthen its one thousand two hundred clubs all over the Philippines to contribute effectively in socio-economic development of the Filipino families, and to help promote the welfare of the youth, the disabled, the sick and the elderly;

WHEREAS, the Federation deserves to have its material and organizational requirements and the services of staff personnel and field workers to carry out its planned activities and to participate in the livelihood programs of the government under the Kilusang Kabuhayan at Kaunlaran; and

WHEREAS, the Federation needs the continued support of the public to enable it to accomplish its charitable undertakings.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the National Federation of Women’s Clubs of the Philippines, Inc. to conduct a nationwide fund drive from July 1, 1985 to July 1, 1986.

I hereby enjoin all citizens and residents of this country to give generously of their means to enable the Federation to succeed in its humanitarian endeavors.

All funds generated by virtue of this Proclamation and all donations or contributions to the Federation shall be exempted from all forms of taxes and shall constitute allowable deductions in full for income tax purposes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of July in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
