MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 615

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 382 DATED APRIL 15, 1968, ENTITLED “DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 22 TO 28 OF EVERY YEAR AS ELECTRONICS AND TELECOMMUNICATIONS WEEK.”

Proclamation No. 382 dated April 15, 1968, entitled “Declaring the Period from July 22 to 28 of Every Year as Electronics and Telecommunications Week,” is hereby amended by transferring the celebration of Electronics and Telecommunications Week to the first week of December of every year.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
