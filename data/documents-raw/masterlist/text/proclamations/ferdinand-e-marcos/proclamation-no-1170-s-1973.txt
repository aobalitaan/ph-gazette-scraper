TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1170

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 30 TO AUGUST 5, 1973, AS CIVIL ENGINEERING WEEK.

WHEREAS, the Engineering Profession has contributed a lot to the progress of modern civilization;

WHEREAS, the Association of Structural Engineers of the Philippines celebrates the twelfth anniversary of its founding during the period from July 30 to August 5, 1973; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus public attention on the important role the civil engineers play in the development of our country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by Order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from July 30 to August 5, 1973, as Civil Engineering Week, under the auspices of the Association of Structural Engineers of the Philippines and the Board of Civil Engineering.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
