TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 828

DECLARING MONDAY, MARCH 8, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CEBU CITY.

WHEREAS, Monday, March 8, 1971, marks the opening of the East Visayas Athletic Meet to be held at Cebu City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Cebu City should be given full opportunity to appreciate the significance of said meet which is designed to promote sportsmanship, brotherhood and cooperation among the people of the Eastern Visayas;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, March 8, 1971, as a special public holiday in Cebu City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
