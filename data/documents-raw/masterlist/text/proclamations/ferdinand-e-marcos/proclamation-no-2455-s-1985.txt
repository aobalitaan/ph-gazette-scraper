MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT

OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 2455

ESTABLISHING AS WATERSHED FOREST RESERVATION FOR THE PURPOSE OF PROTECTING, MAINTAINING, OR IMPROVING ITS WATER YIELD AND PROVIDING A RESTRAINING MECHANISM FOR INAPPROPRIATE FOREST EXPLOITATION AND LAND-USE, A PARCEL OF LAND IN THE PROVINCES OF SULTAN KUDARAT AND SOUTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, PHILIPPINES.

WHEREAS, water supply is indispensable to the socio-economic development and well-being of Allah Valley;

WHEREAS, it is necessary to protect and properly manage the watershed of Allah Valley, particularly the upper reaches and tributaries of Allah river in order to maintain or improve its water supply and to restrain inappropriate forest exploitation and land-use;

WHEREAS, reserving the said land for watershed purposes is in the best interest of the public;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, upon recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources and in accordance with Section 1 of Republic Act 3891, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and set aside as watershed forest reserve, subject to private rights, if there be any, and to the operation of previous proclamations reserving any portion thereof for specific purposes, a parcel of land located in the Municipalities of Isulan, Banga, Surallah, and Kiamba, Province of Sultan Kudarat and South Cotabato more particularly described as follows:
