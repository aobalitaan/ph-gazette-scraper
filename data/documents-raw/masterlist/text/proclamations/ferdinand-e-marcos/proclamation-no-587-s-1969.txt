MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 587

DECLARING SATURDAY, AUGUST 9, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IBAJAY, AKLAN.

By virtue of the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, August 9, 1969, as special public holiday in the Municipality of Ibajay, Aklan, being the birth anniversary of the late Col. Alejandro Melchor Sr., and to enable the residents’ of that municipality to fittingly observe the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
