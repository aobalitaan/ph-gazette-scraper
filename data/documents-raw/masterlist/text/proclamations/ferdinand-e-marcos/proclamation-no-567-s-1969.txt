MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 567

FURTHER EXTENDING THE EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 497 dated December 19, 1968, to extend its educational and fund campaign up to June 30, 1969; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs additional time to raise more funds to achieve its goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the educational and fund campaign of the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association up to December 31, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.0

Done in the City of Manila, 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
