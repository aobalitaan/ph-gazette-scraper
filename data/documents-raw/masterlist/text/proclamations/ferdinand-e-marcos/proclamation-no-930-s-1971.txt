TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 930

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 22 TO 28, 1971, AS RURAL ELECTRIFICATION WEEK.

WHEREAS, the electrification of the entire country is vital to the general welfare of the people and indispensable to the progress of the nation; and

WHEREAS, it is the policy of this Administration to pursue this objective vigorously;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from November 22 to 28, 1971, as “Rural Electrification Week” under the auspices of the National Electrification Administration and other entities concerned.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
