TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 658

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 610 DATED JANUARY 19, 1960, BY TRANSFERRING LIVESTOCK AND POULTRY WEEK TO THE SECOND WEEK OF APRIL OF EVERY YEAR.

WHEREAS, the third week of February of every year has been designated as Livestock and Poultry Production Week under Proclamation No. 640 dated January 19, 1960; and

WHEREAS, unfavorable weather conditions during the preparatory stages as necessitate the transfer of the celebration to the 2nd week of April;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the second week of April of every year, instead of the third week of February as Livestock and Poultry Production Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
