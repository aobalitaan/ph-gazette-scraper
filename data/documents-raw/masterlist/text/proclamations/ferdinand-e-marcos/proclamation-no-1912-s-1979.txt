MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1912

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE BAND OF MERCY, INC. TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM SEPTEMBER 1, 1979 TO AUGUST 31, 1980

WHEREAS, the Philippine Band of Mercy, Inc. (PBM) undertakes habilitation and rehabilitation projects for the upliftment of the indigent physically handicapped on a national scale;

WHEREAS, the objectives of the PBM seek to make this organization more relevant to the expanding social, medical and economic rehabilitation needs of the underprivileged physically handicapped in the Philippines;

WHEREAS, the PBM is committed to pursuing its Plan of Action and Program Strategies in observance of the International Year of the Child 1979 and the Decade of The Filipino Child 1977–1987 under the Council for the Welfare of Children (CWC);

WHEREAS, in order that the humanitarian and welfare objectives of the PBM may be fully achieved, the moral and financial support of the populace is necessary;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Philippine Board of Mercy. Inc. to conduct its national educational, membership and fund campaign during the period from September 1, 1979 to August 31, 1980.

In view hereof, I hereby call upon all residents of the Philippines, whatever race or creed, business, commercial and industrial firms, professional and civic groups, school personnel and employees of government offices and agencies throughout the country to fully support the educational, membership and fund campaign of the Philippine Band of Mercy, Inc., by giving generously of their means to this fund campaign for the uplift of our less fortunate physically handicapped countrymen, especially the children.

For this purpose, all donation, bequests, gifts received by the Philippine Band of Mercy, Inc. for the implementation of its program activities shall be free from all forms of taxes and shall be deductible in full from the income of the donors in accordance with law, for income tax purposes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
