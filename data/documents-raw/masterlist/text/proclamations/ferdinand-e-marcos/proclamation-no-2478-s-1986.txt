Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

PROCLAMATION NO. 2478

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NOS. 361 AND 630, SERIES OF 1968 AND 1969, THE PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED IN BARANGAY LAMAO, MUNICIPALITY OF LIMAY, BATAAN, EMBRACED IN PROCLAMATION NO. 322 AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT.

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 322 dated July 25, 1956, certain parcels of land situated in Barangay Lamao, Municipality of Limay, Province of Bataan comprising a total area of approximately one hundred eight thousand four hundred forty-one (108,441) square meters were segregated and declared open to disposition in accordance with the provisions of the Public Land Act;

WHEREAS, under Proclamation Nos. 361 and 630 dated March 6, 1968, and November 29, 1969, respectively, several parcels of land, situated in Barangay Lamao, Municipality of Limay, Province of Bataan encompassing that segregated by Proclamation No. 322, have been withdrawn from sale or settlement and reserved for industrial purposes;

WHEREAS, it is the policy of the present administration to promote the equitable distribution of property ownership of lands declared alienable and disposable in accordance with the tenets of social justice;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines by Virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation Nos. 361 and 630, Series of 1968 and 1969, the parcels of land more particularly described and identified in Proclamation No. 322 dated July 25, 1956, save the land wherein the Philippine National Oil Company-Shipping and Transport Corporation’s field terminal is located and declare the same open to disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
