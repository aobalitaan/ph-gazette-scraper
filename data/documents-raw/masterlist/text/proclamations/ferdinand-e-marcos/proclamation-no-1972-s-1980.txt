MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1972

DECLARING THE MONTH OF MAY AS GUIDANCE MONTH IN THE PHILIPPINES.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Guidance and Personnel Association, Inc., a national organization of guidance Counselors and personnel workers, holds its annual convention in the month of May of every year; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary to focus attention on the important role played by guidance counselors and personnel workers in the development of human potentials in this country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the month of May as Guidance Month.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
