MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1831

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 260 DATED JUNE 22, 1951 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE MINDORO AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL SITE RESERVATION SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, ISLAND OF MINDORO, CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT.

Upon the recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 260 dated June 22, 1951, which established the Mindoro Agricultural School site reservation, now Mindoro College of Agriculture and Technology, situated in the municipality of Naujan, province of Oriental Mindoro, island of Mindoro, certain portion of the land embraced therein and declare the same open to disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

BLOCK II

A PARCEL OF LAND (Block II of Mindoro Agricultural School Reservation), situated in the Barrio of Magasawang-Tubig, Municipality of Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, Island of Mindoro. Bounded on the N., by Public Land; on the E., by Magasawang-Tubig River; on the S., by Ibolo River; and on the W., by Public and Bocayao-Grande River. Beginning at a point marked “1”,

thence following Magasawang-Tubig and Ibolo River upstream in a southwesterly direction about 9640.00 m. to point 3;

thence following Bocayao-Grande River downstream in a northeasterly direction about 2700.00 m. to the point of beginning; Containing an area of ONE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED TWENTY (1,120) HECTARES.

All points referred to are indicated and are marked on the ground as follows: point 3, by Old Corner; and the rest by Trees.

Bearings true.

Bona fide occupants of other portions of the school reservation shall be relocated to the vacant portions of the area released herein as far as they can be accommodated.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
