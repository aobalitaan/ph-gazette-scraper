TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1047

DECLARING JULY 26, 1972, AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER AS “ARAW NG IPIL” IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

Upon representations of the Municipal Court of Ipil, Zamboanga del Sur, and by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, do hereby declare July 26, 1972, and every year thereafter as “Araw ng Ipil” in the Municipality of Ipil, Zamboanga del Sur, in commemoration of the formal organization of said municipality.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
