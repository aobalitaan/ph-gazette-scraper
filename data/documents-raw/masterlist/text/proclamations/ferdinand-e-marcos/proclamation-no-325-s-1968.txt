MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 325

AUTHORIZING THE SOS CHILDREN’S VILLAGE PHILIPPINES, INC. TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 1 TO MARCH 1 TO MAY 31, 1968.

WHEREAS additional facilities are needed to provide food, clothing, shelter, medical attention, protection and education to orphaned, abandoned or otherwise homeless children as well as to those born of destitute or incompetent parents;

WHEREAS, civic-spirited citizens and residents of the Philippines who have organized and incorporated a civic, non-profit organization known as the SOS Children’s Village Philippines, Inc. are engaged in a progress of amelioration for the benefit of these unfortunate children; and

WHEREAS, the meritorious mission of the SOS Children’s Village Philippines, Inc. deserves the support of every civic spirited citizen and resident of the Philippines;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the SOS Children’s Village Philippines, Inc. to conduct a National Fund Campaign during the period from March 1 to May 31, 1968. I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality or creed, to give generously of their means so that we may substantially assist in bringing up these unfortunate children.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
