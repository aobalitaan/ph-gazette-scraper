MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 450

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 12, 1968 AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF CAVITE.

WHEREAS, Thursday, September 12, 1968, marks the 72nd anniversary of the execution of the thirteen martyrs of the City of Cavite; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the Cavite should be given the opportunity to venerate the memory of the heroes whose sacrifice to all Filipinos;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby Thursday, September 12, 1968, as a special public holiday in the City of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
