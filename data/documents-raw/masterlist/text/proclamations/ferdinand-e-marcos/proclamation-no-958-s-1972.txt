TANGGAPAN NO PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 958

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE CANCER SOCIETY, INC., TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND DRIVE DURING THE PERIOD FROM FEBRUARY 1 TO JUNE 30, 1972.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc., having successfully pioneered and spearheaded the move towards the prevention and control of cancer, envisions the extension of its free medical services to the rural areas through the establishment of local chapters;

WHEREAS, statistics gathered by the Society from various local clinics and hospitals shows that the prevalence of cancer among rural people particularly is increasing every year; and

WHEREAS, in order to carry out the worthy objectives of the Society, it needs the continued support and cooperation of the public;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc., to conduct a national educational, membership and fund drive during the period from February 1 to June 30, 1972. I enjoin all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality, race or creed, to assist generously in this humanitarian endeavor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
