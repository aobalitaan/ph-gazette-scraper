MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 433

DECLARING THE TIRAD PASS NATIONAL PARK ESTABLISHED UNDER PROCLAMATION NO. 294 DATED JULY 20, 1938, IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICTS OF GREGORIO DEL PILAR AND SIGAY, AND MUNICIPALITIES OF QUIRINO, CERVANTES AND SUYO, ILOCOS SUR, AS TIRAD PASS NATIONAL SHRINE.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the Tirad Pass National Park established under Proclamation No. 294 dated July 20, 1938, in the municipal districts of Gregorio del Pilar and Sigay, and municipalities of Quirino, Cervantes and Suyo, Ilocos Sur, as Tirad Pass National Shrine under the administration of the National Shrines Commission.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
