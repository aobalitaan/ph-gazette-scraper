MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 234

NAMING THE BRIDGE AT NAGTAHAN STREET, CITY OF MANILA, AS MABINI BRIDGE.

WHEREAS, the construction of the new bridge at Nagtahan Street, City of Manila, is almost completed; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper to name the bridge in honor of the memory of one of our greatest patriots who lived in the vicinity thereof, and whose birthday anniversary celebration starts today;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby name the bridge at Nagtahan Street, City of Manila, as “Mabini Bridge” in memory of Apolinario Mabini, the “Sublime Paralytic”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
