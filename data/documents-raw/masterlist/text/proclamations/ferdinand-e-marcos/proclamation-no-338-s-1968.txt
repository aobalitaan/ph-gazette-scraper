MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 338

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 19, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BAGO CITY.

WHEREAS, Monday, February 19, 1968, marks the foundation anniversary of Bago City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Bago City should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate the milestone in their City’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, February 19, 1968, as a special holiday in Bago City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5 th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
