MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 435

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 173 DATED OCTOBER 10, 1963M WHICH RESERVED A PARCEL OF PUBLIC LAND IN TACLOBAN CITY FOR GOVERNMENT CENTER PURPOSES.

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 173, dated October 10, 1963, by reserving the portion of the public domain situated in Camp Bumpus, City of Tacloban, described therein for Social Security System building site purposes instead of for government center site purposes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
