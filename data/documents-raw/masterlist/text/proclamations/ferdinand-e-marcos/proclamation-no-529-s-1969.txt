MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 529

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM APRIL 1 TO 7, 1969, AS “TAX CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK.”

WHEREAS, it is the civic obligation and patriotic duty of every citizens to pay his just taxes promptly, and

WHEREAS, there is need of installing among our citizenry greater consciousness of their obligation and undersecretary the taxpayer’s role in economic progress and nation-building;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from April 1 to 7, 1969, as “TAX CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK” under the auspices of the Department of Finance.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
