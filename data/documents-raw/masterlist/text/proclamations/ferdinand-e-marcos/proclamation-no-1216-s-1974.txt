TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1216

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PANAMIN FOUNDATION, INC. UNTIL JUNE 30, 1974.

WHEREAS, the PANAMIN Foundation, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 1145 dated May 18, 1973 to conduct a national educational and fund campaign from February 29, 1973 to December 31, 1973 for the purpose of raising much needed funds with which to carry on its various humanitarian projects for the benefit of our national minorities who comprise more than twelve percent of our national population;

WHEREAS, the PANAMIN Foundation has not reached its goal of ₱2.5 million; and

WHEREAS, to enable PANAMIN to carry on its laudable activities, it is necessary that its fund campaign be extended for another period;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the national educational fund drive of the PANAMIN Foundation, Inc., until June 30, 1974. I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality race or creed, to assist in this humanitarian endeavor by giving generously of their means. I further call upon all national, provincial, city, and municipal government officials, as well as on all school teachers and student throughout the land, to actively participate in and support this meritorious fund campaign.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
