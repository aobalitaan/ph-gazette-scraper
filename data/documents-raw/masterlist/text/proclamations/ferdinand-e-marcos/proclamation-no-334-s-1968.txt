MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 334

FURTHER EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION UP TO JUNE 30, 1968.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 204, dated May 22, 1967, to conduct a fund drive for the construction of public swimming pools all over the county; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs additional time for the conduct of its drive.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund drive of the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association up to June 30, 1968.

.Done in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of January , in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
