MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2139

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE REHABILITATION FUND, INC., UP TO OCTOBER 31, 1982.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Rehabilitation, Inc. is a non-stock, non-profit organization engaged in providing support and assistance to the projects of the National Commission Concerning Disabled Persons (NCCDP) and all other agencies and instrumentalities concerned with disability prevention and rehabilitation of the disabled;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Rehabilitation Fund, Inc. was authorized under Proclamation No. 2020 to conduct a fund campaign during the period from October 15, 1980 to October 14, 1981;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Rehabilitation Fund, Inc., needs more time to raise more funds to achieve its goals;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Rehabilitation Fund, Inc. up to October 31, 1982.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Acting Presidential Executive Assistant
