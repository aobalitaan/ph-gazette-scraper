MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 398

DECLARING TUESDAY, MAY 28, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CABA, LA UNION.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, May 28, 1968, marks the 205th death anniversary of Diego Silang, one of our foremost national heroes; and

WHEREAS, the people of Caba, La union, where he was born should be afford the opportunity to pay fitting homage to the hero;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines do hereby declare Tuesday, May 28, 1968, as a special public holiday in Caba, La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set may hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
