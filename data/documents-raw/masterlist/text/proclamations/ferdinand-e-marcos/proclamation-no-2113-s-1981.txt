MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2113

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 5 AS GENERAL SANTOS CITY DAY

WHEREAS, Saturday, September 5, 1981, marks the 13th charter anniversary of General Santos City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of that city should be given the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare every September 5 as General Santos City Day but the said day shall be a working day in accordance with Letter of Instructions No. 1087 dated November 26, 1980.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
