MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1518

DECLARING FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 14, 1975, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BULACAN.

WHEREAS, Friday, November 14, 1975, marks the centennial year of the birthday of General Gregorio H. del Pilar, the hero of Tirad Pass;

WHEREAS, the people of Bulacan desire to celebrate with appropriate ceremonies this rare occasion of the 100th year of the birthday of one of the greatest sons of their province;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Friday, November 14, 1975, as a special public holiday in Bulacan;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source: Malacañang Records Office
