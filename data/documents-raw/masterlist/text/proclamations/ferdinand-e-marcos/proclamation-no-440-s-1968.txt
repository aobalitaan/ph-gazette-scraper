MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 440

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 5 DATED AUGUST 2 1946 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE NATIONAL COCONUT CORPORATION WAREHOUSE RESERVATION SITUATED IN LEGASPI CITY AND RESERVING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN FOR PLANT QUARANTINE BUILDING AND FUMIGATING CHAMBER SITES PURPOSES OF THE BUREAU OF PLANT INDUSTRY.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 5 dated August 2, 1946 which established the National Coconut Corporation Warehouse reservation in Legaspi City, and reserve the land embraced therein for plant quarantine building and fumigating chamber sites purposes under the administration of the Director of Plant Industry, subject to private rights, if any there be.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
