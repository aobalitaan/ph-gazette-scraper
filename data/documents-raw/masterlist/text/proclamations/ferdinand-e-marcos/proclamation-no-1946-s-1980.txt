MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1946

DECLARING SATURDAY, FEBRUARY 16, 1980, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CALOOCAN CITY.

WHEREAS, on Saturday, February 16, 1980, the City of Caloocan celebrates its foundation day; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary to focus attention on this significant day in the history of said City;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, February 16, 1980, a special public holiday in Caloocan City, but the date shall be regular working day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
