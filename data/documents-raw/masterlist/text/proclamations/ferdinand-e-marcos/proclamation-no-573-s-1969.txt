MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 573

RESERVING AS PERMANENT FORESTS CERTAIN PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED JIN NUEVA ECIJA, NUEVA VIZCAYA, BONTOC, CAGAYAN, BULACAN, PANGASINAN, QUEZON, LAGUNA, CAMARINES SUR AND ILOILO.

Upon the recommendation of the Director of Forestry and approval of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 1826 of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended by Republic Act No. 3092, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and set aside as permanent forest reserves, subject to private rights, if there be any, certain parcels of the public domain more particularly described as follows:

Parcel No. 1 (Magat River Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land beginning at a point “1” with latitude 70˚-40’-29” and longitude 121˚-30’-40” in the Province of Nueva Vizcaya;

the point of beginning; containing an area of four billion three hundred eight million six hundred five thousand (4,308,605,000) square meters, more or less. All Points referred to are indicated on Plan No. 1”

Parcel No. 2 (Chico River Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land beginning: at a point “1” with Latitude 17°-40′-29″ and Longitude 121°-26′-54″ in the Province of Bontoc;

the point of beginning; containing an area of Thirty three hundred thirty one million seven hundred sixty two thousand (3,331,762,000) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on Plan No. 2.”

Parcel No. 3 (Abulug River Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land beginning at a point “1” with Latitude 17°-53’11” and Longitude 120°-55’-33” in the Province of Cagayan;

the point of beginning; containing an area of Nineteen hundred fifty six million five hundred ninety three thousand four hundred thirty seven (1,956,593.437) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on Plan No. 3.”

Parcel No. 4 (Peñaranda River Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land beginning at appoint “1” being 3,875.00 meters, South from a Point with Latitude 15°-25’-00” and Longitude 121°-05’-00” in the Province of Nueva Ecija;

The point of beginning; containing an area of One hundred forty eight million eight hundred seventy seven thousand one hundred twelve (148,877,112) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on Plan No. 4.

Parcel No. 5 (Angat River-Bustos Dam Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of public land (designated as Lot “C” in the accompanying NPC Plan) beginning at a point marked “1” on plan which is the same point marked “3” of the Angat Watershed Lot “B” (Proclamation No. 599 series of 1959) being S 10°-14’-“ E, 8483.90 meters more or less from BLLM No. 2, Norzagaray in the Province of Bulacan;

The point of beginning; containing an area of Two hundred eighty five million five hundred thousand (285,500.000) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on Plan No. 5.”

NOTE:

(1) Points “17” to “34” to Point “21” to “4”, respectively of reservation under Proclamation No. 71 dated March 10, 1927 as amended by Proclamation No. 505 dated December 4, 1965.

(2) Point “2”, “1”, “35” and “34” common to Point “2”, “3”, “4” and “5”, respectively of reservation under Proclamation No. 599 dated June 23, 1959.

Parcel No. 6 (Ambayawan River Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land beginning at a point “1” with Latitude 16°-07’-09” and Longitude 120°-45’-32” in the Province of Pangasinan;

the point of beginning; containing an area of Three hundred thirty six million eight hundred seventy eight thousand nine hundred thirty seven (336,878,937 ) square meters more or less. All points referred to are indicated on Plan No; 6.”

Parcel No. 7 (Umiray River Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land beginning at a point “1” with Latitude 14°-58’-11” and Longitude 121°-21’-36” in the Province of Quezon;

The point of beginning; containing an area of Two hundred thirteen million six hundred twenty four thousand (213,624,000) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on Plan No. 7.”

Parcel No. 8 (Kaliwa River Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land beginning at a point “1” being 3148.51 meters 32°-41’-00” W from a point with Latitude 14°-20’-00” and Longitude 121°-25’-00” in the Province of Quezon;

Parcel No. 9 (Caliraya-Lumot Rivers Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land (designated as the Caliraya and Lumot Reservoir Forest Reserve on NIC Plan) beginning at a Point “1” on the plan being N-09˚-40’-00”W, 4,920 meters more or less from BLLM No. 1 of Cavinti, Laguna;

the Point of beginning; containing an area of One hundred seven million seven hundred seven thousand fifty four (107,707,054) square meters, more or less. All Points referred to are indicated on Plan No. 9.”

Parcel No. 10 (Barit River – Lake Buhi Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land beginning at a Point “1” with Latitude 13˚-24’-28” and Longitude 123˚-26’-01” in the Province of Camarines Sur;

the Point of beginning; containing an area of One hundred eighty three million seven hundred ninety eight thousand (183,798,000) square meters, more or less. All Points referred to are indicated on Plan No. 10”

Parcel No. 11 (Jalaur River Forest Reserve)

“A parcel of land beginning at a point “1” with Latitude 1°00’-00” and Longitude 122°-39’-30” in the Province of Iloilo;

All boundaries are subject to actual survey by precise instruments and establishment of the permanent corners.

Improvement and development of pertinent portions of the watershed shall be undertaken in specific or cooperative projects by the Bureau of Forestry, with the cooperation of the Reforestation Administration, National Waterworks and Sewerage Authority, National Irrigation Administration, Bureau of Public Works, National Power Corporation and Bureau of Soils. The services of other agencies may be called upon whenever necessary.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOSE J. LEIDO JR. Assistant Executive Secretary
