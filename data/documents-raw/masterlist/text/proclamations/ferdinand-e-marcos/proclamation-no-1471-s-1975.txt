MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1471

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM AUGUST 1 TO AUGUST 7, 1975, AS NATIONAL ELECTRIFICATION WEEK

WHEREAS, it is the declared national policy of the government to foster the electrification program of the entire country; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting to focus public attention on the accomplishments of the government towards electrification;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO. MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from August 1 to August 7, 1975, as National Electrification Week under the auspices of the National Electrification Administration and other involved entities and organizations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of July in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Date: July 31, 1975
