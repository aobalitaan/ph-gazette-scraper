MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1613

AUTHORIZING THE ZONTA CLUB OF MAKATI, METRO MANILA, TO CONDUCT A FUND DRIVE FROM JANUARY 15 TO MARCH 15, 1977.

WHEREAS, the Zonta Club of Makati, Metro Manila, a non-profit civic organization, is engage in several social action projects, among which are the Silahis Project at Carmona, Cavite; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs the support and cooperation of the public to enable it to carry on with its projects;

NOW THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize said organization to conduct a fund drive from January 15 to March 15, 1977.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
