MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 200-A

RESERVING FOR NATIONAL IMPROVEMENT PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE FORESHORE OF THE DISTRICT OF SAN NICOLAS, PARDO AND TANGKE, TALISAY, CEBU, ISLAND OF CEBU.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for National Improvement purposes, under the administration of the Province of Cebu, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of public domain, situated along the foreshore of Calero, Sawang, Duljo, Mambaling, Basak, Inayawan, San Nicolas; and of Tangke, Talisay, Cebu, Island of Cebu, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows, to wit:

Pls- __________

Proposed for National Improvement Reservation (Project “A”)

“A PARCEL OF LAND (Pls- ______, the Proposed National Improvement Reservation), situated along the foreshore of barrios Calero, Sawang, Duljo, Mambaling, Basak, Inayawan, San Nicolas; and of Tangke, Talisay, Cebu. Bounded on the N., by Cebu Cadastre; on the E., by Pls-124; on the S., by Cebu Harbor; on the W., by barrio Tangke, Talisay, Beginning at a point marked “1”/l of Pls-124 on the attached sketch plan, being project “A”:

beginning; containing an approximate area of FIVE MILLION THREE HUNDRED EIGHTY SIX THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED (5,386,800) Square Meters.

NOTE: All data are approximate and subject to change based on future surveys.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
