MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 596-A

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 563 DATED MAY 23, 1969, ENTITLED “DECLARING A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN THE ISLANDS OF SIARGAO AND BUCAS GRANDE.”

Proclamation No. 563 dated May 23, 1969, entitled “Declaring a State of Public Calamity in the Islands of Siargao and Bucas Grande,” is hereby amended by including the Province of Surigao del Norte in the area covered by the said proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
