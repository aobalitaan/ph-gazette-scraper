TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 796

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE KIWANIS CLUB OF MAKATI TO MARCH 31, 1971.

WHEREAS, the Kiwanis Club of Makati was authorized under Proclamation No. 694 dated May 21, 1970, to conduct a fund drive for its cottage construction projects in Gaches Village; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs more time for the conduct of its drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the Kiwanis Club of Makati to March 31, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) PONCIANO G.A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
