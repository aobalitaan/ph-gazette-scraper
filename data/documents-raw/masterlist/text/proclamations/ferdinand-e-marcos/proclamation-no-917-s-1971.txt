TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 917

AUTHORIZING THE SPECIAL CHILD ASSOCIATION OF CEBU INC., TO CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN FROM NOVEMBER 15, 1971, TO FEBRUARY 20, 1972, IN THE VISAYAS AND MINDANAO, AREA.

WHEREAS, the Special Children Association of Cebu, Inc., is a non-profit, non-stock organization dedicated to the education, care and training and mentally retarded and physically handicapped children; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs the support of the public, for its worthy projects and services to indigent, retarded children;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Special Child Association of Cebu, Inc., to conduct a fund campaign from November 15, 1971, to February 20, 1972. I ask all residents of the Visayas and Mindanao region to support the drive to insure its success.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
