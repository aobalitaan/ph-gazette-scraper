MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION No. 1220

RESERVING FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE MAJOR FERDINAND E. MARCOS VETERANS REGIONAL HOSPITAL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF PALITUD, MUNICIPALITY OF BAYOMBONG, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for building site purposes of the Major Ferdinand E. Marcos Veterans Regional Hospital, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the barrio of Palitud, Municipality of Bayombong, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, Island of Luzon, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

“Technical Description Portion of Lot 2884 and 2879, Bayombong Cad. 45 Extension

A parcel of land on plan (portion of Lot 2884 and 2879, Bayombong Cad. 45 Extension) situated in the Barrio of Palitud, Municipality of Bayombong, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the NE., by Lot 1813, Bayomboim Cad. 45 Ext, on the SE., and SW., by property of NVSIT, and on the NW, by Lot 2921, Bayombong Cad. 45 Ext.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on the Plan being S. 2 deg. 12′ W., 348.00 M to point 2;

point of beginning; containing an area of One Hundred One Thousand and Eight Hundred Thirty-nine square meters more or less.

“All points referred to are on Plan and are marked on the ground as follows: Points “2” and “3” by P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 CM. and the rest are old points. Date of survey was October 2-3, 1973 executed by Primo R. Costes, Geodetic Engineer.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
