MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 916

DECLARING SATURDAY, OCTOBER 9, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY.

WHEREAS, the last day for registration of voters as provided in Republic Act No. 6388, otherwise known as the Election Code of 1971, falls on Saturday, October 9, 1971; and

WHEREAS, in the public interest October 9, 1971, may be declared a special public holiday to give unregistered voters the opportunity to exercise their rights of suffrage in the coming elections;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, October 9, 1971, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary
