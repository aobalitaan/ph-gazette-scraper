TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 888

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 30, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN MANDAUE CITY.

WHEREAS, Monday, August 30, 1971, marks the anniversary of the formal organization of Mandaue City; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the inhabitants of this City should be afforded the opportunity to fully celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, August 30, 1971, as a special public holiday in Mandaue City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MECHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
