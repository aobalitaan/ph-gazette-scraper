MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2049

DECLARING TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 10, 1981, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF LAOAG AND THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE.

WHEREAS, on February 10, 1981, the City of Laoag will celebrate its fiesta which will be a homecoming celebration for all those who given distinction and honor to the City and the Province of Ilocos Norte in their respective fields of endeavors;

WHEREAS, the residents of Laoag City and the Province of Ilocos Norte desire to have full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with the fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, February 10, 1981, as a special public holiday in the City of Laoag and the Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 29th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
