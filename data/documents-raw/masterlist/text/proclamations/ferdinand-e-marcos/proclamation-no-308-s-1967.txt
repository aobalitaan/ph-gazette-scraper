MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 308

DECLARING FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 24, 1967, A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN DUMAGUETE CITY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL.

WHEREAS, the 24th of November marks the founding of the City of Dumaguete in Negros Oriental 19 years ago; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Dumaguete City be afforded the time to honor and celebrate the memorable occasion;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the 24th day of November, 1967, a special public holiday in the City of Dumaguete, Negros Oriental to celebrate the 19th anniversary of the founding of the said city.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
