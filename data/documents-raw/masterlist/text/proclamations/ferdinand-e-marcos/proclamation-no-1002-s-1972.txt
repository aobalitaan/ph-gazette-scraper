TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1002

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MAY 28 TO JUNE 4, 1972, AS “PALARONG PILIPINO” WEEK.

By order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, do hereby declare the period from May 28 to June 4, 1972, as “Palarong Pilipino” Week under the auspices of the Palarong Pilipino Committee, the Sports Development Foundation and National Sports Associations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
