MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 657

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 525 DATED DECEMBER 29, 1965, WHICH EXCLUDED CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND FROM THE GOVERNMENT CENTER RESERVATION IN ZAMBOANGA CITY AND REVERTING THE SAME PARCELS AS PART OF THE GOVERNMENT CENTER RESERVATION.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 525 dated December 29, 1965, which excluded certain parcels of land from the government center reservation in Zamboanga City (Petit Barracks) and opened the same to disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act, and revert the same parcels, subject to private rights, if any there be, as government center reservation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of February in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary
