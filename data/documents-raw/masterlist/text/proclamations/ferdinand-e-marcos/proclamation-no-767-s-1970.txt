OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 767

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE TUBERCULOSIS SOCIETY, INC, UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1970.

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 712 elated June 26, 1970 the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. is conducting its National Educational and Fund Campaign from August 1 to October 31, 1970:

WHEREAS, it is imperative for the Society to fully subscribe its goal of P600.000 in order to maintain its humanitarian services to victims of tuberculosis and to protect the community from the White Plague; and

WHEREAS, the P600.000 goal can only be realized by granting the Society additional time to conclude its fund-raising activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the national educational and fund campaign of the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. from November 15, 1970 to January 15, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and cause the seal, of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
