TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 746

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 2, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN IRIGA CITY.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 2, 1970, marks the charter anniversary of the City of Iriga; and

WHEREAS, the residents should be given the opportunity to commemorate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 2, 1970, as a special public holiday in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
