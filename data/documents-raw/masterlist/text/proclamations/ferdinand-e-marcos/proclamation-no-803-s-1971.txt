MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 803

ESTABLISHING AS TANAP WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE THE PARCEL OF PUBLIC DOMAIN PER W. R. MAP NO. 17, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF BURGOS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE.

Upon recommendation of the Director of Forestry, approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the provisions of Section 1826 of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended by Republic Act No. 3092, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or other disposition and establish and proclaim as Tanap Watershed Forest Reserve, to protect and conserve the headwaters of Carumsi Creek, subject to private rights, if any there be, the parcel of public domain located in the Municipality of Burgos, Province of Ilocos Norte, under the administration and control of the Director of Forestry, who shall have the authority to regulate the use and occupancy of said forest reservation and the cutting, collection and removal of timber and other forest products therein in accordance with forest law and regulations.

The aforementioned parcel of public domain is shown in W.R. Map No. 17, and is more particularly described as follows:
