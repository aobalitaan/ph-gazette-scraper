TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1395

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE FUND DRIVE OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR TRACK AND FIELD ASSOCIATION TO MAY 30, 1975.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Track and Field Association were authorized under Proclamation No. 1361, dated December 17, 1974 to conduct a Fund Drive from December 1974 to February 1975, to finance the Philippine participation in the Second Asian Track and Field Championships to be held in Seoul, Korea, in June 1975; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Track and Field Association need additional time to enable them to raise the necessary funds for the Philippine participation in the said sports event and to prosecute their age-group program;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund drive of the Philippine Amateur Track and Field Association up to May 30, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary
