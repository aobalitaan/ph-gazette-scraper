TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 795

EXTENDING NATIONAL SHRINES COMMISSION FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF CORREGIDOR AND MT. SAMAT NATIONAL SHRINES UP TO JUNE 30, 1971.

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission was authorized under Proclamation No. 638 dated December 31, 1969, to extend its fund campaign for the development of Corregidor and Mt. Samat national shrines up to December 31, 1970; and

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission needs more time to realize its fund campaign goals to pursue its on-going projects on Corregidor and Mt. Samat National Shrines;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby further extend the National Shrines Commission fund campaign period up to June 30, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
