MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 298

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL BUILDING SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MABINI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of General Services, and pursuant to the provisions of Section 64 (e) of the Revised Administrative Code in relation to Section 1 of Republic Act No. 477, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for Municipal building site purposes under the administration of the Municipality of Mabini, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the private domain, situated in the Municipality of Mabini, Province of Davao, and more particularly described as follows:

Block 25, Psd-46341

(Municipal Building Site)

A parcel of land (Block 25 of the subdivision plan Psd-46341, being a portion of Lot 92, Psd-34108), situated in the Municipality of Mabini, Province of Davao, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the South, points 1-2-3-4 by Road Lot 1; on the West, points 4-5 by Road Lot 1; on the North, points 5-6-7-8 by Road Lot 1; and on the East, points 8-1 by Road Lot 2 (National Highway), all of the subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 59 deg. 17′ E., 650.03 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 20, Pantukan Cadastre,

thence S. 51 deg. 20′ W., 4.93 m. to point 2;

thence N. 88 deg. 12′ W., 103.50 m. to point 3;

thence N. 46 deg. 28′ W., 3.57 m. to point 4;

thence N. 4 deg. 31′ W., 129.60 m. to point 5;

thence N. 47 deg. 30′ E., 4.69 m. to point 6;

thence S. 81 deg. 24′ E., 106.00 m. to point 7;

thence S. 37 deg. 28′ E., 4.42 m. to point 8;

thence S. 4 deg. 31′ E., 116.00 m. to point 1;

point of beginning; containing an area of FOURTEEN THOUSAND AND NINETY-ONE (14,091) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by P.L.S. Cyl. Cons. Mons. 15 x 60 cms.; bearings true; date of the Subdivision Survey, April 22 to June 12, 1955.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
