MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 436

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM AND LIBRARY, INC. EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND DRIVE UP TO SEPTEMBER 30, 1968.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 326 dated March 11, 1968, authorized the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc. to conduct its 1968 Educational, Membership and Fund Campaign during the period from March 1 to July 30, 1968; and

WHEREAS, the closing of the school year before the middle of the campaign period and other unforeseen factors have hampered the progress of the campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the 1968 Educational, Membership and Fund Campaign up to September 30, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
