TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1010

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE COOPERATIVE CREDIT UNION LEAGUE, INC.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 943 dated December 21, 1971, authorized the Philippine Credit Union League, Inc. to conduct a national fund campaign from February to May 1972; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Credit Union League, Inc. needs more time to realize its goal and to involve as many participants as possible to bring the credit union message to all the Filipino people;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Cooperative Credit Union League, Inc. to October 31, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
