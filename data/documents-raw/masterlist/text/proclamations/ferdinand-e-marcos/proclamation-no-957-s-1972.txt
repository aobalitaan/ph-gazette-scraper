MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 957

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 952, DATED JANUARY 12, 1972, WHICH DECLARED THE EXISTENCE OF A STATE OF CALAMITY IN THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE.

WHEREAS, the existence of a state of public calamity was declared in Leyte under Proclamation No. 952 dated January 12, 1972, due to typhoon “ASIANG”; and

WHEREAS, extensive destruction to life and property has similarly been wrought upon the province of Southern Leyte by typhoon “ASIANG;”

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 952 dated January 12, 1972, by including the province of Southern Leyte in the calamity area.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
