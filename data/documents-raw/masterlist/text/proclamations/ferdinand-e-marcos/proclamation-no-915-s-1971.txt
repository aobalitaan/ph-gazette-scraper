TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 915

DECLARING FRIDAY, OCTOBER 8, 1971, AS PHILIPPINE LIONS SERVICE DAY.

WHEREAS, the Lions International is a voluntary organization of civic-spirited men working together on organized civic projects and in creating goodwill among all nations;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, in recognition of the role of the Lions in nation-building, do hereby declare Friday, October 8, 1971, as Philippine Lions Service Day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
