MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 630

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 361 DATED MARCH 6, 1968, RESERVING FOR INDUSTRIAL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN IN LAMAO, MUNICIPALITY OF LIMAY, PROVINCE OF BATAAN.
