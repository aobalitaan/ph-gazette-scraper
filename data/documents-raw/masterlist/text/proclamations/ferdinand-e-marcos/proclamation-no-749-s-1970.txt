TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 749

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 1970, AS “ARAW NG OLONGAPO.”

In order to secure the cooperation and participation, as well as enhance the interest, of Olongapo residents in the history and progress of their city, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines* do hereby declare Thursday, September 3, 1970, as “Araw ng Olongapo.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundreds and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
