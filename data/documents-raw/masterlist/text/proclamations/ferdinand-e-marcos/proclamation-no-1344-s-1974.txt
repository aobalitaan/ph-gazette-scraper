TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1344

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 8 TO 14, 1974, AS MANAGEMENT CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK

WHEREAS, it is the policy of the Government to promote management consciousness in both the public and private sectors to help achieve the development goals of the Nation;

WHEREAS, management consciousness is essential to promote better business practices, increase production and improve public administration and services, which are vital prerequisites to regional economic development, social progress and stability; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Council of Management is sponsoring of Fifth Philippine Management Congress from December 8 to 14, 1974, during which management experts, practitioners and educators from the Philippines and other Asian-Pacific countries will explore the avenues for greater regional cooperation among Asian nations;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from December 8 to 14, 1974, as “MANAGEMENT CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
