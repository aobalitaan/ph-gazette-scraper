MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1392

DECLARING FEBRUARY 26, 27 AND 28, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAYS.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 1366-A authorizes the holding of a referendum on February 27, 1975 on certain vital issues affecting the national interest; and

WHEREAS, it is deemed necessary to give our people the full opportunity to participate in the said referendum;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers in me vested by law, do hereby declare February 26, 27 and 28, 1975 as special public holidays.

I hereby call upon all qualified citizens to exercise their right to vote on the issues to be presented to them on referendum day, February 27, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
