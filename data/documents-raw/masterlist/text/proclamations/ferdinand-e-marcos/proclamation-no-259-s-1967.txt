MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 259

RESERVING FOR THE NORTHERN MINDANAO DEMONSTRATION FISH FARM, A PORTION OF PUBLIC LANDS SITUATED AT LALA, LANAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE COMMISSION OF FISHERIES.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 73 of Act No. 4003, as amended, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for Demonstration Fish Farm and for such other purposes as would develop the fishpond industry, under the administration of the Fisheries Commission, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain portion of marshy land situated in the Town of Lala, Province of Lanao del Norte, Island of Mindanao and more particularly described as follows:
