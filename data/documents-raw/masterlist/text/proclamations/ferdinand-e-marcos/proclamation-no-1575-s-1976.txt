MALACANANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1575

DECLARING AUGUST 30, 1976, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN.

WHEREAS, the thirtieth of August marks the birth anniversary of the late Marcelo H. del Pilar of Plaridel, Bulacan, one of the outstanding heroes in our country’s struggle for freedom and democracy; and

WHEREAS, in recognition of the outstanding services of the late Marcelo H. del Pilar, it is most fitting and proper to give the people of Bulacan full opportunity to celebrate his natal day with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare August 30, 1976, as a special public holiday in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
