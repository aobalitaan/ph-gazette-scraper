TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 814

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 24, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CEBU CITY.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, February 24, 1971, marks the charter anniversary of the City of Cebu; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Cebu City should be given opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Wednesday, February 24, 1971, as a special public holiday in Cebu City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
