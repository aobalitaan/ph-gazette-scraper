TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1097

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE NARCOTICS FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC., UP TO JUNE 30, 1973.

WHEREAS, the Narcotics Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 997 dated April 30, 1972, to conduct a fund drive for curbing drug addiction, with preventive and rehabilitative efforts, up to December 31, 1972; and

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs more time for the conduct of its drive to attain its fund goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the Narcotics Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., up to June 30, 1973.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
