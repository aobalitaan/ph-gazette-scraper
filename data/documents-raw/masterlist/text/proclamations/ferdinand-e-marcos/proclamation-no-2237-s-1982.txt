MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2237

WITHDRAWING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND RESERVED FOR MARINE FISHERIES MULTI-PURPOSE FARM UNDER PROC. NO. 1282 DATED JUNE 21, 1974 AND PLACING THE SAME UNDER THE CONTROL AND SUPERVISION OF THE PHILIPPINE TOURISM AUTHORITY FOR DEVELOPMENT PURPOSES.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw certain parcels of land from the operation of Proc. No. 1282 dated June 21, 1974, which reserved certain lands of the public domain for Marine Fisheries Multi-Purpose Farm purposes and reserve the same for tourism development purposes under the control and supervision of the Philippine Tourism Authority, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 1

Beginning at a Point marked “1”, thence:

beginning. Containing an area of Ten Thousand (10,000) Square Meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by B.L. Cyl. Concrete monuments. Bounded on the SW., along line 1-2 by Boulevard; on the NW., along line 2-3 by the marine demonstration fish farm; on the NE., along line 3-4 Lingayen Gulf; and on the SE., along line 4-1 by Lot 2.

Lot No. 2

Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 2, being N. 30-54 E., 4679.80 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 1, Mun. of Alaminos, Pangasinan thru MSI-1993; thence

point of beginning.

Containing an area of Nine Thousand Eight Hundred and eleven (9,811) Square Meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows; Point 2 by “X” on Conc. Wall; Points 3 and 4 by Conc. Posts; and the rest by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by Lot 1; on the NE., along lines 2-3-4 by School of Fisheries; on the SE., along line 4-5 by Road; and on the SW., along line 5-1 by Proposed Boulevard.

Lot No. 3

Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 3, being N. 31-44 E., 4672.30 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 1, Mun. of Alaminos, Pangasinan thru MSI-1993; thence

point of beginning.

Containing an area of Twenty-Three Thousand Three Hundred and Ninety-seven (23,397) Square Meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by Road; on the NE., along line 2-3 by School of Fisheries; on the NE., and SE., along lines 3-4-5-6-7-8-9 by Lingayen Gulf and on the SW., along lines 9-1 by Proposed Boulevard.

Subject to more precise survey.

NOTE: This survey is a portion of Lingayen Gulf.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila this 6th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
