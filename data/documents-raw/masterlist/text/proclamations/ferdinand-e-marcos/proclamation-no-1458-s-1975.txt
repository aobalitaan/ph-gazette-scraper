MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1458

RESERVING A CERTAIN PORTION OF LAND SITUATED AT RIO HONDO, ZAMBOANGA CITY, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, AS A SETTLEMENT AREA

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve a parcel of land situated in the Rio Hondo-Mariki Island area, Zamboanga City, Island of Mindanao, as a Settlement Area, in the name of the Filipino Muslim Community, under the Administration of the Southern Philippines Development Administration, which land is more particularly described as follows:

A parcel of land (Identified as Lot-1-A Psu-125350) situated at Rio Hondo, Zamboanga City, Island of Mindanao; Bounded on the North, Along lines 6-7-8-9 by Lot-1, Psu-125349; on the East, Along Lines 9-10-1 by Lot-1-B, Psu-125350; on the Southwest, Along lines 4–5 by Public lands; on the Northwest, Along lines 5–6 by Lot-2, Psu-125350. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, bearing S 25 deg. 11′ E., 1748.48 m. from Mon 203, Zamboanga Cadastre No. 14.

point of beginning, containing an area of TWO HUNDRED THIRTY FIVE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED EIGHTY SEVEN (235,587) SQUARE METERS.

NOTE: All data are approximate and subject to change based on future surveys.

The provisions of existing proclamations in conflict with this proclamation are hereby repealed.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 7th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
