TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 942

DECLARING FRIDAY, DECEMBER 31, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, the thirtieth day (Thursday) of December, 1971, and the first day (Saturday) of January, 1972, being holidays, the thirty-first day (Friday) of December 1971, may be declared a special public holiday to enable the people to enjoy an uniterrupted three-day holiday without prejudice to the public interest;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby proclaim Friday, December thirty-first, nineteen hundred and seventy-one, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I nave hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
