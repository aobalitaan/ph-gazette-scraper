TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1461

AUTHORIZING THE QUEZON CITY YOUTH DEVELOPMENT FOUNDATION, INC. TO CONDUCT A FUND RAISING CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD JULY 15 TO DECEMBER 15, 1975.

WHEREAS, quite a number of juvenile delinquents detained at the Quezon City Jail, have for lack of space, to mix with the adult offenders;

WHEREAS, there is an urgent need for an intensive campaign to rehabilitate our juvenile delinquents and to raise funds for the construction of a home for them so as to house them separately from the adult offenders and improve their living conditions;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Quezon City Youth Development Foundation, Inc. to conduct a fund raising campaign during the period from July 15 to December 15, 1975. I call upon all citizens and residents of the Greater Manila Area, irrespective of race and creed, to support the campaign by giving generously of their means to alleviate the miserable plight of our misguided youth.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
