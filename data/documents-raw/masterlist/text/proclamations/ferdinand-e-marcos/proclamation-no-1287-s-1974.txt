TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1287

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATION AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PANAMIN FOUNDATION, INC. UNTIL DECEMBER 31, 1974

WHEREAS, the PANAMIN Foundation, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 1145 dated May 18, 1973, to conduct a national education and fund campaign from February 29, 1973, to December 31, 1973, and was extended under Proclamation No. 1216 to June 30, 1974, for the purpose of raising much needed funds with which to carry on its various humanitarian projects for the benefit of our national minorities who comprise more than twelve percent of our national population; and

WHEREAS, to enable PANAMIN to carry on its laudable activities, it is necessary that its fund campaign be extended;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby further extend the national education fund drive of the PANAMIN Foundation, Inc., until December 31, 1974. I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality, race or creed, to assist in this humanitarian endeavor by giving generously of their means.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
