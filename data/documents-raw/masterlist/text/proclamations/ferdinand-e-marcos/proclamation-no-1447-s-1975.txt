MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1447

DECLARING TUESDAY, JULY 1, 1975, AS MONCADA CENTENNIAL YEAR AND A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN MONCADA, TARLAC.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, July 1, 1975, marks the centenary of the founding of the municipality of Moncada, province of Tarlac; and

WHEREAS, the people of Moncada desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this milestone in their town’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, July 1, 1975, as Moncada Centennial Year and a special public holiday in Moncada, Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
