TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 925

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE NARCOTICS FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC. UP TO MARCH 31, 1972.

WHEREAS, the Narcotics Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 846 dated May 3, 1971, to conduct a fund drive for curbing drug addiction, with preventive and rehabilitative efforts, up to September 30, 1971; and

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs more time for the conduct of its drive to attain its fund goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the Narcotics Foundation of the Philippines, Inc. up to March 31, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
