TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION No. 1255

AUTHORIZING THE SPECIAL CHILD ASSOCIATION OF CEBU, INC. TO CONDUCT A CAMPAIGN FOR PURPOSES OF A NATIONAL RAFFLE FOR THE BENEFIT OF THE PUPILS OF ST. MARTIN DE PORRES SCHOOL FROM MAY 1 TO AUGUST 31, 1974.

WHEREAS, the Special Child Association of Cebu, Inc., which is a non-stock, non-profit organization registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission, owns and manages St. Martin de Porres School for mentally retarded and hearing impaired children;

WHEREAS, it is the only school of its kind outside of Manila and the only residential school for this kind of children in the Philippines;

WHEREAS, in order to carry out the worthy objectives of the Association, it needs the support and cooperation of the public;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, Ferdinand E. Marcos, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Special Child Association of Cebu, Inc., to conduct a campaign during the period from May 1 to August 31, 1974, for purposes of the national raffle to be held for the sole benefit of the pupils of St. Martin de Porres School. I enjoin all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality, race or creed, to assist generously in this humanitarian endeavor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
