MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2348

DECLARING MONDAY, APRIL 2, 1984, “SERGIO OSMEÑA, JR. DAY” IN THE PROVINCE OF CEBU AND ITS COMPONENT CITIES, INCLUDING CEBU CITY.

WHEREAS, Sergio Osmeña, Jr. was one of the great sons of Cebu who distinguished himself as a statesman, a politician and a good public servant;

WHEREAS, the people of the Province of Cebu and its component cities including Cebu City should be given full opportunity to commemorate his dedicated service with fitting ceremonies upon his final arrival after a long sojourn in a foreign country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law do hereby declare Monday, April 2, 1984, as “Sergio Osmeña, Jr. Day” and a special non-working holiday in the province of Cebu and its component cities, including Cebu City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-four.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) MANUEL M. LAZARO Presidential Assistant for Legal Affairs
