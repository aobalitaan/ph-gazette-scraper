MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 569

DECLARING TUESDAY, JUNE 24, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MANILA

WHEREAS, the 398th anniversary foundation of the City of Manila falls on June 24, 1969; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting, that the people of Manila be given ample opportunity to celebrate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, June 24, 1969, as a special public holiday in the City of. Manila.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
