MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 431

RESERVING FOR FRESHWATER DEMONSTRATION FISH FARM AND NURSERY SITE PURPOSES UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE PHILIPPINE FISHERIES COMMISSION A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippine, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for freshwater demonstration fish farm and nursery site purposes under the administration of the Philippine Fisheries Commission, subject to private rights, if any thereby, and to the exclusion of the 30-meter wide strip along each bank of the Calios River, certain parcel of land of the Public domain situated in the municipality of Sta. Cruz, province of Laguna, island of Luzon, more particularly described as follows:

Proposed Reservation for FRESHWATER DEMONSTRATION FISH FARM AND NURSERY Site.

A PARCEL OF LAND (the proposed reservation for Fresh Water Demonstration Fish Farm and Nursery Sites), situated in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, province of Laguna. Bounded on the E., along lines 1-2-3-4, by Lots 986 and 988, Sta. Cruz Cad. And Calios River; on the NE., along lines 4-5-6, by Calios River; on the SE., along lines 6-7-8, by Lot 1273, along line 8-9, by Lot 1093 and along line 9-10 by Lot 1350, all of Sta. Cruz Cad.; and on the SE., W., and NW., along lines 10-11-12-1, by Laguna Lake Beginning at a point marked “1” on the attached Sketch Plan and identical to Corner 5 of Lot 986, Sta. Cruz Cad.,

of beginning; containing an approximate area of ONE HUNDRED FIFTY FOUR THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED SEVENTY ONE (154,571) Square Meters.

NOTE: All data are approximate and subject to change based on future surveys.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
