MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 863

RESERVING FOR COMMUNAL FISHING GROUND PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN COMPRISING A PORTION OF THE FORESHORE FRONTING TAMBAC BAY AND LINGAYEN GULF ALONG THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALAMINOS, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for communal fishing ground purposes, subject only to the proposed barrio site in Lucap, certain parcels of land of the public domain comprising a portion of the foreshore fronting Tambac Bay and Lingayen Gulf along the Municipality of Alaminos, Province of Pangasinan, Island of Luzon, covering an area of approximately 2,000 hectares, which are more particularly described as follows:

Beginning at a point marked BLLM No. 1, Alaminos, Pangasinan, to corner 1 N30º-00’W 10,500.00 meters, thence

All permits, lease agreements or agreements of any kind, and pending sales application, saltwork applications, fishpond applications, foreshore lease applications or other similar applications covering or involving areas embraced in this proclamation are hereby deemed rejected or cancelled in the public interest. Sales or free patents or titles issued over any portion of the reservation in violation of the Public Land Act or existing laws and regulations shall, after proper proceedings, be cancelled.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
