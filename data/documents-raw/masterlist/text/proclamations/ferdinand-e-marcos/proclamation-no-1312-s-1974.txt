Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1312

DECLARING DECEMBER 14, 1974 AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER AS FIRE SERVICE RECOGNITION DAY

WHEREAS, there is necessity to focus public attention on the important role of our firefighters in saving of human lives and properties, sometimes sacrificing their lives in the process; and

WHEREAS, the Association of Fire Fighters of the Philippines desires to celebrate Fire Service Recognition Day on December 14 of every year;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare December 14, 1974 and every year thereafter as Fire Service Recognition Day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of September in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
