PROCLAMATION NO. 2480

WITHDRAWING FROM MARIKINA WATERSHED RESERVATION AND RESERVING FOR RESETTLEMENT PURPOSES CERTAIN PORTIONS OF SAID RESERVATION SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF ANTIPOLO, IN THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL, ISLAND OF LUZON, PHILIPPINES UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE METROPOLITAN WATERWORKS AND SEWERAGE SYSTEM IN COOPERATION WITH THE APPROPRIATE AGENCIES.

Upon the recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby reserve for resettlement purposes under the administration of the Metropolitan Waterworks and Sewerage System, in coordination with the Ministry of Agrarian Reform, Ministry of Human Settlements and other appropriate government agencies, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future classification and survey certain parcels of land more particularly described as follows:

A. SAN ISIDRO VALLEY

A parcel of land being a portion of the Marikina Watershed, IN-12, and more particularly known as the San Ysiro Valley situated in the Municipality of Antipolo, Province of Rizal, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the North and Northwest, along lines 8-9-1-2-3-4; on the East, along lines 4-5-6-7; and on the South, along lines 7-8, by portion of the Marikina Watershed, IN-12. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N 12º17’W, 1474.07 m. to point “2”;

Thence N 2º45’E, 758.82 m. to point “3”; Thence N 20º14’E, 4673.56 m. to point “4”; Thence S 0º37’W, 4063.70 m. to point “5”; Thence S 40º02’E, 1952.23 m. to point “6”; Thence S 16º15’E, 732.28 m. to point “7”; Thence S 52º29’W, 3230.64 m. to point “8”; Thence N 48º59’W, 1118.05 m. to point “9”; Thence N 0º46’W, 1218.46 m. to point of beginning. Containing an area of Fifteen Million Seventy One Thousand Four Hundred Fifty One (15,071,451) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: points “1”, “2”, “3”, “4” and “6”, peaks of Mts. Campana, Cauacaua, Sumarapsap, Masarat and Tinucan respectively; points “5” and “7”, peaks of Pinaglintikan Hill and Bobo Hill, respectively; point “8”, rock; and point “9”, atranca. Point “4” is equivalent to point “30” of Marikina Watershed, IN-12. Bearings true; date of original survey March 1-July 1, 1909.

B. PARCEL I

A parcel of land (being a portion of the Marikina Watershed, IN-12) situated in the Municipality of Antipolo, Province of Rizal, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the West and North, along lines 1-2-3 by portion of Marikina Watershed, IN- 12; on the East, alone lines 3-4, by barrio Sta. Ines, Tanay, Rizal; on the South, along lines 4-1, by San Ysiro Valley. Beginning at a point marked “1” on the sketch plan, being N 5º04’W, 5518.35 m. to point “2”;

Thence N 87º14’E, 2064.37 m. to point “3”; Thence S 25º01’E, 1675.00 m. to point “4”; Thence S 29º14’W, 4673.56 m. to point of beginning. Containing an area of Eight Million Eight Hundred Sixty Seven Thousand Nine Hundred Twenty Two (8,867,922) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: Points “1” and “4”, peaks of Mts. Sumarapsap and Masarat, respectively; points “2” and “3” stakes. Point “4” is equivalent to corner 30 of Marikina Watershed, IN-12. Bearings true, date of original survey March 1-July 1, 1909 and that the preparation of the sketch plan is March 15, 1983.
