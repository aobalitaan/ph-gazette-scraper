MALACAñANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 766

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 339 DATED SEPTEMBER 3, 1956, WHICH AMENDED PROCLAMATION NO. 357 DATED DECEMBER 2, 1952, AND VESTING IN THE GOVERNOR OF THE LAND AUTHORITY THE POWER TO ADMINISTER AND DISPOSE OF SUCH AREAS EMBRACED THEREIN.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and the Governor of the Land Authority, in order to promote administrative efficiency in the speedy distribution and titling of settlement lots within the area embraced in the settlement project established under Proclamation No. 357 dated December 2, 1952, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested upon me by law, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 339, Series of 1956, which amended said Proclamation No. 357, and transfer to the Governor of the Land Authority the power and authority to dispose of the lands covered thereby.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred, and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES

Source: Malacañang Records Office
