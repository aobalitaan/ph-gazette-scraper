TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION No. 1204

DECLARING NOVEMBER 26, 1973 TO DECEMBER 2, 1973 AS AMATEUR RADIO WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Radio Association, Inc. celebrates its 41st anniversary on November 26, 1973; and

WHEREAS, there is a necessity to focus public attention on the important role the Association plays in radio broadcasting service in times of national emergencies, disasters and calamities, and in promoting and fostering friendly international relations;

NOW THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines, do hereby declare November 26, 1973 to December 2, 1973 as Amateur Radio Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
