MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1569

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 16, 1976, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN PASAY CITY.

WHEREAS, the City of Pasay celebrates the sixteenth day of August as “Araw ng Pasay” marking the foundation of the City twenty-nine years ago; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the residents of Pasay City be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, August 16, 1976, as a special public holiday in Pasay City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
