TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1341

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHESTS AND COUNCILS IN THE PHILIPPINES TO DECEMBER 31, 1974

WHEREAS, the Community Chests and Councils in the Philippines were authorized under Proclamation No. 1268 dated May 30, 1974, to conduct fund campaigns from June 1 to October 31, 1974, to support the services of their welfare agencies; and

WHEREAS, the Community Chests organizations need additional time to enable them to realize their fund campaign goals;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by the order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the annual educational and fund campaign of the Community Chests and Councils in the Philippines to December 31, 1974.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
