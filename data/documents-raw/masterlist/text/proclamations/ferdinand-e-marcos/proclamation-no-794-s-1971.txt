TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 794

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM FEBRUARY 8 TO 15, 1971, AS SMALL SCALE INDUSTRIES WEEK.

WHEREAS, small scale industries have been contributing in a certain degree to the economic development of our country;

WHEREAS, the development of small scale industries as a vital force in economic stability of the country is one of the major concerns of this demonstration; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus public attention on the role small scale industries play in national progress;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from February 8 to 15, 1971, as Small Scale Industries Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) PONCIANO G.A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
