MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 743

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM AUGUST 11 TO 17, 1970, AS “PHILIPPINE CONSTRUCTION WEEK.”

WHEREAS, the construction industry touches the very core of our economic development program;

WHEREAS, the technical knowledge, special skills and resources of our Filipino contractors contribute greatly to the progress and the growth of the nation;

WHEREAS, it is desirable to recognize the important role the Philippine construction industry has played in our development program and in promoting national progress; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Contractors Association, on August 17, 1970, completes 25 years of existence characterized by tremendous improvement in construction techniques and greater utilization of modern construction equipment;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, do hereby declare August 11 to 17, 1970, as “Philippine Construction Week” and enjoin all concerned to contribute to the success of the celebration of the Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
