TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1024

DECLARING THURSDAY, JUNE 15, 1972, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY

WHEREAS, Thursday, June 15, 1972, marks the 22nd charter anniversary of the City of Cagayan de Oro; and

WHEREAS, the residents should be afforded an opportunity to commemorate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thursday, June 15, 1972, as a special public holiday in the City of Cagayan de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
