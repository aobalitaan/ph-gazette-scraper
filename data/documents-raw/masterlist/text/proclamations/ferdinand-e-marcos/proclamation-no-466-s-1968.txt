MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 466

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE CHESHIRE HOME, INC. (SINAG-TALA) UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1968.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Cheshire Home, Inc. (Sinag-Tala) was authorized to conduct a fund campaign from February 16 to August 16, 1968, under Proclamation No, 339 dated February 5, 1968, for its sick and disabled people’s home and allied civic projects; and

WHEREAS, the said organization needs additional time for the conduct of its drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the educational and fund campaign of the Philippine Cheshire Home, Inc., up to December 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
