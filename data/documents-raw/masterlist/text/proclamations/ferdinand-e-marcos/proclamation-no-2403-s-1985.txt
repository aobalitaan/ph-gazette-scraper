MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2403

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL SHOOTING ASSOCIATION TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM JANUARY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1985.

WHEREAS, the Philippine National Shooting Association is an organization committed to the development of target-shooting as a National Sport;

WHEREAS, the Association seeks to develop among Filipino shooters the highest degree of excellence in the sport and enhance the competitive position of the country in international competitions;

WHEREAS, the development needs of this sport require utmost support from all sectors to enable it to pursue its goal and objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Philippine National Shooting Association (PNSA) to conduct a national fund campaign during the period from January 1 to December 31, 1985.

I call upon all citizens of the Philippines, shooting enthusiasts all sport-minded individuals, government and private corporations to give generously of their means to this laudable undertaking.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
