TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 656-A

DECLARING THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 19, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BAGO CITY

WHEREAS, Thursday, February 19, 1970, marks the anniversary of the Charter of Bago City; and

WHEREAS, the people of the City should be given opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thursday, February 19, 1970, as a special public holiday in Bago City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
