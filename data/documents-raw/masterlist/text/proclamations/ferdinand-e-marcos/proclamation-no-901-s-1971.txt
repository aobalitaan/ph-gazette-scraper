TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 901

DECLARING TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 7, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF CAVITE.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, September 7, 1971, marks the thirty-first anniversary of the charter of the City of Cavite; and

WHEREAS, the people of the city should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in the history of their city;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary; by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, September 7, 1971, as a special public holiday in the City of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MECHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
