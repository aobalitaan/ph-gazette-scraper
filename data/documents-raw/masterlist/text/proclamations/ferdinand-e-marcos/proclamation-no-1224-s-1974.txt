TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION No. 1224

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JANUARY 20 TO 26, 1974, AS NATIONAL YOUTH WEEK.

WHEREAS, today’s youth, realizing the changes for the better wrought under the New Society, clamor for more participation in nation-building and in the formation of a mature, responsible and upright citizenry that should go with a developing nation; and

WHEREAS, they are now willing to share in the collective effort to bring about a society that is compassionate, relevant, and strong;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from January 20 to 26, 1974, as National Youth Week. I call upon all the young citizens of the Philippines to spearhead activities and projects contributing to national development and present a united force in coordination with Government efforts to build .a stronger society.

In order to insure the success of the occasion, an Executive Committee is hereby created to be Chairmanned by the Secretary of Education and Culture and composed of such members as may be designated by him.

The Committee shall initiate and implement all measures as may be necessary to fulfill the objectives of this Proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
