MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2442

DECLARING TUESDAY, AUGUST 20, 1985, AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF LUCENA.

WHEREAS, the 24th foundation anniversary of the City of Lucena falls on Tuesday, August 20, 1985; and

WHEREAS, the people therein desire to commemorate this milestone in the history of their city with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, August 20, 1985, “Araw ng Lucena ’85” as a non-working special public holiday in the City of Lucena.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
