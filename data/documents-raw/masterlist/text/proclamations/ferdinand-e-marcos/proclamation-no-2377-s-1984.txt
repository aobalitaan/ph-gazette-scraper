MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2377

FURTHER AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 2374, AS AMENDED, WHICH DECLARED THE EXISTENCE OF A PUBLIC CALAMITY IN CERTAIN PROVINCES AND CITIES IN THE COUNTRY.

I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES, BY VIRTUE OF THE POWERS VESTED IN ME BY LAW, DO HEREBY FURTHER AMEND PROCLAMATION NO. 2374, AS AMENDED, DECLARING THE EXISTENCE OF A PUBLIC CALAMITY IN CERTAIN PROVINCES AND CITIES IN THE COUNTRY TO INCLUDE THEREIN THE PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL NORTE.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I HAVE HEREUNTO SET MY HAND AND CAUSED THE SEAL OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES TO BE AFFIXED.

DONE IN THE CITY OF MANILA, THIS 12TH DAY OF SEPTEMBER, IN THE YEAR OF OUR LORD, NINETEEN HUNDRED AND EIGHTY-FOUR.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

BY THE PRESIDENT: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
