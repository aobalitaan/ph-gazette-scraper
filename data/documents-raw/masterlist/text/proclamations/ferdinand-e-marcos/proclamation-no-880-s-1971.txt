TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 880

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL SHRINES COMMISSION FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF CORREGIDOR AND MT. SAMAT NATIONAL SHRINES UP TO JUNE 30, 1972.

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission was authorized under Proclamation No. 795 dated January 12, 1971, to extend its fund campaign for the development of Corregidor and Mt. Samat National Shrines up to June 30, 1971; and

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission needs more time to realize its fund campaign goals to pursue its on-going projects on Corregidor and Mt. Samat National Shrines;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO M. MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby further extend the National Shrines Commission fund campaign period up to June 30, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MECHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
