MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 544

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND DRIVE OF THE PHILIPPINE CANCER SOCIETY, INC.

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 494 dated December 16, 1968, the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc. was authorized to conduct a national educational, membership and fund drive from January 1 to March 31, 1969; and

WHEREAS, said society needs additional time for the conduct of a more effective and productive drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested upon me by law, do hereby extend the educational, membership and fund drive of the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc. to June 30, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the “seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of March in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
