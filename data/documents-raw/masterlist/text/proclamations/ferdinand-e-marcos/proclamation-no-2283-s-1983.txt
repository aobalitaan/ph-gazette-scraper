MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2283

DECLARING DAGAT-DAGATAN AS THE SITE OF THE KKK REGIONAL PROCESSING CENTER FOR THE NATIONAL CAPITAL REGION

WHEREAS, the Ministry of Human Settlements under its Shelter-Cum-Livelihood Program have succeeded in integrating the provisions of shelter and livelihood for its beneficiaries;

WHEREAS, there is a necessity to activate a KKK Regional Processing Center in the National Capital Region to serve as a show case for the establishment, operation and maintenance of self-reliant and economically viable agro-industrial processing area; and

WHEREAS, Dagat-Dagat is an area wherein massive government investments have been poured to vigorously implement a comprehensive and integrated shelter and livelihood related programs and projects in order to achieve political commitments envisioned to benefit the greatest number of people;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, and in relation to Section 8 of the Executive Order No. 866, do hereby declare Dagat-Dagatan as the site of the KKK Regional Processing Center for the National Capital Region.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-three.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
