MALACANANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1571

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 1570, DECLARING THURSDAY, AUGUST 19, 1976, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN QUEZON CITY.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 1570 dated August 16, 1976, by extending the special public holiday to Quezon Province, the birthplace of the late President Manuel L. Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
