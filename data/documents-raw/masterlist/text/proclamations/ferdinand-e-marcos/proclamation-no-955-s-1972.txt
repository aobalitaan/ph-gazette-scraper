TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 955

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 6 TO 12 OF EVERY YEAR AS NATIONAL PET WEEK

WHEREAS, animal pets have brought joy and consolation to man; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to inculcate in the minds and hearts of our youth the virtues of kindness and mercy to our pets which have delighted humanity from time to time immemorial;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from March 6 to 12 of every year as National Pet Week under the auspices of the Philippine Kennel Club, the Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals and other organizations involved.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) Alejandro Melchor Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) Roberto V. Reyes Assistant Executive Secretary
