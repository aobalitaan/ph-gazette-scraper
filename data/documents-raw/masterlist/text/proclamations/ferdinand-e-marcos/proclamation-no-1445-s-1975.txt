TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1445

EXTENDING THE FUND DRIVE OF THE AMATEUR BOXING ASSOCIATION.

WHEREAS, the Amateur Boxing Association of the Philippines was authorized under Proclamation No. 1405 dated March 24, 1975, to conduct a nationwide fund drive from March 15 to May 31, 1975; and

WHEREAS, to carry on its laudable activities, the Association needs additional time for the conduct of its fund drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, do hereby extend the nationwide fund drive of the Amateur Boxing Association of the Philippines up to December 31, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
