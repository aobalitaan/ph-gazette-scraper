TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1128

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 1 TO OCTOBER 31, 1973 FOR THE ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGNS OF COMMUNITY CHEST AND COUNCIL ORGANIZATIONS IN THE PHILIPPINES.

To enable Community Chest and Council Organizations in the Philippines to carry on the funding of community health and welfare services as well as the promotion of economic well-being in their respective areas, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from June 1 to October 31, 1973 for the Annual Educational and Fund Campaigns of the following Community Chest and Council organizations and designating their respective areas for solicitation:

I call upon residents, firms and organizations in the area of their respective operation to support these campaigns and to give generously to the Community Chest and Council organizations mentioned above which unite into one annual appeal campaigns for the health and welfare needs of the various Red Feather Agencies as well as the promotion of the economic well-being in their respective areas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
