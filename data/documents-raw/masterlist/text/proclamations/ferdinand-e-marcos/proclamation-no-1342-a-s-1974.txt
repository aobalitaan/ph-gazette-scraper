MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1342-A

DECLARING MONDAY, NOVEMBER 4, 1974, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BOHOL AND TAGBILARAN CITY

WHEREAS, Monday, November 4, 1974, marks the 78th birth anniversary of the late Carlos P. Garcia, former President of the Philippines, first President of the 1971 Constitutional Convention and one of the outstanding leaders of this country; and

WHEREAS, the people of Bohol, his birthplace, should be given full opportunity to pay homage to one of their province’s greatest sons;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, November 4, 1974, as a special public holiday in the Province of Bohol and Tagbilaran City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary
