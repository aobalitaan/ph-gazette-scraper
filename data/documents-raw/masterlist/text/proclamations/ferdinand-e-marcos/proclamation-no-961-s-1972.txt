TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 961

DECLARING SATURDAY, MARCH 4, 1972, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, March 4, 1972, as a special public holiday in the City of Puerto Princesa in commemoration of the 100th year of existence of Puerto Princesa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
