TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 876

EXTENDING UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1971 THE PERIOD WITHIN WHICH THE PHILIPPINE BADMINTON ASSOCIATION, INC., MAY CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 747 dated December 15, 1971, authorized the Philippine Badminton Association (PBA) to conduct a national fund campaign from January 1 to June 31, 1971; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Badminton Association, Inc., an affiliate of the PAAF, needs more time to realize its fund campaign goal of P500,000.00 to carry out its objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the fund campaign of the Philippine Badminton Association, Inc., up to December 31, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MECHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
