MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 114

DECLARING MONDAY, OCTOBER 31, 1966, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, November 1, 1966, has been declared a special public holiday to give the people full opportunity to observe All Saints Day with all its religious fervor; and

WHEREAS, October 30, 1966, being Sunday, October 31, 1966 (Monday), may also be declared a special public holiday without prejudice to the public service, to enable the people, particularly those having offices in Manila and suburbs, to go to the provinces to pay their respects to their departed relatives.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declared Monday, October 31, 1966, as a special public holiday.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
