MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 406

DECLARING MONDAY, JULY 1, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN SOUTHERN LEYTE.

WHEREAS, Monday, July 1, 1968 marks the chapter anniversary of the Province of Southern Leyte; and

WHEREAS, the people of the province should be given opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their province’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines do hereby declare Monday, July 1, 1968, as a special public holiday in Southern Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set may hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
