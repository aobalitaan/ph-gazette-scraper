MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 416

DECLARING JUNE 24, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MANILA ON THE OCCASION OF “ARAW NG MAYNILA” ON MANILA FOUNDATION DAY.

WHEREAS, the City of Manila during the past six years has fittingly observed June 24th as its founding anniversary;

WHEREAS, this yearly celebration accentuates Manila’s historical role as symbol of our national unity and indeed, the very seat of our national identity; and

WHEREAS, the people of Manila are entitled to a full day of commemorating this important date not only in their history but in that of the whole nation;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare June 24, 1968, as a special public holiday in the City of Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff

Signed on June 18, 1968: Declaring June 24, 1968, as a special public holiday in the city of Manila on the occasion of "Araw ng Maynila" on Manila Foundation Day.
