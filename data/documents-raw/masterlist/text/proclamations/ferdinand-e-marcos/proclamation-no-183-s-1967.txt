MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 183

AUTHORIZING THE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM AND LIBRARY, INC., TO CONDUCT ITS EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM MARCH 1 TO JULY 31, 1967.

WHEREAS, it should be our vital concern to exerts all efforts to promote the well-being of our children and to develop them into good and useful citizens and enlightened leaders of our country;

WHEREAS, civic projects for the development of the latent capabilities of our youth and the provision of proper facilities for such development should be encourage ; and

WHEREAS, the Children Museum and Library, Inc., has its objectives geared to these ends;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc., to conduct an educational, membership and fund campaign from March 1 to July 31, 1967. I enjoin all government officials and employees, private entities, citizens and other nationals to support this humanitarian movement.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
