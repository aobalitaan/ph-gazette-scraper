MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1864

CALLING THE SANGGUNIANG PAMPOOK OF REGIONS IX AND XII TO THEIR INAUGURAL SESSION.

WHEREAS, majority of the members of the Sangguniang Pampook of Regions IX and XII have already been proclaimed as winners in the recently conducted elections in said regions;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me under Section 8 of Batas Pambansa Blg. 20, do hereby call the Sangguniang Pampook to their inaugural session on June 22, 1979.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
