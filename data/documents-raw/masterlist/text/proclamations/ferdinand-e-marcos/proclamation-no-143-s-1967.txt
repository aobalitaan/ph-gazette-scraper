MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 143

RESERVING PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT FOR THE ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK AND THE UNITED NATIONS ORGANIZATION.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 1 of Republic Act No. 4649, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for building site purposes, free of charge, of the Asian Development Bank, its subsidiary bodies and affiliated institutions and United Nations Organization, its subsidiary bodies, specialized agencies and other affiliated institutions, during the entire period of their legal existence, certain parcels of land of the private domain situated in Pasay City, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
