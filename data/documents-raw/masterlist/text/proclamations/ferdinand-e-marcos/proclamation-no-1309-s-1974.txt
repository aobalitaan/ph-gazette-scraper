TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1309

DECLARING OCTOBER 1974 AS INTERNATIONAL TRADE AND UNITED NATIONS MONTH

WHEREAS, United Nations Day falls on October 24 of each year;

WHEREAS, the Chamber of International Trade, in cooperation with various government and private entities, has programmed month-long activities on the occasion revolving on the theme “Expanding Frontiers for National Growth and World Understanding Through Trade, Tourism, Industry and Exchange of Information;”

WHEREAS, the activities need the full support of the Government, which has launched a policy of breaking down the ideological barriers among countries by more exchange of culture, goods and ideas among them;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare October 1974 as International Trade and United Nations Month under the auspices of the Chamber of International Trade and other involved organizations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
