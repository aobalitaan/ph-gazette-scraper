TANGGAPANG NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1418

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE RETIRED VETERANS ASSOCIATION TO JUNE 30, 1975.

WHEREAS, the Armed Forces of the Philippines Retired Veterans Association, Inc. (AFPREVAI), has been authorized under Proclamation No. 1334 to conduct a fund campaign from October 15, 1974 to April 15, 1975, to raise funds for the construction and maintenance of the AFP Retired Veterans Hall and Museum; and

WHEREAS, the AFPREVAI needs additional time to realize its fund campaign goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the AFPREVAI from April 15 to June 30, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary
