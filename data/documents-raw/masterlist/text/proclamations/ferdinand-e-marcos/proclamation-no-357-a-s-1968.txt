MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 357-A

DECLARING SATURDAY, MARCH 2, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABATUAN, ILOILO

WHEREAS, the seventy-seventh birthday of the late Tomas Confesor, statesman and outstanding resistance leader, falls on Saturday, March 2, 1968; and

WHEREAS, the people of Cabatuan, Iloilo, should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate his birthday with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Saturday, March 2, 1968, as a special public holiday in Cabatuan, Iloilo

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
