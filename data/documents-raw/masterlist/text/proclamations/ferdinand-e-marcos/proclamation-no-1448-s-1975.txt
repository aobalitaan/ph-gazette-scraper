MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1448

DECLARING TUESDAY, JULY 1, 1975, AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY IN SOUTHERN LEYTE.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, July 1, 1975, marks the fifteenth charter anniversary of the province of Southern Leyte; and

WHEREAS, the people of Southern Leyte desire to celebrate with appropriate ceremonies this milestone in the history of their province;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, July 1, 1975, as a special public holiday in Southern Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
