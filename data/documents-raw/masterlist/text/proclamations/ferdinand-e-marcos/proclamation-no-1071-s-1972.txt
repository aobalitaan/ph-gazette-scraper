TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1071

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 19 TO 25, 1972 AS FAMILY PLANNING WEEK.

WHEREAS, rapid population growth is one of the gravest problems facing the country today;

WHEREAS, one of the solutions to this problem is long-range population education and family planning programs; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to call the attention of the public on the seriousness of the problem and the solutions presented by population education and family planning;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from November 19 to 25, 1972, as Family Planning Week, designating the Family Planning Organization of the Philippines and the Population Commission to coordinate all publicity and promotional efforts necessary therefor, and exhorting all agencies, institutions and organizations, both public and private, to extend assistance and cooperation for the successful observance of the week.

Proclamation No. 883 dated August 2, 1971, is hereby modified accordingly.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
