MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 291

RESERVING FOR FISHERY OFFICE SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT SITUATED IN ILOILO CITY.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, pursuant to Section 64 (e) of the Revised Administrative Code, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for fishery office site purposes, under the administration of the Philippine Fisheries Commission, two parcels of land of the private domain of the Government situated in Iloilo City, Island of Panay, and more particularly described in the Bureau of Lands plan I.R. 363, as follows:

“Two parcels of land (Lot 298-A and Lot 298-B-1, Lr-363) situated in the City of Iloilo, Island of Panay. Lot 298-A-1: Bounded on the N.E. by Lot No. 953, Iloilo Cadastre; on the S.E. by a road, Lot No. 298-A-2; S.W. by Lot No 298-A-2; N.W. by Lot No. 298-B-1. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 65-05 W., 330.54 m. from BLLM 1, Iloilo Cadastre, Case 4;

thence N. 58 deg. 50’W., 14.05 m. to point “2”

thence N. 33 deg. 46’E., 21.70 m. to point “3”

thence S. 38 deg. 21’E., 6.77 m. to point “4”

thence S. 64 deg. 53’E., 7.34 m. to point “5”

thence S. 32 deg. 47’W., 20.09 m. to point of the

beginning; containing an area of TWO HUNDRED SEVENTY NINE (279) SQUARE METERS, more or less. Conc. 15 x 60 cm.; bearings true; date prepared, July 19, 1962.

beginning; containing an area of ONE HUNDRED SIXTY FOUR (164) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows: Points 2, by P.S. marked on Conc. Flooring; point 5, “X” marked on Conc. Gutter; and the rest by Old B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cm.; bearings true; date prepared, July 19, 1962.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Assistant Executive Secretary
