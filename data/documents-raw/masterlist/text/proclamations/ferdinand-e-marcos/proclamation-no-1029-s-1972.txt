TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1029

DECLARING TUESDAY, JUNE 20, 1972, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN DAGUPAN CITY

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, June 20, 1972, as a special public holiday in Dagupan City in commemoration of that city’s charter anniversary.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
