TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1070

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 7, 1972, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BAIS CITY.

WHEREAS, Thursday, September 7, 1972, marks the anniversary of the foundation of Bais City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Bais City desire to celebrate this milestone of their City’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thursday, September 7, 1972, as a special public holiday in Bais City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Source: Malacañang Records Office
