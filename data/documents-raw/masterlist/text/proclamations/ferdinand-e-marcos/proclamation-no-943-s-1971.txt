TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 943

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE COOPERATIVE UNION LEAGUE TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE FUND DRIVE.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Cooperative Union League is an organization designed to encourage and promote the credit union movement in the Philippines; and

WHEREAS, the League needs the support of the public in its information, education and training program;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Philippine Cooperative Credit Union League to conduct a nationwide fund drive from February 1 to May 31, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of December, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
