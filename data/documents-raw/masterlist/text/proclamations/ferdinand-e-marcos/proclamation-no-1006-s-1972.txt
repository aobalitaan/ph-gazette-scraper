TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1006

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE TUBERCULOSIS SOCIETY, INC., TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 1 TO OCTOBER 31, 1972.

WHEREAS, tuberculosis continues to sap the vitality of the Filipino people, hampering the social advancement of the masses and seriously curtailing the economic productivity of the nation;

WHEREAS, the national government and the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc., have been steadfast partners in the common effort for the control of the White Plague, particularly in extending medical, social and educational services to the afflicted; and

WHEREAS, these indispensable services can be continued only with the moral and financial support of a concerned citizenry;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc., to conduct its National Educational and Fund Campaign during the period from July 1 to October 31, 1972.

I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, regardless of nationality or creed, to give generously of their means to this charitable endeavor so as to consolidate gains thus far attained in the TB control program, and to enhance public health.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
