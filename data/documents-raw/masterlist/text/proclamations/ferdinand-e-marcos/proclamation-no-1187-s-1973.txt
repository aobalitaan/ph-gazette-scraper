TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION No. 1187

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 11 TO 17, 1973, AS MANAGEMENT CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK.

WHEREAS, it is the policy of the Government to promote management consciousness in both the public and private sectors to help achieve the developmental goals of the nation;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Council of Management is sponsoring a Fourth Philippine Management Congress from November 14 to 16, 1973, which will be attended by management experts, practitioners and educators from the Philippines and other Asian-Pacific countries; and

WHEREAS, management consciousness is essential to promote better business practices, increase production and improve public administration and services, which are vital prerequisites to economic development, social progress and stability;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E, MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from November 11 to 17, 1973, as “MANAGEMENT CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
