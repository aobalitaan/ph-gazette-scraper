MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1669

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 21, 1977, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, September 21, 1977, we celebrate our National Thanksgiving Day to commemorate the fifth anniversary of the New Society which ushered in an era of peace and prosperity in the country;

WHEREAS, the New Society in its five years of existence, has taken great strides in its effort towards the social and economic upliftment of the life and well-being of the common man;

WHEREAS, the advent of the New Society is undoubtedly the most significant milestone in our struggle for progress; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that we pause for a day from our work and ponder on the significance of this momentous event in our history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 21, 1977, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
