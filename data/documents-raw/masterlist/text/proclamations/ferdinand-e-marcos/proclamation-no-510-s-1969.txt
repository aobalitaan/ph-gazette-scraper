MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 510

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 326 DATED JANUARY 2, 1968, WHICH CREATED THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL COMMITTEE FOR THE GANDHI CENTENARY.

The composition of the Committee created under Proclamation No. 326 dated January 2, 1968, is hereby amended to read as follows:

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
