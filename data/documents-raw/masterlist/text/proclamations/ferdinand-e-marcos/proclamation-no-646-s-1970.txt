TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 646

EXTENDING THE PHILIPPINE MENTAL HEALTH ASSOCIATION FUND DRIVE UP TO APRIL 30, 1970.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Mental Health Association (PMHA) was authorized under Proclamation No. 603 dated September 5, 1969, to conduct a fund drive up to December 31, 1969; and

WHEREAS, said organization needs more time for the conduct of its drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ERNESTO M. MACEDA, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund drive of the Philippine Mental Health Association up to April 30, 1970.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
