MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 547

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 1, 1969, TO JUNE 30, 1970, AS PHILIPPINE NATIONAL SPORTS YEAR.

WHEREAS, sports is vital to youth development, health promotion and citizenship training;

WHEREAS, sports fosters pride, human fellowship and international understanding;

WHEREAS, sports development in the country has been hampered, because of lack of public involvement and support; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary to focus attention on sports and, by this token, generate public enthusiasm, involvement and support to sports development;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND .E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from July 1, 1969 to June 30, 1970 as Philippine National Sports Year under the auspices of the Philippine Amateur Athletic Federation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
