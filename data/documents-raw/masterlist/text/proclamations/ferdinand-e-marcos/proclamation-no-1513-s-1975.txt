MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1513

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 20 TO 25, 1975, AS UNITED NATIONS WEEK

WHEREAS, the Chamber of International Trade shall observe United Nations Week by a week-long program of activities which center around the theme: “The Role of Women in the Promotion of International Peace and Trade Development;” and

WHEREAS, there is a need to draw the attention and interest of businessmen as well as the general public on the increasing importance of the role that women must play in international trade and goodwill among nations;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from October 20 to 25, 1975, as United Nations Week under the auspices of the Chamber of International Trade and other involved organizations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES

By the Acting Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
