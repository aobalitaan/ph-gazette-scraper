MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 732

DECLARING THE 1ST MONDAY OF DECEMBER UP TO THE FOLLOWING SUNDAY OF EVERY YEAR AS PHILIPPINE AVIATION WEEK.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the 1st Monday of December up to the following Sunday first whole week of December) of every year, effective the year 1970, as Philippine Aviation Week in appreciation of the vital role of aviation in the progress and security of the nation.

I enjoin all government officials and employees and all citizens of the Philippines to lend their full assistance and cooperation in the appropriate observance of this Week.

Proclamation No. 90 dated March 12, 1963, is hereby revoked.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source: University of the Philippines, College of Law Library
