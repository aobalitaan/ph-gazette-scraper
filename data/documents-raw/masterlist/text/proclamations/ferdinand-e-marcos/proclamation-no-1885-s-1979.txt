MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1885

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM SEPTEMBER 3 TO 10, 1979, AS “ELECTRICAL TECHNOLOGY WEEK”

WHEREAS, the first Conference of Electrical Engineers and Contractors of ASEAN Countries will be held from September 7 to 10, 1979, under the sponsorship of the Society of Philippine Electrical Contractors (SPEC) with the special participation of the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers;

WHEREAS, electrical technology, as a vital factor in the economic development of a nation, is a potent force in the implementation of expanded electrification programs and development of any progressive or underdeveloped country; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting to focus national and international attention on the significant role of electrical engineers and contractors in the country’s expansion and development programs;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, Prime Minister and President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the law, do hereby declare the period from September 3 to 10, 1979, as “Electrical Technology Week” under the sponsorship of the Society of Philippine Electrical Contractors.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. this 17th day of August, in the City of Manila, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN B. ANCHETA Presidential Assistant for Economic and Development Affairs
