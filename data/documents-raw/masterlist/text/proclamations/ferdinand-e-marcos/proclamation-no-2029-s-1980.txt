MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2029

RESERVING AS CIVIL RESERVATION FOR MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY (MSU) SITE-GENERAL SANTOS UNIT AND HUMAN SETTLEMENTS FOR LANDLESS FAMILIES A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AS DESCRIBED ON BUREAU OF FOREST DEVELOPMENT MAP C.R.-198, SITUATED IN GENERAL SANTOS CITY, PHILIPPINES.

Upon recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby set aside as Civil reservation for Mindanao State University (MSU) Site-General Santos Unit and Human Settlements for landless families, subjects to provide rights if any there be, that parcel of land containing an aggregate area of approximately 556 hectares, situated in General Santos City, as shown and described in the attached BFD Map C.R.-198 and more particularly described as follows:

A parcel of land beginning at a point “1” on the plan identical to corner 1, alienable or disposable, Cotabato Project No. 47-G, a bearing of S 62º E,. 260 meters to point 2;

thence S 62º E, 270 meters to point 3; thence S 62º E, 260 meters to point 4; thence S 62º E, 260 meters to point 5; thence S 62º E, 260 meters to point 6; thence S 62º E, 260 meters to point 7; thence S 62º E, 120 meters to point 8; thence S 45º W, 1,060 meters to point 9; thence S 07º E, 770 meters to point 10; thence N 66º W, 250 meters to point 11; thence S 31º W, 200 meters to point 12; thence N 67º W, 250 meters to point 13; thence N 58º W, 250 meters to point 14; thence N 59º W, 250 meters to point 15; thence N 47º W, 240 meters to point 16; thence N 41º W, 140 meters to point 17; thence N 75º W, 1,050 meters to point 18; thence S 80º W, 470 meters to point 19; thence due North, 1,370 meters to point 20; thence East, 1,230 meters to point 21; thence due North, 530 meters to point 22; thence N 82º E, 950 meters to point 23; thence S 02º E, 590 meters to point 24; thence S 45º W, 60 meters to point 1.

There is excluded from the operation of this proclamation a strip of 15 meters in width on each of any public trail therein used as outlet for timber and other forest products, and a strip of 20 meters in width from the highest bank on each side of any stream for stream bank protection in such areas that will be released as not needed for forest purposes.

The utilization of forest products existing in the area shall remain to be subject to Forest and Internal Revenue Laws and Regulations.

The area shall automatically revert to its former status as timberland in the event that the same is no longer needed by the Mindanao State University and Human Settlements.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Senior Presidential Assistant
