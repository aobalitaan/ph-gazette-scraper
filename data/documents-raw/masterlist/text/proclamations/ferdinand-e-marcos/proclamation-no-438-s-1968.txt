MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 438

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARRIO BULALACAO, MUNICIPALITY OF MANKAYAN, PROVINCE OF BENGUET.

Upon the recommendation of the Director of Forestry, approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Director of Public Schools, subject to private rights, if any there be a certain parcel of land of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Bulalacao, Municipality of Mankayan, Province of Benguet, which parcel of public domain is more particularly described in B.F. Map C.R. 67, as follows:

Beginning at a point marked “1”, which is N 41 deg. E., 129 m. from LC corner 2 of Block XXXIV, alienable and disposable, Project No. 13, L.C. 1185,

thence S 32 deg. 38′ W.,

112.96 m. to point 2;

thence N 79 deg. 34′ W.,

40.50 m. to point 3;

thence N 50 deg. 05′ W.,

60.80 m. to point 4;

thence S 78 deg. 04′ W.,

72.38 m. to point 5;

thence S 86 deg. 46′ W.,

35.98 m. to point 6;

thence N 12 deg. 00′ E.,

55.58 m. to point 7;

thence N 36 deg. 31′ E.,

130.79 m. to point 8;

thence S 67 deg. 07′ E.,

233.48 m. to point of

beginning; containing an area of 3.67 hectares more or less.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
