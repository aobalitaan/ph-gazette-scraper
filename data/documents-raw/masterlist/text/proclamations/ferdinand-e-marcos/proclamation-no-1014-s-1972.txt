TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1014

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MAY 21 TO 27, 1972, AS “REALTOR’S WEEK”

WHEREAS, the real estate industry has been instrumental in the development of cities and urban areas; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus attention on the desirability of the real estate industry to gear its activities and policies to much needed social changes and reforms;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from May 21 to 27, 1972, as “Realtors’ Week” under the auspices of the Philippine Association of Realtors Boards and other involved organizations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
