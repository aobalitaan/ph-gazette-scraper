MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 498

DECLARING TUESDAY, DECEMBER 31, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, the thirtieth day (Monday) of December, 1969, and the first day (Wednesday) of January, 1969 being holidays, the thirty-first day (Tuesday) of December 1968, may be declared a special public holiday to enable the people to enjoy an uninterrupted three-day holiday without prejudice to the public interest;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby proclaim Tuesday, December thirty-first, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
