TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1375

DECLARING JANUARY 21, 1975, AS EULOGIO RODRIGUEZ, SR. DAY IN RIZAL PROVINCE.

WHEREAS, January 21 marks the birth anniversary of the late Eulogio Rodriguez, Sr., former Governor of Rizal, Representative, Senator and President of the Senate; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable at least to commemorate his birthday in the province of his birth in appreciation of his dedication to public service;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare January 21, 1975, as Eulogio Rodriguez, Sr. Day in the Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
