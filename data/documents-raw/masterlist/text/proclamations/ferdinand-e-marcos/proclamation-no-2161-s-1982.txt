MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2161

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE MENTAL HEALTH ASSOCIATION, INC., UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1982.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Mental Health Association, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 2076 to conduct a national membership, educational and fund campaign during the period from January 1 to December 31, 1981; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs additional time to raise more funds to achieve its goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Mental Health Association, Inc. up to December 31, 1982.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
