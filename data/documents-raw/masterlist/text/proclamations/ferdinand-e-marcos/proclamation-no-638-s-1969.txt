MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 638

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE NATIONAL SHRINES COMMISSION FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF CORREGIDOR AND MT. SAMAT NATIONAL SHRINES TO DECEMBER 31, 1970.

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission was authorized under Proclamation No. 507 dated December 31, 1968 to extend its fund campaign for the development of Corregidor and Mt. Samat national shrines up to December 31, 1969;

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission needs more time to realize its fund campaign goals to pursue its planned projects on Corregidor and Mt. Samat national shrines;

NOW, THEREFORE, L FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby further extend the period of the National Shrines Commission Fund Campaign up to December 31, 1970.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and paused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of December, the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
