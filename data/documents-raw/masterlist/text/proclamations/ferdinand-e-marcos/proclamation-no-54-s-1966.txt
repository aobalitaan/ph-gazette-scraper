MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 54

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF ADDITION HILLS, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JUAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 64 (e) of the Revised Administrative Code, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Director of Public Schools, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the private domain situated in the Barrio of Addition Hills, Municipality of San Juan, Province of Rizal, which are more particularly described as follows:

Lot 1, Block 230, Psd-1430 (Sht. 2)

A parcel of land (Lot 1, Block 230 of the subdivision plan Psd-1430 (Sheet 2), being a portion of Lot 15-J-15, described on plan Psd-1134, LRC (GLRO) Record No. 917), situated in the Municipality of San Felipe Neri, Province of Rizal, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the NE., points 5 to 1, by Lot 18, Block 230; on the SE., points 1 to 2, by Road Lot 4, on the SW., points 2 to 4, by Road Lot 2; and on the NW., points 4 to 5, by Lot 2, Block 230, all of the subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 59 deg. 59′ E., 1774.92 m. from B.L.L.M. #1; Mp. of San Felipe, Neri;
