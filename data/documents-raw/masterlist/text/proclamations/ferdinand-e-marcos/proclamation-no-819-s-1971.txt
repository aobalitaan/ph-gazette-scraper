TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 819

AUTHORIZING THE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM AND LIBRARY, INC. TO CONDUCT ITS EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM MARCH 1 TO SEPTEMBER 30, 1971.

WHEREAS, it is a vital concern of society to promote the well-being of our children and their development into good and useful citizens and enlightened leaders of our country;

WHEREAS, civic projects designed to develop the latent capabilities of our youth and to provide proper facilities for such development should be encouraged and maintained; and

WHEREAS, the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc. carries out a program with objectives geared to these ends;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc. to conduct an educational, membership and fund campaign from March 1 to September 30, 1971. I enjoin all government officials and employees, private entities and other nationals to support this humanitarian movement.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
