TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1201

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 25 TO 31, 1973, AS ANIMAL AND PET WEEK.

WHEREAS, animal and other pets have brought joy and consolation to man; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to inculcate in the minds and hearts of our youth the virtues of kindness and mercy to our animal and other pets which have delighted humanity from time immemorial;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from October 25 to 31, 1973 as Animal and Pet Week under the auspices of the Philippine Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
