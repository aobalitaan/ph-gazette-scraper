MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2370

DECLARING THURSDAY, AUGUST 9, 1984, AS ROQUE B. ABLAN, SR. DAY AND A SPECIAL NON-WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE AND IN THE CITY OF LAOAG.

In commemoration of the birthday of the late Governor Roque B. Ablan, Sr. of Ilocos Norte, one of the outstanding resistance leaders during the last war who has displayed extraordinary courage against the enemy, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, August 9, 1984, as Roque B. Ablan, Sr. Day and a special non-working public holiday in the province of Ilocos Norte and in the City of Laoag.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-four.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
