MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1913

RESERVING FOR BAGONG LIPUNAN IMPROVEMENT OF SITES AND SERVICES (BLISS) SITE PURPOSES OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF TABUK, OF A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARANGAY OF NAMBARAN, MUNICIPALITY OF TABUK, PROVINCE OF KALINGA-APAYAO, ISLAND OF LUZON

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or resettlement and reserve for Bagong Lipunan Improvement of Sites and Services (BLISS) site purposes under the administration of the Municipal Government of Tabuk, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Nambaran, municipality of Tabuk, province of Kalinga-Apayao, Island of Luzon, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

Plan Sgs-2-05-001435-D

A parcel of land (as shown on Plan Sgs-2-05-001435-D) situated in the Barrio of Nambaran, Municipality of Tabuk, Province of Kalinga-Apayao.

Bounded on the NE., and E., along lines 1-2-3 by NIA-CRIP PROPOSED DIVERSION CANAL; on the SE., and SW., along lines 3-4-5-6- by Lot No. 447 (portion), Pls-93 and on the NW., along line 6-1 by National Road.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on Plan Sgs-2-05-001435-D, being N. 35 deg. 56’E, 1852.70 m. from BLLM #17, Pls-93;

thence S. 21 deg. 51’E, 210.00 m. to point 2; thence S. 11 deg. 59’E, 318.00 m. to point 3; thence S. 52 deg. 00’E, 197.02 m. to point 4; thence N. 38 deg. 00’W, 290.00 m. to point 5; thence N. 38 deg. 00’W, 197.48 m. to point 6; thence N. 52 deg. 00’E, 394.92 m. to point 1, of the beginning, containing an area of ONE HUNDRED FIFTY THOUSAND AND TWO (150,002) SQUARE METERS. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. Bearings true. This survey was executed in accordance with law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder by Frank M. Belen, Geodetic Engineer, on May 14 to 18, 1979, and approved on June 28, 1979.

Note: This survey is equivalent to Lot 15705, a portion of Lot 447, Pls-93.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
