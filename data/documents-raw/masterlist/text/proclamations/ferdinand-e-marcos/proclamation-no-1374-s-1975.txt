TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1374

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MAY 5 TO 11, 1975, AS BARONG FILIPINO WEEK.

WHEREAS, the First National Barong Filipino Exposition will be held in Manila on May 5 to 11, 1975, under the auspices of the 1st National Barong Pilipino Exposition; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus public attention on the national attire so as to promote wider use, as well as boost its export potential;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from May 5 to 11, 1975, as Barong Filipino Week under the auspices of the First National Barong Pilipino Exposition organizers and all involved organization.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RAMON B. CARDENAS Assistant Executive Secretary
