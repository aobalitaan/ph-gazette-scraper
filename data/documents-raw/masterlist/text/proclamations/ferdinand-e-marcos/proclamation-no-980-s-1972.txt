TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 980

DECLARING MARCH 16, 1972, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN DAVAO CITY

WHEREAS, Thursday, March 16, 1972, marks the fifth anniversary of the charter of the City of Davao; and

WHEREAS, the people of that city should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s his­tory;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MAR­COS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thurs­day, March 16, 1972, as a special public holiday in Davao City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Source: Malacañang Records Office
