MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 430

DECLARING MONDAY, JULY 22, 1968, AS SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BATANGAS AND LIPA CITY.

WHEREAS, Monday, July 22, 1968, marks the birthday of Apolinario Mabini, the Sublime Paralytic, whose ideals and principles in life are now the nation’s treasured legacy; and

WHEREAS, the people of his birthplace should be given the opportunity to remember Apolinario Mabini and celebrate his birth anniversary with appropriate ceremonies;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
