MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 51

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 435, DATED JANUARY 23, 1932, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE LOO SETTLEMENT FARM SCHOOL RESERVATION, NOW THE LOO NATIONAL VOCATIONAL SCHOOL RESERVATION, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUGUIAS, BENGUET, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON, CERTAIN PARCELS OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND RESERVING THE SAME FOR MUNICIPAL SITE PURPOSES OF THE SAID MUNICIPALITY.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 435, dated January 23, 1932, which established the Loo Settlement Farm School Reservation, now the Loo National Vocational School Reservation, situated in the Municipality of Buguias, Benguet, Mt. Province, Island of Luzon, certain parcels of the land embraced therein and reserving the same for municipal site purposes of the said municipality, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to the condition that should the municipality cease to have any more need for the said land, the same shall automatically revert to the Loo National Vocational School Reservation, which parcels are more particularly described as follows, to wit:

PARCEL I

A PARCEL OF LAND, situated in the Municipality of Buguias, Benguet, Mt. Province. Bounded on the NE. and E., by Road; on the SE., SW. and W., by Agno River; and on the NW., by property of Loo Farm School. Beginning at a point marked “1”, being N. 2 deg. 28′ W., 2261.84 m. from B.L.B.M. 1, Bo. of Loo, Mpl. Dist. of Buguias, Mt. Province,

beginning; containing an approximate area of THIRTY THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED TEN (30,110) Square Meters.

PARCEL II

A PARCEL OF LAND, situated in the Municipality of Buguias, Benguet, Mt. Province. Bounded on the NE., by Road; on the S., by Guibang Creek; on the SW., by Agno River; and on the NE., by Creek. Beginning at a point marked “1”, being N. 1 deg. 13′ E., 1908.22 m. from B.L.B.M. 1, Bo. of Loo, Mpl. Dist. of Buguias, Mt. Province.

beginning containing an approximate area of FOUR THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED EIGHTY TWO (4,682) Square Meters.

PARCEL III

A PARCEL OF LAND, situated in the Municipality of Buguias, Benguet, Mt. Province. Bounded on the NE., by Lot 5 of plan Swo-12484; on the SE., by Guibang Creek; on the SW., by Road; and on the NW., by Lot 3 of plan Swo-12484. Beginning at a point marked “1”, being N. 9 deg. 58′ E., 1788.50 m. from B.L.B.M. 1, Bo. of Loo, Mpl. District of Buguias,

beginning; containing an approximate area of SIXTEEN THOUSAND AND ELEVEN (16,011) Square Meters.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
