MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 509

DECLARING JANUARY 18, TO 25, 1969 AS SMALL SCALE INDUSTRIES WEEK.

WHEREAS, small scale industries have been contributing in a certain degree to the economic development of our country; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus public attention on its importance;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from January 18 to 25, 1969, as Small Scale Industries Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
