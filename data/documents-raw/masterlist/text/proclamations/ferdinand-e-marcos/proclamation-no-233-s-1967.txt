MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 233

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 251 DATED MAY 4, 1951, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE WORKINGMEN’S VILLAGE IN THE CITY OF ILOILO, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND OPENING THE SAME TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PULIC LAND ACT.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 251 dated May 4, 1951, which reserved a tract of land in the City of Iloilo for Workingmen’s Village, certain parcels of land embraced therein and open the same to disposition under the Public Land Act, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:

Lot 1

A parcel of land (Portion of Proclamation No. 251, Lot 1) situated in Barrio Lapuz, District of La Paz, Iloilo City, Island of Panay. Bounded on the SW along lines 1-2 by Lot 1-A, Philippine Railway Co.; on the NW along lines 2-3-4 by Municipal road; on the NE along lines 4-5-6 by the proposed road and on the SE along lines 6-1 by Proclamation 251. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S. 38 deg. 41 min. E. 1,604.42 meters from B.B.M. No. 57, Iloilo Cadastre;

beginning, containing an area of six thousand eight hundred forty five (6,845) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground, and all corners are P.S. Cyl, Conc. mons. 15 x 60 cm.; bearings true; date of survey, July 7, 1967.

Lot 2

A parcel of land (Portion of Proclamation 251, Lot 2) situated in Barrio Lapuz, District of La Paz, City of Iloilo, Island of Panay. Bounded on the NW. along line 1-2-3 by the Municipal road; on the NE along lines 3-4-5 by the proposed road; on the SE along lines 5-6-7 by Proclamation 251 and on the SW. along lines 6-7-8 by the proposed road. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S 42 deg. 02 min. E., 1,511.71 meters from B.B.M. No. 57, Iloilo Cadastre;

beginning, containing an area of nine thousand two hundred eighty three (9,283) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground and all corners are P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cm.; bearings, true; date of survey, July 7, 1967.

Lot 3

A parcel of land (Portion of Proclamation 251, Lot 3), situated in Barrio Lapuz, District of La Paz, City of Iloilo, Island of Panay. Bounded on the NW along lines 1-2 by the Municipal road; on the NE along lines 2-3 by Proclamation 251; on the SE along lines 3-4-5 by Proclamation 251; and on the SW. along lines 4-5-1 by the proposed road. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S 47 deg. 17 min. E., 1,494.12 meters from B.B.M. No. 57, Iloilo Cadastre;

beginning, containing an area of three thousand eight hundred seventy two (3,872) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground and all corners are P. S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cm.; bearings, true; date of survey, July 7, 1967.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set may hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City Manila, this 21st, day of July, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
