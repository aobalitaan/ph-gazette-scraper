MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 399

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 178 DATED MARCH 4, 1967, A PORTION OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND RESERVING THE SAME FOR PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION BUILDING SITE PURPOSES.

By virtue of the powers bested in me by law, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of proclamation No. 178 dated March 4, 1967 which reserved a parcel of land of the private domain situated in Quezon City for Land Transportation Commission building site purposes, a portion of the land embraced therein and reserve the same for building site purposes of the Public Service Commission, under the administration of the Public Service Commissioner, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
