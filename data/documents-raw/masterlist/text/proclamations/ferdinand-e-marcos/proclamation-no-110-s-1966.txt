MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 110

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE 1966 NATIONAL ANTI-TB FUND AND EDUCATIONAL CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE TUBERCULOSIS SOCIETY, INC., TO DECEMBER 31, 1966.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 55 dated July 28, 1966, authorized the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc., to conduct its National Fund and Educational Campaign from August 19 to September 30, this year;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc., needs more time to raise its fund campaign goal this year; and

WHEREAS, the success of the Anti-TB fund campaign is deemed of paramount importance to the continued effort in the prevention and control of the White Plague in this country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the Anti-TB Fund and Educational Campaign of the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc., up to December 31, 1966.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
