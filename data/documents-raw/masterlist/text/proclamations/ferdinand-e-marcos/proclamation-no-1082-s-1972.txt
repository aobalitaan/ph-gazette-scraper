TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1082

FURTHER AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 1046 DATED JULY 12, 1972, AS AMENDED BY PROCLAMATION NO. 1060 DATED AUGUST 12, 1972, DECLARING A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN CERTAIN PROVINCES.

By order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 1046 dated July 12, 1972, as amended by Proclamation No. 1060 dated August 12, 1972, so as to include Zamboanga del Sur, which has also suffered from floods, drought and lawless elements, as calamity area.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
