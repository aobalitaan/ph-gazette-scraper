MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1355

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 983 DATED MARCH 9, 1972, BY RESERVING FOR ADDITIONAL RESETTLEMENT PURPOSES OF THE RESIDENTS OF PANTABANGAN, NUEVA ECIJA, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTABANGAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 983, dated March 9, 1972, by the President of the Philippines, reserves for resettlement purposes of the residents of Pantabangan, who will be displaced by reservoir inundation, certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the municipalities of Bongabon and Pantabangan, Province of Nueva Ecija; Maria Aurora, Sub-province of Aurora; and Dupax, Province of Nueva Vizcaya;

WHEREAS, within the areas reserved in Maria Aurora, Sub-province of Aurora, and Dupax, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, there are cultural minorities who have already developed substantial areas and whose rights have to be duly protected;

WHEREAS, in the interest of the development of Palayan City which has territorial jurisdiction over the parcel of land known as the Bongabon Stock Farm and identified as Parcel “E” in Proclamation No. 983 the area to be allocated therein for the resettlement of the people of Pantabangan has been reduced from 2,102.9117 hectares more or less to about 1,100 hectares only; and

WHEREAS, on account of the foregoing reasons and the fact that additional families living in remote areas within the future reservoir have been identified as also requiring resettlement, there is a need for amending Proclamation No. 983;

NOW, THEREFORE, upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 983 dated March 9, 1972, as to include as additional resettlement area for persons affected by the Pantabangan Dam, Parcel “F” as follows:
