TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1161

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND DRIVE OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR VOLLEYBALL ASSOCIATION TO OCTOBER 31, 1973

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Volleyball Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 1093 dated December 7, 1972, to continue its national educational, membership and fund drive up to June 30, 1973; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs additional time to enable it to realize its objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the national educational, membership and fund drive of the Philippine Amateur Volleyball Association to October 31, 1973.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
