MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2068

DECLARING APRIL 7, 1981 AS THE PLEBISCITE DAY FOR THE RATIFICATION OF BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 93 CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY OF BIEN UNIDO, BOHOL.

WHEREAS, under Batas Pambansa Blg. 93, creating the Municipality of Bien Unido in the Province of Bohol, it is a prerequisite that the barangays affected thereby should be consulted in a plebiscite to be conducted within 120 days from December 24, 1980, the date of approval of said Act;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare April 7, 1981 as Plebiscite Day in Bien Unido, Tuboran, Mandawa, Nueva Estrella, Nueva Esperanza, Puerto San Pedro, Sagasa, Pinamgo, Bilangbilangan Daku, Bilangbilangan Diyot, Hingotanan, Maomaoan, and Malingan; the islands of Cabantulan and Macaina; and the shoals of Dunggoan Daku, Dunggoan Gamay, and Labonga, all in the municipality of Talibon; and Barangay Liberty in the Municipality of Trinidad, which barangays shall form the new Municipality of Bien Unido, Bohol.

The Commission on Elections shall supervise the plebiscite in the above-mentioned barangays in accordance with Section 4 of Batas Pambansa Blg. 93, and shall submit its report to the President of the Philippines within thirty (30) days from the date of the plebiscite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
