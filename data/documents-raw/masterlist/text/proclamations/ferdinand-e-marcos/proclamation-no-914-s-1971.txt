TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 914

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE TUBERCULOSIS SOCIETY, INC. UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1971.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. was authorized under Proclamation No. 841-A dated April 22, 1971 to conduct its national educational and fund campaign from July 1 to October 31, 1971;

WHEREAS, fund campaign limitations occasioned by the November 8 election and other factors have constrained the Society to postpone urgent fund-raising projects; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary that the anti-TB campaign be extended to finance the Society’s free services to TB-afflicted citizens;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the national educational and fund campaign of the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. up to December 31, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
