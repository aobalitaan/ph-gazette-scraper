MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 602

EXTENDING UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1969, THE PERIOD WITHIN WHICH THE PHILIPPINE BADMINTON ASSOCIATION, INC., MAY CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 531 dated March 12, 1969, authorized the Philippine Badminton Association, Inc., to conduct a Special Fund Campaign from March 1 to August 31, 1969; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Badminton Association, Inc., an affiliate of the PAAF, needs more time to realize its fund campaign goal of P500,000.00 to carry out its meritorious objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the fund campaign of the Philippine Badminton Association Inc., up to December 31, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
