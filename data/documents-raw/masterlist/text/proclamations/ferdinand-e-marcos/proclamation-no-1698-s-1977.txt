MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1698

DECLARING SATURDAY, DECEMBER 31, 1977, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, Saturday, December 31, 1977, may be declared a special public holiday without detriment to the public interest to give the people a longer enjoyment of the yuletide season;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, December 31, 1977, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of December , in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
