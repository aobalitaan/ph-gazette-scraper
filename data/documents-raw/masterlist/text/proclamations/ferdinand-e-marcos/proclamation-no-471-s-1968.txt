MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 471

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF THE BAGUIO TOWNSITE RESERVATION SITUATED IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO, ISLAND OF LUZON, A CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS CHAPTER X OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, Presidential of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of the Baguio Townsite Reservation, situated in the City of Baguio, Island of Luzon, a certain portion of the land embraced therein and declare the same open to disposition under the provisions of Chapter X of the Public Land Act, which portion is more particularly described as follows:
