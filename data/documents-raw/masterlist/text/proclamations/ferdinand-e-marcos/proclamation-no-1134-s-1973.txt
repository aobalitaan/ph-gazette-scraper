TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1134

AUTHORIZING YOUTH AND STUDENT TRAVEL ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP, AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM MAY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1973

WHEREAS, the Youth and Student Travel Association of the Philippines is a non-profit civic organization with the objective of promoting tourism among the youth and students of the country; and

WHEREAS, to carry out its objectives it needs the moral and financial support of the public;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Youth and Student Travel Association of the Philippines to conduct a nationwide educational, membership and fund campaign during the period from May 1 to December 31, 1973.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
