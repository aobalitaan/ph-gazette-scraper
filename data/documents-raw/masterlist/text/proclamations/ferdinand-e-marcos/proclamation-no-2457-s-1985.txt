MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2457

EXTENDING THE NATIONWIDE EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE CONSTITUTION ASSOCIATION (PHILCONSA) FOR A PERIOD OF ONE YEAR.

WHEREAS, the PHILCONSA was authorized under Proclamation No. 2329, dated 17 January 1984 to conduct a national educational and fund drive for a period of one year from January 17, 1984; and

WHEREAS, the PHILCONSA needs more time to realize its laudable objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the PHILCONSA to conduct a nationwide educational and fund campaign for a period of one (1) year from the issuance of this proclamation.

I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines to participate actively and give generously of their means to this laudable undertaking.

All contributions or donations to the PHILCONSA during this fund drive shall be exempt from income and other forms of taxes and shall constitute as allowable deductions in full for income tax purposes of the contributors or donors.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
