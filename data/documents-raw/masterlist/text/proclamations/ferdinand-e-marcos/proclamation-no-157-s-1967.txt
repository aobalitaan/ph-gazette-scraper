MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 157

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 547, DATED JANUARY 28, 1933, ESTABLISHING AS LAL-LO FOREST RESERVE FOR WATERSHED, SOIL PROTECTION, TIMBER PRODUCTION AND OTHER FOREST USES, CERTAIN PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAL-LO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, ISLAND OF LUZON, PHILIPPINES.

Pursuant to Section 1826 of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended by Republic Act No. 3092, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 547, dated January 28, 1933, which established the LAL-LO, FOREST RESERVE, by excluding certain portions which have been classified as alienable and disposable.
