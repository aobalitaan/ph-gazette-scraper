MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 335

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 793 DATED SEPTEMBER 29, 1961, WHICH INCLUDED CERTAIN PORTIONS OF LAND AND THE NAUJAN LAKE PROPER (WATER ARMA) FROM THE NAUJAN LAKE NATIONAL PARK AND RESTORING THE LAKE PROPER AS PART OF THE NAUJAN LAKE NATIONAL PARK.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, president of the Philippines, do hereby revoke proclamation no. 793 dated September 29, 1961, which excluded certain portions of land and the Naujan Lake proper (water Area) from the Naujan lake national park, and restore the Naujan lake proper to its original status is part of the Naujan lake national park, which shall be open to fishing by small fishermen in accordance with rules and regulations to be promulgated by Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources upon the recommendation of the Director of Parks and Wildlife and  the Commissioner of Fisheries.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of January , in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
