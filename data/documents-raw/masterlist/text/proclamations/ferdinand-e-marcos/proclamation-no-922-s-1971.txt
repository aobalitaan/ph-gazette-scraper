TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 922

DECLARING TUESDAY, OCTOBER 26, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CATANDUANES.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, October 26, 1971, marks the 25th anniversary of the charter of Catanduanes; and

WHEREAS, the residents should be given the opportunity to commemorate this milestone in their province’ history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, October 26, 1971, as a special public holiday in Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
