MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2326

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE RECORDS MANAGEMENT ASSOCIATION (PRMA) TO CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM JANUARY 1, 1984 TO DECEMBER 31, 1984.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Records Management Association (PRMA) will host the First International Records Management Conference in 1985; and

WHEREAS, in the pursuit of the numerous tasks involved in the fulfillment of this international commitment, the PRMA needs the cooperation and support of the public, particularly in raising funds necessary for the successful holding of the Congress;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Philippine Records Management Association to conduct a fund campaign during the period from January 1, 1984 to December 31, 1984.

I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality and creed to give generously and participate actively in this drive and help in the successful holding of the Congress.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Baguio, this 20th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-three.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
