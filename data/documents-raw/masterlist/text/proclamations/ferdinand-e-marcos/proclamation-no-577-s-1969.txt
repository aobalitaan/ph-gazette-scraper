MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 577

DECLARING FRIDAY, JULY 11, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN VALENZUELA, BULACAN

WHEREAS, Friday, July 11, 1969, marks the birthday centenary of Dr. Pio Valenzuela, one of the pillars of the Philippine revolution against Spain and exemplary public servant; and

WHEREAS, the people of Valenzuela, Bulacan, should be afforded an opportunity to render homage to the illustrious hero;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, July 11, 1369, as a special public holiday in Valenzuela, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
