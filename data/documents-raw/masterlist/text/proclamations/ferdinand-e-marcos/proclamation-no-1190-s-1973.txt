TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1190

DECLARING OCTOBER 12, 1973, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN QUEZON CITY.

WHEREAS, Friday, October 12, 1973, marks the 34th Foundation Anniversary of Quezon City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Quezon City should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, October 12, 1973, as a special public holiday in Quezon City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
