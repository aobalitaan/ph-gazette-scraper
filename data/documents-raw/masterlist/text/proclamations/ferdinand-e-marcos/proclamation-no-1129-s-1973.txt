TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1129

AUTHORIZING THE MISAMIS ORIENTAL COUNCIL, BOY SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES, TO CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM APRIL 15, 1973 TO DECEMBER 31, 1973.

WHEREAS, the Misamis Oriental Council, Boy Scouts of the Philippines, needs the support of the public to finance its participation in the coming Golden Jubilee of the Boy Scouts of the Philippines to be held on December 28, 1973, to January 4, 1974, at Makiling, Los Baños, Laguna, as well as for its operation up to December 31, 1973;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Misamis Oriental Council to conduct a fund campaign during the period from April 15 to December 31, 1973.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
