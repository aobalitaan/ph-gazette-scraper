MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2397

FURTHER EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE UNITED WAY PHILIPPINES, INC. UP TO MAY 31, 1985.

WHEREAS, the United Way Philippines, Inc. was authorized under Proclamation No. 2323 to conduct a fund campaign from June 1, 1983 to May 31, 1984; and

WHEREAS, the foundation needs additional time to raise more funds for its laudable goals;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the United Way Philippines, Inc. up to May 31, 1985.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
