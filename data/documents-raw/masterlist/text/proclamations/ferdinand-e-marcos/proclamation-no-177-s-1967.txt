MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 177

DECLARING THURSDAY, MARCH 2, 1967, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABATUAN, ILOILO.

WHEREAS, the seventy-sixth birthday of the late Tomas Confesor, stateman and outstanding resistance leader, falls on Thursday, March 2, 1967; and

WHEREAS, the people of Cabatuan, Iloilo, should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate his birthday with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Thursday, March 2, 1967, as a special public holiday in Cabatuan, Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
