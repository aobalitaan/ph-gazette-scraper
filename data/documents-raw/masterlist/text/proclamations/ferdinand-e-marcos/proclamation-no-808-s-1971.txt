TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 808

EXTENDING THE PHILIPPINE MEDICAL ASSOCIATION FUND DRIVE UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1971

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period given to the Philippine Medical Association to conduct its fund drive under Proclamation No. 724, dated July 16, 1970, to December 31, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
