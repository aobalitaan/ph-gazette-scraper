MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2460

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Ministry of Education, Sports and Culture, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Carmen, province of Davao, island of Mindanao, which parcels are more particularly described as follows:

Lot A-2, Csd-11-002956-D (Carmen Community High School)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot A-2, Csd-11-002956-D), situated in Poblacion, Municipality of Carmen, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by National Highway; on the NE., along line 2-3 by Road; on the SE., & SW., along lines 3-4-1 by Lot A-1, Csd-11-002956-D. Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot A-2, Csd-11-002956 on plan being S. 67 deg. 58′ E., 676.06 m. from BLIM 175, Cad-276, Tagum Cadastre;

point of beginning. Containing an area of THIRTY NINE THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED NINETY ONE (39,991) SQUARE METRES. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. Bearings true. This lot was surveyed in accordance with law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder by Rogelio A. Trabado, Geodetic Engineer on May 11, 1976 and was approved on June 22, 1976.

NOTE: This lot is identical to Lot 10801, Cad-276, Tagum Cadastre.

Lot A-3, Csd-11-002956-D (Carmen Elementary School)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot A-3, Csd-11-002956-D situated in Poblacion, Municipality of Carmen, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, Bounded on the NW., along lines 1-2-3-4-5-6-7 by National Highway; on the NE., along lines 7-8-9-10-11-12 by Ising River; on the SE., along line 12-13 by Lot A-1, Csd-11-002956-D; on the SW., along line 13-14 by Road; and on the NW., along line 14-1 by National Highway. Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot A-3, Csd-11002956-D on plan being N. 82 deg. 15′ E., 788.41 m. from BLLM 175, Cad-276, Tagum Cadastre;

point of beginning. Containing an area of FORTY FIVE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED (45,700) SQUARE METERS. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows: Corners 1-2-3-4-5-6 by Old BL; Corners 7-8-19-10-11-12 by Old Points and the rest by P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cm. Bearings true. This lot was surveyed in accordance with law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder by Rogelio A. Trabado, Geodetic Engineer on May 11, 1976 and was approved on June 22, 1976.

Note: This lot is identical to Lot 10802, Cad-276, Tagum Cadastre.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of October in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
