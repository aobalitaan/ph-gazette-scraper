MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 75

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 812 DATED DECEMBER 27, 1961, WHICH REDUCED THE AREA OF THE MTS. BANAHAW-SAN CRISTOBAL NATIONAL PARK, SITUATED IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, MUNICIPALITIES OF LUCBAN, TAYABAS, SARIAYA, CANDELARIA, AND DOLORES, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, AND RIZAL, NAGCARLAN, LILIO AND MAJAYJAY, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, ISLAND OF LUZON, AND RESTORING THE AREA EXCLUDED THEREFROM AS PART OF THE MTS. BANAHAW-SAN CRISTOBAL NATIONAL PARK

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 812 dated December 27, 1961, and restore the original area of the Mts . Banahaw-San Cristobal National Park in the City of San Pablo, Municipalities of Lucban, Tayabas, Sariaya, Candelaria and Dolores, Province of Quezon, and Rizal, Nagcarlan, Lilio and Majayjay, Province of Laguna, Island of` Luzon, as established under Proclamation No. 716, series of 1941.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
