MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 443

DECLARING MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 2, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BAGUIO CITY.

WHEREAS, September 1, 1968, marks the fifty-ninth anniversary of the charter of Baguio City, the summer capital of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, the residents should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history; and

WHEREAS, September 1 this year falls on a Sunday hence, the necessity of declaring the following day a special public holiday to enable city officials and employees to participate in the projected ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, September 2, 1968 as, special public holiday in Baguio City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
