MALACAñANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 772

DECLARING FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 27, AND SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 28, 1970, AS SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY.

WHEREAS, this country will be honored, for the first time in history, with a Papal Visit; and

WHEREAS, it behooves our people to make his visit a truly memorable and heartwarming one by honoring him with our presence in all the occasions where the public is welcome or invited;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, November 27, and Saturday, November 28, 1970, as special public holidays.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our Lord   nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source: Malacañang Records Office
