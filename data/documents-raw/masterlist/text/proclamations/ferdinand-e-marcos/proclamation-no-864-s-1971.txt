MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 864

RESERVING A SITE FOR PROPOSED BUS TERMINAL A PORTION OF THE NORTH HARBOR, TONDO, MANILA.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Public Works and Communications and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for bus terminal purposes, under the administration of the Public Service Commission, subject to private rights, if any there be, a portion of the North Harbor, Tondo, Manila, and more particularly described as follows:

Beginning at a point mark “1” which is in the direction S 76°07′ away from the Southeast corner of the existing Pier 12 at North Harbor, Tondo, Manila, with a distance of 153.91 meters;

thence N. 3°14′ W, a distance of 200.00 meters to point 2 thence N. 85°48′ E, a distance of 54.00 meters to point 3 thence S. 3°14′ E, a distance of 200.00 meters to point 4 thence S. 35°48′ W, a distance of 54.00 meters to point 1

the point of beginning, containing an area of 10,800 square meters, more or less. Points 1 to 4 referred to are indicated in the attached plan.

Note: All data are approximate and subject to change based on future surveys.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
