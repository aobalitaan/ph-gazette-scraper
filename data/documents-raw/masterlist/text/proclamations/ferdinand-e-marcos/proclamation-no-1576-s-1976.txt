MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1576

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 30, 1976, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN SAN JUAN, RIZAL.

WHEREAS, on Monday, August 30, 1976, the residents of San Juan, Rizal, celebrates the 80th anniversary of the Battle of Pinaglabanan; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper to give the residents of San Juan, Rizal, full opportunity to celebrate this momentous event in our history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, August 30, 1976, as a special public holiday in San Juan, Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
