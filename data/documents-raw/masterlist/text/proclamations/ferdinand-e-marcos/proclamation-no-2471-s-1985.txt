MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2471

DECLARING JANUARY 3, 1986, AS “ANTERO S. SORIANO DAY” IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANZA, PROVINCE OF CAVITE.

WHEREAS, in its Resolution No. 32-A, series of 1985, the Sangguniang Bayan of Tanza, Cavite requests that January 3, 1986, be declared as a non-working special public holiday in the Municipality of Tanza, Province of Cavite, to be known as “Antero S. Soriano Day”, to mark the 100th Birth Anniversary of the late Antero S. Soriano, who was born on January 3, 1986 and was a former Governor of the Province of Cavite (1912-1919); Senator representing the 5th District comprising the provinces of Cavite, Batangas, Tayabas, Mindoro and Marinduque (1919-1925); Representative of the Province of Cavite from 1925 until his death; and a member of the second Philippine Independence Commission to the United States of America in 1922; and

WHEREAS, the residents of the Municipality of Tanza; Province of Cavite desire to celebrate Antero S. Soriano’s 100th Birth Anniversary with fitting ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare January 3, 1986, as a non-working special public holiday in the Municipality of Tanza, Province of Cavite, to be known as “Antero S. Soriano Day”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
