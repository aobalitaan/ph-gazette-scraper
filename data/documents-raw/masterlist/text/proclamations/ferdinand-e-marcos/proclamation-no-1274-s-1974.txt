TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1274

DECLARING JULY 7-13, 1974, AS PUBLIC HEALTH WEEK

WHEREAS, the First Asian Regional Conference of Public Health Associations will be hosted by the Philippine Public Health Association, Inc. in Manila on July 8-11, 1974;

WHEREAS, there is a necessity to focus public attention on the important role of developing and strengthening voluntary health organizations to support government health programs;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare July 7-13, 1974, as Public Health Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
