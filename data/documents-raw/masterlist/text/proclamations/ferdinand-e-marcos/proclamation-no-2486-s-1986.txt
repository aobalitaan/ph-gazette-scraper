MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2486

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 10, 1986 AS A WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION.

WHEREAS, the bicentennial anniversary of the foundation of the municipality of San Fernando, Province of La Union falls on Monday, February 10, 1986; and

WHEREAS, The people therein desire to commemorate this milestone in the history of their town with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, February 10, 1986, as a working special public holiday in the municipality of San Fernando, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
