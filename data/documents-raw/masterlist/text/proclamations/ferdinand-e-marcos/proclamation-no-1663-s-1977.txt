MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1663

DECLARING TUESDAY, AUGUST 30, 1977, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN SAN JUAN, METRO MANILA.

WHEREAS, the residents of San Juan, Metro Manila celebrate the 81st anniversary of the historic Battle of Pinaglabanan; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper to give the people of San Juan, Metro Manila full opportunity to celebrate this momentous event in our history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, August 30, 1977, as a special public holiday in San Juan, Metro Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
