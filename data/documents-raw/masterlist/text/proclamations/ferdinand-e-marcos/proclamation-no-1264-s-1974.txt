TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1264

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE CANCER SOCIETY, INC. FOR THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 16 TO DECEMBER 30, 1974.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc. was authorized under Proclamation No. 1222 dated January 15, 1974, to extend its educational and fund campaign up to June 16, 1974; and

WHEREAS, the Society needs additional time to raise more funds to achieve its fund goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc. from June 16 to December 30, 1974.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
