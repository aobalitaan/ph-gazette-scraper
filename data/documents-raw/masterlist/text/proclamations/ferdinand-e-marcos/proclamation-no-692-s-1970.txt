TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 692

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 15, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN SILANG, CAVITE

WHEREAS, Monday, June 15, 1970, marks the birthday anniversary of General Vito Belarmino, one of our able generals during the revolution against Spain and the Philippine American Wars; and

WHEREAS, the people of Silang, Cavite, his birthplace, should be given full opportunity to celebrate his birthday with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, June 15, 1970, as a special public holiday in Silang, Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
