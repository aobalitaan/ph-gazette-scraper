TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 769

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 743, DATED AUGUST 25, 1970, BY TRANSFERRING THE CELEBRATION OF THE PHILIPPINE CONSTRUCTION WEEK TO THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 23 TO 29, 1970.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby transfer the celebration of the Philippine Construction Week from the period August 11 to 17, 1970, as promulgated under Proclamation No. 743 dated August 25, 1970 to the period from November 23 to 29, 1970.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
