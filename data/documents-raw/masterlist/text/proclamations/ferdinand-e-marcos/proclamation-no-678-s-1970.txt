TANGGAPAN NG -PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION. No. 678

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 1 TO AUGUST 15, 1970 FOR THE TWENTY-SECOND ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST.

To enable the Community Chest to carry on the funding of community welfare services, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from June 1 to August 15, 1970, for its twenty-second annual educational and fund campaign.

I call upon all residents, firms and organizations especially in the City of Manila, Quezon City, Pasay City, Caloocan City and municipalities of Makati, Malabon, Mandaluyong, Marikina, Navotas, Las Piñas, Paranaque, Pasig and San Juan, Province of Rizal, to support this campaign and to give generously to the Community Chest which unites into one annual appeal the 35 fund campaigns of the following welfare agencies:

I authorize and urge all government officials and employees including all school authorities and teachers, to accept, for the Community Chest, fund-raising responsibilities and to give it full support and dedicated leadership in their respective communities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
