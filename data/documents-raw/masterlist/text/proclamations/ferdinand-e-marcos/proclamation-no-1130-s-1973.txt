TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1130

DECLARING SATURDAY, APRIL 21, 1973, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, the 20th (Good Friday) and the 22nd (Sunday) days of April, nineteen hundred and seventy-three being public holidays, the 21st day of the same month, may be declared as a special public holiday without prejudice to the public interest;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, April 21, 1973, as a special public holiday;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
