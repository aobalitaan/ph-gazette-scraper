TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1322

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 27, 1974, AS GENERAL MALVAR DAY IN BATANGAS PROVINCE AND CITIES OF BATANGAS AND LIPA

WHEREAS, Friday, September 27, 1974, marks the anniversary of the birth of the late General Miguel Malvar, one of the last generals to surrender in the revolutionary wars against Spain and the United States; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable at least to commemorate the event in the province of his birth and emulate his exemplary love of country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, September 27, 1974, a General Malvar Day in Batangas Province and cities of Batangas and Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
