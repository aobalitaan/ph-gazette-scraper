MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 129

DECLARING NOVEMBER 29 OF EACH YEAR AS HOME EXTENSION DAY.

In order that the people of the Philippines may more fully appreciate and realize the potentialities of the Filipino home not only in its effective management but also in the development of household arts and industries in a manner to strengthen its structure, assure its material sufficiency, contribute to its aesthetic effectiveness and promote moral stability, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the 29th day of November of each year as Home Extension Day .

I call upon all government and non-government entities and all residents of the Philippines to focus their attention on this movement and lend their full cooperation in the appropriate observance thereof under the auspices of the Agricultural Productivity Commission.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
