TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1124-A

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 1124 ISSUED ON MARCH  30, 1973, WHICH DECLARED APRIL 2, 1973 AS A  SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF  BULACAN TO HONOR THE MEMORY OF FRANCISCO  (BALAGTAS) BALTAZAR.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 1124, issued on March 30, 1973,  declared Monday, April 2, 1973 as a special public holiday in  the Province of Bulacan to honor the memory of Francisco (Balagtas) Baltazar; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary that the memory of Francisco  Balagtas, the Prince of Filipino poets being thus honored, whose  literary masterpiece is yet unequalled in the literary history  of this country, should be remembered not only in the Province of Bulacan but also throughout the Philippines;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS,  President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation  No. 1124, issued on March 30, 1973, by declaring that while Monday, April 2, 1973 may be observed as a special public holiday  only in the Province of Bulacan, the same date should be observed as Balagtas Day throughout the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
