MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2254

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 408 DATED JUNE 16, 1965, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM BUILDING SITE RESERVATION SITUATED IN BAGUIO CITY BY CORRECTING THE TECHNICAL DESCRIPTIONS OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN.

Upon the recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 408, series of 1965, which established the Social Security System building site reservation situated in Baguio City by correcting the technical descriptions of the land embraced therein as follows:

Nr.-174 (Social Security System)

“A parcel of land (as shown on plan Nr-174, L.R.C. Record No. ), situated in the Res. Sec. “A”, City of Baguio. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by Harrison Road; on the N., along line 2-3, by property of Susa Cerantes; on the NE., along line 3-4, by property of Bureau of Forestry; on the E., along line 4-5, by property of Bureau of Mines; on the S., along line 5-6, by property of Benguet, Ifugao, Bontoc, Apayao, Kalinga (Lot 2, Swo-19505 (portion); and on the NW., along line 6-1, by Harrison Road. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 12 deg. 07’W., [458.64] 548.64 m. from Triangulation Station “Happy”, Baguio Townsite.

beginning; containing an area of TWO THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED (2,500) SQUARE METERS. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: points 1 and 2 by Spikes on Trees, point 3 by Cross marked on Conc. Pavement and the rest by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons.; bearings true, date of survey, March 27, 1962. Date approved, Nov. 13, 1963.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of January in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-three.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MANUEL M. LAZARO Presidential Assistant for Legal Affairs
