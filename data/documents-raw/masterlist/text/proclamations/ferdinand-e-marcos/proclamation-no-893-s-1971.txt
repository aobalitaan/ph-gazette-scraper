TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 893

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN OLONGAPO CITY.

WHEREAS, the 3rd of September marks the charter anniversary of Olongapo City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Olongapo City should be given full opportunity to celebrate its charter anniversary with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, September 3, 1971, as a special public holiday in the City of Olongapo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MECHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
