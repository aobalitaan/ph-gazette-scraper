TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESSIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 845

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR ATHLETIC FEDERATION TO CONDUCT ITS NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM THE PERIOD FROM MAY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1971.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Athletic Federation (PAAF) seeks to encourage physical education, promote athletic sports among amateurs and develop athletes for international competition; and

WHEREAS, the above projects cannot be attained without the support of our citizens, so that our youth of today may grow into healthy and physically fit leaders;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from May 1 to December 31, 1971, as the educational and fund campaign period of the Philippine Amateur Athletic Federation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
