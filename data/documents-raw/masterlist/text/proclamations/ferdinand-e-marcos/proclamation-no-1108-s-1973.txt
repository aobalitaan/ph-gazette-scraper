TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1108

DECLARING THE PERIOD MARCH 1-7 AS OFFICE MACHINES WEEK.

WHEREAS, office machines are vital tools for nation-building, serving as the handmaiden of industry, embracing all fields from science to education, from accounting to journalism, from marketing to banking;

WHEREAS, office machines make a significant contribution to the growth of the Philippine economy, by enhancing the efficient operation of industrial firms, enabling them to achieve considerable increases in production output;

WHEREAS, the recent technological advances in office machines will be show-cased at a trade exhibit to be held by the Philippine Office Machines Distributors Association (POMDA) from March first to third.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FLORES A. BAYOT, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period March 1-7 as “Office Machines Week”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) FLORES A. BAYOT Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
