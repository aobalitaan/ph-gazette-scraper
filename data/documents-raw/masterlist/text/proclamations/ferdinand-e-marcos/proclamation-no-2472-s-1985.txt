MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2472

RECLASSIFYING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF OLONGAPO AND THE MUNICIPALITY OF SUBIC, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, FROM NAVAL RESERVATION UNDER PROCLAMATION NO. 328, DATED JANUARY 2, 1968, TO ALIENABLE AND DISPOSABLE LAND UNDER COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 141, AS AMENDED.

Upon recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby reclassify two (2) parcels of land of the public domain, identified as Parcels 3 and 4, Swo-40367, situated in the City of Olongapo and Municipality of Subic, Province of Zambales, and more particularly described in Proclamation No. 328, dated January 2, 1968, from naval reservation under Proclamation No. 328 to alienable and disposable land under Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended.

The foregoing parcels of land of the public domain are hereby placed under the joint administration of the Bureau of Lands and the City of Olongapo and shall be disposed of by the Director of Lands under Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, upon prior recommendation of the City Mayor of the City of Olongapo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
