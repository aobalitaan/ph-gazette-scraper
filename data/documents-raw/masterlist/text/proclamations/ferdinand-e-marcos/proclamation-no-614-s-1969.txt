MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 614

DECLARING OCTOBER 4, 1969 AS SHOE INDUSTRY DAY.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Shoe Industry is one of the country’s major cottage industries; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting to give due recognition to the contribution of the shoe industry to economic development;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippine, do hereby declare October 4, 1969, as Shoe Industry Day under the auspices of the First Philippine Shoe Festival.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
