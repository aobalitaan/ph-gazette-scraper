MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 410

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JANUARY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1969 AS GENERAL EMILIO AGUINALDO CENTENNIAL YEAR AND AUTHORIZING THE AGUINALDO CENTENNIAL COMMISSION TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN.

WHEREAS, the Aguinaldo Centennial Commission was created under Executive Order No. 92 dated October 23, 1967, to take charge of the celebration of the centennial of General Emilio Aguinaldo and to build a fitting monument to perpetuate the memory of the President of the First Philippine Republic; and

WHEREAS, the Commission need the support of all freedom loving Filipinos;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from January 1 to December 31, 1969 as General Emilio Aguinaldo Centennial Year and authorize the Aguinaldo Centennial Commission to conduct a national fund campaign for the above purposes from June 12, 1968 to June 12, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set may hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
