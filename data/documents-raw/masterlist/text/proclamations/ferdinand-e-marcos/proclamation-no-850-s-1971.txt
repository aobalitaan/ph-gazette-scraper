MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 850

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 527 DATED DECEMBER 1, 1932 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL SITE RESERVATION IN THE CITY OF LAOAG AND DECLARING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF ACT 3038, IN RELATION TO THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 527 dated December 1, 1932, which established the provincial hospital site reservation situated in the City of Laoag and declare the land embraced therein open to disposition under the provisions of Act 3038, in relation to the provisions of the Public Land Act.

IN WITNESS THEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
