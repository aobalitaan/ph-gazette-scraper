MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 936

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 64, DATED AUGUST 6, 1925, A PORTION OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE DEVELOPMENT BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES ON A NEGOTIATED FEE BASIS.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of General Services, and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 64, dated August 6, 1925, which reserved a parcel of land of the private domain, situated in the City of Baguio, Island of Luzon, for park site purposes, a certain portion of the land embraced therein and declare the same open to disposition for building site purposes of the Development Bank of the Philippines on a negotiated fee basis, which portion is more particularly described as follows, to wit:

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

A parcel of land situated in the Residence Section “A”, Baguio Townsite, Island of Luzon, bounded on all sides by Public Road. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 850 28’E., 367 m. from B.L. No. 8, Baguio Townsite;

Thence N. 47 deg . 34’W., 18.74 m. to point 2; Thence N. 47 deg . 25’E., 54.49 m. to point 3; Thence S. 47 deg . 34’E., 18.74 m. to point 4; Thence S. 42 deg . 26’W., 54.49 m. to point

of beginning containing an area of One Thousand and Twenty one (1,021) square meters, more or less. Date of Survey, April 1, 1971.

Note: All data are approximate and subject to future verification and approval.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
