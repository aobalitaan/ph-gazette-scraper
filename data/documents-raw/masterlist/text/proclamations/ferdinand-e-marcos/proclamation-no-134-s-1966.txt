MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 134

DECLARING SATURDAY, DECEMBER 31, 1966, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC

WHEREAS, the thirtieth day (Friday) of December, 1966, and the first day (Sunday) of January, 1967, being holidays, the thirty-first day (Saturday) of December, 1966, may be declared a special public holiday to enable the people to enjoy an uninterrupted four-day holiday without prejudice to the public interests;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby proclaim Saturday, December thirty-first, nineteen hundred and sixty-six, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
