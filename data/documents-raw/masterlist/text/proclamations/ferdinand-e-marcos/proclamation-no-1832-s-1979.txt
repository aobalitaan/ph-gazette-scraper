MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1832

RESERVING FOR PUERICULTURE CENTER AND MUNICIPAL RURAL HEALTH UNITS OFFICES SITES PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KIDAPAWAN, PROVINCE OF NORTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources, and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale of settlement and reserve for puericulture center and municipal rural health units offices sites purposes under the administration of the Municipal Government of Kidapawan, subject to private rights, if any there be and to the provisions of Executive Order No. 113, series of 1955, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in the Municipality of Kidapawan, province of North Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
