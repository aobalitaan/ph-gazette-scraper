TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 963

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM FEBRUARY 15 TO JUNE 15, 1972, FOR THE 1972 NATIONAL MEMBERSHIP AND EDUCATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE MENTAL HEALTH ASSOCIATION, INC. (PMHA).

WHEREAS, good mental health is essential in enabling people to adjust to existing situations and to give their share in the common task of nation-building;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Mental Health Association, Inc., during its two decades of existence has demonstrated the value and importance of its services in the promotion of good mental health and in the preventive, curative and rehabilitative aspects of mental illness; and

WHEREAS, it is a proven fact that the more members are enrolled in the movement the greater and the wider the outreach of its educational campaign for good mental health is achieved;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from February 15 to June 15, 1972, as the period for the 1972 National Membership and Educational Fund Campaign of the Philippine Mental Health Association. I call upon all government officials, employees, citizens and residents to assist in this campaign and to give it their wholehearted support.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
