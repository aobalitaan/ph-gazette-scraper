TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 685

DECLARING JULY 21, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, AND IN THE CITIES OF DAGUPAN AND SAN CARLOS.

WHEREAS, July 21, 1970, marks the centennial anniversary of the birth of the late Don Daniel Maramba, former Provincial Governor, Representative and Senator from Pangasinan;

WHEREAS, Don Daniel Maramba had been instrumental in effecting several public improvements in his province; and

WHEREAS, the people of his province should be given full opportunity to celebrate the centennary of his birth with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, July 21, 1970, as a special public holiday in the province of Pangasinan, and in the cities of Dagupan and San Carlos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
