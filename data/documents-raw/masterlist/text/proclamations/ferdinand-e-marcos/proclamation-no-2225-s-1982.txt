Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

PROCLAMATION NO. 2225

RESERVING FOR EXPANSION SITE PURPOSES OF THE NATIONAL STEEL CORPORATION CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF ILIGAN, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for expansion site purposes under the administration of the President, National Steel Corporation, subject to the private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in the City of Iligan, Island of Mindanao, which parcels are more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 4254, Cad. 292

Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 4254, Cad. 292, Iligan Cadastre, being N. 32-58’E., 545.61 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 7; Cad 292, Iligan Cadastre, thence

point of beginning, containing an area of Seventeen Thousand Seven Hundred Twenty-five (17,725) Square Meters;

Lot 4255, Cad. 292

Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 4255, Cad. 292, Iligan Cadastre, being N. 63-53′ E., 447.67 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 7, Cad. 292, Iligan Cadastre, thence

point of beginning, containing an area of Two Thousand Nine Hundred and Ninety (2,990) Square Meters.

This proclamation is subject further to the condition that the occupants of the above-described lots shall be compensated for the value of their improvements and that they will be relocated to the area set aside by the National Steel Corporation, in coordination with the National Housing Authority and the City Government of Iligan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 19th day of July, 1982.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
