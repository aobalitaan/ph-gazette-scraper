TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 974

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 21, 1972, A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS AND ALL CHARTERED CITIES THEREIN.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, February 21, 1972, as a special public holiday in the Province of Batangas and all chartered cities therein on the occasion of the burial of the late Gov. Antonio Carpio.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
