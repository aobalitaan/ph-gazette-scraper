TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1162

DECLARING NOVEMBER 4 TO 11, 1973, AS VOLLEYBALL CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK

WHEREAS, the promotion of sports for the development of a sound mind and healthy body is one of the policies of the Government; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Volleyball Association of the Philippines is sponsoring from November 4 to 11, 1973, nationwide competitions and seminars on volleyball coachings and mechanics of competition in an effort to share in the overall physical fitness program of the Government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from November 4 to 11, 1973, as Volleyball Consciousness Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
