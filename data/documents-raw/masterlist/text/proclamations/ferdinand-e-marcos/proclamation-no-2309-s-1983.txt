MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2309

EXTENDING THE EDUCATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE MGA LINGKOD BAYAN UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1984

WHEREAS, the Mga Lingkod Bayan was authorized under Proclamation No. 2217 dated July 3, 1982, as amended by Proclamation No. 2243 dated November 19, 1982, to conduct a national and educational fund campaign during the period from June 15 to December 31, 1982;

WHEREAS, the organization needs more time to raise funds to achieve its goals;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Mga Lingkod Bayan up to December 31, 1984 and grant the same terms and conditions as found in Proclamation No. 2217 dated July 3, 1982, as amended by Proclamation No. 2243 dated November 19, 1982.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen hundred and eighty-three.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TAVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
