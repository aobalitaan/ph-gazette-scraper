MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1958

DECLARING HOLY SATURDAY, APRIL 5, 1980, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY.

WHEREAS, Holy Saturday, which falls on April 5, 1980 may be declared a special public holiday without prejudice to the public interest to give the people continuous lenten holidays;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Holy Saturday, April 5, 1980, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
