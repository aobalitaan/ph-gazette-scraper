TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1363

DECLARING FRIDAY, DECEMBER 27, 1974, AS JUAN SUMULONG DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL.

WHEREAS, Friday, December 27, 1974, marks the birth centenary of Juan Sumulong, an illustrious man, nationalist, patriot, jurist, writer, and statesman; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to recall his outstanding contribution to the development of the Philippines as a nation;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, December 27, 1974, as JUAN SUMULONG DAY in the Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
