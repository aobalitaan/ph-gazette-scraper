MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 280

DECLARING THURSDAY, OCTOBER 12, 1967, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN QUEZON CITY.

WHEREAS, the 28th anniversary of the creation of Quezon City falls on October 12, 1967; and

WHEREAS, the people of Quezon City should be afforded the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thursday, October 12, 1967, as a special public holiday in Quezon City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
