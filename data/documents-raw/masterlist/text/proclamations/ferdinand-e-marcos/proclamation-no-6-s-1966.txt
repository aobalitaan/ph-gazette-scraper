MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 6

DECLARING FRIDAY, JANUARY 28, 1966, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF CAVITE.

In commemoration of the 105th birthday anniversary of Julian Felipe, composer of the Philippine National Anthem, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Friday, January 28, 1966, as a special public holiday in the City of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
