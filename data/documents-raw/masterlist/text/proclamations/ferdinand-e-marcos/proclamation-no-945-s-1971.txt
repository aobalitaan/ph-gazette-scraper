PROCLAMATION NO. 945

AUTHORIZING THE 3RD WORLD MEN’S SOFTBALL CHAMPIONSHIP ORGANIZING COMMITTEE TO CONDUCT A FUND DRIVE FROM JANUARY 1 TO MARCH 12, 1972.

WHEREAS, the Philippines .has been chosen as site for the Third World Men’s Softball Championship scheduled at the Rodriguez Sports Center, Marikina, Rizal, from March 4 to 12, 1972; and

WHEREAS, the Organizing Committee needs public support for the various facilities and amenities incident to the tournament to ensure its success and for the convenience of PARTICIPANTS and visitors;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Organizing Committee, THIRD WORLD MEN’S SOFTBALL CHAMPIONSHIP, to conduct, a fund drive from January 1 to March 12, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Source: Malacañang Records Office
