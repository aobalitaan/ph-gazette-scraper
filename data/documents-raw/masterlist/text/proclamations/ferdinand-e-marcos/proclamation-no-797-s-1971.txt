TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 797

AUTHORIZING THE BOY SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES, PASAY CITY COUNCIL, TO CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN FROM JANUARY 16 TO APRIL 15, 1971.

WHEREAS, the Boy Scouts of the Philippines, Pasay City Council, needs funds for the reconstruction of its typhoon-damaged headquarters, the focal point of its character building program; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs the support of all civic-spirited citizens to enable the organization to carry on with its program;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Boy Scouts of the Philippines, Pasay City Council, to conduct a fund drive from January 16 to April 15, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) PONCIANO G.A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
