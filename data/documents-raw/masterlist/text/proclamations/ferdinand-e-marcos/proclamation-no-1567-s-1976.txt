MALACANANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1567

AUTHORIZING THE GIRL SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM AUGUST 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1976.

WHEREAS, youth development and training is a primary concern of the New Society in its efforts to build a strong, healthy, and responsible citizenry with the mandate of the New Constitution;

WHEREAS, the Girl Scouts of the Philippines (GSP), for the past 36 years, has consistently shown its effectivity as a vehicle for character building and citizenship education;

WHEREAS, the Government, in recognition of this valuable contribution to national development, has included Girl Scouting in the high school curriculum; and

WHEREAS, funds should be generated to meet the needs of the GSP movement so brought about by its inclusion in the curriculum and its community service projects;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Girl Scouts of the Philippines to conduct a national fund drive during the period from August 1 to December 31, 1976. I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines to wholeheartedly support this campaign and give generously of their means to enable the Girl Scouts of the Philippines to carry on its important task.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
