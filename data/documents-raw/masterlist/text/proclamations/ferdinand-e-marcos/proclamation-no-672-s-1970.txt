MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 672

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE GENERAL EMILIO AGUINALDO NATIONAL CENTENNIAL COMMISSION TO DECEMBER 31, 1970.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 566 dated June 9, 1969, authorized the General Emilio Aguinaldo National Centennial Commission to conduct a national fund campaign up to December 31, 1969, to perpetuate the memory of the President of the First Philippine Republic, General Emilio Aguinaldo; and

WHEREAS, the Commission needs more time to realize its fund campaign goal to pursue its project to construct an Aguinaldo Memorial and to rehabilitate/restore the Aguinaldo Shrine in Kawit, Cavite;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the General Emilio Aguinaldo National Centennial Commission up to December 31, 1970.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary
