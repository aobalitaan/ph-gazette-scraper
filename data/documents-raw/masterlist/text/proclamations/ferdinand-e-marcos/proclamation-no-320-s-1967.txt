MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 320

AUTHORIZING THE RESEARCH FOUNDATION IN PHILIPPINE ANTHROPOLOGY AND ARCHEOLOGY, INC., TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN.

WHEREAS, the Research Foundation in Philippine Anthropology and Archeology is a non-profit organization dedicated to the propagation and preservation of Philippine culture, awards scholarships and fellowships, finances field research in anthropology and archeology, and sponsors various cultural projects;

WHEREAS, one of its projects this year, in cooperation with the Chinese government, is an exhibition of Chinese artifacts and porcelain found in the Philippines; and

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs the support of the public for its various cultural and historical projects;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Research Foundation in Philippine Anthropology and Archeology, Inc., to conduct a fund campaign from November 1, 1967, to January 31, 1968. I urge all residents to support the drive to ensure its success.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
