MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 191

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND DRIVE OF THE PHILIPPINE CANCER SOCIETY, INC., UP TO MAY 31, 1967.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc., was authorized to conduct a national educational, membership and fund drive during the period from January 1 to March 31, 1967, under Proclamation No. 137 dated January 13, 1967; and

WHEREAS, the Society needs an extension of the period of its fund campaign to meet the P300,000.00 goal to carry out its activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national educational, membership and fund drive of the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc., up to May 31, 1967.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
