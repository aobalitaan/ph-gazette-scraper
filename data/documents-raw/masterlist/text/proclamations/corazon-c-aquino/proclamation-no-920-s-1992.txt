MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 920

CONFIRMING PROCLAMATION NO. 2313, DATED SEPTEMBER 21, 1983, WHICH DECLARED LOTS 140 AND 141 SITUATED IN BAGUIO TOWNSITE, CITY OF BAGUIO, ISLAND OF LUZON, AS MILITARY RESERVATION

Pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby confirm Proclamation No. 2313, dated September 21, 1983, declaring Lots 140 and 141 situated in Baguio Townsite, City of Baguio, Island of Luzon, as military reservation, a copy of which Proclamation is herewith attached as Annex “A”.

This proclamation shall take effect upon its publication in the Official Gazette.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
