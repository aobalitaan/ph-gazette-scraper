MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 275

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL AND NATIONAL GOVERNMENT CENTER SITES PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF PANACAN, MUNICIPALITY OF NARRA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and reserve for Municipal High School and National Government Centers sites purposes certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in the Barrio of Panacan, Municipality of Narra, Province of Palawan, which are more particularly described as follows:
