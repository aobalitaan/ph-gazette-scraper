MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 263

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE OLYMPIC COMMITTEE TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN FROM JUNE 1, 1988 TO MAY 30, 1989.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Olympic Committee intends to conduct a national fund campaign to raise funds for its preparation for the Olympic Games in Seoul this September and for the Southeast Asia Games next year; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Olympic Committee needs the support of the private and public sectors to realize its laudable objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Philippine Olympic Committee to conduct a national fund campaign for the period from June 1, 1988 to May 30, 1989.

I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality or creed, to give generously and participate actively in this campaign.

All gifts, bequests, donations and/or contributions to the Committee shall be governed by the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 507, dated July 16, 1974.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of May in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
