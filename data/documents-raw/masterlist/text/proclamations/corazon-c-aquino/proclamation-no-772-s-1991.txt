MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 772

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 7 TO 14, 1991 AS “PEACE TOYS WEEK”

WHEREAS, there is a need to continue the pursuit of peace education among the youth;

WHEREAS, the celebration of “Peace Toys Week” this year will again invite public awareness on the ill effects of war toys to our youth;

WHEREAS, the successful observance of the event will require the support of both government and the people;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare September 7 to 14, 1991 as “Peace Toys Week” under the auspices of the National Federation of Women’s Club of the Philippines.

I call upon all residents regardless of nationality, race or creed, the public and private sectors, institutions and organizations to lend the full support and assistance to ensure the successful and meaningful observance of “Peace Toys Week.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
