MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 911

DECLARING A PERIOD OF NATIONAL MOURNING OVER THE DEATH OF LORENZO M. TAÑADA, FORMER SENATOR OF THE REPUBLIC

WHEREAS, the death of Lorenzo M. Tañada, former Senator of the Republic, nationalist and statesman, is a great loss to our people and had caused deep sorrow among them;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby proclaim a period of national mourning beginning May 30, 1992 for our beloved Lorenzo M. Tañada and to continue up to the date of interment. During this period, flags of all government buildings and installations throughout the country shall be flown at half mast.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 29th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
