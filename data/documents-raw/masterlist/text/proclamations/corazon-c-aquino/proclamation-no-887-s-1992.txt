MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 887

FURTHER AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 2029, DATED NOVEMBER 11, 1980, AS AMENDED BY PROCLAMATION NO. 2162, DATED FEBRUARY 12, 1982, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY-GENERAL SANTOS UNIT CIVIL RESERVATION AND THE HUMAN SETTLEMENT SITES FOR LANDLESS FAMILIES LOCATED IN GENERAL SANTOS CITY

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Proclamation No. 2029, series of 1980, as amended by Proclamation No. 2162, series of 1982, which established the Mindanao State University-General Santos Unit Civil Reservation and the Human Settlement sites for landless families located in Uhaw, Tambler, General Santos City, by identifying the areas reserved for the Mindanao State University Site and the Human Settlements Sites respectively and transferring the administration of the Human Settlements Sites from the defunct Ministry of Human Settlements to the City Government of General Santos, subject to private rights if any there be, which areas are more particularly described as follows, to wit:
