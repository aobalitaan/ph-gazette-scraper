MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 314

DECLARING SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 8, 1988, AS “IRIGA CITY DAY”.

WHEREAS, Saturday, September 3, 1988, marks the 20th Charter Anniversary of Iriga City; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus public attention on the accomplishments and progress of the City so as to stimulate the interest, cooperation and participation of the residents therein;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, September 3, 1988, as “Iriga City Day”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of September in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
