MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 448

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY MALABBAC, MUNICIPALITY OF IGUIG, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, ISLAND OF LUZON, PHILIPPINES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement, and reserve for school site purposes of the Malabbac Elementary School, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at Barangay Malabbac, Municipality of Iguig, Province of Cagayan, and more particularly described as follows, to wit:

A parcel of land Lot 442, Pls-601-D situated at Malabbac, Municipality of Iguig, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the SW along line 1-2 by Lot 438, line 2-3 by Lot 436; on the NW along line 3-4 by Road; on the NE along line 5-6 by a Feeder Road; on the SE along lines 6-7-8 by Lot 441; on the SW along line 8-1 by Lot 439, Pls-601-D, Iguig, Cagayan. Beginning at a point marked “1” of plan being S 11 deg. 14’E, 3507.92 m. from BLLM # 1, Pls-601-D, Iguig, Cagayan.

thence S. 71 deg. 27’W., 81.16 m. to pt. 2; thence S. 67 deg. 41’W., 46.75 m. to pt. 3; thence N. 23 deg. 10’W., 138.74 m. to pt. 4; thence N. 74 deg. 31’E., 149.23 m. to pt. 5; thence N. 71 deg. 50’E., 45.65 m. to pt. 6; thence S. 15 deg. 11’E., 74.76 m. to pt. 7; thence S. 15 deg. 11’E., 58.06 m. to pt. 8; thence S. 78 deg. 12’W., 48.23 m. to pt. 1; point of beginning

containing an area of TWENTY FOUR THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED FIFTY TWO (24,352) sq. m. more or less. All corners are marked by P.S. cyl. conc. mons. 15 x 60 cm. Bearings true. Date of original survey on June 10, 1958 by Surveyor Luis Salanga and was approved on February 15, 1973.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
