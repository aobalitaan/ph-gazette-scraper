MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 377

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN POBLACION, COMPOSTELA, DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement, and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Municipality of Compostela, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows, to wit:

Lot 37-A Csd-11-010153-D (Compostela Municipal High School)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 37-A, Csd-11-010153-D) situated in the Municipality of Compostela, Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the Northeast, along lines 4-5-6 and 1 by Lot 37-B, Csd-11-010153-D; on the Southeast, along line 1-2 by Road; and the Southwest, along line 2-3 by Road; and on the Northwest along line 3-4 by Lot 37-B Csd-11-010153-D. Beginning at a point marked “1” on Plan being S. 31 deg. E, 571.01 M. from BLLM No. 5, Compostela, Cad-282.
