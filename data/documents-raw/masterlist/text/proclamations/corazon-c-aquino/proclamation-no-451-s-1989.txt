MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 451

DECLARING THURSDAY, AUGUST 31, 1989, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES AND IN THE CITY OF OLONGAPO

WHEREAS, the 82nd birthday of the late President RAMON F. MAGSAYSAY falls on August 31, 1989;

WHEREAS, in the few years that he served our people President Magsaysay proved himself an earnest defender of the common man, dedicated himself to the improvement of the public service, and worked vigorously for the advancement of the Republic; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that at least the people of his birthplace be afforded full opportunity to celebrate his birthday with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, August 31, 1989, as a special day in the province of Zambales and in the City of Olongapo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
