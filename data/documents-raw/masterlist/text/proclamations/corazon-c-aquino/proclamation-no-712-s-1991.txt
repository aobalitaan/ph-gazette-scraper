MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 712

DECLARING THE WEEK APRIL 13 TO 21 OF EVERY YEAR AS “POSITIVE THINKING WEEK”

WHEREAS, positive thinking enriches one’s personality, and keeps God’s blessings flowing into the ‘Cup of Life;’

WHEREAS, being positive in mind and spirit creates an environment filled with love, peace and harmony;

WHEREAS, positive thinking brings one closer to the ideals of truth, beauty and happiness;

WHEREAS, given the significance of positivism to various art organizations, it is but proper to give due recognition to the initiatives of these organizations in the promotion of positivism;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the week April 13 to 21 of every year as “Positive Thinking Week.”

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12th day of April in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) OSCAR M. ORBOS Executive Secretary
