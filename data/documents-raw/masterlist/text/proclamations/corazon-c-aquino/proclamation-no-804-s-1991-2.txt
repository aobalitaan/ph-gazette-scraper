MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 804

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE KORONADAL CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Koronadal Central Elementary School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, which is more particularly described as follows, to wit:

A parcel of land (Lot No. 879, Ts-1032), situated in the Poblacion, Municipality of Koronadal (Marbel), Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the NE., along lines 1-2-3-4 by Jose Rizal Street; on the SE., along line 4-5 by Rafael Alunan Avenue; on the SW., along lines 5-6-7-8 by Road; and on the NW., along line 8-1 by Sinsuat Street. Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot No. 879, Ts-1032, being N. 71° 39′ W., 2922.47 m. from Triangulation Station “TUKADILAS”; Thence . . . .

N. 84° 12′ E., 4.15 m. to point 2;

S. 49° 42′ E., 243.50 m. to point 3;

S. 05° 31′ E., 3.95 m. to point 4;

S. 40° 17′ W., 304.06 m. to point 5;

S. 89° 27′ W., 4.13 m. to point 6;

N. 49° 42′ W., 243.32 m. to point 7;

N. 03° 48′ W., 4.22 m. to point 8;

N. 40° 19′ E., 303.54 m. to point

of beginning. Containing an area of SEVENTY SEVEN THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED FORTY EIGHT SQUARE METERS AND FIFTY SIX SQUARE DECIMETERS (77,148.56).

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground. Bearings true.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
