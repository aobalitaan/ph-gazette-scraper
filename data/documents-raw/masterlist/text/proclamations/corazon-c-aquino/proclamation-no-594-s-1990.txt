MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 594

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 2 TO 7 OF EVERY YEAR AS PHILIPPINE BUSINESS WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine Chamber of Commerce and Industry is recognized as a voice of Philippine Business and representative of the private business sector in its relationship with the Government;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Chamber of Commerce and Industry serves as a forum for the development of a consensus of the business community on matters of economic and business significance;

WHEREAS, since its organization, the Philippine Chamber of Commerce and Industry has advocated greater participation of Filipino businessman in economic development;

WHEREAS, it is necessary to focus the attention of the nation to the progress that Philippine business has achieved;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from July 2 to 7 of every year as Philippine Business Week under the auspices of the Philippine Chamber of Commerce and Industry.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of June in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
