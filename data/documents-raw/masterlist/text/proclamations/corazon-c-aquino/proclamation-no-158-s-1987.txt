PROCLAMATION NO. 158

ESTABLISHING AS HAPOL-AGAN FOREST RESERVE, THE PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANGKAYAN, PROVINCE OF BENGUET, ISLAND OF LUZON, PHILIPPINES
