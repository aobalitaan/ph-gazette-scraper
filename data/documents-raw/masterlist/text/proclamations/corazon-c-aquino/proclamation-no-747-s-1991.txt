MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 747

DECLARING ADDITIONAL MUNICIPALITIES IN THE PROVINCE OF TARLAC UNDER A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN VIEW OF THE CONTINUING AND THREATENING ERUPTIONS OF MT. PINATUBO.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare that a state of public calamity exists in the following municipalities of Tarlac province, in view of the continuing and threatening eruptions of Mt. Pinatubo:

Anao, Camiling, Gerona, La Paz, Mayantoc, Moncada, Paniqui, Pura, Ramos, San Clemente, San Manuel, Tarlac, Sta. Ignacia and Victoria

All departments and other government agencies concerned are hereby directed to implement and execute relief and rehabilitation work in accordance with existing operational plans and directives and orders issued in connection with calamities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 27th of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) OSCAR M. ORBOS Executive Secretary
