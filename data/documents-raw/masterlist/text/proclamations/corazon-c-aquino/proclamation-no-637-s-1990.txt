MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 637

DECLARING MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 17, 1990, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOS BAÑOS, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA.

WHEREAS, Monday, September 17, 1990, marks the 375th Foundation Anniversary of the Municipality of Los Baños, Province of Laguna; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of Los Baños be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27, Chapter 7, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, September 17, 1990, as a special day in the Municipality of Los Baños, Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
