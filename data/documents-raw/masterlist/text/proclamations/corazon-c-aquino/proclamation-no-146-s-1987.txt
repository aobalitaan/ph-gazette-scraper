MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 146

DECLARING TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 1, 1987, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BAGUIO CITY.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, September 1, 1987, marks the 78th anniversary of the charter of Baguio City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Baguio City should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
