MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 68

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE GIRL SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES UP TO OCTOBER 15, 1987.

WHEREAS , the Girl Scouts of the Philippines was authorized under Proclamation No. 2462 dated October 17, 1985 to conduct a national fund campaign from October 17, 1985 to October 16, 1986; and

WHEREAS , the Girl Scouts of the Philippines needs additional time to raise more funds for its laudable goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national fund campaign of the Girl Scouts of the Philippines up to October 15, 1987.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary
