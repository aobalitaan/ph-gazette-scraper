MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 193

SUPPLEMENTING PROCLAMATION NO. 191 DATED NOVEMBER 27, 1987 BY DECLARING THE EXISTENCE OF A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN THE PROVINCES OF RIZAL, LAGUNA, ROMBLON, MINDORO ORIENTAL, MINDORO OCCIDENTAL, CAMARINES NORTE AND CATANDUANES.

I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order the inclusion of the Provinces of Rizal, Laguna, Romblon, Mindoro Oriental, Mindoro Occidental, Camarines Norte and Catanduanes in the areas where a state of public calamity exists in view of the severe destruction of property and loss of life therein brought by typhoon “Sisang”.

This Proclamation shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) ADOLFO S. AZCUNA Acting Executive Secretary
