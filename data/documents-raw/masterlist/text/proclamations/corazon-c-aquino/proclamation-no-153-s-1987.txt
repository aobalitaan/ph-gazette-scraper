MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 153

DECLARING MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 28, 1987, AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE.

WHEREAS, the 78th birth anniversary of the late Wenceslao Q. Vinzons, a native son of the Province of Camarines Norte, falls on September 28, 1987;

WHEREAS, the courage and love of country with which the late Wenceslao Q. Vinzons offered his life during the last war best exemplify Filipino patriotism; and

WHEREAS, the Province of Camarines Norte should be given full opportunity to celebrate the event with fitting ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, September 28, 1987, as a non-working special day in the Province of Camarines Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
