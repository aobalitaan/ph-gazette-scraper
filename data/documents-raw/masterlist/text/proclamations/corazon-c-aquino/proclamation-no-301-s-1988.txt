MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 301

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 31, 1988, AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY IN LIPA CITY.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, August 31, 1988, marks the 41st anniversary of the creation of Lipa City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Lipa City should be afforded an opportunity to participate in the ceremonies on that historic occasion;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Wednesday, August 31, 1988, as a special holiday in Lipa City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 5th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
