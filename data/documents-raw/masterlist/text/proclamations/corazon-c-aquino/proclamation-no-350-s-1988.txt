MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 350

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 1101, DATED JANUARY 16, 1973, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE LEYTE REGIONAL PRISON (LRP), AND REVERTING THE AREA SET ASIDE THEREFOR AND ITS PERMANENT FACILITIES THEREAT TO THE JURISDICTION AND CUSTODY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES.

Upon the recommendation of the Director of Prisons and with the concurrence of the Secretary of Justice and the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 1101, dated January 16, 1973, and revert the area of 861.60 hectares set aside for the Leyte Regional Prison and its permanent facilities thereat to the jurisdiction and custody of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 25th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
