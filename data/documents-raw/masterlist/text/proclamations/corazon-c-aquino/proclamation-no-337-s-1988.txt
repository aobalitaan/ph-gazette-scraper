MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 337

DECLARING FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 4, 1988, AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BOHOL AND THE CITY OF TAGBILARAN.

WHEREAS, Friday, November 4, 1988, is the birthday of the late President Carlos P. Garcia, an illustrious son of the Province of Bohol, who distinguished himself as a statesman and who dedicated most of his life to the service of his country; and

WHEREAS, the people of the Province of Bohol and the City of Tagbilaran should be given full opportunity to perpetuate and honor his memory with appropriate ceremonies on his natal day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Friday, November 4, 1988, as a special holiday in the Province of Bohol and the City of Tagbilaran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 26th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
