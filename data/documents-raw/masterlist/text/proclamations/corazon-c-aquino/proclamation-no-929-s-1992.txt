MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 929

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JULY 1, 1992, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF TAGBILARAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, July 1, 1992, marks the 26th Charter Anniversary of the City of Tagbilaran;

WHEREAS, the residents of the City of Tagbilaran desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this event in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, July 1, 1992, as a special day in the City of Tagbilaran;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
