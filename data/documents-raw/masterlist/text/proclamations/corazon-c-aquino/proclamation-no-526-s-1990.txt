MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 526

DECLARING SATURDAY, FEBRUARY 24, 1990, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN CEBU CITY.

WHEREAS, Saturday, February 24, 1990, marks the 53rd Charter Anniversary of Cebu City; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of Cebu City be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, February 24, 1990, as a special day in Cebu City. The day shall be a non-working day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (SGD.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
