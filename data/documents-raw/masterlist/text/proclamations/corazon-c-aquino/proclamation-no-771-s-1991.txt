MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 771

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM AUGUST 22-24, 1991, AS “NATIONAL PRINTING WEEK”

WHEREAS, the printing industry renders invaluable service in the promotion of arts and culture, and in the dissemination of information;

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus the attention of the public on the vital role played by the printing industry in the general effort to promote national progress;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from August 22-24, 1991, as “National Printing Week” under the auspices of the Printing Industries Association of the Philippines, Inc.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
