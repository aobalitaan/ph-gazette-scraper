MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 485

DECLARING THE EXISTENCE OF A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN CERTAIN AREAS OF REGIONS I, II, III AND THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION AFFECTED BY TYPHOON “TASING.”

WHEREAS, according to the Secretary of National Defense and Chairman, National Disaster Coordinating Council (NDCC), certain areas suffered and the Cordillera Administrative Region; and

WHEREAS, to cushion the possible economic impact of the typhoon and to enable the agencies concerned to immediately implement measures to alleviate the sufferings and hardships of the disaster victims, there is need to declare the existence of a state of publicly calamity in the aforementioned areas.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby proclaim that a state of public calamity exist in the following areas:

Region I

Region II

Region III

Region IV

Ilocos Sur

Isabela

Tarlac

Aurora

La Union

Nueva Viscaya

Nueva Ecija

Pangasinan

Quirino

Cabanatuan City

Dagupan City

San Jose City

San Carlos City

Palayan City

Cordillera Administrative Region

Ifugao Kalinga Benguet Baguio City

All departments and other government agencies concerned are hereby directed to implement and execute relief and rehabilitation work in accordance with existing operational plans and directives and orders issued in connection with calamities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
