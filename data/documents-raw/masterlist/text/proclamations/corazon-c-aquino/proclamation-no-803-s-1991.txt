MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 803

RESERVING FOR OFFICE, WAREHOUSE, DRIER AND OTHER SITE FACILITIES PURPOSES OF THE NATIONAL FOOD AUTHORITY CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement, and reserve for office, warehouse, drier and other site facilities purposes, under the administration of the National Food Authority, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in Poblacion, Municipality of Compostela, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows, to wit:
