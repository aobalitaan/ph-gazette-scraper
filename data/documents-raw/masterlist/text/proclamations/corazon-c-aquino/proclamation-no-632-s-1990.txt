MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 632

RESERVING FOR PROVINCIAL GOVERNMENT CENTER SITE PURPOSES OF THE PROVINCE OF ISABELA, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY ALIBAGU, MUNICIPALITY OF ILAGAN, PROVINCE OF ISABELA.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement, and reserve for Provincial Government Center Site purposes of the Province of Isabela, subject to private rights, if there by any, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, situated in Barangay Alibagu, Municipality of Ilagan, Province of Isabela, and more particularly described as follows, to wit:

A parcel of land, plan Ap-2-02-000722, which is identical to Lot 8200, Cad. 403, as surveyed for PROPOSED GOVERNMENT CENTER OF ISABELA, situated in the Barrio of Alibagu, Municipality of Ilagan, Province of Isabela, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the W., along line 1-2 by Lot 13888, line 2-3 by Lot 13889, line 3-4 by Lot 13890, line 4-5 by Lot 14166, line 5-6 by Lot 14167, line 6-7 by Lot 14168, line 7-8 by Lot 14169, line 8-9 by Lot 14170, line 9-10 by Lot 14171, line 10-11 by Lot 14172, line 11-12 by Lot 14173, lines 12-13-14 by Lot 11986 all of Cad. 403; on the NW., along lines 14-15-16-17-18-19 by National Road; on the NE., along lines 19-20- 21-22-23 by Lot 13877, Cad. 403 (Port.) CR. 81, Lot 1, the Provincial Government of Isabela, on the E., along line 23-24 by Baligatan Creek; on the SE., along lines 24-25-26-27-28 by Lot 354, Pls-625; line 28-1 by Lot 8201, claimed by Miguel Medina. Beginning at a point marked “1” of plan being S. 28 deg. 46’W., 5997.94 m. for BLLM No. 1, Cad. 403;

beginning, containing an area of FIVE HUNDRED THIRTY EIGHT THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED SEVEN (538,507) SQUARE METERS more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows: All corners are marked by B.L. cyl. conc. mons. 15 x 60 cm. Bearings grid; date of original survey on July 28, 1983 and that the survey was executed by Geodetic Engineer Juan P. Ortiz on January 10, 1989 and was approved on April 3, 1989.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR.
