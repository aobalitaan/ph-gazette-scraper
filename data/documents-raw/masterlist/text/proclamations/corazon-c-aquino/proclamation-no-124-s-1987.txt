MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 124

ESTABLISHING AS ABASIG-MATOGDON WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF LABO AND SAN VICENTE, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, ISLAND OF LUZON, PHILIPPINES.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C.
