MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 720

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE GREENFIELD HIGH SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARANGAY OF GREENFIELD, MUNICIPALITY OF MAGPET, PROVINCE OF NORTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement, and reserve for school site purposes of the Greenfield High School, under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Barangay of Greenfield, Municipality of Magpet, Province of North Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 382-A, Csd-12-004665
