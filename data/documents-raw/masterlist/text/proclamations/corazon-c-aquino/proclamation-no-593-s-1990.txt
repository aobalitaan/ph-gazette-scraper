MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 593

DECLARING SUNDAY, JULY 1, 1990, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF DIPOLOG

WHEREAS, Sunday, July 1, 1990, marks the foundation anniversary of the city;

WHEREAS, considering its historical significance, the people of the City of Dipolog should be accorded the opportunity to celebrate the foundation day of the city in meaningful ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Sunday, July 1, 1990, as a special public holiday in the City of Dipolog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
