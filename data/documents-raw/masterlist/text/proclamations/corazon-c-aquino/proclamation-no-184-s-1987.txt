MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 184

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE PALAWAN STATE COLLEGE A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF TINIGUIBAN, CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, ISLAND OF PALAWAN.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barrio Tiniguiban, City of Puerto Princesa, Island of Palawan, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:
