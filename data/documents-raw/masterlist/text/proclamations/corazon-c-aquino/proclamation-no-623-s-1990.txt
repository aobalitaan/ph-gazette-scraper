MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 623

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE MANAT BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY MANAT, MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Manat Barangay High School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Manat, Municipality of Nabunturan, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot 5000-B, Csd-11-008664-D identical to Lot 12991, Pls-2 (Compostela Public Land Subdivision)
