PROCLAMATION NO. 502

ESTABLISHING AS MANANGA WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE FOR PURPOSES OF PROTECTING, MAINTAINING, OR IMPROVING ITS WATER YIELD AND PROVIDING RESTRAINING MECHANISMS FOR INAPPROPRIATE FOREST EXPLOITATION AND DISRUPTIVE LAND-USE, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF TALISAY, MINGLANILLA AND CEBU CITY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, PHILIPPINES.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and set aside as Mananga Watershed Forest Reserve, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to the operations of previous proclamations reserving any portion thereof for specific purposes and to the implementation of socially-oriented projects and other forestry development programs consistent with the objectives of the proclamation as may be determined by the Department of Environment and Natural Resources Secretary, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Municipalities of Talisay and Minglanilla, Province of Cebu, and Cebu City, as shown in the Forest Management Bureau Map W.R.
