MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 622

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE BARCELONITA SCHOOL OF FISHERIES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY BARCELONITA, MUNICIPALITY OF CABUSAO, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Barcelonita School of Fisheries, under the administration of the Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Barcelonita, Municipality of Cabusao, Province of Camarines Sur, Island of Luzon, which is more particularly described as follows:

Plan Nr-05-000004 (Barcelonita Fisheries School)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Nr-05-000004), situated in Barangay Barcelonita, Municipality of Cabusao, Province of Camarines Sur, Island of Luzon.
