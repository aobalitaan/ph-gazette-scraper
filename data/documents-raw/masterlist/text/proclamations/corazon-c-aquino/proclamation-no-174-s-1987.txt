MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 174

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 287, DATED AUGUST 14, 1964, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOUTH UPI, PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO, AND DECLARING SAME AS ALIENABLE OF DISPOSABLE FOR TOWNSITE PURPOSES.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 287, dated August 14, 1964, which established the Mount Talayan Forest Reserve, and declare as alienable and disposal for townsite purposes, subject to the provision of the Public Land Act and to applicable laws and regulations governing townsite settlement, a certain parcel of land embraced therein situated in the Municipality of South Upi, Province of Maguindanao, as shown in Bureau of Forest Development Map No. Maguindanao PMD No. 12-001 and technically described, to wit:

1. A strip of 15 meters in width on each side of any public trail therein used as outlets for timber and other forest products, and a strip of 20 meters in width on each side from the highest bank of any stream are hereby retained as forest lands and must be maintained under permanent forest cover.

2. The extraction and utilization of any forest products remaining on the area herein excluded [declared alienable or disposable lands shall be subject to Forest and International Revenue Laws and Regulation.

3. Naturally grown trees still remaining after the area herein declared alienable or disposable land has been occupied and developed shall no longer be cut or harvested but shall be maintained and preserved as part of the permanent vegetation for areas to be reserved as Greenbelt pursuant to Letter of Instruction No. 917 and its implementing rules and guidelines.

4. The Regional Director of Environment and Natural Resources, in coordination with the local government officials concerned shall identify and delineate areas to be established, developed and maintained as forest or tree parks for the municipality or barangays therein, pursuant to Letter of Instruction No. 1312 and its implementing rules and regulations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of October, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO
