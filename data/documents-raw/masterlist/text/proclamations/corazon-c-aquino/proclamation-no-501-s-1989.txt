MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 501

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 3 TO 9, 1989, AS “(ENT) EAR, NOSE, THROAT CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK”

WHEREAS, the Philippine Society of Otolaryngology — Head and Neck Surgery, Inc., will launch a campaign wherein the public is made aware of the significance of early detection, prevention and treatment of ear, nose, throat, head and neck diseases;

WHEREAS, this will include ENT-Head and Neck operations to be performed in the different hospitals throughout the country and will be free of charge to indigent patients; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus national attention on the importance of this venture particularly to the indigent patients of our country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from December 3 to 9, 1989, as “(ENT) EAR, NOSE, THROAT, CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK” under the auspices of the Philippine Society of Otolaryngology — Head and Neck Surgery, Inc.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President:

(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
