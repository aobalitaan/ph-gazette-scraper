MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 403

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE PHILIPPINE FOUNDATION FOR THE REHABILITATION OF DISABLED UP TO MARCH 24, 1990

WHEREAS, the Philippine Foundation for the Rehabilitation of Disabled was authorized to conduct a national fund campaign up to March 24, 1989 under Proclamation No. 231, dated March 24, 1988; and

WHEREAS, the said Foundation needs additional campaign period to effectively realize its objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national fund campaign period of the Philippine Foundation for the Rehabilitation of Disabled up to March 24, 1990.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
