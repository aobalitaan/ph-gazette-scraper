MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 815

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE KORONADAL NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Koronadal National Comprehensive High School (KNCHS), under the administration of the Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Municipality of Koronadal, Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

A parcel of land, Lot No. 878, Ts-10-1032, situated in the Municipality of Koronadal, Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao.
