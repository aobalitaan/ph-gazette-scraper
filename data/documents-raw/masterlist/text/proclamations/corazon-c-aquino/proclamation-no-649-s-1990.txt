MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 649

GRANTING THE PHILIPPINE MENTAL HEALTH ASSOCIATION A NEW PERIOD TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN

WHEREAS, the Philippines Mental Health Association under Proclamation No. 404, series of 1989, was granted an extension to conduct a national membership and fund campaign until May 6, 1990;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Mental Health Association needs additional time to conduct its fund campaign in order to raise more funds so as to realize its objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby grant the Philippine Mental Health Association a new period, from October 1, 1990 to September 30, 1991 within which to conduct its national membership and fund campaign.

For this purpose, all donations, bequests and gifts received by the Philippine Mental Health Association for the implementation of its programs and activities shall be free from all forms of taxes and shall be deductible in full from the income of the donor for purposes of computing his income tax liability, subject to the pertinent provisions of Sections 94 (a) (3) and (b) (2) and 29 (h) (2) (C) (i) of the National Internal Revenue Code, as amended.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 5th day of October in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
