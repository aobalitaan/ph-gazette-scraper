PROCLAMATION NO. 508

CALLING THE CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO A SPECIAL SESSION

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by the Constitution, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby call the Congress of the Philippines to a special session on January 3, 1990 to consider the Conference Committee reports on these two urgent bills:

1. AN ACT VESTING ADDITIONAL POWERS TO THE PRESIDENTIAL COMMISSION CREATED UNDER ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 146, SERIES OF 1989, TO CONDUCT A FACT-FINDING INVESTIGATION OF THE 1989 REBELLION AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

2. AN ACT CREATING AN “ENERGY STANDBY FUND” TO SUPPORT THE OIL PRICE STABILIZATION FUND, APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF FIVE BILLION PESOS THEREFORE, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 28th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Eighty-Nine.
