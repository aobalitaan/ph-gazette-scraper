MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 681

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE    PURPOSES OF THE MALTANA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN MALTANA, TAMPAKAN, SOUTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Maltana Elementary School, under the Administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Maltana, Municipality of Tampakan, Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao and more particularly described as follows:

Lot M-27-B, Csd-11-009031, identical to Lot 3325, Pls 246-D-2 (Maltana Elementary School)
