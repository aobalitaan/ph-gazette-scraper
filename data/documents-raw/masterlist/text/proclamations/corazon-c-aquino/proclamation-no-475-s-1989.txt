MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 475

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE LEUKEMIA FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC., TO FEBRUARY 28, 1990

WHEREAS, the Leukemia Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 272, dated June 20, 1988, to conduct a national educational and fund campaign for the period August 1, 1988 to August 1, 1989; and

WHEREAS, the Leukemia Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., needs additional time to raise more funds for its laudable goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national educational and fund campaign period of the Leukemia Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., up to February 28, 1990.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of October in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
