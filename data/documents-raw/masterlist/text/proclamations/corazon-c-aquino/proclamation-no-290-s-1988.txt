MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 290

DECLARING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO, ISLAND OF LUZON, AS TEACHER’S CAMP RESERVATION

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in the City of Baguio, Island of Luzon, as Teacher’s Camp Reservation, under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels are more particularly described as follows:
