MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 208

RESERVING FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE BUREAU OF LANDS CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARRIO LALAKAY, MUNICIPALITY OF LOS BAÑOS, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and reserve for building site purposes of the Bureau of Lands, subject to private rights if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in Barrio Lalakay, Municipality of Los Baños, Province of Laguna, Island of Luzon, which are more particularly described as follows:
