MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 757

DECLARING THURSDAY, JULY 25, 1991, AS EX-GOVERNOR DOMINADOR M. CAMERINO DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Thursday, July 25, 1991, marks the 12th death anniversary of Ex-Governor Dominador M. Camerino;

WHEREAS, the people of Imus, Cavite, his birthplace, should be afforded an opportunity to observe the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, July 25, 1991, as Ex-Governor Dominador M. Camerino Day in the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite. The day, however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) MAGDANGAL B. ELMA Acting Executive Secretary
