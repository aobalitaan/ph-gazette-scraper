MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 426

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARANGAY OF SIBULAN, MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Sibulan Elementary School under the administration of the Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in the Barangay of Sibulan, Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao, which are more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 720-A, Bsd-11-000882

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot No. 720-A, Bsd-11-000882) situated in Sibulan, Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the SW., along line 1-2 by Lot 730, Cad. 275; and on the NW., NE., and SE., along lines 2-3-4-1 by Lot 720-B, Bsd-11-000882. Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 720-A, Bsd-11-000882, being N. 33-15 E., 813.19 m. from BLLM # 56, Cad. 275 to corner 1;

point of beginning, containing an area of FOUR THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED AND NINETY SIX (4,996) SQUARE METERS. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons., Bearings true. This lot was surveyed in accordance with law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder; Original Survey from November 1938 to February 1940 and that of the subdivision survey by S.H. Mendoza, Geodetic Engineer, on January 10, 1985 and approved on June 14, 1985.

Lot No. 730-A, Csd-11-003744-D (identical to Lot 17007, Cad. 275)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 730-A, Csd-11-003744-D) situated in Sibulan, Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by Lot 730-C Csd-11-003744-D; on the NE., and SE., along lines 2-3-4 by Lot 720, Cad. 275; and on the SW., along line 4-1 by Lot 730-B, Csd-11-003744-D. Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 730-A, Csd-11-003744-D, being N. 23-10 E., 816.75 m. from BLLM # 56, Cad-275 to corner 1;

point of beginning, containing an area of Five THOUSAND (5,000) SQUARE METERS. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows: point 3 by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons. and the rest by P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. Bearings true. This lot was surveyed in accordance with law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder; Original Survey from November 1938 to February 1940 and that of the subdivision survey by Antonio L. Caputolan, Geodetic Engineer on December 5, 1976 and approved on December 16, 1976.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June , in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
