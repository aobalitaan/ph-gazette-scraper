MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 706

DECLARING TUESDAY, MARCH 19, 1991, AS “LA TRINIDAD DAY” IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, the 19th day of March of every year since time immemorial and by tradition has been marked as the patronal town fiesta in the Municipality of La Trinidad, Province of Benguet in honor of St. Joseph;

WHEREAS, to enable the townfolk and residents of the said Municipality to commemorate the event with fitting ceremonies and festivities, it is not appropriate to declare the said date as a special day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, March 19, 1991, as “La Trinidad Day” in La Trinidad, Benguet. The said day, however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) OSCAR M. ORBOS Executive Secretary
