MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 596

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE VISAYAN VILLAGE CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN VISAYAN VILLAGE, TAGUM, DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Visayan Village Central Elementary School under the administration of the Department, of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Visayan Village, Municipality of Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 1398-E, Csd-6524-D equivalent to Lot 6350, Cad. 276, Tagum Cadastre

(VISAYAN VILLAGE CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL)

A parcel of land, Lot No. 1398-E Csd-6524-D equivalent to Lot No. 6350, Cad 276 situated in Barangay Visayan Village, Municipality of Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao.
