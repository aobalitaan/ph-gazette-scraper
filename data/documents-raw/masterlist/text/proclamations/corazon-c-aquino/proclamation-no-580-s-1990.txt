MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 580

DECLARING THE YEAR 1991 AS “THE CENTENNIAL OF THE PUBLICATION OF DR. JOSE RIZAL’S EL FILIBUSTERISMO” AND CREATING A COMMITTEE FOR THE PURPOSE

WHEREAS, El Filibusterismo , one of Dr. Jose Rizal’s immortal novels dedicated to the martyrdom of the three Filipino priests, Fathers Mariano Gomez, Jose Burgos and Jacinto Zamora, came out of the press in Ghent, Belgium on September 18, 1891;

WHEREAS, El Filibusterismo exposed the malpractices of the Spanish colonial administration in the Philippines and advocated the separation of the Philippines from Spain;

WHEREAS, the year 1891 formed part of the period of awakening of the Filipinos’ awareness as a nation;

WHEREAS, it is noteworthy to instill in the hearts and minds of our citizenry, most especially the youth, the relevance of Rizal’s ideals as embodied in the novel;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the year 1991 as the centenary of the publication of El Filibusterismo .
