MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 754

DECLARING THURSDAY, JULY 11, 1991, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VALENZUELA, METRO MANILA

WHEREAS, Thursday, July 11, 1991, marks the 31st Charter Anniversary of the Municipality of Valenzuela, Metro Manila;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the residents of the Municipality of Valenzuela be given full opportunity to celebrate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 27, Chapter 7, Book I, of the Revised Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Thursday, July 11, 1991, as a special day in the Municipality of Valenzuela, Metro Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be afixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) MAGDANGAL B. ELMA Acting Executive Secretary
