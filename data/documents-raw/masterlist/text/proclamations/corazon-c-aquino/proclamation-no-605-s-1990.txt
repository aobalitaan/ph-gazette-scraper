PROCLAMATION NO. 605

ESTABLISHING AS TIPULU-AN MAU-IT RIVER WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE FOR PURPOSES OF PROTECTING, MAINTAINING, OR IMPROVING THE WATER YIELD AND PROVIDING RESTRAINING MECHANISM FOR INAPPROPRIATE FOREST EXPLOITATION AND DISRUPTIVE LAND-USE, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIBALOM, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, ISLAND OF PANAY, PHILIPPINES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and pursuant to section 18 of Presidential Decree No. 705, otherwise known as the Revised Forestry Code of the Philippines, as amended, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and set aside as Tipulu-an Mau-it River Watershed Forest Reserve, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to the operations of previous proclamation reserving any portion thereof, for specific purposes, the parcel of land of the public domain located in the Municipality of Sibalom, Province of Antique, Island of Panay, Philippines, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:
