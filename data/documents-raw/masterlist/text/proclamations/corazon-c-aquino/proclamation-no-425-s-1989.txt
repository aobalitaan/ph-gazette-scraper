MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 425

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE REHABILITATION AND PLACEMENT FOUNDATION FOR THE DISABLED, INC. UP TO JUNE 30, 1990

WHEREAS, the authority of the Rehabilitation and Placement Foundation for the Disabled, Inc. (RPFD) to conduct a national fund campaign was last extended under Proclamation No. 323 dated 12 September 1988, covering the period ending 30 June 1989;

WHEREAS, further extension of RPFD’s fund drive will enable it to expand its programs and projects for the disadvantaged and physically handicapped;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national fund campaign period of the Rehabilitation and Placement Foundation for the Disabled, Inc., up to June 30, 1990.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
