MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 127

ESTABLISHING AS WATERSHED FOREST RESERVATIONS FOR THE PURPOSE OF PROTECTING, MAINTAINING, OR IMPROVING ITS WATER YIELD AND PROVIDING A RESTRAINING MECHANISM FOR INAPPROPRIATE FOREST EXPLOITATION AND LAND-USE, THE PARCELS OF LAND AND IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF LANTAPAN, VALENCIA, PANGANTUKAN AND MARAMAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, PHILIPPINES.

Upon recommendation of the Minister (now Secretary) of Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 1 of Republic Act No. 3092, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement and set aside as Watershed Forest Reserve, subject to private rights if there be any, and to the operation of previous proclamations reserving any portion thereof for specific purposes, two (2) parcels of the public domain located in the Municipalities of Lantapan, Valencia, Pangantukan and Maramag, Province of Bukidnon, more particularly described as follows:
