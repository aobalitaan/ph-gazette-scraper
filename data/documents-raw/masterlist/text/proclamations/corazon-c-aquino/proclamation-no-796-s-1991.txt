MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 796

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 18, 1991, “AS MUNICIPALITY OF SALUG DAY” IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SALUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 18, 1991 marks the 32nd Foundation Anniversary of the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte;

WHEREAS, the residents of the Municipality of Salug desire to celebrate the event with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 18, 1991, as “Municipality of Salug Day” in the Municipality of Salug, Province of Zamboanga del Norte. The day, however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
