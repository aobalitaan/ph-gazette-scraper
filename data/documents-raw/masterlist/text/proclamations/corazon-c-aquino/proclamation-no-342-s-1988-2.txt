MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 342

DECLARING THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 24, 1988, AS “DUMAGUETE CITY DAY”.

WHEREAS, Thursday, November 24, 1988, marks the 40th Charter Anniversary of Dumaguete City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Dumaguete City desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, November 24, 1988, as “Dumaguete City Day”. The day, however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 10th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
