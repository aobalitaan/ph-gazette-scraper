MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 521

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM FEBRUARY 5 TO 11, 1990, AS OFFICE MACHINE WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine Office Machine Distributors Association (POMDA), is an industry-oriented organization composed of prestigious distributors of well-known office machines which seeks relevance with the needs and anticipates the requirements of the business and government sectors;

WHEREAS, POMDA has been instrumental in promoting modern business methods relevant to the country’s present development progress; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary to focus national attention on the important role of modern office equipment as tools of economic progress and development.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from February 5 to 11, 1990, as Office Machine Week under the auspices of the Philippine Office Machine Distributors Association, Inc.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of February in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
