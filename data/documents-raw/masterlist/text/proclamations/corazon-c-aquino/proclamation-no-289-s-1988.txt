MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 289

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 27, DATED SEPTEMBER 4, 1986, BY TRANSFERRING THE “ALAY LAKAD PARA SA KABATAAN DAY” TO THE FIRST SUNDAY OF SEPTEMBER OF EVERY YEAR.

Proclamation No. 27, dated September 4, 1986, is hereby amended by transferring the “Alay Lakad Para sa Kabataan Day” to the first Sunday of September instead of the second Sunday of September of every year.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 18th day of JULY in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
