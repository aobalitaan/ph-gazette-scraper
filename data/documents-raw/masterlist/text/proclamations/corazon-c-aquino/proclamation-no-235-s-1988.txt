MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 235

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE FOUNDATION FOR YOUTH DEVELOPMENT IN THE PHILIPPINES, INC., TO APRIL 15, 1989.

WHEREAS, the Foundation for Youth Development in the Philippines, Inc., (FYDP) was authorized under Proclamation No. 98, dated April 14, 1987, to conduct a national educational and fund campaign for a period of one (1) year from April 14, 1987;

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs more time for the conduct of its drive to attain its fund goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national educational and fund campaign of the Foundation for Youth Development in the Philippines, Inc., until April 15, 1989.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
