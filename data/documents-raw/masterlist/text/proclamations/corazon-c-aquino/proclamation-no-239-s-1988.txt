MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 239

SUSPENDING THE EFFECTIVITY OF PROCLAMATION NO. 202, DATED DECEMBER 21, 1987, EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 15, DATED APRIL 27, 1922, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE BUSOL FOREST RESERVATION, A CERTAIN PARCEL OR PARCELS OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN OR PORTIONS THEREOF SITUATED IN PACDAL, BAGUIO CITY.

I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby suspend the effectivity of Proclamation No. 202, dated December 21, 1987, excluding from the operation of Proclamation No. 15, dated April 27, 1922, which established the Busol Forest Reservation, a certain parcel or parcels of land embraced therein or portions thereof situated in Pacdal, Baguio City.

This Proclamation shall take effect immediately and until further orders from this Office.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
