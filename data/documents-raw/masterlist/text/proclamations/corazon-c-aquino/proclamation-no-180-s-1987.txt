MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 180

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL AND MUNICIPAL SITES PURPOSES OF THE OPOL NATIONAL SECONDARY TECHNICAL SCHOOL AND OPOL MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF PONTACAN, MUNICIPALITY OF OPOL, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and reserve for school and municipal sites purposes of the Opol National Secondary Technical School and Opol Municipal Government, under the administration of the Director of Secondary Education and the Opol Municipal Government, respectively, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain, situated in the Barrio of Pontacan, Municipality of Opol, Province of Misamis Oriental, Island of Mindanao, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
