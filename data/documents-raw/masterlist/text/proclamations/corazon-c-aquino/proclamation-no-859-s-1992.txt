MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 859

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 26, 1992, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN ZAMBOANGA CITY

WHEREAS, Wednesday, February 26, 1992, marks the 55th Foundation Anniversary of Zamboanga City;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of Zamboanga City be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, February 26, 1992, as a special day in Zamboanga City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
