MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 494

GRANTING THE TRANSFER OF OWNERSHIP TO THE FOOD TERMINAL INCORPORATED (FORMERLY THE GREATER MANILA TERMINAL FOOD MARKET) CERTAIN PARCELS OF THE LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUIG, METRO MANILA.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby grant the transfer of ownership to the Food Terminal Incorporated (formerly the Greater Manila Terminal Food Market) of the parcels of land reserved for its use under Proclamation No. 347, series of 1963, as amended by Proclamation No. 467, series of 1968, which are more particularly described as follows:

A PARCEL OF LAND (Swo-13-000540), portion of Parcel 3, Psu-2031); situated in the Barrio of Bicutan, Municipality of Taguig, Province of Rizal. Bounded on the SE., along line 1-2 by Lot 10202, Case 17, MCadm-590-D (Lot 1, Swo-13-000258, Proclamation No. 2476); along lines 2-3-4-5-6-7 by Lot 10203, Case 17, MCadm-590-D (Lot 2, Swo-13-000258, Proclamation No. 2476); on the SW., along lines 7-8 by Psu-7740 Amd. (now Cad-290, Parañaque Cadastre); along lines 8-9-10 by Psu-69134 Amd. (now Cad-299, Parañaque Cadastre); along line 10-11 by Lot 2, Psu-152410 (now Cad-299, Parañaque Cadastre); along lines 11-12-13 by Lot 1, Psu-152410 (now Cad-299, Parañaque Cadastre); on the W., along line 13-14 by Manila Railways Company; on the NW., along lines 14-15-16-17 by Manila Technical Institute, Proclamation No. 1160 (Parcel 3, Psu-2031-portion); on the N., along lines 17-18-19-20-21-22-23-24-25 by Veterans Federation of the Philippines-Proclamation No. 192 (Parcel 3, Psu-2031-portion); and on the NE., along lines 25-26-27-28-1 by Lot 10202, Case 17, MCadm-590-D (Lot 1, Swo-13-000258, Proclamation No. 2476). Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 43-01’W., 3,398.93 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 1, MCadm-590-D; thence

Containing an area of ONE MILLION ONE HUNDRED NINETY NINE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED THIRTY SEVEN (1,199,237) SQUARE METERS.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows: Point 1 by BBM No. 35; Point 2 by BBM No. 36; Point 7 by BBM No. 8-A; Points 8 and 9 by U.S. Conc. Mons.; Points 10, 11, and 13 by Old Pls; Point 12 by Old Conc. Mon; Points 14, 15, and 16 by G.S.; Points 25, 26, 27 and 28 by Old P.S.; and the rest by G.S. marked on Conc. Posts. Surveyed on August 15-18; August 22 and 23, 1989, and was approved on September 19, 1989.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President:

(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
