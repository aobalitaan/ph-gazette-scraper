MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 777

GRANTING THE GIRL SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES A NEW PERIOD TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN

WHEREAS, the leadership of the Girl Scouts of the Philippines (GSP) has lined up its “Corporate Goals for the Triennium 1991-1994” in the furtherance of its objectives and purposes;

WHEREAS, the said organization, being a non-stock and non-profit organization, needs the support of the public and private sectors to enable it to attain its corporate goals for the said period;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby grant a new period from August 1, 1991 to July 31, 1992 to conduct a national educational fund campaign.

For this purpose, all donations bequests and gifts received by the Girl Scouts of the Philippines for the implementation of its programs and activities shall be free from all forms of taxes and shall be deductible in full from the income of the donor for purposes of computing his income tax liability, subject to the pertinent provisions of Sections 94 (a) (3) and (b) (2) and 29 (h) (2) (C) (i) of the National Internal Revenue Code, as amended.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
