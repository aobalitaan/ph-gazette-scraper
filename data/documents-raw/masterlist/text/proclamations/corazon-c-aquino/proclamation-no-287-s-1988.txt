MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 287

DECLARING THE FOURTH WEEK OF SEPTEMBER OF EVERY YEAR AS “LAW AND JUSTICE WEEK”.

WHEREAS, the Department of Justice will be celebrating its 90th anniversary on September 26, 1988; and

WHEREAS, the occasion will serve as a fitting reaffirmation and reminder of the Government’s commitment to secure a regime of law and justice for our people;

NOW, THEREFORE I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the fourth week of September of every year as “Law and Justice Week” under the auspices of the Department of Justice.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
