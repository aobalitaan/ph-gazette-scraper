MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 338-B

FURTHER AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 338 DATED OCTOBER 26, 1988, AS AMENDED, DECLARING THE EXISTENCE OF A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN CERTAIN AREAS OF THE COUNTRY.

Proclamation No. 338 dated October 26, 1988, is hereby further amended to include Regions II, VI and XI, which were also heavily hit by typhoon “Unsang,” among the regions therein specified where a state of public calamity exists.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 31th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
