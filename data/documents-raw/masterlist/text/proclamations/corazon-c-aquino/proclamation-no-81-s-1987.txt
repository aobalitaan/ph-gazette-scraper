MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 81

DECLARING MONDAY, MARCH 2, 1987, AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER AS “SENATOR TOMAS CONFESSOR’S DAY” IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOILO.

WHEREAS , Monday, March 2, 1987, is the birth anniversary of the late Senator Tomas Confessor, a leader of the resistance movement in Panay and Romblon; and

WHEREAS , the people of Iloilo should be given the opportunity to celebrate the event with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, March 2, 1987 and every year thereafter, as “Sen. Tomas Confessor’s Day” in the Province of Iloilo. The day, however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary
