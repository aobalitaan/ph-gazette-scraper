MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 89

AUTHORIZING THE LEAGUE OF WOMEN VOTERS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE FUND RAISING CAMPAIGN FROM MARCH 1 TO JULY 15, 1987

WHEREAS , the League of Women Voters of the Philippines, a non-partisan organization, was founded on July 15, 1937 as a result of the approval, in a Plebiscite held on April 30, 1937, of the grant of woman suffrage;

WHEREAS , the League of Women Voters of the Philippines needs funds for the celebration of its Golden Jubilee on July 15, 1987;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the League of Women Voters of the Philippines to conduct a nationwide fund raising campaign from March 1 to July 15, 1987. I urge everyone to give generously, consistent with their means, and support actively this campaign.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 11th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
