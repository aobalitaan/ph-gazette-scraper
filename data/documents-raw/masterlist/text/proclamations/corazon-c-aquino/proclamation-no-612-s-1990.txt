MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 612

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE STA. ANA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY 28, POBLACION, CITY OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Sta. Ana National High School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay 28, Poblacion, City of Davao, Island of Mindanao and more particularly described as follows:

Lot 2, Ccs-11-000472-D Identical to Lot 10217, Cad-102 (STA. ANA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL)

A parcel of land, Lot 2, Ccs-11-000472-D, identical to Lot 10217, Cad-102, Davao Cadastre, situated in Barangay 28, Poblacion, City of Davao, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the North along line 1-2 by Lot 3, Ccs-11-000472-D; on the East along lines 2-3-4 by a Road (Gov. Sales St.); on the South along line 4-5 by Lot 1, Ccs-11-000472-D and on the West along line 5-1 by a Road (Damaso Suazo St.). Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 57 deg. 12’E., 1124.00 meters from Mon. No. 19, Davao Townsite,

105.01 meters to point 2;

82.19 meters to point 3;

0.58 meters to point 4;

105.00 meters to point 5;

78.92 meters to point of

beginning, containing an area of EIGHT THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED EIGHTY EIGHT (8,488) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows: points 1, 2, 4 and 5 by “PS” cyl. conc. mons. 15 x 40 cms. and point 3 is an old point. Bearings true; date of original survey February 1916-May 1920, and that of the subdivision survey on October 27, 1988 by Geodetic Engineer Agustin M. Vedua and was approved on November 17, 1988.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of July in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
