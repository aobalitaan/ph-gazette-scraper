MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 190

DECLARING AS CIVIL RESERVATION FOR MUNICIPAL SITE OF NAGTIPUNAN, QUIRINO, A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN COVERING 250 HECTARES, SITUATED IN BARANGAY PONGGO OF SAID MUNICIPALITY

Upon the recommendation of the Director of the Bureau of Forest Development, with the concurrence of the Minister of Natural Resources (now Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources), and pursuant to Section 18 of Presidential Decree 705, otherwise known as the Revised Forestry Code of the Philippines, as amended, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare as civil reservation for municipal site a certain parcel of land of the public domain located in Barangay Ponggo, Municipality of Nagtipunan, Province of Quirino, as shown and described in BFD Map CR-376, which is described as follows:

Beginning at a point designated as corner “1” which is S 63°20’W, approximately 1,350 meters from the junction of Tangliao and Magagat-gegat Creek;

Thence, following Magagat-gegat Creek upstream generally NW direction, about 250 meters to corner 2; Thence, following Magagat-gegat Creek upstream, generally NW direction about 1,300 meters to corner 3; Thence, Due East, about 500 meters to corner 4; Thence, N 58°00’E, about 1,475 meters to corner 5; Thence, N 78°00’E, about 425 meters to corner 6; Thence, S 67°00’E, about 275 meters to corner 7; Thence, N 72°00’E, about 700 meters to corner 8; Thence, Due South, about 565 meters to corner 9; Thence, S 16°00’E, about 150 meters to corner 10; Thence, S 25°00’E, about 135 meters to corner 11; Thence, N 40°00’W, about 80 meters to corner 12; Thence, N 84°00’W, about 90 meters to corner 13; Thence, S 25°00’W, about 180 meters to corner 14; Thence, N 83°00’W, about 120 meters to corner 15; Thence, N 72°00’W, about 100 meters to corner 16; Thence, N 65°00’W, about 150 meters to corner 17; Thence, N 46°00’W, about 175 meters to corner 18; Thence, S 47°00’W, about 150 meters to corner 19; Thence, N 65°00’W, about 180 meters to corner 20; Thence, S 45°00’W, about 120 meters to corner 21; Thence, S 26°00’W, about 150 meters to corner 22; Thence, S 47°00’W, about 300 meters to corner 23; Thence, N 85°00’W, about 130 meters to corner 24; Thence, S 21°00’W, about 260 meters to corner 25; Thence, S 33°00’W, about 240 meters to point of beginning, containing an area of 250 hectares, more or less, subject to private rights, if any there be and to the following conditions:

1. The administration and management of the area covered by the reservation shall be jointly under the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and the Department of Local Government. For this purpose, a five-year development plan shall be submitted to the Office of the President by the Municipal Council of Nagtipunan, Quirino within one year from approval hereof, furnishing the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and the Forest Management Bureau copies thereof.

2. The extraction and utilization of timber and other forest products remaining in the area shall be subject to forest and Internal Revenue Laws and Regulations.

3. In the event that the area is no longer needed for the purpose stated, or its development has already been abandoned for a period of two (2) years, this proclamation is automatically withdrawn, revoked or cancelled and the subject area regain its former status as part and parcel of the public domain.

The northern portion of the subject area containing about forty nine (49) hectares, which is within the Manglad Forest Reserve, established under Proclamation No. 124, dated November 25, 1966, is automatically excluded from the operation of said proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 24th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
