MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 739

DECLARING ADDITIONAL AREAS UNDER A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN VIEW OF THE CONTINUING AND THREATENING ERUPTIONS OF MT. PINATUBO.

WHEREAS, the National Disaster Coordinating Council recommends that the Municipalities of Cabangan, Castillejos, and San Felipe of Zambales Province and the Municipality of San Fernando of Pampanga Province, including Dinalupihan, Bataan, be also declared under a state of public calamity in view of the continuing and threatening eruptions of Mt. Pinatubo;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare that a state of public calamity exists in the municipalities of the provinces hereunder indicated:

All departments and other government agencies concerned are hereby directed to implement and execute relief and rehabilitation work in accordance with existing operational plans and directives and orders issued in connection with calamities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) MARIANO SARMIENTO II. Deputy Executive Secretary
