MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 885

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE UNITED WAY PHILIPPINES, INC., UP TO MARCH 31, 1993

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 717, dated April 22, 1991, extended the authority of the United Way Philippines, Inc. to conduct a national fund campaign up to March 31, 1992;

WHEREAS, the United Way Philippines, Inc., needs additional time to raise more funds for its laudable objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national fund campaign period of the United Way Philippines, Inc., up to March 31, 1993;

For this purpose, all donations, bequests and gifts received by the United Way Philippines, Inc. for the implementation of its programs and activities shall be free from all forms of taxes and shall be deductible in full from the income of the donor for purposes of computing his income tax liability, subject to the pertinent provisions of Sections 94 (a) (3) and (b) (2) and 29 (h) (2) (C) (i) of the National Internal Revenue Code, as amended.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of April in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
