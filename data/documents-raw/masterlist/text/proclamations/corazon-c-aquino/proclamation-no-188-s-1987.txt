MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 188

DECLARING TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 24, 1987, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN DUMAGUETE CITY.

WHEREAS, the 24th of November marks the founding of the City of Dumaguete 39 years ago; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Dumaguete City be afforded the time to celebrate the memorable occasion;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the 24th of November, 1987, as a special day in the City of Dumaguete.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
