MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 338-A

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 338 DATED OCTOBER 26, 1988 DECLARING THE EXISTENCE OF A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN CERTAIN AREAS OF THE COUNTRY.

Proclamation No. 338 dated October 26, 1988, is hereby amended to include Region I which also suffered damages to crops, livestock and property caused by typhoon “Unsang”, as one of the areas where a state of public calamity exists.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 26th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
