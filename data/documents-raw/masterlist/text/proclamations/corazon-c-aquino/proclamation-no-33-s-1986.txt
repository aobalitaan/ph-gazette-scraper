M A L A C A Ñ A N G MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 33

DECLARING SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 27, 1986, AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS AND THE CITIES OF LIPA AND BATANGAS.

In commemoration of the 121st birth anniversary of the hero and patriot, General Miguel C. Malvar, the last Filipino general to surrender to the Americans during the Filipino-American War, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, September 27, 1986, as a non-working special public holiday in the Province of Batangas and the Cities of Lipa and Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-six.

(SGD) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary
