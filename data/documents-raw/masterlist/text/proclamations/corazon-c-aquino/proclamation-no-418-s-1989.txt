MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 418

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 365, DATED JANUARY 27, 1989, BY DECLARING TUESDAY, JULY 18, 1989, AS “PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO DAY”

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 365, dated January 27, 1989, by declaring Tuesday, July 18, 1989, instead of Friday, July 14, 1989, as “Province of South Cotabato Day”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
