MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 680

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE MALTANA BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN MALTANA, TAMPAKAN, SOUTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Maltana Barangay High School, under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Maltana, Municipality of Tampakan, Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao and more particularly described as follows:

Lot M-27-A, Csd-11-009031, identical to Lot 3324, Pls 246-D-2 (Maltana Barangay High School)
