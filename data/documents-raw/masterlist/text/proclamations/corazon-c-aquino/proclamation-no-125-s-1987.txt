MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 125

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE MERCEDES SCHOOL OF FISHERIES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF SAN ROQUE, MUNICIPALITY OF MERCEDES, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes, under the administration of the Minister of Education and Culture, subject to private right, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the barrio of San Roque, municipality of Mercedes, Province of Camarines Norte, Island of Luzon, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:
