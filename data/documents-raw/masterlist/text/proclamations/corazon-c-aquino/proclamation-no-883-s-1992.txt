MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 883

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE MAGUGPO PILOT CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARANGAY OF MAGUGPO, MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Magugpo Pilot Central Elementary School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Barangay of Magugpo, Municipality of Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows, to wit:

A PARCEL OF LAND, Lot 501-C, Csd-11-010394-D, equivalent to Lot 14527, Cad-276, Tagum Cadastre situated in the Barangay of Magugpo, Municipality of Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao.
