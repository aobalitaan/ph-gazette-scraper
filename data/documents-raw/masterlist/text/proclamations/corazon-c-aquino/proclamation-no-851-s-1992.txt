MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 851

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM FEBRUARY 3 TO 9, 1992, AS OFFICE MACHINE INDUSTRY WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine Office Machine Distributors Association (POMDA) is an industry-oriented organization composed of distributors of well-known office machines which seeks relevance with the needs and anticipates the requirements of the business and government sectors;

WHEREAS, POMDA has been instrumental in promoting modern business methods relevant to the country’s present development progress;

WHEREAS, it is necessary to focus national attention on the important role of modern office equipment as tools of economic progress and development.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from February 3 to 9, 1992, as Office Machine Industry Week under the auspices of the Philippine Office Machine Distributors Association, Inc.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
