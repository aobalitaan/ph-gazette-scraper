MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 90

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 122, DATED MARCH 25, 1966, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE SARA-SAN DIONISIO FOREST RESERVE BY EXCLUDING THEREFROM A 78-HECTARE PARCEL OF LAND AND DECLARING THE SAME ALIENABLE AND DISPOSABLE FOR AGRICULTURAL PURPOSES.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 122, dated November 25, 1966, which established the Sara-San Dionisio Forest Reserve, and declare as alienable or disposable for agricultural purposes that parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Municipality of San Dionisio, Province of Iloilo, Island of Panay, more particularly described as follows:

From corner marked 1 in the attached map of LC Project No. 51-B of San Dionisio, Iloilo, which is S82º 11′ E, 4,175.60 meters from PBM No. 15;

Containing an approximate area of SEVENTY EIGHT (78) HECTARES, subject to the following conditions:

1. A strip of 20 meters wide on both sides from the highest bank of any stream is reserved as forest land to be maintained under forest cover at all times.

2. Naturally grown trees shall no longer be cut or harvested but shall instead be maintained and preserved as part of the permanent vegetation or to be reserved as greenbelt pursuant to LOI 1917 and its implementing guidelines.

3. The Regional Directors of the Bureau of Forest Development and Lands, in coordination with appropriate agencies of Government, shall identify and delineate areas to be established, developed and maintained as forest or tree parks for the barangays in the area herein excluded as alienable or disposable pursuant to LOI No. 1313 and its implementing rules and regulations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary
