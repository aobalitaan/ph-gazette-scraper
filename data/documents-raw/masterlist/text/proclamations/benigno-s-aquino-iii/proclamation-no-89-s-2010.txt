MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 89

DECLARING THURSDAY, DECEMBER 30, 2010, AS A SPECIAL (NON- WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IBAAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on December 30, 2010, the people of the Municipality of Ibaan will be celebrating their traditional town fiesta;

WHEREAS, December 30 has always been declared as a regular holiday as it commemorates the death anniversary of our National Hero, Dr. Jose Rizal;

WHEREAS , Proclamation 1841, s. 2009, moved the celebration of the death anniversary of Dr. Jose Rizal from December 30, 2010 to December 27, 2010, making the former a working holiday;

WHEREAS, the people of the Municipality of Ibaan should be given ample time to celebrate their town fiesta through appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by the power of His Excellency , BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, December 30, 2010, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ibaan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila this 29 th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
