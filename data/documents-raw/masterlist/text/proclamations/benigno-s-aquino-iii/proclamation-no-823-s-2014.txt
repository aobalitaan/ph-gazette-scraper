MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 823

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, Friday, 15 August 2014, marks the commemoration of the Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Bulacan;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Province of Bulacan the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 15 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 15 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
