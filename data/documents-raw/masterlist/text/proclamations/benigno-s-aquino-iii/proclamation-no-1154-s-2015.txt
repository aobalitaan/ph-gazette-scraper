MALACAÑANG PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1154

DECLARING TUESDAY, 1 DECEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADRE GARCIA, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 1 December 2015, marks the 66 th Foundation Day of the Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 1 December 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
