MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1185

DECLARING TUESDAY, 12 JANUARY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENCIA, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 12 January 2016, marks the Foundation Day of the City of Valencia, Bukidnon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Valencia, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 12 January 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Valencia, Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of December in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
