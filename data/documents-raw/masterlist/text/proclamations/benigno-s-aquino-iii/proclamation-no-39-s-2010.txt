MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 39

DECLARING TUESDAY, OCTOBER 5, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Tuesday, October 5, 2010 marks the celebration of “Araw ng Sta. Cruz” in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Davao del Sur;

WHEREAS, in order to give the constituents of the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Davao del Sur, the opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare October 5, 2010 as special non-working day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, October 5, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
