MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 871

DECLARING MONDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BACOOR

WHEREAS, the City of Bacoor marks the celebration of Araw ng Bacoor on Monday, 29 September 2014;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Bacoor the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 29 September 2014, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 29 September 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bacoor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
