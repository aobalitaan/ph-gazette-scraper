MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1074

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 JULY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON­-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley marks the celebration of the “Araw ng Nabunturan” on Thursday, 23 July 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 23 July 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
