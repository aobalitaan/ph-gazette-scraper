MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 997

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 MAY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TAYABAS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, the celebration of the Mayohan Festival of the City of Tayabas, Quezon will be held on Friday, 15 May 2015;

WHERAS, Mayohan sa Tayabas Festival honors the glory as well as blesses the travails and sacrifices of the past;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tayabas, Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 15 May 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tayabas, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
