MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 274

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 2 NOVEMBER 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 2 November 2011, the Province of Misamis Occidental marks the commemoration of its Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Misamis Occidental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 2 November 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
