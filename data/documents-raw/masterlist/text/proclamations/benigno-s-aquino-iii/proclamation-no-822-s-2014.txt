MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 822

DECLARING FRIDAY, 1 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, Friday, 1 August 2014, marks the 66 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Compostela, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 1 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOAJR. Executive Secretary
