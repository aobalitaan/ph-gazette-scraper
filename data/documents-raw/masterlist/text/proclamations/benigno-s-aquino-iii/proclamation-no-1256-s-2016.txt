MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1256

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 MAY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS , Thursday, 26 May 2016, marks the celebration of the Charter Day of the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, 26 May 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
