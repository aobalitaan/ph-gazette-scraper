MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 969

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, Thursday, 26 February 2015, marks the commemoration of the annual town anniversary of the Municipality of Buug, Zamboanga Sibugay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Buug, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 26 February 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Buug, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
