MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1087

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 AUGUST 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, the City of Davao celebrates the “Kadayawan” Festival every third week of August;

WHEREAS, on Monday, 24 August 2015, the City of Davao marks its “Araw ng Pasasalamat” as part of the weeklong celebration of the “Kadayawan” Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 24 August 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
