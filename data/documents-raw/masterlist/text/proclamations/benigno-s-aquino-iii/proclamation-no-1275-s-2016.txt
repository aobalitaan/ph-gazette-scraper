PROCLAMATION NO. 1275

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING LOCATED AT BARANGAY 1 SAN FRANCISCO, SAN NICOLAS, ILOCOS NORTE, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748
