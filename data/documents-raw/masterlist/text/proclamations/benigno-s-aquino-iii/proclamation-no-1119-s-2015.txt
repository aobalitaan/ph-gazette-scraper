PROCLAMATION NO. 1119

DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND, SITUATED AT BARANGAY LA MESA, CALAMBA CITY, LAGUNA, AS INCLUSIONS TO THE EXISTING LIGHT INDUSTRY AND SCIENCE PARK ll-SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate several parcels of land, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations and Resolution No. 12-482 (s. 2012) of the PEZA Board of Directors, located at Barangay La Mesa, Calamba City, Laguna, as inclusions to the existing Light Industry And Science Park ll-Special Economic Zone, with an aggregate area of Twenty-Four Thousand One Hundred Forty-Four (24,144) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following consolidated technical descriptions:
