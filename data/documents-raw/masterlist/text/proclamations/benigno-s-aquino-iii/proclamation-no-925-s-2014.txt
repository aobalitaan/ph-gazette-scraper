MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 925

DECLARING MONDAY, 8 DECEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Agoo, La Union marks the celebration of its 436 th Foundation Day anniversary on Monday, 8 December 2014;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Agoo, La Union be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 8 December 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
