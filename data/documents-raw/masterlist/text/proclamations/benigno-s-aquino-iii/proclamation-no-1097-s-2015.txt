MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1097

DECLARING FRIDAY, 11 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUPI, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, Friday, 11 September 2015, marks the 61st Foundation Day and 6th Agten Tufi Festival of the Municipality of Tupi, South Cotabato;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Tupi, Province of South Cotabato be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 11 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tupi, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the Philippines:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
