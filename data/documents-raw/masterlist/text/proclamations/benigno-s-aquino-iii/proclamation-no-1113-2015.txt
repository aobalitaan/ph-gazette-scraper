MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1113

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, Friday, 25 September 2015, marks the 155th Pueblo Day (Charter Day) of the Municipality of Naval, Province of Biliran;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Naval, Biliran be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 25 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Naval, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
