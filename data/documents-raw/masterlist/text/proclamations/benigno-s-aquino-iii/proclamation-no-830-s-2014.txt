MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 830

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on Friday, 22 August 2014, the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte marks the commemoration of its 63 rd Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte, the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 22 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 22 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Execute Secretary
