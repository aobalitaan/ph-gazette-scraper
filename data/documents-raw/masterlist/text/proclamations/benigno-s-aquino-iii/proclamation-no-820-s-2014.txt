PROCLAMATION NO. 820

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND, LOCATED AT BARANGAY TAMBLER, GENERAL SANTOS CITY, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 , AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate several parcels of land, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 13-181 (s. 2013), of the PEZA Board of Directors, as a Special Economic Zone, located at Barangay Tambler, General Santos City, with an aggregate area of Four Hundred Sixty-Seven Thousand Seven Hundred Fifteen (467,715) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following consolidated technical description:
