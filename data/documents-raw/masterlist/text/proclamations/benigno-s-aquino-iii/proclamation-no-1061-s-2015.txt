MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1061

DECLARING THURSDAY, 2 JULY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, the City of Pasig marks the celebration of its 442 nd anniversary on Thursday, 2 July 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 2 July 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
