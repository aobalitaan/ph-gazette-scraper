MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1141

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING, LOCATED AT AYALA AVENUE, BARANGAY BEL-AIR, MAKATI CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011) and 15-240 (s. 2015) and Resolution No. 15-118 (s. 2015) of the PEZA Board of Directors, as an Information Technology (IT) Center, to be known as the Tower 6789, with a gross floor area of Fifty-Nine Thousand Eight Hundred Seven (59,807) square meters, which stands on two parcels of land, located at Ayala Avenue, Barangay Bel-Air, Makati City, containing an area of Two Thousand Four Hundred (2,400) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:

Lot 15 (LRC) Psd-6132 (TCT No. 006-2011000756)
