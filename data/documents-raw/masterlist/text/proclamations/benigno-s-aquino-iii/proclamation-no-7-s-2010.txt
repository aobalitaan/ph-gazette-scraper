MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 7

DECLARING FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 12, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, QUEZON

WHEREAS, Friday, November 12, 2010, the Municipality of Gumaca, Quezon marks its Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Gumaca, Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, November 12, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gumaca Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
