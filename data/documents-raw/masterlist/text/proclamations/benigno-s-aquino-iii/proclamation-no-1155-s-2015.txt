MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1155

DECLARING SATURDAY, 7 NOVEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUENCA, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Saturday, 7 November 2015, marks the 139th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 7 November 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of November in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
