MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 939

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JANUARY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS, the City of Navotas marks the commemoration of its 109 th Foundation Day anniversary on Friday, 16 January 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Navotas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 16 January 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
