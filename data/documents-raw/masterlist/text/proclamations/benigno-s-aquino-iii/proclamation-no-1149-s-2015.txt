MALACAÑANG PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1149

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on Friday, 13 November 2015, the Municipality of Pantukan, Compostela Valley marks the celebration of its Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pantukan, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 13 November 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pantukan, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
