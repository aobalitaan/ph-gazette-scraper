MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 861

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG

WHEREAS, the City of Tacurong marks its 13 th Cityhood Anniversary on Thursday, 18 September 2014, dubbed as “Talakudong Festival 2014;”

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 18 September 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
