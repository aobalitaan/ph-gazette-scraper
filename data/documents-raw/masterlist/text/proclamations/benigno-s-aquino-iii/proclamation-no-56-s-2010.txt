BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES 
PROCLAft/IATION NO. 56 
DECLARING NOVEMBER 10-13, 2010 AS THE "NATIONAL NEUROLOGY 
DAYS" 
WHEREAS, the Philippine Neurological Association is the lead organization 
concerned with the delivery of neurological care, education and research for the 
promotion of health and well-being of our people; 
WHEREAS, there is a need to Increase awareness and understanding of 
neurological illnesses to enable the citizenry to take active roles in the prevention of 
such illnesses and to avail of accessible and available care for the afflicted ones; 
WHEREAS, a commemoration of the "National Neurology Days" will benefit 
various communities which are in need of neurological care from the simultaneous 
outreach programme to be undertaken nationwide which will provide neurological 
services, conduct of scientific activities and other advocacy programs; 
WHEREAS, it is proper to declare a "National Neurology Days" to help attain 
a sustained and dedicated commitment among the various sectors of society 
towards alleviating the plight of those afflicted with neurological illnesses. 
NOW, THEREFORE, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the 
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare 
November 10-13, 2010 as the "National Neurology Days". 
The Philippine Neurological Association, the Department of Health, the 
Department of Social Welfare and Development, the Department of Education and 
the different non-government sectors are hereby enjoined to effectively collaborate to 
ensure a beneficial and productive commemoration of the event. 
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal 
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. 
Done in this 5th day of 
Thousand and Ten.
