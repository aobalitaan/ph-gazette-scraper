MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 36

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 29, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN RAFAEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 29, 2010, marks the 260 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of San Rafael, Province of Bulacan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San Rafael, Province of Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 29, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Rafael, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21 st day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
