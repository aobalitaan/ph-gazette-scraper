MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 870

DECLARING FRIDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIQUIJOR, PROVINCE OF SIQUIJOR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Siquijor marks the commemoration of its 42 nd year anniversary as the capital town of the Province of Siquijor on Friday, 12 September 2014;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Siquijor, Province of Siquijor the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 12 September 2014, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 12 September 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Siquijor, Province of Siquijor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
