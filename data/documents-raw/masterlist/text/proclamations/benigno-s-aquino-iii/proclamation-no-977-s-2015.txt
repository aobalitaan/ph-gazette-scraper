MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 977

DECLARING SATURDAY, 7 MARCH 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 7 March 2015, the City of Tagum marks the commemoration of its Foundation Day anniversary to be known as the Araw ng Tagum;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagum be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 7 March 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
