MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1156

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Province of Oriental Mindoro marks its 65th Foundation Day on 15 November 2015;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Oriental Mindoro to celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Governor requested that Friday, 13 November 2015, be declared as a special (non-working) day instead of 15 November 2015, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Oriental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 13 November 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
