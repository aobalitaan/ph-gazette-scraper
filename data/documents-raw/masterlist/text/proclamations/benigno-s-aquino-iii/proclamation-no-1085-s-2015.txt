MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1085

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 AUGUST 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISULAN, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Isulan, Province of Sultan Kudarat marks its 58th Foundation Day and the 4th Hamungaya Festival on 30 August 2015;

WHEREAS, in order for the people of the Municipality of Isulan, Sultan Kudarat to participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor has requested that 28 August 2015, instead of 30 August 2015 which falls on a Sunday, be declared as a special non-working day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 28 August 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Isulan, Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
