MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 37

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 29, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACOOR, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 29, 2010, marks the annual celebration of the “Araw ng Bacoor” of the Municipality of Bacoor, Province of Cavite;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Bacoor, Province of Cavite, the opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare September 29, 2010 as special non-working day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 29, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bacoor, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
