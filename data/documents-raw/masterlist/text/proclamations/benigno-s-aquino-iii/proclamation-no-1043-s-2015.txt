MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1043

DECLARING SATURDAY, 20 JUNE 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, Saturday, 20 June 2015 marks the celebration of the Charter Day of the City of Cotabato;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cotabato be given full opportunity to participate in and celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 20 June 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
