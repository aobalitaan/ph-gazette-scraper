MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1270

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING, LOCATED AT NO. 961 JUAN LUNA STREET, BARANGAY 7, TONDO, MANILA, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolutions Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011) and 15-240 (s. 2015), and Resolution No. 15-165 (s. 2015) of the PEZA Board of Directors, as an Information Technology Center, to be known as The Orchard Tower Manila, with a gross floor area of Fifteen Thousand Three Hundred Thirty-Six (15,336) square meters, more or less, which stands on two parcels of land located at No. 961 Juan Luna Street, Barangay 7, Tondo, Manila, with an aggregate area of One Thousand One Hundred Sixty-Five square meters and Eighty decimeters (1,165.80) more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
