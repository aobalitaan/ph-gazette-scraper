MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 992

DECLARING THURSDAY, 30 APRIL 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Thursday, 30 April 2015, marks the celebration of the Tourism and Cultural Day (town fiesta) of the Municipality of Calatagan, Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Calatagan, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 30 April 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calatagan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
