MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1059

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 249 (S. 2000) BY RESERVING TWO PARCELS OF LAND IN BARANGAY LIBERTAD, BUTUAN CITY, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, AS THE BUTUAN CITY UPTOWN GOVERNMENT COMPLEX, AND AUTHORIZING ITS TRANSFER TO THE CITY GOVERNMENT OF BUTUAN

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 249 (s. 2000), by reserving two parcels of land in Barangay Libertad, City of Butuan, Island of Mindanao, as the Butuan City Uptown Government Complex, and authorizing the transfer to the City Government of Butuan the said parcels of land, more particularly described as follows, to wit:

LOT A-2-B-1, Psd-13-022535

A parcel of land (Lot A-2-B-1 of the subdivision plan Psd-13-022535, being a portion of Lot A-2-B, Psd-13-015497), situated in Barangay Libertad, Butuan City.

Bounded on the SE., along 1-2 by Road; on the NE., along line 2-3 by National Highway; on the SE., along line 3-4 by B, Pcs-10-001550; on the SW., along lines 4-5-6 by Lot 394, Cad-84; on the SW., along lines 5-6-7 by Lot 395, Cad-84; of the Subdivision Plan; on the NW., along line 8-9 by Lot A-2-A, Psd-13-015497; on the NW., along line 9-10 by Road; on the NE., along line 10-11 by National Highway; and on the NE., along lines 11-12-13-14-15-16-17 by Lot A-1, Psd-13-001143; lines 17-18-19-1 by Lot A-2-B-2, Psd-13-022535.

Beginning at the point marked “1” on the plan, being N., 22 deg. 31’E 525.51 m. from BLLM No. 17, CAD-84, thence;

beginning. Containing an area of FIFTY THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED TWENTY-SIX (50,826) SQUARE METERS , more or less.

Lot-1, PCS-10-001617

A parcel of land (Lot-1, Pcs-10-001617), situated in Barangay Libertad, Butuan City.

Bounded on the NE., along lines 1-2, 2-3, 3-4 and 4-5 by Lot A-2, Psd-13-001143; on the E., along line 5-6 by Lot 2, Pcs-10-001617; on the SW., along line 6-7 by Lot 2, Pcs-10-001617 and on the NW., along line 7-8 and 8-1 by Lot A-2, Psd 13-001143.

Beginning at point marked “1” on plan, being N.00 deg. 57’W., 419.22 meters from BLLM 13, Cad-84, thence;

beginning. Containing an area of EIGHT THOUSAND AND NINETY (8,090) square meters more or less.

The parcels of land shall be transferred to the City Government of Butuan on the condition that the said parcels of land shall not be used for any purposes other than government offices.

IN WITNESS THEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed hereto.

DONE, in the City Of Manila, this 16th day of June in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
