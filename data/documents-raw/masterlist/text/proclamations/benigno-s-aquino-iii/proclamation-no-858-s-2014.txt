MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 858

DECLARING MONDAY, 8 SEPTEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DIGOS

WHEREAS, Monday, 8 September 2014, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Digos;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Digos the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 8 September 2014, as a special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 8 September 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of August, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
