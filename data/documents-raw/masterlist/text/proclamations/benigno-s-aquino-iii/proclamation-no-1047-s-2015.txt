MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1047

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 JUNE 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 16 June 2015, marks the 65 th founding anniversary of the Municipality of Pamplona, Negros Oriental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pamplona, Negros Oriental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 16 June 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pamplona, Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
