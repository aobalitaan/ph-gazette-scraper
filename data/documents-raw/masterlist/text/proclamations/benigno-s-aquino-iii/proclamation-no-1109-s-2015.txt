MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1109

DECLARING TUESDAY, 1 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JASAAN, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 1 September 2015, marks the creation of the Municipality of Jasaan, Misamis Oriental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Jasaan, Misamis Oriental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 1 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Jasaan, Province of Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
