MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 805

DECLARING TUESDAY, 1 JULY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DIPOLOG

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 1 July 2014, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Dipolog;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dipolog be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 1 July 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Dipolog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
