MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 828

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, 10 August 2014 marks the commemoration of Lorenzo Tañada, Sr. Day by the Municipality of Gumaca, Province of Quezon;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Gumaca, Quezon to celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Sangguniang Bayan has requested that Monday, 11 August 2014 be declared as special non-working day instead of 10 August 2014, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Gumaca, Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 11 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
