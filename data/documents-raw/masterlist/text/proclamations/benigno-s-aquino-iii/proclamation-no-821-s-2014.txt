MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 821

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 JULY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DAPITAN

WHEREAS, Friday, 25 July 2014, the City of Dapitan marks the celebration of the feast of Saint James the Greater and the holding of the Kinabayo Festival;”

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dapitan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III do hereby declare Friday, 25 July 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Dapitan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of July, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
