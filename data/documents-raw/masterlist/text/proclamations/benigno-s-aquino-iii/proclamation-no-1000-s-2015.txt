MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1000

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 MAY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PALAWAN

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections en banc promulgated on 07 April 2015, Resolution No. 15-0284, setting the date for the Recall Elections for Mayor in the City of Puerto Princesa, Palawan, on 08 May 2015;

WHEREAS, there is a need to declare 08 May 2015 as a special non-working holiday to afford the registered voters of the City of Puerto Princesa, Palawan the opportunity to participate fully in the recall elections;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 08 May 2015, as a special (non-working) holiday in the City of Puerto Princesa, Palawan.

IN WITNESS THEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed hereto.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 20th day of April, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
