MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 27

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 16, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province was founded on September 16, 1908;

WHEREAS, Thursday, September 16, 2010 marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province;

WHEREAS, in order to give the constituents of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province, the opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare September 16, 2010 as special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, September 16, 2010 as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
