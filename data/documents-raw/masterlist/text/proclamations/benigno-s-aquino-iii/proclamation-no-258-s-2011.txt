MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 258

DECLARING FRIDAY, 7 OCTOBER 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Friday, 7 October 2011, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Imus, Cavite;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Imus, Cavite, the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 7 October 2011, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 7 October 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
