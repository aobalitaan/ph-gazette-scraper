MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1064

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 JULY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALUNGON, SARANGANI PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 15 July 2015, the Municipality of Malungon, Sarangani Province marks the celebration of its Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Malungon, Sarangani Province be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 15 July 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Malungon, Sarangani Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
