MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 836

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 19 August 2014, marks the anniversary of the signing of the independence as a municipality of the Municipality of Angono, Province of Rizal;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Angono, Rizal the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 19 August 2014, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 19 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Angono, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
