MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 927

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 DECEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, the City of Cotabato marks the celebration of its “Unity Day” on Monday, 08 December 2014;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cotabato be given full opportunity to participate in and celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 08 December 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
