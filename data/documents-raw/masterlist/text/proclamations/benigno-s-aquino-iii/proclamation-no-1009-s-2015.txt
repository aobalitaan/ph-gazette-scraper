MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1009

DECLARING FRIDAY, 8 MAY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAMILING, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, Friday, 8 May 2015, marks the celebration of the annual cultural festivities of the Municipality of Camiling, Tarlac;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Camiling, Tarlac, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 8 May 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Camiling, Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
