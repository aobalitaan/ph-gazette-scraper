MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 907

DECLARING MONDAY, 1 DECEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADRE GARCIA, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Monday, 1 December 2014, marks the 65 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 1 December 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
