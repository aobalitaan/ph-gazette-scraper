MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1253

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 MAY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SUAL, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sual, Pangasinan marks its Foundation Day on Friday, 20 May 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Sual, Pangasinan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 20 May 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sual, Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
