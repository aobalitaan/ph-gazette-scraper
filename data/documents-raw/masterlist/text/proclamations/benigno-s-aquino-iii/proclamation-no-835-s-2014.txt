MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 835

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 JULY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, Friday, 25 July 2014, marks the commemoration of the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Maasin, Iloilo;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Maasin, Iloilo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 25 July 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maasin, Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
