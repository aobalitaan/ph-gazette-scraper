MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1023

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 JUNE 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAGAY

WHEREAS, Thursday, 11 June 2015 marks the 19 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Sagay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sagay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 11 June 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Sagay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
