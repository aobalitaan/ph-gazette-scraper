MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 95

DECLARING THURSDAY, JANUARY 20, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, January 14-22, 2011, marks the celebration of the Tarlac City Festival, with the theme “Tubo ng Tarlac, Tubong Tarlac”;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of Tarlac City the opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, the Office of the City Mayor requested that January 20, 2011, be declared as special non-working day.

NOW THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, January 20, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
