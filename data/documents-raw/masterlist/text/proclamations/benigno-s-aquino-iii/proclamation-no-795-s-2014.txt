MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 795

DECLARING TUESDAY, 15 JULY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING DAY IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION (CAR)

WHEREAS, the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) marks its Foundation Day anniversary on Tuesday, 15 July 2014, with the theme “Boosting the Economic Competitiveness of the Cordilleras for ASEAN 2015;”

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of CAR be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 15 July 2014, as a special (non-working) day in CAR.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
