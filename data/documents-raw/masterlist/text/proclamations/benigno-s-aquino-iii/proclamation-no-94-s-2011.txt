MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 94

DECLARING TUESDAY, JANUARY 18, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN SANTA ROSA CITY, LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Tuesday, January 18, 2011, marks the 219 th Foundation Day anniversary of Santa Rosa City, Laguna;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Santa Rosa City, Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, January 18, 2011, as special (non-working) day in Santa Rosa City, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
