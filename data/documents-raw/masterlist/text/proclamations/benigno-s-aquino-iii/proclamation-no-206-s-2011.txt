MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 206

DECLARING FRIDAY, 5 AUGUST 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CANDELARIA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, Friday, 5 August 2011, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Candelaria, Quezon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Candelaria, Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 5 August 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Candelaria, Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
