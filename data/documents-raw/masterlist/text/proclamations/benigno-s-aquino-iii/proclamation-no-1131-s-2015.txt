MALACAÑANG PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1131

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 OCTOBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CALBAYOG

WHEREAS , Friday, 16 October 2015, marks the 67th Charter Day of the City of Calbayog;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calbayog be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 16 October 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Calbayog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

Bu authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
