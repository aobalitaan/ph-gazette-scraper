MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 942

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JANUARY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BOCAUE, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on Friday, 16 January 2015, the Municipality of Bocaue, Bulacan will mark the commemoration of the heroism of General Eusebio Roque, a native of Bocaue;

WHEREAS, General Eusebio Roque presided over the Katipunan community of Balangay Dimasalang and led the Battle of Kakarong de Sili in 1897;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bocaue, Province of Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 16 January 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bocaue, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
