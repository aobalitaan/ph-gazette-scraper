MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 59

DECLARING SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 25, 2010, AS “ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING DAY”

WHEREAS, the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers of the Philippines, Inc. (IIEE), will hold its 35th Annual National Convention on November 24 to 27, 2010;

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus national attention on the importance of the electrical engineering profession and highlight its role in nation building.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare November 25, 2010 as “electrical Engineering Day”, under the auspices of the Institute of Integrated electrical Engineers of the Philippines, Inc., I call upon all electrical engineers and students to give the observance of the day their full support.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in this 5 th day of November, in the year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Ten.

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
