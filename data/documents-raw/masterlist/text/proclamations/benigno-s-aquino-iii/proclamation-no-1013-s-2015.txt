MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1013

DECLARING FRIDAY, 8 MAY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT ROXAS, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 4869 approved on 8 May 1967 created the Municipality of President Roxas, Cotabato;

WHEREAS, the Municipality of President Roxas, Cotabato marks its foundation day anniversary on Friday, 8 May 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of President Roxas, Cotabato be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Friday, 8 May 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of President Roxas, Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
