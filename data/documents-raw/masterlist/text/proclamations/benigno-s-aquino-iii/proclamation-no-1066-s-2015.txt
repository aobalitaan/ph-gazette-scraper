MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1066

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 1 JULY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALUBIJID, MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 1 July 2015, the Municipality of Alubijid, Misamis Oriental, marks the celebration of its 75 th Anniversary, known as “Araw ng Alubijid’’ ;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Alubijid, Misamis Oriental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 1 July 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Alubijid, Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
