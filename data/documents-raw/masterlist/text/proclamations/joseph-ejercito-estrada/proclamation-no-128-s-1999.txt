MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 128

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAYS CULIS AND SUMALO, MUNICIPALITY OF HERMOSA, PROVINCE OF BATAAN AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as a special economic zone, subject to the provisions of the Republic No. 7916, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 99-103 dated 14 April 1999 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located in Barangay Culis and Sumalo, Municipality of Hermosa, Province of Bataan, henceforth to be known as SUBIC-HERMOSA CYBER CITY , consisting of NINE HUNDRED TWENTY THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED ELEVEN (928,811) square meters , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
