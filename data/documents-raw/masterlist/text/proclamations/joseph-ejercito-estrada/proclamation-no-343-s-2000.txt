MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 343

DECLARATION OF THE 18.00-HECTARE PROPERTY OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES AS THE SITE OF THE GOVERNMENT CENTER IN REGION III AND AUTHORIZATION OF REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT COUNCIL TO ADMINISTER THE PROPERTY FOR ITS PROPER USE IN COORDINATION WITH OTHER NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES BASED IN REGION III

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the 18.00-hectare property of the Republic of the Philippines as the site of the Regional Government Center in Region III and accordingly authorize the Regional Development Council (RDC) to administer the property for its proper use in coordination with government agencies based in Region III, Pampanga. The RDC III is hereby authorized to administer the property to ensure its proper use in coordination with other national government agencies based in Region III.
