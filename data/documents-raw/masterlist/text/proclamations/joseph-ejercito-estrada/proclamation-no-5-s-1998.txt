MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 5

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 10, 1998 AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, August 10, 1998, marks the 100th birth anniversary of Lorenzo M. Tañada, an outstanding Senator and nationalist;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the residents of the Province of Quezon, his birth place should at least be given full opportunity to celebrate the day with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book 1, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, August 10, 1998 as a special day in the Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
