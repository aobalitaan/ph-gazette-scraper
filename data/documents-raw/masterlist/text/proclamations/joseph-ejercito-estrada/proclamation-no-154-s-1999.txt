MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 154

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY STA. MARIA, MUNICIPALITY OF CALAUAG, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes, a certain parcel of land, situated in Barangay Sta. Maria, Municipality of Calauag, Province of Quezon, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

Lot, Ts-350, Calauag, Quezon

(Identical to Lot 6280, Cad-206)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 1, Ts-350, Calauag, Quezon, identical to Lot 6280, Cad-206) situated in Barangay Sta. Maria, Municipality of Calauag, Province of Quezon, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by Lot 4045 (Portion), Cad-206; on the NE., along line 2-3 by Road Lot 152; on the E., along line 3-4 by Lot 135; along line 4-5 by Lot 134; along line 5-6 by Lot 133; on the SE., along line 6-7 by Lot 132; and along line 7-8 by Lot 131; all of Ts-350; and on the SW., along 8-1 by Lot 3997 (Jose dela Costa), Cad-206, Calauag, Cadastre. Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 1, Ts-350, being N. 67 deg. 18’W., 1177.24 m. from BBM No. 38, Calauag Cadastre, thence;

beginning, containing an area of FIVE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED EIGHTY THREE (5,683) SQUARE METERS . All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cms. Bearing true. Date of Survey, October 8-23, 1962 and was approved August 13, 1973 by Florentino Arellano, Jr., Public Land Surveyor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
