MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 193

RESERVING FOR MARKET SITE AND MUNICIPAL HALL SITE OF PADADA A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT LIMONSO, MUNICIPALITY OF PADADA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to Section 17 of Republic Act No. 7160, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserved for Market Site and Municipal Hall Site purposes under the administration of the Municipality of Padada, Province of Davao del Sur, subject to private rights, if any there be a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at Barangay Limonso, Municipality of Padada, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao which is more particularly described as follows:
