MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 235

DECLARING SATURDAY, FEBRUARY 12, 2000, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN

WHEREAS, Saturday, February 12, 2000, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Kidapawan;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the good people of the City of Kidapawan be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Saturday, February 12, 2000, as a special day in the City of Kidapawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31 sT day of January, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
