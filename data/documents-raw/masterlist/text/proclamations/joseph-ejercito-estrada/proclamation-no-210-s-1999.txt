MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 210

DECLARING MONDAY, NOVEMBER 15, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABUCGAYAN, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Cabucgayan will be celebrating its 50 th Foundation Anniversary on Monday, November 15, 1999;

WHEREAS, the residents of the Municipality of Cabucgayan desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this event in their town’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book 1, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, November 15, 1999, as a special day in the Municipality of Alicia, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 Th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
