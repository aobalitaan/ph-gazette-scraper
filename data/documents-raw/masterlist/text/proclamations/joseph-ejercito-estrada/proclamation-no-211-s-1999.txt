MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 211

DECLARING MONDAY, NOVEMBER 15, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VICTORIA, LAGUNA

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Victoria will be celebrating its 50th Foundation Anniversary on Monday, November 15, 1999;

WHEREAS, the residents of the Municipality of Victoria desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this event in their town’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, November 15, 1999, as a special day in the Municipality of Victoria, Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
