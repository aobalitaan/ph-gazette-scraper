MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 341

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF IGUIG CENTRAL SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED IN AJAT, IGUIG, CAGAYAN

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement, and reserve for school site purposes of Iguig Central School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Ajat, Iguig, Cagayan, which is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 2, PSU 2-01-002994

A parcel of land, Lot 2, PSU-2-01-002994, situated in Ajat, Municipality of Iguig, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2 by the National Road, on the S., along line 2-3 by Lot 3, along line 3-4 by Lot 2, both of PSU-2-01-002547, on the SW., along line 4-5 by Mariano Rey, on the NW., along line 5-6 by Apolinaria Maralli, and along line 6-1 by Lot 1 of the subdivision plan.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N 05 deg. 53’E., 767.84 m. from BLLM No. 1, Pls-601-D, thence

213.70 m. to point 2;

49.93 m. to point 3;

108.91 m. to point 4;

208.20 m. to point 5;

73.00 m. to point 6;

110.47 m. to point 1,

which is the point of beginning, containing an area of THIRTY FIVE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED FORTY-EIGHT (35,148) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows: corners 2-3-4 by old PS and the rest by P.S. cyl. conc. mons. 16 x 60 cms. Bearings True. Date of original survey N/A and that the subdivision survey was executed by Geodetic Engineer Maximino S. Tumalinan on July 20, 1984 and was approved on October 5, 1984.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 12 Th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
