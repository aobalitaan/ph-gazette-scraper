MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 374

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE CONSTITUTION ASSOCIATION (PHILCONSA) TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN FOR A PERIOD OF ONE (1) YEAR

WHEREAS, the Philippine Constitution Association (PHILCONSA) for over 30 years has been upholding and protecting the Philippine Constitution. Inspite of its achievements in this area, PHILCONSA does not have a headquarter which it can call its own;

WHEREAS, the PHILCONSA needs the support of the general public and the citizenry in realizing this worthy endeavor;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Philippine Constitution Association (PHILCONSA) to conduct a national fund campaign for a period of one (1) year effective upon the promulgation of this Proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
