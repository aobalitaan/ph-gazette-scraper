MALACAÑANG Manila BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 204

RESERVING AS MODERN SATELLITE GRAINS CENTER SITE OF THE NATIONAL FOOD AUTHORITY A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARRIO LIWAN WEST, MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF KALINGA, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and reserve for Modern Satellite Grains Center Site purposes of the National Food Authority, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barrio Liwan West, Municipality of Rizal, Province of Kalinga, Island of Luzon, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:
