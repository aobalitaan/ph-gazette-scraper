MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 347

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 2, 2000, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF BUTUAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, August 2, 2000, marks the 50th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Butuan;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Butuan be given the opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, August 2, 2000, as a special day in the City of Butuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
