MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 98

DECLARING MONDAY, APRIL 19, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN TARLAC CITY

WHEREAS, Monday, April 19, 1999 marks the foundation anniversary of Tarlac City into a component city;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of Tarlac City be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, April 19, 1999, as a special day in Tarlac City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RAMON B. CARDENAS Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
