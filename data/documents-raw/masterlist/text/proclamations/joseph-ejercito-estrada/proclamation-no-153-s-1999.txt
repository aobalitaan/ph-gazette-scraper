MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 153

DECLARING AN AREA FOR MUNICIPAL PARK AND RECREATION AREA DEVELOPMENT PROJECT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF PONTEVEDRA, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

Pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby declare an area for Municipal Park and Recreation Area of the Municipality of Pontevedra, Negros Occidental containing an area of 197,381 square meters subject to private rights if any there by and to final delimitations and survey which portions are more particularly described as follows:

A parcel of land for Municipal Reservation situated in Poblacion, Municipality of Pontevedra, Province of Negros Occidental, Island of Negros.

Bounded on the SE by FIS-4875-D; on the NE by Residential Private Lots; on the NW and WEST by Guimaras Strait and on the SOUTH by Public Land;

Beginning at a point marked a “1” on plan being N 27° 42′ W. 180.86 meters from B.L.L.M. No. 2, Pontevedra Cadastre, thence;

Containing an area of ONE HUNDRED NINETY SEVEN THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED EIGHTY-ONE (197,381) square meters.

All corners marked P.S. Conc. Monument. 15×60 C.M. Corners not otherwise described are old points.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have herewith set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 28 th day of July, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
