MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 62

AUTHORIZING THE COMMUNITY CHEST FOUNDATION, INC., TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN FOR A PERIOD OF ONE (1) YEAR

WHEREAS, the Community Chest Foundation, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 661 dated September 27, 1995, to conduct a national educational and fund campaign for a period of one (1) year;

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs additional time to attain its goals set under the above mentioned campaign activity;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Community Chest Foundation, Inc., to conduct a national educational and fund campaign for a period of one (1) year, effective upon the promulgation of this Proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
