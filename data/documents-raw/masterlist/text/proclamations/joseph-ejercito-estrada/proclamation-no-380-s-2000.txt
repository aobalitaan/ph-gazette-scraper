MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 380

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 363, DATED AUGUST 24, 2000, WHICH IS THE MALANGAS COAL MINES RESERVATION, SITUATED IN PAYAO, ALICIA, IMELDA, SIAY, DIPLAHAN AND BAYOG, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

WHEREAS, the technical descriptions of the boundaries of Parcels I and II of Proclamation No. 363 contain inaccuracies;

WHEREAS, as a result of the inaccuracies in Parcel I a portion of the townsite of Imelda, Zamboanga del Sur, previously excluded from Proclamation No. 528, dated February 26, 1969, by Proclamation No. 233, dated February 29, 1988, is again a part of the reserved Malangas Coal Mines Reservation, while those of Parcel II resulted to an error in the polygon closure;

NOW, THEREFORE, upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend the technical descriptions of Parcels I and II of Proclamation No. 363, dated August 24, 2000, as follows:

PARCEL I

A parcel of land, portion of Proclamation No. 528, Malangas Coal Mines Reservation, beginning at a point marked “1” (7-40-00 N., 122-52-30 E), being N. 72 0 54′ W., 16,294.39 m. from BLLM No. 1, Pls-547, Malangas Public Land Subdivision.

containing an area of EIGHTY THREE MILLION TWO HUNDRED ELEVEN THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED FIFTY NINE (83,211,959) SQUARE METERS, more or less.

PARCEL II

A parcel of land, portion of Proclamation No. 528, Malangas Coal Mines Reservation, beginning at a point marked “1” (7-36-00 N., 122-54-00 E.), being N. 78 0 36′ W., 13,074.64 m. from BLLM No. 1, Pls-547, Malangas Public Land Subdivision.

containing an area of THIRTY SEVEN MILLION EIGHT HUNDRED FORTY FIVE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED FIFTY SIX (37,845,756) SQUARE METERS, more or less.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18 th day of September in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
