MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 251

DECLARING MARCH 8 OF EVERY YEAR AS “ARAW NG COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE”, AND TO FURTHER DECLARE WEDNESDAY, MARCH 8, 2000, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE

WHEREAS, Wednesday, March 8, 2000, marks the 2 nd Foundation Day Anniversary of Compostela Valley Province;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Compostela Valley Province be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their province’s history with appropriate ceremony;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare March 8 of every year as “Araw ng Compostela Valley Province”, and to further declare Wednesday, March 8, 2000, as a special day in Compostela Valley Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6 Th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
