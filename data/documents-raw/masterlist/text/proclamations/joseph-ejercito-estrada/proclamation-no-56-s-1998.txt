MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 56

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARANGAYS OF CAMAGONG AND TALISAY, MUNICIPALITY OF NASIPIT, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL NORTE, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 96-266 dated 14 August 1996 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located in the Barangays of Camagong and Talisay, Municipality of Nasipit, Province of Agusan del Norte, as a special economic zone to be known as the NASIPIT AGUSAN DEL NORTE INDUSTRIAL ESTATE (NANIE) consisting of SIX HUNDRED TWENTY THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED EIGHTY NINE (620,689) SQUARE METERS more or less, as defined by the following technical description:

Note: TIE LINE: N. 47° 51’W., 833.54 m. from BLLM No. 7 CAD-169, NASIPIT CADASTRE

Note: TIE LINE: N. 64° 33′ E., 559.65 m. from B.B.M. No. 12 CAD-169, NASIPIT CADASTRE

Note: TIE LINE: N. 46° 31′ 1085.47 m. B.B.M. No. 12 CAD-169, NASIPIT CADASTRE.

IN WITNESS THEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and cause the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed hereto.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of DECEMBER, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety Eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
