MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 54

DECLARING TUESDAY, DECEMBER 15, 1998, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF NAGA

WHEREAS, Tuesday, December 15, 1998, marks the 50th Charter Day Anniversary of the City of Naga;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Naga be given full, opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book 1, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Tuesday, December 15, 1997, as a special day in the City of Naga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of NOVEMBER, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
