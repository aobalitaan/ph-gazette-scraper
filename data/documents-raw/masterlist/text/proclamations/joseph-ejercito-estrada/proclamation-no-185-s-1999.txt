MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 185

AUTHORIZING THE YOUNG WOMEN’S CHRISTIAN ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN FOR A PERIOD OF ONE (1) YEAR

WHEREAS, the Young Women’s Christian Association (YWCA) of the Philippines has for the last fifty (50) years been deeply and actively involved in. the physical-leadership-spiritual-socio-cultural and economic development and empowerment of the Filipino women, youth and girls;

WHEREAS, the YWCA has thirty-four (34) local associations to implement the programs and services nationwide;

WHEREAS, the YWCA needs the support of all civic minded citizens to enable the organization to carry on with laudable projects;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Young Women’s Christian Association of the Philippines to conduct a national fund campaign for a period of one (1) year effective upon the promulgation of this proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30 Th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
