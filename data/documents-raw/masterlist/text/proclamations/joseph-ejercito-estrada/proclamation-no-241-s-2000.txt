PROCLAMATION NO. 241

ESTABLISHING AS KABULNAN RIVER WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE FOR THE PURPOSE OF PROTECTING, MAINTAINING OR IMPROVING THE WATER YIELD AND PROVIDING RESTRAINING MECHANISM FOR INAPPROPRIATE FOREST EXPLOITATION AND DISRUPTIVE LAND-USE, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF ESPERANZA, ISULAN, BAGUMBAYAN, NINOY AQUINO, KALAMANSIG AND PALEMBANG, ALL IN THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT, MUNICIPALITY OF AMPATUAN IN THE PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO AND MUNICIPALITY OF LAKE SEBU IN THE PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO, PHILIPPINES
