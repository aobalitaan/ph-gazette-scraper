PROCLAMATION NO. 354

DECLARING MOUNT TIMOLAN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF SAN MIGUEL, GUIPOS AND TIGBAO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR AS PROTECTED AREA AND ITS PERIPHERAL AREAS AS BUFFER ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO 7586 (NIPAS ACT OF 1992), AND SHALL BE KNOWN AS MOUNT TIMOLAN PROTECTED LANDSCAPE

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby set aside and declare Mount Timolan situated in the Municipalities of San Miguel, Guipos and Tigbao, Province of Zamboanga del Sur as protected area and its peripheral areas as Buffer Zone, subject to prior valid private rights, if any there be, as described in the DENR NIPAS Map No.
