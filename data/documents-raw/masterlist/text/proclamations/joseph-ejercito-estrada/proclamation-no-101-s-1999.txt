MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 101

DECLARING APRIL 28 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 3134 created the former Municipality of Opon into a City of Lapu-Lapu;

WHEREAS, thenceforth the citizenry of the City of Lapu-Lapu has been consistently observing April 28 as their Charter Day Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the citizenry of the City of Lapu-Lapu be given full opportunity to celebrate this event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me, do hereby declare April 28 of every year as a Special Day in the City of Lapu-Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 31 st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
