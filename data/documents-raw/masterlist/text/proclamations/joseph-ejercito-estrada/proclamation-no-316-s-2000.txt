MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 316

DECLARING THE PINDASAN ISLAND MANGROVE WILDERNESS AREA, KOPIAT ISLAND AND SURROUNDING PORTIONS OF DAVAO GULF SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MABINI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE AS A PROTECTED AREA PURSUANT TO RA 7586 (NIPAS ACT OF 1992), AND SHALL BE KNOWN AS MABINI PROTECTED LANDSCAPE AND SEASCAPE

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and pursuant to the powers vested upon me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby set aside and declare the Pindasan Island Mangrove Wilderness Areas, Kopiat Island and surrounding portions of Davao Gulf situated in the Municipality of Mabini, Province of Davao del Norte as Protected Area under the category of Protected Landscape and Seascape, subject to private rights, and without prejudice to the rights of indigenous peoples as provided for in R.A. No. 8371, DENR Administrative Order No. 93-02 and other related rules and regulations, if any, as described in DENR NIPAS Map No. R-11-07;
