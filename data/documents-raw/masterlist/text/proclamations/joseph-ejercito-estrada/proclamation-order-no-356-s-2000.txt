MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 356

DECLARING AUGUST 29, 2000 AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER AS “ARAW NG PAGBILAO” IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAGBILAO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, the Sangguniang Bayan of the Municipality of Pagbilao, Province of Quezon, adopted and approved Resolution No. 161 dated July 12, 2000 declaring August 29 as “Araw ng Pagbilao”;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pagbilao be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare August 29, 2000 and every year thereafter as “Araw ng Pagbilao”. The day, however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15 Th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
