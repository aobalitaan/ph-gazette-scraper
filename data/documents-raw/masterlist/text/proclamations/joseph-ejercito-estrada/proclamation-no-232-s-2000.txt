MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 232

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 11, 2000, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF TALISAY

WHEREAS, Friday, February 11, 2000, marks the foundation anniversary of the City of Talisay into a component city;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the City of Talisay be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Friday, February 11, 2000, as a special day in the City of Talisay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
