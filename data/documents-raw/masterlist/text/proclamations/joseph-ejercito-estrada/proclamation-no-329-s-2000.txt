MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 329

DECLARING SATURDAY, JULY 1, 2000, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF DIPOLOG

WHEREAS, Saturday, July 1, 2000, marks the 87 th Foundation Anniversary of the City of Dipolog;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the City of Dipolog be given the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Saturday, July 1, 2000, as a special day in the City of Dipolog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17 Th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
