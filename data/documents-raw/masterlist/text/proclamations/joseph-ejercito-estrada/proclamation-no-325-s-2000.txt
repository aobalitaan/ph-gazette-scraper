MALACAÑANG Manila BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 325

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF MAGTU-OD ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED IN BARANGAY MAGTU-OD, CITY OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement, and reserve for school site purposes of Magtu-od Elementary School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS), subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at Barangay Magtu-od, City of Davao, Island of Mindanao, which is more particularly described as follows:

Plan-SGS-11-001142

A PARCEL OF LAND, Lot 11280, Cad 102 (SGS-11-001442, portion of Lot 1082, Cad 102) situated at Barangay Magtu-od, City of Davao, Island of Mindanao.

Bounded on the NW., and SE., along lines 1-2-3-4 by H-143575; on the SW., along line 4-1 by H-155147.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 33 deg. 21 W., 3771.09 m. from BLLM No. 21, Cad 102; thence

99.76 m. to point 2;

200.93 m. to point 3;

100.00 m. to point 4;

199.20 m. to point 1,

containing an area of NINETEEN THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED TEN (19,810) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All corners are “old PLS” Cyl. conc. Mons. 15×60 cm. Bearings true. Date of original survey February 1916 to May 1920; Date of subdivision survey; August 22, 1997; Surveyed by Geodetic Engineer Moises N. Miranda, and approved on December 11, 1997.

The tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease or other disposition until again declared alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have herewith set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12 Th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
