MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 222

WHEREAS , Republic Act Nos. 8754 and 8755 provide for the conversion of the Municipality of Tuguegarao, Cagayan, and the Municipality of Malolos, Bulacan, respectively, into component cities, with each statute mandating the holding of a plebiscite for the ratification of the proposed conversion;

WHEREAS , the plebiscite day for the ratification of the conversion of the said municipalities is scheduled on Saturday, December 18, 1999.

WHEREAS , to enable the residents of the said municipalities to actively participate in the scheduled political exercise, there is a need to declare the plebiscite day as special day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, December 18, 1999, as a non-working special day in the said municipalities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of December, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
