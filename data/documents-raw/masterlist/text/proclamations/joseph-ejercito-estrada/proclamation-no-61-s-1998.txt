MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 61

DECLARING SATURDAY, JANUARY 2, 1999 AS A SPECIAL DAY THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY

WHEREAS, Saturday, January 2, 1999 may be declared as a special day without detriment to public service.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, January 2, 1999 as a special day throughout the country.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30 th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RAMON B. CARDENAS Acting Executive Secretary
