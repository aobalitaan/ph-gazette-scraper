MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 35

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 153 DATED MARCH 19, 1993, BY TRANSFERRING THE OBSERVANCE OF THE SECRETARIES WEEK TO THE LAST FULL WEEK OF APRIL OF EVERY YEAR INSTEAD OF THE THIRD WEEK OF APRIL OF EVERY YEAR

WHEREAS , the Philippine Association of Secretaries is recognized for its vital role in promoting professionalism in the private sector;

WHEREAS , Secretaries’ Week is internationally celebrated on the last full week of April of each year with the intervening Wednesday designated as Secretaries’ Day;

WHEREAS , the Philippine Association of Secretaries should be given the opportunity to commemorate its annual anniversary with fitting ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 153 dated March 19, 1993, by transferring the observance of the Secretaries’ Week to the last full week of April of every year, instead of the third week of April of every year, under the auspices of the Philippine Association of Secretaries.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and one.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) RENATO S. DE VILLA Executive Secretary
