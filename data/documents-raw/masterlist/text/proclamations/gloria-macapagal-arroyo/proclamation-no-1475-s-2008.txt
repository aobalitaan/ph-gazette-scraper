PROCLAMATION NO. 1475

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 532 DATED FEBRUARY 1, 1995 BY EXCLUDING A PORTION OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY CAWAG, MUNICIPALITY OF SUBIC, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, ISLAND OF LUZON, WITH AN AREA OF ONE MILLION SEVEN HUNDRED FIFTY SIX THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED TWENTY (1,756,920) SQUARE METERS, MORE OR LESS, FROM THE COVERAGE OF THE SUBIC SPECIAL ECONOMIC AND FREEPORT ZONE AND DECLARING THE SAME AS ALIENABLE AND DISPOSABLE FOR NON-AGRICULTURAL PURPOSES AND FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF A MULTIPLE USE ZONE OR A GREEN ECO-VILLAGE, FOR DISPOSITION UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 532 dated February 1, 1995, which delineated the metes and bounds of the Subic Special Economic and Freeport Zone, by excluding a portion of land with an aggregate area of One Million Seven Hundred Fifty Six Thousand Nine Hundred and Twenty (1,756,920) square meters, more or less, situated at Barangay Cawag, Municipality of Subic, Province of Zambales, Island of Luzon, from its coverage, and declaring the same as alienable and disposable for non-agricultural purposes and for the development of a multiple use zone or a green eco-village, for disposition under the administration of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, subject to valid, prior and existing rights, if any there be, and to final survey, and more particularly described as follows:

Proposed Multiple Use Zone or Eco-Village

A parcel of land (as shown on plan Multiple Use Zone or a Green Eco-Village L.R.C. No. ), situated in the Barrio of Cawag, Municipality of Subic, Province of Zambales, which is a portion of Lot 4262, Cad. 547-D.

Bounded on the NE. along line 1-2 by Lot 565; along lines 2-7 by Lot 4262 (Port.) both of Subic Cadastre; on the S., along line 7-8 by Swo-03-000504; on the W., along line 8-14 by Lot 4262 (Port.) Subic Cadastre; on the N., along line 14-15 by Lot 1; along line 15-16 by Lot 2 both of Sgs-03-001105-D; along line 16-18 by Lot 2, Sgs-03-001072; along line 18-19 by Lot 3; along line 19-22 by Lot 4; along line 22-23 by Lot 3, all of Sgs-03-001105-D; along line 23-24 by Lot 4262 (Port.) Subic Cadastre; along line 24-26 by Lot 7, Sgs-03-000905-D; along line 26 to 34-1 by Lot4262 (Port.) Subic Cadastre.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 71 deg 28″ E., 3211.23 m. from B.L.LM. #5, Subic Cadastre, thence;

Containing an area of One Million Seven Hundred Fifty Six Thousand Nine Hundred and Twenty (1,756,920) square meters; more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by Old Points.

The reserved tracts of land shall be non-alienable under the provisions of the Public Land Act or by Proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 17 th day of March in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
