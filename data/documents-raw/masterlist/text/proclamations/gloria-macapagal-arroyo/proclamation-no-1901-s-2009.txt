PROCLAMATION NO. 1901

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF BINCUNGAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL AND PIPISAN MAUG NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY BINCUNGAN, CITY OF TAGUM, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale disposition or settlement and reserved for school site purposes of Bincungan Elementary School and Pipisan Maug National High School under the administration of the Department of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows to wit:
