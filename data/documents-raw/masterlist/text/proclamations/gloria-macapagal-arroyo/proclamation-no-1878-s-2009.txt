PROCLAMATION NO. 1878

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 30, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Masbate was declared as a component city of the Province of Masbate by virtue of R.A. 8807 dated September 30, 2000;

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 30, 2009, marks the 9 th Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Masbate;

WHEREAS, in order to give the residents of the City of Masbate, the opportunity to celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare September 30, 2009 as special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 30, 2009, as special (non-working) day in Masbate City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
