MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 684

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 389, DATED MARCH 17, 1989, WHICH RESERVED A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN FORT BONIFACIO, TAGUIG, METRO MANILA FOR THE USE OF NATIONAL MAPPING AND RESOURCE INFORMATION AUTHORITY (NAMRIA), BY SEGREGATING A PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND RESERVING THE SAME AS NAMRIA HOUSING SITE

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 389, dated March 17, 1989, which reserved a parcel of land located in Fort Bonifacio, Taguig, Metro Manila for the use of National Mapping and Resource Information Authority (NAMRIA), by segregating a portion of the land embraced therein containing an area of TWENTY FOUR THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED SIXTY ONE (24,161) SQUARE METERS , more or less, and reserving the same as NAMRIA Housing Site, subject to actual survey, and to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 10334-B

A PARCEL OF LAND, (LOT 10334-B, being a portion of Lot 10334, SWO-13-000363 covered by Proclamation No. 2165, as amended by Proclamation No. 389), situated in Fort Bonifacio, Taguig, Metro Manila, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the North along lines 1-2-3 by Lot 8063 (portion), Psu-2031, Mcadm-590-D, Taguig Cadastral Mapping (now SWO-007607-001235-D; on the East, along line 3-4 by Married Officers Quarter (JUSMAG); on the. containing an area of TWENTY FOUR THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED SIXTY ONE (24,161) SQUARE METERS , more or less.

The housing site shall be administered and disposed of by the National Housing Authority (NHA) under existing applicable laws, rules and regulations, such as Republic Act No. 7227, otherwise known as the Urban Development and Housing Act of 1992, in coordination with the National Mapping and Resource Information Authority (NAMRIA). Medium Rise Buildings shall be developed in the area to accommodate as many beneficiaries as possible.

The Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources is hereby authorized to issue a Special Patent covering the area herein reserved for housing purposes in favor of the NHA.

The NHA shall reimburse the national government the value of the land after the final payment of the areas reserved for housing purposes by the qualified beneficiaries.

The portions of the area herein reserved that are being used or earmarked for public or quasi-public purposes shall be excluded from such disposition.

A Local Inter-Agency Committee (LIAC) is hereby created, with the Housing and Urban Development Coordinating Council (HUDCC) as Chair, the NAMRIA and the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) as members. The LIAC shall formulate the implementing rules and regulations of this proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done, in the City of Manila, this 5th day of August in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
