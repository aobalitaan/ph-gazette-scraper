MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 887-A

DECLARING TUESDAY, AUGUST 9, 2005, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE ENTIRE AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO

WHEREAS , the elections in the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) will be conducted on Monday, August 8, 2005;

WHEREAS , Proclamation No. 887 dated August 5, 2005 declared Monday, August 8, 2005, as Special (Non-Working) Day in the entire ARMM, and Monday and Tuesday, August 8-9, 2005, as Special (Non-Working) Days in all public schools in Cotabato City and all schools outside the ARMM duly designated by the COMELEC as counting and Canvassing venues for the ARMM Elections;

WHEREAS , there is a need to declare August 9, 2005 as a special (non-working) day in the entire ARMM in order to cover possible spill-over counting/canvassing activities in some areas;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Tuesday, August 9, 2005, as special (non-working) day in the entire Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM).

DONE in the City of Manila, this 6th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand Five.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
