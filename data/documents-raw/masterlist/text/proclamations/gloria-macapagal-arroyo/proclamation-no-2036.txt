PROCLAMATION NO. 2036

DECLARING MONDAY, APRIL 12, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS, Monday, April 12, 2010, the Archdiocese of Zamboanga marks the centennial anniversary of its creation as a Diocese;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Zamboanga City be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LEANDRO R. MENDOZA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, April 12, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the City of Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 8th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President;

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Deputy Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs
