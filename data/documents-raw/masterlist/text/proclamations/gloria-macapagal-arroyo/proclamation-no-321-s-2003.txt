MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 321

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 3, 2003, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN

WHEREAS, Monday, February 3, 2003, marks the 53 rd Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Cabanatuan;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the City of Cabanatuan be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, February 3, 2003 as a special day in the City of Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23 rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
