PROCLAMATION NO. 1458

DECLARING MONDAY, MARCH 10, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TANAUAN, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Monday, March 10, 2008, marks the 7 th Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Tanauan, Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanauan be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, March 10, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the City of Tanauan, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of February in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
