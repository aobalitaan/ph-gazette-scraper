PROCLAMATION NO. 1798

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 6 TO 12, 2009 AS “PAGDIRIWANG WEEK” IN CELEBRATION OF THE 111 th ANNIVERSARY OF PHILIPPINE INDEPENDENCE

WHEREAS, June 12, 2009 marks the celebration of the 111 th anniversary of Philippine Independence;

WHEREAS, May 28 to June 12 was declared as National Flag Days, the historic period representing the collective effort of the Filipino Nation to attain independence;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper to declare the period from June 6 to 12, 2009 as “ Pagdiriwang Week “ in celebration of the 111 th Anniversary of Philippine Independence;

WHEREAS, the declaration will complement the Filipino Heritage Festival featured in Seattle-Center, USA and the ‘ Pagdiriwang 2009- A Filipino Cultural & Art ‘ event on June 6 to 12, 2009 by the Filipino Cultural Heritage Society of Washington, USA.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby proclaim the period from June 6 to 12, 2009 as “Pagdiriwang Week” in celebration of the 111 th anniversary of Philippine Independence.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:

Executive Secretary
