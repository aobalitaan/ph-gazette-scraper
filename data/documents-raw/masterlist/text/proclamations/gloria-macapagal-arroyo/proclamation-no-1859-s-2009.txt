PROCLAMATION NO. 1859

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 4, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, Friday, September 4, 2009, marks the 55 th Foundation Day anniversary and 14 th Kariyawan Festival of the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, September 4, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
