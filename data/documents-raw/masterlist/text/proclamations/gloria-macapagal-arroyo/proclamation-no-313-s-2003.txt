MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 313

RESERVING FOR BARANGAY MULTI-PURPOSE PLAZA A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY BAGUMBAYAN MUNICIPALITY OF LUPON, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement, and reserve for multi-purpose plaza site purposes of Barangay Bagumbayan, Municipality of Lupon, Province of Davao Oriental, Island of Mindanao, under the Administration of the Interior and Local Government, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Bagumbayan, Municipality of Lupon, Province of Davao Oriental, Island of Mindanao, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 71, Ccs-11-000612 portion of Lots 173 and 174, Gss-11-059 (Bagumbayan Multi-Purpose Plaza)

A parcel of land known as Lot 71, Ccs-11-000612, a portion of Lots 173 and 174, Gss-11-059, situated in Barangay Bagumbayan, Municipality of Lupon, Province of Davao Oriental, Island of Mindanao.
