PROCLAMATION NO. 1430

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY DON JOSE, STA. ROSA, LAGUNA, TO FORM PART OF THE EXISTING GREENFIELD AUTOMOTIVE PARK SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 90-011 dated January 27, 1999 of the Board of Directors of PEZA, an area located at Barangay Don Jose, Sta. Rosa, Laguna as a special economic zone to form part of the existing Greenfield Automotive Park Special Economic Zone, consisting of ONE HUNDRED FIFTY NINE THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED SEVENTY NINE (159,379) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
