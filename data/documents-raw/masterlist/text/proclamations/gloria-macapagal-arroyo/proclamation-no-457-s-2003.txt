MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 457

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 12, 2003, AS A SPECIAL (NON- WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS , September 13 of every year has been declared as special non-working holiday through Republic Act No. 6702 to commemorate the anniversary of the Battle at Pulang Lupa in the Province of Marinduque;

WHEREAS , September 13, 2003, this year’s 103 rd anniversary, falls on a Saturday;

WHEREAS , Friday, September 12, 2003, is the day during which the activities pertaining to the event will be held; and

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of the Province of Marinduque be given full opportunity to celebrate with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, September 12, 2003, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
