PROCLAMATION NO. 1947

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AS SCHOOL SITE FOR MAXINGAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL SITUATED IN THE BARANGAY MAXINGAL, MUNICIPALITY OF LALLO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, ISLAND OF LUZON AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES-DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION (RP-DepEd)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve as school site purposes for MAXINGAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL under the administration of Department of Education (DepEd) certain parcels of land of the public domain, subject to private rights, if any there be, situated in Barangay Maxingal, Municipality of Lallo, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon and more particularly described as follows:
