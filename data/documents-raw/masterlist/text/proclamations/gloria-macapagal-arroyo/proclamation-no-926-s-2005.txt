MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 926

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING TO BE KNOWN AS ROBINSONS BIG R SUPERCENTER CAINTA JUNCTION WHICH STANDS ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED ALONG ORTIGAS AVENUE EXTENSION, BARANGAY STO. DOMINGO, CAINTA, RIZAL, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY BUILDING PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Building, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolutions No. 00-411 and 05-193 dated 29 December 2000 and 29 June 2005, respectively, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), the Robinsons Big R Supercenter Cainta Junction, which stands on a parcel of land of the private domain, situated along Ortigas Avenue Extension, Barangay Sto. Domingo, Cainta, Rizal, with an area of Thirty Two Thousand Five Hundred and Three (32,503) Square Meters , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
