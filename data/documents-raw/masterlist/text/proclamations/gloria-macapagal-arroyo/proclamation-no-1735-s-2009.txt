PROCLAMATION NO. 1735

RESERVING FOR GOVERNMENT HOUSING SITE PURPOSES OF THE PROVINCIAL GOVERNMENT OF ABRA AND FURTHER GRANTING OWNERSHIP IN FAVOR OF THE PROVINCIAL GOVERNMENT OF ABRA, PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED IN BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF PEÑARRUBIA, PROVINCE OF ABRA, ISLAND OF LUZON
