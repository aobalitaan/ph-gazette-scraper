MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2086

DECLARING FRIDAY, JUNE 18, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DON CARLOS, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS , Friday, June 18, 2010, the Municipality of Don Carlos, Province of Bukidnon marks the celebration of the “Araw ng Don Carlos”, its 44 th Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Don Carlos, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LEANDRO R. MENDOZA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, June 18, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Don Carlos, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEANDRO R. MENDOZA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) AGUSTIN S. DIZON Deputy Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs
