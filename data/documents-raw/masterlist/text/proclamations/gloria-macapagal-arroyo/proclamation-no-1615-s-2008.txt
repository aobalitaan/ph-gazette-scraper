PROCLAMATION NO. 1615

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED ALONG MC ARTHUR HIGHWAY, SAN MIGUEL, TARLAC CITY, PROVINCE OF TARLAC AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 08-183 dated 31 March 2008 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Robinsons Luisita as an Information Technology Center which stands on a parcel of land with a area of TWELVE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED THREE (12,703) square meters, more or less, located along Mc Arthur Highway, San Miguel, Tarlac City, Province of Tarlac as defined by the following technical description:
