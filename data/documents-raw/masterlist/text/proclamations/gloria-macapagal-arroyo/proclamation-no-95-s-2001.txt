MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 95

SEGREGATING CERTAIN AREA FROM THE PARCEL OF LAND COVERED BY TRANSFER CERTIFICATE OF TITLE NO. 142162 SITUATED IN PASAY CITY, METRO MANILA REGISTERED IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THE LAND REGISTRATION ACT IN THE NAME OF THE MANILA INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT AUTHORITY (MIAA) AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION AS THE SITE OF THE MIAA EMPLOYEES HOUSING PROJECT IN ACCORDANCE WITH THE PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 3038, IN RELATION TO THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT (COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 141 AS AMENDED)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby segregate certain areas from the parcel of land covered by Transfer Certificate of Title No. 142162, situated in Pasay City, Metro Manila, registered in accordance with the provisions of the Land Registration Act in the name of the Manila International Airport Authority (MIAA) and declaring the same open to disposition as the site of the MIAA Employees Housing Project in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 3038, in relation to the provisions of the Public Land Act (C.A. No. 141, as amended).

Such part or parts of the area herein declared open for disposition under Act. No. 3038, in relation to the provisions of the Public Land Act, as amended, which are being used or earmarked for public or quasi-public purposes shall be excluded from such disposition. Existing roads or proposed roads within the area shall conform with the provisions of Executive Order No. 113, series of 1995.

The boundary of the proclaimed area is subject to final survey.

The Department of Environment and Natural Resources, National Capital Region, shall conduct or supervise the actual boundary survey and subdivision survey of the proclaimed area. In the execution of the subdivision survey of the proclaimed area, open spaces for parks, playgrounds, barangay hall, school site and sufficient roads and alleys for the convenience of the residents thereat shall be strictly provided.

The housing project referred to herein shall be administered by the Manila International Airport Authority (MIAA) and, for this purpose, the Department of Environment and Natural Resources shall provide assistance in the disposition of homelots to qualified officials and employees of the MIAA and its bureaus, offices and agencies.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and one.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
