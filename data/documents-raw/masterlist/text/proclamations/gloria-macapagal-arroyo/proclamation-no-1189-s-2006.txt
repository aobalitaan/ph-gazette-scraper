MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1189

DECLARING THE FIRST WEEK OF DECEMBER AS BUSINESS EDUCATION WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine Council of Deans and Educators in Business (PCDEB), is an organization of deans, chairs, coordinators and heads of the different business schools in the Philippines;

WHEREAS, the primary objectives of the PCDEB is to upgrade business education to respond to changes brought about by the dynamic environment of business; to continuously improve the quality of instruction in business education; to make business education play an important role in the building and improvement of the country’s economy; to develop professionalism among business educators; and to organize and encourage scientific research in the area of global business;

WHEREAS, the PCDEB during these past years pioneered in recognizing outstanding business educators working closely with companies manifesting a high level of social responsibility and corporate citizenship;

WHEREAS, the PCDEB had been involved in the education of our youth in the area of responsible citizenship and good governance.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the first week of December as Business Education Week .

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
