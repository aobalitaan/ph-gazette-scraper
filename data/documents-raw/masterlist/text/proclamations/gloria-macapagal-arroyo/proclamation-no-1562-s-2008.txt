PROCLAMATION NO. 1562

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING KNOWN AS THE ASIAN STAR BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON TWO PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT LOTS 1 & 2, BLOCK 2, PHASE 1, FILINVEST CORPORATE CITY, ALABANG, MUNTINLUPA CITY, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 07-395 dated 21 August 2007, as amended by Resolution No. 08-084 dated 15 February 2008, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Asian Star Building as Information Technology Center located at Lots 1 and 2, Block 2, Phase 1, Filinvest Corporate City, Alabang, Muntinlupa City, which stands on two parcels of land of the private domain with an aggregate area of Two Thousand Nine Hundred Thirty Five (2,935) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
