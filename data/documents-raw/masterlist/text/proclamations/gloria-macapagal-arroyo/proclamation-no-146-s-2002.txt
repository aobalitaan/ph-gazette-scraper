MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 146

DECLARING TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 12, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING DAY IN THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN

WHEREAS , Tuesday, February 12, 2002, marks the Fourth Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Kidapawan;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Kidapawan be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Tuesday, February 12, 2002, as a special non-working day in the city of Kidapawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
