MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1097

DECLARING MONDAY, JULY 24, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF GINGOOG

WHEREAS, Sunday, July 23, 2006, marks the 46 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Gingoog;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Gingoog celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, July 24, 2006 be declared as special (non-working) day instead of July 23, 2006, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, July 24, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
