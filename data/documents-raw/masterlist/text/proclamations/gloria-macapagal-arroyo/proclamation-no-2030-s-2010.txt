PROCLAMATION NO. 2030

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, MARCH 31, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PANABO

WHEREAS, Wednesday, March 31, 2010, marks the 9 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Panabo;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Panabo full opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare March 31, 2010 as a non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LEANDRO R. MENDOZA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, March 31, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the City of Panabo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Deputy Executive Secretary
