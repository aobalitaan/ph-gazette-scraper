MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1202

DECLARING CERTAIN PARCELS OF RECLAIMED LAND AS ALIENABLE AND DISPOSABLE LAND TO BE DISPOSED OF IN FAVOR OF THE PHILIPPINE RECLAMATION AUTHORITY (PRA) SITUATED IN THE CITY OF BACOLOD, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, ISLAND OF VISAYAS

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby declare certain parcels of reclaimed land as alienable and disposable and to be disposed of in favor of the Philippine Reclamation Authority (PRA) situated in the City of Bacolod, Province of Negros Occidental, Island of Visayas and more particularly described as follows:

The Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources is hereby authorized to issue Special Patent in favor of the Philippine Reclamation Authority.
