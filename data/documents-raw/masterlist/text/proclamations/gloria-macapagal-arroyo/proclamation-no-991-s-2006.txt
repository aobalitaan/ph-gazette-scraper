MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 991

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 13, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PARAÑAQUE

WHEREAS , Monday, February 13, 2006, marks the foundation day anniversary of the City of Parañaque;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Parañaque be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, February 13, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the City of Parañaque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Sec (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
