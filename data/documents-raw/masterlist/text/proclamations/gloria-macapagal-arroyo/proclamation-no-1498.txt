PROCLAMATION NO. 1498

DECLARING THURSDAY, JUNE 19, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA.

WHEREAS, Thursday, June 19, 2008, marks the 147 th birth anniversary of Dr. Jose P. Rizal, our national hero;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Laguna and the City of Calamba  be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies keeping with his ideals and teachings.

NOW, THERFORE I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Thursday, June 19, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Laguna and the City of Calamba.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
