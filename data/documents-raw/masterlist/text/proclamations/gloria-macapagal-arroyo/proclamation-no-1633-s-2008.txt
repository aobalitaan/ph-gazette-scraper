PROCLAMATION NO. 1633

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 609 DATED APRIL 20, 2004, WHICH RESERVED CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR KALAYAAN PUMP STORAGE PLANT COMPLEX SITE OF THE NATIONAL POWER CORPORATION (NAPOCOR) BY GRANTING OWNERSHIP IN FAVOR OF NAPOCOR, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF KALAYAAN AND LUMBAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 609 dated April 20, 2004, which reserved certain parcel of land of the public domain for Kalayaan Pump Storage Plant Complex Site of the National Power Corporation (NAPOCOR), by granting ownership in favor of NAPOCOR, situated in the Municipalities of Lumban and Kalayaan, Province of Laguna, Island of Luzon, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
