MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 729

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF BALUNTO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY SAN JOSE, GENERAL SANTOS CITY, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of Balunto High School under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain containing an area of THIRTY THOUSAND (30,000) SQUARE METERS , more or less, situated in Barangay San Jose, General Santos City, Island of Mindanao, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Plan Mr-12-000001

A PARCEL OF LAND (Plan Mr-12-000001) , situated in Barangay of San Jose, City of General Santos, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on West, along line 1-2 by Plan F-11-03-014265-D of Juanaria Suficiencia and along line 2-3 by the land claimed by Tula Sulanting; on the North, along line 3-4 by Public Land; on the East, along line 4-5 by land claimed by Gorenio Mariano; and on the South, along line 5-1 by Plan Mr-11-000014-D, school site of San Jose Elementary School.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on the approved plan, being S. 88 deg. 42′ W., 14,664.36 meters from BLBM No. 1, “MAKAR”; thence:

103.27 m. to point 2;

161.40 m. to point 3;

114.17 m. to point 4;

264.60 m. to point 5;

113.35 m. to point of

beginning, containing an area of THIRTY THOUSAND (30,000) SQUARE METERS , more or less.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 40 cm. Bearings true.

This lot was surveyed in accordance with existing laws, rules and regulations on June 9, 2000 by Geodetic Engineer Abner F. Lagsub and was approved on November 29, 2000.

The tract of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease, or other disposition until again declared alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
