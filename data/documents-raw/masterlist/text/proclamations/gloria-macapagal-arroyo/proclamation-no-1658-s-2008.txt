PROCLAMATION NO. 1658

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING KNOWN AS CONNECTICUT ARCADE ESTABLISHED ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT CONNECTICUT STREET, GREENHILLS SHOPPING CENTER, SAN JUAN CITY, METRO MANILA, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Center, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000, as. amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004 and Resolution No. 08-270 dated 29 May 2008 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Connecticut Arcade established on a parcel of land located at Connecticut Street, Greenhills Shopping Center, San Juan City, Metro Manila containing an area of ONE HUNDRED FIFTY THREE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED SEVENTY NINE (153,179) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
