MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1469

DECLARING FRIDAY, MARCH 14, 2008 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASSI

WHEREAS , Friday, March 14, 2008, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Passi.

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Passi be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, March 14, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the City of Passi.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
