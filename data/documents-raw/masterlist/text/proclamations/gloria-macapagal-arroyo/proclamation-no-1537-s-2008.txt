PROCLAMATION NO. 1537

RESERVING AS SITE FOR THE BUREAU OF FIRE PROTECTION AND MUNICIPAL TRIAL COURT OF BROOKE’S POINT, PALAWAN, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN POBLACION I, MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE’S POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve as sites for the Bureau of Fire Protection and Municipal Trial Court Site purposes, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, situated in Poblacion I, Municipality of Brooke’s Point, Province of Palawan, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT NO. 58, CAD-796-D

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot. 58, CAD-796-D) situated in Poblacion I, Municipality of Brooke’s Point, Province of Palawan, Island of Palawan.

Bounded on the E., along line 1-2 by Lot No. 162, Cad-796-D, Brooke’s Point Cadastre; on the S., along line 2-3 by Rizal Street; on the W., along line 3-4 by Mr. Rodriguez Street; and on the N., along line 4-1 by Lot No. 3361-E, Cad-796-D, Brooke’s Point Cadastre. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S., 34 deg. 11’W., 337.41 m. from BLLM No. 1, Cad-796-D, Brooke’s Point Cadastre, thence:

Containing an area of ONE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED THIRTY NINE (1,239) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on plan and are marked on the ground by Old P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cms. Bearing: True. This plan Cad. 796-D was surveyed on November 21-26, 1983 by Silverio C. Habab, Geodetic Engineer, in accordance with the law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder, was approved on September 2, 1985.

The tract of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease or other mode of disposition through proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 17 th day of June in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:

Executive Secretary
