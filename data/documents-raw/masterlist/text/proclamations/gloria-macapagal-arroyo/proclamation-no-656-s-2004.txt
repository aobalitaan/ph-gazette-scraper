MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 656

DECLARING FRIDAY, JULY 2, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS , Friday, July 2, 2004 marks the 431 st Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Pasig;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, July 2, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 29 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
