MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 184

DECLARING MAY 8 OF EVERY YEAR AS “ARAW NG HERMOSA” IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HERMOSA, PROVINCE OF BATAAN

WHEREAS , it is the custom of the people in the Municipality of Hermosa to celebrate May 8 of every year as their “Araw ng Hermosa”;

WHEREAS , a celebration should be held on the said date to commemorate this historical occasion in the lives of every citizen of Hermosa, Bataan;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare May 8 of every year as “Araw ng Hermosa” in the Municipality of Hermosa, Province of Bataan, The day however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
