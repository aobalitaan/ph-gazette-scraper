PROCLAMATION NO. 177

PROPOSED PROCLAMATION CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED IN BARANGAYS SAN RAFAEL AND STA. ANASTACIA, MUNICIPALITY OF STO. TOMAS, IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Anastacia, Municipality of Sto. Tomas, Province of Batangas, henceforth to be known as LIGHT INDUSTRY AND SCIENCE PARK III, consisting of FIVE HUNDRED SIXTY FIVE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED SEVENTY ONE (565,671) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
