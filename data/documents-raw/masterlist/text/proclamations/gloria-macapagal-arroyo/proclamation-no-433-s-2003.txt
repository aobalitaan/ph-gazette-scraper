MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 433

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 2003, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS , Wednesday, September 3, 2003, marks the 35 th Charter Foundation anniversary;

WHEREAS , the people of the City of Iriga should be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with fitting ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA. MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, September 3, 2003, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
