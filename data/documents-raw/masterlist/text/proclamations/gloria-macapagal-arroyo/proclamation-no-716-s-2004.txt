MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 716

DECLARING SATURDAY, OCTOBER 16, 2004 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CALBAYOG

WHEREAS , Saturday, October 16, 2004, marks the Charter Anniversary of the City of Calbayog;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calbayog be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Saturday, October 16, 2004 as special (non-working) day in the City of Calbayog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By authority of the President: ( Sgd .) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
