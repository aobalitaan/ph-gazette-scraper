MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 223

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 468 DATED SEPTEMBER 26, 1994, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE WAHIG-INABANGA RIVERS WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE BY EXCLUDING ALL ALIENABLE AND DISPOSABLE LANDS THEREFROM

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 468 dated September 26, 1994, which established the Wahig-Inabanga Rivers Watershed Forest Reserve by excluding therefrom all lands previously declared as alienable and disposable.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
