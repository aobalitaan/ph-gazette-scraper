MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1032

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 11 TO 14, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAYS IN THE CITIES OF CEBU, MANDAUE AND LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS , the Cities of Cebu, Mandaue and Lapu-Lapu will host the ASEAN Summit on December 11 to 14, 2006;.

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of these cities be given full opportunity to participate in this important event.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare the period from December 11 to 14, 2006, as special (non-working) days in the Cities of Cebu, Mandaue and Lapu-Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
