MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 295

DECLARING MONDAY, DECEMBER 2, 2002, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN PASAY CITY

WHEREAS, Monday, December 2, 2002, marks the 139 th Foundation Day Anniversary of Pasay City ;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the residents of Pasay City be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, December 2, 2002, as a special day in Pasay City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand Two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
