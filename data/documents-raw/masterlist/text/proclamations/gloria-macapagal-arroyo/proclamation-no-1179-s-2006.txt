PROCLAMATION NO 1179

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN LOCATED AT PAMALICAN ISLAND, BARANGAY MANAMOK, CUYO, PALAWAN, AS A SPECIAL TOURISM ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as a tourism economic zone, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations and Resolutions No. 02-342 dated 10 October 2002 and 05-451 dated 06 December 2005, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, located at Pamalican Island, Barangay Manamok, Cuyo, Palawan, consisting an area of Eight Hundred Ninety Seven Thousand Four Hundred Thirty Four and 50/100 (897,434.50) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
