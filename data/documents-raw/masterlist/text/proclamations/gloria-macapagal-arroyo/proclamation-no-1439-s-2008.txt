PROCLAMATION NO. 1439

DECLARING FRIDAY, JANUARY 18, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN SANTA ROSA CITY, LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Friday, January 18, 2008, marks the 216 th Foundation Day anniversary of Santa Rosa City, Laguna;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Santa Rosa City, Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, January 18, 2008, as special (non-working) day in Santa Rosa City, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
