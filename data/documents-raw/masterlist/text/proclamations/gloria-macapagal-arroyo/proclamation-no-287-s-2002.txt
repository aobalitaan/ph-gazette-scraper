MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 287

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF STO. NIÑO, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition, settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Department of Education a parcel of land situated in Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Sto. Niño, Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 337-BB, Csd-11-008464-d, Identical to Lot 6006, Cad-409 a portion of Lot 337 Cad-409 (Sto. Niño National High School)
