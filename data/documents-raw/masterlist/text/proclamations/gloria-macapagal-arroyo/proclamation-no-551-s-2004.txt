MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 551

DECLARING TWO PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED IN BARANGAYS LALAKAY AND BAMBANG, MUNICIPALITY OF LOS BAÑOS, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, ISLAND OF LUZON, FOR ROAD RIGHT-OF-WAY PURPOSES UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT OF LOS BAÑOS

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and reserve for road right-of-way purposes under the administration of the Local Government of Los Baños, Laguna, two parcels of land located in Barangays Lalakay and Bambang, Municipality of Los Baños, Province of Laguna, Island of Luzon, containing an aggregate area of Four Thousand Nine Hundred Eighty (4,980) square meters , which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
