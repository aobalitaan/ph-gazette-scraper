MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 468

DECLARING TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 23, 2003, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, BILIRAN

WHEREAS, Tuesday, September 23, 2003, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Naval, Biliran; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Naval, Biliran, be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Tuesday, September 23, 2003, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Naval, Biliran, to be known as “Pueblo Day” .

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 15 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
