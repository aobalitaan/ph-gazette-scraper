MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1317

DECLARING AS CIVIL RESERVATION AS THE SITE OF THE LA TRINIDAD WATER DISTRICT CONCRETE RESERVOIR, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED AT LUBAS, LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET, ISLAND OF LUZON, PHILIPPINES

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from occupation, entry, sale, lease or other disposition and reserve the same as site of the La Trinidad Water District Concrete Reservoir, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land located in Lubas, La Trinidad, Benguet, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION (LOT NO. 11194, CAD-1024-D)

A parcel of land (Lot No. 11194, Cad-1024-D), situated at Lubas, La Trinidad, Province of Benguet, Island of Luzon.
