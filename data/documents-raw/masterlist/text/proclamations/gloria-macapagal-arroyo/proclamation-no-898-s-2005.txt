MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 898

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 16, 2005, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS , Friday, September 16, 2005, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province, should be given full opportunity to commemorate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, September 16, 2005, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Five.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
