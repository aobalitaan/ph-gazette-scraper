PROCLAMATION NO. 1209

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING TO BE KNOWN AS MEGACENTER WHICH STANDS ON SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT CORNER GENERAL TINIO AND MELENCIO STREETS, CABANATUAN CITY, NUEVA ECIJA AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority , I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Center, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolutions No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and 05-077 dated 22 March 2005 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), the Megacenter, which stands on several parcels of land of the private domain, situated at corner General Tinio and Melencio Streets, Cabanatuan City, Nueva Ecija, with an aggregate area of Nineteen Thousand Eight Hundred Thirty Three (19,833) Square Meters , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
