MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 492

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 16, DATED APRIL 27, 1922 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE CAVE FOREST RESERVATION ON A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN CRYSTAL CAVE, CITY OF BAGUIO, ISLAND OF LUZON, BY EXCLUDING A CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME FOR EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 16 dated April 27, 1922, which established the Cave Forest Reservation on a parcel of land located in Crystal Cave, City of Baguio, Island of Luzon by excluding a certain portion of the land embraced therein and declaring the same for educational use of the Bakakeng Central Barangay, which excluded parcel is more particularly described as follows:
