MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 539

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, COTABATO PROVINCE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO FOR THE USE OF THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT OF CARMEN FOR VARIOUS PUBLIC PURPOSES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by the virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve certain parcels of land of the public domain situated at Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Carmen, Cotabato Province, Island of Mindanao for the use of the Municipal Government of Carmen for various public purposes, subject to private rights, if any there be, and actual ground survey, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:

The Department of Environment and Natural Resources shall retain jurisdiction over portion covered by public land applications and shall continue to process such application. All vested rights inside the reservation, if there be any, shall be respected.
