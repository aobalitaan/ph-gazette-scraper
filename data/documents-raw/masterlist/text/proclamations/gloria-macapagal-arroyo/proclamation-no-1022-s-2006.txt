MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1022

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY TAMA, MUNICIPALITY OF HERMOSA, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE HENCEFORTH TO BE KNOWN AS HERMOSA ECOZONE INDUSTRIAL PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as a special economic zone, henceforth to be known as Hermosa Ecozone Industrial Park subject to the provisions of Republic Act No 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 96-417 dated 23 December 1996 as amended by Resolution No. 06-021 dated 26 January 2006 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located in Barangay Tama, Municipality of Hermosa, Province of Bataan, consisting of ONE MILLION FOUR HUNDRED TWENTY THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED THIRTY THREE (1,420,433) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:

LOT 1328 T-202586 Sk-03-003975

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot-1328 of the Cadastral Survey of Hermosa as shown on plan Sk-03-003975 L.R.C. Rec. No. ), situated in the Barrio of Tama, Municipality of Hermosa, Province of Bataan. Bounded on the NW., along line 30-1 to 3 by Lot 1329 Hermosa Cadastre; on the E., and SE., along line 3-4-5 by Lot 2, Psu-2927 (Sk-03-003975); on the SW., and NW., along line 5 to 30 by Raya Creek; and Taric Creek.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N., 03 deg. 31′ E, 939.94 m. from BLLM No. 6, Hermosa Cadastre;

beginning, containing an area of TWO HUNDRED TWO THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED EIGHT (202,608) SQUARE METERS , more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by Old PS cyl. conc. mons. 15 x 60 cms. Bearings Grid; date of original survey, August 1928-October 1931. This lot was prepared under Sk-03-003975 which was approved on February 1, 2005.
