MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 739

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE MINDANAO POLYTECHNIC STATE COLLEGE, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT LOURDES, ALUBIJID, MISAMIS ORIENTAL, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue f the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for school site purposes of the Mindanao Polytechnic State College; a certain parcel of land of the public domain, classified as timberland under Land Classification Map No. 2768 certified and approved on November 20, 1972, containing an area of TWO MILLION NINE HUNDRED TWENTY ONE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED AND NINETEEN (2,921,419) SQUARE METERS , situated in Barangay Lourdes, Municipality of Alubijid, Province of Misamis Oriental, Island of Mindanao, subject to final survey, which parcel of timberland is more particularly described as follows:

This proclamation shall be deemed automatically revoked or cancelled in the event that the reservation no longer serves the purpose for which it was originally established and/or has been abandoned by the reservee and the area covered thereby be forthwith reverted to the mass of forestland under the administrative jurisdiction of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources through its appropriate agency.
