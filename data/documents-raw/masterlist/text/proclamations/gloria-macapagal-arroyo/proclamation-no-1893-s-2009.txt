PROCLAMATION NO. 1893

RESERVING AS SCHOOL SITE FOR JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL-BUCAYAO ANNEX, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY BUCAYAO, CITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or disposition and reserve as school site purposes for Jose J. Leido, Jr. Memorial National High School – Bucayao Annex, under the administration of the Department of Education (DepEd), subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain, situated in Barangay Bucayao, City of Calapan, Province of Oriental Mindoro, which are more particularly described as follows:
