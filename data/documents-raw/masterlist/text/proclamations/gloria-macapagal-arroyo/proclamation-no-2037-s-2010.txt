MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2037

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT BARANGAY BEBELADAN, EL NIDO, PALAWAN, AS TOURISM ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 02-342 dated 10 October 2002 and Resolution No. 09-268 dated 10 June 2009, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, a parcel of land located at Barangay Bebeladan, El Nido, Palawan as tourism economic zone to be known as Green Tourism Ecozone-Pangulasian, with an area of Fifty Thousand (50,000) Square Meters , more or less, as defined by the following consolidated technical description:
