PROCLAMATION NO. 1988

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON TWO PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT CORNERS AVANCEÑA AND LOCSIN STREETS, BARANGAY NORTH SAN JOSE, ILOILO CITY AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 08-386 dated 31 July 2008 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Iloilo Commercial Development Corporation Building as an Information Technology Center which stands on two parcels of land of the private domain located at corners Avanceña and Locsin Streets, Barangay North San Jose, Iloilo City, Province of Iloilo, containing an aggregate area of One Thousand Three Hundred Eighty (1,380) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
