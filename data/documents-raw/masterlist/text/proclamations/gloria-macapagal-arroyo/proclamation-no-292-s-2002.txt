MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 292

DECLARING DECEMBER 24, 2002 AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY

WHEREAS , December 25, 2002, Christmas Day and December 31, 2002, the Last Day of the Year, are non-working holidays throughout the country;

WHEREAS , December 24, 2002 falls on a Tuesday and December 25, 2002, Christmas Day, falls on a Wednesday;

WHEREAS , December 24 has, in the past, been declared a special public holiday;

WHEREAS , by tradition, the Filipinos celebrate the Christmas holidays by visiting their families, relatives and friends;

WHEREAS , to give the Filipino people full opportunity to celebrate and enjoy the Christmas holidays with their families, relatives and friends, it is necessary to declare December 24, 2002 as a special (non-working) day throughout the country without prejudice to the public interest;

WHEREAS , the Secretary of the Department of Tourism recommends the issuance of this Proclamation;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare December 24, 2002 as a special (non-working) day throughout the country.

City of Manila, 18 November, 2002.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
