MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1013

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING TO BE KNOWN AS OCTAGON IT BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT SAN MIGUEL AVENUE, ORTIGAS CENTER, PASIG CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY BUILDING PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Building, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolutions No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and 04-222 dated 13 July 2004 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), the Octagon IT Building, which stands on certain parcels of land of the private domain, situated at San Miguel Avenue, Ortigas Center, Pasig City, with an area of One Thousand Seven Hundred Seventy Nine (1,779) Square Meters , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
