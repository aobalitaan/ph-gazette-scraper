MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 509

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY ARPILI, MUNICIPALITY OF BALAMBAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU, AS PART OF WEST CEBU INDUSTRIAL PARK SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended, and, upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as part of West Cebu Industrial Park Special Economic Zone, pursuant to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 96-406 dated 23 December 1996 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, a parcel of land located in the Barangay of Arpili, Municipality of Balamban, Province of Cebu, with an area of ONE HUNDRED SIXTY SIX THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED TWENTY ONE (166,821) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
