MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1151

DECLARING FRIDAY, OCTOBER 20, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON- WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL NEGROS

WHEREAS , Friday, October 20, 2006, the Province of Oriental Negros will be celebrating the Buglasan Festival;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Oriental Negros be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, October 20, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Oriental Negros.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
