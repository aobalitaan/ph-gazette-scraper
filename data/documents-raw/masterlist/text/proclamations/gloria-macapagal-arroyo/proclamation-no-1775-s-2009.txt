PROCLAMATION NO. 1775

RESERVING A PORTION OF THE LAND COVERED BY TRANSFER CERTIFICATE OF TITLE NO. 215553 ISSUED IN THE NAME OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES AS SCHOOL SITE FOR SAN PEDRO APARTADO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, SITUATED IN BARANGAY SAN PEDRO APARTADO, MUNICIPALITY OF ALCALA, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL – ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve a portion of the land covered by Transfer Certificate of Title (TCT) No. 215553 issued in the name of the Republic of the Philippines as school site of San Pedro Apartado Elementary School, situated in Barangay San Pedro Apartado, Municipality of Alcala, Province of Pangasinan, Island of Luzon, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
