MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 922

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY DILAG, MUNICIPALITY OF TABUK, PROVINCE OF KALINGA, ISLAND OF LUZON AS SITE OF THE DILAG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Dilag Elementary School, under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain containing an area of NINE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED FIFTY ONE (9,751) SQUARE METERS , more or less, situated at Barangay Dilag, Municipality of Tabuk, Province of Kalinga, Island of Luzon, subject to private rights if any there be, which parcel of land is now particularly described as follows:
