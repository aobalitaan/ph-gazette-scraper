MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1237

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF UGAC ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, SITUATED IN BARANGAY STA. ROSA, MUNICIPALITY OF IGUIG, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of Ugac Elementary School, under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, situated in Barangay Sta. Rosa, Municipality of Iguig, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is now particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 2, Ccn-(af)-02-000007 Consolidation of Lot 1953 & 1997, Pls-601-D

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot No. 2, Ccn-(af)-02-000007, a consolidation of Lot 1953 & 1997, Pls-601-D as surveyed for Ugac Elementary School, situated in the Barrio of Sta. Rosa, Municipality of Iguig, Province of Cagayan. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by Lot 1; Road of the Subdivision Plan; on the W., along line 2-3 by Road; (6.0 meters wide); on the NW., along line 3-4 by Lot 1999; on the SE., and NE., along lines 4-5-6 by Lot 1996; on the NE., along line 6-7 by Lot 1955; on the SE., along line 7-8 by Lot 1954; and on the SW., along line 8-1 by Barrio Road (10.00 meters wide), all of Pls-601-D.

Beginning at point marked “1” on plan being N. 39-15 W., 3021.26 m. from BLLM No. 1, Pls-601-D, thence:

Containing an area of FOURTEEN THOUSAND THIRTY NINE (14,039) SQUARE METERS more or less. All points referred to are indicated on plan and are marked on the ground by PS Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 40 cms. in diameter. Bearings true. And that of the Subdivision Survey was executed by Geodetic Engr. Pedro Antonio H. Lingan on September 20, 2001 and was approved on December 12, 2001.

The tract of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease, or other disposition until again declared as alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9th day of February , in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
