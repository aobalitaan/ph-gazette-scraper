PROCLAMATION NO. 1794

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 312 DATED DECEMBER 8, 1967, WHICH RESERVES FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PORTIONS OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT FORMERLY KNOWN AS FORT SAN PEDRO, BY GRANTING OWNERSHIP OF THE PORTION THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP), SITUATED IN THE CITY OF ILOILO, ISLAND OF PANAY

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No, 312 dated December 8, 1967, which reserved for Government Office Building site purposes certain parcels of land of the private domain, by granting ownership of a portion thereof in favor of the Philippine National Police (PNP), situated in the City of Iloilo, Island of Panay, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

NR-06-000024

(Philippine National Police)
