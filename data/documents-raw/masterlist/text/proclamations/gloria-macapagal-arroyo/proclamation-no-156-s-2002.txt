PROCLAMATION NO. 156

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY CANLUBANG, MUNICIPALITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO BE ANNEXED INTO AND FORM PART OF THE CARMELRAY INDUSTRIAL PARK SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended, and, upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as part of Carmelray Industrial Park Special Economic Zone, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution Nos. 00-226 dated 27 July 2000 and 96-320 dated 25 September 1996 of the PEZA Board of Directors, four (4) lots, all located in Barangay Canlubang, Municipality of Calamba, Province of Laguna, consisting of SIXTY SEVEN THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED THIRTY TWO (67,232) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description
