PROCLAMATION NO. 1801

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND WITHIN THE WEST VISAYAS INTEGRATED AGRICULTURAL RESEARCH CENTER FOR HOUSING SITE PURPOSES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE-REGIONAL FIELD UNIT NO. VI, SITUATED IN BARANGAY HAMUNGAYA, DISTRICT OF JARO, CITY OF ILOILO, ISLAND OF PANAY

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve certain parcel of land within the West Visayas Integrated Agricultural Research Center, located in Barangay Hamungaya, District of Jaro, City of Iloilo, Island of Panay, containing an aggregate area of FIFTY THOUSAND (50,000) SQUARE METERS, more or less, for housing site purposes of the Department of Agriculture-Regional Field Unit No. VI , subject to private rights, it any there be and to final survey , which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows to wit:
