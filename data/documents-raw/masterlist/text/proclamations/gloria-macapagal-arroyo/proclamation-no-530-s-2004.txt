MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 530

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 188, DATED AUGUST 28, 1937 WHICH RESERVED A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARRIO TINIGUIBAN, CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, FOR MILITARY PURPOSES, BY EXCLUDING A CERTAIN PORTION THEREOF AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN FOR DISPOSITION FOR RESIDENTIAL PURPOSES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 188 dated August 28, 1937 which reserved for military purposes, a certain parcel of land located at Barrio Tiniguiban, City of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan, by excluding a certain portion of the land embraced therein and declaring the same open for disposition for residential purposes under the provisions of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel if land to be excluded is more particularly described as follows:

LOT NO. 2592-A, CSD-04-015832

Such part or parts of the area herein reserved which are being used or earmarked for public or quasi-public purposes, shall be excluded from such disposition.

The Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR)-Regional Office IV-MIMAROPA, in coordination with the Housing and Urban Development Coordinating Council (HUDCC) shall supervise the boundary and subdivision survey of the proclaimed area. In the conduct of said survey, the aforementioned offices shall ensure that the areas intended for open space, parks, playgrounds, barangay hall, school site, roads and alleys and for other public or quasi-public purposes is provided for the convenience of the residents thereat.

An Inter-Agency Committee chaired by HUDCC, DENR as Vice-Chair, Presidential Commission for the Urban Poor (PCUP), City Government of Puerto Princesa and a representative of the People’s Organization (PO) in the area as members, is hereby created to formulate the implementing rules and regulations of this proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
