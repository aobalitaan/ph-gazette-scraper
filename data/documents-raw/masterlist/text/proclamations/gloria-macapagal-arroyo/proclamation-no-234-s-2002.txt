MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 234

EXCLUDING A PORTION OF FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (500,000) SQUARE METERS FROM NATIONAL BILIBID PRISON RESERVATION ADMINISTERED BY THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE (DOJ) IN BRGY. POBLACION, CITY OF MUNTINLUPA, REGISTERED UNDER THE NAME OF THE COMMONWEALTH OF THE PHILIPPINES WITH TRANSFER CERTIFICATE OF TITLE NOS. 183326, 183327, 183328 AND 183329, CONTAINING AN AGGREGATE AREA OF 4,310,872 SQUARE METERS MORE OR LESS, AS SOCIALIZED HOUSING SITE FOR MUNTINLUPA INFORMAL SETTLER FAMILIES OCCUPYING DANGER AREAS

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude the 500,000 square meters National Bilibid Prison (NBP) property situated in Poblacion, City of Muntinlupa, as Socialized Housing Site for Muntinlupa informal settler families living in danger areas, which portions of land are more particularly described as follows:

containing an area of FIVE HUNDRED THOUSAND (500,000) SQUARE METERS MORE OR LESS .

Such part or parts of the area herein declared open for disposition which are being used as earmarked for public or quasi-purposes shall be excluded from such disposition. Except in favor of the government or any of each branches or agencies, all lands disposed under this Proclamation shall not be subject to alienation and encumbrances for a term of ten (10) years from the issuance of the title in case of sale, or execution of contract in case of lease, nor shall they become liable to the satisfaction of any debt contracted prior to the expiration of said period; but the improvements on the land may be mortgaged to qualified persons, associations or corporations.

For purposes of formulating and adopting the implementing guidelines for the proper disposition of the said property, a Local Inter-Agency Committee (LIAC) is hereby created. The committee shall be chaired by the City Government of Muntinlupa and co-chaired by the HUDCC, with the following agencies and organization as members:

1. Department of Environment and Natural Resources 2. Presidential Commission for the Urban Poor 3. A representative from the federation of the people’s organizations and/or a representative from a non-government organization

The LIAC shall invite the concerned Office of the Congressional Representative to participate in its activities to further expedite the disposition program herein set out.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
