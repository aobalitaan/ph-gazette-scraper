PROCLAMATION NO. 1623

RESERVING PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AS SITE FOR THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT BUILDING OF THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT OF TUBA, BENGUET, SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF TUBA, PROVINCE OF BENGUET, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve parcels of land of the public domain as site for the Municipal Government Building of Tuba, Benguet, situated in Poblacion, Tuba, Benguet, subject to private rights, if any there be, which portion particularly described as follows:

LOT 1, SWO-CAR-000711

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 1, Swo-CAR-000711) situated at urban code 131113, Barangay of Poblacion, Municipality of Tuba, Province of Benguet, Island of Luzon.. Bounded on the NE., along lines 1-2-3 and SE along line 3-4 and SW along line 4-5 by Lot 2 of the plan; on the NW along line 5-1 by Municipal Government of Tuba (Proclamation No. 526). Beginning at a point marked “1”on plan, being N. 84 deg. 32″E., 78.42 m. from BLLM No. 1-A, Cad-656-D, Tuba Cadastre, thence:

beginning, containing an area of FIVE HUNDRED (500) square meters, more or less. All corners are marked by P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 40 cms. Except corner 1 by Old P.S. Cyl. Cone. Mons. 15 x 60 cms. Bearing True. Date of Survey: November 3, 2004; executed by Engr. Igme S. Wakat and was approved on May 4, 2006.

Beginning at appoint marked “1” on plan, being N.84 deg. 32’E., 78.42 m. from BLLM No. 1-A, Cad-656-D, Tuba Cadastre, thence:

Except corners 1 & 3 by Old P.S. and cor. 21 & 22 by Old B.L cyl. conc. mons. 15 x 60 cms. Bearing Grid. Date of survey: November 3, 2004; executed by Engr. Igme S. The area covered by the proclamation shall be under the administrative jurisdiction, supervision, and control of the Local Government Unit of Tuba, Benguet.

The tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease or other mode of disposition through proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 15 th day of September in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

Acting Executive Secretary
