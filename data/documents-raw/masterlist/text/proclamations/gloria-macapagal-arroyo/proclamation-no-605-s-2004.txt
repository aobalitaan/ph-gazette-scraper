MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 605

DECLARING THURSDAY, JULY 1, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN SAN CARLOS CITY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS , the City of San Carlos, Negros Occidental, celebrates its Charter Day anniversary on July 1, 2004;

WHEREAS , the residents of the City of San Carlos, conscious of the historical and cultural significance of the event, desire to celebrate that momentous day with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, July 1, 2004, as special (non-working) day in San Carlos City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
