PROCLAMATION NO. 1394

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR STATION/OFFICE SITE OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP) PANTUKAN, SITUATED IN BARANGAY KINGKING, MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department or Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserved for Police Station/Office site of the Philippine National Police (PNP) Pantukan, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Kingking, Pantukan, Compostela Valley, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows to wit:

Lot 137-A, Csd-11-015793- D

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 137-A. Csd-11-015793-D, situated in Barangay Kingking, Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley, Island of Mindanao.

Date of original survey was issued on February 15 to March 20, 1983 and that of the Subdivision Survey was executed on August 22, 2003 by Geodetic Engr. Dennis B. Suaybaguio and was approved on September 27, 2004.

The tract of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, safe, lease, or other disposition until again declared as alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24 th day of September in the year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Seven.
