PROCLAMATION NO. 733

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY LOMA, MUNICIPALITY OF BIÑAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 (An Act Amending Republic Act 7916, otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995”) and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 03-286 dated 11 November 2003 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at Barangay Loma, Municipality of Biñan, Province of Laguna, as a special economic zone, consisting of ONE HUNDRED TWENTY FIVE THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED EIGHTY SEVEN (125,387.00) square meters , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
