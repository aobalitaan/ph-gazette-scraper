PROCLAMATION NO. 1504

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY GANADO, BIÑAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, AS AN INCLUSION TO THE EXISTING LAGUNA INTERNATIONAL INDUSTRIAL PARK SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 07-438 dated 24 September 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at Barangay Ganado, Biñan, Laguna as an inclusion to the existing Laguna International Industrial Park Special Economic Zone, consisting of TEN THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED EIGHTY EIGHT (10,488) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following descriptions:
