MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 516

DECLARING THURSDAY, DECEMBER 18, 2003, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO

WHEREAS , the City of Tuguegarao was created on December 18, 1999, by virtue of Republic Act No. 8755;

WHEREAS , Thursday, December 18, 2003, marks the 4 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Tuguegarao;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, December 18, 2003, as special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 17 th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
