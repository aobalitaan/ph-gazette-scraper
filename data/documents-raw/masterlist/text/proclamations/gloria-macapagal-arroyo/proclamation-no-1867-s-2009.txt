PROCLAMATION NO. 1867

DECLARING TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 1, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, Tuesday, September 1, 2009, marks the 95 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Cotabato;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Province of Cotabato the opportunity to celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare September 1, 2009 as special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Tuesday, September 1, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
