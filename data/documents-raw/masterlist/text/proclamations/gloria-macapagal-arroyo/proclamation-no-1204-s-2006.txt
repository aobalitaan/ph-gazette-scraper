MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1204

DECLARING TUESDAY, JANUARY 2, 2007, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. TERESITA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Tuesday, January 2, 2007, marks the erection of the Shrine of the Patron Saint of the Municipality of Sta. Teresita, Batangas, St. Therese of the Child Jesus

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Sta. Teresita, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Tuesday, January 2, 2007, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Teresita, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA ExecutiveSecretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
