PROCLAMATION NO. 1929

RESERVING AS SCHOOL SITE FOR RUS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY REYNA REGENTE, MUNICIPALITY OF BANGAR, PROVINCE OF LA UNION, ISLAND OF LUZON AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES – DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION (RP-DepEd)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or any form of disposition or settlement and reserve as school site purposes for Rus Elementary School, under the administration of the Department of Education over certain parcel of land of the public domain, subject to private rights, if any there be, situated in Barangay Reyna Regente, Municipality of Bangar, Province of La Union, Island of Luzon, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
