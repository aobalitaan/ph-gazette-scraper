MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 398

DECLARING FRIDAY, JUNE 20, 2003, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN COTABATO CITY

WHEREAS, Friday, June 20, 2003, marks the 44 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Cotabato;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cotabato be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, June 20, 2003, as a special (non-working) day in Cotabato City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
