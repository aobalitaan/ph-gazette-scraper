MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 422

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT AYALA TRIANGLE, BARANGAY BEL-AIR, MAKATI CITY AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (IT) PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology (IT) Park, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 02-313 dated 26 September 2002 of the PEZA Board of Directors, a parcel of land of the private domain situated at Ayala Triangle, Barangay Bel-Air, Makati City, consisting of FIFTY THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED FORTY FIVE (50,145) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
