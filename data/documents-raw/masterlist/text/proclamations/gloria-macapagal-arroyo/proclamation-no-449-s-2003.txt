MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 449

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED AT SITIO PENGED, NANGALISAN, TUBA, PROVINCE OF BENGUET, ISLAND OF LUZON, FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE TUBA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement certain parcels of land of the public domain situated at Nangalisan, Tuba, Benguet and reserving the same as site for school and educational purposes of Tuba National High School, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 1, Psu-88651 Amd

A PARCEL OF LAND , (Lot 1, Psu-88651 Amd.), situated at Nangalisan, Tuba, Province of Benguet, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the E., and SE., along line 1‑2 by Lot 2, Psu-88651 Amd, portion of a Provincial Road; on the S., along line 2–3 by Lot 2, Psu-88650 claimed by Antonio Cachero; on the SW., along line 3-4 by Lipay River (50 m. wide); on the W., & NW., along line 4-1 by the property of Juan Doctolero.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S. 88-07’E, 1708 m. from BLLM No. 2, Bo. of Galiano, Municipality of Aringay thence;

157.97 m. to point 2;

22.68 m. to point 3;

40.00 m. to point 4;

120.60 m. to point of beginning,

containing an area of ONE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED EIGHTY NINE (1,789) SQUARE METERS , more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: Point 1 by ”x” on rock and all corners not otherwise described are PS Cyl. Conc. Mon. 15 x 40 cms. Bearings true. Date of survey on August 2, 2000, executed by Igme S. Warat and was approved on September 12, 2001.

The tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease, or other disposition until again declared alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
