MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 543

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN BARANGAY VALENCIA, QUEZON CITY AS SOCIALIZED HOUSING SITE AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN FOR DISPOSITION IN FAVOR OF THE ACTUAL AND BONAFIDE OCCUPANTS

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by the virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve a certain parcel of land, a portion of the Old Santolan Road, located in Barangay Valencia, Quezon City as socialized housing site under the administration of the National Housing Authority and declare the same open for disposition in favor of the actual and bonafide occupants, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
