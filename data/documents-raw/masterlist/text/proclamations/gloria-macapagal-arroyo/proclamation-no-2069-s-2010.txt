PROCLAMATION NO. 2069

PROPOSED PROCLAMATION CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING KNOWN AS EROS BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON A 1,941 SQUARE METER LOT LOCATED AT CORNER REAL, DR. V. LOCSIN & CERVANTES STREETS, BRGY. 3, DUMAGUETE CITY, NEGROS ORIENTAL, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 10-056 dated 15 February 2010 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the EROS Building as an Information Technology Center which was established on a lot located at Corners Real, Dr. V. Locsin & Cervantes Streets, Brgy. 3, Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental, containing an area of One Thousand Nine Hundred and Forty One (1,941) Square Meters , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
