MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1092

DECLARING TUESDAY, JULY 4, 2006 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CADIZ

WHEREAS , Tuesday, July 4, 2006, marks the 39 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Cadiz;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Tuesday, July 4, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of June , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
