PROCLAMATION NO. 1530

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JUNE 18, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DON CARLOS, BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Wednesday, June 18, 2008, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Don Carlos, Bukidnon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Don Carlos, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, June 18, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Don Carlos, Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
