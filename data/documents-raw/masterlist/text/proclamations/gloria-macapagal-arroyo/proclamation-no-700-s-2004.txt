MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 700

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 30, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MANDAUE

WHEREAS , Monday, August 30, 2004, marks the 35 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Mandaue;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mandaue be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Acting Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, August 30, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of Mandaue.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
