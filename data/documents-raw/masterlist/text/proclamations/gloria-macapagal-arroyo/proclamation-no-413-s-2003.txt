MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 413

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 30, 2003 AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, June 29, 2003, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Cagayan;

WHEREAS, this year June 29 falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to allow maximum participation of all officials of the Province of Cagayan, June 30, 2003, Monday, may be declared as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Cagayan.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, June 30, 2003, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
