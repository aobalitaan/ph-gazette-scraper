PROCLAMATION NO. 1557

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN LOCATED AT SALES ROAD CORNER ANDREWS AVENUE, VILLAMOR AIRBASE, PASAY CITY, AS TOURISM ECONOMIC ZONE WITH INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY COMPONENT PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916; otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations and Resolutions No. 02-342 dated 10 October 2002 and Resolution No. 07-349 dated 30 July 2007, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, several parcels of land to be known as Newport City Cyber Tourism Zone as tourism economic zone with information technology component located at Sales Road Corner Andrews Avenue, Villamor Airbase, Pasay City, with an aggregate area of Two Hundred Fifty One Thousand Nine Hundred Forty Three (251,943) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
