PROCLAMATION NO. 1450

DECLARING FRIDAY, JANUARY 25, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CADIZ

WHEREAS, Friday, January 25, 2003, marks the 34 th Dinagsa Festival of the City of Cadiz;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, January 25, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Deputy Executive Secretary
