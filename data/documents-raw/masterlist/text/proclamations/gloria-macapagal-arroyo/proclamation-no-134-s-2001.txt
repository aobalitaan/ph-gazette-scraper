MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 134

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 573, S. 1969, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE “MAGAT RIVER FOREST RESERVE”, LOCATED IN MASOC, BAYOMBONG, NUEVA VIZCAYA, BY EXCLUDING A PARCEL OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND RESERVING THE SAME AS A CIVIL RESERVATION FOR SCOUT CAMP SITE PURPOSES

Pursuant to Section 18 of Presidential Decree No. 705, otherwise known as the “Revised Forestry Code”, as amended, and Section 83, Chapter XII, Title V of Commonwealth Act No. 141, otherwise known as “Public Land Act”, and upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 573 dated 26 June 1969, which, among others, established the “Magat River Forest Reserve”, located in Masoc, Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya, by excluding a parcel of land embraced therein and reserving the same as a civil reservation for scout camp site purposes, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

This Proclamation is subject to private rights, if any there be, and to the following conditions:

1. The administration and management of the area covered by the reservation shall be jointly under the DENR and the Department of Education (DepEd), without prejudice however to the execution of an agreement among the DENR, the DepEd and the Provincial Government of Nueva Vizcaya for the management of the area in accordance with the purpose hereof. Accordingly, a five (5)-year development plan shall be submitted to the Office of the President by the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of Nueva Vizcaya within one (1) year hereof, furnishing the DepEd, DENR, National Mapping and Resources Information Authority, and the Forest Management Bureau, copies thereof.

2. In the event that the area is no longer needed for the purpose stated, or its development has already been abandoned for a period of two (2) years, this proclamation is automatically withdrawn, revoked or cancelled and the subject area regains its original status as part of the forest lands.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10 th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and one.
