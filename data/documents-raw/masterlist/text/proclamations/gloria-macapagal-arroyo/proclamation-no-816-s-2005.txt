MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 816

DECLARING AS CIVIL RESERVATION FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF LAGAWE CENTRAL SCHOOL, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED IN MUNICIPALITY OF LAGAWE, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby declare as civil reservation for school site purposes of Lagawe Central School under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of public domain situated at Barangay Poblacion South, Municipality of Lagawe, Province of Ifugao, Island of Luzon, containing an area of Twenty Four Thousand Two Hundred Eight (24,208) square meters, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
