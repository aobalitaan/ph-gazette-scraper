PROCLAMATION NO. 1933

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING KNOWN AS PLDT SAMPALOC BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT ESPAÑA BOULEVARD, ROTONDA, QUEZON CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Board of Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 08-050 dated 22 January 2008, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the PLDT Sampaloc Building as Information Technology Center located at España Boulevard, Rotonda, Quezon City, which was constructed on a parcel of land of the private domain with an area of Four Thousand Four Hundred Ninety and 50/100 (4,490.50) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
