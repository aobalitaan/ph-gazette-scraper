PROCLAMATION NO. 1845

DECLARING FRIDAY, JULY 31, 2009 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, Friday, July 31, 2009, marks the 109 th Anniversary of the Battle of Paye in the Province of Marinduque;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Marinduque be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GABRIEL S. CLAUDIO, Acting Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, July 31, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
