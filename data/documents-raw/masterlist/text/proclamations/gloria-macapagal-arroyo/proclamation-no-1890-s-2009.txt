PROCLAMATION NO. 1890

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 18, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Friday, September 18, 2009, marks the 57 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Zamboanga del Sur;

WHEREAS, in order to give the residents of the Province of Zamboanga del Sur, the opportunity to participate in this meaningful event, it is but fitting and proper to declare September 18, 2009 as non-working holiday.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, September 18, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
