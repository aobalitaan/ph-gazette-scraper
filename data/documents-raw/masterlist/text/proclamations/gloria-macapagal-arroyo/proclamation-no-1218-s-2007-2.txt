MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1218

DECLARING 2007 AS THE “YEAR OF THE FILIPINO UROLOGISTS”

WHEREAS , the Philippine Urological Association, Inc. (PUA), which was founded and inaugurated on September 15, 1957 for the advancement of the art and science of urology, will be celebrating its Golden Anniversary.

WHEREAS , PUA aims to deliver the highest quality of preventive, therapeutic and rehabilitative care to the Filipinos suffering from diseases and malignancies of the genitourinary tract;

WHEREAS , the diseases and malignancies of the genitourinary tract, particularly the kidney, urinary bladder and the prostate, have emerged in recent decades as among the leading causes of debility and death among Filipinos;

WHEREAS , these conditions have been estimated to rise significantly and continue to evolve as among the leading health problems as the aging population increases exponentially affecting the economy and the burden of healthcare resources allocation of the country.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare 2007 as the “Year of the Filipino Urologists”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
