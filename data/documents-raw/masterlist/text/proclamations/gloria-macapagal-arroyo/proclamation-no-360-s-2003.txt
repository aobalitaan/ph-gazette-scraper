MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 360

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 743, DATED OCTOBER 25, 1934, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE SEMI-TEMPERATE FRUIT STATION RESERVATION OF THE BUREAU OF PLANT INDUSTRY IN BAGUIO CITY, A PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN SITUATED IN RES. SEC. “K”, BAGUIO CITY AND RESERVING THE SAME FOR SOCIALIZED HOUSING PURPOSES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 743, Series of 1934 which established the Semi-temperate Fruit Station Reservation of the Bureau of Plant Industry in Baguio City by segregating a portion of the land embraced therein containing an area of Nine Thousand (9,000) Square Meters and reserving the same for socialized housing purposes under the disposition of the National Housing Authority, subject to private rights, if any there be, and authorizing the issuance of a Special Patent over the said portion of land in favor of the National Housing Authority, which portion is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 8-A, IR-CAR-393 Amd

A PARCEL OF LAND, Lot 8-A, IR-CAR-393 Amd , situated at Res. Sec. “K”, City of Baguio, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the SE., along line 8–9 by Lot 8-C, of the Amd. Plan; along lines 9-10-11 and on the S., along line 11–12 and on the SW., along line 12–13, and on the W., along line 13-1 by Lot 8-B of the Amd. Plan; on the NW., along lines 1-2-3 and on the N., along line 3–4 and on the NE., along lines 4-5-6 by Dangwa Road portion of Lot 8-B of the Amd Plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 42 ° 07′ W., 464.56 m. from Triangulation Station “CROSS” Baguio Townsite, thence:

beginning, containing an area of NINE THOUSAND (9,000) SQUARE METERS , more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by PS Cyl. Conc, Mons. 15 x 40 cms. Bearings true; Date of Survey August 25-26, 1999 executed by Engr. Emmanuel M. Pacleb and approved on August 21, 2000.

The_ Department of Environment and Natural Resources is hereby authorized to issue a Special Patent over the parcel of land reserved herein in favor of the National Housing Authority.

This proclamation is subject to a minimum road right of way requirements of the Department of Public Works and Highways pursuant to E.O. No. 621.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
