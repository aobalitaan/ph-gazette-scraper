MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1769

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 8, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS , June 6, 2009, marks the 57 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Zamboanga del Norte;

WHEREAS , to enable the people of the Province of Zamboanga del Norte, celebrate the occasion, the Governor requested that Monday, June 8, 2009, be declared as special (non-working) day;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Zamboanga del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, June 8, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of May , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
