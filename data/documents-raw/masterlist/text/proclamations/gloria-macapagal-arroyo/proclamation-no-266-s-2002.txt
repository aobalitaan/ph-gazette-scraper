MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 266

DECLARING MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 30, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MASBATE

WHEREAS , Monday, September 23, 2002, marks the 2 nd Anniversary of the Cityhood of Masbate;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of the City of Masbate be given full opportunity to celebrate its 2 nd Charter Day with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, September 30, 2002, as a special non-working holiday in the City of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
