PROPOSED PROCLAMATION CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING KNOWN AS EPLDT VENTUS JUPITER BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON AN 11,864 SQUARE METER LOT LOCATED AT NICANOR GARCIA STREET CORNER JUPITER STREET, BARANGAY BEL-AIR, MAKATI CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748
