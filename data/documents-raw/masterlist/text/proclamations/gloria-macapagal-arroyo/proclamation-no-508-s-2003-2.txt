MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 508

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED AT BARANGAY LARAP, MUNICIPALITY OF JOSE PANGANIBAN, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, ISLAND OF LUZON, AS SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT. NO 8748.

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended, and, upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Special Economic Zone, pursuant to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 99-168 dated 30 June 1999 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, a parcel of land situated at Barangay Larap, Municipality of Jose Panganiban, Province of Camarines Norte, with an area of THREE HUNDRED THOUSAND (300,000) SQUARE METERS , more or less as defined by the following technical descriptions:
