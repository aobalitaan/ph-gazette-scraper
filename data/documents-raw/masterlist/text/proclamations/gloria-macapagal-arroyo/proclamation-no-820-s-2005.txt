MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 820

DECLARING AS CIVIL RESERVATION FOR MULTI-PURPOSE DEVELOPMENT PROJECT SITES PURPOSES UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE PROVINCIAL GOVERNMENT OF IFUGAO, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED IN BRGY. POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF LAGAWE, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I , GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby declare as civil reservation for multi-purpose development project sites purposes under the administration of the Provincial Government of Ifugao, certain parcels of land of public domain located in Brgy. Poblacion, Municipality of Lagawe, Province of Ifugao, Island of Luzon, containing an aggregate area of Sixteen Thousand Five Hundred Twenty Five (16,525) square meters, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
