PROCLAMATION NO. 1563

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING KNOWN AS LOPUE’S EAST I.T. CENTRE LOCATED ALONG BURGOS STREET CORNER CARLOS HILADO NATIONAL HIGHWAY, BACOLOD CITY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916 (The Special Economic Zone Act of 1995), as amended by Republic Act No. 8748, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 06-066 dated 27 February 2006, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Lopue’s East I.T. Centre as an Information Technology Center, which stands on several parcels of land of private domain located at Burgos Street corner Carlos Hilado National Highway, Bacolod City, Negros Occidental, with an aggregate area of Ten Thousand Nine Hundred Seventy Six (10,976) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
