PROCLAMATION NO. 1473

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT 105 H. V. DE LA COSTA STREET, SALCEDO VILLAGE, MAKATI CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 as AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 07-111 dated 15 March 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Trafalgar Plaza as an Information Technology Center located at 105 H.V. de la Costa Street, Salcedo Village, Makati City, which stands on certain parcel of land of the private domain with an area of One Thousand One Hundred Eighty Eight (1,188) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
