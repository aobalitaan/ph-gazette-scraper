MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 631

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 590 DATED MARCH 29, 2004, WHICH RESERVED A PORTION OF LAND SITUATED IN PACO, CITY OF MANILA AS HOUSING SITE OF THE OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF BUDGET AND MANAGEMENT (DBM), BY DECLARING THE AREA AS SITE OF THE DBM OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES QUARTERS AND PLACING THE SAME UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION AND MANAGEMENT OF THE DBM

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 590, dated March 29, 2004 which reserved an area of FIVE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED SEVENTEEN (5,517) square meters , more or less, situated in the District of Paco, City of Manila as housing site of the officials and employees of the Department of Budget and Management (DBM), by declaring the area as DBM Officials and Employees Quarters and placing the same under the administration and management of DBM, subject to final survey, which portion is more particularly described as follows:

A PARCEL OF LAND, (Portion of Lot No. 7, before Lot No. 1-New-C, Block No. 933, Cad Case No. 64, Cadastral survey of the City of Manila, G.L.R.O. Cad, Record No. 319), situated in the District of Paco, City of Manila.

Bounded on the S.E., along 1-2-3 by EXISTING ROAD; on the S.W., along line 3-4 by Portion of Lot 7 before Lot No. 1-New-C, Blk 933, Cad-13; on the N.W., along lines 4-5 by PASIG RIVER; and on the N. E., along lines 5-6-7-1 by portion of Lot 7, before Lot No. 1-New-C, Blk 933, Cad 13, containing an area of Five Thousand Five Hundred Seventeen (5,517) square meters , more or less.

The DBM, as administrator of the property may enter into any lawful agreement for the development of the area herein reserved. For the effective administration of the property, the DBM is hereby authorized to cause the issuance of a separate title in the name of the Republic of the Philippines covering the area subject of this Proclamation.

The DBM Employees and Officials Quarters shall be used as transient quarters/dwellings of its employees and officials on a lease basis only. Related to this, DBM shall issue the guidelines in the allocation, use and appropriate rentals of the DBM Employees and Officials Quarters.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of May , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
