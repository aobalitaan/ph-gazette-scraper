PROCLAMATION NO. 1807

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 63 DATED AUGUST 6, 1925 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE BAGUIO GOVERNMENT CENTER RESERVATION IN RES. SEC. “A”, ENGINEER’S HILL, BAGUIO CITY BY SEGREGATING A PORTION THEREOF AND RESERVING THE SAME AS BUILDING SITE FOR THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION (DPWH-CAR)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby segregate from the operation of Proclamation No. 63, Series of 1925, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at Residence Section “A”, Engineer’s Hill, Baguio City, Island of Luzon and reserving the same as Building Site for the Department of Public Works and Highways – Cordillera Administrative Region (DPWH-CAR), subject to the private rights, if there be any, covering the land which is more particularly described as follows:
