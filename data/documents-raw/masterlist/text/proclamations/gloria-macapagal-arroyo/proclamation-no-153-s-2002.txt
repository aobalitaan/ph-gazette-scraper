MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 153

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 13, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING DAY IN THE CITY OF PARAÑAQUE

WHEREAS , Republic Act No. 8507 converting the Municipality of Parañaque into a highly-urbanized city was signed into law on February 13, 1998;

WHEREAS , Wednesday, February 13, 2002, marks the Fourth Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Parañaque;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of the City of Parañaque be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, February 13, 2002, as a special non-working day in the City of Parañaque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12 th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
