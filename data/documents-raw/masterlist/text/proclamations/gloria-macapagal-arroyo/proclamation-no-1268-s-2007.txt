MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1268

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, APRIL 11, 2007, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BOCAUE, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS , Wednesday, April 11, 2007, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Bocaue, Province of Bulacan;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bocaue, Province of Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, April 11, 2007, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bocaue, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
