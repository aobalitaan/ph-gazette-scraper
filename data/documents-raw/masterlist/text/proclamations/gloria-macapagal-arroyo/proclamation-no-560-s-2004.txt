MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 560

RESERVING FOR RECREATIONAL SITE FOR THE CITY GOVERNMENT OF ZAMBOANGA CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT PETTIT BARRACKS, ZAMBOANGA CITY, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement, and reserve for the use of the City of Zamboanga, as Recreational, Parks and Tourist Site, certain parcel of the public domain located in Pettit Barracks, Zamboanga City, Island of Mindanao, subject to actual survey and delineation on ground and to private rights, if there be any, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
