PROCLAMATION NO. 1456

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 22, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN PEDRO, LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Friday, February 22, 2008, marks the celebration of “San Pedro Day” and the feast day of the “Chair of Saint Peter the Apostle” in the Municipality of San Pedro, Laguna;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San Pedro, Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, February 22, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Pedro, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
