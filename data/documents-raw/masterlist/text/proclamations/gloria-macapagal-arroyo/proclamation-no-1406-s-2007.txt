MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1406

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 5, 2007, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS , Wednesday, December 5, 2007, marks the 42 nd Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, IGNACIO R. BUNYE , Acting Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, December 5, 2007, as special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) IGNACIO R. BUNYE Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
