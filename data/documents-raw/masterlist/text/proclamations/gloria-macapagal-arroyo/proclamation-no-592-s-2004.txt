MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 592

DECLARING APRIL 3, 2004 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUISIANA, LAGUNA

WHEREAS , April 3, 2004 marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Luisiana, Laguna;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Luisiana, Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in the municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , do hereby declare April 3, 2004 as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Luisiana, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 30th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
