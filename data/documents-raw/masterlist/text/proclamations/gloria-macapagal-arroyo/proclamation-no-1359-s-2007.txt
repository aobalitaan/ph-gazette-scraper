PROCLAMATION NO. 1359

DECLARING FRIDAY, AUGUST 24, 2007 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN ”

WHEREAS , the City of Kidapawan will be celebrating the 6 th Timpupo Festival on August 18 to 24, 2007, with the theme “Timpupo…Hando…Pasasalamat”;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Kidapawan be given gull opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, August 24, as special (non-working) day in the City of Kidapawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
