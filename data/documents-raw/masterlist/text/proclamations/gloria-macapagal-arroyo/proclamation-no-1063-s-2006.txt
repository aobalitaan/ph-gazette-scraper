MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1063

DECLARING MONDAY, MAY 22, 2006 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN GINGOOG CITY

WHEREAS , the City of Gingoog celebrates its annual fiesta on Monday, May 22, 2006, in honor of their patroness, Sta. Rite de Casia;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL‑ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, May 22, 2006, as special (non-working) day in Gingoog City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
