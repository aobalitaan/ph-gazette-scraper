PROCLAMATION NO. 1748

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR ENERGY PARK SITE PURPOSES OF THE CITY GOVERNMENT OF TAGUM, SITUATED IN APOKON, TAGUM CITY, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale disposition or settlement and reserve for Energy Park site purposes of the City Government of Tagum, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Apokon, Tagum City, province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows to wit:
