MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 900

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THE UNIONBANK PLAZA BUILDING SITUATED AT MERALCO AVENUE CORNERS ONYX AND SAPPHIRE STREETS, ORTIGAS CENTER, ORANBO, PASIG CITY, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY BUILDING PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Building subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolutions Nos. 00-411 and 03-348 dated 29 December 2000 and 22 December 2003 respectively, of the PEZA Board of Directors, the UnionBank Plaza building, which stands on a 2,093-square meter lot, more or less, located along Meralco Avenue Corners Onyx and Sapphire Streets, Ortigas Center, Oranbo, Pasig City, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
