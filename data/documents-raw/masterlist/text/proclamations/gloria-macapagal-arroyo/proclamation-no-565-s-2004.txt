MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 565

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, MARCH 10, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TANAUAN, BATANGAS

WHEREAS , Wednesday, March 10, 2004, marks the 3 rd Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Tanauan, Batangas;

WHEREAS , March 9 marks the birth anniversary of former President Jose P. Laurel;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanauan be given full opportunity to celebrate these two (2) occasions with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, March 10, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of Tanauan, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
