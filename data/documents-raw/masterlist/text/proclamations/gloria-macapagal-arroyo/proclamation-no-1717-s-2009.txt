PROCLAMATION NO. 1717

DECLARING SATURDAY, JANUARY 31, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANOLO FORTICH, BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Saturday, January 31, 2009, the Municipality of Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon marks the observance of Public Servants’ Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Saturday, January 31, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
