MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 481

DECLARING TUESDAY, OCTOBER 21, 2003, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIGCAWAYAN, COTABATO

WHEREAS, Tuesday, October 21, 2003, marks the Golden Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Pigcawayan, Cotabato; and

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pigcawayan, Cotabato, be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Tuesday, October 21, 2003, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pigcawayan, Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2 nd day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
