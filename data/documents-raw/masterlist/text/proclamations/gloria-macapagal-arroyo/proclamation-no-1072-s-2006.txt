PROCLAMATION NO. 1072

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THE MARVIN PLAZA BUILDING, LOCATED AT 2153 CHINO ROCES AVENUE CORNER HERRERA STREET, MAKATI CITY, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY BUILDING PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the. Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology (IT) Building, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolutions No. 05-104 dated 15 April 2005, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), the Marvin Plaza Building, which was established on a lot, located at 2153 Chino Roces Avenue corner Herrera Street, Makati City, containing an area of THREE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED (3,500) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
