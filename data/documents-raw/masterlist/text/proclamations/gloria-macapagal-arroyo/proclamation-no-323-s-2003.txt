MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 323

NAMING THE EXECUTIVE SECRETARY’S OFFICE, PREMIER GUEST HOUSE, MALACAÑANG PALACE AS THE CORAZON C. AQUINO ROOM

WHEREAS, under Resolution No. 2, dated May 6, 1998, the National Historical Institute proclaimed the Malacañang Palace as National Historical Landmark;

WHEREAS, every past administration in the country reflects a particular period in our nation’s rich history; and

WHEREAS, it is only fitting and proper that Malacañang Palace, residence and Office of the President of the Philippines, should recognize the historic connection past presidents have with Malacañang Palace.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby name as Corazon C. Aquino Room the Executive Secretary’s Office in the Premier Guest House where she used to work as President from 1986-1992, in recognition of her great service to the nation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
