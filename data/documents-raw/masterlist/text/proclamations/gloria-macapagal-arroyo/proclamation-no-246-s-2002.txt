MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 246

DECLARING MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 02, 2002, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO FOR THE CELEBRATION OF ITS 93 RD CHARTER ANNIVERSARY

WHEREAS , Sunday, September 01, 2002 marks the 93 rd Charter Anniversary of the City of Baguio;

WHEREAS , in order for the people of this city to actively participate and give meaning to this special celebration, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of the City of Baguio has requested that the aforesaid date be declared on Monday, September 02, 2002;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Baguio be given the full opportunity to celebrate their Charter Anniversary with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, September 02, 2002, as a special day in the City of Baguio.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
