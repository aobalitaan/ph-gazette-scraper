PROCLAMATION NO. 1698

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH SHALL STAND ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT NO. 2305 CHINO ROCES AVENUE EXTENSION, MAKATI CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 08-080 dated 15 February 2008 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Eco Plaza as an Information Technology Center which shall stand on a parcel of land of the private domain located at No. 2305 Chino Roces Avenue Extension, Makati City, containing an aggregate area of Five Thousand (5,000) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
