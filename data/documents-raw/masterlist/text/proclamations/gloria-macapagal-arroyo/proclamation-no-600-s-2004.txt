MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 600

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY CAMBANOGOY, NOW ASUNCION, DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Department of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the power vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdrawn from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserved for school site purposes under the administration of the Department of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Cambanogoy, now Asuncion, Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
