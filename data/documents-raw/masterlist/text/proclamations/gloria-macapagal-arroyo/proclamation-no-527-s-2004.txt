MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 527

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE CITY OF LEGAZPI, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY LEGAZPI PORT, CITY OF LEGAZPI, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby set aside and reserve for market site purposes of the City of Legazpi, Island of Luzon, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 20752, CAD-47 IDENTICAL TO LOT 19257-D, CDS-001368

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 20752, Cad-47, identical to Lot 19257-D, Cds-001368, Legazpi Cadastre), situated in Barangay Legazpi Port, City of Legazpi, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the SE., along lines 1-2-3, by Lot 20753, Cad-47, Legazpi Cadastre (Circumferential Road); on the SE., and SW., along lines 3-4-5-6, by Lot 770-A, Psd-05-001887; on the NW., along lines 6-7, by Lot 20748, Cad-47, Legazpi Cadastre; and on the NE., along lines 7-8-1, by Lot 1058-G, Psd-05-001887.
