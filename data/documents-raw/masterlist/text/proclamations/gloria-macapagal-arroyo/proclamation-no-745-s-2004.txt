MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 745

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING, TO BE KNOWN AS ROBINSONS PLACE NOVALICHES IT BUILDING, WHICH STANDS ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT 1199 QUIRINO HIGHWAY CORNER MALIGAYA ROAD, BARANGAY PASONG PUTIK, NOVALICHES, QUEZON CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY BUILDING PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Building, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution Nos. 00-411 and 04-211 dated 29 December 2000 and 25 June 2004, respectively, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), the Robinsons Place Novaliches IT Building , which stands on a parcel of land of the private domain, situated at 1199 Quirino Highway corner Maligaya Road, Barangay Pasong Putik, Novaliches, Quezon City with an area of One Hundred Four Thousand Four Hundred Seventy Four (104,474) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
