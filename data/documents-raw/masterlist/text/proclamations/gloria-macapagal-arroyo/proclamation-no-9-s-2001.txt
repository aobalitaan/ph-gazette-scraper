MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 9

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 26, 2001, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN ZAMBOANGA CITY

WHEREAS, Monday, February 26, 2001, marks the 64 th Charter Anniversary of Zamboanga City;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of Zamboanga City be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, February 26, 2001, as a special day in Zamboanga City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 23 rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand one.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) RENATO S. DE VILLA Executive Secretary
