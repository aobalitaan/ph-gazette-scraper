PROCLAMATION NO. 1657

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 875, SERIES OF 1996, RESERVING A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SEGREGATING A PORTION THEREOF AND RESERVING THE SAME AS SITE OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP) SITUATED AT BARANGAY MAGSAYSAY, MUNICIPALITY OF SAGUDAY, PROVINCE OF QUIRINO, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserve for Headquarters/Office site purposes of the Philippine National Police of Saguday, Quirino, subject to private rights, if any there be, a parcel of land more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 25798-A, Csd-AF-02-002315-A

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot No. 25798-A, Block 18, Csd-AF-02-002315-A, which is equivalent to Lot 66964, Cad. 211, being a portion of Lot 25798, Cad. 211 as surveyed for Philippine National Police, situated in Barangay Magsaysay, Municipality of Saguday, Province of Quirino, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the N., along line 1-2 by Lot 25798-B; on the E., along line 2-3 by Calle Espiritu; on the S and W., along line 3-4-1 by Lot 25798-B all of the subdivision plan Csd-AF-02-002319-D.

Beginning at point marked “1” on plan being S. 29-45’E., 521.77m from BLLM No. 114, Cad. 211, thence:

containing an area of FIVE HUNDRED (500) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on plan and are marked on the ground by PS cyl. conc. mons. 15 x 40 cms. in diameter. Bearing true. The Subdivision Survey was executed by Geodetic Engr. Marcos Dacanay on September 2, 2003 and was approved on November 9, 2004.

The tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease, or other disposition until again declared as alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done the in the City of Manila this 28 th day of October, in the year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Eight.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:

Executive Secretary
