MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 553

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 27, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS , Friday, February 27, 2004, marks the 65 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of General Santos and Kalilangan Festival;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of the City of General Santos be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, February 27, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of General Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 18th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
