MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 608

RESERVING FOR OFFICE SITE PURPOSES OF THE PROVINCIAL SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY CENTER A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARRIO CALUMPANG, GENERAL SANTOS CITY, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdrawn from sale or settlement and reserve for office site purposes of the Provincial Science and Technology Center, under the administration of Department of Science and Technology, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain and situated at Barrio Calumpang, General Santos City, Island of Mindanao, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

Block 11-A, Csd-11-013082-D (Provincial Science and Technology Center)

A PARCEL OF LAND, Block 11-A, Csd-11-013082-D, portion of Block 11 Ts-11-000089-D, identical to Lot 801, Ts-11-000089-D, situated at Barrio Calumpang, General Santos City, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the N., along lines 2-3 by Road; on the E., and S., along lines 3-4-5 by block 11-B, of the subdivision plan; and on the W., along lines 5-1-2 by Road. Beginning from appoint marked “1” on plan being S., 32 deg. 23′ W., 2,516.72 m from BLBM No. 1, Makar, Thence:

beginning, containing an area of ONE THOUSAND FIFTY SIX (1,056) square meters, more or less. Point 1 and 2 are “Old PS” and the rest are “PS” Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 40 cm. Bearings true. Date of original survey January 6 to March 10, 1992 and that of the Subdivision Survey October 8, 1996 by Geodetic Engineer Anastacio Navato, Jr. and approved on December 23, 1996

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of April in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
