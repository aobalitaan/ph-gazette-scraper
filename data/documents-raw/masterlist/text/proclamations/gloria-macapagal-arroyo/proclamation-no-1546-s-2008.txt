PROCLAMATION NO. 1546

DECLARING MONDAY, JULY 7, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAMBAJAO, PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN

WHEREAS, Sunday, July 6, 2008, marks the Charter Day anniversary of the Municipality of Mambajao, Province of Camiguin;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Mambajao, Province of Camiguin, celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, July 7, 2008, be declared as special (non-working) day instead of July 6, 2008, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Mambajao, Province of Camiguin, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, July 7, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mambajao, Province of Camiguin.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
