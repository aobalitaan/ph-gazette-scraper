MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 640

DECLARING JUNE 19, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA AND PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS , June 19, 2004 marks the 143 rd birth anniversary of Dr. Jose P. Rizal, our national hero;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the residents of the City of Calamba, his birthplace, and the Province of Laguna, should be given full opportunity to celebrate the day with appropriate ceremonies in keeping with his ideals and teachings.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare June 19, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba and Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
