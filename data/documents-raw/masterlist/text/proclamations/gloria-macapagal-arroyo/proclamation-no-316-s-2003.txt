MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 316

DECLARING MONDAY, JANUARY 20, 2003, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF LIPA

WHEREAS, Monday, January 20, 2003, the City of Lipa celebrates its city fiesta in honor of its patron saint St. Sebastian ;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the City of Lipa be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, January 20, 2003 as a special day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and three.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
