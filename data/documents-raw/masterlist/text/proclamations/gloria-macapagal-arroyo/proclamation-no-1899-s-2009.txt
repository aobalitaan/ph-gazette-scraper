PROCLAMATION NO. 1899

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE BALONG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY BALONG, MUNICIPALITY OF TABUK, PROVINCE OF KALINGA, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the power vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition, settlement, and reserve for school site purposes for Balong Elementary School, under the administration of the Department of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated at Barangay Balong, Municipality of Tabuk, Province of Kalinga, Island of Luzon, particularly described as follows:
