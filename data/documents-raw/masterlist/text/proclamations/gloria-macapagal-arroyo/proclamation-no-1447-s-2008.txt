PROCLAMATION NO. 1447

PROCLAMATION AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL PROCLAMATION NO.2292, DATED MAY 21, 1983, RESERVING FOR SETTLEMENT PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND EXCLUDING A PORTION OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AS MILITARY CAMP OF THE 8 TH INFANTRY BATTALION OF THE PHILIPPINE ARMY SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA RITA, WESTERN SAMAR
