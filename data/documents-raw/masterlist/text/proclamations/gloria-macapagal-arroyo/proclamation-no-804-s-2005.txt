MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 804

RESERVING FOR CEMETERY SITE PURPOSES OF THE CITY OF TAGUM, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY LA FILIPINA, TAGUM CITY, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for cemetery site purposes of the City of Tagum, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at Barangay La Filipina, Tagum City, Island of Mindanao, under the administration of the City Government of Tagum, containing an area of Sixteen Thousand Six Hundred Thirty One (16,631) square meters , subject to private rights, if any there by, which parcel of land are more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 28, Ccs-331
