PROCLAMATION NO. 1677

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT E. RODRIGUEZ JR. AVENUE (C-5 ROAD) CORNER EASTWOOD AVENUE, BARANGAY BAGUMBAYAN, QUEZON CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Board Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 08-315 dated 26 June 2008 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, a building known as MDC 100 as Information Technology Center located at E. Rodriguez Jr. Avenue (C-5 Road) corner Eastwood Avenue, Barangay Bagumbayan, Quezon City, which stands on a parcel of land of the private domain with an area of Three Thousand Three Hundred Thirty Three and 33/100 (3,333.33) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
