MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 369

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JUNE 18, 2003, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, Wednesday, June 18, 2003, marks the 37th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Ifugao;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ifugao be given full opportunity to celebrate the event with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, June 18, 2003, as a special day in the Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
