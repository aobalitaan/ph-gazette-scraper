PROCLAMATION NO. 989

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY MACTAN, LAPU-LAPU CITY, CEBU AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 (An Act Amending Republic Act 7916, otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995”) and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000, as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004 and Resolution No. 05-045 dated 14 February 2005 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at Barangay Mactan, Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu, as Information Technology Park, consisting of Nineteen Thousand One Hundred Sixty Seven (19,167) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
