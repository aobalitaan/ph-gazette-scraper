MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 534

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 4, 2004 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, Wednesday, February 4, 2004 marks the 250 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of San Fernando;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Fernando, Pampanga be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, February 4, 2004, as a special (non-working) day in the city of San Fernando, Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16th day of January , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: ( Sgd .) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
