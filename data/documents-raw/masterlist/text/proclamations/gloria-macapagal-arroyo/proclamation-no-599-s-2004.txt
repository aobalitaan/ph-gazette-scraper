MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 599

AMENDING PROCLAMATION 423, DATED JULY 12, 1957 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE FORT ANDRES BONIFACIO (FORMERLY FORT WILLIAM MCKINLEY) MILITARY RESERVATION, BY SEGREGATING CERTAIN PORTIONS OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY LOWER BICUTAN, TAGUIG, METRO MANILA AND RESERVING THE SAME AS SITE OF THE SPORTS COMPLEX OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUIG

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation 423, which established the Fort Andres Bonifacio Military Reservation, by segregating a certain portion of the land embraced therein located at Brgy. Lower Bicutan, Municipality of Taguig, Metro Manila, and reserving the same as site of the Sports Complex of the Municipality of Taguig, Metro Manila subject to private rights, if there be any, and to final survey, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTIONS

A PARCEL OF LAND (DESCRIBED AS LOT 2-B) being a portion of Lot 8057 MCadm 590-D Taguig Cadastral Mapping, under Proclamation No. 7227, situated in Barangay Lower Bicutan, Municipality of Taguig, Metro Manila, Island of Luzon, bounded on the East along Lines 1-2-3 by lot 2-A (Proposed Site of the DPWH Housing Project); on the South along Line 3-4 by Road Lot 15, Psd-13-002579 (Gen. Santos); on the West along Lines 4-5-6 by Proclamation No. 376, National Science Development Board Building, and on the North along Line 6-1 by Lot 2, Swo-13-000258, Upper Bicutan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of April , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
