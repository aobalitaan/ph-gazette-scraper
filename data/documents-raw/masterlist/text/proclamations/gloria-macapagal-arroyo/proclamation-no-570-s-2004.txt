MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 570

DECLARING TIMPOONG AND HIBOK-HIBOK MOUNTAIN RANGE SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF MAMBAJAO, MAHINOG, SAGAY AND CATARMAN, PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN AS A PROTECTED AREA AND ITS PERIPHERAL AREAS AS BUFFER ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT 7586 (NIPAS ACT OF 1992) AND SHALL BE KNOWN AS TIMPOONG-HIBOK-HIBOK NATURAL MONUMENT.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested upon me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby set aside and declare Timpoong and Hibok-Hibok Mountain Range which is a parcel of land situated in the Municipalities of Mambajao, Mahinog, Sagay and Catarman, Province of Camiguin as Protected Area under the category of Natural Monument, subject to private rights, and without prejudice to the rights of the indigenous people as provided for in R.A. No. 8371, DENR Administrative Order 93-02 and other related rules and regulations, if any there be, as described in the DENR NIPAS Map No. R0-10-03:

The boundaries of the Natural Monument and its buffer zone shall be subject to further ground survey and delineation.

The Timpoong‑Hibok-Hibok Natural Monument and its Buffer Zone shall be under the management jurisdiction of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and shall be administered in accordance to the provisions of the NIPAS Act and its Implementing Rules and Regulations.

All contracts, permits or licenses for the extraction or utilization of natural resources already existing prior to this Proclamation shall, subject to national interest, be respected until their expiration.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
