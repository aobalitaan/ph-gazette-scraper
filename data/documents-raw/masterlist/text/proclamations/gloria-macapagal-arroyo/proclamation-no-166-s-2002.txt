MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 166

DECLARING THURSDAY, MARCH 21, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING DAY IN THE CITY OF VICTORIAS, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS , Thursday, March 21, 2002, marks the 4th Charter Day Anniversary of the City of Victorias into a component city of Negros Occidental;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Victorias be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Thursday, March 21, 2002, as a special non-working day in the City of Victorias.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 15 th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
