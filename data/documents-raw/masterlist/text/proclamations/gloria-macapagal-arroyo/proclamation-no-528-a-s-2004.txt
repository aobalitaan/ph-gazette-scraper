MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 528-A

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 7, 2004, AS SPECIAL HOLIDAY IN ZAMBOANGA CITY

WHEREAS, the death of Mayor Maria Clara Lobregat is a great loss to the people of Zamboanga City and has caused deep sorrow among them; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of Zamboanga City be given the chance to pay their last respect to the late mayor.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, January 7, 2004, as special holiday in Zamboanga City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: ( Sgd .) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
