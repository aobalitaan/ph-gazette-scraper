PROCLAMATION NO. 1525

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 16, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur celebrates its 60 th Foundation Day anniversary on Monday, June 16, 2008;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL–ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, June 16, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
