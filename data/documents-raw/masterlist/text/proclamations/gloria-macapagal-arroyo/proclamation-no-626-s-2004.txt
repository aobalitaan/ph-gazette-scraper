PROCLAMATION NO . 626

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Special Economic Zone, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 03-322 dated 11 December 2003 of the PEZA Board of Directors, parcels of land situated at Food Terminal Complex, East Service Road, Barangay Bicutan, Taguig, Metro Manila, consisting an aggregate area of TWO HUNDRED FORTY THREE THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED SIXTY ONE (243,961) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
