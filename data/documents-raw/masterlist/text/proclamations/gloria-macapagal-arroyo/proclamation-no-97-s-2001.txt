MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 97

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM SEPTEMBER 10 TO 16, 2001, AS “PRODUCTION MANAGEMENT WEEK”

WHEREAS, the Production Management Association of the Philippines (PROMAP), a non-partisan, and non-stock organization was established to promote industrial development through better and more efficient management of the production system;

WHEREAS, PROMAP will be holding its 14 th National Convention to be held at The Westin Philippine Plaza Hotel on September 13 to 14, 2001, with the theme: “IN TOUCH WITH GLOBAL TECHNOLOGY IN 2001 AND BEYOND”;

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus national attention on current issues in our economy specially in the manufacturing sector;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from September 10 to 16, 2001, as “Production Management Week” under the auspices of the Production Management Association of the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and one.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
