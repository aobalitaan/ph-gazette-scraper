PROCLAMATION NO. 1433

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT MALHACAN ROAD, MALHACAN, MEYCAUAYAN CITY, BULACAN AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 07-377 dated 08 August 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at Malhacan Road, Malhacan, Meycauayan City, Bulacan, as Information Technology Park, consisting of ONE HUNDRED FORTY SIX THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED EIGHTY THREE (146,383) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
