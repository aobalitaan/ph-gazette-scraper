MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 986

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED AT CEBU BUSINESS PARK, CEBU CITY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Park, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 05-333 dated 29 September 2005 and Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004 and further by Resolution No. 05-314 dated 13 September 2005, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), certain parcels of land located at Cebu Business Park, Cebu City, Province of Cebu, containing an area of FIVE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED THIRTY EIGHT (5,138) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
