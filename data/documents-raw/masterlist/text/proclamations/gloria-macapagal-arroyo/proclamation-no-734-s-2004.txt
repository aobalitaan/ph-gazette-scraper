MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 734

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 1, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAINTA, RIZAL

WHEREAS, Wednesday, December 1, 2004, celebrates the 433 rd Founding Anniversary of the Municipality of Cainta; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Cainta be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, December 1, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cainta, Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By authority of the President: ( Sgd .) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
