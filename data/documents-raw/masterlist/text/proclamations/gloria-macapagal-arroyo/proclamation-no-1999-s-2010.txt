PROCLAMATION NO. 1999

RESERVING FOR PUBLIC MARKET SITE PURPOSE, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGSINGAL, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or any other form of disposition or settlement and reserve for public market site purposes, under the administration of the Local Government situated in the Municipality of Magsingal, Province of Ilocos Sur, Island of Luzon, which the parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
