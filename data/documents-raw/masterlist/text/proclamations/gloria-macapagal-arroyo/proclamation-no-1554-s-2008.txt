PROCLAMATION NO. 1554

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT CABANGAAN POINT, BARANGAY CAWAG, MUNICIPALITY OF SUBIC, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES TO FORM PART OF THE EXISTING SUBIC SHIPYARD SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748
