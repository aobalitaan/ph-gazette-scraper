PROCLAMATION NO. 1839

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING KNOWN AS MARIA CRISTINA BUILDING ESTABLISHED ON PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED ALONG OSMEÑA BOULEVARD, FUENTE OSMEÑA CAPITOL SITE, CEBU CITY AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000, as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004 and Resolution No. 06-574 dated 18 December 2006, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Maria Cristina Building with a gross floor area of 6,960 square meters, more or less, established on parcels of land located along Osmeña Boulevard, Fuente Osmeña Capitol Site, Cebu City, containing an area of ONE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED FORTY (1,740) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
