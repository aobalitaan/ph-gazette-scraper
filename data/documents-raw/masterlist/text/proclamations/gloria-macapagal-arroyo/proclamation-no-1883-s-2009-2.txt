PROCLAMATION NO. 1883

RESERVING AS SCHOOL SITE FOR SANCHEZ MIRA SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY SANTOR, MUNICIPALITY OF SANCHEZ MIRA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement a certain parcel of land of the public domain and reserve as school site purposes for Sanchez Mira School of Arts and Trades, under the administration of the Department of Education (DepEd), subject to private rights if any there be, situated in Barangay Santor, Municipality of Sanchez Mira, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
