PROCLAMATION NO. 1506

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH SHALL STAND ON A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN LOCATED AT ARCHBISHOP REYES AVENUE, CEBU BUSINESS PARK, MABOLO, CEBU CITY AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 06-389 dated 25 August 2006 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the FLB Corporate Center as in Information Technology Center which shall stand on a parcel of land of the private domain located at Archbishop Reyes Avenue, Cebu Business park, Mabolo, Cebu City with an area of Two Thousand Three Hundred Thirty Six (2,336) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
