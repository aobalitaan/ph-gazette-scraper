MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1381

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY APAS, CEBU CITY, PROVINCE OF CEBU AS AN ANNEXATION TO THE EXISTING ASIATOWN IT PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916 (The Special Economic Zone Act of 1995), as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 , I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004 and Resolution No. 06-479 dated 31 October 2006 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at Barangay Apas, Cebu City, as an annexation to the existing Asiatown IT Park consisting of an area of TWELVE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED THIRTY THREE (12,633) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
