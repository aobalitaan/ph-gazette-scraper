PROCLAMATION NO. 1448

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT PRINCETON STREET CORNER SHAW BOULEVARD, MANDALUYONG CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 07-420 dated 06 September 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Sun Plaza as an Information Technology Center which stands on a certain parcel of land of private domain located at Princeton Street comer Shaw Boulevard, Mandaluyong City, containing an area of One Thousand Two Hundred Fifty Seven (1,257) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
