PROCLAMATION NO. 1792

RESERVING FOR THE SITE OF SALUAM HIGH SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY BALUAN, GENERAL SANTOS CITY, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES – DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement, reserving for school site purposes of the Baluan High School, and granting ownership thereof in favor of the Republic of the Philippines – Department of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, more particularly bounded and described as follows:

Lot No. 556-A, Csd-12-007854-D
