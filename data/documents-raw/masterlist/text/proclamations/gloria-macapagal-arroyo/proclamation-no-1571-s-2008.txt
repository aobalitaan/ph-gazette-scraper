MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1571

RESERVING FOR OCCIDENTAL MINDORO POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY MURTHA, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO, ISLAND OF MINDORO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserve for Occidental Mindoro Polytechnic College site purposes certain parcels of land of the public domain, situated in Barangay Murtha, Municipality of San Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro, Island of Mindoro, under the administration of the Occidental Mindoro National College, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:

LOT NO. 3, Swo-41202

A parcel of land (Lot No. 3, Swo-41202), situated in Barangay Murtha, Municipality of San Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the NW., along lines 1-2-3 by Lot No. 2330, Pls-33; on the NE., along lines 3-4-5-6 by National Road; on the SE., along lines 6-7-8-9-10-11-12 by National Road; and on the NW., along lines 12-13-1 by Lot No. 2330, Pls-33.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 85-20’E., 739.58 meters from BLLM No. 64, San Jose Public Subdivision, Pls-33, thence:

beginning, containing an area of TWO HUNDRED THIRTY THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED EIGHTY EIGHT (230,788) square meters, All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by Pls, Old P.L.S/BL and Old P.S. are cyl. conc. Mons 15 x 60 cm. Bearing true.

The plan Swo-41202 was surveyed on July 2 ‑ August 8, 1968 by Domingo B. Gordovez, Geodetic Engineer, in accordance with the law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder, and was approved on September 21, 1968.

LOT NO. 4, Swo-41202

A parcel of land (Lot No. 4, Swo-41212), situated in Barangay Murtha, Municipality of San Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2 by Public Land; on the East., along lines 2-3-4-5-6-7 by River; on the SW., along line 7-8 by Lot No. 1, Swo-41202; and on the West., along lines 8-9-10-11-1 by National Road.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 63-35’E., 1,449.13 meters from BLLM No. 64, San Jose Public Land Subdivision, Pls-33, thence:

beginning, containing an area of ONE HUNDRED SIXTY ONE THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED SEVENTY TWO (161,972) square meters, All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by Pls, Old P.L.S/BL and Old P.S. are cyl. conc. Mons 15 x 60 cm. Bearing true.

The plan Swo-41202 was surveyed on July 2 ‑ August 8, 1968 by Domingo B. Gordovez, Geodetic Engineer, in accordance with the law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder, and was approved on September 21, 1968.

LOT NO. 2308, Pls-33

A parcel of land (Lot No. 2308, Pls-33), situated in Barangay Murtha, Municipality of San Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the NW., along lines 1-2 by Lot No. 2309, Pls No. 33; on the NE., along line 2-3 by Lot No. 2311, Pls-33; on the SE., along line 3-4 by Road, and on the SW., along line 4-1 by Lot No. 2307, Pls-33.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 57-18’E., 1,887.22 meters from BLLM No. 50, San Jose Public Land Subdivision, Pls-33, thence:

beginning, containing an area of SEVENTY SIX THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED NINETY NINE (76,499) square meters, All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by Pls, Old P.L.S/BL and Old P.S. are cyl. conc. Mons 15 x 60 cm. Bearing true.

The plan Pls-33 was surveyed on June 1934-November 1937 by Pedro Gabayan, Geodetic Engineer, in accordance with the law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder, and was approved on July 30, 1938.

The tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease, or other mode of disposition until again declared alienable by proclamation of the President.

In WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30 th day of JULY in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
