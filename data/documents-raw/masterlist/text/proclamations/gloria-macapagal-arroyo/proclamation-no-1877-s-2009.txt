PROCLAMATION NO. 1877

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 10, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN JOSE DEL MONTE, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, Thursday, September 10, 2009, marks the 9 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of San Jose del Monte with the theme: “Produktibong San Joseño Nagpupunyagi, Sa Hamon ng Buhay, Nagwawagi”;

WHEREAS, in order to give the residents of the City of San Jose del Monte, Bulacan the opportunity to celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare September 10, 2009 as special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Thursday, September 10, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the City of San Jose del Monte, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
