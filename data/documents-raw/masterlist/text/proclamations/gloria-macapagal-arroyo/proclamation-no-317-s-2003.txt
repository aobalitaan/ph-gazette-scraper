MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 317

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 12, 2003, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, February 12, 2003, marks the 5 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Kidapawan;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the City of Kidapawan be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, February 12, 2003 as a special day in the City of Kidapawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and three.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
