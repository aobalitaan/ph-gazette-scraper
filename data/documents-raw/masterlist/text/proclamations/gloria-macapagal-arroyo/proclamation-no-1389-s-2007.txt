PROCLAMATION NO. 1389

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY MAGDUG, MUNICIPALITY OF GOV. GENEROSO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, ISLAND OF MINDANAO AS SITE OF THE MAGDUG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resource and by virtue of the powers vested in me by the law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Magdug Elementary School, under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain containing an area of FIFTY SEVEN THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED SIXTY (57,260) SQUARE METERS, more or less, situated in Barangay Magdug, Municipality of Gov. Generoso, Province of Davao Oriental, Island of Mindanao, subject to private rights if any there be, which parcel of land is a now particularly described as follows;
