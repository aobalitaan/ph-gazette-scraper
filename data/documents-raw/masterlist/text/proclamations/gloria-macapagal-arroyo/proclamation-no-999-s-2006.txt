MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 999

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED IN BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO AS SITE OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCIAL HEADQUARTERS

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by the virtue of the powers vested in me by the law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve a certain parcel of land of the public domain located in Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley Province, Island of Mindanao as site of the Philippine National Police Compostela Valley Provincial Headquarters under the administration of the Department of Interior and Local Government, containing an area of Twenty Four Thousand Six Hundred Three (24, 603) square meters , more or less, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 3474-C, CSD-11-010251

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 3474-C, Csd-11-010251, being a portion of Lot 3474, Pls-2), located in Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley Province, Island of Mindanao.

Bounded on the N., along line 7-1 by Lot 3474-B, Csd-11-010251; on the SE., along lines 1-2-3-4-5 by Second Street; on the SW and NW., along lines 5-67-1 by Lot 3474-B, Csd-11-010251.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 40 cm. Date of original survey, October 1, 1952 to June 20, 1953 and the Subdivision Survey November 10-11, 1988 by Geodetic Engineer Andres C. Gumapac and was approved on July 18, 1989.

The tract of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease or other disposition until again declared alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of February, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
