MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 795

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITES PURPOSES OF THE MALPALON ELEMENTARY AND NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOLS, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY MALPALON, MUNICIPALITY OF CALINTAAN, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO, ISLAND OF MINDORO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school sites purposes of the Malpalon Elementary and National High Schools, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated at Barangay Malpalon, Municipality of Calintaan, Province of Occidental Mindoro, Island of Mindoro, under the administration of the Department of Education, containing an aggregate area of THIRTY SEVEN THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED THREE (37,203) SQUARE METERS , subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Five.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
