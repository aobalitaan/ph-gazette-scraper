MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1354

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING KNOWN AS VITRO INTERNET DATA CENTER BUILDING ESTABLISHED ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT CARLOS P. GARCIA (C5) CORNER DANNY FLORO STREET, PASIG CITY AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748
