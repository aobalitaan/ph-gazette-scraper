MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 573

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 501 DATED DECEMBER 21, 1939 WHICH RESERVED CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO FOR RADIO STATION PURPOSES BY DECLARING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AS DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATION REGION I OFFICE SITE AND HOUSING SITE OPEN FOR DISPOSITION IN FAVOR OF QUALIFIED OCCUPANTS

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 501 dated December 21, 1939, which reserved certain parcels of land located in the City of Baguio for Radio Station purposes by declaring the land embraced therein as Department of Transportation and Communication (DOT ) Region I office site and housing site open for disposition in favor of qualified occupants, subject to private rights, if there be any, road right of way requirements, and to actual survey, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
