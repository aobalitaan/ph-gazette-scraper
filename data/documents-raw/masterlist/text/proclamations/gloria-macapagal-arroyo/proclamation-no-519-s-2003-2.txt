MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 519

RESERVING FOR THE USE OF THE BUREAU OF CUSTOMS, PORT OF PULUPANDAN A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PULUPANDAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, ISLAND OF NEGROS

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL -ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or any other forms of disposition, and reserve for use of the Bureau of Customs, Port of Pulupandan, under the administration of the Bureau of Customs, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, located at Barangay Zone 5, Pulupandan, Negros Occidental, Island of Negros, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Ng-06-000026

A PARCEL OF LAND (Ng-06-000026) (proposed reservation for the Bureau of Customs Port of Pulupandan) situated in Poblacion, Municipality of Pulupandan, Province of Negros Occidental, Island of Negros. IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
