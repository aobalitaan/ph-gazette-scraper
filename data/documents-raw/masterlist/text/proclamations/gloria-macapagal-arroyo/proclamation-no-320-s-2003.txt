MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 320

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 14, 2003, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, Friday, February 14, 2003, marks the 5 th Charter Anniversary of the City of Valenzuela;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the City of Valenzuela be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, February 14, 2003, as a special day in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of January , in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
