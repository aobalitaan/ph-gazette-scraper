PROCLAMATION NO. 79

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 13, 2001 AND TUESDAY, AUGUST 14, 2001 AS SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAYS IN THE AUTONOMOUS REGION IN MUSLIM MINDANAO (ARMM)

WHEREAS, Tuesday, August 14, 2001, should be declared a special non-working holiday to give the people the fullest opportunity to participate in the said elections;

WHEREAS, Monday, August 13, 2001, should also be declared as a special non-working holiday to allow qualified voters whose polling precincts are distant from their work stations or temporary residences, to participate in the plebiscite.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday August 13, 2001 and Tuesday, August 14, 2001, as special non-working holidays in the fifteen (15) provinces and fourteen (14) cities in the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and one.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
