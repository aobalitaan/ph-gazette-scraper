MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 428

DECLARING A PERIOD OF NATIONAL MOURNING OVER THE DEATH OF EMMANUEL V. PELAEZ, FORMER VICE-PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

WHEREAS, the death of Emmanuel V. Pelaez, former Vice-President of the Philippines, is a great loss to our people and has caused a deep sorrow among them;

WHEREAS, it is fitting to set aside a period of national mourning to honor a great Filipino statesman who has served his nation well during his long public service culminating in his ascendancy to the second highest office in our land.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare a period of national mourning on July 31, 2003 up to August 1, 2003. During this period, flags of all government buildings and installations throughout the country shall be flown at half-mast.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 30TH day of July, in the year of our Lord, two thousand and three.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
