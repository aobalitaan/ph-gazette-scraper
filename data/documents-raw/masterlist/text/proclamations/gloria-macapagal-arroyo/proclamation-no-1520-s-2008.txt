PROCLAMATION NO. 1520

DECLARING SATURDAY, JUNE 21, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANOLO FORTICH, BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Saturday, June 21, 2008, marks the Charter Day anniversary of the Municipality of Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Saturday, June 21, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
