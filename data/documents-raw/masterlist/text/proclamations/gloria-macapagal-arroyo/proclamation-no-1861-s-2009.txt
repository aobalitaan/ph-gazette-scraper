PROCLAMATION NO. 1861

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 67 DATED DECEMBER 21, 1962 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE CAMP GENERAL MACARIO B. PERALTA, JR. IN BARANGAY GANZON, MUNICIPALITY OF JAMINDAN, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, ISLAND OF PANAY BY EXCLUDING 200 HECTARES EMBRACED THEREIN AND RESERVING THE SAME AS ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES (AFP) OFF-BASE HOUSING SITE

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 67, dated December 21, 1962, which established the Camp General Macario D. Peralta, Jr. by excluding 200 hectares (2,000,000 square meters) embraced therein for reservation as Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP) Off-Base Housing Site purposes, situated in Barangay Ganzon, Municipality of Jamindan, province of Capiz, Island of Panay, subject to private rights and final ground survey, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
