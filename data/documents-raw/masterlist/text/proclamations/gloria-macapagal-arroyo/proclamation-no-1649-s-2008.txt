PROCLAMATION NO. 1649

DECLARING FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 7, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUENCA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Friday, November 7, 2008, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Cuenca, Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Cuenca, Province of Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, November 7, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cuenca, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15 th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
