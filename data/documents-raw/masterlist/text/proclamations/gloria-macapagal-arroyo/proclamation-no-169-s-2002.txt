MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 169

RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES AND DECLARING AS ULUGAN BAY NAVAL RESERVATION, UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF NATIONAL DEFENSE, CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN ULUGAN BAY AREA, BARANGAY BAHILE, PUERTO PRINCESA CITY, ISLAND OF PALAWAN

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or any other form of disposition, and reserve for military purposes under the jurisdiction and administration of the Department of National Defense specifically for the establishment of a Naval Station, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future survey, certain parcels of land of the public domain, situated in Ulugan Bay Area, Barangay Bahile, Puerto Princesa, Island of Palawan with an aggregate area of 18,381,019 square meters which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:

Proposed Lighthouse/Harbor Defense Station (Area I)

A PARCEL OF LAND situated in the Barrio of Bahile, City of Puerto Princesa, Island of Palawan

Bounded on the W., and N., along lines 1-2-3-4 by South China Sea; along lines 4-5-6 by Ulugan Bay; and on the SE., along line 7-1 by Manabori Bay and Banag Hill. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being. S. 31-42″ W., 1,998.12 m. from C and GS Triangulation Station “North”, thence,

point of beginning

Containing an area of TWO MILLION FIVE HUNDRED FIFTY ONE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED SEVEN (2,551,707) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by concrete monuments 20x20x60 cm. marked P.N.

Proposed Site of Naval Operating Base Ulugan (Area II)

A PARCEL OF LAND situated in the Barrio of Bahile, City of Puerto Princesa, Island of Palawan.

Bounded on the NE., along lines 1-2-3 by portion of Puerto Princesa Wildlife Sanctuary and Eco-Tourism Center; on the E., along lines 3-4-5 by Oyster Bay; on the SW., along lines 6-7-8 by portion of Puerto Princesa Wildlife Sanctuary and Eco-Tourism Center; and on the W., and NW., by South China Sea. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S. 24-54″ W., 4,639.01 m. from C and GS Triangulation Station “North”; thence,

point of beginning

Containing an area of FIFTEEN MILLION EIGHT HUNDRED TWENTY NINE THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED TWELVE (15,829,312) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by concrete monuments 20x20x60 cm. marked P.N.

The tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease or other disposition until again declared alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 ST day of April, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
