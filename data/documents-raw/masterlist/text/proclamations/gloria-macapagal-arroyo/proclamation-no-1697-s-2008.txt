PROCLAMATION NO. 1697

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED ALONG EDSA CORNER NORTH AVENUE, QUEZON CITY AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 08-385 dated 31 July 2008, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the SMNE IT Center as Information Technology Center which stands on a parcel of land located along EDSA corner North Avenue, Quezon City with an area of Sixteen Thousand Seventy Six (16,076) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:

Lot 1-A

Portion of Lot 1, (LRC) Psd-104291

SK-00-000626

A parcel of land (Lot 1-A, being a portion of Lot 1, (LRC) Psd-104291, L.R.C. Rec. No.), situated in the Barangay Bagong Pag-asa Tagapo and Sto. Kristo, Quezon City, Province of Metro Manila, Island of Luzon.
