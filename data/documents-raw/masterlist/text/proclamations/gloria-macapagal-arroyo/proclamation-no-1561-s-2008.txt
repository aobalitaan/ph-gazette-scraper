PROCLAMATION NO. 1561

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED WITHIN THE CAMP JOHN HAY MILITARY RESERVATION, BARANGAY KADACLAN, BAGUIO CITY AS AN INCLUSION TO THE EXISTING BAGUIO CITY ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO THE PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, The Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL – ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as an inclusion to the existing Baguio City Economic Zone (BCEZ), subject to the provisions of Republic Act No 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations and Resolution No. 06-577 dated 18 December 2006 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), an area located within the Camp John Hay Military Reservation in Barangay Kadaclan, Baguio City, consisting of FORTY EIGHT THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED SEVENTY (48,270) SQUARE METERS, MORE OR LESS, as defined by the following technical description:
