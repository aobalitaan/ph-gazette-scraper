PROCLAMATION NO. 1609

RESERVING FOR AGRO-INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT CENTER SITE, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY MAASIN, MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE’S POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for Agro-Industrial Development Center Site, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Maasin, Municipality of Brooke’s Point, Province of Palawan, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
