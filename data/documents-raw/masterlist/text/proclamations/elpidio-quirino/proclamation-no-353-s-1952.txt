MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 353

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 217 DATED NOVEMBER 3, 1950, BY DESIGNATING THE MANAGING DIRECTOR OF DZFM, “THE PEOPLE’S STATION,” AS A MEMBER OF THE NATIONAL COMMITTEE ON CLEAN-UP-WEEK.

Proclamation No. 217 dated November 3, 1950, entitled “Designating the period from November 9 to November 15 of each year as Clean-Up-Week and creating committees for the purpose,” is hereby amended so as to include the Managing Director of DZFM, “The People’s Station,” in the membership of the National Committee therein created.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
