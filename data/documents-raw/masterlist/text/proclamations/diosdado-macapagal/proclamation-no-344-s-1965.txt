MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 344

RESERVING FOR MARKET SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MERCEDES, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of section 83 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for market, site purposes, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future survey, a parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Municipality of Mercedes, Province of Camarines Norte, Island of Luzon and more particularly described as follows:

Proposed Market Site No. 1

A parcel of land (The Proposed Market Site No. 1), situated in the Municipality of Mercedes (formerly a part of the Mp of Daet), Province of Camarines Norte. Bounded on the NW, along lines 1-2-3-4, by Provincial Road; on the NE., along line 4-5, by Li Sena Giap; on the SE., along line 5-6, by Daet River; and on the SW., along lines 6-7-1, by Sofronio Abainza (Now) by Dy. Loco. Beginning at a point marked 1 on the attached Sketch Plan, thence N. 65 deg. 41 min E., 18.96 meters to point 2; thence N. 64 deg. 52 min. 12.02 meters to point 3; thence N. 62 deg. 12 min. E., 10.61 meters to point 4; thence S. 20 deg. 29 min. E., 31,71 meters to point 5; thence S. 56 deg. 21 min. W., 40.95 meters to point 6; thence N. 25 deg. 00 min. W., 30.76 meters to point 7; thence N. 15 deg. 18 min. W., 8.91 meters to the point of beginning; containing an approximate area of 1,476 square meters.

NOTE :

All data are approximate and subject to change based on future surveys.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Secretary of Justice concurrently Acting Executive Secretary
