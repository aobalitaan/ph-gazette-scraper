PROCLAMATION NO. 516

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF THE CENTRAL LUZON SANITARIUM RESERVATION LOCATED IN THE CITY OF CALOOCAN, ISLAND OF LUZON, CERTAIN PORTIONS OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF ACT 1120, AS AMENDED

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of the Central Luzon Sanitarium Reservation situated in the City of Caloocan, Island of Luzon, certain portions of the land embraced therein and declare the same open to disposition under the provisions of Act 1120, as amended, which portions are more particularly described as follows:
