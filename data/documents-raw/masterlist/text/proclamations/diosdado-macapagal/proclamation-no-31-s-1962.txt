MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 31

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 1 TO JULY 15, 1962, FOR THE FOURTEENTH ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST

To enable the Community Chest to carry on its community welfare services, I, Diosdado Macapagal, Pi evident of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from June 1 to July 15, 1962, for its fourteenth annual educational and fund campaign.

I call upon all residents, firms, and organizations in the City of Manila, Quezon City, Pasay City, Caloocan City, and municipalities of Makati, Malabon, Mandaluyong, Navotas, Parañaque, and San Juan, Province of Rizal, to support this fund campaign and to give generously to the Community Chest which unites into one annual appeal the fund campaigns of the following welfare agencies:

I authorize and urge at government officials and employees including school authorities and teachers, to accept for the Community Chest, fund-raising responsibilities and to give it full support and dedicated leadership in their respective communities.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixteenth.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

AMELITO R. MUTUC Executive Secretary
