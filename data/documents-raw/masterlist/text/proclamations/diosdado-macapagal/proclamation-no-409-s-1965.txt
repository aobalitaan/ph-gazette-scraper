MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 409

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE ELKS CEREBRAL PALSY PROJECTS, INC., UP TO AUGUST 31, 1965.

WHEREAS, the period from May 1 to June 30, 1965, was set aside for the national educational and fund campaign of the Elks Cerebral Palsy Project, Inc.;

WHEREAS, the Elks Cerebral Palsy Project, Inc. Was not able to solicit funds from public and private schools due to the change of the school calendar this year; and

WHEREAS, the Elks Cerebral Palsy Project, Inc. needs additional time to realize its quota of P300,000.00 to continue its activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national educational and fund campaign of the Elks Cerebral Palsy Project, Inc. up to August 31, 1965.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
