MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 251

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL HEROES COMMISSION FUND CAMPAIGN UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1964

WHEREAS, the National Heroes Commission was authorized to conduct a fund-raising campaign in connection with its Mabini Centennial projects from May 1 to 31, 1964; and

WHEREAS, the National Heroes Commission needs ad­ditional time to prosecute the fund drive successfully;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the National Heroes Commission fund campaign up to December 31, 1964.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary
