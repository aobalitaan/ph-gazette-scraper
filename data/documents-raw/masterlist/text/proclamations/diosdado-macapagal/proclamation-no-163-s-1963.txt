MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 163

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 27, 1963, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS AND LIPA CITY.

In commemoration of the birthday of the hero and patriot, Miguel Malvar, the last Filipino general to surrender to the Americans during the Filipino-American war, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Friday, September 27, 1963, as a special public holiday in the province of Batangas and Lipa City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-three.

(SGD. )DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RUFINO G. HECHANOVA Executive Secretary
