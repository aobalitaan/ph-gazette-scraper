MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 465

DECLARING SUNDAY, OCTOBER 3, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY.

WHEREAS, the first death anniversary of the late Justiniano Borja, former Mayor of Cagayan de Oro City, falls on Sunday, October 3, 1965; and

WHEREAS, the people of said city request that they be given ample time to honor with appropriate ceremony their departed city executive who served them as such from 1954 up to the time of his demise in 1964;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Sunday, October 3, 1965, as, a special public holiday in Cagayan de Oro City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philipines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
