MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 213

DECLARING SATURDAY, MARCH 28, 1964, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, the twenty-seventh day (Good Friday) and the twenty-ninth day (Sunday) of March, 1964, being holidays, the twenty-eighth day (Saturday) may be declared a special public holiday without prejudice to the public interest;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 80 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby proclaim Saturday, March 28, 1964, as special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary
