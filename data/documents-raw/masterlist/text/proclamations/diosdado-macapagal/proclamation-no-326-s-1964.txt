MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 326

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 29 TO DECEMBER 5, 1964, AS PUBLIC RELATIONS WEEK

In order to foster the practice of good public relations and courtesy discipline among government officials and employees, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from November 29, 1964 to December 5, 1964 as Public Relations Week under the auspices of the National Association of Government Public Relations Officers of the Philippines, during which the heads of the various departments, bureaus, offices, agencies, and instrumentalities of the Government, including government-owned and controlled corporations, shall conduct an information and education campaign to carry out the objective of this proclamation.

I call upon all citizens and residents, as well as schools, educational institutions, and civic organizations to support this campaign by means of appropriate programs promoting good public relations and courtesy in public offices.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
