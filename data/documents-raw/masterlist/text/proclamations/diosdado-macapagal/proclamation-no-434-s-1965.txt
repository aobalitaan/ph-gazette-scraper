MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 434

SERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VICTORIA PROVINCE OF MINDORO ORIENTAL, ISLAND OF MINDORO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 64(d) of the Revised Administrative Code, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes, under the administration of the Director of Public Schools, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain property of the public domain of the Government situated in the Municipality of Victoria, Province of Mindoro Oriental, Island of Mindoro, and more particularly described as follows;

PROPOSED SCHOOL SITE

A parcel of land situated in the Barrio of Bethel, Municipality of Victoria, Province of Oriental Mindoro, Island of Mindoro. Bounded on the N., by public lands, on the E., by Creek, on the S., by public lands, and on the W., by Road. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 28 deg. 35’W., 431.47 a. from B.L.L.M. No. 51 of Victoria Cadastre;

the beginning; containing an area of TWENTY THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED SIXTY SEVEN (20,567) Sq. meters more or less, Subject to future survey,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of August, in the year of Cur Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

(SGD. )DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
