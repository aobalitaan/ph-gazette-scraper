MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 455

RESERVING FOR HOSPITAL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF ILOILO, ISLAND OF ILOILO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for hospital site purposes under the administration of the City Government of Iloilo, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future survey, a certain parcel of land of the private domain situated in the City of Iloilo, island of Iloilo, and more particularly described as follows, to wit:

Lot 1051-B (Proposed City Hospital Site)

A parcel of land (Lot 1051-B, the proposed City Hospital Site), situated in the City of Iloilo. Bounded on all sides, by proposed road. Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being; S. 77 deg, 45 min . E., 1,223.71 meters from B.L.L.M. 1, Iloilo Cad. thence S. 31 deg. 21 min. W., 202.30 meters to point 2; thence N. 32 deg. 31) min. W., 151.00 meters to point 3; thence N. 34 deg. 55 min. E., 134.13 meters to point 4; thence S. 59 deg. 30 min. E., 127.50 meters to the point of beginning; containing an approximate area of 22,245 square meters.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
