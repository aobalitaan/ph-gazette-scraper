MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 345

RESERVING FOR TOWNSITE PURPOSES OF THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT OF OLUTANGA A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF SUBANIPA, MUNICIPALITY OF OLUTANGA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for townsite purposes of the Municipal Government of Olutanga, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future subdivision survey in accordance with the development plan to be approved by the National Planning Commission, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Barrio of Subanipa, Municipality of Olutanga, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, Island of Mindanao, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Mr-1192 (Municipal Government of Olutanga—Municipal Reservation)
