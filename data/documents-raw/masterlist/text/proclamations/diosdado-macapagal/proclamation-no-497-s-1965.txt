MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 497

RESERVING FOR CAPITOL BUILDING PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYOMBONG, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for capitol building site purposes under the administration of the Provincial Government of Nueva Vizcaya, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the private domain situated in the municipality of Bayombong, province of Nueva Vizcaya, Island of Luzon, which are more particularly described as follows:

Lot 517 Bayombong Cadastre Cad-45

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 517 of the cadastral survey of Bayombong), situated in the Municipality of Bayombong, Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2, by National Road (Calle Real); on the NE., along line 2-3, by Calle Ochoteco; on the SE., along line 3-4, by Public Land; and on the SW., along line 4-1 by Calle Bonifacio.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 63 deg, 26′ E., 994.18 m. from B.L.L.M. 1, Bayombong Cad.,

beginning containing an area of ONE HUNDRED FOUR THOUSAND AND THIRTY FOUR (104,034) Square Meters, All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by Old Points; bearings true; date of the cadastral survey, Oct., 1913-June, 1914.

Lot 519 Bayombong Cadastre Cad-45

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 519 of the cadastral survey of Bayombong), situated in the Municipality of Bayombong Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

Bounded on the SE., NE., and SE., along lines 1-2-3-4 by Lot 517; on the SW., along line 4-5, by Lot 518; and on the SW., NW., and NE., along lines 5-6-7-8-9-1, by Lot 517, all of Bayombong Cadastre,

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 69 deg. 10′ E., 1170.38 m. front B.L.L.M. 1, Bayombong Cadastre,

beginning; containing an area of FOUR THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED NINETY ONE (4,191) Square Meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by Old Points; bearings true; date of the cadastral survey, Oct., 1913 – June, 1914.

Lot 520 Bayombong Cadastre, Cad-45

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 520 of the Cadastral survey of Bayombong), situated in the Municipality of Bayombong, Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

Bounded on the SE., SW., NW., and NE., along lines 1-2-3-4-1, by Lot 517 Bayombong Cadastre.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 72 deg. 42′ E., 1558.34 m. from B.L.L.M. 1, Bayombong Cadastre,

beginning; containing an area of TWO THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED, EIGHTY EIGHT (2,888) square meters, All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by Old Points; bearings true; date of the cadastral survey, Oct., 1913—June, 1914.

The portion of 9,880 square meters, more or less, of Lot No. 517, Cad. 45, which was sold in favor of the Compania General de Tobacos de Filipinas shall be excluded from the operation of this proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

(SGD.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
