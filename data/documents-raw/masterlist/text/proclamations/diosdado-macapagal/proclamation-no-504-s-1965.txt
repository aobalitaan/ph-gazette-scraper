MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 504

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 251 DATED MAY 4, 1951 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE WORKINGMEN’S VILLAGE RESERVATION (BARRIO OBRERO) SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF LAPUZ NORTE, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF LA PAZ, CITY OF ILOILO, ISLAND OF ILOILO, A CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 251 dated May 4, 1951, which established the Workingmen’s Village Reservation (Barrio Obrero) situated in the Barrio of Lapuz Norte, Municipal District of La Paz, City of Iloilo, Island of Iloilo, a certain portion of the land embraced therein and declare the same open to disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act, which portion is more particularly described as follows:

PROPOSED “BODEGA” of Asturias Sugar Central

A parcel of land (the proposed “BODEGA” of Asturias Sugar Central, being a portion of Barrio Obrero), situated in the Barrio of Lapuz Norte, Municipal District of La Paz, City of Iloilo. Bounded on the SW., along line 1-2, by Philippine Railway Company; and on the NW., NE. and SE., along lines 2-3-4-1, by Barrio Obrero. Beginning at a point marked “1” on the attached Sketch Plan,

thence N. 51 deg. 45 min. W., 72.00 meter to point 2; thence N. 38 deg. 45 min, E., 237.00 meter to point 3; thence S. 48 deg. 30 min. E., 72.00 meter to point 4; thence S. 38 deg. 44 min. W., 232.91 meter to the point of beginning; containing an area of sixteen thousand nine hundred seven (16,907) square meters, more or less.

NOTE: All data are approximate and subject to change based on future survey.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
