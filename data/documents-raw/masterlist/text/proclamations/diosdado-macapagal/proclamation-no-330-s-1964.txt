MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 330

RESERVING FOR THE USE AND ADMINISTRATION OF THE VETERANS FEDERATION OF THE PHILIPPINES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT, SITUATED IN THE CITY OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of General Services and pursuant to the provisions of section 64 (e) of the Revised Administrative Code in relation with section 1 of Republic Act No. 477, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for the use and administration of the Veterans Federation of the Philippines, subject to private rights, if any there be, a parcel of land of the private domain of the Government situated in the City of Davao, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot A-2-A-a-B-2-B-1 Psd-17184 (City of Davao)

A parcel of land (Lot A-2-A-a-B-2-l) of the subdivision plan Psd-17184, being a portion of Lot A-2-A-2-B-2-B-2 described on plan Psd-5458, G.L.R.O. Record No.), situated in the City of Davao, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the N., by Lot A-2-A-2-B-2-C-2-B-12-C-1 of plan Psd-11987, Lot A-2-A-2-B-2-C-1 of plan Psd-6416 and Lot A—2-A-2-B-1 of plan Psd-4197; on the E., by Lot A-2-A-2-B-2-C-1 of plan Psd-6416 and lot A-2-1 of Plan Psd-2738; on the S., by Lot A-2-A-2-B-2-B-2 of the subdivision plan; and on the W., by Lot A-2-A-2-B-2-C-2-B-12-C-1 of plan Psd-1187. Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan being N. 23 deg. 51 min. W., 139.83 meters more or less from Monument No. 5, Davao Townsite, thence N. 84 deg. 55 min. E., 15.01 meters to point 2; thence S. 5 deg. 05 min. E., 20.00 meters to point 3; thence S. 87 deg. 16 min. W., 80.07 meters to point 4; thence N. 5 deg. 04 min. W., 25.59 meters to point 5; thence N. 84 deg. 56 min. E., 34.99 meters to point 6; thence S. 5 deg. 06 min. E., 8.88 meters to point 7; thence N. 84 deg. 55 min. E., 30.00 meters to point of beginning; containing an area of 1,779 square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and, on the ground point 4 is marked by P. L. S. Conc, mons.; and the rest, by old pls. conc. mons. Bearing true, declination 2 deg. 05 min. E., date of original survey September 7-8, 1914, and that of the subdivision survey May 19 & 25, 1940. Note: The survey formerly in the barrio of Sta. Ana, Municipality of Davao.

Lot 6-B-2-B-C-3-A Psd-6416 (City of Davao)

A parcel of land (Lot 6-B-2-B-C-3-A of the subdivision plan Psd-6416, being a portion of Lot No. 6-B-2-B-2-C-3, barrio of Sta. Ana, Municipality of Davao, Province of Davao. Bounded on the N. by Lot No. 6-B-2-B-2-C-C-B of the subdivision plan; on the E., by Lot No. 6-B-2-B-2-B, Psd-4197; and on the S., and W. by Lot No. 6-B-2-B-2-C-2, Psd-5458, beginning a point marked 1 on the plan, being N. 23 deg. 51 min. W., 139.00 meters from Monument No. 5; thence S. 84 deg, 55 min. W., 30.00 meters to point 2; thence N. 5 deg. 06 min. W., 8.00 meters to point 3; thence N. 84 deg. 55 min. E., 30.00 meters to point 4; thence S. 5 deg. 05 min. E., 8.87 meters to the point of beginning; containing an area of 266 square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and on the ground points 1 & 2 are marked by old points; and the rest by P. L. S. cone, monuments; Bearing true; declination 2 deg. 02 min. E., date of original survey September 7-8, 1914 and that of the subdivision survey July 2, 1930.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
