MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 206

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 70, DATED SEPTEMBER 23, 1954, WHICH RESERVED THE PARK AND MARKET SITES SITUATED IN ZAMBOANGA CITY, A CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND RESERVING THE SAME FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE DEVELOPMENT BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 70, dated September 23, 1954, which reserved the park and market sites situated n Zamboanga City, a certain portion of the land embraced therein, and reserve the same for building site purposes under the administration of the Chairman of the Development Bank of the Philippines, subject to private rights, if any there be, which portion of land is more particularly described as follows, to wit:

Sgs-2503 (The Development Bank of the Philippines)

A parcel of land (as shown on plan Sgs-2503), situated in the City of Zamboanga, island of Mindanao. Bounded on the NE., along line 2-2, by Portion of Lot 2046, Zamboanga Townsite; on the SE., along line 2-3, by Portion of Lot 2046, Zamboanga Townsite; on the SW. and NW., along lines 3-4-1, by property of the City Gov’t, of Zamboanga (Mr-1066-Portion). Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being S. 61° 52′ W., 69.91 meters from Mon. S, Zamboanga Cad., thence “S 56° 52′ L., 19.99 meters to point 2; thence S. 29° 59′ W., 49.92 meters to point 3; thence N. 56° 53′ W., 19.99 meters to point 4; thence N. 29° 59′ E., 49.93 meters to the point of beginning; containing 1 an area of 997 square meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: points 1. 2, and 3, by B.L. marked on Pavement; and point 4, by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons.; bearings true; date of survey, May 29-June 3, 1963 and approved on June 20, 1963. NOTE: This is Lot 2054, a portion of Lot 2046 of Zamboanga Townsite, Cad. 14.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the. Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary
