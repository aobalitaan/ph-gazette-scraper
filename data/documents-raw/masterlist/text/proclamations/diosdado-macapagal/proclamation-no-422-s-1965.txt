MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 422

DECLARING THE ORGANIZATION AND ELECTION OF THE MEMBERS OF THE PHILIPPINE PRESS COUNCIL PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 4363.

WHEREAS, Section 3 of Republic Act No. 4363 provides that the Act shall take effect only if and when, within thirty days from its approval, the newspapermen in the Philippines shall organize and eject the members of a Philippine Press Council, a private agency of newspapermen whose function shall be to promulgate a Code of Ethics for them and the Philippine Press, investigate violations thereof, and censure any newspaperman or newspaper guilty of any violation of the said Code;

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 4363 vests in the President of the Philippines the authority and obligation to ascertain and proclaim, the fact that the Philippine Press Council has been organized and its members have been duly elected in accordance therewith; and

WHEREAS, upon documents presented tome and verification of the contents, I find and have ascertained that the Philippine Press Council was in fact organized by the newspapermen of the Philippines on July 14, 1965, in accordance with and pursuant to Section 3 of Republic Act No. 4363 and the following elected as members:

Ex-Justice Alejo Labrador Ex-Justice Pastor Endencia Dr. Gloria Feliciano, director, U.P. Institute of Journalism and Mass Communications Joaquin P. Roces, president, Philippine Newspaper Publishers Association and member of the executive bojard, International Press Institute Nereo Andolong, president, National Press Club Felix G. Gonzalez, editor, Manila Daily Bulletin;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of Republic Act No. 4363, do hereby proclaim that the Philippine Press Council was organized and its members were duly elected within the period specified in said law.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
