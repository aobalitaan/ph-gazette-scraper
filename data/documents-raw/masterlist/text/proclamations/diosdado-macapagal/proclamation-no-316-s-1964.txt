MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 316

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE 1964 NATIONAL FUND AND EDUCATIONAL CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE TUBERCULOSIS SOCIETY, INC. TO DECEMBER 31, 1964.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. has been authorized under Proclamation No, 269 dated July 20, 1964, to conduct its 1964 National Fund and Educational Campaign from August 19 to September 30, 1964;

WHEREAS, it needs more time to realize its fund campaign goal this year to enable it to keep up its sustained effort in the control and prevention of the White Plague in this country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend, the period of the Anti-TB Fund and Educational Campaign of the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. up to December 31, 1964.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

(SGD.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
