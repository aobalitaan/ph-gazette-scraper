MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 430

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 16 TO 24, 1965, AS NATIONAL FREEDOM-FROM-HUNGER CAMPAIGN WEEK.

WHEREAS, the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations has designated the period from October 16 to 24, 1965, as the worldwide Freedom-From-Hunger Campaign Week;

WHEREAS, the celebration of Freedom-From-Hunger Campaign Week has far-reaching significance in the life of our people; and

WHEREAS, there is a real need for this celebration in order to awaken our people to the stark reality of the problem of hunger and malnutrition and to arouse their interest in the need for greater food production to ensure adequate supply of nutritious food;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from October 16 to 24, 1965, as National Freedom-From-Hunger Campaign Week under the auspices of the National Committee on Freedom-From-Hunger Campaign.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

(SGD.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
