MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 433

CALLING FOR A SPECIAL ELECTION TO FILL AN EXISTING VACANCY IN THE MUNICIPAL COUNCIL OF ATOK, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE.

WHEREAS, this Office is informed that a vacancy exists in the Municipality of Atok, same province, for failure to elect the 6th councilor during the last elections of November 12, 1963;

NOW, THEREFORE, by virtue of the authority conferred upon me by Section 21(c) in relation to Section 22 of Republic Act No. 180, otherwise known as the Revised Election Code, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby proclaim and call a special election in the said municipality for the purpose of electing the 6th member of the Municipal Council of Atok, Mountain Province, to serve the unexpired term of the vacancy therein; which elections shall take place on Tuesday, the ninth day of November, nineteen hundred and sixty-five, to coincide with the general elections for President, Vice-President, eight senators, and members of the House of Representatives.

The provisions of the Revised Election Code insofar as they are applicable shall be followed in the conduct of the elections herein called.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
