MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 494

DECLARING MONDAY, NOVEMBER 22, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MATAASNAKAHOY, BATANGAS.

WHEREAS, the thirty-second death anniversary of the late Lt. Col. Antonio Mandigma, first mayor of Mataasnakahoy , Batangas, falls on Monday, November 22, 1965; and

WHEREAS, the people of said municipality request that they be given ample time to honor him with appropriate ceremony;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, November 22, 1965, as a special public holiday in the municipality of Mataaanakahoy, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
