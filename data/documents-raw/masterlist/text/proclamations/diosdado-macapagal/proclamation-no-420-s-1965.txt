MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 420

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JULY 21, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FABIAN, PANGASINAN.

WHEREAS, the birthday anniversary of the late Juan Alvear, statesman, educator, member of the First Philippine Assembly and former Governor of Pangasinan falls on July 21, 1965 and

WHEREAS, the people of San Fabian, Pangasinan, his birthplace, should be given an opportunity to celebrate the occasion with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Wednesday, July 21, 1965, as a special public holiday in the Municipality of San Fabian, Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
