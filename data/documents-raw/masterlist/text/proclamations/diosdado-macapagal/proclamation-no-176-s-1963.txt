MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 176

DECLARING THE THIRD WEEK OF OCTOBER OF EVERY YEAR AS FISH CONSERVATION WEEK

WHEREAS, the diet of the masses of our people is deficient in protein;

WHEREAS, fish is one of the cheapest sources of this vital substance;

WHEREAS, the Philippines can be self-sufficient in fish if our fishery resources are properly developed and exploited and illegal fishing stopped; and.

WHEREAS, it has become necessary that the people must be shown the importance of fish and other aquatic products to their well-being and to the economy of the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the third week of October of every year as Fish Conservation Week to be observed throughout the country in the manner that the following Committees shall deem proper and fitting:

National Committee

Provincial Committee

Chartered City Committee

Municipal Committee

(Where there is no fishery officer in the locality, the Chairman of the Committee shall appoint the Executive Officer thereof).

The above Committees are hereby empowered to appoint such sub-committee as may be necessary to carry into effect the objectives of this proclamation.

Proclamation No. 101, dated October 26, 1948, as amended, is hereby revoked.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-three.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RUFINO G. HECHANOVA Executive Secretary
