MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 54

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 811, DATED DECEMBER 27, 1961, BY TRANSFERRING THE ADMINISTRATION AND DEVELOPMENT OF THE PARK RESERVATION ESTABLISHED THEREUNDER TO THE PARKS AND WILDLIFE OFFICE

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 811, dated December 27, 1961, by transferring the administration, jurisdiction, control, and development of the Quezon Memorial Park from the Quezon City Government to the Parks and Wildlife Office.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-two.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Executive Secretary
