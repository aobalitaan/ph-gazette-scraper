MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 46

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBOD, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Director of Public Schools, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the municipality of Tubod, province of Lanao del Norte, island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot 4462 Tubod Public Land Subdivision, Pls-13

A parcel of land (Lot 4462 of Tubod Public Land Subdivision, Pls-13, L. R. C. Record No. ———-), situated in the municipality of Tubod, province of Lanao del Norte. Bounded on all sides by Road. Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being N. 4° 28′ E., 702.06 meters from B.L.L.M. 167, Tubod, Public Land Subdivision, Pls-13, thence N. 56° 25′ W., 500.00 meters to point 2; thence N. 33° 35′ E., 148.50 meters to point 3; thence N. 33° 35′ E., 251.50 meters to point 4; thence S. 56° 20′ E., 501.08 meters to point 5; thence S. 33° 44′ W., 399.24 meters to the point of beginning; containing an area of 200,054 square meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by Old Points; bearings true; date of survey, July 15, 1941.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines, to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-two.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Executive Secretary
