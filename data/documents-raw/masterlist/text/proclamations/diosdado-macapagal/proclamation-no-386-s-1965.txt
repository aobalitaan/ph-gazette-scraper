MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 386

AUTHORIZING THE NATIONAL LEAGUE OF PUERICULTURE CENTERS, INC. TO CONDUCT AN EDUCATION AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM APRIL 16 TO MAY 15, 1965.

WHEREAS, the puericulture centers and maternity houses all over the country have rendered invaluable assistance in the public health program of the government, especially among indigents;

WHEREAS, there is still a dearth of these centers to serve rural areas; and

WHEREAS, the National League of Puericulture Centers, Inc., needs public support to maintain and operate centers in rural and resettlement areas;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the National League of Puericulture Centers to conduct an educational and fund drive from April 16 to May 15, 1965. I call upon all individuals, organizations and corporations to support this campaign and authorize government officials and employees to accept fund raising responsibilities in their respective communities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
