MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 249

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 12 TO 19, 1964, AS FILIPINO RETAILERS WEEK

WHEREAS, Filipino retailers play a vital role in the development of the national economy; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to give impetus to the nationalization of the retail trade;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from June 12 to 19, 1964, as Filipino Retailers Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of May, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary
