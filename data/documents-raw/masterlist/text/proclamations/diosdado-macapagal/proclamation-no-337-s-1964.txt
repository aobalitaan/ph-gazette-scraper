MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 337

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JANUARY 24 TO 30, 1965, AS PHILIPPINE FIBER WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine fiber industry is one of the chief industries of this country on which more than five million Filipinos depend for their support and livelihood; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that there be set aside a week during the year to focus attention on the contribution of the industry to national progress and to encourage our people to accelerate production of Philippine fibers for the benefit of the country at large and the improvement of the lot of our people dependent on the industry in particular;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, DTOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from January 24 to 30, 1965, as Philippine Fiber Week under the auspices of the Bureau of Fiber Inspection Service.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
