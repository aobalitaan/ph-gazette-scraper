MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 335

DECLARING A PERIOD OF NATIONAL MOURNING OVER THE DEATH OF SENATOR EULOGIO RODRIGUEZ, SR.

WHEREAS, the death of Senator and former Senate President Eulogio Rodriguez, Sr., a national figure esteemed by the Filipino people, is a great loss to the entire nation, and has caused deep sorrow among them;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, do hereby proclaim the period from today to the day of the burial of the late Senator Eulogio Rodriguez, Sr., as a period of national mourning. During this period, flags of all government buildings and installations throughout the Philippines shall be flown at half-mast.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
