MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 442

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 370 DATED FEBRUARY 17, 1953, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE PLAZA AND POST OFFICE SITE RESERVATION SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIGCAWAYAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, AND DECLARING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 370 dated February 17, 1953, which established the plaza and post office site reservation situated in the municipality of Pigcawayan, province of Cotabato, and declare the land embraced therein open to disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
