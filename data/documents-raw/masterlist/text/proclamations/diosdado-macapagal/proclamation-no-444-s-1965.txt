MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 444

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 2, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN NUEVA ECIJA AND CABANATUAN CITY.

WHEREAS, September 2, 1965, marks an important event in our struggle for freedom, particularly to the people of Nueva Ecija who rose in revolt against Spain on that day in 1896 under the leadership of General Mariano Llanera; and

WHEREAS, the people of Nueva Ecija and Cabanatuan City should be afforded ample opportunity to celebrate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Thursday, September 2, 1965, as a special public, holiday in Nueva Ecija and Cabanatuan City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
