MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 485

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 420, SERIES OF 1939, AND DECLARING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT, AS AMENDED.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Maeapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 420, series of 1939, and declare the land embraced therein open to disposition to actual occupants and qualified landless persons pursuant to the provisions of the Public Land Act, as amended by Republic Act No. 739.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
