MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 509

RESERVING FOR OFFICE BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE PRESIDENTIAL ASSISTANT ON COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF PORT AREA CITY OF MANILA.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for office building site purposes under the administration of the Presidential Assistant on Community Development, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to the redevelopment plan of the area in construction of the said building, a certain parcel of land of the private domain situated in the District of Port Area, City of Manila, and more particularly described as follows:

Proposed Building Site for PACD

A parcel of land (the proposed building site of the PACD, being a portion of Block 129), situated in the Port Area District, City of Manila. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2, by 8th Street; on the NE., along line 2-3, by remaining portion of Block 129; on the SE., along line 3-4, by 9th Street; and on the SW., along line 4-1, by Atlanta Street. Beginning at a point marked “1” on the attached Sketch plan, being identical to corner 2 of this Block 129 which is at the intersection of 8th Street and Atlanta Street, Port Area District, City of Manila,

thence N. 45 deg. 22′ 19″ E., 67.87 m. to point 2; thence S. 44 deg. 37′ 41″ E., 43.83 m. to point 3; thence S. 45 deg. 22′ 19″ W., 67.87 m. to point 4; thence N. 44 deg. 37′ 41″ W., 43.83 m. to the point of beginning; containing an approximate area of Two thousand nine hundred seventy five (2,975) square meters.

NOTE: All data are approximate and subject to change based on future surveys.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

(Sgd.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
