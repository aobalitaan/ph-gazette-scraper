MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 476

DECLARING TUESDAY, OCTOBER 19, 1965 AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF BACOLOD.

WHEREAS, the twenty-seventh anniversary of the creation of the City of Bacolod falls on October 19, 1965; and

WHEREAS, the people of said city desire that they be afforded ample opportunity to celebrate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Tuesday, October 19, 1965, as a special public holiday m the City of Bacolod.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
