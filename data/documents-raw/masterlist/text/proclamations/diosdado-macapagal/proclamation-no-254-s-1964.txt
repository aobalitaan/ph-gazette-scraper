MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 254

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 6 TO 12, 1964, AS PHILIPPINE FLAG WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine Flag is the symbol of our country and the purest ideals and aspirations of our people;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that a week be set aside to ponder on the Flag, its evolution and history, so that our people may learn to love their country more;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from June 6 to 12, 1964, as Philippine Flag Week under the auspices of the Junior Chamber of the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary
