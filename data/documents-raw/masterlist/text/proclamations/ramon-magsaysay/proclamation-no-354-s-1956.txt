MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 354

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF RESOLUTION NO. 20, SERIES OF 1910, OF THE THEN PHILIPPINE COMMISSION, WHICH RESERVED AS PUBLIC PARK AND RECREATION GROUND A PORTION OF LAND KNOWN AS “BAGUIO PARK NUMBER ONE” CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN OTHERWISE DESCRIBED AS LOTS “A”, “B”, AND “C”, RESERVING TWO PARCELS FOR RADIO TRANSMITTING STATION PURPOSES AND DECLARING THE OTHER OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF SECTION 70 OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT, SITUATED IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Resolution No. 20, series of 1910, of the then Philippine Commission, which reserved as public park and recreation ground a portion of land known as “Baguio Park Number One”, certain parcels of land embraced therein otherwise described as lots “A”, “B”, and “C”, and reserve the two parcels indicated as lots “B” and “C” for radio transmitting station purposes under the administration of the Director of Telecommunications, subject to private rgihts, if any there be, and to future survey and declare the other parcel indicated as lot “A” open to disposition under the provisions of section 70 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended known as the Public Land Act, situated in the City of Baguio, which are more particularly described, to wit:

Lot “A” (The Catholic Vicar Apostolic of the Mountain Province)

A parcel of land (lot “A”, being a portion of lot 39-E-l-C described on plan Psd-44953), situated in the Residence Section “D” , City of Baguio. Bounded on the NE., along line 1-4, by remaining portion of lot 39-B-l-C of plan Psd-44953; along line 4-5, by lot “B” (the Bureau of Telecommunications); along line 5-6, by lot “C” (lot 39-B-l-B of plan Psd-44953); along line 6-7, by lot 141, Residence Section “D”, Baguio Townsite; and along line 7-1, by public land. Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being S. 89° 63′ W., 1,013.34 meters from Triangulation Station Gulick, Baguio Townsite, thence S. 21° 30′ E., 58 meters to point 2; thence S. 31° 30′ E., 62 meters to point 3; thence S. 20° 00′ W., 34 meters to point 4; thence N. 71° 54′ W., 114.62 meters to point 5; thence N. 16° 45′ E., 98.28 meters to point 6; thence N. 17° 37′ E., 20 meters to point 7 ; thence S. 72° 53′ E., 34 meters to the point of beginning; containing an approximate area of 10,000 square meters.

NOTE: All data are approximate and subject for future surveys.

Bounded on the NE., by lot 141, Residence Section “D”, Baguio Townsite; on the SE., by lot 1 of plan Psd-16771; on the SW., by lots 136, 135, 134, and 133, Baguio Townsite; and on the NW., by lot 39-B-l of: Baguio Townsite. IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines
