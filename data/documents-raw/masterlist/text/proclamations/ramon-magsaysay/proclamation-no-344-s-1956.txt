MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 344

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM SEPTEMBER 17 TO 23, 1956, AS LABOR-MANAGEMENT COOPERATION WEEK

WHEREAS, in order to raise living standards through greater economic production, it is necessary to promote labor-management cooperation with a view to minimizing work stoppage which results in economic waste; and

WHEREAS, it is essential that this progress of labor-management cooperation be assured of public endorsement and support;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from September 17 to 23, 1956, as Labor-Management Cooperation Week. I call upon all government offices and instrumentalities through out the .country as well as all private entities and individuals to participate In the proper observance of said period, to give full publicity to the promotion activities organized in connection therewith, and to cooperate with the Department of Labor in its task of fostering cooperation between labor and management in the pursuit of its program for industrial peace. I especially call on the schools, various chambers, professional organizations and societies, civic organizations and service clubs to devote one day of such period to a program for the promotion of knowledge of ways and means to bring about labor and management cooperation in their economic dealings with one another and the public.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
