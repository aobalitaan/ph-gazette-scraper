MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 387

TRANSFERRING THIS YEAR’S CELEBRATION OF “PHILIPPINE INDUSTRY AND MADE-IN-THE-PHILIPPINES PRODUCTS WEEK” TO THE THIRD WEEK OF MARCH

WHEREAS, the last week of February of every year is “Philippine Industry and Made-in-the Philippines Products Week” under Proclamation No. 222 dated December 3, 1955; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Chamber of Industries requests that this year’s celebration of the occasion be transferred to the third week of March;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby transfer this year’s celebration of “Philippine Industry and Made-in-the-Philippines Products Week” to the third week of March.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
