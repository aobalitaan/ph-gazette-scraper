MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 35

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 24, SERIES OF 1923, AND DECLARING OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND LAW THE PARCEL OR PARCELS OF LAND COVERED THEREBY SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF CALAUAG AND GUINAYANGAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provision of section 88 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I hereby revoke Proclamation No. 24, series of 1923, and declare the parcel or parcels of land covered thereby open to disposition under the provisions of the same Act.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-four, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eighth.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
