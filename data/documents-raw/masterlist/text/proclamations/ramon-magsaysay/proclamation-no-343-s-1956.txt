MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 343

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 48 ; SERIES OF 1916, WHICH ESTABLISH THE SCHOOL SITE RESERVATION SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NULING, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, A CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to. the provisions of sections 83 and 88 of Commonwealth Act 141, as amended, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Executive Order No. 48, series of 1916, which established the school site reservation situated in the municipality of Nuling, province of Cotabato, island of Mindanao, a certain portion of land embraced therein and declare the same open to disposition under the provisions of said Act, which portion of land is more particularly described, to wit:

A parcel of land (portion of lot No. 2077, Nuling cadastre 52) bounded on the NE., by Pablo Salvador, and Hadji Kong; on the SE., by Yman Creek and Hadji Kong; on the SW., by river and Salimbao Market site; and on the NW., by school site and road; containing an area of 2.9495 hectares, more or less.

NOTE: This portion is subject to future survey.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
