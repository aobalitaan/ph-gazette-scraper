MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 134

DECLARING SATURDAY, MARCH 19, 1955, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAGAY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

Pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, March 19, 1955, as a special public holiday in the municipality of Sagay, Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-five, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the ninth.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
