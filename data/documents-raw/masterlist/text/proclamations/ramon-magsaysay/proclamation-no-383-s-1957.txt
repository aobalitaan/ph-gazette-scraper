MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 383

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 121, DATED DECEMBER 18, 1936, WHICH RESERVED FOR AIRPLANE LANDING SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE SITIO OF SAN VICENTE, BARRIO OF SAN ROQUE, MUNICIPALITY OF TARLAC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC, ISLAND OF LUZON, AND RESERVING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN FOR PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL SITE PURPOSES

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of sections 83 and 88 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 121, dated December 18, 1936, which reserved for airplane landing site purposes certain parcels of the public domain situated in the sitio of San Vicente, barrio of San Roque, municipality of Tarlac, province of Tarlac, island of Luzon, and reserve the land embraced therein for provincial hospital site purposes under the administration of the Bureau of Hospitals, subject to private rights, if any there be, and which parcel of land is more particularly described, to wit:

A parcel of land (Airplane Landing Reservation under Executive Proclamation 121), situated in the sitio of San Vicente, barrio of San Roque, municipality of Tarlac, province of Tarlac. Bounded on the W., along lines 1-2, by George L. Parks Subdivision; along lines 2-7, by road; along lines 7-12, by lot 1092, Tarlac cadastre; along lines 12-13, by lot 1543, Tarlac cadastre; and along lines 13-25 and 25-1, by George L. Parks Subdivision. Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being S. 44° 50′ E., 585.36 meters from B.B.M. 82, Tarlac cadastre, thence N. 27° 56′ E., 226.16 meters to point 2; thence S. 68° 52′ E., 29.05 meters to point 3; thence S. 69° 56′ E., 15.13 meters to point 4; thence S. 69° 14′ E., 60.93 meters to point 5; thence N. 75° 40′ E., 14.28 meters to point 6; thence S. 69° 06′ E., 31.06 meters to point 7; thence S. 17° 40′ W., 81.33 meters to point 8; thence S. 17° 40′ W., 11.60 meters to point 9; thence S. 17° 40′ W., 187.12 meters to point 10; thence S. 22° 40′ W., 12.08 meters to point 11; thence S. 17° 40′ W., 186.61 meters to point 12; thence N. 74° 44′ W., 205.74 meters to point 13; thence N. 21° 05′ E., 18.54 meters to point 14; thence N. 20° 35′ E., 20.53 meters to point 15; thence N. 20° 49′ E., 19.87 meters to point 16; thence N. 20°! 57′ E., 20.86 meters to point 17; thence N. 20° 45′ E., 19.31 meters to point 18; thence N. 20° 52′ E., 23.33 meters to point 19; thence N. 21° 23′ E., 20.02 meters to point 20; thence N. 20° 07′ E., 30.43 meters to point 21; thence N. 20° 40′ E., 27.91 meters to point 22; thence N. 20′ 39′ E., 11.20 meters to point 2B; thence N. 20° 38′ E., 28.26 meters to point 24; thence N. 20°’ 23′ E., 20.01 meters to point 25; thence N. 19° 24′ E., 12.62 meters to the point of beginning; containing an approximate area of 90,747 square meters.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines
