MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 389

DCELARING TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 19, 1957, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN QUEZON PROVINCE

WHEREAS, the 68th birthday anniversary of the late Aurora Aragon-Quezon, one of our outstanding women leaders, falls on February 19, 1957; and

WHEREAS, the people of Quezon province, her birthplace, desire that they be given full opportunity to celebrate her birthday anniversary with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Tuesday, February 19, 1957, as a special public holiday in Quezon province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in th City of Manila, this 16th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
