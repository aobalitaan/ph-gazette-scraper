MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 18

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 298 DATED DECEMBER 13, 1951, BY SUBJECTING THE DISPOSITION OF THE LAND RESERVED THEREUNDER TO THE PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 477

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, and upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, 1, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 298 dated December 13, 1951, in the sense that the land of the public domain reserved thereunder shall be subject to disposition under the provisions of section 3 of Republic Act No. 477.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-four, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eighth.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
