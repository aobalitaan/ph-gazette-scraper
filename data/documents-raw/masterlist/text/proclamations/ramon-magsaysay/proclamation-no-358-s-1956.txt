MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 358

DECLARING SATURDAY, OCTOBER 20, 1956, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LEYTE AND IN THE CITIES OF TACLOBAN AND ORMOC

WHEREAS, the twelfth anniversary of the landing on the shores of Leyte of the United States Armed Forces in their misison to liberate the country from enemy occupation falls on October 20, 1956; and

WHEREAS, the people of Leyte, to whom such historic event is of special significance, desire to celebrate its anniversary with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Saturday, October 20, 1956, as a special public holiday in the province of Leyte and in the cities of Tacloban and Ormoc.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
