MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 360

REVOKING PROCLAMATIONS NOS. 141 AND 309 DATED MARCH 29, 1955, AND JUNE 30, 1956, RESPECTIVELY, AND RESERVING THE LANDS EMBRACED THEREIN FOR RAMIE TEXTILE MILLS SITE PURPOSES OF THE NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT COMPANY

Pursuant to the provisions of .section 64( e ) of the Revised Administrative Code in relation to section 1 of Republic Act No. 477, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 141 dated March 29, 1955, and Proclamation No. 309 dated June 30, 1956, and reserve the parcels of land embraced therein, together with the improvements existing thereon, for Ramie Textile Mills Site, purposes of the National Development Company, subject to private rights, if any there be.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
