MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 342

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 193 DATED SEPTEMBER 23, 1955 BY: EXCLUDING FROM THE RESERVATION ESTABLISHED THEREUNDER SUCH PART OR PARTS THEREOF AS HAVE ALREADY BEEN CERTIFIED ALIENABLE AND AND DISPOSABLE BUT WHICH HAVE NOT AS BEEN DISPOSED OF OR CONTESTED, AND RETURNING TO THE DIRECTOR OF LANDS THE AUTHORITY TO ADMINISTER AND DISPOSE OF SUCH EXCLUDED AREA OR AREAS WHICH ARE SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KABANKALAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested upon me by law, I hereby amend Proclamation No. 193 dated September 23, 1955, by excluding from the reservation established thereunder such part or parts thereof, as have already been certified alienable and disposable but which have not as yet been disposed of, or are contested, and returning to the Director of Lands the authority to administer and dispose of such excluded area or areas which are situated the municipality of Kabankalan, province of Negros Occidental; Provided, That as to the part or parts of the reservation which have already been allocated and assigned to settlers under settlement contracts but for which no patents have as yet been issued, the provisions, conditions and stipulations in the said settlement contracts shall remain in full force and effect.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
