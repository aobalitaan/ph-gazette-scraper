MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 320

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 58, DATED AUGUST 24, 1954, BY DECLARING THAT REPUBLIC ACT NO. 832 SHALL, TAKE EFFECT IN THE PROVINCE OF CAPIZ ON OCTOBER 1, 1956

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 58, dated August 24, 1954, Republic Act No. 832, entitled “An Act to regulate the sale, exchange, or delivery of home-pounded, undermilled, milled, or polished rice, and providing penalty for violation thereof,” shall take effect in the province of Capiz on July 31, 1956.

WHEREAS, that date of effectivity was fixed on the assumption that by that time the necessary facilities to implement Republic Act No. 832 will already have been available in said province; and

WHEREAS, it now appears that, the province of Capiz will not yet have the necessary facilities for the implementation of the law on July 31, 1956;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by section 10 of Republic Act No. 832, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 58, dated August 24, 1954, by declaring that Republic Act No. 832 shall take effect in the province of Capiz on October 1, 1956.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
