MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 40

DECLARING TUESDAY, 30 AUGUST 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MANDAUE

WHEREAS , on Tuesday, 30 August 2016, the City Of Mandaue marks its 47th Charter Day;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mandaue be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE , do hereby declare Tuesday, 30 August 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Mandaue.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
