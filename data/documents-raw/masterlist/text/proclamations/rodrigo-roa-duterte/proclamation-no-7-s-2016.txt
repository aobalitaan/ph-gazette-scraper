MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 7

DECLARING THURSDAY, 14   JULY    2016,   AS   A   SPECIAL    (NON-WORKING) DAY   IN   THE   CITY   OF   GUIHULNGAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS , Thursday, 14 July 2016, marks the Charter Day of the City of Guihulngan, Negros Oriental;

WHEREAS ,  it is but fitting  and proper that  the people  of the City of Guihulngan,    Negros   Oriental   be  given  full  opportunity   to  celebrate   and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE , do hereby declare Thursday,  14 July 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihulngan, Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
