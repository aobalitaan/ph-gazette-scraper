MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 66

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 OCTOBER 2016 AND FRIDAY, 21 OCTOBER 2016, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAYS IN THE CITY OF ANGELES

WHEREAS , the City of Angeles marks the celebration of the Kuliat Festival (Fiestang Kuliat) and the Tigtigan Terakan Keng Dalan, a festival which symbolizes the resilience of the people during the Mt. Pinatubo eruption;

WHEREAS , in order for the people of the City of Angeles to celebrate and participate in the event, the City Mayor has requested that 10 October 2016 (Monday) and 21 October 2016 (Friday) be declared as special non-working days.

NOW , THEREFORE , I , SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE , do hereby declare 10 October 2016 and 21 October 2016 as special (non-working) days in the City of Angeles.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
