MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 34

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 SEPTEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS , Monday, 26 September 2016, marks the 156th Pueblo Day (Charter Day) of the Municipality of Naval, Province of Biliran;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Naval, Biliran be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW , THEREFORE , I , SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE , do hereby declare Monday, 26 September 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Naval, Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
