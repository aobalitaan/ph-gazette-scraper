MALACANAN PALACE

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 80

DECLARING THE MONTH OF OCTOBER 2016 AS VOLLEYBALL MONTH

WHEREAS, the Philippine Super Liga (PSL) will host the 2016 Federation
Internationale de Volleyball (FIVB) Women’s Club World Championship (WCWC) on
18 to 23 October 2016 in Metro Manila;

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus national attention on the event and
stimulate greater awareness and involvement of all sectors of Philippine society in
the aforesaid sport;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the

month of October 2016 as “Volleyball Month.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By the President:
