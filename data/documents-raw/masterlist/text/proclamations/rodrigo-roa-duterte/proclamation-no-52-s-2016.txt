MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 52

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 7 SEPTEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 7 September 2016, marks the 76th Charger Anniversary and 445th Foundation Year of the City of Cavite;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cavite be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE , do hereby declare Wednesday, 7 September 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of August , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
