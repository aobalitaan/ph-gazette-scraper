MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 58

DECLARING   TUESDAY, 20 SEPTEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)  DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY  OF DINGRAS,  PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS , Josefa Llanes Escoda was a World War II Heroine and the Founder of the Girl Scouts of the Philippines;

WHEREAS , the Municipality of Dingras, llocos Norte marks the birth anniversary of Josefa Llanes Escoda on Tuesday, 20 September 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting   and   proper   that   the   people of the Municipality of Dingras, llocos Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Tuesday, 20 September 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dingras, Province of llocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this   7th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
