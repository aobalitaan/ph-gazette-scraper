MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 479

DECLARING MONDAY, 14 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 10952 provides for the postponement of the 23
October 2017 synchronized Barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan elections and the
rescheduling of the said elections to the second Monday of May 2018 or 14 May
2018; and

WHEREAS, Monday, 14 May 2018 should be declared a special (non-
working) day to enable the people to fully exercise their right of suffrage.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law do hereby declare Monday,
14 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day throughout the country,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this2ud day of May, inthe year of Our Lord,
