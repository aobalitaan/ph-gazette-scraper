MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 14

DECLARING THURSDAY, 4 AUGUST 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS , Thursday, 4 August 2016, marks the 4th Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Cabuyao, Laguna;

WHEREAS , it  is  but fitting  and  proper that  the  people  of the  City of Cabuyao,  Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Thursday, 4 August 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabuyao, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
