MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 340

DECLARING SATURDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN CARLOS, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 04 November 2017, the City of San Carlos marks
its annual festival commemorating the renaming of the locality from its former name,
Nabinkalan, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Carlos, Negros Occidental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies:

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE. do hereby declare
Saturday, 04 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of San
Carlos, Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
