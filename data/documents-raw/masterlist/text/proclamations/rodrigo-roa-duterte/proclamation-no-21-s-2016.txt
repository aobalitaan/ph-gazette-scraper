MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 21

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 AUGUST 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF ILOILO

WHEREAS , on Thursday, 25 August 2016, the City of lloilo marks its 79th Charter Day;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of lloilo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE , do hereby declare Thursday, 25 August 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 19th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
