MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 441

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

“«€

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 07 April 2018, Mountain Province marks its 515
Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of Mountain Province fully celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of Mountain Province requested that
Friday, 06 April 2018, instead of 07 April 2018 which falls on a Saturday, be declared
as a special non-working day in Mountain Province; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Mountain
Province be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday, 06
April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof March , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
