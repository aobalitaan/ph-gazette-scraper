MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 46

DECLARING FRIDAY, 9 SEPTEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BAIS

WHEREAS, Friday, 9 September 2016, marks the 48th Charter Day of the City of Bais;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bais be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE , do hereby declare Friday, 9 September 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bais.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
