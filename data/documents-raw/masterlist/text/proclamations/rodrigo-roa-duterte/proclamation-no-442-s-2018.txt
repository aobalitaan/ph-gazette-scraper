MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 442
DECLARING 24 FEBRUARY 2018 AS DAY OF NATIONAL MOURNING

WHEREAS, the passing of renowned National Artist Napoleon V. Abueva, the
“Father of Modern Philippine Sculpture,” is a tragic loss to our nation;

WHEREAS, his passion and dedication for the arts shall always be an
inspiration to the Filipino people; and

WHEREAS, the nation is in solidarity with and joins the grieving of his family

and friends; .

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and law, do hereby
proclaim 24 February 2018 as day of national mourning fo: “ational Artist Napoleon V.
Abueva.

| instruct all government offices, schools and educational institutions, and military
installations in the Philippines to lower the Philippine flag to half-mast on 24 February
2018.

The entire nation is requested to offer prayers for the eternal repose of his soul.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have iiereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila this23rd day of February, in the Year of our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By the President:
