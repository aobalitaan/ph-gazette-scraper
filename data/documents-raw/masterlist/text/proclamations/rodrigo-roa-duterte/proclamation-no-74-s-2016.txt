MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 74

DECLARING 1 MARCH 2017, AND EVERY YEAR
THEREAFTER AS “ASIA-EUROPE MEETING DAY”

WHEREAS, the Republic of the Philippines is one of the founding
members of the Asia-Europe Meeting (ASEM) in 1996, and also one of the
signatories to the Asia-Europe Cooperation Framework in 2000,

WHEREAS, the Republic of the Philippines, as part of its foreign policy.
as an active member of ASEM, wishes to enhance the country’s commitment
to work closely with Asian and European countries, with the objective of
strengihening the relationship between the two regions, in a spirit of mutual
respect and equal parinership;

WHEREAS, the Republic of the Philippines reaffirms its determination
to engage in substantial political, economic and cultural dialogue with Asian
and European countries, and promote initiatives that further the country’s
interesis;

WHEREAS, the members of ASEM, through the Ulaanbaatar
Declaration on ASEM into the Third Decade, designated an ASEM Day (Asia
meets Europe/Europe Meeis Asia Day) and recommended celebrating it
annually on March 1 or any other day during the first week of March as
appropriate with a view to underlining ASEM’s importance and raising its
visibility;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA BDUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby proclaim 1
March 2017 and every year thereafter as “ASEM DAY.”

SECTION 1. The Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA) is hereby
designated as the lead agency in inifiating and implementing appropriate
events and activities in observance and celebration of ASEM Day in the
Philippines.

SECTION 2. All agencies and instrumentalities of the National
Government, including government-owned and/or -conirolled corporations,
are hereby directed to collaborate and cooperate with the DFA in advacaiing
and implementing activities, including fostering ASEM awareness in the
Philippines.

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

SECTION 3. All local government units, as well as the private sector
and non-government organizations, are encouraged to actively participate and
extend their full support and assistance to the DFA, in whatever capacity, for a
meaningful observance of the ASEM Day.

SECTION 4. This Proclamation shall take effect immediately.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this sth day of October in the
