MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 460

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES ONE PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF BALAOA, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF KAYAN, SUBPROVINCE OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Balaoa, municipal district of Kay an, subprovince of Bontoc, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-104507, to wit:

A parcel of land (plan- Psu-104507, G. L. R. O. Record No.          ), situated in the barrio of Balaoa, municipal district of Kayan, Mountain Province (subprovince of Bontoc), Island of Luzon. Bounded on the northeast and northwest by Bortong Creek (6-8 m. wide) ; on the southeast, by provincial road (20 m. wide) to Cabunagan Gate; and on the southwest, by property of Calan et al. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 89° 59′ E., 2,631-40 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Kayan, Mountain Province; thence S. 43° 00′ E., 65.58 m. to point “2;” thence S. 40° 31′ W., 79.27 m. to point “3;” thence N. 57° 20′ W., 74.74 m. to point “4;” thence N. 29° 47′ E., 51.93 m. to point “5;” thence JST. 62° 33′ E., 49.45 m. to point of beginning; containing an area of six thousand eight hundred eighteen (6,818) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “1,” “2,” “3,” and” “4”, are marked by P. L. S. stone monuments; nd point “5,” by cross on-stone boulder; bearings true; declination, 0° 45′ E.; dte of_survey, November 5, 1937-March 18, 1938; and that of the approval, July 2, 1938.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this thirtieth day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
