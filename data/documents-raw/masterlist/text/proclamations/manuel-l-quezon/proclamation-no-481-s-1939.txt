MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 481

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF MANIKI, MUNICIPALITY OF KAPALONG, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Maniki, municipality of Kapalong, Province of Davao, Island of Mindanao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-104894, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-104894, N. 23° 53′ W. 12,867.35 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 2, barrio of Pagsabangan, municipal district of Tagum, thence S. 63° 36′ W. 220.07 m. to point 2; S. 65° 32′ W. 181.55 m. to point 3; S. 77° 33′ W. 232.39 m. to point 4; N. 12° 03′ E. 86.93 m. to point 5; N. 42° 16′ E. 170.64 m. to point 6; N. 57° 42′ E. 78.84 m. to point 7; N. 79° 47′ E. 75.68 m. to point 8; N. 22° 11′ E. 214.32 m. to point 9; S. 81° 13′ E. 148.27 m. to point 10; S. 82° 19′ E. 75.37 m. to point 11; S. 2° 58′ W. 195.53 m. to point 12; S. 58° 35′ E. 27.19 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 128,910 square meters.

Points 1 and 4, old P. L. S./B. L. concrete monuments; points 2 and 3, old nails on trees; points 5, 6, 7, and 8, stakes; and points 9, 10, 11, and 12, P. L. S. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north by property of the municipal district government of Kapalong; on the east, by municipal road; on the southeast, by property of Irene Natividad; and on the northwest, by Libuganon River.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 49′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-104894.

Surveyed: June 30 and February 5, 1938.

Approved: December 5, 1938.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this sixth day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
