MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 758

RESERVING FOR CEMETERY SITE PURPOSES ONE PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF BOLONG, CITY OF ZAMBOANGA, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for cemetery site purposes, under the administration of the Director of Health, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the district of Bolong, City of Zamboanga, island of Mindanao and particularly described in plan Psu-108584, to wit:

Psu-108584

Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan Psu-108584, N.47-48’W. 1870.70m. more or less from B.L.B.M. No. 1, Bo. of Tigbao, Mp. of Bangaan, Zamboanga, thence

N.13-28’W. 191.16m. to point 2; N.81-50’E. 232.32m. to point 3; S.13-05’W. 224.00m. to point 4; S.89-50’W. 236.15m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of FORTY SEVEN THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED EIGHTY EIGHT SQUARE METERS (47, 888), more or less.

Points 1 and 4, B.L. Conc. Mons.; point 2, G.I.S. in Cross on Tree; and point 3, Old B.L. Conc. Mon.

Bounded on the Northeast, by property of Manuela Racho; and on the South, Southwest and Northwest, by property of H. C. Wyatt.

Bearings true. Declination 2-17’E.

Point referred to are marked on plan Psu-108584.

Surveyed: June 8 – 10, 1939.

Approved: November 7, 1939.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this 30 th day of September, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
