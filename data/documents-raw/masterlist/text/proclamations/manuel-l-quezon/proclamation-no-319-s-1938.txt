MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 319

RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF LAGAUE, MUNICIPALITY OF BURNAY, SUB-PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for military purposes, under the administration of the Philippine Army, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Lagaue, municipality of Burnay, Sub-province of Ifugao, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Ir-350, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-350, N. 57° 38′ E. 4,219.00 m. more or less from triangulation station Kiangan, Ifugao, thence S. 35° 42′ W. 15.87 m. to point 2; N. 73° 50′ W. 149.66 m. to point 3; N. 82° 39′ W. 200.62 m. to point 4; N. 38° 49′ W. 36.40 m. to point 5; N. 30° 29′ W. 60.65 m. to point 6; N. 20° 46′ W. 71.68 m. to point 7; N. 25° 59′ W. 104.87 m. to point 8; N. 15° 41′ E. 17.49 m. to point 9; N. 45° 54′ E. 54.06 m. to point 10; N. 11° 32′ W. 44.59 m. to point 11; N. 50° 49′ E. 81.05 m. to point 12; N. 15° 57′ E. 28.05 m. to point 13; N. 23° 12′ E. 41.24 m. to point 14; N. 22° 41′ E. 102.17 m. to point 15; N. 49° 11’ E. 26.18 m. to point 16; N. 23° 20′ E. 51.30 m. to point 17; S. 85° 14′ E. 61.35 m. to point 18; S. 49° 07′ E. 177.49 m. to point 19; S. 73° 03′ E. 47.67 m. to point 20; S. 1° 54′ W. 98.52 m. to point 21; S. 22° 40′ E. 29.68 m. to point 22; S. 62° 21′ W. 44.23 m. to point 23; N. 48° 48′ W. 67.80 m. to point 24; N. 47° 19′ W. 72.89 m. to point 25; N. 38° 39′ W. 86.37 m. to point 26; N. 31° 48′ W. 29.85 m. to point 27; N. 56° 55′ W. 41.61 m. to point 28; S. 15° 03’ W. 71.39 m. to point 29; S. 0° 01′ W. 102.55 m. to point 30; S. 25° 57′ E. 65.75 m. to point 31; S. 59° 55′ E. 235.53 m. to point 32; S. 9° 05′ W. 56.41 m. to point 33; S. 19° 58′ E. 205.06 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 16.6489 hectares.

Point 1, old B. L. concrete monuments; points 2 and 17, crosses on trees; points 4 and 5, crosses on fixed stones; points 6 and 7, crosses on rocks; points 8 and 9, crosses on stones in place; points 10 and 12 to 16, inclusive, stakes; point 11, cross on stump; point 24, cross on fixed rock; points 25 and 27, crosses on stones; and the rest are B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast, by public land claimed by Binnaliw, properties of Lunag Madaoat, Aydennan, Kalingayan, Manoddon and Lunag Madaoat; on the east, by creek (no name) and properties of Bantuwey, Boliyo Kalingayan and Lunag Telay; on the southeast, by property of Bolonan and horse trail; on the south, by horse trail; on the southwest, by bank protection of Ibulao River and properties of Bolonan, Bandao, Kalingayan and Boliyo; and on the northwest, by creek (no name) and properties of Baggao, Ma-tagyo, Manangpa, Boliyo, Baggao, Togak and public land claimed by Binnaliw.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 47′ E. and 0° 50′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-350.

Surveyed: November 26-December 7, 1936.

Approved: April 29, 1937.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fifth day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
