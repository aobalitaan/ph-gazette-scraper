MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 790

POSTPONING TO A LATER DATE THE HOLDING OF SPECIAL ELECTION OF LOCAL OFFICIALS IN DANSALAN, LANAO.

In view of the present emergency, the special election called under Proclamation Numbered Seven hundred and eighty-six, dated December second, nineteen hundred and forty-one, to take place on Tuesday, January thirteenth, nineteen hundred and forty-two, in the municipality of Dansalan, Lanao, for the purpose of electing the Mayor, the Vice Mayor and the Councilors of said municipality, is hereby postponed to a later date which shall be fixed and announced in another proclamation to be issued by the President for the purpose.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this sixteenth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the seventh.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President

Source: University of the Philippines, College of Law Library
