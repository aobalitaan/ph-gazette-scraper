MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 799

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 208 DATED OCTOBER 5, 1937, AND OPENING TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF ACT NO. 2719, AS AMENEDED, TWO PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CAMP 7, MUNICIPALITY OF MINGLANILLA, PROVINCE OF CEBU, ISLAND OF CEBU.

WHEREAS, petitions have been filed for the release of two parcels of coal-bearing land located in the barrio of Camp 7, municipality of Minglanilla, province of Cebu, island of Cebu, from the Talisay-Minglanilla Estate Forest Reserve established under Proclamation Numbered Two hundred and eight, dated October fifth, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven;

WHEREAS, according to the information of the Director of Lands, concurred in by the Director of Forestry and the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, the aforesaid parcels of lands form part of the patrimonial properties of the Government as they are portions of the Talisay-Minglanilla Friar Lands Estate over which the Government holds a Torrens title;

WHEREAS, according to the findings of the Director of Mines, concurred in by the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, the said parcels of lands included in the Talisay-Minglanilla Estate Forest Reserve appears to be mineralized; and

WHEREAS, under the provisions of section fourteen of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and thirty-seven, otherwise known as the Mining Act, lands within reservations for purposes other than mining, which after such reservation is made, are found to be more valuable for their mineral contents than for the purpose for which the reservation was made, may be withdrawn from such reservations by the President of the Philippines only with the concurrence of the National Assembly;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MANUEL L. QUEZON, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation Numbered Two hundred and eight, dated October fifth, under the provisions of Act No. 2710, as amended, the following two parcels of land situated in the barrio of Camp 7, municipality of Minglanilla, province of Cebu, island of Cebu, and described on plans Crps-243 and Bsm,-48 respectively, to wit:

The surface thereof may be leased for not more than three (3) years and rentals paid in advance to the Bureau of Lands at not less than five per cent (5%) per annum of the value of the land in accordance with the provisions of Section 14 of Lands Administrative Order No. 3-4, which lease may be removed for like period at the discretion of the Director of Lands.

Done at the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of December, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the seventh.
