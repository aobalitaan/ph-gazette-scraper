MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 507

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 30, DATED JANUARY 29, 1936, AND DECLARING THE LAND RESERVED THEREUNDER OPEN TO DISPOSITION.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-eight of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby revoke Proclamation Numbered Thirty, dated January twenty-nine, nineteen hundred and thirty-six, and declare the land reserved thereunder open to disposition. .

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighth day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
