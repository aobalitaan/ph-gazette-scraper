MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 741

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF ARMENIA, MUNICIPALITY OF DIMASALANG, PROVINCE OF MASBATE, ISLAND OF MASBATE.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Armenia, municipality of Dimasalang, Province of Masbate, Island of Masbate, and particularly described in plan Psu-75650, to wit:

Psu-75650. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan Psu-75650, S. 72° 29′ W. 54.40 m. more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of Armenia, municipality of Dimasalang, thence S. 7° 1.0′ E. 64.76 rn. to point 2; S. 9° 48′ E. 57.84 m. to point 3; N. 87° 22′ W. 46.06 m. to point 4; N. 7° 57′ W. 114.37 m: to point 5; N. 82° 24′ E. 44.29 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of five thousand two hundred seventeen (5,217) square meters, more or less.

Points 1, 2, 4 and 5, P. L. S. standard concrete monuments; and point 3, old P. L. S.

Bounded on the northeast by properties of Norberto Merioles and Ernesta Vda. de Serrano; on the south, by property of Celestino Salipot; on the southwest, by municipal land (portion barrio site); and on the northwest, by barrio road.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 57′ E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Psu-75650.

Surveyed: November 8, 1929.

Approved: September 8, 1930.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eleventh day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
