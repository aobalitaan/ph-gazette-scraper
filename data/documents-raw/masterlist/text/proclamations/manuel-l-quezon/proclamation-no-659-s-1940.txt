MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 659

RESERVING FOR FISHERY OFFICE AND LABORATORY PURPOSES TWO (2) PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED IN THE CITY OF CEBU.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the City of Cebu, island of Cebu, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16463, to wit:

Lot No. 8603 —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16463, S. 79° 00’E. 311.07 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 2, municipality of Cebu, thence N. 61° 40’ W. 32.10 m. to point 2; N. 28° 27’ E. 37.29 m. to point 3; S.62° 32’ E. 31. 97 m. to point 4; S. 28° 21’ W. 37.77 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1,203 square meters.

Point1, old B. L. concrete monument; point 2, cross cut on concrete side of street; points 3 and 4, B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast by lot No. 8603; on southeast, by lot No. 8579-B of plan Swo-13287; on the SW., by proposed street; and on the northwest, by lot No. 8604

Lot No. 8604 . —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16463, S. 83° 23′ E. 250.51 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 2, municipality of Cebu, thence N. 28° 20′ E. 36.96 m. to point 2; S. 62° 16′ E. 32.12 m. to point 3; S. 28° 27′ W. 37.29 m. to point 4; N. 61° 40′ W. 32.11 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1,194 square meters.

Point 1, old B. L. concrete monument; points 2 and 4, crosses cut on concrete sides of streets; point 3, B. L. concrete monument.

Bounded on the northeast by lot No. 8603; on the southeast, by lot No. 8603; and on the southwest and northwest, by proposed street.

Bearings true. Declination 1° 30′ E. Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16463.

Surveyed: January 22 and 23, 1937. Approved: May 17, 1937.

Note:

This survey was formerly in the barrio of Terraplen, municipality of Cebu.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-third day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
