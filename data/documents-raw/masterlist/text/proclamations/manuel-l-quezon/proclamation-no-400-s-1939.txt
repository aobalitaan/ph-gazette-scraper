MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 400

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 448, DATED FEBRUARY 25, 1932, AND RESERVING FOR PARK AND PLAYGROUND PURPOSES A PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE CITY OF DAVAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation Numbered Four hundred forty-eight, dated February twenty-fifth, nineteen hundred and thirty-two, and reserve for park and playground purposes, under the administration of the City of Davao, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of land situated in the City of Davao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15680, to wit:

A parcel of land (the consolidation plan of Swo-15680, being the consolidation of lots Nos. 138 to 164, 189 to 236, inclusive, 284, 290 to 311, 313 to 320, 374 to 385, 398 to 406, 437 to 449, inclusive, and portions of lots Nos. 470 to 1 473, inclusive, of plan Bsd-4335, G. L. R. O. Record No. ) situated in the City of Davao, Island of Mindanao Bounded on the N. and NE. by lot No. 470-New of plan Bsd-4335-Amd.; on the SE., by proposed road; on the SW., by lot No. 473-New of plan Bsd-4335-Amd.; and on the NW., by lots Nos. 473-New, 472-New, and 471-New of plan Bsd-4335-Amd. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 85° 05′ E., 966.84 m., more or less, from monument No. 19, Davao townsite, thence S. 50° 25′ W., 660.03 m. to point “2;” thence N 39° 35′ W., 222.36 m. to point “3;” thence N. 18° 24′ E., 203.02 m. to point “4;” thence N. 50° 25′ E., 7.86 m. to point “5;” thence N. 50° 25′ E., 175.00 m. to point “6;” thence N. 50° 25′ E., 124.99 m. to point “7;” thence N. 50° 25′ E., 19.96 m. to point “8;” thence N. 86° 46′ E., 198.75 m. to point “9;” thence S. 39° 34′ E., 212.18 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of one hundred ninety-nine thousand one hundred fourteen (199,114) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, point “4” is marked by an intersection; and the rest, by old B. P. W./D. C. S. M. concrete monuments; bearings true; declination, 2° 08′ E.; date of the consolidation sur­vey, March 19, 1935 to February 27, 1936.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fourth day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
