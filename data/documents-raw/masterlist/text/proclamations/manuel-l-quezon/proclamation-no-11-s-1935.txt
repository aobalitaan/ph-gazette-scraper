PROCLAMATION NO. 11

LIFTING THE TARIFF ON RICE IMPORTED BY THE GOVERNMENT AS A RELIEF MEASURE

Whereas, His Excellency, the Governor-General of the Philippine Islands, issued on September twenty-sixth, nineteen hundred and thirty-five, Proclamation Numbered Eight hundred and forty-five declaring a state of emergency and proclaiming therefore all the provisions of Act Numbered Two thousand eight hundred and sixty-eight, entitled “An Act penalizing the monopoly and hoarding of, and speculation in palay, rice, and corn under extraordinary circumstances, regulating the distribution and sale thereof, and authorizing the Governor-General, with the consent of the Council of State, to issue the necessary rules and regulations therefore,” except those relative to price fixing, to be in full force and effect from and after such date and until further orders;

Whereas, said proclamation is still in force due to the continuance of the state of emergency with respect to rice because of drought, typhoon, flood, and similar conditions;

Now, therefore, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers conferred upon me by section one of Act Numbered Four thousand one hundred and ninety-eight, entitled “An Act amending section eleven of the Act entitled ‘An Act to raise revenue for the Philippine Islands, and for other purposes,’ approved by the Congress of the United States on August fifth, nineteen hundred and nine, as amended, for the purpose of admitting free entry of certain merchandise in time of emergency,” do hereby authorize the importation of rice heretofore made or hereafter to be made by or for the account of the Government for distribution among distressed people, free of duty, subject to such rules and regulations as the Insular Collector of Customs may prescribe, with the approval of the Secretary of Finance.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this thirtieth day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-five, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines

By the President:

Secretary of the Interior
