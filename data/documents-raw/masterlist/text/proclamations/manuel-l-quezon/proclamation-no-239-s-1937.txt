MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 239

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 25, DATED JULY 8, 1920, AND RESERVING THE LAND DESCRIBED THEREIN FOR PLANT PROPAGATION STATION PURPOSES.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby revoke Proclamation Numbered Twenty-five, dated July eighth, nineteen hundred and twenty, reserving a parcel of land described therein for provincial purposes, and re-reserve the same for plant prop­agation station purposes under the administration of the Director of Plant Industry.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-third day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: ELPIDIO QUIRINO Secretary of the Interior
