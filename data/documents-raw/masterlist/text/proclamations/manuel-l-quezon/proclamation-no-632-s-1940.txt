MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 632

RESERVING FOR CEMETERY PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF KALAMANSIG, MUNICIPALITY OF LEBAK, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for cemetery purposes, under the administration of the Director of Health, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Kalamansig, municipality of Lebak, Province of Cotabato, island of Mindanao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Pr-176, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-176 N. 69° 11′ E. 954. 25 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of Kalamansig, municipality of Lebak, thence N. 9° 22′ E. 99.06 m. to point 2; S. 79° 53′ E. 49.95 m. to point 3; S. 9° 23′ W. 99.09 m. to point 4; N. 79° 51′ W. 49.90 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 4,946 square meters.

Point 1, old B. L. concrete monument; and the rest are B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north and east by property of Davia Staples; on the south, by property of Pelagio Sumagaysay; and on the west, by property of David Staples.

Bearings true. Declination 2° 30′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-176.

Surveyed: January 9-10, 1940.

Approved: July 27, 1940.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
