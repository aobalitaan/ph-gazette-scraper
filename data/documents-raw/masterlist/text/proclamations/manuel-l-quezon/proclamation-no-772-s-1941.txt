MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 772

RESERVING FOR EXPERIMENTAL STATION PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF AROMAN, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three 01 Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for experimental station purposes, under the administration of the Director of Plant Industry, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of. Aroman, municipal district of Carmen, Province of Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan, Ir-490, to wit:
