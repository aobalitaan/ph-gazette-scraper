PROCLAMATION NO. 292

ESTABLISHING AS MAYON VOLCANO NATIONAL PARK FOR THE BENEFIT AND ENJOYMENT OF THE PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES THE PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF ALBAY, CAMALIG, GUINOBATAN, LIBOG, LIGAO, MALILIPOT, AND TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, ISLAND OF LUZON, AND REVOKING FOR THIS PURPOSE PROCLAMATION NO. 341, SERIES OF 1930.
