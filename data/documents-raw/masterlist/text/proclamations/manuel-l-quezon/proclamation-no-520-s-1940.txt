MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 520

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES THREE PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CONCEPCION, MUNICIPALITY OF CORON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF BUSUANGA.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Concepcion, municipality of Coron, Province of Palawan, island of Busuanga and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-269, to wit:

Lot No. 2. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-269, S. 38″ 03′ E. 232.46 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Mp. of Coron, thence N. 31″ 19′ W. 130.42 m. to point 2; N. 8° 35′ E. 52.95 m. to point 3; S. 89° 33′ E. 56.11 m. to point 4; N. 1°48 E. 29.23 m. to point 5; S. 83° 49′ E. 9.71 m. to point 6; S. 4° 07′ E. 117.17 m. to point 7; S. 2° 11′ W. 66.53 m. to point 8; S. 57° 07′ W. 15.05 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 0.9466 hectare.

Points 2 and 6, crosses RK. in places; points 3 and 4, old B. L.

Bounded on the north by lot No. 2-A and property of Doroteo Dulay; on the east, by public land; on the southeast, by property of Candelario Robles; on the southwest, by street and on the west, by street and lot No. 2-A.

Lot No. 2-A. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-269, N. 80° 23′ E. 90.23 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Mp. of Coron, thence S. 83° 49′ E. 51.78 m. to point 2; S. 1° 48′ W. 29.23 m. to point 3; N. 89° W. 56.11 m. to point 4; N. 5° 41’ E. 34.76 m. to point 5 S. 83° 49′ E. 2.12 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 0.1757 hectare.

Points 3 and 4, old B. L.

Bounded on the north by lot No. 2-B; on the east and south, by lot No. 2; and on the west, by street.

Lot No. 3. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-269, S. 75° 33′ E. 77.86 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Mp. of Coron, thence S. 9° 34′ W. 48.93 m. to point 2; S. 30° 04′ E. 137.05 m. to point 3; S. 66° 09′ W. 35.97 m. to point 4; N. 34° 18′ W. 159.26 m. to point 5; N. 8° 26′ E. 56.15 m. to point 6; S. 83° 58′ E. 54.20 m. to point 1, point of the beginning.

Containing an area of 0.8890 hectare.

Points 3 and 6, G. I. S. on trees; points 2 and 4, crosses RK. in places.

Bounded on the north by Calle Concepcion; on the northeast, by street; on the southeast, by property of Candelario Robles; on the southwest, by creek and public land; and on the west, by property of the municipal government of Coron.

Bearings true. Declination 1° 03′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-269.

Surveyed: February 16, 1932.

Approved: September 21, 1934.

Note:

Lot No. 2 of Concepcion Cad. No. 35 is subdivided into 2-A and 2-B by Mr-269.

Lot No. 2-A = lot No. 1 of Mr-269 as surveyed for the municipal government of Coron.

All corners marked old B. L. are concrete monuments 15 x 60 cms.

All corners not otherwise described are B. L. concrete monuments 15 x 60 cms.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this first day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
