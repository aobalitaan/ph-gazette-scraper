MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 482

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF BUGATAY, MUNICIPALITY OF PEÑABLANCA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Bugatay, municipality of Peñablanca, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-101621, to wit:

A parcel of land (described on the original plan Psu-101621, G. L. R. O. Record No.     ), situated in the barrio of Bugatay, municipality-of Peñablanca, Province of Cagayan. Bounded on the N. by property of Juan Danguilan; on the E., by barrio road, 5 m. wide; on the SE., by barrio Toad, 4 m. wide; on the SW., by barrio road, 5 ½ m. wide; and on the NW., by property of Juan Danguilan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 67°10′ E., 3,063.71 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Peñablanca, Cagayan, thence N. 76° 28′ E., 29.65 m. to point “2;” thence N. 77° 37′ E., 62.30 m. to point “3;” thence S. 78° 31′ E., 45.49 m. to point “4;” thence S. 2° 35′ W., 73.49 m. to point “5;” thence S. 77° 19′ W., 107.90 m. to point “6;” thence N. 16° 39′ W., 89.63 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of ten thousand six hundred eleven (10,611) square meters, more or less.” All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “1,” “2,” and “3” are marked by old P. L. S.concrete monuments; and the rest, by P. L. S. concrete monuments; bearings true; declination, 0° 10′ E.; date of survey, December 16, 1935, and February 10, 1936; and that of the approval, May 7, 1937.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this sixth day of November in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
