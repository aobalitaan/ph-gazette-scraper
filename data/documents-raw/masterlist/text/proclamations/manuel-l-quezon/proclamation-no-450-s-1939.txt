MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 450

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 743, DATED OCTOBER 25, 1934, AND RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES, A PARCEL OF LAND (LOT NO. 261-B) IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation Numbered Seven hundred and forty-three, dated October twenty-fifth, nineteen hundred and thirty-four, and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, the following parcel of land situated in the City of Baguio, and particularly; described in the Bureau of Lands plan Ir-393-Amd.-D, to wit:

Lot No. 261-B. —A parcel of land (lot No. 261-B of the amendment-subdivision plan Ir-393-Amd.-D, being a portion of lot No. 261 of Baguio townsite, G. L. R. O. Record No. 1668), situated in the residence section K, City of Baguio, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the NE. and SE., by lot No. 261—A of the amendment-subdivision plan; on the SW., by lots .Nos. 111-A-2 and 111-A-l of plan Bsd-1179; on the W., by lot No. 111-A-1 of plan Bsd-1179; and on the NW., by lot No. 114-B and lot No. 114-C of plan Bsd-1166. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 54° 32′ W., 437.70 m., more or less, from triangulation station cross, City of Baguio; thence N. 34° 51′ W., 102.30 m. to point “2;” thence N. 9° 47′ E., 87.78 m. to point “3;” thence N. 86° 16′ E., 42.26 m. to point “4;” thence N. 48° 00′ E., 48.11 m. to point “5;” thence S. 49° 32′ E., 26.80 m. to point “6;” thence S. 72° 56′ E., 37.94 m. to point “7;” thence S. 27° “13” W., 128.93 m. to point “8;” thence S. 27° 14′ W., 70.00 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of sixteen thousand thirty4;hree (16,033) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “1,” “5,” “6,” “7,” and “8” are marked by P. L. S. cylindrical concrete monuments; and the rest, by old stakes; bearings true; declination, 0° 09′ E.; date of the original survey, during 1911-1912; and that of the amendment-subdivision survey, July 14-15, 1938.

NOTE.—Lot No. 261-B = Lot No. 270 of Baguio townsite, residence section “K”.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighth day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
