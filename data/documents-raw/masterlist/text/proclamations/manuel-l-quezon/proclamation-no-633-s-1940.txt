MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 633

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF BANGANAN, MUNICIPALITY OF ARITAO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of the Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from ‘ sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Banganan, municipality of Aritao, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-434, to wit:

Lot No. 1. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-434, N. 39° 49′ E. 2,804.87 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 2, municipality of Aritao, thence N. 53° 20′ E. 82.74 m. to point 2; S. 44° 12′ E. 110.64 m. to point 3; S. 49° 34′ W. 82.50 m. to point 4; N. 44°03′ W. 116.06 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 9,315 square meters.

Points 1 and 4, molave posts; point 2, P. L. S. concrete monument; point 3, cross on stone.

Bounded on the northeast by property of Severino Guloy; on the southeast, by callejon; on the southwest, by 1 lot No. 2; and on the northwest, by national road.

Lot No. 2. —Beginning at a point marked 1, on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-434, N. 39° 49′ E. 2,804.87 m., more or less, B. L. L. M. No 2 municipality of Aritao, thence S. 44° 03′ E. 116.06 m. to point 2; S. 48° 00′ W. 20.64 m. to point 3; N. 50° 03′ W. 118.17 m. to point 4; N. 49°46′ E. 33.05 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 3,121 square meters.

Points 1 and 2, molave posts; point 3 and 4, old P. L. S. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast by lot No. 1; on the southeast, by callejon; on the southwest, by property of Silvestre Dulay; and on the northwest, by national road.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 12′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-434.

Surveyed: January 12, 1940. Approved: May 24, 1940.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
