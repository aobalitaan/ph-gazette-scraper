MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 465

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF BAT-BATO, MUNICIPALITY OF ALILEM, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Bat-bato, municipality of Alilem, Province of Ilocos Sur, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-405, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-405, S. 80° 32′ E. 22,268.95 m., more or less, from trian-gulation station Tagudin Church tower, thence E. 59° 33′ W. 85.68 m. to point 2; N. 30° 34′ W. 58.06 m. to point 3; N. 33° 48′ E. 22.82 m. to point 4; N. 74° 52′ E. 62.26 m. to point 5; S. 36° 13′ E. 51.77 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 5,013 square meters.

All corners are crosses on stones marked B. L. 15 by 60 cm.

Bounded on all sides by public land.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 27′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-405.

Surveyed: May 15, 1939.

Approved: August 23, 1939.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this ninth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
