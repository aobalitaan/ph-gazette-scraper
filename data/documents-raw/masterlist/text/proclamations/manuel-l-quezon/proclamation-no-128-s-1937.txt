PROCLAMATION NO. 128

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PROPERTY OF THE COMMONWEALTH OF THE PHILIPPINES SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF ESTIPONA, MUNICIPALITY OF PURA, PROVINCE OF TARLAC, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section sixty-four (e) of Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and eleven, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, the following described parcel of the private property of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, situated in the barrio of Estipona, municipality of Pura, Province of Tarlac, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Bsd-4944, to wit:

Lot No. 1635-A. – A parcel of land (lot No .1635-A of the subdivision plan Bsd-4944, being a portion of lot No. 1635 of the cadastral survey of Pura, G. L. R. O. Cadastral Record No. 102), situated in the barrio of Estipona, municipality of Pura, Province of Tarlac. – Bounded on the north, by Calle San Nicolas; on the east, by lot No. 1635 – B of the subdivision plan; on the south, by Calle Rizal; and on the west, by lot No. 1634. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 4˚ 22’ E., 1,250.51 m., more or less, from B. B. M. No. 7, Pura cadastre; thence N. 3˚ 20’ E., 116.62 m. to point “2;” thence S. 85˚ 05’ E., 18.83 m. to point “3;” thence S. 3˚ 20’ W. 115.73 m. to point “4;” thence N. 87˚ 49’ W., 18.83 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of two thousand one hundred eighty-seven square meters (2,187), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “1” and “2,” are marked by old P. L. S./B. L. concrete monuments; and points “3” and “4,” by B. L. concrete monuments; bearings true; declination, 0˚ 38’ E; date of the original survey, March to September, 1915, and that of the subdivision survey, January 6, 1936.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-first day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the second.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
