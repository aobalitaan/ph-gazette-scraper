MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 458

AMENDING FURTHER PROCLAMATION NO. 360, DATED DECEMBER 24, 1938, AS AMENDED BY PROCLAMATION NO. 390, DATED MARCH 13, 1930, REGARDING THE ALLOTMENT OF FREQUENCIES AND WAVELENGTHS TO THE UNITED PRESS ASSOCIATIONS AND ISSUING A LICENSE FOR THE USE THEREOF.

Proclamation Numbered Three hundred sixty, dated December twenty-fourth, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, as amended by Proclamation Numbered Three hundred ninety, dated March thirteenth, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, is further amended to read as follows:

“By virtue of the powers granted me by section five of Commonwealth Act Numbered Three hundred eighty-seven, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, do hereby allot to the United Press Associations the following frequencies and wavelengths, and license it to use such frequencies and wavelengths for the wireless transmission of wordage, picture, or other matter intended for publication, either in the public press or by means of broadcast radio-news to points designated:

7,485 Kcs. (38.24 m) Hongkong 9,895 Kcs. (30.32 m) Shanghai 14,800 Kcs. (20.27 m) Singapore 19,520 Kcs. (15.36 m) San Francisco, U. S. A.

“It is understood that the authority granted herein does not authorize the United Press Associations to establish and/or operate radio stations under Act Numbered Three thousand eight hundred forty-six, as amended by Commonwealth Act Numbered Three .hundred sixty-five.”

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-sixth day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
