PROCLAMATION NO. 94

RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES A PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PARAÑAQUE, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section sixty-four of Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and eleven, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for military purposes, under the administration of the Chief of Staff, Philippine Army, the following-described parcel of land, situated in the barrio of Tambo, municipality of Parañaque, Province of Rizal, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14653, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14563, N. 7 11’ E. 1,874.37 m. more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Parañaque, thence N. 2 47’ E. 33.69 m. to point 2; S. 82 59’ E. 121.93 m. to point 3; S. 9 10’ W. 239.44 m. to point 4; S. 81 39’ E. 150.70 m. to point 5; S. 10 22’ W. 15.00 m. to point 6; N. 81 30’ W. 150.40 m. to point 7; S. 9 10’ W. 211.20 to point 8; N. 81 44’ W. 104.25 m. to point 9; N. 7 19’ E. 429.05 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 54,112 square meters.

All corners are B. L. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cm.

Bounded on the north, by properties of Visayan Refining Co. and Jose Gabriel; on the east, by properties of Julian Salgado, Juliana Vizcarra Lozano claimed by Petrona Vizcarra, Juliana Bautista, Faustino Lichauco, Esperanza Castillo et al. vs. Paula Vergel et al., Bonifacio Vizcarra Patiño et al. Laureano Gabriel & Crispina Vizcarra, Narciso Mayuga, Jose Gabriel, Calle Real, properties of Marta Faustino, B. A. Green, Isidro Garcia, Julia Bautista, George C. Sellner, Alfonso Rocha, and Jose Gabriel; on the south, by properties of Marta Faustino and Julia Bautista; and on the west, by Manila Bay.

Bearings true. Declination, variable.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14653.

Surveyed: September 3-October 6 and December 10-11, 1920.

Approved: August 28, 1936.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fourteenth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines

By the President:

Secretary of the Interior
