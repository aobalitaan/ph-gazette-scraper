PROCLAMATION NO. 143

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUERO, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, ISLAND OF BOHOL.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the municipality of Duero, Province of Bohol, Island of Bohol, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan SWo-14810, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14810, N. 29˚ 53’ E., 133.56 m. more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 2, municipality of Duero, Bohol, thence S. 64˚ 37’ E. 58.36 m. to point 2; N. 26˚ 37’ E. 30.33 m. to point 3; S. 57˚ 28 E., 36.60 m. to point 4; S. 24˚ 43’ W. 151.13 m. to point 5; N. 68˚ 28’ W. 91.44 m. to point 6; N. 22˚ 49’ E. 131.62 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 13.042 square meters.

All corners are P. L. S. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cm.

Bounded on the northeast, by Calle Santo Niño and property of Manuel Abueva; on the southeast, by seashore of Mindanao Sea; on the southwest, by Calle Hermita; and on the northwest, by provincial road and Calle Rizal.

Bearings true. Declination, 1˚ 06’E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14810.

Surveyed: November 5, 1934.

Approved: March 19, 1935.

True copy approved: February 10, 1937.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Washington, D. C. (for the City of Manila), this seventeenth day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the second.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
