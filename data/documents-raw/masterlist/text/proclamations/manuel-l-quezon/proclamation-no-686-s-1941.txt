MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 686

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL BUILDING SITE AND SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION OF THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF PINGKIAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for municipal building site and school purposes, under the administration of the municipal district of Pingkian, Nueva Vizcaya, and the Director of Education, respectively, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the poblacion of the municipal district of Pingkian, Province of Nueva Vizcaya, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-445, to wit:

Lot No 1, Mr-445 . —Beginning at point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-445. N. 84° 23′ E. 571.24 m., more or less, from B. L. L, M. No. 2. municipal district of Pingkian, thence S. 86° 22′ E. 89.84 m. to point 2; S. 4 ° 25′ W. 46.41 m. to point 3; N. 87 ° 51′ W. 133.60 m. to point 4; N. 7° 43′ E. 150.37 m. to point 5; N. 88° 08′ E. 35.63 m. to point 6; S. 5° 29′ W. 41.08 m. to point 7; S. 4° 15′ W. 60.17 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 10,173 square meters.

Point 6, G. I. S. on tree; and the rest are B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north by public land (mountain) and lot No. 2; on the east, by property of Pedro Añaan, lot No. 2 and property of Tindang Bantayan; on the south, by Kayapa-Aritao trial; and on the west, by property of Waket Umaguing.

Lot No. 2. —Beginning at point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-445, N. 84° 23’ E. 571.24 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 2, municipal district of Pingkian, thence N. 4° 15’ E. 60.17 m. to point 2; 80° 35’ E. 78.66 m. to point 3; S. 8° 11’ E. 53.37 m. to point 4; N. 86° 22’ W. 89.94 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 4,703 square meters.

Point 3, G. I. S. on tree; and the rest are B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north by property of Pedro Añaan; on the east, by property of Tindang Bantayan; and on the south and west, by lot No. 1.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 36′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-445.

Surveyed: March 26, 1940.

Approved: May 24, 1940.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-seventh day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
