PROCLAMATION NO. 116

RESERVING FOR PLAYGROUND, LANDING, AND STREET EXTENSION PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF TUBIGON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, ISLAND OF BOHOL.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-one of Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and seventy-four, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for playground, landing, and street extension purposes, under the administration of the municipal government of Tubigon, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Tubigon, Province of Bohol, Island of Bohol, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-101321, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-101321, N. 89ْ 27’ W., 158.29 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 2, municipality of Tubigon, Bohol, thence N. 52ْ 12’ W., 27.15 m. to point 2; N. 53ْ 26’ W., 106.84 m. to point 3; N. 45ْ 58’ E. 79.51 m. to point 4; S. 47ْ 31’ E. 94.81 m. to point 5; S. 14ْ 06’ W. 66.88 m. to point 6; S. 49ْ 27’ E. 13.29 m. to point 7; S. 41ْ 26’ W. 7.86 m. to point 8; N. 46ْ 58’ W. 11.52 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 8,517 square meters.

Points 1, 5, 6, 7, and 8, old stone monuments.

Bounded on the northeast, by wharf (municipal road) and property of the municipality of Tubigon (lot No. 5, II-3001); on the southeast, by property of the municipality of Tubigon (lot No. 5, II-3001) and Calle Progreso; on the southwest, by property of Baldomera Falcon (II-2917) and Bohol Strait; and on the northwest, by Bohol Strait.

Bearings true. Declination 1ْ 33’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-101321.

Surveyed: January 21, 1936. Approved: May 21, 1936.

NOTE.—All corners not otherwise described are P. L. S. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cm.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-fifth day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the second.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
