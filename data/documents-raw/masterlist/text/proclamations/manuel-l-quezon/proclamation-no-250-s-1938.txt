PROCLAMATION NO. 250

RESERVING FOR PROVINCIAL CAPITOL GROUND AND PARK PURPOSES FOUR PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, ISLAND OF PANAY.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for provincial capitol ground and park purposes, under the administration of the Province of Antique, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the municipality of San Jose, Province of Antique, Island of Panay, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15150, to wit:

Lot No. 1644.—Beginning at point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands Swo—15150, S.83° 01’ E. 11.84 m, from B.L.L.M. No. 1, municipality of San Jose, thence S. 82° 09’ E. 63.03 m. to point 2; S. 6° 46’ W. 48.84 m. to point 3; N. 83° 59’ W. 58.12 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 3,016 square meters.

All corners are B.L. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cm.

Bounded on the north, by Calle Gobernador V. Gella; on the east, by Calle Carentas; on the south, by Calle Gobernador Fullon; and on the west, by Calle Real.

Lot No. 1665.—Beginning at point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15150, N.55° 59’ E. 89.40 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 2, municipality of San Jose, thence S. 7° 20’ W. 66.80 m. to point 2; N. 77° 39’ W. 52.92 m. to point 3; N. 3° 09’ E. 59.70 m. to point 4; S. 85° 18’ E. 57.12 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 3,465 square meters.

All corners are B.L. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cm.

Bounded on the north, by Calle Gobernador Fullon; on the east, by Calle Carentas; on the south, by Calle Teniente I. Natividad; and on the west, by Calle Real.

Lot No. 1666.—Beginning at point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15150, S. 47° 05’ W. 70.01 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 1, municipality of San Jose, thence N. 3° 39’ E. 51.96 m. to point 2; S. 85° 06’ e. 49.65 m. to point 3; S. 3° 30’ W. 52.38 m. to point 4; N. 85° 48’ W. 49.76 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 2,568 square meters.

Point 2, old monument; and the rest are B.L. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cm.

Bounded on the north, by Calle Gobernador V. Gella; on the east, by Calle Real; on the south, by Calle Gobernador Fullon; and on the west, by Calle Coronel S. Salvio.

Lot No. 1667.—Beginning at point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15150, N. 78° 17’ W. 49.22 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 2, municipality of San Jose, thence N. 3° 37’ E. 51.18 m. to point 2; S. 84° 17’ E. 49.62 m. to point 3; S. “1” 00’ W. 59.37 m. to point 4; N. 78° 37’ W. 53.23 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 2,806 square meters.

Points 1 and 2, old monuments; and the rest are B.L. concrete monuments, 15 to 60 m.

Bounded on the north, by Calle Gobernador Fullon; on the east, by Calle Real; on the south, by Calle Teniente I. Natividad; and on the west, by Calle Coronel S. Salvio.

Bearing true. Declination 1° 31 E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15150.

Surveyed: February, 1925-April 1927.

Approved: November 4, 1937.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-seventh day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.
