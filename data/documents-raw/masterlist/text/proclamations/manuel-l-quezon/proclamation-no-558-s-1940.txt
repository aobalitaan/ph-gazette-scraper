MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 558

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF BUNHIAN, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF MAYOYAO, SUBPROVINCE OF IFUGAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Bunhian, municipal district of Mayoyao, sub-province of Ifugao, Mountain Province, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-105999, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-105999, S. 83° 59′ E. 19,501.30 m., more or less, from B. L. Triangulation Station Amuyao, thence S. 6° 27′ E. 154.14 m. to point 2; S. 83° 59′ W. 91.13 m. to point 3; S. 86° 58′ W. 80.62 m. to point 4; N. 3° 47′ W. 146.20 m. to point 5; N. 54° 58′ E. 102.11 m. to point 6; S. 64° 50′ E. 88.26 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 29,354 square meters.

Points 1, 2, 3 and 4, P. L. S. stone monuments; point 5, nail on cross on tree; point 6, cross marked on big boulder.

Bounded on all sides, by public land.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 30′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-105999.

Surveyed: May 3—5, 1938. Approved: October 28, 1938.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-second day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
