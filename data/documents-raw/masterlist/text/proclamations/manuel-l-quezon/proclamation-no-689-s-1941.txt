MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 689

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OP THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF MAÑGALI AND HUNDUAN, MUNICIPAL DISTRICTS OF TANUDAN AND HUNDUAN, RESPECTIVELY, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Mañgali and Hunduan, municipal districts of Tanudan and Hunduan, respectively, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-108267 and Psu-107355, to wit:

(Psur-108207, barrio of Mañgali) —Beginning at point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-108267, S. 52° 42’ E. 16,948.08 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No.1, municipal district of Lubuagan, thence S. 77° 20′ W. 136.33 m. to point 2; N. 22° 47′ W. 71.09 m. to point 3; N, 67° 26’ E. 114.63 M. to point 4; S. 47° 18′ E. 52.56 m. to point 5, S. 20° 03′ E. 46.83 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 10,752 square meters

Points 1, 2 and 3, P. L. S. stone monuments, and points 4 and 5, nails in “X” on trees.

Bounded on the northeast by property of Lag-ao, Allado and Basao (joint owners); on the southeast, by trail 10.00 m. wide; on the southwest, by public land; and on the northwest, by public land.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 22’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-108267.

Surveyed: May 18-31. 1938 and May 2-4, 1939.

Approved: July 26, 1939.

(Psu-107355, barrio of Hunduan) .—Beginning at point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-107355, S. 55° 28′ E. 93.17 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipal district of Hunduan, thence N. 42° 25′ E. 24.15 m. to point 2; N. 31° 28′ W. 12.03 m. to point 3; N. 83° 29′ E. 24.41 m. to point 4; S. 8° 50′ E. 32.12 m. to point 5; S. 42° 32′ E. 30.42 m. to point 6; N. 83° 18′ E. 14.59 m. to point 7; S. 27° 00′ E. 24.11 m. to point 8; S. 76° 23′ E. 32.91 m. to point 9; S. 39° 41′ W. 12.36 m. to point 10; S. 18° 02′ W. 31.28 m. to point 11; S. 35° 01′ W. 8.03 m. to point 12; N. 60° 11′ W. 42.36 m. to point 13; N 54° 50′ W. 21.64 m. to point 14; N. 48° 61′ W. 19.88 m. to point 15; N. 14° 38′ E. 6.97 m. to point 16; N. 31° 55′ E. 8.72 m. to point 17; N. 29° 24′ E. 4.79 m. to point 18; N 47° 15′ W. 46.75 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 5,396 square meters.

Point 1, old B. L. marked on boulder; point 2, old B. L. concrete monument; point 3, intersection points 6, 15 and 16, nails on trees; point 9, nail on “X” on tree; point 10, P. L. S. on boulder; points 11 and 13, old P. L. S. stone monuments; and the rest are P. L. S. stone monuments.

Bounded on the north by properties of Bulanan Pait and Otgalon Hangda-an; on the northeast by properties of Otgalon Hangda-an and Bayayo Ananayo; on hte southeast, by properties of Cabigat Bimmotiog and Pumihik Kinquifigan (joint owners) and the Roman Catholic Church (mission site); on the southwest, by road and property of the municipal district government of Hunduan (presidencia site); and on the northwest, by road and property of the municipal district government of Hunduan (presidencia site).

Bearings true. Declination 0° 42′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-107355.

Surveyed: April 8 and December 24, 1938.

Approved: August 2, 1939.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this seventh day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
