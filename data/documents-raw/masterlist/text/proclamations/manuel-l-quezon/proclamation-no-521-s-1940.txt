MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 521

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BURNAY, SUBPROVINCE OF IFUGAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipal district of Burnay, subprovince of Ifugao, Mountain Province, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands, plan Psu-107507, to wit:

A parcel of land (Plan Psu-107507, G. L. R. O. No.      ), situated in the Poblacion, municipal district of Burney. subprovince of Ifugao, Mountain Province. Bounded on the N. E. by the properties of Malaiyu Pumihic, Daulayan Tumit and trail 6.00 m. wide; on the S. E., by trail 6.00 m. wide: on the S. W., by the properties of Calixto Bubud, Ignacio Tultog and Dumay Buquiag; and on the N. W., by the properties of Tultug Pagahitto and Dudduli Ñgini. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 38° 49′ E., 5,966.75 m., more or less, from Triangulation Station. Kiangan, Ifugao. Mountain Province; thence N. 77° 41′ W., 42.16 m. to point “2”; thence N. 39° 37′ W., 12.88 m. to point “3”; thence N. 25° 27’ W., 38.03 m. to point “4”;  thence N. 27° 47’ E., 40.28 m. to point “5”; thence N. 45° 18’ E., 32.36 m. to point “6”; thence S. 60° 43’ E., 41.53 m. to point “7”; thence S. 72° 53’ E., 37.70 m. to point “8”; thence S. 47° 20’ E., 41.01 m. to point “9”; thence S. 58° 49’ W., 29.61 m. to point “10”; thence S.55° 04’ W., 64.70 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of Nine thousand four hundred (9,400) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground point “2” is marked by big nail on cross cut on bili tree 42 cm. diam. 53 cm. above ground; point “7” by big nail in cross cut on camotiti tree 90 cm. diam. 65 cm. diam. above ground; point “9” by nail in cross cut on orange tree 15 cm. diam. 1 mater above ground; and the rest by P. L. S. concrete monuments 15 x 60 cm.; bearings true, declination 1° 00’ E., date of survey, November 25-29, 1935 and June 20-22, 1938 and that of the approval May 17, 1939.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this first day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
