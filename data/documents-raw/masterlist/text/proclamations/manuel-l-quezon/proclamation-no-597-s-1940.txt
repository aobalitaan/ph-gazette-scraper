MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 597

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, ISLAND OF PANAY.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Concepcion, Province of Iloilo, island of Panay, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-150-A, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of lands plan Mr-150-A, S. 65° 11’ W. 226.54 m., more or less from B. L. L. M. No. 2, municipality of Concepcion, thence S. 71° 52’ E. 28.41 m. to point 2; S. 16° 12’ W. 19.15 m. to point 3; S. 21° 49’ E. 52.81 m. to point 4; S. 71° 37’ E. 26.57 m. to point 5; S. 23° 07’ W. 106.66 m. to point 6; S. 23° 07’ W. 0.34 m. to point 7; N. 71° 44’ W. 88.81 m. to point 8; N. 21° 07’ E. 168.32 m. to point 1, point of the beginning.

Containing an area of 12,355 square meters.

Points 1, 2 and 8, B. L. concrete monuments; 3, 4 and 6, old B. L. concrete monuments; point 5, old concrete monument, center of copper nail; point 7, interior.

Bounded on the northeast by Calle Rizal and property of the Roman Catholic Bishop of Jaro; on the southeast, by properties of the Roman Catholic Bishop of Jaro and the municipal government of Concepcion (school site); on the southwest, by Calle Loreto; and on the northwest, by Calle Felizardo Azucena.

Bearings true. Declination 1° 41′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-150-A.

Surveyed: March 19, 1940.

Approved: May 24, 1940.

Note:

This survey is inside of lot No. 11, II-275 as surveyed for the municipality of Sara.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fifth day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
