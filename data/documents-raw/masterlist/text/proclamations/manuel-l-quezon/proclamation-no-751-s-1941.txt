MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 751

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES ONE PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF DITUCALAN, MUNICIPALITY OF ILIGAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Ditucalan, municipality of Iligan, province of Lanao, island of Mindanao and particularly described in plan Psu-110422, to wit:

Psu-110422

A parcel of land (described on plan, Psu-110422, G.L.R.O. Record No.__________), situated in the barrio of Ditucalan, municipality of Iligan, province of Lanao.

Bounded on the N. by the property of Dalmacio Ganoy, H-144757; on the E. by the properties of Dalmacio Canoy, H-144757, and Natividad Paradela; on the S. by the property of Natividad Paradela;and on the W. by the properties of Natividad Paradela and Dalmacio Canoy, H-144757, and the Barrio Road, 10.00 m. wide. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 64 deg. 07′ E., 3072,71 m. more or less from B.L.B.M. No. 1, Barrio of Buru-on, municipality of Iligan:-

thence S. 33 deg. 08’ W., 57.42 m. to point “2”; thence S. 88 deg. 23 ’ W., 84.90 m. to point “3”; thence N. 6 deg 31’E., 53.14 m. to point “4”; thence N. 18 deg. 18’ E., 39.00 m. to point “5”; thence N. 41deg. 25′ E., 13.01 m. to point “6”; thence N. 41 deg 32′ E.,5.73 m. to point “7”; thence N. 89 deg. 54’ E., 91.58 m. to point “8”; thence S. 6 deg. 25’ W., 53.90 m. to

the point of beginning; containing an area of  TEN THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED FIFTY NINE SQUARE METERS (10,359), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and on the ground, points “1”, “4”and “8” are marked by Old. B. L. Concrete monuments 15 by 60 cm; point “2” by P. L. S. Stone Monument 1 m, by 2 m.; and the rest by P. L. Concrete monuments 15 by 60 cm; bearings true; declination 2 deg. 02’ E.; date of survey, July 1 and 3 1939″, and that of the approval, July 3, 1940.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this 12 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
