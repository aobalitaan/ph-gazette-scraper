MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 694

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF TULAED AND ALIMIT, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF MAYOYAO, SUBPROVINCE OF IFUGAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Tulaed and Alimit, municipal district of Mayoyao, subprovince of Ifugao, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plans Psu-105486 and Psu-105487, to wit:

(Psu-105i86, barrio of Tulaed. —Beginning at point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-105486, S. 12° 15′ E. 3,410.62 m., more or less, from Triangulation Station Amuyao B. L., thence N. 21″ 34′ E. 20.50 m. to point 2; S. 63° 16′ E. 30.14 m. to point 3; S. 35° 01′ E. 20.93 m. to point 4; N. 86° 33′ E. 38.25 m. to point 5; S. 3° 05′ E. 74.90 m. to point 6; S. 26° 19′ W. 41.02 m. to point 7; S. 79° 01′ W. 48.32 m. to point 8; N. 15° 46′ W. 27.94 m. to point, point of beginning.

Containing an area of four thousand five hundred thirty-four square meters (4,534), more or less.

Points 1, 2, 6 and 7, stones marked P. L. S.; points 3, 4 and 5, crosses on trees; point 8, cross on big stone.

Bounded on the north by property of Balaho; on the northeast, by public land and property of Balaho; on the east southeast and south, by property of Annapan; and on southwest and northwest, by public land.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 30’ E.

Point referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Fsu-105486.

Surveyed: May 7, 1938.

Approved: September 22, 1938.

Approved: September 22, 1938. In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fourteenth day of March, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines
