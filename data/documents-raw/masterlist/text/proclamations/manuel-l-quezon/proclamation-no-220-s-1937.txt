PROCLAMATION NO. 220

ESTABLISHING AS BOÑGABON-BALER NATIONAL PARK FOR THE BENEFIT AND ENJOYMENT OF THE PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES THE PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF BOÑGABON AND BALER, PROVINCES OF NUEVA ECIJA AND TAYABAS, ISLAND OF LUZON.
