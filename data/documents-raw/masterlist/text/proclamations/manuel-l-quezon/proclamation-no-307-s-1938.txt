MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 307

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 17, DATED FEBRUARY 19, 1917, AND RESERVING FOR THE USE OF THE PHILIPPINE ARMY, PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN THE CITY OF ILOILO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby exclude from the operation of Executive Order Numbered Seventeen, dated February nineteen, nineteen hundred and seventeen, and reserve for the use of the Philippine Army, under the administration of the Chief of Staff, a parcel of land situated in the City of lloilo, Philippines, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Bcs-79, to wit:

A parcel of land (lot No. 1 of the consolidation and subdivision plan Bcs-79, being a portion of the consolidated lot No. 1063-B of Plan Swo-3388 and lots Nos. 1061 and 1062 of the cadastral survey of Iloilo, G. L. R. O. Record No. 9739), situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Iloilo, Province of Iloilo. Bounded on the NE., by lot No. 1 of plan 11-5188, lots Nos. 1056-A and 1056-B of plan Swo-5278 and lot No. 1055 of Iloilo cadastre: on the SE by Calle Duran; on the SW., by lot No. 2 of the consolidation and subdivision plan; and on the NW., by Calle Zamora. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N 84° 04′ E., 1,149.45 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M No. 1, municipality of Iloilo, thence S. 35° 30′ W., 92.90 m. to point “2;” thence N. 35° 49′ W., 239.80 m. to point “3:”; thence N. 48° 11′ E., 17.13 m. to point “4;” thence N.48° 11′ E., 68.00 m. to point “5;” thence S. 31° 39’ E., 49.00 m. to point “6;” thence S. 31° 39′ E., 60.00 m. to point “7;” thence S. 31° 39′ E., 39.75 m. to point “8;” thence S. 52° 42′ E., 29.28 m. to point “9;” thence 32° 48′ E., 4.64 m. to point “10;” thence S. 44° 38′ E., 38.37 m. to point of beginning; containing an area of eighteen thousand four hundred eighty-seven square meters (18,487), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “1” and “3” marked by B. L. concrete monuments; point “2,” by concrete post; point “8,” by old P. L. S. B. L. concrete monument; and the rest, by old B. L. concrete monuments; bearings true; declination, 1° 48′ E.; date of the original survey, September, 1912-December, 1913, and that of the consolidation and subdivision survey, December 29, 1936.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this tenth day of August in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines the third.

MANUEL L QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
