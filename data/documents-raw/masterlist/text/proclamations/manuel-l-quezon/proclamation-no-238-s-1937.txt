MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 238

RESERVING FOR ROAD PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF SAN PABLO, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ESTEBAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for road purposes, under the administration of the Province of Ilocos Sur, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of San Pablo, municipality of San Esteban, Province of Ilocos Sur, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Pr-167, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-167, N. 4° 40′ W. 765.25 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of San Esteban, thence N. 13° 10′ W. 5.27 m. to point 2; N. 30° 40′ W. 5.37 m. to point 3; N. 45° 35′ W. 5.49 m. to point 4; N. 44° 16′ W. 3.84 m. to point 5; N. 76° 55′ W. 28.85 m. to point 6; N. 75° 22′ W. 17.85 m. to point 7; N. 73° 00′ W. 7.11 m . to point 8; N. 75° 11′ W. 13.60 m. to point 9; N. 76° 55′ W. 10.65 m. to point 10; N. 73° 44′ W. 11.99 m. to point 11; N. 74° 21′ W. 12.68 m. to point 12; N. 3° 27′ E. 7.79 m. to point 13; N. 78° 21′ W. 16.58 m . to point 14; S. 89° 24′ W. 27.72 m. to point 15; S. 0° 36′ E. 1.92 m. to point 16; N. 77° 05′ W. 78.99 m . to point 17; N. 16° 32′ E. 12.30 m. to point 18; S. 79° 22′ E. 83.89 m. to point 19; S. 82° 02′ E. 9.66 m. to point 20; S. 81° 42′ E. 7.49 m. to point 21; S. 79° 31′ E. 15.00 m. to point 22; S. 0° 19′ E. 1.79 m. to point 23; S. 75° 07′ E. 17.40 m. to point 24; S. 75° 12′ E. 14.80 m. to point 25; S. 75° 12′ E. 13.27 m. to point 26; S. 75° 15′ E. 13.94 m. to point 27; S. 75° 16′ E. 47.79 m . to point 28; S. 89° 53′ E. 4.99 m. to point 29; N. 80° 37′ E. 5.03 m. to point 30; N. 78° 44′ E. 7.93 m. to point 31; N. 45° 14′ E. 6.73 m. to point 32; S. 19° 34′ W. 40.17 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 0.3456 hectare.

Points 13, 14, IB, 21, and 23, old B. L. concrete monu­ments; points 16 and 17, old P. L. S. concrete monuments; points 7, 8, 27, 28, 29, 30, 31, and 32, on bank of creek.

Bounded on the north, by public land, properties claimed by Juan Vergara Sipping, Juan Eala, Petra Vergara, Eulogio Andrada, Ruflna Abad, creek, public land (marshy land), and creek; on the east, by provincial road; on the south, by properties claimed by Andres Ayson, Bernardino Abellera (fish pond), creek, properties claimed by Bernardino Abellera, Proceso Empleo, Mariano Abad, Josefa Vergara, Bernardino Abellera, Hipolito Guzman, Compania General de Tabacos de Filipinas claimed by Potenciana Alayan, and Casido Abad; and on the west, by property claimed by Casido Abad and public land.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 32′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-167.

Surveyed: October 24, 1930.

Approved: September 2, 1932.

NOTE.—All corners not otherwise described are B. L. cylin­drical concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cms.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-third day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines
