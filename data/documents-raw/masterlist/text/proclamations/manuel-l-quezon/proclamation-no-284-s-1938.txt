PROCLAMATION NO. 284

ESTABLISHING AS A COAL MINING RESERVATION TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF KABASALAN AND MARGOSATUBIG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eleven of Commonwalth Act Numbered One hundred and thirty-seven, I Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, hereby set apart as coal mining reservation and proclaim it closed to mining location and lease the following-described parcels of the public domain situated in the municipalities of Kabasalan and Margosatubig, Province of Zamboanga, Island of Mindanao to wit:

Parcel No. 1—Beginning at Ticauan Point on the shore of Sibuguey Bay, Province of Zamboanga; thence northeast to location monument No. 1, municipality of Kabasalan; thence eastward to the southernmost point on the shore of Lake Wood, municipality of Margosatubig; thence southward to the mouth of Kumalarang River at Dumaguilas Bay; thence to Lapat point; thence following the coast lines of Tantanang Bay, Canalizo Strait, Locsico Bay, and Sibuguey Bay to the point of beginning.

Parcel No. 2.—Consisting of the whole Islands of Olutanga, Lapinigan, Litayen, Silagui, Lutangan, and Lipari of the municipality of Margosatubig, all located south of parcel No. 1.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this nineteenth day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines

By the President:
