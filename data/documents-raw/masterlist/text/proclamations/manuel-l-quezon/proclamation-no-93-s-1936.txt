PROCLAMATION NO. 93

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL DISTRICT GOVERNMENT SITE A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF ATOK, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-one of Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and seventy-four, as amended, I hereby withdraw form sale or settlement and reserve for municipal district government site, under the administration of the Municipal District of Atok, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the Barrio of Principal, municipal District of Atok, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the Barrio of Principal, Municipal District of Atok, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14593, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14593, S. 9ْ 11’ W. 35.92 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 2, municipal district of Atok, Mountain Province, thence N. 32ْ 32’ W. 27.41 m. to point 2; N. 7ْ 38’ E. 101.82 m. to point 3: N. 75ْ 53’ E. 80.12 m. to point 4; S. 11ْ 26’ E. 114.62 m. to point 5; S. 72ْ 32’ W. 104.08 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 11,658 square meters.

Point 1, old B. L. concrete monument; point 2, cross on rock; points 3, 4, and 5, P. L. S. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north, by property of heirs of Mora; on the northeast and southeast, by property of Filomena Palasi; on the southwest, by property of Cela; and on the northwest, by property of heirs of Mora.

Bearings true. Declination, 0ْ 32’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14593.

Surveyed: October 12-13, 1934. Approved: August 6, 1936.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fourteenth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
