MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 454

RESERVING FOR WAREHOUSE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CALAMAGUI, MUNICIPALITY OF ILAGAN, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for warehouse purposes, under the administration of the National Warehousing Corporation, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Calamagui, municipality of Ilagan, Province of Isabela, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Ir-468, to wit:

Beginning at point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-468, S. 47° 39′ W. 2,181.05 m., more or less, from B. L. L . M. No. 1, municipality of Ilagan, thence N. 69° 18′ W. 205.03 m. to point 2; N. 23° 49′ E. 169.86 m. to point 3; S. 47° 48′ E. 177.24 m. to point 4; S. 4° 15′ W. 109.17 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 2.5017 hectares.

Points 1 and 2, old B. L. concrete monuments; points 3 and 4, B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast and east by public land; on the southwest, by property of Moises L. Cruz; and on the northwest, by provincial road.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 00′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-468.

Surveyed: July 9, 1939.

Approved: August 17, 1939.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of- Manila, this nineteenth day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
