MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 513

REVOKING PHILIPPINE COMMISSION RESOLUTION NO. 10 DATED JANUARY 23, 1913, AND RESERVING THE PARCELS OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN FOR HOSPITAL PURPOSES

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby revoke Philippine Commission Resolution Numbered Ten dated January twenty-three, nineteen hundred and thirteen, and reserve the parcels of land embraced therein for hospital purposes under the administration of the Province of Lanao.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this first day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
