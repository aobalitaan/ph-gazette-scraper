PROCLAMATION NO. 159

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 97, DATED OCTOBER 11, 1909, AND OPENING TO DISPOSITION, A PARCEL OF LAND DESCRIBED AS LOT B IN SUBDIVISION PLAN BSD-4509, LOCATED IN THE BARRIO OF INAGAUAN, MUNICIPALITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN.
