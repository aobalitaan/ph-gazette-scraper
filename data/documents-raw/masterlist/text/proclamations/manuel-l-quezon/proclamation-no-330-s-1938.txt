MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAIMATION NO. 330

FIXING SUNDAY, JANUARY 1, 1939, AS CENSUS DAY

Whereas Proclamation Numbered Three hundred and twenty, dated September seventh, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, amending Proclamation Numbered Two hundred and sixty-six, dated March thirty-first, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, fixed Tuesday, November fifteenth, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, as Census Day; and

Whereas in order that the qualifications of all persons who have applied or have been recommended for census supervisors or enumerators may be adequately verified so that only the best qualified persons will be appointed and in order to give ample time for the training of all census supervisors and enumerators:

Now, therefore, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the provisions of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and seventy, do hereby amend Proclamation Numbered Three hundred and twenty, and fix Sunday, January first, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, instead of November fifteenth, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, as Census Day.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eleventh day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
