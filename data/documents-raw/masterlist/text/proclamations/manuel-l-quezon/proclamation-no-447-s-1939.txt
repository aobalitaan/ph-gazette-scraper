MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 447

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF SIBUTO, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF AWING, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Sibuto, municipal district of Awang, Province of Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psm-547, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15928, which is identical with B. L. 3. M. No. 1, barrio of Sibuto, municipal district of Awang, thence S. 70° 38′ E. 182.48 m. to point 2; S. 23° 03′ W. 146.99 m. to point 3; N. 70° 41′ W. 177.13 m. to point 4; N. 20° 58′ E. 146.88 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 26,386 square meters.

Points 2 and 3, B. L. concrete monuments; point 4, B. L. B. M. No. 2.

Bounded on the northeast, southeast, southwest, and northwest, by property of Prady (Tiruray).

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 43′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15928 (Psm-547).

Surveyed: April 26, 1930.

Approved: May 9, 1932.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this first day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
