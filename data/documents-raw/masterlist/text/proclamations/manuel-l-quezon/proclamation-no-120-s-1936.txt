PROCLAMATION NO. 120

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 48, SERIES OF 1918, RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF GLAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby amended Executive Order Numbered Forty-eight, series of nineteen hundred and eighteen, to read as follows:
