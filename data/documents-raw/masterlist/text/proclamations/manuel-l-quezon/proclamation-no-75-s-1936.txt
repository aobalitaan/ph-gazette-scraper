PROCLAMATION 75

FIXING THE DATE FOR THE ELECTION OF THE THIRD MEMBER OF THE PROVINCIAL BOARD OF BUKIDNON

Pursuant to the provisions of section three of Act Numbered Two thousand four hundred and twenty-nine, I hereby fix September first, nineteen hundred and thirty-six, for the election of the third member of the provincial board of Bukidnon.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eleventh day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines

By the President:

Secretary of the Interior
