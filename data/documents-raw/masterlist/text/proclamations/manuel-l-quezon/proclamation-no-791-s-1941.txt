MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 791

RESERVING FOR HALL SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF NANENG, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF TABUK, SUBPROVINCE OF KALINGA, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for municipal hall site purposes, under the administration of the municipal district of Tabuk, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Naneng, municipal district of Tabuk, subprovince of Kalinga, Mountain Province, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-107553, to wit:

(Psu-107553)

(PRESIDENCIA SITE)

“A PARCEL OF LAND (Plan Psu-107553, G.L.R.O. Rec. No. ), situated on the barrio of Naneng, municipal district of Tabuk, Mountain Province (subprovince of Kalinga), , island of Luzon

Bounded on the N. by public land; on the NE. by Tangob Creek (10-2-m. wide); on the SE. and SW. by trail (20.00m. wide); and on the NW. by public land. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan. Being S.66 deg. 04’W. 50180.88 meters more or less from Triangulation Tuguegarao Church Tower, municipality of Tuguegarao, thence

S. 10 deg. 11’E. 108.97m. to point “2”; thence S. 35 deg. 35’W. 52.70m. to point “3”; thence S. 72 deg. 10’W. 65.20m. to point “4”; thence S. 46 deg. 42’W. 30.78m. to point “5”; thence N. 62 deg. 16’W. 24.89m. to point “6”; thence N. 16 deg. 37’W. 51.72m. to point “7”; thence N. 22 deg. 01’E. 151.37m. to point “8”; thence

S. 82 deg. 17’E. 76.65m. to point of beginning; containing an area of TWENTY THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED SIXTY THREE SQUARE METERS (20,263) more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, point “1” is marked by boulder stone marked P. L. S. 80 x 80 cms.; point “2” by P.L.S. stone 20 x 40 x 60cms; and points “3” to “8” inclusive by Nails on crosses on trees.

Bearings true: Declination 0 deg. 09’E.

Date of surveyed: June 5-10, 1938 and February 1, 1939.

Date of approval: April 27, 1939.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this 18 th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
