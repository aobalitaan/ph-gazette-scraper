PROCLAMATION NO. 97

RESERVING FOR THE EXLUSIVE USE OF NON-CHRISTIANS TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MARCELINO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-two of Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and seventy-four, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for the exclusive use of non-Christians, under the administration of the Director, Bureau of non-Christian Tribes, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Aglao, municipality of San Marcelino, Province of Zambales, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plans Ir-281 and Ir-281-A, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-281, S. 30ْ 59’ E. 2,590.82 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of Aglao, municipality of San Marcelino, thence S. 85ْ 37’ W. 181.94 m. to point 2; N. 89ْ 04’ W. 447.83 m. to point 3; N. 16ْ 30’  W. 632.16 m. to point 4; S. 48ْ  50’  E. 122.32 m. to point 5; S. 74ْ  56’  E. 333.26 to point 6; S. 84ْ 43’ E. 406.88 m. to point 7; S. 12ْ 25’  E. 133.86 m. to point 8; S. 61ْ 02’ W. 99.31 m. to point 9; S. 12ْ 16’ E. 212.42 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 31.3138 hectares.

Points 1 and 4, old P. L. S. concrete monuments; point 2 old cross on tree; point 3, old cross on boulder; points 5, 6, 7, 8, and 9, stakes; points 1, 8, and 9, on bank of Malapo River; points 4, 5, 6, and 7, on bank of Paoen River.

Bounded on the north, by Paoen River and property claimed by the Aglao Agricultural Development Co., Inc.; on the east, by Malapo River; and on the south and west, by public land (forest zone).

Bearings true. Declination, 0ْ 52’ e.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-281.

Surveyed: February 13, 1933. Approved: June 13, 1933.

NOTE.—This survey is portion of Psu-36067, lot No. 1, as surveyed for the Aglao Agricultural Development Co., Inc.

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-281-A, S. 30ْ 59’ E. 2,590.82 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of Aglao, municipality of San Marcelino thence S. 3ْ 02’ W. 371.58 m. to point 2; S. 81ْ 12’ W. 443.34 m. to point 3; S. 80ْ 58’ W. 438.19 m. to point 4; N. 16ْ 26’ E. 381.75 m. to point 5; N. 5ْ 07’ W. 394.90 m. to point 6; N. 1ْ 58’ E. 339.33 m. to point 7; S 16ْ 30’ E. 623.16 m. to point 8; S. 89ْ 04’ E. 447.83 m. to point 9; N. 85ْ 37’ E. 181.94 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 39.9645 hectares.

Points 1 and 7, old P. L. S. concrete monuments; points 2, 3, and 4, galvanized iron spikes on trees; point 5, cross on boulder; point 6, B. L. concrete monument; point 8, old cross on boulder; point 9, old cross on tree.

Bounded on the north, by property of Macarang Negrito Reservation vs. The Aglao Agricultural Development Co., Inc.; on the east, by property of Macarang Negrito Reservation vs. The Aglao Agricultural Development Co., Inc. and forest zone (public land); and on the south and west, by forest zone (public land).

Bearings true. Declination, 1ْ 31’ E.

Point referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-281-A.

Surveyed: June 18-20, 1936. Approved: August 10, 1936.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-first day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.
