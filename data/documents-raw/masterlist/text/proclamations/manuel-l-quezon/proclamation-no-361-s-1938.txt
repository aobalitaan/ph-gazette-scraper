MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION. NO. 361

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF BITE AND COMON, MUNICIPALITY OF ARITAO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Bureau of uducation, subject to private rights if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Bite and Comon, municipality of Aritao, Province of Nueva Vizcaya. Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-395, to wit:

Lot No. 1 (barrio of Bite).— Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-395, N. 88° 03’ E. 41.71 m. from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of Bite, municipality of Aritao, thence S. 42° 35’ W., 54.18 m. to point 2; N. 42° 35’ W. 3.62 m. to point 3; N. 48° 15’ W. 102.28 m. to point 4; N. 42° 41’ E. 0.97 m. to point 5; N. 42° 41’ E. 105.26 m. to point 6; S. 47° 32’ E. 101.43 m. to point 7; S. 41° 47’ W. 47.16 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1.0775 hectares.

Point 3, intersection; point 4, old intersection; point 5, old B. L. cylindrical concrete monument; and the rest arc B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast, by public land; on the southeast, by properties of Apolonio Ordoa and Juan Pera; on the southwest, by property of Baldomero Bustamante; and on the northwest, by property of Domingo Gragasin.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 88′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-395, sheet No. 1.

Surveyed: June 17, 1938. Approved: August 31, 1938.

Lot No. 2 ( barrio of Comon) .—Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-395, S. 55° 28′ W. 158136 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No 1, barrio of Tabueng, municipality of Aritao, thence N. 82° 43′ E. 119.95 m. to point 2; S. 9° 46’ E. 103.69 m. to point 3; S. 8° 43′ W. 158.b8 m. to point 4; N. 4° 56′ W. 98.18 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1.5552 hectares.

All corners are B. L. concrete monuments, 15 by 69 cm.

Bounded on the north, by property of Vicente Barbado; on the east, by barrio road; ; on the south, by property of Marcelino Lime; and on the west, by property of Peliciano Santiago.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 38′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-395, sheet No. 2.

Surveyed: June 14, 1938. Approved: August 31, 1938.

In witness, v/hereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the-Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-fourth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
