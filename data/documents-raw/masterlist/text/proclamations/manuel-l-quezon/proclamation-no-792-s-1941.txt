MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 792

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES ONE PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF SINABSABAN, MUNICIPALITY OF CUARTERO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, ISLAND OF PANAY

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Sinabsaban, Municipality of Cuartero, Province of Capiz, Island of Panay, and particularly described on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-497, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-497, N.21-25’W. 2381.44 m. more or less from B.B.M. No. 36, Dao Cad. No. 147, thence

N. 83-15’W. 146.70 m. to point 2; N. 16-11’E. 75.75 m. to point 3; S. 83-18’E. 139.66 m. to point 4; S. 10-52’W. 75.02 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of TEN THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED FIVE SQUARE METERS (10,705), more or less.

All corners are B.L. Conc. Mons.

Bounded on the Northeast, Southeast, Southwest and Northwest, by Public Land.

Bearings true. Declination 1-28’E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Mr-497.

Surveyed: January 21, 1941.

Approved: October 20, 1941.

N O T E:

This is lot No. 5629 a portion of lot No. 3689 of Dao Cad. No. 147, Case No. 4.

Lot No. 3689 was declared Public Lands on Dec. 29, 1923.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this 18 th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
