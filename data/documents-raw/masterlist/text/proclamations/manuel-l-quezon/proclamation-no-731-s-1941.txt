PROCLAMATION NO. 731

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES SEVEN PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF AMMACIAN, ACIGA, DUGPA, TAGA, TAPPO, AND LIMOS, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF PINUKPUK, SUBPROVINCE OF KALINGA, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Ammacian, Aciga, Dugpa, Taga, Tappo, and Limos, municipal district of Pinukpuk, subprovince of Kalinga, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plans Psu-105903, 105904, 105899, 105762, 105763, and 105606, to wit:
