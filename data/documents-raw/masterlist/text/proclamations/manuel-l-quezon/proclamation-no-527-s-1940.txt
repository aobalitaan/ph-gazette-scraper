MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 527

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES FOUR (4) PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF JOLO, PROVINCE OF SULU, ISLAND OF JOLO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Jolo Province of Sulu, island of Jolo and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Pr-332, to wit:

Lot No. 1 .-Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-232, N. 37° 03’ W. 286.53 m., more or less, from B.L.L.B. No. 2, Mt. Dajo Cad. No. 99, thence S. 45° 51’ W. 42.63 m. to point 2; N. 22° 31’ W. 20.00 m. to point 5; N. 0° 13’ E. 18.76 m. to point 6; N. 0° 13’ W. 0.05 m. to point 7; S. 44° 31’ E. 65.55 m. to point 8; S. 45° 51’ W. 0.04m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1,643 square meters.

All corners are P. L. S. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast by lot No. 4; on the southwest, by Sulu Sea; and on the southwest and west, by proposed road.

Lot No. 2 .—Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-232, S. 73° 17’ W. 96.4 m. from monument No. 2, Jolo Townsite K-8, thence S. 42° 40’ E. 43.03 m. to point 2; S. 45° 51’ W. 51.02 m. to point 3; N. 45° 08’ W. 43.73 m. to point 4; N. 46° 37’ E. 52.89 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 2, 254 square meters.

Points 1, 2, 3 and 4, old stakes.

Bounded on the northeast by lot No. 5-C of plan Bsd-6157; on the southeast and southwest, by lot No. 4; and on the northwest, by lot No. 3

Lot No. 3. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-232, S. 73° 17’ W. 96.4 m. from monument No. 2, Jolo Townsite K-8, thence S. 46° 37’ W. 52.89 m. to point 2; No. 36° 01’ E. 53.90 m. to point 3; S. 42° 51’ E. 9.92 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 262 square meters.

Pointes 1 and 2, old stakes; point 3, Int.

Bounded on the northeast by lot No. 5-C of plan Bsd-6157; on the southeast, by lot No. 2; and on the northwest, by lot No.4.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twelfth day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines
