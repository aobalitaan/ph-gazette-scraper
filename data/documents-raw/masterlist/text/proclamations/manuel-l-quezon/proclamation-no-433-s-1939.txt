MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 433

FIXING THE 19TH DAY OF AUGUST, 1939, AS THE DAY ON WHICH THE AGRICULTURAL AND INDUSTRIAL BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES SHALL BEGIN OPERATIONS.

The Auditor General and the General Manager of the Agricultural and Industrial Bank of the Philippines having certified that the aforesaid institution is ready to begin operations; and inasmuch as the early opening of the Agricultural and Industrial Bank will advance the public interest, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the provisions of section two of Commonwealth Act Numbered Four hundred and fifty-nine, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Agricultural and Industrial Bank, do hereby proclaim the nineteenth day of August, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, as the day on which the Agricultural and Industrial Bank of the Philippines shall begin operations.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this sixteenth day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President

Source: University of the Philippines, College of Law Library
