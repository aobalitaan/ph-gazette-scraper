MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 410

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF BAG-ONG PARDO, MUNICIPALITY OF KOLAMBUGAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights; if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Bag-ong Pardo, municipality of Kolambugan, Province of Lanao, Island of Mindanao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-396, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-396, S. 20° 08′ W. 787.90 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 128, Kolambugan Pls-13, thence S. 44° 27′ E. 209.48 m. to point 2; S. 48° 41′ W. 99.78 m. to point 3; N. 44° 29′ W. 209.80 m. to point 4; N. 48° 52′ E. 99.92 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 2.0902 hectares.

All corners are B. L. concrete monuments. Bounded on the northeast by lot No. 3363; on the southeast and southwest, by road; and on the northwest, by lot No. 2559.

Bearings true. Declination, 1″ 39′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-396.

Surveyed: May 17, 1938.

Approved: October 28, 1938.

NOTE.—This is an advance plan of lot No. 2562 of Kolambugan Public Lands Subdivision Pls-13, case No. 2.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighth day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
