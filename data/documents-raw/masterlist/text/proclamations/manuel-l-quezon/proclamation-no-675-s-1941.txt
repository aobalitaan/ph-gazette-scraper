MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 675

RESERVING FOR WORKING-MEN’S VILLAGE PURPOSES THREE (3) PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF TONDO, CITY OF MANILA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for working-men’s village purposes, under the administration of the Secretary of Labor, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the district of Tondo, City of Manila, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15342-Amd., to wit:

L ot No. 45, Swo-15342-Amd. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15342-Amd., N. 39° 35′ W., 796.58 m. more or less from B. L. L. M. No. 53, Manila Cad. No. 13, thence N. 21° 13′ W. 172.12 m. to point 2; N. 10° 31′ W. 151.44 m. to point 3; S. 72° 27′ E. 33.99 m. to point 4; S. 44° 03′ E. 101.10 m. to point 5; S. 3° 24′ W. 86.96 m. to point 6; S. 14° 25′ E. 58.65 m. to point 7; S. 22° 41′ E. 64.65 m. to point 8; S. 63° 49′ W. 53.32 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 18,450.6 square meters.

Points 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5, old iron bars; points 6 and 7, old stakes; and point 8, old intersection.

Bounded on the northeast, by Estero de Vitas; on the east, by lot No. 46; on the southeast, by lot No. 4 of plan Fl-1783; and on the southwest, by Manila Bay.

Lot No. 46, Swo-15342-Amd. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15342-Amd., N. 22° 28′ W. 993.98 m, more or less from B. L. L. M. No 53, Manila Cad. No. 13, thence S. 40° 48′ E. 35.49 m. to point 2; S. 17° 43′ E. 164.16 m. to point 3; S. 63° 49′ W. 43.10 m. to point 4; N. 22° 41′ W. 64.65 m. to point 5; N. 14° 25′ W. 58.65 m. to point 6; N. 3° 24′ E. 85.96 m. to point 1, of beginning.

Containing an area of 7,647.9 square meters.

Point 1, old iron bar; points 2, 5 and 6, old stakes; and points 3 and 4, old intersections.

Bounded on the northeast, by Estero de Vitas and lot No. 48-A; on the southeast, by lot No. 2 of plan Fl-1783; and on the southwest and west, by lot No. 45.

Lot No. 48-A, Swo-15342-Amd .—Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15342-Amd., N. 19° 38′ W. 801.26 m. more or less from B. L. L. M. No. 53, Manila Cad. No. 13, thence S. 62° 24′ W. 38.51 m. to point 2; S. 63° 49′ W. 3.58 m. to point 3; N. 17° 43′ W. 164.16 m. to point 4; S. 50° 10′ E. 55.30 m. to point 5; S. 27° 36′ E. 20.65 m. to point 6; S. 27° 36′ E. 90.09 m. to point 7; S. 62° 24′ W. 7.34 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 5,174.8 square meters.

Points 1 and 2, old B. L. concrete monuments; points 3 and 7, old intersections; point 4, old stake; point 5, intersection; and point 6, B. L. concrete monument.

Bounded on the northeast, by Estero de Vitas, lot No. 1 of plan Fl-75 and lot No. 51; on the southeast, by lot No. 49-B of plan Bsd-6444 and lot No. 3 of plan Fl-1783; and on the southwest, by lot No. 46.

Bearings true. Declination, variable.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16342-Amd.

Surveyed: October 3, 1930.

Approved: July 26, 1940.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this thirty-first day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
