PROCLAMATION NO. 157

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 39, SERIES OF 1920, AND RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF TAKIAN, MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-eight of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation Numbered Thirty-nine, series of nineteen hundred and twenty, and reserve for military purposes, under the administration of the Chief of staff, Philippine Army, pursuant to section eighty-three of the same Act, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Takian, municipality of La Trinidad, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and described in the Bureau of Lands plan Ir-330-Amd., to wit:

Lot No. 1. – A parcel of land (lot No. 1 of the amendment-subdivision plan Ir-330-Amd., being a portion of the land described on the original paln Ir-330, G. L. R. O. Record No.  ), situated in the barrio of Takian, municipality of La Trinidad, Mountain Province. Bounded on the N., NE., and E., by Baguio-Bontoc Road; and on the S., SW., and W., by lot No. 2 of the amendment-subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 59˚ 18’ E., 1,137.05 m., more or less, from triangulation station North Base, municipality of La Trinidad, thence N. 75˚ 20’ E., 109.79 m. to point “2;” thence S. 59˚ 37’ E., 214.21 m. to point “3;” thence S. 1˚ 18’ E., 174.44 m. to point “4;” thence S. 89˚ 24’ W., 194.90 m. to point “5;” thence N. 82˚ 36’ W., 92.68 m. to point “6;” thence N. 19˚ 54’ W., 218.33 m. to point “7;” thence N. 1. to point “8;” thence S. 76˚ 43’ E., 66.66 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of seventy-nine thousand nine hundred fifty-nine square meters (79,959), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “5,” “6,” and “7,” are marked by galvanized iron spikes in crosses on trees; point “8,” by B. L. concrete monument; and the rest, by old B. L. concrete monuments; bearings true; declination, 0˚ 26’ E.; date of the original survey, November 6-12, 1930, and that of the amendment-subdivision survey, October 5-8, 1936.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of New York, U. S. A. (for the City of Manila), this fourteenth day of June, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the second.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
