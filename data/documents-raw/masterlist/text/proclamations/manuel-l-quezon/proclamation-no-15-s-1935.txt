PROCLAMATION NO. 15

RESERVING TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COTABATO, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, FOR RADIO-POST-OFFICE SITE.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section seventy-three of Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and seventy-four, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for radio-post-office site under the administration of the Director of Posts, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcels of the public domain, situated in the municipality of Cotabato, Province of Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, to wit:

Lot No. 1.— A parcel of land (lot No. 1 of the subdivision plan Bsd-4482, being a portion of lot No. 2427 of the cadastral survey of Cotabato, G. L. R. O. Cadastral Record No. 296), situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Cotabato, Province of Cotabato. Bounded on the NE., by lots Nos. 29 and 2 of the subdivision plan; on the SE., by lots Nos. 2, 27, and 4 of the subdivision plan; on the SW., by lot No. 3 of the subdivision plan; and on the NW., by lots Nos. 28 of the subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands plan, being N. 29 ْ46’ W., 247.81 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1-A, municipality of Cotabato; thence S. 78 ْ24’ W., 15.82 m. to point “2;” thence N. 54 ْ08’ W., 27.02 m. to point “3;” thence N. 21 ْ23’ E., 41.48 m. to point “4;” thence S. 71 ْ39’ E., 20.33 m. to point “5;” thence S. 17 ْ56’ W., 18.38 m. to point “6;” thence S. 70 ْ26’ E. 16.22 m. to point “7;” thence S. 76 ْ49’ W., 22.94 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of one thousand four hundred thirty-two (1,432), square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and on the ground, points “1” to “7;” inclusive, are marked by B. L. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cm.; bearings true; declination, 2 ْ17’ E.; date of the original survey, August, 1916-April, 1917, and that of the subdivision survey, December 16, 1933.

Lot No. 10 .—A parcel of land (lot No. 10 of the subdivision plan Bsd-4482, being a portion of lot No. 2427 of cadastral survey of Cotabato, G. L. R. O. Cadastral Record No. 296), situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Cotabato, Province of Cotabato. Bounded on the NE., by lot No. 8 of the subdivision plan; on the SE., by Lots Nos. 11 and 13 of the subdivision plan; on the SW., by lot No. 12 of the subdivision plan; and on the NW., by lot No. 27 of the subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands plan, being N. 22 ْ31’ W., 248.46 m. more or less from B. L. L. M. No. 1-A, municipality of Cotabato; thence S. 17 ْ46’ W., 14.49 m. to point “2;” thence S. 17 ْ48’ W., 7.19 m. to point “3;” thence N. 70 ْ06’ W., 16.48 m. to point “4;” thence N. 17 ْ39’ E., 21.54 m. to point “5;” thence S. 70 ْ36’ E., 16.53 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of three hundred fifty-seven (357) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and on the ground, points “1,” “2,” “3,” “4,” and “5,” are marked by B. L. concrete monument, 15 by 60 cm.; bearings true; declination, 2 ْ17’ E.; date of the original survey, August, 1916-April, 1917, and that of the subdivision survey, December 16, 1933.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twelfth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-five, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.
