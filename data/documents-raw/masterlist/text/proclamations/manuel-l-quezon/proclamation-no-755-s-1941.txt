MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 755

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES FOUR PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF DIAAT, KADAYAKAN, DIOME AND SUGUIT, MUNICIPALITY OF BALER, PROVINCE OF TAYABAS, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Diaat, Kadayakan, Diome and Suguit, municipality of Baler, province of Tayabas, island of Luzon, and particularly described on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-473, to wit:

Lot No . 1, Mr-473 .

(Bo. of Diaat, Mp. Of Baler)

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-473, N.80-46’W. 2663.94m. More or less from B.L.L.M. No. 42, Baler Cad. No. 185, thence

N.48-54’W. 112. 93m. to point 2; N.45-40’E. 100.90m.to point 3; S.50-45E. 112.02m. to point 4; S.45-00’W. 104m.to point 1, point of beginning

Containing an area of ELEVEN THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED NINETY EIGHT SQUARE METERS (11,498), more or less.

All corners are Stones Marked B. L.

Bounded on all sides, by Public Land.

Bearings true, Declination 1-06’E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands Mr-473, Sheet No. 1

Surveyed: September 19, 1940.

Approved: January 8, 1941
