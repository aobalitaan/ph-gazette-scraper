MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 748

RESERVING FOR RADIO-TELEGRAPH STATION PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BROOKE’S POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for radio-telegraph station purposes, under the administration of the Director of Posts, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipal district of Brooke’s Point, Province of Palawan, Island of Palawan, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Ir-479, to wit:

Ir-479. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-479, S. 3° 00′ W. 241.34 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Brooke’s Point Pls-96, thence S. 82° 25′ E. 29.80 m. to point 2; S. 82° 15′ E. 29.75 m. to point 3; S. 82 08′ E. 29.98 m. to point 4; S. 37° 32′ W. 43.46 m. to point 5; S. 56° 31′ W. 72.25 m. to point 6; S.. 82° 38′ W. 53.00 m. to point 7; N. 51″ 17′ W. 63.94 m. to point 8; N. 9° 34′ E. 65.81 m. to point 9; S. 82° 26′ E. 37.56 m. to point 10; S. 82° 24′ E. 37.65 m. to point 11; S. 83° 05′ E. 15.10 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of twelve thousand nine hundred nine (12,909) square meters, more or less.

Points 5 and 6, stakes; points 7 and 8, old B. L. concrete monuments; and the rest are B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast, by lots Nos. 2 and 3, national road, and lots Nos. 4, 5, and 6; on the southeast and southwest, by Sulu Sea; and on the northwest, by Sulu Sea.

Bearings true. Declination, 2° 47′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-479.

Surveyed: September 26, 1940.

Approved: March 25, 1941.

NOTE.—This is lot No. 1 of Brooke’s Point Public Lands Subdivision, Pls-96.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this ninth day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
