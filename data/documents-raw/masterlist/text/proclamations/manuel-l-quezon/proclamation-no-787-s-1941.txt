MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 787

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES ONE PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF PARISTA, MUNICIPALITY OF LUPAO. PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of Section Eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Parista, municipality of Lupao, province of Nueva Ecija, island of Luzon, and particularly described on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-17262, to wit:

Lot No. 2068, Lupao Cad.

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-17262, S.41-45’E. 2297.74m. more or less from B.B.M. No. 17, Lupao Cad. No. 144, thence

N. 53-05’W. 79.04 m. to point 2; N. 10-26’E. 3.21 m. to point 3; N. 10-26’E. 75.66 m. to point 4; S. 61-50’E. 22.36 m. to point 5; S. 67-09’E. 15.60 m. to point 6; S. 75-48’E. 59.98 m. to point 7; S. 58-54’E. 14.81 m. to point 8; S. 36-53’E. 24.33 m. to point 9; S. 63-43’W. 19.78 m. to point 10; S. 34-54’W. 22.54 m. to point 11; S. 35-40’W. 22.82 m. m. to point 12; S. 51-44’W. 13.81 m. to point 13; S. 52-15’W. 20.01 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of NINE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED NINETEEN SQUARE METERS (9,419), more or less.

Points 2 and 4, Intersections; point 3, Old Tree; and the rest are Old Stakes.

Bounded on the Northeast and Southeast, by Sapang Capanpandanan; on the Southwest, by Road; and on the Northwest, by lots Nos. 2068 and 2069.

Bearings true. Declination 0-45’E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Swo-17262.

Surveyed: April 8-10, 1932.

Approved: June 8, 1932.

NOTE: This is lot No. 1450-A, Psd-8341 = lot No. 2068 of Lupao-Cad. No. 144 Case No. 1.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my-hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, 3 rd day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
