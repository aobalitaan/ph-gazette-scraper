MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 483

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF BANCURO, MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF MINDORO, ISLAND OF MINDORO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Bancuro, municipality of Naujan, Province of Mindoro, Island of Mindoro, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-419, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-419, S. 18° 34′ E. 523.47 m. more or less, from B. L. L._M. No. 145, Naujan cadastre No. 200 Ext., thence S. 9° 35′ E. 104.70 m. to point 2; S. 83° 55′ W. 99.89 m. to point 3; N. 7° 57′ W. 99.82 m. to point 4; N. 81° 07′ E. 96.86 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1.0053 hectares.

All corners are P. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north by lot No. 4575; on the east and south, by lot No. 4577; and on the west, by road.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 48′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-419.

Surveyed: January 27, 1932. : Approved: September 6, 1939.

NOTE.—This is lot No. 4578 of Naujan cadastre No. 200 -Ext., case No. 7.

No decision received.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and ” caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this sixth day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
