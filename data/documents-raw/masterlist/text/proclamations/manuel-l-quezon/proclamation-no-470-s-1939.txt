MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 470

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL BUILDING AND SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES THREE PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF CENTRAL AND PALINA, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF KIBUNGAN, SUBPROVINCE OF BENGUET, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for municipal building and school site purposes, under the administration of the municipal district of Kibungan, Benguet, Mountain Province, and the Director of Education, respectively, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Central and Palina, municipal district of Kibungan, subprovince of Benguet, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu—101472, sheets 1, 2, and 3, to wit:

Lot No. 1 (presidencia site, barrio of Central). —A parcel of land, lot No. 1 of plan Psu-101472 (G. L. R. O. Record No.          ), situated in the barrio of Central, municipal district of Kibungan, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the northeast by properties of Allaguey and Intogay Lagdao; on the southeast, by property of Intogay Lagdao; on the south and southwest, by property of Daoaten Dacolog; and on the northwest, by public lands.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 3° 30′ E. 56.74 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipal district of Kibungan, Mountain Province; thence S. 26° 55′ E. 22.69 m. to point “2;” thence S. 48° 01′ E. 178.89 m. to point “3;” thence S. 41° 04′ W. 36.74 m. to point “4;” thence N. 75°- 16′ W. 73.44 m. to point “5;” thence S. 89° 58′ W. 197.98 m. to point “6;” thence N. 46° 42′ W. 71.92 m. to point “7;” thence N. 63° 46′ E. 225.46 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of thirty thousand one hundred eighteen (30,118) square meters, more or less.
