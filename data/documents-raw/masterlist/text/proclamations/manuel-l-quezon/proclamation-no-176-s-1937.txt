MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 176

RESERVING AND EXCEPTING FROM LEASE, SALE, OR OTHER DISPOSITION LOT NO. 1557 OF NAIC FRIAR LANDS ESTATE, IN CAVITE, CONTAINING AN AREA OF 13,411 SQUARE METERS, MORE OR LESS.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section twenty-one of Act Numbered Eleven hundred and twenty of the Philippine Commission, as amended by subsequent acts of the Philippine Legislature, and upon the condition that the municipality of Naic, Cavite, shall pay to the Bureau of Lands the full value of the property hereby reserved, together with the accrued interest thereon, I hereby reserve and except from lease, sale, or other disposition lot numbered fifteen hundred and fifty-seven of Naic Friar Lands Estate, situated in the municipality of Naic, Province of Cavite, Philippines, as indicated in the subdivision plan and technical description on file in the General Land Registration Office, and containing an area of thirteen thousand four hundred eleven square meters, more or less.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-fourth day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines. the second.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: ELPIDIO QUIRINO Secretary of the Interior
