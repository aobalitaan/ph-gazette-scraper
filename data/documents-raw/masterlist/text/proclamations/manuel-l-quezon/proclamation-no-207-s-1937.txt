MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 207

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 43, SERIES OF 1919, BLOCKS NOS. 171 AND 176, RECLAMATION NO. 1, MANILA HARBOR, AND RESERVING SAME FOR BONDED WAREHOUSE SITES FOR THE MANILA RAILROAD COMPANY.

Upon the recommendation of the Director of Lands, approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby exclude from the operation of Executive Order Numbered Forty-three, series of nineteen hundred and nineteen, blocks numbered one hundred and seventy-one and one hundred and seventy-six, Reclamation Area Numbered One, Manila Harbor, equivalent to blocks numbered seventy-one and seventy-six, respectively, of the old plan, and reserve the same for bonded warehouse sites under the administration of the Manila Railroad Company.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-third day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the second.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: ELPIDIO QUIRINO Secretary of the Interior
