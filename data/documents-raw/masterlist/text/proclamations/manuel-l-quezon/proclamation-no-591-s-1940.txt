MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 591

RESERVING FOR RAILROAD PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF NAGLABRAHAN, MUNICIPALITY OF GUIMBA, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for railroad purposes, under the administration of the Manila Railroad Company, subject to private rights if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Naglabrahan, municipality of Guimba, Province of Nueva Ecija, island of Luzon and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psd-4956, to wit:

Lot No. 479-A.- A parcel of land (lot No. 497-A of the subdivision plan Psd-4956, being a portion of lot No. 497 of cadastral survey of Guimba, G.L.R.O. Cad. Record No. 399), situated in the barrio of Naglabrahan, municipality of Guimba, Province of Nueva Ecija. Bounded on the NE. by lot No. 496-A of plan Psd-4957; on the SE., by lot No. 497-B of the subdivision plan; on the SW., by lot No. 498-A of plan Psd-4954; and on the NW., by lot No. 497-C of the subdivision plan Beginning at a .point marked “1” on plan, being S. 25° 24’ W., 916.62 meters, more or less, from M.B.M. No. 7, municipality Guimba; thence N. 54° 59’ E., 34.46 m. to point “2”; thence S. 12° 16’ E., 32.57 m. to point “3”; thence S. 54° 59’ W., 34.60 m. to point “4”; thence N. 12° 01’ W., 32.64 m to point of beginning; containing an area of one thousand thirty-seven square meters (1,037), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “1” to “4”, inclusive, are marked by P.L.S./M.R. conrete monuments 18 cm. diam. x 72 cm.; bearings true; declination 0° 42’ E.; date of the original survey, November, 1919-February, 1922, and that of the subdivision survey, April 10-July 31, 1928.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-second day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
