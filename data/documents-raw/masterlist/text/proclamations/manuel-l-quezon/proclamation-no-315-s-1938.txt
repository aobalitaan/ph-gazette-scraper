MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 315

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL BUILDING SITE PURPOSES THREE PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALABON, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for municipal building site purposes, under the administration of the municipality of Malabon, Rizal, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcels of the public domain, situated in the municipality of Malabon, Province of Rizal, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Sh-66-Amd.-D, to wit:

Lot No. 1-A. —A parcel of land (lot No. 1-A of the amendment-subdivision plan Sh-66-Amd-D, being a portion of lot No . 1 of plan Sh-66, G. L. R. O. Record No.     ), situated in the poblacion, municipality of Malabon, Province of Rizal. Bounded on the NE., by lot No. 1-B of the amendment-subdivision plan; on the SE., by Calle Rizal; on the SW., by lot “A” of plan Mla-160-Amd-D; and on the NW., by Calle Sacristia. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 67° 44′ W., 141.61 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Mp. of Malabon, Rizal, thence S. 24° 42′ E., 119.27 m. to point “2”; thence S. 58° 31′ W., 47.59 m. to point “3”; thence N. 17′ 30′ W., 122.49 m. to point “4” ; thence N. 59° 17′ E., 32.07 m. to point of beginning; containing an area of four thousand seven hundred thirty square meters (4,730), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, point “1” is marked by a P.L.S. concrete monument; point “2,” by iron tube; and points “3” and “4”, by Old B. L. concrete monuments; bearings true; declination 0° 56 E.; date of original survey, Nov. 22-23, 1915 and that of the amendment-subdivision survey, March 17, 1937.

Lot No 2-A.— A parcel of land (lot No. 2-A of the amendment subdivision plan Sh-66-Amd.-D, being a portion of lot No. 2 of plan Sh-66, G.L.R.O. Record No.     ), situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Malabon, Province of Rizal. Bounded on the NE. and SE., by lot No. 2-B of the amendment-subdivision plan; on the SW., by lot “A” of plan Mla-160-Amd-D; and on the NW., by Calle Rizal. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 50° 10′ W., 151.42 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Mp. of Malabon, Rizal, thence N. 58° 11′ E., 47.40 m. to point “2”; thence S. 19° 49′ E., 66.00 m. to point “3”; thence S. 68° 37′ W., 48.70 m. to point “4”; thence N. 17° 30′ W., 57.25 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of two thousand nine hundred twenty-eight square meters (2,928), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, point “1” is marked by an old concrete monument; and the rest, by iron tubes; bearings true; declination 0° 56′ E.; date of the original survey, November 22-23, 1915 and that of the amendment-subdivision survey, March seventeenth, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven.

STREET LOT

A parcel of land (street lot of the amendment-subdivision plan Sh-66-Amd-D), situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Malabon, Province of Rizal. Bounded on the NE., by provincial road; on the SE., by lot No. 2-A of the amendment-subdivision plan; on the SW., by lot-A of plan Mla-160-Amd-D; and on the NW., by lot No. 1-A of the amendment-subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 56° 51′ W., 145.47 m. more or less from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Malabon, thence N. 58° 31′ E., 47.59 m. to point “2”; thence S. 16° 43′ E., 18.12 m. to point “3”; thence S. 58° 11′ W., 47.40 m. to point “4”; thence N 17° 30′ W., 18.34 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of eight hundred thirty-eight square meters (838), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground point “1” is marked by old B. L. concrete monument; points “2” and “3,” by iron tubes; and point “4”, by old concrete monument; bearings true; declination 0° 56′ E.; date of the amendment survey, March seventeenth, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-fourth day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
