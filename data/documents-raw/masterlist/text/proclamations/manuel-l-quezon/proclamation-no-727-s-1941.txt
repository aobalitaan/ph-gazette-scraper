PROCLAMATION NO. 727

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF IBUNG, MUNICIPALITY OF SOLANO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, ISLAND OF LUZON.
