MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 752

POSTPONING TO A LATER DATE THE HOLDING OF THE SPECIAL ELECTION OF LOCAL OFFICIALS IN DANSALAN LANAO

The special election called under Proclamation No, 745, dated August 14, 1941, to take place on Tuesday, November 11, 1941, in the municipality of Dansalan, province of Lanao, for the purpose of electing the mayor, the vice-mayor and the councilors of the said municipality is hereby postponed to a later date which shall be fixed and announced in another proclamation to be issued by the President for the purpose.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this 18 th day of September in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
