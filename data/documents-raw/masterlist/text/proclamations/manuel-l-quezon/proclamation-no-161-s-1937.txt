PROCLAMATION NO. 161

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 611, SERIES OF 1933, AND ESTABLISHING AS TONGONAN HOT SPRING NATIONAL PARK FOR THE BENEFIT AND ENJOYMENT OF THE PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES THE PARCEL OF PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ORMOC, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, ISLAND OF LEYTE.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, hereby revoke Proclamation Numbered Six hundred and eleven, dated August tirty-first, nineteen hundred and thirty-three, reserving the “Tongonan Hot Spring” for the use of the Insular Government and the general public, a parcel of public domain, situated in the municipality of Ormoc, Province of Leyte, Island of Leyte; and pursuant to the provisions of section one of Act Numbered Thirty-nine hundred and fifteen, entitled “An Act providing for the establishment of national parks, declaring such parks as game refuges, and for the other purposes,” I hereby reserve for park purposes for the benefit and enjoyment of the people of the Philippines and withdraw from sale, settlement, or other disposition, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain situated in the municipality of Ormoc, Province of Leyte, Island of Leyte, and described in Bureau of Forestry map No. N. P.-15, to wit:
