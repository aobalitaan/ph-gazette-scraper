MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 232

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 43, SERIES OF 1919, AND OPENING TO DISPOSITION BLOCK NO. 148, RECLAMATION NO. 1, MANILA HARBOR.

Upon the recommendation of the Secertary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby exclude from the operation of Executive Order Numbered Forty-three, series of nineteen hundred and nineteen, and open to disposition, under the provisions of section eighty-eight of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, block numbered one hundred and forty-eight (block numbered forty-eight of the old plan), Reclamation Area Numbered One, Manila Harbor, City of Manila.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-third day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: ELPIDIO QUIRINO Secretary of the Interior
