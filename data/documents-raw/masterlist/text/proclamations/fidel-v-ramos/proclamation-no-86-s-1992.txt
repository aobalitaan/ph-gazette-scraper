MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 86

RESERVING FOR RESETTLEMENT SITE PURPOSES OF THE MOUNT PINATUBO VICTIMS CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED AT BARANGAYS TAUGTOG AND SAN JUAN, MUNICIPALITY OF BOTOLAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, ISLAND OF LUZON

In consideration of the urgent need to assist the families affected by the eruption of Mt. Pinatubo by providing them with suitable resettlement and livelihood areas conducive to their economic recovery, and upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and the Presidential Task Force created under Memorandum Order No. 369, dated June 26, 1991, and by the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for resettlement and livelihood purposes, subject to private rights if any, and to future classification and survey, a certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in the Barangays of Taugtog and San Juan, Municipality of Botolan, Province of Zambales, Island of Luzon and more particularly described as follows:
