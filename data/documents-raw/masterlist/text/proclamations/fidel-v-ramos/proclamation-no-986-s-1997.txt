MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 986

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE MARBEL I CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARRIO 1, MUNICIPALITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of Marbel I Central Elementary School, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barrio 1, Municipality of Koronadal, Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 202, Ts-1032

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 202, Ts-1032), situated in Barrio 1, Municipality of Koronadal, Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao. Roxas St., on the SW., along lines 8-1-2 by Cudilla St., and on the NW., along lines 2-3-4 by Alunan St. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 57° 36’W., 2,471.21 m. from triangulation Station “Tukadilas” thence:

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10 th day of March in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety seven.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS
