PROCLAMATION NO. 955

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 889, DATED SEPTEMBER 30, 1996 TO INCLUDE CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY CANTAO-AN, MUNICIPALITY OF NAGA, PROVINCE OF CEBU, IN THE AREA DESIGNATED AS THE NEW CEBU TOWNSHIP – ECOZONE
