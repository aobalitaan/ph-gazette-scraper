MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 411

DECLARING SATURDAY, JULY 2, 1994, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PASIG, METRO MANILA

WHEREAS, Saturday, July 2, 1994, marks the 421st Foundation Anniversary of the Municipality of Pasig, Metro Manila;

WHEREAS, the people of Pasig wish to celebrate this milestone in their town’s history with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Saturday, July 2, 1994, as a special day in the Municipality of Pasig, Metro Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
