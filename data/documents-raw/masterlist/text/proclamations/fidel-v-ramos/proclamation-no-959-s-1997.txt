MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 959

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE CALAOCAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF KALINGA-APAYAO, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Calaocan Elementary School, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Calaocan, Municipality of Rizal, Province of Kalinga-Apayao, Island of Luzon, which parcel of land is more particularly described as

Lot No. 4535-A, Csd-2-05 003609-D

A PARCEL OF LAND, (Lot No. 4535-A, Csd-2-05-003609-D), situated in Barangay Calaocan, Municipality of Rizal, Province of Kalinga-Apayao, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by a National Road 60.000 m. wide; on the NE., along line 2-3 by Lot .No. 4535-K, Csd-2-05-003609-D; along line 3-4 by Lot No. 4535-J, Csd-2-05-003609-D; along line 4-5 by Lot No. 4535-I; Csd-2-05-003609-D; along line 5-6 by Lot No. 4535-H, Csd-2-05-003609-D; along line 6-7 by Lot No. 4535-G, Csd-2-05-003609-D, along line 7-8 by Lot No. 4535-B, Csd-2-05-003609-D; and on the SW., along line 8-1 by Lot No. 4533, Pls-245. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 59-55 E., 229.48 meters from BLLM No. 42 of the Liwan Public Land Subdivision, Pls-245, thence:

of beginning, containing an area of FIVE THOUSAND (5,000) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by P.S. cyl. conc. IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety seven.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS
