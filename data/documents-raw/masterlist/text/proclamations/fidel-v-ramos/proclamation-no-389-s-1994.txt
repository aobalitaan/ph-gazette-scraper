MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 389

FURTHER AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 300, DATED AUGUST 2, 1938, AS AMENDED BY PROCLAMATION NO. 223, DATED DECEMBER 3, 1955, ENTITLED “RESERVING FOR CITRUS EXPERIMENT STATION PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARANGAY OF KISOLON, MUNICIPALITY OF IMPASUGONG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON, ISLAND OF MINDANAO”

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources pursuant to the provision of Sections 83 and 88 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, and the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Proclamation No. 300, series of 1938, as amended by Proclamation No. 223, series of 1955, which established the Kisolon Citrus Experiment Station situated in the Barangay of Kisolon, Municipality of Impasugong, Province of Bukidnon, Island of Mindanao, by changing its name to Kisolon Demonstration Farm Reservation and further reducing the area embraced therein from 49,6540 hectares to 10.000 hectares, subject to future survey, and declaring the parcel or parcels of land excluded therefrom open to settlement and disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act, which area is more particularly described as follows, to wit:

KISOLON DEMONSTRATION FARM

A PARCEL OF LAND (Kisolon Demonstration Farm) situated in the Barangay of Kisolon, Municipality of Sumilao (formerly Impasugong), Province of Bukidnon, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the North, by Lot 3, IR-397 Amd.; on the East, by remaining portion; on the South, by Lot 2, IR-397 Amd. (Kisolon Elementary School Site Pr-1020); on the West, by National Highway (60.00 M Wide). Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 24 deg. 06’W., 180.84 m. from BLLM No. 9, Bo. of Kisolon. Thence . . .

N. 20 deg. 20’W., 321.32 m. to point 2; N. 69 deg. 08’E., 299.85 m. to point 3; N. 69 deg. 08’E., 53.87 m. to point 4; S. 27 deg. 39’E., 219.96 m. to point 5; S. 53 deg. 59’W., 151.19 m. to point 6; S. 54 deg. 08’W., 245.06 m. to point of

beginning, containing an area of ONE HUNDRED THOUSAND (100,000) SQUARE METERS more or less, bearings true.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of May in the year of Our Lord, nineteen undred and ninety four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
