MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 121

DECLARING DECEMBER 26, 1992 AND JANUARY 2 1993 AS SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAYS

WHEREAS, the twenty-sixth (Saturday) of December, 1992 and the second (Saturday) day of January, 1993 may be declared as public holidays without prejudice to the public interest;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare December 26, 1992 and January 2, 1993 as special public holidays.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) EDELMIRO A. AMANTE, SR. Executive Secretary
