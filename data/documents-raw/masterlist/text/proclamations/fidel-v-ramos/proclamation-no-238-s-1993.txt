MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 238

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 1993, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS , Friday, September 3, 1993, marks the 25th Foundation Anniversary of the City of Iriga;

WHEREAS , the people of the City of Iriga should be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Friday, September 3, 1993, as a special day in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of AUGUST in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety three

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA Executive Secretary
