MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 456

DECLARING TUESDAY, AUGUST 30, 1994, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF MANDAUE

WHEREAS, Tuesday, August 30, 1994, marks the Charter Day of Mandaue City;

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus public attention on the accomplishments and progress of the City so as to stimulate the interest, cooperation, and participation of the residents therein;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Tuesday, August 30, 1994, as a special day in Mandaue City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

(SGD.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR Executive Secretary
