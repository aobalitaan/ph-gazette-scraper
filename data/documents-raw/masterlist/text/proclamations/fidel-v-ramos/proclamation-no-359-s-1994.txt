MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 359

DECLARING 27 APRIL, 1994 AS A SPECIAL DAY IN LAPU-LAPU CITY TO COMMEMORATE THE LAPU-LAPU CITY CHARTER DAY AND THE BATTLE OF MACTAN

WHEREAS, both the anniversaries of the Lapu-Lapu City Charter Day and the Battle of Mactan fall on 27 April 1994;

WHEREAS, the residents of Lapu-lapu City desire to celebrate, with fitting ceremonies, these events of historical importance to the City and the Nation as a whole;

WHEREAS, Section 27 Chapter 7, Book I of the Administrative Code of 1987 authorizes the President to proclaim a local special day for a particular date, group or place;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare 27 April 1994 as a Special Day in Lapu-lapu City.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
