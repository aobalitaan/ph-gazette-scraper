MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO 871

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM SEPTEMBER 16 TO 22, 1996, AS “MANAGEMENT CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK”

WHEREAS, the Philippine Council of Management (PHILCOMAN) is sponsoring the 28th Management Congress on September 19 to 20, 1996, in Manila, on the theme “WIRING THE PHILIPPINES FOR MANAGING INFORMATION”, which will be attended by management experts, practitioners, educators, and students;

WHEREAS, management consciousness is essential to the promotion of better business practices, increased productivity, and improved public administration and services, which are vital prerequisites to economic development and progress;

WHEREAS, there is a need to promote management consciousness in both the public and private sectors to help achieve the development goals of the nation;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from September 16 to 22, 1996, as “Management Consciousness Week” under the auspices of the Philippine Council of Management and other concerned organizations.

I call upon all management practitioners, educators and students to give the observance of the week their full support.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of September in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety six.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
