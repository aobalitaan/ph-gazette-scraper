MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 351

DECLARING SATURDAY, APRIL 2, 1994, AS A SPECIAL DAY THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY

WHEREAS, April 2, 1994, falls on Saturday between Good Friday and Easter Sunday;

WHEREAS, the said day, April 2, 1994, may be declared a special day without detriment to public service to allow our people uninterrupted opportunity to observe and ponder on the significance of the Lenten season;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, April 2, 1994, a special day throughout the country.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30 th day of March in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
