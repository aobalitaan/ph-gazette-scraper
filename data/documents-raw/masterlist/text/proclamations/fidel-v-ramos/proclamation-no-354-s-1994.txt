MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 354

AUTHORIZING THE TAHANAN OUTREACH PROJECTS AND SERVICES, INC., TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN FOR A PERIOD OF ONE YEAR

WHEREAS, the Tahanan Outreach Projects and Services, Inc. (TOPS), was earlier authorized under Proclamation No. 171, s. 1993, to conduct a national fund campaign for a period of one (1) year:

WHEREAS, TOPS will again conduct a national campaign to raise fund that will ultimately benefit under-privileged children and youth;

WHEREAS, this national fund campaign by TOPS deserves the wholehearted support of all segments of society;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Tahanan Outreach Projects and Services, Inc., to conduct a national fund campaign for a period of one (1) year effective upon the promulgation of this Proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of April in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
