MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 324

DECLARING TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 3, 1994, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN CABANATUAN CITY

WHEREAS , Thursday, February 3, 1994, marks the 44th Foundation Anniversary of Cabanatuan City;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of Cabanatuan City be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Thursday, February 3, 1994, as a special day in Cabanatuan City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 st day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
