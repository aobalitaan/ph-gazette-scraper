MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 404

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 20, 1994, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, Monday, June 20, 1994, marks the 35th Charter Anniversary of the City of Cotabato;

WHEREAS, the people of the City of Cotabato should be given full opportunity to celebrate the day with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, June 20, 1994, as a special day in the City of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of June in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL  V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
