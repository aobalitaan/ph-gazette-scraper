MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 559

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 87, DATED AUGUST 9, 1966, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE DIBUDALAN MOUNTAIN FOREST RESERVE, AND DECLARING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AS AURORA STATE COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY SCHOOL SITE CIVIL RESERVATION, SITUATED IN ZABALI, BALER, AURORA

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 705, otherwise known as the Revised Forestry Code of the Philippines, as amended, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 37, dated August 6, 1966, which established the Dibudalan Mountain Forest Reserve, and declaring the land embraced therein as Aurora State College of Technology School Site Civil Reservation, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to the condition that the removal and utilization of timber and other forest products therein shall be subject to the Forestry Internal Revenue laws and regulation, certain parcel of the public domain (to cover the disestablished Communal Forest Parcel II, Amd. and the proposed Government Center of the Province of Aurora), situated in Barangay Zabali, Baler, Aurora, which is more particularly described as follows:

A parcel of land beginning at a point marked “1” being S 10° 45’W, 1046 m. from BLLM No. 39 Cad. 185, Baler, Aurora.

Thence S 40° 56’E, 794.87 meters to Corner 2; Thence N 89° 46’W, 542.44 meters to Corner 3; Thence N 35° 53’W, 447.89 meters to Corner 4; Thence N 28° 27’W, 169.70 meters to Corner 5; Thence N 26° 32’W, 8.08 meters to Corner 6; Thence N 28° 24’W, 12.26 meters to Corner 7; Thence N 00° 07’E, 51.68 meters to Corner 8; Thence N 26° 24’E, 84.17 meters to Corner 9; Thence N 50° 47’E, 133.13 meters to Corner 10; Thence N 56° 39’E, 40.00 meters to Corner 11; Thence N 72° 48’E, 43.69 meters to Corner 12; Thence N 67° 40’E, 77.73 meters to Corner 13; Thence N 47° 58’E, 78.20 meters to Corner 14; Thence N 15° 42’E, 60.64 meters to Corner 15; Thence N 21° 47’E, 143.61 meters to Corner 16; Thence N 43° 31’E, 21.00 meters to Corner 17; Thence N 01° 43’E, 56.58 meters to Corner 18; Thence N 01° 43’E, 24.61 meters to Corner 19; Thence N 03° 32’W, 315.84 meters to Corner 20; Thence N 03° 33’W, 19.39 meters to Corner 21; Thence N 05° 47’E, 18.95 meters to Corner 22; Thence N 05° 46’E, 150.98 meters to Corner 23; Thence N 17° 26’E, 102.70 meters to Corner 24; Thence N 37° 50’E, 63.50 meters to Corner 25; Thence N 37° 48’E, 6.31 meters to Corner 26; Thence N 21° 04’E, 66.01 meters to Corner 27; Thence N 64° 31’E, 78.98 meters to Corner 28; Thence S 66° 00’E, 5.88 meters to Corner 29; Thence S 45° 30’E, 462.50 meters to Corner 30; Thence S 34° 50’E, 132.33 meters to Corner 31; Thence N 69° 28’E, 55.86 meters to Corner 32; Thence S 34° 44’E, 105.46 meters to Corner 33; Thence N 66° 24’E, 79.61 meters to Corner 34; Thence S 77° 41’E, 60.68 meters to Corner 35; Thence S 53° 02’E, 64.74 meters to Corner 36; Thence S 25° 50’E, 386.52 meters to Corner 37; Thence S 12° 49’W, 445.17 meters to Corner 1;

containing an area of ONE MILLION NINE HUNDRED SIXTY THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED (1,960,600.00) SQUARE METERS.

The area covered by the reservation shall be under the administrative jurisdiction, supervision and control of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports and the Aurora State College of Technology.

In the event that the area is no longer needed for the purpose stated, or its development has already been abandoned for a period of two (2) years, this Proclamation is automatically withdrawn, revoked or cancelled and the subject area regains its original status as part and parcel of the forest land.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of April in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety five.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS
