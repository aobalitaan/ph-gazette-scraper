Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 718

TRANSFERRING AUTHORITY OVER CAMPO ISLAM, ZAMBOANGA FROM PANAMIN TO THE CITY GOVERNMENT

In view of the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 719 and Presidential Decree No. 1414, the affairs in Campo Islam, Zamboanga, a Samal Settlement Project Currently being handled by the Presidential Assistant on Cultural Minorities (PANAMIN) is hereby ordered to be transferred from the PANAMIN to the City Government of Zamboanga.

All concerned are further ordered to work for the smooth and immediate implementation of this Memorandum Order.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, March 23, 1981

Source: Presidential Management Staff
