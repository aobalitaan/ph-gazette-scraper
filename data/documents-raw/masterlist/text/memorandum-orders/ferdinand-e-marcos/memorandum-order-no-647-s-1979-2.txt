Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 647

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 642 DATED FEBRUARY 5, 1979 CREATING A COMMITTEE TO REVIEW MATTERS ALREADY DECIDED BY THE BOARD OF LIQUIDATORS WHICH CONTINUE TO BE CONTROVERSIAL.

Memorandum Order No. 642 dated February 5, 1979, creating a Committee to review all matters already decided by the Board of Liquidators which continue to be controversial is hereby amended to designate Atty. Teodoro Bay of the Legal Office, Office of the President, as Member of the Committee in lieu of Mrs. Nenita Baliao of the Office of the Presidential Assistant.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TAVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

February 20, 1979

Source: Malacañang Records Office
