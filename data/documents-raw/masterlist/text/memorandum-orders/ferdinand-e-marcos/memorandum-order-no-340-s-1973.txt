Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 340

In view of the provisions of Republic Act No. 6551, which provides for the operating expenses of the Staff of the Secretariat of the Asian Development Bank, the new office which will assume the duties, functions and responsibilities of the Philippine Coordinating Committee on the Asian Development Bank and the designation of Honorable Roberto V. Reyes as the Acting Chairman of the Secretariat, the following are also designated to compose the Secretariat, to wit:

a.

Representative of the National Economic & Development Authority

–

Member

b.

Representative of the Department of Foreign Affairs

–

Member

c.

Representative of the Central Bank of the Philippines

–

Member

d.

Representative of the Development Bank of the Philippines

–

Member

e.

Representative of the Department of Trade & Tourism

–

Member

The Secretariat may call upon heads of departments, bureaus and offices, government-owned or controlled corporations and provincial, city and municipal governments, for such assistance as it may need.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, January 29, 1973
