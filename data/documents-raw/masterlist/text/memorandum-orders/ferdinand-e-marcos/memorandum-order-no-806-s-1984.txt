Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 806

In order to provide a direct line of communication with, the autonomous governments of Regions IX and XII, there is hereby created in this Office a SPECIAL DESK FOE THE REGIONAL AUTONOMOUS GOVERNMENTS, the principal function of which is to provide assistance as may be requested by the officials of said governments.

Through this desk, the Regional Autonomous Governments of Regions IX and XII may submit such reports to this Office as may be desired.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, June 4, 1984
