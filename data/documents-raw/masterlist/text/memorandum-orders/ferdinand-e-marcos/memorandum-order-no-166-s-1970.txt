Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 166

SUSPENDING THE WEARING OR UNIFORMS BY LADY EMPLOYEES IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

The wearing of uniforms toy lady employees in the Office of the President, required in Memorandum Order No. 143, series of 1969, is hereby suspended for a period of fifteen (15) days from the date hereof.

After the said period, the aforementioned requirement shall be automatically resumed, unless otherwise prescribed.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) FAUSTINO S. MACUTAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, February 2, 1970
