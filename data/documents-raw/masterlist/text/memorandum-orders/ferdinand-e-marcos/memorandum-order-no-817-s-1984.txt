CREATING AN INVESTIGATING PANEL TO LOOK INTO THE ADMINISTRATIVE CHARGES FILED AGAINST ERNESTO IMBAG OF THE COMPLAINTS AND INVESTIGATIONS OFFICE

A panel to conduct an investigation and to receive evidence relative to the administrative charges filed against Ernesto Imbag of the Complaints and Investigation Office, this Office, is hereby created to be composed of the following:

The panel is authorized to use any lawful means necessary for the expeditious and impartial termination of the investigation. For this purpose, it may call upon any personnel of the Office for assistance.
