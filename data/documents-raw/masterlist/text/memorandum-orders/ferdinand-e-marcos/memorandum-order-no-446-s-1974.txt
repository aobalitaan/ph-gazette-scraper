TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 446

CREATING A SPECIAL COMMITTEE TO WORK OUT A SETTLEMENT OF THE BOUNDARY DISPUTE BETWEEN THE PROVINCES OF RIZAL AND BULACAN.

In order to expedite the settlement of the boundary dispute between the provinces of Rizal and Bulacan by this Office pursuant to Section 2167 of the Revised Administrative Code, there is hereby created a special committee to work out a settlement of said dispute, to be composed of the following:

The Inter-Agency Committee shall be composed of the following:

Atty. Genaro A. Eslava, Office of the President

Chairman

Vice-Governor Arsenio Roldan of Rizal

Member

Prov. Board Member Jesus Mendoza of Bulacan

Member

Engr. Nicolas Aldana of Rizal

Member

Prov. Engr. Teodulo Genir of Bulacan

Member

The Committee shall make the necessary study and investigation of the boundary dispute between said provinces and shall submit its proposed solution within a period of one (1) month from the issue of this Order.

The Committee may call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or instrumentality of the Government for such assistance as it may need in the performance of its task.

All expenses incurred by the Committee in the execution of its task shall be borne share and share alike by the aforementioned provinces.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

July 19, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
