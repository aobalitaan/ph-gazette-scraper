TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 551

AUTHORIZING CERTAIN PERSONNEL OF THE SOCIAL SECRETARY’S OFFICE TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION, FROM NOVEMBER 16 TO DECEMBER 7, 1975.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to the President’s Memorandum No. 192-74-, dated August 20, 1974, the personnel of the Social Secretary’s Office who were assigned by the Social Secretary to render services beyond the regular office hours from November 16 to December 7, 1975, inclusive, to perform specific duties in connection with the visit to the Philippines of President and Mrs. Gerald R. Ford of the United States of America, are hereby authorized to draw additional compensation for overtime services rendered during the said period, pursuant to the provisions of Section 7-I(32) of Commonwealth Act No. 246 as amended, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, December 15, 1975.

Source: Presidential Management Staff
