(OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 493

Effective immediately, there is hereby created a “Price and Commodity Flow Monitoring Team” which is tasked to set up a system for an up-to-date and regular, monitoring of commodity flows and prices. The scheme is envisioned to accomplish the following basic objectives:

a) to have a continuous and accurate data base on the commodity trade flow;

b) to be able to monitor the location of commodity stocks as well as prices at different places in order to avert any future crises concerning prime commodities;

c) to enlist the participation of the private sector in establishing a workable mechanism for prompt monitoring of price and commodity flow; and

d) to be able to come up with forecast models on the supply and demand for such commodities throughout the country.
