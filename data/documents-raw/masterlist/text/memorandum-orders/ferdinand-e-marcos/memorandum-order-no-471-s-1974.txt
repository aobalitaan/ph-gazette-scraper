TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 471

DESIGNATION OF KEY PERSONNEL, NATIONAL COMPUTER CENTER (NCC)

Further to their detail with the Office of the President, the following personnel are hereby designated to assume and discharge the functions and duties, in acting capacities, of NCC Project Managers, effective December 1, 1974:

1. CASIANO B. MENDOZA Cdr 0-4593 PN

2. MARIANO A. CORDERO Ltsg 0-5276 PN

3.ELIEZER N. PINTO, JR. Capt 0-5268 PA

Pursuant to Section 261 of the Revised Administrative Code, these designations hereby authorize above-named personnel to be paid by and receive from the National Computer Center (NCC) this amount corresponding to the difference between the present salaries of their regular positions and the salaries authorized for their respective positions at NCC, together with such reimbursement of expenses incurred in the discharge of their duties as authorized to be paid to then under existing laws, orders, rules and regulations.

By Authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, December 2, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
