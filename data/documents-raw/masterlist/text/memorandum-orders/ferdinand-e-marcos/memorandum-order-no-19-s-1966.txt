Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 19

AUTHORIZING A NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES IN THE APPOINTMENT DIVISION TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE

In view of the exigencies of the service, the Presidential Appointments Secretary is hereby authorized to assign three (3) employees a day in the Appointment Division to render service beyond the regular office hours and during Saturdays, Sundays and holidays with meal allowance for overtime service rendered in accordance with the time schedule prescribed under Memorandum Order No. 94 of this Office dated August 17, 1964, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

This Order shall take effect immediately and until further advice.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, February 16, 1966

Source: Malacañang Records Office
