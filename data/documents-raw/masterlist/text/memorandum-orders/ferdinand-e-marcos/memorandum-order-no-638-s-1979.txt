MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 638

Effective immediately, the following Palace Utility Aides (janitors) in the OP portions of the Administrative Building are hereby assigned permanently to the specific units/areas hereunder indicated to be under the direct supervision of their respective units heads.

All Janitors herein assigned shall observe work hours from 7:00 A. M. to 11: A.M. and from 12:00 noon to 4:00 P.M., Monday through Friday, unless given further assignment, which shall be properly entered on the Daily Attendance Blotters provided for the purpose.

In between their times of arrival and departure, they shall attend to their cleaning job and shall see to it that the cleanliness of their assigned units/areas is maintained.

They shall always say within the premises of their assigned units/ areas during their time of official duty, and be available for call and further assignment anytime, either by their unit heads, the Engineer’s Office or the Administrative Office.

All unit heads shall see to it that the Janitors assigned to their units strictly comply with the foregoing instructions.

Common areas like corridors, stairways, elevators and toilets shall be under the responsibility of the head of the Malacañang Engineer’s Office who shall assign a supervisor therefor.

Attached is an abstract or diagram of the locations of janitorial assignments properly coded in numbers and capital letters.

The Administrative Office shall coordinate and monitor the janitorial services and assignments.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, January 22, 1979
