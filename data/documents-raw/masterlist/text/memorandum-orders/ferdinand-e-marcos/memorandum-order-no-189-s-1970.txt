Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 189

AUTHORIZING NOT MORE THAN SIX EMPLOYEES OF THE PUBLIC INFORMATION (PRESS) EXTENSION UNIT, TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE.

Pursuant to Memorandum Order No. 169, current series, the Chief of the Public Information (Press) Extension Unit is hereby authorized to assign not more than six (6) employees a day in his Unit to render overtime service with meal allowance after the regular office hours, including Saturdays and holidays, effective as of June 16, 1970, subject to the provisions of Memorandum Order No. 54, series of 1967, the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, July 24, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
