TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 519

To aid management in evaluating performance, in developing plans and policies, and in assessing the results of operations, all chiefs of divisions and operating units of the Executive Office are requested to submit to the Presidential Finance Adviser not later than July 10, 1975, a list of all equipment under his responsibility as of June 30, 1975. The report should be submitted in accordance with the attached form.

Processing of requisitions for equipment will be held in abeyance pending submission of the above report.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, June 17, 1975

Reference: LIST OF EQUIPMENT AS OF JUNE 30, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
