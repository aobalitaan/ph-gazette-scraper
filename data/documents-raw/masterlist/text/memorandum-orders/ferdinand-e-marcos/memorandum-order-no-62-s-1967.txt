Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 62

REQUIRING THE CABINET SECRETARIAT TO MAINTAIN STAND-BY PERSONNEL

In view of the impending state of emergency created by the recent signs of possible eruptions of Mayon and Taal volcanoes, the Cabinet Secretariat is hereby directed to re­activate its “twenty-four hour stand, by personnel” to receive messages or information transmitted to this Office by telephone, telegram or any other means of fast communications, to record and relay the same immediately to the proper authorities, and to perform such other duties as may be assigned to it.

For this purpose, three (3) employees of the Cabinet Secretariat are hereby authorized to render overtime service with meal allowance of not more than two (2) units each per day, including Saturdays, Sundays and holidays, as an exception to the provisions of Memorandum Order Ho. 54, current series, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

This Order takes effect immediately and shall continue only until the cessation of the aforementioned state of emergency.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, April 25, 1967
