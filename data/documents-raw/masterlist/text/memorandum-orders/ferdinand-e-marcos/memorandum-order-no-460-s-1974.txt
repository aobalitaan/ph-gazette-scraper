Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 460

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 395, SERIES OF 1973, AS AMENDED BY MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 438 DATED JULY 10, 1974.

Memorandum Order No. 395 issued by this Office on October 19, 1973, creating a Committee to study ways and means to encourage the organization of transport cooperatives, as amended by Memorandum Order No. 438 dated July 10, 1974, is hereby amended by deleting therefrom the name of Mrs. Flora Alabanza, in order that she ceases to be a member of the said Committee. This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, September 28, 1974

Source: Malacañang Records Office
