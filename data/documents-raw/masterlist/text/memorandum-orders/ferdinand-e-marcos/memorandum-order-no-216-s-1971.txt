MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 216

CONSTITUTING THE COMMITTEE TO WHICH ALL REPORTS OF INJURIES AND ILLNESSES OF PERSONNEL OF THIS OFFICE SHALL BE SUBMITTED FOR EVALUATION PURSUANT TO MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 210 OF THIS OFFICE, SERIES of 1968

The following are hereby designated to constitute the committee to which all reports of injuries and illnesses of personnel of this Office, whether compensable or not, shall be submitted for evaluation pursuant to the provisions of Memorandum Circular No. 210 of this Office, series of 1968:

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, February 15, 1971
