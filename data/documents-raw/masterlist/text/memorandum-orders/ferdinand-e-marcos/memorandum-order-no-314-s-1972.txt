TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 314

CREATING AN INTER-AGENCY COMMITTEE TO STUDY THE REORGANIZATION OF GOVERNMENT OPERATIONS IN METROPOLITAN MANILA

There is hereby created a committee to study the reorganization and/or re-structuring of government operations in the metropolitan Manila area. The committee shall concern itself particularly with the inner metropolis, that is the are comprised by the cities of Manila, Quezon City, Pasay and Caloocan and the municipalities of Makati, Mandaluyong and San Juan.

The Committee shall be composed of the following:

The following activities are to be undertaken:

1. To study the systems of municipal/city government in metropolitan Manila and to recommend whatever measures of coordination and integration are deemed appropriate.

2. To study the functions and responsibilities of the National Government in the metropolitan area, and to recommend whatever changes in structure and interrelation with municipal governments are deemed appropriate.

3. To accomplish the Study on or before January 31, 1973.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, November 10, 1972
