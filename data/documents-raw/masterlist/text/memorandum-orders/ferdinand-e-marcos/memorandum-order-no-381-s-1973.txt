TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 381

DESIGNATION OF PERSONNEL OF THE NATIONAL COMPUTER CENTER, OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

Further to their detail with the Office of the President. the following personnel of the National Computer Center (NCC) are hereby designated to assume and discharge the functions and duties, in acting capacities, of the positions indicated effective July 1, 1973:

Pursuant to Section 261 of the Revised Administrative Code, these designations hereby authorize above personnel to be paid by and receive from the National Computer Center the amount corresponding to the difference between the present salaries of their regular positions and the salaries authorized for their respective positions at NCC, together with such reimbursement for expenses incurred in the discharge of their duties as presently authorized to be paid to them under existing laws, orders, rules and regulations.

By Authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, September 19, 1973
