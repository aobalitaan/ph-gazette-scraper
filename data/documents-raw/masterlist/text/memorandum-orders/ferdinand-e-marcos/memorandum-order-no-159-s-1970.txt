Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 159

TERMINATING THE DETAIL OF OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES FROM THE BUREAU OF POSTS TO THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

The detail of officials and employees from the Bureau of Posts to this Office is hereby terminated and they shall report back to the said Bureau for duty effective immediately.

The Chiefs of Divisions and Primary Units of this Office shall advise accordingly the officials and employees concerned in their respective Divisions/Units, and shall require them to secure the usual clearance on their cash and property accountabilities with this Office, if any.

Strict compliance with this Order is hereby enjoined.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, January 9, 1970
