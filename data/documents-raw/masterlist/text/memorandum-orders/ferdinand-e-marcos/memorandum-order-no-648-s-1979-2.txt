Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 648

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING OF A SEMINAR-WORKSHOP ON THE NEW BUDGET PROCESS AND GOVERNMENT ACCOUNTING PROCEDURES.

The holding of a seminar-workshop on the New Budget Process and Government Accounting Procedures in this Office is hereby authorized to be conducted every Monday, Wednesday and Friday, from 1:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. at the Briefing Room, 4th Floor, Administration Building, starting February 28, 1979, March 23, 1979.

The attendance of participants enumerated in the attached list is hereby authorized. All heads of participating units in the Office of the President Proper and participating agencies attached to or under the Office of the President are hereby enjoined to refrain from giving the participants any assignments during the time they are actually attending training in order not to disrupt their attendance therein.

Expenses for the conduct of the training shall be borne by the participating agencies on a pro-rata basis.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, February 26, 1979

Source: Malacañang Records Office
