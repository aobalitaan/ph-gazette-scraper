Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 144

MODIFYING THE MEMORANDUM OF THIS OFFICE DATED SEPTEMBER 23, 1969, CREATING A COMMITTEE TO MEET WITH THE REPRESENTATIVES OF THE FEDERATED MOVEMENTS FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE AND REFORM AND THE FREE FARMERS FEDERATION.

The aforesaid Memorandum of this Office dated September 23, 1969, is hereby further amended so as to reconstitute the members thereof as follows:

Dean Umali, as Vice-Chairman, shall attend to the negotiations with the representatives of the Federated Movements for Social Justice and Reform and the Free Farmers Federation as permanent moderator. He shall assume the responsibilities of the Chairman of the Committee in the absence of Governor Conrado Estrella.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, October 27, 1969
