TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 570

REQUIRING ALL CHIEFS OF DIVISIONS AND OTHER OPERATING UNITS OF THIS OFFICE TO SUBMIT A LIST OF THEIR RESPECTIVE OFFICE FURNITURE AND EQUIPMENT.

In connection with the overall inventory of equipment being conducted in this Office, all chiefs of divisions and other operating units are hereby required to submit to the Finance Office not later than August 30, 1976, a list of all pieces of office furniture and equipment in their respective offices, including those for which they or their subordinates were held accountable.

Strict compliance herewith is hereby enjoined.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, August 10, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
