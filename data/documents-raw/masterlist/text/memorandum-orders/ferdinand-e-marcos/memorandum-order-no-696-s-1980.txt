Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 696

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF A COURSE ON EFFECTIVE COMMUNICATIONS.

In line with the human resource development program of the Administration, and as an exception to the provisions of LOI 981 dated February 4, 1980, the Office of the President will hold an in-service training on Effective Communications to develop and upgrade the competence and proficiency of its personnel in the preparation of office communications. This training will involve the action assistants, secretaries, stenographers and typists. The course will be conducted in series, at two weeks intervals, the first to be held from June 16 to July 15, 1980. The sessions will be from Monday to Friday, from 1:30 to 5:30 p.m. at the Briefing Room, 4th Floor Administration Building, Malacañang.

All heads of primary units are hereby enjoined to .submit their nominations to this training program to the Administration Office not later than June 6, 1980. It is understood that the participants shall be free from any assignments during the time they are attending the training.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, June 3, 1980

Source : Presidential Management Staff
