Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 15

AUTHORIZING MR. CESAR A. DUMLAO, FINANCIAL ASSISTANT, OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT, TO SIGN TREASURY WARRANTS AND CHECKS ISSUED BY THIS OFFICE FOR PAYMENT AT ANY OFFICIAL DEPOSITORY OF THE FUNDS OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Effective this date and until farther advice, Mr. Cesar A. Dumlao, Financial Assistant; is also authorized to sign treasury warrants and checks issued by this Office for payment at any depository of the funds of the Office of the President.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

January 14, 1966

Source: Malacañang Records Office
