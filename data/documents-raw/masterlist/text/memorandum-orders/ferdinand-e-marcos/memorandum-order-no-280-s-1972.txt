Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 280

DIRECTING THE ADOPTION IN THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE OF THE WORK HOURS PRESCRIBED IN MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 557, CURRENT SERIES.

It is hereby directed that the office hours prescribed in Memorandum Circular No. 557, dated March 20, 1972, for the summer season, April 1 to June 15, 1972, be adopted by all the, units of the Executive Office.

The grouping of employees into two and the schedules of the group must be prepared and submitted to the Executive Secretary through the Administrative Office not later than March 27, 1972.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 24, 1972
