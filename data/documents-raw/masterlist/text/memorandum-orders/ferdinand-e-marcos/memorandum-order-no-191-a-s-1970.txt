Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM NO. 191-A

CONSTITUTING THE DEVELOPMENT MANAGEMENT STAFF

In addition to their other duties, the following are hereby designated to form the Development Management Staff, under the Chairmanship of the Executive Secretary, as authorized by Executive Order No. 259, series of 1970:

The Staff is hereby authorized to cal upon any agency or instrumentality of the government for any requirements it may have in support of its functions.

The Staff is also authorized to draw upon personnel from the government or private sector for temporary detail on such basis as it may deem necessary.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, August 1, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
