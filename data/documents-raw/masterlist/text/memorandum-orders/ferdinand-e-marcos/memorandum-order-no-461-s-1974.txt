Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 461

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY AND RECOMMEND RATES THE MANILA ELECTRIC COMPANY SHOULD CHARGE NEA-ASSISTED ELECTRIC COOPERATIVES

There is hereby created a Committee which shall study and recommend rates the Manila Electric Company should charge NEA-assisted electric cooperatives. The Committee shall sit down, deal, and coordinate with the duly authorized representatives of the Manila Electric Company, the electric cooperatives buying MERALCO power, the officials of the national government’s institutions involved in the power sector for the purpose aforementioned. This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, September 30, 1974
