Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 128

CREATING THE ACTION COMMITTEE ON RURAL COMPLAINTS

WHEREAS, the government must be brought closer to the people in the rural areas;

WHEREAS, people in rural areas should be encouraged to ventilate their grievances to the authorities concerned for action; and

WHEREAS, all government and private media should be tapped and the complaints collated and channelled to the agency concerned without delay to ensure early attention or action;

WHEREFORE, an “ACTION, COMMITTEE ON RURAL COMPLAINTS” is hereby-created composed of the following:

The Committee shall have the following duties and functions:

1. Evaluate the nature of the complaints received from the rural areas;

2. Indorse the complaints on the prescribed form to the agency concerned for immediate action;

3. Follow-up the action taken on the complaint problems already forwarded for action;

4. Inform the complainant of the action taken by the agency concerned.

The Committee shall coordinate with the PARGO and shall from time to time report its accomplishments to the President who shall cause such report to be published for the information of the public.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, June 27, 1969
