Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 726

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 722 DATED APRIL 24, 1981 CREATING A WORKING GROUP TO UNDERTAKE RESEARCH AND STUDY THE POSSIBLE CHANGES IN THE STRUCTURE OF THE GOVERNMENT IN VIEW OF THE RECENT AMENDMENTS TO THE CONSTITUTION.

Memorandum Order No. 722 dated April 24 , 1981 creating a Working Group under the immediate supervision of the Chairman, Presidential Commission on Reorganization, to undertake research and study the possible changes in the structure of the Government in view of the recent amendments to the Constitution composed of the officials named therein, is hereby amended to include Ms. ANGELITA C. OFILADA and Ms. SOLEDAD V.BALDO, both of the Presidential Commission on Reorganization, era Members of the Working Group, and Ms. MARIETTA F. PAPILIA and Ms. LOURDES BATARIO, also of the Commission, to provide chemical support to the Group.

This Order takes effect immediately.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, June 4, 1981

Source: Presidential Management Staff
