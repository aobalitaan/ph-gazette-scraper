Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 11

TERMINATING THE DETAIL WITH THIS OFFICE OF EMPLOYEES FROM OTHER GOVERNMENT OFFICES

The detail with this Office of the following employees from other, government offices pursuant to the provisions of Administrative Order No. 42, series of 1937, is hereby terminated effective immediately:

N A M E

MOTHER UNIT

They shall secure the usual clearance on their cash and property accountabilities with this Office, if any, and report immediately for duty to their respective offices.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, January 7, 1966

Source : Presidential Management Staff
