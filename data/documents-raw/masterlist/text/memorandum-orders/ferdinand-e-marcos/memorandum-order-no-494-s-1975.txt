TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 494

CREATING A PROJECT MONITORING GROUP EACH FOR LUBANG AND LOOC UNDER THE PRESIDENTIAL TASK FORCE.

Pursuant to Memorandum Order No, 415, series of 1974, and in order to insure a coordinated and expeditious implementation of projects for the development of Lubang Island, there is hereby created a Project Monitoring Group, under the Presidential Task Force, composed of the following:

Municipal Project Monitoring Team each for Lubang and Looc, composed of their respective:

The Project Monitoring Group shall be under the direct operational supervision of the Task Force Chairman, and shall have the following functions:

1. Initiate Community Projects on self-help basis towards the strengthening of the Bayanihan spirit in the community and the development of self-reliance and pride among the people;

2. Oversee and supervise the effective prosecution of all development Projects within the aforesaid municipalities;

3. Implement fully the Guidelines prescribed by the Presidential Task Force for the Projects;

4. Formulate guidelines and suggestions, and recommend measures to the Presidential Task Force for the effective solution of problems that may be encountered in the prosecution of such projects; and

5. Submit progress reports to the Presidential Task Force on the implementation of said Projects.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, March 4, 1975
