Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 307

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 305 DATED OCTOBER 18, 1972, CONSTITUTING A CABINET PERFORMANCE EVALUATION TASK FORCE TO REVIEW AND EVALUATE PERFORMANCE AUDITS ON GOVERNMENT FINANCIAL AGENCIES.

Mr. Juan Quintos, Jr., Associate Director of the Department of Economic Research, In-charge of the Monetary Sector, Central Bank, is hereby designated as Chairman of the Performance Evaluation Team to audit the Philippine National Bank, in place of Mr. Gabriel Itchon.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, October 30, 1972

Source: Malacañang Records Office
