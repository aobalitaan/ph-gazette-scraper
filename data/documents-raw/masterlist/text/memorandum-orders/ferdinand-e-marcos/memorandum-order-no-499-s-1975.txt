MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 499

AUTHORIZING THE TWENTY-TWO (22) PERSONNEL OF THE MALACAÑANG TELEGRAPH OFFICE TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum 192-74 dated August 20, 1974, authority is hereby granted for the twenty-two (22) personnel of the Malacañang Telegraph Office to render overtime service with one (1) meal allowance a day, including Saturdays, Sundays and holidays, effective February 11 up to 28, 1975, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, March 14, 1975

Source: Malacañang Records Office
