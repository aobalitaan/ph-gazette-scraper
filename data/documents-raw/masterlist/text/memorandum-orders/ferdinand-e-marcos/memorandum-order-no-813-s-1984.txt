Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 813

TO: HEADS OF MINISTRIES, BUREAUS, OFFICES AND AGENCIES

The National Productivity Commission is holding a congress/workshop to discuss government sector productivity. It is also launching the Project for the Efficient Operation of Public Licensing Entities (PEOPLE). These activities are set on October 12, 1984 at 8:30 A.M. to 5:30 P.M. at the Strata 100 Building, Pasig, Metro Manila.

Heads of offices and the chiefs of the management, planning, administrative and financial services are hereby enjoined to attend this workshop.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, 12th October, 1984

Source : Malacañang Records Office
