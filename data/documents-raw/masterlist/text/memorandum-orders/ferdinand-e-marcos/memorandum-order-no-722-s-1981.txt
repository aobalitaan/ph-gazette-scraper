Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 722

CREATING A WORKING GROUP TO UNDERTAKE RESEARCH AND STUDY THE POSSIBLE CHANGES IN THE STRUCTURE OF THE GOVERNMENT IN VIEW OF THE RECENT AMENDMENTS TO THE CONSTITUTION.

Pursuant to LOI No. 1114 and the instructions of the Cabinet Standing Committee, there is hereby created a Working Group under the immediate supervision of the Chairman, Presidential Commission in, Reorganization, to undertake research and study the possible changes in the structure of the Government in view of the recent amendments, to the Constitution, composed of the following:

The group is hereby authorized to call on any government office or agency concerned for such assistance as it may need the accomplishment of its work.

This Order takes effect immediately.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, April 24, 1981
