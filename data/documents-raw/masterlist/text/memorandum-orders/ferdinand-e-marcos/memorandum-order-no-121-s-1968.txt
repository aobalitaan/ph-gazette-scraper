Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 121

SETTING A MASS HIKING OF OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

This will confirm the announcement made during the Flag Ceremony last Monday of the hiking en masse of officials and employees of the Office of the President on Friday, January 3, 1969, starting at 6:30 a.m. from the Luneta to the Office. The assembly place is at the Independence Grandstand.

Everyone is enjoined to participate.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, December 27, 1968
