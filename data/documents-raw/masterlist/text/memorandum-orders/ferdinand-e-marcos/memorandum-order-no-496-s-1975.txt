TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 496

CONSTITUTING A PANEL TO NEGOTIATE WITH THE EXPORT-IMPORT BANK OF JAPAN FOR A LOAN TO FINANCE THE AGUS NO. 2 HYDRO-ELECTRIC PROJECT OF THE NATIONAL POWER CORPORATION

There is hereby constituted a panel to negotiate with the Export-Import Bank of Japan for a loan of ¥2,863,000,000 to partially finance the contract for the supply and installation of the turbine-generator sets and auxiliaries of the Agus River No. 2 Hydroelectric Project of the National Power Corporation, the membership of which is as follows:

All government departments, bureaus, offices, agencies and instrumentalities are hereby enjoined to extend the necessary cooperation and assistance to the Negotiating Panel for the successful completion of its functions.

For immediate compliance.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, March 6, 1975
