TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 671

The temporary detail and designation of the government personnel listed, hereunder, effective upon entrance to duty, to augment the Work Force of the Batasang Fambansa, is hereby authorized until 31 December 1979:

ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES

She shall perform the duties and discharge the responsibilities of the position indicated opposite her name.

She shall be entitled to honoraria in the amount equivalent to fifty percent (50%) of her basic salary chargeable against the Batasan funds and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

By Authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, November 23, 1979

Source: Presidential Management Staff
