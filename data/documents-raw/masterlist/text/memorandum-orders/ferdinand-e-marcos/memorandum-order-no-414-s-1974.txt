TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 414

CREATING AN INTER-AGENCY TASK FORCE TO UNDERTAKE THE RELOCATION OF FAMILIES IN BO. NABACAAN, VILLANUEVA, MISAMIS ORIENTAL

There is hereby created an Inter-Agency Task Force to plan, coordinate and supervise the relocation of the residents of Bo. Nabacaan, Villanueva, Misamis Oriental to an appropriate site. This relocation is necessitated by the steps now being taken to set up an industrial estate in that area.

The Inter-Agency Task Force shall be composed of the following:

This Task Force is charged with the following duties and responsibilities:

1. To choose an adequate site for the relocation of Bo, Nabacaan;

2. To prepare or cause the preparation of plans and designs for the development; of this relocation site;

3. To program and administer the use of funds & appropriated for this project;

4. To supervise the implementation of these development plans;

5. To supervise the transfer of families from Bo. Nabacaan to the relocation site; and

6. To administer the area until the Industrial Estate Authority shall have been established.

The Inter-Agency Committee shall continue to exist and exercise its functions as provided for in this Memorandum Order until such time as an Industrial Estate Authority is established for the area.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, 22 March 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
