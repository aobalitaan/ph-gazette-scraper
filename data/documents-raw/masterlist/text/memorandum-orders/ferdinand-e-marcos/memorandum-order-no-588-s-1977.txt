TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 588

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF A SUPERVISORY TRAINING FOR EFFECTIVE ADMINISTRATIVE MANAGEMENT IN ACCORDANCE WITH LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 518.

Pursuant to Letter of Instructions No. 518 whereby the President directed the simultaneous launching of a skill-development-oriented supervisory training program in all government offices in coordination with the Civil Service Commission and accordingly, ordered all heads of departments and offices to see to it that this training is immediately conducted in their respective agencies, all supervisors in the Office of the President who have completed the CSC-sponsored Junior Executive Training Program including those who are presently attending the same course, are hereby required to attend a Supervisory Training for Effective Administrative Management to be conducted by this Office every Monday, Wednesday and Friday, from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. at the Briefing Room starting April 18, 1977, up to June 27, 1977.

All heads of primary units are hereby enjoined to authorize their JET graduates and participants of the current JET course who compose the core of division chiefs of this Office to attend this training program.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, April 11, 1977
