TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (Office of the President of the Philippines)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 592

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 395 DATED OCTOBER 19, 1977, AS AMENDED, ENTITLED: “CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY WAYS AND MEANS TO ENCOURAGE PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLE DRIVER, OWNERS AND OPERATORS TO ORGANIZE THEMSELVES INTO COOPERATIVES.

Memorandum Order No. 1595 dated October 19, 1973, as amended by Memorandum Order No. 438 of July 10, 1974, Memorandum Order No. 460 of September 28, 1974, and Memorandum Order No. 491 of February 19, 1975, is hereby further amended to include as members of the Committee created thereunder, the following:

1. The Director-General, Integrated National Police; and

2. The Commanding Officer, PC Metrocom.

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, May 30, 1977
