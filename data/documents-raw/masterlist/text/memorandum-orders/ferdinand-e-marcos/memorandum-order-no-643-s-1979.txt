TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 643

AUTHORIZING THREE (3) EMPLOYEES OF THE MALACAÑANG GARAGE TO COLLECT ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION FOR OVERTURE SERVICE RENDERED.

In view of exigencies of the service, not more than three (3) employees office Malacañang Garage are hereby authorized to collect additional compensation for overtime service actually rendered, but not exceeding four (4-) hours each in a day, pursuant to the provisions of Section 7-1-(32) of Commonwealth Act No. 246, as amended, and as an exception to Presidential Memorandum No. 19,2-74-, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, February 6, 1979

Source: Presidential Management Staff
