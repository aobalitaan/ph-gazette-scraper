TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 388

ENJOINING ALL DEPARTMENTS, BUREAUS, OFFICES, AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE GOVERNMENT TO COOPERATE IN MAKING THE NATIONAL FLOATING EXPOSITION A SUCCESS.

A National Floating Exposition designed to promote local products and cottage and small-scale industries, as well as the Administration’s “Buy Filipino” program, will be jointly sponsored by the Federation of Provincial Press Clubs of the Philippines (FPPC) and the Philippine Chamber of Handicraft Industries (PCHI).

The success of this joint undertaking of FPPC and the PCHI would depend largely on the cooperation and support of the public and private sectors.

All offices and agencies of the Government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are therefore hereby enjoined to support in every possible way the National Floating Exposition to the end that the objectives thereof may be realized.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 4, 1973
