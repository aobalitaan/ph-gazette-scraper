Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 261

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE WELCOME AS WELL AS THE FUNERAL ARRANGEMENTS AND INTERMENT OF THE LATE COL. JESUS VILLAMOR, HERO OF WORLD WAR II.

WHEREAS, Col.  Jesus Villamor, hero of World War II, died Thursday morning in a Washington hospital after suffering from lingering ailment;

WHEREAS, Col. Villamor demonstrated the highest tradition of Filipino valor and patriotism, when he single-handedly opposed Japanese zeros at the outbreak of the pacific war;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that adequate welcome and funeral arrangements be accorded Col. ViIlamor in grateful recognition of his indomitable courage and willingness to offer his life that all of us may live in peace and freedom;

NOW, THEREFORE, there is hereby created a Committee to take charge of the welcome, funeral arrangements and interment of the late Col. Villamor composed of the following:

The Committee is hereby empowered to call upon any department, bureau or office for such assistance as it may need in the performance of its functions.

This Order takes effect immediately.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, October 29, 1971
