Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 12

ANNOUNCING THE CONDUCT OF A MANAGEMENT AUDIT IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

The Office of the Executive Secretary is undertaking, jointly with the Program Implementation Agency, a management audit of the Office of the President aimed at streamlining its organization and procedures.

In order to facilitate the work of the survey team, all Heads of Offices are enjoined to extend to the survey team their full cooperation and assistance.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, January 7, 1966

Source: Malacañang Records Office
