Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 692

REQUIRING ALL OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF THIS OFFICE TO HAVE THEIR YEARLY MEDICAL CHECK-UP AT THE MALACAÑANG CLINIC

All officials and employees of this Office shall submit themselves to an annual medical check-up at the Malacañang Clinic in accordance with the schedule to be prepared by the Personnel Division, Administrative Office. The medical check-up shall be comprehensive consisting of X-Ray, PCG for those who are 40 years old and above, complete blood count, including blood type and stool and urine examination.

The medical check-up shall commence on April 14, 1980, at a rate of not less than 20 employees a day, and shall continue until all our officials and employees shall have finished their medical check-ups.

Please be guided accordingly.

(Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, March 28, 1980
