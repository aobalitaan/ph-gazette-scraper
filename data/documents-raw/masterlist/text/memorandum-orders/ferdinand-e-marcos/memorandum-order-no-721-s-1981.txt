Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER No. 721

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF A REFRESHER COURSE IN STENOGRAPHY AND TYPING.

In line with the Human Resources Development Program of this Office, the Training Division is hereby authorized to hold a “REFRESHER COURSE IN STENOGRAPHY AND TYPING” to improve the skills and competence of the stenographers and typists scheduled as follows:

ORIENTATION: (Participants of both courses) April 27 and 28, 1981 (2:00-4:30 p.m.)

STENOGRAPHY: April 29 – May 12, 1981 (2:00-4:30 p.m. daily)

TYPING:

1st series: May 16, 23,. 30, June 6, 13 & 20, 1981 (9:00-11:30 a.m. every Saturday)

2nd series: July 11, 18, 25, Aug. 1, 8 & 15, 1981 (9:00-11:30 a.m. every Saturday)

All heads of primary units are hereby enjoined to send their recommendees to the Training Division and to refrain from giving the participants any assignments during the time they are actually attending the training in order not to disrupt their attendance therein.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila,

April 24, 1981
