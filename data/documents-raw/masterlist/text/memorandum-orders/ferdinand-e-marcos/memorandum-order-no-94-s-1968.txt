Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 94

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE AN INVENTORY OF CERTAIN PROPERTIES OF THE DEFUNCT EMERGENCY EMPLOYMENT ADMINISTRATION.

There is hereby created a committee in this Office, composed of the following:

to conduct a physical inventory of the supplies, materials, equipment and other properties of the defunct Emergency Employment Administration, that are housed in the present Wilding of the EEA Staff of this Office in Manila.

The Committee shall start the inventory immediately and shall submit its report within fifteen (15) days from the date hereof, to this Office, through the Presidential Staff Secretary.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, February 15, 1968

Source: Malacañang Records Office
