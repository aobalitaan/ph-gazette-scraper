Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 327

EXCUSING OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THIS OFFICE AT 12:00 NOON TODAY.

In connection with the Maligayang Pasko Festival for children to be held at the Malacañang grounds this afternoon, officials and employees in this Office are hereby excused beginning at 12:00 noon today. It is understood, however, that a skeletal force shall remain in the various divisions or primary units to attend to urgent official business.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, December 18, 1972

Source: Malacañang Records Office
