TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 583

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 520, DATED JUNE 19, 1975.

Memorandum Order No. 520, dated June 19, 1975, “Creating a Committee to Pass upon the Claims for Terminal Leaves and Retirement Gratuities of Former Employees of the Defunct Emergency Employment Administration,” is hereby amended so as to designate Mr. CESAR G. RUBIO as Chairman of the said Committee, vice Mr. Teofilo C. Gelvezon, retired, effective as of January 1, 1977.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, January 18, 1977

Source: Presidential Management Staff
