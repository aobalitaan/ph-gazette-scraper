TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 484

Memorandum Order No. 475 dated December 19, 1974, constituting the Philippine Panel of delegates to the 8th Session of the FAO Intergovernmental Group on Hard Fibers to be held in Manila on January 20-25, 1975, is hereby amended so as to include therein as members the following delegates:

Mr. BIENVENIDO TANTOCO, Sr., President, Rustan Pulp & Paper Co.

General HANZ MENZI, President, Menzi Development Corporation

Mr. JOSE YULO, Jr., Canlubang Pulp & Paper Co., Inc.

Mr. ANTONIO FLORENDO, Abaca Planter, Davao

Mrs. RAMONA P. MIGUEL, Board of Investments

Mr. FRANCISCO TAMOLANG, FORPRIDECOM, Los Baños

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, January 24, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
