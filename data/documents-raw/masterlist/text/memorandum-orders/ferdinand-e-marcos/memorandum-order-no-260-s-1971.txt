Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 260

CREATING AN AD HOC COMMITTEE TO DRAFT GUIDELINES IN REVIEWING OPERATING CONTRACTS BETWEEN LOCAL PETROLEUM CONCESSIONAIRES AND FOREIGN OIL EXPLORATION COMPANIES.

An Ad Hoc Committee is hereby created to draft guidelines for use in reviewing operating contracts’ between local petroleum concessionaires and foreign oil exploration companies, with a view to determining whether the provisions of such contracts are in substantial compliance with the Constitution, the Corporation Law, the Anti-Dummy Law, and other applicable laws,

The Committee shall be composed of the following:

In the drafting of these guidelines, the Committee shall ensure that they are not only consistent with Constitutional and legal requirements, but are also sufficiently liberal to attract into petroleum exploration ventures significant amounts of risk capital, both foreign and domestic.

The Committee is hereby enjoined to submit its report within two weeks.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 10, 1971
