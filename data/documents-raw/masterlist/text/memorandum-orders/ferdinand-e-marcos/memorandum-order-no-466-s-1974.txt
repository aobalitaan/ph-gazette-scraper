TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 466

CREATING AN INTER-AGENCY COMMITTEE TO STUDY, EVALUATE AND MAKE RECOMMENDATIONS REGARDING THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE PARAÑAQUE AREA “B” HOUSING PROJECT.

There is hereby created in Inter-Agency Committee to study ways and means of accelerating the development of Parañaque Area “B” Housing Project, otherwise known as the “Malacañang Village,” and to assist in the implementation of the adjustment of prices for the lots in said Housing Project, in line with the Presidential Directive thereon, dated September 24, 1974.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
