Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 164

DESIGNATING THE ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE SECRETARY FOR LOCAL GOVERNMENT AFFAIRS TO SIGN CORRESPONDENCE THAT USED TO BE PREPARED FOR THE SIGNATURE OF THE ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE SECRETARY FOR ADMINISTRATIVE AFFAIRS, IN THE LATTER’S ABSENCE.

For expediency, official correspondence that are, under Memorandum Order No. 139, dated August 26, 1969, prepared for the signature of the Assistant Executive Secretary for Administrative Affaire shall be prepared for the signature of, and signed by, the Assistant Executive Secretary for Local Government Affairs, in the absence of or whenever the former is out of town or on official trips to the provinces.

This Order takes effect on January 19, 1970.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, January 19, 1970

Source: Malacañang Records Office
