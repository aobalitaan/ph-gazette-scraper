Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 783

In connection with its yearly administration of the Career Executive Service Performance Evaluation System (CESPES), the Career Executive Service Board, through its technical staff, will conduct a workshop for ratees in this Office at 10:00 a.m. on April 14, 1983 at the Briefing Room. Considering the significance of the CESPES which seeks to recognize and reward highly meritorious performance and to provide developmental assistance where it is due, all Career Executive Service Officers occupying CES or Non-CES positions, CES eligibles in CES positions, and all incumbents of CES positions whether or not they have gone through the CES Development Programs in the Office of the President are hereby enjoined to attend the aforementioned briefing.

In case any incumbent is not included in the attached roster furnished by the CESB, kindly submit your name to the Administrative Office on or before April 11, 1983 so that the prescribed materials shall be provided.

For your compliance.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, April 6, 1983
