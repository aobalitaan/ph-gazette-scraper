OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 621-C

The temporary detail and designation of the government personnel listed, hereunder, effective upon entrance to duty, to constitute the Skeletal Work Force of the Batasang Pambansa which shall provide the requisite technical, staff and clerical services for the Batasan, is hereby authorized:

DOCUMENTATION SERVICES

COMMITTEE SERVICE

They shall perform the duties and discharge the responsibilities of the position indicated opposite their respective names.

They shall be entitled to honoraria in the amount equivalent to fifty percent (50%) of their basic salaries chargeable against the Batasan funds and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, July 7, 1978
