Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 129

REQUIRING THE ASSIGNMENT OF LADY RECEPTIONISTS IN ALL UNITS OF THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE.

In order to satisfactorily attend to and service official callers, all units of the Executive Office are hereby required to maintain lady receptionists in their respective jurisdictions, the same to be chosen from their female employees. In making a choice, the unit head shall take into consideration not only personality but general, knowledge of the workings of the unit as well.

This Order shall take effect on August 1, 1969.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, July 30, 1969
