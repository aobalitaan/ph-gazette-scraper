TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 433

AUTHORIZING ONE (1) EMPLOYEE OF THE STAFF SECRETARIAT TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICES WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum Order No. 373, series of 1973, Mr. HUMERTO PERUCHO of the staff Secretariat as hereby authorized to render overtime services with meal allowance, including Saturdays, Sundays and holidays effective June 1, 1974, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounnting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, July 17, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
