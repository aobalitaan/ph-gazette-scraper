Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 81

PROHIBITING THE SELLING OF FOODSTUFFS BY UNAUTHORIZED VENDORS WITHIN THE MALACAÑANG COMPOUND.

With the operation of the Malacañang Canteen, the catering of foodstuffs, including those in “fiambreras” by outside orany other parties, whether ambulant or concessionaire-like, to employees or the public within the Malacañang Compound, is hereby strictly prohibited effective immediately.

The Provost Office and the Malacañang Engineer’s Office shall see to it that this Order is observed and enforced.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, November 29, 1967

Source: Malacañang Records Office
