TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 407

AUTHORIZING THE PERSONNEL OF THE MALACAÑANG CLINIC TO OBSERVE OFFICE HOURS FROM 8:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M., MONDAYS THROUGH FRIDAYS.

In the interest of the service, the personnel of the Malacañang Clinic are hereby authorized to observe the following office hours from Mondays through Fridays, as an exception to the provisions of General Order No. 40, series of 1975:

The Presidential Staff Director of the Malacañang Clinic shall, however, continue to provide sufficient number of necessary personnel from 5:00 P.M. to 8:00 P.M. during the said days, for emergency services, and assign skeleton force from 9:00 A.M. to 7:00 P.M. on Saturdays, Sundays and holidays.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, February 18, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
