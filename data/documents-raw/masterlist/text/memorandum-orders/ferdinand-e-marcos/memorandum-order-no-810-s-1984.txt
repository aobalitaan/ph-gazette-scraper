Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 810

The following employees named hereunder, all officers of the Sentro ng Pagkakaisa sa Pambansang Wika of this Office are hereby authorized to attend the annual meeting of the Sanggunian ng Wika in observance of National Language Week, under the auspices of the Institute of National Language to be held on August 14, 1984 at Camp Aguinaldo, Quezon City:

In this connection, each of the above-listed employees is authorized to collect reimbursement FOR FIFTY PESOS (₱50.00) membership fee and THIRTY PESOS (₱30.00) annual dues, chargeable against the funds of this Office, subject to the availability thereof and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, August 14, 1984
