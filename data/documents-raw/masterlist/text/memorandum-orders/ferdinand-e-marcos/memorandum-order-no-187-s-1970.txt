Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER No. 187

TO: All Personnel Concerned Office of Presidential Assistant on Housing and Resettlement Agency

SUBJECT: Validation of the appointments signed by then PAHRA Secretary Esteban Bernido in conformity with the 2nd Indorsement of this Office dated October 24, 1969 considering that the said employees are De Facto officers.
