Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 809

TERMINATING THE DETAIL IN OTHER GOVERNMENT AGENCIES OF CERTAIN EMPLOYEES OF THIS OFFICE.

This Office is now in the process of reviewing its personnel effectiveness and needs in conjunction with the implementation of LOI No. 146 dated February 7, 1984, directing the implementation of economy measures in government and generation of savings for fiscal year 1984.

In this connection, the detail of officials and employees of this Office with the agencies indicated opposite their respective names, as appearing in the attached list, is hereby terminated effective on August 15, 1984.

Accordingly, the officials and employees in the afore-said list shall secure their respective clearances from money and/or property accountability, if any, and shall report back to this Office not later than August 16, 1984. Failure to report as herein directed shall be considered sufficient ground for their being dropped from the rolls of this Office.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, August 8, 1984

Source : Malacañang Records Office
