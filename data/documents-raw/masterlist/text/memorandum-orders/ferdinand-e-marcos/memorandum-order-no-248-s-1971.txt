Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 248

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 212, SERIES OF 1971, ENTITLED “CREATING A PUBLIC ASSISTANCE UNIT IN THIS OFFICE.”

Memorandum Order No. 212, series of 1971, entitled “Creating a Public Assistance Unit in this Office,” is hereby amended so as to change the name of the said Unit to “Presidential Public Assistance Office,” which shall be directly under the President but shall remain under the administrative supervision of the Executive Secretary.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, July 29, 1971
