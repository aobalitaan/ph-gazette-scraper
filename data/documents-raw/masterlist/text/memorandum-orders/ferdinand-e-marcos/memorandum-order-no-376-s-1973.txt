TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 376

AUTHORIZING THREE (3) LAWYERS IN THE LEGAL OFFICE TO OBSERVE OFFICE HOURS DURING REGULAR WORKDAYS FROM 10:00 A.M. TO 7:00 P.M.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum Order No. 361 dated June 13, 1973, authority is hereby granted for three (3) lawyers in the Legal Office of this Office to observe office hours during regular workdays from 10:00 A.M. to 7:00 P.M.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO B. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, August 31, 1973
