Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 168

REVOKING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 161 DATED JANUARY 9, 1970, AS SUPPLEMENTED BY MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 161-A DATED JANUARY 12, 1970.

Memorandum Order No. 161 dated January 9, 1970, as supplemented by Memorandum Order No. 161-A dated January 12, 1970, entitled “CREATING IN THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE A STAFF ARM TO BE KNOWN-AS INTERNAL PERSONNEL SERVICES UNIT AND FURTHER AMENDING FOR THIS PURPOSE MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 1 DATED JANUARY 7, 1958” is hereby revoked.

The personnel and equipment of the said unit are hereby reverted to the Administrative Office where they originally belonged.

The Recommendation Section integrated Into the Internal Personnel Services Unit by Memorandum Order No. 161-A dated January 12, 1970, is hereby placed under the direct supervision and control of the Executive Secretary.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, February 11, 1970
