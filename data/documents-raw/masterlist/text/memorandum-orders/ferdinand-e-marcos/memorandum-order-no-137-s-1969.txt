Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 137

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 133 DATED AUGUST 15, 1969.

Memorandum Order No. 133 dated August 15, 1969, entitled “ESTABLISHING A PERFORMANCE AWARD SYSTEM IN THE OFFICE”OF THE PRESIDENT,” is hereby amended so as to include within its scope outstanding division performance awards on a yearly basis, to be determined by a Committee to be created by the Executive Secretary for the purpose and which shall establish its own criteria for selection.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, August 25, 1969
