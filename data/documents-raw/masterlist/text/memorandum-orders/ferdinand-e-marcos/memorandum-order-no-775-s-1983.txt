Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 775

A Beautification Committee is hereby constituted to initiate and recommend appropriate steps to provide an atmosphere conducive to efficient performance and to monitor on a day to day basis the state of cleanliness and orderliness of the office premises and the proper maintenance and repair of all facilities and utilities. The Committee shall submit a daily report of its findings to the Engineer’s Office. The Engineer’s Office, for this purpose, shall assign a task force to take priority action on all matters brought to its attention by the Committee.
