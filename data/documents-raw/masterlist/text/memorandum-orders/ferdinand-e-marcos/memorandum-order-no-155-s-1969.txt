Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 155

PRESCRIBING THE HOURS OF OFFICIAL BUSINESS FROM DECEMBER 11, 1969 TO JANUARY 6, 1970.

In view of the onset of the Christmas season and the usual slackening of the volume of work occasioned thereby, the Executive Office shall be open for official business from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. during the period, from December 11, 1969, up to and including January 6, 1970, instead of from 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. as prescribed in Memorandum Order No. 130 dated July 31, 1969.

Observance of the 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. schedule shall be resumed automatically beginning January 7, 1970.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, December 11, 1969

Source: Malacañang Records Office
