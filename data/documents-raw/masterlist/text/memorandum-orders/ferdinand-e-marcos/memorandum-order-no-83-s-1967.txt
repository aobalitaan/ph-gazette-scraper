Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 83

Memorandum Order No. 82 dated November 29, 1967, creating a Special Rizal Day Celebration Committee is hereby amended so as to read as follows:

CREATING A “NATIONAL RIZAL DAY COMMITTEE.”

In order to ensure the successful celebration throughout the Philippines of this year’s death anniversary of Dr. Jose P. Rizal on December 30, there is hereby created a special committee to take charge of all arrangements for the festivities composed of the following:

The Committee shall meet at the call of the Chairman and for the purpose of discharging its functions, may create such sub-committees as may be necessary.

The Committee is hereby authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or instrumentality of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in discharging its duties and functions.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, December 13, 1967
