TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 534

ENJOINING OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT TO ATTEND THE CEREMONIES OF THE OCCASION OF THE 3RD ANNIVERSARY OF THE NEW SOCIETY AT THE LUNETA ON SEPTEMBER 19, 1975.

In pursuance of our Memorandum Circular No. 365, dated September 12, 1975, all officials and employees in the office of the President are hereby enjoined to attend the ceremonies at the Luneta commemorating the 3rd Anniversary of the New Society on September 19, 1975. All officials and employees of the office are therefore requested to assemble as a group in front of the Quirino Grandstand on or before 8:45 a.m. to participate in the said ceremonies.

The Chiefs of units in the Executive Office should see to it that all employees under then shall comply strictly with the provisions of this Order by assigning a responsible official to check attendance thereat.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, September 12, 1975

Source : Presidential Management Staff
