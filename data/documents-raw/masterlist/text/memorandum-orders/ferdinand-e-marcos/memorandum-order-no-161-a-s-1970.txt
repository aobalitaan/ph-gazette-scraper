Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 161-A

INCORPORATING INTO TEE INTERNAL PERSONNEL SERVICES UNIT THE PRESENT RECOMMENDATIONS SECTION WHICH HENCEFORTH SHALL BE KNOWN AS RECOMMENDATIONS SERVICE

The present Recommendations Section is hereby incorporated into the Internal Personnel Services Unit and henceforth shall be known as Recommendations Service. Under the supervision of the Presidential Personnel Officer, its functions shall be the following;

1. To receive callers in need of assistance in their search for a job and drafts letters of recommendation for them.

2. To follow up action on recommendations made, with the end in view of obtaining positive results, if possible.

3. To keep statistics on the number of people attended to and those who succeed in landing a job on the strength of recommendations made.

4. To draft letters of acknowledgment of and draft replies to letters addressed to the President relative to requests/recommendations for employment.

5. To prepare and submit reports as required.

6. To draft all communications pertinent to the above functions.

7. To perform such other functions as may be assigned to it from time to time.

This Memorandum Order supplements Memorandum Order No. 161 and shall take effect on January 20, 1970.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, January 12, 1970
