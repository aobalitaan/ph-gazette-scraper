Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 192

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 102, SERIES OF 1964, SO AS TO GRANT WOMEN EMPLOYEES THE OPTION NOT TO WEAR THE PRESCRIBED UNIFORM DURING FRIDAYS.

Memorandum Order No. 102, dated September 11, 1964, as amended, requiring all women employees in the Office of the President to wear the uniform therein prescribed during attendance in office, is hereby further amended so as to give them the option not to wear the prescribed uniform during Fridays.

This Order shall be effective immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, August 12, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
