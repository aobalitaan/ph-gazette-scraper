Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 270

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO DRAW UP THE IMPLEMENTING DETAILS OF ARTICLE 2186 OF THE CIVIL CODE.

A Committee is hereby created to draw up the implement-of details of Article 2186 of the; Civil Code of the Philippines which provides:

“Every owner of a motor vehicle shall file with the proper government office a bond executed by a government-controlled corporation or office, to answer for damages to third persons. The amount of bond and other terms shall he fixed by the competent public official.”

The Committee shall toe composed of the following:

The government-controlled corporation or office referred to in said Article 2186 of the Civil Code shall toe the Government Service Insurance System.

The Committee shall submit to this Office within sixty (60) days from the date of this Order, a complete plan and program for implementing the above-quoted provision of the Civil Code.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, February 3, 1972
