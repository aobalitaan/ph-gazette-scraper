TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 666

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 102, SERIES OF 1964 AS AMENDED, BY PRESCRIBING FEW SETS OF UNIFORMS FOR LADY EMPLOYEES IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Memorandum Order No, 102, dated September 11, 1964, as amended, requiring all lady employees in the Office of the President to be in uniform during attendance in office, is hereby further amended so as to prescribe two (2) new sets of uniform both tailored cut into a three-piece fashion, consisting of inner blouse, vest and skirt.

All lady employees, except those specifically exempted from wearing; uniforms under H.O. No. 603, series of 1977, shall begin wearing the new sets of uniforms effective October 1, 1979, with the following variations: the gray uniform matched with pink blouse shall be worn on Mondays and Wednesdays; the uniform in flesh color matched with green blouse, on Tuesdays and Thursdays; and the usual “Wash Day”; Friday, where the wearing of the uniforms herein prescribed is optional.

Strict compliance with the provisions of this Order and those of Memorandum Order No. 102, series of 1964, as amended, and Memorandum Order No. 371, series of 1973, which are not inconsistent herewith, is hereby enjoined.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBC C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, September 26, 1979

Source: Presidential Management Staff
