TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 392

AUTHORIZING, FIFTEEN (15) EMPLOYEES OF THE MALACAÑANG ENGINEER’S OFFICE TO COLLECT NOT MORE THAN TWO (2) UNITS MEAL ALLOWANCE PER DAY.

The following fifteen (15) employees of the Malacañang Engineer’s Office, in view of the nature of their duties, are hereby authorized to collect not more than two (2) units meal allowance per day for overtime service rendered, including Saturdays, Sundays and holidays, as an exception to Memorandum Order No. 373, current series, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 15, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
