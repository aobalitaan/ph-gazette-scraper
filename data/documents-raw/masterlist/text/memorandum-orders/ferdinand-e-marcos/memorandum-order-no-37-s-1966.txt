Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 37

CREATING AN INVENTORY COMMITTEE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

There is hereby created an Inventory Committee in this Office composed of the following:

The Committee shall conduct the annual physical inventory for the fiscal year ended June 30, 1966, of all properties and equipment of this Office, including those at Poro Point, La  Union and the Mansion and Guest Houses, Baguio City, as well as those assigned to other bureaus and offices. It shall submit a complete report as soon as possible, including such recommendations as it may deem necessary.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, July 19, 1966

Source: Malacañang Records Office
