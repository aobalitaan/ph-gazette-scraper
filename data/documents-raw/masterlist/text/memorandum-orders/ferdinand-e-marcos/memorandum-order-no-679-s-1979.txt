TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 679

AUTHORIZING BARANGAY VOLUNTEER MEDICS IN THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL VOLUNTEER SERVICE COORDINATING OFFICE TO DRAW TRAVELING ALLOWANCE.

In accordance with the letter of the Ministry of the Budget dated July 27, 1979 and pursuant to the provisions of Section 17-g of Presidential Decree No- 985, the THIRTY SIX (36) additional Barangay Volunteer Medics of the Philippine National Volunteer Service Coordinating Office named in the list attached to said letter are hereby granted traveling allowance of FORTY PESOS (₱40.00) each per month effective January 1, 1979, and for the duration of the performance of their duties and responsibilities, unless sooner terminated, chargeable against the appropriation of said office, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, December 13, 1979

Source: Presidential Management Staff
