TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 404

AUTHORIZING ONE (1) EMPLOYEE OF THE BUILDINGS AND GROUNDS DIVISION TO COLLECT MORE THAN ONE (1) UNIT MEAL ALLOWANCE.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum Order No. 373, series of 1973, Mr. SALVADOR ESMEJARDA of the Buildings and Grounds Division is hereby authorised to collect more than one (1) unit meal allowance for overtime service rendered in connection with the performance of his duties in the Malacañang Palace, effective December 16, 1973.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, January 24, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
