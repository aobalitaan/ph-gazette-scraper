MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 222

CREATING A TECHNICAL COMMITTEE FOR SMALL ARMS MANUFACTURE

To assure appropriate evaluation of all information and data relevant to small arms manufacture, there is hereby created a Technical Committee in the National Security Council composed of the following:

The Technical Committee shall gather all information and data on the various proposals for establishing a local small arms plant/arms manufacture submitted for consideration of the National Security Council. It shall also present to the NSC all relevant information needed to arrive at a decision as to the best proposal to be selected.

To assist the Technical Committee in its tasks, the following are designated Consultants/Advisers:

Brig Gen Antonio Tamayo, CG, AFPSC Commodore Octavio Posadas, NSE, HPN Colonel Primitive Mital, PAF DEPOT, HPAF Colonel Florencio dela Cruz, C, ORD, AFP Mr. Agustin Cancio, Private Sector

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy one.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, March 19, 1971
