TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDEITT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 682

In view of the exigencies of the service, the part-time detail of the following staff members of the Ministry of the Budget and or the national Economic and Development

Authority to the Batasang Pambansa effective upon assumption of duty is hereby authorized pursuant to the provisions of Administrative Order No. 42, series of 1937:

with entitlement to honoraria in the amount not exceeding fifty percent (50%) of their basic salary, chargeable against the staff funds of Minister Jaime C. Laya as Member of the Batasang Pambansa, subject to the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

January 9, 1980

Source : Presidential Management Staff
