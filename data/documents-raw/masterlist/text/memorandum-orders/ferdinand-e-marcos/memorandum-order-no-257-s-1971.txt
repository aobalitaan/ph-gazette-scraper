Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 257

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO CONDUCT AN ON-THE-SPOT INSPECTION OF THE PREMISES AND RACING FACILITIES OF THE MANILA JOCKEY CLUB, INC.

A Committee is hereby created to conduct an ocular inspection of the premises and racing facilities of the San Lazaro Hippodrome with a view to determining whether or not it may “be permitted to resume operations as a racing entity. The Committee shall he composed of the following:

The members of the Committee shall personally conduct an on-the-spot inspection of the premises of the San Lazaro Hippodrome with particular attention to determining whether the racing facilities are adequate and sufficient for the comfort, convenience and protection of all racing segments concerned, in accordance with the pertinent provisions of & Racing Law (Rep. Act No. 309) and the Racing Rules,

The Committee shall submit its recommendation as soon as practicable.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 6, 1971
