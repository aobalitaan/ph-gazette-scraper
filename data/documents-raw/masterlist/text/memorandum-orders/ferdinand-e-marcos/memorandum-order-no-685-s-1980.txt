TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDEITT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 685

AUTHORIZING THE ATTENDANCE OF EMPLOYEES AND KEY PERSONNEL IN A LAY FORUM ON HEART DISEASE ON FEBRUARY 28, 1980 AT 2:00 P.M.

In observance of the Heart Month and to create awareness of the importance of heart disease prevention and care, the Philippine Heart Association and ICI-Imperial Pharmaceuticals are sponsoring a lay forum on heart disease in connection with the project “OPERATION LONG LIFE” in this Office.

The forum will be held on February 28, 1980, at 2:00-4:00 p.m., in the Briefing Room, 4th Floor, Administration Building, where leading cardiologists will give lectures and conduct open forum-discussions during the 2-hour session.

All heads of primary units and offices are hereby enjoined to require their employees and key personnel whose ages are 40 and above to attend the said forum.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, February 4, 1980

Source : Presidential Management Staff
