TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 349

OFFICIALLY NAMING THE BUILDING SITUATED AT 1115 J. P. LAUREL STREET, MANILA, AND DESIGNATING THE ADMINISTRATOR THEREOF.

1. Effective immediately the “building situated at 1115 J.P. Laurel St., San Miguel, Manila, and presently housing the Office of the Executive Secretary, some units of the Executive Office, and the Development Management Staff shall be officially named as the Executive Office Building, San Miguel Annex, Malacañang.

2. The Managing Director, Development Management Staff is hereby designated Administrator for the Executive Office Building, San Miguel Annex. He shall be responsible to the Executive Secretary for the proper management of the building and its facilities.

3. The Engineering Division, Malacañang, shall be responsible for the upkeep, repairs, and maintenance of the building, its offices and facilities. The Chief, Engineering Division shall coordinate with the Administrator on the operational requirements therefor.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, 5 March 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
