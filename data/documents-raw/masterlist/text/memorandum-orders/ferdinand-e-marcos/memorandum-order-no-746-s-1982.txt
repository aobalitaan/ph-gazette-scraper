MALACAÑANG Manila

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 746

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF A SEMINAR ON MEMBERSHIP EDUCATION ON COOPERATIVES.

In line with the Cooperatives Development Program of the Government, the Malacañang Employees Cooperatives (MECOOP) is her-by authorized to hold a “Membe about cooperatives through continues education and training.

The seminar will be held for two weeks duration on half-day sessions. (Mornings only) the schedule is as follows:

January 25-29/82 – First week (8:00-12:00 noon) February 1-5/82 – Second week (8:00-12:00 noon)

A participant is required to attend the seminar for one week only.

All heads of primary units are hereby enjoined to send their recommendees to the Finance Extension Office, Training Unit, MECOOP.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, January 19, 1982

Source: Presidential Management Staff
