Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 827

AUTHORIZING THE CREATION OF A COMMITTEE ON DISPOSAL OF WASTE MATERIALS AND UNSERVICEABLE EQUIPMENT

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 888, dated March 18, 1983, a disposal committee is hereby created composed of the following:

The Committee shall determine all material and equipment which are already old and unserviceable, appraise their value, and decide the manner of their disposition, provided a report on every disposal made by the Committee shall be submitted to the undersigned.

This Order takes effect immediately.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, September 9, 1985
