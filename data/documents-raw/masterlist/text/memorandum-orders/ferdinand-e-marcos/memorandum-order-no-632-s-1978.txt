TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 632

The temporary detail and designation of the government personnel listed hereunder, effective upon entrance to duty but not earlier than 1 September 1978, to constitute the Work Force of the Batasang Pambansa which shall provide the requisite technical, staff and clerical services for the Batasan, is hereby authorized:

OFFICE OF THE SPEAKER

She shall perform the duties and discharge the responsibilities of the position indicated opposite her name.

She shall be entitled to honoraria in the amount equivalent to fifty percent (50%) of her basic salarv chargeable against the Batasan funds and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

By Authority of the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, December 7, 1978
