TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 345

DESIGNATING THE CHAIRMEN AND MEMBERS OF THE AMNESTY COMMISSIONS

Pursuant to Presidential Decree No. 124 and Administrative Order No. 350, of the Office of the President of the Philippines, both dated February 2, 1973, and as recommended by the Secretary of Justice and the Secretary of National Defense, the following named District Judges of the Courts of First Instance and officers of the Armed Forces of the Philippines are hereby designated to compose the Amnesty Commissions as hereunder indicated:

First Commission for Luzon and Palawan, all provinces, cities and islands traditionally belonging to Luzon and Palawan:

Second Commission for the Visayas, all islands traditionally comprising the said group, and all provinces and cities within the said group of islands:

Third Commission for the islands of Mindanao, Sulu Archipelago, all islands traditionally belonging to the said group, all provinces and cities situated therein:

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, February 8, 1973.

Source: Presidential Management Staff
