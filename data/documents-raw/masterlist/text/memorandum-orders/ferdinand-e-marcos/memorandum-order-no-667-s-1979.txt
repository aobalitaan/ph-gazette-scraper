TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 667

CREATING A CLEANLINESS COORDINATING COMMITTEE IN THIS OFFICE IN CONNECTION WITH THE “TULUNGAN SA KALINISAN” PROGRAM OF THE METRO MANILA COMMISSION.

In cooperation with the “Tulungan sa Kalinisan” Program (Phase II) of the Metropolitan Manila Commission, a Cleanliness Coordinating Committee in this Office is hereby created composed of the following:

The Committee shall plan, coordinate, monitor and supervise the implementation of its cleanliness program in coordination with the Tulungan sa Kalinisan Secretariat of the Metropolitan Manila Commission.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, October 4, 1979
