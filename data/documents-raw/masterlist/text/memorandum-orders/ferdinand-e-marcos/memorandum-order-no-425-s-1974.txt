TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 425

CREATING MOBILE TEAMS OF THE RAD MINDANAO PROGRAMS.

Effective immediately there is hereby created Mobile Teams of the RAD Mindanao Programs to identify priority projects within the predominantly Muslim Mindanao areas, specifically for the Lanao, Zamboanga and Cotabato areas.

The respective Mobile Teams shall be composed of the following members representing the different government agencies involved in the development programs of Mindanao.

1. Bureau of Public Highways

a) Anonias Putong b) Manuel Aleoris c) Lorenzo Fidelezo

2. Bureau of Public Works

a) Arsenio Villafria b) Nelson Galvez c) Alfredo Trono

3. National Irrigation Administration

a) Mario Lara b) Dependian Ibrahim c) Jose Hipe 4. Infrastructure Operations Center

a) Henry Aglipay c) Graciano Malapira c) Orlando Ventura

5. Local Water Utilities Administration

a) Carlos Leano, Jr. b) Primitivo Alava c) Benjamin Panganiban

6. Budget Commission

a) Jose Sempio b) Aiden Velasco c) Benjamin V. Mendoza

7. RAD Mindanao Task Force

a) Roberto L. Abling b) Emilio de Quiros, Jr. c) Lourdes P. Gaviola d) Alberto Santos e) Simplicio Endaya f) Ida Estioko g) Leopoldo Bungubung h) Exsuan Dagbusan i) Mastura Usman j) Tahil Lidasan

Respective members of each Mobile Team shall provide immediately the listings of priority projects within the area under consideration, guidelines in pursuing project implementation/development, and criteria for financial support of said projects.

All expenses incidental to the operations of the RAD Mindanao Mobile Teams are chargeable against the RAD Mindanao Budget as authorized under P.D. 290 subject to the availability of funds, the accounting and auditing requirements and the existing rules and regulations of the General Auditing Office.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, 26 April 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
