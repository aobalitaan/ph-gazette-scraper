Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 32

AUTHORIZED A NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENTIAL ASSISTANT ON HOUSING AND RESETTLEMENT TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE.

In view of the exigencies of the service, the Presidential Assistant on Housing and Resettlement is hereby authorized to assign not more than five (5) employees in his office to render service beyond the regular office hours, excluding Sundays. The said employees are hereby authorized to draw meal allowance for overtime service rendered in accordance with the time schedule prescribed under Memorandum Order No. 94 of this Office dated August 17, 1964, chargeable against the appropriations for the said office, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, and to the certification by the Presidential Assistant on Housing, or his duly authorized representative, in the corresponding time records as to the authority to render the overtime service and as to the time indicated thereon.

This Order takes effective as of June 1, 1966.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, June 9, 1966

Source: Malacañang Records Office
