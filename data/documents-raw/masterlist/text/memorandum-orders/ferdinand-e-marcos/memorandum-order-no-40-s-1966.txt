Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 40

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY THE ORGANIZATION, FUNCTIONS AND MEMBERSHIP COMPOSITION OF THE PROPOSED COMMISSION ON NATIONAL GOALS.

There is hereby created a Committee to study the organization, functions and membership composition of the proposed Commission on National Goals.

The Committee shall be composed of the following:

1. Hon. Narciso Ramos, Chairman 2. Hon. Eduardo Z. Romualdez, Member 3. Hon. Carlos P. Romulo, Member 4. Hon. Rafael M. Salas, Member 5 Hon. Filemon C. Rodriguez, Member 6. Hon. Alfonso Arellano, Member

The Committee shall submit its report and recommendations within one month from the date hereof.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, August 11, 1966
