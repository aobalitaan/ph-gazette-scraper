Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 211

RECONSTITUTING THE PRESIDENTIAL STEEL COMMITTEE.

The Presidential Steel Committee constituted under the letter directive dated February 10, 1966, and as reconstituted under the letter directive dated June 11, 1968, both issued by this Office, is hereby further reconstituted as follows:

The Committee shall perform the same functions and take the same responsibilities as specified in the letter-directive of February 10, 1966, mentioned above.

The Board of Investments is hereby designated as the Technical Staff of the Committee.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, January 20, 1971
