Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 294

ADVICE OF TERMINATION OF SERVICES

The following casual employees in this Office are hereby advised that, for lack of funds, their employment in the Small Farmers Commission, Office of the President, has to be, as it is hereby terminated effective at the close of office hours on June 30, 1972:

They shall secure the usual clearance on their cash and property accountabilities with this Office, if any, and submit their Sworn Statement of Assets and Liabilities as of the said date, within (30) days thereafter, pursuant to the provisions of Republic Act No. 3019.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, July 14, 1972
