Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 311

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO HANDLE THE PROPOSED ORGANIZATION OF THE BUREAU OF TRANSPORTATION.

Pursuant to Letter of Instructions No. 11 dated November1, 1972, there is hereby created a Committee to organize the Bureau of Transportation composed of the following:

The Committee shall prepare the following:

a. Statement of specific functions of organizational units down to Section level;

b. Staffing pattern;

c. Proposed budgetaryrequirements (Proposed budget for FY 1974); and

d. Estimated workload of the proposed Bureau based on the past workloads of the agencies which will be merged in the proposed Bureau.

The report and recommendation of the Committee shall be submitted to the Secretary of Public Works, Transportation and Communications within two (2) weeks from the date hereof.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, November 8, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
