Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 264

DISSOLVING THE STAFF TO MONITOR PRESIDENTIAL PRIORITY PROGRAMS AND TRANSFERRING THE FUNCTIONS THEREOF TO THE DEVELOPMENT MANAGEMENT STAFF, EXECUTIVE OFFICE

The Staff to monitor Presidential Priority Programs, constituted under Memorandum Order No. 232, dated May 13, 1971, and the positions of Program Data Coordinators designated in relation thereto are hereby dissolved.

The functions of the staff so dissolved are hereby transferred to the Development Management Staff, Executive Office.

This Order amends Memorandum Order No. 232, series of 1971.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, November 26, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
