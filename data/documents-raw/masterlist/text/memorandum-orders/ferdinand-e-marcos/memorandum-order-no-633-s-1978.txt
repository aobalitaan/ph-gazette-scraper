TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (Office of the President of the Philippines)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 633

The temporary detail and designation of the government personnel listed, hereunder, effective upon entrance to duty, to constitute the Skeletal Work Force of the Batasang Pambansa which shall provide the requisite technical, staff and clerical services for the Batasan, is hereby authorized:

FINANCE AND MANAGEMENT SERVICES

ACCOUNTING SERVICE

He shall perform the duties and discharge the responsibilities of the position indicated opposite his name.

He shall be entitled to honoraria in the amount equivalent to fifty percent (50%) of his basic salary chargeable against the Batasan funds and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

By Authority of the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, December 7, 1978
