Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES) MALACAÑANG, MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 191

DESIGNATING THE ACTING PROJECT DIRECTOR FOR THE INTENSIFIED AND ACCELERATED IMPLEMENTATION OF LAND REFORM IN NUEVA ECIJA.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate ACA Regional Director Arsenio V. Exconde, Acting Project Director of the Intensified and Accelerated Implementation of Land Reform in Nueva Ecija, under the joint auspices of the National Land Reform Council and the National Food and Agriculture Council.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of August, in the Year of Our Lord, Nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

Source: Presidential Management Staff
