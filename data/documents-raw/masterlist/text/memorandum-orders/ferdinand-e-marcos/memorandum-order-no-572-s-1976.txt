TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 572

AUTHORIZING THE DETAIL OF CERTAIN GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL TO THE SECRETARIAT OF THE BATASAN BAYAN

The detail of the employees of offices mentioned in the attached list to the Secretariat of the Batasan Bayan is hereby authorized effective September 20, 1976.

The heads of the offices concerned are hereby directed to instruct the employees under then to report immediately to Secretary Emilio M, de la Cruz, II for specific assignment.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, September 19, 1976

Reference: Government personnel detailed to the secretariat of the Batasan Bayan under Memorandum Order No. 572 dated September 19, 1976.

Source: Presidential Management Staff
