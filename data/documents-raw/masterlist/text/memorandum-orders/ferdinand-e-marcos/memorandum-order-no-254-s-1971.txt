Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 254

AUTHORIZING ATTY. VICENTE A. GALANG TO ACT ON AND SIGN, FOR AND ON BEHALF OF THE EXECUTIVE SECRETARY, MATTERS, PAPERS AND/OR COMMUNICATIONS WHICH ARE ROUTINE IN CHARACTER.

Atty. VICENTE A. GAIANG, Junior Presidential Staff Assistant of this Office, is hereby empowered and authorized to attend to, act upon and sign, for and on behalf of the Executive Secretary, matters, papers and/or communications which are routine in character not requiring the formulation of policy nor the promulgation of decision.

For the above-stated purpose, Atty. VICENTE A. GALANG shall be given, and is hereby so given, the designation of Special Assistant to the Executive Secretary and shall exercise” the authority to act and sign herein granted as such.

So ordered.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, September 6, 1971

Source: Malacañang Records Office
