Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 824

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF A SEMINAR ON MEMBERSHIP EDUCATION ON COOPERATIVES

In line with the Cooperatives Development Program of the Government, the Malacañang Employees Cooperatives (MECOOP) is hereby authorized to hold a “Membership Education Seminar” to enhance the knowledge of its members about cooperatives through continuous education and training.

The seminar will be held for one week from August 2 6 to 30, 1985 at 1:00-5:00 p.m.

All heads of primary units are hereby enjoined to send their recommendees to the Finance Extension Office, Training Unit, MECOOP.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, April 22, 1985
