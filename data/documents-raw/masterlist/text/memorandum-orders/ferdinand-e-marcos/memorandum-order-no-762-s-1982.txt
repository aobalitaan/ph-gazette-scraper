Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 762

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF AN ORIENTATION COURSE ON COURTESY AND THE PROPER USE OF TELEPHONE.

To enhance the awareness of our employees assigned to answer office telephones, of courtesy and the proper use of telephone, the Personnel Office is hereby authorized, to hold an Orientation Course on Courtesy and the Proper Use of Telephone on November 17, 1982, at 2:00 p.m. at the Briefing Room, 4th Floor, of the Administration Building.

Employees assigned to answer office telephones in each unit of this Office are requested to attend the orientation.

All heads of primary units are hereby enjoined to send at least two names of such employees to the Personnel Office on or before Friday, November 12, 1982.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, November 11, 1982
