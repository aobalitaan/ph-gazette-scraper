Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 25

AUTHORIZING A NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES IN THE PROTOCOL DIVISION TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE.

In view of the exigencies of the service, the Presidential Protocol Officer is hereby authorized to assign five (5) employees in the Protocol Division to render service beyond the regular office hours and during Saturdays, Sundays and holidays with meal allowance for overtime service rendered in accordance with the time schedule prescribed under Memorandum Order No. 94 of this Office dated August 17, 1964, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements,

This Order takes effect as of March 5, 1966

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, March 31, 1966

Source: Malacañang Records Office
