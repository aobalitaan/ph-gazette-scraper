Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 98

CREATING A NATIONAL OCMMITTIE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE CELEBRATION OF PHILIPPINE INDEPENDENCE DAY ON JUNE 12, 1968.

There is hereby created a National Committee to take charge of the celebration of Philippine Independence Day on June 12, 1963, composed of the following:

The Committee shall meet at the call of the Chairman and for the purpose of discharging its functions, may create such sub-committees as may be necessary.

The Committee is hereby authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or instrumentality of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in discharging its duties and functions.

By authority of the President: RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, April 5, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
