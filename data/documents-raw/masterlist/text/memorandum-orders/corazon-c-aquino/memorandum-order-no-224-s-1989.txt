Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

Memorandum Order No. 224

CONSTITUTING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE MANAGEMENT OF THE MALACANAN PALACE

There is hereby constituted a Committee to take charge of the administration, maintenance, preservation and accountability of property and assets of the Malacanan Palace composed of the following:

The Malacanang Household shall turn over to the Committee the administration, maintenance, preservation and accountability of the Malacanan Palace, including such property, assets and personnel as may be deemed part of the Palace.

The Committee shall coordinate with the Museo Foundation in connection with the latter’s function pertaining to the operation and management of the public tours at the Palace.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

Manila, March 13, 1989

Source : Presidential Management Staff
