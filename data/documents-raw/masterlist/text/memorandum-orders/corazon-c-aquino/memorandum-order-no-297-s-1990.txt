Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 297

RECONSTITUTING THE SELECTION BOARD IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

The Selection Board in the Office of the President created under Memorandum Order No. 248 of this Office dated August 21, 1989, is hereby reconstituted as follows:

Deputy Executive Secretary Mariano Sarmiento II

The Head of the Personnel Office shall act as the Secretary of the Board.

As herein reconstituted, the Selection Board shall perform the same functions as defined in the aforecited Memorandum Order and shall review and pass upon proposed appointments and promotions.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, May 31, 1990
