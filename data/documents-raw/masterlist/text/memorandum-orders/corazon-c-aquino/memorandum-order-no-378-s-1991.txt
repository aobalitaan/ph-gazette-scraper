Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 378

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 376 DATED JULY 23, 1991 CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE STATE VISIT OF HIS MAJESTY SULTAN HAJI HASSANAL BOLKIAH, SULTAN AND YANG-DIPERTUAN OF BRUNEI DARUSSALAM ON AUGUST 17 TO 20, 1991 TO INCLUDE HON. RAMON MONTANO AND HON. TOMAS SYQUIA, IN LIEU OF HON. JESUS MARAVILLA, AS MEMBERS OF THE NATIONAL COMMITTEE.

Memorandum Order No. 376 dated July 23, 1991 creating a National Committee to take charge of the state visit of His Majesty Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah, Sultan and Yang-Dipertuan of Brunei Darussalam on August 17 to 20, 1991, is hereby amended to include Hon. RAMON MONTANO, Undersecretary, Department of the Interior and Local Government, and Hon. TOMAS SYQUIA, Director-General for Protocol, Department of Foreign Affairs, in lieu of Hon. Jesus Maravilla, as Members of the National Committee.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary

Manila, July 25, 1991
