Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 50

DIRECTING THE MINISTRY OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS TO COMPLY WITH THE PROVISIONS OF PROCLAMATIONS NOS. 469-A and 469-B, S. 1965

The Ministry of Public Works and Highways shall turn over to the General Services Administration that portion of the reservation being encroached upon by that Ministry, which is part of the area clearly defined under Proclamation No. 469-B, S. 1965, and open to disposition in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act Numbered Four Hundred and Seventy-Seven, as amended.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, November 19, 1986
