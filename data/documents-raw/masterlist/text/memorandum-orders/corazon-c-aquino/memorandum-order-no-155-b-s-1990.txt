Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 155-B

SUPPLEMENTING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 155-A DATED FEBRUARY 10, 1988 PROVIDING CLARIFICATORY INFORMATION ON THE GRANT OF INCENTIVE/MEDICAL ALLOWANCE AND REQUIRING THE REFUND OF SUCH BENEFITS WHICH WERE PAID WITHOUT LEGAL BASIS

A supplement to Memorandum Order No. 155-A is hereby issued as follows:

“To ease the burden of paying back unauthorized incentive/medical allowances, agencies may allow their employees to return and/or refund such funds within a period of two (2) years from the date of receipt of this Order.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

Manila, January 8, 1990.

By authority of the President (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
