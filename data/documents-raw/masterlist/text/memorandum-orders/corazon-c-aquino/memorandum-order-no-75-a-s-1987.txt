Malacañang Manila

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 75-A

GRANTING SALARY INCREASES FOR POSITIONS IN THE NATIONAL CENSUS AND STATISTICS OFFICE

The increase in salaries of Career Executive Service positions in the National Census and Statistics Office (NCSO) by five (5%) percent and the salaries of the rank and file positions in the same office by thirty (30%) percent is hereby authorized effective January 1, 1987:

The Secretary of Budget and Management shall issue the necessary rules and regulations to implement this Memorandum Order.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, March 26, 1987
