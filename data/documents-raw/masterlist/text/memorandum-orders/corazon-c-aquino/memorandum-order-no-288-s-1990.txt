Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 288

REORGANIZING THE AD HOC PROJECT FACILITATION COMMITTEE

The Project Facilitation Committee created by Memorandum Order No. 131, Series of 1987, is hereby reorganized as an ad hoc support mechanism in the Office of the President to be composed of the following:

In addition to the functions under Memorandum Order No. 131, Series of 1987, the Project Facilitation Committee shall provide support to the Committee on Official Development Assistance (CODA) in the performance of the latter’s functions and responsibilities.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

April 2, 1990
