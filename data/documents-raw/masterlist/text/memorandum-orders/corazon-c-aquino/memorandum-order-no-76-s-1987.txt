MALACAÑANG Manila

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 76

CREATING A COMMUNITY EMPLOYMENT AND DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM (CEDP) INTER-AGENCY COMMITTEE

WHEREAS, to speed up economic recovery, the Government has launched a nationwide community development program which is rural-based and employment oriented, known as the Community Employment and Development Program (CEDP);

WHEREAS, there is a need to coordinate and monitor the implementation of various projects of this Program to ensure the judicious utilization of public funds;

IN VIEW THEREOF, the Community Employment and Development Program (CEDP) Inter-Agency Committee, hereinafter referred to as the Inter-Agency Committee, is hereby created, composed of the following:

The Inter-Agency Committee shall be responsible to the President on any matter concerning the Community Employment and Development Program and shall have the following functions:

A) Formulate the Community Employment and Development Program plans and programs;

B) Coordinate the efforts of all agencies involved in the program;

C) Act on any problem reported;

D) Evaluate the Community Employment and Development Program in terms of plans and program; and

E) Report directly to the President significant information or any matter requiring Presidential attention or action.

The Chairpersons of the said Committee shall be authorized to implement the objectives of this monitoring system.

The National Economic and Development Authority shall act as the Secretariat of this Inter-Agency Committee.

The amount necessary for the implementation of this monitoring system shall be shouldered by the different departments concerned.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, 30 March 1987
