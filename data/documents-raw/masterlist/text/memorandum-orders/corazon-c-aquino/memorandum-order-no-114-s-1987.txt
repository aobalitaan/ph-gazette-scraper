Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 114

CREATING AN AD-HOC COMMITTEE TO CONDUCT INVENTORY OF SUPPLIES AND OTHER TANGIBLE ASSETS OF THE HOUSE EMPLOYEES HOMESITE ADMINISTRATION AND PERFORM OTHER PERTINENT FUNCTIONS SPECIFIED IN JOINT CIRCULAR NO. 3-82 OF THE COMMISSION ON AUDIT, MINISTRY OF FINANCE AND OFFICE OF BUDGET AND MANAGEMENT, DATED JANUARY 18, 1982

In view of the abolition of the House Employees Homesite Administration (HEHA) under Memorandum Order No. 100, dated July 28, 1987, there is hereby created an Ad-Hoc Committee to conduct inventory of equipment, supplies and other tangible assets of HEHA and perform other pertinent functions specified in Joint Circular No. 3-82 of the Commission on Audit, Ministry of Finance and Office of Budget and Management, dated January 18, 1982, with Mrs. ANSELMA DOMONDON, HEHA Administrator as Chairman and the following as members: a representative each from Finance Office, (Property Unit), Accounting Office, Auditing Office and Malacañang Record Office, to be designated by the respective chiefs of said offices.

The Committee shall finish its task before the turn-over of said assets and records from this Office to the National Housing Authority effective October 1, 1987.

In this connection, Director ADOLFO BUSTRIA and Mrs. ANSELMA DOMONDON, are hereby designated representatives of the Office of the President (OP) and the HEHA, respectively, as members of the Inter-Agency Standing Committee created under the aforecited Joint-Circular No. 3-82, dated January 18, 1982.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.)SAMILI N. BARLONGAY Acting Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, September 29, 1987
