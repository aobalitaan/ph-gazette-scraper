Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 227

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Finance, Memorandum Order No. 226 dated March 20, 1989 creating a National Committee to take charge of the official visit of His Excellency Noboru Takeshita, Prime Minister of Japan, is hereby amended to include Hon. Diosdado M. Macapagal, Jr., Undersecretary of Finance, as a Member of the Committee, representing the Department of Finance.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 29th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, March 30, 1989
