Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 171

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 62, DATED OCTOBER 28, 1963, AS AMENDED BY MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 291 DATED JULY 5, 1972, ENTITLED, “CREATING A RECORDS MANAGEMENT IMPROVEMENT COMMITTEE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.”

Memorandum Order No. 62, dated October 28, 1963, as amended, creating a Committee on Records Retention and Disposal for the Office of the President, is hereby further amended to reconstitute the membership thereof to be composed of representatives from the following offices:

Administrative Office Correspondence Office Finance Office Legal Office Office of the Assistant Executive Secretary for Legislatio Malacañang Public Assistance Cente Malacañang Records Offic Office of the Executive Secretary Deputy Executive Secretary (OES/ODES)

The heads of the aforementioned offices are hereby authorized to designate their respective representatives to the Committee.

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) MAGDANGAL B. ELMA Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, May 3, 1988
