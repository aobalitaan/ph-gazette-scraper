Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 379

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 377 DATED JULY 23, 1991 CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE OFFICIAL VISIT OF HER ROYAL HIGHNESS MAHA CHAKRI SIRINDHORN OF THAILAND FROM AUGUST 27, 1991 TO SEPTEMBER 1, 1991, TO INCLUDE HON. RAMON MONTANO AND HON. TOMAS SYQUIA, IN LIEU OF HON. JESUS MARAVILLA, AS MEMBERS OF THE NATIONAL COMMITTEE.

Memorandum Order No. 377 dated July 23, 1991 creating a National Committee to take charge of the official visit of Her Royal Highness Maha Chakri Sirindhorn of Thailand from August 27, 1991 to September 1, 1991, is hereby amended to include Hon. RAMON MONTANO, Undersecretary, Department of the Interior and Local Government, and Hon. TOMAS SYQUIA, Director-General for Protocol, Department of Foreign Affairs, in lieu of Hon. Jesus Maravilla, as Members of the National Committee.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary

Manila, July 25, 1991
