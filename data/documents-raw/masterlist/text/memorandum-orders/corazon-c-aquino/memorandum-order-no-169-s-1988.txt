Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 169

MODIFYING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 62 DATED JANUARY 15, 1987 AUTHORIZING THE CONTINUED EXEMPTION FROM CLASSIFICATION BY THE ORGANIZATION, PRODUCTIVITY AND IMPROVEMENT BUREAU OF APPOINTMENTS TO POSITIONS PAID FROM CERTAIN LUMP SUM APPROPRIATIONS IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Memorandum Order No. 62 is hereby modified to the extent that appointments to positions paid from the lump-sum appropriations of the Cabinet Secretariat are hereby included in the coverage of the aforementioned Memorandum Order.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, April 26, 1988
