Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 108

AUTHORIZING THE NEWLY REORGANIZED ENERGY REGULATORY BOARD TO ADOPT THE OP PAY PLAN

The Board of Energy prior to its reorganization into the Energy Regulatory Board under Executive Order No. 172 was previously authorized to grant its personnel inflation-connected allowance chargeable against its income per P.D. No. 1206 as amended by P.D. No. 1573.

Executive Order No. 172 does not have a provision for the new Energy Regulatory Board to continue to use a portion of its income for personnel benefits, hence, it can no longer grant said allowance.

Consistent with the policy of the National Government not to diminish the established compensation level of its employees, the newly reconstituted Energy Regulatory Board is hereby authorized to adopt the OP Pay Plan under E.O. No. 530.

The amount needed to implement this authority shall be taken from the agency’s appropriations.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, September 10, 1987
