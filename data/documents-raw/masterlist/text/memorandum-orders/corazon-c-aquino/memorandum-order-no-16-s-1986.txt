Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 16

In order to safeguard the confidential nature and security of matters discussed during Cabinet Meetings and for reasons of economy, Mrs. Dalisay L. Nazareno, Assistant Cabinet Secretary is hereby directed that only Members of the Cabinet and the officials specified in the following list, and others that may be authorized by the undersigned, shall be furnished copies of Cabinet Agenda and Minutes:

This is part of the continuing efforts of adopting appropriate measure to forestall the possibility of such unauthorized and premature disclosure of confidential information contained in documents of the Cabinet as would be prejudicial to the interest of the nation.

Strict compliance with this order is hereby enjoined.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

June 10, 1986
