MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 14

MODIFYING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 1 DATED FEBRUARY 28, 1986, AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 13 DATED APRIL 23, 1986, CREATING THE PRESIDENTIAL COMMISSION ON GOOD GOVERNMENT.

Executive Order No. 1 dated February 28, 1986, as amended by Executive Order No. 13 dated April 23, 1986, creating the Presidential Commission on Good Government, is hereby modified to the extent that the Commission is likewise hereby exempted from the ban on capital outlays, subject to the policies and guidelines issued by the President.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, June 10, 1986
