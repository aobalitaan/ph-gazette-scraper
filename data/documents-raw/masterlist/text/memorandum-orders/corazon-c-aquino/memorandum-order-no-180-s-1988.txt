MALACAÑANG Manila

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 180

AUTHORIZING THE NEWLY REORGANIZED ECONOMIC INTELLIGENCE AND INVESTIGATION BUREAU TO ADOPT THE OP PAY PLAN

The Economic Intelligence and Investigation Bureau which is mandated to supervise, monitor and coordinate all intelligence and investigation operations in the Department of Finance, in the implementation of its new staffing pattern, is hereby authorized to adopt the OP Pay Plan under E.O. No. 530, effective not earlier than January 1, 1988.

Funds needed to implement this Order shall be taken from the current year’s programmed appropriation of the EIIB.

The Secretary of Budget and Management shall issue the necessary rules and regulations to implement this Memorandum Order.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, July 11, 1988
