MALACAÑANG Manila

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 74

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO UNDERTAKE A STUDY ON PUBLIC SECTOR UNIONISM AND TO RECOMMEND APPROPRIATE MEASURES THEREON

WHEREAS, Section 8, Article III of the 1987 Constitution provides that “The right of the people, including those employed in the public and private sectors, to form unions, associations, or societies for purposes not contrary to law shall not be abridged”;

WHEREAS, as there has been no substantial experience on the subjects of public sector unionism and mass actions in government institutions, it is necessary that an exhaustive study on these matters be made;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby order:

SECTION 1. There is hereby created a Committee to undertake a study on public sector unionism to be composed of the following:

SECTION 2. The Committee shall submit to the Office of the President its findings and recommendations within sixty (60) days from the date of this Memorandum Order.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(SGD.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
