MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 239

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 125 RECLUSTERING THE CABINET MEMBERS INTO SMALLER GROUPS AND RECONSTITUTING THE MEMBERSHIP THEREOF

Memorandum Order No. 125 dated November 6, 1987 is hereby amended to include therein as “Cluster F” the committee created under Administrative Order No. 58 dated February 16, 1988, as amended.

“Cluster F” shall deal primarily with vital issues and concerns affecting peace and order and law enforcement.

Administrative Order 58 dated February 16, 1988, as amended, is hereby revoked.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila this 29th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO
