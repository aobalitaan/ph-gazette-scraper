Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 360

CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE VISIT OF HIS EXCELLENCY J. DANFORTH QUAYLE, VICE-PRESIDENT OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA ON MAY 23, 1991.

There is hereby created a Committee to take charge of the arrangements connected with the Visit of His Excellency, J. Danforth Quayle, Vice-President of the United States of America on May 23, 1991 to be composed of:

The Committee shall be responsible for the planning, coordination and effective execution of all arrangements to be made in connection with the official visit.

The Committee is authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or any instrumentality of the government, including government-owned and controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in discharging its duties and functions. The Committee is likewise authorized to create sub-committees as may be necessary.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) OSCAR M. ORBOS Executive Secretary

Manila, May 8, 1991
