MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 385

CREATING A MULTI-SECTORAL COMMITTEE TO REVIEW THE RECOMMENDATIONS OF THE PUBLIC ESTATES AUTHORITY RELATIVE TO THE DEVELOPMENT OF THE ALABANG STOCK FARM IN MUNTINLUPA, METROPOLITAN MANILA.

The Public Estates Authority has been directed to develop the Alabang Stock Farm in Muntinlupa, Metropolitan Manila, on behalf of the National Government subject to certain conditions provided in Memorandum Order No. 371 dated June 27, 1991. Under present policy, the recommendations of the Public Estates Authority are subject to review by the Office of the President.

There is hereby created a Multi-Sectoral Committee to undertake a review of the report, the process undertaken, and the recommendations of the Public Estates Authority relative to the development of the Alabang Stock Farm.

The Committee shall submit to the Office of the President its findings and recommendations within thirty (30) days from the date hereof.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

Manila, August 19, 1991.
