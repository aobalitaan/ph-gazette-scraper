Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 111

DIRECTING ALL CABINET MEMBERS TO ADVISE THE PRESIDENT OF INACCURATE OR ADVERSE MEDIA ACCOUNTS AFFECTING THEIR DEPARTMENT

All Cabinet members are hereby directed to forthwith advise the President in writing of all media accounts that (a) misquote them or (b) portray their actions or activities inaccurately as to place the Administration in a bad light. Efforts should likewise be exerted to correct the misquotations or inaccuracies.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

September 24, 1987
