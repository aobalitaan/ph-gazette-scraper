Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 309

REVOKING LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 750, DATED OCTOBER 16, 1978

Letter of Instructions No. 750, dated October 16, 1978, entitled “Relative To The Procurement And Acquisition of EDP Equipment And Services” is hereby revoked.

Accordingly, procurement of computer equipment and services, whether on a lease-rental arrangement or purchase basis and regardless of the amount involved, shall no longer require approval by this Office.

Memorandum Order No. 237, S. of 1989, and Letter of Instructions No. 1380, S. of 1984 shall govern acquisition and use of Information Technology (IT) Resources in government.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 30th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
