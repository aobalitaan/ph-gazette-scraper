Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 158

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 144, DATED DECEMBER 29, 1987.

Memorandum Order No. 144, dated December 29, 1987, “Creating a Management Improvement Committee in the Office of the President,” is hereby amended so as to designate Deputy Executive Secretary Magdangal B. Elma as Chairman of the Committee and to include the Assistant Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs and Assistant Secretary Jeremias R. Flores as members thereof, effective as of February 19, 1988.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, February 24, 1988
