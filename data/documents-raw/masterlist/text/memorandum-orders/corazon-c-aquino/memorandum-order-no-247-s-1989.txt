Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 247

CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE STATE VISIT OF HIS EXCELLENCY HUSSAIN MUHAMMAD ERSHAD, PRESIDENT OF THE PEOPLE’S REPUBLIC OF BANGLADESH

There is hereby created a National. Committee to take charge of the arrangements for the State Visit of His Excellency Hussain Muhammad Ershad, President of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh, to be composed of the following:

The Honorable Raul S. Manglapus Secretary of Foreign Affairs

–

Chairman

The Honorable Fidel V. Ramos Secretary of National Defense

–

Co-Chairman

The Honorable Manuel T. Yan Undersecretary of Foreign Affairs

–

Member

The Honorable Jose F. Mabanta Undersecretary of Public Works & Highways

–

Member

The Honorable Felicia.no M. Gacis, Jr. Undersecretary of National Defense

–

Member

The Honorable.Victor M: Ordoñez Undersecretary of Education, Culture and Sports

–

Member

The Honorable Raul Boncan Undersecretary of Trade and Industry

–

member

The Honorable Narzalina Z. Lim Undersecretary of Tourism

–

Member

The Honorable Jesus C. Beringuela Undersecretary of Budget & Management

–

Member

The Honorable Felix B. Bautista Press Undersecretary

–

Member

The Honorable Sergio A. Barrera Chief of Presidential Protocol

–

Member

The Honorable Clemencio F. Montesa Director-General for Protocol Department of Foreign Affair

–

Member

The Committee shall be responsible for the planning, coordination, and smooth execution of all arrangements to be made in connection with the State Visit.

The Committee is authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or any instrumentality of the government, including government-owned and controlled corporations,- for such assistance as it may need in discharging its duties and functions. The Committee is likewise authorized to create*sub-committee as necessary.

This accordingly amends Memorandum Order No. 244 dated July 14, 1989 regarding this same matter.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, August 17, 1989
