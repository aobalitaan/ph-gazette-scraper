Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 162-B

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 162, AS AMENDED, CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE OFFICIAL VISIT OF HIS EXCELLENCY FELIPE GONZALEZ, PRESIDENT OF THE GOVERNMENT OF SPAIN.

Memorandum Order No. 162 dated March 16, 1988, as amended, creating a National Committee to take charge of the official visit of His Excellency Felipe Gonzalez, President of the Government of Spain, is hereby further amended to include Hon. Rafael Perez de Tagle, Deputy Press Secretary, as Member of the Committee vice Hon. Danilo D. Gozo.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, April 20, 1988
