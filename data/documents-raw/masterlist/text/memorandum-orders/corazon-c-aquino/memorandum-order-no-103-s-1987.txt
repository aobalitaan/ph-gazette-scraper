Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 103

CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE STATE VISIT OF PRESIDENT VIRGILIO BARCO VARGAS OF COLOMBIA.

There is hereby created a National Committee to take charge of the arrangements connected with the State visit of President Virgilio Barco Vargas of Colombia:

The Committee shall be responsible for the planning, coordination, and smooth execution of all arrangements to be made in connection with the State visit.

The Committee is authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or any instrumentality of the government, including government-owned and controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in discharging its duties and functions. The Committee is likewise authorized to create sub-committees as necessary.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
