Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 310

Pursuant to the CSC Memorandum Circular No. 33, enjoining all heads of government bureaus, agencies, regional branches and offices, including government-owned and controlled corporations, to call for an employee assembly to discuss issues and problems affecting their employment conditions, the training staff of the Personnel Office is hereby authorized to convene for the purpose a representative group of employees of this Office coming from all our primary units on any appropriate date but not later than August 20, 1990, at a suitable place, if the briefing room will not be available.

All unit heads are hereby enjoined to send to this assembly their respective representatives belonging to level 1 and level 2 of not more than five (5) employees from each level.

(Sgd.) MARIANO SARMIENTO II Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, July 31, 1990
