MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 263

PROVIDING THE LIST OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO BE MONITORED BY THE GOVERNMENT CORPORATE MONITORING AND COORDINATING COMMITTEE (GCMCC) FOR CALENDAR YEAR 1990

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 236, dated July 22, 1987, “Strengthening the Government Corporate Monitoring and Coordinating Committee (GCMCC) and For Other Purposes,” the following government-owned and/or controlled corporations (GOCCs), including their subsidiaries are hereby approved, as recommended, to be monitored by the GCMCC in Calendar Year 1990:

1. Export Processing Zone Authority 2. Home Insurance and Guarantee Corporation 3. Home Mutual Development Fund 4. Light Rail Transit Authority 5. Local Water Utilities Administration 6. Manila International Airport Authority 7. Metro Manila Transit Corporation 8. Metropolitan Waterworks and Sewerage System 9. National Development Company 10. National Electrification Administration 11. National Food Authority 12. National Home Mortgage Finance Corporation 13. National Irrigation Administration 14. National Housing Authority 15. National Power Corporation 16. Philippine Airlines, Inc. 17. Philippine Deposit Insurance Corp. 18. Philippine Export and Foreign Loan Guarantee Corporation (PHILGUARANTEE) 19. Philippine Fisheries Development Authority 20. Philippine National Oil Company 21. Philippine National Railways 22. Philippine Ports Authority 23. Philippine Retirement Authority 24. Philippine Tourism Authority 25. Public Estates Authority

GOCCs which have subsidiaries shall submit to the GCMCC consolidated statements and reports. The GCMCC, however, is empowered to look into the individual statements and reports of subsidiary GOCCs if warranted by the circumstances.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

Manila, November 16, 1989
