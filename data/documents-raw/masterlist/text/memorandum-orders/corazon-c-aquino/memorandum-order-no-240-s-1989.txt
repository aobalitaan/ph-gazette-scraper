Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 240

CONSTITUTING A TASK FORCE TO ADDRESS WOMEN’S CONCERNS IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (PROPER)

Pursuant to the provisions of Executive Order No. 348, “Approving and Adopting The Philippine Development Plan For Women For 1989 To 1992”, a Task Force is hereby constituted in the Office of the President (Proper) to be composed of a coordinator and three representatives each from the rank-and-file, middle management, and senior management, to be designated by the Deputy Executive Secretary.

The Task Force shall act as the focal point for women’s concerns in the Office of the President (Proper) and shall perform the functions pertaining to such focal point as set forth in Executive Order No. 348 and (National Commission on the Role of Filipino Women) Memorandum Order No. 89-1, “Implementing Guidelines for the Philippine Development Plan For Women (PDPW)”.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) MAGDANGAL B. ELMA Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, June 3, 1989
