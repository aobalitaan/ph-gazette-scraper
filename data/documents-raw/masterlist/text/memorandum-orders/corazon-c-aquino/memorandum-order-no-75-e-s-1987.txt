Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 75-E

GRANTING SALARY INCREASES FOR POSITIONS IN THE MOVIE AND TELEVISION REVIEW AND CLASSIFICATION BOARD (MTRCB) AND THE VIDEOGRAM REGULATORY BOARD (VRB)

The increase in salaries of Career Executive Service positions by 5% and the salaries of the rank and file positions by 30% for the Movie and Television Review and Classification Board and the Videogram Regulatory Board is hereby authorized effective April 1, 1986.

The Secretary of the Budget shall issue the necessary rules and regulations to implement this Memorandum Order.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, March 26, 1987
