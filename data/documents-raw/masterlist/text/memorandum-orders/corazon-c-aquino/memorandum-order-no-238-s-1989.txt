Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 238

DIRECTING ALL AGENCIES OF THE GOVERNMENT IMPLEMENTING INFRASTRUCTURE PROJECTS TO POST DETAILS OF NATIONALLY-FUNDED PROJECTS AT CONSPICUOUS PLACES

To ensure effective monitoring and the timely adoption of remedial steps against delays and sub-standard implementation of infrastructure projects, all concerned agencies of the government implementing nationally-funded infrastructure projects are hereby directed to post in a billboard details of the said projects at conspicuous places.

For purposes of uniformity in the implementation and enforcement of this Memorandum Order, all government agencies concerned are hereby enjoined to adopt to the extent applicable, the provisions on billboards of Memorandum Order No. 175 dated May 23, 1988.

The implementation of local government projects funded from national government funds shall continue to be governed by Memorandum Order No. 175 dated May 23, 1988 and Memorandum Order No. 175-A dated May 8, 1989.

Strict compliance with this Memorandum Order is hereby enjoined.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, May 29, 1989
