Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 212

REQUIRING HEADS OF OFFICES LOCATED WITHIN THE MALACANANG COMPLEX TO COMPLY WITH THE SECURITY PROCEDURES BEING OBSERVED IN THE MALACANANG PALACE AREA.

To enable the PSG to effectively enforce security measures in the area, all heads of offices located within the Malacanang Complex are hereby required to comply with the security procedures being observed by notifying or informing the PSG in advance of their planned activities, particularly on the entry of invited guests during night-time.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

Manila, December 27, 1988.

(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
