Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 51-A

LIFTING THE CLEARANCE REQUIREMENT UNDER EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 1016, S. OF 1985, AS AMENDED BY MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 51, S. OF 1986, FOR THE EXPORT OF AIRCRAFT PARTS AND COMPONENTS THEREOF

In order to simplify further the rules and regulations on the processing of requests for clearance to export controlled products, the clearance requirement under Executive Order No. 1016, S. of 1985, as amended by Memorandum Order No. 51, S. of 1986, for the export of aircraft parts and the components thereof is hereby lifted.

Accordingly, the authority of the Department of Transportation and Communications under Memorandum Order No. 51, S. of 1986, shall cover only whole aircraft which have been previously imported into the country and those locally manufactured or assembled by local aviation firms and enterprises registered with the Board of Investments, Department of Trade and Industry.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, April 26, 1990
