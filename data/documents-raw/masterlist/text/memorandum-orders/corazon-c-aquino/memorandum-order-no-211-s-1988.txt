MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 211

DIRECTING THE NAIA MANAGEMENT TO PROVIDE A PLACE OR SPECIFIC AREA IN ITS PREMISES FOR THE USE AND PRESENCE OF MEDIA

The NAIA Management is hereby directed to provide a place or specific area in its premises for the use and presence of media.

Duly accredited members of the media shall have access and be restricted to such media room or area, unless they are actually engaged in the coverage of specific events.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

December 20, 1988

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
