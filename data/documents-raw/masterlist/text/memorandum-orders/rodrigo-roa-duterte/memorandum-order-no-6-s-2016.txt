MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. _06

 

RECONSTITUTING THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT
CULTURAL AFFAIRS COMMITTEE

The OP Cultural Affairs Committee (OPCAC), created under Special Order
No. 522, s. 1999, as amended, is hereby further reconstituted as follows:

Chairperson - Deputy Executive Secretary for Finance and Administration
Vice-Chairperson - Social Secretary
Members - Representative from the Office of the Executive Secretar

- Head, HRMO

- Head, Finance Office

- Head, Engineering Office

- Head, Malacafiang Records Office

- Head, Malacafiang Motor Pool

- Head, Assets Management Office

- Head, Protocol Office

- Head, internal House Affairs Office

- Head, Presidential Museum and Library

- President, Office of the President Employees’ Network

OPCAC shail still continue to perform its mandate as provided under $.O. No.
522, s. 1999.

The Human Resource Management Office shail serve as secretariat to the above-
mentioned Committee.

By authority of the President:

me

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary

Manila, 13 October 2016
