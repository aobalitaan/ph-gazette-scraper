MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 115

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO INVESTIGATE THE CHARGES OF MR. JESUS ALMARIO AGAINST MR. REGINALD VELASCO, FSO I

A Committee is hereby created to investigate the administrative charges filed by Mr. Jesus Almario against Mr. Reginald Velasco, FSO I, to be composed of –

Chief Presidential Legal Counsel or his duly designated representative as Chairman;

Secretary of Foreign Affairs or his duly designated representative as member; and

Assistant Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs or his duly designated representative as member.

The Committee shall have all the powers and prerogatives of an investigating committee under the Administrative Code of 1987 including the power to summon witnesses, administer oath or take testimony or evidence relevant to the investigation by subpoena ad testificandum and subpoena duces tecum .

The Committee is hereby empowered to call upon and secure the assistance of any department, office, agency, or instrumentality of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporation in the performance of its duties and discharge of its functions.

The Committee shall convene immediately, conduct the investigation in the most expeditious manner and terminate the same as soon as practicable from its first scheduled date of hearing.

The Committee shall submit its report and recommendation to the Executive Secretary as soon as practicable from the time the case is submitted for decision.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of April in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Three.

By authority of the President (Sgd.) ANTONIO T. CARPIO Chief Presidential Legal Counsel
