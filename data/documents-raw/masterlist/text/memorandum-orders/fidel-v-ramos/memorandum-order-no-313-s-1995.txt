MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 313

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 96 ENTITLED “REORGANIZING THE COOPERATIVE BANKING COMMITTEE”

Memorandum Order No. 96, dated February 27, 1993, entitled “Reorganizing the Cooperative Banking Committee” is hereby amended so as to include the following as members of the Committee:

President of the Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation (PDIC);

Secretary of the Department of Finance (DOF).

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12 th day of October in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Five.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
