MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 219

FURTHER AMENDING SEC. 7 OF MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 179 DATED NOVEMBER 7, 1993.

SEC. 7 of MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 179, series of 1993, creating a special committee for the conversion of non-immigrant visas (indefinite) under Section 47, paragraph (A), sub-paragraph (2) of the Immigration Act, is hereby further amended so as to extend the filing of application under the Order from June 30, 1994 to October 31, 1994.

It is understood that only children who have reached the age of 21 but are suffering from physical or mental infirmity shall be allowed to benefit from the application of their parents.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 20 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
