MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 379

DIRECTING THE FORMATION OF A HOST COUNCIL IN PREPARATION FOR HOLDING OF APEC MEETINGS IN DAVAO CITY

WHEREAS, the National Organizing Commission for the Asia Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC-NOC) 1996 was created by virtue of Administrative Order No. 160, s. of 1994, to take charge of the administrative preparations for the Philippine hosting of APEC Meetings in 1996;

WHEREAS, the Philippine hosting of APEC 1996 is a national effort and all agencies of government, officials, agents, individuals from the public and private sector and non-government organizations may be called upon to provide assistance to the APEC-NOC in this national endeavor;

WHEREAS, the APEC-NOC Executive Committee (APEC-NOC-EC), the APEC-NOC’s operating arm, performs its day to day activities through its designated Director General;

WHEREAS, the Senior Officials Meeting (SOM III) has selected Davao City as the venue of its August 15–23, 1996 meetings to demonstrate to the officials of the APEC economies the on-going countryside economic development and the potentials for further progress of the BIMP/EAGA area;

WHEREAS, there is a need to organize a Host Council in Davao City to assist the APEC-NOC for the successful preparation of SOM III.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order the organization of a Host Council in Davao City to assist the APEC-NOC for the successful preparation of SOM III.

SEC. 2. Functions of the Host Council. – In pursuit of its objectives, the Davao APEC Host Council shall have the following authorities and functions:

(1) Assist the APEC-NOC-EC in the preparation of the hosting of SOM III;

(2) Develop and implement its own workplan in coordination with APEC-NOC-EC to achieve its mandated functions;

(3) Appoint the head and members of the various committees;

(4) Mobilize private sector participation and support to the hosting of APEC Meetings in Davao City; and

(5) Promote the trade and tourism potentials of Davao and the nearby provinces to the delegates of APEC.

SEC. 3. Effectivity. This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 1 st day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Six.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
