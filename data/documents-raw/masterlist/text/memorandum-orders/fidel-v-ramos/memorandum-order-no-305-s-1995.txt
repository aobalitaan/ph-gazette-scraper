MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 305

CREATING A SELECTION COMMITTEE TO SCREEN NOMINEES TO THE ZAMBOANGA CITY SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE AUTHORITY

There is hereby created a Selection Committee to screen and process nominees for the Zamboanga City Special Economic Zone (ZAMBOECOZONE) Authority to be composed of the following:

The Selection Committee shall adopt a selection process and criteria to carry out its objectives.

Technical and Secretariat Support shall be provided by the Mindanao Economic Development Council.

The Special Committee shall also recommend to the President the budgetary allocation necessary for the initial operation of RA 7903 and identify possible sources thereof.

The Task Force is hereby given 60 days to submit its recommendations to the President.

This Memorandum shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 18 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Five.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
