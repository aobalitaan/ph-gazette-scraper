MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 128

DIRECTING THE CONTINUED EFFECTIVITY OF MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 73, DATED JANUARY 4, 1993

The Officials designated in Memorandum Order No. 73, dated January 4, 1993, to act on certain matters and sign documents “By authority of the President” are, in addition to the Executive Secretary, hereby authorized to continue discharging their respective functions as provided under the said Order until otherwise revoked.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 2 d day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Three.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) ANTONIO T. CARPIO Chief Presidential Legal Counsel
