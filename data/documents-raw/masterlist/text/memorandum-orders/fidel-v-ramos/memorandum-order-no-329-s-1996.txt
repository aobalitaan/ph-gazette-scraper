Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 329

CREATING AN OP MANAGEMENT REVIEW COMMITTEE

To further enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of the various operating units of the Office of the President and in the interest of the service, there is hereby created an OP Management Review Committee consisting of the following:

The said Committee shall have the following functions:

1 Review the organizational structure of the OP and update the proposed reorganization plan not later than 15 March 1996.

2. Clarify areas of coordination and reporting system among all OP operating units, including Presidential Assistants System and the Presidential Management Staff.

For the information and guidance of all concerned.

BY AUTHORITY OF THE PRESIDENT: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

Manila, January 11, 1996
