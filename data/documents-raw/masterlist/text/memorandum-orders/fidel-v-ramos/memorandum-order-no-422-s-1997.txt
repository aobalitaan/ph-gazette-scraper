Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 422

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 388, DATED JULY 15, 1996

Memorandum Order No. 388, dated July 15, 1996, is hereby amended, so as to authorize Ms. EDWINA D. DAQUIUAG , Presidential Staff Officer VI, and Chief of the Career and Personnel Development Division, Personnel Office, to disburse the amount of TEN THOUSAND PESOS ( P10,000.00 ), representing payment of P6,000.00 for the instructor of Hataw exercises and P4,000.00 for incidental expenses to be incurred by the participants of Hataw III, chargeable against the funds of the Sports and Physical Fitness Committee, subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

(Sgd.) LUIS C. LIWANANG II Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, March 14, 1997.
