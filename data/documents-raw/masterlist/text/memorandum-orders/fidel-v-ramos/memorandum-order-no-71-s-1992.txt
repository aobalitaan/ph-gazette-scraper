Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 71

RECONSTITUTING THE SELECTION BOARD IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

The Selection Board, Office of the President, constituted under Memorandum Order No. 408, S. 1991, is hereby reconstituted pursuant to LOI 799, dated January 29, 1979, as follows:

The Personnel Office shall provide secretarial services to the Board.

As herein reconstituted, the Selection Board shall perform the same functions as defined in the aforementioned LOI.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDELMIRO A. AMANTE, SR. Executive Secretary

Manila, December 22, 1992
