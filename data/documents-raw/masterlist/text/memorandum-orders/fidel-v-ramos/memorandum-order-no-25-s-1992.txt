Office of the President of the Philippines Malacanang

6 August 1992

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 25

ALL UNITS IN THE OP PROPER

In connection with the Flag Raising Ceremony being held every Monday at eight o’clock in the morning in front of the Kalayaan Building, please be guided by the following sequence of activities as desired by the President:

1. Pambansang Awit 2. Panunumpa sa Watawat 3. Message by a government official on current national issues 4. PFVR’s Message 5. Pilipinas Kong Mahal
