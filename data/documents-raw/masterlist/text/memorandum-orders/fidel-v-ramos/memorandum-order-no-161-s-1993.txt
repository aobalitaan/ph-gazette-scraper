Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 161

FURTHER AMENDING THE GUIDELINES ON OFFICE UNIFORMS IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

Memorandum Order No. 127 dated May 3, 1993 as amended is hereby amended further so as to schedule the wearing of the new deep salmon outfit to start officially on August 24, 1993 and every Tuesday thereafter.

Observance of the provisions of the Memorandum Circular of the Civil Service Commission dated April 23, 1991, prescribing the Dress Code for all government officials and employees is hereby reiterated. Particularly mentioned here is the prohibition of tight-fitting, seductive, micromini dresses, walking shorts, tight pants, and the wearing of slippers in the lobbies and corridors.

The disciplinary action stipulated for violations of the Dress Code shall take effect on March 25, 1993.

Please be guided accordingly.

(Sgd.) LEONARDO A. QUISUMBING Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, 18 August 1993
