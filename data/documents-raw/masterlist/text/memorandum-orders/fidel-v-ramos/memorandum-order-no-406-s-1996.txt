MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 406

DESIGNATING THE SECRETARY OF JUSTICE AS THE ACTING CHAIRMAN OF THE DANGEROUS DRUGS BOARD AND THE SECRETARY OF HEALTH OR HER REPRESENTATIVE AS VICE CHAIR THEREOF

WHEREAS, there is an acknowledged need for stricter law enforcement, prosecution and apprehension of all drug traffickers, whether national or international;

WHEREAS, the Philippines continues to be one of the major transshipment points for international drug syndicates due to its easily penetrable airports, seaports and long irregular coastline which provide ideal entry- and exit points for drug smuggling;

WHEREAS, there is an urgent need to sharpen the tools that the government can use to finally eradicate the drug menace.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 17, Chapter 5, Title I, Book III of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby designate the Secretary of Justice as the Acting Chairman of the Dangerous Drugs Board. The Secretary of Health or her representative shall be the Vice-Chair thereof.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Six.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
