Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 274

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 272, DATED MARCH 21, 1995, AND ENJOINING STRICT COMPLIANCE OF ALL UNITS WITH THE HEREIN PRESCRIBED WORK SCHEDULES

Memorandum Order No. 272 dated, March 21, 1995 is hereby amended to reinstitute the work schedule adopted under Memorandum Order No. 256, dated February 6, 1995, with some modifications, as follows:

1. The following frontline offices shall observe a 24 hours schedule:

Office of the Sr. Dep. Exec. Sec. Office of the President Coordinating Center (OPCC) Presidential Action Center (PACE) Authentication Unit (with 1 Collection Ofcr., Cash Div.) Malacañang Clinic Administrative Office

with their personnel being apportioned at the time slots listed below:

Mondays to Fridays 7AM – 4PM 8AM – 5PM 9AM – 6PM 10AM – 7PM 7PM – 7AM Saturdays 7AM – 4PM 10AM – 7PM

All the other offices not listed above shall observe a 12-hour schedule daily (Monday to Friday), and maintain a skeletal force on Saturdays.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEONARDO A. QUISUMBING Sr. Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, March 29, 1995
