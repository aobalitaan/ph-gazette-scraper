Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 254

Effective as of this date, overtime services at the National Program For Unification and the Development Council (NPUDC) and total overtime payments for its employees shall be governed by the provisions of Memorandum Order No. 152, dated July 26, 1993.

All existing authorities issued to NPUDC employees to render overtime are hereby revoked. Henceforth, request for new authority shall be indorsed to the Office of the President Proper, through its Finance Office, by the Head of the Secretariat (NPUDC), in accordance with the requirements indicated in Memorandum Order No. 152.

Strict compliance is hereby enjoined.

Manila, January 27, 1995

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEONARDO A. QUISUMBING Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
