Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 147

AUTHORIZING THE CONDUCT OF ORIENTATION SEMINAR ON THE IMPLEMENTATION OF THE DRESS CODE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

Pursuant to Memorandum Order No. 127, dated May 3, 1993 of the Office of the President, implementing Memorandum Circular No. 14, s. 1991 the Civil Service Commission (Dress Code) the attendance of representatives of our lady employees from the different offices and major units in the Office of the President in the Orientation Seminar on July 14-16, 1993 at 1:30-3:30 P.M. at the Briefing Room is hereby authorized.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEONARDO A. QUISUMBING Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila July 14, 1993
