Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 412

CREATING A TECHNICAL WORKING GROUP TO STUDY AND SUBMIT RECOMMENDATIONS ON THE PROPOSAL OF THE BODONG DEVELOPMENT COOPERATIVE, INC. TO PRIVATIZE, REHABILITATE, REOPEN AND DEVELOP THE BATONG BUHAY GOLD MINES, INC.

In line with the government’s privatization program, there is hereby created a Technical Working Group (TWG) to study and facilitate the proposal of the Bodong Development Cooperative, Inc. (BDCI) to privatize, rehabilitate, reopen and develop the Batong Buhay Gold Mines, Inc. (BBGMI) in Pasil, Kalinga.

The TWG shall be composed of a representative from the Office of the Executive Secretary, as Chairman; two (2) representatives from the BDCI, who will be designated by its President; and two (2) representatives from the Asset Privatization Trust, to be designated by its Chief Executive Trustee, as members.

The TWG is hereby directed to submit its report and recommendations to the President, through the Office of the Executive Secretary, within three (3) months from the effectivity of this Memorandum.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

Manila, January 9, 1997
