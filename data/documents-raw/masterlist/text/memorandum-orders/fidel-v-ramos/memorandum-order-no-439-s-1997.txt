Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 439

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE TOURISM AUTHORITY TO SPONSOR THE HOLDING OF THE 4TH PHILIPPINE INTERNATIONAL KARTING PRIX AND ENJOINING THE SUPPORT OF ALL CONCERNED SECTORS

WHEREAS, the 4th Philippine International Karting Prix will be staged this year;

WHEREAS, this prestigious event will project a positive image of the Philippines and help attract more tourists into the country;

IN VIEW THEREOF, the Department of Tourism through its instrumentality the Philippine Tourism Authority is hereby authorized to sponsor and support the holding of the 4th Philippine International Karting Prix.

Concerned offices and agencies of the government, as well as concerned sectors, are hereby enjoined likewise to extend the necessary cooperation and assistance to ensure the success of the event.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

Manila 7 August 1997
