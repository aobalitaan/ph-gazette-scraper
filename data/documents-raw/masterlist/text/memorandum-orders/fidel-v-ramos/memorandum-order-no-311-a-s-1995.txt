MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 311-A

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 311, CREATING A TASK FORCE ON NATIONAL FOOD SECURITY UNDER THE LEGISLATIVE-EXECUTIVE DEVELOPMENT ADVISORY COUNCIL (LEDAC)

Memorandum Order No. 311, dated 6 October 1995, Creating a Task Force on National Food Security Under the Legislative-Executive Development Advisory Council (LEDAC) is hereby amended insofar as the membership thereof is concerned, as follows:

The Task Force shall immediately convene to conduct a thorough review of the National Food Logistics System and submit its recommendations not later than November 15, 1995.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Five.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
