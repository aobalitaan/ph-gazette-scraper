Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING OF WEEK-LONG ACTIVITIES IN THE OBSERVANCE OF THE 94TH ANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION OF THE PHILIPPINE CIVIL SERVICE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

Pursuant to CSC Memorandum Circular No. 24, s. 1994, enjoining all officials and employees of the different departments, bureaus, and agencies of the national and local governments, including government-owned and controlled corporations, state universities and colleges in all celebrations in the observance of the Philippine Civil Service’s 94th Anniversary, the holding of week-long activities in OP, as well as the payment of expenses to be incurred in connection :herewith, chargeable against appropriate funds from the OP (Proper) and PMS, is hereby authorized.

The celebration focuses on the theme: “MAMAMAYAN MUNA, HINDI MAMAYA NA” to underscore the government-wide campaign for prompt, courteous, efficient and responsive public service, designed to heighten the role of citizens in government. The schedule of activities is as follows:

Ms. Clarita F. Rodulfo, Moral Recovery Officer for OP (Proper), is designated to coordinate with the OP-MRP Steering Committee, the SIAC. PMS, and with the CSC Field Office and the Presidential Security Group in Malacanang for the conduct of said activities.

(Sgd.) LEONARDO A. QUISUMBING Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

August 30, 1994
