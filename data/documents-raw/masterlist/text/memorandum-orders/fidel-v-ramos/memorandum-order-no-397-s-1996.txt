MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 397

CREATING A SPECIAL MONITORING GROUP TO OVERSEE THAT THE GRP- MNLF PEACE AGREEMENT IS SUSTAINED

In order to attain sustainable peace agreement in Mindanao, there is hereby created a Special Monitoring Group to conduct the following:

a. Identify all ranking MNLF Officers and Field Commanders involved in the Peace Process and categorize them into combatants and non-combatants;

b. Compile their individual profiles taking into account the size of their respective armed following; and

c. Tap qualified key officials and/or members of the MNLF for appointment to government posts.
