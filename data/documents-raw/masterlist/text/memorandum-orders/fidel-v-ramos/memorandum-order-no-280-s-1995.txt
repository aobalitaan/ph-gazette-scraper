MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 280

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 267 DATED MARCH 7, 1995 CONSTITUTING AN AD-HOC COMMITTEE FOR THE CONVERSION OF THE PHILIPPINE REFUGEE PROCESSING CENTER COMPLEX BY ADDING TO SECTION 1 THEREOF, THE GOVERNOR, PROVINCE OF BATAAN AND THE MAYOR, MUNICIPALITY OF MORONG, BATAAN, AS MEMBERS

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Section 1 of Memorandum Order No. 267 dated March 7, 1995, to read as follows:

“Section 1. Composition — There is hereby constituted an ad hoc inter-agency committee to oversee the Conversion of the PRPC Complex (Committee) to be composed of the Secretary of the Interior and Local Government as Chairman, and the following as members:

The government officials hereinabove enumerated shall serve in an “ex-officio” capacity and may be represented by their duly designated representatives. Rep. Felicito Payumo, who has distinguished himself in the field of economics, shall serve as the Technical Adviser of the Committee. The Presidential Management Staff shall provide technical and administrative support to the Committee.”

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Five.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
