Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 398

REQUIRING MEMBERS OF THE CABINET AND HEADS OF OFFICES INCLUDING GOVERNMENT OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, TO GIVE PRIORITY TO ATTENDING CONGRESSIONAL AND COURT HEARINGS

Under Memorandum Order No. 112 dated September 29, 1987, this Office adopted guidelines on appearances of Cabinet members during congressional hearings/inquiries.

In this connection, all Cabinet members and heads of offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby reminded to give priority to attending Congressional hearings pursuant to the said Order.

They are likewise required, in the event that they cannot attend such hearings, to send their representatives not lower than undersecretary level.

The same policy shall be observed by all concerned with respect to court hearings.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

Manila, 24 September 1996
