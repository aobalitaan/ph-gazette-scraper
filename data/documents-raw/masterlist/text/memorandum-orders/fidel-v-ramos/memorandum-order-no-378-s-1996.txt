Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 378

AUTHORIZING THE CONDUCT OF INFORMATION DRIVE ON GSIS AND PAG-IBIG MEMBERSHIP, BENEFITS AND PRIVILEGES AND HEALTHY LIVING TIPS FOR OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

In view of the need for officials and employees of this Office to understand and to raise awareness of their membership, benefits, as well as their privileges, as members of the Government Service Insurance System and the PAG-IBIG Home Development Mutual Fund; and to know the basic and vital tips on healthy living for career men and women, the Career and Personnel Development Division, Personnel Office, will be conducting the above-mentioned information drive on June 25, 26, and 27, 1996 at the third floor, MIS Training Room, Old GSIS Building, Arroceros, Manila.

In this connection, please submit the name/s of your staff who are members of the said entities and who are interested to participate in the said information drive to the CPDD, Personnel Office, not later than June 21, 1996.

(Sgd.) LUIS C. LIWANAG II Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, June 18, 1996
