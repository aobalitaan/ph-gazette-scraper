Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 440

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 429, DATED APRIL 30, 1997.

Memorandum Order No. 429, dated April 30, 1997, is hereby amended to authorize the conduct of the third in the three Series of the Course on Attitude and Courtesy Reinforcement Through Personality Development on August 25-29, 1997.

The amount of Thirty Four Thousand Five Hundred Pesos (P34,500.00) is hereby authorized to be disbursed from the allocation for training for the purpose.

All Chiefs of Primary Offices/Units are hereby enjoined to support the said course and submit the names of their nominees to the CPDD, Personnel Office, not later than August 20, 1997.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LUIS C. LIWANAG II Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, August 8 1997
