Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 167

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 413, CREATING COMMITTEE TO OVERSEE THE CONSTRUCTION OF THE PEOPLE POWER MONUMENT IN EDSA

Memorandum Order No. 413, dated 10 January 1993, creating a Committee to oversee the constructions of the People Power Monument in EDSA is hereby amended insofar as the membership thereof is concerned as follows:

–

–

–

–

–

The Committee shall immediately convene to assess the status of the project and submit reports and recommendations as may be necessary to the Executive Secretary for their immediate implementation.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, September 22 1993
