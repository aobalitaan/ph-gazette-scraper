Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 430

CONSTITUTING A COMMITTEE TO SUPPORT THE PHILIPPINE CANDIDATURE FOR THE INTERNATIONAL LABOR ORGANIZATION DIRECTOR-GENERAL POST

There is hereby constituted a Committee to support the Philippine candidature for the International Labor Organization (ILO) Director-General Post to be headed by a representative from the Office of the Executive Secretary, with a rank of Undersecretary, as Chairman, with the following as members:

The Undersecretary of Labor and Employment

The Undersecretary of Foreign Affairs

The Committee shall be tasked to undertake and coordinate all efforts for the successful election of the Philippine’s candidate to the ILO Director General post.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

Manila, 21 May 1997
