MEMORANDUM ORDER 45

DIRECTING THE IMPLEMENTATION OF A NATIONAL GOVERNMENT VOLUNTEER PROGRAM

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 164 series 1964, as amended by Executive Order No. 635, series 1980, creating the Philippine National Volunteer Service Coordinating Agency (PNVSCA), and mandating it to organize a national volunteer service program, the PNVSCA is hereby directed to:

The sum of P100,000 from the President’s Social Fund shall be released to the PNVSCA as augmentation fund for disbursement to activities related to the Program.

Submit a report on the status of your compliance with the above instructions, through the Office of the Executive Secretary, copy furnished the Presidential Management Staff, within 30 days from receipt hereof.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

Manila, 7 December 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:

Executive Secretary
