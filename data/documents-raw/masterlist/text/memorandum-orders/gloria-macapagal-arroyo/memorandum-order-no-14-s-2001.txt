MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 14

In the interest of the service and in line with Executive Order No. 262 with the Approved Implementing Rules and Regulations, the Bids and Awards Committee in the Office of the President is hereby reconstituted with the following composition:

The said Committee shall have jurisdiction over government contracts for services, infrastructure and other construction projects, including labor and materials incidental thereto and the procurement of goods and supplies for both project-related and normal/regular operation and maintenance activities of the Office of the President. Likewise, it shall be responsible for the determination of eligibility, conduct of bidding, evaluation of bids, postqualification of the lowest calculated bid and recommending award of contracts.

The Bids and Awards Committee as herein reconstituted shall convene immediately to take up pending matters particularly those requiring public bidding.

This Order which supersedes Special Order No. 87-01 shall take effect immediately.

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, 25 April 2001
