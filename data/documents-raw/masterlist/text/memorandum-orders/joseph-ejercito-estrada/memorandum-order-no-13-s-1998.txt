MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

Memorandum Order No. 13

REDESIGNATING THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENTIAL CONSULTANT ON MILITARY AFFAIRS AS THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENTIAL ADVISER ON MILITARY AFFAIRS

In order to simplify and give emphasis on the role of the military adviser inherent to his mandate as an in-house assistant to the President and Commander-In-Chief, Armed Forces of the Philippines, the Office of the Presidential Consultant on Military Affairs (OPCMA) is hereby redesignated as Office of the Presidential Adviser on Military Affairs (OPAMA).

This Memorandum shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of August, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
