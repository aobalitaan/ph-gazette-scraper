Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

September 10, 1998

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 20

SUBMISSION OF ACCOMPLISHMENT REPORT OF ALL LOCALLY-FUNDED PROJECTS UNDER THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (PROPER)

In connection with the ongoing subcommittee deliberations by the House Committee on Appropriations and the Senate Committee on Finance on the proposed 1999 budget of the Office of the President, all locally-funded projects under the Office of the President are hereby directed to submit to the Finance Office on or before September 25, 1998, a copy of available accomplishment reports of your office/project since its creation.

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
