MALACAÑANG Manila

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 29

DIRECTING THE CONSOLIDATION OF ALL REAL PROPERTY REGISTERED IN THE NAME OF NATIONAL GOVERNMENT OFFICES AND AGENCIES, EXCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, IN THE NAME OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

In order to implement a rational policy of management of land resources of the government and to allow for a maximum utilization of these resources, all national government agencies and offices, excluding government-owned and/or controlled corporations and local government units, are hereby directed to submit to the President, through the Executive Secretary, a list of all real properties in their name for consolidation in the name of the Government of the Republic of the Philippines.

The Land Registration Authority is likewise directed to identify from their records and submit a list of all real properties owned and registered in the name of all national government offices and agencies, excluding government-owned and controlled corporations and local governments, to the President through the Executive Secretary.

A complete report shall be submitted within fifteen (15) days from issuance of this Order.

For strict and immediate compliance.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
