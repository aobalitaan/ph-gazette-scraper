Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 103

PLACING THE OFFICE OF BUDGET AND CORPORATE AFFAIRS UNDER THE SUPERVISION OF THE DEPUTY EXECUTIVE SECRETARY

In the interest of the service and to promote proper coordination of work and facilitate the flow of documents in the Office of the Executive Secretary, the Budget and Corporate Affairs (OBCA) is hereby placed under the direct supervision of Deputy Executive Secretary Luis C. Liwanag.

Accordingly, all papers being processed by OBCA should now be submitted to DES Liwanag.

Papers to be signed “By authority of the President” should, however, continue to be prepared for signature of the Senior Deputy Executive Secretary.

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

19 May 2000
