Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 71

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING OF ACTIVITIES IN OBSERVANCE OF THE 99 TH ANNIVERSARY OF THE PHILIPPINE CIVIL SERVICE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

The holding of Program of Activities in the Office of the President is hereby authorized, pursuant to Civil Service Commission Circular No. 10, s. 1999, enjoining all heads of government agencies of the national and local governments, including government-owned and controlled corporations and state universities and colleges in the observance of the 99 th Anniversary of the Philippine Civil Service, with the theme “Serbisyo Sibil: Bagong Sigla sa Bagong Siglo.” Authorized, too, is the payment of moderate expenses to be incurred in connection therewith, chargeable against appropriate funds from the Office of the President and the Presidential Management Staff. Said activities will consist of the following:

The OP-PMS SIAC Secretariats and the OP Employee Welfare and Special Events Service (EWSES) will assist the hosts in connection with the holding of the above-states activities.

Full cooperation and support of all heads of primary offices and units are enjoined to ensure the success of the month-long celebration of the 99 th Philippine Civil Service in Malacañang.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

Manila, August 16, 1999.
