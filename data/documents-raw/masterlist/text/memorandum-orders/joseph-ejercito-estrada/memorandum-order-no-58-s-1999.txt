Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 58

AUTHORIZING OP PERSONNEL TO AVAIL OF CENTENNIAL LEAVE PRIVILEGE, PURSUANT TO ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 55 DATED FEBRUARY 25, 1999

Pursuant to Administrative Order No. 55 dated February 24, 1999, which amended Administrative Order No. 392, series of 1998, Office of the President personnel are hereby authorized to avail of a three-day Centennial Leave with pay at anytime from 6 June 1998 until 15 June 1999, on a continuous or staggered basis, to allow them and their families to take part in the “Expo Filipino Family Day” Program or to visit any of the Centennial Landmarks within Metro Manila or nearby Provinces.

Any official or employee who avails of the leave privilege shall be required to submit a report and/or evidence (tickets, pictures, etc.) to the Personnel Office to establish that he/she personally visited the Expo Filipino and/or any Centennial or Historical Landmarks.

For compliance.

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

March 31, 1999.
