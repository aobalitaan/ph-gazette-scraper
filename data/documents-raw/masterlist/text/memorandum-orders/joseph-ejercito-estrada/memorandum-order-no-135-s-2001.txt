MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 135

RECONSTITUTING THE PRESIDENT’S PERSONNEL GROUP

The President’s Personnel Group [PPG], which is primarily responsible for screening at the level of the Office of the President, all recommendees for Presidential appointment to government positions, is hereby reconstituted, as follows:

The Chairman shall provide the Staff who will continue to maintain the database system on Presidential appointees.

All provisions of existing circular and other issuances that are otherwise inconsistent with this Order are hereby modified and repealed accordingly.

This Order shall take effect immediately. JAN 16 2001

( Sgd .) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA
