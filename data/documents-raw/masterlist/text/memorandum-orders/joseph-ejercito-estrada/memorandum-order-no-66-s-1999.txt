Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 66

AUTHORIZING THE CONDUCT IN THIS OFFICE OF A MEMBERSHIP SEMINAR ON COOPERATIVES

Cooperatives have become an emergent profession in the country which have basically catered to the needs of the people. The Malacañang Employees Cooperative (MECOOP) has become partner of the Office of the President in responding to the needs of its employees and members.

In order for the officials and employees of the Office of the President to become fully aware of the benefits and services offered by cooperatives particularly the Malacañang Employees Cooperative, it is necessary that a Membership Seminar on Cooperatives be conducted in coordination with the MECOOP Board of Directors and Staff and the Career and Personnel Development Division, Personnel Office, starting on June 29, 1999 to July 2, 1999, at 1:00-5:00 P.M. at the 4 th Floor of the Mabini Hall (Social Hall).

All interested officials and employees of this Office are hereby enjoined to enlist with the CPDD, Personnel Office on or before June 25, 1999.

(Sgd.) RIC TAN LEGADA Deputy Executive Secretary on Admin & Finance

June 17, 1999.
