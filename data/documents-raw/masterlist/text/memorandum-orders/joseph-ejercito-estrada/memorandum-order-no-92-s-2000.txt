Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 92

Attendance at Flag Raising Ceremonies

Pursuant to Republic Act No. 6713, otherwise known as the Code of Conduct and Ethical Standards, all officials and employees of the Office of the President are hereby enjoined to attend the regular 8:00 a.m. Flag Raising Ceremonies held every Monday at Kalayaan Grounds, Malacañang.

Heads of offices are hereby advised to support the OP Cultural Affairs Committee (OPCAC) in ensuring the implementation of this Memorandum Circular, including the wearing of Filipiniana Attire during Flag Raising Ceremonies, pursuant to CSC Memorandum Circular No. 2, s. 1999, designating Mondays as “Araw ng Watawat at Katutubong Kasuotan.”

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

February 24, 2000.
