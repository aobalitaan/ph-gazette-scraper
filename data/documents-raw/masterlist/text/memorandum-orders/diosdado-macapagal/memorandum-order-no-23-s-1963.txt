MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 23

REQUIRING CHIEFS OF DIVISIONS OR PRIMARY UNITS TO SUBMIT FOR APPROVAL THE AFTERNOON DUTY SCHEDULE.

In connection with Memorandum Circular No. 21 of this Office of even date, prescribing the office hours for the afternoon duty enjoined under Executive Order No. 37, dated March 20, 1963 during the period from April 1 to June 15, 1963, chiefs of divisions and other primary units in this Office are hereby required to submit, for approval, the afternoon schedule of at least one-half of the personnel of their respective divisions or units not later than March 28, 1963.

Notwithstanding the duty schedule that may be approved, it is understood that the chief of division or primary unit may require any number of personnel in his division or unit to render afternoon duty and or overtime service when the exigencies of the service so demand.

A copy of the afternoon duty schedule, as approved, shall be furnished the Administrative Division.

SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Executive Secretary

Manila, March 22, 1963
