MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 79

CREATING A COMMITTEE FOR THE REVISION OF THE UNIFORM OF LADY EMPLOYEES IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

In connection with Memorandum Order No. 27 dated April 24, 1963, as amended, requiring lady employees in the Office of the President to be in uniform during attendance in office, there is hereby created a committee to study and propose a revised uniform in lieu of that prescribed in said Order. The committee shall be composed of the following:

Mrs. Milagros Pulido

Chairman

Member

Mrs. Angeles Pineda

Member

Mrs. Simeona San Juan

Member

Mrs. Consuelo Nazario

Member

The uniform to be adopted shall be simple and shall not be of costly material.

The committee shall submit its recommendation within two weeks from the date hereof.

By authority of the President:

CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, April 27, 1964
