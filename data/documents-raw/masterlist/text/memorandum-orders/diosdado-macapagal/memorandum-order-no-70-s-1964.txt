MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 70

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING OF AN IN-SERVICE TRAINING CLASS IN SECRETARIAL DEVELOPMENT IN THIS OFFICE.

For the purpose of developing desirable attitudes and skills in our employees, there is hereby authorized the holding of an In-Service Training Class In Secretarial Development for the stenographers, typists, and clerks of this Office. The sessions shall be conducted every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 2:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. from March 18 to May 6, 1964 at the Conference Room of the Budget Commission.

All chiefs of divisions and primary units in this Office are hereby urged to submit a list of their nominees to this course (at least two from each division and primary unit) to the Training Officer, Administrative Division, not later than March 13, 1964. For more detailed information regarding the course, attached is the program or outline thereof.

By authority of the President:

CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, March 4, 1964
