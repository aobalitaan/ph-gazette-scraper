MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 107

REQUIRING ALL EMPLOYEES OF THE NATIONAL PROVINCIAL AND MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENTS IN SURIGAO DEL NORTE TO REPORT FOR RELIEF DUTY TO THE PROVINCIAL GOVERNOR THEREOF.

In view of the destruction of public buildings in Surigao del Norte, all government employees therein, including teachers, ho are not working in activities pertaining to relief, reconstruction and rehabilitation, are required to help in relief work, if they have no regular work to do in their respective offices, pending resumption of their normal functions.

For the purpose, they are required to report for relief duty to the Provincial Governor who shall make the necessary assignments to the various relief and rehabilitation services in the affected areas.

By authority of the President:

JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, November 24, 1964
