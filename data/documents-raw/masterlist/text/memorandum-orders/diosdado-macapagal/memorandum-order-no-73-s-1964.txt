MEMORANDUM ORDER No. 73

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO REVIEW THE ECONOMIC RELATIONS BETWEEN THE PHILIPPINES AND JAPAN.

For the purpose of reviewing the economic relations between the Philippines and Japan, a committee is hereby created composed of the following:

Chairman

Member

Member

Member

Hon. Armand V. Fabella, Director-General, Program Implementation Agency

Member

Hon. Sixto K. Roxas Jr., Presidential Adviser on Economic Affairs

Member

The committee is authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or instrumentality of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in carrying out its work.

It shall submit its report and recommendations as soon as possible.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, March 10, 1964
