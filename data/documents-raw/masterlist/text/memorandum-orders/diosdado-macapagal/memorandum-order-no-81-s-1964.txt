MEMORANDUM ORDER No. 81

CREATING A SPECIAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF A NATIONAL EXPOSITION ON PHILIPPINE PROGRESS.

A special committee to take charge of the National Exposition on Philippine Progress to be held at the National Library, Manila, starting June 12, 1964, is hereby created composed of the following:

The committee is charged with over-all responsibility of conducting the exposition, including planning and collection of such materials as necessary for the above purpose.

The committee, through its chairman, is hereby authorized to secure such information and materials directly from all government agencies and entities as shall be necessary to discharge the above functions.

It shall submit its report and recommendations as soon possible.

This order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, May 12, 1964
