MEMORANDUM ORDER No. 106

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO FIND WAYS AND MEANS BY WHICH THE GOVERNMENT COULD PURCHASE IBM OR SIMILAR MACHINES.

Upon the recommendation of the Presidential Committee created under Executive Order No. 40 dated June 1, 1963, a committee is hereby created to find ways and means by which the government could purchase IBM or similar machines for its use and employ its own personnel to handle its maintenance and operation, instead of hiring such machines which is expensive to the government.

The Committee shall be composed of the following:

Chairman

Member

Member

Member

The Committee is hereby authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency, or instrumentality of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in the performance of its duties.

The Committee shall submit its report and recommendation as soon as possible.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Secretary of Justice Concurrently Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, November 18, 1964
