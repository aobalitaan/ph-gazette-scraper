MEMORANDUM ORDER No. 93

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 39 OF THIS OFFICE SERIES OF 1963

Memorandum Order No. 39 dated July 17, 1963, providing for the holding of a staff meeting every Monday morning, is hereby amended so as to hold the staff meeting every Tuesday at 9:00 sharp in the morning at the Conference Room, unless cancelled or postponed to another day and/or time and place. It shall attended by the following:

The executive Secretary may call upon other officials and employees to be present at the meeting.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, August 15, 1964
