MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1210

TO: The Minister of Natural Resources

As part of the effort to meet national export goals, it is desired that optimum economic activity, be stimulated in Mindanao, particularly in the mining sector.

Consistent with these goals, it is directed that the Kingking mining property situated in Pantucan, Davao del Norte be put into production at the earliest possible date. To accomplish this expeditiously, the Kingking property should be developed in such a manner as to facilitate ore processing at the Amacan Copper Concentrator of North Davao Mining Corporation.

It is likewise directed that North Davao Mining Corporation undertake the development of the project and its implementation without prejudice to the rights of the legal claimant of the property, and that a mechanism of royalty payment be worked out by the Ministry of Natural Resources to insure fair treatment of the claim owners.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

Source : Presidential Management Staff
