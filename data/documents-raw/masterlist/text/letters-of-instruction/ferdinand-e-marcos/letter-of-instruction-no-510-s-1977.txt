MALACAÑANG MANILA

February 18, 1977

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 510

TO: The Flag Officer in Command Philippine Navy

The Commanding General Philippine Air Force

The Commanding General Philippine Army

The Chief of Constabulary

The Director of Lands

In the line with the program of improving the national security and for greater administrative efficiency, you are hereby directed to locate appropriate areas all over the country to be proclaimed as naval reservations under the administration of either the Philippine Navy or the Philippine Coast Guard.

Likewise the Commanding Generals of the other major services are directed to locate appropriate areas for military reservations for the purpose of national security or of increased administrative efficiency.

The Director of Lands shall cause the survey of these proposed reservations and shall submit the necessary drafts of proclamations for my approval.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
