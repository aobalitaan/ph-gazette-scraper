MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 804

AMENDING LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 779.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Letter of Instructions No. 779 dated December 6, 1978, entitled “Allowances and Payments Made to National Officials by Local Government Units”, by exempting Judges and Fiscals effective immediately from the provision of said Letter of Instructions prohibiting officials or employees of the national government from receiving additional compensation from any province, city, municipality or barangay .

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
