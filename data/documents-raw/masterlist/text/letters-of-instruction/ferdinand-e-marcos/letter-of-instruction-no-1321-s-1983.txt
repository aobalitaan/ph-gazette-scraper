Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1321

DIRECTING THE SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM TO CONDUCT ACTUARIAL STUDIES WITH A VIEW TO UPGRADING FURTHER PENSION BENEFITS FOR RETIREES

Recognizing the need to protect the pension benefits of retirees covered by the Social Security Law (Rep. Act. No. 1161, as amended) from the ill-effects of world-wide inflation considering that they are the most adversely affected thereby, you are hereby directed to conduct actuarial studies with a view to upgrading substantially the pensions benefits for retirees, without any increase in premium contributions from covered employers and employees.

You shall submit to me the report of your studies within three (3) months from the date of this issuance.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Eighty-Three.

( Sgd .) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
