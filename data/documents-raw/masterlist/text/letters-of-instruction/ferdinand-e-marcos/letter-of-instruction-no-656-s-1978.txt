MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 656

The Governor Central Bank of the Philippines

The Commissioner Bureau of Customs

Pursuant to Section 16(1) of R.A. 5186 and in support of the Electronics Local Content program, which is intended to rationalize the manufacture of television receivers, sound reproducing appliances, radio receivers, and combinations and components thereof utilizing local materials on a progressively increasing local content annually, importation of parts, components and supplies prescribed for local manufacture as indicated in Annex A shall be restricted. No foreign exchange will be utilized for the importation of the said components and parts without a certification of the BOI that the importer is not covered by the Program.

The BOI is directed to make periodic review of the above Program and to make the appropriate recommendation to the President on the continued restriction of the importation of the said parts and components.

Manila, January 10, 1978

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

Source : CDAsia

Reference: Annex A
