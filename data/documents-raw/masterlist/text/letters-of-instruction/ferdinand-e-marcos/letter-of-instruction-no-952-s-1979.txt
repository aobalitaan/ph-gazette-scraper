MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 952

WHEREAS, there should be strict adherence to the principle that promotions in the judiciary and prosecution service, as in all other sectors of the government should be based on merit and qualifications; and

WHEREAS, there is a necessity to provide an authentic guide to determine the real merit and qualifications of candidates for promotion in the judiciary and prosecution service to ensure that only those deserving are promoted;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order and direct that individual rating sheets for judges, provincial, city and assistant fiscals be prepared as may guide and objective basis for the promotion or transfer of said officials, such rating sheets to include in appropriately titled columns the monthly number of cases disposed of or handled, number of pending cases, educational qualifications, length of service and such other criteria as may be deemed necessary in the evaluation of the qualification of the candidates.

Henceforth, no promotion or transfer of any of the abovementioned officials shall be approved by the undersigned unless the recommendation therefor is supported by the corresponding individual rating sheet.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
