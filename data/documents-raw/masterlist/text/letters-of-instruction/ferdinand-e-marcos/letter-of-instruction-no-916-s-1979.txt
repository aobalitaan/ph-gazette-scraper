MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 916

I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, do hereby issue the following instructions:

In the case of contracts for security, janitorial and similar services with at least eighty (80) per cent labor content, adjustments in mandatory emergency living allowances provided for under Presidential Decree No. 1634 shall be borne by the principal.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
