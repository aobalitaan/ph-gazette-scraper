MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 616

(AMENDMENT OF LETTER OF INSTRUCTION NO. 34)

In order to give more participation and power to the Barangay Council and the Kabataang Barangay Council of Tatalon, in securing the area from the intrusions of any person or persons “not contemplated as beneficiaries of Republic Act 2616 as amended”, Letter of Instructions No. 34 is amended as follows:

SECTION 3 – The City Mayor of Quezon City, the Barangay Council and the Kabataang Barangay Council of Tatalon are hereby ordered to secure the area referred to herein immediately upon receipt hereof and prevent any person or persons not contemplated as beneficiaries in the aforementioned law and as determined in accordance herewith from entering and occupying any portion of the aforesaid estate, and to render such assistance as may be necessary or as may be requested by the General Manager of the National Housing Authority in connection with the implementation of this Letter of Instructions.

Immediate and strict compliance with this Letter of Instructions is desired.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
