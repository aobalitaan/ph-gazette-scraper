MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION No. 575

TO ALL GOVERNMENT OFFICES CONCERNED

In view of the nature of the Subic Ship Repair Yard Project to be financed under the 5th Project Loan Package of the Overseas Economic Cooperation Fund of Japan as contrasted to the regular infrastructure projects of the government Colonel Generoso F. Tanseco, President of the Philippine Shipyard and Engineering Corporation, is hereby designated as Implementing Officer of the said project in lieu of Undersecretary Oscar Rodriguez as exemption to the provision of LOI No. 398, S-1976.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
