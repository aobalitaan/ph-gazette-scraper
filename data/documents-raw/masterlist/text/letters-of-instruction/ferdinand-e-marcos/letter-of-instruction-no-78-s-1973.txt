Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 78

The Governor Central Bank of the Philippines

In support of the Masagana 99 rice production program, you are hereby directed to release by November the total amount of ₱300 million to the National Grains Authority for its palay, corn and feed grains procurement program.

Strict compliance with this Letter of Instructions is desired.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
