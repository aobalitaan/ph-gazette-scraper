MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION NO. 499

TO: The Secretary of Trade The Governor, Central Bank of the Philippines The Chairman, Philippine Export Council The Secretary of Tourism The President, Philippine National Bank The Director-General, National Economic and Development Authority The Governor, Board of Investments The Chairman, Development Bank of the Philippines The General Manager, Government Service Insurance System The Chairman, National Science Development Board The Chairman, Social Securities System The Chairman, Philippine International Trading Corporation

Pursuant to Presidential Decree No. 1075 dated January 26, 1977, and in order to implement the provisions of Section 4 of the same Decree, the following agencies and instrumentalities shall appropriate out of their budgets contributions to a fund campaign to support the export development drive to Japan launched through major Philippine participation at the 12th Tokyo International Trade Fair, listed hereunder:

All abovementioned Government agencies are hereby directed to remit abovementioned amounts to the Confederation of Philippine Exporters, Inc., a corporate body authorized to accept contributions and donations from both the Government sector and the private sector and to implement the total program authorized herein provided for by Presidential Decree No. 865: Provided, however, That all funds that shall be committed and expended shall be subject to audit by the Commission on Audit.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
