MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

Letter of Instructions No. 540

TO: All Government Offices, Agencies and Instrumentalities, Including the Concerned Local Government Units

In order to ensure the prompt implementation of the first copper smelter project, there is hereby constituted an inter-agency task force composed of:

The task force shall provide effective support and assistance to the Philippine Associated Smelting and Refining Corporation in terms of:

a. Mobilizing and coordinating all assistance necessary from various government agencies;

b. Identifying problem areas and expediting solutions thereof; and

c. Performing other support activities as may be necessary to implement the copper smelter project.

The task force is hereby authorized to call upon any sector or Government agency or instrumentality to provide such assistance as may be necessary for the task force to carry out its functions effectively.

The Presidential Management Staff shall act as the Secretariat for the task force.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
