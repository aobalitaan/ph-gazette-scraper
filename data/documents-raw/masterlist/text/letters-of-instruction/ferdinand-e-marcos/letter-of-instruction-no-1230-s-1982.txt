Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1230

DIRECTING CONTINUED NEGOTIATIONS FOR A $110 MILLION LOAN FROM THE SAUDI ARABIA DEVELOPMENT FUND

In order to expand training opportunities for the Muslim population, you are hereby directed to pursue negotiations towards securing a $110 M. loan from the Saudi Arabia Development Fund. This loan is specifically being sought to set up training centers in Zamboanga, Dipolog, Marawi, Cotabato and Sulu for Muslim workers.

You are to submit a report on the implementation of this directive within thirty (30) days.

DONE in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 1st day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty two.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
