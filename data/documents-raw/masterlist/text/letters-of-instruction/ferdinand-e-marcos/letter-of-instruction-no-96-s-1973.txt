MALACAÑANG Manila

July 5, 1973

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 96

In line with the policy to protect the consuming public against illegal practices in connection with the supply, distribution and sale of prime commodities, you are hereby constituted as a Committee to review and propose amendments to the Price Control Law with the end in view of making the law more effective and at the same time encourage more production of basic or prime commodities.

The Committee herein constituted is hereby directed to meet immediately and submit proposed measures and recommendations urgently needed in pursuance of the Government’s economic and social development programs.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
