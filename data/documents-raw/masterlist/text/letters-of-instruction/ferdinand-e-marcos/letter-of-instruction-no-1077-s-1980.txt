Malacañang Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1077

DIRECTING THE PREPARATION OF REGIONALIZED BUDGET PROGRAMS OF NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS

WHEREAS, certain projects undertaken by the national government agencies and government-owned or controlled corporations are vital in the attainment of regional development goals;

WHEREAS, the full coordination of national government agencies, government-owned or controlled corporations, and local government units on project scope, location and timing will ensure greater effectiveness of government efforts in attaining regional and local development strategies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby Order and Instruct:

1. The ministries and government-owned or controlled corporations concerned shall prepare project listings for the following:

OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT National Electrification Administration Electrical Distribution Systems Mini-Hydro Power Plants through Electric Cooperatives

MINISTRY OF AGRICULTURE Bureau of Agricultural Extension Promotion of Improved Farm Management Practices

MINISTRY OF EDUCATION AND CULTURE Office of the Minister Implementation of Programs for Higher Education Implementation of Programs for General, Secondary, Fishery, Trade, Industrial Arts and Other Vocational Education Courses Aid to Provincial, Municipal, Barangay and City High Schools

MINISTRY OF ENERGY National Power Corporation Power Generation and Transmission Projects

MINISTRY OF HEALTH Office of the Minister Purchase and Distribution of Drugs and Medicines Operation and Maintenance of Sanitaria and Skin Clinics Operation and Maintenance of Hospitals, including Regional Mental Hospitals

MINISTRY OF HUMAN SETTLEMENTS Office of the Minister BLISS Projects

National Housing Authority Construction of New Housing Units Improvement of Sites and Services Urban and Rural Upgrading

Human Settlements Development Corporation Urban Renewal Projects New Town Development and Related Projects

MINISTRY OF NATIONAL DEFENSE Integrated National Police Construction of Police Stations and Jails

MINISTRY OF NATURAL RESOURCES Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources Fish Seed Production and Dispersal

Bureau of Forest Development Reforestation and Afforestation, including Forest Ecosystem Management (PROFEM) Reforestation and Afforestation Activities of Local Government Units

MINISTRY OF PUBLIC HIGHWAYS Office of the Minister Repair and Maintenance of National Roads and Bridges Construction, Rehabilitation and Improvement of National Roads and Bridges (including Priority National Roads and Bridges) Construction of New Provincial, City and Municipal Roads and Bridges Construction of Barangay Roads and Bridges Repair and Maintenance of Provincial City Roads and Bridges Repair and Maintenance of Municipal Roads and Bridges Repair and Maintenance of Barangay Roads and Bridges

MINISTRY OF PUBLIC WORKS Office of the Minister Construction, Rehabilitation and Improvement of Health Center Buildings (BHS/BHU) Construction, Rehabilitation and Improvement of School Buildings, including Priority School Buildings Construction, Rehabilitation and Improvement of Public Buildings and Facilities Construction, Rehabilitation and Improvement of Artesian Wells and Springs Construction, Rehabilitation and Improvement of Communal Irrigation Systems

Farm Systems Development Corporation Expansion of the Barangay Irrigators’ Service Association (BISA) Program and Farm Systems Development

Local Waterworks Utilities Administration Water Supply Improvement Projects for Urban Centers Outside Metro Manila

Metropolitan Waterworks and Sewerage System Water Supply, Sewerage and Sanitation Program for Metro Manila

National Irrigation Administration Erosion Control and Watershed Management Construction and Rehabilitation of National and Communal Irrigation Projects/Systems Pump Irrigation Projects Groundwater Development Projects Power Generation by Reservoir Projects Power Generation by Mini-Hydros in National Irrigation Systems

MINISTRY OF SOCIAL SERVICES AND DEVELOPMENT Office of the Minister Supplemental Feeding Program for Malnourished Pre-School Children

MINISTRY OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATIONS Bureau of Air Transportation Repair and Maintenance of Airport Horizontal Facilities Repair and Maintenance of Airports, Air Navigation Facilities, Buildings and Installations

2. The listings shall include the implementation schedules, total cost estimates and cash requirements of the projects for the year, identified by Region/ Province/Municipality, observing the format prescribed by the Ministry of the Budget.

3. The ministries and corporations concerned shall coordinate with local government officials in the selection of projects and determination of project sites, taking into consideration the priorities of their respective regional development plans.

4. The listings shall be submitted to the Ministry of the Budget not later than November 15, 1980 for consolidation and submission for the President’s approval.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and eighty.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
