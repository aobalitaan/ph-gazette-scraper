MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1208

WHEREAS, it has come to the attention of this Office that frequent occurrences of fire in residential houses and commercial establishments are traceable to, or caused by, faulty electrical wiring primarily due to the sub-standard quality of locally produced electrical wires;

WHEREAS, fires cause untold misery to the victims as well as wreak havoc on the economy of the nation; and

WHEREAS, there is an imperative need to establish quality-control systems in all firms manufacturing electrical wirings to ensure production of quality materials.

NOW THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby order the Minister of Trade and Industry and the Chairman of the Board of Investments to require all firms engaging in the manufacture of electrical wirings to establish in their factories quality-control systems under the supervision of quality-control officers who shall be vested with authority and responsibility to give clearance for the sale to the public of electrical wirings, such clearance to be imposed as a prerequisite before any manufacturer of electrical wirings of any size or type is permitted to sell his products.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
