MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION NO. 327

To: The Secretary, Department of Local Government & Community Development The Secretary, Department of Public Works and Transportation Communications The Director General, National Economic Development Authority Chairman, Philippine National Oil Company Chairman, Oil Industry Commission

For reasons of national policy, it is essential that the fuel price support for certain designated transport and other industries be continued. Accordingly, the effectivity of Letter of Instruction No. 280 as extended by Letter of Instruction No. 306 is hereby further extended for another period of three (3) months beginning October 1, 1975 and ending December 31, 1975.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of October, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
