MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO.1410

DIRECTING THE COORDINATION OF PRICE ADJUSTMENTS

WHEREAS, there is an urgent need to closely coordinate the various price adjustments which have become necessary;

WHEREAS, uncoordinated actions by government ministries and government corporations may lead to unwarranted increases which might result in an inflationary spiral;

WHEREAS, public announcements of all such price adjustments must be made in a clear and understandable manner to the public;

NOW, THEREFORE I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Law, do hereby order and direct all government ministries and government corporations:

1. To submit all proposed price adjustments to the Presidential Coordinating Committee headed by the Minister of Trade and Industry who, after evaluation, shall submit its recommendations to the President for approval; and

2. To desist from making any announcements about price adjustment without prior clearance from the said Coordinating Committee.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 24th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-four.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
