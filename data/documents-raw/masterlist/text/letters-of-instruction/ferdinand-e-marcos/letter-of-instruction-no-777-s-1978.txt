MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 777

It is hereby ordered that all intelligence operations conducted by your Office should be coordinated with the National Intelligence & Security Authority (NISA). Whatever is the nature of the operations, the Ministries, Bureaus, Offices or Corporations concerned, must clear first their operations with the National Intelligence & Security Authority which is turn, will render a report thereon direct to the President.

DONE in the City of manila , this 28th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Seventy-Eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
