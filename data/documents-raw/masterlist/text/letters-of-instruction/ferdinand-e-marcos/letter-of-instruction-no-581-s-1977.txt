MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 581

The Secretary of National Defense The Secretary of Foreign Affairs The Secretary of Finance The Secretary of Local Governments & Community Development The Secretary of Health The Secretary of Trade The Governor, Central Bank of the Philippines The President, Philippine National Bank

To ensure the viability of privately organized and managed pilgrimages to Mecca by Muslim Filipinos in the manner the Hajj of 1976 was conducted under the provisions of Letter of Instructions Number 450, I hereby direct that you provide on a continuing basis the necessary advisory, administrative, technical and supervisory assistance to Muslim Filipinos on their pilgrimages to Mecca.

The Department of National Defense is designated the primary action office for the purpose of coordinating the participation of all government entities involved in these privately managed pilgrimages. It shall be the principal point of contact between the government and the leaders/organizers of these pilgrimages.

The Secretary of National Defense shall choose from among the applicants for each Hajj period the Muslim leaders and the groups that shall be recognized as representatives of the pilgrims capable of managing the pilgrimage wholly on their own, from the selection of their respective members, the choice of and the contracting for their transportation, to the funding of their activities. He shall issue such rules and guidelines and organize such bodies as he may deem necessary for the safe and orderly pilgrimages to Mecca.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
