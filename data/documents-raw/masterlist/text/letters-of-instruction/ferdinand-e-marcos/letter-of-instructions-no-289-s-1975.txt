MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 289

ACCEPTING THE RESIGNATION OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY AND DESIGNATING THE VICE-PRESIDENT FOR COMMUNITY AND EXTENSION SERVICES AS OFFICER-IN-CHARGE AND ORDERING HIM TO INSTITUTE REFORMS THEREIN

In view of the findings and recommendations of the Committee created under Executive Order No. 445 and pursuant to Proclamation No. 1081 dated September 21, 1972 and by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution as President of the Republic of the Philippines, and further to promote the objectives of the Mindanao State University:

1. Upon consultation with the members of the Board of Regents, the resignation of Dr. Mauyag M. Tamano from the position of President of the Mindanao State University is hereby accepted effective July 1, 1975.

2. Attorney Tocod D. Macaraya, Vice-President for Community and Extension Services of the Mindanao State University, is hereby designated as Officer-in-Charge of the said University effective July 1, 1975 with all the powers and privileges vested in the Office of the President, and is hereby ordered to undertake the following tasks:

a. To organize a Team to undertake a management audit of the Mindanao State University, including its external units, in order to identify areas and causes of corruption and inefficiency, and to submit to the Office of the President within one month from the date of these instructions the Team’s recommendations for achieving economy, efficiency and sound management in the administration of the University;

b. To take appropriate action against, and/or terminate the services of officials and employees of the University who are found or may be found to be unfit or unnecessary for service to the University or otherwise found to be notoriously undesirable; and

c. To perform such other duties and functions as may be necessary to institute proper reforms in the University.

Attorney Macaraya is authorized to call upon all agencies of Government, including State colleges and universities, government-owned or controlled corporations, and local government units for any assistance needed in the performance of the tasks assigned him.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of July in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
