Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION NO. 1353

Pursuant to the country’s objective of developing indigenous energy resources, the Philippines National Oil Company and its subsidiaries have continuously undertaken exploration and development programs designed to make possible the full utilization of our geothermal, coal, uranium and oil reserves.

The larger amounts of investments required by these projects and the increasing difficulty in obtaining foreign financing have put PNOC’s fund sourcing capability under tremendous pressure. In order to alleviate this financing strain, we hereby direct the Treasurer of the Philippines to release PERSOS SIX HUNDRED TWENTY-TWO MILLION AND SEVEN HUNDRED THOUSAND (P622, 700, 000.00) out of the Special Fund created pursuant to Section 8 (j) of Republic Act No. 6173, as amended, to reimburse PNOC for actual exploration and development costs incurred in 1982 and to fund exploration expenditures expected in 1983.

PNOC is directed to submit to the President of the Philippines a report accounting for the disbursements and utilization of the foregoing funds not later than the end of the fiscal year.

This Letter of Instruction shall take effect immediately.

Signed in the City of Manila this 19 th day of September in the year of Our Lord Nineteen Hundred and Eighty-Three.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

Source : Presidential Management Staff
