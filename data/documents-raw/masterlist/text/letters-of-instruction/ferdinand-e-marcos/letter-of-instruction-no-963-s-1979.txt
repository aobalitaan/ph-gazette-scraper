MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 963

WHEREAS, the Ministry of Energy, in the performance of its functions as the Government’s primary agency in the formulation and implementation of the country’s energy resource development program is in need of adequate space/site for the offices and facilities of its different bureaus;

WHEREAS, the Philippine National Oil Company, in the discharge of its functions as the agency charged with fostering conditions relative to oil or petroleum operations and the exploitation of other energy resources conclusive to a balanced and sustainable growth of the economy likewise needs space/site for the offices of the Energy Companies which it has sired for this purpose;

WHEREAS, the Bureau of Energy Development of the Ministry of Energy and the PNOC Exploration Corporation and PNOC Energy Development Corporation at present already have offices at the Philippine National Petroleum Center (PNPC) Compound at the Fort Bonifacio Military Reservation;

NOW, THEREFORE, in the interest of the continued effective discharge of the concerned bureaus/affiliates of the foregoing agencies, the Ministry of Defense is hereby directed:

1. To allocate, allot and/or allow unto the Ministry of Energy for its use and occupancy and that of its bureaus and/or offices the hereinbelow described parcel of land situated at the Fort Bonifacio Military Reservation with an area of Eighteen Thousand Nine Hundred and Fifty-Seven (18,957) square meters, to wit:

“A parcel of land situated within the Fort Bonifacio Reservation, Rizal. Bounded on the N and E by open spaces on the S by area claimed by Philippine National Oil Company and on the W by Merritt Road, Beginning at a point marked “1” being N 64 deg 52’E, 6338.20 m. from BLLM No. 1, Maricaban, Pasay City;

“A parcel of land situated within the Fort Bonifacio Reservation, Rizal. Bounded on the N. by area claimed by Bureau of Energy Development; and on the E and S and W by open spaces. Beginning at a point marked “1”, being N 64 deg. 57’E, 6338.67 m. from BLLM No. 1, Maricaban, Pasay City;

The Ministry of Energy and the Philippine National Oil Company shall respectively have full control over, as well as the administration, management and responsibility for development of, the respective areas mentioned above including the construction or establishment of such improvements as they respectively determine.

This Letter of Instructions shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
