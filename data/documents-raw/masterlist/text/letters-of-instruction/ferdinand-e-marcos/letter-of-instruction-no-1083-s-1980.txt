MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1083

TO: All Heads of Ministries, Bureaus, Offices, and Other Instrumentalities of the Government, Including Local Governments and Government-Owned or Controlled Corporations

WHEREAS, Batas Pambansa Blg. 72 has provided for the taking of an integrated census every ten years beginning in the year 1980;

WHEREAS, pursuant to the aforecited law, the National Economic and Development Authority (NEDA) has submitted the preliminary results of the population census of the Philippines by municipality/city/province as conducted by the National Census and Statistics Office;

WHEREAS, under Batas Pambansa Blg. 72, the final report of the population census is to be made before the end of 1980;

WHEREAS, there is a current great demand of the population count from all sectors including the public.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, hereby direct and order as follows:

1. Pending the submission of the final report of the population at the barangay level, the preliminary results of the census population of the Philippines by municipality/city/province, as submitted by the NEDA is hereby approved for official use by all agencies and instrumentalities of the government, including local governments and government-owned or controlled corporations, in their planning and/or appraisal of development activities relating to population and its distribution as of May 1, 1980 and other population-related matters.

2. This Letter of Instructions shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of Nov. in the year of Our Lord Nineteen Hundred Eighty.

( Sgd .) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
