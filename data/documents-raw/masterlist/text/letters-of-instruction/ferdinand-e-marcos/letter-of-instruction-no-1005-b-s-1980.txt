Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MALACANANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1005-B

In addition to the officials authorized to sign communications in the Office of the President pursuant to Letter of Instructions No. 342 dated December 1, 1975, Presidential Assistant Joaquin T. Venus, Jr., is hereby authorized to sign by my authority or order communications, decisions, resolutions, actions or other matters I assign to him for appropriate action or immediate implementation from time to time as the need arises or exigencies of the service so require.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
