MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 89

and

Proclamation No. 1152, approved and issued today on the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, excludes from the operation of Proclamation No. 69 dated September 23, 1954, for disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act, a portion of land described therein and containing an area of One Thousand Eight Hundred Fifty-Four (1,854) square meters.

In view of the necessity of maintaining the said area indefinitely for public use, especially in the interest of tourism, you are hereby directed to see to it that the disposition of the portion thus excluded in said Proclamation No. 1152 shall be limited to a lease for a reasonable period, and that the purpose for which such lease may be allowed shall be limited to the use of the area as a parking and promenade area.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
