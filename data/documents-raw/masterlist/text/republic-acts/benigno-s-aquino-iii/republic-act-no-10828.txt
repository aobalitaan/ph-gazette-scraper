[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10828 ]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the Municipality of Alabel, Province of Sarangani a Regular District Office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO).

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation and Communications shall include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Regular District Office of the LTO in the Municipality of Alabel, Province of Sarangani, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was passed by the House of Representatives on August 10, 2015, amended by the Senate on January 19,2016, and which amendments were concurred in by the House of Representatives on January 27, 2016.

Approved: MAY 19 2016

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III President of the Philippines
