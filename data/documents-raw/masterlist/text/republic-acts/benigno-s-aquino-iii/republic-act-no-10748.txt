Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Sixteenth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10748 ]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN MANUEL NORTE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION TO DR. MANUEL T. CASES SR. ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of an Elementary School. – The name of San Manuel Norte Elementary School in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union is hereby changed to Dr. Manuel T. Cases Sr. Elementary School.

SEC. 2. Location of the Main Campus. – The main campus of the Dr. Manuel T. Cases Sr. Elementary School shall continue to remain in its present location in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union.

SEC. 3. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.

Approved,

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON President of the Senate

(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 4, 2014 and December 7, 2015, respectively.

Approved: MAR 07 2016

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III President of the Philippines
