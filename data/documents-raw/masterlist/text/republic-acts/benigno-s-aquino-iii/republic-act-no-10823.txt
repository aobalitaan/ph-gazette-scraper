[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10823 ]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF KASIBU NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL BEING ADMINISTERED BY THE TECHNICAL EDUCATION AND SKILLS DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (TESDA) IN BARANGAY PUDI, MUNICIPALITY OF KASIBU, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA TO NUEVA VIZCAYA POLYTECHNIC INSTITUTE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Kasibu National Agricultural School being administered by the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) in Barangay Pudi, Municipality of Kasibu, Province of Nueva Vizcaya is hereby changed to Nueva Vizcaya Polytechnic Institute.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette .

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 3, 2014 and February 2, 2016, respectively.

Approved: MAY 18 2016

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III President of the Philippines
