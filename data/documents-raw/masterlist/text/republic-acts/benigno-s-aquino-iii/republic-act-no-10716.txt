Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Sixteenth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand fifteen.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10716 ]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BATASAN HILLS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY BATASAN HILLS, CITY OF QUEZON, METRO MANILA TO CORAZON C. AQUINO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of a National High School. – The name of Batasan Hills National High School in Barangay Batasan Hills, City of Quezon, Metro Manila is hereby changed to Corazon C. Aquino National High School.

SEC. 2. Location of the Main Campus. – The main campus of the Corazon C. Aquino National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Batasan Hills, City of Quezon, Metro Manila.

SEC. 3. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its official publication in the Official Gazette.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 24, 2014 and October 5, 2015, respectively.

(Sgd.) OSCAR G. YABES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) MARILYN B. BARUA-YAP Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on DEC 10 2015 Without the signature of the President In accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
