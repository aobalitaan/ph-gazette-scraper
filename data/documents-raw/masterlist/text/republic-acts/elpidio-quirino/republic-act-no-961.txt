H. No. 3510

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 961]

AN ACT TRANSFERRING CERTAIN APPROPRIATIONS FOR PUBLIC WORKS PROJECTS UNDER THE HEADING “BATANGAS,” SUBSECTION ( a ), TITLE “A. MISCELLANEOUS,” SECTION ONE OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED SIX HUNDRED SEVENTY.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sub-heading “Other Public Works Projects,” under the heading “Batangas,” subsection ( a ), Title “A. Miscellaneous,” section one of Republic Act Numbered Six hundred seventy, is amended by increasing the appropriation for items numbers fourteen and twenty-three and eliminating items seventeen and twenty-one.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 20, 1953.
