H. No. 9327

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7817]

AN ACT NAMING THE GRANADA-MURCIA ROAD AND EXTENSION (CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD) WHICH IS A NATIONAL ROAD IN BACOLOD CITY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AS THE CARLOS HILADO HIGHWAY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Granada-Murcia Road and Extension (Circumferential Road), a national road in Bacolod City, Negros Occidental, is hereby named as the Carlos Hilado Highway.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on August 30, 1994 and September 7, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: NOV 10 1994

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines
