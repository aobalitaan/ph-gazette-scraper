H. No. 5637

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8058]

AN ACT AMENDING SECTION TWO OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 260, AS AMENDED, DECLARING AS HISTORICAL LANDMARK ALL ANCESTRAL HOUSES ALONG RIZAL STREET, SILAY CITY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section 2 of Presidential Decree No. 260, as amended, is hereby further amended to include as historical landmark all ancestral houses along Rizal Street, Silay City, Negros Occidental.

SEC. 2. The executive director and/or chairman of the National Historical Institute shall implement the provisions of this Act.

SEC. 3. All laws, decrees, orders, rules and regulations and other issuances or parts thereof inconsistent with this Act shall be repealed or amended accordingly.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE C. DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 16, 1995 and February 20, 1995, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on JUN 15 1995 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
