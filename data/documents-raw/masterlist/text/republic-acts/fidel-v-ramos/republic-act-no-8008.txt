H. No. 3658

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8008]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE DANAWAN NATIONALIZED HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY DANAWAN, MUNICIPALITY OF MAGSAYSAY, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN INTO THE DANAWAN SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Danawan Nationalized High School in Barangay Danawan, Municipality of Magsaysay, Province of Palawan, is hereby converted into a science high school to be known as the Danawan Science High School.

SEC. 2. The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall promulgate the necessary rules and regulations for the implementation of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged against the current year’s appropriations of the Danawan National High School. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE C. DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 24, 1994 and February 27, 1995, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on May 25, 1995, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
