REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7805

AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 28 OF EVERY YEAR AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF CAVITE TO BE KNOWN AS JULIAN FELIPE DAY

Section 1. In celebration of the birth anniversary of Professor Julian Felipe and in recognition of his talent and ingenuity in the masterful constitution of the national anthem, the 28th of January of every year is hereby declared as a non-working special public holiday in the City of Cavite to be known as Julian Felipe Day.

Section 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Lapsed into law on September 1, 1994 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
