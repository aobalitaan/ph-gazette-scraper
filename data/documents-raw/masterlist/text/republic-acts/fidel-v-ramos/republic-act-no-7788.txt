H. No. 1229

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7788]

AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 13 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LASAM, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO BE KNOWN AS “ADLAW TI LASAM”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipality of Lasam was declared an independent municipality by viryue of republic Act No. 507 signed on June 13, 1950 by President Elpidio Quirino.

SEC. 2. June 13 of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of lasam, Province of Cagayan, to be known as “Adlaw ti Lasam.”

SEC. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 24, 1994 and May 12, 1994, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on August 8, 1994 without the President’s signature in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
