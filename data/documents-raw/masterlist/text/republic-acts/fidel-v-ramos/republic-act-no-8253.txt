[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8253]

AN ACT DECLARING JULY ONE OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAHINOG, PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE KNOWN AS “MAHINOG CHARTER DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. July one is the charter day anniversary of the Municipality of Mahinog, Province of Camiguin, and its celebration is highlighted with colorful parade and beauty pageant.

SEC. 2. July one of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking public holiday in the Municipality of Mahinog.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 28, 1995 and November 27, 1996, respectively.
