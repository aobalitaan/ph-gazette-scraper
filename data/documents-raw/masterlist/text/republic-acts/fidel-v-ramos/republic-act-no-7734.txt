H. No. 603

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7734]

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A BARANGAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY RUBEN E. ECLEO, SR., MUNICIPALITY OF CAGDIANAO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE RUBEN E. ECLEO, SR. NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFORE

SECTION 1. There shall be established a national high school in Barangay Ruben E. Ecleo, Sr., Municipality of Cagdianao, Province of Surigao del Norte, under the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, to be known as the Ruben E. Ecleo, Sr. National High School.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be funded out of the appropriations authorized by Republic Act No. 7663 for the Department of Education, Culture and Sports intended for the operational expenses of newly legislated high schools. Thereafter, the amount needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on August 3, 1993 and February 17, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives
