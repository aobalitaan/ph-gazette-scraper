H. No. 2240

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1254]

Be enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Sub-items one to sixteen of Item ( d ) for the Province of Samar, sub-section ( e ), Title B, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred and twenty, otherwise known as the Public Works Act of nineteen hundred and fifty-three, are hereby amended to read as follows:

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 10, 1955.
