Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Tenth Congress

First Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-five.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8206]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MABILA HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SARANGANI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, TO ALBERTO OLARTE, SR. NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Mabila High School in the Municipality of Sarangani, Province of Davao del Sur, is hereby changed to Alberto Olarte, Sr. National High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 28, 1996 and June 6, 1996, respectively.

(Sgd.) HAZEL P. GACUTAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on SEP 05 1996 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
