H. No. 561

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7745]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL AT ADDALAM REGION, MUNICIPALITY OF JONES, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO BE KNOWN AS THE ADDALAM REGION HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a high school in the Addalam Region at Barangay Tupax, Jones, Isabela, to cater to high school students of Barangays Addalam, Barangcuag, Tupax, san Roque, and Linamanan, all in the Municipality of Jones, Province of Isabela, to be known as the Addalam Region High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.

SEC. 3 The amount necessary to carry out on the provisions of this Act shall be funded out of the appropriations authorized by Republic Act No. 7663 for the Department of Education, Culture and Sports intended for the operational expenses of newly legislated high schools. Thereafter, the amount needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on August 12, 1993 and February 17, 1994, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on June 19, 1994 without the President’s signature in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
