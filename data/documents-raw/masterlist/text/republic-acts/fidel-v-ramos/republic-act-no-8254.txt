[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8254]

AN ACT DECLARING JULY EIGHT OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUINSILIBAN, PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE KNOWN AS “GUINSILIBAN CHARTER DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. July eight is the charter day anniversary of the Municipality of Guinsiliban, Province of Camiguin, and its celebration is highlighted with colorful parade, participated in by all barangays, stage drama, sports festival, and beauty pageant.

SEC. 2. July eight of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking public holiday in the Municipality of Guinsiliban.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 28, 1995 and November 27, 1996, respectively.
