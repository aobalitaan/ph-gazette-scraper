Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Fifth Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila on Monday, the twenty second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7631]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MAGUINDA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MAGUINDA, BUTUAN CITY, TO SANTIAGO B. ECLEO, SR., MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Maguinda Elementary School in Barangay Maguinda, Butuan City, is hereby changed to Santiago B. Ecleo, Sr., Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES President of the Senate

(Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on April 5, 1990 and January 27, 1992, respectively.

(Sgd.) ANACLETO D. BADOY, JR. Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: JUL 17 1992

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines
