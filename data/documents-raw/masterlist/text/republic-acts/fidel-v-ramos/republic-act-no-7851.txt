H. No. 12385

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7851]

AN ACT DECLARING THE 24TH DAY OF SEPTEMBER OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN CALAMBA, LAGUNA, TO COMMEMORATE THE BIRTH ANNIVERSARY OF THE LATE SPEAKER JOSE YULO, SR.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 24 of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in Calamba, Laguna, to commemorate the 24th anniversary of the late Speaker Jose Yulo, Sr.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on August 1, 1994 and October 3, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: DEC 24 1994

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines
