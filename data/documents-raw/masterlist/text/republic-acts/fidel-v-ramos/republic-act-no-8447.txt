Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-seven.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8447]

AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF LOS ARCOS NATIONAL SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PROSPERIDAD, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the Municipality of Prosperidad, Province of Agusan del Sur, Los Arcos National High School, under the control and supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports.

SEC. 2. This school shall be known officially as the Los Arcos National High School.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE C. DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 20, 1997 and October 6, 1997, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on JAN 06 1998 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
