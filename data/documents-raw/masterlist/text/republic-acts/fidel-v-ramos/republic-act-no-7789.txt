Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7789]

AN ACT DECLARING DECEMBER 18 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALLESTEROS, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO BE KNOWN AS “ ALDAW TI BALLESTORES ”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipality of Ballesteros, Province of Cagayan, was founded on December 18, 1911 by virtue of Executive Order No. 79.

SEC. 2. December 18 of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Ballesteros, Province of Cagayan, to be known as “ Aldaw ti Ballesteros .”

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 24, 1994 and May 12, 1994, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law without the signature of the President on August 8, 1994, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
