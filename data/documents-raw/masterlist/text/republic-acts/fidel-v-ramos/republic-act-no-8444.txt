Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Tenth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-seven.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8444]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LONOY, MUNICIPALITY OF JAGNA, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE LONOY BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a barangay high school in Barangay Lonoy, Municipality of Jagna, Province of Bohol, to be known as the Lonoy Barangay High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 28, 1996 and October 6, 1997, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on DEC 29 1997 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
