H. No. 693

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7695]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MANAGOPAYA PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANATE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO FRANCISCO S. BACTUNG MEMORIAL PRIMARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Managopaya Primary School in the Municipality of Banate, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Francisco S. Bactung Memorial Primary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 31, 1993 and February 3, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law without the signature of the President on April 9, 1994, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
