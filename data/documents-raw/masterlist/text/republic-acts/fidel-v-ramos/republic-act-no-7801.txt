H. No. 645

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7801]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE CONCEPCION COLLEGE OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONCEPCION, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, INTO A POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CONCEPCION POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Concepcion College of Fisheries in the Municipality of Concepcion, Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a polytechnic college, to be known as the Concepcion Polytechnic College.

SEC. 2. The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall take the necessary steps for the immediate implementation of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged against the current year’s appropriations of the Concepcion College of Fisheries. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE C. DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 4, 1994 and May 31, 1994, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law without the signature of the President on September 1, 1994, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
