H. No. 30380

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Fifth Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-second day of July nineteen hundred and ninety-one

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7632]

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE MANAY DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANAY, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Manay District Hospital in the Municipality of Manay, Province of Davao Oriental, is hereby increased from ten (10) to twenty-five (25) beds.

SEC 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.

SEC 3. This Ad shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES President of the Senate

(Sgd.) RAMON V. MITRA Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 3 ,1992 and January 27, 1992, respectively.

(Sgd.) ANACLETO D. BADOY JR. Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: JUL 17, 1992

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines
