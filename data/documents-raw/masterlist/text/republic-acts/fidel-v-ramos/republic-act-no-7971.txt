H. No. 1343

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7971]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TEN-BED MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGUINALDO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE AGUINALDO MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a ten-bed capacity municipal hospital in the Municipality of Aguinaldo, Province of Ifugao, to be known as the Aguinaldo Municipal Hospital.

SEC. 2. The Department of Health, in consultation and coordination with the local government officials concerned, shall promulgate such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary for the establishment of the hospital shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law; thereafter, funds necessary for the maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of the Local Government Code or the applicable law at the time.

SEC. 4. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 17, 1995 and February 2, 1995, respectively.

Approved: 30 MAR 1995

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines
