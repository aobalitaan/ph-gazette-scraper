H. No. 7350

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7984]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LA UNION, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, a high school in Barangay La Union, Municipality of San Isidro, Province of Davao Oriental.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall implement this Act so as to make the school in operation during the first school term after the effectivity of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to implement the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 16, 1994 and February 15, 1995, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on APR 22 1995 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
