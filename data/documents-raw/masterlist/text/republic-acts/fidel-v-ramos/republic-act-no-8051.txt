H. No. 144

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8051]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE ALBAY PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN LEGAZPI CITY A REGIONAL TRAINING AND TEACHING HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BICOL REGIONAL TRAINING AND TEACHING HOSPITAL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Albay Provincial Hospital in Legazpi City is hereby converted into a regional training and teaching hospital to be known as the Bicol Regional Training and Teaching Hospital.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Health shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on October 17, 1994 and February 20, 1995, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on JUN 15 1995 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
