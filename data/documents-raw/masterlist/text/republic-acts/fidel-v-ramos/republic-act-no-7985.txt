H. No. 7600

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7985]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUERO, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE SAN ISIDRO HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, a high school in Barangay San Isidro, Municipality of Duero, Province of Bohol, to be known as the San Isidro High School.

SEC. 2 The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3 The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 15, 1994 and February 15, 1995, respectively.

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on APR 22 1995
