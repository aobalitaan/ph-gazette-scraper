[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8312]

AN ACT SEPARATING THE LIGAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL EXTENSION CLASSES LOCATED IN BARANGAY BINODEGAHAN, MUNICIPALITY OF PIODURAN, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, FROM THE LIGAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND CONVERTING THE SAME INTO A SEPARATE AND INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE PIODURAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Ligao National High School Extension Classes, located in Barangay Binodegahan, Municipality of Pioduraa, Province of Albay, is hereby separated from the Ligao National High School and converted into a national high school to be known as the Pioduran National High School.

SEC. 2. All personnel records, assets, including buildings, sites and improvements thereon, funds and obligations of the Ligao National High School Extension Classes are hereby transferred to the Pioduran National High School.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the Ligao National High School-Binodegahan Extension Classes. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the Pioduran National High School shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on July 24, 1996 and March 10, 1997, respectively.

(Sgd.) LORENZO E. LEYNES, JR. Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on JUN 20 1997 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
