H. No. 297

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7707]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PAGLAUM STATE COLLEGE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, TO CARLOS C. HILADO MEMORIAL STATE COLLEGE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Paglaum State College in the Municipality of Talisay, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby changed to Carlos C. Hilado Memorial State College.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE C. DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 26, 1993 and February 3, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law without the signature of the President on May 5, 1994, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
