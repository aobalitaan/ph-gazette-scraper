Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Eleventh Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty fourth day of July, two thousand.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9052]

AN ACT RECLASSIFYING THE SUB-DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE IN THE THIRD CONGRESSIONAL DISTRICT OF CEBU, PROVINCE OF CEBU INTO A REGULAR ENGINEERING DISTRICT OFFICE TO BE KNOWN AS THE THIRD ENGINEERING DISTRICT OF CEBU AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREOF

Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled.

SECTION 1. The present third congressional sub-district engineering office in the Province of Cebu created pursuant to the Department of Public Works Order No. 109-A is hereby reclassified into a regular engineering district office to be known as the Third Engineering District of Cebu.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into laws and thereafter.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provisions of this Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) AQUILINO PIMENTEL JR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 23, 2000 and January 29, 2001, respectively.

(Sgd.) LUIGARDO B. BARBO Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: March 30, 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
