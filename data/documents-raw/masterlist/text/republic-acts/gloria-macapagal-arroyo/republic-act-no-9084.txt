Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Eleventh Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand.

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9084

AN ACT TO ESTABLISH THE UMIRAY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY UMIRAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGALAN IN THE PROVINCE OF AURORA AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established a national high school in Barangay Umiray in the Municipality of Dingalan, Province of Aurora to be known as the Umiray National High School.

SEC 2. The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC 4. This Act shall take effect immediately upon approval thereof.

Approved,

(Sgd.) AQUILINO Q. PIMENTEL JR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 31, 2000 and February 5, 2001, respectively.

(Sgd.) LUTGARDO B. BARBO Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: APR 08 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
