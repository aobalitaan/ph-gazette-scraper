Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Fourteenth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9754]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN NARCISO VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN SAN NARCISO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON TO GODOFREDO M. TAN MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES

SECTION 1. Renaming of High School. — The name of San Narciso Vocational High School in the Municipality of San Narciso, Province of Quezon is hereby changed to Godofredo M. Tan Memorial School of Arts and Trades.

SEC. 2. Effectivity . — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE President of the Senate

(Sgd.) PROSPERO C. NOGRALES Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on June 11, 2009 and August 25, 2009, respectively.

(Sgd.) EMMA LIRIO REYES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) MARILYN B. BARUA-YAP Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: November 10, 2009

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
