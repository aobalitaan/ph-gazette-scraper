Eleventh Congress

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9092

AN ACT DECLARING EVERY 3RD DAY OF SEPTEMBER AS ARAW NG LABANAN SA IMUS, A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, CAVITE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 3 of every year is hereby declared as Araw ng Labanan sa Imus, a special working holiday in the Municipality of Imus, Cavite, in commemoration of the historic “Labanan sa Imus.”

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect immediately after its publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.

Approved,

(Sgd.) AQUILINO Q. PIMENTEL JR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 6, 2001 and June 8, 2000, respectively.

(Sgd.) LUTGARDO B. BARBO Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: APR 08 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
