Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Fourteenth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9776]

AN ACT DECLARING AUGUST 10 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN SAN JOSE CITY, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. August 10 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the City of San Jose, Province of Nueva Ecija to commemorate its creation as a city.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE President of the Senate

(Sgd.) PROSPERO C. NOGRALES Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 16, 2008 and August 24, 2009, respectively.

(Sgd.) EMMA LIRIO-REYES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) MARILYN B. BARUA-YAP Secretary General House of Representatives
