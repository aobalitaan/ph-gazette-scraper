Congress of the Philippines

Metro Manila

Twelfth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of July, two thousand three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9249]

AN ACT RENAMING THE BULACAN NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL STATE COLLEGE (BNASC) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ILDEFONSO, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, AS THE BULACAN AGRICULTURAL STATE COLLEGE (BASC)

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION. 1. The Bulacan National Agricultural State College (BNASC) in the Municipality of San Ildefonso, Province of Bulacan, is hereby renamed as the Bulacan Agricultural State College (BASC).

SEC. 2. All laws or decrees inconsistent with the provision of this Act are hereby deemed amended accordingly.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved:

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on September 3, 2002 and November 24, 2003, respectively.

(Sgd.) OSCAR G. YABES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: FEB 19 2004

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
