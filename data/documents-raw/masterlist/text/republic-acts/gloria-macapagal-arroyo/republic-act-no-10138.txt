[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10138]

AN ACT CONVERTING SHOE AVENUE IN BARANGAYS SAN ROQUE, STA. ELENA AND STO. NIÑO, CITY OF MARIKINA INTO A NATIONAL ROAD

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Shoe Avenue in barangays San Roque, Sta. Elena and Sto. Niño, City of Marikina is hereby converted into a national road.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Public Works and Highways shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement the provision of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE President of the Senate

(Sgd.) PROSPERO C. NOGRALES Speaker of the House of Representatives
