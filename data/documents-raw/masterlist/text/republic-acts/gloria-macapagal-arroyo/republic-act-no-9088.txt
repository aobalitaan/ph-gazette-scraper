Metro Manila

Eleventh Congress

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9088]

AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 17 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALAMBAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU TO COMMEMORATE THE DEATH ANNIVERSARY OF PRESIDENT RAMON B. MAGSAYSAY, SR.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In commemoration of the death anniversary of President Ramon B. Magsaysay Sr. in Mount Manunggal, Balamban, Cebu, March 17 of every year is hereby declared as a special working holiday in the Municipality of Balamban, Province of Cebu.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) AQUILINO Q. PIMENTEL JR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 6, 2001 and October 2, 2000, respectively.

(Sgd.) LUTGARDO B. BARBO Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: APR 08 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
