[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9641]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF KAPITAN RAMON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KAPITAN RAMON, CITY OF SILAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL TO DON FELIX T. LACSON MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of a National High Schoo l. — The name of Kapitan Ramon National High School in Barangay Kapitan Ramon, City of Silay, Province of Negros Occidental is hereby changed to Don Felix T. Lacson Memorial National High School.

SEC. 2. Effectivity . — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette .

Approved: May 29, 2009
