Republic of the Philippines

Congress of the Philippines

Metro Manila

Eleventh Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9041

AN ACT NAMING THE NATIONAL ROAD FROM CROSSING CORONACION TO BARANGAY TALUBANGI IN THE CITY OF KABANKALAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL AS JUSTICE JESUS Y. PEREZ MEMORIAL HIGHWAY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled

SECTION 1. Justice Jesus Y. Perez was the first and only Kabankalanon to serve as Justice to the Philippine Court of Appeals.

SEC. 2. To perpetuate his memory, the national road from Crossing Coronacion to Barangay Talubangi in the City of Kabankalan, Province of Negros Occidental is hereby named Justice Jesus Y. Perez Memorial Highway.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved.

(Sgd.) AQUILINO PIMENTEL JR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 7, 2000 and February 5, 2000, respectively.

(Sgd.) LUTGARDO B. BARBO Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: MAR 22 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
