Republic of the Philippines

Congress of the Philippines

Metro Manila

Eleventh Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9030

AN ACT RENAMING THE ASINGAN-STA. MARIA BRIDGE IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF ASINGAN AND STA. MARIA IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN AS THE NARCISO RAMOS BRIDGE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled

SECTION 1. The Asingan-Sta. Maria Bridge connecting the municipalities of Asingan and Sta. Maria in the Province of Pangasinan is hereby renamed Narciso Ramos Bridge in honor of the late Narciso Ramos as a fitting tribute to a life devoted to public service, for his invaluable contributions to nation-building, and for his inspiring leadership and exemplary statesmanship worthy of admiration and emulation by the present and future generations of Filipinos.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved.

(Sgd.) AQUILINO PIMENTEL JR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate January 24, 2000 and February 5, 2000, respectively.

(Sgd.) LUTGARDO B. BARBO Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: MAR 05 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
