Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Eleventh Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty fourth day of July, two thousand.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9047]

AN ACT INCREASING THE NUMBER OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO TO THE SANGGUNIANG PANLALAWIGAN OF RIZAL FROM THE PRESENT ONE (1) SANGGUNIAN MEMBER TO TWO (2) MEMBERS, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED EIGHTY-FIVE HUNDRED AND EIGHT

Be it enacted by the Senate and the House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled.

SECTION 1. Section 54 of Republic Act No. 8508, otherwise known as the Charter of the City of Antipolo, is hereby amended to read as follows:

“SEC. 54. Election of Provincial Governor and Sangguniang Panlalawigan Members for the Province of Rizal. — (A) The qualified voters of the City of Antipolo shall be qualified to vote in the elections for provincial governor, vice governor, and the city’s representatives to the sangguniang panlalawigan of the Province of Rizal; and

“(b) In the local elections following the 1998 elections and all local elections thereafter, only the qualified voters of the City of Antipolo shall be qualified to vote for two (2) sangguniang panlalawigan members who shall be the city’s representatives to the sangguniang panlalawigan of the Province of Rizal.”

SEC. 2. All existing laws or parts thereof inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby repealed accordingly.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) AQUILINO PIMENTEL JR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 14, 1999 and February 8, 2001, respectively.

(Sgd.) LUIGARDO B. BARBO Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: March 22, 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
