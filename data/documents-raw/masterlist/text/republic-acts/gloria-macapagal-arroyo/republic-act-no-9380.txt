Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Thirteenth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand six.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9380]

AN ACT DECLARING MAY 6 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILAR, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON TO COMMEMORATE THE DEATH ANNIVERSARY OF MAYOR MANUEL T. SIA

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. To commemorate the death anniversary of Mayor Manuel T. Sia, Municipal Mayor of the Municipality of Pilar, Province of Sorsogon, May 6 of every year is hereby declared a special working holiday in the said municipality.

SEC 2.This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 14, 2005 and December 18, 2006, respectively.

(Sgd.) OSCAR G. YABES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: MAR 06 2007

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
