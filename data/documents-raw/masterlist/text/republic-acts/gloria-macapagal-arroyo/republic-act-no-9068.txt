Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Eleventh Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand.

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9068

AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 5 OF EVERY YEAR AS “REMEMBRANCE DAY IN ORMOC CITY” A REGULAR WORKING HOLIDAY IN ORMOC CITY, TO COMMEMORATE THE NOVEMBER 5, 1991 TRAGEDY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November 5 of every year is hereby declared as “Remembrance Day in Ormoc City”, a regular working holiday for the City of Ormoc, Province of Leyte.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in at least two (2) national newspapers of general circulation.

Approved,

(Sgd.) AQUILINO Q. PIMENTEL JR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) ARNULFO P. FUENTEBELLA JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

The Act which is consolidation of House Bill No. 7271 and Senate Bill No. 1861 was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 19, 2000 and October 2, 2000, respectively.

(Sgd.) LUTGARDO B. BARBO Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: APR 08 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
