Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Fourteenth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9779]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LAMO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LAMO, MUNICIPALITY OF DUPAX DEL NORTE, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA TO DUPAX DEL NORTE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of a National High School. — The name of Lamo National High School in Barangay Lamo, Municipality of Dupax del Norte, Province of Nueva Vizcaya is hereby changed to Dupax del Norte National High School.

SEC 2. Location of the Main Campus. — The main campus of the Dupax del Norte National High School shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Lamo, Municipality of Dupax del Norte, Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

SEC 3. Effectivity . — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette .

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE President of the Senate

(Sgd.) PROSPERO C. NOGRALES Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 28, 2008 and August 17, 2009, respectively.

(Sgd.) EMMA LIRIO-REYES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) MARILYN B. BARUA-YAP Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: NOV 18 2009

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
