Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Fourteenth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9747]

AN ACT RENAMING THE OCCIDENTAL MINDORO NATIONAL COLLEGE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO AS THE OCCIDENTAL MINDORO STATE COLLEGE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Occidental Mindoro National College in the Municipality of San Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro is hereby renamed as the “Occidental Mindoro State College”.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE President of the Senate

(Sgd.) PROSPERO C. NOGRALES Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 12, 2008 and August 24, 2009, respectively.

(Sgd.) MARILYN B. BARUA-YAP Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: November 10, 2009

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
