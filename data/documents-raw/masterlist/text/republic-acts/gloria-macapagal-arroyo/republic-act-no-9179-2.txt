Republic of the Philippines

Congress of the Philippines

Metro Manila

Twelfth Congress

First Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand one.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9179]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LAVEZARES AGRICULTURAL AND INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL, MUNICIPALITY OF LAVEZARES, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR TO BASILIO B. CHAN MEMORIAL AGRICULTURAL AND INDUSTRIAL SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Lavezares Agricultural and Industrial School in the Municipality of Lavezares, Province of Northern Samar is hereby changed to Basilio B. Chan Memorial Agricultural and Industrial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 6, 2002 and August 7, 2002, respectively.

(Sgd.) OSCAR G. YABES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: NOV 13 2002

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
