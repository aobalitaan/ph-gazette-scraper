H. No. 1228

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1163]

AN ACT AMENDING AND REPEALING CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED NINE HUNDRED TWENTY, REGARDING PUBLIC WORKS PROJECTS FOR THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item 44( z ) for the Province of Rizal, paragraph ( e ), title B, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty, is amended to read as follows:

P81,000.00”

SEC. 2. Items 2 ( ag ) and 2 ( ah ) for the Province of Rizal, paragraph ( a ), title H, section one of Republic Act Numbered Nine hundred twenty, are repealed.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect as of June twenty, nineteen hundred fifty-three.

Approved, June 18, 1954.
