REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 3274

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6719

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SAN ANDRES ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ANDRES, MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO MIGUEL ARANDA MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the San Andres Elementary School in Barangay San Andres, Municipality of Malvar, Province of Batangas, is hereby changed to Miguel Aranda Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 17, 1989.
