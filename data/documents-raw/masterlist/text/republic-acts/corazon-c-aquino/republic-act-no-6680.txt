H. No. 126

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6680

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LINAPACAN, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, TO MUNICIPALITY OF GAUDENCIO E. ABORDO

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Municipality of Linapacan, Province of Palawan, is hereby changed to Municipality of Gaudencio E. Abordo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval by a majority of votes cast in a plebiscite to be conducted in accordance with law.

Approved, November 4, 1988.
