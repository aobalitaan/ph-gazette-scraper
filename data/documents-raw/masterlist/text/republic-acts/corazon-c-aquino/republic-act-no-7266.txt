H. No. 3917

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7266

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE CELESTINO GALLARES MEMORIAL HOSPITAL IN TAGBILARAN CITY, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, FROM TWO HUNDRED (200) TO TWO HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE (225) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Celestino Gallares Memorial Hospital in Tagbilaran City, Province of Bohol, is hereby increased from two hundred (200) to two hundred twenty-five (225) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 20, 1992.
