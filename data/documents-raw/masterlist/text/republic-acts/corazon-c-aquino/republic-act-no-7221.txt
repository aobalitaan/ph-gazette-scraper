REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 24731

Fifth Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7221

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE WAWA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN SAN AGUSTIN VILLAGE (FORMERLY SITIO WAWA-BUKID) IN BARANGAY MOONWALK, MUNICIPALITY OF PARAÑAQUE, METRO MANILA, TO SAN AGUSTIN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Wawa Elementary School in San Agustin Village (formerly Sitio Wawa-Bukid) in Barangay Moonwalk, Municipality of Parañaque, Metro Manila, is hereby changed to San Agustin Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 11, 1992.
