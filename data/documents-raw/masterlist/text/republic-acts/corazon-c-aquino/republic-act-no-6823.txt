REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 15571

Third Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6823

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE IBA ESTE PRIMARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALUMPIT, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, TO DR. VICENTE REYES MEMORIAL SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Iba Este Primary School in the Municipality of Calumpit, Province of Bulacan, is hereby changed to Dr. Vicente Reyes Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, December 18, 1989.
