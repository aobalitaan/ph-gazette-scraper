REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 5958

Fifth Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7231

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE KABANKALAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KABANKALAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Kabankalan District Hospital located in the Municipality of Kabankalan, Province of Negros Occidental, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 19, 1992.
