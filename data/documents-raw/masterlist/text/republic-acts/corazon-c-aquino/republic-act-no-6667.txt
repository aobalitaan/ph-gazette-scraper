H. No. 1702

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6667

AN ACT RENAMING THE JANIUAY NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JANIUAY, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS THE DON TIBURSIO A. LUTERO NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Janiuay National Comprehensive High School in the Municipality of Janiuay, Province of Iloilo, is hereby changed to Don Tibursio A. Lutero National Comprehensive High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1988.
