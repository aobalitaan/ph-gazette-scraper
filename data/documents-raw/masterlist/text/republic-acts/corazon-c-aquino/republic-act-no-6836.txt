REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

No. 14010

Third Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6836

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE AGRIFINA CIRCLE, A CIRCULAR ROAD NEAR THE LUNETA PARK IN THE CITY OF MANILA, TO TEODORO F. VALENCIA CIRCLE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the circular road located within the vicinity of Luneta Park, City of Manila, is hereby changed from Agrifina Circle to Teodoro F. Valencia Circle.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, January 5, 1990.
