H. No. 1684

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6673

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE GENERAL TRIAS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GENERAL TRIAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO PAARALANG PANG-ALAALA KAY DIEGO MOJICA (DIEGO MOJICA MEMORIAL SCHOOL)

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the General Trias Elementary School in the Municipality of General Trias, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Paaralang Pang-alaala kay Diego Mojica (Diego Mojica Memorial School).

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, September 5, 1988.
