REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 575

Fifth Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7284

AN ACT CONVERTING THE RURAL HEALTH UNIT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GATTARAN, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, INTO A FIVE-BED CAPACITY HEALTH INFIRMARY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE GATTARAN HEALTH INFIRMARY, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The rural health unit in the Municipality of Gattaran, Province of Cagayan, is hereby converted into a five-bed capacity health infirmary, to be known as the Gattaran Health Infirmary.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 24, 1992
