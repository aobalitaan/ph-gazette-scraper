H. No. 291

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7256

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE BAIS DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN BAIS CITY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Bais District Hospital in the City of Bais, Province of Negros Oriental, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 20, 1992.
