H. No. 21500

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6817

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BOBON SCHOOL OF FISHERIES IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BOBON, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR, TO ELADIO T. BALITE MEMORIAL SCHOOL OF FISHERIES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Bobon School of Fisheries in the Municipality of Bobon, Province of Northern Saniar, is hereby changed to Eladio T. Balite Memorial School of Fisheries.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, December 18, 1989.
