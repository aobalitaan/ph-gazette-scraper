H. No. 7083

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6738

AN ACT DECLARING MAY FIVE OF EVERY YEAR AS OZAMIZ DAY AND A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL AND ITS COMPONENT CITIES IN HONOR OF THE LATE SENATOR JOSE F. OZAMIZ

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. May Five of every year is hereby declared as Ozamiz Day and a special working holiday in the Province of Misamis Occidental and the component cities thereof in honor of the late Senator Jose F. Ozamiz.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, August 4, 1989.
