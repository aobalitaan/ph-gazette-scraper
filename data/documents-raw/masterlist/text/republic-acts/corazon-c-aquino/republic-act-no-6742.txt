H. No. 8995

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6742

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BATAAN NATIONAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES IN BALANGA, BATAAN, TO MEDINA LACSON DE LEON NATIONAL SCHOOL OF ARTS AND TRADES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Bataan National School of Arts and Trades in Balanga, Bataan, is hereby changed to Medina Lacson de Leon National School of Arts and Trades.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, August 4, 1989.
