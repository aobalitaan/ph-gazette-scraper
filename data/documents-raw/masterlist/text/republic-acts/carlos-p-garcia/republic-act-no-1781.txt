H. No. 6927

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1781]

AN ACT AMENDING ITEM ( d ) GROUP II, PARAGRAPH (43), SUBSECTION ( f ) OF SECTION TWO OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN, ENTITLED “AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS.”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item ( d ), group II, paragraph (43) Quezon Province, subsection ( f ) of section two of Republic Act Numbered Four hundred eleven is amended to read as follows:

Lavides Bridge, Atimonan Completion and construction of protection works for the approaches of the bridge and the adjacent landing

P20,000.00”

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 21, 1957.
