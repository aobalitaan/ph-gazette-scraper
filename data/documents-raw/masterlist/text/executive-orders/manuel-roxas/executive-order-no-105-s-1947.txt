MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 105

AUTHORIZING THE ACTING AIRPORT ADMINISTRATOR OF THE INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT AT NICHOLS FIELD TO EXECUTE AND SIGN LEASE CONTRACTS

In order to enable the Acting Airport Administrator of the International Airport at Nichols Field to carry out expeditiously the functions entrusted to him under Executive Order No. 100, dated October 23, 1947, I, Manuel Roxas, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority conferred upon me by section 567 of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended by Commonwealth Act No. 261, do hereby authorize the said Acting Airport Administrator to execute and sign on behalf of the Republic of the Philippines all contracts of lease involving real properties within the International Airport at Nichols Field.

Done at the City of Manila, this 22nd day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-seven, and the Independence of the Philippines, the second.

(Sgd.) MANUEL ROXAS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) N. ROXAS Acting Executive Secretary
