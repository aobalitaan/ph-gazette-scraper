MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 116

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 85, SO AS TO POSTPONE THE ORGANIZATION OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MISAMIS

It appearing that the chief municipal officials of the municipality of Calamba, Province of Occidental Misamis, created under Executive Order No. 85, dated September 3, 1947, have not been appointed and have not qualified and, consequently, the corporate existence of the said municipality has not begun, I, Manuel Roxas, President of the Philippines, do hereby postpone to February 1, 1948 the organization of said municipality.

Done at the City 0f Manila, this 1st day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the second.

(Sgd.) MANUEL ROXAS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) N. ROXAS Acting Executive Secretary
