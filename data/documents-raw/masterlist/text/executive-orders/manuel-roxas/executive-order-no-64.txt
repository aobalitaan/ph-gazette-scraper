MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 64

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 304, DATED OCTOBER 8, 1040, AS AMNEDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 26, DATED NOVEMBER 15, 1946, CREATING INVESTIGATING COMMITTEES ON VETERANS’PENSIONS

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, Manuel Roxas, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend paragraph one of Executive Order No. 304, dated October 8, 1940, as amended by Executive Order No. 26, dated November 15, 1946, by adding at the end of said paragraph the following proviso:

“Provided, however, That the per diems and traveling expenses that may be incurred by the members of the Investigating Committees in connection with their work as such shall be paid from the funds from which their respective salaries are being paid.”

Done at the City of Manila, this 24 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the first.

(Sgd.) MANUEL ROXAS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) EMILIO ABELLO Chief of the Executive Office
