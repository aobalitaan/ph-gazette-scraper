MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 323

REVISING FURTHER EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 135, DATED DECEMBER 31, 1937, AS AMENDED, ESTABLISHING A CLASSIFICATION OF ROADS

Executive Order No. 135, dated December 31, 1937 amended, is hereby further amended, so as to include the following roads in the classification of National Roads:

Done at the City of Manila this fifth day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

(sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
