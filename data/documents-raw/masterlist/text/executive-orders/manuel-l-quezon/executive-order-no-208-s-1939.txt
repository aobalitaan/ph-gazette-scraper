MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 208

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 194, DATED MARCH 13, 1939, ENTITLED “REVISING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 135, DATED DECEMBER 31, 1937, ESTABLISHING A CLASSIFICATION OF ROADS”

Pursuant to the recommendation of the National Transportation Board created by Executive Order Numbered forty-five, dated July sixth, nineteen hundred and thirty-six, the Lumbang – Caliraya Dam Site from Km. 106.14 to Km. 116.90 Length Km. 10.76, is hereby included in the classification of national roads in the Province of Laguna.

Executive Order Numbered One hundred ninety-four, dated March thirteenth, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, is hereby amended accordingly.

Done at the City of Manila, this fifteenth day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President:

JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
