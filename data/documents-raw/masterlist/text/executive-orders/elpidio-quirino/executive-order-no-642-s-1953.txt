MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 642

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 607 DATED JUNE 27, 1953, ENTITLED “CREATING A COMMISSION TO ASCERTAIN AND STUDY THE CONDITIONS OBTAINING IN THE RECLAIMED AREAS AND FORESHORE LANDS IN THE DISTRICT OF TONDO, MANILA”

The first paragraph of Executive Order No. 607 dated June 27,1958, entitled “Creating a Commission to Ascertain and Study the Conditions Obtaining in the Reclaimed Areas and Foreshore Lands in the District of Tondo, Manila,” is hereby amended to read as follows:

“For the purpose of ascertaining and studying the actual conditions obtaining in the reclaimed areas and foreshore lands in the District of Tondo, Manila, with a view to the adoption of adequate and effective measures to solve the housing and other social problems in said areas, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create a Commission composed of the following:

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eighth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
