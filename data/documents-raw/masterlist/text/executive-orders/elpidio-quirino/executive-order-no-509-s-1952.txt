MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 509

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 499, DATED APRIL 24, 1952, ENTITLED “CREATING A COMMITTEE TO MAKE A FINAL STUDY AND ADVISE THE PRESIDENT ON THE PROPOSED REVISION OF THE TRADE AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE PHILIPPINES AND THE UNITED STATES.”

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Executive Order No. 499, dated April 24, 1952, by designating the Honorable Jose Roy, Congressman from Tarlac, as a member of the Committee therein created, to replace the Honorable Estanislao Fernandez, Congressman from Laguna.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
