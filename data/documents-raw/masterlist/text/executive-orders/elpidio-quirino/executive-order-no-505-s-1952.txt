MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 505

ABOLISHING THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF SUNGUD IN THE PROVINCE OF LANAO AND ANNEXING THE SAME TO THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF SAGUIARAN, SAME PROVINCE.

Upon the recommendation of the Provincial Board of Lanao and pursuant to the provisions of section 68 of the Revised Administrative Code, the municipal district of Sungud in the Province of Lanao is hereby abolished and annexed to the municipal district of Saguiaran, same province.

This Order shall take effect on July 1, 1952.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
