MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 550

TERMINATING THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS AT THE PANIQUI-ON AND TALAVE TOLL BRIDGES, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL NEGROS

The total cost of the Paniqui-on and Talave Toll Bridges, in the Province of Occidental Negros, plus interest at the rate of four per cent per annum, having been fully recovered, as certified in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 3500, as amended, it is hereby ordered that the collection of tolls at the Paniqui-on and Talave Toll Bridges be terminated.

This Order shall take effect upon receipt hereof by the Provincial Treasurer of Occidental Negros.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
