MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 584

TERMINATING THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS AT THE TUMAUINI BRIDGE, ISABELA

The total cost of the Tumauini Bridge in the Province of Isabela, plus interest thereon at the rate of four per cent per annum, having been fully recovered, as certified in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 3500, as amended, it is hereby ordered that the collection of tolls at the Tumauini Bridge be terminated.

This Order shall take effect upon receipt of copy hereof by the Provincial Treasurer of Isabela.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
