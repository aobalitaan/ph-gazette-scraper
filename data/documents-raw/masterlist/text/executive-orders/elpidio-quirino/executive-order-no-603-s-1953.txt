MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 603

DESIGNATING THE PRICE STABILIZATION CORPORATION TO DISTRIBUTE AND ALLOCATE FLOUR TO FLOUR CONSUMERS

By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 4 of Republic Act No. 509, entitled “An Act declaring national policy, authorizing the President of the Philippines for a limited period to fix ceiling prices of commodities and to promulgate rules and regulations regarding prices of commodities to effectuate such policy, and authorizing the appropriation of a certain sum for the purpose,” and upon the recommendation of the Price Administration Board I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines’, do hereby designate the Price Stabilization Corporation to supervise the sale and distribution of flour to flour consumers throughout the Philippines.

This Order shall take effect July 1, 1953.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
