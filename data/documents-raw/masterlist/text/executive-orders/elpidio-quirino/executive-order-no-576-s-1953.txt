MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 576

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 330, DATED JULY 1, 1950, CREATING THE NATIONAL SECURITY COUNCIL

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of National Defense, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Executive Order No. 330, dated July 1, 1950, as amended by Executive Order No. 437 dated April 28, 1951, so as to make Col. Leoncio S. Tan (Inf.), Secretary of the National Security Council, vice Captain Rafael Pargas, PN.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
