MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 525

TERMINATING THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS AT THE SUAGUE BRIDGE, ILOILO

The total cost of the Suague Bridge in the Province of Iloilo, plus interest thereon at the rate of four percent per annum, having been fully recovered, as certified, in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 3500, as amended, it is hereby ordered that the collection of tolls at the Suague Bridge be terminated

This Order shall take effect upon receipt of copy hereof by the Provincial Treasurer of Iloilo.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and the Independence of Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
