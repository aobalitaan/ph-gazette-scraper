MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 435

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 361, EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 381, EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 382, AND EXECUTIVE ORDER No. 404, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 of Republic Act No. 509, entitled “An Act declaring national policy, authorizing the President of the Philippines for a limited period to fix ceiling prices of commodities and to promulgate rules and regulations regarding prices of commodities to effectuate such policy, and authorizing the appropriation of a certain sum for the purpose,” and upon the recommendation of the Price Administration Board, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby order:

SECTION 1. Section 4 of Executive Order No. 382, dated December 12, 1950, in so far as it sets up the maximum selling prices of Mello-Cup Coffee, is hereby repealed by setting up new ceiling prices as follows:

FOODSTUFF (IMPORTED)

Coffee Roasted:

Mello-Cup brand

SEC. 2. Section 1 of Executive Order No. 361, dated November 3, 1950, is hereby amended by increasing and setting up new ceiling prices for Kleenex and Kotex as follows:

DRUGS, MEDICINES AND HOUSEHOLD REMEDIES (IMPORTED)

Commodity

SEC. 5. This Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines
