MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 439

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 318, DATED MAY 25, 1950, CREATING THE INTEGRITY BOARD

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Executive Order No. 318, dated May 25, 1950, creating the Integrity Board, as amended by Executive Order No. 344, dated September 7, 1950, and Executive Order No. 358, dated November 1, 1950, so as to have said body composed of the following:

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) TEODORO EVANGELISTA Executive Secretary
