MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 582

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED 449 AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

By virtue of the powers vested in me by section 3 of Republic Act No. 509, entitled “An Act declaring national policy, authorizing the President of the Philippines for a limited period to fix ceilings prices of commodities and to promulgate rules and regulations regarding prices of commodities to effectuate such policy, and authorizing the appropriation of a certain sum for the purpose,” and upon the recommendation of the Price Administration Board, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby order:

SECTION 1. Section 1 of Executive Order No. 449, dated June 9, 1951, is hereby amended by decreasing and setting up new ceiling prices of locally manufactured grade pads, intermediate pads and composition notebooks as follows:

SCHOOL SUPPLIES (LOCAL)

SEC. 2. The ceiling prices fixed in this Order include the 7 per cent sales tax.

SEC. 3. This Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
