MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 607

CREATING A COMMISSION TO ASCERTAIN AND STUDY THE CONDITIONS OBTAINING IN THE RECLAIMED AREAS AND FORESHORE LANDS IN THE DISTRICT OF TONDO, MANILA

For the purpose of ascertaining and studying the actual conditions obtaining in the reclaimed areas and foreshore lands in the District of Tondo, Manila, with a view to the adoption of adequate and effective measured to solve the housing and other social problems in said areas, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create a Commission composed of the following:

The Commission shall submit its report and recommendations to the President of the Philippines within six months from the date of this Order.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
