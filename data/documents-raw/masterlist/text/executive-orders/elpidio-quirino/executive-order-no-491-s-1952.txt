MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 491

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24, DATED NOVEMBER 12, 1946, AS AMENDED, ENTITLED ”CREATING THE NATIONAL ADVISORY HEALTH COUNCIL”

The second paragraph of Executive Order No. 24, dated November 12, 1946, as amended, is hereby further amended so as to make the President of the Philippines Mental Health Association a member of the National Advisory Health Council therein created.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
