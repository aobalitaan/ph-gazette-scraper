MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 522

DECLARING THE LUMBAN BRIDGE IN THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA AS A NATIONAL TOLL BRIDGE AND FIXING A SCHEDULE FOR THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS THEREON.

By virtue of the powers vested in my by Section 5 of Commonwealth Act No. 330, and upon recommendation of the Secretary of Public Works and Communication, I, ELPIDIO QUIRINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the Lumban Bridge in the province of Laguna as a national toll bridge and direct that the following tools be collected thereon effective as of August 1, 1949:

The proceeds derived from the collection of tolls as herein provided shall be applied to the repair and maintenance of Lumban Toll B ridge.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and the Independence of Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
