MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 656

REDUCING THE 1952 TAXES ON ALL REAL PROPERTIES LOCATED IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE.

The public interest so requiring, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the. powers vested in me by section 53 of Commonwealth Act No, 470, as amended by Commonwealth Act No. 534, and upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Finance, do hereby remit the 50 per cent of the 1952 taxes on all real properties located in the Province of Camarines Norte on condition that all unpaid taxes and penalties corresponding to 1951 and previous years and the remaining 50 per cent of the 1952 taxes shall be paid on or before December 31, 1953.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and. fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eighth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
