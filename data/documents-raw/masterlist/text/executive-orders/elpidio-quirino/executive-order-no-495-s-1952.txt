MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 495

TERMINATING THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS AT THE MAKATO BRIDGE, CAPIZ

The total cost of the Makato Bridge in the Province of Capiz, plus interest thereon at the rate of four per cent per annum, having been fully recovered, as certified in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 3500, as amended, it is hereby ordered that the collection of tolls at the Makato Bridge be terminated.

This Order shall take effect upon receipt of copy hereof by the Provincial Treasurer of Capiz.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
