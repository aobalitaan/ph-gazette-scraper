MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 569

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 536 DATED OCTOBER 21, 1952, BY CHANGING THE COMPOSITION OF THE NATIONAL TRAFFIC COMMISSION CREATED THEREIN.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Executive Order No. 536, dated October 21, 1952, creating the National Traffic Commission, by designating the President of the Philippine Safety Council or his representative, in lieu of its Executive Director, as a member of said Commission.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
