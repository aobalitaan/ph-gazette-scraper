MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 316

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 278, DATED OCTOBER 10, 1949, AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 288, DATED OCTOBER 21, 1949, CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY OF JULITA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE.

Executive Order No. 278, dated October 10, 1949, as amended by Executive Order No. 288, dated October 21) 1949, is further amended by segregating the barrios of of San Pablo and Posod from the municipality of Julita, Province of Leyte, and restoring said barrios to the municipality of Burawen, same province.

Executive Order No. 288, dated October 21, 1949, is hereby revoked.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fourth.

ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) TEODORO EVANGELISTA Executive Secretary
