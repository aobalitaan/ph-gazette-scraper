MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 330

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 300 DATED OCTOBER 12, 2000, WHICH DECLARED AND DELINEATED THE LOAY PORT ZONE UNDER THE ADMINISTRATIVE JURISDICTION OF THE PHILIPPINE PORTS AUTHORITY
