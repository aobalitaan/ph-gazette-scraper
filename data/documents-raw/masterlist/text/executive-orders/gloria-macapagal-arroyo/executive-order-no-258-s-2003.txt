MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 258

RECONSTITUTING THE NATIONAL TASK FORCE DIWALWAL

WHEREAS , the Office of the President had issued Executive Order No. 217 dated June 17, 2003 creating the National Task Force Diwalwal to address the urgent need to formulate specifically focused policies and coordinate the implementation of measures thereof by all concerned government agencies with respect to the vital concerns of the government and to rationalize all mining activities in the area;

WHEREAS , recent developments in the course of implementing Executive Order No. 217 have necessitated the reconstitution of the National Task Force Diwalwal to streamline its functioning and better address issues affecting stakeholders as represented by concerned government agencies not included in the original composition of the Task Force.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:
