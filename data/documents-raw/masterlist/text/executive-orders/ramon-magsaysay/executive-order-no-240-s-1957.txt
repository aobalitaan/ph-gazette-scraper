MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 240

ABOLISHING THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAGUDPUD IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, AND RESTORING THE DIFFERENT BARRIOS COMPOSING THIS MUNICIPALITY TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANGUI, SAME PROVINCE, TO WHICH THEY BELONGED PRIOR TO THEIR BECOMING PARTS OF PAGUDPUD.

In the interest of the public welfare and pursuant to the provisions of section sixty-eight Of the Revised Administrative Code, the Municipality of Pagudpud in the province of Ilocos Norte, as organized by Executive Order No. 13, dated February 14, 1954, is hereby abolished, and the barrios thereof are hereby restored to the municipality of Bangui, same province, to which they formerly belonged.

The abolition and restoration herein made shall take effect upon the expiration of thirty days from the date of receipt by the Mayor of Pagudpud from the Provincial Governor of Ilocos Norte of a true copy of this order.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
