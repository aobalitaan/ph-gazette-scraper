MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 72

TERMINATING THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS AT THE DIGDIG TOLL BRIDGE, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA.

The total cost of the Digdig Toll Bridge, in the Province of Nueva Ecija, plus interest at the rate of 4% per annum, having been fully recovered, as certified in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 3500, as amended, it is hereby ordered that the collection of tells at the Digdig Toll Bridge be terminated.

This Order shall take effect upon receipt of copy hereof by the Provincial Treasurer of Nueva Ecija.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-four, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the ninth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
