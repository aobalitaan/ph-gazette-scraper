MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 69

TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF OTEIZA, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO, FROM ITS PRESENT SITE TO THE BARRIO OF MARIHATAG, SAME MUNICIPALITY

Upon the recommendation of the Provincial Board of Surigao and pursuant to the provisions of section 68 of the Revised Administrative Code, the seat of government of the municipality of Oteiza, Province of Surigao, is hereby transferred to the barrio of Marihatag, same municipality.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-four, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the ninth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
