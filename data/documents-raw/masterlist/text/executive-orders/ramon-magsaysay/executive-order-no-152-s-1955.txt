MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 152

CREATING THE BARRIOS OF MABINIT AND BAGACAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LEGASPI, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

Pursuant to the provisions of section 68 of the Revised Administrative Code, there are hereby created in the municipality of Legaspi, province of Albay, two barrios to be known as the barrios of Mabinit and Bagacay to consist respectively of the sitios with the same names of the barrios of Bogña and Homapon of said municipality.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila this 31st day of December, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and fifty-five, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the tenth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARIANO YENKO, JR. Assistant Executive Secretary
