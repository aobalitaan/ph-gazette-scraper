MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 229

TERMINATING THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS AT THE INDIANA TOLL BRIDGE, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA

By virtue of the powers vested in me by low and upon the recommendation of the Undersecretary of Public Works and Communications, I, RAMON MAGSAYSAY, President of the Philippines, do hereby order the termination of the collection of tolls at the Indiana Toll Bridge in Bambong, Nueva Vizcaya.

This order shall take effect upon receipt of a copy hereof by the Provincial Treasurer of Nueva Vizcaya.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
