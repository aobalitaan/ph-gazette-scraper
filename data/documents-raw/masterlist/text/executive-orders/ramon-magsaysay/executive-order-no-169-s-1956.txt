MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 169

ORGANIZING THE SITIO OF DAHA-DIOT IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, INTO A REGULAR BARRIO

Upon the recommendation of the Provincial Board of Leyte and pursuant to the provisions of section 68 of the Revised Administrative Code, the portion of the territory of the municipality of San Isidro, province of Leyte, known as the sitio of Daha-diot, is hereby constituted into an independent barrio under the name of Daha-diot. This reduces accordingly the territory of the barrio in the municipality of San Isidro to which the sitio of Daha-diot now belongs.

The changes herein made shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the tenth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FORTUNATO DE LEON Acting Executive Secretary
