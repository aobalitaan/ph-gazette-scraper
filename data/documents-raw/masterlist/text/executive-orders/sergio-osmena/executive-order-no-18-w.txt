OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES WASHINGTON, D. C.

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 18-W

PROVIDING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE COMMONWEALTH OF THE PHILIPPINES DURING THE FISCAL YEAR ENDING JUNE THIRTIETH, NINETEEN HUNDRED AND FORTY-FIVE.

By virtue of the powers vested in me be the Constitution and pursuant to the provisions of the Emergency Powers Law, I, Sergio Osmeña, President of the Philippines, do hereby set aside the following sums, or so much thereof as may be necessary out of any funds of the Government of Commonwealth of the Philippines not otherwise appropriated, for the operation of said Government now temporarily established in Washington D.C., United States of America, for the fiscal year ending June thirtieth, nineteen hundred and forty-five:

A. OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

I. SALARIES AND WAGES

II. SUNDRY EXPENSES

Sundry expenses, including travelling expenses, postal, telegraph, and other communication service; supplies and materials; illumination and power services; rental and repairs of buildings; printing of reports and documents; and other services
