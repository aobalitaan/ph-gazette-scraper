MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 43

APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATING EXPENSES OF THE ALABANG LABORATORIES FOR THE PERIOD FROM FEBRUARY 27 TO JUNE 30, 1945

WHEREAS, whatever balance remaining in the Revolving Fund of the Alabang Laboratories created under Commonwealth Act No. 245 had disappeared or has now become worthless as a result of Japanese occupation; and

WHEREAS, it is deemed imperative to continue the activities of this institution to meet the need for vaccines and sera to prevent any possible outbreak of epidemics in the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SERGIO OSMEÑA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and pursuant to the provisions of the Emergency Powers Law, do hereby order that:

SALARIES AND WAGES

Epidemiology, Statistics and Public Health Administration

Sanitary Bacteriology and Immunology

Power Plant

Parasitology

Sanitary Engineering, Industrial, Physiology and Chemistry

Clerical Unit

SUNDRY EXPENSES

FURNITURE AND EQUIPMENT

SPECIAL PURPOSE

Done at the City of Manila, this 26th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Forty-Five, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the tenth.

(Sgd.) SERGIO OSMEÑA President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOSE S. REYES Secretary to the President
