MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 39

APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF ONE MILLION PESOS FOR CIVILIAN DEFENSE

By virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and laws of the Philippines, I, SERGIO OSMEÑA, President of the Philippines, do hereby order;

Done at the City of Manila, this tenth day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-five, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the tenth.

(Sgd.) SERGIO OSMEÑA President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOSE S. REYES Secretary to the President

Source: Malacañang Records Office

Office of the President of the Philippines. (1945). [Executive Order Nos. : 1W – 109]. Manila : Malacañang Records Office.
