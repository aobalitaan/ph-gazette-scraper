MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 443

By the President of the Republic of Philippines

Modifying the rates of export duty on certain exported articles as provided in R.A. 1937, as amended by P.D. 230, with respect to copper ore and concentrates.

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by Section 515 of P.D. 230, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby direct and order that:

SECTION 1. The article specifically listed hereunder as classified under Section 514 of Republic Act 1937 as amended by P.D. 230 shall pay the following rates of export duty:

Mineral Products

(1) Metallic ores and concentrates

(a) Copper 2%

SECTION 2. This Order shall take effect on January 1, 1975.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of December, in the Year of Our Lord, 1974.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
