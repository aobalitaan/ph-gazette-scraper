MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 103

REQUIRING THAT ALL PROPERTIES BELONGING TO THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT, OTHER THAN LANDS SOLD OR LEASED UNDER THE PUBLIC LAND ACT, SHALL BE ADVERTISED OR BIDDED FOR SALE OR LEASE ONLY UPON PRIOR APPROVAL OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby order that all properties belonging to the national government, other than lands sold or leased under the Public Land Act, be advertised or bidded for sale or lease only upon prior approval of the Office of the President.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
