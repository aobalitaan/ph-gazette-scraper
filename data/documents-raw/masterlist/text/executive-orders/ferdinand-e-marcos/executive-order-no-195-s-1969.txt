MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 195

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 180 DATED APRIL 25, 1969, ENTITLED “RECONSTITUTING THE COMMITTEE ON WOOD INDUSTRIES DEVELOPMENT UNDER THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.”

The second paragraph of Executive Order No. 180 dated April 25, 1969, is hereby amended as follows:

“2. The Committee shall be composed of fourteen members:

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
