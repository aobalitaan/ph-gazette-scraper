MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 317

CREATING THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE OF THE SUMMER WORKCAMP FOR THE CHILDREN OF THE TONDO FIRE VICTIMS

Pursuant to the power vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby create the Executive Committee of the Summer Workcamp for the children of the Tondo fire victims.

The Committee shall be composed of the following members:

The committee shall have the following functions:

1. To formulate policies concerning logistical and personnel requirements governing the project.

2. To organize the different committees that will implement the workcamp proper.

3. To establish areas of cooperation and integration among the different agencies involved.

4. To make recommendations on the actual implementation of the project.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
