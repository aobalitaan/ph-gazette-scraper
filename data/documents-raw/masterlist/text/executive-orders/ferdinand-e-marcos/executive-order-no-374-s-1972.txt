MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 374

CREATING A CABINET COMMITTEE TO STUDY AND RECOMMEND SOLUTIONS TO THE CHINESE PROBLEM.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby create a cabinet committee to study and recommend solutions to the local Chinese problem as it affects national security and protection of our sovereignty, composed of the following:

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
