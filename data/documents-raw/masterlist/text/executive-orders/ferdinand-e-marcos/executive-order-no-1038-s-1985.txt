MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 1038

MODIFYING THE RATES OF DUTY ON IMPORTED RAYON ARTICLES AS PROVIDED UNDER PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1464, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE TARIFF AND CUSTOMS CODE OF 1978

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 926 provided that effective January 1, 1985, the tariff duty for regenerated fibres (including rayon) shall be increased to 20%;

WHEREAS, there has been a delay in the implementation of the rayon project and local textile mills have to import raw materials for, their requirements;

WHEREAS, there is a need to maintain the 1984 tariff rate of 10% for rayon to enable the textile industry to be competitive in the world market;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, and Section 401 of the Tariff and Customs Code, as amended, do hereby order and direct:

SECTION 1. The rate of import duty under heading Nos. 56.01 and 56.02 of the Code are hereby amended to read as follows:
