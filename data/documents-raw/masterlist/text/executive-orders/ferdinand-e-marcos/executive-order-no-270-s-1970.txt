MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 270

The composition of the National Parks Development Committee created under Executive Order No. 30 dated January 14, 1963, as amended, is hereby further amended to read as follows:

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

By the President:
