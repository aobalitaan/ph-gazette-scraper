MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 142

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 92 DATED OCTOBER 23, 1967, CREATING THE GENERAL EMILIO AGUINALDO NATIONAL CENTENNIAL COMMISSION

The composition of the GENERAL EMILIO AGUINALDC NATIONAL CENTENNIAL COMMISSION created under Executive Order No. 92 dated October 23, 1967, is hereby amended to read as follows:

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
