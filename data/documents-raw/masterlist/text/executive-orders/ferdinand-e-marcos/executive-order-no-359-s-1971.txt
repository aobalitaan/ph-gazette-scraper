MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 359

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 225 DATED APRIL 21, 1970, ENTITLED “RECONSTITUTING THE FINANCIAL POLICY COMMITTEE AND RENAMING IT FINANCIAL AND FISCAL POLICY COMMITTEE”.

Executive Order No. 225 dated April 21, 1970, is hereby amended to include the Executive Secretary or his representative as member of the Financial and Fiscal Policy Committee.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
