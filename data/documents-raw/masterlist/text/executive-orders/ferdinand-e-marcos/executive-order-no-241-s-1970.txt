MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 241

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 225 DATED APRIL 21, 1970, WHICH CREATED THE FINANCIAL AND FISCAL POLICY COMMITTEE.

Executive Order No. 225 dated April 21, 1970, is hereby amended by including as invited to attend the Financial and Fiscal Committee created therein the Chairman of the Senate Finance Committee and the House Appropriations Committee when the Financial and Fiscal Policy Committee considers the priority listing of projects under continuing appropriations.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Executive Secretary
