MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 265

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 85 DATED DECEMBER 15, 1956, ESTABLISHING A RAT-PROOF BUILDING ZONE IN MANILA PORT

Executive Order No. 85 dated December 15, 1954 is hereby amended by reducing the rat-proof zone in the North Harbor port area as follows:

“1. A line parallel to and at a distance of One Hundred Forty Six (146) meters from the North Harbor slipline from Pier 4 to Pier 16 and extending from point one (1) at the South boundary of the Tondo Foreshore Land on Azcarraga Extension as defined under Republic Act 1597 to point two (2) at the south boundary of the Tondo Youth Foundation Reservation, as indicated in the attached plan;

2. A line coinciding and co-equal with the west boundary line of the Tondo Youth Foundation Reservation extending from point three (3) to point four (4), as indicated in the attached plan;

3. A line parallel to and at a distance of forty (40) meters from the east boundary of the existing North Harbor Service Road and extending from point five (5) at the north boundary of the Tondo Youth Foundation Reservation to point six (6) on the south boundary of the Manila Slaughterhouse Compound, as indicated in the attached plan.”

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
