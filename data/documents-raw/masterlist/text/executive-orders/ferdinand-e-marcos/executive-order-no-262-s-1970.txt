EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 262

Pursuant to the authority vested in me by Section 90 of the National Defense Act as amended, in conjunction with Executive Order No. 4, S-46, as amended by Executive Order No. 10, S-46, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby prescribe the following rates of monthly quarters allowances for commissioned officers of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, effective this date:

The Secretary of National Defense shall promulgate rules and regulations to carry out the provisions hereof.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of September, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twenty fourth.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
