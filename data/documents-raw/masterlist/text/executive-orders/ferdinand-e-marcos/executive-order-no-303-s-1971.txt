MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 303

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 11, SERIES OF 1966, AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 220 DATED MARCH 21, 1970, CREATING THE ANTI-SMUGGLING ACTION CENTER

Executive Order No. 11, series of 1966, creating the Anti-Smuggling Action Center, as amended by Executive Order No. 220 dated March 21, 1970, is hereby further amended by placing the Center under the direct supervision and control of the Secretary of Finance.

All orders, rules and regulations inconsistent herewith are hereby modified or repealed accordingly.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
