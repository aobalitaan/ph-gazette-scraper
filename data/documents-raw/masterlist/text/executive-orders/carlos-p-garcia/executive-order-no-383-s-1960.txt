MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 383

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 72, DATED DECEMBER 3, 1936, ESTABLISHING A CLASSIFICATION OF PORTS

Executive Order No. 72, dated December 3, 1936, is hereby further amended so as to include the following ports in the classification of National Ports open to coastwise trade:

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fourteenth.

(Sgd.) CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) NATALIO P. CASTILLO Executive Secretary
