MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 23

PRESCRIBING THE BASE PAY OF OFFICERS OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES

Pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 90′ of the National Defense Act, as amended, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby prescribed the following rates of base pay for the officers of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, effective as of October 1, 1962:

Executive Order No. 4, dated July 11, 1946, as amended, is hereby modified accordingly.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-two.

(Sgd.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Executive Secretary
