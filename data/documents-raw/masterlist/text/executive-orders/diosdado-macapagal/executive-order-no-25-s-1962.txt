MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 25

DECLARING THE AGOO BEACH ROAD, LA UNION, FROM KILOMETER 236.295 TO KILOMETER 242.957 OR 6.662 KILOMETERS AS NATIONAL SCEONDARY ROAD

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Public Works and Communications and pursuant to the provisions of section 26, Article VIII, of Republic Act No. 917, in relation to Executive Order No. 113, series of 1955, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the Agoo Beach Road, La Union, from kilometer 236.295 to kilometer 242.957 or 6.662 kilometers, as national secondary road, provided that the minimum road right-of-way shall not be less than 20.00 meters.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-two.

(Sgd.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Executive Secretary
