MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 191

DECLARING THE MUNICIPAL PORT OF POCTOY, ODIONGAN, ROMBLON, AS A NATIONAL PORT OPEN TO COASTWISE TRADE.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Executive Order No. 72, series of 1936, so as to declare the municipal port of Poctoy, Odiongan, Romblon, as a national port open to coastwise trade.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

(Sgd.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
