MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 131

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 106, DATED OCTOBER 1, 1964, CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY OF MINA IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOILO

Pursuant to the provisions of section 68 of the Revised Administrative Code, Executive Order No. 106, dated October 1, 1964, creating the municipality of Mina in the province of Iloilo is hereby amended so as to include the barrio of Nabitasan of the municipality of Pototan, said province, as one of the barrios to comprise the municipality of Mina.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

(Sgd.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
