PRESIDENTIAL DECREE No. 368

FURTHER EXTENDING THE EFFECTIVITY OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 333, AS AMENDED, DATED NOVEMBER 26, 1973

As an emergency measure against the current international oil crisis, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers in me vested by the Constitution as Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, and pursuant to Proclamation No. 1081, dated September 21, 1972, as amended, do hereby order and decree that the effectivity of Presidential Decree No. 333, as amended by Presidential Decree No. 343, be further extended to a period of ninety (90) days from and after the date of the effectivity of this Decree.

This Decree shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of January, 1974.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
