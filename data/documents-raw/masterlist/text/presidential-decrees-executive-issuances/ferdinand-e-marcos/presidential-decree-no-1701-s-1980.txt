MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1701

TRANSFERRING, CEDING AND CONVEYING TO THE PHILIPPINE TOURISM AUTHORITY CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT LOCATED IN TERNATE, CAVITE, FOR TOURISM DEVELOPMENT PURPOSES

By virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby transfer, cede and convey to the Philippine Tourism Authority (PTA) for tourism development purposes a certain parcel of land of the public domain of the government located in Ternate, Cavite, which parcel of land is generally described as follows:

“A parcel of land (Lot 4) being a portion of SWO-000145 situated in Barrio Zapang, Municipality of Ternate, Province of Cavite. Bounded on points 1-20 by SWO-04-000145, Calumpang Naval Reservation, points 21-26 by Lot SWO-04-000496. The PTA may not alienate the land or any portion thereof without the prior authority of the President of the Philippines, although it may encumber the same with any government financing institution for development purposes.

All laws, Proclamation No. 307 dated November 20, 1967, orders and regulations inconsistent herewith are hereby repealed and modified accordingly.

This Decree shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
