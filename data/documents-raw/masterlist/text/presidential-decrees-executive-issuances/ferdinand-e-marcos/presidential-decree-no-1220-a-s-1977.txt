PRESIDENTIAL DECREE No. 1220-A

AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1220, ENTITLED “GRANTING CITIZENSHIP TO DESERVING ALIENS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.”

Upon recommendation of the Special Committee on Naturalization, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Annex “A” of Presidential Decree No. 1220 by including therein the name of Mr. Ricardo K. Ching, who has been found deserving and recommended by the Committee for citizenship but whose name was inadvertently omitted in the final list.

This Decree shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Source: Malacañang Records Office
