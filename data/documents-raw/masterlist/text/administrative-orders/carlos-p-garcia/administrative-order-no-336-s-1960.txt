MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 336

MODIFYING ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 327, DATED MARCH 26, 1960, BY CONSIDERING AS RESIGNED JUSTICE OF THE PEACE RICARDO GARCIA OF JOLO, SULU.

After considering the representations contained in the motion for reconsideration filed by Mr. Ricardo Garcia, who was removed from office as justice of the peace of Job, Sulu, under Administrative Order No. 327, dated March 26, 1960, particularly the circumstance of his having been in the public service for more than 29 years, I am satisfied that he deserves a less severe penalty.

WHEREFORE, Administrative Order No. 327, dated March 26, 1960, is hereby modified in the sense that Mr. Ricardo Garcia is considered resigned, effective as of the receipt of said Administrative Order No. 327, without prejudice to receiving retirement benefits under the law.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

(Sgd.) CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) NATALIO P. CASTILLO Executive Secretary
