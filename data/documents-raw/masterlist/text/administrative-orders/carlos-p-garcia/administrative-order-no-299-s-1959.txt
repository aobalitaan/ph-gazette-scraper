MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 299

AMENDING ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 293, DATED APRIL 17, 1959, AUTHORIZING THE USE OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES SHIP LAPU-LAPU FOR THE 1959 FLOATING EXPOSITION AND CULTURAL MISSION TO NEIGHBORING COUNTRIES IN THE FAR EAST.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Administrative Order No. 293, dated April 17, 1950, by including Formosa among the countries to be visited by the 1959 Floating Exposition and Cultural Mission on board the Philippine ship Lapu-Lapu.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

(Sgd.) CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
