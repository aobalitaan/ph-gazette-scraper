MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 300

CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE THIRTEENTH ANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES ON JULY 4, 1959

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby create a National Committee to formulate plans and devise ways and means for the appropriate celebration of the Thirteenth Anniversary of the Republic of the Philippines on July 4, 1959. The Committee shall be composed of the following:

The Committee shall meet at the call of the Chairman and, for the purpose of discharging its functions, may create such subcommittees as may be necessary.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

(Sgd.) CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
