MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 158

GRANTING FULL AND PLENARY PARDON TO FELICIANO M. NAGAC, FORMER SERGEANT, ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES.

Feliciano M. Nagac, who was dishonorably discharged from the service as sergeant, Armed Forces of the Philippines, on September 30, 1955, for violations of the 95th and 97th Articles of War, is hereby granted full plenary pardon.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-five, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the tenth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARIANO YENKO, JR. Assistant Executive Secretary
