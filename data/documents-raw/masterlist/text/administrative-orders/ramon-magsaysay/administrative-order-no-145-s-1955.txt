MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 145

AMENDING ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 134, DATED AUGUST 13, 1955, ENTITLED “CREATING AN INTERDEPARTMENTAL COMMITTEE ON ATOMIC ENERGY.”

Pursuant to the power rested in me by law, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Administrative Order No. 134, dated August 13, 1955, by designating Mr. Alfredo G. Eugenio, Civil Defense. Adviser, as a member of the Interdepartmental Committee on Atomic Energy therein created.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-five, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the tenth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
