MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 189

AMENDING THE COMPOSITION OF THE NATIONAL COMMITTEE CREATED IN ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 178 DATED FEBRUARY 10, 1956, TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE TENTH ANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES ON JULY 4, 1956

The composition of the National Committee created in Administrative Order No. 178 dated February 10, 1956, to take charge of the Tenth Anniversary Celebration of the Republic of the Philippines on July 4, 1956, is hereby amended as follows:

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the tenth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FORTUNATO DE LEON Acting Executive Secretary
