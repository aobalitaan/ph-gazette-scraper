MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 167

AUTHORIZING THE PAYMENT OF THREE MONTHS’ SALARY IN ADVANCE TO OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES OF THE GOVERNMENT

In view of the present emergency, and pursuant to the provisions of Commonwealth Act No. 670 the payment of salaries in advance not exceeding a period of three months to all officers and employees of the National, provincial, city, or municipal governments, including their agencies and instrumentalities, is hereby authorized.

Done at the City of Manila this twelfth day of December, Nineteen hundred and forty-one, in the year of Our Lord, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
