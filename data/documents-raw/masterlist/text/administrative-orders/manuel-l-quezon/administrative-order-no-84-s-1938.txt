MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 84

RESTORING THE NAME OF THE PHILIPPINE CONSTABULARY BAND

The Philippine Military Band shall hereafter be called the Philippine Constabulary Band. For purposes of administration, however, the band shall continue to belong to the Philippine Army.

All orders in conflict herewith are hereby revoked.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-eight day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines
