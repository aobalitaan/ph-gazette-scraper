MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 214

AMENDING ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 157 DATED FEBRUARY 1, 1990 CREATING THE NATIONAL ORGANIZING COMMITTEE FOR THE FOURTH ASIAN AND PACIFIC MINISTERIAL CONFERENCE ON SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT TO INCLUDE THE SECRETARY OF THE INTERIOR AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT, IN LIEU OF THE SECRETARY OF NATIONAL DEFENSE, AS MEMBER OF THE NATIONAL ORGANIZING COMMITTEE

I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby amend Administrative Order No. 157 dated February 1, 1990 creating the National Organizing Committee for the Fourth Asian and Pacific Ministerial Conference on Social Welfare and Development scheduled to be held in Manila in October 1991, to include the Secretary of the Interior and Local Government, in lieu of the Secretary of National Defense, as Member of the National Organizing Committee.

This Administrative Order shall take effect immediately

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) OSCAR M. ORBOS Executive Secretary
