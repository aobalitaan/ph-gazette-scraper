MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 3-A

MODIFYING THE COMPOSITION OF THE COMMITTEE CHARGED WITH THE DISPOSITION OF PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT PROPERTIES LOCATED IN TOKYO AND KOBE, JAPAN

SECTION II of Administrative Order No. 3 is hereby modified and shall henceforth read as follows:

This Administrative Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of August in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred And Eighty-Six.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary
