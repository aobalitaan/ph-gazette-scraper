MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 3-C

MODIFYING THE COMPOSITION OF THE COMMITTEE CHARGED WITH THE DISPOSITION OF PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT PROPERTIES LOCATED IN TOKYO AND KOBE, JAPAN

SECTION II of Administrative Order No. 3 is hereby further modified and shall henceforth read as follows:

This Administrative Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of January in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
