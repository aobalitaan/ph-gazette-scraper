MALACAÑANG Manila

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 413

ORDERING THE REINSTATEMENT WITH PROPER PENALTY OF MESSRS. ABDULFARID MAGLANGIT, ALFRED E. LISONDRA AND JOVITO L. DOMINGO, ALL OF THE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION AND CULTURE

WHEREAS, Mssrs. Maglangit, Lisondra and Domingo were among the 2,000 officials and employee ordered separated from the service on September 19, 1975;

WHEREAS, the Department of Education and Culture has recommend the reinstatement of Mssrs. Maglangit, Lisondra and Domingo.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby order the immediate reinstatement of Messrs. ABDULFARID MAGLANGIT, ALFRED E. LISONDRA and JOVITO L. DOMINGO in the Department of Education and Culture, provided that during the period from the date of their separation from the service up to their reinstatement in accordance with this Order, they shall be considered under suspension without pay.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of July in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
