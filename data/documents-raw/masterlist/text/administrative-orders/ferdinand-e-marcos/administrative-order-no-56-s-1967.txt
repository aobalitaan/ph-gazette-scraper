MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 56

DIRECTING THAT TRANSMITTAL OF COMPLAINTS AND GRIEVANCES TO THE PRESIDENT, THROUGH THE FACILITIES OF THE BUREAU OF TELECOMMUNICATIONS, BY MEANS OF WIRE OR TELEGRAPHIC SERVICES, BY FARMERS CONCERNING PROBLEMS ON RICE PRODUCTION, BE CHARGED AT MINIMAL COST OF TEN CENTAVOS

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby direct that the Bureau of Telecommunications make available its facilities to farmers by transmitting their complaints and grievances to the President concerning problems on rice production, by means of wire or other telegraphic services, at the minimal cost of ten centavos.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
