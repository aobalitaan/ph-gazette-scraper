MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 214

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO UNDERTAKE THE PREPARATIONS FOR THE INAUGURATION OF THE “DAMBANA NG KAGITINGAN” ON APRIL 9, 1970

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby create a committee to undertake the preparations for the inauguration of the “DAMBANA NG KAGITINGAN” on April 9, 1970, composed of the following:

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary
