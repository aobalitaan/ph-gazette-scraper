November 6, 1972

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 390

In the interest of the administration of justice and pursuant to the provisions of Section 89 of Republic Act No. 3068, otherwise known as the Charter of the City of Tacloban, Mr. LUCIO F. SAAVEDRA, Municipal Judge of Babatngon, Leyte, is hereby authorized to hear and decide the cases enumerated hereunder belonging to the City Court of Tacloban, at dates to be determined by him, in view of the inhibition therein of City Judge Nicolas A. Superable, Jr. and Acting City Judge Francisco Martinez:

(Sgd.) VICENTE ABAD SANTOS Secretary of Justice
