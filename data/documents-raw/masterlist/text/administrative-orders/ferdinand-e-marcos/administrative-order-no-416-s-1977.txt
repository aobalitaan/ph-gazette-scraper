MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 416

ORDERING THE REINSTATEMENT IN THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE WITH PROPER PENALTY OF ONE HUNDRED FIFTY-THREE (153) OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES RECOMMEND BY THE APPEALS COMMITTEE CREATED UNDER ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 370. SERIES OF 1975

WHEREAS, the following one hundred fifty-three (153) officials and employees were among the 2,000 officials and employees ordered separated from the service in September, 1975:

I. DEPARTMENT OF NATURAL RESOURCES

A. Bureau of Mines—

B. Bureau of Lands—

C. Bureau of Fisheries & Aquatic Resources—

II. DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC HIGHWAYS

III. DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS, TRANSPORTATION &COMMUNICATIONS

A. Bureau of Public Works—

B. Bureau of Telecommunications—

C. Bureau of Posts—

B. Bicol River Basin Council—

E. Land Transportation Commission—

F. Specialized Regulatory Board

IV. DEPARTMENT OF TRADE

V. GOVERNMENT AGENCIES/CORPORATIONS/COMMISSIONS

A. Commission on Elections—

B. Commission on National Integration—

C. Commission on Population—

D. Development Bank of the Philippines

E. Government Service Insurance System

F. Metropolitan Waterworks &Sewerage System

G. National Economic Development Authority—

H. National Power Corporation—

I. Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office—

J. Philippine National Bank—

K. Securities & Exchange Commission—

WHEREAS, the Appeals Committee created under Administrative Order No. 370, series of 1975, to take cognizance of petitions for reconsideration of dismissed officials and employees has recommended their reinstatement in the government service:

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby order their immediate reinstatement in t416heir respective offices provided that during the period from the date of their separation from the service up to their reinstatement in accordance with this Order, they shall be considered under suspension without pay.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

BY THE PRESIDENT:

(Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
