lAs/ISCs are organized and developed to serve as NIA's partners 
in the efficient and effective operation and maintenance (OSGM) of 
irrigation systems. Their capability to manage their organizations 
and undertake O & M responsibilities are developed and sustained 
through training, close monitoring and the provision of other

lAs/ISCs, there is a need for an instrument for assessing the 
level of their functionality or performance.
