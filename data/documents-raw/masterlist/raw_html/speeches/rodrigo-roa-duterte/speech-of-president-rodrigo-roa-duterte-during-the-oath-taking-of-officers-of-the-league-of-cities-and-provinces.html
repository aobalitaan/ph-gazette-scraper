Speech of President Rodrigo Roa Duterte During The Oath Taking Of Officers Of The League Of Cities And Provinces

July 28, 2016

SPEECH OF
PRESIDENT RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE
DURING THE OATH TAKING OF OFFICERS OF THE LEAGUE OF CITIES AND PROVINCES
[Delivered on July 27]

Kindly sit down. Thank you.

I’ll dispense with the acknowledgements [of our ?] presence. Lahat naman tayo nandito magkakilala na. And, you know I was for the longest time Mayor of Davao City. A good 23 years. Kaya naiilang ako pag magtindig-tindig kayo, even doon sa National Security Council.

Sanay ako ng… kaya I’d rather you address me as Mayor kasi mas maganda pakinggan. Ang Presidente kasi minsan… naiilang ako. I am not used to this kind of life.

Alam mo ‘nong unang panahon, I said, I was the weeping boy. Lahat na pati ‘yung extrajudicial killings. [Padre?] sino ba naman may gustong pumatay ng tao. We cannot build a nation over the dead bodies of our own citizens.

Ang problema ‘yung binigyan ng armas lahat, everybody was holding a licensed gun. Umuso na ‘yung criminality. Kasi ‘yung… ang style nila noon sa Davao: Umakyat sila ng bahay. ‘Yung mga mayaman, iho-hold nila, at maghingi sila ng ransom. Hindi man maibigay kaagad kasi pera mo nasa bangko. And you know in the bank you cannot withdraw one million, two million, three million at one time para ibigay mo doon sa kidnappers. So they stayed in the house. They just while away their time, raping the wife, raping the maids and the daughters.

Kaya ‘yun ang umpisa ng [rage?] ko diyan sa criminality. Eh, prosecutor ako eh. Akalain mo pagdating ng droga ‘yun na. Nagre-rape ng bata. Pinapatay ‘yung tatay. Sinusuntok ‘yung nanay kung hindi magbigay ng pera. Alam ninyo ‘yan. Nagnanakaw.

Then, came the problem of criminality. Because they have to have the fix everyday, kinukuha nila sa ibang tao. So they wanted money to come to their hands at the expense of somebody else’s life. Kasi kaya nandito, sinasaksak nila, hold up dito, hold up doon. Until such time that it became a [vogue?], a fad sa mga kriminal na mag-hit muna. Whatever the professions. Ano pa sila, in the practice. Halos lahat tumitira na kasi hindi na matatakot. You lose the sense of fear pag naka-shabu ka.

Ang problema niyan, sabi ng mga forensics, one year of using shabu almost everyday would shrink the brain of a person. Nandito ba si PDEA? Sid, maupo ka lang. You just join us, dito. He’s General Lapeña who’s our PDEA. He was my chief of police many years ago. He earned his force in Davao while also working as special council. Mga junior officers [inaudible] sila. He coasted along to be my chief of police and [RT?]. Together with, si Delfin Lorenzana, the Secretary of Defense. He was assigned there. He brought in the first… second ranger scout rangers sa Davao, Martial Law years.

So ngayon, I was appalled by the misery and agony. Sabi ng PDEA three years, two years ago, that there are three million addicts in this country. So that’s why we have this serious problem. Kinausap ko ‘yung congressman, mag-tulong kayo because ang — Sa kampanya ko, I was never a serious and [was more?] of a man when I was campaigning and I was saying: “Do not destroy the youth of this land.”

Now, I cannot divulge to you everything that transpired or what was given to us by the security agencies all over the world. But, if you are asking me now. “Bakit itong,” sabi, “si Duterte ‘yung maliliit lang. Where’s the big fish? Bakit ‘yung mga pobre?”

Walang big fish dito. Kasi ‘yung noon meron. Pero when they… because they have this mansions and cars, enjoying life. But when they realize that killing time was coming, nag-sibatan na. Now, they are using modern technology. They have this high, refined digital map. He will give the orders to his lieutenants here. Sabihin lang niya, itong sa mapa tingnan niya. “O, i-focus mo sa Tondo, o Pandacan, o dito. I-drop ninyo diyan banda sa… tapos ‘yung pera kunin mo, may naka-parking doon. May babae kunin mo lang ‘yung bayad.”

O, how are we supposed to deal with the problem? Sinabi ko na… wanted to confront them. Hindi lang dito. It’s everywhere, in Asia. Kaya ‘yung, blanko ‘yung amin diyan kung sino ‘yung mga drug lords, walang mga mukha because they are out of the country. I would need international warrant of arrest to arrest or go to war. But can we afford it? Walang mag—

So dito, you know, let me, I said — Baka ma-offend kayo. Lolo ko Chinese. Pure Chinese — Lam. Nanay ko, Maranao. Ganito ‘yan, ang Instik, okay lang ‘yan sila sa wholesale, wholesale. ‘Yung may ginansya. Maliit nga ‘yung tubo pero kung the entire Pandacan pati Tondo mo, at the end of the day ang collection mo milyon.

Kaya ang nakikita nila, sabi cradling a dead cadaver there. Ma-drama pa. Because there is the apparatus. Unless I destroy the apparatus, they will have a problem. Three presidents from now would not be able to solve it. Masyado kasing corrosive ang pera eh. So they can buy, you know, judges, fiscals, the police, mayors, governors, ganon talaga eh, pera.

And one time, there was this operation in Davao. You know sabi niya: “Buhayin mo lang ako, pati ‘yung dalawang [inaudible] na babae. We’ll deliver you 500 million.” Eh kung ikaw pulis ka lang, ang sweldo mo maski na prosecutor, maski na judge. Bigyan ka ng 200 million for the case o 50 million. Talagang babagsak ‘yan.

Alam mo, sinasabi ko na sa inyo. Wala na ako sa politika. I cannot hanker for more, presidente na ako. But in this fight, I will stake my honor, my life and the presidency. Impeach? Sige, impeach. At least libre na ako. O ‘di sige na-impeach ako, ‘di alis na ako. Ganon. Wala man akong problema diyan. Wala akong ilusyon dito sa… In the first place, ayaw ko nga tumakbo eh. Kaya sabi ko: Nadisgrasya. Nandito ako. Malaki ang problema ko. Tulungan ninyo ako. Kaya ako sabi ko talaga, mag-pranka ako, hindi ako ano diyan sa… tatapusin ko ito. Tatapusin ko. Ako ang inabot eh. Kayo eh. ‘Yan ang mahirap.

This… it’s not a… it’s a drug problem. It’s a drug crisis. 300… 170,000 surrenderees. Is that a police problem? The police cannot solve it. They are into it. Mind you, hindi ‘yan galing sa amin intriga, galing ‘yan sa intercept. A country friend of ours provided us the intercept. Hindi nga ako makaka-panood e. I have to go to every policeman. Totoo ito.

Kasi you know… pinaaral ka diyan sa PMA. Gastos ng gobyerno, four years. Pagka-opisyal mo, bigyan ka ng rango, career and honorable name. Tapos hanggang, then you waste everything by committing treason against your own country.

I cannot show you kasi ano e. It’s a matter of security. But kunwari lang dalhin ko doon and I will give you the matrix of how it’s being played. So, transshipment tapos dito nagluluto. Ngayon, wala na masyado kasi patay talaga eh.

This serious problem now reaching millions. Mabuti’t nandito kayo at tutulong kayo. So I’ve been trying to figure out how I can cope up with the problem. At tsaka sabihin ko sa inyo: Francis, alam mo kung ituro ka, takbo ka presidente. Walang… Anong ipalit mo doon

Nagpaloko lang kasi ako na… Gusto ko suntukin ‘yung — Hanggang pag-ano pa lang, pag-upo ko pa lang ng first day na hindi pa ako na-proclaim sa Davao, hanggang alas-tres, alas-kwatro.

Ngayon — started at 3 o’clock. Tapos pag-uwi ko. Magba-[barge?] ako, matulog. Wala na lahat. Pagod ka na, ‘yung problema. Tapos tinanong ko ang sweldo, 130 —‘Di mabuti pa nag-mayor ako. ‘Yun lang pala. Ewan ko nga kung may allowance pa ako dito o wala. Hindi ako makatanong. Wala man akong matanungan. Mag-akyat ako diyan. Wala — Totoo, alam mo man? What’s really pushing me to move is my love for my country. Mahal ko talaga ang bayan ko [applause] and everybody, alam ko.

There’s a huge problem. The enormity and the dimension of which has never been shown in the past in this country. Eh ang malas natin tayo ang inabutan. Eh ako. Alam mo — Anong sabihin ko? ‘Wag, you know that is bad. Ano ako, pari?

Because in this country alam ninyo ‘yan. Kasi kayo mga abugado. Nawala na ‘yung essence of fear to violate a law. Kasi nabibili na eh. With the short, corrosive effect of money, kayang bilihin, sad to say, hanggang sa itaas. Hindi matapos ang problema eh. May protector. Sila mismo ang nagluluto. Ngayon, hindi na, kasi alam ng mga big time, talagang yayarain sila. So nag-sibatan.

But, the problem is with the state of technology now, they can use ganon, that easy. So magtanong kayo, maski kami dito sa aming matrix, blangko ang mga pangalan… ay ang mukha. Ang pangalan nandyan. I don’t know if it’s really the… they’re using the real name. Mahirap ang labanan na ‘to. Kaya timely talagang punta kayo dito because I knew or I know that there is not so much space. Kaya sabi ko sa military, the Armed Forces, to open up their camps.

Kasi ‘yung mga military naman ang malalaking reservation. Open up your camps and I will [seat?] there something. Parang barracks style na… ‘Yung recalcitrant or those guys na ayaw talagang magpakulong o hindi na, it’s no longer of service to humanity because they are — Padala na lang natin doon.

We just to have to control their flow of movement. Ang masakit kasi, even the brightest of our children are into drugs. Binibiktima na. What I [inaudible] is I said the sense of accountability. So, how do you do it? Eh ‘di ang apparatus. Sirain mo talaga ang apparatus. Hanggang maubos, wala kang magawa. Kayo, may magawa kayo? Ilan ang inyo diyan ngayon, surrenderees? May magawa ba kayo diyan?

And let me put it bluntly to you, what can you do? [inaudible] Pasok mo sa rehab. Labas ‘yan ganon pa rin. So ‘yung, in the spirit of, you know, I said we cannot build a nation by killing people over the bodies of your fellow citizens.

But I’ll have to control. So ‘yung sira na, you have to check with them if they are talagang ma-resuscitate pa, ika nga. Lagay na lang natin sa diyan. Anyway, we don’t need any legal basis. The legal basis is we take him in for his own protection. That’s why we are allowed to arrest insane people. For compulsory confinement. Why? It is to protect him from harm and to protect the public. But eventually they have to move out.

Chief of Staff, sir, I assure you that just try to help us for the time being, but until such time that we’ll be able to build really ’yung parang talagang rehab centers all over the country. And it would take time and money.

But as a stopgap measure, just to come up with something that’s viable, lagay na lang natin sa mga kampo muna. High wire lang. Para… buti ‘yan. Matakot man sila. For the meantime na mag-rehab. So we have to spend so much about doctors. I know that because may rehab facility ako sa Davao. It’s a 50-bed capacity. Linagay ko ‘yan, ke sabi nila pinapatay ko raw ‘yung mga bata. ‘Wag kayo maniwala. Maski gusto ko, if I’m given that right, [inaudible] hindi ko kayang mag-patay ng bata pati babae, in the meantime na wala tayong space. Kasi pagka umaga, they have to, you know, exercise, that’s part of the rehab e. But ang nakita ko ang pinaka—

Kasi pagka umaga, they have to, you know, exercise, that’s part of the rehab e. But ang nakita ko ang pinaka. I mean Senator Sotto had a good [inaudible] for a, itong rehabilitation.

Pero, nakita ko ang pinaka— ‘Yung dumikit talaga, ang based on faith. ‘Yung sumasali sa ano… Karamihan nakita ko sa Davao, okay. Born again na ano… Sila, sila and medyo nakaka-manage na… to win daily away from drugs.

‘Yung iba wala. ‘Yung mga technical, technical turuan sila wala. So ‘yun ang problema ko. I… so much sa… but I said I have to destroy the apparatus and it’s a very cruel game in this world na you earn a living at the expense of your fellowmen.

Pag-tinamaan mo anak ko babae, lalaki, wala na, wala na, wala ng silbi. Should you deprived of a future, you know why. Hindi naman tayo mayaman. Pag tanda natin, we have to be with our – parang Chinese tayo, Filipino. Sila na ‘yan magbibili ng medisina, sila na ‘yung magpakain sa iyo. Wala, wala talaga, wala na tayong silbi sa mundo and you place in jeopardy my very own existence.

Baka patayin ako ng anak ko or you deprive me of a future life. Kasi ikaw diyan, karamihan niyan makita ko. Well isa diyan magtayo kayo ng home for the aged. Ako may home for the aged ako. Gasto ko 33 million. Comfortable life sila, marami ng natapos. Lahat may — Ayoko lang ‘yung mag-sugal. May TV everything, but ayoko ‘yun, maski ‘yung bara-baraha. Ayaw ko kasi mag-pustahan ‘yan, wala rin ibang, pero kawawa eh. Tayo maalanganin. Magpunta tayo ng home for the aged. Ba-biyahe pa ako ng Tarlac kasi hind ako kilala… Davao, kawawa ang… may Rody o. Andiyan lang sa—

Saan tayo pupunta? ‘Di sa anak natin. Eh, ngayon kung patayin mo ‘yung anak ko spiritually, saan ako papunta?

So, actually threatened tayong lahat eh. Hindi lang natin alam. Isipin mo lang, dalawang anak mo. Pag may-anak ka na adik sa bahay mo may problema ka na for all time. ‘Yan iba diyan, naghihiwalay ang mga asawa. Alam mo bakit? They start to blame each other. Tapos maghiwalay. Kagaya ng mamatayan ka ng anak. ‘Yung iba diyan if you cannot handle the agony, maghiwalay kayo mag-asawa. Because the other girl would always say: “Eh ikaw kasi eh [inaudible].” It’s not easy to lose a child. Dead or… it’s not easy to lose a child. Wala ng isip.

So, actually it’s our very existence, ang nakapusta dito. Kaya ako, sabi ko, ulitin ko: Ipupusta ko my honor, my life, even the presidency. I can let go of this. Kaya I’ve offered, I offered to Congress, if you can fast track the federal set-up, so that it would bring peace to Mindanao particularly.

I will deal with the Abu Sayyaf later on. It’s closely connected also with the aspiration of the Moro people. This historical injustice… [inaudible], sabi ko madalian ninyong… then you have a— My advice to you guys is do not go for a pure parliament. Patay tayo. This is not a country na walang… nag-lahat na sumusunod ng batas.

We have sadly and without offending my race, we have not reached that kind of level of civilization. Pwede lang mga ano… But you have to maintain the institution or the presidency.

My advice is copy, copy the French set-up. We have to have because ang Filipino gusto talaga niya mag-boto ng Presidente ‘yan. Hindi mo kunin sa kanila ‘yan eh. So wala man sila. Tingnan mo ang importante sa eleksyon. You’d really see, except the… ikaw mismo ang mayor. ‘Yan mga senador pati iba wala na, they lost their significance, ‘di ba? Minsan mag-sabi… bahala na kung sino, kung ayaw mo, kung ayaw mo mag-boto, ‘di huwag.

We take for granted that they will choose their own. Hindi natin dinidikdik ‘yan pagka… ito si senador, wala na ‘yan. Pero ikaw presidente pati mayor, ‘yan ang sinisigurado mo. ‘Yung konsehal, pwede nang matalo ‘yan, pwedeng manalo. Depende sa swerte niya sa buhay. Ganon ‘yan. So maintain the… if they can shorten the proceedings, you have a Constitution that’s federal. In three or two years, then you have to elect a president. Of course that would automatically, legally hindi na pwede ako pwede, so I will offer to resign, two years, if you have the federal setup, I would resign as President. Three years sibat ako, four years, ay salamat. And it would be really a… pag ako nagtra- trabaho ako… do not be, do not feel short changed. My will to, to work is there. But if you ask me if I’m happy, I am not. It’s a lonely, pag-uwi ko doon trabaho, tapos gigisingin [President knocks] “Sir, gising na sir kasi alas-tres…”

Minsan, when my father died and there was a temporary interruption sa — There were times na naglalakad ako at umiiyak ako pero sikreto lang… na I know that it’s the life, ganoon talaga ang buhay. But again, it’s getting late I, I would like to repeat my song, mahirap talaga ito mabigat. I would like also to thank Allan for expediting my sharing with you this moment.

Kumain na kayo? Kurot naman bakante na ‘no? Sige ihiwa… Kami pala wala pa. But, if there’s anything, ako wala na akong politika. Hindi naman ako maka-ano. Nandiyan na ako sa… I will never engage in — Except politically, maybe during election time. Pero kung maglapit kayo why the problem. Do not be hesitant. There’s no really reason for you to avoid me. Wala na ako. Tapos na ako.

Bilib ako totoo kasi Defense Secretary Delfin Lorenzana. Pagsabi ko sa order ko na any… the following day may folder na kaagad, ganon ang… tsaka ang gusto ko military. You know, do not hesitate to use the military, the police, and all government. Pero maasahan ninyo in terms of lahat ano… it’s only the military and the police. Military kasi pag punta ka sa kampo. Sabihin mo: “Saan ‘yung CO ninyo? “Wala dito.” “Sino ‘yung second? Sige kailangan ko ng — Without… Kaya sila in-increase ko ‘yung sweldo. Without any hesitation, pag sinabi mong maski saan… Mamasapano, you go there and die. Wala. Wala, kaya ito sila.

Sabi ko: My justification is I cannot run a country with a corrupt police. Huwag puro hingi dito, hingi doon at tumatanggap ng droga. Sabihin ko: At least meron sila, dahan-dahan lang, twice kayo, sinabi ko. Campaign promise ‘yun. Bakit, kung hindi, patay ka.

Ang mahirap ang starting salary, entry is 14,000. Kung nandito ka sa Maynila, paano mo pakainin ‘yung pamilya mo. Eh ‘di ganon ka. That’s really… pag hindi pa magkuha ang dimensions ng problema, wala talaga.

I will help you. Kasi do not, do not… Huwag mong haluan ‘yang utak mo ng politika, politika.

Ako, ang tao pag nag-shake hands sa akin, o nagsabi “[Adre?], surrender ako, talo na ako, tumba na ako, okay na ako.
