PROCLAMATION NO. 2247

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NOS. 548, DATED APRIL 19, 1969 and 217, DATED FEBRUARY 16, 1929, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE AMBUKLAO-BINGA WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE AND CENTRAL CORDILLERA FOREST RESERVE, RESPECTIVELY, FOR WATERSHED PURPOSES AND DECLARING AS ALIENABLE OR DISPOSABLE LANDS CERTAIN PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF LA TRINIDAD, TUBLAY AND ITOGON, PROVINCE OF BENGUET.

Upon the recommendation of the Director of Forest Development, concurred in by the Minister of Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Chapter IX of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended and by the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E.
