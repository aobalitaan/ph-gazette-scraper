PROCLAMATION NO. 936

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED IN THE CITY OF OLONGAPO, ISLAND OF LUZON, FOR THE USE OF THE CITY OF OLONGAPO
