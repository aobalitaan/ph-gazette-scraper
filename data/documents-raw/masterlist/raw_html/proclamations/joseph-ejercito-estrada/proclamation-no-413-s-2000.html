Proclamation No. 413, s. 2000

Signed on November 21, 2000

MALACAÑANG
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 413

DECLARING THE MAYON VOLCANO NATIONAL PARK SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF LIGAO, GUINOBATAN, CAMALIG, DARAGA, LEGAZPI CITY, STO. DOMINGO, MALILIPOT AND TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, ISLAND OF LUZON, AS PROTECTED AREA PURSUANT TO R.A. 7586 (NIPAS ACT OF 1992) AND SHALL BE KNOWN AS MAYON VOLCANO NATURAL PARK

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested upon me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby set aside and declare the Mayon Volcano National Park situated in the Municipalities of Ligao, Guinobatan, Camalig, Daraga, Legazpi City, Sto. Domingo, Malilipot and Tabaco, Province of Albay, Island of Luzon, as protected area under the category of Natural Park, subject to private rights and without prejudice to the rights of the indigenous peoples as provided for in R.A. 8371, DENR Administrative Order No. 93-02 and other related rules and regulations, if any, as described in DENR-NIPAS Map R-5-06.
