MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 224

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED AT BARANGAY MARIBULAN, MUNICIPALITY OF ALABEL, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI, ISLAND OF MINDANAO AS AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, BENIGNO S.
