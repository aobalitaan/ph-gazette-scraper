PROCLAMATION NO. 976

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 1970 (S. 1970) BY RECLASSIFYING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND, LOCATED AT OLD AIRPORT, BARANGAY MANDURRIAO, ILOILO CITY, PREVIOUSLY CREATED AND DECLARED AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK, TO A MIXED-USE SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY AND TOURISM), PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748
