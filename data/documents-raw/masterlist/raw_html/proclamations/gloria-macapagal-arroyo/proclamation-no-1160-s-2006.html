Proclamation No. 1160, s. 2006

Signed on October 17, 2006

MALACAÑANG
Manila

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1160

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 458, DATED AUGUST 29, 1994, “RESERVING THE FOREGOING TWENTY PARCELS OF LAND AT MANGGAHAN FLOODWAY COMPLEX FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF MEDIUM RISE SOCIALIZED AND LOW COST HOUSING PROJECT AND ITS ALLIED STRUCTURES FOR THE BENEFIT OF SOME 6,700 URBAN POOR FAMILIES AND THE DESERVING LANDLESS-HOMELESS EMPLOYEES OF THE PROVINCIAL GOVERNMENT OF RIZAL”

WHEREAS, there is an urgent need to further strengthen the implementation of controlling the escalating squatting at the Berm slope of the Manggahan Channel and the continuous devastation of the environment in the area;

WHEREAS, despite of the Ordinance and Laws regarding Anti-squatting, the Municipal and City Government have no consistent drive against informal settling but instead contributed by tolerating such illegal act at their respective area of jurisdiction;

WHEREAS, this illegal act contributed mainly in the pollution of the channel and the Laguna Lake, devastating the environmental sanitation and ecological significance of the area and the aquatic resources and marine life of the Lake through discharging of garbage and sewer waste directly into the channel;

WHEREAS, this act cannot be tolerated much less sanctioned by the Government, otherwise the implementation of the Anti-squatting Law and the Clean and Green objective will be frustrated and therefore, an amendment to this effect is an Order;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines and by virtue of the power vested in me by law and in relation to the recommendations of the Provincial Government of Rizal and other agencies of the government, PCRGA, PCUP, CDA, DENR to the draft feasibility study submitted by P.C. Cruz and Associates, approved by HUDCC, per Memorandum of Agreement, dated January 8, 2002, do hereby repeal, include and reserve;

(Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA
