Proclamation No. 576, s. 2004

Signed on March 10, 2004

MALACAÑANG
Manila

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 576

DECLARING MANLELUAG SPRING NATIONAL PARK SITUATED AT BARANGAY MALABOBO, MUNICIPALITY OF MANGATAREM, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN AS PROTECTED AREA AND ITS PERIPHERAL AREAS AS BUFFER ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT 7586 (NIPAS ACT OF 1992) AND SHALL BE KNOWN AS “MANLELUAG SPRING PROTECTED LANDSCAPE”

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby set aside and declare the Manleluag Spring National Park situated at Barangay Malabobo, Municipality of Mangatarem, Province of Pangasinan, as Protected Area under the category of Protected Landscape, subject to private rights and without prejudice to the rights of the indigenous people as provided for in R.A. No. 8371, DENR Administrative Order 02, Series of 1993, and other related rules and regulations, if any there be, as described in DENR NIPAS Map R0-1-01.

The Protected Area and its Buffer Zone shall be subject to actual ground survey and delineation.

These areas shall be under the management authority of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and shall be administered in accordance with the provisions of the NIPAS Act of 1992.

All contracts, permits and/or licenses for the extraction or utilization of natural resources already existing prior to this proclamation shall, subject to national interest, be respected until its expiration.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 10th day of March, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:
(Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO
Executive Secretary
