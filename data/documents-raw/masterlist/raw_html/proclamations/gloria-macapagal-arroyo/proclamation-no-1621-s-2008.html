PROCLAMATION NO. 1621

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAYS BUANOY AND ARPILI, BALAMBAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU TO FORM PART OF THE EXISTING WEST CEBU INDUSTRIAL PARK SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 96-405 dated 23 December 1996 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, several parcels of land located at Barangays Buanoy and Arpili, Balamban, Province of Cebu to form part of the existing West Cebu Industrial Park Special Economic Zone, with an aggregate area of TWO HUNDRED FORTY FIVE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY SIX (245,186) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
