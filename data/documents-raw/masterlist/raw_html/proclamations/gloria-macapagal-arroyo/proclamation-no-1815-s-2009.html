BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1815

DESIGNATING MT. MANTALINGAHAN MOUNTAIN RANGE SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF BATARAZA, BROOKE’S POINT, SOFRONIO ESPAÑOLA, QUEZON AND RIZAL, ALL IN THE PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AS PROTECTED LANDSCAPE AREA PURSUANT TO R.A. 7586 OR THE NATIONAL INTEGRATED PROTECTED AREAS SYSTEM (NIPAS) ACT OF 1992

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and the Palawan Council for Sustainable Development (PCSD) and pursuant to the powers vested upon me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby set aside and designate Mt. Mantalingahan Mountain Range situated in the Municipalities of Bataraza, Brooke’s Point, Sofronio Española, Quezon and Rizal, all in the Province of Palawan as “Protected Area under the NIPAS and shall be known as Mt. Mantalingahan Protected Landscape, subject to prior rights and without prejudice to the rights of indigenous peoples as provided for in RA 8371, DENR Administrative Order No. 93-02 and other related rules and regulations, if any there be, more particularly as described as follows:

Any valid contract, permit or license for the extraction or utilization of natural resources therein already existing prior to this Proclamation shall subject to national interest and existing laws, rules and regulations, be respected until its expiration. Areas covered by such contracts, permits or licenses which are found not viable for development after assessment and/or exploration shall automatically form part of the MMPL. Likewise, all property and private rights within the MMPL already existing and/or vested prior to this Proclamation shall be respected in accordance with existing laws.

The Mt. Mantalingahan Protected Landscape shall be under the administrative jurisdiction of the DENR in partnership with the PCSD and shall be administered in accordance with Republic Act No. 7586 in harmony with Republic Act No. 7611 or the Strategic Environmental Plan of Palawan (SEPP) Act and other relevant laws, rules and regulations. The DENR shall organized the Mt. Mantalingahan Protected Landscape Management Board pursuant to the provisions of RA 7586 within six (6) months upon issuance hereof.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Nine.
