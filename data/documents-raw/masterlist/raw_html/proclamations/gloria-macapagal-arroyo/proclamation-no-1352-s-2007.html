PROCLAMATION NO. 1352

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATARAZA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AS AN ANNEXATION TO THE EXISTING RIO TUBA EXPORT PROCESSING ZONE PURSUANT TO THE PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, The Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL – ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as an annexation to the existing Rio Tuba Export Processing Zone, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 06-548 dated 28 November 2006 as amended by Resolution No. 07-114 dated 15 March 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), an area located in the Municipality of Bataraza, Province of Palawan, consisting of TEN MILLION SEVEN HUNDRED SEVENTY EIGHT THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED SIXTY FOUR (10,778,364) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
