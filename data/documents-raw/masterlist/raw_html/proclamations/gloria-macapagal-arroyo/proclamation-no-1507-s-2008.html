Proclamation No. 1507, s. 2008

Signed on May 9, 2008
MALACAÑANG
Manila

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION No. 1507

DECLARING A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR PUBLIC BEACH AND ECO-TOURISM PURPOSES LOCATED ALOONG THE COAST OF “PUNTA” BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF ZAMBOANGUITA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by the law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare certain parcel of land of the public domain located along the coast of “Punta”, Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Zamboanguita, Province of Negros Oriental for Public Beach and Eco-Tourism purposes under the administration of the Local Government of the Municipality of Zamboanguita, Province of Negros Oriental, subject to final ground survey and private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land more particularly described as follows”

“A parcel of land, situated in Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Zamboanguita, Province of Negros Oriental”

Bearing true, surveyed by Geodetic Engineer Constancio D. Silva on November 18, 1997.

The tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease, or other mode of disposition until again declared alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set may hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9th day of May, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:

(Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA

Executive Secretary
