Proclamation No. 2012, s. 2010

Signed on March 1, 2010
MALACAÑANG
Manila

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 2012

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 1049 DATED APRIL 7, 2006, WHICH SEGREGATED A PORTION OF CAMP ROBERT EDWARD LUCERO IN BARANGAYS POBLACION AND NASAPIAN, MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, FROM THE COVERAGE OF PROCLAMATION NO. 1804 DATED NOVEMBER 23, 1978 FOR SOCIALIZED HOUSING SITE PURPOSES

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 1049 dated April 7, 2006, which segregated a portion of Camp Robert Edward Lucero Military Reservation located in Barangays Poblacion and Nasapian, Municipality of Carmen, Province of Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, from the coverage of Proclamation No. 1804 dated November 23, 1978 and reserved the same for socialized housing as follows:

Paragraph two, three, four and five of Proclamation No. 1049 are hereby amended to read as follows:

“Thirteen (13) hectares or One Hundred Thirty Thousand and Two (130,002) square meters of the area herein reserved shall be disposed of to the actual occupants thereof by the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) in accordance with the provisions of the Public Land Act, Republic Act No. 730 and other applicable rules and regulations and shall be under the administration of the Municipal Government of Carmen, Province of Cotabato. This area shall be subject to actual ground survey and is more particularly described as follows:

Paragraph seven is hereby amended to read as follows:

“A Local Inter-Agency Committee (LIAC) is hereby created to be chaired by the Local Government of Carmen and Co-Chaired by the representatives from the NHA and the AFP Housing Board with DENR and HUDCC as members. The LIAC is hereby empowered to issue and promulgate the implementing rules and regulations (IRR) for the two (2) lots covered by this proclamation.”

Paragraph nine is hereby amended to read as follows:

“The DENR is hereby authorized to issued Sales Patent in favor of the qualified civilian occupants for the 13 hectares, while for the 7 hectares, the DENR Secretary is authorized to issue the Special Patent in favor of NHA. The NHA shall remit to the National Treasury thru the DENR the value of the lot based on the appraisal as determined by the DENR.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:

(Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA

Executive Secretary
