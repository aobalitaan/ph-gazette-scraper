PROCLAMATION NO. 1773

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED IN BARANGAY SAN ANTONIO, MUNICIPALITY OF BIÑAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000, as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004, and Resolution No. 07-236 dated 13 June 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, several parcels of land located at Barangay San Antonio, Biñan, Laguna, as Information Technology Park to be known as One Asia Business Center, with an aggregate area of Forty Eight Thousand Thirty Five (48,035) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
