W., 43.75 meters to the point of beginning; containing an area of 813 square meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: points 2 by P.L.S. B. L. Conc. Mon.; point 4 by Stake; and the rest, by B.L. Conc. Mons.; bearings true; date of the cadastral survey, June, 1932—May, 1937.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL
President of the Philippines

By the President:
CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR
Acting Executive Secretary
