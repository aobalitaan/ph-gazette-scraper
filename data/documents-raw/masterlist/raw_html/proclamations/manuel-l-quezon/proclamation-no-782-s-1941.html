Proclamation No. 782, s. 1941

Signed on November 22, 1941

MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 782

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TEN PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF BALIWANAN, DIBAGAT, DAGARA, MUSIMUT AND NAGBALAYAN, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF KABUGAO; LANGNAO; MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BAYAG; ALLAGUIA, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF PINUKPUK; LAYA AND TUGA, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF TABUC; AND TONGLAYAN, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF ANTONIN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE (SUB-PROVINCES OF APAYAO, KALINGA AND BONTOC), ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section Eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Baliwanan, Dibagat, Dagara, Musimut and Nagbalayan, municipal district of Kabugao; Langnao; municipal district of bayag; Allaguia, municipal district of Pinukpuk; Laya and Tuga, municipal district of Tabuc; and Tonglayan, municipal district of Antonin, Mountain Province (Sub-Provinces Of Apayao, Kalinga and Bontoc), island of Luzon, and particularly described on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-105682, Psu-105683, Psu-105684, Psu-105720, Psu-105721, Psu-105728, Psu-105756, Psu-105757, Psu-105753 and Psu-105765, to wit:
