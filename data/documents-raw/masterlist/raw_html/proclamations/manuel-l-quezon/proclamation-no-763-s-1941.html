Signed on October 15, 1941

MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 763

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES SEVEN PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF TALOCTOC, SUMADEL, KARAGAWAN, NANAWATAN, KATABLANGAN, TALIFUGO, AND TAWIT, MUNICIPAL DISTRICTS OF TANUDAN, TINGLAYAN, KABUGAO, CONNER, AND LUNA, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE (SUBPROVINCES OF KALINGA AND APAYAO), ISLAND OF LUZON.
