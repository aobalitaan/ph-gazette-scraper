PROCLAMATION NO. 184

ESTABLISHING AS MAHAGNAO NATIONAL PARK FOR THE BENEFIT AND ENJOYMENT OF THE PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES THE PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF BURAUEN AND LA PAZ, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, ISLAND OF LEYTE
