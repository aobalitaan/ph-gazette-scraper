Proclamation No. 401, s. 1989

Signed on April 11, 1989

MALACAÑANG
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 401

EXCLUDING CERTAIN AREA FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION OF NO. 223, DATED NOVEMBER 16, 1937, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE BIAK-NA-BATO NATIONAL PARK AND DECLARING THE SAME AS MINERAL, WATERSHED AND FOREST RESERVATIONS.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby redefine the boundaries of the Biak-na-Bato National Park in San Miguel, Bulacan, established under Proclamation No. 223, dated November 16, 1937, by excluding therefrom certain parcels of land and setting aside the same as mineral reservation, watershed forest reserve and forest reserve, subject to private rights, if any there be, under the control and administration of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources. The new boundaries of aforesaid national park and the excluded areas are more particularly described as follows:
