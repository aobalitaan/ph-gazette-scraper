Proclamation No. 727, s. 1991

Signed on May 13, 1991

MALACAÑANG
MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 727

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 204, DATED DECEMBER 28, 1928, AS AMENDED, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE MAKAR TOWNSITE RESERVATION SITUATED IN BARANGAY OF MAKAR, MUNICIPALITY OF BUAYAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, BY EXCLUDING THE UNTITLED LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN FOR DISPOSITION PURSUANT TO THE PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 730.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Proclamation No. 204, dated December 28, 1928, as amended by Proclamation No. 149, dated April 16, 1955, which established certain parcel of land situated in the Municipality of Buayan, Province of Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, as Makar Townsite Reservation, by excluding therefrom the untitled lots and declaring the same open for disposition under the provisions of Republic Act No. 730, the technical descriptions of which are as follows:
