Executive Order No. 443, s. 1951

Signed on May 29, 1951

MALACAÑANG
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 443

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED THREE HUNDRED AND FORTY-THREE, EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED THREE HUNDRED AND SEVENTY-THREE, EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED FOUR HUNDRED AND EIGHT, EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED FOUR HUNDRED AND TWENTY-ONE AND EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED FOUR HUNDRED AND TWENTY-SEVEN, AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

SEC 6. The ceiling prices herein fixed include the 17 per cent special excise tax on Foreign Exchange, 7 per cent sales tax and 1 per cent municipal tax for Construction Materials and Textiles, and 7 per cent sales tax and 1 per cent municipal tax for American and Canadian Flour.

SEC. 7. This Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO
President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE
Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
