SPECIAL ORDER NO.993

DESIGNATING THE EXECUTIVE SECRETARY AS OFFICER-IN-
CHARGE DURING THE PRESIDENT’S OFFICIAL VISIT TO VIET NAM

ON 08-11 NOVEMBER 2017

WHEREAS, the President and some key members of his Cabinet will be in Viet
Nam on 08-11 November 2017 for an official visit to attend the 25" ~apec Economic

Leaders’ Meeting;

WHEREAS, to ensure continuity of government service, it is necessary to
designate an Officer-in-Charge (OIC) to take care of the day-to-day operations in the
Office of the President and oversee the general administration of the Executive
Department

NOW, THEREFORE, !, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:

1. The Executive Secretary shall act as OIC to take care of thé day-to-day

from 08 November 2017 to 11 November 2017.

2. All departments, agencies, and instrumentalities of the Government shall
assist the Executive Secretary in the discharge of his functions as Stated herein.

3. All acts of the Executive Secretary for and on behalf of the President
pursuant to this Order shall be deemed acts of the President unless disapproved or
reprobated by the President.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 7" day of Novernber, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
