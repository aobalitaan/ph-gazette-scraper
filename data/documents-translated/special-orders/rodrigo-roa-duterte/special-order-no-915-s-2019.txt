SPECIAL ORDER NO. 915

DESIGNATING THE SENIOR DEPUTY EXECUTIVE SECRETARY AS
ACTING EXECUTIVE SECRETARY FROM 28 AUGUST 2019 TO 01
SEPTEMBER 2019

WHEREAS, the Executive Secretary will be accompanying the President in
China on 28 August 2019 to 01 September 2019; and

WHEREAS, to ensure continuity of government service, it is necessary to
designate an Acting Executive Secretary to take care of the day-to-day operations in
the Office of the President;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, by virtue of the powers
vested in me by law, do hereby designate Senior Deputy Executive Secretary Michael
P. Ong as Acting Executive Secretary from 28 August 2019 to 01 September 2019.

All departments, agencies, and instrumentalities of the Government shall assist
the Acting Executive Secretary in the discharge of his functions as stated herein.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 28° day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Nineteen.

By Authority of the President:
