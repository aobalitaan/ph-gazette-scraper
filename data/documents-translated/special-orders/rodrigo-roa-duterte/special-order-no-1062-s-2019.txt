SPECIAL ORDER NO. 1062

DESIGNATING SENIOR DEPUTY EXECUTIVE SECRETARY MICHAEL

Na AS ACTING EXECUTIVE SECRETARY ON 01 TO 06 OCTOBER

WHEREAS, the Executive Secretary will be accompanying the President during
his Official Visit to the Russian Federation on 01 to 06 October 2019; and

WHEREAS, to ensure continuity of government service, it is necessary to
designate an Acting Executive Secretary to take care of the day-to-day operations in
the Office of the President;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, by virtue of the powers
vested in me by law, do hereby designate Senior Deputy Executive Secretary Michael
P. Ong as Acting Executive Secretary from 01 to 06 October 2019.

All departments, agencies, and instrumentalities of the Government shall assist the
Acting Executive Secretary in the discharge of his functions as stated herein.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 1stday Of October | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By Authority of the President:

te

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary”
