SPECIAL ORDER NO. 1163

DESIGNATING THE SENIOR DEPUTY EXECUTIVE SECRETARY AS
ACTING EXECUTIVE SECRETARY FROM 27 To 34 DECEMBER
2017

WHEREAS, the Executive Secretary will be on Personal leave from 27 to 31
December 2017: .

WHEREAS, to ensure continuity of government service, it is necessary to
designate an Acting Executive Secretary to take care of the day-to-day operations in
the Office of the President and assist the President in the general administration of the
Executive Department;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR c. MEDIALDEA, by the powers vested in
me by the law, do hereby designate Senior Deputy Executive Secretary Menardo |.
Guevarra as Acting Executive Secretary from 27 December 2017 to 31 December

All departments, agencies, and instrumentalities of the Government shall assist
the Acting Executive Secretary in the discharge of his functions as stated herein.

DONE in the Gity of Manila, this day of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

. ve.
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
