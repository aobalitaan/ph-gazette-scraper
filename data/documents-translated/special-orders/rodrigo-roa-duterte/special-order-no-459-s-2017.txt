SPECIAL ORDER NO. 459

DESIGNATING THE SENIOR DEPUTY EXECUTIVE SECRETARY AS
ACTING EXECUTIVE SECRETARY OURING THE PRESIDENT'S
OFFICIAL VISITS TO RUSSIA AND THE UNITED ARAB EMIRATES
(UAE) ON 22-29 MAY 2017

WHEREAS. the Executive Secretary will join the President and some members
of the Cabinet in the official visits to Russia and the UAE on 22-29 May 2017,

WHEREAS. Executive Order No. 292 (s. 1987) or the Administrative Code of
1987 provides that the President may temporanly designate an officer already in the
government service or any other competent person to perform the functions of an
office in the Executive branch, appointment to which is vested in him by law, when the
Officer regularly appointed to the office is unable to perform his duties by reason of
absence

WHEREAS. in the exigency of service, it is necessary to designate an Acting
Executive Secretary during the official visits to Russia and the UAE;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic
of the Philippines. by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:

i Senior Deputy Executive Secretary Menardo |. Guevarra ts hereby
designated as Acting Executive Secretary during the time that the Executive Secretary
is outside the Philippines from 22 May 2017 to 29 May 2017.

2 Ali departments, agencies, and instrumentalities of the Government shall
assist the Acting Executrve Secretary in the discharge of his functions as stated

herein

DONE in the City of Manila, this22ndday of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Seventeen.

By the President.
J SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
