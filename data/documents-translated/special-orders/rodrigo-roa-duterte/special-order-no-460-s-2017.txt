MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

SPECIAL ORDER NO.460

CONSTITUTING A CARETAKER COMMITTEE DURING THE
PRESIDENT’S OFFICIAL VISITS TO RUSSIA AND THE UNITED
ARAB EMIRATES (UAE) ON 22-29 MAY 2017

WHEREAS. the President and some key members of the Cabinet will be in
Russia and the UAE for official visits on 22-29 May 2017;

WHEREAS. io ensure continuity of government service, it is necessary to
constitute a Caretaker Committee which will take care of the day-to-day operations in
the Office of the President and oversee the general administration of the Executive
Department

NOW, THEREFORE, i, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:

1, A Caretaker Committee is hereby constituted to be composed of
Department of Budget and Management Secretary Benjamin E. Diokno, Department
of Eavironment and Natural Resources Acting Secretary Roy A. Cimatu. Philippine
National Police Deputy Director General Ramon Colet Apolinario and Senior Deputy
Executive Secretary Menardo |. Guevarra, in his capacity as Acting Executive
Secretary

2 The Committee shall take care of the day-to-day operations in the Office
of the President and oversee the general administration of the Executive Department
it shall, if necessary, act for and on behalf of the President, except on matters in which
the President is required by the Constitution to act in person, during the time that the
President is outside the Philippines from 22 May 2017 to 29 May 2017.

3 All departments, agencies, and instrumentalities of the Government shall
assisi the Committee in the discharge of its functions as stated herein.

4 Ali acts of the Committee for and on behalf of the President pursuant to
ihis Order shall be deemed acts of the President unless disapproved or reprobated by

ihe President

DONE in the City of Manila, thist2ndday of May. in the year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Seventeen, ae am,
