MANILA

SPECIAL ORDER NO. 528

DESIGNATING THE SECRETARY OF THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE
AS OFFICER-IN-CHARGE ON 28 MAY 2019 TO 01 JUNE 2019

WHEREAS, the President will be in Tokyo, Japan on 28 May 2019 to 01 June
2019 to participate in the 25" International Conference on the Future of Asia and related
events; and

WHEREAS, to ensure continuity of government service, it is necessary to
designate an Officer-in-Charge to take care of the day-to-day operations and oversee
the general administration of the Executive Department;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:

1. Department of Justice (DOJ) Secretary Menardo |. Guevarra shall act as
Officer-in-Charge to take care of the day-to-day operations and general administration
of the Executive Department, and he shall, if necessary, act for and on behalf of the
President, except on matters that the President is required by the Constitution to act in
person, during the time that the President is outside the Republic of the Philippines from
28 May 2019 to 01 June 2019.

2. All departments, agencies, and instrumentalities of the Government shall
assist DOJ Secretary Guevarra in the discharge of his functions as stated herein.

3. All acts of DOJ Secretary Guevarra for and on behalf of the President
pursuant to this Order shall be deemed acts of the President unless disapproved or
reprobated by the President.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 27 day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Nineteen.

By the President:
