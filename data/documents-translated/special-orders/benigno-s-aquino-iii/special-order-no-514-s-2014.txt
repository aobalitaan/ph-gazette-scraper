Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

SPECIAL ORDER NO. 514

CREATING THE INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY TASK-FORCE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

In order to perform its fundamental role of assisting the President in executing his mandated functions as stipulated in Article VIII of the 1987 Constitution, and in line with the President’s continuing authority to reorganize the administrative structure of the Office of the President under Section 31, Chapter 10, Title III, Book III of the Administrative Code of 1987, the Office of the Executive Secretary (OES) aims to further strengthen its institutional capacities by promoting improvements in its internal, functional and structural configuration and operational mechanisms.

To this end, the Information and Communications Technology Taskforce (ICTTF) in this Office is hereby created. The ICTTF shall be provisional in nature, and would complement the functions of the existing Management Information Systems Office (MIS). As there is a need to make primarily strategic, tactical, or operational decisions, which requires inputs from diverse backgrounds, the ICTTF shall be a cross-functional team composed of members from different offices. This Order shall take effect immediately.

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
