Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

SPECIAL ORDER NO. 92

Pursuant to the Guidelines in filling-out the Statement of Assets, Liabilities and Net Worth, as adopted in CSC Resolution No. 1300173, dated 24 January 2013, and for administrative efficiency, the following officials shall administer oath to OP Proper officials and employees declaring their SALNs for the year 2012 and onwards, until further advice:

Administering Official

Declarants

This Order shall not, however, preclude any OP Proper official or employee from taking his/her oath before a notary public.

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila , April 12, 2013
