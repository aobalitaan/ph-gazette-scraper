MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

GENERAL ORDER NO. 50

SUSPENDING THE CURFEW IN CONNECTION WITH ALL SAINTS’ DAY

WHEREAS, honoring our dead on All Saints Day is one of our cherished traditions;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby suspend the curfew throughout the Philippines from midnight of October 31, 1974, up to four o’clock in the morning of November 2, 1974.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of October, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

Source: Legislative Library, House of Representatives
