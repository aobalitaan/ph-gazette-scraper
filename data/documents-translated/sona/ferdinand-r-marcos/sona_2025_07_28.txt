Of our twenty-four active volcanoes in the country, four are currently restless, especially Kanlaon in Negros.

Because of the successive disasters, especially typhoons, it is important to instill the principle of continuous improvement, where we learn [and] always improve with each experience. We remedy where we fell short, and where we should excel further.

Along with important infrastructure, we will add more modern evacuation centers that we have already built. Our schools should no longer be used as evacuation centers.

For effective response, the Mobile Command and Control Vehicles of DOST are helpful, which we have distributed to eleven LGUs nationwide.

To our countrymen: no preparation of ours will succeed without your help.
