By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Executive Order No. 318, dated May 25, 1950, creating the Integrity Board, as amended so as to have said body composed of the following:

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
