MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 595

TERMINATING THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS AT THE BUED AND MANAT TOLL BRIDGES, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

The total cost of the Bued and Manat Toll Bridges, in the Province of Pangasinan, plus interest at the rate of 4 per cent per annum, having been fully recovered, as certified in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 3500, as amended, it is hereby ordered that the collection of tolls at the Bued and Manat Toll Bridges be terminated.

This Order shall take effect upon receipt of copy hereof by the Provincial Treasurer of Pangasinan.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
