MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 599

RETURNING TO THE OLD POBLACION (CENTRO) THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

Upon the recommendation of the Provincial Board of Isabela and pursuant to the provisions of section 68 of the Revised Administrative Code, the seat of government of the municipality of San Pablo, Isabela, is hereby returned from the barrio of Auitan where it was transferred by Executive Order No. 109, series of 1946, to the old poblacion or Centro of said municipality.

This transfer shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
