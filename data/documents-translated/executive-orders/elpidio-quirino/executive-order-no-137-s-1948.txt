MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 137

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 12, DATED AUGUST 19, 1946, ENTITLED “REORGANIZING THE QUEZON MEMORIAL COMMITTEE, CREATED UNDER EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBER SEVENTY-NINE, DATED 17 DECEMBER 1945.”

The first paragraph of Executive Order No. 12, dated August 19, 1946, entitled “Reorganizing the Quezon Memorial Committee, created under Executive Order No. 79, dated 17 December 1945,” is hereby amended to read as follows:

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the second.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) EMILIO ABELLO Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Museum and Library

Office of the President of the Philippines. (1948). [Executive Order Nos. : 128-297]. Manila : Presidential Museum and Library.
