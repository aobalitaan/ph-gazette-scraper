MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 514

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED THREE HUNDRED AND NINETY AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

By virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 3 of Republic Act No. 509 entitled “AN ACT DECLARING NATIONAL A POLICY, AUTHORIZING THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES FOR A LIMITED PERIOD TO FIX CEILING PRICES OF COMMODITIES AND TO PROMULGATE RULES AND REGULATIONS REGARDING PRICES OF COMMODITIES TO EFFECTUATE SUCH POLICY, AND AUTHORIZING THE APPROPRIATION OF A CERTAIN SUM FOR THE PURPOSE,” and upon the recommendation of the Price Administration Board, I, ELPIDIO QUIRINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby order:

SECTION 1. Section 5 of Executive Order No. 390, dated December 27, 1950, is hereby amended by changing the phrase “Sugar packed in Cellophane or Cotton bags” so as to read as follows:

“Sugar packed in cellophane, cotton or other bags of equivalent quality . . . . . P0.05 additional per kilo.

SEC. 2, This Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
