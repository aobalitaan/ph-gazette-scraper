MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 191

EXTENDING THE PROVISIONS OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 62, DATED JUNE 21, 1947; ENTITLED “REGULATING RENTALS FOR HOUSES AND LOTS FOR RESIDENTIAL BUILDINGS,” UNTIL DECEMBER 31, 1949.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and laws; of the Philippines, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby order that Executive Order No. 62, dated June 21, 1947, entitled j “Regulating Rentals for Houses and Lots for Residential Buildings,” shall be in force until December 31, 1949.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the third,

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QURINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) TEDORO EVANGELISTA Executive Secretary
