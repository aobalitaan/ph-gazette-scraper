MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 454

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 24, DATED NOVEMBER 12, 1946, AS AMENDED, ENTITLED “CREATING THE NATIONAL ADVISORY HEALTH COUNCIL”

The second paragraph of Executive Order No. 24, dated November 12, 1946, as amended, is hereby further amended so as to make the President of the Colegio Medico-Farmaceutico de Filipinas, Inc., and the Chairman of the Board of Pharmaceutical Examiners Members of the National Advisory Health Council therein created.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
