MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 583

CLASSIFYING NEWLY ORGANIZED MUNICIPALITIES IN THE PHILIPPINES

Upon the recommendation of the Auditor General and the Secretary of Finance and by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, the following newly organized municipalities are hereby given their respective initial tentative classifications as hereunder indicated, effective as of the dates said municipalities respectively began to exist, the same to continue until said municipalities shall have been reclassified pursuant to the provisions of section 5 of Republic Act No. 554:

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
