MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 394

CREATING AN ACCOUNTING OFFICE AND AN AUDITING UNIT FOR THE PHILIPPINE ARMY

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, and upon the recommendation of the Reorganization Commission, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby order:

1. There are hereby created an Accounting Office and an Office of the Representative of the Auditor General to handle the accounting and auditing work of the Philippine Army, the said units to be composed of the following personnel at salaries set opposite each:

(a) Accounting Office:

₱84,480.00

(b) Office of the Representative of the Auditor General

₱45,600.00

2. The appropriations needed for the aforesaid accounting and auditing units shall be taken from the following appropriations of the Philippine Army:

Total ₱130,380.00

3. This Order shall take effect as of July 1, 1950.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) TEODORO EVANGELISTA Executive Secretary
