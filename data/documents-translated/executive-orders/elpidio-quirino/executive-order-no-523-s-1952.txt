MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 523

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 318, DATED MAY 25, 1950, CREATING THE INTEGRITY BOARD.

By virtue of the powers vested in my by law, I, ELPIDIO QUIRINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Executive Order No. 318, dated May 25, 1950, creating the Integrity Board, as amended, so as to have said body composed of the following:

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and the Independence of Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
