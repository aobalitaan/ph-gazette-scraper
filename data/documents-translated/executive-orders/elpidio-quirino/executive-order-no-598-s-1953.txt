MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 598

REDUCING THE 1952 TAXES ON ALL REAL PROPERTIES LOCATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF ARTECHE, BALANGUINGA, CANAVID, DOLORES, GIPAPAD, MASLOG, MAYDOLONG, MERCEDES, ORAS, QUINAPONDAN, SAN POLICARPIO, SULAT AND TAFT, ALL IN THE PROVINCE OF SAMAR

The public interest so requiring, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 53 of Commonwealth Act No. 470, as amended by Commonwealth Act No. 534, and upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Finance, do hereby remit the 50 per cent of the 1952 taxes on all real properties located in the municipalities of Arteche, Balanguinga, Canavid, Dolores, Gipapad, Maslog, Maydolong, Mercedes, Oras, Quinapondan, San Policarpio, Sulat and Taft, all in the Province of Samar, on condition that all the unpaid taxes and penalties corresponding to 1951 and previous years and the remaining 50 per cent of the 1952 taxes shall be paid on or before June 30, 1953.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
