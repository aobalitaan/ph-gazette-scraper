MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 619

DESCRIBING THE BOUNDARY LINE BETWEEN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF PARANG AND NULING, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, AND AMENDING ACCORDINGLY EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED TWO HUNDRED NINETEEN, SERIES OF 1949

Pursuant to the provisions of section sixty-eight of the Revised Administrative Code, the boundary line between the municipalities of Parang and Nuling both of the Province of Cotabato, is hereby denned and fixed as follows:

From a point, at the center of the mouth of the old Simuay River, approximately one kilometer north of the sitio of Simuay, in the muncipality of Nuling, on the seashore, following the bed of this river as surveyed by District Engineer Marcelino N. Samson to the source thereof in Lake Balut; thence in a straight line northeasterly to a point somewhere north of and adjacent to the sitio of Pingping, municipality of Nuling, on the boundary between the municipal district of Balut, now a part of the municipality of Nuling, and the former municipal district of Buldon, now a part of the municipality of Parang; thence southeasterly in a straight line, following the same Balut-Buldon boundary until its intersection with the Manuangan River somewhere south of the sitio of Migkato, municipality of Parang. (References used in this description: Sketch Map of Simuay Siver, scale 1:10,000, surveyed by B. Buendia, General Foreman, E. Madamba, Foreman-Instrumentman, and j. Eeyna, Instrumentman,. all of the Office of the District Engineer of Cotabato, and submitted by District Engineer Marcelino N. Samson; and Bureau of Public Works Road Map of Cotabato Province, scale 1:250,000.)

Executive Order No. 219, series of 1949, is hereby amended accordingly.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eighth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
