MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 551

REDUCING THE 1952 TAXES ON ALL REAL PROPERTIES LOCATED IN THE CITY OF LEGASPI

The public interest so requiring, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 41 of Republic Act No. 306, as amended by Republic Act No. 457, and upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Finance, do hereby remit the 50 per cent of the 1952 taxes on all real properties located in the City of Legaspi on condition that all the unpaid taxes and penalties corresponding to 1951 and previous year and the remaining 50 per cent of the 1952 taxes shall be paid on or before December 31, 1952.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
