MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 441

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 259, DATED AUGUST 30, 1949, CREATING A FIRE PREVENTION BOARD

Executive Order No. 259, dated August 30, 1949, as amended by Executive Order No. 315, dated May 5, 1950, and Executive Order No. 416, dated February 21, 1951, creating a Fire Prevention Board, is hereby further amended by including in the membership thereof, under Group One (Government), the Manager of the Metropolitan Water District.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
