EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 449

REPEALING EXECUTIVE ORDER NUMBERED THREE HUNDRED AND THIRTY-SEVEN AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

SEC. 3. The ceiling prices fixed in this Order include the 17 per cent special excise tax on Foreign Exchange, both on the basic materials for locally manufactured articles and on the imported finished products, 7 per cent sales tax and 1 per cent municipal tax.

SEC. 4. This Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines
