MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 640

TERMINATING THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS AT THE IMUS-ABUBOT TOLL BRIDGE, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

The total cost of the Imus-Abubot Toll Bridge, in the Province of Cavite, plus interest at the-rate of 4 per cent per annum, having been fully recovered, as certified in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 3500, as amended, it is hereby ordered that the collection of tolls at the Imus-Abubot Toll Bridge be terminated.

This Order shall take effect upon receipt of copy hereof by the Provincial Treasurer of Cavite.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eighth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
