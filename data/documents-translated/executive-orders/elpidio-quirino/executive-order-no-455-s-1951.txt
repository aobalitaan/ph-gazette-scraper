MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 455

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 398, DATED JANUARY 5, 1951, ENTITLED “REORGANIZING THE DEPORTATION BOARD,” AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 425, DATED MARCH 14, 1951

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Executive Order No. 398, dated January 5, 1951, as amended by Executive Order No. 425, dated March 14, 1951, by constituting the Deportation Board as follows:

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of June, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and fifty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
