MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 127

TRANSFERRING THE SEAT OF GOVERNMENT OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LONGOS, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, FROM THE POBLACION OF LONGOS TO THE BARRIO OF SAN JUAN OF THE SAME MUNICIPALITY

Pursuant to the provisions of section sixty-eight of the Revised Administrative Code and upon the recommendation of the municipal council of the municipality of Longos, Province of Laguna, the provincial board of Laguna, and the Secretary of the Interior, the seat of government of the municipality of Longos is hereby transferred from the poblacion of Longos to the barrio of San Juan of the same municipality.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the second.

(Sgd.) MANUEL ROXAS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) EMILIO ABELLO Executive Secretary
