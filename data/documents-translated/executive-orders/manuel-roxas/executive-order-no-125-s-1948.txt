MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 125

ABOLISHING THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BANNAGAO IN THE PROVINCE OF ISABELA AND ANNEXING THE TERRITORY THEREOF TO THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF AURORA, SAME PROVINCE

Upon the recommendation of the provincial governor of Isabela, concurred in by the Secretary of the Interior and pursuant to the provisions of section 68 of the Revised Administrative Code, the municipal district of Bannagao as organized by Executive Order No. 13, dated March 2, 1920, in the Province of Isabela, is hereby abolished and the territory thereof annexed to the municipal district of Aurora, as a separate and independent barrio thereof.

Executive Order No. 13, dated March 2, 1920, is hereby amended accordingly.

Done at the City of Manila, this 23rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the second.

(Sgd.) MANUEL ROXAS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) EMILIO ABELLO Executive Secretary
