MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 214

COMPOSITION OF THE APPRAISAL COMMITTEE CREATED UNDER EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 132, DATED DECEMBER 27, 1937

The Appraisal Committee created under Executive Order Numbered One hundred thirty-two, dated December twenty-seventh, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, shall hereafter be composed of the provincial assessor, as Chairman, and the district engineer and the provincial auditor, as members, of the province where the land is located: Provided, That if the property is situated in a chartered city, the Appraisal Committee shall be composed of the city assessor, as Chairman, and the city engineer and the city auditor, as members.

Executive Order Numbered One hundred thirty-two, dated December twenty-seventh, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, is hereby amended accordingly.

Done at the City of Manila, this tenth day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President:

JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
