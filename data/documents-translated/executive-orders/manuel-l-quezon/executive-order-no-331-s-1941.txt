MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 331

REVISING FURTHER EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 135 DATED DECEMBER 31, 1937, AS AMENDED, ESTABLISHING A CLASSIFICATION OF ROADS

Executive Order No. 135, dated December 31, 1937, as amended, is hereby further amended so as to include the following roads in the classification of National Roads.

From Km.

To Km.

Length Km.

Allen-Tinambacan-Oquendo Junction Road

Dimasalang-Cataingan-Placer Road

and to exclude therefrom the following National Roads which have been declared provincial roads:

Allen-Catarman-Oquendo Road

Masbate-Milagros-Aroroy Road

Done at the City of Manila, this eleventh day of March, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
