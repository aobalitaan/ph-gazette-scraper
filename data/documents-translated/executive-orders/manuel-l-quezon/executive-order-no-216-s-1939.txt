MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 216

COMPOSITION OF THE NATIONAL RELIEF BOARD CREATED UNDER EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 197, DATED MARCH 24, 1939

The National Relief-Board created under Executive Order Numbered One hundred ninety-seven, dated March twenty-fourth, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, shall hereafter be composed of the Secretary of the Interior as Chairman, and the Secretary of Finance, the Secretary of Public Works and Communications, the Secretary of Public Instruction who may be represented by the Commissioner of Health and Welfare, and the Secretary of Labor, as members. The Commissioner of the Budget shall be the Executive Officer of the Board.

Executive Order Numbered One hundred ninety-seven, dated March twenty-fourth, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, is hereby amended accordingly.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-eighth day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President:

JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
