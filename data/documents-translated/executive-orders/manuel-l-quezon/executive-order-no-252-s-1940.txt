MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 252

GRANTING AUTHORITY TO THE COMMISSIONER OF THE CENSUS TO ORDER AND APPROVE THE PUBLICATION OF CENSUS REPORTS

Pursuant to the provisions of Sections 11 and 12 of Commonwealth Act No. 170, known as the Census Act, the Census Commissioner is hereby granted authority to order and approve from time to time, the publication of partial or complete reports, bulletins, and other information secured and/or compiled by the Census Office in connection with the census taken in accordance with the provisions of Commonwealth Act No. 170.

Done at the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
