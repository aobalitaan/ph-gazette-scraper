MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 338

PLACING THE BUREAU OF IMMIGRATION UNDER THE SUPERVISION AND CONTROL OF THE DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE

Pursuant to the power vested in me under section 2 of Commonwealth Act No. 613, I, MANUEL L. QUEZON, President of the Philippines, do hereby direct that the Bureau of Immigration be placed, for administrative purposes, under the supervision and control of the Department of Justice.

Done at the City of Baguio, this 1st day of May, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
