MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 198

MAKING ALLOTMENTS OF THE APPROPRIATIONS FOR THE PHILIPPINE ARMY AUTHORIZED BY COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 300, IN ADDITION TO THOSE INCLUDED IN EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 190, CURRENT SERIES, FOR EXPENDITURES OF THE EXECUTIVE DEPARTMENTS OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT DURING THE PERIOD FROM JANUARY 1 TO JUNE 30, 1939

The following allotments of appropriations authorized by Commonwealth Act Numbered Three hundred are made for the Philippine Army in addition to those included in Executive Order Numbered One hundred and ninety, dated Feb­ruary twenty-eighth, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, for expenditures of the executive departments of the National Government during the period from January first to June thirtieth, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine:

(8) PHILIPPINE ARMY

A. COMMISSIONED OFFICERS AND CADETS

B. REGULAR FORCE—ENLISTED MEN

PURPOSE II.—MAINTENANCE. OF PERSONNEL

The grand total of the funds allotted up to this date from the appropriations provided in Commonwealth Act Numbered Three hundred, including those authorized in Executive Order Numbered One hundred and ninety, current series, should, therefore, amount to thirty-eight million five hundred eighty-nine thousand four hundred and seventy pesos.

Done at the City of Manila, this fourth day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President:

JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
