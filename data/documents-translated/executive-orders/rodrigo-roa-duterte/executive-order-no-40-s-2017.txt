BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 40

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 9 (5. 2016) FOR THE PURPOSE
OF STRENGTHENING THE FUNCTIONAL ARRANGEMENTS IN THE

OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

WHEREAS, Executive Order (EO) No. 9 (8. 2016) transferred the Public
Concerns Office (PCO) from the Presidential Management Staff (PMS) to the Office of
the Cabinet Secretary (OCS) and placed it under the direct control and supervision of
the Strategic Action and Response (STAR) Office of the OCS;

WHEREAS, EO No. 9 also placed two PMS Offices, namely the Cabinet Support
Office (CSO) and the Directives Monitoring Office (DMO), under the operational control
of the OCS in relation to policy direction and coordination of Cabinet and other

Presidential meetings;

WHEREAS, to rationalize the reporting lines of the PCO, CSO and DMO based
on thei; actual functions, there is a need to revert their direct control and supervision to
the PMS to ensure alignment with the Presidential perspective, direction and priorities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Philippines,
by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, do hereby
order:

SECTION 1. Public Concerns Office. The PCO is hereby transferred back to
the control and supervision of the PMS. PCO shall continue to institute feedback
mechanisms for requests for assistance, complaints and grievances received by offices
under the Office of the President and referred to other concerned government agencies
or instrumentalities,

The PCO shall also provide feedback to the Special Assistant to the President on
actions taken by agencies as regards requests for assistance, complaints and
grievances referred to them.

SECTION 2. Cabinet Staff Support. In accordance with EO No. 1 (s. 2016), the

PMS shall continue to support the Cabinet Secretary in his role as Secretary to the
Cabinet through the provision of substantive and logistical support, including the: a)
