MALACANAN PALACE

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 167

STRENGTHENING THE PHILIPPINE COMMISSION ON WOMEN,
FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 208 (S. 1994), AS
AMENDED, FOR THE PURPOSE

WHEREAS, Section 14, Article Il of the Constitution declares it a policy of the
State to recognize the role of women in nation-building and ensure the fundamental
equality before the law of women and men;

WHEREAS, Presidential Decree (PD) No. 633 (s. 1975) created the National
Commission on the Role of Filipino Women (NCRFW) to ensure the full integration of
women in economic, social and cultural development at national, regional and
international levels;

WHEREAS, Executive Order (EO) No. 208 (s. 1994), as amended by EO No.
268 (s. 1995), further defined the composition, powers and functions of the NCRFW;

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 9710 or the “Magna Carta of Women” (MCW),
renamed the NCRFW as the Philippine Commission on Women (PCW), and
designated the Commission as the primary policy-making and coordinating body on
women and gender equality concerns, as well as the overall monitoring and oversight
body to ensure the effective implementation of the MCW;

WHEREAS, considering the expanded role and scope of work of the PCW,
there is a need to strengthen the organizational structure of the Commission, including
its current composition; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VIl of the Constitution provides that the
President shall have control of all executive departments, bureaus and offices;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, do hereby order:

Section 1. Amendment. Section 1 of EO No. 208, as amended, is hereby
further amended to read as follows:

“Section 1. Composition of the Commission. The PCW shall
be composed of the following members:

The incumbent members of the Commission, however, shall
continue to hold office unless replaced or removed by the President.

The heads of government agencies mentioned are
authorized to designate their permanent alternates with a rank not
lower than Assistant Secretary to represent them during
Commission meetings and other official functions. The ex-officio
member shall inform the Commission in writing of such
designation.

There is hereby created a PCW Secretariat, which shall be
headed by an Executive Director, and assisted by two (2) Deputy
Executive Directors, who shall, among other functions, supervise
the provision of requisite technical and administrative services to
the members of the Commission.”

Section 2. Unaffected Provisions. Except as expressly modified by this
Order, all other provisions of EO No. 208, as amended, shall remain in full force and
effect.

Section 3. Repeal. All orders, rules, regulations and issuances or parts thereof,
which are inconsistent with this Order, are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

Section 4. Effectivity. This Order shall take effect immediately.
