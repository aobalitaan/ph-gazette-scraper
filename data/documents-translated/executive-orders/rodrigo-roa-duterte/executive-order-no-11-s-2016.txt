MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

  
     

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 11

EXTENDING FURTHER THE TERM OF SOUTH COTABATO/NORTH
COTABATO/SULTAN KUDARAT/ SARANGANI/GENERAL SANTOS CITY
(SOCSKSARGEN) AREA DEVELOPMENT PROJECT OFFICE (ADPO) TO
31 DECEMBER 2022, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE EXECUTIVE
ORDER NO, 507 (S. 2006), AS AMENDED

WHEREAS, the term:of the SOCSKSARGEN ADPO expires on 31 December 2016
pursuant to Executive Order (EO) No. 507 (s. 2006), as amended by EO No. 16 (s. 2010);

WHEREAS, in order to continue growth, ensure that programs and projects
supported by the national government are fully implemented and to facilitate sustainable
mechanism for agri-fishery industry competitiveness, there is a need to extend the term of
the SOCSKSARGEN ADPO;

NOW THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Philippines, by
virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, do hereby order

SECTION 1. The provision on the term of the SOCSKSARGEN ADPO in Section 1
of EO 507 (s. 2006), as amended, is hereby further amended to read as follows:

"“SOCSKSARGEN ADP Office Term. The term of the ADP Office is
hereby extended to 31 December 2022.”

SECTION 2. The initial funds necessary for the implementation of this Order shall be
taken from such available sources as may be identified by the Department of Budget and
Management. Succeeding funding for the operational requirements of the SOCKSARGEN
ADPO shall be included in the budget of the Department of Agriculture (DA) in the Genaral
Appropriations Act based on the work and financial program as submitted by the DA and
endorsed by the Regional Development Council XII

SECTION 3. Repealing Clause. All other rules, regulations and issuances or parts
thereof which are inconsistent with this EO are hereby repealed or modified accordingly,

SECTION 4. Effectivity. This Order shall take effect on 01 January 2017,

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of December , Inthe year of our
Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
