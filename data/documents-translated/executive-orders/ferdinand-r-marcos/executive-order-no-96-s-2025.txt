MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

EXECUTIVE ORDERNO. 96

TRANSFERRING THE ATTACHMENT OF THE NATIONAL
COMMISSSION OF SENIOR CITIZENS TO THE DEPARMENT OF
SOCIAL WELFARE AND DEVELOPMENT, AND FOR OTHER
PURPOSES

WHEREAS, Section 2 of Republic Act (RA) No. 11350 or the “National
Commission of Senior Citizens (NCSC) Act” declares it a policy of the State to adopt
measures that will advance the health and well-being of senior citizens, and implement
various programs and services that will ensure a supportive and enabling environment
for the elderly;

WHEREAS, Section 3 of RA No. 11350 established the NCSC under the Office
of the President (OP), to ensure full implementation of laws, policies, and programs
for the promotion and protection of the rights and well-being of senior citizens;

WHEREAS, Section 11 of RA No. 11350 provides that all functions, programs,
projects and activities of the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD)
for the formulation, implementation, and coordination of social welfare and
development policies and programs for the poor, vulnerable, and disadvantaged
senior citizens shall be transferred to NCSC;

WHEREAS, Section 5 of RA No. 9994 or the “Expanded Senior Citizens Act of
2010,” as amended, established the Social Pension for Indigent Senior Citizens
(SPISC) Program to provide monetary grant to eligible senior citizens to support their
daily subsistence and medical needs;

WHEREAS, under Section 5 of RA No. 11916, the implementation, distribution,
and management of the SPISC Program shall be transferred from DSWD to NCSC
within a period not exceeding three (3) years from the effectivity of the said law:

WHEREAS, Section 3, Chapter 1, Title XVI, Book IV of Executive Order (EO)
No. 292 or the “Administrative Code of 1987” mandates the DSWD to, among others,
formulate, develop, and implement plans, programs, and projects in the field of social

welfare and development, and coordinate all activities pertaining to the implementation
of programs and services for the disabled, the aging, and other socially disadvantaged;

WHEREAS, pursuant to the Administration's Eight (8)-Point Socioeconomic
Agenda, which includes improving bureaucratic efficiency, a strong organizational link
between the NCSC and DSWD must be established to further strengthen policies and
programs for senior citizens, with the goal of enhancing delivery of social services and
promoting the overall well-being and protection of the elderly;

WHEREAS, under Section 31, Chapter 10, Title Ill of EO No. 292, the
President, in order to achieve simplicity, economy, and efficiency, shall have the
continuing authority to reorganize the administrative structure of the OP, and for this
purpose, may transfer any agency under the OP to any other department or agency;

WHEREAS, Section 6(3) of RA No. 12231 or the “Government Optimization
Act” authorizes the President to transfer or integrate functions from one (1) agency to
another which can better perform the same, or split functions of agencies, bureaus,
and offices that may be conflicting or multifarious; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all Executive departments, bureaus, and offices;

NOW THEREFORE, |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
do hereby order:

Section 1. Attachment. The NCSC is hereby attached to DSWD for purposes
of policy and program coordination.

Section 2. Implementation of the SPISC Program. The implementation,
distribution, and management of the SPISC Program shall continue to be under the
DSWD. For this purpose, the DSWD may seek the assistance of the NCSC to ensure
the successful implementation of said Program.

Section 3. Separability. If any part or provision of this Order is held invalid or
unconstitutional, the provisions not affected thereby shall remain in full force and
effect.

Section 4. Repeal. All orders, rules and regulations, and other issuances or
parts thereof which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Order, are hereby
repealed or modified accordingly.

Section 5. Effectivity. This Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 18th day of September , inthe
year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.

By the President:
