MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 107

UPDATING THE BASE PAY SCHEDULE AND INCREASING THE
SUBSISTENCE ALLOWANCE OF THE MILITARY AND UNIFORMED
PERSONNEL

WHEREAS, Section 13 of Presidential Decree (PD) No. 985 or “The Budgetary
Reform Decree on Compensation and Position Classification of 1976,” as amended,
authorizes the President to revise periodically the salary schedule and wage schedule
of government employees and personnel;

WHEREAS, Item 1 of Congress Joint Resolution (JR) No. 4 (s. 2009) stipulates
that the Base Pay Schedule of military and uniformed personnel (MUP) shall take into
account prevailing economic realities and create professionalism, exemplary
performance, and commitment to service;

WHEREAS, Item 17(iv) of JR No. 4 further provides that the Department of
Budget and Management (DBM) shall recommend, for the consideration and approval
of the President, the updating of the Base Pay Schedule for MUP, as well as the
policies on and levels of allowances, benefits, and incentives applicable to all
government personnel;

WHEREAS, given the prevailing economic circumstances, there is a need to
update the base pay schedule and increase the subsistence allowance of MUP in
recognition of their vital role in nation building, and in order to boost their morale and
maintain a competent, committed, agile, and healthy workforce;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
do hereby order:

Section 1. Coverage. The updated Base Pay Schedule herein shall apply to
all military personnel under the Department of National Defense (DND) and uniformed
personnel under the Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG), the
Philippine Coast Guard (PCG), the Bureau of Corrections (BuCor), and the National
Mapping and Resource Information Authority (NAMRIA).

Section 3. Implementation Schedule. The updated Base Pay Schedule in
Section 2 hereof shall be implemented in three (3) tranches, with the first tranche
beginning on 1 January 2026, the second tranche beginning on 1 January 2027, and
the third and final tranche beginning on 1 January 2028.

Section 4. Subsistence Allowance. The Subsistence Allowance of all MUP
shall be at the rate of Three Hundred Fifty Pesos (P350.00) per day, effective 1
January 2026.

Section 5. Funding. The amounts necessary to implement the increase in the
Base Pay and Subsistence Allowance in Fiscal Year (FY) 2026 shall be charged
against any available appropriations under the FY 2026 General Appropriations Act
and any other available appropriations, subject to relevant budgeting, accounting, and
auditing rules and regulations.

The funding requirements for the adjustment in the Base Pay for FYs 2027 and
2028 in Section 2 hereof and the increase in the Subsistence Allowance in Section 4
hereof in the succeeding FYs shall be included in the proposed annual National
Expenditure Program to be submitted to Congress.

Section 6. Mandatory Review of the MUP Pension System. An Inter-Agency
Technical Working Group (IATWG) is hereby created to be composed of
representatives from the: (i) DBM; (ii) Department of Finance; (iii) Bureau of the
Treasury; and (iv) Government Service Insurance System. The IATWG shall perform
the following duties and functions:

a. Conduct a comprehensive review of the current MUP pension system, with the
objective of ensuring a sustainable and equitable pension system;

b. Formulate and recommend draft legislation, as may be necessary, based on the
results of the review; and

c. Perform such other functions as may be directed by the President and/or the
Executive Secretary.

All MUP agencies are hereby directed to provide the necessary technical,
administrative, and logistical support to the IATWG to facilitate the conduct of the
comprehensive review. For this purpose, within 30 days from the effectivity of this
Order, the member-agencies of the IATWG and the concerned MUP agencies shall
designate their respective authorized representatives to participate in the review
process.

The IATWG shall, within 180 days upon effectivity of this Order, submit to the
Office of the President, through the Office of the Executive Secretary, a report detailing
the status of the review process.

Section 7. Implementing Guidelines. Within 90 days from the effectivity of
this Order, the DBM shall issue guidelines necessary to implement the provisions of
this Order, which shall include, among others, the updating of the Base Pay Schedule
and the adjustment of the Subsistence Allowance.

Section 8. Separability. If any part or provision of this Order is held invalid or
unconstitutional, the provisions not affected thereby shall remain in full force and
effect.

Section 9. Repeal. All orders, rules and regulations, and issuances or parts
thereof inconsistent with this Order are hereby repealed, amended, or modified
accordingly.

Section 10. Effectivity. This Order shall take effect immediately upon its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.

Year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.
