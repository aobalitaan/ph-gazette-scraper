MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 433

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 263, DATED AUGUST 15, 1957, ENTITLED “CREATING THE PINAGLABANAN COMMEMORATIVE COMMISSION”

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Executive Order No. 263, dated August 15, 1957, by designating Mr. Cesar M. Lorenzo as Member and Secretary and Mr. Otillo Arellano as Member of the Pinaglabanan Commemorative Commission in lieu of Dr. Ramon Angeles and the late Juan M. Arellano, respectively.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of June, in the year, of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

(Sgd.) CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) NATALIO P. CASTILLO Executive Secretary
