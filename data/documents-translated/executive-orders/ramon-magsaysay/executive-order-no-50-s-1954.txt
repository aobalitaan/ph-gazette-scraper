MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 50

TERMINATING THE COLLECTION OF TOLLS AT THE HIBAGNAN TOLL BRIDGE, PROVINCE OF LEYTE

The total cost of the Hibagnan Toll Bridge, in the province of Leyte, plus interest at the rate of 4% per annum, having been fully recovered, as certified in accordance with the provisions of Act No. 3500, as amended, it is hereby ordered that the collection of tolls at the Hibagnan Toll Bridge be terminated.

This Order shall take effect upon receipt of copy hereof by the Provincial Treasurer of Leyte.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-four, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the ninth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
