MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 125

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 124, DATED AUGUST 25, 1955

By virtue of the powers nested in me by law, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Executive Order No. 124, dated August 25, 1955, so as to make the separation of the barrio of Bicos from the municipality of Rizal, province of Nueva Ecija, and its annexation to the municipality of Llanera, same province, effective January 1, 1956.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-five, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the tenth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
