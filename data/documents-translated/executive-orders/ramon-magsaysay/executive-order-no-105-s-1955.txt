MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 105

DESIGNATING THE COMMISSIONER OF PUBLIC HIGHWAYS AS AN ADDITIONAL MEMBER OF THE NATIONAL TRAFFIC COMMISSION CREATED IN EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 536 DATED OCTOBER 21, 1952, AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 569 DATED FEBRUARY 17, 1953

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the Commissioner of Public Highways as an additional member of the National Traffic Commission created in Executive Order No. 536 dated October 21, 1952, as amended by Executive Order No. 569 dated February 17, 1953.

Executive Order No. 536, as amended, is modified accordingly.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-five, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the ninth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
