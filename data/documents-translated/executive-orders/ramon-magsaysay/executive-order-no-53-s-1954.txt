MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 53

AMENDING FURTHER THE FIRST PARAGRAPH OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 79, DATED DECEMBER 17, 1945, ENTITLED “CREATING A QUEZON MEMORIAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE NATION-WIDE CAMPAIGN TO RAISE FUNDS FOR THE ERECTION OF A NATIONAL MONUMENT IN HONOR OF THE LATE PRESIDENT MANUEL L. QUEZON”

The first paragraph of Executive Order No. 79, dated December 17, 1945, entitled “Creating a Quezon Memorial Committee to Take Charge of the Nation-wide Campaign to Raise Funds for the Erection of a National Monument in Honor of the Late President Manuel L. Quezon”, as amended, is hereby further amended so as to include the President of the Quezon Society as additional member thereof.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-four, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the ninth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
