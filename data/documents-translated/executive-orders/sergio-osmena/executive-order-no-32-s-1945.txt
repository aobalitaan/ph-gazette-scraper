MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 32

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 25 PROMULGATING RULES AND REGULATIONS CONCERNING CURRENCY, BOOKS AND ACCOUNTS OF BANKS AND THEIR BRANCHES AND AGENCIES IN THE PROVINCES, PROVINCIAL, CITY AND MUNICIPAL TREASURIES AND OTHER GOVERNMENT ACCOUNTABLE OFFICES AND DEBT MORATORIUM

By virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and the laws of the Philippines, I, SERGIO OSMEÑA, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Title III, Debt Moratorium, of Executive Order No. 25, dated November 18, 1944, to read as follows:

III. DEBT MORATORIUM

Done at the City of Manila, this 10th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-five, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the tenth.

(Sgd.) SERGIO OSMEÑA President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOSE S. REYES Secretary to the President

Source: Malacañang Records Office

Office of the President of the Philippines. (1945). [Executive Order Nos. : 1W – 109]. Manila : Malacañang Records Office.
