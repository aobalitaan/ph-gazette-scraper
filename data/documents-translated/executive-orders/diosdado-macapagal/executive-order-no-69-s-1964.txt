MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 69

DESIGNATING THE COMMITTEE CREATED UNDER EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 30, DATED JANUARY 14, 1963, AS THE NATIONAL PARKS DEVELOPMENT COMMITTEE

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the Committee created under  Executive Order No. 30, dated January 14, 1963, as the National Parks Development Committee. The Committee shall take charge of the development of national parks, especially the Quezon Memorial Park and the Luneta Park.

The Committee is hereby empowered to create subcommittees as it may deem necessary to carry out its duties.

Executive Order No. 30, dated January 14, 1963, is hereby amended accordingly.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

(Sgd.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
