BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 60

DECLARING THAT THE NATIONAL ANTHEM OF THE PHILIPPINES SHALL BE SUNG ONLY IN ITS FILIPINO LYRICS

WHEREAS, the development and propagation of the National Language of the Philippines is one of the dreams of the nation’s heroes, in addition to being mandated by the Constitution of our Republic;

WHEREAS, certain occurrences happening in our archipelago, especially abroad, regarding the singing of our National Anthem in foreign languages are highly noticeable and inconsistent with the spirit of our nationhood; and

WHEREAS, the Filipino language is already one of our official languages.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law and in order to give life to the objectives of the Constitution and of Commonwealth Act No. 570, do hereby declare and order that the National Anthem of the Philippines shall be sung in its Filipino lyrics only on any occasion, whether here or abroad.

Done in the City of Manila, this nineteenth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-three.

(Signed) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

(Signed) RUFINO G. HECHANOVA Executive Secretary
