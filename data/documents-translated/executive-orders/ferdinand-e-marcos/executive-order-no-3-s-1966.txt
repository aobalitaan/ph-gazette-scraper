MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 3

AMENDING THE COMMITTEE CREATED UNDER EXECUTIVE NO. 30 DATED JANUARY 14, 1963, AS AMENDED BY EXECITUVE ORDER NO. 69 DATED FEBRUARY 7, 1964

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MRCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Executive Order No. 30 dated January 14, 1963, as amended, by designating the following to compose the National Parks Development Committee created thereunder:

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen and sixty-six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source : Malacañang Records Office
