MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 319

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 243, DATED JULY 7, 1970, ENTITLED “CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE FOR MINERAL EXPLORATIONS AND SURVEY OPERATIONS.”

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend the composition of the Committee created under Executive Order No. 243, dated July 7, 1970, as follows:

The Committee shall, in addition to its functions, recommend the adoption of Government policies, legislation, rules and regulations leading to accelerated capital investment formation, survey and drilling operations and allied activities, and perform such other functions on oil and mineral exploration and development as the President may direct.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
