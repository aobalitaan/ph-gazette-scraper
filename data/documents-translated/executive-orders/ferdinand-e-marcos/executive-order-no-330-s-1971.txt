MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 330

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 205 DATED JANUARY 9, 1970, ENTITLED “CREATING THE FOREIGN TRADE COUNCIL”

The composition of the Foreign Trade Council created under Executive Order No. 205 dated January 9, 1970, is hereby amended as follows:

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
