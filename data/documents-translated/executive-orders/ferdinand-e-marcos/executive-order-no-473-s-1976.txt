MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 473

MAKING THE SOLICITOR GENERAL A MEMBER OF THE CABINET

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby order that the Solicitor General shall be a member of the Cabinet with all the rights, honors and privileges pertaining to the position.

This Executive Order supersedes Executive Order No. 454.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
