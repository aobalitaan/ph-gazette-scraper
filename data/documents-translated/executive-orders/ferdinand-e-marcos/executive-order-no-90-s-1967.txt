MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 90

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 84 DATED SEPTEMBER 6, 1967, ENTITLED, “AUTHORIZING THE TEMPORARY TRANSFER OF THE ARCHIVES OF THE REPUBLIC FROM THE BUREAU OF RECORDS MANAGEMENT TO THE NATIONAL LIBRARY.”

Executive Order No. 84 dated September 6, 1967, entitled, “Authorizing the temporary transfer of the archives of the Republic from the Bureau of Records Management to the national Library”, is hereby amended by requiring that only those records which are 25 years old or over shall be transferred to, classified, recorded and kept by the National Library. All other records shall remain with the Bureau of Records Management.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
