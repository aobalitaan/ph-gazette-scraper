MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 370

TRANSFERRING THE ADMINISTRATION AND CUSTODY OF THE AGUINALDO MANSION IN KAWIT, CAVITE, FROM THE NATIONAL MUSEUM TO THE NATIONAL HISTORICAL COMMISSION.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby transfer the administration and custody of the Aguinaldo Mansion in Kawit, Cavite, from the National Museum to the National Historical Commission.

Executive Order No. 73 dated March 13, 1964, and other orders inconsistent herewith are hereby revoked or amended accordingly.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
