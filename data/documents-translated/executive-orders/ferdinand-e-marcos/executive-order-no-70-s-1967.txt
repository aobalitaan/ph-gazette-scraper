MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 70

TRANSFERRING CERTAIN FUNCTIONS OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT TO HEADS OF DEPARTMENTS, OFFICES AND ENTITIES.

In order to streamline the functions of the Office of the President and in accordance with the policy of decentralization, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby order the transfer of the following functions now being undertaken by the Office of the President to the heads of departments, offices or entities as indicated hereunder:

Function

Office to Handle

The provisions of Executive Order No. 298 dated August 12, 1940, as amended, Executive Order No. 201 dated January 14, 1949, and Executive Order No. 175 dated November 11, 1930, and rules and regulations as are in conflict herewith are hereby modified or repealed accordingly.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
