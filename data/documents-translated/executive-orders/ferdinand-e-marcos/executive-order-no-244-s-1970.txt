MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 244

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 168 DATED FEBRUARY 5, 1969, ENTITLED “CREATING A SMALL FARMERS COMMISSION”.

The membership of the Commission created under Executive Order No. 168 dated February 5, 1969, is hereby amended to read as follows:

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source: Malacañang Records Office

Office of the President of the Philippines. (1970). [Executive Order Nos. : 141-280]. Manila : Malacañang Records Office.
