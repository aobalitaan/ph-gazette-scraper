MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 288

AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 155, DATED OCTOBER 28, 1968, WHICH CREATED THE GOMEZ, BURGOS, ZAMORA CENTENNIAL COMMISSION

Executive Order No. 155, dated October 28, 1968, is hereby amended by designating Fr. Horacio de la Costa as Chairman, vice Justice Jose Ma. Paredes, who has been elected delegate to the Constitutional Convention, and by including Justice Calixto Zaldivar and Mr. Carlos Quirino as additional members of the GOMEZ, BURGOS, ZAMORA CENTENNIAL COMMISSION created therein.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
