MALACAÑANG

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 173

ABOLISHING THE NATIONAL YOUTH COORDINATING COUNCIL

By virtue of the powers vested upon me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby abolish the National Youth Coordinating Council created under Executive Order No. 83 dated September 6, 1967.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
