MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 356

CREATING A SPECIAL EVALUATION AND AWARDS COMMITTEE TO EVALUATE BID PROPOSALS AND PROPOSED NEGOTIATED CONTRACTS OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL RAILWAYS, NATIONAL POWER CORPORATION AND PEOPLE’S HOMESITE AND HOUSING CORPORATION.

WHEREAS, there is an urgent need to ensure a coordinated and expeditious implementation of the Rehabilitation and Selective Modernization Projects of the Philippine National Railways (PNR) and of the foreign-assisted projects of the National Power Corporation (NPC) and the People’s Homesite and Housing Corporation (PHHC);

NOW, THEREFOR, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create a Special Evaluation and Awards Committee which shall evaluate all prequalification requirements, bid proposals and proposed negotiated contracts and make or approve awards thereof, composed of the following:

All bid proposals and proposed negotiated contracts for the Rehabilitation and Selective Modernization projects of PNR and the foreign assisted projects of NPC and PHHC shall no longer be processed or evaluated by the Boards of Directors and existing award committees in PNR, NPC, PHHC, the Permanent Cabinet Committee on Public Bidding or the Permanent Cabinet Committee on Negotiated Contracts, but are hereby automatically transferred to the jurisdiction of this Special Evaluation and Awards Committee which process, evaluate and approve same in accordance with existing laws or rules applicable thereto.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of December, in the Year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
