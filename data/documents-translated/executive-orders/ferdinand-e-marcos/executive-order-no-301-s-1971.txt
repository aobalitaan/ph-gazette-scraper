MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 301

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 155 DATED OCTOBER 28, 1968, AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 288 DATED FEBRUARY 6, 1971, WHICH CREATED THE GOMEZ, BURGOS, ZAMORA CENTENNIAL COMMISSION

Executive Order No. 155 dated October 28, 1968, as amended by Executive Order No. 288 dated February 6, 1971, is further amended by reconstituting the Gomez, Burgos, Zamora Centennial Commission as follows:

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source : Malacañang Records Office

Office of the President of the Philippines. (1971). [Executive Order Nos. : 281 – 365 ]. Manila : Malacañang Records Office.
