CLASSIFYING MUNICIPAL DISTRICTS IN THE PHILIPPINES AND DECLARING CERTAIN MUNICIPAL DISTRICTS AS MUNICIPALITIES

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
