MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 676

APPROVING THE 1981 INVESTMENT PRIORITIES PLAN

In accordance with the specific recommendations stated in the memorandum of the Chairman of the Board of Investments dated 30 March 1981, the proposed 1981 Investment Priorities Plan is hereby approved.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant

Source: Malacañang Records Office
