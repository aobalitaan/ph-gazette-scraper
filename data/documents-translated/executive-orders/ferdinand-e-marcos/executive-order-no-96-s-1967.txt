MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 96

DECREEING THAT ALL GOVERNMENT EDIFICES, BUILDING AND OFFICES BE NAMED IN FILIPINO.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby decree that henceforth all government edifices, buildings and offices shall be named in Filipino.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
