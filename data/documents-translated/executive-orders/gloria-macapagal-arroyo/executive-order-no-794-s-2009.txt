MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 794

CREATING A PRESIDENTIAL TASK FORCE ON EMERGENCY EMPLOYMENT

WHEREAS, the generation of employment and livelihood opportunities for targeted income groups is part of the country’s best preparation in thwarting the problems brought about by the global economic crisis;

NOW THEREFORE, I, GLORIA M. ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create a Presidential Task Force on Emergency Employment to be chaired by the President, with the following as members:

DONE on this 10 th day of April in the Year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Nine.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
