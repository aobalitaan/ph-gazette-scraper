MALACAÑANG MANILA

EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 229

FURTHER AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 175, DATED APRIL 30, 1994, AS AMENDED BY EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 223, DATED FEBRUARY 7, 1995, WHICH CREATED A NORTHWESTERN LUZON GROWTH QUADRANGLE COMMISSION

I, FIDEL V. RAMOS , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby further amend Section 1 of Executive Order No. 175, dated April 30, 1994, as amended by Executive Order No. 223, dated February 7, 1995, which created a Northwestern Luzon Growth Quadrangle Commission, to include the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, Secretary of Transportation and Communications, Secretary of Public Works and Highways, and Secretary of Energy as Commission Members.

This Executive Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-five.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
