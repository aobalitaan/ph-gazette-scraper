Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 84

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO REVIEW MARITIME SAFETY ADMINISTRATION/FUNCTIONS

In view of the study for the separation of the Philippine Coast Guard (PCG) from the Philippine Navy (PN) and its eventual placement under the Department of Transportation and Communications (DOTC), there is hereby created a Committee to Review Maritime Safety Administration/Functions, composed of the following:

The Committee shall study, review, and analyze maritime safety functions as enumerated under Executive Order No. 125-A. The feasibility of either amending Executive Order No. 125-A using Presidential authority under the Administrative Code of 1987, or preparing an administrative bill to accomplish the desired result shall be determined.

A technical committee may be created by the Committee to provide technical support.

The study and recommendation of the Committee shall be submitted within three (3) months hereof.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately

DONE in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Three.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ANTONIO T. CARPIO Chief Presidential Legal Counsel
