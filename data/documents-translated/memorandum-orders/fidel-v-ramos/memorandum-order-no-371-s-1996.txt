MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 371

CREATING AN INTER-AGENCY TASK FORCE TO FAST-TRACK INVESTIGATION OF SUSPICIOUS FIRE INCIDENTS IN GOVERNMENT OFFICES AND HEAVILY-INSURED PRIVATE BUILDINGS

There is hereby created an Inter-Agency Task Force to conduct speedy investigation of suspicious fire incidents in government offices and heavily-insured private buildings, immediate apprehension of suspected arsonists and fast disposition of arson cases filed in court.

The Task Force shall be composed of the following:

Within thirty (30) days upon issuance of this Order, the Task Force shall submit to the Office of the President the implementing guidelines to effect an intensified preventive drive against incidents of arson and appropriate measures to enable the speedy apprehension of suspected arsonists and subsequent filing of charges against them.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 15 th day of May in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Six.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
