SUBJECT : CONGRESSIONAL PARTICIPATION IN ALL LOAN
PROGRAMS AND DEBT NEGOTIATIONS

DATE : 13 July 1992

In a meeting with the Senators held last 11 July 1992,
the issue on the need for better participation/consultation
with the Legislature on all loan programs and debt
negotiations was raised.

In this regard, please ensure that all loan programs
and debt negotiations are done in consultation with the

Legislature.
