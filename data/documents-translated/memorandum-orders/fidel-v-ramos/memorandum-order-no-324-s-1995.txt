Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 324

There is hereby created a task force to prepare a comprehensive plan aimed at optimizing tourism, investment and trade opportunities of Club Intramuros, Manila Hotel and the Walled City (of Intramuros). The Task Force shall likewise address the continuing degeneration of the Walled City brought about by the squatters, deteriorating buildings and lack of teamwork among concerned agencies.

The Task Force shall submit its first progress report to the Office of the President, through the Office of the Executive Secretary, within thirty (30) days from issuance of this Order, and the plan not later than 30 June 1996.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
