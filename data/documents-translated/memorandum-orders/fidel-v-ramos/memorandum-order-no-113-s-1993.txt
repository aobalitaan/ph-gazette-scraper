MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 113

EXTENDING THE DEADLINE OF THE PRESIDENTIAL AD-HOC COCONUT LEVY FUNDS AUDIT COMMITTEE UNDER ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 19

The Presidential Ad-Hoc Coconut Levy Funds Audit Committee which was created pursuant to Administrative Order No. 19 (s. 1992) and was directed to submit its findings and recommendations not later than ninety (90) days from 9 November 1993 is, upon the recommendation of its Chairman, former Ambassador Emmanuel Pelaez, to afford the Committee more time considering the voluminous records that it has to collate, audit and evaluate, hereby authorized to complete its work and submit its findings and recommendations to the President not later than ninety (90) days from the date of this order.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 31st day of March in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Three.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ANTONIO T. CARPIO Chief Presidential Legal Counsel
