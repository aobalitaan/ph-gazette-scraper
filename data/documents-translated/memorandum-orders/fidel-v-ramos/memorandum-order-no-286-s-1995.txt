MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 286

MODIFYING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 271

To enable the Presidential Fact-Finding and Policy Advisory Commission on the Protection of Overseas Filipinos to judiciously finish its tasks as defined under Executive No. 231 dated 20 March, 1995, the period set in Memorandum Order No. 271, series of 1995, for the submission of a final report, the disposition of all pending matters being handled by the Commission and the winding up of its operation is hereby extended up to July 15, 1995.

Memorandum Order No. 271 is modified accordingly.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 29 th day of June in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-five.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
