MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 272

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 256 DATED FEBRUARY 5, 1995 ADOPTING A NEW WORK SCHEDULE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (PROPER) PURSUANT TO MC NO. 111 DATED JANUARY 17, 1995

Memorandum Order No. 256, dated February 6, 1995, prescribing a new flexi-time work schedule in the Office of the President (Proper), the same is hereby amended after assessing its effectiveness, as follows:

All other offices not mentioned above shall observe regular office hours (8AM – 5PM) on Mondays to Fridays, and may report for official duty on Saturdays, as may be necessary.

As much as practicable, the flexi-time will be adopted by allowing the personnel to work in shifts taking into account the number of staffs in a particular office. Overtime service shall be resorted to only when necessary and payment thereof will be in accordance with existing rules and policy on the matter.

Those who will be on official duty on Saturdays shall observe a day-off on the following Monday, provided that in the event they cannot avail themselves of this off-setting arrangement in the exigency of the service, they shall be entitled to overtime pay by the hour for their services on the weekend.

All officials and employees, regardless of their work schedule, are required to be present at the Flag Ceremony on Mondays.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEONARDO A. QUISUMBING Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, March 21, 1995
