Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 59

CREATING A CORDILLERA SELECTION COMMITTEE TO SCREEN NOMINEES TO THE CORDILLERA EXECUTIVE BOARD (CEB)

There is hereby created a Cordillera Selection Committee to screen and process nominees for members of the Cordillera Executive Board (CEB):

Office of the Executive Secretary

National Security Council Secretariat

National Economic and Development Authority

The Committee is hereby given thirty (30) days to submit its recommendation to the Office of the President.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDELMIRO A. AMANTE, SR. Executive Secretary

Manila, 29 October 1992
