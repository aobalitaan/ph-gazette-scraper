Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 333

There is hereby created an Advisory Group which shall assist the Over-all Supervisor of the various Communication Task Forces, created pursuant to Memorandum Order No. 327 (s. 1995).

The Council shall be composed of Secretary of Justice Teofisto T. Guingona, Jr., Secretary of Budget Salvador M. Enriquez, Jr., the Chief President Legal Counsel, Presidential Adviser on National Security Jose T. Almonte, and Presidential Adviser on Political Matters Gabriel Claudio.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

Manila, January 19, 1996
