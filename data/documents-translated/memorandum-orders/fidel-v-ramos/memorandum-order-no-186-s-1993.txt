MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 186

CREATING AN AD-HOC COMMITTEE TO STUDY THE PROPOSED TRANSFER OF BOTH HOUSES OF CONGRESS TO THE PNB COMPLEX IN ROXAS BOULEVARD

There is hereby created an ad-hoc committee on the proposed transfer of both houses of Congress to the PNB Complex in Roxas Boulevard, composed of the following:

The Committee shall convene immediately to study the proposal, take necessary steps to pursue the same, and submit its recommendation not later than 31 December 1993.

This Order takes effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila this 30th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
