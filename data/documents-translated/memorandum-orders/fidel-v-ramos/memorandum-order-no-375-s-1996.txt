MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 375

DESIGNATING THE CHAIRMAN AND MEMBERS OF THE PRESIDENTIAL FACT-FINDING COMMISSION ON GOVERNMENT INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PROJECTS

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 341 dated June 05, 1996, the following are hereby designated to constitute the Presidential Fact-Finding Commission on Government Information Technology Projects:

The Commission is hereby enjoined to immediately convene and perform its tasks as defined in the above-cited Executive Order.

The Commission shall, as far as practicable, submit a monthly progress report to the President through the Executive Secretary and a comprehensive final report within ninety (90) days from its constitution, unless a longer period is needed and approved by the President.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and ninety-six.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
