Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 441

AUTHORIZING THE CONDUCT IN THIS OFFICE OF AN INFORMATION DRIVE ON THE REVISED GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE SYSTEM’S (GSIS) CHARTER (RA 8291) AND OTHER CHANGES ON BENEFITS AND PRIVILEGES ACCORDED TO ITS MEMBERS.

To update our officials and employees who are members of the Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) on the new retirement benefits under the revised GSIS Charter (RA 8291), the Personnel Office, through the Career and Personnel Development Division (CPDD), in coordination with the GSIS, will be conducting a Five-Session Info Drive from Monday to Friday, starting September 8 to 12, 1997, at 1:30-2:30 P.M., at the MIS Room, Old GSIS Building, Arroceros, Manila.

Please submit to the CPDD the names of your interested members.

(Sgd.) LUIS C. LIWANAG II Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, August 14, 1997
