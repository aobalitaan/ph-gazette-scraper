MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 437

TO : ALL CABINET OFFICIALS

SUBJECT : DISCLOSURE OF FINANCIAL AND BUSINESS

DATE : 13 July 1992 ~

In a meeting with the Senators held last 11 July 1992,
the issue on the requirements for the confirmation of
appointments was raised. k

In view of the above and in compliance with existing
laws, all cabinet officials are recommended to prepare the
necessary documents required for a full disclosure of your
financial and business interest and effect the divestment of
your assets where conflicts of interest may arise.
