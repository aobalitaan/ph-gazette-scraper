Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO . 463

FURTHER RECONSTITUTING THE SELECTION BOARD FOR FIRST AND SECOND LEVEL POSITIONS AND THE SPECIAL BOARD FOR THIRD LEVEL CAREER POSITIONS IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (PROPER)

The composition of the Selection Board in the Office of the President for the First and Second Level positions, created under Memorandum Order No. 248, dated 21 August 1989, which was reconstituted under Memorandum Order No. 100, dated 04 March 1993, and Memorandum Order No. 423, dated March 21, 1997, is hereby further reconstituted as follows:

The composition of the Special Board in the Office of the President to screen candidates to vacant career positions of directors, created under Memorandum Order No. 46, dated 13 October 1992, which was reconstituted under Memorandum Order No. 99, dated 04 March 1993, and Memorandum Order No. 423, dated March 21, 1997, is hereby further reconstituted as follows:

The Director of the Personnel Office shall serve as Secretary of both Boards.

(Sgd.) ALEXANDER P. AGUIRRE Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, February 6, 1998
