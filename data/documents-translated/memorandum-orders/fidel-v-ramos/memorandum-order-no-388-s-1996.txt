[ Memorandum Order No. 388, July 15, 1996 ]
AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 348, DATED MARCH 8, 1996.
Memorandum Order No. 348, dated March 8, 1996, is hereby amended, so as to authorize the Personnel Office, through the CPDD, to disburse the amount of Ten Thousand Pesos (P10,000.00), representing payment for the instructors for aerobics/dance exercise for 12 sessions, payable to the UP Human Kinetics, chargeable against the funds of the Sports Committee, subject to the Accounting and Auditing rules.
