MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 351

There is hereby created a Committee of Peers which shall look into and evaluate the merits of the various allegations raised against Secretary Hilarion J. Ramiro, Jr., Department of Health, including those contained in the privilege speech delivered by Senator Juan M. Flavier on 13 March 1996.

The Committee shall be composed of Secretary Teofisto T. Guingona, Jr., as Chairman, with Chief Presidential Legal Counsel Renato L. Cayetano and Secretary William G. Padolina as Members.

The Committee shall submit its report and recommendations to the Office of the President, thru the Office of the Executive Secretary, not later than 31 March 1996.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 14 th day of March in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Six.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
