Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 197

AUTHORIZING THE CONDUCT OF “BIGAY ALAM AY BIGAY BUHAY” AND “PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT” SEMINARS IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Pursuant to Proclamation No. 227 dated March 17, 1988, declaring the month of March as “WOMEN’S ROLE IN HISTORY MONTH,” this office will conduct two (2) sets of seminars as a tribute to the Filipina and a recognition of her true worth, namely:

1. “Bigay Alam ay Bigay Buhay” – a seminar on cancer prevention on March 24, 1994 from 8:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon and 1:30-5:00 p.m.; and

2. “Personality Development Seminar – a seminar scheduled on March 25, 1994 from 8:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon and 1:30 to 5:00 p.m.

These seminars will be held at the Briefing Room, Administration Building.

The attendance of three (3) lady employees from the different offices and major units of the Office is hereby authorized.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEONARDO A. QUISUMBING Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, March 23, 1994
