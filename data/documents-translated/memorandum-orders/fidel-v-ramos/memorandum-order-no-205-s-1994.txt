MALACAÑANG Manila

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO 205

ENJOINING ALL LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN TO SUPPORT THE KABATAAN: 2000

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 139, all Governors and Mayors are hereby urged to implement and provide funding thereof the appropriate programs and activities for students and out-of-school youths in their respective localities from April, 1994 onwards to support the KABATAAN: 2000. For this purpose, the participation of the Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) should be maximized.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of April in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
