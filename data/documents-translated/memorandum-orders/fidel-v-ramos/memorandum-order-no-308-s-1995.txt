Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 308

There is hereby created an Inter-Agency Task Force to expedite the liquidation of outstanding cash advances of the various departments and agencies of the Executive Department as of December 31, 1994. The Task Force shall be chaired by Deputy Executive Secretary Luis C. Liwanag II, with one Undersecretary each from the following departments.

1. Department of Education Culture and Sports (DECS)

2. Department of Agriculture (DA)

3. Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH)

4. Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA)

5. Department of Transportation and Communications (DOTC)

6. Department of Interior and Local Government (DILG)

7. Department of National Defense (DND)

8. Presidential Management Staff (PMS)

9. Two (2) representatives from the Office of the President

The Task Force shall submit its report to the Office of the President through the Executive Secretary with specific recommendations not later than September 15, 1995.

By authority of the President (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

25 August 1995
