Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 356

CREATING A LEDAC TASK FORCE ON HEADQUARTERING BILLS PROGRAM FOR COMMERCIAL AND SUPPORT SERVICES

There is hereby created a LEDAC Task Force on Headquartering Bills Program for Commercial and Support Services to be composed of the following:

The Task Force shall be responsible for consolidating all the bills pending and those still to be filled in the Senate and in the House seeking the creation of a hospitable climate for multi-national companies to transfer their headquarters and operations in the country, including the setting-up of one-stop-shop processing facility.

This Memorandum shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 29th day of March in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Six.

BY AUTHORITY OF THE PRESIDENT: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
