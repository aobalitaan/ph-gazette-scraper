MALACAÑAN PALACE

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 55, October 19, 1992 ]
RENAMING THE HOUSEHOLD OFFICE AS THE INTERNAL HOUSE AFFAIRS OFFICE
The Household Office is hereby renamed as the Internal House Affairs Office of the Private Office of the Office of the President Proper.
