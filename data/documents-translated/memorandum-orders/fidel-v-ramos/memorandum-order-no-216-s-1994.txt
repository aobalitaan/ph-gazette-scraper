Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 216

RECONSTITUTING THE COMPOSITION OF THE COMMITTEE ON RECORDS AND DISPOSAL FOR THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (PROPER)

Memorandum Order No. 171, series of 1988, is hereby reconstituted to be composed of heads of offices of the following:

Malacañang Records Office Legal Office Administrative Office Finance Office Office of the Executive Secretary/Sr. Deputy Executive Secretary Presidential Action Center

The Committee shall not only participate in the evaluation and appraisal of records for disposition purposes, but shall also take part in the development of improved systems and procedures in records management.

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEONARDO A. QUISUMBING Sr. Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, June 29, 1994

Source : Malacañang Records Office
