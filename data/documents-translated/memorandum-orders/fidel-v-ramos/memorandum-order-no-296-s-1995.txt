Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 296

CREATING A HOUSING COMMITTEE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

1. There is hereby created in the Office of the President (Proper) a Housing Committee, hereinafter referred to as the Committee, composed of the following:

The representative of the Executive Secretary to the National Housing Authority Board of Directors shall act as the Secretary.

2. The Committee shall have the following functions:

(a) To conduct a survey of OP officials and employees who are in need of a housing unit and have not previously availed of any housing benefit in the OP;

(b) To identify real property suitable for housing;

(c) To submit OP Housing Program for endorsement to the President; and

(d) To perform such other functions in furtherance of the foregoing.

3. This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

Source: CDAsia

Manila, July 26, 1995
