MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 318, October 25, 1995 ]
THE NATIONAL BUREAU OF INVESTIGATION IS HEREBY DIRECTED TO CONDUCT A FORMAL INQUIRY INTO THE REPORTED UNSCRUPULOUS PRACTICES BOTH IN THE PRIVATE AND PUBLIC SECTORS WHICH CAUSE THE SHORTAGE OF RICE AND CORN IN THE MARKET.
The NBI shall submit its report and recommendations to the Office of the President, through the Office of the Executive Secretary, not later than November 30, 1995.
