Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 262

Memorandum Order No. 222, series of 1971, creating a Technical Committee for Small Areas Manufacture, is hereby amended to road as follows:

The Technical Committee shall gather all information and data on the various proposals for establishing a local small arms plant/arms manufacture submitted for consideration of the national Security Council. It small also present to the NSC all relevant information needed to arrive at a decision as to the boat proposal to be selected.

So assist the Technical Committee in its tasks, the following are designated Consultants/Advisers:

CG, AFP Supply Center CO, Naval Shore Establishment, HPN CO, PAF DEPOT, HPAF Chief, Ordinance & Chemical Service, AFP Mr. Agustin Cancio, Private Sector

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
