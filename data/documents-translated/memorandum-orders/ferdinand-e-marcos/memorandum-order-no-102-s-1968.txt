BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 102, May 28, 1968 ]
ACTIVATING THE PRESIDENTIAL SECURITY AGENCY
To attain the objectives of Memorandum Order No. 59, this Office, dated September 30, 1963, as amended, the Presidential Security Agency which has been unfilled is activated.
Major Fabian C. Ver is designated Director of Security effective June 1, 1968.
