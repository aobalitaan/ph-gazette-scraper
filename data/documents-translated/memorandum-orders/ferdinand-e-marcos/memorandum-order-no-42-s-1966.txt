Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 42

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY THE ORGANIZATION OF THE DIFFERENT OFFICES, SCREEN AND PASS UPON ALL APPOINTMENTS OF LOCAL OFFICIALS FOR THE NEWLY CREATED PROVINCES IN THE MOUNTAIN PROVINCE.

There is hereby created a Committee to conduct a thorough study of the organization of the different offices, screen and pass upon, for recommendation to the President, all proposals or recommendations for appointments of local officials for the new provinces created under Republic Act No. 4695; namely, Benguet, Mountain Province, Ifugao and Kalinga-Apayao, to he composed of the following;

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, August 26, 1966
