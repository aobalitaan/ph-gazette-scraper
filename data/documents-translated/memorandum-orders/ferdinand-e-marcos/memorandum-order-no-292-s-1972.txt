Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 292

In view of the necessity and urgency of settling the existing boundary dispute between the provinces of Misamis Oriental and Agusan del Norte, a Survey Team is hereby created to be composed of the following officials from the Bureau of Lands to assist the Office of the President in determining the true and correct boundary line between the above-mentioned provinces:

The Survey Team shall proceed immediately to the area concerned and shall submit its report to the

Executive Secretary within the soonest possible time.

So ordered.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, July 6, 1972
