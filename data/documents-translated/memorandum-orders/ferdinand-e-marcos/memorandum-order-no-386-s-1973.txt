TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 386

AUTHORIZING THE PERSONNEL OF THE PRIVATE OFFICE AND PRESIDENTIAL LIBRARY TO OBSERVE DAILY OFFICE HOURS FROM 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to General Order No. 30, the personnel of the Private Office and the Presidential Library are hereby authorized to observe daily office hours from 8:00 A.M. to 8:00 P.M.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, September 28, 1973
