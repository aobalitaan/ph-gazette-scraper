MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 136, August 25, 1969 ]
REQUIRING THE SUBMISSION OF PERFORMANCE AND ATTENDANCE REPORTS.
Beginning the current week, all heads of units in the Office of the President are hereby required to submit weekly performance reports to the Executive Secretary, through the Presidential Staff Secretary who shall collate the same, and weekly reports on daily attendance of their respective personnel through the Administrative Division, also for collation.
