Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 28

AUTHORIZING A NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES IN THE OFFICE OF THE SOCIAL SECRETARY TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE.

In view of the exigencies of the service, the Social Secretary is hereby authorized to assign four (4) employees in her office to render service beyond the regular office hours and during Saturdays, Sundays and holidays with meal allowance for overtime service rendered in accordance with the time schedule prescribed under Memorandum Order No. 94 of this Office dated August 17, 1964, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

This Order takes effect as of February 1, 1966.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, May 17, 1966

Source: Malacañang Records Office
