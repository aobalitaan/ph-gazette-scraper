Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 323

DESIGNATING THE REPRESENTATIVES OF THE DIFFERENT DIVISIONS AND OFFICES IN THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE TO THE PUBLIC INFORMATION UNIT CREATED, UNDER MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 317, DATED NOVEMBER 20, 1972.

In consonance with Memorandum Order No. 317, dated November 20, 1972, the following are designated as represenatives of the different divisions and offices in the Executive Office:

The abovementioned representatives are authorized to appoint their alternates in their respective divisions or offices.

The Malacañang Information and Assistant Unit is hereby constituted as the Secretariat of the Public Information Unit.

The Assistant Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs is hereby authorized to issue memorandum orders and circulars to properly implement Memorandum Order No. 317, dated November 20, 1972.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, December 12, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
