Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 183

ALLOWING THE DISMISSAL OF OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES AT 8:45 A.M. ON MAY 26, 1970, FOR THE PURPOSE HEREUNDER INDICATED.

The closing ceremonies of the Seminar on Management Techniques for Middle Management will be held on May 26, 1970, at the Heroes Hall, Malacañang Palace starting at 9:00 A.M. His Excellency, President Ferdinand E. Marcos, will be the guest speaker.

In view hereof, all officials and employees are hereby requested to attend the above-mentioned ceremonies, and for the purpose, their dismissal at 8:45 A.M. on the said date is hereby authorized.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, May 23, 1970

Source: Malacañang Records Office
