Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 196

ALLOWING EACH OFFICE PRINCIPAL UNIT IN THIS OFFICE TO SEND NOT MORE THAN TWO (2) PARTICIPANTS IN THE SEMINAR ON “ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND THE LEGISLATIVE PROCESS”.

In line with the policy Office to allow officials and employees to attend, as much as possible, seminars or in-service training to enable them to become more effective in the performance of their duties, each office or principal unit in this Office is hereby authorized to send on official time not more than two (2) participants in the seminar on “Economic Development and the Legislative Process”, jointly sponsored by the Office, House of Representatives, starting at 3:00 o’clock p.m. today, at the auditorium of the Philippine Banking Corporation, P.B.C. Building, Port Area, Manila.

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, August 24, 1970

Source: Malacañang Records Office
