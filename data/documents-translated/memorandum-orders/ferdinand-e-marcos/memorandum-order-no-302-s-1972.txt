Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 302

REITERATING STRICT COMPLIANCE WITH MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 204, SERIES OF 1970.

In line with the security measures being instituted in this Office, strict compliance with the provisions of Memorandum Order No. 204, series of 1970, “Requiring Officials, and Employees in the Office of the President to Wear Nameplates,” is hereby reiterated.

In this connection, the second paragraph of the aforementioned Order is hereby amended so as to authorize officials and employees of this Office to carry their nameplates outside of the premises of the Malacañang Compound. Each and everyone is, however, enjoined to use his/her nameplate solely for identification, in addition to his/her identification card, and is hereby warned against the use thereof for any unlawful or illicit purpose.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, September 25, 1972
