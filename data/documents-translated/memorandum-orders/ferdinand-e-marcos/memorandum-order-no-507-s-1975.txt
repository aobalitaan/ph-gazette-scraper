TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 507

AUTHORIZING ELEVEN (11) PERSONNEL OF THE PROVOST OFFICE TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICES.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum No. 192-74 dated August 20, 1974, the following eleven (11) personnel of the Provost Office, this Office, are hereby authorized to render overtime services during the State Visit in the country of the President of Romania on April 9-13, 1975, inclusive, with additional compensation for overtime services actually rendered by them is connection therewith pursuant to the provisions of Section 7-1 (32) of Commonwealth Act No. 246, as amended, accounting and auditing requirements:

1. Maj. Benjamin 0. Feliciano

2. MSgt.Fidel Barredo

3. MSgt.Josue Laserna

4. MSgt.Herminiano Malapitan

5. SSgt.Arturo Aragon

6. SSgt.Antonio Palencia

7. SSgt.Matias Torres

8. SSgt.Bonifacio Avila

9. SSgt.Diogenes Vargas,

10. Sgt. Juanito Sapigao

11. Sgt. Carlos Bascos

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, April 24, 1975
