Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 614

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF A SUPERVISORY TRAINING FOR EFFECTIVE ADMINISTRATIVE MANAGEMENT IN ACCORDANCE WITH LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS No. 518.

Pursuant to Letter of Instructions No. 518 whereby the President has authorized the conduct of skill-development-oriented supervisory training program in all government offices in coordination with the Civil Service Commission, all supervisors in the Office of the President and in offices under or attached to it who have completed the CSC-sponsored Junior Executive Training Program with no less than five (5) subordinates are hereby required to attend a Supervisory Training for Effective Administrative Management to be conducted by this Office every Monday, Wednesday and Friday, from 1:30 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. either at the following:

Date

Place

All heads of primary units are hereby enjoined to authorize qualified JET graduates based on the above-mentioned guidelines to attend this training program and to refrain from giving the participants any assignments during the time they are actually attending training in order not to disrupt their attendance therein.

Expenses for the conduct of the training shall be borne by the participating agencies on a pro-rata basis.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, April 25, 1978
