Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 791

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING IN THIS OFFICE OF A TRAINING WORKSHOP ON IMPROVED RECORDS MANAGEMENT: SYSTEMS AND PROCEDURES

To enable our employees who are involved in the creation, maintenance and disposition of records to have a better understanding of the improved systems and procedures of records management, the Training Staff of this Office is hereby authorized to hold a Training Workshop on Improved Records Management: System and Procedures from August 8 to 17, 1983, from 8:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m. at the Briefing. Room, 4th Floor of the Administration Building.

All heads of primary units are hereby enjoined to submit the names of their participants to the Personnel Office on or before. Friday, July 15, 1983.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, July 12, 1983
