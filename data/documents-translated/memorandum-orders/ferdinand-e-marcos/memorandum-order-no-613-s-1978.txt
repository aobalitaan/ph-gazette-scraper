Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 613

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 62, SERIES OF 1963, “CREATING A RECORDS MANAGEMENT IMPROVEMENT COMMITTEE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.”

Memorandum Order No. 62, series of 1963, as amended, “Creating a Records Management Improvement Committee in the Office of the President,” is hereby further amended so as to include among the functions of the Committee, the evaluation and appraisal of disposable inactive records, and non-record materials including periodicals, journals, newspapers, magazines, etc., in the various units of this Office.

Effective immediately, all heads of primary and other units in this Office shall turn over periodically all said disposable materials to the Malacañang Records Office for evaluation and appraisal of the Committee.

Strict compliance with this Order is hereby enjoined.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, April 25, 1978

Source: Malacañang Records Office
