Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 20

REQUIRING THE SUBMISSION OF THE LIST OF EMPLOYEES UNDER EACH DIVISION OR PRIMARY UNIT IN THIS OFFICE WHO ARE BEING PAID PROM THE LUMP SUM APPROPRIATIONS.

In order to provide an equitable distribution of personnel in the Office of the President who are being paid from, the lump sum appropriations, Chiefs of Division and Heads of Primary units shall submit a list of employees under their respective divisions or units who are being paid from the lump sum appropriations not later than five o’clock tomorrow afternoon.

(Sgd.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 1, 1966

Source: Malacañang Records Office
