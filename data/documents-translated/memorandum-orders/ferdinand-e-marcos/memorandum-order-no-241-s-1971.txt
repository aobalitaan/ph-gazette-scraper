BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 241, July 13, 1971 ]
DESIGNATING THE EDP DIRECTOR OF THE NATIONAL COMPUTER CENTER (NCC)
Pursuant to Section 3 of Executive Order No. 322, series of 1971, establishing the National Computer Center (NCC), Mr. JUAN M. SANCHEZ is hereby designated as EDP Director, charged with the direct administration and operational management of the National Computer Center.
