TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 514

EXTENDING THE PERIOD COVERED BY THE AUTHORITY GRANTED TO CERTAIN PERSONNEL OF THE MALACAÑANG ENGINEER’S OFFICE TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICES WITH ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION.

Paragraph 2 of Memo. Order No. 512 dated May 22, 1975 is hereby amended to mean six (6), instead of two (2) other employees who are similarly authorized to render overtime services in connection with the Mini Park Project under Memorandum Order No. 501, dated March 24, 1975 are likewise hereby allowed to extend such services from April 13 to July 31, 1975, as an exception to said Memorandum 192-74, subject to the availability of funds and the usual audit.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, May 29, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
