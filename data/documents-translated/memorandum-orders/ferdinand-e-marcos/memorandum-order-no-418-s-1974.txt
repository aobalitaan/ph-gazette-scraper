Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 418

Memorandum Order No. 413 dated March 21, 1974, authorizing not more than six (6) Records Office personnel as may be assigned by the Presidential Staff Director therein to render overtime service with one (1) meal a day On Fridays, Saturdays, Sunday’s and holidays effective February 1, 1974 and until June 30, 1974, is hereby amended so as to authorize the said personnel to render overtime services after regular office hours, subject to the same terms and conditions set forth in the aforesaid Order.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 26, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
