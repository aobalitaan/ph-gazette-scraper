MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 221

CREATING A VALUATION COMMITTEE FOR THE PURPOSE OF APPRAISING THE PROPERTY OF THE NATIONAL MARKETING CORPORATION (NAMARCO) MORTGAGED WITH THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL BANK (PNB)

With the end in view of effecting the transfer of real properties owned by the National Marketing Corporation (NAMARCO) in favor of the Philippine National Bank (PNB) by way of dation in payment, for the partial satisfaction of that corporation’s obligations to the bank, a Valuation Committee, is hereby created to appraise the real properties so held to be composed, of:

The Committee is directed to submit its report to the Office of the President upon completion of the valuation.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, March 18, 1971
