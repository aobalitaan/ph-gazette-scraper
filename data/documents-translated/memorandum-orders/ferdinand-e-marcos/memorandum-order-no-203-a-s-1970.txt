Office of the President of the Philippines

Manila, November 11, 1970

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 203-A

CONSTITUTING THE PANEL TO NEGOTIATE THE PARTICIPATION OF UNION OIL COMPANY OF CALIFORNIA IN THE EXPLORATION AND DEVELOPMENT OF GEOTHERMAL RESOURCES AT TIWI, ALBAY.

The following are hereby designated to form a panel to negotiate the participation of Union Oil Company of California in the exploration and development of geothermal resources at Tiwi, Albay:

The panel is hereby instructed to meet with officials designated by Union Oil Company of California to negotiate the arrangements for Union Oil’s participation.

In the performance of its functions, the panel is authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or instrumentality of the government, including government owned or controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need.

Upon completion of the negotiations, the panel shall submit its report and recommendations to the President, thru the Chairman of the Power Development Council.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
