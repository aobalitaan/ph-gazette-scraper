MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 574, September 29, 1976 ]
AMENDING THE COMPOSITION OF THE INTER-AGENCY COORDINATING COMMITTEE FOR THE REHABILITATION OF DISPLACED POPULATION IN PANTABANGAN, NUEVA ECIJA.
The composition of the Inter-Agency Coordinating Committee for the Rehabilitation of Displaced Population in Pantabangan, Nueva Ecija, created under Memorandum Order No. 544 dated November 3, 1975, is hereby amended as follows:
Department of Agrarian Reform
Chairman
National Housing Authority
Member
Department of Local Government and Community Development
Member
Department of Natural Resources
Member
Department of Agriculture
Member
Human Settlements Program, Development Academy of the Philippines
Member
Department of Industry
Member
Bureau of Forest Development
Member
Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources
Member
The Provincial Government of Nueva Ecija
Member
The Municipal Government of Pantabangan, Nueva Ecija
Member
This Order shall take effect immediately.
