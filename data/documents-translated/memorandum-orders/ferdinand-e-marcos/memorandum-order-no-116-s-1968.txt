FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 107 DATED JULY 12, 1968, REGARDING THE FLAG CEREMONY IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.
Memorandum Order No. 107 dated July 12, 1968, directing the holding of a Flag Ceremony every Monday morning and Friday afternoon in the Office of the President, is hereby amended so as to re-schedule the ceremony on Mondays from 7:45 to 8:00 A.M.
