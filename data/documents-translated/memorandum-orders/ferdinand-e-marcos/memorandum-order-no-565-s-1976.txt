Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 565

AUTHORIZING CERTAIN PERSONNEL OF THE OFFICE OF THE SOCIAL SECRETARY TO DRAW ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION FOR OVERTIME SERVICE ACTUALLY RENDERED FROM MAY 21 TO JUNE 7, 1976.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Presidential Memorandum No. 192-74, dated August 20, 1974, the following personnel of the Office of the Social Secretary;

are hereby authorized to draw additional compensation for overtime service actually rendered from May 21 to June 7, 1976, pursuant to the provisions of Section 7-l (32) of Commonwealth Act No. 246, as amended, in connection with the preparation for the state visit to Rome and Moscow of the president and the First Lady, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, June 10, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
