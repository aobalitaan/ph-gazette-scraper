TANGGAPANG NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 390

AUTHORIZING THE STAFF OF THE ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE SECRETARY FOR DEVELOPMENT AFFAIRS TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH TWO (2) MEALS A DAY ON SATURDAYS, SUNDAYS AND HOLIDAYS.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 373 dated August 24, 1973, authority is hereby granted for the Staff of the Assistant Executive Secretary for Development Affairs to render overtime services with two (2) meals a day on Saturdays, Sundays and holidays, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 15, 1973 Source: Presidential Management Staff
