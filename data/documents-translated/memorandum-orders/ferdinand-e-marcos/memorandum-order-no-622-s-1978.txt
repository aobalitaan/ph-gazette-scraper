TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 622

REQUIRING ALL CONCERNED IN THIS OFFICE TO INDICATE THEIR COMPLETE NAMES IN PRINT, POSITIONS, AND UNITS, AND TO SIGN THEIR NAMES IN RECEIVING OFFICIAL CORRESPONDENCE.

In order to easily trace correspondence being received in this Office by mail, by telegram or radiogram, and by personal delivery, and. in the interest of expeditious disposition thereof, all concerned are hereby required to indicate their complete names in print, positions and units and sign their names accordingly whenever receiving such correspondence.

This Order takes effect immediately.

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, July 17, 1978
