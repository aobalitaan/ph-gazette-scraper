MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 303, October 10, 1972 ]
ABOLISHING THE PRESIDENTIAL PUBLIC ASSISTANCE OFFICE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT
The Presidential Public Assistance Office in this Office created under Memorandum Order No. 212, as amended by Memorandum Order No. 248, both series of 1971, is hereby abolished; and all its functions, personnel, equipment and supplies are hereby transferred to the Special Action Unit of this Office, effective immediately.
