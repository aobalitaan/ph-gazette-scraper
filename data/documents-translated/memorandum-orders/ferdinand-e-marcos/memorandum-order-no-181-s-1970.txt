Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 181

AUTHORIZING NOT MORE THAN EIGHT EMPLOYEES OF THE SECRETARIAT, ACTION UNIT, TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE.

Pursuant to Memorandum Order No. 169, current series, the Assistant Executive Secretary for Administrative Affairs is hereby authorized to assign as part of the skeletal force of the Secretariat of the Special Action Unit, this Office, not more than eight (8) employees a day to render overtime service with meal allowance after the regular office hours, including Saturdays and holidays, effective as of March 16, 1970, subject to the provisions of Memorandum Order No. 54, series of 1967, the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, April 13, 1970
