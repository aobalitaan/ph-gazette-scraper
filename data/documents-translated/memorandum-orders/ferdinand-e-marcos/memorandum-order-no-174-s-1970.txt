Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 174

CREATING A SPECIAL COMMITTEE TO STUDY THE PROBLEMS PRESENTED BY STUDENT COUNCILS FROM VARIOUS SCHOOL AND UNIVERSITIES.

A Committee is hereby created to study the problems presented by student councils from various schools and universities collapsed of the following:

Secretary Carlos P. Romulo

Chairman

Secretary Gregorio Feliciano

Member

Secretary O. D. Corpus

Member

Secretary Alejandro Melchor

Member

Secretary Manuel Syquio

Member

The Committee may call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or instrumentality of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in the performance of its functions.

The Committee shall submit its report and recommendations to the President as soon as possible.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, March 25, 1970
