TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 520

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO PASS UPON THE CLAIMS FOR TERMINAL LEAVE AND RETIREMENT GRATUITIES OF FORMER EMPLOYEES OF THE DEFUNCT EMERGENCY EMPLOYMENT ADMINISTRATION

A committee to handle the processing and payment of the claims for terminal leaves and retirement gratuities of 6,560 former employees of the defunct Emergency Employment Administration is hereby created to be composed of the following members;

The Committee shall adopt the necessary procedure and guidelines to insure the processing and payment of the aforementioned claims in a most expeditious manner. The operational expenses of the Committee shall be charged against the funds of the EEA.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, June 19, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
