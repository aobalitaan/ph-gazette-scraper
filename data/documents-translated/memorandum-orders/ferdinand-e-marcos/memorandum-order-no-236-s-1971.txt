Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 236

EXCUSING OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE AT 2:45 P.M. ON JUNE 2, 1971.

The closing ceremonies of the Seminar on Records Management will be held on June 2, 1971, starting at 3:00 p.m., at the Maharlika Hall, Executive Building.

Officials and employees in this Office are hereby requested to attend the said ceremonies. For this purpose, they are hereby excused from office beginning at 2:45 p.m. on the said date, provided that Chiefs of Divisions and Primary Units shall assign skeleton force in their respective Units to attend to official transactions.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, June 2, 1971

Source: Malacañang Records Office
