Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 185

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 128 DATED JUNE 27, 1969, ENTITLED “CREATING THE ACTION COMMITTEE ON CITIZENS COMPLAINTS.”

Memorandum Order No. 128 dated June 27, 1969, as amended by Memorandum Orders Nos. 173 and 180 dated February 28 and April 10, 1970, respectively, entitled “CREATING THE ACTION COMMITTEE ON CITIZENS COMPLAINTS,” is hereby further amended so as to include Mr. JOSE C. CRUZ of the Complaints and Investigation Office (formerly PARGO) in the membership of the said Committee.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, June 17, 1970

Source: Malacañang Records Office
