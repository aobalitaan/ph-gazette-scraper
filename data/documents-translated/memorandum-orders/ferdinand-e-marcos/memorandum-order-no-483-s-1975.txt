TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 483

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 475, DATED DECEMBER 19, 1974, “CONSTITUTING THE PHILIPPINE PANEL OF DELEGATES TO THE 8TH SESSION OF THE FAO INTERGOVERNMENTAL GROUP ON HARD FIBERS, JANUARY 20-25 1975 IN MANILA.”

Memorandum Order No. 475, dated December 19, 1974, constituting the Philippine Panel of Delegates to the 8th Session of the FAO Intergovernmental Group on Hard Fibers, January 20-25, 1975, in Manila”, is hereby amended to name Governor Caesar Lanuza of the Board of Investments as Chairman m lieu of Undersecretary J.D. This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, January 17, 1975 Source: Presidential Management Staff
