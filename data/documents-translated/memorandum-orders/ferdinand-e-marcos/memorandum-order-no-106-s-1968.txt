[ Memorandum Order No. 106, July 5, 1968 ]
RENAMING THE INFRASTRUCTURE OPERATIONS ROOM (IOR) MENTIONED UNDER ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 113, SERIES OF 1968.
The Infrastructure Operations Room (IOR), which denoted the Operations Staff created under Administrative Order No. 113, series of 1968, to service the Executive Committee for the implementation of infrastructure programs created under Executive Order No. 114-A, series of 1968, is hereby renamed as the Infrastructure Operations Center (IOC).
