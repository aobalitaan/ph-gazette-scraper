MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 560

AUTHORIZING CERTAIN PERSONNEL OF THE SOCIAL SECRETARY’S OFFICE TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION, FROM FEBRUARY 7 TO FEBRUARY 15, 1976

In the interest of the service and as an exception to the President’s Memorandum No. 192-74, dated August 20, 1974, the personnel of the Social Secretary’s Office who were assigned by the Social Secretary to render services beyond the regular office hours from February 7 to 15, 1976, inclusive, to perform specific duties in connection with the preparations for the state visit to the Philippines of the United Nations Secretary General and Madamma Kurt Waldheim, are hereby authorized to draw additional compensation for overtime services rendered during the said period, pursuant to the provisions of Section 7-I(32) of Commonwealth Act No. 246 as amended, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, February 17, 1976
