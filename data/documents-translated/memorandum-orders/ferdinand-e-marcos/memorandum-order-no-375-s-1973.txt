TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 375

AUTHORIZING THREE (3) STAFF MEMBERS OF THE LEGAL OFFICE TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH TWO (2) MEALS A DAY ON SATURDAYS, SUNDAYS AND HOLIDAYS.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum Order No. 373 dated August 24, 1973, authority is hereby granted for three (3) staff members of the Legal Office to render overtime service with two (2) meals a day on Saturdays, Sundays and holidays, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, August 31, 1973
