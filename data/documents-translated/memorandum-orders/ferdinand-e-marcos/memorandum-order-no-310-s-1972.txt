Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 310

CREATING A TASK FORCE TO LIQUIDATE THE OPERATIONS OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL COOPERATIVE BANK.

There is hereby created a Task Force to liquidate the operations of the Philippine National Cooperative Bank and to transfer all the assets of the said Bank to the Philippine National Bank.

The Task Force shall be composed of the following:

Mr. Ramon B. Cardenas, Development Management Staff

Chairman

Mr. Victor Macalincag, Department of Finance

Vice-Chairman

Mrs. Josefina V. Segui, Presidential Economic Staff

Member

Mr. Fernando Baluyot, Presidential Economic Staff

Member

Mr. Cayetano S. Sarmago, Development Management Staff

Member

Atty. Alexander Brillantes, Philippine Co-operative Credit Union League

Member

The Task Force shall arrange the immediate transfer of all assets of the Philippine National Cooperative Bank to the Philippine National Bank. Furthermore, arrangements with respect to funds and operations of the Philippine National Bank in support of the Cooperatives Development Program shall likewise be made.

You are hereby directed to submit your report two weeks from the date hereof.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, November 6, 1972
