Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 743

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 395 DATED OCTOBER 19, 1973 AS AMENDED, ENTITLED: “CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY WAYS AND MEANS TO ENCOURAGE PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLE DRIVERS, OWNERS AND OPERATORS TO ORGANIZE THEMSELVES INTO COOPERATIVES.”

Memorandum Order No. 395 dated October 19, 1973, as amended by Memorandum Order No. 438 of July 10, 1974, Memorandum Order No. 460 of September 28, 1974, Memorandum Order No. 491 of February 19, 1975, Memorandum Order No. 592 of May 30, 1977 and Memorandum Order No. 615 of April 27, 1978, is hereby further amended so as to include Mr. Jose P. Lavares, Acting President of Metro Manila Transit Corporation, as ex-officio Member of the Committee created thereunder.

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) Juan C. Tuvera Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila , December 21, 1981

Source: Presidential Management Staff
