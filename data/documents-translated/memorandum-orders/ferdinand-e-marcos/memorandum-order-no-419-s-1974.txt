TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 419

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE RECEPTION OF THE PHILIPPINE BASKETBALL DELEGATION TO THE PEOPLE’S REPUBLIC OF CHINA.

The Philippine Basketball delegation to the People’s Republic of China headed by Ambassador Eduardo Romualdez is arriving this coming Sunday at 2:30 p.m. via Philippine Air Lines. The reservoir of goodwill and international understanding that the said delegation may have generated between the People’s Republic of China and the Republic of the Philippines is of inestimable value and significance. It is, therefore, fitting and proper that the delegation be welcomed and received by our countrymen with appropriate ceremonies. Accordingly, there is hereby created a Committee for the purpose composed of the following:

The Committee shall coordinate with the DPI, including KBS, to insure full press coverage for the occasion.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By Authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, March 29, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
