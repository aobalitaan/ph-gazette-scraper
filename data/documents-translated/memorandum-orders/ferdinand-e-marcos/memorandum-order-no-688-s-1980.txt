TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS

(OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 688

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 395 DATED OCTOBER 19, 1973, CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY WAYS AND MEANS TO ENCOURAGE AND ASSIST PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLE DRIVERS, OWNERS AND OPERATORS TO ORGANIZE THEMSELVES INTO COOPERATIVES.

Memorandum Order No. 395 dated October 19, 1973, as amended by Memorandum Order No. 4-38 dated July 10, 1974-, creating a committee to study ways and means to encourage and assist public utility vehicle drivers, owners and operators to organize themselves into cooperatives, is hereby further amended to designate the following as ex-officio Members of the committee:

1. The Chairman Board of Transportation

2. The Director Bureau of Land Transportation

3. The Group Commander Constabulary Highway Patrol Group

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, February 29, 1980

Source : Presidential Management Staff
