Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 279

EXCUSING FROM OFFICE CERTAIN EMPLOYEES IN CONNECTION WITH THEIR PARTICIPATION IN THE MALACAÑANG SUMMER ATHLETIC LEAGUE.

In connection with the holding this year of the Malacañang Summer Athletic League, all officials and employees of office’s under the Office of the President which are members of the league are hereby excused from office during their participation in any sports event, .as well as during their scheduled tryouts for selection as members of their respective athletic teams and in the practice sessions thereof, provided that the said tryouts and practice sessions shall be held only in the afternoons.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 22, 1972
