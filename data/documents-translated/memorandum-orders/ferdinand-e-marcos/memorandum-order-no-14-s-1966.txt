Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 14

ENJOINING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES TO PARTICIPATE IN OR PROVIDE SUPPORT TO THE SURVEY OF THE CATTLE INDUSTRY IN THE PHILIPPINES, AND CREATING A COMMITTEE TO COORDINATE THE ACTIVITIES OF SAID AGENCIES

All agencies are hereby enjoined to participate in or provide support to the survey of the cattle industry which will be undertaken by the Philippine Government with assistance coming from the United Nations Technical Assistance Board.

In order to coordinate the activities of the various agencies of the government, an later-Agency Committee on the Survey of the Cattle Industry is hereby created with the following membership:

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, January 14, 1966
