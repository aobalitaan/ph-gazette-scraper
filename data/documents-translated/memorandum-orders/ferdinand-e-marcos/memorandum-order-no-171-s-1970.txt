Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 171

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO IMPLEMENT EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 208, CURRENT SERIES, WHICH MADE THE PRESIDENTIAL AGENCY ON REFORMS AND GOVERNMENT OPERATIONS A DIVISION UNDER THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE.

To implement Executive Order No. 208, current series, which made the presidential Agency on Reforms and Government Operations (PARGO) a division under the Executive Office to be known as Complaints and investigation Office (CIO), there is hereby created a committee composed of —

Asst. Exec. Sec. Ponciano G.A. Mathay

Chairman

Capt. Quirico P. Evangelista (PN ret.)

Member

Atty. Mariano Serrano

Member

Atty. Fernando C. Santico

Member

Atty. Jose Olaguera

Member

The committee shall submit, as soon as practicable, its findings, report and recommendation to effectuate the complete integration of the defunct PARGO into this Office as a primary unit (CIO) thereof.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, February 24, 1970
