Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 288

AMENDING ORDER NO. 247, SERIES OF 1971, ENTITLED “CONSTITUTING PERFORMANCE TEAMS TO CONDUCT PERFORMANCE AUDITS ON: THE NATIONAL POWER CORPORATION, THE NATIONAL ELECTRIFICATION ADMINISTRATION, THE DEVELOPMENT BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES, THE OFFICE OF ECONOMIC COORDINATION, AND THE DEPARTMENT OF LABOR”.

Memorandum Order No. 247 dated July 28, 1971, is hereby amended so as to include the following: Mrs. Preciosa David, representative of the Budget Commission, Mr. Romeo Solis, representative of the Reorganization Commission and Messrs. Renaud Muyeo and Nestor Seballos, representatives of the Management Service, as members of the Performance Evaluation Team constituted to conduct performance audit on the Development Bank of the Philippines.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, May 12, 1972
