Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 291

CREATING A RECORDS MANAGEMENT IMPROVEMENT COMMITTEE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Memorandum Order No. 62, series of 1963, “Creating a Committee on Records Retention and Disposal for the Office of the President,” is hereby amended so as to change its name to Records Management Improvement Committee and to include among its functions those enumerated in DGS Department Order No. 4, series of 1968.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, July 5, 1972
