OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 334

DESIGNATING THE CHAIRMEN AND MEMBERS OF THE AMNESTY COMMISSIONS

Pursuant to Presidential Decree No. 95 and Administrative Order No. 349, of the Office of the President of the Philippines, both dated January 11, 1973, and as recommended by the Secretary of Justice and the Secretary of National Defense, the following-named District Judges of the Courts of First Instance and officers of the Armed Forces of the Philippines are hereby designated to compose the Amnesty Commissions as hereunder indicated:

First Commission for the provinces of Cotabato and South Cotabato and the cities of Cotabato and General Santos:

District Judge Mama Busran – Chairman Colonel Cirilo A, Bueno, Jr., PC – Member Major Jose Y. Sison, PA – Member

Second Commission for the province of Lanao del Norte and the city of Iligan:

District Judge Moises Dalisay – Chairman Lieutenant Colonel Jose S. Castillo, PC – Member Major Artemio Q. Gumtang, JAGS (PA) – Member

Third Commission for the province of Lanao del Sur and the city of Marawi:

District Judge Emeterio Cui – Chairman Lieutenant Colonel Rogaciano G. Espiritu, PC – Member Lieutenant Colonel Rafael P. Mascariñas, PC – Member

Fourth Commission for the province of Zamboanga del Sur and the cities of Zamboanga and Pagadian:

District Judge Abdulhamid Bidin – Chairman Lieutenant Colonel Roberto L. Abad, PC – Member Captain Felimon L. Corpuz, JAGS (PC) – Member

Fifth Commission for the province of Zamboanga del Norte and the cities of Dipolog and Dapitan:

District Judge Rafael T. Mendoza – Chairman Lieutenant Colonel Adolfo M. Bringas, PC – Member Lieutenant Colonel Limneo L. PIaton, JAGS (PAF) – Member

Sixth Commission for the province of Palawan:

District Judge Jose F. Mendoza – Chairman Lieutenant Colonel Jose R. Domingo, PC – Member Major Vicente Yap, JAGS (PAF) – Member

Seventh Commission for the province of Sulu:

District Judge Felix T. Barbers – Chairman Colonel Jimmy MB Bangcola, PC (GSC) – Member Major Julian A. Alzaga, PC -Member

Eighth Commission for the city of Basilan:

District Judge Regino Hermosisima, Jr. -Chairman Captain July V. Cordoviz, PC – Member Major Francisco V. Samala, Jr., JAGS (PC) – Member

Ninth Commission for those cases in the above-memtioned provinces and cities referred to the military authorities on or before February 28, 1973 for trial or with a view to trial by the military tribunals:

Brigadier General Tagumpay A. Nañadiego, AFP – Chairman Colonel Hamilton B. Dimaya, JAGS (PC) GSC – Member Assistant Chief State Prosecutor Fernando de Leon – Member

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, January 12, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
