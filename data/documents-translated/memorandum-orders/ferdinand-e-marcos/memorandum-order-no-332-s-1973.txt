Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 332

ATTENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 318 DATED NOVEMBER 22, 1972 CREATING AN INTERAGENCY TEAM TO CONDUCT AN OVERALL MANAGEMENT/FINANCIAL AUDIT OF THE MANILA ELECTRIC COMPANY.

Memorandum Order No. 318 dated November 22, 1972, creating an Inter-Agency Team to conduct an overall management/financial audit of the Manila Electric Company is  hereby amended so as to include the following named individuals as members of the said team:

Mr. Pablo Bumanlag Chief Supervising Auditor General Auditing Office

Mr. Erasto Viray Assistant Internal Auditor National Power Corporation

Mr. Edmundo J. Bulabao OIC, Presidential Economic Staff

Mr. Ignacio B. Santos Investigator National Power Corporation

Miss Carmelita A. Reyes Secretary I, National Power Corporation

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.)  ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, January 3 ,1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
