MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 310-A

AMENDING THE COMPOSITION AND TERMS OF REFERENCE OF THE TASK FORCE TO LIQUIDATE THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL COOPERATIVE BANK

Memorandum Order No. 310, dated 6 November 1972 creating a Task Force to liquidate the operations of the Philippine National Cooperative Bank and to effect the smooth transfer of the net assets of the PNCB to the PNB is hereby amended to include the following:

Mrs. Carlota Valenzuela, Superintendent of Banks t Central Bank

–

Chairman

Mr. Danilo Ursua, Assistant to the Chairman, Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation

–

Vice-Chairman

Mr. Victor Macalingcag – Department of Finance

–

Member

Mrs. Josefina V. Segui – Presidential Economic Staff

–

Member

Mr. Fernando Baluyot, Presidential Economic Staff

–

Member

Atty. Alexander Brillantes – Philippine Cooperatives Credit Union League

–

Member

The terms of reference for the Task Force is likewise amended based on the initial report of the original group. Liquidation of the Bank shall now be prosecuted under Sec. 29 of R.A. 265.

You are hereby directed to submit your report two weeks from the date hereof.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, November 14, 1972
