Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 3

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 12 DATED MAY 13, 1958 CREATING A COMMITTEE TO PROCESS APPOINTMENT PROPOSALS.

Memorandum Order No. 12 dated May 13, 1958 creating a committee to process appointment proposals under the provisions of Administrative Order No. 171,  series of 1956 is hereby further amended so as to constitute the following as chairman and members of the said committee:

Mr. Isaias del Rosario – – – Chairman

Atty. Fernando C. Santico – Member

Atty. Jeremias R. Flores – – Member

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, December 31, 1965
