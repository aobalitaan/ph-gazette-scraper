MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 176, April 6, 1970 ]
PLACING THE PROTOCOL OFFICE IN THE PRIVATE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.
Effective immediately, the Protocol Office, which heretofore has been under the Executive Secretary, is hereby placed in the Private Office of the President.
