Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 13

CREATING AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE TO PLAN, COORDINATE AND IMPLEMENT ACTIVITIES IN PREPARATION FOR THE THIRD FAR EASE SYMPOSIUM ON NUTRITION.

The Philippine Government has accepted an invitation expended by the American Embassy on behalf of the Interdepartmental Committee for National Defense (ICNND), a U.S. Government Agency, to act as host and to co-sponsor the Third Far East Symposium on Nutrition in Manila on January 23-30, 1967.

The Executive Committee is hereby authorized to appoint such working committees as may be necessary to insure effective arrangements for the symposium.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, January 10, 1966
