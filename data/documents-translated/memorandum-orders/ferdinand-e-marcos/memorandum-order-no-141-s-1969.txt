Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 141

REQUESTING SUBMISSION OF WEEKLY REPORTS TO THE EXECUTIVE SECRETARY.

In addition to the weekly performance report required under Memorandum Order No. 136, current series, all Chiefs of Divisions in this Office are hereby requested to collate communications acted on by their respective divisions and to submit weekly reports thereon, similar to the attached copy of the Memorandum dated August 29, 1969, of Major Carmelo L. Agbayani, Acting Presidential Assistant on Military Affairs.

For the Executive Secretary: ANTONIO C. CARAG Special Assistant to the Executive Secretary

Manila, September 10, 1969
