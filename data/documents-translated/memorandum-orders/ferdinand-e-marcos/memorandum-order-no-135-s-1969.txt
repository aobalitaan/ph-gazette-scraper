CREATING A COMMITTEE TO LOOK INTO THE PROBLEMS OF THE LEGAL OFFICE.
There is hereby created a Committee composed of the following:
Assistant Executive Secretary Ponciano G.A. Mathay
Chairman
Solicitor Antonio M. Martinez
Member
Atty. Antonio C. Carag
Member
to look into the problems of the Legal Office and recommend possible solutions thereto.
The Committee shall endeavor to finish its work and submit a report thereon as soon as possible.
