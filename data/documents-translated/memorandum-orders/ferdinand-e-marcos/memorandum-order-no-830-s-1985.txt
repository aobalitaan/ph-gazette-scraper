Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 830

AUTHORIZING THE CONDUCT OF A LECTURE IN THIS OFFICE ON THE DIFFERENT BENEFITS PROVIDED FOR BY THE GOVERNMENT SERVICE INSURANCE SYSTEM

In order to assist and acquaint our employees with the rules and regulations embodied in the GSIS employee’s benefits such as sickness, disability, survivorship and retirement benefits under PD 1146, and such other plans as the Optional Life Insurance, College Education Assurance, Hospitalization Insurance and Life Insurance Plans, the Training Staff of this Office, in coordination with the GSIS, is hereby authorized to conduct a three (3) day lecture on all benefits provided for under PD 1146 and other plans of the System.

The lecture will be conducted on October 16-18, 1985, on separate sessions, at 9:30-11:30 a.m. and 1:30-3:30 p.m. at the Briefing Room of the Administration Building to accommodate our officials and employees.

All heads of primary units and offices are hereby enjoined to schedule the participation of their employees in this lecture.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, October 15, 1985
