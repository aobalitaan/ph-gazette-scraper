Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 10

DESIGNATING THE CHAIRMAN AND MEMBERS OF THE PRESIDENTIAL RICE AND CORN COMMITTEE

Pursuant to the provisions of Executive Order No. 6 dated January 25, 1962, the following are hereby designated to compose the Presidential Rice and Corn Committee created therein:

The Committee shall organize itself to function immediately and shall render periodic reports on the progress of its operations to the President and the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, January 6, 1966
