[ Memorandum Order No. 761, October 29, 1982 ]
In the interest of the service, the temporary detail to the Batasang Pambansa of Mr. GERONIMO DUMPIT of the Philippine Virginia Tobacco Administration effective October 1, 1982, is hereby authorized with entitlement to honoraria in the amount equivalent to fifty percent (50%) of his basic salary, chargeable against the Batasang Pambansa funds and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.
