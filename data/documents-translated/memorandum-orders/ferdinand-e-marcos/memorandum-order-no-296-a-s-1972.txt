Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 296-A

CREATING STEERING COMMITTEE TO ASSIST IN THE DEVELOPMENT AND EXTENSION PROJECTS OF PHILIPPINE RAILWAY COMPANY

In order to implement the recommendations of the Committee created under Memorandum Order No. 296, S-1972, the members herein are hereby reconstituted to compose a Steering Committee on the development and extension projects of the Philippine Railway Company (PRC).

The Committee shall, in addition to its previous functions, perform the following:

1. To prepare a coordinated and integrated planning for the improvement of existing facilities and the proposed extension project;

2. To assist in the preparation of a project proposal for assistance by financial institutions; and

3. To provide continuous assistance and advice to PRC management as will insure the economical and efficient implementation of the plans.

The Committee is hereby authorized to call on any department, bureau, agency of office, including government-owned or controlled corporations as well as local government instrumentalities for such assistance as it may need in the discharge of its duties and functions.

The Committee shall meet at the call of the Chairman and shall submit final recommendations to implement the plans and subsequently, during the project execution, to render progress reports as necessary.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of September, 1972.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
