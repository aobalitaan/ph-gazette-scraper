MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 212, January 29, 1971 ]
CREATING: A PUBLIC ASSISTANCE UNIT IN THIS OFFICE.
There is hereby created a Public Assistance Unit in this Office to attend to the increasing number of callers in this Office who seek assistance for employment here and abroad, for free transportation passes, relief assistance, and other matters.
This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.
