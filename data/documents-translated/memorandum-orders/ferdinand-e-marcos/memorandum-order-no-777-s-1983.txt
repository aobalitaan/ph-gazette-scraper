MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 777

DESIGNATING DEPUTY PRESIDENTIAL REGIONAL MONITORING OFFICERS/DEPUTY HEADS OF LTP-PMS MONITORING UNITS

In line with Executive Order No. 819, the following are hereby designated as Deputy Presidential Regional Monitoring Officers/Deputy Heads of Lupong Tagapagpaqanap ng Pook (LTP) – Presidential Management Staff (PMS) Monitoring Units:

Their duties, functions and system of operations shall be governed by the pertinent guidelines contained in the Presidential Regional Monitoring Office/LTP-PMS Monitoring Units Operations Manual prepared by the Presidential Management Staff, Office of the President.

This order shall take effect immediately.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

March 3, 1983
