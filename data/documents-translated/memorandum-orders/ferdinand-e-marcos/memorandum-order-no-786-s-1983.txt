MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 786, April 22, 1983 ]
In the interest of the service, the temporary detail of Cpl. Eliseo Q. Soberano to the Batasang Pambansa as Security to the Honorable Speaker Querube C. Makalintal effective 1 January 1983 is hereby authorized.
He shall be entitled to an honorarium in an amount not exceeding fifty percent (50%) of his basic salary chargeable against the funds of Batasan, subject to the usual accounting and auditing requirements.
