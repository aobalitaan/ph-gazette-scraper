Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 269

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 267 OF THIS OFFICE, SERIES OF 1971.

Memorandum Order No. 267, series of 1971, designating Supervising Security Officers in the Executive Office is hereby amended so as to confirm the designation of the following Security Officers for each division/primary unit in the Executive Office as indicated hereunder:

The above-named Security Officers shall be under the direct supervision of their respective Supervising Security Officers in case of absence, transfer and resignation or separation from the service of the Security Officer, the chief of division/primary unit concerned shall immediately designate a substitute or replacement, advising accordingly the Malacañang Provost Officer, the Administrative Office and the Office of the Executive Secretary

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, February 2, 1972
