MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 205

DESIGNATING THE PROJECT DIRECTOR AND DEPUTY PROJECT DIRECTOR FOR THE NLRC-NFAC PILOT INTEGRATED PROGRAM FOR NUEVA ECIJA.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate UPCA Director Basilio N. delos Reyes and ACA Regional Director Arsenio V. Exconde as Project Director and Deputy Project Director, respectively, of the Integrated Development Program for Nueva Ecija, under the joint auspices of the National Land Reform Council and the National Food and Agricultural Council.

This supersedes Memorandum Order No. 191 dated August 1, 1970.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
