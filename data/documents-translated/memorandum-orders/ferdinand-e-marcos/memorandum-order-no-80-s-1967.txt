MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 80

A Committee is hereby created to study the various committees or bodies created by executive orders, administrative orders, circulars etc., to determine (a) those whose functions or activities may be overlapping or conflicting with a view to reconciling, consolidating and updating the same, (b) those which should be allowed to continue as necessary, and (c0 those which should be abolished for having outlived their usefulness.

The Committee shall be composed of the following:

Atty. Mariano Serrano

Atty. Alfredo Pio de Roda

Atty. Jose M. Valdellon

A representative from the U.P. Public Institute of Administration to be designated by the Dean

Atty. Federico A. Sugue

Mr. Pedro Landicho

The Committee shall submit its report and recommendation not later than November 30, 1967.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, October 26, 1967
