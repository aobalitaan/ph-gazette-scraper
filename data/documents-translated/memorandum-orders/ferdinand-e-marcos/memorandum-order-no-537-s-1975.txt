4Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 537

AUTHORIZING CERTAIN PERSONNEL OF THE SOCIAL SECRETARY’S OFFICE TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION, FROM SEPTEMBER 13 TO 30, 1975

In the interest of the service and as an exception to the President’s Memorandum No. 192-74,dated August 20, 1974, the personnel of the Social Secretary’s Office who were assigned by the Social Secretary to render services beyond the regular office hours from September 13 to 30, 1975, inclusive, to perform specific duties in connection with the official trip of the First Lady to New Guinea and Mew York and the visit to the Philippines of Former Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, the Rt. Hon. Edward Heath, are hereby authorized to draw additional compensation for overtime services rendered during the said period, pursuant to the provisions of, Section 7-1(32) of Commonwealth Act No. 246, as amended, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, October 2, 1975
