TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 383

AUTHORIZING THE PERSONNEL OF THE MALACAÑANG HOUSEHOLD TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH TWO (2) MEALS A DAY ON SATURDAYS, SUNDAYS AND HOLIDAYS.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 373 dated August 24, 1973, authority is hereby granted for Malacañang Household personnel to render overtime services with two (2) meals a day on Saturdays, Sundays and holidays, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, September 25, 1973
