TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDEITT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 681

The temporary detail and designation of the government personnel listed hereunder, effective upon entrance to duty, to augment the work force of the Batasang Pambansa, is hereby authorized:

The personnel abovementioned are hereby assigned to the Batasang Pambansa staff of the Honorable Gerardo P. Sicat, Minister of Economic Planning, and they shall be entitled to such honoraria or allowances as may be fixed by him chargeable against the amount allotted for his staff as an Assemblyman, subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Senior Presidential Assistant

December 24, 1979

Source: Presidential Management Staff
