TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 676

AMENDING MEMORANDUM NO. 669 DATED NOVEMBER 5, 1979, AUTHORIZING VOLUNTEER WORKERS IN THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL VOLUNTEER SERVICE COORDINATING OFFICE (PNVSCO) TO DRAW ALLOWANCES.

In accordance with the letter o Ministry of Budget to the PNVSCO dated November 9, 1979, Memorandum Order No. 669 doted November 5, 1979, authorizing volunteer workers in the Philippine National Volunteer Service Coordinating Office to draw allowances, is hereby amended so as to grant the thirty four (34) Domestic Volunteer Workers allowances at the rate of FOUR HUNDRED PESOS (₱400.00) each per month and Ms. ELORFINA PE BENITO SAUCO, Project Volunteer Officer, at the rate of ONE THOUSAND PESOS (1,000) per month, effective July 1, 1979, subject to the name terms and conditions specified in the said Order.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, December 5, 1979

Source: Presidential Management Staff
