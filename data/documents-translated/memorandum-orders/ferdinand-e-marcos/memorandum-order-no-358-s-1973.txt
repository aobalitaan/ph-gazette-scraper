MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 358, May 22, 1973 ]
PLACING THE COMPLAINTS AND INVESTIGATION OFFICE UNDER THE DIRECT AND IMMEDIATE SUPERVISION OF THE ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE SECRETARY ROBERTO REYES.
In the interest of the service, the Complaints and Investigation Office is hereby placed under the direct and immediate supervision of the Assistant Executive Secretary Roberto Reyes.
This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.
