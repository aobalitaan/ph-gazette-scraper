Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 69

CREATING AN ACTION GROUP TO INSURE EFFECTIVE EXECUTION AND IMPLEMENTATION OF INSTRUCTIONS, ORDERS AND POLICIES ON THE PEACE AND ORDER CAMPAIGN INITIATED BY MALACAÑANG AND THE PLANS FORMULATED BY THE PEACE AND ORDER COUNCIL.

There is hereby created an ACTION GROUP to be composed of the following:

to insure effective execution and implementation of instructions, orders and policies on the peace and order campaign initiated by Malacañang and the plans formulated by the Peace and Order Council.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, July 25, 1967

Source: Malacañang Records Office
