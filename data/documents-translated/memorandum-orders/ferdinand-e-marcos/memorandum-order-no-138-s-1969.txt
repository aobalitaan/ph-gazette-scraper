Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 138

REQUIRING CERTIFICATION OF LOCAL TRIPS.

Effective immediately, all drivers, members of the Malacañang Household, and other parties concerned who undertake trips outside Manila on official mission are hereby required to secure a certificate of travel completed (form attached) from Col. Fabian Ver or the officer-in-charge of the mission in the provinces and cities and attach the same to their Travelling Expense Vouchers. The certificate herein required shall be in addition to trip tickets or travel orders issued by the Office.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, August 26, 1969
