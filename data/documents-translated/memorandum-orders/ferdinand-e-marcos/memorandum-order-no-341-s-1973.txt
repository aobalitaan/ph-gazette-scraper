TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 341

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 314-A DATED DECEMBER 5, 1972, designating the members of the panel on police services created by the Inter-Agency Committee.

Memorandum Order No. 314-A dated December 5, 1972, designating the members of the panel on police services created by the Inter-Agency Committee to study the reorganization of government operations in Metropolitan Manila is hereby amended so as to designate as additional member, of the said panel Brig. Gen. Cicero Campos, Deputy Chief PC for Home Defense.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, January 29, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
