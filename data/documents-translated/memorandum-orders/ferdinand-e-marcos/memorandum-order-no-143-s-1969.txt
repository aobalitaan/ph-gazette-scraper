Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 143

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 102, SERIES OF 1964, AS AMENDED, BY PRESCRIBING A NEW UNIFORM FOR WOMEN EMPLOYEES IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Memorandum Order No. 102 dated September 11, 1964, as amended by Memorandum Order No. 73 dated August 11, 1967, requiring all women employees in the Office, of the President to wear the uniform therein prescribed during attendance in office, is hereby further amended so as to prescribe a new uniform with the following specifications the material shall be German khaki, plain pink, one-piece dress with, two-inch vide collar and mini-sleeves, three pink square plastic buttons with rounded corners forming a vertical line below the collar; seamlines from the armline, forming a semi-arc in the bodice down to the hemline, semi A-line skirt. (See attached sketch).

All lady employees shall wear the uniform not later than October 27, 1969. Newly appointed lady employees shall be in the prescribed uniform not later than fifteen (15) days from the date of assumption of office.

Materials for three (3) dresses have been furnished by this Office free of charge to each of the women employees in the office roster on August 30, 1969. Lady employees appointed thereafter shall procure their own materials.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, October 4, 1969
