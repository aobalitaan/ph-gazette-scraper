Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 184

IMPLEMENTING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 219, CURRENT SERIES, WHICH ABOLISHED THE PRESIDENTIAL ANTI-CRIME COORDINATING OFFICE.

To implement Executive Order No. 219, current series, abolishing the Presidential Anti-Crime Coordinating Office and transferring its funds, equipment, property and records to the Peace and Order Coordinating Council, this Office hereby authorizes payment of the salaries and meal allowances of the personnel of the defunct Presidential Anti-Crime Coordinating Office until June 30, 1970, subject to availability of funds and the usual audit, for the purpose of effecting a smooth winding up of the activities of the agency and the transfer of funds, equipment, property and records, and enabling all personnel concerned to secure clearance of accounts and property responsibility.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, June 8, 1970

Source: Malacañang Records Office
