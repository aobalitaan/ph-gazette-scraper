TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 543

INVITING ALL OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF THE OFFICE TO PAY THEIR LAST RESPECTS TO THE LATE DR. GUILLERMO C. DE VEGA

The remains of the late Dr. GUILLERMO C. DE VEGA will be brought to the Malacañang Compound tomorrow morning, October 31st, and lie in state at the main lobby of the Administration Building for two hours beginning at 8:30 A.M.

All officials and employees are hereby invited to come and pay their last respects to our beloved co-worker. They should be at Gate 3 not later than 8:00 A.M. to be on hand for the arrival of the remains and the half-masting of the flag. It is suggested that lady employees be in their navy blue uniform with the red checkered collar removed or tucked in.

All heads of office/primary unit will please her to it that this Order is brought to the attention of all employees under them.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, October 30, 1975

Source : Presidential Management Staff
