TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 563

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING OF THE SEMINAR ON CONTROLS IN FISCAL OPERATIONS IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

In line with the current trend to streamline and update the government procedure in fiscal operations to keep it attune with present fiscal and economic policies, the holding of a seminar on Controls in Fiscal Operations from March 15 to May 7, 1976, inclusive, in this Office, is hereby authorized.

Mr. Floriño O.Ibañez of the Administrative Office is hereby designated as Coordinator of the seminar with a cash advance to defray all such necessary expenses as are allowable under existing regulations and/or approved by the Presidential Finance Adviser, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, March 26, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
