Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 52

ASSIGNING APPROPRIATE SPACE TO THE STAFF OF ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE SECRETARY GILBERTO M. DUAVIT ASSIGNED TO HANDLE THE ACTIVITIES AND PROPERTIES OF THE DEFUNCT PARGO.

In view of the forthcoming expiration of the lease contract covering the building now occupied by the Office of the Presidential Agency on Reforms and Government Operations, the staff of Assistant Executive Secretary Gilberto Vi. Duavit who have been assigned to handle the activities and take over the records, funds, and properties of the said Agency are hereby assigned an appropriate office space in the Administration Building located .at Otis Street, Pandacan, Manila, turned over by the Bureau of Animal Industry to the Office of the President. The Malacañang Engineer shall assist Secretary Duavit in putting the said building in proper shape for purposes of the aforementioned staff.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, January 17, 1967

Source: Malacañang Records Office
