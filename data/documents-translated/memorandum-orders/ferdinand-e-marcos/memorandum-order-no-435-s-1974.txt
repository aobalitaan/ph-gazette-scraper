TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 435

In view of the acceptance by the Philippine Government and the National Power Corporation of the proposals of Amex International Limited (AMEX) in connection with the financing of two (2) 600 MW Nuclear power plants as contained in its letters dated June 10 and June 14, 1974, a team is hereby created to coordinate and direct all activities involved I the procurement of the necessary financing for the plants.

The team shall be composed of the following:

1.

Mr. Ramon R. Ravanzo General Manager National Power Corporation

–

–

–

Member

8,

Mr. Jose Torres Chief, Nuclear Power Div. National Power Corporation

–

Member

9.

Mr. Guillermo V. Soliven Director, Management of External Debts & Invisible Accounts Department Central Bank of the Philippines

–

Member

The Team is hereby directed to meet immediately and, in collaboration with AMEX and Westinghouse, take such steps as are necessary towards the accomplishment of its assigned task.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, June 27, 1974
