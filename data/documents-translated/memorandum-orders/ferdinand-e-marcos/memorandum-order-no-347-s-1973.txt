Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 347

SETTING A MASS HIKING EXERCISE OF OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

In line, with the President’s ongoing program of human resources development, the Office of the President will hold a hiking exercise en masse of its officials and employees on Monday, February 26, 1973, starting at 6:30 A. M. from the Luneta to this Office. Participants will assemble infront of the Quirino Grandstand.

Considering the need for physical rejuvenation of government employees, more than any group of workers because of the demands of their duties, everyone is enjoined to participate in this initial exercise which will be followed later by other physical fitness activities.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, February 22, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
