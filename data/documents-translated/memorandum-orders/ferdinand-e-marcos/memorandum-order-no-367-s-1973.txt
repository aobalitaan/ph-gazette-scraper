TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 367

ANNOUNCING THE THIRD COMPUTER PROGRAMMING COURSE FOR GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL AND URGING SUBMISSION OF NOMINEES IN THIS OFFICE TO THE SAID COURSE, IF ANY.

The National Computer Center at Camp Aguinaldo, Quezon City, is now accepting applications for its 3rd Computer Programming Course for government personnel. Applicants from this Office who desire to attend the course must possess the following qualifications:

1. Holding or earmarked to occupy the position of computer programmer, operator, systems analyst, or other positions related to the computerization activities of the agency.

2. Holder of a Bachelor of Arts/Science degree.

3. Not more than 35 years of age.

4-. Must pass the NCC qualification test for programmers.

The training starts on August 13, 1973.

All Pleads of Divisions and Primary Units in this Office may submit their nominees, if any, to the Administrative Office for transmission to the NCC.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, July 25, 1973
