Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 324

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO UNDERTAKE A GENERAL STUDY AND, POSSIBLY UPDATING, OF ALL PRESIDENTIAL DECREES AND ORDERS ISSUED BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES.

There is hereby created a Committee to undertake a general study and, possibly updating of all Presidential Decrees and Orders issued by the President of the Philippines with the view to amending or revising those which need to be amended or revised, or promulgating such Decrees and Orders as may be necessary. The Committee shall be composed of the following:

The Committee shall complete its work within a period of forty-five (45) days from the date hereof.

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, December 13, 1972
