Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 413

AUTHORIZING NOT MORE THAN SIX (6) EMPLOYEES TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH ONE (1) MEAL A DAY ON FRIDAYS, SATURDAYS, SUNDAYS AND HOLIDAYS.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum Order #373, dated August 24, 1973, authority is hereby granted for not more than six (6) Records Office personnel as may be assigned by the Presidential Staff Director therein, to render overtime services with one (1) meal a day on Fridays, Saturdays, Sundays and Holidays, effective February 1, 1974, an d until June 30, 1974, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 21, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
