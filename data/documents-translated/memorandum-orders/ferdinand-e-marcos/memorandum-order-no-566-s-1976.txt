Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 566

AUTHORIZING CERTAIN PERSONNEL OF THE MALACAÑANG ENGINEERING OFFICE TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION, FROM JULY 1 TO AUGUST 15, 1976.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to the President’s Memorandum No. 192-74, dated August 20, 1974, seventeen (17) men of the Malacañang Engineering Office who were assigned by the Presidential Staff Director of the Malacañang Engineering Office to render services beyond the regular office hours from July 1 to August 15, 1976 to perform specific duties in connection with the construction of the Visitor’s Shed at Gate No. 4 are hereby authorized to draw additional compensation for overtime services rendered during the said period, pursuant to the provision of Section 7-1 (32) of Commonwealth Act No. 246, as amended, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, July 15, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
