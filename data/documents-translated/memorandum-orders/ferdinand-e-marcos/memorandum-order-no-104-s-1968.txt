Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 104

ADDITIONAL COMPENSATION OF NURSES IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT IN LIEU OF QUARTERS, SUBSISTENCE AND LAUNDRY ALLOWANCES.

Pursuant to the provisions of Section 3 of Republic Act No. 649, the four (4) nurses employed in this Office, assigned to public health nursing, occupying items Nos. 236, 227-1, 227-2 and 229, page 34, Republic Act No. 5170, are hereby authorized to draw additional compensation of FIFTY-EIGHT PESOS (P58.00) each per month, in lieu of quarters, subsistence and laundry allowances, effective as of October 1, 1967, of which P50.00 each per month shall be chargeable against item No. 234, page 34 of the sane Act and P8.00 each per month chargeable against any appropriate fund of this Office, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, June 17, 1968

Source: Malacañang Records Office
