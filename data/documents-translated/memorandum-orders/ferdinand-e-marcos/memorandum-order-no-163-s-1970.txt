Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 163

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO INVESTIGATE THE COMPLAINT OF COUNCILOR FLORO CRUZ OF OLONGAPO CITY AGAINST AN OFFICIAL OF THIS OFFICE.

There is hereby created a Committee composed of be following officials to investigate the complaint of Councilor Floro Cruz of Olongapo City that the sum of ₱30,000.00 is being raised to be given to a Malacañang official allegedly in consideration of the approval of a pending bill exempting the sale of commercial lots in Olongapo City for public bidding:

Assistant Executive Secretary

Ponciano G. A. Mathay

Chairman

Atty. Mariano Serrano

Member

Atty. Antonio Nuesa

Member

The Committee shall submit its report to this Office within one week from receipt hereof.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, January 12, 1970

Source: Malacañang Records Office
