Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 63

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER MO. 28, SERIES OF 1966, AUTHORIZING A NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES IN THE OFFICE OF THE SOCIAL SECRETARY TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE.

In view of the exigencies of the service, Memorandum Order No. 28, series of 1966, authorizing the Social Secretary to assign four (4) employees in her office to render overtime service with meal allowance, is hereby amended so as to increase the said number of employees to nine (9) effective Immediately and until further advice.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, April 28, 1967

Source : Malacañang Records Office
