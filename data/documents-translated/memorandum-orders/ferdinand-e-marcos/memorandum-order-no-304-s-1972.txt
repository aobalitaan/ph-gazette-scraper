Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 304

CREATING AN INTER-AGENCY COMMITTEE TO DRAFT AN OPERATIONS MANUAL ON LAND REFORM.

There is hereby established an inter-agency committee to draft an Operations Manual on Land Reform. The Manual shall be for the use of the Department of Agrarian Reform, the Armed Forces of the Philippines, and other public and private entities that may participate in the land reform program.

The committee shall be composed of the following:

Mr. Jose Medina, Jr Director, Bureau of Farm Management, Department of Agrarian Reform

Chairman

Atty. Carlos Umil Special Assistant, Department of Agrarian Reform

Member

Lt-Col Bernardo A. Ramos Chief Operation Branch OJ3, GHQ, AFP, Department of National Defense

Member

Mr. Eduardo Corpuz Director, Agriculture Programs Office Presidential Economic Staff

Member

Maj Umberto Rodriguez Branch Chief, Project Management National Computer Center

Member

Lt-Col Romeo Solina Director, Development Management Staff Office of the Executive Secretary

Member

The committee is hereby directed to:

1. Specify the sub-programs and enumerate all activities/procedures required in implementation;

2. Sort out activities/procedures into homogeneous groups or phases;

3. Specify the implementing agencies, bureaus, offices and-financial institutions and their respective activities/responsibilities; and to study their interrelationship;

4. Trace the flow of financial and material inputs through each phase of the Land Reform Program;

5. Design and construct a simplified Operations Manual for use by all entities implementing the Program;

6. Accomplish the Operations Manual on or before October 27, 1972.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, October 13, 1972
