Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 251

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 234 DATED MAY 18, 1971, CONSTITUTING THE PRESIDENTIAL COMMISSION CREATED TO INVESTIGATE DISTURBANCES INCIDENT TO RALLIES, RIOTS AND DEMONSTRATION.

Memorandum Order No. 234, dated May 18, 1971, constituting the Presidential Commission created by the President under Administrative Order No. 287, dated May 14, 1971, is hereby amended so as to designate Atty. FELICIANO BELMONTE, President of the Manila Junior Chamber of Commerce, and Mr. ANTONIO POLICARPIO, President National Labor Committee, as members of the Presidential Commission vice Dr. ALFONSO AYESA and Atty. IGNACIO LACSINA, respectively.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

BY AUTHORITY OF THE PRESIDENT:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, August 27, 1971
