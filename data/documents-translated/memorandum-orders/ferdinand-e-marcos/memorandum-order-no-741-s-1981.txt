Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 741

In the interest of the service, the temporary detail to the Batasang Pambansa, of the government personnel listed, hereunder, effective 16 December 1981, is hereby authorized:

He shall perform the duties and discharge the responsibilities of the abovestated position, and such related duties as the Chairman thereof, and the Secretary-General of the Batasan may assign to him.

He shall be entitled to the honoraria in the amount equivalent to fifty percent (50%) of his basic salary, chargeable against the Batasan funds and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and regulations.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, 9 December 1981

Source: Malacañang Records Office
