TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 610

CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE BOARD CIRCULAR NO. 6

All offices under the supervision of, or attached to, the Office of the President are hereby directed to disseminate to their officials and employees the enclosed copies of Career Executive Service Board Circular No. 6 containing guidelines for admission of graduates from the National Defense College of the Philippines (NDCP) and of the Senior Executive Development Program by the Philippine Executive Academy (PEA) to the Career Executive Service Development Program.

(Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, March 31, 1978

Reference: Additional Guidelines for Selection and Admission of Government Personnel into the Career Executive Service Development Program
