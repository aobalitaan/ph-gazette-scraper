Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 399

TO: All Chiefs of Divisions and Heads of Operating Units in the Executive Office, as well as all Agencies and Offices under the Office of the President

This will confirm the verbal instructions of the Executive Secretary transmitted by telephone on September 26, 1973, requiring all Chiefs of Divisions and Heads of Operating Units in the Executive Office, as well as all Heads of Offices and Agencies under the Office of the President, to submit their accomplishment reports covering the period from July 1, 1972 to June 30, 1973. Up to the present time however, only a few offices have submitted their reports.

As sufficient time has elapsed for the preparation of said reports, compliance with the above instructions by 5 November 1973 is hereby enjoined.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, November 26, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
