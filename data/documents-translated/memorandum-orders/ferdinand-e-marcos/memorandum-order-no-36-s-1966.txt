Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 36

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO CONDUCT A MANAGEMENT AUDIT OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

There is hereby created a Committee composed of the following:

to conduct a management audit of the Office of the President with a view to streamlining the organizational set-up and operating procedures therein.

All units in the Executive Office and other entities under the Office of the President are hereby enjoined to extend full cooperation and render such assistance as may be requested by the Committee in connection with the management audit.

The Committee shall endeavor to finish its work and submit a report thereon as soon as possible.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL N. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, July 18, 1966
