Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 445

AUTHORIZING NOT MORE THAN SIX (6) EMPLOYEES TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH ONE (1) MEAL A DAY ON SATURDAYS, SUNDAYS AND HOLIDAYS.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum Order No. 373 dated August 24, 1973, authority is hereby granted for not more than six (6) Records Office personnel as may be assigned by the Presidential Staff Director therein, to render overtime services with one (1) meal a day on Saturdays Sundays and holidays, effective July 1 up to 31, 1974, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting said auditing requirements.

By authority of the President:: Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, July 19, 1974

Source: Malacañang Records Office
