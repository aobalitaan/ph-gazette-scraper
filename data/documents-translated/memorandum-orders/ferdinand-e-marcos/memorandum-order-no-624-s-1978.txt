Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 624

The temporary detail and designation of the government personnel listed, hereunder, effective July 6, 1978, to Constitute the Skeletal Work Force of the Batasang Pambansa which shall provide the requisite technical, staff and clerical services for the Batasan, is hereby authorized, to wit:

OFFICE OF THE SECRETARY

He shall perform the duties and discharge the responsibilities of the position indicated opposite his name based on the Plantilla of Personnel of the Batasan Secretariat.

He shall be entitled to honoraria in the amount equivalent to thirty-five percent (35i) of his basic salary chargeable against the Batasan funds and subject to the usual accounting and auditing procedures.

By Authority of the President:

By the Authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, 14 August 1978
