TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 423

AUTHORIZING EIGHT (8) EMPLOYEES OF THE MALACANANG GARAGE TO RENDER OVERTIME SERVICE WITH MEAL ALLOWANCE.

The following eight (8) employees of the Malacañang Garage, in view of the nature of their duties, are hereby authorized to collect not more than one (1) unit meal allowance per day for overtime service rendered during Saturday, Sundays and holidays, effective April 1, 1974, as an exception to Memorandum Order No. 373, current series, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements,

Two (2) Night Car Dispatchers

From 3:00 p.m. to 11:00 p.m. and

From 11:00 p.m. to 7:00 a.m.

Two (2) Gasoline Station Attendants

From 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

Two (2) Auto Mechanics

From 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

One (1) Car Washer

From 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

One (1) Janitor

From 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, April 18, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
