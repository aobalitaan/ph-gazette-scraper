Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 59

REQUIRING PRICE CLEARANCE BY THE SOCIAL SECRETARY OF VISITORS OF THE FIRST LADY.

It has been observed lately that certain employees of the Office accompany visitors of the First Lady directly to the Office of the Social Secretary without first se­curing clearance from Mrs. Rebecca Knapp at the Office of the Appointments Secretary in the Executive Building, thereby causing undue inconvenience to all concerned.

In order to attain a smooth flow and reception of such visitors in the Palace, it is hereby directed that employees accompanying visitors must secure a pass for them from Mrs. Knapp. Those without such a pass shall not be allowed entry at the concrete gate to the Palace by the guard on duty thereat.

For the information and guidance of all concerned.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 29, 1967
