Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 273

CREATING A COMMITTEE IN THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE TO COMPILE AND DIGEST ALL IMPORTANT DECISIONS AND OPINIONS OF THE LEGAL OFFICE SIGNED AND PROMULGATED FROM THE YEAR 1960 to 1971.

There is hereby created a Committee in the Executive Office which shall undertake the compilation, the preparation of accurate digests, and the numbering of the most important decisions and opinions of the Legal Office from January 1960 to December 1971.

The Committee shall he composed of the following:

All Chiefs and Assistant Chiefs of Divisions are enjoined to cooperate with the Committee by providing them with copies of decisions and opinions duly signed and promulgated by this Office of a legal nature. All lawyers and action assistants of the Legal Office shall compile and digest the cases respectively pertaining to them, in such form as the Committee may prescribe, and submit them, through their group heads, to the Committee.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, February 24, 1972
