Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 729

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF A ONE WEEK SEMINAR IN MEMBERSHIP EDUCATION ON COOPERATIVES.

In line with the Cooperatives Development Program of the Government, the Malacañang Employees Cooperatives (MECOOP) is hereby authorized to hold a one week seminar in Membership Education on Cooperatives to enhance the knowledge in cooperatives of all the members of the MECOOP.

The such seminars of one week duration each will be held. The schedule of the seminars is as follows:

July 27-31, 1981 – First week (8:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon) Aug. 3-7, 1981 – Second week (8:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon)

All heads of primary units are hereby enjoined to submit the names of their participants who are members of MECOOP to MECOOP Training unit at Finance Extension Office, not later than July 15, 1981.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, July 14, 1981

Source: Presidential Management Staff
