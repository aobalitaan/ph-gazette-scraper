TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 547

CREATING AN INTERIM COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF EFFECTING THE PROVISIONS OF LETTER OF IMPLEMENTATION NO. 26 DATED SEPTEMBER 26, 1975, IMPLEMENTING THE ABOLITION OF THE OFFICE OF ECONOMIC COORDINATION.

In order that the provisions of Letter of Implementation No. 26 dated September 26, 1975, may be properly and expeditiously carried out, there is hereby created an Interim Committee to take charge of coordinating the immediate implementation thereof composed of the following:

Hon. Ramon 3. Cardenas Assistant Executive Secretary

–

Chairman

Hon. Nicanor Fuentes Deputy Director-General N E D A

–

Vice-Chairman

Mr. Silverio Sarmiento Budget Commission

–

Member

Miss Angelita Ofilade Reorganization Commission

–

“

Representative of the Commission on Audit

–

“

The Committee is hereby authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office or agency of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporation, for such assistance as it may need in the accomplishment of its task. It shall submit its report within sixty (60) days from the date of this Order.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, November 17, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
