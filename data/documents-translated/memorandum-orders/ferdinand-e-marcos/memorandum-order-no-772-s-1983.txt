Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 772

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 395 DATED OCTOBER 19, 1973, AS AMENDED, ENTITLED “CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY WAYS AND MEANS TO ENCOURAGE PUBLIC UTILITY VEHICLE DRIVERS, OWNERS AND OPERATORS TO ORGANIZE THEMSELVES INTO COOPERATIVES.”

Memorandum Order No. 395 dated October 19, 1973, as amended by Memorandum Order No. 438 of July 10, 1974, Memorandum Order No. 460 of September 28, 1974, Memorandum Order No. 491 of February 19, 1975, Memorandum Order No. 592 of May 30, 1977, Memorandum Order No. 615 of April 27, 1978, and Memorandum Order No. 743 of December 21, 1981, is hereby further amended so as to designate the Director, Bureau of Cooperatives Development, as ex-officio Member of the Committee created thereunder.

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, February 21, 1983
