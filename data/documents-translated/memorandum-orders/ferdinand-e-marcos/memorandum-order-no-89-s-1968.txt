Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 89

REGISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLES OWED BY OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

For the convenience of officials and employees in the Office of the President who are motor vehicle owners, the Commissioner of Land Trans­portation is assigning a representative from his office to register vehicles owned by officials and employees in this Office, as well as issue corresponding 1968 car plates therefor, at the Malacañang Garage, during office hours on January 25-26, 1968.

Officials and employees concerned in this Office who wish to avail themselves of this facility are requested to bring their motor vehicles, together with the necessary registration papers, to the Malacañang Parking Lot in accordance with the following schedule:

The Malacañang Motor Pool Supervisor shall make available a convenient space in the Malacañang Garage for the purpose and assist the LTC representative to facilitate the registration.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, January 24, 1968

Source: Malacañang Records Office
