TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 387

AUTHORIZING TWO (2) PERSONNEL OF THE BUILDING AND GROUNDS DIVISION TO COLLECT MORE THAN ONE (1) UNIT MEAL ALLOWANCE.

In the interest of the service and as an exception to Memorandum Order No. 373, current series, Messrs. SALVADOR ESMEJARDA and JOAQUIN GESTOPA, both of the Building and Grounds Division, are hereby authorized to collect more than one (1) unit meal allowance for overtime services rendered effective August 24, 1973.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 2, 1973
