Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 283

DESIGNATING CERTAIN UNITS IN THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE TO SUPERVISE THE UPKEEP OF THE OFFICES AND FACILITIES ON THE DIFFERENT FLOORS OF THE ADMINISTRATION BUILDING.

In order to insure the proper maintenance of the offices, including comfort rooms, stairways, closets, corridors and other facilities in the Administration Building (Executive Office Wing), the following units are hereby designated to supervise, in collaboration with the other units in the same floor and the Engineer’s Office, the upkeep of their respective areas in each floor, as indicated hereunder:

The chiefs of the supervising units mentioned above shall designate an inspector each who shall check on the cleanliness and proper condition of the areas under their respective supervision. The supervising unit head shall submit a daily progress report to the Executive Secretary, together with his comment and recommendation.

In order to provide an incentive to the different janitors and -mother maintenance workers assigned within the Executive Office Wing of the Administration Building, monthly prizes will be awarded to the janitors and the supervising unit of the floor which has been kept the -cleanest during the particular month. For this purpose, a committee is hereby created to select nominees for the monthly awards composed of the following:

The Committee shall promulgate the rules and regulations to govern the selection of nominees and shall submit to the Executive Secretary within the week after each month the recommendees for awards for the preceding month.

This Order shall take effect on April 1, 1972.

By the authority of the President: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, April 3, 1972
