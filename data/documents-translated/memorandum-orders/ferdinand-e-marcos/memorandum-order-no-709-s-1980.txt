Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM NO. 709

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 701 CREATING AN AEROSPACE TASK FORCE

Inasmuch as the National Cartography, Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing Center (NCPRSC) is engaged in activities associated with operation of space satellites and designating Col. Recaredo Albano, Executive Director of the NCPRSC, to represent the Office of the President in the said task group in place of Col. Victor Natividad.

Further, the Task Group is hereby given an additional three-month period to finalize its report.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Senior Presidential Assistant

8 December 1980

Source : Presidential Management Staff
