TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 389

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 128 DATED JUNE 27, 1969, CREATING THE PRESIDENTIAL ACTION COMMITTEE ON CITIZENS COMPLAINTS.

Memorandum Order No. 128, as amended by Memorandum Orders Nos. 173, 180, 185 and 268 dated February 28, 1970, April 10, 1970, June 17, 1970 and January 21, 1972, creating the Presidential Action Committee on Citizens Complaints (PACCC), is hereby further amended so as to reorganize the said Committee with the NMPC Director as Chairman, and one additional member from each of the following offices:

Department of Justice Executive Secretary’s office National Media Production Center Voice of the Philippines Philippine Broadcasting Service Office of Civil Relation (OCR) Department of National Defense Department of Agrarian Reform Department of Local Government and Community Development Department of Labor Rural Broadcasters Council (RBC)

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 4, 1973
