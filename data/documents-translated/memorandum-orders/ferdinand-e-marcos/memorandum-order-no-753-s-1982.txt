Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 753

In view of the worsening land controversies in Surigao del Sur and for the speedy settlement of such cases, there is hereby created an inter-agency committee to be composed by the following:

Presidential Regional, Officer for Development

–

Chairman

Provincial Governor Ministry of Human Settlements

–

Member

Provincial Action Officer Ministry of Agrarian Reform

–

Member

District Officer Ministry of Agriculture

–

Member

District Office Bureau of Forest Development

–

Member

District Office

–

Member

Municipal Mayors concerned

–

Members

Representatives of parties affected

–

Members

It shall be the responsibility of the committee to conduct in-depth investigations and to recommend solutions to the land problems. It shall also undertake extensive land surveys, census and come up with a program for those settlers who may be displaced.

The findings/recommendations shall then be submitted to the Presidential Management Staff not later than June 30, 1982.

This order shall take effect immediately.

By Authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

May 17, 1982

Source: Presidential Management Staff
