TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 405

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 62, S-1963, AS AMENDED BY MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 291, DATED JULY 5, 1972, ENTITLED, “CREATING A RECORDS MANAGEMENT IMPROVEMENT COMMITTEE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.”

Memorandum Order No. 62, dated October 28, 1963, creating a Committee on Records Retention and Disposal for the Office of the President, which was amended by Memorandum Order No. 291, dated July 5, 1972, renaming the body thus created as “Records Management Improvement Committee,” and including among its functions those enumerated in DGS Department Order No. 4, series of 1968, is hereby further amended to reconstitute the membership in the Committee, as follows:

Melquiades dela Cruz Presidential Staff Director Records Office

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, January 28, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
