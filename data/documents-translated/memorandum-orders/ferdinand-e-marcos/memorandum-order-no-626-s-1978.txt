Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 626

The temporary detail and designation of the government personnel listed, hereunder, effective upon entrance to duty, to Constitute the Skeletal Work Force of the Batasang Pambansa which shall provide the requisite technical, staff and clerical services for the Batasan, is hereby authorized, to wit:

They shall perform the duties and discharge the responsibilities of the positions indicated opposite their names based on the Plantilla of Personnel of the Batasan Secretariat,

They shall be entitled to honoraria in the amounts equivalent to thirty-five percent (35%) of their basic salaries chargeable against the Batasan funds and subject to the usual accounting and auditing procedures.

By the Authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, 14 August 1978
