Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 749

Mr. GREGORIO L. BALANZA, JR., Chief of the Field Coordinating Staff, Office of Media Affairs’ National Capital Regional Office, who is on part-time detail in the Office of Hon. Emmanuel N. Pelaez, Member of the Batasang Pambansa and Minister of State for Foreign Affairs is hereby authorized to receive honoraria in the amount of not exceeding fifty percent (50%) of his basic salary, chargeable against the funds of the Batasang Pambansa, subject to the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TAVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, February 23, 1982
