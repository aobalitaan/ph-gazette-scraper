Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 182

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE RECEPTION OF MISS AURORA PIJUAN, MISS INTERNATIONAL.

WHEREAS, Miss Aurora Pijuan, by being chosen Miss International in Beauty Contest held in Osaka, Japan, among many participants from other countries, has given honor and added prestige to the Philippines;

WHEREAS, in connection with the campaign to promote tourism in this country, there is a need to make use of this event to focus international attention to the Philippines, its people and its scenic and other attractions;

NOW, THEREFORE, there is hereby created a Committee to take charge of the reception of Miss Aurora Pijuan, Miss International, upon her arrival in Manila, to be composed of:

Gilberto M. Duavit

Chairman

Assistant Executive Secretary

Member

Gregorio Araneta Commissioner of Tourism

Member

Salvador Maskardo Customs Collector

Member

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, May 22, 1970
