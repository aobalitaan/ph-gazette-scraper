Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 707

AUTHORIZING THE CONDUCT OF INFORMATION DRIVE ON SECURITY MEASURES AND EMERGENCY PROCEDURES.

In line with the need for an intensified campaign for security and to familiarize our employees with all the security measures and emergency procedures adopted to be undertaken in case of fire, earthquake and other emergencies, it is deemed necessary to conduct an information drive on the subject in all units and offices.

The Administrative Office thru its Training Staff, and in coordination with military/security personnel, is hereby assigned to  conduct the necessary briefings in the various offices and units in the Office of the President proper.

All heads of offices are hereby directed to extend their cooperation and assistance to the Training Staff in this afternoon drive.

(Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, November 27, 1980

Source : Presidential Management Staff
