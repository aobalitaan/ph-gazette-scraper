AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 432. CURRENT SERIES.
Memorandum Order No. 432, dated June 10, 1974, Implementing in the Executive Office the Two Sets of Work Hours Prescribed in General Order No. 48, dated June 8, 1974, is hereby amended so as to exempt therefrom the personnel of the Private Office of the President and the Presidential Library, both under Lt. Col. Irineo Zabala.
