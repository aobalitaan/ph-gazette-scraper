Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 85

REVOKING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 62, CURRENT SERIES

Memorandum order Ho. 62 of this office dated April 25, 1967, “Requiring the Cabinet Secretariat to Maintain Stand-By Personnel to receive messages or information, in connection with the impending state of emergency created by the signs of possible eruptions of Mayon and Taal Volcanoes, transmitted to this office by telephone, telegram or any other means of fast communications, to record and relay the same immediately to the proper authorities, and authorizing for the purpose three (5) employees of the Cabinet Secretariat to render overtime service with meal allowance of not more than two units each per day, is hereby revoked effective December 13, 1967.

The said duties assigned to the aforementioned stand-by personnel are hereby transferred to the Office of the Senior Aide-de-Camp to the President.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, December 20, 1967

Source: Malacañang Records Office
