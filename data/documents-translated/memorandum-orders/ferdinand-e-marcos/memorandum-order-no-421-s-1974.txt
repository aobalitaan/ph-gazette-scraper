TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 421

CREATING AN INTER-AGENCY COMMITTEE TO PREPARE AN OPERATIONS PLAN FOR THE SECOND SOUTHEAST ASIAN MINISTERIAL CONFERENCE ON TRANSPORT AND COMMUNICATIONS.

In connection with the forthcoming Second Southeast Asian Ministerial Conference on Transport and Communications to be held in Manila in July, 1974, there is hereby created an Inter-Agency Committee to prepare the Operations Plan to be composed of the following:

The Committee shall meet upon the call of the Chairman, and is authorized to call upon all government departments, bureaus, offices and agencies for such assistance as it may deem necessary in discharging its functions.

The Committee shall submit a Report of the Operations Plan to the Office of the President not later than 30 June 1974

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, 15 April 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
