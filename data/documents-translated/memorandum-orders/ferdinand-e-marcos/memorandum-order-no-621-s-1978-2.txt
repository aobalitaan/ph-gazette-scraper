Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 621

The temporary detail and designation of the government personnel listed, hereunder, effective upon entrance to duty, to Constitute the Skeletal Work Force of the Batasang Pambansa which shall provide initially, the requisite technical, staff and clerical services for the Batasan, is hereby authorized, to wit:

They shall perform the duties and discharge the responsibilities of the positions indicated opposite their names based on the Plantilla of Personnel of the Batasan Secretariat,

They shall be entitled to honoraria in the amount equivalent to thirty five percent ( 35%) of their basic salaries chargeable against the Batasan funds, subject to the usual accounting and auditing procedures.

By Authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, 30 June, 1978
