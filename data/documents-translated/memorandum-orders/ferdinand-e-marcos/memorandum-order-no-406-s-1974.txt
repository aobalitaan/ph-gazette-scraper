TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 406

PRESCRIBING PROCEDURE FOR THE RENEWAL OF MALACAÑANG CAR STICKERS.

In view of the approval of the issuance of new Malacañang car stickers for use on the cars of officials and employees of the Office of the President, Budget Commission and the Department of Public Information, all concerned are hereby advised to get in touch with the personnel officer of their respective offices who will start issuing forms for the renewal of their car stickers starting February 18, 1974.

The Office of the Assistant Executive Secretary for Administrative and Financial Affairs, the Office of the Provost Marshall and the Malacañang Information and Assistance Unit will coordinate in the implementation of this matter.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, February 18, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
