MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 617, May 22, 1978 ]
DESIGNATING THE POLITICAL AFFAIRS UNIT, THIS OFFICE, AS THE SECRETARIAT TO ASSIST, IN ADDITION TO ITS PRESENT DUTIES, THE LEAGUE OF PROVINCIAL GOVERNORS AND CITY MAYORS
The Political Affairs Unit, this Office, headed by Atty. Leopoldo Mendoza, Presidential Staff Director, is hereby designated as the Secretariat to assist, in addition to its present duties, the League of Provincial Governors and City Mayors.
