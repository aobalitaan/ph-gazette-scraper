Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 170

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 107 DATED JULY 12, 1968, REGARDING THE FLAG CEREMONY IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Memorandum Order No. 107 dated July 12, 1968, as amended, directing the holding of a Flag Ceremony every Monday morning and Friday afternoon in the Office of the President, is hereby further amended so as to reschedule the ceremony on Mondays at 8:00 A.M. sharp, instead of from 8:45 to 9:00 A.M. The Friday afternoon ceremony shall, as before, start at 4:45 o’clock, sharp.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, February 19, 1970

Source: Malacañang Records Office
