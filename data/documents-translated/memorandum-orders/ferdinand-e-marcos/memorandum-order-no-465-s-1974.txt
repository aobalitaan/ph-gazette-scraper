TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 465

DESIGNATING THE SECRETARY OF PUBLIC INFORMATION AS EX-OFFICIO MEMBER OF COMMITTEES TO CELEBRATE SPECIAL WEEKS.

WHEREAS, in the celebration of the various “Weeks” proclaimed by the President, Department of Public Information should participate more fully in the formulation of communication programs undertaken in connection with the occasions;

WHEREFORE, the Secretary of Public Information, or his duly authorized representative, is designated ex-officio member, in the cases where he is not yet a member of all committees or organizations designated to take charge of the special “Weeks” declared by the President from time to time.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

October 28, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
