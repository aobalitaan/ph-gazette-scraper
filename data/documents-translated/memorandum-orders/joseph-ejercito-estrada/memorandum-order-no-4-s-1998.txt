RECONSTITUTING THE SELECTION BOARD FOR FIRST AND SECOND LEVEL
CAREER POSITIONS IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (PROPER)

The Selection Board in the Office of the President is hereby reconstituted as
follows:

The Selection Board for Levels I and II shall be composed of the following:

Chairman - Deputy Executive Secretary

for Administration and Finance
Vice-Chairman - Director, Human Resource Management Office
Members - Head or Office-In-Charge of the Office/Unit

where the vacancy exists
- Representatives of the OP Employees Union
a. one for the 1st Level
b. another for the 2nd Level

All previous issuances/provisions inconsistent herewith are hereby superseded
and/or modified accordingly.

\

This order shall take effect on July 1, 1998.
