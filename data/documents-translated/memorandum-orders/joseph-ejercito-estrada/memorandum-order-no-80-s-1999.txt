Office of the Presient of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 80

CREATING A SPECIAL COMMITTEE IN THE OFFICE OF THE EXECUTIVE SECRETARY TO STUDY AND IMPLEMENT THE INTEGRATION/RECRUITMENT OF THE CORDILLERA PEOPLE’S LIBERATION ARMY (CPLA) TO THE AFP/PNP IN RELATION TO SECTION 6 CORDILLERA REGIONAL SECURITY FORCE OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 220 S. 1987 CREATING THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

In line with the government’s policy of strengthening peace and order in the country, particularly in the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR), there is hereby created a Committee in the Office of the Executive Secretary to review, study and implement the integration/recruitment of the CPLA to the AFP/PNP with relation to Section 6 of Executive Order No. 220 s. 1987, composed of the following:

The committee is hereby directed to conduct dialogue and consultations with the different Cordillera People’s Liberation Army (CPLA) factions or as a whole;

The committee is further directed to submit its report and recommendations to the President, through the Office of the Executive Secretary, not later than 31 December 1999.

The Office of the President Proper shall source out funds for this activity.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

Manila, December 1, 1999.
