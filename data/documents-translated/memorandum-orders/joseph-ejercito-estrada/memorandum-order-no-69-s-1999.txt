Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 69

Pursuant to the President’s directive to fast track the implementation of the President’s Bridge Program, the following personnel are hereby designated to comprise the Oversight Committee of the President’s Bridge Program – Technical Working Group:

The general function of Oversight Committee on the President’s Bridge Program –

(1) overseeing and monitoring the over-all implementation of the whole program on inter-agency basis;

(2) planning, conduct of discussions, and threshing out of details and administrative arrangements necessary for the implementation of the PBP;

(3) implementing and/or coordinating with various components of the PBP, including financing, physical implementation, and supervision of the Program; and

(4) providing periodic report to the President.

A TWG Secretariat is hereby established and a Program Liaison Officer designated to facilitate the conduct of and documentation of reporting requirements of the President’s Bridge Program, as follows:

The TWG shall draw its operating funds from the projects under the President’s Bridge Program with the above named personnel entitled to allowances and other benefits allowable under existing government rules and regulations.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

Manila, 26 July 1999.
