Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 27

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY AND REVIEW EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 220 S. 1987 CREATING THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

In line with the government’s policy of strengthening effective delivery of basic services and effective governance, there is hereby created a Committee to review and study Executive Order No. 220 s. 1987, composed of the following:

The Chief, Cordillera Peoples Liberation Army (CPLA) acting as Chairman and the CEB Executive Director, CRA representative, CBA representative, as members will represent the Cordillera panel in this Committee.

The Review Committee is hereby directed to submit its report and recommendation to the President, through the Office of the Executive Secretary, within one (1) month from the effectivity of this Memorandum.

The Office of the President Proper shall source out funds for this activity.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authoriy of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

Manila, 24 September 1998
