Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 116

Pursuant to the various directives of the President to fast-track the implementation of the President’s Bridge Program “Tulay ni Erap” Projects, Salam (Peace) Bridges and “Tulay ni Erap sa Barangay”, concerned personnel of both projects are hereby consolidated and designated to comprise the Oversight Committee of the President’s Bridge Program – Technical Working Group:

The general functions of the Oversight Committee on the President’s Bridge Program are as follows:

(1) overseeing and monitoring the over-all implementation of the whole program on an inter-agency basis;

(2) planning, conduct of discussions, and threshing out of details and administrative arrangements necessary for the implementation of the PBP;

(3) implementing and/or coordinating with various components of the PBP, including financing, physical implementation, and supervision of the Program; and

(4) providing periodic reports to the President.

Project Monitoring shall be headed by Mr. Alvin L. Cabatit in coordination with the various DILG Regional Directors to ensure the project’s integrity and transparency.

Previous directives and other issuances inconsistent herewith are hereby rescinded.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
