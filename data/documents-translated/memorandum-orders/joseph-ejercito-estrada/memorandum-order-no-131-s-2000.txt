MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 131

RECONSTITUTING THE PRESIDENT’ PERSONNEL GROUP

The President’s Personnel Group which will primarily be responsible for screening, at the level of the Office of the President, all recommendees for Presidential appointment to government positions, is hereby reconstituted as follows:

The Presidential Management Staff will continue to maintain the database system on Presidential appointees and provide secretariat support to the Group with assistance from the existing Personnel Group Secretariat.

All provisions of existing circular and other issuances that are otherwise inconsistent with this Order are hereby modified and repealed accordingly.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

Manila, 22 December 2000
