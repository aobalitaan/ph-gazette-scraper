Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 83

AUTHORIZING THE HOLDING OF ACTIVITIES IN OBSERVANCE OF CHRISTMAS ’99 IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (PROPER)

For a meaningful observance of Christmas ’99 before the Jubilee Year 2000, the holding of activities in the Office of the President (Proper) is hereby authorized to be spearheaded by the Office of the Executive Secretary, in coordination with the OP Christmas Program Committee, the OP Cultural affairs Committee and the Malacañang Chaplaincy, with the participation of the Colleges, Universities, School and Barangays within the vicinity of Malacañang.
