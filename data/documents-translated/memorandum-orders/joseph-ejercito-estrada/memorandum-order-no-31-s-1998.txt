Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 31

CREATING A SELECTION COMMITTEE FOR THE PRESIDENCY OF THE MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY SYSTEM

There is hereby created a Selection Committee for the position of President, Mindanao State University [MSU] System, composed of the following:

The Committee shall review the qualifications and capabilities of applicants/recommendees for the position of President of MSU, and make the appropriate recommendation to the President, through the President’s Personnel Group.

The Office of the Executive Secretary shall provide secretariat and administrative support to the Committee.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 day of October 1998.

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
