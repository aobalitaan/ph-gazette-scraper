Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 101

AUTHORIZING THE CONDUCT OF A SEMINAR ON PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT FOR WOMEN EMPOWERMENT IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (PROPER).

Personality Development is one basic need of a career woman in order to enhance the totality of her person. It is a reflection of her empowerment in handling her job well.

In this connection, a Seminar on Personality Development for Women Empowerment will be conducted on May 16 and 17, 2000 at 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon at the Mabini Social Hall.

Our women employees are hereby encouraged to participate in this project of the OP Gender and Development (GAD) Concerns Committee, in coordination with the Career and Personnel Development Division, Personnel Office, and Mr. James Cooper. Please enlist with the Personnel Office on Monday, May 15, 2000.

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

15 May 2000
