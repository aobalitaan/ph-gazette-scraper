MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 59

APPROVING THE 1999 INVESTMENT PRIORITIES PLAN

Pursuant to Article 29 of the Omnibus Investments Code of 1987, the attached 1999 Investment Priorities Plan (IPP) is hereby approved.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication as required under Article 31 of the Omnibus Investments Code of 1987.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 13 th of APRIL in the year of our Lord Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Nine.

( Sgd .) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: ( Sgd .) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
