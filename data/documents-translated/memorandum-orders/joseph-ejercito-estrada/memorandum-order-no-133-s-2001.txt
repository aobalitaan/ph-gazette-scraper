MALACAÑANG MANILA

Memorandum Order No. 133

Amending Memorandum Order No. 98 and Designating the signatories to Vouchers and Checks for President’s Social Fund

To expedite the signing/processing of PSF checks, M.O. No. 98 is hereby amended as follows:

1. Disbursement vouchers shall be processed and payments shall be effected by PMS. Signatories to the vouchers shall be the following:

The PMS Chief Accountant and Cashier shall be in charged of the preparation of vouchers, checks and financial reports as may be needed.

2. All checks shall be signed and countersigned by the following:

Signing Official – Secretary Ramon B. Cardenas or

Undersecretary Demetrio L. Ignacio, Jr.

Countersigning Official — ADMO Head, PMS

This Memorandum Order supersedes any contrary provisions in all other issuances relative to the above and shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of January in the year of our Lord two thousand one.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) Ramon B. Cardenas Acting Executive Secretary
