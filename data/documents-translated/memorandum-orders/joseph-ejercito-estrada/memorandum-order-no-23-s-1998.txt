MALACAÑANG Manila

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 23

To ensure the efficient management of our road network which is vital for promoting the global competitiveness of Philippine business and for sustaining the socio-economic progress of the country, you are hereby directed to oversee the conduct and implementation of an institutional study for better roads and ensure that steps are taken towards the adoption of the recommended reforms. Towards this end, you shall coordinate with the concerned private sector organizations and other stakeholders for their inputs in the study.

You are further directed to present the study’s final recommendation to Cabinet Cluster D (Physical Infrastructure Support Cluster) prior to its endorsement to the President and the Cabinet for approval.

Submit a monthly status report on the above instructions to my Office, through the Office of the Executive Secretary, copy furnished the Head, Presidential Management Staff and Cabinet Secretary.

For compliance.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA SEP 29 1998

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
