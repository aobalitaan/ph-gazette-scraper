Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 25

AUTHORIZING THE PRESIDENTIAL ASSISTANT FOR POVERTY ALLEVIATION, NGO AND PO TO SIGN IN BEHALF OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT THE MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT BETWEEN D.A.R.E. INTERNATIONAL, INC. AND PHILDARE, INC. FOR THE DRUG ABUSE RESISTANCE EDUCATION PROGRAM

The Presidential Assistant for Poverty Alleviation, NGO and PO is hereby authorized to represent the Office of the President of the Republic of the Philippines and sign the corresponding Memorandum of Agreement attached herewith at the meeting in the United States of America between the duly authorized representatives of the D.A.R.E. International, Inc. and the Philippine Drug Abuse Resistance Education (PhilDARE), Inc.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA
