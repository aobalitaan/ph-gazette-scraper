Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 15

INCLUSION OF DIRECTOR FLORIÑO IBAÑEZ, PERSONNEL OFFICE AS MEMBER OF THE INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (IT) COMMITTEE AS RECONSTITUTED PURSUANT TO MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 8

In the interest of public service and in view of the training and human resources development requirements of computerization and information technology programs and activities, Director Floriño Ibañez, Personnel Office, is hereby designated as a member of the Information Technology (IT) Committee as reconstituted pursuant to Memorandum Order No. 8 dated August 3, 1998.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

August 21, 1998
