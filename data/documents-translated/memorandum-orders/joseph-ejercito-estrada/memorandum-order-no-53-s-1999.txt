Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 53

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 31 DATED OCTOBER 27, 1998, CREATING A SELECTION COMMITTEE FOR THE PRESIDENCY OF THE MINDANAO STATE UNIVERSITY

The composition of the Selection Committee created under Memorandum Order No. 31 dated October 27, 1998, is hereby amended as follows:

Professor Jose Endriaga who shall act as the Secretary of the Committee will be a non-voting member.

The Committee is authorized to enlist the services of Atty. Rashid Saber and Dr. Camar Umpah as Resource Persons to assist in the selection process.

This Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

Manila, February 18, 1999.
