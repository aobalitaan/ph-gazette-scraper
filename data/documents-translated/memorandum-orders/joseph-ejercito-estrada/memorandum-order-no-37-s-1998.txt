MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 37

APPOINTING THE CHAIRPERSON OF THE PRESIDENTIAL COMMISSION ON THE URBAN POOR AS THE VICE-CHAIRPERSON OF THE NATIONAL ANTI-POVERTY COMMISSION REPRESENTING THE GOVERNMENT SECTOR

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA , do hereby appoint the Chairperson of the Presidential Commission on the Urban Poor as the Vice-Chairperson of the National Anti-Poverty Commission representing the government sector. With this appointment, the Chairperson of the PCUP shall have the rank of Cabinet Secretary.

This Order takes effect immediately.

Done this 16 th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
