MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 35

RECONSTITUTION OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (OP) PROGRAM ON AWARDS AND INCENTIVES FOR SERVICE EXCELLENCE (PRAISE) COMMITTEE

Memorandum Order No. 12, dated 14 March 2011, creating the OP – PRAISE Committee, pursuant to Civil Service Commission Memorandum Circular No. 1, s. 2001, is hereby reconstituted so as to include one (1) representative from the OP Career Executive Service Officers’ Association Inc., as member.

All other provisions of Memorandum Order No. 12, s. 2011 shall remain valid and in effect.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary

Manila, April 2, 2012
