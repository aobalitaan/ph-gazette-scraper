augmentation of legal assistance from the Office of the Solicitor General (OSG) due to

Corporate Counsel;

Government of the Philippines, its agencies and instrumentalities and its officials and 
agents on matters requiring the services of a lawyer and that, when authorized by the 
President or head of the office concerned, it shall also represent government owned or 
controlled corporations.
