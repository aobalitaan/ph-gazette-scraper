MEMORANDUM ORDER NO.5

AUTHORIZING THE CONTINUANCE IN OFFICE IN HOLD OVER CAPACITY OF CHAIRMEN AND MEMBERS OF THE VARIOUS PROFESSIONAL REGULATORY BOARDS UNDER THE SUPERVISION OF THE PROFESSIONAL REGULATION COMMISSION

In order that there will be no interruption or disruption in the activities of the Professional Regulatory Boards, the chairman and members named hereunder, are hereby authorized to continue in office in hold over capacity until October 31, 2010 or until their respective replacements have been appointed or designated, whichever comes first, unless they are reappointed in the meantime:

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila this 8 th day of October in the Year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
