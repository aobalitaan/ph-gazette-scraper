MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 36

REQUEST FOR THE CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE
DEPUTATION OF THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES (AFP) AND
THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (PNP), FOR THE PURPOSE OF
ENSURING FREE, ORDERLY, HONEST, PEACEFUL AND CREDIBLE
CONDUCT OF THE SPECIAL BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG
KABATAAN (SK) ELECTIONS IN BARANGAY LIPAT-ON, CALATRAVA,
NEGROS OCCIDENTAL; BARANGAY TULAY, MADAMBA, LANAO DEL
SUR; AND, BARANGAYS BIALAAN AND PATONG, BAYANG, LANAO
DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), through its Resolution
No. 9384 (s. 2012), resolved to request the concurrence of the President in the
deputation of law enforcement agencies and other instrumentalities of the
government, including the AFP and PNP, in connection with the 12 May 2012 special
barangay and SK elections in Barangay Lipat-on, Calatrava, Negros Occidenial;
Barangay Tulay, Madamba, Lanao del Sur; and, Barangays Bialaan and Patong,
Bayang, Lanao del Sur;

WHEREAS, COMELEC Resolution No. 9384 was issued pursuant to
COMELEC Resolution dated 16 June 2011 declaring a failure of election at Barangay
Lipat-on Calatrava, Negros Occidental and declaring the proclamation of Ned
Damondamon as Punong Barangay and all Kagawads of said barangay as null and
void; COMELEC Resolution dated 27 December 2011 declaring a failure of election
and ordering the conduct of a special election in Barangay Tulay, Madamba, Lanao
del Sur; and, COMELEC Resolution dated 7 February 2012 declaring a failure of
election in Barangays Bialaan and Patong, Bayang, Lanao del Sur, annulling the
proclamation of the ostensible winners and ordering the conduct of a special election
thereat, as clarified in COMELEC Minute Resolution No. 12-0313 dated 27 March
2012; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 2 (4), Article IX-C of the Constitution, the
COMELEC is empowered to deputize, with the concurrence of the President, law
enforcement agencies and instrumenitalities of the Government, including the AFP,
for the exclusive purpose of ensuring free, orderly, honest, peaceful, and credible
elections.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines,
by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, hereby
concur with COMELEC Resolution No. 9384 deputizing law enforcement agencies
and instrumentalities of the Government, including the AFP and PNP, to assist the
