rank of at least an Assistant Secretary

- Head, Legal Office
- Head, Finance Office

- One (1) Representative from the OP Career Executive
Service Officers’ Association (OP-CESOAI)

One (1) Alternate Representative from OP-CESOAI

- One (1) Representative from the Office of the President
Employees’ Network (OPEN) for 2™ level positions

One (1) Alternate Representative from OPEN for 2™ level
positions

- One (1) Representative from OPEN for 1*' level positions

positions
