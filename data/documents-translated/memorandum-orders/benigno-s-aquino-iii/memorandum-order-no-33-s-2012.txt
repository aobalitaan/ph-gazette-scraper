MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 33

RECONSTITUTION OF THE DISPOSAL COMMITTEE IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

Memorandum Order No. 3, dated July 17, 2010, reconstituting the Disposal Committee in the Office of the President, is hereby amended, so as to designate Undersecretary RONALDO A. GERON, Head, Discipline Office, as Chairman of the Committee, vice former Assistant Executive Secretary Ericson A. Alcovendaz, and Director EDGAR RYAN B. FAUSTINO, Head, Presidential Museum and Library, as additional Member.

All other provisions of Memorandum Order No. 3, s.2010 shall remain valid and in effect.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary

Manila,   22   February, 2012
