MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 16

AMENDING SECTION 12 OF MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 109 DATED 15 AUGUST 2000 ENTITLED: “ESTABLISHING A PROVIDENT FUND FOR THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT – PROPER PERSONNEL”

WHEREAS, the Office of the President (OP) Provident Fund, also known as the Fund, was established pursuant to Memorandum Order (M.O.) No. 109 , dated 15 August 2000, to provide supplementary benefits to, and improve the quality of life, work and general welfare of OP-Proper employees who become members of the Fund;

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 12 of Memorandum Order (M.O.) No. 109 and Section 1, Article VII of its Implementing Rules and Regulations, the Fund shall be managed and administered by a Board of Trustees composed of seven (7) members;

WHEREAS, the Human Resource Management Office is the primary unit mandated to administer and oversee balanced personnel programs, that includes employees’ welfare and development;

WHEREAS, the Board sees the wisdom of engaging, as members of the Board of Trustees, the services, expertise, inputs of the Human Resource Management Office and the Legal Affairs Office Directors in its aim to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of managing and administering the affairs, business and properties of the Fund;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO, III, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law. do hereby order:

Section 1. Section 12 “Administration of the OP Provident Fund” of Memorandum Order No. 109, s. 2000 is hereby amended to read as follows:

Administration of the OP Provident Fund . – There is hereby constituted a Board of Trustees, which shall administer and manage the affairs, businesses and properties of the OP Provident Fund, to be composed of the following:

a) The Executive Secretary, as Chairman;

b) The Deputy Executive Secretary for Finance and Administration, as Vice-Chairman;

c) The Director, Finance Office;

d) The Director, Human Resource Management Office;

e) The Director, Legal Office;

f) Two (2) 3 rd level officials to be appointed by the duly constituted CESO organization, as Members;

g) One (1) 2 nd level representative to be appointed by the recognized Employees’ Association/ Union, as Member; and

h) One (1) 1 st level representative to be appointed by the recognized Employees’ Association/ Union, as Member.

Section 2. All other provisions of Memorandum Order No. 109, s. 2000 shall remain valid and in effect.

Section 3. This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12 th day of May in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand Eleven.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
