MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 375

DESIGNATING THE SECRETARY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS, THE SECRETARY OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATIONS, THE PRESS SECRETARY AND THE PROVINCIAL GOVERNORS OF RIZAL, LAGUNA, BATANGAS, PAMPANGA, PANGASINAN AND NUEVA ECIJA AS MEMBERS OF THE NATIONAL EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE CREATED UNDER MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 344, SERIES OF 1991

WHEREAS , in view of the significance of the commemoration of the 50th anniversary of World War II, there is a need to increase the membership of the National Executive Committee created under Memorandum Order No. 344, series of 1991;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:

Section 1. The Secretary of Foreign Affairs, the Secretary of Transportation and Communications, the Press Secretary and the Provincial Governors of Rizal, Laguna, Batangas, Pampanga, Pangasinan and Nueva Ecija are hereby designated as members of the National Executive Committee created under Memorandum Order No. 344, series of 1991.

Sec. 2. This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) MAGDANGAL B. ELMA Acting Executive Secretary
