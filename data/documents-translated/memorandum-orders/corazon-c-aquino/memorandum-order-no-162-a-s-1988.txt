Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 162-A

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 162 DATED MARCH 16, 1988 CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE OFFICIAL VISIT OF HIS EXCELLENCY FELIPE GONZALEZ, PRESIDENT OF THE GOVERNMENT OF SPAIN.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Foreign Affairs, Memorandum Order No. 162 dated March 16, 1988 creating a National Committee to take charge of the official visit of His Excellency Felipe Gonzalez, President of the Government of Spain, is hereby amended to include Honorable Juan Jose Rocha, Envoy Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the President to the Kingdom of Spain, as Member of the National Committee.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, March 25, 1988
