MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 249, August 24, 1989 ]
APPROVING THE 1989 INVESTMENT PRIORITIES PLAN
Pursuant to Executive Order No. 226, otherwise known as the Omnibus Investments Code of 1987, and as recommended by the Board of Investments, the attached 1989 Investment Priorities Plan is hereby approved.
This Memorandum Order shall take effect after fifteen (15) days following the completion of its publication in at least one newspaper of general circulation.
