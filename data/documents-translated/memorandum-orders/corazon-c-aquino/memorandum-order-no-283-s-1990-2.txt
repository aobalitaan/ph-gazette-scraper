MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 283

APPROVING THE IMPLEMENTING GUIDELINES, RULES AND REGULATIONS TO NATIONAL EMERGENCY MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 6 DATED JANUARY 4, 1990

In accordance with Section 1 of the National Emergency Memorandum Order No. 6, dated January 4, 1990, the attached Department Order No. A-04 of the Department of National Defense entitled “Implementing Guidelines, Rules and Regulations To Presidential National Emergency Memorandum Order No. 6 Dated January 4, 1990,” is hereby approved to be effective immediately upon publication thereof and this Memorandum Order at least two (2) national newspapers of general circulation.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
