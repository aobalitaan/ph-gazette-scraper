Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 207

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO OVERSEE THE LANDSCAPING OF THE MALACAÑANG GROUNDS

In view of the need to develop and improve the landscape of the Malacañang grounds, there is hereby created a Committee to oversee the said landscaping composed of the following:

The Committee shall be assisted by the National Parks Development Committee in the preparation of the master plan and the execution thereof after its approval by this Office.

The Committee may call for assistance upon any department, agency or instrumentality of the government as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Order.

The Committee shall submit periodic reports and recommendations to the Executive Secretary during the implementation of this project.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, December 6, 1988
