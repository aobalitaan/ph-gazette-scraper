Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 244

CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE OFFICIAL VISIT OF HIS EXCELLENCY HUSSAIN MUHAMMAD ERSHAD, PRESIDENT OF THE PEOPLE’S REPUBLIC OF BANGLADESH

There is hereby created a National Committee to take charge of the arrangements for the Official Visit of His Excellency Hussain Muhammad Ershad, President of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh, to be composed of the following:

The Committee shall be responsible for the planning, coordination, and smooth execution of all arrangements to be made in connection with the Official Visit.

The Committee is authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or any instrumentality of the government, including government-owned and controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in discharging its duties and functions. The Committee is likewise authorized to create sub-committees as necessary.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, July 14, 1989
