OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 57

ENJOINING ALL OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT AND ITS ATTACHED AGENCIES TO REGISTER DURING THE GENERAL REGISTRATION OF VOTERS PURSUANT TO EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 50, S. 1986

It is the civic duty of every Filipino citizen to register and vote in every election, referendum or plebiscite, more importantly and urgently in the ratification of the new constitution. In view of this duty, all officials and employees of the Office of the President and its attached agencies are hereby enjoined to register during the general registration of voters as provided for under Executive Order No. 50, dated October 20, 1986. They are likewise requested to urge the members of their family to do the same.

All officials and employees shall, after the registration dates, show their registration cards to their heads of offices, who shall thereafter submit to this Office a report on their compliance herewith.

(SGD.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR.

Manila, December 12, 1986
