Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 290

AUTHORIZING THE SECRETARY OF THE PRESIDENTIAL MANAGEMENT STAFF TO ACT ON ALL REQUESTS FOR REALIGNMENT AND COST ADJUSTMENT OF SCHOOL BUILDING PROJECTS AND OTHER PROGRAMS UNDER THE PRESIDENT’S SOCIAL FUND

The Secretary of the Presidential Management Staff (PMS) is hereby authorized to act on all requests for realignment and cost adjustment of school building projects and other programs under the President’s Social Fund (PSF).

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, April 20, 1990
