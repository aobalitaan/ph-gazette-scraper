MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 255, September 18, 1989 ]
SUSPENDING OF THE OPERATION OF THE TASK FORCE ANTI-GAMBLING
The operation of the Task Force Anti-Gambling is hereby suspended effective immediately and to continue until further order from this Office.
