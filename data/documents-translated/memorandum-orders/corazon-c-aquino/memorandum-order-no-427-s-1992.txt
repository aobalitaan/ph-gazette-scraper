MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 427, April 30, 1992 ]
APPROVING THE 1992 INVESTMENT PRIORITIES PLAN
Pursuant to Article 29 of the Omnibus Investment Code of 1987, the attached 1992 Investment Priorities Plan is hereby approved.
This Memorandum Order shall take effect upon its publication as required under Article 31 of the Omnibus Investment Code of 1987.
DONE in the City of Manila, this 30th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.
