MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 294

CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE “WORKING VISIT” OF HIS EXCELLENCY ABDOU DIOUF, PRESIDENT OF SENEGAL.

There is hereby created a National Committee to take charge of the arrangements connected with the Working Visit of His Excellency Abdou Diouf, President of Senegal, to be composed of:

The Committee shall be responsible for the planning, coordination and smooth execution of all arrangements to be made in connection with the Working Visit.

The Committee is authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or any instrumentality of the government, including government-owned and controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in discharging its duties and functions. The Committee is likewise authorized to create sub-committees as necessary.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, May 17, 1990
