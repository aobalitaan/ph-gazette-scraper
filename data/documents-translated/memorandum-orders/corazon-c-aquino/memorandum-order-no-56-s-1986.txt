Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 56

CREATING A TASK FORCE TO STUDY THE 60-30-10 PLAN INVOLVING SUGAR LANDS IN NEGROS OCCIDENTAL.

WHEREAS, the economy of the Province of Negros Occidental is in a depressed state caused by the slump of sugar demand in the world market;

WHEREAS, some 90% of its two million people are directly or indirectly dependent on sugar, of which 270,000 workers are reportedly employed in growing sugarcane; and

WHEREAS, the depressed state of Negros economy has magnified the threat of Communist insurgency thus requiring immediate rehabilitation and diversification.

ACCORDINGLY, a Task Force is hereby created with the objective of studying the 60-30-10 plan involving sugar lands in Negros Occidental which was one of the issues presented to the President during her Presidential Regional Consultation in Region VI.
