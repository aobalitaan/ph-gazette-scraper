Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 67

MODIFYING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 62 DATED JANUARY 15, 1987 AUTHORIZING THE CONTINUED EXEMPTION FROM CLASSIFICATION BY THE OFFICE OF COMPENSATION AND POSITION CLASSIFICATION OF APPOINTMENTS TO POSITIONS PAID FROM CERTAIN LUMP-SUM APPROPRIATIONS IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Memorandum Order No. 62 is hereby modified to the extent that appointments to positions paid from lump-sum appropriations in the Task Force Anti-Gambling are hereby included in the coverage of the aforementioned Memorandum Order.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, January 22, 1987
