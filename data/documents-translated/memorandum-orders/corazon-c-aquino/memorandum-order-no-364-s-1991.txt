MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 364

PRESCRIBING REGULATIONS ON THE SUBMISSION OF MEDICAL CERTIFICATES TO SUPPORT APPLICATIONS FOR SICK LEAVE

Effective immediately, applications of employees in the Office of the President Proper for sick leave of absence exceeding five (5) days shall be supported by a medical certificate issued by the Malacañang Clinic. In cases where medical certificates are issued by physicians who are not connected with our Clinic, the same shall be evaluated and endorsed by the Malacañang Clinic.

Medical certificates supporting sick leave applications shall be evaluated by the Director of the Clinic or any of his Assistant Directors who shall make appropriate recommendations on the duration of the period of the sick leave.

Manila, May 17, 1991
