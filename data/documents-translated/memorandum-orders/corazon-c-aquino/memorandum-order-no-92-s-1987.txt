MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 92

-

For bettér coordination and more efficient use of
human and material resources, the Office of the Building
Administrator (OBA) is hereby abolished and its functions,
records and properties and equipment are transferred to
the Engineer's Office. Employees assigned to the OBA from
other units are returned to their mother units; those who
were specifically hired/employed for assignment to the OBA
are also transferred to the Engineer's Office.

This Order, shall take effect immediately.
