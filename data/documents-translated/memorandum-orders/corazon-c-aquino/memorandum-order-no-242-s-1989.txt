MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 242

DESIGNATING THE GOVERNOR OF THE CENTRAL BANK AND THE CHAIRMAN OF THE COORDINATING COUNCIL ON THE PHILIPPINE ASSISTANCE PROGRAM AS MEMBERS OF THE NATIONAL ECONOMIC AND DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY BOARD

Pursuant to Section 4 of Executive Order No. 230, Series of 1987, the Governor of the Central Bank and the Chairman of the Coordinating Council on the Philippine Assistance Program  are hereby designated as members of the National Economic and Development Authority Board.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO
