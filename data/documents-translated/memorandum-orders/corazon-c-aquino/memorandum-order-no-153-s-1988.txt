MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 153

PROVIDING THE LIST OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO BE MONITORED BY THE GOVERNMENT CORPORATE MONITORING AND COORDINATING COMMITTEE (GCMCC) FOR CALENDAR YEAR 1988.

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 236 dated July 22, 1987, Strengthening the Government Corporate Monitoring and Coordinating Committee (GCMCC) and For Other Purposes, the following government-owned and/or controlled corporations (GOCC), including their subsidiaries are hereby approved, as recommended by the Committee, to be monitored by the GCMCC in Calendar Year 1988:

GOCCs which have subsidiaries shall submit to the GCMCC consolidated statements and reports. The GCMCC, however, is empowered to look into the individual statements and reports of subsidiary GOCCs if warranted by the circumstances.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

Manila, January 29, 1988
