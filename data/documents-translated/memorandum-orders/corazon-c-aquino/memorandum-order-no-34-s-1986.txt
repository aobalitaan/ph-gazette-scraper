[ Memorandum Order No. 34, September 10, 1986 ]
REVOKING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 1413 – DECLARING THE THEME “ISANG BANSA, ISANG DIWA,” AS THE NATIONAL MOTTO OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES, AND INCORPORATING IT IN THE NATIONAL SEAL.
Presidential Decree No. 1413 – Declaring the Theme “Isang Bansa, Isang Diwa” as the national motto of the Republic of the Philippines, and incorporating it in the National Seal, – is hereby revoked.
This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.
CORAZON C. AQUINO
Manila, September 10, 1986
