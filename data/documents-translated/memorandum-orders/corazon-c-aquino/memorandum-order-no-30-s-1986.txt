MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 30, August 20, 1986 ]
DECLARING THE OFFICE OF THE VICE-PRESIDENT AS A CRITICAL AGENCY OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT
The Office of the Vice-President is hereby declared a critical agency.
The Compensation Plan for the Office of the President (Proper) authorized under E.O. No. 530 dated March 31, 1979 shall apply to all permanent positions in the Office of the Vice-President effective July 1, 1986.
The Ministry of Budget and Management is directed to implement this Order.
