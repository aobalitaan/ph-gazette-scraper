Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 29

All Heads of Ministries, Agencies, Government Corporations and Instrumentalities of the Government, Including Provincial and Local Governments

Non-Existence of the Presidential Regional Assistant Monitoring Services (PRAMS) within the Office of the President

The Presidential Regional Assistant Monitoring Services (PRAMS) is a non-existent agency within the Office of the President. Its alleged Executive Director Maximino Gamido Y Buenaventura is likewise not connected, in any capacity, with this Office.

It is also reiterated, for the purpose of clarity, that all activities pertinent to the Presidential Regional Monitoring Office (PREMO) per Executive Order 819, Series of 1982, has been suspended by Memorandum Order No. 22 of the Office of the President dated 15 July 1986 as signed by the Deputy Executive Secretary.

This order shall take effect immediately.

(Sgd.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, August 18, 1986
