MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 224-A, April 11, 1989 ]
AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 224 DATED MARCH 13, 1989 CONSTITUTING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE MANAGEMENT OF THE MALACANAN PALACE.
Memorandum Order No. 224 constituting a Committee to take charge of the administration, maintenance, preservation and accountability of property and assets of the Malacanan Palace is hereby amended to include the Presidential Finance Assistant as member of the Committee.
This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.
