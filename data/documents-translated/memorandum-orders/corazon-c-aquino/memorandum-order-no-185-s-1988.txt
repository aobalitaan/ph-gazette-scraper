Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 185

IMPLEMENTING CERTAIN SECURITY-RELATED MEASURES WITHIN THE PREMISES OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Effective immediately, strict observance of the following rules is enjoined:

1. The Security Access Tag issued by the Presidential Security Group (PSG) to every OP personnel shall be worn by him/her while within the premises of the Office of the President.

2. A Visitor Tag shall be issued by the PSG personnel to every visitor or other non-OP personnel that is allowed access to the premises of the Office of the President.

3. As workers are scheduled to work overtime, the Personnel Office shall furnish the PSG with the list thereof.

Please be guided accordingly.

(Sgd.) MAGDANGAL B. ELMA Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, August 2, 1988
