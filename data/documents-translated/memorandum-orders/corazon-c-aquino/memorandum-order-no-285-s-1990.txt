MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 285, March 2, 1990 ]
Upon recommendation of DSWD Secretary Mita Pardo de Tavera in her letter of February 6, 1990, the Main Processing Station (MPS) established in this Office to review indemnity claims of victims of the M/V Dona-Paz and M/V Dona Marilyn tragedies, is hereby closed effective immediately.
Accordingly, all members of the staff assigned to the Station are hereby returned to their respective mother units.
