Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 6

PLACING CERTAIN AGENCIES OF THE GOVERNMENT UNDER THE ADMINISTRATIVE SUPERVISION OF THE MINISTRY OF HEALTH.

WHEREAS, there is a need to place certain agencies of the government heretofore operating independently and/or falling under various ministries, under one Ministry for purposes of effective supervision and formulation of policies, plans and programs; and

WHEREAS, it is most desirable to continuously improve the administrative and functional relationship among agencies to remain responsive to the needs of the service.

IN VIEW THEREOF, the following agencies of the government are hereby placed under the administrative supervision of the Ministry of Health:

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, April 8, 1986
