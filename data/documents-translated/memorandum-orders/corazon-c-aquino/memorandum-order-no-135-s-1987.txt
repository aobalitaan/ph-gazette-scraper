Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 135

AMENDING THE MEMORANDUM DATED JANUARY 28, 1987 CREATING THE CABINET CRISIS COMMITTEE.

The Memorandum dated January 28, 1987 which created a Cabinet Crisis Committee to attend to the matter of preventing a recurrence of the January 26th, 1987 attempted coup and how to meet a similar situation should it occur, is hereby amended to include the Secretary of Local Government, Hon. Luis Santos, as a Member of the said Committee.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, November 20, 1987
