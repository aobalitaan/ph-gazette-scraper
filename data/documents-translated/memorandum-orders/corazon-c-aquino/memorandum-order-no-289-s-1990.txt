MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 289

DESIGNATING THE PRESIDENTIAL COORDINATING ASSISTANTS FOR AGRO-INDUSTRIAL, ECONOMIC AND FINANCIAL AFFAIRS, AND FOR HUMAN RESOURCES AND PUBLIC WELFARE AS MEMBERS OF THE NATIONAL ECONOMIC AND DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY BOARD

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 230, S. of 1987, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the Presidential Coordinating Assistants for Agro-Industrial, Economic and Financial Affairs, and for Human Resources and Public Welfare as Members of the National Economic and Development Authority (NEDA) Board.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 5th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
