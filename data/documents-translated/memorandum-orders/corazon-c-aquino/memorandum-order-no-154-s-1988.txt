Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 154

CREATING A SUB-COMMITTEE ON INVENTORY OF PASSENGERS OF M/V DOÑA PAZ TO FURTHER ASSIST THE RELATIVES OF VICTIMS AND SURVIVORS

WHEREAS; there is a need to facilitate and expedite the identification of recipients of “Bigay-Puso Fund” and other legal claims of survivors and victims of the M/V Doña Paz and M/T Vector collision.

WHEREFORE, a Sub-Committee on Inventory of Passengers of M/V Doña Paz is hereby created.

The Sub-Committee shall be composed of the following:

The Sub-Committee shall have the following powers and functions:

1. Coordinate and integrate all efforts of both the government and private sectors in dealing with the problem of identifying the passengers of the M/V Doña Paz;

2. Formulate a uniform set of policies and guidelines for the inventory and identification of passengers of the M/V Doña Paz;

3. Provide a forum for discussion and/or resolution of all issues/problems and/or conflicts that may arise between or among claimants insofar as these relate to the inventory and identification of passengers of the M/V Doña Paz; and

4. Request the assistance of any department, bureau, office or agency, as may be necessary to enable the Sub-Committee to perform and accomplish its duties and responsibilities.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) SAMILO N. BARLONGAY Acting Deputy Executive Secretary
