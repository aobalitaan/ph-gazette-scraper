MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 419

PROVIDING THE SUPPLEMENTAL LIST OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO BE COVERED BY THE PERFORMANCE EVALUATION SYSTEM OF THE GOVERNMENT CORPORATE MONITORING AND COORDINATING COMMITTEE (GCMCC) FOR CALENDAR YEAR 1992

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 236 dated July 22, 1987, entitled: “Strengthening the Government Corporate Monitoring and Coordinating Committee (GCMCC) and for Other Purposes,” the following government-owned and/or controlled corporations (GOCCs) including their subsidiaries are hereby approved, as recommended, to be covered by the performance evaluation system of the GCMCC in Calendar Year 1992:

Philippine Heart Center 31. Philippine Institute for Development Studies 32. Philippine International Convention Center, Inc. 33. Philippine National Red Cross 34. PNOC Energy Development Corporation 35. PNOC Exploration Corporation 36. PNOC Shipping and Transport Corporation 37. PNOC Tankers Corporation 38. Philippine Trade Exhibition Center 39. GOCCs shall submit to the GCMCC consolidated statements and reports. The GCMCC, however, is empowered to look into the individual statements and reports of subsidiary GOCCs.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO
