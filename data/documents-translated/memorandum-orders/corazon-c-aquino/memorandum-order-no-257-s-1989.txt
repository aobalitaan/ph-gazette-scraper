Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 257

DESIGNATING THE HONORABLE DIMASANGKAY A. PUNDATO, EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR, OFFICE ON MUSLIM AFFAIRS AS MEMBER OF THE COMMITTEE CREATED UNDER MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 244 DATED JULY 19, 1989

The Honorable Dimasangkay A. Pundato, Executive Director, Office on Muslim Affairs, is hereby designated Member of the Committee created under Memorandum Order No. 244 which takes charge of the official visit of His Excellency Hussain Muhammad Ershad, President of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh.

The aforementioned Memorandum Order No. 244 is hereby amended accordingly.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, September 26, 1989
