MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 78, April 1, 1987 ]
To inculcate reverence, esteem, and devotion to our flag, all officials and employees of the Office of the Deputy Executive Secretary shall converge infront of the Administration Building during Mondays at 8:00 a.m. and Fridays at 5:00 p.m. for flag raising and lowering ceremonies.
Strict compliance herewith is enjoined.
