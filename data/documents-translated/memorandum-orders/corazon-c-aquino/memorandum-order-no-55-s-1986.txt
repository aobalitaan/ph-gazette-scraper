Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 55

Pending specific policy issuance by the Office of the President regarding the accurate interpretation of Executive Order No. 696 dated 27 May 1981, as amended by Executive Order No. 771 dated 4 January 1982, graduates of the National Defense College of the Philippines belonging to the civil service and graduates of the Career Executive Service Development Program who have not yet been appointed to a CESO Rank shall continue receiving the compensation and other benefits authorized under the Career Executive Service Board Circular No. 8, s. 1982.

(Sgd.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, December 11, 1986
