MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 325, October 11, 1990 ]
GRANTING HAZARD PAY TO NBI AGENTS AND THEIR SUPERVISORS EFFECTIVE JANUARY 1, 1990
The NBI Agents and their superior officers are hereby granted a minimum monthly hazard pay of ₱500 as an exception to the provisions of Section 37 of the General Provisions of R.A. 6831.
Funds needed to implement this Memorandum Order shall be taken from the current savings from appropriations of the NBI.
