[Memorandum Order No. 361, May 13, 1991]
SUSPENDING THE EFFECTIVITY OF MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 358, SERIES OF 1991
In view of the reorganization of the Presidential Action Center, Memorandum Order No. 358, dated May 7, 1991, is hereby suspended.
All orders and acts issued pursuant to Memorandum Order No. 358, dated May 7, 1991, are likewise hereby suspended.
OSCAR M. ORBOS
Manila, May 13, 1991
