MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 386

PROVIDING THE LIST OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND/OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO BE MONITORED BY THE GOVERNMENT CORPORATE MONITORING AND COORDINATING COMMITTEE (GCMCC) FOR CALENDAR YEAR 1992.

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 236 dated July 22, 1987, “Strengthening the Government Corporate Monitoring and Coordinating Committee (GCMCC) and for Other Purposes,” the following government-owned and/or controlled corporations (GOCCs) including their subsidiaries are hereby approved, as recommended, to be monitored by the GCMCC in Calendar Year 1992.

1. Center for International Trade Exhibitions and Missions Inc. 2. Export Processing Zone Authority 3. Home Insurance and Guarantee Corporation 4. Home Mutual Development Fund 5. Light Rail Transit Authority 6. Livelihood Corporation 7. Local Water Utilities Administration 8. Manila International Airport Authority 9. Metro Manila Transit Corporation 10. Metropolitan Waterworks and Sewerage System 11. National Development Company 12. National Electrification Administration 13. National Food Authority 14. National Home Mortgage Finance Corporation 15. National Housing Authority 16. National Irrigation Administration 17. National Power Corporation 18. National Tobacco Administration 19. Natural Resources Development Corporation 20. Philippine Crop Insurance Corporation 21. Philippine Deposit Insurance Corporation 22. Philippine Export and Foreign Loan Guarantee Corporation 23. Philippine Fisheries Development Authority 24. Philippine International Trading Corporation 25. Philippine National Oil Company 26. Philippine National Railways 27. Philippine Ports Authority 28. Philippine Retirement Authority 29. Philippine Rice Research Institute 30. Philippine Tourism Authority 31. Philippine Veterans Investment Development Corporation 32. PHIVIDEC Industrial Authority 33. Public Estates Authority 34. Southern Philippines Development Corporation 35. Strategic Investment Development Corporation 36. Sugar Regulatory Administration 37. Technology and Livelihood Research Center

GOCCs which have subsidiaries shall submit to the GCMCC consolidated statements and reports. The GCMCC, however, is empowered to look into the individual statements and reports of subsidiary GOCCs if warranted by the circumstances.

This Memorandum Order takes effect immediately.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

Manila, August 26, 1991
