MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 77, March 31, 1987 ]
SUSPENDING THE IMPLEMENTATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 73
The implementation of Executive Order No. 73, dated November 25, 1986, is hereby suspended until June 30, 1987 pursuant to the Cabinet resolution dated January 21, 1987.
This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.
