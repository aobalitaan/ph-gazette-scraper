Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 150

AUTHORIZING THE INTER-AGENCY TASK FORCE UNDER MEMO ORDER NO. 149, DATED JANUARY 20, 1988, TO CHARGE ITS EXPENSES AGAINST APPROPRIATE FUNDS OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

In order to effectively assist the relatives of the victims and the survivors of the M/V Doña Paz-M/T Vector collision, the Inter-Agency Task Force is hereby authorized to charge its expenses against appropriate funds of the Office of the President subject to the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, January 26, 1988
