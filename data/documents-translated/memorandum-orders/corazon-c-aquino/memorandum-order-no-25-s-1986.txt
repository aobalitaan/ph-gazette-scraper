MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 29

CREATING AN INTER- AGENCY COMMITTEE TO REVIEW
EXISTING SUGAR MILL CAPACITY.

There is hereby created an Inter-Agency Committee to
review the existing sugar mill capacity in relation to the target
production levels to be composed of a representative each from
_ the following government agencies:

1. National Economic and Development Authority
2. Ministry of Finance

3. Ministry of Agriculture and Food

4. Philippine National Bank

5. Sugar Regulatory Administration

The Committee shall enlist the assistance of any govern-
ment agencies concerned to facilitate the accomplishment of its
task.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately. *

By authority of the President:

2

OKER P. ARROYO
Executive Secretary

Manila, July 29, 1986
