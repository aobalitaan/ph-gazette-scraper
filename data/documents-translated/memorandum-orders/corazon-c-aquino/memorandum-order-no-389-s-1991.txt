MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 389

CONSTITUTING A TECHNICAL-SCIENTIFIC COMMITTEE UNDER THE TASK FORCE ON THE REHABILITATION OF AREAS AFFECTED BY THE ERUPTIONS OF MT. PINATUBO CREATED UNDER MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 369, SERIES OF 1991.

WHEREAS, the then Secretary of the Department of National Defense and Chairman of the National Disaster Coordinating Council has submitted an updated report on the eruptions of Mt. Pinatubo, its effects and the relief operations and services being undertaken and rendered in the affected areas; and

WHEREAS, he has recommended the constitution of a Technical-Scientific Committee under the Task Force on the Rehabilitation of Areas Affected by the Eruptions of Mt. Pinatubo created under Memorandum Order No. 369, series of 1991, to immediately undertake studies on mud and lahar flows and to provide immediate and long-range appropriate measures to reduce the hazards of such flows:

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:

1. The constitution of a Technical-Scientific Committee under the Task Force on the Rehabilitation of Areas Affected by the Eruptions of Mt. Pinatubo created under Memorandum Order No. 369 to be composed of the following:

The Committee shall primarily undertake studies on the flows of mud and lahar and recommend immediate and long-range measures to reduce the hazards of such flows and to minimize the damages caused by the same to the affected areas.

2. This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 30th day of August, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
