MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 230, April 10, 1989 ]
APPROVING THE GUIDELINES FOR THE NO-DOLLAR IMPORTATION OF SECOND-HAND MOTOR VEHICLES OF RETURNING RESIDENTS/IMMIGRANTS/QUALIFIED DONEES
Pursuant to the provisions of Executive Order No. 248 dated July 24, 1987 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Investments, the Guidelines for the No-Dollar Importation of Second-Hand Motor Vehicles of Returning Residents/Immigrants/Qualified Donees are hereby approved.
This Memorandum Order shall take effect after (15) days following the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
