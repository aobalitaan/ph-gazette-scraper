BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 33, September 9, 1986 ]
MODIFYING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 27 DATED AUGUST 7, 1986 CREATING AN INDEPENDENT OFFICE OF POLITICAL AFFAIRS.
Memorandum Order No. 27 dated August 7, 1986 is hereby modified to the extent that one position of Deputy Minister is hereby added to the four (4) positions already created in the said Order.
