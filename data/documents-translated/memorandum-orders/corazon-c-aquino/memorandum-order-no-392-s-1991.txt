MALACAÑANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 392

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 369, SERIES OF 1991, ENTITLED “CREATING A TASK FORCE ON THE REHABILITATION OF AREAS AFFECTED BY THE ERUPTION OF MT. PINATUBO AND ITS EFFECTS”

WHEREAS, Memorandum Order No. 369, s. of 1991, created a Task Force to integrate and rationalize the Government’s rehabilitation efforts in view of the extensive damage caused by the eruption of Mt. Pinatubo;

WHEREAS, there is a need for the inclusion of other vital and critical government agencies in the various Committees of said Task Force;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:

SECTION 1. Section 2 of Memorandum Order No. 369, s. of 1991, is hereby amended to include, in addition to those already mentioned, the following agencies as members of the following standing Committees:

SEC 2. Section 9 of Memorandum Order No. 369, s. of 1991, is hereby amended to include the Secretary of Foreign Affairs as among those responsible for generating financial and technical assistance from foreign sources.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
