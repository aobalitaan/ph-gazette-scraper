MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 88

GRANTING SALARY INCREASES FOR THE COURT STENOGRAPHIC REPORTERS OF THE REGIONAL TRIAL COURTS AND OTHER LOWER COURTS OF THE JUDICIARY

A 15% salary increase in basic pay of the court stenographic reporters of the Regional Trial Courts and other lower courts of the Judiciary is hereby authorized, effective March 1, 1987, in lieu of the 5% increase granted under Executive Order No. 153. The adjusted basic monthly salary of court stenographic reporters, incorporating the abovementioned salary increase, shall be as follows:

P1,972 1,786 1,616 1,463 1,463 1,972 1,463

By Authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary

Manila, June 2, 1987
