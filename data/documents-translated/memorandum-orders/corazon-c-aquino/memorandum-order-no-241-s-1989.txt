Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 241

DIRECTING THE DEPARTMENT OF BUDGET AND MANAGEMENT TO PREPARE THE INTERIM ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE, PLANTILLA POSITIONS, AND SALARY SCALES FOR PEOPLE’S TELEVISION 4

To stabilize the operations of People’s Television 4 and ensure the security of tenure of its employees, the Department of Budget and Management is hereby directed to prepare the interim organizational structure, plantilla positions and salary scales for People’s Television 4, in consultation with the Network’s General Manager and the Office of the Press Secretary, and within ninety (90) days from date hereof, to submit a report thereon to the Office of the President for its appropriate action.

Manila, June 7, 1989

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
