MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 218-A, June 22, 1989 ]
EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF IMPORTATION AUTHORIZED UNDER MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 218, SERIES OF 1989
Upon recommendation of the Board of Investments, the period of importation authorized under Memorandum Order No. 218, dated February 10, 1989, is hereby extended from June 30, 1989 to September 30, 1989, subject to such guidelines as may be promulgated by the Board of Investments.
This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.
