Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 117

CREATING A BARTER TRADE INTER-AGENCY COMMITTEE

In order to ensure quick and effective inter-agency coordination in the formulation and implementation of government policies relating to barter trade, there is hereby created a Barter Trade Inter-Agency Committee which shall be composed of representatives from the following departments and agencies of the government:

The Barter Trade Inter-Agency Committee shall have the following duties and responsibilities:

a. To advise the President on matters regarding barter trade;

b. To coordinate the activities of individual offices, agencies and other instrumentalities of the government concerned with barter trade;

c. To review and coordinate government policies relating to barter trade in accordance with the mandate of Memorandum Order No. 24 dated July 29, 1986.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, October 12, 1987
