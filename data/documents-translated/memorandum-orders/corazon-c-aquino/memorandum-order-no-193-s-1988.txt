Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 193

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE TOURISM AUTHORITY TO SPONSOR AND SUPPORT THE STAGING OF THE 3RD PHILIPPINE INTERNATIONAL KARTING PRIX

WHEREAS, the 3rd Philippine International Karting Prix will be held this year;

WHEREAS, this prestigious event will project an image of the Philippines as a capable host country;

IN VIEW THEREOF, the Philippine Tourism Authority is hereby authorized to be a sponsor of and to support the staging of the 3rd Philippine International Karting Prix to be held this year.

The Philippine Tourism Authority may call upon offices and agencies of the government for assistance to insure the successful staging of the event.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, October 4, 1988
