Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 121

CLARIFYING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 118, DATED OCTOBER 15, 1987 PROVIDING FOR THE SIGNING AUTHORITY IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

Memorandum Order No. 118, dated October 15, 1987, is hereby clarified to indicate that the same does not apply to the Presidential Consultant on Military Affairs (OPCMA), the Special Assistant to the President, and officials of the Appointments Office, the Protocol Office, the Correspondence Office and other officials holding office in the Premiere Guest House.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) SAMILO N. BARLONGAY Acting Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, October 19, 1987
