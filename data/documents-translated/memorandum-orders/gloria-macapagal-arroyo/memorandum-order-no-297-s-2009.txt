MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 297

ASSIGNING NUMBER 17 AS (PROTOCOL) LICENSE PLATES TO MOTOR VEHICLES USED BY METROPOLITAN TRIAL COURT (MTC), METROPOLITAN TRIAL COURT IN THE CITIES (MTCC), MUNICIPAL TRIAL COURT (MTC) AND SHA’RIA COURT JUDGES

The Number “17” as (Protocol) License Plates is hereby assigned to Motor Vehicles Used by First Level Court Judges, i.e., Metropolitan Trial Court (MTC), Metropolitan Trial Court in the Cities (MTCC), Municipal Trial Court (MTC), and Sha’ria Court.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately after its publication in a national newspaper of general circulation.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 2 nd day of March in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary
