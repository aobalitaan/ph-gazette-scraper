MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 41

AUTHORIZING THE EXECUTIVE SECRETARY TO SIGN THE PRESIDENTIAL CLEARANCE FOR THE CONDUCT OF ADMINISTRATIVE INVESTIGATION AGAINST SENIOR POLICE OFFICERS WHOSE APPOINTMENTS ARE VESTED WITH THE PRESIDENT

WHEREAS, Section 2.03 of National Police Commission (NAPOLCOM) Memorandum Circular No. 96-010, s. of 1996, states in part that: “Senior Superintendents and higher ranking officers whose appointments are vested in the President of the Philippines shall be investigated in accordance with these rules with prior clearance from the President”;

WHEREAS, the function of signing the presidential clearance for purposes of conducting administrative investigation against senior police officers with the rank of Senior Superintendent may be delegated to the Executive Secretary;

IN VIEW THEREOF, the Executive Secretary is hereby authorized to sign presidential clearance for the conduct of administrative investigation against senior police officers pursuant to NAPOLCOM Memorandum Circular No. 96-010, s. of 1996.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of November, in the year of our Lord, two thousand and one.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:

Executive Secretary
