MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 39

ADOPTING THE GUIDELINES FOR THE AUGMENTATION PAY
OF BUREAU OF IMMIGRATION (BI) PERSONNEL UNDER
MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 24 (S. 2018) AS GUIDELINES FOR
BI SPECIAL PROVISION NO. 1 OF THE GENERAL
APPROPRIATIONS ACT FOR FISCAL YEAR 2019

WHEREAS, MO No. 24 (s. 2018), which provides for the implementing
guidelines for BI Special Provision No. 1 of the Fiscal Year (FY) 2018 General
Appropriations Act (GAA) as regards the use of BI express lane fees and
charges to augment the salaries of BI personnel, has expired on 31 December
2018;

WHEREAS, Congress has yet to pass an Immigration Modernization
Law, which upgrades the current compensation system of the BI;

WHEREAS, there is a need for an interim measure to augment the
salaries of BI employees, in recognition of the indispensability of their functions,
the apparent disparity between the basic pay of BI employees and those of
agencies performing comparable functions, and to promote the continuous and
unhampered delivery of basic government services;

WHEREAS, pursuant to BI Special Provision No. 1, “Immigration Fees
and Collections,” under Republic Act No. 10964 or the General Appropriations
Act for FY 2019 (FY 2019 GAA), the express lane fees and charges to be
collected by the BI may be used to augment the salaries of BI personnel; and

WHEREAS, Part III(C), Par. 1 of the President's Veto Message on the
FY 2019 GAA provides that the implementation of BI Special Provision No. 1
shall be subject to the provisions of Memorandum Order (MO) No. 24 (s. 2018)
and subsequent issuances for the purpose;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:

1. The guidelines for the augmentation pay of BI personnel under
Section 1 of MO No. 24 are hereby adopted as guidelines for Bl
Special Provision No. 1, “Immigration Fees and Collections,” under
the FY 2019 GAA; and

2. This Order shall be effective from 01 January 2019 until 31
December 2019, or until the passage of an _ Immigration

Modernization Law, or a law which would increase the base pay
rates of BI personnel, whichever comes first.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this27thday of September _, in the year

of our Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
