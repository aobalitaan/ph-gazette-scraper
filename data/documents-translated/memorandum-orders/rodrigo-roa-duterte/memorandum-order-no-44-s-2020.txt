MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 44

CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE DEPUTATION OF LAW
ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE
GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE
AND THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES, IN CONNECTION
WITH THE 22 FEBRUARY 2020 PLEBISCITE TO RATIFY REPUBLIC
ACT NO. 11328

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), through its Resolution
No. 10646 (s. 2020), resolved to request for the concurrence of the President in the
deputation of law enforcement agencies and instrumentaities of the Government,
including the Philippine National Police (PNP) and the Armed Forces of the Philippines’
(AFP), in connection with the 22 February 2020 plebiscite to ratify Republic Act (RA)
No. 11328;

WHEREAS, RA No. 11328 provides for the creation of Barangay Lacnog West
through the separation of the sitios of Guina-ang, Madopdop, Mallango, Lanlana, and
San Pablo from Barangay Lacnog, City of Tabuk, Kalinga; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 2 (4), Article IX-C of the Constitution, the
COMELEC is empowered to deputize, with the concurrence of the President, law
enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the Government, including the AFP, for
the exclusive purpose of ensuring free, orderly, honest, peacsful, and credible elections;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, '’esident of the Philippines,
by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, hereby
concur with COMELEC Resolution No. 10646, deputizing the AFP and law enforcement
agencies and instrumentalities of the Government, in connection with the 22 February
2020 plebiscite.

The foregoing law enforcement agencies and other concerned agencies are
hereby directed to coordinate and cooperate with the COM*..EC in the performance of
their duties and functions.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 21st day of February , in the
year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
