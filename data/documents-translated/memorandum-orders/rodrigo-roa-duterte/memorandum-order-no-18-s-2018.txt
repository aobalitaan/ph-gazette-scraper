MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
MEMORANDUM ORDER NO.18

CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE DEPUTATION OF THE ARMED
FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES, THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE
AND OTHER LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE GOVERNMENT FOR THE 05 JANUARY
2018 PLEBISCITE TO RATIFY THE DIVISION OF VARIOUS BARANGAYS
IN THE CITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), through its
Resolution No. 10235 (s. 2017), resolved to request the concurrence of the
President in the deputation of the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP), the
Philippine National Police (PNP) and other law enforcement agencies and
instrumentalities of the Government, in connection with the 05 January 2018
plebiscite to ratify the following:

1. Division of Barangay North Bay Boulevard South (NBBS), City of Navoias into
three (3) distinct and independent barangays io be known as Barangay NBBS
Proper, NBBS Kauniaran and NBBS Dagat-Dagatan;

2. Division of Barangay Tangos, City of Navotas into two (2) distinct and
independent barangays to be known as Barangays Tangos North and Tangos
South; and

3. Division of Barangay Tanza, City of Navotas into two (2) distinct and
independent barangays io be known as Barangays Tanza 1 and Tanza 2.

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 10933 provides for the division of the
Barangay NBBS in the City of Navoias;

WHEREAS, RA No. 10934 provides for the civision of Barangay Tangos in
the City of Navotas;

WHEREAS, RA No. 10935 provides for the division of Barangay Tanza in the
City of Navotas; and

 

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 2 (4), Article IX-C of the Constitution, the
COMELEC is empowered io deputize, with the concurrence of the President, law
enforcement agencies and insirumentalities of the Governmeni, including the AFP,

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

for the exclusive purpose of ensuring free, orderly, honest, peaceful, and credible
elections.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, hereby concur with COMELEC Resolution No. 10235, deputizing the AFP, the
PNP and other law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the Government,
in connection with the 05 January 2018 plebiscite to ratify the division of various
barangays in the City of Navotas.

The foregoing law enforcement agencies and other concerned agencies are
hereby directed to coordinate and cooperate with the COMELEC in the performance
of their duties and functions.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January , in the year
of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
