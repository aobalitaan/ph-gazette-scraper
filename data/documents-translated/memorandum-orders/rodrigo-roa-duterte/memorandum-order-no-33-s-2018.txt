MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 33

Pursuant to the applicable provisions under the Revised Omnibus Rules on
Appointments and Other Human Resource Actions, the OP Selection and
Promotions Board (OPSPB) for 1% and 2 Level Career Positions, created under
Memorandum Order No. 248, s. 1989, as amended, is hereby renamed as Human
Resource Merit Promotion and Selection Board (OP-HRMPSB). The said Board shall
be constituted as follows: '

HRMPSB for 1° and 2" Level Career Positions

Chairperson - The Deputy Executive Secretary for Finance and Administration
Vice-Chairperson - The Deputy Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs
Members - The Deputy Executive Secretary for General Administration

- The Head, Human Resource Management Office
- The Head, Legal Office
- One (1) Representative each from:
a. 1° Level Positions
b. 2" Level Positions
- Head of Office where the vacancy exists

The OP Special Board for 3 Level Career Positions, created under
Memorandum Order No. 46, s. 1992, as amended, shall still be constituted as
follows:

OPSB for 3% Level Career Positions

Chairperson - The Executive Secretary or his duly authorized representative
Vice-Chairperson - The Deputy Executive Secretary for Finance and Administration
Members - The Deputy Executive Secretary for Internal Audit

- The Head, Human Resource Management Office

- The Head, Legal Office

- One (1) Representative from the OP Career Executive Service
Officers Association, Inc.

- Head of Office where the vacancy exists

The Human Resource Management Office shall serve as secretariat to the
above-mentioned Boards.

This supersedes Memorandum Order No. 05, dated 26 September 2016.
