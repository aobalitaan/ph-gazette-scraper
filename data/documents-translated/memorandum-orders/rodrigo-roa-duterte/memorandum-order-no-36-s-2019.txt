MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 36

CONSTITUTING THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (OP) PROPER DRUG-FREE
WORKPLACE COMMITTEE

Pursuant to Sec. 4, Memorandum Circular No. 89, dated 17 December 2015, which
requires government offices to formulate and adopt drug-free workplace programs and
authorized drug testing among its human resources, and Board Regulation No. 2 (s. 2009) of the
Dangerous Drugs Board, the OP Proper Drug-Free Workplace Committee (OPDFWC) is hereby
constituted as follows:

Chair - Representative from the Office of the Executive Secretary

Members - Representative from the Office of the Deputy Executive Secretary for
Finance and Administration
Representative from the Office of the Deputy Executive Secretary for
Legal Affairs
Representative from the Human Resource Management Office
Representative from the Finance Office
1st and 2° Level Representatives from the Office of the President
Employees Network (OPEN)
Head Doctor of the OP Clinic

The OPDFWC shall have the following responsibilities:

1. Oversee the formulation and implementation of the drug-free workplace policy in OP
Proper;

2. Initiate training programs for supervisors in pursuit of the above policy;

3. Initiate continuing employee education and awareness programs on dangerous drugs;

4. Initiate and adopt values formation, family enhancement and such other related and
relevant programs; and

5. Recommend internal rules pertaining to restrictions in reporting to work while
undergoing treatment or intervention.

The Human Resource Management Office shall provide secretarial and administrative
support to the Committee.
