MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 14

RECONSTITUTION OF THE INVENTORY COMMITTEE
IN THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT

Pursuant to Section 490 of the Government Accounting and Auditing Manual
(GAAM) Volume |, the Office of the President Inventory Committee (OPIC) is hereby
reconstituted as follows:

Chair - Head, Assets Management Office (AMO)

Vice Chair - Head, Finance Office (FO)

Members - Chief, Supply and Property Management Division - AMO
Chief, Accounting Division — FO
Representative from Malacafiang Motorpool Office

Representative from Information
and Communications Technology Office

Representative from Malacafiang Engineering Office

A representative from the Commission on Audit shall be requested to witness the
inventory-taking.

The Committee shall be in charge of the annual and semi-annual physical
inventory-taking of all supplies, materials, equipment and buildings of the Office of the
President, in accordance with Sections 490 and 491 of GAAM Volume |.

The AMO Inventory Section shall assist the OPIC in handling all its technical and
administrative concerns, as well as safekeeping and systematic filing of its documents
and records.

All units in the Office of the President are hereby enjoined to cooperate with the
Committee in its mandated duties and responsibilities.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By Authority of the President:
