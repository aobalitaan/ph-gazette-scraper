MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 20

CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE DEPUTATION OF THE ARMED
FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES, THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE
AND OTHER LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE GOVERNMENT FOR THE VARIOUS
PLEBISCITES INVOLVING DIFFERENT LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), through its
Resolution No. 10255 (s. 2018), resolved to request the concurrence of the
President in the deputation of the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP), the
Philippine National Police (PNP) and other law enforcement agencies and
instrumentalities of the Government, in connection with the following Plebiscites:

1. 24 March 2018 Plebiscite in the entire Municipality of Dumalneg, Province of
Ilocos Norte;

2. 24 March 2018 Plebiscite in Barangay Amduntog, Municipality of Asipulo,
Province of Ifugao; and

3. 07 April 2018 Plebiscite in the Municipality of Bumbaran, Province of Lanao
del Sur.

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 10955 provides for the division of
Barangay Dumalneg in the Municipality of Dumalneg, Province of Ilocos Norte;

WHEREAS, RA No. 10957 provides for the creation of a Barangay in the
Municipality of Asipulo, Province of Ifugao;

WHEREAS, Muslim Mindanao Autonomy Act No. 316 provides for the change
of name of the Municipality of Bumbaran into Municipality of Amai Manabilang in the
Province of Lanao Del Sur; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 2 (4), Article IX-C of the Constitution, the
COMELEC is empowered to deputize, with the concurrence of the President, law
enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the Government, including the AFP,
for the exclusive purpose of ensuring free, orderly, honest, peaceful, and credible
elections.

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, hereby concur with COMELEC Resolution No. 10255, deputizing the AFP, the
PNP and other law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the Government,
in connection with the abovementioned Plebiscites.

The foregoing law enforcement agencies and other concerned agencies are
hereby directed to coordinate and cooperate with the COMELEC in the performance
of their duties and functions.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of March , in the year
of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
