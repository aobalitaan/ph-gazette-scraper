MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 21

CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE DEPUTATION OF LAW ENFORCEMENT
AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING
THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES, FOR THE 14 MAY 2018
SYNCHRONIZED BARANGAY AND SANGGUNIANG KABATAAN ELECTIONS

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), through its Resolution No.
10207 (s. 2017), resolved to request the concurrence of the President in the deputation of
law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the Government, including the Armed
Forces of the Philippines (AFP), in connection with the 23 October 2017 Synchronized
Barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan (Sk) Elections;

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 10952 provides for the postponement of the 23
October 2017 Elections and the rescheduling of the said Elections to the second Monday of
May 2018;

WHEREAS, on 20 March 2018, the COMELEC En Banc decided to adopt
COMELEC Resolution No. 10207 for purposes of the 14 May 2018 Barangay and SK
Elections; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 2 (4), Article IX-C of the Constitution, the COMELEC
is empowered to deputize, with the concurrence of the President, law enforcement agencies
and instrumentalities of the Government, including the AFP, for the exclusive purpose of
ensuring free, orderly, honest, peaceful, and credible elections.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Philippines, by
virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, hereby concur with
COMELEC Resolution No. 10207, deputizing the law enforcement agencies and
instrumentalities of the Government, including the AFP, in connection with the 14 May 2018
Synchronized Barangay and SK Elections.

The foregoing law enforcement agencies and other concerned agencies are hereby
directed to coordinate and cooperate with the COMELEC in the performance of their duties
and functions.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 13th day of April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
