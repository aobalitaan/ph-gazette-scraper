MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 09

DESIGNATING THE SECRETARY OF ENERGY AND THE SECRETARY
OF INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY AS
MEMBERS OF THE ASEAN NATIONAL ORGANIZING COMMITTEE

WHEREAS, pursuant to Article 31, Chapter X of the ASEAN Charter, the
Philippines has assumed the Chairmanship of the ASEAN Summit for 2017;

WHEREAS, Administrative Order (AO) No. 48 (s.2015) created the ASEAN-
NOC and tasked it, among others, to perform all acts that will ensure the success of
the hosting;

WHEREAS, the Secretary of Transportation is a member of the ASEAN-NOC
tasked to chair the Committee on Transport and Communications which shall ensure
the availability, efficiency, and security, in coordination with the Security Committee,
of all transportation and telecommunications requirements for the ASEAN 2017
hosting, and facilitate the safe and secure movement of persons, cargo, and
equipment in all the activities of the ASEAN 2017 hosting;

WHEREAS, AO No. 48 created the Infrastructure Committee which shall direct
the overall maintenance of all ASEAN Conference facilities and critical infrastructure,
including the availability of energy services;

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 10844 renamed the Department of
Transportation and Communications into the Department of Transportation (DOTr),
and created the Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT),
which has authority to formulate, recommend and implement national policies, plans,
programs and guidelines that will promote the development and use of information and
communication technology;

WHEREAS, Section 27, Chapter 9, Title III, Book Ill of Executive Order No.
292, otherwise known as the “Administrative Code of 1987,” empowers the
Executive Secretary to implement presidential directives, orders and decisions.

NOW THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, by authority and order
of the President, do hereby declare the following:

SECTION 1. The DICT Secretary and the DOE Secretary shall be members
of the ASEAN-NOC. The DICT Secretary shall sit as the co-chairperson of the
Committee on Transport and Communications and the DOE Secretary shall be a
member of the Infrastructure Committee.

SECTION 2. Effectivity - This Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof December _ in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
