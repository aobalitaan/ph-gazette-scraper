BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 19

CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE DEPUTATION OF THE ARMED
FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES, THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE
AND OTHER LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE GOVERNMENT FOR THE 05 JANUARY
2018 PLEBISCITE TO RATIFY THE CREATION OF BARANGAY NAIBUAN
IN SAN JOSE, OCCIDENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), through its
Resolution No. 10232 (s. 2017), resolved to request the concurrence of the
President in the deputation of the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP), the
Philippine National Police (PNP) and other law enforcement agencies and
instrumentalities of the Government, in connection with the 05 January 2018
plebiscite to ratify the creation of Barangay Naibuan in the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Occidental Mindoro;

WHEREAS, Barangay Naibuan shall consist of Sitios Lagnas, Naibuan,
Ibanag, Hobkob, Abong, Malumak, Kianay, Libertad, Lowaliw, Ilya, Yabat, Salidang,
Upper Koywan, Dayaga and Timbangan, which shall be taken out of Barangay
Batasan in the Municipality of San Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro;

WHEREAS, Ordinance No. 33 (s. 2009) of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of
Occidental Mindoro approved the separation of Sitio Naibuan from Barangay
Batasan of San Jose, Occidental Mindoro;

WHEREAS, Sangguning Bayan of San Jose, Occidental Mindoro issued
Resolution No. 4203 (s. 2017) which provides the fifteen (15) sitios that will comprise
Barangay Naibuan; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 2 (4), Article IX-C of the Constitution, the
COMELEC is empowered to deputize, with the concurrence of the President, law
enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the Government, including the AFP,
for the exclusive purpose of ensuring free, orderly, honest, peaceful, and credible
elections.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, hereby concur with COMELEC Resolution No. 10232, deputizing the AFP, the
PNP and other law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the Government,

in connection with the 05 January 2018 Plebiscite to ratify the creation of Barangay
Naibuan in the Municipality of San Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro.

The foregoing law enforcement agencies and other concerned agencies are
hereby directed to coordinate and cooperate with the COMELEC in the performance
of their duties and functions.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January , in the year
