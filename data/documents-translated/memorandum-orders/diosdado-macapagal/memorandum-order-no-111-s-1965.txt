MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 111

CREATING A STAFF FOR THE MUNICIPAL MAYORS AND BARRIO CAPTAINS DAY IN THE EXECUTIVE OFFICE.

There is hereby created a Staff for the Municipal Mayors and Barrio Captains Day in the Executive Office which shall assist the President on the occasion of Municipal Mayors and Barrio Captains Day. It shall be composed of the following:

Technical Assistant

Actg. Cabinet Secretary

Pres. Asst. on Public Works

Pres. Staff Assistant

Pres. Staff Assistant

Technical Assistant

Technical Assistant

Technical Assistant

Technical Assistant

Head Exec. Asst., SWA

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Secretary of Justice Concurrently Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, January 12, 1965
