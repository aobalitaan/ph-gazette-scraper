AUTHORIZING ACTING ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE SECRETAY JUAN S. CANCIO TO SIGN FOR AND IN THE ABSENCE OF THE EXECUTIVE SECRETARY.
In my absence, all matters usually acted upon by myself, such as PAF plane approvals and other similarly urgent matters, should be referred to Atty. Juan S. Cancio, Acting Assistant Executive Secretary, who will act upon them as per policy and practice.
