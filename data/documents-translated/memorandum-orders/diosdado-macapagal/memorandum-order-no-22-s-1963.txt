MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 22

AUTHORIZING MR. DIOSDADO DEL ROSARIO TECHNICAL ASSISTANT IN THE FINANCE DIVISION, TO SIGN ALL TREASURY WARRANTS AND CHECKS IN THE AMOUNTS NOT EXCEEDING P5,000.00 ISSUED BY THIS OFFICE FOR PAYMENT AT ANY OFFICIAL DEPOSITORY OF THE FUNDS OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.

Effective immediately and until further advice, all treasury warrants and checks in the amounts not exceeding P5,000.00 issued by his Office for payment at any official depository of the funds of the Office of the President shall be prepared for the signature of Mr. Dioscoro del Rosario, Technical Assistant, Finance Division.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Executive Secretary

Manila, March 12, 1963
