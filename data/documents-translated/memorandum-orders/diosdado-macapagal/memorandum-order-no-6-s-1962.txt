MALACAÑAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 6, April 12, 1962 ]
REQUIRING IMPLEMENTATION OF DECISIONS MADE BY THE PRESIDENT, THE EXECUTIVE SECRETARY, OR ANY OF THE ASSISTANT EXECUTIVE SECRETARIES WITHIN TWENTY-FOUR HOURS.
It is hereby directed that the official or employee concerned shall, within 24 hours, take action or such steps as may be necessary towards the implementation of the decisions and directives made or issued by the President, the Executive Secretary, or any of the Assistant Executive Secretaries.
Strict compliance herewith is enjoined.
