BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
[ Memorandum Order No. 68, January 31, 1964 ]
CREATING A COMMITTEE ON PHYSICAL EXAMINATION FOR THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT.
Pursuant to Memorandum Circular No. 44 of this Office dated September 25, 1963, as amended, providing for the creation of a Committee on Physical examination in each department, there is hereby created a Committee on Physical Examination for the Office of the President composed of the following:
Dr. Francisco Casanova
Chairman
Dr. Gregorio Mateo
Member
Dr. Hermogenes Lorenzo
Member
This Order shall take effect immediately.
