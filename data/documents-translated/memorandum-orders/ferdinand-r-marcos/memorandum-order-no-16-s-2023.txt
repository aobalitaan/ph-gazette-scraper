MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. _ 16

RECONSTITUTING THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT (OP) HUMAN RESOURCE
MERIT PROMOTION AND SELECTION BOARD (HRMPSB)
FOR FIRST AND SECOND LEVEL CAREER POSITIONS

The OP Human Resource Merit Promotion and Selection Board, renamed and
constituted under Memorandum Order No. 33, s. 2018, is hereby reconstituted as follows:

Chairperson - The Deputy Executive Secretary for Finance and Administration
Vice-Chairperson - The Deputy Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs
Member - The Deputy Executive Secretary for General Administration

- Two (2) Representatives from the Office of the Executive Secretary
- The Head, Human Resource Management Office
- One (1) Representative each from the OP Employees’ Network for:
a. 1%tLevel Positions
b. 24 Level Positions
- Head of Office where the vacancy exists

The Human Resource Management Office shall serve as secretariat to the OP-
HRMPSB.

This supersedes all previous Memorandum Orders issued on the constitution of the
above-mentioned Board.

By authority of the President:
