MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 14

CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE DEPUTATION OF LAW
ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE
GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE AND
THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES, INCONNECTION WITH THE
29 JULY 2023 PLEBISCITE TO RATIFY THE MERGING OF VARIOUS
BARANGAYS AND RENAMING OF THE MERGED BARANGAYS AND
OTHER AFFECTED BARANGAYS IN THE CITY OF BACOOR, CAVITE

WHEREAS, City Ordinance No. 275-2023 (s. 2023) of the Sangguniang Panlungsod
of Bacoor provides for the merging of various barangays and renaming of the merged
barangays and other affected barangays in the City of Bacoor, Cavite;

WHEREAS, the Commission on Election (COMELEC), through its Resolution No.
10920 (s. 2023), resolved to request the concurrence of the President in the deputation of
law enforcement agencies and other instrumentalities of the Government, including the
Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP), in connection with the 29 July 2023 Plebiscite to ratify
the merging of various barangays in the City of Bacoor, Cavite; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 2 (4), Article IX-C of the Constitution, the COMELEC
is empowered to deputize, with the concurrence of the President, law enforcement agencies
and instrumentalities of the Government, including the AFP, for the exclusive purpose of
ensuring free, orderly, honest, peaceful, and credible elections;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the Philippines,
by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, hereby concur
with COMELEC Resolution No. 10920 (s. 2023), deputizing the AFP, the Philippine National
Police, and other law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the Government, in
connection with the 29 July 2023 Plebiscite to ratify the merging of various barangays and
renaming of the merged barangays and other affected barangays in the City of Bacoor,
Cavite.

The foregoing law enforcement agencies and other concerned agencies are hereby
directed to coordinate and cooperate with the COMELEC in the performance of their duties
and functions.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately. °

DONE, in the City of Manila, this17th day of July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.
