MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM ORDERNO. 27

CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE DEPUTATION OF LAW
ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE
GOVERNMENT IN CONNECTION WITH THE 13 JULY 2024
PLEBISCITE TO RATIFY THE CREATION OF BARANGAY
JUAN-LORETO TAMAYO IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUPI, SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 20 February 2023, the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of South
Cotabato passed Resolution No. 158 (s. 2023) entitled, “A Resolution Enacting
Ordinance No. 14 (s. 2023)’;

WHEREAS, Ordinance No. 14 of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of South
Cotabato creates the new barangay of Juan-Loreto Tamayo in the Municipality of Tupi,
South Cotabato;

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) En Banc, through
Resolution No. 11002 (s. 2024), resolved to request Presidential concurrence in the
deputation of law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the government for
the 13 July 2024 plebiscite to ratify the creation of Barangay Juan-Loreto Tamayo;

WHEREAS, Section 2(4), Article IX-C of the Constitution empowers the
COMELEC to deputize law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the
government to ensure free, orderly, honest, peaceful, and credible elections, subject
to the concurrence of the President;

WHEREAS, Section 18, Article VII of the Constitution provides that the
President shall be the Commander-in-Chief of all armed forces of the Philippines; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all executive departments, bureaus, and offices;

NOW, THEREFORE, |!, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
and as requested in COMELEC Resolution No. 11002, hereby CONCUR with the
COMELEC’s deputation of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, Philippine National
Police, and other law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the government

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

in connection with the 13 July 2024 plebiscite to ratify the creation of Barangay
Juan-Loreto Tamayo in the Municipality of Tupi, South Cotabato.

The foregoing law enforcement agencies and other concerned agencies are
hereby directed to coordinate and cooperate with the COMELEC in the performance
of their duties and functions, subject to existing laws, rules and regulations.
