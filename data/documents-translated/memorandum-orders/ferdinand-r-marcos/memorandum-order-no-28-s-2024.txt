MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 28

APPROVING THE PRIVATIZATION OF UNITED COCONUT
PLANTERS BANK - SAVINGS BANK (UCPB-SB)

WHEREAS, Executive Order (EO) No. 142 (s. 2021) was issued, approving the
merger between United Coconut Planters Bank (UCPB) and Land Bank of the
Philippines (LBP), with LBP as the surviving entity;

WHEREAS, the said merger between UCPB and LBP transferred all assets
and liabilities of UCPB to LBP, including its ownership shares in UCPB-SB, thus, by
reason of the said merger, UCPB-SB has become a subsidiary of LBP;

WHEREAS, Section 5(a) of Republic Act (RA) No. 10149, otherwise known as
the “GOCC Governance Act of 2011,” empowers the Governance Commission for
GOCCs (GCG), in consultation with the department agency to which a GOCC is
attached, to determine whether a GOCC should be reorganized, merged, streamlined,
abolished or privatized;

WHEREAS, the GCG finds the privatization of UCPB-SB to be in the best
interest of the State to eliminate unnecessary overlap in the mandates and functions
of UCPB-SB and LBP;

WHEREAS, UCPB-SB is no longer achieving its expected objectives and
purpose as a GOCC;

WHEREAS, to address the challenges on the operating expenses and revenue
growth of UCPB-SB, the GCG has determined that the operations of UCPB-SB is best
carried out by the private sector;

WHEREAS, the privatization of UCPB-SB will: (i) provide LBP additional
capital; (ii) improve its financial ratios; and (iii) transform its non-tradeable equity
investment into cash which could be used towards addressing the more critical funding
requirements of the bank;

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 5(a)(3) of RA No. 10149, the GCG, in
consultation with the Department of Finance, recommended the privatization of UCPB-
SB through the sale of LBP’s 97.55% ownership shares in UCPB-SB;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:
SECTION 1. Privatization of UCPB-SB. The privatization of UCPB-SB is

hereby approved. Hence, LBP shall dispose of all its shares of stock in UCPB-SB
through sale to any qualified private person, natural or juridical, subject to pertinent

 

laws, rules and regulations, guidelines, and requirements of concerned government
agencies.

SECTION 2. Compliance with Relevant Laws. The LBP and GCG are
hereby directed to ensure that the Privatization of UCPB-SB complies with the
following laws, rules and regulations, among others:

a. RA No. 11232 or the “Revised Corporation Code of the Philippines,” on the
appraisal rights of minority stockholders;

b. RA No. 7653, as amended by RA No. 11211 or the “New Central Bank Act, on
securing the prior approval of the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas;”

c. RA No. 10667 or the “Philippine Competition Act,” on compulsory notification for
mergers and acquisitions, as may be applicable; and

d. Such other applicable laws, rules, and regulations.

SECTION 3. Separability. If any provision of this Order is declared invalid or
unconstitutional, the other provisions not affected thereby shall continue to be valid
and subsisting.

SECTION 4. Repeal. All orders, rules and regulations, issuances or any part
thereof, which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Order, are thereby repealed
or modified accordingly.

SECTION 5. Effectivity. The Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 06 dayof August , in the year
of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.
