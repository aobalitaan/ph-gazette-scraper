MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 34

CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE DEPUTATION OF
LAW ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES
OF THE GOVERNMENT IN CONNECTION WITH THE
12 MAY 2025 NATIONAL AND LOCAL ELECTIONS

WHEREAS, Section 8 of Article VI of the Constitution provides that the
regular election of Senators and Members of the House of Representatives shall
be held on the second Monday of May;

WHEREAS, Section 42 of Republic Act (RA) No. 7160 or the “Local
Government Code of 1991,” as amended, states that the elections for local officials
shall be held every three (3) years on the second Monday of May;

WHEREAS, Section 4 of RA No. 7056 provides that the first regular election
of twelve (12) Senators, all Members of the House of Representatives, and the
elective provincial, city, and municipal officials shall be held on the second Monday
of May 1995, and on the same day for every period of three (3) years thereafter;

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) En Banc, through
Resolution No. 11095 (s. 2025), resolved to request the President's concurrence in
the deputation of law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the
government for the 12 May 2025 National and Local Elections (NLE);

WHEREAS, Section 2(4), Article IX-C of the Constitution empowers the
COMELEC to deputize law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the
government to ensure free, orderly, honest, peaceful, and credible elections,
subject to the concurrence of the President;

WHEREAS, Section 18, Article VII of the Constitution provides that the
President shall be the Commander-in-Chief of all armed forces of the Philippines;
and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President
the power of control over all executive departments, bureaus, and offices;

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

NOW, THEREFORE, |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, and as requested in COMELEC Resolution No. 11095, hereby CONCUR with
the COMELEC’s deputation of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, Philippine
National Police, and other law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the
government in connection with the conduct of the 12 May 2025 NLE.

Consistent with their respective mandates, the foregoing law enforcement
agencies and other concerned agencies are hereby directed to coordinate and
cooperate with the COMELEC in the performance of their duties and functions,
subject to existing laws, rules and regulations.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 21st dayof Match , in the
year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.
