MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 11

REVERTING THE ADMINISTRATION AND MANAGEMENT OF THE
SOCIO-CIVIC PROJECTS FUND TO THE OFFICE OF THE EXECUTIVE
SECRETARY

Taking into account Section 6 of Executive Order No. 1 (s. 2022) which provides
that the Office of the Special Assistant to the President and the Presidential Management
Staff shall provide support and assistance to the Executive Secretary in the overall
management of the development process, the administration and management of the
Socio-Civic Projects Fund (SCPF) established in accordance with Section 7(a) of
Presidential Decree No. 1869 (s. 1983), is hereby reverted to the Office of the Executive
Secretary.

For this purpose, the Office of the Executive Secretary may, among others, enter
into agreements, and disburse funds from the SCPF, in accordance with relevant laws,
rules and regulations.

All relevant orders, rules and regulations, including Office Order No. 02 (s. 2016),
are hereby amended accordingly. Unaffected provisions of Office Order No. 02 shall
remain in force and effect.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 16th dayof November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Two.

By authority of the President:

MALACANANG RECORDS OFFICE
