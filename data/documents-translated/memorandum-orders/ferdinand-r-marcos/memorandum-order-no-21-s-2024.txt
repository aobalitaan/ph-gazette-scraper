MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 21

CONCURRENCE OF THE PRESIDENT IN THE DEPUTATION OF LAW
ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE
GOVERNMENT IN CONNECTION WITH THE 9 MARCH 2024
PLEBISCITE TO RATIFY THE CREATION OF BARANGAYS SULTAN
COROBONG, SULTAN PANOROGANAN, AND ANGOYAO IN THE
CITY OF MARAWI, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of the City of Marawi, Province of
Lanao del Norte approved City Ordinance Nos. 18-11, 19-11 and 20-11 (s. 2023)
creating Barangays Sultan Corobong, Sultan Panoroganan, and Angoyao,
respectively;

WHEREAS, on 5 February 2024, the Commission on Elections (COMELEC)
En Banc, through Resolution No. 10973, resolved to request Presidential concurrence
in the deputation of the Armed Forces of the Philippines (AFP), Philippine National
Police (PNP), and other law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the
government for the 9 March 2024 plebiscite to ratify the creation of the
abovementioned three (3) barangays;

WHEREAS, Section 2(4), Article IX-C of the Constitution empowers the
COMELEC to deputize law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the
government, such as the AFP and PNP, to ensure free, orderly, honest, peaceful, and
credible elections, subject to the concurrence of the President;

WHEREAS, Section 18, Article Vil of the Constitution provides that the
President shall be the Commander-in-Chief of all armed forces of the Philippines; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all executive departments, bureaus, and offices

NOW, THEREFORE, |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
hereby CONCUR with COMELEC Resolution No. 10973, deputizing the AFP, PNP,
and other law enforcement agencies and instrumentalities of the government in
connection with the 9 March 2024 plebiscite to ratify the creation of Barangays Sultan

Corobong, Sultan Panoroganan, and Angoyao in the City of Marawi, Province of
Lanao del Norte.

The foregoing law enforcement agencies and other concerned agencies are

of their duties and functions, subject to existing laws, rules and regulations.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 1st dayof March ,in
the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.
