MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 06

AUTHORIZING THE IMPLEMENTATION OF GENDER SENSITIVITY TRAINING
PROGRAM AND RELATED ACTIVITIES FOR THE HUMAN RESOURCES
OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT PROPER

Consistent with the provisions of applicable laws, issuances, rules,
regulations, and international commitments, all OP Proper human resources are
hereby directed to participate in the Gender Sensitivity Training Program that will be
held starting October 2022, in an appropriate venue to be determined by the Office
of the President — Gender and Development Focal Point System (OP-GFPS), as well
as in all succeeding Gender and Development related activities that will be
developed and prepared thereafter.

The OP-GFPS, in coordination with the Office of the Deputy Executive
Secretary for Finance and Administration (ODESFA), is hereby directed to
strategically schedule the participation of all OP Proper human resources in the said
Program so as not to disrupt service delivery and the usual office routines.

The corresponding implementation procedure and cost per activity shall be
separately prepared, approved, and/or issued by the undersigned, or by the DESFA
through a Project/Activity Design and/or a Special Order.

This Memorandum Order shail take effect immediately.

 

Manila, 07 September 2022
