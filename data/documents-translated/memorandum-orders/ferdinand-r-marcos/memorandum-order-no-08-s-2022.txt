AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 06, SERIES 2016, TO FURTHER
RECONSTITUTE THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT CULTURAL AFFAIRS
COMMITTEE

Memorandum Order No. 6, dated 13 October 2016, which reconstituted the
Office of the President Cultural Affairs Committee (OPCAC) that was created by
virtue of Special Order No. 522, dated 20 August 1999, is hereby amended so as to
further reconstitute the OPCAC as follows:

Designation Designee
Chairperson : Deputy Executive Secretary for Finance and Administration
Vice-Chairperson : Social Secretary
Members : Head, Human Resource Management Office

Head, Finance Office

Head, Assets Management Office

Head, Engineering Office

Head, Malacahang Records Office

Head, Malacahang Museum

Head, Procurement Management Service
Representative from the Protocol Office

President, Office of the President Employees’ Network

The OPCAC is hereby directed to organize and plan an annual program of
cultural activities in the OP to highlight the Filipino identity as well as to sharpen our
sense of nationalism and patriotism. The said cultural activities include the annual
OP summer and Christmas activities, among other traditional and cultural activities
that may be organized and planned by the OPCAC for implementation. Funds for the
purpose shall be made chargeable against the funds of the OP, subject to its
availability, and to pertinent government budgeting, procurement, accounting, and
auditing laws, issuances, and rules and regulations.

The Office of the DESFA-Technical Service Office and the Human Resource
Management Office shall jointly provide secretariat and administrative support
services to the OPCAC.

This Memorandum Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:
