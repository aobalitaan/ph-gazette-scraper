[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8302]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE REBOKON NATIONALIZED BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY REBOKON, MUNICIPALITY OF DUMALINAO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, INTO A NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE REBOKON AGRICULTURAL AND VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Rebokon Nationalized Barangay High School in Barangay Rebokon, Municipality of Dumalinao, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is hereby converted into an agricultural school to be known as the Rebokon Agricultural and Vocational High School.

SEC. 2. The School shall, in addition to its present secondary curriculum, offer agricultural, technical and other vocational subjects.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue the necessary rules and regulations for the conversion and operation of the school, and for the effective implementation of this Act.

SEC. 4. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the Rebokon Nationalized Barangay High School are transferred to and absorbed by the Rebokon Agricultural and Vocational High School.

SEC. 5. The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged against the current year’s appropriations of the Rebokon National High School. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the Rebokon Agricultural and Vocational High School shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 6. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 21, 1996 and March 17, 1997, respectively.

(Sgd.) LORENZO E. LEYNES, JR. Secretary of the Senate
