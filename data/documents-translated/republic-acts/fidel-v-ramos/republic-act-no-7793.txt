H. No. 1993

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7793]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE IPILAN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE IPILAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE’S POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Ipilan Barangay High School is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the Ipilan National High School.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 1, 1994 and April 28, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law without the signature of the President on August 8, 1994, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
