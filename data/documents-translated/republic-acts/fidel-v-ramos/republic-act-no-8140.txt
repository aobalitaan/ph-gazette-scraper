Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8140]

AN ACT CREATING THE GOGON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DONSOL, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, AND PROVIDING FUNDS THEREFOR.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a national high school in the Barangay Gogon, Municipality of Donsol, Province of Sorsogon, under the supervision of the Department of Education Culture and Sports, to be known as the Gogon National High School.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriation Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 16, 1994 and February 15, 1995, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on JULY 16 1995 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
