H. No. 1619

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8137]

AN ACT CREATING A TWENTY-FIVE-BED CAPACITY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RAMON, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, TO BE KNOWN AS THE RAMON MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND THE APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a twenty-five-bed capacity municipal hospital in the Municipality of Ramon, Province of Isabela, to be known as the Ramon Municipal Hospital.

SEC. 2. The Department of Health, in consultation and coordination with the local government officials concerned, shall promulgate the rules and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary for the establishment of the hospital shall be included in the General Appropriation Act of the year following its enactment into law; thereafter, funds necessary for the maintenance and operation of the hospital shall be provided in accordance with the provisions of the Local Government Code of the applicable law at the time.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 16, 1995 and February 15, 1995, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on JULY 16 1995 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
