H. No. 4930

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7802]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF MINUHANG BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICPALITY OF BARUGO, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO CELESTINO DE GUZMAN MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Minuhang Barangay High School in the Municipality of Barugo, Province of Leyte, is hereby changed to Celestino de Guzman Memorial National High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE C. DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on October 8, 1993 and May 31, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law without the signature of the President on September 1, 1994, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
