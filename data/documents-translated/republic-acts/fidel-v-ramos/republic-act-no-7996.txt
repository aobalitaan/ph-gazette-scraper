[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7996]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE DR. GERARDO SABAL MEMORIAL MUNICIPAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVERIA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE DR. GERARDO SABAL MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Dr. Gerardo Sabal Memorial Municipal High School in the Poblacion, Municipality of Claveria, Province of Misamis Oriental, is hereby converted into a national high school to be known as the Dr. Gerardo Sabal Memorial National High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations and guidelines as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE C. DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 16, 1994 and February 15, 1995, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on APR 22 1995, without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
