H. No. 9174

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7786]

AN ACT NAMING THE NATIONAL HIGHWAY BEGINNING FROM BARANGAY ANAYAN, MUNICIPALITY OF PILI UP TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAGONOY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, AS THE GOVERNOR JOSE T. FUENTEBELLA NATIONAL HIGHWAY.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The national highway beginning from Barangay Anayan in the Municipality of Pili up to the Municipality of Lagonoy, Province of Camarines Sur, shall be named as the Governor Jose T. Fuentebella National Highway.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 1, 1994 and May 12, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law without the signature of the President on August 8, 1994, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
