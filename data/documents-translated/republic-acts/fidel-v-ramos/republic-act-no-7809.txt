H. No. 12291

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7809]

AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 14 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANCHEZ MIRA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, TO BE KNOWN AS “ ALDAW TI SANCHEZ MIRA”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipality of Sanchez Mira, Province of Cagayan, was founded on September 14, 1892 by the Spanish Governor General and the members of the Catholic hierarchy assigned thereat.

SEC. 2. September 14 of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Sanchez Mira. Province of Cagayan to be known as “Aldaw ti Sanchez Mira”

SEC. 3. This Act shall lake effect upon its approval Aldaw ti Sanchez Mira.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on July 26, 1994 and May 31, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law without the signature of the President on September 16, 1994, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
