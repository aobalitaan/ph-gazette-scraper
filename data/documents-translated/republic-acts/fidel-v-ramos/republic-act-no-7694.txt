H. No. 2876

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7694]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SISON NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SISON, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, TO TOLEDO S. PANTILO, SR. MEMORIAL NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives or the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the public secondary school in the Municipality of Sison, Province of Surigao del Norte shall be changed from Sison National High School Toledo S. Pantilo, Sr. Memorial National High School.

SEC. 2. Henceforth, said school shall be known for all intents and purposes, as Toledo S. Pantilo, Sr. Memorial National High School.

SEC. 3. The Department of Education, Culture and Sports, and all its instrumentalities shall be duly notified of the new name of said public secondary school.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 8, 1993 and February 3, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law without the signature of the President on April 9, 1994, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
