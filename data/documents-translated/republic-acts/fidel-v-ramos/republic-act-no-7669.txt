H. No. 7599

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7669]

AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TWENTY-SEVEN OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JUAN TO COMMEMORATE THE ANNIVERSARY AND FORMATION OF SAN JUAN AS AN INDEPENDENT MUNICIPALITY BY VIRTUE OF PHILIPPINE COMMISSION ACT NO. 1625

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. March Twenty-seven of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking public holiday in the Municipality of San Juan, to be known as “Araw ng San Juan,” to commemorate the anniversary and formation of San Juan as an independent municipality by virtue of Philippine Commission Act No. 1625.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 26, 1993 and October 8, 1993, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on January 10, 1994 without the President’s signature in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
