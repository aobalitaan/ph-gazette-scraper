Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty- second day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-six.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8242]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF BARANGAY PUG-OS IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINAIT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO BARANGAY DEAN LEOPOLDO YABES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Barangay Pug-os in the Municipality of Sinait, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Barangay Dean Leopoldo Yabes.

SEC. 2. This Act shall effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) NEPTALI A. GONZALES President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on January 25, 1996 and September 30, 1996, respectively.

(Sgd.) HAZEL P. GACUTAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: DEC 20 1996
