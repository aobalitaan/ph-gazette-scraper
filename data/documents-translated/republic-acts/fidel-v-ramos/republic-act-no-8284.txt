[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8284]

AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE CHANGE OF STATUS OF THE LOPEZ NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL OF LOPEZ, FOURTH DISTRICT OF QUEZON, FROM NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE LOPEZ NATIONAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR THE OPERATION AND MAINTENANCE THEREOF

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall cause the change of status of the Lopez National High School in the Municipality of Lopez, Fourth District of Quezon, from national high school to national comprehensive high school.

SEC. 2. The subject school shall be known as the Lopez National Comprehensive High School.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged against the current year’s appropriations of the Lopez National High School. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on August 15, 1996 and March 3, 1997, respectively.

(Sgd.) LORENZO E. LEYNES, JR. Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on MAY 03 1997 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
