H. No. 679

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7697]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE LICANAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN ISIDRO BUNAWAN DISTRICT, DAVAO CITY, TO PABLO PIATOS, SR. ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Licanan Elementary School in Barangay San Isidro, Bunawan District, Davao City, is hereby changed to Pablo Piatos, Sr. Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 26, 1993and February 3, 1994, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law without the signature of the President on April 17, 1994 in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
