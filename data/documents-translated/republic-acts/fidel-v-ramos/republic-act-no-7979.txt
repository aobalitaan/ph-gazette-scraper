H. No. 11697

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7979]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF DINAS MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINAS, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, TO DON SANTIAGO A. NAHIAL, SR. MEMORIAL HOSPITAL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name Dinas Municipal Hospital in the Municipality of Dinas, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, is hereby changed to Don Santiago A. Nahial, Sr. Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 31, 1994 and February 15, 1995, respectively.

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on APR 22 1995 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
