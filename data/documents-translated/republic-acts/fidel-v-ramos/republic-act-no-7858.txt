Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7858]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF RIZAL COLLEGE OF AGRICULTURE AND TECHNOLOGY IN SAMPALOC, MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO RIZAL STATE COLLEGE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Rizal College of Agriculture and Technology located in Sampaloc, Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Rizal State College.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senile on September 21, 1994 and November 15, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: JAN 21 1995

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines
