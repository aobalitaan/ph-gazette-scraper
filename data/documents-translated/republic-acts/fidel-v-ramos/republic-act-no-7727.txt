H. No. 7258

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7727]

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

AN ACT CONVERTING THE TUNGA BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

SECTION 1. The Tunga Barangay High School in the Municipality of Tunga, Province of Leyte, is hereby converted into a national high school, to be known as the Tunga National High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 1, 1994 and February23, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: May 19, 1994

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines
