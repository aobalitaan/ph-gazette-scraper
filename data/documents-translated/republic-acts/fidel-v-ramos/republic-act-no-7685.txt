H. No. 8457

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7685]

AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 16 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF DAVAO, TO BE KNOWN AS “ARAW NG DABAW”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The City of Davao was created by virtue, of Commonwealth Act No. 51 which took effect on March 16, 1936.

SEC. 2. March 16 of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking public holiday in the City of Davao, to be known as the “Araw ng Dabaw.”

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on October 8, 1993 and November 25,1993, respectively.

Approved: February 23, 1994

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines
