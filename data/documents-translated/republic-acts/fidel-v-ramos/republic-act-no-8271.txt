[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8271]

AN ACT PROVIDING FOR THE SEPARATION OF MARBEL 7 HIGH SCHOOL ANNEX SITUATED IN BARANGAY CONCEPCION, KORONADAL, SOUTH COTABATO, FROM THE MARBEL 7 HIGH SCHOOL (MAIN), INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CONCEPCION NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Marbel 7 High School Annex in the Municipality of Koronadal, Province of South Cotabato, is hereby separated from the Marbel 7 High School (Main) and shall operate independently under the supervision of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports and be known as the Concepcion National High School.

SEC. 2. All personnel, records, assets and properties including buildings, sites and improvements thereof, obligations, funds and appropriations of the Marbel 7 High School Annex are hereby transferred to the Concepcion National High School.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged against the current year’s appropriations of the Marbel 7 National High School-Annex (Concepcion High School). Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the Concepcion National High School shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 27, 1996 and December 2, 1996, respectively.

(Sgd.) LORENZO E. LEYNES, JR. Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on MAR 01 1997 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
