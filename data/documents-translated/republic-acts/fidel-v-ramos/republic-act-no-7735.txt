H. No. 1768

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7735]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARAGAY POBLACION IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAWAB, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE LORENZO S. SARMIENTO, SR. NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a national high school in Barangay Poblacion in the Municipality of Mawab, Province of Davao, to be known as the Lorenzo S. Sarmiento, Sr. National High school.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be funded out of the appropriations authorized by Republic Act No. 7663 for the department of Education, Culture and Sports intended for the operational expenses of newly legislated high schools. Thereafter, the amount needed for its operation and maintenance shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 23, 1994 and February 17, 1994, respectively.

Approved:

JUN 03 1994

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines
