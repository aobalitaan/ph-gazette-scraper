H. No. 11892

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7980]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PUG-OS (SINAIT) ELEMENTARY SCHOOL LOCATED AT BARANGAY PUG-OS, MUNICIPALITY OF SINAIT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, TO DEAN LEOPOLDO YABES MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Pug-os (Sinait) Elementary School located at Barangay Pug-os, Municipality of Sinait, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby changed to Dean Leopoldo Yabes Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE C. DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on October 25, 1994 and February 20, 1995, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on APR 22 1995 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
