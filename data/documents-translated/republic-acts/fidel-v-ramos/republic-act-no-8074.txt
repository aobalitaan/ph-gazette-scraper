Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8074]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL TRADE SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADADA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE CARMELO DE LOS CIENTOS SR. NATIONAL TRADE SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a national trade school in the Municipality of Padada, Province of Davao del Sur, to be known as the Carmelo de los Cientos Sr. National Trade School, hereinafter referred to as the School.

SEC. 2. The School shall offer trade secondary education proper, as well as secondary technical-vocational courses to carry out its objectives.

SEC. 3. The Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act.

SEC. 4. The amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect immediately upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 16, 1995 and February 15, 1995, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) CAMILO L. SABIO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on JUN 19 1995 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
