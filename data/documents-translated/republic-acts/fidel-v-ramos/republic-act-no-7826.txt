Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7826]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE BUTIQUE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL ANNEX IN BARANGAY BUTIQUE, MUNICIPALITY OF PARACELIS, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE BUTIQUE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Butique National High School Annex in Barangay Butique, Municipality of Paracelis, Mountain Province, is hereby separated from the Paracelis National High School in the Municipality of Paracelis, Mountain Province, and converted into an independent national high school to be known as the Butique National High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports shall issue such orders, rules and circulars as may be necessary for the implementation of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 24, 1994 and August 25, 1994, respectively.

(Sgd.) EDGARDO E. TUMANGAN Secretary of the Senate
