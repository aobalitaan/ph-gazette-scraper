H. No. 8150

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Second Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-sixth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety three.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7672]

AN ACT DECLARING JUNE EIGHTEEN OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Province of Benguet was created by virtue of Republic Act No. 4965 which was enacted into law on June 18, 1966.

SEC. 2. It is hereby declared that June Eighteen of every year as a nonworking public holiday in the Province of Benguet.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on March 26, 1993 and October 8, 1993, respectively.

Approved:

FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

Lapsed into the law on January 10, 1994 without the President’s signature in accordance with Article VI, Section 27(1) of the Constitution.
