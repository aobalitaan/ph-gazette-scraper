H. No. 2147

Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fifth day of July, nineteen hundred and ninety-four.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7812]

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

AN ACT CONVERTING THE ROSARIO BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN ROSARIO, ALOGUINSAN, CEBU, INTO A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE ROSARIO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

SECTION 1. The barangay high school of Barangay Rosario in the Municipality of Aloguinsan, Cebu, is hereby convened into a national high school to be known as Rosario National High School.

SEC. 2. Upon organization of Rosario National High School, all assets, liabilities, personnel, equipment and records of Rosario Barangay High School shall be transferred to the Rosario National High School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports is hereby empowered to promulgate such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 4. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) EDGARDO J. ANGARA President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA, JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 24, 1994 and August 25, 1994, respectively.
