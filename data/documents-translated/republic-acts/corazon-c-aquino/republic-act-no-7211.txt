H. No. 24377

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7211

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE SCHOLASTICA ROMERO DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUBAO, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA, FROM FIFTY (50) TO SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Scholastica Romero District Hospital in the Municipality of Lubao, Province of Pampanga, is hereby increased from fifty (50) to seventy-five (75) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 6, 1992.
