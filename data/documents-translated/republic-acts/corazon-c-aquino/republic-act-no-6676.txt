H. No. 436

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6676

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIANO MARCOS IN THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT TO LAMBAYONG

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipality of Mariano Marcos in the Province of Sultan Kudarat shall hereafter be known as the Municipality of Lambayong.

SEC. 2. All laws or decrees inconsistent with any provision of this Act are hereby deemed amended accordingly.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, October 2, 1988.
