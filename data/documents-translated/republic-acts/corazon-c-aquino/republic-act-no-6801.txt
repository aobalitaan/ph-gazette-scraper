H. No. 20208

Third Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6801

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BONGABONG CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO MAGDALENA UMALI SUYON MEMORIAL SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Bongabong Central Elementary School in the Municipality of Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro, is hereby changed to Magdalena Umali Suyon Memorial School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, December 12, 1989.
