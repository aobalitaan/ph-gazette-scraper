H. No. 16597

Third Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6791

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE STA. ISABEL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY STA. ISABEL, CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO ANTONIA MANUEL MAGCASE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Sta. Isabel Elementary School in Barangay Sta. Isabel, City of San Pablo, Province of Laguna, is hereby changed to Antonia Manuel Magcase Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, November 29, 1989.
