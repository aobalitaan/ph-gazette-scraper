H. No. 174

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7258

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE ILIGAN CITY DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN ILIGAN CITY, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE, FROM SEVENTY-FIVE (75) TO ONE HUNDRED FIFTY (150) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Iligan City District Hospital in Iligan City, Province of Lanao del Norte, is hereby increased from seventy-five (75) to one hundred fifty (150) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 20, 1992.
