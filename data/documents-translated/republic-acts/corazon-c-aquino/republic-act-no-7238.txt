REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 11163

Fifth Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7238

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DUMANGAS DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUMANGAS, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, FROM FIFTY (50) TO SEVENTY-FIVE (75) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Dumangas District Hospital in the Municipality of Dumangas, Province of Iloilo, is hereby increased from fifty (50) to seventy-five (75) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 19, 1992.
