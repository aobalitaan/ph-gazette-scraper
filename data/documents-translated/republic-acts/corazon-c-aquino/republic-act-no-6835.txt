REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 9305

Third Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6835

AN ACT TO NAME THE ROAD FROM CANAYAON JUNCTION TO ROXAS PARK IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GARCIA HERNANDEZ, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, AS THE HONORABLE CRUZ P. RANARIO AVENUE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The road from Canayaon Junction to Roxas Park in the Municipality of Garcia Hernandez, Province of Bohol, is hereby named as the Honorable Cruz P. Ranario Avenue.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, January 5, 1990.
