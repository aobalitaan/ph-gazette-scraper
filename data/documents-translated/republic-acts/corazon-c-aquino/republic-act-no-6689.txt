H. No. 5262

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6689

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TIGAON NORTH CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIGAON, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, TO FELIX PLAZO MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Tigaon North Central Elementary School located in the Municipality of Tigaon, Province of Camarines Sur, is hereby changed to Felix Plazo Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, February 10, 1989.
