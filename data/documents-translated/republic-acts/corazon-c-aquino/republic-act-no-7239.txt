REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 17464

Fifth Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7239

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE MADDELA MEDICARE COMMUNITY HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MADDELA, PROVINCE OF QUIRINO, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Maddela Medicare Community Hospital in the Municipality of Maddela, Province of Quirino, is hereby increased from ten (10) to twenty-five (25) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 19, 1992.
