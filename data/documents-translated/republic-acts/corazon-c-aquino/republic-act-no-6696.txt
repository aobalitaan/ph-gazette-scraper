H. No. 1443

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6696

AN ACT NAMING A NATIONAL HIGHWAY FROM THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAVIA UP TO THE MUNICIPALITY OF JANIUAY, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS THE TOMAS CONFESOR HIGHWAY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The national highway starting from Km. 10 + 000 in the Municipality of Pavia to Km. 33 + 000 in the Municipality of Janiuay shall be known as the Tomas Confesor Highway.

SEC. 2. Any law, ordinance, administrative order, designation or identification shall be considered repealed or modified accordingly.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, February 10, 1989.
