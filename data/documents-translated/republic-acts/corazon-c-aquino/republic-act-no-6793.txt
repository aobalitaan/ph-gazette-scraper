H. No. 1368

Third Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6793

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE BATAC GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, TO ITS ORIGINAL NAME MARIANO MARCOS MEMORIAL HOSPITAL, AND FOR SOME OTHER PURPOSES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Batac General Hospital in the Municipality of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby changed to its original name Mariano Marcos Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, December 3, 1989.
