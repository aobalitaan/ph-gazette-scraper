H. No. 2159

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6881

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TEN-BED MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANAOAG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE MANAOAG MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a ten-bed municipal hospital in the Municipality of Manaoag, Province of Pangasinan, to be known as the Manaoag Municipal Hospital.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Health and/or his duly authorized representatives are hereby directed to issue directives for the implementation of this Act.

SEC. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be charged against the approbations authorized for the purpose under the Fiscal Year 1989 General Appropriations Act. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the herein hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. Before the construction of the hospital, the Secretary of Health shall issue a written assurance that personnel of the hospital will be appointed and that funds for its maintenance and operation have been appropriated.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 8, 1990.
