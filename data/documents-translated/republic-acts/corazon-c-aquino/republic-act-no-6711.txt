REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 9327

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6711

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE MEDICION ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN MEDICION 2ND, MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO GOV. DOMINADOR CAMERINO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Medicion Elementary School in the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite, is hereby changed to Gov. Dominader Camerino Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, February 10, 1989.
