H. No. 3114

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7214

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE DR. JOSE RIZAL MEMORIAL DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Dr. Jose Rizal Memorial District Hospital in the Municipality of Calamba, Province of Laguna, is hereby increased to seventy-five (75) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be charged against the lump-sum appropriations authorized for the purpose under the current General Appropriations Act. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the operation and maintenance of the hospital shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 6, 1992
