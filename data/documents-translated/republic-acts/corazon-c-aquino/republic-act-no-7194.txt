REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 554

Fifth Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7194

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE NAVAL GENERAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, SUBPROVINCE OF BILIRAN, LEYTE, FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Naval General Hospital in the Municipality of Naval, Subprovince of Biliran, Leyte, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, February 25, 1992.
