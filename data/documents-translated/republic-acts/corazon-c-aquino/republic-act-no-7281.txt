H. No. 23951

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7281

AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TEN OF THE EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ABRA TO BE KNOWN AS ABRA DAY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. March Ten of every year is hereby declared as a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Abra to commemorate the creation of the Province, to be known as Abra Day.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 24, 1992
