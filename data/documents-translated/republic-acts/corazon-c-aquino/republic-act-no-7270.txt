H. No. 803

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7270

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE MALIMONO DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALIMONO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE, FROM TEN (10) TO TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Malimono District Hospital in the Municipality of Malimono, Province of Surigao del Norte, is hereby increased from ten (10) to twenty-five (25) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 20, 1992.
