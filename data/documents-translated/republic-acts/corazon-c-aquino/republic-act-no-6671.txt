H. No. 337

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6671

AN ACT RENAMING BARANGAY CAPUTATAN IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGONDON, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY SAN MIGUEL AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Barangay Caputatan in the Municipality of Maragondon, Province of Cavite, is hereby renamed as Barangay San Miguel.

SEC. 2. The Department of Local Government and Community Development, through the Provincial Board of Cavite, and the Municipal Council of Maragondon, Province of Cavite, is hereby directed to carry out the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, September 5, 1988.
