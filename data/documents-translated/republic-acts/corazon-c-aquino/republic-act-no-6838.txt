REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 15810

Third Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6838

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE SUOP ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY IGANGON, MUNICIPALITY OF ASUNCION, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, TO IGANGON ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Suop Elementary School in Barangay Igangon, Municipality of Asuncion, Province of Davao, is hereby changed to Igangon Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, January 5, 1990.
