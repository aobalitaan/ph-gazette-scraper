H. No. 22814

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7283

AN ACT DECLARING MARCH TWO OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOILO

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In order to honor the memory of the late Tomas Confesor as a model Filipino citizen and inspiration of our people, March Two of every year is hereby declared as a special working holiday in the whole province of Iloilo.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 24, 1992
