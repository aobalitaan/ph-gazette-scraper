H. No. 21418

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6771

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DENNIS MOLINTAS MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BOKOD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET, TO DENNIS MOLINTAS MEMORIAL HOSPITAL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Dennis Molintas Municipal Hospital in the Municipality of Bokod, Province of Benguet, is hereby changed to Dennis Molintas Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. Any amount necessary for the implementation of this Act shall be charged to the appropriations of the Department of Health.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, November 29, 1989.
