H. No. 4544

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6745

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE IRAYA BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HAMTIC, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, TO GOVERNOR JULIO V. MACUJA MEMORIAL COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Iraya Barangay High School in the Municipality of Hamtic, Province of Antique, is hereby changed to Governor Julio V. Macuja Memorial Comprehensive High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, August 11, 1989.
