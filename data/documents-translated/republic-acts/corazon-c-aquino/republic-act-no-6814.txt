AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF VILLA NORTE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALABAT, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, TO CIPRIANO B. ENCARNADO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Villa Norte Elementary School in the Municipality of Alabat, Province of Quezon, is hereby changed to Cipriano B. Encarnado Elementary School and shall henceforth be known and designated as the Cipriano B. Encarnado Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
