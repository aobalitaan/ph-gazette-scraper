REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 5371

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6705

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE PARANG I ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PARANG, MUNICIPALITY OF MARIKINA, METRO MANILA, TO FORTUNE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Parang I Elementary School in Barangay Parang, Municipality of Marikina, Metro Manila, is hereby changed to Fortune Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, February 10, 1989.
