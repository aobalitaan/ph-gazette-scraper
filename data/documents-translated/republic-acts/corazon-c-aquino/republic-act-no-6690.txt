H. No. 4577

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6690

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE TANAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO SIMEON R. BENDAÑA, SR. MEMORIAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of the Tanay Elementary School located in the Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, is hereby changed to Simeon R. Bendaña, Sr. Memorial Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, February 10, 1989.
