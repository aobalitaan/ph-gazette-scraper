REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 1047

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6721

AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER THIRTEEN OF EACH YEAR A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In order to commemorate the birth anniversary of the late Speaker Eugenio Perez, November Thirteen of each year is hereby declared a special non-working public holiday in the Province of Pangasinan.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, March 20, 1989.
