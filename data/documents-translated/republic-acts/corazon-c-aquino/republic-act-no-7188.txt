REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 1247

Fifth Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7188

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE NORALA DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Norala District Hospital in the Province of South Cotabato, is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) to fifty (50) beds.

SEC. 2. The amount necessary to carry out the provisions of this Act shall be included in the General Appropriations Act of the year following its enactment into law and thereafter.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, January 30, 1992.
