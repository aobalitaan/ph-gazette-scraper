REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 942

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6710

AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 1 OF EACH YEAR AS BAGUIO CHARTER DAY AND A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO IN ORDER TO COMMEMORATE THE ANNIVERSARY OF THE SAID CITY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In order to commemorate the anniversary of the City of Baguio, September 1 of each year is hereby declared as Baguio Charter Day and a special non-working holiday in the said city.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, February 10, 1989.
