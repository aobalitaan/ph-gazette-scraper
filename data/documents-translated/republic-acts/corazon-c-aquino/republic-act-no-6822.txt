REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 21479

Third Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6822

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE CALLAWA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN DAVAO CITY TO GORGONIO TAJO, SR. NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Callawa National High School in Davao City shall hereafter be known as the Gorgonio Tajo, Sr. National High School, in honor of its donor, the late Gorgonio Tajo, Sr.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports is hereby empowered, authorized and directed to issue rules and regulations to implement the purpose of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, December 18, 1989.
