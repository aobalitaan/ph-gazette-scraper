REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7601

AN ACT DECLARING FEBRUARY 11 OF EACH YEAR GOVERNOR EVELIO B. JAVIER DAY, A SPECIAL NONWORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCES OF ANTIQUE, CAPIZ, AKLAN AND ILOILO

SECTION 1. In order to commemorate the death anniversary of the late Governor Evelio B. Javier, February Eleven of each year is hereby declared Governor Evelio B. Javier Day, a special nonworking public holiday in the Provinces of Antique, Capiz, Aklan and Iloilo.

SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved: June 3, 1992

Published in the Official Gazette , Vol. 88 No. 29 page 4663 on July 20, 1992.
