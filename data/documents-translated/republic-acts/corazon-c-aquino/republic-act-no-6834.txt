REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 3776

Third Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6834

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE JOSE MENCIO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ATOK, PROVINCE OF BENGUET, TO ITS ORIGINAL NAME OF ATOK NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Jose Mencio National High School in the Municipality of Atok, Province of Benguet, shall hereafter be known as the Atok National High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, January 5, 1990.
