REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES

H. No. 4116

Second Regular Session

REPUBLIC ACT NO. 6706

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF THE DADIANGAS NORTH “A” ELEMENTARY SCHOOL TO JOSE P. LAUREL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Dadiangas North “A” Elementary School in Cahilsot District, General Santos City, South Cotabato, shall hereafter be known and referred to as the Jose P. Laurel Elementary School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without executive approval, February 10, 1989.
