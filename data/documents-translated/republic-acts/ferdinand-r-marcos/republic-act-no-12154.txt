AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
CATANAUAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, INTO A DISTRICT
OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The extension office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) located in the Municipality of Catanauan, Province of
Quezon, is hereby converted into a district office to be known as the
LTO Bondoc Peninsula District Office.

Src. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
shall immediately include in the Department’s program the
operationalization of the LTO Bondoc Peninsula District Office in
the Province of Quezon, the initial funding of which shall be charged.

against the current year’s appropriation of the LTO Catanauan
Extension Office. Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included
in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
