[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 122586 ]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY DOOS DEL NORTE, MUNICIPALITY OF
HINDANG, PROVINCE OF LEYTE TO BE KNOWN AS
THE DOOS DEL NORTE NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the supervision
of the Department of Education, a national high school in Barangay
Doos del Norte, Municipality of Hindang, Province of Leyte to be known
as the Doos del Norte National High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
Doos del Norte National High School, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
