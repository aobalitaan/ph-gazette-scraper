AN ACT UPGRADING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN CANDON CITY, PROVINCE
OF ILOCOS SUR, INTO A CLASS “B” DISTRICT OFFICE
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation Office
(LTO) in Candon City, Province of Ilocos Sur, is hereby upgraded into
a Class “B” district office.

Suc, 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in the
Department’s program the upgrading into a Class “B” district office of
the LTO District Office in Candon City, Province of Ilocos Sur, the
initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year’s
appropriation of the existing LTO District Office in Candon City.
Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

Sc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
