[ REPUBLIC ACT No.12141 ]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE LOCATED IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF BURGOS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
NORTE, INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The extension office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) located in the Municipality of Burgos, Province of Ilocos
Norte, is hereby converted into a regular district office, to be known
as the Burgos LTO District Office,

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation shall
include in the Department’s programs the operationalization of the
Burgos LTO District Office, the initial funding of which shall be


charged against the current year’s appropriation of the Burgos LTO
Extension Office located in the Municipality of Burgos, Province of
Ilocos Norte. Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
