AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXISTING LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE (LTO) EXTENSION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF SAN PASCUAL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS INTO
A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Land Transportation Office (LTO)
Extension Office in the Municipality of San Pascual, Province
of Batangas, is hereby converted into a regular district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the LTO Extension Office in the Municipality of San Pascual,
Province of Batangas, the funding of which shall be included in
the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

SkEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
