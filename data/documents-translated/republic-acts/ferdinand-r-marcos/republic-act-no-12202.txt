AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO JOB
CALLARACKAL OOMMEN

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Job Callarackal Oommen is hereby granted
Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges, and prerogatives,
as well as the duties and obligations appurtenant thereto, under the
Constitution and the laws of the Republic of the Philippines.

SEC. 2. Job Callarackal Oommen shall take the Oath of
Allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines before an officer duly
authorized to administer the same. The Oath of Allegiance shall be
registered with the Bureau of Immigration.

SEC. 3. Upon registration of the Oath of Allegiance, the Bureau
of Immigration shall issue a Certificate of Naturalization to Job
Callarackal Oommen, who shall thereupon enter into the full enjoyment
of Philippine citizenship.

Suc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazetie or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
