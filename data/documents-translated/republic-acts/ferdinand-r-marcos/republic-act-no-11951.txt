AN ACT CONVERTING THE NABITASAN ELEMENTARY
SCHOOL IN BARANGAY NABITASAN, LA PAZ
DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY INTO AN INTEGRATED
SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE NABITASAN
INTEGRATED SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Nabitasan Elementary School in
Barangay Nabitasan, La Paz District, Iloilo City is hereby
converted into an integrated school to be known as the
Nabitasan Integrated School.

SEc. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of
the Nabitasan Elementary School are hereby transferred to
and absorbed by the Nabitasan Integrated School.

Src. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the Nabitasan Integrated School, the funding of which shall
be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of
this Act, the Secretary of Education shall formulate the
rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act.
Such implementing rules and regulations shall provide for
the school’s organization, administration, operations and
management, in accordance with Republic Act No. 9155, or
the “Governance of Basic Education Act of 2001”, Republic Act
No. 10533, or the “Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013”,
and other basic education-related laws.

Sec. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
