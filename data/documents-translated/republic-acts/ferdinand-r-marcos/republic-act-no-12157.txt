[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12157]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF LAMUT, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, INTO A CLASS “C”
DISTRICT OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the
Municipality of Lamut, Province of Ifugao, is hereby upgraded into a
Class “C” district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in the
Department’s program the operationalization of the Class “C” LTO
District Office in the Municipality of Lamut, Province of Ifugao,
the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current
year’s appropriation for the said office. Thereafter, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

 

 

; SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
