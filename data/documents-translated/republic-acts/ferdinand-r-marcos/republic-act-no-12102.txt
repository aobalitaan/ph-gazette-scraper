[ REPUBLIC ACT No.1210°2 ]

AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 3 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALIBO,
PROVINCE OF AKLAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE “KALIBO
FOUNDATION DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November 3 of every year is hereby declared
a special working holiday in the Municipality of Kalibo, Province of
Aklan, to be known as the “Kalibo Foundation Day”. Upon proclamation
of the President, November 3 of every year shall be a special
non-working holiday in the Municipality of Kalibo, Province of Aklan.

Src. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
