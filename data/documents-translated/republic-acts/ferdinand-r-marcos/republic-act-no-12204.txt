[ REPUBLIC Act No. 12204]

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE AMANG
RODRIGUEZ MEMORIAL MEDICAL CENTER IN THE
CITY OF MARIKINA, METRO MANILA, FROM FIVE
HUNDRED (500) TO ONE THOUSAND (1,000) BEDS,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The authorized bed capacity of the Amang
Rodriguez Memorial Medical Center (ARMMC) in the City of
Marikina, Metro Manila, shall be increased from five hundred (500)
to one thousand (1,000) beds.

SEC. 2. The existing professional and healthcare services
and facilities of the ARMMC are hereby upgraded to conform with
and be commensurate to the increase in bed capacity as provided
in Section 1 hereof.

SEC. 3. The existing workforce of the ARMMC, which
includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel, shall be
increased correspondingly.

Sec. 4. The Secretary of Health shall, after consultation
with the Medical Center Chief of the ARMMC, and in coordination
with the Secretary of Budget and Management and the Chairperson
of the Civil Service Commission, determine the additional new
positions to be created.

SEC. 5. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department's program the implementation of this Act,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEC. 6. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected
shall remain in full force and effect.

SEC. 7. Republic Act No, 9419, entitled “An Act Increasing
the Bed Capacity of the Amang Rodriguez Memorial Medical
Center From One Hundred Fifty (150) to Three Hundred (800)
Beds and Appropriating Funds Therefor”, as amended by
Republic Act No. 11287, entitled “An Act Increasing the Bed
Capacity of the Amang Rodriguez Memorial Medical Center
in the City of Marikina From Three Hundred (300) to Five
Hundred (500) Beds, Upgrading the Service Facilities and
Professional Health Care Therein, and Appropriating Funds
Therefor’, is hereby further amended. All other laws, decrees,
orders, issuances, rules and regulations, or parts thereof which
are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby
repealed, amended, or modified accordingly.

SEC. 8. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
