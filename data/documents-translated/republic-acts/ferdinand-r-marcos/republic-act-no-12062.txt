[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12062]

AN ACT DECLARING JULY 2 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN PASIG CITY TO BE KNOWN
AS THE “ARAW NG PASIG”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. July 2 of every year is hereby declared a special
nonworking holiday in Pasig City to be known as the Araw ng Pasig.

 

Src. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,

      

FRANCIS “CHIZ” G, ESCUDERO. FE G.R ALDEZ

was passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate of
the Philippines on September 5, 2022 and August 27, 2024, respectively.
