AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RAMON
MAGSAYSAY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, A
DISTRICT HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS ZAMBOANGA DEL
SUR FIRST DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the Municipality of
Ramon Magsaysay, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, a district hospital
to be known as the Zamboanga del Sur First District Hospital, which
shall be under the direct control and supervision of the Provincial
Government of Zamboanga del Sur.

Src. 2. Any future increase in bed capacity, healthcare
facilities, service capability, and human resource complement shall
be based on the hospital development plan that shall be prepared by
the Chief of Hospital, in consultation with the Department of Health.

SEC. 3. The Provincial Government of Zamboanga del Sur shall
provide the necessary funds for the establishment of the Zamboanga
del Sur First District Hospital. The Secretary of Health shall include
in the Department's subsidy program its support for the capital outlay
requirements of the said hospital.
