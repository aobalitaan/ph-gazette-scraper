AN ACT DECLARING MAY 28 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
MALALAG, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, TO BE
KNOWN AS “ARAW NG MALALAG”, JN COMMEMORATION
OF ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. May 28 of every year is hereby declared a special
working holiday in the Municipality of Malalag, Province of Davao del
Sur, to be known as “Araw ng Malalag’, in commemoration of its
foundation anniversary. However, upon proclamation of the President,
May 28 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in the
Municipality of Malalag, Province of Davao del Sur.

 

Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
