AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF UBAY, IN THE SECOND DISTRICT OF BOHOL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a district office of the
Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Ubay,
Province of Bohol.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization of the
LTO District Office in the Municipality of Ubay, Province of Bohol,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.
