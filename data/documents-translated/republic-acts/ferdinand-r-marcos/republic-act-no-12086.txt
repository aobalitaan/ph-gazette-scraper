AN ACT SEPARATING THE LAPUYAN NATIONAL HIGH
SCHOOL —- KARPOK EXTENSION IN BARANGAY
KARPOK, MUNICIPALITY OF LAPUYAN, PROVINCE
OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR FROM THE LAPUYAN
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO
AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE
KNOWN AS THE KARPOK NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION:1. The Lapuyan National High School — Karpok
Extension in Barangay Karpok, Municipality of Lapuyan, Province of
Zamboanga del Sur is hereby separated from the Lapuyan National
High School and converted into an independent national high school
to be known as the Karpok National High School.

Src. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the
Lapuyan National High School — Karpok Extension are hereby
transferred to and absorbed by the Karpok National High School.

SEC, 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Karpok
National High School, the initial funding of which shall be charged
against the current year’s appropriation of the Lapuyan National High
School —Karpok Extension. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the
continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

SeEc. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules
and regulations shall provide for the school’s organization,
administration, operations and management, in accordance with
Republic Act No, 9155, or the “Governance of Basic Education Act
of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the “Enhanced Basic Education
Act of 2018”, and other basic education-related laws.

Src. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
