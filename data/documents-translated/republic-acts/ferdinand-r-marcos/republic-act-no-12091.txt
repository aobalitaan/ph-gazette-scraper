Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Bokong National High School — Tabak
Annex in Barangay Upper Pulacan, Municipality of Labangan,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur is hereby separated from the Bokong
National High School and converted into an independent national
high school to be known as the Tabak National High School.

Src. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of
the Bokong National High School — Tabak Annex are hereby
transferred to and absorbed by the Tabak National High School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Tabak
National High School, the initial funding of which shall be charged
against the current year’s appropriation of the Bokong National

High School — Tabak Annex. Thereafter, the amount necessary
for the continued operation of the school shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

Sic. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules
and regulations shall provide for the school’s organization,
administration, operations and management, in accordance with
Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of Basic Education Act
of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the “Enhanced Basic Education
Act of 2013”, and other basic education-related laws.

Src. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
