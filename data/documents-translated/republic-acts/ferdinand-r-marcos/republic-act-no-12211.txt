AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE CITY OF LUCENA, PROVINCE
OF QUEZON, A GENERAL HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS
THE LUCENA CITY HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the City of Lucena,
Province of Quezon, a Level 1 general hospital, with a capacity of one
hundred (100) beds, to be known as the Lucena City Hospital, which
shall be under the direct control and supervision of the Department of
Health (DOH).

SEc. 2. To ensure the provision of integrated and efficient
healthcare services in the Province of Quezon, the Lucena City Hospital
shall consult and coordinate with the Provincial Government of
Quezon in the formulation and implementation of its hospital
development plan.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include in
the Department’s program the implementation of this Act, the funding
of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
