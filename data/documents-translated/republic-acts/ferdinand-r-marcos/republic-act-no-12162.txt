AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXISTING LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE (LTO) EXTENSION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF SAN MIGUELIN THE SECOND DISTRICT, PROVINCE
OF ILOILO INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, The existing Land Transportation Office (LTO)
extension office in the Municipality of San Miguel in the Second District,
Province of Iloilo, is hereby converted into a regular district office.

Src. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
regular LTO District Office in the Municipality of San Miguel, Second
District, Province of Iloilo, the funding of which shall be included in
the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

; SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
