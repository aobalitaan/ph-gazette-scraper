[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11949]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY BOLILA, MUNICIPALITY OF MALITA,
PROVINCE OF DAVAO OCCIDENTAL TO BE KNOWN
AS THE BOLILA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the
supervision of the Department of Education, a national high school
in Barangay Bolila, Municipality of Malita, Province of Davao
Occidental to be known as the Bolila National High School.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization of
the Bolila National High School, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this
Act, the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and
regulations implementing the provisions of this Act. Such
implementing rules and regulations shall provide for the school’s
organization, administration, operations and management, in
accordance with Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of
Basic Education Act of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the
“Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013”, and other basic
education-related laws.

SEc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general
circulation.

Approved,
