[ REPUBLIC Act No.12002 |

AN ACT CREATING A DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE
IN THE FIRST LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT OF THE
PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A district engineering office of the Department
of Public Works and Highways is hereby created in the First
Legislative District, Province of Negros Occidental, to be located
in the City of San Carlos, and referred to as the Negros Occidental
Fifth District Engineering Office.

SEc. 2. The Negros Occidental Fifth District Engineering
Office shall serve the cities of Escalante and San Carlos and the
municipalities of Toboso, Calatrava and Salvador Benedicto,
all located in the First Legislative District of the Province of
Negros Occidental.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall
include in the Department's program the operationalization of
the Negros Occidental Fifth District Engineering Office, the
funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Sec. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
after its publication in the Official Gazette or ina newspaper of

  

general circulation.
