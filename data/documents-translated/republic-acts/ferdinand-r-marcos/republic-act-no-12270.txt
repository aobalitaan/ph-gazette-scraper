[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12270]

AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 22 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
MATANAO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, IN
COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June 22 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the Municipality of Matanao, Province of
Davao del Sur, in commemoration of its foundation anniversary.
However, upon proclamation of the President, June 22 of every
year shall be a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of
Matanao, Province of Davao del Sur.

 

/ SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
