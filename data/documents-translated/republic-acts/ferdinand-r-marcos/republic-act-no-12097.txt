[ REPUBLIC Act No. 12097}

AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 20 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF GUINAYANGAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, IN
COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines ia
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June 20 of every year is hereby declared a special
working holiday in the Municipality of Guinayangan, Province
of Quezon, in commemoration of its founding anniver sary.
Upon proclamation of the President, June 20 of every year shall be
a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Guinayangan,
Province of Quezon.

 

 

Suc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
