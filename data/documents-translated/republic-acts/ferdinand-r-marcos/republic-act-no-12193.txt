[ REPUBLIC ACT No.4 2193]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE CITY
OF BAGUIO INTO A CLASS “A” DISTRICT
OFFICE, PROVIDING POSITIONS THERETO, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation Office
(LTO) in the City of Baguio is hereby converted into a Class “A” district
office, providing the appropriate personnel for its operation.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in the
Department’s program the operationalization of the LTO Class “A”
District Office in the City of Baguio, the initial funding of which shall
be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the said office.
Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

 

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
