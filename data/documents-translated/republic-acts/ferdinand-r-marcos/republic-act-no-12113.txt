AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE CITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE
OF NUEVA ECIJA, A LEVEL Il GENERAL HOSPITAL TO
BE KNOWN AS THE SEN. EDGARDO J. ANGARA GENERAL
HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the City of
San Jose, Province of Nueva Ecija, a Level II general
hospital with a one hundred (100)-bed capacity to be known
as the Sen. Edgardo J. Angara General Hospital, which shall be
under the direct control and supervision of the Department of Health
(DOH).

SEC. 2. The Sen. Edgardo J. Angara General Hospital shall
prioritize prenatal testing, obstetrics and gynecological services,
pediatrics care, lactation programs, nutrition services, neonatal
care, immunization services, postpartum depression support,
education classes for parenting, and women’s and children’s health
awareness,

Src. 8. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department's program the implementation of this Act, the

funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Src. 4. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected
shall remain in full force and effect.

Src. 5. All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or modified
accordingly.

Sac. 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
