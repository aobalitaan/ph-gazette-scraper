[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 49187]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF TRENTO, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR, INTO
A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The extension office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Trento, Province of Agusan del
Sur, is hereby converted into a regular district office, to be known as
the LTO District Office of Trento, Agusan del Sur.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
LTO District Office in the Municipality of Trento, Province of Agusan
del Sur, the funding of Which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 8. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
