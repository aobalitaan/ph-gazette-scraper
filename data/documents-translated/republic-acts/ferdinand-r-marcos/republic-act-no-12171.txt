[REPUBLIC Act No. 12171]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE SATELLITE OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
SAN JACINTO, TICAO ISLAND, PROVINCE, OF MASBATE,
INTO AREGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

 

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

 

SECTION 1. The satellite office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) located in the Municipality of San Jacinto, Ticao Island,
Province of Masbate, is hereby converted into a regular district office.

LA

FERDINAND R MUALDEZ MARCOS JR.
President of the Philippines

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
regular LTO District Office in the Municipality of San Jacinto,
Province of Masbate, the initial funding of which shall be charged
against the current year’s appropriation of the LTO Satellite Office
in the Municipality of San Jacinto, Ticao Island, Province of Masbate.
Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.
