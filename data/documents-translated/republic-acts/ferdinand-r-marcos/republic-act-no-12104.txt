[REPUBLIC Act No.12104]

AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 22 OF EVERY YEAR A
SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF GUIGUINTO, PROVINCE OF BULACAN, IN
CELEBRATION OF THE HALAMANAN FESTIVAL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, January 22 of every year is hereby declared a special
working holiday in the Municipality of Guiguinto, Province of Bulacan,
in celebration of the Halamanan Festival. Upon proclamation of the
President, January 22 of every year shall be a special non-working
holiday in the Municipality of Guiguinto, Province of Bulacan.

Sc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
