[ REPUBLIC ACT No.12186 ]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAS PINAS LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) LICENSING
EXTENSION OFFICE INTO A REGULAR LTO LICENSING
CENTER AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Licensing Extension Office of the Land
Transportation Office (LTO) located in Las Pifias City is hereby
converted into a regular licensing center to be known as the Las Pifias
City LTO Licensing Center.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Las
Pifias City LTO Licensing Center, the initial funding of which shall


be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the Las Pifias
LTO Licensing Extension Office. Thereafter, such sum as may be
necessary for the continued operation of the Las Pifas City
LTO Licensing Center shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
