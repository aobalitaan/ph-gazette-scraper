[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 172260 +

AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
SAN MIGUEL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR,
A DISTRICT HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE
ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR SECOND DISTRICT HOSPITAL,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR,

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representaives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the Municipality of
San Miguel, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, a district hospital to be
known as the Zamboanga del Sur Second District Hospital, which
shall be under the direct control and supervision of the Provincial
Government of Zamboanga del Sur.

SEC. 2. The Provincial Government of Zamboanga del Sur shall
manage the Zamboanga del Sur Second District Hospital, and prepare,
in consultation with the Department of Health, a hospital development
plan that is aligned with the Philippine Health Facility Development
Plan. Any future increase in bed capacity, healthcare facilities, service
capability, and human resource complement shall be based on the
hospital development plan.

SEc. 3. The Provincial Government of Zamboanga del Sur shall
provide the necessary funds for the establishment, maintenance,
and other operating expenses of the Zamboanga del Sur Second
District Hospital. The Secretary of Health shall include in the
Department’s subsidy program, support for the capital outlay
requirements of the hospital.

Sc. 4. Ifany provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional,
the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall remain in full
force and effect.

Src. 5. All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules
and regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

Src. 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
