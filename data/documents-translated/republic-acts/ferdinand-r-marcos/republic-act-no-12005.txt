[ REPUBLIC ACT No.72005 ]

AN ACT CREATING A DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE
IN THE THIRD LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT OF THE
PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A district engineering office of the
Department of Public Works and Highways is hereby created
in the Third Legislative District, Province of Iloilo, to be
located in the Municipality of Pototan, and referred to as the
Tloilo Sixth District Engineering Office.

SEC. 2. The Iloilo Sixth District Engineering Office
shall serve the Municipalities of Badiangan, Bingawan, Cabatuan,
Calinog, Janiuay, Lambunao, Maasin, Mina, and Pototan, all
located in the Third Legislative District of the Province of Iloilo.

SEC. 8. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways
shall include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the Sixth District Engineering Office in the Province of
Tloilo, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Suc. 4, This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
