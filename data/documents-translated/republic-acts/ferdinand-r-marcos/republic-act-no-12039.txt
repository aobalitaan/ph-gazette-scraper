[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12039]

AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 1 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON, TO BE KNOWN
AS “ARAW NG BUKIDNON”, IN COMMEMORATION
OF ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 1 of every year is hereby declared
a special non-working holiday in the Province of Bukidnon, to be
known as “Araw ng Bukidnon”, in commemoration of its
foundation anniversary.

Suc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
