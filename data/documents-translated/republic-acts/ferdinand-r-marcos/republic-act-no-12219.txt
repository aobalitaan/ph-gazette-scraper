AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER 27 OF EVERY YEAR A
SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF
SARANGANI IN RECOGNITION OF THE UNIQUE
CONTRIBUTION OF THE INDIGENOUS PEOPLES OF
SARANGANI TO THE RICH AND DIVERSE CULTURAL
HERITAGE OF THE PHILIPPINES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. October 27 of every year is hereby declared a special
working holiday in the Province of Sarangani in recognition of the
unique contribution of the indigenous peoples of Sarangani to the rich
and diverse cultural heritage of the province and of the country. Upon
proclamation of the President, October 27 of every year shall be a
special non-working holiday in the said province.

 

Src. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
