‘| REPUBLIC AcT No. 12059]

AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 15 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE
OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, TO BE KNOWN AS THE
“ARAW NG ORIENTAL MINDORO”, IN COMMEMORATION
OF ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November 15 of every year is hereby declared
a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Oriental Mindoro,
to be known as “Araw ng Oriental Mindoro”, in commemoration
ofits foundation anniversary.

Src. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
_ general circulation.

Approved,
FRANCIS “CHIZ” G, ESCUDERO F A G. ROMUALDEZ

of Representatives

respectively.
