AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF EASTERN BONTOC
NATIONAL AGRICULTURAL AND VOCATIONAL HIGH
SCHOOL IN BARANGAY LINGOY, MUNICIPALITY OF
BARLIG, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE TO BARLIG NATIONAL
AGRICULTURAL AND VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Eastern Bontoc National Agricultural
and Vocational High School in Barangay Lingoy, Municipality
of Barlig, Mountain Province is hereby changed to Barlig
National Agricultural and Vocational High School.

SEC. 3. Republic Act No. 4444, entitled “An Act to
Establish a National Agricultural and Vocational High School
in the Municipal District of Barlig, Mountain Province, to be
Known as Eastern Bontoc National Agricultural and Vocational
