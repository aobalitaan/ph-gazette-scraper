AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE AMAI
PAKPAK MEDICAL CENTER IN THE CITY OF
MARAWI, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, FROM
FOUR HUNDRED (400) TO EIGHT HUNDRED (800) BEDS,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, The authorized bed capacity of the Amai Pakpak
Medical Center (APMC) in the City of Marawi, Province of Lanao
del Sur, shall be increased from four hundred (400) to eight
hundred (800) beds.

SEC. 2. The existing professional healthcare services and
facilities of the APMC are hereby upgraded to conform with and
be commensurate to the increase in bed capacity as provided in
Section 1 hereof.

SEC. 8. The existing workforce of the APMC, which
includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel, shall be
increased correspondingly.

Src. 4. The Secretary of Health shall, after consultation
with the Medical Center Chief of the APMC, and in coordination
with the Secretary of the Department of Budget and Management
and the Chairperson of the Civil Service Commission, determine
the additional new positions to be created.

SEC. 5. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the implementation of this Act,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Sic. 6. Ifany provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional,
the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall remain in full
force and effect.

SEC. 7, All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules
and regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed
or modified accordingly.

SEC. 8. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazetie or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
