AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE
SOUTHERN TAGALOG REGIONAL HOSPITAL IN THE
CITY OF BACOOR, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, FROM
ONE HUNDRED (100) TO THREE HUNDRED (300) BEDS,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The authorized bed capacity of the Southern Tagalog
Regional Hospital in the City of Bacoor, Province of Cavite, shall be
increased from one hundred (100) to three hundred (300) beds.

Suc. 2. The existing professional healthcare services and
facilities of the Southern Tagalog Regional Hospital shall also be
upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to the increase in bed
capacity as provided in Section 1 hereof.

SEC. 3. The existing workforce of the Southern Tagalog Regional
Hospital, which includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel, shall
be increased correspondingly.

SEC. 4, The Secretary of Health shall, after consultation with
the Medical Center Chief of the Southern Tagalog Regional Hospital,
and in coordination with the Secretary of Budget and Management
and the Chairperson of the Civil Service Commission, determine the
additional new positions to be created.

Sec. 5. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the implementation of this Act,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Skc. 6. Ifany provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional,
the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall remain in full
force and effect,

SEC. 7. All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

Suc. 8. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
