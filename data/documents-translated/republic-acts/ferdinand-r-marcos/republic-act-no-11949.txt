AN ACT CONVERTING THE KIDAMAN ELEMENTARY
SCHOOL IN BARANGAY KALBAY, MUNICIPALITY
OF JOSE ABAD SANTOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO
OCCIDENTAL INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO
BE KNOWN AS THE KIDAMAN INTEGRATED
SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Kidaman Elementary School in Barangay
Kalbay, Municipality of Jose Abad Santos, Province of Davao
Occidental is hereby converted into an integrated school to be
known as the Kidaman Integrated School.

SsEc. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the
Kidaman Elementary School are hereby transferred to and
absorbed by the Kidaman Integrated School.

Sc. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization of
the Kidaman Integrated School, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this
| Act, the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and
regulations implementing the provisions of this Act. Such
implementing rules and regulations shall provide for the school’s
organization, administration, operations and management, in
accordance with Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of
Basic Education Act of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the
“Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013”, and other basic
education-related laws.

/ SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
