[REPUBLIC AcT No. 12165]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
KALAMANSIG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a district office
of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality
of Kalamansig, Province of Sultan Kudarat, to be known as
“LTO Kalamansig District Office”.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
LTO District Office in the Municipality of Kalamansig, Province of
Sultan Kudarat, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.
