[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12272]

AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER 5 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DELSUR, TO BE KNOWN
AS “ARAW NG STA. CRUZ’, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS
FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. October 5 of every year is hereby declared a special
working holiday in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Davao
del Sur, to be known as “Araw ng Sta. Cruz”, in commemoration of its
foundation anniversary. However, upon proclamation of the President,
October 5 of every year shall be a special non- working holiday in the
Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Davao del Sur.

 

 

Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
geneval circulation.
