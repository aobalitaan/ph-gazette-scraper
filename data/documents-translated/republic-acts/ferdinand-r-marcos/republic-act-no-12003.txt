AN ACT CREATING A DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE
IN THE THIRD LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT OF THE
PROVINCE OF BATAAN, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A district engineering office of the Department
of Public Works and Highways is hereby created in the Third
Legislative District, Province of Bataan, to be located in the
Municipality of Bagac, and referred to as the Bataan Third District
Engineering Office.

SEC. 2. The Bataan Third District Engineering Office shall
serve the Municipalities of Bagac, Dinalupihan, Mariveles and
Morong, all located in the Third Legislative District of the
Province of Bataan.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall
include in the Department's program the operationalization of
the Bataan Third District Engineering Office, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
‘general circulation.
