[ REPUBLIC Act No. 119981}

AN ACT RE-NUMBERING THE CEBU FIRST, SECOND, THIRD
AND FOURTH DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICES TO
CONFORM AND CORRESPOND, RESPECTIVELY,
WITH THE NUMBERING OF THE LEGISLATIVE
DISTRICTS IN THE PROVINCE OF CEBU THEREBY
REPEALING REPUBLIC ACTS NUMBERED 9043
AND 9218

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The current district engineering offices in the
Provinceof Cebu shall be renumbered accordingly to conform
and correspond, respectively, with the numbering of the four (4)

 

legislative districts of the province. Each district engineering office
shall serve the cities and municipalities under the respective
legislative district.

SEC. 2. The Cebu First District Engineering Office, to be
located within the First Legislative District of the Province of
Cebu, shall serve the Cities of Talisay, Carcar and Naga and
the Municipalities of Minglanilla, Sibonga and San Fernando.
The Cebu Second District Engineering Office, to be located within
the Second Legislative District of the Brovince of Cebu, shall serve
the Municipalities of Argao, Dalaguete, Alcoy, Baljoon, Oslob,
Santander and Samboan, The Cebu Third District Engineering
Office, to be located within the Third Legislative District of the
Province of Cebu, shall serve the City of Toledo and the
Municipalities of Aloguinsan, Asturias, Balamban, Barili,
Pinamungajan and Tuburan. Finally, the Cebu Fourth District
Engineering Office, to be located within the Fourth Legislative
District of the Province of Cebu, shall serve the City of Bogo and
the Municipalities of Bantayan, Daanbantayan, Madridejos,
Medellin, San Remegio, Santa Fe, Tabogon and Tabuelan.

SEC. 8. Within sixty (60) days from the effectivity of this
Act, the Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall issue the
necessary rules and regulations for the effective implementation
of this Act.

SEC. 4. Republic Act No. 9043, entitled “An Act Constituting
the Second Engineering District in the Province of Cebu, into
Two Separate and Distinct Engineering Districts, One to be
Located in the Municipality of Minglanilla and the Other in the
Municipalities of Argao, Both in the Province of Cebu”, and
Republic Act No. 9218, entitled “An Act Constituting the First
District Engineering Office in the Province of Cebu, into Two
Separate and Distinct Engineering Offices, One to be Located in
the City of Cebu and the Other in the City of Danao, Both in the
Province of Cebu’, are hereby repealed accordingly.

Suc. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general
circulation.

Approved,
