AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 7 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
CUENCA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, TO BE KNOWN
AS “CUENCA FOUNDATION DAY”, IN COMMEMORATION
OF ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November 7 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the Municipality of Cuenca, Province of
Batangas to be known as “Cuenca Foundation Day”, in commemoration
of its founding anniversary. Upon proclamation of the President,
November 7 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in
the Municipality of Cuenca, Province of Batangas.
