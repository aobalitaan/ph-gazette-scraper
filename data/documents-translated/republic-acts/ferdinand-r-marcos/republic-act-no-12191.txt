[ REPUBLIC AcT No.12191 ]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF BAYAMBANG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, INTO
A CLASS “C” DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The extension office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Bayambang, Province of
Pangasinan, is hereby converted into a Class “CO” district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in the
Department’s program the operationalization of the Class “C”
LTO District Office in the Municipality of Bayambang, Province
of Pangasinan, the initial funding of which shall be charged against

the current year’s appropriation of the LTO Bayambang Extension
Office. Thereafter, the funding shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper ‘of
general circulation.
