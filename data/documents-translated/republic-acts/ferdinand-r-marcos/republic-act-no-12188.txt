AN ACT CONVERTING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN SORSOGON CITY,
PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, INTO A CLASS “B” DISTRICT
OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in Sorsogon City, Province of Sorsogon, is hereby
converted into a Class “B” district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in the
Department’s program the operationalization of the Class “B”
LTO District Office in Sorsogon City, Province of Sorsogon, the initial
funding of which shall be charged against the current year’s
appropriation of the said office. Thereafter, the funding of which shall
be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

PUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a national newspaper of
general circulation.
