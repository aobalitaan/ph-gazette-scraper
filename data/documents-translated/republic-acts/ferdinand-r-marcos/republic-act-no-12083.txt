AN ACT SEPARATING THE BUCANA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL—
HATIB ASAMUDDIN EXTENSION IN BARANGAY BUCANA,
MUNICIPALITY OF SIOCON, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL NORTE FROM THE BUCANA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL,
CONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT ELEMENTARY
SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE HATIB ASAMUDDIN
ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS
THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

‘

SECTION 1. The Bucana Elementary School — Hatib
Asamuddin Extension in Barangay Bucana, Municipality of Siocon,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte is hereby separated from the
Bucana Elementary School and converted into an independent
elementary school to be known as the Hatib Asamuddin
Elementary School.

SEC. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the
Bucana Elementary School — Hatib Asamuddin Extension are
hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Hatib Asamuddin
Elementary School.

SEC, 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Hatib
Asamuddin Elementary School, the initial funding of which shall
be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the Bucana
Elementary School — Hatib Asamuddin Extension, Thereafter, the
amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules
and regulations shall provide for the school’s organization,
administration, operations and management, in accordance with
Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of Basic Education Act
of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the “Enhanced Basic Education
Act of 2018”, and other basic education-related laws.

Sec. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
