AN ACT UPGRADING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
AGOO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION, FROM A CLASS “C” TO
A CLASS “B” DISTRICT OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union, is
hereby upgraded from a Class “C” to a Class “B” district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in-the
Department's program the operationalization of the LTO Agoo District
Office in the Province of La Union, the initial funding of which shall
be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the said office.
Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.
