AN ACT CREATING A DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE
IN THE SECOND LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT OF THE
PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, A district engineering office of the Department
of Public Works and Highways is hereby created in the Second
Legislative District, Province of Davao del Norte, to be located in
the Municipality of Carmen, and referred to as the Davao del Norte
Second District Engineering Office.

SEC. 2. The Davao del Norte Second District Engineering
Office shall serve the City of Panabo, the Island Garden City of
Samal and the municipalities of B.E. Dujali, Carmen and Santo
Tomas, all located in the Second Legislative District of the Province of
Davao del Norte.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
Davao del Norte Second District Engineering Office, the funding of
which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Src. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
