[REPUBLIC ACT No. 712

AN ACT UPGRADING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE CITY OF
DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, FROM
CLASS “D” TO CLASS “C” AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation Office
(LTO).in the City of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur is hereby upgraded
from a Class “D” to a Class “C” district office.

Sec. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department's program the upgrading of the regular
LTO District Office in the City of Digos, the initial funding of which
shall be charged against the current year’s appropriation of LTO
Region XI. Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

 

 

: SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
