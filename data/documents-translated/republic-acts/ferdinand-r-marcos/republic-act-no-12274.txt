[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12274 ]

AN ACT DECLARING APRIL 1 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO,
TO BE KNOWN AS “ARAW NG STA. CRUZ’, IN
COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. April 1 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz,
Province of Occidental Mindoro, to be known as “Araw ng
Sta. Cruz’, in commemoration of its foundation anniversary.
Upon proclamation of the President, April 1 of every year shall
be a special non-working holiday in the said municipality.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
