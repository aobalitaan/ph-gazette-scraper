[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11947]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE BUKID ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
IN BARANGAY BUKID, MUNICIPALITY OF JOSE
ABAD SANTOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO OCCIDENTAL
INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN
AS THE BUKID INTEGRATED SCHOOL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Bukid Elementary School in Barangay
Bukid, Municipality of Jose Abad Santos, Province of Davao
Occidental is hereby converted into an integrated school to
be known as the Bukid Integrated School.

Src. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of
the Bukid Elementary School are hereby transferred to and
absorbed by the Bukid Integrated School.

Src. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the Bukid Integrated School, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

/ Src. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of
this Act, the Secretary of Education shall formulate the
rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act.
Such implementing rules and regulations shall provide for
the school’s organization, administration, operations and
management, in accordance with Republic Act No. 9155, or
the “Governance of Basic Education Act of 2001,” Republic ‘Act
No. 10533, or the “Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013,”
and other basic education-related laws.

/ SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
