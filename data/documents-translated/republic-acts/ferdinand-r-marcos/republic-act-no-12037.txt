[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12037]

AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 2 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF
LA UNION TO BE KNOWN AS “LA UNION DAY”, IN
COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. March 2 of every year is hereby declared a special
nonworking holiday in the Province of La Union to be known as
“La Union Day”, in commemoration of its founding anniversary.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
