[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12132 ]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE LOCATED IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LINGAYEN, PROVINCE OF
PANGASINAN, INTO A CLASS “B” DISTRICT OFFICE,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Lingayen, Province of Pangasinan,
is hereby upgraded into a Class “B” district office.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in the
Department’s program the operationalization of the upgrading
into,a Class “B” district office of the LTO District Office in the
Municipality of Lingayen, Province of Pangasinan, the initial

 

funding of which shall be charged against the current year’
appropriation of the LTO Lingayen District Office. Thereafter
the funding of which shall be included in the annual Genera
Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days afte:
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper o
general circulation.
