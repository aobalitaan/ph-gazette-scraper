AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTO
TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, A
MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE SANTO
TOMAS MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING

FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in

Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the Municipality of
Santo Tomas, Province of Davao del Norte, a municipal hospital to be
known as the Santo Tomas Municipal Hospital, which shall be under
the direct control and supervision of the Provincial Government of

Davao del Norte.

Src. 2. Any future increase in bed capacity, healthcare
facilities, service capability, and human resource complement shall
be based on the hospital development plan that shall be prepared by
the Chief of Hospital, in consultation with the Department of Health.

SEc. 3. The Provincial Government of Davao del Norte shall
provide for the necessary funds for the establishment of the Santo
Tomas Municipal Hospital. The Secretary of Health shall include in
the Department’s subsidy program, support for the capital outlay

requirements of the said hospital.

Src. 4. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall
remain in full force and effect.

Suc. 5. All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or modified
accordingly.

SEC. 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
