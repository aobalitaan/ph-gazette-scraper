[ REPUBLIC ACT NO.42443 ]

AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 7 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF
BASILAN, INCOMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION
ANNIVERSARY TO BE KNOWN AS “ARAW NG
BASILAN”, AND IN RECOGNITION OF THE “TENNUN
PAKARADJAAN” AS THE OFFICIAL FESTIVAL OF THE
PROVINCE OF BASILAN

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. March 7 of every year is hereby declared a special
non-working holiday in the Province of Basilan, in commemoration of
its foundation anniversary, to be known as “Araw ng Basilan”, and in
recognition of the “Tennun Pakaradjaan’” as the official festival of the
Province of Basilan.

Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
