AN ACT UPGRADING THE DR. CATALINO GALLEGO NAVA
PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF JORDAN, PROVINCE OF GUIMARAS, INTO
A LEVEL II HOSPITAL, RENAMING IT AS THE
DR. CATALINO GALLEGO NAVA MEDICAL CENTER,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, The Dr. Catalino Gallego Nava Provincial Hospital
in the Municipality of Jordan, Province of Guimaras is hereby upgraded
into a Level IT hospital and renamed as the Dr. Catalino Gallego Nava
Medical Center.

SEC. 2. The authorized bed capacity of the Dr. Catalino Gallego
Nava Medical Center shall be increased from twenty-five (25) to two
hundred (200) beds. Any future increase in bed capacity shall be
consistent with the hospital development plan that shall be prepared

by the Chief of Hospital of the Dr. Catalino Gallego Nava Medical
Center, and submitted to the Department of Health.

SEC. 3. The existing professional health care services and
facilities of the Dr. Catalino Gallego Nava Medical Center shall also
be upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to its upgrading
as a Level II hospital and the increase in bed capacity as provided for
in Sections 1 and 2 hereof.

Src. 4. The existing workforce of the Dr. Catalino Gallego Nava
Medical Center, which includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel,
shall be increased correspondingly.

Sec. 5. The Provincial Government of Guimaras shall provide
the necessary funds to cover the maintenance and other operating
expenses of the Dr. Catalino Gallego Nava Medical Center. It shall
also appropriate funds for the additional human resource complement
to support the increase in bed capacity and the corresponding upgrading
ofits service facilities and professional health care services.

SEc. 6. The Secretary of Health shall include in the
Department’s subsidy program, support for the capital outlay
requirements of the Dr. Catalino Gallego Nava Medical Center.

Sec. 7. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected
shall remain in full force and effect.

Sc. 8. All laws, executive orders, issuances and rules
and regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

Suc. 9. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
