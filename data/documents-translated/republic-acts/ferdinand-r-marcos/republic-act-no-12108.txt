AN ACT ESTABLISHING A COASTAL AQUACULTURE
CENTER TO SUPPORT AND REHABILITATE THE
BUREAU OF FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCES
NORTHERN MINDANAO BRACKISH WATER
AQUACULTURE FISH FARM IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF LALA, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the supervision
of the Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources of the Department
of Agriculture (DA-BFAR), a coastal aquaculture center to further
enhance the operations of the BFAR Northern Mindanao Brackish
Water Aquaculture Fish Farm in the Municipality of Lala, Province
of Lanao del Norte.

SEC. 2. Prior to the establishment of the coastal aquaculture
center, the DA-BFAR shall conduct a full-scale feasibility study
to comply with the requirements of the Department of Budget
and Management.

The DA-BFAR shall conduct continuing technological verification
and experimentation on coastal aquaculture, especially as they apply
to local conditions, and to discover new methods and technology that
will rejuvenate the fishing industry.

Suc. 8. The Secretary of Agriculture shall include in the
Department’s programs the conduct of the feasibility study and the
consequent establishment of the coastal aquaculture center, and the
rehabilitation of the BFAR Northern Mindanao Brackish Water
Aquaculture Fish Farm in the Municipality of Lala, Province of Lanao
del Norte, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEc. 4, Within sixty (60) days from the effectivity of this Act,
the Secretary of Agriculture shall promulgate the necessary rules and
regulations to implement the provisions of this Act.

Src. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
