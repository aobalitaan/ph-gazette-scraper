AN ACT CONVERTING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN NAGA CITY,
PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR INTO A REGULAR
CLASS “A” DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the City of Naga, Province of Camarines Sur, is hereby
converted from a Class “C” into a Class “A” district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
shall immediately include in the Department’s program the
operationalization of the Class “A” LTO District Office in the
City of Naga, Province of Camarines Sur, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
