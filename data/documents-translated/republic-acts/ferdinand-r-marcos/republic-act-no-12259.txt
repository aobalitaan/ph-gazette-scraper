[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 422595 ]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE PATAG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY PATAG, MUNICIPALITY OF IROSIN, PROVINCE
OF SORSOGON INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE
ENOWN AS THE PATAG INTEGRATED SCHOOL AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacied by the Senate and House of Represeniatives of ihe Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, The Patag Hlementary School in Barangay Patag,
Municipality of Ivosin, Province of Sorsogon is hereby converted into
an integrated school to be known as the Patag Integrated School.

SBe. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the Patag
Elementary School ave hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Patag
Integrated School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Hducation shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the opevationalization of the Patag
Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules
and regulations shall provide for the school’s organization,
administration, operations, and management, in accordance with
Republic Act No. 9155 or the “Governance of Basic Education Act
of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533 or the “Enhanced Basic Education
Act of 2013”, and other basic education-related laws.

Src. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
