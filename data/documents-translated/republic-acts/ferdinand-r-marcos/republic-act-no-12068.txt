[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12068]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE BATAAN GENERAL HOSPITAL
AND MEDICAL CENTER IN THE CITY OF BALANGA,
PROVINCE OF BATAAN, TO A MULTI-SPECIALTY
HOSPITAL, INCREASING ITS BED CAPACITY FROM
FIVE HUNDRED (500) TO ONE THOUSAND (1,000) BEDS,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Department of Health (DOH) is hereby
mandated to upgrade the Bataan General Hospital and Medical
Center (BGHMO), which is located in the City of Balanga, Province
of Bataan, into a multi-specialty hospital.

Suc. 2. The authorized bed capacity of the BGHMC is hereby
increased from five hundred (500) to one thousand (1,000) beds.

 

.

S&c. 3. The existing professional health care services
and facilities of the BGHMC shall be upgraded to conform with and be
commensurate to the upgrading of its specialty capability and increase
in bed capacity as provided for in Sections 1 and 2 hereof.

Sc. 4. The existing workforce of the BGHMOC, which
includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel, shall be
increased correspondingly.

Src. 5. The Secretary of Health shall, after consultation
with the Medical Center Chief of the BGHMC, and in coordination
with the Secretary of the Department of Budget and Management
and the Chairperson of the Civil Service Commission, determine
the additional new positions to be created.

Sic. 6. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the implementation of this Act,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Sec. 7. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall

remain in full force and effect.

Sec. 8. All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules
and regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed
or modified accordingly.

SEc. 9. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.

Approved,
