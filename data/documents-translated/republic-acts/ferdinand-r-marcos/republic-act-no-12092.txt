AN ACT CONVERTING THE CALATEO ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
IN BARANGAY CALATEO, MUNICIPALITY OF JUBAN,
PROVINCE OF SORSOGON INTO AN INTEGRATED
SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE CALATEO INTEGRATED
SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Calateo Elementary School in Barangay
Calateo, Municipality of Juban, Province of Sorsogon is hereby
converted into an integrated school to be known as the Calateo
Integrated School.

Suc. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the
Calateo Elementary School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by
the Calateo Integrated School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immedi ately include
in the Department's program the operationalization of the Calateo
Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

’

\ SEc. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules
and regulations shall provide for the school’s organization,

: administration, operations and management, in accordance with

| Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of Basic Education Act
of 2001”, Republic Act No. 105383, or the “Enhanced Basic Education
Act of 2013”, and other basic education-related laws.

Sec. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
