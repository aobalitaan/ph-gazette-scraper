[ REPUBLIC AcT No.12301 ]

AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 17 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
MAGSAYSAY, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, IN
COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June 17 of every year is hereby declared a special
working holiday in the Municipality of Magsaysay, Province of Davao
del Sur, in commemoration of its foundation anniversary. However,
upon proclamation of the President, June 17 of every year shall be a
special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Magsaysay,
Province of Davao del Sur.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
