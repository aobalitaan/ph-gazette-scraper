AN ACT UPGRADING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE CITY OF ALAMINOS,
PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, FROM A CLASS “D” TO A
CLASS “B” DISTRICT OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the City of Alaminos, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby
upgraded from a Class “D” to a Class “B” LTO district office.

Sc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the upgrading of the LTO
District Office in the City of Alaminos, Province of Pangasinan,
the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current

year’s appropriations of the existing LTO district office. Thereafter,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
