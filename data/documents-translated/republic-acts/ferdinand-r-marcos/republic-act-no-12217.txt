[REPUBLIC AcT No. 12217]

AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 14 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO, IN
CELEBRATION OF THE “FEAST DAY OF THE HOLY CROSS”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 14 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of
Occidental Mindoro, in celebration of the “Feast Day of the Holy Cross”.
Upon proclamation of the President, September 14 of every year shall
be a special non-working holiday in the said municipality.

; SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
