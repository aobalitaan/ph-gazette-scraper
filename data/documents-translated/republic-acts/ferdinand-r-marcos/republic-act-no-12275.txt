AN ACT DECLARING THE KUYAMIS FESTIVAL AS
THE OFFICIAL FESTIVAL GF THE PROVINCE OF
MISAMIS ORIENTAL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION i. The Kuyamis Festival is hereby declared the official
festival of the Province of Misamis Oriental.

Smc. 2. The second Friday of January of every year is hereby
declared a special non-working holiday in the province in celebration
of the Kuyamis Festival.

SEC. 3. To carvy out the purpose of this Act, there is hereby
created a Special Festival Fund to be charged against any available
fund of the province, including its shave from the National Tax

 

Allotment. The Province of Misamis Oriental is also authorized to
receive donations and contributions from private individuals,
corporations, and non-government organizations,

Src. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
