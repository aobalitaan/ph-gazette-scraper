[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12088]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY SUROP, MUNICIPALITY OF GOVERNOR
GENEROSO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL TO BE
KNOWN AS THE SUROP NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the supervision
of the Department of Education, a national high school in Barangay
Surop, Municipality of Governor Generoso, Province of Davao
Oriental to be known as the Surop National High School.

Sec. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization of the
Surop National High School, the funding of which shall be included
in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules
and regulations shall provide for the school’s organization,
administration, operations and management, in accordance with
Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of Basic Education Act
of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10538, or the “Enhanced Basic Education
Act of 2018”, and other basic education-related laws.

Src. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
