AN ACT UPGRADING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE CITY OF MATI,
PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, FROM CLASS “D” TO
CLASS “C” AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the City of Mati, Province of Davao Oriental, is hereby
upgraded from a Class “D” to a Class “C” district office.

Ssc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of a
Class “C” LTO District Office in the City of Mati, Province of Davao
Oriental, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the
current year’s appropriation of LTO Region XI. Thereafter, the funding
of which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
