[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11940]

AN ACT EXTENDING THE TERM OF OFFICE OF THE
PRESIDENT OF THE ADIONG MEMORIAL STATE
COLLEGE FROM THREE (3) YEARS TO FOUR (4) YEARS
IN ACCORDANCE WITH REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8292,
OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE “HIGHER EDUCATION
MODERNIZATION ACT OF 1997”, FURTHER
AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT
NO. 7935, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8651

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

 

SECTION 1. Section 9 of Republic Act No. 7935, as amended
by Republic Act No. 8651, is hereby further amended to read
as follows:

“Sec. 9. The State College shall be headed
by a president who shall render full-time service.
The president shall be appointed by the Board,
subject to its guidelines, qualifications, and
standards and upon the recommendation of its
duly constituted search committee, for a term of
four (4) years, eligible for reappointment for
another term.
‘Sex a
SEc. 2. The Governing Board of the State College, in
consultation with the Commission on Higher Education, shall
formulate the guidelines to fully implement the provisions of this
Act within ninety (90) days from its effectivity.

SEc. 3. The provisions of Republic Act No. 8292, otherwise
known as the “Higher Education Modernization Act of 1997”, shall
form part of this Act.

Sec. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
