[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12038]

AN ACT DECLARING AUGUST 11 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SAMAR
TO BE KNOWN AS “SAMAR DAY”, IN COMMEMORATION
OF ITS FOUNDATION DAY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. August 11 of every year is hereby declared a
special nonworking holiday in the Province of Samar to be known as
“Samar Day”, in commemoration of its Foundation Day.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
