[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 12081]

AN ACT SEPARATING THE BINAGASBASAN NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL - DENRICA HIGH SCHOOL ANNEX
IN BARANGAY DENRICA, MUNICIPALITY OF
GARCHITORENA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR FROM
THE BINAGASBASAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL,
CONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE DENRICA
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Binagasbasan National High School —
Denrica High School Annex in Barangay Denrica, Municipality of
Garchitorena, Province of Camarines Sur is hereby separated from
the Binagasbasan National High School and converted into an
independent national high school to be known as the Denrica
National High School.

SEc. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the
Binagasbasan National High School — Denrica High School Annex
are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Denrica National
High School.

SEc. 8. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department's program the operationalization of the Denrica
National High School, the initial funding of which shall be charged
against the current year’s appropriation of the Binagasbasan
National High School — Denrica High School Annex. Thereafter,
the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall
be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules
and regulations shall provide for the school’s organization,
administration, operations and management, in accordance with
Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of Basic Education Act
of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the “Enhanced Basic Education
Act of 2013”, and other basic education-related laws.

SEc. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
