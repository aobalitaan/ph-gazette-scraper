[ REPUBLIC Act No. 12084]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF NEW PARTIDO HIGH
SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CATAGBACAN, MUNICIPALITY
OF GOA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO NEW
PARTIDO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of New Partido High School in
Barangay Catagbacan, Municipality of Goa, Province of Camarines
Sur is hereby changed to New Partido National High School.

 

Suc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after

general circulation.
