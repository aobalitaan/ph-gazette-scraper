AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 21 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY
OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, IN
COMMEMORATION OF ITS CHARTER ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. March 21 of every year is hereby declared a special
non-working holiday in the City of Calapan, Province of Oriental
Mindoro, in commemoration of its Charter Anniversary.

Src. 2. Repealing Clause. — Republic Act No. 8986
declaring March 21 as a working holiday in the City of Calapan
is hereby repealed.

 

SEc. 8. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
