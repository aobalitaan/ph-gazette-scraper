[REPUBLIC AcT No. 12218]

AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 6 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN QUEZON CITY, TO BE KNOWN
AS “TANDANG SORA DAY’, IN COMMEMORATION OF
THE BIRTH ANNIVERSARY OF MELCHORA AQUINO,
ALSO KNOWN IN PHILIPPINE HISTORY AS THE
“MOTHER OF THE KATIPUNAN”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. January 6 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in Quezon City, to be known as “Tandang
Sora Day”, in commemoration of the birth anniversary of Melchora
Aquino, also known in Philippine history as the “Mother of the
Katipunan”. Upon proclamation of the President, January 6 of every
year shall be a special non-working holiday in Quezon City in
commemoration of the said anniversary.

 

SEc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
