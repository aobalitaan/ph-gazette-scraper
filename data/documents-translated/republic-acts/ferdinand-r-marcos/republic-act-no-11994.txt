AN ACT CREATING AN ADDITIONAL DISTRICT
ENGINEERING OFFICE IN ZAMBOANGA CITY,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A new district engineering office of the
Department of Public Works and Highways is hereby created in
Zamboanga City which shall be known as the Zamboanga City
First District Engineering Office. The existing District
Engineering Office in Zamboanga City, located in Barangay
Divisoria, shall now be known as the Zamboanga City Second
District Engineering Office.

SEC. 2. The Zamboanga City First District Engineering
Office shall be located in the Zamboanga City Special Economic
Zone - Zamboanga Freeport Authority in Barangay Talisayan
and shall serve the barangays of Ayala, Baliwasan, Baluno,

SEC. 8. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways
shall include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of Zamboanga City First and Second District Engineering
Offices, the funding ef which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

Ssc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
