[ REPUBLIC Act No. 12198]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY LATUAN, ZAMBOANGA CITY TO BE KNOWN
AS THE LATUAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the supervision
of the Department of Education, a national high school in Barangay
Latuan, Zamboanga City to be known as the Latuan National
High School.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Latuan
National High School, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

Suc. 3. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules
