[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12202]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA,
PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, A GENERAL HOSPITAL TO BE
KNOWN AS CALAMBA CITY GENERAL HOSPITAL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the City of Calamba,
Province of Laguna, a general hospital to be known as the Calamba
City General Hospital (CCGH), which shall be under the direct
control and supervision of the City Government of Calamba, Province
of Laguna.

SEc. 2. Any future increase in bed capacity, healthcare
facilities, service capability, and human resource complement shall
be based on the hospital development plan that shall be prepared by
the Chief of Hospital, in consultation with the Department of Health.

SEC. 3. The City Government of Calamba, Province of
Laguna, shall provide the necessary funds for the establishment
of the CCGH. The Secretary of Health shall include in the
Department’s subsidy program, support for the capital outlay
requirements of the said hospital.

Suc. 4. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall
remain in full force and effect.

SEc. 5. All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly. “

Src. 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
