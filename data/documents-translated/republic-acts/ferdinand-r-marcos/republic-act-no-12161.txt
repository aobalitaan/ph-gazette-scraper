[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12161]

AN ACT CREATING A CLASS “D” DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF NABUA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation. — A regular Class “D” District Office
of the Land Transportation Office is hereby created in the Municipality
of Nabua, Province of Camarines Sur.

SEC. 2. Funding. — The Secretary of the Department of
Transportation shall immediately include in the Department’s program
the operationalization of the LTO District Office in the Municipality
of Nabua, Province of Camarines Sur, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

SEC. 3. Effectivity.—This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
after its publication in the Official Gazette or in any newspaper of

general circulation.
