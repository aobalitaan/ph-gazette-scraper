[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12082]

AN ACT SEPARATING THE SIOCON CENTRAL SCHOOL ~ J.P.
BRILLANTES EXTENSION IN BARANGAY JOSE P.
BRILLANTES, SR., MUNICIPALITY OF SIOCON, PROVINCE

-OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE FROM THE SIOCON
CENTRAL SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO AN
INDEPENDENT ELEMENTARY SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN
AS THE JOSE P. BRILLANTES ELEMENTARY SCHOOL,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled: .

SECTION 1. The Siocon Central School — J.P. Brillantes
Extension in Barangay Jose P, Brillantes, Sr., Municipality of Siocon,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte is hereby separated from the Siocon
Central School and converted into an independent elementary school
to be known as the Jose P. Brillantes Elementary School.

SEC. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the
Siocon Central School —J.P. Brillantes Extension are hereby transferred
to and absorbed by the Jose P. Brillantes Elementary School,

SEc. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Jose P,
Brillantes Elementary School, the initial funding of which shall be
charged against the current year’s appropriation of the Siocon Central
School-—J.P. Brillantes Extension. Thereafter, the amount necessary
for the continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act,

SEC. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules
and regulations shall provide for the school's organization,
administration, operations and management, in accordance with
Republic Act No, 9155, or the “Governance of Basic Education Act of
2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the “Enhanced Basic Education Act
of 2013”, and other basic education-related laws.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
