| REPUBLIC ACT No. 12183]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE
(LTO) DAPA DISTRICT OFFICE INTO A CLASS “C”
LTO DISTRICT OFFICE, RENAMING THE SAME AS
SIARGAO LTO DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Land Transportation Office (LTO)
district office in the Municipality of Dapa, Province of Surigao
del Norte (LTO Dapa District Office) is hereby upgraded into a Class “C”
LTO District Office and renamed as Siargao LTO District Office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
Siargao LTO District Office in the First District of the Province of
Surigao del Norte, the initial funding of which shall be charged against
the current year’s appropriation of the LTO Dapa District Office.

Thereafter, such sum as may be necessary for the continued operation
of the Siargao LTO District Office shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

SkEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
