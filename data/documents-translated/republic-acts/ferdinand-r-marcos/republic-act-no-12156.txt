[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12156]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE (LTO) EXTENSION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF BUUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY, INTO A
REGULAR LTO DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Land Transportation Office (LTO) Extension
Office in the Municipality of Buug, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay,
is hereby converted into a regular LTO district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in the
Department’s program the operationalization of the LTO District Office
in the Municipality of Buug, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay, the
initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year’s
