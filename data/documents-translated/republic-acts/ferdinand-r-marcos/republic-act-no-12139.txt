[ REPUBLIC ACT No.4 2139 ]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF ROSALES, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, INTO A
CLASS “A” DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Rosales, Province of Pangasinan,
is hereby converted into a Class “A” district office,

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation shall
immediately include in the Department’s program the
operationalization of the Class “A” LTO District Office in the
Municipality of Rosales, Province of Pangasinan, the initial funding
of which shall be charged against the current year’s appropriation
of the LTO Rosales District Office. Thereafter, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general

circulation.
