AN ACT UPGRADING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE LOCATED IN
LAOAG CITY, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, INTO A
CLASS “A” DISTRICT OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in Laoag City, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby upgraded
into a Class “A” district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in the
Department’s program the operationalization of the Class “A” LTO
District Office in Laoag City, Province of Ilocos Norte, the initial funding
of which shall be charged against the current year’s appropriation of
the LTO Laoag District Office. Thereafter, the funding of which shall
be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

 

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
