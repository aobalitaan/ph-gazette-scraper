[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12281}

AN ACT RENAMING BARANGAY NEW ALABANG VILLAGE
IN THE CITY OF MUNTINLUPA AS BARANGAY
AYALA ALABANG

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Barangay New Alabang Village in the City of
Muntinlupa is hereby renamed as Barangay Ayala Alabang.

SEC. 2. The renaming of Barangay New Alabang Village as
Barangay Ayala Alabang shall be effective upon ratification of the
majority of the votes cast by qualified voters in a plebiscite to be
conducted and supervised by the Commission on Elections in the present
Barangay New Alabang Village within one hundred twenty (120) days
after the effectivity of this Act.

The expenses for such plebiscite shall be borne by the City
of Muntinlupa.

 

Sec. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
