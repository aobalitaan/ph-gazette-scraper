[REPUBLIC ACT No. 12190 ]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE CLASSIFICATION OF THE PARTIDO
DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIGAON, PROVINCE
OF CAMARINES SUR, FROM CLASS “D” TO CLASS “C” AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, The Partido District Office of the Land
Transportation Office (LTO) located in the Municipality of Tigaon,
Province of Camarines Sur, is hereby upgraded from a Class “D” toa
Class “C” district office.

Sic. 2. The Secretary of 'I'ransportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the LTO
Partido District Office in the Municipality of Tigaon, Province of
Camarines Sur, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.
