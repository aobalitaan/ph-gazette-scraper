[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 12176]

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE TOMAS
LACHICA DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
JOSE ABAD SANTOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO OCCIDENTAL,
FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) TO FIFTY (50) BEDS, AND

APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in

Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The authorized bed capacity of the Tomas Lachica
District Hospital in the Municipality of Jose Abad Santos, Province
of Davao Occidental, shall be increased from twenty-five (25) to
fifty (50) beds. Any future increase in bed capacity shall be consistent
with the hospital development plan that shall be prepared by the Chief
of Hospital of the Tomas Lachica District Hospital and submitted to

the Department of Health.

Sic. 2. The existing professional healthcare services and
facilities of the Tomas Lachica District Hospital shall also be upgraded
to conform with and be commensurate to the increase in bed capacity
as provided in Section 1 hereof.

SEC. 8. The existing workforce of the Tomas Lachica District
Hospital, which includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel, shall
be increased correspondingly.

Src. 4. The Provincial Government of Davao Occidental shall
provide the necessary funds to cover the maintenance and other
operating expenses of the Tomas Lachica District Hospital. It shall
also appropriate funds for the additional human resource complement
to support the increase in bed capacity and the corresponding upgrading
ofits service facilities and professional healthcare services.

Src. 5. The Secretary of Health shall include in the
Department’s subsidy program, support for the capital outlay
requirements of the Tomas Lachica District Hospital.

SEC. 6. Ifany provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional,
the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall remain in full
force and effect.

Src. 7. All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

SEC. 8. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
