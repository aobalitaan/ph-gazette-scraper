[REPUBLIC Act No. 12258 ]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL,
TO BE KNOWN AS THE TANAY NATIONAL SCIENCE HIGH
SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be ii enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, There is hereby established, under the supervision
of the Department of Education, a national science high school in the
Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, to be known as the Tanay
National Science High School.

Suc. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Tanay
National Science High School, the funding of which shall be included
in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 8. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules

and regulations shall provide for the school’s organization,
administration, operations, and management, in accordance with
Republic Act No. 9155 or the “Governance of Basic Education Act
of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10538 or the “Enhanced Basic Education
Act of 2013”, and other basic education-related laws.

Src. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazetie or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
