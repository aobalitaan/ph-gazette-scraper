[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12172]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
NAIC, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT
OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The extension office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) located in the Municipality of Naic, Province of Cavite, is
hereby converted into a regular district office.

SEc, 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization ofthe LTO
District Office in the Municipality of Naic, Province of Cavite, the
initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year’s
appropriation of the LTO Extension Office in the Municipality of Naic.
Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

 

; SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
