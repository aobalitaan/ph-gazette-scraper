AN ACT SEPARATING THE SAN PABLO NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL -—SACBULAN EXTENSION IN BARANGAY
SACBULAN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE
OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR FROM THE SAN PABLO
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO
AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE
KNOWN AS THE SACBULAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The San Pablo National High School ~ Sacbulan
Extension in Barangay Sacbulan, Municipality of San Pablo,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur is hereby separated from the
San Pablo National High School and converted into an independent
national high school to be known as the Sacbulan National High School.

Src. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the
San Pablo National High School — Sacbulan Extension are hereby
transferred to and absorbed by the Sacbulan National High School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
Sacbulan National High School, the initial funding of which shall
be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the San
Pablo National High School — Sacbulan Extension. Thereafter, the
amount necessary for the continued operation of the school shall
be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing
rules and regulations shall provide for the school’s organization,
administration, operations and management, in accordance with
Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of Basic Education Act
of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the “Enhanced Basic Education
Act of 2013”, and other basic education-related laws.

Src. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
