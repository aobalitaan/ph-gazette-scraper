AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE LOCATED IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF RODRIGUEZ (MONTALBAN),
PROVINCE OF RIZAL, INTO A DISTRICT OFFICE TO
BE KNOWN AS THE LTO MONTALBAN DISTRICT OFFICE
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The extension office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Rodriguez (Montalban), Province
of Rizal, is hereby converted into a district office to be known as the
LTO Montalban District Office. ‘

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization of the LTO
Montalban District Office. Thereafter, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
