[ REPUBLIC ACT No.12004 ]

AN ACT CREATING A DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE
IN THE THIRD LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT OF THE.
PROVINCE OF BULACAN, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1, A district engineering office of the Department
of Public Works and Highways is hereby created in the Third
Legislative District, Province of Bulacan, to be located in the
Municipality of San Rafael, and referred to as the Bulacan Third
District Engineering Office.

SEC. 2. The Bulacan Third District Engineering Office
shall serve the municipalities of San Rafael, Dofia Remedios
Trinidad, San Miguel and San Ildefonso, all located in the
Third Legislative District of the Province of Bulacan.


SEC. 3. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the Bulacan Third District Engineering Office, the funding of
which shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act,

SEc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
