[REPUBLIC AcT No. 12105]

AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 8 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAVIA,
PROVINCE OF ILOILO, TO BE KNOWN AS “PAVIA DAY”,
TO COMMEMORATE ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. January 8 of every year is hereby declared a special
working holiday in the Municipality of Pavia, Province of Iloilo, to be
known as “Pavia Day”, to commemorate its founding anniversary.
Upon proclamation of the President, January 8 of every year shall be
aspecial non-working holiday in the Municipality of Pavia, Province
of Hoilo.

 

 

Src. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
