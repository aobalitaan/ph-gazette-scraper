[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11943]

AN ACT SEPARATING THE MARIANO PERALTA NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL - DATU DANWATA EXTENSION IN
BARANGAY DATU DANWATA, MUNICIPALITY OF
MALITA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO OCCIDENTAL FROM
THE MARIANO PERALTA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL,
CONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE GASPAR
DANWATA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Mariano Peralta National High
School — Datu Danwata Extension in Barangay Datu Danwata,
Municipality of Malita, Province of Davao Occidental is hereby
separated from the Mariano Peralta National High School
and converted into an independent national high school to be
known as the Gaspar Danwata National High School.
