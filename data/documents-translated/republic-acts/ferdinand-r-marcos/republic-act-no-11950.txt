AN ACT CONVERTING THE HIBAO-AN ELEMENTARY
SCHOOL IN BARANGAY HIBAO-AN_ SUR,
MANDURRIAO DISTRICT, ILOILO CITY INTO AN
INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS
THE HIBAO-AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Hibao-an Elementary School in Barangay
Hibao-an Sur, Mandurriao District, Iloilo City is hereby converted
into an integrated school to be known as the Hibao-an
Integrated School.

Sc. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the
Hibao-an Elementary School are hereby transferred to and
absorbed by the Hibao-an Integrated School.

Src. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the Hibao-an Integrated School, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this
Act, the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and
regulations implementing the provisions of this Act. Such
implementing rules and regulations shall provide for the school’s
organization, administration, operations and management, in
accordance with Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of
Basic Education Act of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the
“Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013”, and other basic
education-related laws.

Src. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
