[ REPUBLIC ACT No. $2273 ]

AN ACT DECLARING APRIL 24 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
SULOP, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, TO BE KNOWN
AS “ARAW NG SULOP’, IN COMMEMORATION OF
ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. April 24 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the Municipality of Sulop, Province of
Davac del Sur, to be known as “Araw ng Sulop”, in commemoration
of its foundation anniversary. However, upon proclamation of the
President, April 24 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday
in the Municipality of Sulop, Province of Davao del Sur.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
