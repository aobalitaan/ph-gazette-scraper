[ REPUBLIC ACT NO.12433 ]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF NAGUILIAN, PROVINCE OF LA UNION, INTO A
“CLASS D” DISTRICT OFFICE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE LTO
DISTRICT OFFICE OF NAGUILIAN, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Land Transportation Office (LTO) Extension
Office in the Municipality of Naguilian, Province of La Union, is hereby
converted into a “Class D” district office, to be known as the LTO
District Office of Naguilian.

«, SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
shall immediately include in the Department's program the
operationalization of the “Class D” LTO District Office in the

 

 

Municipality of Naguilian, Province of La Union, the initial
funding of which shall be charged against the current year’s
appropriation of the existing LTO Naguilian Extension Office.
Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

Sc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
