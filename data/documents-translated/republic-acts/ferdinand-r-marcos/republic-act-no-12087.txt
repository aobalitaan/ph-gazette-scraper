AN ACT SEPARATING THE PAULINO DARI NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL ~ LIGUAC EXTENSION IN BARANGAY
LIGUAC, MUNICIPALITY OF PITOGO, PROVINCE
OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR FROM THE PAULINO
DARI NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO
AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE
KNOWN AS THE LIGUAC NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Paulino Dari National High School — Liguac
Extension in Barangay Liguac, Municipality of Pitogo, Province
of Zamboanga del Sur is hereby separated from the Paulino Dari
National High School and converted into an independent national
high school to be known as the Liguac National High School.

Sec. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the
Paulino Dari National High School — Liguac Extension are hereby
transferred to and absorbed by the Liguac National High School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
Liguac National High School, the initial funding of which shall be
charged against the current year’s appropriation of the Paulino
Dari National High School — Liguac Extension. Thereafter,
the amount necessary for the continued operation of the school
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of
this Act, the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and
regulations implementing the provisions of this Act. Such
implementing rules and regulations shall provide for the school’s
organization, administration, operations and management,
in accordance with Republic Act No. 9155, or the “Governance of
Basic Education Act of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533, or the “Enhanced
Basic Education Act of 2013”, and other basic education-related laws.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
