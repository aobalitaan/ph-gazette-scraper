[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12040]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE BANGUI DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANGUI, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
NORTE, INTO A LEVEL II DISTRICT HOSPITAL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Bangui District Hospital in the Municipality
of Bangui, Province of Ilocos Norte, is hereby upgraded into a Level II
district hospital.

Suc. 2. Any future increase in bed capacity shall be consistent
with the hospital development plan that shall be prepared by the Chief
of Hospital of the Bangui District Hospital and submitted to the
Department of Health. *

Sec. 3. The existing professional health care services and
facilities of the Bangui District Hospital shall also be upgraded to
conform with and be commensurate to its upgrading as a Level II
hospital and the increase in bed capacity as provided for in Sections 1
and 2 hereof.

Sec. 4. The existing workforce of the Bangui District Hospital,
which includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel, shall be
increased correspondingly.

Sc. 5. The Provincial Government of Locos Norte shall provide
the necessary funds to cover the maintenance and other operating
expenses of the Bangui District Hospital. It shall also appropriate funds
for the additional human resource complement to support the increase
in bed capacity and the corresporiding upgrading of its service facilities
and professional health care services.

SEc. 6. The Secretary of Health shall include in the
Department’s subsidy program, support for the capital outlay
requirements of the Bangui District Hospital.

SEc. 7. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall
vemain in full force and effect.

Sc, 8, All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

Sec. 9. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
