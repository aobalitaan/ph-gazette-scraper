[ REPUBLIC ACT No. $1937]

AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO JUSTIN
DONTA BROWNLEE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Justin Donta Brownlee is hereby granted
Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and
prerogatives, as well as the duties and obligations appurtenant
thereto, under the Constitution and the laws of the Republic of
the Philippines.

Sec. 2. Justin Donta Brownlee shall take the Oath of
Allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines before an officer
duly authorized to administer the same. The Oath of Allegiance
shall be registered with the Bureau of Immigration.

Sec. 3. Upon registration of the Oath of Allegiance, the
| Bureau of Immigration shall issue a Certificate of Naturalization
| to Justin Donta Brownlee who shall thereupon enter into the full
| . enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.

Sec. 4. This Act shall take effect immediately upon its
| publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general
j circulation.
