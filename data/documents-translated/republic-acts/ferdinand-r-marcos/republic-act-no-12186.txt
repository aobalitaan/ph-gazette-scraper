[ REPUBLIC ACT NO.12186]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN SAN CARLOS CITY,
PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, INTO A CLASS “B” DISTRICT
OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Land Transportation Office (LTO) District Office
in San Carlos City, Province of Pangasinan, is hereby converted into
a Class “B” district office.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
Class “B” LTO District Office in San Carlos City, Province of
Pangasinan, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.
