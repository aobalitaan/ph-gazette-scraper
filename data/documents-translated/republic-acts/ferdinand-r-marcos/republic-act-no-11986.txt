[REPUBLIC ACT No. 11986]

AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF BAROBO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, ;
TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY GUINHALINAN

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

 

SECTION 1. Creation of Barangay Guinhalinan. —
There is hereby created a barangay in the Municipality of
Barobo, Province of Surigao del Sur, to be known as
Barangay Guinhalinan.
