[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 3.2293]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE QUIRINO PROVINCE MEDICAL
CENTER IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABARROGUIS,
PROVINCE OF QUIRINO, INTO A LEVEL III GENERAL
HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Quirino Province Medical Center (QPMC) in
the Municipality of Cabarroguis, Province of Quirino, is hereby
upgraded into a Level III general hospital under the direct supervision
and control of the Provincial Government of Quirino.

Sec. 2. The authorized bed capacity of the QPMC shall be
increased from one hundred fifty (150) beds to two hundred fifty (250)
beds. Any future increase in bed capacity shall be consistent with its
hospital development plan.

Sgc. 3. The existing professional healthcare services and
facilities of the QPMC shall also be upgraded to conform with and be
commensurate to the upgrading of its functional capacity as a
Level III general hospital and the increase in its bed capacity, as
provided in Sections 1 and 2 hereof.

Sec. 4. The existing workforce of the QPMC, which
includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel, shall be
increased correspondingly.

SEc. 5. The Medical Center Chief shall prepare the hospital
development plan of the QPMC, to be approved by the Provincial
Governor, and submitted to the Department of Health. The plan shall
prescribe the implementation milestones for the upgrading of the QPMC
into a Level III general hospital and the increase in its bed capacity.

SEc. 6. The Provincial Government of Quirino shall provide the
necessary funds to cover the maintenance and other operating expenses
of the QPMC. It shall also appropriate funds for the additional human
resource complement to support the upgrading of its professional
healthcare services and facilities, and the increase in its bed capacity.

SEc. 7. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include in
the Department’s subsidy program for the next three (3) years its
support for the capital outlay requirements to upgrade and expand
the hospital facilities and medical equipment of the QPMC as a
Level III general hospital under its hospital development plan.

SEc. 8. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall
remain in full force and effect.

Spc. 9. All laws, executive orders, issuances, rules, and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or modified
accordingly.

SEc. 10. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
