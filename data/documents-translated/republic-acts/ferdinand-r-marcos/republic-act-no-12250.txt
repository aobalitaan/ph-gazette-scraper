[REPUBLIC AcT No. 12250 ]

AN ACT DECLARING AUGUST 30 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF SAN JUAN,
TO BE KNOWN AS “ARAW NG PINAGLABANAN’, IN
COMMEMORATION OF THE BATTLE OF PINAGLABANAN,
WHICH OCCURRED ON AUGUST 30, 1896

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of. Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. August 30 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the City of San Juan, to be known as
“Araw ng Pinaglabanan”, in commemoration of the Battle of
Pinaglabanan, which occurred on August 30, 1896. However, upon
proclamation of the President, August 30 of every year shall be a
special non-working holiday in the City of San Juan,

  

MT

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
