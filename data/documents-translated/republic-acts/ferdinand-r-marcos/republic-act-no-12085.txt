[REPUBLIC ACT No. 12085}

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN SEBASTIAN FISHERIES
HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN SEBASTIAN,
MUNICIPALITY OF LAGONOY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES
SUR TO SAN SEBASTIAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of San Sebastian Fisheries High
School in Barangay San Sebastian, Municipality of Lagonoy,
Province of Camarines Sur is hereby changed to San Sebastian
National High School.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
