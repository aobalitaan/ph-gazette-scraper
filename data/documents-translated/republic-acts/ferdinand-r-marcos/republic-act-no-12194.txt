[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 42194]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN LIGAO CITY, PROVINCE
OF ALBAY, FROM A CLASS “D” TO A CLASS “B” DISTRICT
OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) located in Ligao City, Province of Albay, is hereby
upgraded from a Class “D” to a Class “B” district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in the
Department’s program the operationalization of the LTO Ligao City
District Office in the Province of Albay, the initial funding of which
shall be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the said
office. Thereafter, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

 

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
