[ REPUBLIC ACT No.12100 ]

SPECIAL

AN ACT DECLARING JULY 28 OF EVERY YEAR A
NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN CABADBARAN CITY,
PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL NORTE, TO BE KNOWN AS
“CABADBARAN DAY”, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITs

FOUNDATION DAY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of ‘Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. July 28 of every year is hereby declared a special
nonworking holiday in Cabadbaran City, Province of Agusan
del Norte, to be known as “Cabadbaran Day”, in commemoration of its

foundation day.

|

|| SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
