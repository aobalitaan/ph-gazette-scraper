AN ACT DECLARING JULY 8 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE
OF BASILAN, IN CELEBRATION OF THE FEAST OF
ST. ELIZABETH OF PORTUGAL, TO BE KNOWN AS
FIESTA ALEGRIA DE ISABELA

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. July 8 of every year is hereby declared a special
working holiday in the City of Isabela, Province of Basilan, in
celebration of the feast of St. Elizabeth of Portugal, to be known as
Fiesta Alegria de Isabela. Upon proclamation of the President,
July 8 of every year shall be a special non-working holiday in the
said city.

     
 

  

SEc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
