AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 12 OF EVERY YEAR A
SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY
OF VALENZUELA, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS
FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November 12 of every year is hereby declared
a special non-working holiday in the City of Valenzuela, in
commemoration of its foundation anniversary.

 
 

REPUB!

; SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
