[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 12076]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE CITY OF VICTORIAS,
PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, A GENERAL
HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS THE VICTORIAS
CITY GENERAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the City of
Victorias, Province of Negros Occidental, a general hospital to
be known as the Victorias City General Hospital, which shall
be under the direct control and supervision of the City Government
of Victorias.

SEC. 2. The City Government of Victorias shall manage
the Victorias City General Hospital, and prepare, in consultation
with the Department of Health, a hospital development plan
that is aligned with the Philippine Health Facility Development
Plan. Any future increase in bed capacity, healthcare facilities,
service capability, and human resource complement shall be based
on the hospital development plan.

Suc. 3. The City Government of Victorias shall provide
the necessary funds for the establishment of the Victorias City General
Hospital. The Secretary of Health shall include in the Department's
subsidy program, support for the capital outlay requirements of the
Victorias City General Hospital.

Sgc. 4, If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall
remain in full force and effect.

Src. 5. All laws, executive orders, issuances, and rules
and regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

SEC. 6, This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
