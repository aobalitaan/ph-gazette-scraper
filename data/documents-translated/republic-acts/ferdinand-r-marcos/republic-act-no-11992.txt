[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11992]

AN ACT CREATING A DISTRICT ENGINEERING
OFFICE IN THE SECOND LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT
OF THE PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A district engineering office of the Department
of Public Works and Highways is hereby created in the Second
Legislative District, Province of Southern Leyte, to be located in
the Municipality of Sogod, and referred to as the Southern Leyte
Second District Engineering Office.

SEC. 2. The Southern Leyte Second District Engineering
Office shall serve the Municipalities of Anahawan, Hinunangan,
Hinundayan, Libagon, Liloan, Pintuyan, St. Bernard, San
Francisco, San Juan, San Ricardo, Silago and Sogod, all

 

located in the Second Legislative District of the Province of
Southern Leyte.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the Southern Leyte Second District Engineering Office, the
funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Suc. 4, This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
