[REPUBLIC ACT No.12251 ]

AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 21 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN

MATEO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, TO BE KNOWN AS “ARAW

NG BAYAN NG SAN MATEO”, IN COMMEMORATION OF
ITS FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 21 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the Municipality of San Mateo, Province
of Rizal, to be known as “Araw ng Bayan ng San Mateo” in
commemoration of its foundation anniversary. Upon proclamation
of the President, September 21 of every year shall be a special
non-working holiday in the said municipality.
