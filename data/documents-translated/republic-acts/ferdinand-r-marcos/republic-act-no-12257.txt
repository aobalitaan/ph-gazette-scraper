[REPUBLIC AcT No.12257 ]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE ALEGRIA ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
IN BARANGAY ALEGRIA, MUNICIPALITY OF BARCELONA,
PROVINCE OF SORSOGON, INTO AN INTEGRATED
SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS THE ALEGRIA INTEGRATED
SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Alegria Elementary School in Barangay Alegria,
Municipality of Barcelona, Province of Sorsogon, is hereby converted
into an integrated school to be known as the Alegria Integrated School.

Sc. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records of the Alegria
Elementary School are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the Alegria
Integrated School.

. SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately include in
the Department’s program the operationalization of the Alegria
Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 4. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of this Act,
the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules and regulations
implementing the provisions of this Act. Such implementing rules and
regulations shall provide for the school’s organization, administration,
operations, and management, in accordance with Republic Act No. 9155
or the “Governance of Basic Education Act of 2001”, Republic Act
No. 10533 or the “Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013”, and other
basic education-related laws.

Sc. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
