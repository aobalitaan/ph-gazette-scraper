[ REPUBLIC ACT No.12126 ]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING ALAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO)
DISTRICT OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SULOP,
PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established a district office of the
Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Sulop,
Province of Davao del Sur.

Sic. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the LTO
District Office in the Municipality of Sulop, Province of Davao del
Sur, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

 

SEC, 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
Approved,
