[ REPUBLIC ACT NO.1 2222 ]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY TUMITUS, ZAMBOANGA CITY TO BE
KNOWN AS THE TUMITUS NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in
Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the supervision
of the Department of Education, a national high school in
Barangay Tumitus, Zamboanga City to be known as the Tumitus
National High School.

Src. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of the
Tumitus National High School, the funding of which shall be included
in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of
this Act, the Secretary of Education shall formulate the rules
and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act. Such
implementing rules and regulations shall provide for the school’s
organization, administration, operations, and management, in
accordance with Republic Act No. 9155 or the “Governance of
Basic Education Act of 2001”, Republic Act No. 10533 or the “Enhanced
Basic Education Act of 2013”, and other basic education-related laws.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
