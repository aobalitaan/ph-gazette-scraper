AN ACT DECLARING THE SEVENTH DAY OF NOVEMBER
OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NATIONAL WORKING
HOLIDAY IN COMMEMORATION OF THE
ESTABLISHMENT OF THE FIRST PHILIPPINE
MOSQUE AND THE INTRODUCTION OF ISLAM IN
THE PHILIPPINES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as
the “Sheikh Karim’ul Makhdum Day”.

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. — It is the policy of the
State to instill inclusivity to all regardless of religious beliefs,
in line with the constitutional right to freely exercise and enjoy
their religion. Moreover, it is the State’s policy to provide due
recognition to the indispensable contributions of Muslim Filipinos
in the development of Philippine culture and civilization.

 

 

 
 

SEC. 3. National Holiday. — Every seventh (7%) day of
November is hereby declared as “Sheikh Karim’ul Makhdum
Day”, a special national working holiday in commemoration
of the establishment of the first Philippine Mosque, and the
introduction of Islam in the Philippines.

SEC. 4. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15)
days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in at least
two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
