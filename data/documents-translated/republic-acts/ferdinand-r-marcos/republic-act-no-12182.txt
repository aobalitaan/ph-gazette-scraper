AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY CALABASA, ZAMBOANGA CITY TO BE KNOWN
AS THE CALABASA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND

APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in

Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the supervision
of the Department of Education, a national high school in Barangay
Calabasa, Zamboanga City, to be known as the Calabasa National

High School.

SEC. 2, The Seeretary of Education shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the operationalization of the Calabasa
National High School, the funding of which shall be included in the

annual General Appropriations Act.
