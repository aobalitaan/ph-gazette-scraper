AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
IN BARANGAY AMBIONG, MUNICIPALITY OF
LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET TO BE
KNOWN AS AMBIONG NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There ig hereby established, under the
supervision of the Department of Education, a national high
school in Barangay Ambiong, Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet to be known as Ambiong National
High School.

Sic. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization
of the Ambiong National High School, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 8. The Secretary of Education shall formulate the
rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act.

Sc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after

its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
