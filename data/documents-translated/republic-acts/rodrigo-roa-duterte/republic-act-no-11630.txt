[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 116 30]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY MARCOS, MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA,
PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR TO BE KNOWN AS
THE SANTA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the
supervision of the Department of Education, a national high
school in Barangay Marcos, Municipality of Santa, Province
of Ilocos Sur ta, be known as the Santa National High School.

SEc. 2. The Secretary’of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the Santa National High School, the funding of which shall
be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 3. Within sixty (60) days from the approval of this
Act, the Secretary of Education shall formulate and issue
the rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this
Aet.

Sec. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
