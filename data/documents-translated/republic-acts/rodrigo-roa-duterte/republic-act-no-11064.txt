[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11064]

ANACT RENAMING PANGLAO ISLAND CIRCUMFERENTIAL
ROAD TRAVERSING BARANGAYS TANGNAN,
BIL-ISAN, LOOC, POBLACION, DANAO, TAWALA,
BOLOD AND LIBAONG IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF PANGLAO AND BARANGAYS TOTOLAN,
SONGCULAN, TABALONG, BINGAG, DAO, SAN
ISIDRO, BIKING 1, BIKING 2, CATARMAN,
MAYACABAC, POBLACION AND UNION IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF DAUIS, AS ANOS FONACIER
CIRCUMFERENTIAL ROAD (DAUIS-PANGLAO)

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In recognition of the remarkable achievements
and contributions of the late Anos Fonacier in the development
of Bohol’s tourism, the Panglao Island Circumferential Road
which traverses barangays Tangnan, Bil-isan, Looc, Poblacion,
Danao, Tawala, Bolod and Libaong in the Municipality of

Panglao and barangays Totolan, Songculan, Tabalong, Bingag,
Dao, San Isidro, Biking 1, Biking 2, Catarman, Mayacabac,
Poblacion and Union in the Municipality of Dauis, is hereby
renamed as Anos Fonacier Circumferential Road (Dauis-Panglao).

| SEc. 2. The Dep artment of Public Works and Highways
| is hereby directed to implement the provision of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after

the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in
any newspaper of general circulation.
