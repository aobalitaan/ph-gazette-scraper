AN ACT CONVERTING THE ROAD STRETCHING FROM
BARANGAY ANDUYAN, MUNICIPALITY OF TUBAO,
PROVINCE OF LA UNION TO BARANGAY SAN
PASCUAL, MUNICIPALITY OF TUBA, PROVINCE OF
BENGUET INTO A NATIONAL ROAD AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The road stretching from Barangay Anduyan,
Municipality of Tubao, Province of La Union to Barangay San
Pascual, Municipality of Tuba, Province of Benguet as described
in the technical description under the succeeding section of this
Act is hereby converted into a national road.
