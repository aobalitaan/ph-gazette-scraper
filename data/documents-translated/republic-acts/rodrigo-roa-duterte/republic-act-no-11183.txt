[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11183]

AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 28 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA MARGARITA, PROVINCE
OF SAMAR, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS
FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY, TO BE KNOWN AS
THE “SANTA MARGARITA FOUNDATION DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November 28 of every year is hereby
declared a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality
of Santa Margarita, Province of Samar, in commemoration
of its founding anniversary, to be known as the “Santa Margarita
Foundation Day”. When the holiday falls on a Saturday or a
Sunday, the special nonworking day shall be observed on the
Monday that follows.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

 

general circulation.
