[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 31526]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PAG-ASA NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY RAWIS, CITY OF
LEGAZPI, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, TO LEGAZPI CITY
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Pag-asa National High School in
Barangay Rawis, City of Legazpi, Province of Albay, is hereby
changed to Legazpi City National High School.

SEC. 2. The main campus of the Legazpi City National High
School shall continue to remain in ita present location in Barangay
Rawis, City of Legazpi, Province of Albay.

 

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
