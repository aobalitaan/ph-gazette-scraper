AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE
OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAGSANJAN, PROVINCE OF
LAGUNA AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION l. There shall be established a regular district
office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality
of Pagsanjan, Province of Laguna which shall serve the Fourth
Congressional District of Laguna and the neighboring provinces
and towns.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
-include in the Department’s programs the operationalization of
the regular LTO district office in the Municipality of Pagsanjan,
Province of Laguna, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.
