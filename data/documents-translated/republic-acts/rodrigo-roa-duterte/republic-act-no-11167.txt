[REPUBLIC Act No. 11167}

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE
BILIRAN PROVINCIAL HOSPITAL IN ITS PROPOSED
RELOCATION SITE IN BARANGAY LARRAZABAL,
MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN,
FROM SEVENTY-FIVE (75) TO TWO HUNDRED (200)
BEDS, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Biliran Provincial
Hospital in its proposed relocation site in Barangay Larrazabal,
Municipality of Naval, Province of Biliran, shall be increased
from seventy-five (75) to two hundred (200) beds.

SEc. 2. The existing hospital facilities and professional
health care services of the Biliran Provincial Hospital are
hereby upgraded and increased to conform with and be
commensurate to the increase in bed capacity. The number
of medical personnel shall be correspondingly increased.

Src. 3. The Provincial Government of Biliran shall
provide the necessary funds to support the increase in
bed capacity of the Biliran Provincial Hospital and the
corresponding upgrading of its facilities and services. The
Secretary of Health shall include in the Department’s
program, subsidy support for the capital outlay requirements
of the said hospital.

Sec. 4. Republic Act No. 7194, entitled “An Act
Increasing the Bed Capacity of the Naval General Hospital
in the Municipality of Naval, Subprovince of Biliran, Leyte,
From Twenty-Five (25) to Fifty (50) Beds, and Appropriating
Funds Therefor” and Republic Act No. 3875, entitled “An Act
Converting Naval Emergency Hospital in the Municipality
of Naval, Province of Leyte, Into a General Hospital to
be Known as Naval General Hospital, and Authorizing the
Appropriation of Funds Therefor” are hereby repealed.
All laws, decrees, ordinances, rules and regulations, executive or
administrative orders, and other presidential issuances
inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed, amended or
modified accordingly.

S&c. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
