AN ACT DECLARING MOUNT BULAYLAY IN THE

ECIJA A TOURIST DESTINATION, PROVIDING FOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Mount Bulaylay located in the
Municipality of Cuyapo, Province of Nueva Ecija is hereby
declared a tourist destination. As such, its development shall be
prioritized by the Department of Tourism (DOT), and shall be
subject to the rules and regulations governing the development
of tourist destinations.
