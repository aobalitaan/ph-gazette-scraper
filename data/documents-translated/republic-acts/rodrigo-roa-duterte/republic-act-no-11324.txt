AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF GOVERNOR
CELESTINO GALLARES MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
(GCGMH) IN TAGBILARAN CITY, PROVINCE OF
BOHOL FROM TWO HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE (225) TO
FIVE HUNDRED TWENTY-FIVE (525) BEDS AND
UPGRADING ITS SERVICES AND FACILITIES,
AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT
NO. 7266, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR
AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Increase in Authorized Bed Capacity. — The
authorized bed capacity of Governor Celestino Gallares
Memorial Hospital (GCGMH) is hereby increased from two
hundred twenty-five (225) to five hundred twenty-five (525)
beds, amending for the purpose Republic Act No. 7266. The
increase in bed capacity shall be implemented consistent with
the five (5)-year development plan of the hospital.

SEC. 2. Upgrade of Existing Service Facilities and
Professional Health Care Services. — The existing service
facilities and professional health care services of GCGMH are
hereby upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to the
increase in authorized bed capacity as provided in Section 1
hereof. The GCGMH shall submit to the Department of Health
its proposed development and the additional clinical
departments and special units it requires to meet the increased
authorized bed capacity.

SEC. 3. Implementation and Appropriation. — The
Secretary of Health shall immediately include in the
Department’s programs the implementation of this Act, the
funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. Separability Clause. — If any provision or part
of this Act is declared unconstitutional, the remaining parts
or provisions not affected shall remain in full force and effect.

SEC. 5. Repealing Clause. — All other laws, decrees,
orders, issuances, rules and regulations, or parts thereof which
are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby
repealed, amended or modified accordingly.

SEC. 6. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a
newspaper of general circulation.
