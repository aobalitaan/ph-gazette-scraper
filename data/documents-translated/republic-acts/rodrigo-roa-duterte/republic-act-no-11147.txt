[RepuBLic Act No. 11147]

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE
NARRA MUNICIPAL HOSPITAL IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF NARRA, PROVINCE OF
PALAWAN FROM TEN (10) TO ONE HUNDRED
(100) BED CAPACITY, AND NAMING IT AS THE
GOVERNOR ALFREDO MENDOZA ABUEG, SR.
MEMORIAL HOSPITAL AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The authorized bed capacity of the Narra
Municipal Hospital in the Municipality of Narra, Province of
Palawan is hereby increased from ten (10) to one hundred
(100) bed capacity and shall be now known as the Governor
Alfredo Mendoza Abueg, Sr. Memorial Hospital.

SEC. 2. The existing service facilities and professional
health care services of the Governor Alfredo Mendoza Abueg,
Sr. Memorial Hospital are hereby upgraded to conform with
and be commensurate to the increase in bed capacity as
provided in Section 1 hereof.

Sec. 3. All personnel, records, assets and properties,
including buildings, sites, and improvements thereof, obligations,
funds and appropriations of the Narra Municipal Hospital
are hereby transferred to the Governor Alfredo Mendoza
Abueg, Sr. Memorial Hospital.

Src. 4. The Provincial Government of Palawan shall
provide for the necessary funds to support the increase in bed
capacity of the Governor Alfredo Mendoza Abueg, Sr. Memorial
Hospital and the corresponding upgrading of its facilities and
services. The Secretary of Health shall mclude in the Department’s
program the subsidy support for the capital outlay requirements
of the said hospital.

SEC. 5. Republic Act No. 6917, establishing a ten (10)-bed
capacity Narra Municipal Hospital, is hereby repealed. All other
laws, decrees, orders, issuances, rules and regulations, or parts
thereof which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act
are hereby repealed.

SEC. 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
