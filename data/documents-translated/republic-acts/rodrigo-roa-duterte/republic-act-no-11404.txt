[REPUBLIC Act No. 11404}

AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF LIMAY, PROVINCE OF BATAAN TO BE KNOWN
AS BARANGAY ST. FRANCIS II

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation of Barangay St. Francis I. — There
is hereby created a barangay in the Municipality of Limay,
Province of Bataan to be known as Barangay St. Francis II.

SEC. 2. Territorial Boundaries. — The territorial

boundaries of Barangay St. Francis II shall be identified as

SEc. 3. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. —
The plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission
on Elections (COMELEC) on September 14, 1997 pursuant
to Provincial Ordinance No. 11, s. 1996 of the Sangguniang
Panlalawigan of the Province of Bataan, which ratified the
creation of Barangay St. Francis II, shall serve as substantial
compliance with the plebiscite requirement under Section
10 of Republic Act No. 7160, otherwise known as the “Local
Government Code of 1991”, as amended.

SEC. 4. Incumbent Barangay Officials. — The incumbent
barangay officials of Barangay St. Francis II shall continue to
hold office until such time that their successors have been duly

* elected and qualified.

SEc. 5. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. — All public
infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein
at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred
without cost or compensation to and shall be administered by
Barangay St. Francis I.

SEc. 6. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. — Barangay
St. Francis II shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment
(IRA) sharespursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160,
otherwise known as the “Local Government Code of 1991”,
as amended.

SEC. 7 Effectivity.— This Act shall take effect fifteen (15)
