Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Molmol Elementary School in Barangay
Molmol, Municipality of Jose Abad Santos, Province of Davao
Occidental is hereby converted into an integrated school to be
known as the Mariano Anoy Sangay Integrated School.

Src. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of

the Molmol Elementary School are hereby transferred to and
absorbed by the Mariano Anoy Sangay Integrated School.
