[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11378]

AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 16 OF EVERY YEAR A
SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF
BAYBAY, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, TO BE KNOWN AS
“BAYBAY CITY CHARTER DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June 16 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the City of Baybay, Province of Leyte,
to be known as “Baybay City Charter Day”.

SEC. 2. On June 16 of every year, the City of Baybay,
Province of Leyte, shall engage in festivities and conduct
ceremonies and programs in cognizance ofits history and culture,
to be led by its officials and employees in coordination with
civic, sports, religious and nongovernmental organizations and
private businesses.

 

Sec. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
