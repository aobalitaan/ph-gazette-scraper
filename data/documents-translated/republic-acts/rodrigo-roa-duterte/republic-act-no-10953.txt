AN ACT DIVIDING BARANGAY PAMPANGA IN THE CITY
OF DAVAO INTO THREE (3) DISTINCT AND
INDEPENDENT BARANGAYS TO BE KNOWN AS
BARANGAY PAMPANGA, BARANGAY ALFONSO
ANGLIONGTO, SR., AND BARANGAY VICENTE
HIZON, SR.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Division of Barangay Pampanga. — Barangay
Pampanga in the City of Davao is hereby divided into
three (3) distinct and independent barangays to be known as:
(1) Barangay Pampanga; (2) Barangay Alfonso Angliongto, Sr.;
and (3) Barangay Vicente Hizon, Sr.

boundaries of each barangay created in the immediately
preceding section shall be as follows:

On the Southwest (SW), pts. 12 to 21, by the shoreline of
Davao Gulf; and

On the Northwest (NW), pts. 22 to pt. 1, by Barangay
Vicente Hizon, Sr., Case 14.

SEC. 3. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. — The
plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on
Elections (COMELEC) in Barangay Pampanga on
July 25, 2004, pursuant to City Ordinance No. 00161-03 of
the Sangguniang Panlungsod of the City of Davao, which
ratified the creation of Barangay Alfonso Angliongto, Sr. and
of Barangay Vicente Hizon, Sr., shall serve as substantial
compliance with the plebiscite requirement under Section
10 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise
known as the "Local Government Code of 1991".

SEC. 4. Incumbent Barangay Officials. — The incumbent
barangay officials of barangays Pampanga, Alfonso
Angliongto, Sr., and Vicente Hizon, Sr., City of Davao shall
continue to hold office until such time that their successors
have been duly elected and qualified.

SEC. 5. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. — All public
infrastructure and facilities for public use existing
therein at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby
transferred without cost or compensation to and shall be
administered by the new barangays.

SEC. 6. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. — Barangays
Pampanga, Alfonso Angliongto, Sr. and Vicente Hizon, Sr.
shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA)
shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160,

as amended, otherwise known as the "Local Government
Code of 1991".

REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

SEC. 7. Separability Clause. — If, for any reason,
any part or provision of this Act is declared unconstitutional
or invalid, other parts or provisions which are not affected
thereby shall continue to be in full force and effect.

SEC. 8. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect
fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette
or in a newspaper of general circulation.
