[REPUBLIC Act No. 11823]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF
THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIPOS, PROVINCE OF
ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shail be established a regular district
office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality
of Guipos, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, to be known as the
“LTO District Office of Guipos”.

Src. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
(OT?) shall immediately include in the Department's program
the operationalization of the LTO district office in the
Municipality of Guipos, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, the

funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Src. 3. The Secretary of the DOTr shall issue such rules
and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions
of this Act.

its publication in the Officiah Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
