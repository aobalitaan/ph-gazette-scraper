[ REPUBLIC Act No. 11179]

AN ACT SEPARATING THE CABANGAHAN NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL - CORDOVA NATIONAL HIGH
SCHOOL EXTENSION IN BARANGAY DAY-AS,
MUNICIPALITY OF CORDOVA, PROVINCE OF CEBU
FROM THE CABANGAHAN NATIONAL HIGH
SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO AN INDEPENDENT
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS
CORDOVA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

 

! Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Cabangahan National High
School — Cordova National High School Extension in Barangay
! Day-as, Municipality of Cordova, Province of Cebu is hereby
| separated from the Cabangahan National High School
and converted into an independent national high school to be
known as Cordova National High School.

Sec. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records
of the Cabangahan National High Schooi — Cordova
National High School Extension are hereby transferred to and
absorbed by the Cordova National High School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the Cordova National High School, the initial funding of
which shall be charged against the current year’s
appropriation of the Cabangahan National High School —
Cordova National High School Extension. Thereafter,
the amount necessary for the continued operation of
the school shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. The Secretary of Education shall formulate
the rules and regulations necessary to carry out the
purpose of this Act within one hundred twenty (120) days
after its effectivity.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazetie or in any newspaper
of general circulation.

Approved,
