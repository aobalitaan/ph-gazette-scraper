[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11732]

AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO
FENG LI

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Mr. Feng Li is hereby granted Philippine
citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives,
as well as the duties and obligations appurtenant thereto
under the Constitution and the laws of the Republic of
the Philippines.

Sec. 2. Mr. Feng Li shall take the Oath of Allegiance
to the Republic of the Philippines before an officer duly
authorized to administer the same. The Oath of Allegiance
shall be registered with the Bureau of Immigration.

SEc. 3. Upon registration of the Oath of Allegiance,
the Bureau of Immigration shall issue a Certificate of
Naturalization to Mr. Feng Li who shall thereupon enter
into the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.

SEc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
