AN ACT RENAMING THE ADIONG MEMORIAL
POLYTECHNIC STATE COLLEGE IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF DITSAAN-RAMAIN, PROVINCE

STATE COLLEGE”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Adiong Memorial Polytechnic State College
located in the Municipality of Ditsaan-Ramain, Province of

Lanao del Sur, is hereby renamed as the Adiong Memorial State
College.
