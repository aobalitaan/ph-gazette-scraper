AN ACT ESTABLISHING A MULTI-SPECIALTY HOSPITAL
IN THE CITY OF TAYABAS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON,
TO BE KNOWN AS THE SOUTHERN LUZON
MULTI-SPECIALTY MEDICAL CENTER, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a multi-specialty
hospital in the City of Tayabas, Province of Quezon to be known
as the Southern Luzon Multi-Specialty Medical Center, which
shall be under the direct control and supervision of the
Department of Health (DOH). The hospital shall be designated
as one of the apex hospitals of the Southern Luzon regions.

Any future increase in bed capacity and upgrading of health
care services and facilities shall be based on the hospital
development plan which shall be prepared by the DOH.

SEC. 2, The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department’s program the implementation of this Act, the
funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. The Secretary of the DOH shall prepare and issue
the necessary rules and regulations for the effective implementation
of this Act consistent with the hospital development plan.

Sec. 4. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected
shall remain in full force and effect.

SEc. 5. Alllaws, executive orders, issuances, and rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

SEC. 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
