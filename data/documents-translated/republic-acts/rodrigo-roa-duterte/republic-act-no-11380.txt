AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 9 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MINA, PROVINCE OF ILOILO,
IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING DAY

r 2 ;
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 9 of every year is hereby declared
a special working holiday in the Municipality of Mina,
Province of Iloilo, in commemoration of its founding day.
Upon proclamation of the President, September 9 of every year
shall be a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Mina,
Province of Hoilo.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
