AN ACT DECLARING OCTOBER 28 OF EVERY
YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO OCCIDENTAL
IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING
ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. October 28 of every year is hereby declared
a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Davao
Occidental in commemoration of its founding anniversary.

a

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
