[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11611)

AN ACT RECONSTITUTING THE LANAO DEL NORTE
DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE IN THE
PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE INTO TWO (2)
SEPARATE DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICES,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Lanao del Norte District, Engineering
Office, under the supervision of the Department of Public Works
and Highways, shall be reconstituted into two (2) separate district
engineering offices to be named as Lanao del Norte First District
Engineering Office and Lanao del Norte Second District
Engineering Office, respectively.

SEc. 2. The Lanao del Norte First District Engineering
Office which shall be located within the First Congressional

District of the Province of Lanao del Norte shall serve the
municipalities of Baroy, Tubod, Kolambugan, Maigo, Bacolod,
Kauswagan, Linamon, Tagoloan, Baloi, Matunggao and Pantar.
The Lanao del Norte Second District Engineering Office which
shall be located within the Second Congressional District shall
serve the municipalities of Pantao Ragat, Poona Piagapo, Munai,
Tangkal, Magsaysay, Salvador, Lala, Sultan Naga Dimaporo
(SND), Kapatagan, Sapad and Nunungan.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of the Department of Public Works
and Highways shall include in the Department’s program the
operationalization of the Lanao del Norte First and Second District
Engineering Offices, the initial funding of which shall be charged
against the current year’s appropriations of the Lanao del Norte
District Engineering Office. Thereafter, the amount necessary
for the continued operation of the said district engineering offices
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 4. Republic Act No. 9035, constituting the inland,
interior and remotely-situated municipalities of the Province of
Lanao del Norte into a separate highway engineering district is
hereby repealed accordingly.

SEc. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or ina newspaper of general
circulation.
