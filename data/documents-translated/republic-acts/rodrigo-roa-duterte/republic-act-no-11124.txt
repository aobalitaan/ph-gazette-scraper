[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11124]

AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 7 OF EVERY
YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN
THE CITY OF CAVITE, PROVINCE OF CAVITE,
IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING
ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 7 of every year is hereby
declared a special nonworking holiday in the City of Cavite,
Province of Cavite, in commemoration of its founding
anniversary.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a
newspaper of general circulation.
