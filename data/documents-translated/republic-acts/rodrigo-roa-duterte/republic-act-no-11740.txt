[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 117401]

AN ACTESTABLISHING A DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF BALINGASAG, MISAMIS ORIENTAL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a district office of
the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of
Balingasag, Misamis Oriental.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
shall immediately include in the Department’s program the
operationalization of the LTO District Office in the Municipality
of Balingasag, Misamis Oriental, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.
