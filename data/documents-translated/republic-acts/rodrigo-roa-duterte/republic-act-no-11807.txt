[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11807]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CENTRAL MULTI-SPECIES
MARINE HATCHERY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
LIBON, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the
supervision of the Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources of
the Department of Agriculture (BFAR-DA), a central multi-species
marine hatchery in the Municipality of Libon, Province
of Albay.

Sgc. 2, The BFAR-DA shall, within two (2) years after
its construction, transfer the management of the central
multi-species marine hatchery to the local government of Libon
through a memorandum of agreement. It shall implement a
training and phasing-in program for local government personnel
on the management and operation of the central hatchery.


SEC. 3. The Secretary of Agriculture shall include in the
Department's programs the establishment of the central
multi-species marine hatchery in the Municipality of Libon,
Province of Albay, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act. The local government unit
concerned shall set aside funds from any available local revenue
in an amount deemed appropriate for the operationalization of
the central hatchery. xX

SEc. 4. Within sixty (60) days from the effectivity of this
Act, the Secretary of Agriculture shall issue the rules and
regulations on the supervision, operation, monitoring, and the
eventual transfer of the management of the central multi-species
marine hatchery to the local government of Libon.

Suc. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
