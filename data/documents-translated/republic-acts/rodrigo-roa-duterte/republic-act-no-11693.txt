[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11693]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF
THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAMALAYAN, ORIENTAL
MINDORO INTO A DISTRICT OFFICE TO BE KNOWN
AS THE LTO DISTRICT OFFICE — PINAMALAYAN AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The extension office of the Land Transportation
"Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Pinamalayan, Province of
Oriental Mindoro is hereby converted into a “Class B” District
Office to be known as the Land Transportation Office (LTO)
District Office ~ Pinamalayan.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the LTO District Office in the Municipality of Pinamalayan,
Oriental Mindoro, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after

its publication in the Official Gazette or in at least two (2)
newspapers of general circulation.
