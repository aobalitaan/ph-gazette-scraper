[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11733]

AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO
PATRICK FRANCOIS RENUCCI

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Mr. Patrick Francois Renucci is hereby granted
Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and
prerogatives, as well as the duties and obligations appurtenant
thereto under the Constitution and the laws of the Republic of
the Philippines.

SEC. 2. Mr. Renucci shall take the Oath of Allegiance to
the Republic of the Philippines before an officer duly authorized
to administer the same. The Oath of Allegiance shall be registered
with the Bureau of Immigration.
