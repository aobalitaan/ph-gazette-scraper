AN ACT DECLARING MAY 22 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE
OF GUIMARAS IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS
ANNIVERSARY AS A PROVINCE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. May 22 of every year is hereby declared a
special nonworking holiday in the Province of Guimaras in
commemoration of its anniversary as a province.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
