AN ACT UPGRADING THE DR. PAULINO J. GARCIA
MEMORIAL RESEARCH AND MEDICAL CENTER
EXTENSION HOSPITAL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
TALAVERA, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, TO A
LEVEL II HOSPITAL AND INCREASING ITS BED
CAPACITY FROM FIFTY (50) TO ONE HUNDRED (100)
BEDS TO BE NOW KNOWN AS THE TALAVERA
GENERAL HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Dr. Paulino J. Garcia Memorial Research
and Medical Center Extension Hospital in the Municipality of
Talavera, Province of Nueva Ecija, is hereby upgraded into a
Level If Hospital to be now known as the Talavera General
Hospital.

The authorized bed capacity of the Talavera General
Hospital is hereby increased from fifty (50) to one hundred (100)
beds.

The Secretary of the Department of Health (DOH) shall
implement the increase in bed capacity of the Talavera General
Hospital within a period of five (5) years from the approval of
this Act.

Src. 2. The existing hospital facilities and professional
health care services of the Talavera General Hospital are hereby
upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to the increase
in bed capacity. The number of medical personnel shall
be correspondingly increased.

Sec. 3. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department’s programs the implementation of this Aci,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEc. 4. Republic Act No. 8527, increasing the bed capacity
of Dr. Paulino J. Garcia Memorial Research and Medical
Center Extension Hospital, is hereby repealed. All other laws,
decrees, orders, issuances, rules and regulations and parts thereof
which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are
hereby repealed.

Ssc. 5. Within sixty (60) days after the approval of this
Act, the DOH shall promulgate the necessary rules and regulations
for the effective implementation of this Act.

Sec. 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
