AN ACT UPGRADING THE AMAI PAKPAK MEDICAL
@ENTER IN MARAWI CITY, PROVINCE OF LANAO
DEL SUR AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Amai Pakpak Medical
Center in Marawi City, Province of Lanao del Sur is hereby
increased from two hundred (200) to four hundred (400) beds.

Sc. 2. The existing hospital facilities and professional
health care services of the Amai Pakpak Medical Center are
hereby upgraded and increased to conform with and be
commensurate to the increase in bed capacity. The number of
medical personnel shall be correspondingly increased.

SEc. 3. The increase in the bed capacity of Amai Pakpak
Medical Center shall be implemented incrementally for a period
of not more than two (2) years from the approval of this Act.

Sec. 4. The construction and upgrading of the hospital
facilities and services shall be under the direct supervision and
control of the Department of Health.
