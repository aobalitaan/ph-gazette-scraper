AN ACT RENAMING THE PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA
VALLEY AS THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Change of Name. — The Province of
Compostela Valley is hereby renamed as the Province of
Davao de Oro.

SEC. 2. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. — The
renaming of the Province of Compostela Valley as the
Province of Davao de Oro shall be subject to ratification by a
majority of the votes cast by the qualified voters in a plebiscite to
be conducted in the present Province of Compostela Valley.

The Commission on Elections (COMELEC) shall
conduct and supervise the plebiscite in compliance with
Section 13(g) of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise
known as the “Local Government Code of 1991”.

SEC. 3. Repealing Clause. — Republic Act No. 8470
creating the Province of Compostela Valley is hereby
amended. All other laws, decrees, executive orders, and
rules and regulations which are inconsistent with this Act
are hereby amended, repealed or modified accordingly.

| | SEc. 4. Effectivity. -— This Act shall take effect
| | fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette
| or in any newspaper of general circulation.
