[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 417921]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE
OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO)
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE
OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the
supervision of the Department of Transportation (DOTzr),
a regular district office of the Land Transportation Office
(LTO) in the Municipality of Calamba, Province of Misamis
Occidental.

SEC. 2. The LTO district office shall cater to the needs
of the people from all the barangays of the municipalities
of Calamba, Sapang Dalaga, Concepcion, Baliangao and
Plaridel in the Province of Misamis Occidental and the
Municipality of Rizal in the Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

-

Src. 3. The Secretary of Transportation shall
immediately include in the Department’s program the
operationalzation of the regular LTO district office in the
Municipality of Calamba, Province of Misamis Occidental,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

Src. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,

   

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives
was passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate
of the Philippines on January 20, 2021 and January 31, 2022,

respectively.

MYRA MARIE D, VILLARICA
Secretary of the Senate
