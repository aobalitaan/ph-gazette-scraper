Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Sisigon Elementary School in Barangay
Sisigon, Municipality of Matnog, Province of Sorsogon is hereby
converted into an integrated school to be known as Sisigon
Integrated School.

SEC. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of

absorbed by the Sisigon Integrated School.
