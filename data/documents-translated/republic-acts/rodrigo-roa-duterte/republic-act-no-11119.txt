AN ACT DECLARING APRIL 3 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
PROVINCE OF ALBAY; INCLUDING THE CITIES
THEREIN, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS

FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY, TO BE KNOWN
AS “ALBAY DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. April 3 of every year is hereby declared
a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Albay,
including the cities therein, in commemoration of its
founding anniversary, to be known as “Albay Day”.

 

SEc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a
newspaper of general circulation.
