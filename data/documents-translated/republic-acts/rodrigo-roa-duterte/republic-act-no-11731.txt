[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11731]

AN ACT RENAMING THE CARMEN-SANTO TOMAS ROAD,
STRETCHING FROM JUNCTION AGUSAN-DAVAO
ROAD FRONTING THE BATTLE OF ISING
MONUMENT IN BARANGAY ISING, TRAVERSING
THROUGH BARANGAYS NEW CAMILING, ALEJAL
AND MAGSAYSAY, ALL IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
CARMEN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, AS
SATURNINO R. SILVA ROAD

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Carmen-Santo ‘Tomas Road, stretching
from Junction Agusan-Davao Road fronting the Battle of Ising
Monument in Barangay Ising, traversing through barangays New
Camiling, Alejal and Magsaysay, all in the Municipality of
Carmen, Province 6f Davao del Norte, is hereby renamed as
Saturnino R. Silva Road.


SEC. 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways
shall issue the necessary rules, orders, and circulars to implement
the provisions of this Act within sixty (60) days from its effectivity.

Suc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a néwspaper of
general circulation.
