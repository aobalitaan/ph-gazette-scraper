AN ACT RENAMING THE ROAD TRAVERSING
BARANGAYS SAN JOSE, TOLENTINO WEST,
TOLENTINO EAST, FRANCISCO, SUNGAY WEST
(LEFT SIDE), SUNGAY EAST (RIGHT SIDE), SUNGAY
EAST, IRUHIN WEST, IRUHIN CENTRAL, IRUHIN
EAST AND DAPDAP WEST IN THE CITY OF
TAGAYTAY, PROVINCE OF CAVITE TO ISAAC 0.
TOLENTINO AVENUE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The road traversing barangays San Jose,
Tolentino West, Tolentino East, Francisco, Sungay West
(Left side), Sungay East (Right side), Sungay East, [ruhin West,
Iruhin Central, Iruhin East and Dapdap West in the City of
Tagaytay, Province of Cavite is hereby renamed as Isaac O.
Tolentino Avenue.

 

 

SEC. 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways
shall include in their annual budget the funds necessary for
the implementation of this Act including posting and
maintenance of signages thereat.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after

its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
