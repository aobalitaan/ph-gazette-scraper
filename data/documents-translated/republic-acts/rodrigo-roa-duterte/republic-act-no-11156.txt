AN ACT ESTABLISHING A LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE (LTO) DISTRICT OFFICE IN THE CITY
OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a district office of
the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the City of Sipalay,
Province of Negros Occidental.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
(DOTr) shall immediately include in the Department’s
programs the operationalization of the LTO District Office
in the City of Sipalay, Province of Negros Occidental, the
funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

 

 

SEc. 3. The Secretary of the DOTr shall issue rules
and regulations necessary for the effective implementation
of this Act within sixty (60) days from the effectivity
of this Act.

Src. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
