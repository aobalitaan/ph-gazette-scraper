AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF INOYONAN HIGH
SCHOOL IN BARANGAY INOYONAN, MUNICIPALITY
OF BULA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO
FELIPE P. PANTON HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Inoyonan High School in
Barangay Inoyonan, Municipality of Bula, Province of
Camarines Sur is hereby changed to Felipe P. Panton High
School.

SEC. 2. The main campus of the Felipe P. Panton
High School shall continue to remain in its present location in
Barangay Inoyonan, Municipality of Bula, Province of Camarines
Sur.

 

Src. 38. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
