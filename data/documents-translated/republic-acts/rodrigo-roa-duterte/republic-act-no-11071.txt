AN ACT CONVERTING THE MAMAGUM ELEMENTARY
SCHOOL IN BARANGAY MAMAGUM, MUNICIPALITY
OF SULTAN NAGA DIMAPORO, PROVINCE OF LANAO
DEL NORTE INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL
TO BE KNOWN AS MAMAGUM INTEGRATED
SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Mamagum Elementary School in
Barangay Mamagum, Municipality of Sultan Naga Dimaporo,
Province of Lanao del Norte is hereby converted into an integrated
school to be known as Mamagum Integrated School.

Sc. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records
of the Mamagum Elementary School are hereby transferred
to and absorbed by the Mamagum Integrated School.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the Mamagum Integrated School, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Src. 4. The Secretary of Education shall formulate
the rules and regulations implementing the provisions of
this Act within one hundred twenty (120) days after its effectivity.

Src. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper

of general circulation.
