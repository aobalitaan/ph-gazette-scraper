AN ACT DECLARING MAY 3 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE,
IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING
ANNIVERSARY, TO BE KNOWN AS “STA. CRUZ
DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled::

SECTION 1. May 3of every year is hereby declared a special
working holiday in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of
Marinduque, to be known as “Sta. Cruz Day”, in commemoration
of its founding anniversary. Upon proclamation of the President,
May 3 of every year shall be a special nonworking holiday in the
Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Marinduque.
