[ REPUBLIC ACT No.1 1066]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE (LTO) LICENSING CENTER IN THE
CITY OF BATANGAS, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS
INTO A REGULAR LICENSING CENTER TO BE
KNOWN AS THE BATANGAS CITY LICENSING
CENTER OF BATANGAS CITY, BATANGAS AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Land Transportation Office (LTO)
Licensing Center in the City of Batangas, Province of Batangas
is hereby converted into a Regular Licensing Center to be
known as Batangas City Licensing Center.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
a Regular LTO Licensing Center to be known as the Batangas
City Licensing Center, the funding of which shall be charged
against the current year’s appropriations of the Batangas City
Licensing Center. Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary
for the continued operation of Batangas City Licensing Center
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 8. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
following the completion ofits publication in the Official Gazette
or in two (2) national newspapers of general circulation.
