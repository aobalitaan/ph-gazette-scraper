[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 462%]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PARACELIS
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY
POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF PARACELIS,
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE TO SERAPIO GAWAN
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Paracelis National High School
in Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Paracelis, Mountain
Province is hereby changed to Serapio Gawan National High
School. .

SEC. 2. The main campus of the Serapio Gawan National
High School shall continue to remain in its present location
in Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Paracelis, Mountain
Province.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in at least two (2)
newspapers of general circulation.
