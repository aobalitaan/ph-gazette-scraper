[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 1161]

AN ACT CREATING THE ILIGAN CITY DISTRICT
ENGINEERING OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A district engineering office is hereby created
in the Lone Legislative District of the City of Iligan to be known
as the Iligan City District Engineering Office.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Public Works
and Highways shall include in the Department’s program the
operationalization of a separate district engineering office in the
City of Iligan, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

oe

Sec. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general
circulation.
