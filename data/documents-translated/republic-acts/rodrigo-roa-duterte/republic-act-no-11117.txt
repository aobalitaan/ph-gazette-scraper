[REPUBLIC Act No.11117}

AN ACT DECLARING JULY 4 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE,
IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING
ANNIVERSARY, TO BE KNOWN AS “ARAW NG
LANAO”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. July 4 of every year is hereby declared a
special nonworking holiday in the Province of Lanao del Norte,
in commemoration of its founding anniversary, to be known
as “Araw ng Lanao”.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
