[REPUBLIC Act No. 11349]

AN ACT CREATING THREE (3) ADDITIONAL BRANCHES
OF THE REGIONAL TRIAL COURT IN THE THIRD
JUDICIAL REGION TO BE STATIONED AT MACABEBE,
PAMPANGA, FURTHER AMENDING FOR THE
PURPOSE SECTION 14(C) OF BATAS PAMBANSA
BLG. 129, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS “THE JUDICIARY
REORGANIZATION ACT OF 1980”, AS AMENDED,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Three (3) additional Regional Trial Court
branches of Macabebe, Pampanga, with seats thereat, are hereby
created in the Third Judicial Region.

SEc. 2. The Supreme Court shall assign the respective and
corresponding branch number for the branches newly created.

SEC. 3. The Chief Justice of the Supreme Court shall
immediately cause the inclusion in the Court’s program the
implementation of this Act.
