[ REPUBLIC AcT No.11613)]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
IN BARANGAY MAGANHAN, CITY OF BAYBAY,
PROVINCE OF LEYTE TO BE KNOWN AS
MAGANHAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the
supervision of the Department of Education (DepEd), a
national high school in Barangay Maganhan, City of Baybay,
Province of Leyte to be known as Maganhan National High
School.

SEc. 2. The amount necessary for the operationalization
of the Maganhan National High School shall be charged
against those authorized in the current appropriations of the
DepEd. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the implementation
of this Act shall be included in the annual General Appropriations
Act.

SEC. 3. Within one hundred twenty (120) days from the
approval of this Act, the DepEd, in coordination with the
local governments of the City of Baybay and Barangay
Maganhan, shall issue the rules and regulations implementing
the provisions of this Act.

Sec. 4. All other laws, decrees, executive orders, rules
and regulations, and other issuances inconsistent with this
Act are hereby repealed or amended accordingly.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
