AN ACTRENAMING THE MUNICIPALITY OF LISTA IN THE
PROVINCE OF IFUGAO AS THE MUNICIPALITY OF
ALFONSO LISTA

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Municipality of Lista in the Province of
Ifugao is hereby renamed as Alfonso Lista.

Src. 2. The renaming of the Municipality of Lista shall be
effective upon ratification of the majority of the votes cast by
qualified voters in a plebiscite to be conducted and supervised by
the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) in the present
Municipality of Lista.

The expenses for such plebiscite shall be borne by the
Municipality of Lista, Province of Ifugao.


SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after

its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
