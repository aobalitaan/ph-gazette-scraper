[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11716]

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE
NORTHERN MINDANAO MEDICAL CENTER IN
CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY, AMENDING FOR THE
PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7938 AS AMENDED BY
REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10677, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section 2 of Republic Act No. 7938, as amended
by Republic Act No. 10677, is hereby further amended to read as
follows:

“SEC. 2. The health care services and facilities
of the Northern Mindanao Medical Center (NMMC)
shall be upgraded to conform with and be
commensurate to the bed capacity increase from six
hundred (600) to one thousand two hundred (1,200)
beds.

“Any future increase in bed capacity shall be
consistent with the hospital development plan which
shall be prepared by the Department of Health
(@OH).”

SEc. 2. Section 3 of the same Act is hereby amended to
read as follows:

“SEC. 3. The number of medical personnel,
the budget for personnel services and maintenance
and other operating expenses of the NMMC shall
correspondingly be increased. The Secretary of the
DOH shall, in coordination with the Secretary of the
Department of Budget and Management and the
Chairperson of the Civil Service Commission,
and after consultation with the Chief of NMMC,
determine the additional plantilla positions to be
created pursuant to this Act.”

SEC. 3. Section 4 of the same Act is hereby amended to
read as follows:

“SEC. 4. The increase in bed capacity of the
NMMC from six hundred (600) to one thousand two
hundred (1,200) beds shall be implemented
immediately upon approval of this Act.”

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
