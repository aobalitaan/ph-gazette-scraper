[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11824]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE KINANGAN ELEMENTARY
SCHOOLIN BARANGAY KINANGAN, MUNICIPALITY
OF MALITA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO OCCIDENTAL
INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN
AS KINANGAN INTEGRATED SCHOOL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Kinangan Elementary School in Barangay
Kinangan, Municipality of Malita, Province of Davao Occidental
is hereby converted into an integrated school to be known as
Kinangan Integrated School. .

Src. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of the
Kinangan Elementary School are hereby transferred to and
absorbed by the Kinangan Integrated School.
