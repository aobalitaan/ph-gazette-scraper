AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF LILOAN, PROVINCE OF CEBU
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be a district office of the Land
Transportation Office (LTO) to be located in the Municipality of
Liloan, Province of Cebu.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
(OTr) shall immediately include in the Department's programs
the operationalization of the LTO District Office in the
Municipality of Liloan, Province of Cebu, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.


SEC. 3. The Secretary of the DOTr shall issue the rules
and regulations necessary for the effective implementation of this
Act.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
