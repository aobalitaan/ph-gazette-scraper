[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11450]

AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY IN THE CITY OF
DASMARINAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE TO BE
KNOWN AS BARANGAY VICTORIA REYES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation of Barangay Victoria Reyes. — There
is hereby created a barangay in the City of Dasmarifias, Province
of Cavite to be known as Barangay Victoria Reyes.

SEC. 2. Territorial Boundaries. - The territorial
boundaries of Barangay Victoria Reyes shall be identified as

SEC. 3. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. — The
plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission
on Elections (COMELEC) pursuant to Provincial Ordinance
No. 2000-004 of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the Province
of Cavite, which ratified the creation of Barangay Victoria
Reyes as proclaimed by the Plebiscite Board of Canvassers on
October 19, 2008 shall serve as substantial compliance with
the requirement under Section 10 of Republic Act No. 7160,
otherwise known as the “Local Government Code of 1991”,
as amended.

Ssc. 4. Incumbent Barangay Officials. — The incumbent
elected barangay officials of Barangay Victoria Reyes shall
continue to hold office until their successors shall have been

_elected and qualified.

Sec. 5. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. — All public
infrastructure and facilities existing therein at the time of the
approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or
compensation to and shall be administered by the new Barangay
Victoria Reyes. .

Src. 6. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. — Barangay
Victoria Reyes shall be entitled to the internal revenue allotment
(IRA) share pursuant to Section 285 of the Local Government
Code of 1991, as amended.

 

 

SEC. 7. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a
newspaper of general circulation.
