[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11132]

AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 21 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FABIAN, PROVINCE OF
PANGASINAN, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS
FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. March 21 of every year is hereby declared a
special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of San Fabian,
Province of Pangasinan, in commemoration of its founding
anniversary.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
