[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11622]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY TALIPAPA, QUEZON CITY, METRO
MANILA TO BE KNOWN AS THE TALIPAPA
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the
supervision of the Department of Education, a national high
school in Barangay Talipapa, Quezon City, Metro Manila to be
known as the Talipapa National High School.

Src. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization
of the Talipapa National High School, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. Within ninety (90) days from the approval of
this Act, the Secretary of Education shall issue the rules and
regulations implementing the provisions of this Act.

*

SEC. 4..This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
