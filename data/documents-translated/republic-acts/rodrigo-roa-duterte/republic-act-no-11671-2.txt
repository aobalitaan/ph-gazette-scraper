[REPUBLIC Act No. 11671]

AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS
BARANGAY LA MEDALLA IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF PIODURAN, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation of Barangay La Medalla. — The
Sitio of La Medalla is hereby separated from Barangay
Caratagan in the Municipality of Pioduran, Province of Albay,
and constituted into a distinct and independent barangay to
be known as Barangay La Medalla.

SEC. 2. Territorial Boundaries. — The territorial
boundaries of Barangay La Medalla are hereby described
as follows:

“Bounded by the following barangays of the Municipality
of Pioduran: on the North by Barangay Flores; on the Northeast
by Barangay Binodegahan; on the Southeast by Barangay
Caratagan; on the Southwest by Barangay Banawan; on the West
by Barangay Marigondon; and on the Northwest by Barangay
Panganiran.

SEC. 3. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. — The
plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on
Elections (COMELEC) in Barangay La Medalla, pursuant to
Provincial Ordinance No. 09-93 dated July 1, 1993 and approved
on August 9, 1993 by the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the
Province of Albay, which ratified the creation of Barangay
La Medalla as proclaimed by the Provincial Board of Canvassers
on November 21, 1993, shall serve as substantial compliance with
the plebiscite requirement under Section 10 of Republic Act
No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the “Local Government
Code of 1991”.

SEc. 4. Incumbent Barangay Officials. — The incumbent
barangay officials of Barangay La Medalla shall continue to hold
office until such time that their successors have been duly elected
and qualified.

Sec. 5. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. — All public
infrastructure and facilities existing therein at the time of the
approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or
compensation to and shall be administered by the new barangay.

SEC. 6. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. — Barangay
La Medalla shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA)
shares pursuant to Section 285 of the Local Government Code of
1991, as amended.

SEC. 7. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect
fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in
a newspaper of general circulation.

Approved,

   

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives
