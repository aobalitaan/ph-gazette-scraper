[ REPUBLIC Act No. 11461]

AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 26 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF
DASMARINAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, AND ALLITS
BARANGAYS TO BE KNOWN AS THE “FOUNDATION
DAY OF THE CITY OF DASMARINAS”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November 26 of every year is hereby declared
a special working holiday in the City of Dasmariiias, Province of
Cavite, and all its barangays to be known as the “Foundation
Day of the City of Dasmarifias”. Upon proclamation of the
President, November 26 of every year shall be a special
nonworking holiday in the City of Dasmarifias, Province
of Cavite.

 

Sec. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
after its aided in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
