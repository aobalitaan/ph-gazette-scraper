[REPUBLIC AcT No. 11143]

AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 15 OF EVERY YEAR A
SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF OPOL, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS
ORIENTAL, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS
FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY, TO BE KNOWN
AS “ARAW NG OPOL”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June 15 of every year is hereby declared
a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Opol,
Province of Misamis Oriental, in commemoration of its
founding anniversary, to be known as “Araw ng Opol”.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
