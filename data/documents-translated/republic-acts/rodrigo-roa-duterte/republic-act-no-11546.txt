AN ACT REAPPORTIONING THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN
INTO SIX (6) LEGISLATIVE DISTRICTS

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Province of Bulacan is hereby reapportioned
into six (6) legislative districts to take effect in the 2022 national
and local elections. Each legislative district of the province shall
