AN ACT DECLARING JULY 4 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF CADIZ,
PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, IN
COMMEMORATION OF ITS CHARTER DAY
ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. July 4 of every year is hereby declared
a special nonworking holiday in the City of Cadiz, Province
of Negros Occidental, in commemoration of its charter
day anniversary.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days following
the completion of its publication in the Official Gazette or in
a newspaper of general circulation.
