AN ACT RENAMING THE HILLTOP RIZAL PHILIPPINE
NATIONAL POLICE (PNP) COMMAND IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL
AS CAMP GENERAL LICERIO GERONIMO

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Hilltop Rizal Philippine National Police
(PNP) Command in the Municipality of Taytay, Province of
Rizal is hereby renamed as Camp General Licerio Geronimo.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
