[REPUBLIC Act No. 11142]

AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 1 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF JASAAN, PROVINCE OF
MISAMIS ORIENTAL, IN COMMEMORATION
OF ITS FOUNDING. ANNIVERSARY, TO BE
KNOWN AS “ARAW NG JASAAN”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 1 of every year is hereby declared
a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Jasaan,
Province of Misamis Oriental, in commemoration of its
founding anniversary, to be known as “Araw ng Jasaan”.

 

Skc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
