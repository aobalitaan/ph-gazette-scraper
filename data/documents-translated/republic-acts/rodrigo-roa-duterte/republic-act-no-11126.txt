[REPUBLIC ACT No. 11126]

AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 22 OF EVERY
YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT
IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING
ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November 22 of every year is hereby
declared a special nonworking holiday in the Province of
Sultan Kudarat in commemoration of its founding
anniversary.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
