AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE
AMANG RODRIGUEZ MEMORIAL MEDICAL
CENTER IN THE CITY OF MARIKINA FROM THREE
HUNDRED (300) TO FIVE HUNDRED (500) BEDS,
UPGRADING THE SERVICE FACILITIES AND
PROFESSIONAL HEALTH CARE THEREIN, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

 

Be tt enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The authorized bed capacity of the Amang
Rodriguez Memorial Medical Center (ARMMC) in the City of
Marikina is hereby increased from three hundred (300) to five
hundred (500) beds.

SEC. 2. The existing hospital facilities and professional
health care services of the ARMMC are also hereby upgraded
and increased to conform with and be commensurate to the
increase in bed capacity.

SEC. 3. Upon prior consultation with the ARMMC, the
Department of Health shall, in coordination with the
Department of Budget and Management and the Civil Service
Commission, determine the additional plantilla positions to be
created and filled up in the workforce of the ARMMC.

SEC. 4. The increase in the bed capacity of the ARMMC
from three hundred (300) to five hundred (500) beds and the
upgrading of its facilities as provided for under Sections 1 and
2 of this Act shall be implemented consistent with the five
(5)-year development plan of the hospital.

SEC. 5. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department’s programs the implementation of this Act,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEC. 6. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected
shall remain in full force and effect.

SEC. 7. Republic Act No. 7209, as amended by Republic
Act No. 9419, is hereby further amended. All other laws,
decrees, orders, issuances, rules and regulations, or parts
thereof which are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act
are hereby repealed, amended or modified accordingly.

SEC. 8. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
