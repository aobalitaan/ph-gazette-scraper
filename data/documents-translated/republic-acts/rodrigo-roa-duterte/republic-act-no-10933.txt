[ REPUBLIC Act No. 10933 ]

AN ACT DIVIDING BARANGAY NORTH BAY BOULEVARD
SOUTH (NBBS) IN THE CITY OF NAVOTAS INTO
THREE (3) DISTINCT AND INDEPENDENT
BARANGAYS TO BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY NBBS
PROPER, BARANGAY NBBS KAUNLARAN AND

BARANGAY NBBS DAGAT-DAGATAN

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Division of North Bay Boulevard South. —
Barangay North Bay Boulevard South (NBBS) in the City of
Navotas is hereby divided into three (3) distinct and
independent barangays to be known as Barangay NBBS
Proper, Barangay NBBS Kaunlaran and Barangay NBBS
Dagat-Dagatan.
