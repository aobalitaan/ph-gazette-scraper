f REPUBLIC Act No. 11196]

AN ACT UPGRADING THE LABUAN PUBLIC HOSPITAL
IN ZAMBOANGA CITY INTO A LEVEL II HOSPITAL
TO BE KNOWN AS THE LABUAN GENERAL
HOSPITAL, INCREASING ITS BED CAPACITY FOR
GENERAL CARE SERVICES TO ONE HUNDRED (100),
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Labuan Public Hospital located
in Zamboanga City is hereby upgraded into a Level II
Hospital to be known as the Labuan General Hospital and its
authorized bed capacity for general care services increased to
one hundred (100).

SEC. 2. The existing hospital facilities and professional
health care services of the Labuan General Hospital are
hereby upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to the
increase in its bed capacity as provided in Section 1 hereof.

SEC. 3. The Department of Health shall, in coordination
with the Department of Budget and Management and the
Labuan General Hospital, determine the additional staff. plantilla
positions to be created pursuant to this Act.

Suc. 4. The increase in bed capacity of the Labuan
General Hospital to one hundred (100) beds for general care
services shall be implemented for a period of not more than
four (4) years from the approval of this Act.

SEC. 5. All laws, decrees, orders, issuances, rules
and regulations or parts thereof inconsistent with the
provisions of this Act are hereby repealed or modified
accordingly.

SEC. 6. The Secretary of Health shall immediately
include in the Department’s programs the implementation of
this Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 7. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper
of general circulation.
