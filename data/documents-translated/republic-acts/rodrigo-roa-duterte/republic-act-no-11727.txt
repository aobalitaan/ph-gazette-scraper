[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11727]

AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 21 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN TAGAYTAY CITY,
PROVINCE OF CAVITE IN COMMEMORATION OF
ITS FOUNDATION DAY TO BE KNOWN AS “CHARTER,
DAY OF TAGAYTAY CITY” OR “ARAW NG LUNGSOD
NG TAGAYTAY”, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE
REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10775

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section 1 of Republic Act No. 10775 is hereby
amended to read as follows:

“SECTION 1. June 21 of every year is
hereby declared a special nonworking holiday
in Tagaytay City, Province of Cavite in
commemoration of its foundation day to be
known as “Charter Day of Tagaytay City” or
“Araw ng Lungsod ng Tagaytay”.


SEC. 2. Section 2 of Republic Act No. 10775 is
hereby repealed.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation. ,
