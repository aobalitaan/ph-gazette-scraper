[ REPUBLIC Act No. 11182]

AN ACT MOVING THE DATE OF “CATARMAN DAY”
TO AUGUST 30 OF EVERY YEAR, AMENDING FOR
THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9203, ENTITLED
“AN ACT DECLARING THE FIFTEENTH (15T) DAY
OF AUGUST OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL
NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF CATARMAN, PROVINCE OF CAMIGUIN, TO BE
KNOWN AS ‘CATARMAN DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section 1 of Republic Act No. 9203 is hereby
amended to read as follows:

“SECTION 1. August 30 of every year is hereby
declared a special nonworking holiday in the
Municipality of Catarman, Province of Camiguin, to
commemorate its foundation day anniversary, to be
known as ‘Catarman Day’.”

 

 

SEC. 2. The Municipal Government of Catarman in
the Province of Camiguin shall disseminate to its component
barangays, as well as to the Provincial Government of Camiguin,
through means it deems proper and expedient, the change of
the date of Catarman Day, for proper coordination of activities.

Sec. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
