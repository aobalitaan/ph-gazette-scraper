[REPUBLIC Act No. 11497]

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE LAS
PINAS GENERAL HOSPITAL AND SATELLITE
TRAUMA CENTER IN LAS PINAS CITY, METRO
MANILA FROM TWO HUNDRED (200) TO FIVE
HUNDRED (500) BEDS, UPGRADING ITS
PROFESSIONAL HEALTH CARE SERVICES AND
FACILITIES, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS

THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the

Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The authorized bed capacity of the Las Pifias
, General Hospital and Satellite Trauma Center (LPGHSTC) in
, Las Pifias City, Metro Manila is hereby increased from two hundred

(200) to five hundred (500) beds.

vw

SEC. 2. The existing professional health care services and
facilities of the LPGHSTC are also hereby upgraded to conform
with and be commensurate to the increase in bed capacity as
provided for in Section 1 hereof.

SEC. 3. The existing workforce of the LPGHSTC, which
includes doctors, nurses and support personnel, shall also be
increased correspondingly. The Secretary of the Department of
Health (OH) shall, in coordination with the Secretary of the
Department of Budget and Management (DBM) and the
Commissioner of the Civil Service Commission (CSC), and after
consultation with the Chief of the LPGHSTC, determine the
additional new positions tobe created and filled up.

Sec. 4. Any future increase in bed capacity, service
capability and staffing requirements shall be consistent with the
hospital development plan which shall be prepared and submitted
by the Medical Center Chief of the LPGHSTC to the DOH to
reflect the developmental goals of the hospital.

SEC. 5. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the program of the DOH the implementation of this Act, the
funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEC. 6. The Secretary of Health, in coordination with the
Secretary of the DBM and the Commissioner of the CSC shall,
within ninety (90) days from the effectivity of this Act, promulgate
the necessary rules and regulations for its effective implementation.

SEC. 7. If any part or provision of this Act is declared
unconstitutional or invalid, the remaining parts or provisions
not affected shall remain in full force and effect.

Suc. 8. Alllaws, decrees, issuances, rules and regulations
or parts thereof inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

SEC. 9. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
