AN ACT CREATING A NEW DISTRICT ENGINEERING
OFFICE IN THE THIRD LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT OF
THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DELNORTE AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the
supervision of the Department of Public Works and Highways,
an additional district engineering office for the Third
Legislative District of the Province of Zamboanga del Norte.
It shall be known as the Fourth District Engineering Office
and shall be located in-the Municipality of Siocon, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte.

Src. 2. The Fourth District Engineering Office shall
serve the municipalities of Baliguian, Siraway, Siocon,
and Sibuco, all within the Third Legislative District of the
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

Src. 3. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall
include'in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the Fourth District Engineering Office in the Province of
Zamboanga del Norte, the funding of which shall be included
in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SrEc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
