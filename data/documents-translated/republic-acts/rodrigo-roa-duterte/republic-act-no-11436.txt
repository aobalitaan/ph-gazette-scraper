[REPUBLIC Act No. 11436]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MAMBURAO, PROVINCE OF
OCCIDENTAL MINDORO AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled: i

SECTION 1. There shall be established a district office
of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality
of Mamburao, Province of Occidental Mindoro.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s programs the operationalization
of the LTO District Office in the Municipality of Mamburao,
Province of Occidental Mindoro, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
