[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11428]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE
OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MORONG, PROVINCE OF
RIZAL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a regular district
office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality
of Morong, Province of Rizal. :

Src. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization of
the LTO District Office in the Municipality of Morong, Province
of Rizal, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

 

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general
circulation.
