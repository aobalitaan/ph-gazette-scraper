[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11536]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SEPARATE SCHOOLS DIVISION
OFFICE IN THE CITY OF CANLAON, PROVINCE OF
NEGROS ORIENTAL, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE
SECTION 86 OF REPUBLIC ACT NO, 3445, ENTITLED
“AN ACT CREATING THE CITY OF CANLAON”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the

Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section 86 of Republic Act No. 3445 ig hereby
deleted and a new Section 86is hereby inserted, to read as follows:

“SECTION 86. Establishment of a Division
City Schools. — There shall be established,
under the supervision of the Department of

Education (DepEd), a City Schools Division Office

iv

“

in the City of Canlaon which shall have
jurisdiction over the school districts within the
City of Canlaon and headed by a division
superintendent who must possess the necessary
qualifications required by the DepEd.

“The staff complement for the City Schools
Division Office of the City of Canlaon shall also

possess the necessary qualifications required by
the DepEd.

“The salaries of the superintendent,
supervisors, principals, teachers, nonteaching
personnel and other operational expenses of the
primary, intermediate high schools and other
public schools in the city shall be borne by the
national government: Provided, That the powers
and duties of the local government, as contained
in the Local Government Code in support of the
schools, shall not be abridged, curtailed or
diminished.” 3

SEC. 2. The amount necessary for the operationalization of
the City Schools Division Office in the City of Canlaon shall be
charged against those authorized in the current appropriations
of the DepEd. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the
implementation of this Act shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act. ‘

SEC. 8. Within ninety (90) days from the effectivity of
this Act, the DepEd, in coordination with the city government of
Canlaon, shall issue rules and regulations implementing the
provisions of this Act.

Sec. 4. Allother laws, decrees, executive orders, rules and
regulations and other issuances inconsistent with this Act are
hereby repealed or amended accordingly.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
generai circulation.
