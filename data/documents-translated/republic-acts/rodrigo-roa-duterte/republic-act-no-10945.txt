AN ACT ESTABLISHING A MULTI-SPECIES MARINE
HATCHERY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PEREZ,
PROVINCE OF QUEZON AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be ii enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the
supervision of the Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources
of the Department of Agriculture (BFAR-DA), a multi-species
marine hatchery in the Municipality of Perez, Province
of Quezon.

SEC. 2. The BFAR-DA shall, within two (2) years after
the construction of the multi-species marine hatchery, transfer
the management of the same to the local government unit
concerned through a memorandum of agreement. It shall
implement a training and phasing-in program for local
government personnel on the management and operation of
the hatchery.

SEc. 3. The Secretary of Agriculture shall include in
the Department's programs the establishtment of the
multi-species marine hatchery in the Municipality of Perez,

Province of Quezon, the funding of which shall be included
in the annual General Appropriations Act. The local
government unit concerned shall set aside funds from
any available local revenue in an amount deemed
appropriate for the operationalization of the hatchery.

Src. 4. Within sixty (60) days from the passage of this
Act, the Secretary of Agriculture shall issue the rules and
regulations on the supervision, monitoring, transfer, or
withdrawal by the BFAR of the multi-species marine hatchery.

Sec. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
after its publication in the Official Gazetie or in a newspaper
of general circulation.
