[REPUBLIC AcT No. 11437]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE
OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO)
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RONDA IN THE 7TH
DISTRICT OF THE PROVINCE OF CEBU AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

'

SECTION 1. There is hereby established a regular
district office of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) to be
located in the Municipality of Ronda in the 7 District of the
Province of Cebu.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the LTO Regular District Office in the Municipality of Ronda in
the 7 District of the Province of Cebu, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
