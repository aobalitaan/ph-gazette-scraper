[ REPUBLIC Act No, 11073}

AN ACT CONVERTING THE MABUHAY ELEMENTARY
SCHOOLIN BARANGAY MABUHAY, MUNICIPALITY
‘OF SULTAN NAGA DIMAPORO, PROVINCE OF
LANAO DEL NORTE INTO AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL
TO BE KNOWN AS MABUHAY INTEGRATED SCHOOL,

AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Mabuhay Elementary School in
Barangay Mabuhay, Municipality of Sultan Naga Dimaporo,
Province of Lanao del Norte is hereby converted into an
integrated school to be known as Mabuhay Integrated School.

Sec. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities, and records
of the Mabuhay Elementary School are hereby transferred
to and absorbed by the Mabuhay Integrated School.

SEc. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the Mabuhay Integrated School, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Src. 4. The Secretary of Education shall formulate
the rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this
Act within one hundred twenty (120) days after its effectivity.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper

of general circulation.
