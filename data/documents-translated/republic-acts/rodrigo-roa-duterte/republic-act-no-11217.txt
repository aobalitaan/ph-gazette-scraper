[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11217]

AN ACT DECLARING JANUARY 17 OF EVERY YEAR
AS JAMES LEONARD TAGLE GORDON DAY,
A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
CITY OF OLONGAPO AND IN THE SUBIC BAY
FREEPORT ZONE, IN RECOGNITION OF THE
ACHIEVEMENTS, CONTRIBUTIONS AND HEROISM
OF JAMES LEONARD TAGLE GORDON

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. January 17 of every year is hereby declared
a special nonworking holiday in the City of Olongapo,
Province of Zambales, and in the Subic Bay Freeport Zone
to be known as the “James Leonard Tagle Gordon Day”,
in recognition of the achievements, contributions and
heroism of James Leonard Tagle Gordon.

 

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazetie or in a newspaper
of general circulation.
