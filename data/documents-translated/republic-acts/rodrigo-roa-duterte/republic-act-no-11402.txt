[REPUBLIC Act No. 11402}

AN ACT SEPARATING THE SITIO OF ASCOMO
FROM BARANGAY PULUNGMASLE IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF GUAGUA, PROVINCE OF
PAMPANGA AND CONSTITUTING IT INTO A
DISTINCT AND INDEPENDENT BARANGAY TO
BE KNOWN AS BARANGAY ASCOMO

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation of Barangay Ascomo. — The sitio of
Ascomo is hereby separated from Barangay Pulungmasle in the
Municipality of Guagua, Province of Pampanga to constitute it
into a distinct and independent barangay to be known as Barangay
Ascomo.

Sec. 2. Territorial Boundaries. — The territorial
boundaries of Barangay Ascomo shall be identified as follows:

Src. 3. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. —
The plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission
on Elections (COMELEC) on March 10, 1994 pursuant
to Provincial Ordinance No. 26, Series of 1993 of the
Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the Province of Pampanga,
which ratified the creation of Barangay Ascomo shall serve as
substantial compliance with the plebiscite requirement under
Section 10 of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise
known as the “Local Government Code of 1991”.

Src. 4. Barangay Officials. - The incumbent elected
barangay officials of Barangay Ascomo shall continue to hold
office until such time that their successors have been duly
elected and qualified.

Sc. 5. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. — All public
infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein
at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred
without cost or compensation to and shall be administered
by the new barangay.

Src. 6. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. — Barangay
Ascomo shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment
(IRA) shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160,
as amended, otherwise known as the “Local Government Code
of 1991”.

Suc. 7. Effectivity. - This Act shall take effect fifteen
-(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in any

newspaper of general circulation.
