[RepuBLIC Act No. 11273]

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY FOR GENERAL
HOSPITAL SERVICES OF THE EVERSLEY CHILDS
SANITARIUM HOSPITAL IN MANDAUE CITY,
PROVINCE OF CEBU, FROM FIFTY (50) BEDS TO TWO
HUNDRED (200) BEDS, RENAMING THE HOSPITAL AS
THE EVERSLEY CHILDS SANITARIUM AND GENERAL
HOSPITAL, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

 

 

 

 

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Eversley Childs Sanitarium Hospital in
Mandaue City, Province of Cebu is hereby upgraded into a
general hospital to be known as the Eversley Childs Sanitarium
and General Hospital.

SEc. 2. The existing service facilities and professional
health care services of the Eversley Childs Sanitarium and General
Hospital are hereby upgraded, commensurate to the reapportion
of its authorized bed capacity as provided in Section 1 of this Act.

The existing workforce of the Eversley Childs Sanitarium
and General Hospital shall be increased correspondingly. The
Department of Health (DOH) shall, in coordination with the
Department of Budget and Management and the Civil Service
Commission, and after consultation with the Eversley Childs
Sanitarium and General Hospital, determine the additional
plantilla positions to be created and filled up.

Src. 38. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department’s programs the implementation of this Act,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Sec. 4. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected
shall remain in full force and effect.

Sec. 5. All laws, decrees, rules and regulations
inconsistent with this Act shall be deemed amended or repealed
accordingly.

SEc. 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
