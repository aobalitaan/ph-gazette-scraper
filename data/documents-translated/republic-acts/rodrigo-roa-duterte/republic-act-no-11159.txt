AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO
MAYLEEN TING

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Ms. Mayileen Ting is hereby granted Philippine
citizenship with all the rights, privileges and prerogatives,
as well as the duties and obligations appurtenant thereto.

Src. 2. Ms. Mayleen Ting shall take the Oath of Allegiance
to. the Republic of the Philippines before an officer duly
authorized to administer the same. The Oath of Allegiance shall
be registered with the Bureau of Immigration.

SEc. 3. Upon registration of the Oath of Allegiance, the
Bureau of Immigration shall issue a Certificate of Naturalization
to Ms. Mayleen Ting who shall thereupon enter into the full
enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.

Sec. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in any newspaper of

general circulation.
