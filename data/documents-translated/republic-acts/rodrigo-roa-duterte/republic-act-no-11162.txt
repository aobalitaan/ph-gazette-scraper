AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN EXTENSION OFFICE OF
THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO)
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE'S POINT,
PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the
supervision of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) Palawan
District Office, an Extension Office of the LTO in the
Municipality of Brooke’s Point, Province of Palawan.

SEC, 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department's programs the operationalization of
the LTO Extension Office in the Municipality of Brooke’s Point,
Province of Palawan, the funding of which shall be included in
the annual General Appropriations Act.
