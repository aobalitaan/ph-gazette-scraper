AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF
THE CAGAYAN VALLEY MEDICAL CENTER (CVMC)
IN TUGUEGARAO CITY, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN,
FROM FIVE HUNDRED (500) TO ONE THOUSAND
(1,000) BEDS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS
THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Cagayan Valley
Medical Center (CVMC) shall be increased from five hundred
(500) to one thousand (1,000) beds. Any future increase in

 

bed capacity shall be consistent with the hospital
development plan which shall be prepared and submitted
by the Medical Center Chief of the CVMC to the Department
of Health (DOH) to reflect the development goals of the
hospital. .

Suc. 2. The existing professional health care services
and facilities of the CVMC are also hereby upgraded to
conform with and be commensurate to the in¢rease in bed
capacity as provided for in Section 1 hereof, consistent with
the hospital development plan.

SEC. 3. The existing workforce of the CVMC, which
includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel, shall also
be increased correspondingly, consistent with the hospital
development plan. The Secretary of the DOH shall, after
consultation with the Medical Center Chief of the CVMC,
and in coordination with the Secretary of the Department
of Budget and Management and the Commissioner of the
Civil Service Commission, determine the additional new
positions to be created and filled up.

Sec. 4. The Secretary of Health shall immediately
include in the program of the DOH the implementation of
this Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

SkEc. 5. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not
affected shall remain in full force and effect.

SEC. 6. All laws, executive orders, issuances, rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed
or modified accordingly.

SEC. 7. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a national
newspaper of general circulation.

Approved,
