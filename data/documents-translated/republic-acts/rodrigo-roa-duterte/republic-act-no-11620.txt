[REPUBLIC AcT No. 11620]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF SAN MANUEL
HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY SAN MANUEL,
CITY OF SAN JOSE DEL MONTE, PROVINCE
OF BULACAN, TO SAN MANUEL NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of San Manuel High School in
Barangay San Manvel, City of San Jose Del Monte, Province
of Bulacan, is hereby changed to San Manuel National High
School.

Sec. 2. The main campus of the San Manuel National
High School shall continue to remain in its present location
in Barangay San Manvel, City of San Jose Del Monte, Province
of Bulacan.

SEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
‘general circulation.
