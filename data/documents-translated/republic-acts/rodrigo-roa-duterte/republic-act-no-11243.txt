[RepuBLic Act No. 11243}

AN ACT REAPPORTIONING THE FIRST LEGISLATIVE
DISTRICT OF THE PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO
THEREBY CREATING THE LONE LEGISLATIVE
DISTRICT OF GENERAL SANTOS CITY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Reapportioning of the First Legislative
District. — The First Legislative District of the Province of
South Cotabato is hereby reapportioned in order to create the
Lone Legislative District of General Santos City to commence
in the next national and local elections after the effectivity of
this Act. The Legislative Districts of the Province shall now
be composed of the following:

(a) First Legislative District:

(1) Polomolok
(2) Tampakan
(8) Tupi

(b) Second Legislative District:

(1) Koronadal City
(2) Banga

(8) Lake Sebu

(4) Norala

(5) Santo Nifio
(6) Surallah

(7) Tantangan
(8) TBoli

() Third Legislative District:
(1) General Santos City.

SEC. 2. Holdover. — The incumbent Representatives of
the present First and Second Legislative Districts of the
Province of South Cotabato shall continue to represent
the districts until the new Representatives shall have been
elected and qualified.

Src. 3. Rules and Regulations. — The Commission on
Elections shall issue the necessary rules and regulations to
implement this Act within thirty (30) days after its effectivity.

SEC. 4. Repealing Clause. — All laws, rules and
regulations which are inconsistent with this Act are hereby
repealed, amended or modified accordingly.

SEc. 5. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in
a newspaper of general circulation.
