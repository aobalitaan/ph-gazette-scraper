AN ACT DECLARING JULY 23 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL

WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF BATANGAS,

PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, IN COMMEMORATION OF

| ITS FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY, TO BE KNOWN AS
| i “BATANGAS CITY FOUNDATION DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
| Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. July 23 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the City of Batangas, Province of
Batangas, in commemoration of its founding anniversary, to be
known as “Batangas City Foundation Day”. Upon proclamation
of the President, July 23 of every year shall be a special
nonworking holiday in the City of Batangas, Province of Batangas.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general
circulation.
