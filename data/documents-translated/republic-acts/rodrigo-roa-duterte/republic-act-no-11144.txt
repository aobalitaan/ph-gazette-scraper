AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 19 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
WHOLE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA IN HONOR
OF THE BIRTH ANNIVERSARY OF OUR
NATIONAL HERO, DR. JOSE P. RIZAL, TO BE
KNOWN AS “ARAW NG KAPANGANAKAN NI
DR. JOSE P. RIZAL”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June 19 of every year is hereby declared
a special nonworking holiday in the whole Province of Laguna
in honor of the birth anniversary of our national hero,
Dr. Jose P. Rizal, to be known as “Araw ng Kapanganakan ni
Dr. Jose P. Rizal’.
