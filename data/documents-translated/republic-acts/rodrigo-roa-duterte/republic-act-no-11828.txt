AN ACT NAMING THE FIRST CEBU-MACTAN BRIDGE
TRAVERSING THE MACTAN CHANNEL LOCATED
AT A.C. CORTES AVENUE IN BARANGAY LOOC,
MANDAUE CITY UP TO THE MANDAUE-MACTAN
ROAD IN BARANGAY PAJO, LAPU-LAPU CITY, BOTH
IN THE PROVINCE OF CEBU, AS SERGING VELOSO
OSMENA JR. BRIDGE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The First Cebu-Mactan Bridge traversing the
Mactan Channel located at A.C. Cortes Avenue in Barangay Looc,
Mandave City up to the Mandaue-Mactan Road in Barangay Pajo,
Lapu-Lapu City, both in the Province of Cebu, comprising a total
distance of 854 meters, is hereby named as Serging Veloso Osmefia
dr. Bridge.

-

SEc. 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways
shall issue the necessary rules, orders, and circulars to implement *-
the provisions of this Act within sixty (60) days from its effectivity.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
