SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of

~the LTO district office in the Municipality of Aliaga, Province of

Nueva Ecija, the funding of which shall te included i in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
