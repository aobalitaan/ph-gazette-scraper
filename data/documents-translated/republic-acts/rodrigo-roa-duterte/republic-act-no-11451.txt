[RepuBLic Act No.11491]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT BRANCH OF THE LAND
TRANSPORTATION OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF MARAMAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established a district branch of
the Land Transportation Office (LTO) in the Municipality of
Maramag, Province of Bukidnon.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of a
district branch of the LTO in the Municipality of Maramag,
Province of Bukidnon, the funding of which shall be included in
the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

SxEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or ina newspaper of general
circulation.
