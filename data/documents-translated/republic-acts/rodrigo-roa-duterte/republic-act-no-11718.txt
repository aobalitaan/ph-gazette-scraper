[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11718]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TERTIARY GENERAL HOSPITAL
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ABULUG, PROVINCE
OF CAGAYAN VALLEY, TO BE KNOWN AS THE
NORTHWESTERN CAGAYAN GENERAL HOSPITAL,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established a tertiary general
hospital in the Municipality of Abulug, Province of Cagayan Valley
to be known as the Northwestern Cagayan General Hospital
(NWCGBH), which shall be under the direct control and supervision
of the Department of Health (DOH). Any future increase in bed


capacity shall be consistent with the hospital development plan
which shall be prepared by the DOH.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the DOH shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the implementation of this
Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. Within ninety (90) days from the effectivity of this

Act, the Secretary of the DOH shall prepare and issue the .

necessary rules and regulations for its effective implementation
consistent with the hospital development plan.

SEc. 4. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected
shall remain in full force and effect.

Src. 5. All laws, executive orders, issuances, rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

SEc. 6. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
