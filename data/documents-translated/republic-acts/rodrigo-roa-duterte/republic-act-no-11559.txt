[ REPUBLIC Act No. 11559}

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE
NAGUILIAN DISTRICT HOSPITAL IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF NAGUILIAN, PROVINCE OF LA
UNION FROM TWENTY-FIVE (25) BEDS TO FIFTY (50)
BEDS AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The authorized bed capacity of the Naguilian
District Hospital in the Municipality of Naguilian, Province of
La Union is hereby increased from twenty-five (25) beds to fifty
(50) beds. Any future increase in bed capacity shall be consistent
with the hospital development plan.

SEC. 2. The existing professional health care services and
facilities of the Naguilian District Hospital are hereby upgraded
to conform with and be commensurate to the increase in its bed
capacity as provided in Section 1 hereof.

SEC. 3. The Provincial Government of La Union shall
provide the necessary funds to support the increase in bed capacity
of the Naguilian District Hospital and the corresponding
upgrading ofits facilities and services.

SEC. 4. The amount of Two hundred million pesos
(P200,000,000.00) is hereby appropriated for the implementation
of this Act which shall be included and funded under the 2022
General Appropriations Act under the Health Facilities
Enhancement Program (HFEP) of the Department of Health.

SEC. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days att
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
