2011-7-048, Series of 2011 of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of
the Province of Sarangani, which ratified the creation of Barangay
Ladol as proclaimed by the Plebiscite Board of Canvassers on
August 18, 2012, shall serve as substantial compliance with the

SEC. 4. Incumbent Barangay Officials. — The incumbent
elected barangay officials of Barangay Ladol shall continue to
hold office until such time that their successors have been duly
elected and qualified.

Src. 5. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. — All public
infrastructure and facilities existing therein at the time of the
approval of this Act are hereby transferred without cost or
compensation to and shall be administered by the new Barangay
Ladol.

SEC. §. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. — Barangay
Ladol shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA) shares
pursuant to Section 285 of the Local Government Code of 1991,
as amended.

SEC. 7. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen (15)
days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper
of general circulation.
