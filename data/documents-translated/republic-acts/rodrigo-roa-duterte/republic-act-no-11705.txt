[REPUBLIC Act No. 11705]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING IN THE CITY OF CANDON,
PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR, A TERTIARY HOSPITAL,
TO BE KNOWN AS THE ILOCOS SUR MEDICAL
CENTER, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established in the City of
Candon, Province of Ilocos Sur a tertiary hospital, to be known
as the Ilocos Sur Medical Center, which shall be under the
direct control and supervision of the Department of Health (DOH).

Sc. 2. The DOH shall develop a hospital development plan
to define the service capacity, developmental goals and targets of
the hospital. Any future increase in bed capacity and upgrading

of health care facilities, service capability, and human resource
complement shall be based on the hospital development plan.

Src. 3. The DOH shall consult the local government
units in the Province of Ilocos Sur and the City of
Candon, and the other stakeholders in determining the site of
the hospital.

SEc. 4. The Secretary of Health shall immediately
include in the Department's program the implementation of
this Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 5. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not
affected shall remain in full force and effect.

Src. 6. All laws, executive orders, issuances, rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

Src. 7. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
