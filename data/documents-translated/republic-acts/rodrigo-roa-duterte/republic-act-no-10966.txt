[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 10966]

AN ACT DECLARING DECEMBER 8 OF EVERY
YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN
THE ENTIRE COUNTRY TO COMMEMORATE THE
FEAST OF THE IMMACULATE CONCEPTION
OF MARY, THE PRINCIPAL PATRONESS OF
THE PHILIPPINES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. December 8 of every year is hereby
declared a special nonworking holiday in the entire
country to commemorate the Feast of the Immaculate

Conception of Mary, the Principal Patroness of
the Philippines.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15)
days after its publication in the Official Gazette or
in a newspaper of general circulation.
