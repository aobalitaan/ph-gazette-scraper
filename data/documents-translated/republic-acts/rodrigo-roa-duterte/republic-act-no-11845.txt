AN ACT RENAMING THE CAGAYAN DE ORO CONVENTION
CENTER LOCATED AT BARANGAY INDAHAG,
CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY AS AQUILINO Q. PIMENTEL,
JR. INTERNATIONAL CONVENTION CENTER

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Cagayan de Oro Convention Center located
at Barangay Indahag, Cagayan de Oro City is hereby renamed as
Aquilino Q. Pimentel, Jr. International Convention Center.


SEc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
