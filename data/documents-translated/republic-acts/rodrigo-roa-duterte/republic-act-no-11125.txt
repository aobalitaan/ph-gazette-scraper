AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 17 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL
NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF
AGUSAN DEL SUR IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS
FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June 17 of every year is hereby declared
a special nonworking holiday in the Province of Agusan del
Sur in commemoration of its founding anniversary.

 

 

 

 

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
