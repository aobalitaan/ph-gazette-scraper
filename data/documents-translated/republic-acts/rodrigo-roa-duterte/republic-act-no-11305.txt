AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY MAUWAY, CITY OF MANDALUYONG,
TO BE KNOWN AS THE SENATE PRESIDENT
NEPTALI A. GONZALES INTEGRATED SCHOOL
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Establishment of an Integrated School. -
There is hereby established, under the supervision of the
Department of Education, an integrated school in Barangay
Mauway, City of Mandaluyong, National Capital Region,
to be known as the Senate President Neptali A. Gonzales
Integrated School.

SEC. 2. Appropriations. — The Secretary of Education
shall immediately include in the Department’s program the
operationalization of the Senate President Neptali A. Gonzales
Integrated School, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

AN ACT CONVERTING INTO AN INDEPENDENT
NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TO BE KNOWN AS
THE CURRIMAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL THE
CURRIMAO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL ~ PIAS-
GAANG CAMPUS IN BARANGAY PIAS NORTE,
MUNICIPALITY OF CURRIMAO, PROVINCE OF
ILOCOS NORTE, AND SEPARATING IT FROM ITS
MOTHER SCHOOL, THE CURRIMAO NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY POBLACION 2,
MUNICIPALITY OF CURRIMAO, PROVINCE OF
ILOCOS NORTE, HEREINAFTER RENAMED THE
WILBUR C. GO NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

 

 

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Currimao National High School — Pias-
Gaang Campus in Barangay Pias Norte, Municipality of
Currimao, Province of Ilocos Norte is hereby converted into
an independent national high school to be now known as
the Currimao National High School, and separated from
its mother school, the Currimao National High School
situated in Barangay Poblacion 2, Municipality of Currimao,
Province of Ilocos Norte.

The mother school situated in Barangay Poblacion 2,
Municipality of Currimao, Province of Ilocos Norte shall
henceforth be known as the Wilbur C. Go National High
School.

Src. 2. All personnel, assets, liabilities and records of
the Currimao National High School —Pias-Gaang Campus in
Barangay Pias Norte, Municipality of Currimao, Province of
Ilocos Norte are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the
newly converted national high school, now known as the
Currimao National High School.

SkEc. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the newly converted national high school, now known as the
Currimao National High School. The current year’s
appropriation of the mother school situated in Barangay
Poblacion 2, Municipality of Currimao, Province of [locos Norte
shall be absorbed by and remain with the now renamed Wilbur
C. Go National High School. Thereafter, the amount necessary
for the continued operation of both the Currimao National
High School and the Wilbur C. Go National High School
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Suc. 4. The Secretary of Education shall issue the rules
and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of this Act
within one hundred twenty (120) days after its effectivity.

 

SEc, 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
