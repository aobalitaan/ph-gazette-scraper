[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11049]

AN ACT RENAMING THE MOLINO BOULEVARD
TRAVERSING BARANGAY TALABA 4 AND
BARANGAY SAN NICOLAS 3 IN THE CITY OF
BACOOR, PROVINCE OF CAVITE AS BACOOR
BOULEVARD

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Molino Boulevard which traverses
barangays Talaba 4, Niog 3, Ligas 2, Ligas 3, Bayanan,
Mambog 4, Molino 2, Molino 3, San Nicolas 1 and San
Nicolas 3 in the City of Bacoor, Province of Cavite is hereby
renamed as Bacoor Boulevard.

Sec. 2. The Department of Public Works and
Highways shall place appropriate markers along Bacoor
Boulevard and issue the necessary orders and circulars to
implement the provision of this Act.

 

SEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days
after its publication in the Official Gazette or in at least
two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
