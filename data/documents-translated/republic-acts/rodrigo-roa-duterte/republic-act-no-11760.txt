[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 41760)

AN ACT CREATING AN EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE
MARITIME INDUSTRY AUTHORITY IN VIGAN
CITY, ILOCOS SUR, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. An extension office of the Maritime Industry
Authority (MARINA) shall be created in Vigan City, Ilocos Sur.
The extension office shall provide services for the residents of the
Province of Ilocos Sur and other nearby provinces.

. Sec. 2. The Administrator of the MARINA shall
immediately include in the Agency’s programs the
operationalization of the MARINA Extension Office in Vigan
City, Ilocos Sur, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.
