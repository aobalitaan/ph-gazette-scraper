AN ACT EXPANDING THE MANDATE AND SERVICE
CAPABILITY OF THE BICOL SANITARIUM IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF CABUSAO, PROVINCE OF
CAMARINES SUR, TO BE KNOWN AS THE BICOL
REGION GENERAL HOSPITAL AND GERIATRIC
MEDICAL CENTER, UPGRADING ITS SERVICE
FACILITIES, AUTHORIZING THE INCREASE OF ITS
MEDICAL PERSONNEL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS
THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The mandate and services of the Bicol
Sanitarium located in the Municipality of Cabusao, Province
of Camarines Sur is hereby expanded to include sanitaria,
general hospital services, and specialized health care services
for adult and ageing population, as well as a center for
research and development in the field of geriatric medicine.

The Bicol Sanitarium shall be renamed as the Bicol
Region General Hospital and Geriatric Medical Center
(BRGHGMC).

SEC. 2. The existing hospital facilities and professional
health care services of the BRGHGMC are hereby upgraded
and increased to conform with and be commensurate to the
increase in bed capacity. The facility will continuously work
to manage and support the leprosy eradication program of the
Department of Health (DOH) especially in Bicol. The number
of medical personnel shall be correspondingly increased.

SEC, 3. All personnel, medical and non-medical records,
assets and properties, including land and buildings, facilities,
and equipment, sites, and improvements thereof. obligations,
funds, and appropriations of the Biéol Sanitarium, including
the Bicol Skin Clinic located in Legazpi City, Province of
Albay are hereby transferred to BRGHGMC.

SEC. 4. The DOH, in coordination with the Civil Service
Commission, the Department of Budget and Management and
DOH Regional Health Office V, shall promulgate the
conversion plan and organizational and staffing pattern of the
said specialty medical center.

SEC. 5. The Secretary of Health shall immediately include
in the Department's programs the operationalization of the
BRGHGMC for a period of not more than three (3) years, the
initial funding of which shall be charged against the current
year’s appropriations of the Bicol Sanitarium. Thereafter, the
amount necessary for its continued operation shall be included
in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 6. The DOH, through its Regional Health Office V
Director and Medical Center Chief, in coordination with
concerned national and local government units, shall also prepare

the mechanisms for sourcing supplementary funds to augment
the operations of the BRGHGMC.

SEC. 7. Act 1711 of the Philippine Commission on the
establishment of the Bicol Treatment Station in Legazpi City,

 

Albay, is hereby repealed. All laws, executive orders,
issuances, rules and regulations inconsistent with this Act are
hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

SEC. 8. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
