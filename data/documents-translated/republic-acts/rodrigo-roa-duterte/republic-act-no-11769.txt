AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE
CITY OF GENERAL TRIAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senaté and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A district office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) is hereby established in the City of General Trias,
Province of Cavite.

Sec. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department's programs the operationalization of
the LTO district office in the City of General Trias, Province of
Cavite, the funding of which shall be included i in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

S&c. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15)-days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
