[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11761]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE SATELLITE OFFICE OF THE
MARITIME INDUSTRY AUTHORITY LOCATED IN
MAASIN CITY, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE
INTO AN EXTENSION OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Maritime Industry Authority
(MARINA) Satellite Office in Maasin City, Province of Southern
Leyte is hereby converted into a MARINA Extension Office. The
Extension Office shall provide services for the residents of the
Province of Southern Leyte and its neighboring municipalities
of Inopacan, Hilongos, Bato and Matalom, Province of Leyte.

SEC. 2. The Administrator of the MARINA shall
immediately include in the Agency’s programs the
operationalization of the MARINA Extension Office in Maasin

te,

. SEC. 3. Within sixty (60) days from the effectivity of this
Act, the Secretary of Transportation, through the Administrator
of the MARINA, shall issue the necessary rules and regulations
for its implementation.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general
circulation.
