[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11608]

AN ACT RENAMING ROOSEVELT AVENUE LOCATED IN
LEGISLATIVE DISTRICT I OF QUEZON CITY AS
FERNANDO POE JR. AVENUE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Roosevelt. Avenue located in Legislative
District I of Quezon City is hereby renamed as Fernando Poe Jr.
Avenue.

SEc. 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways
shall issue the necessary rules, orders, and circulars to implement
the provisions of this Act within sixty (60) days from its effectivity

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or ina newspaper of general
circulation.
