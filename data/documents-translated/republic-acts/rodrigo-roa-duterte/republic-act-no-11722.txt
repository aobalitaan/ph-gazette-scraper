[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11722]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE SULU SANITARIUM IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF JOLO, PROVINCE OF SULU,
INTO A GENERAL HOSPITAL TO BE KNOWN AS
THE SULU SANITARIUM AND GENERAL HOSPITAL,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Sulu Sanitarium in the Municipality
of Jolo, Province of Sulu is hereby converted into a general
hospital to be known as the Sulu Sanitarium and General
Hospital, under the direct supervision and control of the
Department of Health (DOH).

SEc. 2. Any future increase in bed capacity shall be
consistent with the hospital development plan that shall be
prepared by the Chief of Hospital of the Sulu Sanitarium and
General Hospital and submitted to the DOH.

Src. 3. The existing professional health care services
and facilities of the Sulu Sanitarium and General Hospital
shall also be upgraded to conform with and be commensurate to
the increase in bed capacity as provided for in Section 2 hereof.

Src. 4. The existing workforce of the Sulu Sanitarium
and General Hospital, which includes doctors, nurses, and
support personnel, shall be increased correspondingly.

SEc. 5. The Secretary of the DOH shall, after consultation
with the Chief of Hospital of the Sulu Sanitarium and General
Hospital, and in coordination with the Secretary of the
Department of Budget and Management and the Chairperson
of the Civil Service Commission, determine the additional new
positions to be created and filled up.

SEc. 6. The Secretary of the DOH shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the implementation of
this Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

Sec. 7. If any part or provision of this Act shall be held
unconstitutional or invalid, other provisions which are not
affected thereby shall continue to be in full force and effect.

Sec. 8. All laws, decrees, orders, issuances, rules and
regulations that are inconsistent with the provisions of this
Act are hereby repealed, amended or modified accordingly.

SEC. 9. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

 

 

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives
was passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate
of the Philippines on March 17, 2021 and January 31, 2022,
respectively.
