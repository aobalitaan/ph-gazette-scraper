AN ACT PROVIDING FREE IRRIGATION SERVICE,
AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT
NO. 3601, AS AMENDED, APPROPRIATING FUNDS
THEREFOR AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Short Title. - This Act shall be known as
the “Free Irrigation Service Act”.

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. — It is the policy of the
State to promote comprehensive rural development, food
self-sufficiency, equitable access to opportunities and sustained
productivity as key strategies to raise the quality of life in
rural areas and attain overall national development.

The State shall also ensure that vital support services
are made available, particularly irrigation service, through the
continued construction, repair and maintenance of necessary
irrigation facilities, to increase production of agricultural crops,
encourage productivity, and increase the incomes of farmers.
