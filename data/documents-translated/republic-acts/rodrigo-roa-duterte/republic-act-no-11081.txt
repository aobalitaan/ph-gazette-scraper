AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF
VETERANS REGIONAL HOSPITALIN BAYOMBONG,
NUEVA VIZCAYA FROM TWO HUNDRED (200) TO
FIVE HUNDRED (500), UPGRADING ITS SERVICES,
FACILITIES AND PROFESSIONAL HEALTH CARE,
AUTHORIZING THE INCREASE OF ITS MEDICAL
PERSONNEL, CHANGING ITS NAME TO REGION
Il TRAUMA AND MEDICAL CENTER, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

 

Be it enacted by the Senaie and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The authorized bed capacity of the Veterans
Regional Hospital in the Municipality of Bayombong, Province
of Nueva Vizcaya is hereby increased from two hundred (200)
to five hundred (500) beds.

bo

SEC. 2. The Veterans Regional Hospital shall now be
known as the Region I] Trauma and Medical Center.

SEc. 3. The existing facilities and professional health
care services of the Region II Trauma and Medical Center are
hereby upgraded to conform and be commensurate to the
increase in its authorized bed capacity as provided for in
Section 1 hereof.

Sec. 4. The existing workforce of the Region Il Trauma
and Medical Center shall also be correspondingly increased,
The Department of Health shall, in coordination with the
Department of Budget and Management and the Civil
Service Commission, and after consultation with the Region II
Trauma and Medical Center, determine the additional
plantilla positions to be created and filled up.

SEc. 5. The increase in bed capacity of the Region II
Trauma and Medical Center from two hundred (200) to
five hundred (500) beds shall be implemented for a period of
not more than three (3) years from the approval of this Act.

SEC. 6. The Secretary of Health shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the implementation of
this Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

 

SEC. 7. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazetie or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
