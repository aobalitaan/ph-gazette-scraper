[ REPUBLIC ‘ACT No. 11819)” -

AN ACT CONVERTING THE SATELLITE OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) LOCATED
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INITAO, PROVINCE
OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL INTO A REGULAR LTO
DISTRICT OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS
THEREFOR ~

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Satellite Office of the Land Transportation
Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Initao, Province of Misamis
Oriental is hereby converted into a regular LTO district office to
be known as the LTO District Office of Initao, Misamis Oriental.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of a regular LTO district office in the Municipality of Initao,
Province of Misamis Oriental, the initial funding of which
shall be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the


Initao LTO Satellite Office located in the Municipality of Initao,
Province of Misamis Oriental. Thereafter, such sums as may be
necessary for the continued operation of the regular LTO district
office shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. Within ninety (90) days from the effectivity of this
Act, the Secretary of Transportation shall promulgate the rules
and regulations necessary for its effective implementation.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
