AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO BRUCE
DONALD MCTAVISH

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Mr. Bruce Donald McTavish is hereby granted
Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and
prerogatives, and is likewise vested with the duties and
obligations appurtenant thereto.

Src. 2. Mr. Bruce Donald McTavish shall take the Oath
of Allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines before an officer
duly authorized to administer the same. The Oath of Allegiance
shall be registered with the Bureau of Immigration.

SEc. 8. Upon registration of the Oath of Allegiance,
the Bureau of Immigration shall issue a Certificate of
Naturalization to Mr. McTavish who shall thereupon enter
into the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.

Src. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in any newspaper of
general circulation.
