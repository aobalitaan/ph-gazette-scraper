AN ACT CREATING A CLASS D DISTRICT OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, CAMARINES SUR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. A regular Class “D” District Office of the
Land Transportation Office (LTO) is hereby created to be located
in the Municipality of Pamplona, Camarines Sur.

SEC. 2. The Assistant Secretary of the LTO, in consultation
with the Secretary of the Department of Transportation
(DOTz), shall formulate the necessary rules and regulations to
implement this Act.

 

SEc. 3. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the LTO District Office in the Municipality of Pamplona,
Camarines Sur, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazetie or in any newspaper of
general circulation.
