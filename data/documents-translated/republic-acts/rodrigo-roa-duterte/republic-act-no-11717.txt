[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11717]

AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF THE
VICENTE SOTTO MEMORIAL MEDICAL CENTER,
LOCATED IN CEBU CITY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, FROM
ONE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED (1,200) TO ONE
THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED (1,500) BEDS, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The bed capacity of the Vicente Sotto Memorial
Medical Center (VSMMC), located in Cebu City, Province of Cebu,
shall be increased from one thousand two hundred (1,200) to one
thousand five hundred (1,500) beds. Any future increase in bed
capacity shall be consistent with the hospital development plan
which shall be prepared by the Department of Health (DOH).

SEC. 2. The existing professional health care services and
facilities of the VSMMC are also hereby upgraded to conform with
and be commensurate to the increase in bed capacity as provided
for in Section 1 hereof.

SEc. 3. The existing workforce of the VSMMC, which
includes doctors, nurses, and support personnel, shall also be
increased correspondingly. The Secretary of the DOH shall, after
consultation with the Chief of the VSMMC, and in coordination
with the Secretary of the Department of Budget and Management
and the Chairperson of the Civil Service Commission, determine
the additional new positions to be created and filled up.

SEc. 4. The Secretary of the DOH shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the implementation of this
Act, the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Sec. 5. If any provision or part of this Act is declared
unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected
shall remain in full force and effect.

SEC. 6. All laws, executive orders, issuances, rules and
regulations inconsistent with this Act are hereby repealed or
modified accordingly.

SEC. 7. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
