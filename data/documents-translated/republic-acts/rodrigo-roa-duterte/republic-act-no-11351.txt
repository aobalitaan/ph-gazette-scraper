AN ACT UPGRADING THE SUB-DISTRICT ENGINEERING
OFFICE LOCATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
CARMONA, PROVINCE OF GAVITE INTO A
REGULAR DISTRICT ENGINEERING OFFICE
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The sub-district engineering office located in
the Municipality of Carmona, Province of Cavite, is hereby
upgraded into a regular district engineering office to be known
as the Cavite Third District Engineering Office.

SEC. 2. The district engineering office shall serve the
municipalities of Carmona, Silang and General Mariano Alvarez
of the Fifth Legislative District of the Province of Cavite and
the cities of Bacoor and Dasmarifias of the Second and Fourth
Legislative Districts of the Province of Cavite, respectively.

 

REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the Cavite Third District Engineering Office in the Province of
Cavite, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

Suc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette ov in a newspaper of
general circulation.
