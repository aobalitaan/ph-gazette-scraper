AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE - MEDELLIN EXTENSION OFFICE
INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Land Transportation
Office — Medellin Extension Office located in the Municipality
of Medellin, Province of Cebu is hereby converted into a regular
Land Transportation Office (LTO) District Office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation.
shall issue rules and regulations necessary for the effective
implementation of this Act. F

 

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s programs the operationalization
of a regular district office in the Municipality of Medellin,
Province of Cebu, the initial funding of which shall be
charged against the current year’s appropriations of the
Land Transportation Office —- Medellin Extension Office.
Thereafter, such sums as may be necessary for the continued
operation of the LTO District Office shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

Sgc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of

general circulation.
