[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 41'742]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE (LTO) EXTENSION OFFICE AT THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SIGMA, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ
INTO A REGULAR LTO DISTRICT OFFICE TO BE
RELOCATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUMALAG,
PROVINCE OF CAPIZ AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The LTO Extension Office located in the
Municipality of Sigma, Province of Capiz is hereby converted
into a regular LTO district office and relocated to its original
location at the Municipality of Dumalag, Province of Capiz.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
shall immediately include in the Department’s program the
operationalization of the LTO-Dumalag District Office in the
Province of Capiz, the initial funding of which shall be charged

against the current year’s appropriation of the LTO-Sigma
Extension Office. Thereafter, suchsums as may be necessary for
the continued operation of the LTO&%Dumalag District Office shall-
be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazetie or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
