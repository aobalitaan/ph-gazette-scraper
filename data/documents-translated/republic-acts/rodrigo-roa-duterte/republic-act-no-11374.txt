[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11374]

AN ACT DECLARING APRIL 5 OF EVERY YEAR A
SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF
PANGASINAN, IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS
FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY, TO BE KNOWN AS
THE “PANGASINAN DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. April 5 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the Province of Pangasinan, in
commemoration of its founding anniversary, to be known as the
“Pangasinan Day”. Upon proclamation of the President, April 5
of every year shall be a special nonworking holiday in the Province
of Pangasinan.

 

SEc. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
