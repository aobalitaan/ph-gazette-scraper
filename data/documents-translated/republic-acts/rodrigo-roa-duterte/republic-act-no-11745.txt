[ REPUBLIC Act No. 11746}

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT OFFICE OF
THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CATAINGAN IN THE
3RD. DISTRICT OF MASBATE, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled: -

SECTION 1. There is hereby established a district office
of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) to be located in the
Municipality of Cataingan which shall serve the 3" District of
Masbate and its neighboring towns.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation.
(DOTr) shall immediately include in the DOTr’s programs the
operationalization of the LTO District Office in the Municipality


of Cataingan, Province of Masbate, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. The Secretary of Trangportation shall formulate
the necessary rules and regulations to carry out the purpose of
this Act.

Sec. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
