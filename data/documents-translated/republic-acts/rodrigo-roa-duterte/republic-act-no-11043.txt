AN ACT NAMING THE DIVERSION ROAD IN SORSOGON
CITY, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON AS SALVADOR
H. ESCUDBERO III DIVERSION ROAD

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The diversion road in Sorsogon City,
Province of Sorsogon shall hereinafter be known as
Salvador H. Escudero III Diversion Road.

Suc. 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways
shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to
implement the provision of this Act.

 

Src. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette.
