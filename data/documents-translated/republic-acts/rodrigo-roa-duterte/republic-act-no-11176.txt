AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY PINAYAG, MUNICIPALITY OF KAYAPA,
PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA TO BE KNOWN
AS PINAYAG NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the
supervision of the Department of Education, a national high
school in Barangay Pinayag, Municipality of Kayapa, Province of
Nueva Vizcaya to be known as Pinayag National High School.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization of
the Pinayag National High School, the funding of which shall
be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall formulate the
rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act
within ninety (90) days after its effectivity.

SEC. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in any newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
