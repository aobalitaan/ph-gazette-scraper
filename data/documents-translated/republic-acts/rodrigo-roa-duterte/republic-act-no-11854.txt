AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE OF
THE LAND TRANSPORTATION FRANCHISING AND
REGULATORY BOARD (LTFRB) IN THE CITY OF
DASMARINAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the
supervision of the Land Transportation Franchising and
Regulatory Board (LTFRB) Main Office in Quezon City, aregular
district office of the LTFRB in the City of Dasmarifias,
Province of Cavite. :


,

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s programs the operationalization of
the LTFRB regular district office in the City of Dasmarifias,
Province of Cavite, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
