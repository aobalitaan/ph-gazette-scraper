AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN
AS BARANGAY POBLACION 2 _ IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF VILLANUEVA, PROVINCE
OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation of Barangay Poblacion 2. —
There is hereby created a barangay to be known as Barangay
Poblacion 2 in the Municipality of Villanueva, Province of
Misamis Oriental.

Sec. 2. Territorial Boundaries. — The territorial
boundaries of Barangay Poblacion 2 shall be identified as
follows:

Bounded on the Southwest (SW), along line 1 to 2 by
Barangay of Katipunan;

On the West (W), along lines 2 to 5 by Barangay
of Poblacion 3;

On the Northwest (NW), along lines 5 to 39 by Barangay
of Poblacion 1;

On the Northeast (NE), along lines 39 to 56 by Barangay
of Poblacion 1;

Along lines 56 to 59 by Barangay of Imelda;
Along lines 59 to 61 by Barangay of Dayawan;

On the East (E), along line 61 to 62 by Barangay
of Dayawan;

On the Southeast (SE), along lines 62 to 73 by Barangay
of Dayawan;

On the Southwest (SW), along lines 73 to 84 by Barangay
of Dayawan;

Along lines 84 to 1 by Barangay of Katipunan.

Containing an area of 832,454 square meters, more or less.

SEC. 3. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. — The
plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission
on Elections (COMELEC) pursuant to Provincial Ordinance
No. 914-2006 of the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the
Province of Misamis Oriental, which ratified the creation
of Barangay Poblacion 2 as proclaimed by the Plebiscite Board
of Canvassers on April 22, 2006, shall serve as substantial
compliance to the plebiscite requirement under Section 10
of Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as
the “Local Government Code of 1991”.

SEC. 4. Barangay Officials. — The incumbent elected
barangay officials of Barangay Poblacion 2, Municipality
of Villanueva, Province of Misamis Oriental shall continue
to hold office until such time that their successors have been
duly elected and qualified.

 

SEc. 5. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. —
All public infrastructure and facilities for public use
existing therein at the time of the approval of this Act
are hereby transferred without cost or compensation to
the new barangay and shall be administered by the same.

Ssc. 6. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. —
Barangay Poblacion 2 shall be entitled to Internal Revenue
Allotment (IRA) shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic
Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the
“Local Government Code of 1991”.

Suc. 7. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in any newspaper of general
circulation.
