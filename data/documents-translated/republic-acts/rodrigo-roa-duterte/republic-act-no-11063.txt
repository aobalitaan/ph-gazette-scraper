AN ACT CONVERTING THE CALOOCAN CITY EXTENSION
OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE
(LTO) LOCATED IN MALABON CITY, METRO MANILA
INTO A REGULAR LTO DISTRICT OFFICE AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Caloocan City Extension Office of
the Land Transportation Office (LTO) located in Malabon City,
Metro Manila is hereby converted into a regular LTO district
office and thereafter referred to as the Malabon City LTO
District Office.

Sac. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
(DOTr) shall immediately include in the Department's program
the operationalization of the Malabon City LTO District Office,
the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current

year’s appropriations of the existing Caloocan. City LTO Extension
Office in Malabon City. Thereafter, such sums as may be
| necessary for the continued operation of the Malabon City LTO
District Office shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

| Suc. 3. The Secretary of the DOTr shall promulgate
the necessary rules and regulations for the effective
implementation of this Act.

Sec. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
