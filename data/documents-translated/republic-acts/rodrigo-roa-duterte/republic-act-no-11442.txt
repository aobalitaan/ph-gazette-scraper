[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11442]

AN ACT DECLARING FEBRUARY 3 OF EVERY YEAR A
SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF
BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO BE KNOWN AS
“BINAN LIBERATION DAY” .

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. February 8 of every year is hereby declared a
special working holiday in the City of Bifian, Province of Laguna,
in commemoration of the liberation of the town of Bifian, now the
City of Bifian, from the Japanese Imperial Forces, to be known as
“Bifian Liberation Day’. Upon proclamation of the President,
February 3 of every year shall be a special nonworking holiday in
the City of Bifian, Province of Laguna.

 

 

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or ina newspaper of general
circulation.
