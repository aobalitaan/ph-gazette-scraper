[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11762]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF PINAGLABANAN
HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY PINAGLABANAN,
MUNICIPALITY OF GOA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES
SUR TO PINAGLABANAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The name of Pinaglabanan High School in
Barangay Pinaglabanan, Municipality of Goa, Province of
Camarines Sur is hereby changed to Pinaglabanan National
High School.

Src. 2. The main campus of the Pinaglabanan National
High School shall continue to remain in its present location in
Barangay Pinaglabanan, Municipality of Goa, Province of
Camarines Sur.


Suc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
