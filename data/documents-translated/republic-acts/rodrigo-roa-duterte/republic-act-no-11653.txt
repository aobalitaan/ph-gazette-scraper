[REPUBLIC Act No. 11653}

AN ACT SEPARATING THE LYDIA D. MARTINEZ

MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL ~ SUGOD SENIOR

HIGH SCHOOL EXTENSION IN BARANGAY SUGOD,

BACON DISTRICT, SORSOGON CITY, PROVINCE

OF SORSOGON FROM THE LYDIA D. MARTINEZ

” MEMORIAL HIGH SCHOOL, CONVERTING IT INTO

AN INDEPENDENT SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL TO BE

KNOWN AS SUGOD SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Lydia D. Martinez Memorial High
School—Sugod Senior High School Extension in Barangay Sugod,
Bacon District, Sorsogon City, Province of Sorsogon is hereby
separated from the Lydia D. Martinez Memorial High School and
converted into an independent senior high school to be known as
Sugod Senior High School.

Sec. 2. Allpersonnel, assets, liabilities and records of the
Lydia D. Martinez Memorial High School — Sugod Senior High
School Extension are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the
Sugod Senior High School.

“SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the Sugod Senior High School, the initial funding of which shall
be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the
Lydia D. Martinez Memorial High School — Sugod Senjor High
School Extension. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the
continued operation of the school shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 4. Within sixty (60) days after the approval of this
Act, the Secretary of Education shall promulgate the rules and
regulations implementing the provisions of this Act.

SEc. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
