[ REPUBLIC Act No. 11123)

AN ACT DECLARING NOVEMBER 15 OF EVERY
YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO
IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDING
ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. November 15 of every year is hereby
declared a special nonworking holiday in the Province of
Occidental Mindoro in commemoration of its founding
anniversary.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
, its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
