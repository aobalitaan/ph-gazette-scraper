[REPUBLIC Act No.11373]

AN ACT DECLARING MARCH 31 OF EVERY YEAR
A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE
WHOLE PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE,
IN COMMEMORATION OF THE FIRST MASS HELD
IN LIMASAWA ISLAND, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN
LEYTE, TO BE KNOWN AS THE “FIRST MASS DAY”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. March 31 of every year is hereby declared
a special working holiday in the whole Province of
Southern Leyte, in commemoration of the first mass held
in Limasawa Island, Province of Southern Leyte, to be known
as the “First Mass Day”. Upon proclamation of the President,
March 31 of every year shall be a special nonworking holiday
in the whole Province of Southern Leyte.

Src. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
