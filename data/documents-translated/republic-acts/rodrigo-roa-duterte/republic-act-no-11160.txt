AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO
FATHER JULIAN C. MAZANA

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Fr. Julian C. Mazana is hereby granted
Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and
prerogatives, as well as the duties and obligations
appurtenant thereto.

Src. 2. Fr. Julian C. Mazana shall take the Oath of
Allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines before an officer
duly authorized to administer the same. The Oath of Allegiance
shall be registered with the Bureau of Immigration.

Src. 3. Upon registration of the Oath of Allegiance,
the Bureau of Immigration shall issue a Certificate of
Naturalization to Fr. Julian C. Mazana who shall thereupon
enter into the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.

 

REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

/ SEc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in any newspaper of
general circulation.
