[RepuBLIC Act No. 11264]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
IN BARANGAY FRANCISCO HOMES-MULAWIN,
CITY OF SAN JOSE DEL MONTE, PROVINCE OF
BULACAN TO BE KNOWN AS MULAWIN NATIONAL
HIGH SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS
THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the
supervision of the Department of Education, a national high
school in Barangay Francisco Homes-Mulawin, City of San
Jose del Monte, Province of Bulacan, to be known as Mulawin
National High School.

 

 

REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES
PRRD 2016 - 010198

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the Mulawin National High School, the funding of which shall
be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Education shall formulate the
rules and regulations implementing the provisions of this Act.

Sec. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
