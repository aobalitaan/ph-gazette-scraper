[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11747]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE MABALACAT CITY EXTENSION
OFFICE OF THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE
(LTO) LOCATED IN MABALACAT CITY, PAMPANGA
INTO A REGULAR LTO DISTRICT OFFICE, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled: 7

SECTION 1. The Mabalacat City Extension Office of the
Land Transportation Office (LTO) located in Dau, Mabalacat City,
Pampanga is hereby converted into a regular LTO District
Office and thereafter referred to as the Mabalacat City LTO
District Office.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
shall immediately include in the Department's program the
operationalization of the Mabalacat City LTO District Office, the
initial funding of which shall be charged against the current year’s
appropriations of the existing LTO extension office. Thereafter,

 

such sums as may be necessary for the continued operation of the
Mabalacat City LTO District Office shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

t

SeEc. 3. The Secretary of thé Department of Transportation
shall promulgate the necessary rules and regulations for the
effective implementation of this Act.

SEc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

 

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives
was passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate
of the Philippines on January 27, 2020 and January 31, 2022,
respectively.

MYRA MARIE D. VILLARICA
Secretary of the Senate
