[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11789 |

AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF THE
LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO) IN THE
SECOND DISTRICT OF THE PROVINCE OF BOHOL
INTO A “CLASS C” LTO DISTRICT OFFICE AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Land Transportation Office (LTO)
Extension Office in the Second District of the Province of Bohol is
hereby converted into a “Class C” LTO district office.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall include in
the Department’s program the operationalization of a “Class C”
LTO district office in the Second District of the Province of Bohol,
the initial funding of which shall be charged against the current

year’s appropriation of the Extension Office located in the
Municipality of Talibon, Province of Bohol. Thereafter, such
sums as may be necessary for the continued operation of the
“Class C” LTO district office shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in at least two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
