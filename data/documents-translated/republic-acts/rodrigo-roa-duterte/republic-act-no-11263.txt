[ RepuBLic Act No. 11263]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL IN
BARANGAY TANDANG SORA, QUEZON CITY, METRO
MANILA TO BE KNOWN AS TANDANG SORA
SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established, under the
supervision of the Department of Education, a senior high
school in Barangay Tandang Sora, Quezon City, Metro Manila
to be known as Tandang Sora Senior High School.

Sec. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the Tandang Sora Senior High School, the funding of which
shall be included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

 

Sec. 3. The Secretary of Education shall issue rules
and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of
this Act.

Src. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
