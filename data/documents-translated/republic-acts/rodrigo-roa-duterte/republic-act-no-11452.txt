[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11452]

AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE (LTO) EXTENSION OFFICE LOCATED IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSALES, PROVINCE OF
PANGASINAN INTO A REGULAR LTO DISTRICT
OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Land Transportation Office
(LTO) Extension Office located in the Municipality of Rosales,
Province of Pangasinan, is hereby converted into a regular LTO
district office to be known as the LTO-Rosales District Office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shallimmediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization
of the LTO-Rosales District Office, the initial funding of which
shall be charged against the current year’s appropriation of the

 

LTO-Rosales Extension Office. Thereafter, such sums as may
be necessary for the continued operation of the LTO-Rosales
District Office shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Sec. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
