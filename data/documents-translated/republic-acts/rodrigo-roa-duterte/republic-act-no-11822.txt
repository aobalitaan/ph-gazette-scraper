AN ACT CONVERTING THE LAND TRANSPORTATION
OFFICE (LTO) EXTENSION OFFICE IN THE CITY
OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, INTO A REGULAR
LTO DISTRICT OFFICE, AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Passi Land Transportation Office
(LTO) Extension Office in the City of Passi, Province of Iloilo, is
hereby converted into a regular LTO district office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department's programs the operationalization of
the regular LTO district office in the City of Passi, Province of
Iloilo, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the
current year’s appropriation of the Passi LTO Extension Office.
