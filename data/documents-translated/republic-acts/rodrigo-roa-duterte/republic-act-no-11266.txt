AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL IN
SITIO CABAGAAYAN, BARANGAY SIBULAN, TORIL
DISTRICT, DAVAO CITY TO BE KNOWN AS THE
CABAGAAYAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

Secrion 1. There is hereby established, under the
supervision of the Department of Education, an elementary
school in Sitio Cabagaayan, Barangay Sibulan, Toril District,
Davao City to be known as the Cabagaayan Elementary School.

Suc. 2. The Secretary of Education shall immediately
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
Cabagaayan Elementary School, the funding of which shall be
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of the Department of Education
shall formulate the rules and regulations implementing the
provisions of this Act.

Sec. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general
circulation.
