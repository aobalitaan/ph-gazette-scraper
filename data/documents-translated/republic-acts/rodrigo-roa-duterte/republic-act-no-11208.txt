AN ACT INCREASING THE BED CAPACITY OF RIZAL
MEDICAL CENTER IN PASIG CITY FROM THREE
HUNDRED (300) TO FIVE HUNDRED (500) BEDS
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The authorized bed capacity of the Rizal
Medical Center (RMC) in Pasig City is hereby upgraded from
three hundred (300) to five hundred (500) beds.

SEc. 2. ‘The existing medical equipment and facilities,
its operating funds and: construction of the needed
infrastructure of the RMC are hereby upgraded to conform
with and commensurate to the increase in bed capacity as
provided in Section 1 hereof.

Src. 3. The increase in bed capacity of RMC from
three hundred (300) to five hundred (500) beds shall be
implemented for a period of not more than three (3) years from
the approval of this Act. The Secretary of Health shall
