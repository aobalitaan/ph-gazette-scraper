[ REPUBLIC ACT NO. 11609]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE SECOND DISTRICT
ENGINEERING OFFICE IN THE MUNICIPALITY
OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO,
AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the
supervision of the Department of Public Works and Highways,
a district engineering office to be located in the Municipality
of Compostela, Province of Davao de Oro, to be known as the
Second District Engineering Office.

SEc. 2. The Second District Engineering Office shall
serve the municipalities of Monkayo, Compostela, New Bataan,
Montevista, and Maragusan, all located in the First Legislative
District of the Province of Davao de Oro.

SEC. 3. The Secretary of Public Works and Highways shall
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the Second District Engineering Office of the Province of Davao
de Oro, the funding of which shall be included in the annual
General Appropriations Act.

SEc. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general
circulation.
