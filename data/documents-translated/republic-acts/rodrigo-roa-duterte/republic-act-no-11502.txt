AN ACT DECLARING THE MONTH OF OCTOBER OF EVERY
YEAR AS THE “NATIONAL COOPERATIVE MONTH”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The month of October of every year is hereby
declared as the “National Cooperative Month.”

SEC. 2. The Cooperative Development Authority, in
consultation with and in partnership with the cooperative
development councils, cooperatives development officers, national
and local federations and union of cooperatives, and other agencies
of the government, shall conduct activities and programs that

 

 

 

promote the principles and values of cooperatives and encourage
cooperative movement.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
