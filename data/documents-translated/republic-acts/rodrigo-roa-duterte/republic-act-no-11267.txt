[RepuBLIC Act No. 112677]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN INTEGRATED NATIONAL
SCHOOL IN BARANGAY CAPUY, CITY OF
SORSOGON, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON TO BE
KNOWN AS CAPUY INTEGRATED NATIONAL
SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Establishment of an Integrated National
School. — There is hereby established, under the supervision of
the Department of Education, an integrated national school in
Barangay Capuy, City of Sorsogon, Province of Sorsogon to be
known as Capuy Integrated National School.

SEC. 2. Appropriations. — The Secretary of Education
shall immediately include in the Department’s program the
operationalization of the Capuy Integrated National School, the
funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

Sc. 3. Implementation. — The Secretary of Education
shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to
carry out the purpose of this Act.

Suc. 4. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
