[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11739]

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE
OF THE LAND: TRANSPORTATION FRANCHISING
AND REGULATORY BOARD (LTFRB) IN THE
CITY OF BACOOR, PROVINCE OF CAVITE AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the
supervision of the Land Transportation Franchising and
Regulatory Board (LTFRB), a regular district office of the
LTFRB in the City of Bacoor, Province of Cavite.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s:program the operationalization
of the LTFRB Regular District Office in the City of Bacoor,
Province of Cavite, the funding of which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation,
