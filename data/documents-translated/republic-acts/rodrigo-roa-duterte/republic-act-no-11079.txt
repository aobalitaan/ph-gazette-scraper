REPUBLIC Act No. 11075]

AN AGT MANDATING THE INTEGRATION OF THE
MAASIN CITY COLLEGE INTO THE SOUTHERN
LEYTE STATE UNIVERSITY (SLSU) AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Maasin City College in Maasin City,
Southern Leyte is hereby integrated into the Southern Leyte
State University (SLSU) and shall be known as the Southern.
Leyte State University —Maasin City Campus.

Sc. 2. All assets, real and personal, personnel and
records of the Maasin City College, as well as liabilities or

obligations, are hereby transferred to the SLSU. The positions,
rights, and security of tenure of faculty members and
personnel employed at the Maasin City College under existing
laws prior to the integration into the SLSU shall be respected.

All parcels of land belonging to the government -

occupied by the Maasin City College are hereby declared to
be the property of the SLSU and shall be titled under its name:
Provided, That should the SLSU cease to exist or be abolished
or would no longer need such parcels of land aforementioned,
the same shall revert to the local government units (LGUs)
concerned.

Sec. 3. The amount necessary to carry out the initial
implementation of this Act shall be charged against the
current year’s funding of the Maasin City College provided
by the City of Maasin, Southern Leyte. The City shall continue
to provide the same funding to the SLSU — Maasin City
Campus until such time that its full funding requirement is
included in the annual General Appropriations Act.

Src. 4. Within sixty (60) days after the effectivity of
this Act, the Commission on Higher Education and the SLSU
shall promulgate the necessary rules and regulations to
implement this Act.

SEc. 5. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.

Approved,
