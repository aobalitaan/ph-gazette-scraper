[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11748]

AN ACT CREATING A REGULAR LTO DISTRICT OFFICE
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IBAJAY, PROVINCE OF
AKLAN, TO BE KNOWN AS THE “WESTERN AKLAN
LTO CENTER”, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS
THERFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There shall be established, under the
supervision of the Department of Transportation, a regular LTO
District Office in the Municipality of Ibajay in the Second District
of Aklan to be known as the “Western Aklan Land Transportation
Office (LTO) Center.”

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall issue the
rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purpose of
this Act.

SEc. 3. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department’s programs the operationalization of
the Western Aklan LTO Center in the Municipality of Ibajay,
Province of Aklan, the funding af which shall be included in the
annual General Appropriations Act.

Src. 4. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in the Official Gazette or in two (2) newspapers of
general circulation.
