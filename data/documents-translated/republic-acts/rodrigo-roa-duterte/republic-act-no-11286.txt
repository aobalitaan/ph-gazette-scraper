[ Repustic Act No. 11286 ]

AN ACTINCREASING THE BED CAPACITY FOR TERTIARY
GENERAL HEALTH CARE SERVICES OF THE
DR. JOSE N. RODRIGUEZ MEMORIAL HOSPITAL
AND SANITARIUM IN TALA, CALOOCAN CITY FROM
TWO HUNDRED (200) TO EIGHT HUNDRED (800)
BEDS, REAPPORTIONING THE AUTHORIZED TWO
THOUSAND (2,000) BED CAPACITY OF THE HOSPITAL,
AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT
NO. 9420, AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Reapportion of Bed Capacity. — The
authorized two hundred (200) beds for tertiary general hospital
services of the Dr. Jose N. Rodriguez Memorial Hospital and
Sanitarium located in Tala, Caloocan City is hereby increased
to eight hundred (800) beds, thereby reapportioning the
authorized two thousand (2,000) bed capacity of the said


hospital amending for the purpose Republic Act No. 9420. The
remaining one thousand two hundred (1,200) beds are for
leprosy services.

SEC. 2. Upgrade of Existing Service Facilities. — The
existing professional health care services and facilities of the
Dr. Jose N. Rodriguez Memorial Hospital and Sanitarium are
hereby upgraded to conform and be commensurate to the
increase in the bed capacity for tertiary general hospital
services, as provided in Section 1 hereof.

SEC. 3. Upgrade of Existing Professional Health Care
Services. — The existing workforce of the Dr. Jose N.
Rodriguez Memorial Hospital and Sanitarium shall also be
correspondingly increased. The Department of Health (DOH),
in coordination with the Department of Budget and
Management (DBM) and the Civil Service Commission (CSC),
and after consultation with the Dr. Jose N. Rodriguez
Memorial Hospital and Sanitarium, shall determine the
additional plantilla positions to be created pursuant to this Act.

SEC. 4. Implementation. — The increase in bed capacity
for tertiary general hospital use of the Dr. Jose N. Rodriguez
Memorial Hospital and Sanitarium from two hundred (200) to
eight hundred (800) beds, shall be implemented consistent with
the development plan of the hospital.

SEC. 5. Appropriation. — The Secretary of Health shall
include in the Department’s program the operationalization of
the increase in beds for tertiary general health care purposes
in the Dr. Jose N. Rodriguez Memorial Hospital and
Sanitarium from two hundred (200) to eight hundred (800)
beds, the initial funding of which shall be charged against the
current year’s appropriations of the hospital. Thereafter, the
funds necessary for its continued operation shall be included
in the General Appropriations Act (GAA).

SEC. 6. Separability Clause. — If any provision or part
of this Act is declared unconstitutional, the remaining parts
or provisions not affected shall remain in full force and effect.

SEC. 7. Repealing Clause. — All other laws, decrees,
orders, issuances, rules and regulations, or parts thereof which
are inconsistent with the provisions of this Act are hereby
repealed, amended or modified accordingly.

 

SEC. 8. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a
newspaper of general circulation.
