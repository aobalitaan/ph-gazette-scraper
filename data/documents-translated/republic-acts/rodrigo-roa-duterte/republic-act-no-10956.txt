AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS
BARANGAY CARE IN THE CITY OF TARLAC,
PROVINCE OF TARLAC

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation of Barangay Care. — There is hereby
created a barangay to be known as Barangay Care in the City of
Tarlac, Province of Tarlac.

Sec. 2. Territorial Boundaries. — The territorial
boundaries of Barangay Care shall be identified as follows:

Bounded on the Northeast (NE) and Southeast (SE),
along lines 1 to 61, by Barangay Tibag (PB-03-000061);

On the Southeast (SE) and Southwest (SW), along
lines 61 to 95, by Barangay Tibagan (PB-03-00006 1;


On the Southwest (SW) and Northwest (NW), along
lines 95 to 106, by Tarlac City versus Municipality of San Jose,
both in Tarlac;

On the Northwest (NW), along lines 106 to 108, by San
Jose Cadastre (PB-03-000060);

On the Northeast (NE), along lines 108 to 114,
by Tarlac City versus Municipality of San Jose, Tarlac;

On the North (N) and West (W), along lines 114 to 116,
by San Jose Cadastre (PB-03-000060);

On the Northeast (NE) and Northwest (NW), along
lines 116 to 143 to the point of beginning by Barangay Sapang
Maragul (PB-03-000061), all of Tarlac City.

SEC. 3. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. — The
plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on
Elections (COMELEC) on March 14, 1993 pursuant to Provincial
Ordinance No. 2, Series of 1991, dated September 5, 1991 of the
Sangguniang Panlalawigan of the Province of Tarlac, which
ratified the creation of Barangay Care, shall serve as substantial
compliance with the plebiscite requirement under Section 10 of
Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as the
"Local Government Code of 1991".

SEC. 4. Incumbent Barangay Officials. — The incumbent
barangay officials of Barangay Care shall continue to hold office
until such time that their successors have been duly elected and
qualified.

SEC. 5. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. — All public
infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein at the
time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred without
cost or compensation to and shall be administered by the new
barangay.

SEc. 6. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. — Barangay
Care shall be entitled to Internal Revenue Allotment (IRA)
shares pursuant to Section 285 of Republic Act No. 7160, as
amended, otherwise known as the "Local Government Code
of 1991".

SEC. 7. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a
newspaper of general circulation.
