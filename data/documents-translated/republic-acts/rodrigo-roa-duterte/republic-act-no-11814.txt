[ REPUBLIC ACT No. 11814]

AN ACT RENAMING THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO,
PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE AS THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SAWATA AND 'ITS BARANGAY
SAWATA AS, BARANGAY POBLACION, AMENDING
FOR THE PURPOSE REPUBLIC ACT NO, 9265,
ENTITLED “AN ACT CREATING THE MUNICIPALITY
OF SAN ISIDRO IN THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO
DEL NORTE” ‘

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION I. The Municipality of San Isidro in the Province
of Davao del Norte is hereby renamed as the Municipality
of Sawata.

SEC. 2. The present Barangay Sawata referred to in
Republic Act No. 9265, entitled “An Act Creating the Municipality
of San Isidro in the Province of Davao del Norte”, is hereby
renamed as Barangay Poblacion.
