[REPUBLIC Act No.11134]

AN ACT DECLARING JUNE 11 OF EVERY YEAR A
SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY
OF SAGAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL,
IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS FOUNDATION DAY
ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. June 11 of every year is hereby declared a
special nonworking holiday in the City of Sagay, Province of
Negros Occidental, in commemoration of its foundation day
anniversary.

 

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
