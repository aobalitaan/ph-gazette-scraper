AN ACT CONVERTING THE SATELLITE OFFICE OF THE
MARITIME INDUSTRY AUTHORITY LOCATED IN
CALBAYOG CITY, PROVINCE OF SAMAR INTO
AN EXTENSION OFFICE AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The existing Maritime Industry Authority
(MARINA) Satellite Office in Calbayog City, Province of Samar is
hereby converted into a regular MARINA Extension Office. The
extension office shall provide services for the residents of the
provinces of Northern Samar, Samar, and Eastern Samar.

Sec. 2. The Administrator of MARINA shall immediately
include in the Agency’s programs the operationalization of the
MARINA Extension Office in Calbayog City, Province of Samar,
the funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall become effective fifteen (15) days
after its publication in the Official Gazette or ina newspaper of
general circulation.
