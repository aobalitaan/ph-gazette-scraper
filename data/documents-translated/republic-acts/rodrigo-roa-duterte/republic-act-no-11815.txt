AN ACT ESTABLISHING A DISTRICT OFFICE OF
THE LAND TRANSPORTATION OFFICE (LTO)
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LABO IN THE
FIRST DISTRICT OF CAMARINES NORTE, AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. There i hereby established a district office
of the Land Transportation Office (LTO) which shall be located.
in the Municipality of Labo, and which shall serve the First
District of Camarines Norte comprised of the municipalities of
Capalonga, Jose Panganiban, Labo, Paracale, and Santa Elena.

SEc. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Transportation
shall immediately include in the Department’s program the
