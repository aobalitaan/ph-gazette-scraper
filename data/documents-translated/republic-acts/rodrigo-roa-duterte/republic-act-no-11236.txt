AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO
MOHAMAD WASSIM NANAA

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Mr. Mohamad Wassim Nanaa is hereby
granted Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and
prerogatives, as well as the duties and obligations appurtenant
thereto.

SEC. 2. Mr. Mohamad Wassim Nanaa shall take the Oath
of Allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines before an officer
duly authorized to administer the same. The Oath of Allegiance
shall be registered with the Bureau of Immigration.

SEC. 3. Upon registration of the Oath of Allegiance, the
Bureau of Immigration shall issue a Certificate of Naturalization
to Mr. Mohamad Wassim Nanaa who shall thereupon enter
into the full enjoyment of Philippine citizenship.

 

REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES
