AN ACT CONVERTING THE EXTENSION OFFICE OF
THE LAND TRANSPORTATION. OFFICE (LTO) IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF JAVIER, PROVINCE OF
LEYTE, INTO A REGULAR DISTRICT OFFICE AND
APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled: 7

SECTION 1. The extension office of the Land Transportation.
Office (LTO) in the Municipality of Javier, Province of Leyte, is
hereby converted into a regular district office to be known as the
LTO Javier District Office.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of Transportation shall immediately
include in the Department's program the operationalization of
the regular LTO district office in the Municipality of Javier,
Province of Leyte, the initial funding of which shall be charged
