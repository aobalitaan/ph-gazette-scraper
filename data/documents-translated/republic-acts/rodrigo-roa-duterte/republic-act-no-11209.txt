[ REPUBLIC AcT No. 11209]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF LUPON SCHOOL
OF FISHERIES IN LUPON, DAVAO ORIENTAL, INTO
THE DAVAO ORIENTAL POLYTECHNIC INSTITUTE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Section 1 of Batas Pambansa Blg. 298 is hereby
amended to read as follows:

“SECTION 1. There shall be established a school
of fisheries in the Municipality of Lupon, Province of
Davao Oriental, to be known as the Davao Oriental
Polytechnic Institute.”

Src. 2. All laws, decrees, executive orders, rules,
regulations or parts thereof inconsistent with the provisions
of this Act are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

   

Suc. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after
its publication in the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of
general circulation.
