AN ACT CREATING A BARANGAY TO BE KNOWN AS
BARANGAY DON ALBINO T. TARUC IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SOCORRO, PROVINCE OF
SURIGAO DEL NORTE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Creation of Barangay Don Albino T. Taruc.
— Puroks I, II, II] and IV are hereby separated from
Barangay Navarro and constituted into a new barangay
to be known as Barangay Don Albino T. Taruc in the
Municipality of Socorro, Province of Surigao del Norte.

SEC. 2. Territorial Boundaries. — The territorial
boundaries of Barangay Don Albino T. Taruc shall be
identified as follows:

Bounded on the Southwest (SW), Northwest (NW)
and Northeast (NE) along lines 31-1 to 16 by Barangay Navarro
Case 1, Socorro, Surigao del Norte;

On the East (E) along lines 16 to 31 by the Pacific Ocean.

The technical description of Barangay Don Albino
T. Taruc is as follows:

Containing an area of 409,944 square meters, more or less.

SEC. 3. Conduct and Supervision of Plebiscite. — The
plebiscite conducted and supervised by the Commission on
Elections (COMELEC) on August 27, 1995 pursuant to Provincial
Ordinance No. 001, Series of 1995 of the Sangguniang
Panlalawigan of the Province of Surigao del Norte, which
ratified the creation of Barangay Don Albino T. Taruc,
shall serve as substantial compliance with the plebiscite
requirement under Section 10 of Republic Act No. 7160,
as amended, otherwise known as the “Local Government
Code of 1991”.

 

Sec. 4. Incumbent Barangay Officials. — The incumbent
barangay officials of Barangay Don Albino T. Taruc shall
continue to hold office until such time that their
successors have been duly elected and qualified.

Suc. 5. Public Infrastructure and Facilities. — All public
infrastructure and facilities for public use existing therein
at the time of the approval of this Act are hereby transferred
without cost or compensation to and shall be administered
by the new barangay.

Sec. 6. Internal Revenue Allotment Shares. — Barangay
Don Albino T. Taruc shall be entitled to Internal Revenue
Allotment (IRA) shares pursuant to Section 285 of
Republic Act No. 7160, as amended, otherwise known as
the “Local Government Code of 1991”.

SEC. 7. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette or in a
newspaper of general circulation.
