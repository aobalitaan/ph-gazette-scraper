AN ACT DECLARING APRIL 3 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUISIANA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA TO CELEBRATE THE “DAY OF COMPLETE FREEDOM AS THE TOWN OF LUISIANA, LAGUNA” AND THE “PANDANAN FESTIVAL”

SECTION 1. April 3 of every year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the Municipality of Luisiana, Province of Laguna to celebrate the “Day of Complete Freedom as the Town of Luisiana, Laguna” and the “Pandanan Festival”.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its complete publication in at least two newspapers of general circulation in the Philippines.
