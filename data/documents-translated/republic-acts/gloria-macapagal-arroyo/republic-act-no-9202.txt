[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9202]

AN ACT DECLARING AUGUST TENTH OF EACH YEAR AS A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MAASIN, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. August ten (August 10) of each year is hereby declared a special nonworking holiday in the City of Maasin, Province of Southern Leyte to commemorate its Charter Day Anniversary.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on February 11, 2003 and January 22, 2003, respectively.

(Sgd.) OSCAR G. YABES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives
