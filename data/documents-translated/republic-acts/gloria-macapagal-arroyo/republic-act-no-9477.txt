Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Thirteenth Congress

Third Special Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9477]

AN ACT RENAMING THE GAPAN-SAN FERNANDO-OLONGAPO ROAD (GSO ROAD) AS JOSE ABAD SANTOS AVENUE (JASA)

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Gapan-San Fernando-Olongapo Road (GSO Road) is hereby renamed as Jose Abad Santos Avenue (JASA).

SEC 2. All laws, decrees, orders and issuances or parts thereof inconsistent herewith are hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

SEC 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 29, 2005 and February 19, 2007, respectively.

(Sgd.) OSCAR G. YABES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: MAY 22 2007

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
