REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9092

AN ACT DECLARING EVERY 3RD DAY OF SEPTEMBER AS DAY OF THE BATTLE OF IMUS, A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, CAVITE

SECTION 1. September 3 of every year is hereby declared as Day of the Battle of Imus, a special working holiday in the Municipality of Imus, Cavite, in commemoration of the historic “Battle of Imus.”

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect immediately after its publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
