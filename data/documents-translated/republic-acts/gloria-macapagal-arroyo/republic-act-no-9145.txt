Republic of the Philippines

Congress of the Philippines

Metro Manila

Eleventh Congress

Sixth Special Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-eighth day of May, two thousand one.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9145]

AN ACT TO RECLASSIFY A PORTION OF THE LAND UNDER CADASTRAL SURVEY 800-D, MODULE 2, PROJECT NO. 1-LC MAP 203 IN BARANGAY SANTA MONICA, PUERTO PRINCESA CITY, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN AS AGRICULTURAL LAND OPEN TO DISPOSITION

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION. 1. That portion of the territory of Barangay Sta. Monica in the City of Puerto Princesa, Province of Palawan, consisting of nine hundred eighty-two hectares and five thousand six hundred sixty centares of land under cadastral survey 800-D, module 2, classified as timber land under Project No. 1-LC Map 203, is hereby reclassified as agricultural land, open to disposition, except that portion covered by Presidential Proclamation No. 16, dated September 11, 1998.

SEC. 2. The Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources shall issue all the necessary orders, rules and circulars to implement the provisions of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved:

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on May 28, 2001 and March 19, 2001, respectively.

Approved: JUL 30 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
