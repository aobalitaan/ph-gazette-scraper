Republic of the Philippines

Congress of the Philippines

Metro Manila

Thirteenth Congress

Third Special Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9488]

AN ACT RENAMING THE SAGRADA-LIDONG ROAD EXTENDING FROM THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR TO THE PROVINCE OF ALBAY AS DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL HIGHWAY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The Sagrada-Lidong Road extending from Sagrada, Buhi in the Province of Camarines Sur to Lidong, Polangui in the Province of Albay is hereby renamed as Diosdado Macapagal Highway.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on June 6, 2005 and February 19, 2007 respectively.

(Sgd.) OSCAR G. YABES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: JUN 20 2007

GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines

Lapsed into law on JUN 24 2007 without the signature of the President, in accordance with Article VI, Section 27 (1) of the Constitution.
