Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Thirteenth Congress

Third Special Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9476]

AN ACT RENAMING THE ENTIRE PORTION OF GEN. TRIAS-AMADEO-TAGAYTAY ROAD FROM GOVERNOR’S DRIVE TO TAGAYTAY CITY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE TO CRISANTO M. DE LOS REYES AVENUE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. In due recognition of the generous contributions and unending support of Crisanto M. De Los Reyes in the patriotic cause of our Filipino soldiers in Cavite during the Spanish occupation, the Gen. Trias — Amadeo — Tagaytay Road from Governor’s Drive to Tagaytay City is hereby renamed as Crisanto M. De Los Reyes Avenue.

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect upon approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on June 6, 2005 and February 19, 2007, respectively.

(Sgd.) OSCAR G. YABES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: MAY 22 2007

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
