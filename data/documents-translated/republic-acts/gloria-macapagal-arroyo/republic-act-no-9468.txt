Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Thirteenth Congress

Third Special Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the nineteenth day of February, two thousand seven.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9468]

AN ACT NAMING BAY BOULEVARD LOCATED IN PASAY CITY, EXTENDING UP TO PARAÑAQUE CITY, AS JOSE W. DIOKNO BOULEVARD

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. The approximately 4.38 kilometer stretch of road located in the cities of Pasay and Parañaque, which is now known as Bay Boulevard and which begins at its intersection with Roxas Boulevard where Gil Puyat Avenue terminates (specifically from Roxas Boulevard heading west past the World Trade Center, veering south past the GSIS Complex to Libertad Channel — 1.45 kilometers) passing through CBP 1 Island “A” (Libertad Channel to Redemptorist Channel — 1.28 kilometers) and CBP 1 Island “B” and “C” (Redemptorist Channel to Asiaworld Property — 1.65 kilometers), is hereby renamed as Jose W. Diokno Boulevard.

SEC 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways is hereby directed to place the appropriate street names and markers along Jose W. Diokno Boulevard.

SEC 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) MANNY VILLAR President of the Senate

(Sgd.) JOSE DE VENECIA JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 20, 2006 and February 19, 2007, respectively.

(Sgd.) OSCAR G. YABES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: MAY 15 2007

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
