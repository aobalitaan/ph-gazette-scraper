Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila

Fourteenth Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-seventh day of July, two thousand nine.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9750]

AN ACT DECLARING SEPTEMBER 10 OF EVERY YEAR A SPECIAL NONWORKING HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF SAN JOSE DEL MONTE, PROVINCE OF BULACAN TO BE KNOWN AS THE “FOUNDATION DAY OF THE CITY OF SAN JOSE DEL MONTE”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. September 10 of every year is hereby declared to be a special nonworking holiday in the City of San Jose del Monte, Province of Bulacan to commemorate its founding day and to be known as the “Foundation Day of the City of San Jose del Monte”.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE President of the Senate

(Sgd.) PROSPERO C. NOGRALES Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on November 17, 2009 and August 24, 2009, respectively.

(Sgd.) EMMA LIRIO REYES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) MARILYN B. BARUA-YAP Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: November 10, 2009

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
