REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9091

AN ACT DECLARING EVERY 28TH DAY OF MAY AS DAY OF THE BATTLE OF ALAPAN A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, CAVITE

SECTION 1. May 28 of every year is hereby declared as Day of the Battle of Alapan a special working public holiday in the Municipality of Imus, Cavite, in commemoration of the historic “Battle of Alapan.”

SEC 2. This Act shall take effect immediately after its publication in two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
