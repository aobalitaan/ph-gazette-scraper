Republic of the Philippines

Congress of the Philippines

Metro Manila

Eleventh Congress

Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-fourth day of July, two thousand

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 9031]

AN ACT DECLARING MAY 27 OF EVERY YEAR AS A SPECIAL WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR AND IN THE CITIES OF NAGA AND IRIGA IN COMMEMORATION OF ITS ANNUAL FOUNDATION ANNIVERSARY

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled

SECTION 1. May 27 of every year is hereby declared as a Special Working Public Holiday in the entire Province of Camarines Sur including the cities of Naga and Iriga in commemoration of its foundation anniversary as a political unit.

SEC. 2. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved.

(Sgd.) AQUILINO PIMENTEL JR. President of the Senate

(Sgd.) ARNULFO P. FUENTEBELLA Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on December 18, 2000 and October 2, 2000, respectively.

(Sgd.) LUTGARDO B. BARBO Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) ROBERTO P. NAZARENO Secretary General House of Representatives

Approved: MAR 05 2001

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO President of the Philippines
