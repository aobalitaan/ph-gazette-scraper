AN ACT DECLARING THE LAST FULL WEEK OF AUGUST
AS ARMED FORCES OF THE FILIPINO PEOPLE WEEK.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Short Title. — This Act shall be known as the
"Armed Forces of the Filipino People Week".

Sec. 2. Armed Forces of the Filipino People Week. — To
give due recognition to the important role and contributions of
past and present Filipino miilitary officers atid personnel, the last
full week of August is hereby declared the Armed Forces of the
Filipino People Week.

SEc. 3. Lead Agency. — The Department of National
Defense (DND) shall urge all citizens to recognize an "Armed
Forces of the Filipino People Week" and shall hold programs and
activities in observance of the event.


SEc. 4, Implementing Rules and Regulations. — The DND
shall promulgate the appropriate rules and regulations necessary
for the effective implementation of this Act within sixty (60) days
from its approval.

Src. 5. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after ita publication in the Official Gazette or in at least
two (2) newspapers of general circulation.
