Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled: *

SEcTION1. A four (4)-lane circumferential road that
spans 14.3217 kilometers and commences at the junction of the
Doilo-Dumangas Coastal Road in Balabago, Jaro District, Hoilo

City, then follows a general westward direction along the floodway’s
south bank towards Buhang, Jaro; Tacas, Jaro and Ungka II,
Pavia, then traverses southwards to the Mandurriao District,
Iloilo City-Pavia-San Miguel tri-border, and ends at the

shall be named as the “President Corazon C. Aquino Avenue”.

SEc. 2. The Department of Public Works and Highways
shall issue the necessary rules, orders and circulars to implement
the provisions of this Act.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its
publication in a newspaper of general circulation.
