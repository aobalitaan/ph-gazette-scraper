[ REPUBLIC ACT NO.10148]

AN ACT GRANTING PHILIPPINE CITIZENSHIP TO MARCUS
EUGENE DOUTHIT

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Marcus Eugene Douthit is hereby granted
Philippine citizenship with all the rights, privileges and
prerogatives appurtenant thereto.

Sec. 2. Upon taking and registration of the oath of
allegiance to the Republic of the Philippines, Marcus Eugene
Douthit shall enter upon the full enjoyment of Philippine
citizenship.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
