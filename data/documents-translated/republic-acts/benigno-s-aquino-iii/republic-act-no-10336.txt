AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN INTEGRATED SCHOOL
IN BARANGAY ADDITION HILLS, CITY OF
MANDALUYONG TO BE KNOWN AS ADDITION
HILLS INTEGRATED SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING
FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled

SECTION 1. Establishment of an Integrated School. —
There is hereby established, under the supervision of the
Department of Education, an integrated school in Barangay
Addition Hills, City of Mandaluyong, National Capital Region to
be known as Addition Hills Integrated School.

SKC. 2. Appropriations. — The Secretary of Education
shall immediately include in the Department's program the
operationalization of the Addition Hills Integrated School, the
funding of which shall be included in the annual General
Appropriations Act.

SEC. 3. Implementation. — The Secretary of Education
shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to
carry out the purpose of this Act.


SEC. 4. Effectivity. — This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
