Republic of the Philippines Congress of the Philippines Metro Manila Fifteenth Congress Third Regular Session

Begun and held in Metro Manila, on Monday, the twenty-third day of July, two thousand twelve.

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 10267]

AN ACT CHANGING THE NAME OF GUSA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL IN BARANGAY GUSA, CITY OF CAGAYAN DE ORO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL TO GUSA REGIONAL SCIENCE HIGH SCHOOL – X

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Renaming of a National High School. – The name of Gusa National High School in Barangay Gusa, City of Cagayan de Oro, Province of Misamis Oriental is hereby changed to Gusa Regional Science High School – X.

SEC. 2. Location of the Main Campus. – The main campus of Gusa Regional Science High School – X shall continue to remain in its present location in Barangay Gusa, City of Cagayan de Oro, Province of Misamis Oriental.

SEC. 3. Effectivity. – This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.

Approved,

(Sgd.) JUAN PONCE ENRILE President of the Senate

(Sgd.) FELICIANO BELMONTE JR. Speaker of the House of Representatives

This Act which originated in the House of Representatives was finally passed by the House of Representatives and the Senate on October 4, 2010 and July 24, 2012, respectively.

(Sgd.) EMMA LIRIO-REYES Secretary of the Senate

(Sgd.) MARILYN B. BARUA-YAP Secretary General House of Representatives
