[REPUBLIC ACT No. 10329]

AN ACT SEPARATING THE MINALABAC NATIONAL HIGH
' SCHOOL — ANTIPOLO ANNEX IN BARANGAY
ANTIPOLO, MUNICIPALITY OF MINALABAC,
PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR FROM THE
MINALABAC NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CONVERTING
ITINTO AN INDEPENDENT NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL
TO BE KNOWN AS ANTIPOLO NATIONAL HIGH
SCHOOL AND APPROPRIATING FUNDS THEREFOR

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the
Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Separation and Conversion into a National
High School. — The Minalabac National High School — Antipolo
Annex in Barangay Antipolo, Municipality of Minalabac, Province
of Camarines Sur is hereby separated from the Minalabac
National High School and converted into an independent national
high school to be known as Antipole National High School.

SEC. 2. Transfer of Assets and Liabilities. - All personnel,
assets, liabilities and records of the Minalabac National High School
— Antipolo Annex are hereby transferred to and absorbed by the
Antipolo National High School.

SEC. 3. Appropriations. — The Secretary of Education
shall immediately include in the Department's program the
operationalization of the Antipolo National High School, the initial
funding of which shall be charged against the current year's
appropriations of the Minalabac National High School — Antipolo
Annex. Thereafter, the amount necessary for the continued
operation of the school shall be included in the annua! General
Appropriations Act.

SgEc. 4. Implementation. — The Secretary of Education
shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to
carry out the purpose of this Act.

Sec. 5. Effectivity. ~ This Act shall take effect fifteen
(15) days after its publication in the Official Gazette.
