H. No. 6821

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1999]

AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED ONE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED AND ELEVEN, ENTITLED “AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS.”

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Item B–362.12, appendix A, title B, Highways (Feeder and Secondary Roads), page one hundred sixty-eight–A, for the Province of Negros Oriental, of Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred and eleven is hereby amended to read as follows:

“B–362.12: Doldol Junction–Bontod Feeder Road—5 Kms. New Road

SEC. 2. Item B–362.12, appendix A, title B, Highways (Feeder and Secondary Roads), page one hundred seventy–A, for the Province of Negros Oriental, of Republic Act Numbered One thousand four hundred and eleven is hereby amended to read as follows:

“B–362.17: Lipayo–Mag-aso–Kanghangin Feeder Road, Dauin—Construction—7 kms. New Road

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 22, 1957.
