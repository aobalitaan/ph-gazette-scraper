H. No. 3968

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 2869]

AN ACT AMENDING CERTAIN ITEMS OF REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED TWENTY-THREE HUNDRED AND ONE, ENTITLED “AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS FOR PUBLIC WORKS AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES”.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. On page 154 of Republic Act Numbered Twenty-three hundred and one, item (23) under Group II (Projects for the Second District, Negros Oriental), embraced by subsection c (Re implementation of Republic Act Numbered hundred thirty-six, as amended), is hereby amended to read as follows:

P5,000.00”

SEC. 2. On page 441 of the same Act, item ( k ) under Group II (Projects for the Second District, Negros Oriental), under subsection d of Section two thereof, is hereby amended to read as follows:

P5,000.00”

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Enacted without Executive approval, June 19, 1960.
