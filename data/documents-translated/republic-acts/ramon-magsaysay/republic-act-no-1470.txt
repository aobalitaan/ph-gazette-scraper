H. No. 4855

[REPUBLIC ACT NO. 1470]

AN ACT TO AMEND AND REPEAL CERTAIN ITEMS IN REPUBLIC ACT NUMBERED FOURTEEN HUNDRED ELEVEN, REGARDING PUBLIC WORKS PROJECTS FOR THE PROVINCE OF BOHOL.

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the Philippines in Congress assembled:

SECTION 1. Items seventy-six to eighty-seven, paragraph II, subsection h , for the Province of Bohol, section three of Republic Act Numbered Fourteen hundred eleven, are amended to read as follows:

SEC. 2. Items eighty-eight to one hundred four, paragraph II, subsection h , for the Province of Bohol, section three of the same Act, are repealed.

SEC. 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval.

Approved, June 14, 1956.
