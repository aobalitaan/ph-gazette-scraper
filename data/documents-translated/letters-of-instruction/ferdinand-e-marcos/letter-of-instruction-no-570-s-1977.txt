MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION NO. 570

Paragraph 4 of the LOI No. 417 is hereby amended to read as follows:

4. Grants of frequencies and capacities on any route by any airline in the sole prerogative of the CAB, it being more current in relation to changing economic circumstances. The CAB may also grant temporary operating permits to international airlines on international routes in the national interest. All grants of frequencies, capacities and/or new routes shall always be subject to the approval of the Office of the President.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 30th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
