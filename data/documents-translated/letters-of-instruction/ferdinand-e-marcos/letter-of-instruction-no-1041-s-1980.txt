MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1041

AMENDING LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1006

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Letter of Instructions No. 1006 dated April 14, 1980, by directing and ordering that the Integrated Sports Complex therein provided shall be constructed on the Reclamation Area along Roxas Boulevard, Manila.

This Letter of Instructions shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
