MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTERS OF INSTRUCTION NO. 470

TO: National Economic and Development Authority

In order to accelerate all economic agreements within ASEAN, the NEDA is directed to organize a cabinet-level committee on Economic matters pertaining to ASEAN, to be composed of Economic Ministers, to include the following:

Secretary of Industry Secretary of Trade Secretary of Finance Secretary of Agriculture Secretary of Public Works, Transportation and Communication Governor, Central Bank of the Philippines Secretary of Economic Planning (NEDA Director-General)

This Committee may be augmented in membership as the requirements of the ASEAN Economic Ministers expand. The Secretary of Foreign Affairs will, for coordinative purpose, be also a member of this Committee.

The Committee will be under the coordination of the NEDA.

The Committee should meet immediately to make recommendations for the structuring of the ASEAN secretariat with respect to all economic committees in this secretariat, so that I can give instructions in this matter.

The Central Bank will provide support as required for the operations of the secretariat.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of October in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
