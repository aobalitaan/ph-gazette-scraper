MALACAÑANG Manila

July 20, 1976

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 435

Pursuant to General Order No. 1 and in order to achieve the objectives of the Philippine Population and Family Planning Program, you are hereby directed to facilitate the implementation of the programs of the Commission on Population (POPCOM), Office of the President, by authorizing Provincial Governors and City Mayors to gradually and progressively assume the responsibility of funding the costs of all population and family planning related activities and projects agreed upon by the POPCOM and the Provincial Governors and City Mayors for their respective jurisdictions.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
