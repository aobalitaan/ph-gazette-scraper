Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 798

TO: The Chairman Board of Censors for Motion Pictures

WHEREAS, members of the Philippine Motion Picture Industry, in collaboration with the defunct Bureau of Standards for Mass Media, formulated in 1974 a Philippine Motion Picture Industry Code of Ethics and a Media Code of the Philippine Motion Picture Industry;

WHEREAS, there is urgent need for the motion picture industry to adopt these Codes;

The Board of Censors for Motion Pictures is therefore hereby instructed to adopt the aforementioned Codes and to enforce their implementation by the members of the industry.

DONE IN THE City of Manila, this 24th day of January, in the year of our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Seventy-Nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
