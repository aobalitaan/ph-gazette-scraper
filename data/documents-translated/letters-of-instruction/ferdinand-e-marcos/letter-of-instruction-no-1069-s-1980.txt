MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION NO. 1069

In order to enable the cottage industry sector to maintain its share in the world market for cottage and handicraft products and sustain its continued viability as a vehicle for expansion for employment, you are hereby directed to exempt cottage industries from the provisions of P.D. 1713, an act providing for an increase in the minimum daily wage rates and for additional mandatory living allowances.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of October 1980, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
