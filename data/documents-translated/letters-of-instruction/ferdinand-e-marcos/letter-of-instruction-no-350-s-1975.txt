LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 350

TO : 1. The Secretary of National Defense 2. The Secretary of Local Government and Community Development 3. The Secretary of Finance 4. All Others Concerned In order to carry out the mandate of the New Constitution particularly Section 12, Article XV thereof, which provides that the state shall establish and maintain an Integrated National Police whose organization, administration and operation shall be provided by law, you are hereby directed to implement fully, effective January 1, 1976, the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 765 dated August 8, 1975 providing for the constitution of the Integrated National Police and Presidential Decree No. 632 dated January 6, 1975 providing for the budgetary administration of the Integrated National Police.

This Letter of Instruction shall be transmitted to all Provincial Governors, City Mayors, Municipal Mayors, Provincial/City/Municipal Board and Councils, Provincial City/Municipal Treasurers and other officials concerned.

For immediate and strict compliance.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
