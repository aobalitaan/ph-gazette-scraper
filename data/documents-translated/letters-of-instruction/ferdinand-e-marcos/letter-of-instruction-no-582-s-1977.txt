MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION NO. 582

The Governor Province of Cebu

In view of the representations made by the Provincial Government of Cebu for the transfer of the Cebu Club Filipino golf course to the Philippine Tourism Authority for development as part of the nation’s golf course circuit plans and the need for such facilities for tourism purposes, it is hereby directed that:

a) A negotiation be conducted between PTA and the Provincial Government of Cebu for the turn-over of the said property to the Authority;

b) The PTA shall have control and administration of the area for tourism purposes.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
