MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO 1118

Gentlemen:

In order to facilitate the immediate establishment and operations of a branch office in the Philippines of Save the Children, an American-based, non-stock, non-profit humanitarian organization, you are hereby directed to undertake the following:

1) For: Securities and Exchange Commission

Expedite the issuance of the appropriate certificate of registration that would allow Save the Children to operate in the Philippines.

2) For: Board of Investments

Expedite the issuance of appropriate certificate of exemption from BOI registration to Save the Children, as a foreign-based non-stock, non-profit humanitarian organization.

3) For: Commission of Immigration and Deportation

Furnish appropriate entry and travel documents to all alien officials and staff members of Save the Children who shall be assigned or come to the Philippines in connection with the operations of said organization.

4) For: Metropolitan Manila Commission

Expedite issuance of appropriate local permits and licenses necessary for the establishment of a branch office for Save the Children.

For immediate compliance.

( Sgd .) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

April 11, 1981
