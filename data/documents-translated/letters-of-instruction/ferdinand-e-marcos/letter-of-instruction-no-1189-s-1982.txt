MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1189

In view of the fact that the Philippine Fire Protection Association, Inc. (PFPA) has ceased to operate since August 31, 1981 and in consonance with the objectives of integrated effort on fire safety, Presidential Letter of Instruction No. 727, dated August 4, 1981 is hereby amended by deleting therefrom the Philippine Fire Protection Association, Inc. and replacing said member with the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers, Incorporated.

The President of the Integrated Electrical Engineers, Incorporated shall assume the duties and responsibilities of the President of the Philippine Fire Protection Association, Inc., relative to the National Fire Safety Council.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20 th day of January in the Year of Our Lord Nineteen hundred and eighty two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
