MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 218

In view of the need to ensure that the adjustments of contract prices of public works projects, pursuant to Presidential Decree No. 454, as amended by Presidential Decree No. 459, are reasonable and consistent, I hereby order you to submit to the National Economic and Development Authority (NEDA) the decisions of the Committee for review and approval. The decisions of the NEDA on the actions of the Committee shall be final upon approval by the President.

The NEDA shall no longer be represented in the Committee on Price Adjustment.

Done in the City of Manila this 14th day of October in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
