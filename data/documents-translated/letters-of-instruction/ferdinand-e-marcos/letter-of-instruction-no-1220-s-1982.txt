MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1220

WHEREAS, the Office of the President has been swamped with requests for authority to purchase motor vehicles inspite of my Letters of Instructions banning or limiting said purchases.

WHEREFORE, I hereby reiterate that purchase of motor vehicles shall remain banned, unless the purchase is absolutely necessary for the operation of the government office or agency concerned.

HENCEFORTH, all requests for exceptions to this Order shall be submitted to, and shall be decided by, the Executive Committee.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
