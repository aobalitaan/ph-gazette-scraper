MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS No. 343

TO : The Governor Central Bank of the Philippines

The Commissioner Bureau of Customs

The Director Civil Aeronautics Administration

The Chairman of the Board Philippine Aerospace Development Corporation

Henceforth, no importation of the Britten-Norman Islander aircraft, either as a complete unit or in knocked-down form, shall be allowed unless undertaken by the Philippine Aerospace Development Corporation in line with its progressive manufacturing program for this aircraft. Furthermore, no importation of any other aircraft similar in type or category of the Britten-Norman Islander aircraft shall be allowed unless certified by the Philippine Aerospace Development Corporation as necessary and not in conflict with the aforementioned program.

To implement these instructions, the Governor of the Central Bank, in coordination with the above-named agencies, is hereby directed to draw up the necessary rules and regulations, to include a definition by the Civil Aeronautics Administration of the types or categories of aircraft to be covered by these instructions.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
