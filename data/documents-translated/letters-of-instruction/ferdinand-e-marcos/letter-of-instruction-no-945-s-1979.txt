MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 945

HOSTING BY THE PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT OF THE 39TH PLENARY MEETING OF THE INTERNATIONAL COTTON ADVISORY COMMITTEE

WHEREAS, the development of a viable cotton-growing industry in the country is a specific thrust of the National Government;

WHEREAS, membership of the Philippines in the International Cotton Advisory Committee, and participation in the activities, will be beneficial to the national cotton development program;

WHEREAS, the convention facilities of the Philippines are a source of pride for the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President and Prime Minister of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the hosting by the Philippine Government of the 39th Plenary Meeting of the International Cotton Advisory Committee to be held in Manila in 1980, and in connection therewith:

1. The Philippine Cotton Corporation is hereby designated as the over-all coordinator for this undertaking, and shall have primary responsibility for its success. As such, it shall have the following functions and powers:

a. Plan and implement all requisite activities; b. Coordinate and supervise the activities of all personnel and offices concerned; c. Administer the funds to be released for this undertaking, and revert all unexpended balances, if any, to the Ministry of the Budget.

2. The Ministry of the Budget is directed to release to the Philippine Cotton Corporation the amount of P200,000 from the International Commitments Fund to cover the expenses for this undertaking.

3. The Ministry of Agriculture, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Tourism, and the Cotton Research and Development Institute are instructed to render whatever assistance may be required within their capabilities towards ensuring the success of this undertaking.

4. The President of the Philippine Cotton Corporation is hereby authorized to act and sign for and in behalf of the Philippine Government on all matters pertaining to the International Cotton Advisory Committee.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
