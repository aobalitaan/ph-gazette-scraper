MALACAÑANG MANILA

August 6, 1974

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 206

TO : The Director of Lands

You are hereby directed to survey and segregate an area of seven (7) hectares of the Pasonanca Park in Zamboanga City, which shall be transferred to the Philippine Tourism Authority for its tourism development projects.

The area to be segregated is marked in the attached map.

In implementing this order, you shall coordinate with the Secretary of Tourism, who has been earlier instructed to seek the concurrence of the Municipal Government of Zamboanga City to the segregation and transfer of the seven-hectare lot.

Your immediate action on this matter is enjoined.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
