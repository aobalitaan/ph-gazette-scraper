MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 35

TO : The Secretary of National Defense

In order to further protect the interests of the Government in the Iligan Integrated Steel Mills, Inc. and in furtherance of Letter of Instructions No. 27, dated October 14, 1972, you are hereby ordered to take over and control the assets of the following corporations:

1. Jacinto Steel Incorporated 2. Jacinto Iron and Steel Sheets Corporation 3. J & P Shipping Corporation 4. Beatriz Marketing and Trading Corporation (all of which are located at Pereyra Building, Magsaysay Boulevard, Santa Mesa, Manila) 5. Perro Products, Incorporated Vicente Madrigal Building Ayala Avenue, Makati, Rizal

These firms are owned or controlled by Fernando Jacinto.

Your immediate action is desired.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of October, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
