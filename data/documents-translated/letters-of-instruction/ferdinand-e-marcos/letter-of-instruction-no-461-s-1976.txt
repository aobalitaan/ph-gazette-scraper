MALACAÑANG Manila

September 10, 1976

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION NO. 461

In view of the resignation and/or retirement from the government service of some members of the Inter-Agency Executive Committee and its Technical Staff for the implementation of the Philippine-Australian Development Assistance Program (PADAP), created under Memorandum Order No. 455 of this office, dated September 23, 1974, and in order that other offices who are similarly involved in the implementation of the said program may be properly represented, the membership of the said Committee and Technical Staff is hereby reconstituted as follows:

INTER-AGENCY EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE:

INTER -AGENCY TECHNICAL STAFF:

The functions and authority of the Inter-Agency Executive Committee provided under the said Memorandum Order No. 455 shall continue to be exercised by the herein reconstituted Committee and Technical Staff for the implementation of the Philippine-Australian Development Assistance Program.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
