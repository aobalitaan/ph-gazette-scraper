MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 265-A

AMENDING LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 265 CREATING THE PERFORMANCE AUDIT TEAM ON LOCAL ELECTIVE OFFICIALS

TO:

2. All Others Concerned

WHEREAS, an evaluation by the Performance Audit Team created under Letter of Instructions No. 265, series of 1975, of the job requirements, time limitation and other constraints involved in actual performance auditing shows a need to extend the duration of the project by at least three months;

WHEREAS, provisions for funding which is a vital element of the project have not been specified in the original Letter of Instructions;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby order and direct that:

1. The deadline for submission of the final performance audit reports to the President shall not be later than August 31, 1975.

2. The amount of One Million Pesos (₱1,000,000.00) from funds of the Government not otherwise appropriated, is hereby transferred to the Chairman of the Performance Audit Team to be programmed by him for the purpose of implementing or carrying into effect the provisions of the aforesaid Letter of Instructions as he may deem fit.

These instructions shall be implemented immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred-and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
