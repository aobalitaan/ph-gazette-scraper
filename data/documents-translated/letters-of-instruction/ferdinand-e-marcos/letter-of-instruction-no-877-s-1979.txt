MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 877

In accordance with specific recommendations of the Investment Coordination Committee made in its meeting of April 3, 1979, as embodied in NEDA Resolution No. 15, series of 1979, the project listings in the following Priorities Plan are hereby approved:

1. The Twelfth Investment Priorities Plan; 2. The Tenth Export Priorities Plan; 3. The Fifth Public Utilities Priorities Plan; 4. The Second Agriculture Investment Priorities Plan; and 5. The Second Tourism Investment Priorities Plan.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of JUNE, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy nine.

( Sgd .) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
