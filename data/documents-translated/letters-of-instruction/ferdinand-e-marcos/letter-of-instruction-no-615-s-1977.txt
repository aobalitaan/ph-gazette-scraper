MALACASANG
MANILA

LETTERS OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 615
To : The Secretary of Public Highways

The Secretary of Tourism

With the confirmation of the existence of
burial caves in the mountains of Bagulin, La Union,
which I have deckred as national cultural treasure,
the roads leading to the site are hereby classified
as a national road which should be upgraded and
made passable to make the site accessible to tourists
and the general public.

Done in the City of Manila this 17th day
of etobe: in the year of Our Lord, nineteen
hundred and seventy-seven.
