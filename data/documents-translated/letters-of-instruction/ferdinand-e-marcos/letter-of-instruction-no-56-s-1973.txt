Office of the President of the Philippines

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION NO. 56

TO: The Chairman and Administrator National Grains Authority Quezon City

Due to the current world shortage of protein ingredients, resulting in increasing import prices, delayed shipments, thereby threatening the continuous availability of animal feeds, you are hereby instructed to:

1. Import necessary quantities of ingredients, concentrates, and where available ready made animal feeds to arrive in the Philippines at the earliest possible time but not later than May, 1973.

2. Whatever commodity is imported, to sell the same to the members of the Philippine Association of Feed Millers, Inc. and/or bona fide members of swine and poultry raiser’s cooperatives and/or associations.

Immediate and strict compliance with this Letter of Instruction is desired.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
