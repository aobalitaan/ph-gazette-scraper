MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1010

In accordance with specific recommendations of the Investment Coordination Committee made in its meeting of April 14, 1980, as embodied in NEDA Resolution No. 8, series of 1980, the project listings in the following Priorities Plans are hereby approved:

1. The Thirteenth Investment Priorities Plan; 2. The Eleventh Export-Priorities Plan; 3. The Sixth Public Utilities Priorities Plan; and 4. The Third Agricultural Investment Priorities Plan.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
