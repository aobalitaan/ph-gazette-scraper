Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1129

SALE BY PHILIPPINE AEROSPACE DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION OF ITS L-100-20 AIRCRAFT

WHEREAS, the conditions of the moment are uncertain, brought about by unstable international situations, as illustrated by recent acts of agitation and terrorism, thereby requiring certain precautionary measures in the interest of national security;

NOW, THEREFORE, for reasons of national security, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby prohibit the sale and/or delivery of PADC’s remaining three (3) L-100-20 aircraft to its present buyer to which PADC has granted the option to purchase.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 19th day of May in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

( Sgd .) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
