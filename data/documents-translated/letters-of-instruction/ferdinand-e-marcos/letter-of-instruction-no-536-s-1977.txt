MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 536

TO: THE LAND BANK BOARD

On the occasion of Labor Day, 1 May 1977, it is my pleasure to direct you to donate to the Trade Unions Congress of the Philippines (TUCP) the LBP lot along Sta. Potenciana Street in Intramuros consisting of 1,000 square meters, where the TUCP will establish its national headquarters. The TUCP has been giving its fullest cooperation to the Government in the pursuit of the goals of the New Society, especially the promotion of unity with peace and justice. The TUCP deserves this gesture of appreciation.

May 1, 1977.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
