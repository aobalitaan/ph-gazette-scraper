TYPE/NO.: Letter of Instructions No. 1095

DATE: December 16, 1980

TITLE: DIRECTING THE MINISTRY OF TRADE TO IMPLEMENT COFFEE AGREEMENT AND DESIGNATING THE MINISTRY OF TRADE AND THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL BANK AS THE CERTIFYING AGENCY AND AGENT, RESPECTIVELY OF THE INTERNATIONAL COFFEE ORGANIZATION.

ABSTRACT:

Directs the Ministry of Trade to implement the International Coffee Agreement and formulate its guidelines. Appoints the Ministry of Trade and the Philippine National Bank as the Certifying Agency and Agent respectively and authorities them to exercise powers and duties necessary to carry out the provisions of the Letter of Instructions and the Coffee Agreement. Authorities the Ministry of Trade to collect reasonable fees in connection with the implementation of the Letter of Instruction which shall accrue to the General Fund, provided that 50 per centum of such income be immediately utilized for payment of international obligations in accordance with the Agreement, for maintenance and operating expenditures that the Ministry may incur in implementing same Agreement and such other expenses to be incurred in the promotion of coffee exports. Further directives the Central Bank of the Philippines, the Bureau of Plant Industry, the Bureau of Customs and all other government agencies, bureaus and offices concerned with the processing and approval of export and import documents to make the necessary arrangement with the Certifying Agency and Agent to enable them to expeditiously carry out their functions. Effective November 1, 1980.

December 17, 1980
