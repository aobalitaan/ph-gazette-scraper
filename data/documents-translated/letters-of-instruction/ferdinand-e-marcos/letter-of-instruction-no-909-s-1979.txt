MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 909

AMENDING LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 779

By virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Letter of Instructions No. 779 dated December 6, 1978, entitled “Allowances and payments Made to National Officials by Local Government Units”, by exempting members of the Integrated National Police effective immediately from the provisions of said Letter of Instructions prohibiting officials and employees of the national government from receiving additional compensation from any province, city, municipality or barangays , Provided, That these amounts are over and above the 18% contribution of the city and municipal governments as required by Presidential Decree No. 632.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
