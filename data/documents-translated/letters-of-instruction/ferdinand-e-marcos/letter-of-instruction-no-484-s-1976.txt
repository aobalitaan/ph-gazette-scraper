MALACANANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 484

Henceforth, the President of Export Processing Zone Chamber of Exporters and Manufacturers (EPZCEM) will be an ex-officio member of the Export Processing Zone Authority Board of Commissioners to fill up the representative of the private sector as provided for by Presidential Decree No. 66.

This order shall take effect immediately and shall continue unless revoked by me.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
