{ MALACANANG C
MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS No. 1249
TO: All Members of the Cabinet

Henceforth, any availment from the National
Security Fund or any Confidential or Intelligence
Funds shall be solely upon my authority or direction.
Before their release, the purpose of their use shall
be explained to me confidentially -

Done in the City of Manila, this Zlst day of
June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and

eighty-two.
