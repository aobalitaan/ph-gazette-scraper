MALACAÑANG MANILA

October 7, 1972

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 24

TO : The Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources

In order to facilitate the implementation of the program of reforms of the Government and to speed up the present drive to weed out undesirable public officials and employees, you are hereby directed to take the following actions with respect to your letter to the President, dated October 4, 1972, recommending disciplinary action against 220 officials of the Bureau of Lands and the Bureau of Forestry.

You shall:

1. Dismiss the 85 officials and employees of the Bureau of Lands and the 51 officials and employees of the Bureau of Forestry, included in the list headed by Isagani Sabiniano, in accordance with your recommendations.

2. Consider as resigned the 27 officials of the Bureau of Lands, included in the list headed by Monti Macatbar, also in accordance with your recommendation.

3. Retire the 57 employees of the Bureau of Lands included in the list headed by Armando Azul, also in accordance with your recommendation.

Immediate implementation of this Order is desired.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
