LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 326

TO: The Secretary of Natural Resources The Director of Lands

To expedite the disposition and sale of friar lands within the Pangarap Village, Makatipo, Caloocan City, pursuant to Presidential Decree No. 293, and Commonwealth Act No. 32, and in order to insure the development of said village as viable human settlement, you are hereby authorized and directed to proceed with the sale of all disposable lots in that project without public bidding to bona fide members of the Malacañang Homeowners Association, Incorporated.

All Letters of Instructions, Executive Orders, Circulars, Rules and Regulations or parts thereof that are inconsistent herewith are hereby revoked or modified accordingly.

This Letter of Instructions shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
