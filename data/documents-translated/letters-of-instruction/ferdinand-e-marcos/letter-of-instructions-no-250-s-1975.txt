MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTION No. 250

To: The Governor Central Bank of the Philippines

Further to Letter of Instructions No. 199 dated June 29, 1974, addressed to the Secretary of National Defense and General Fabian Ver, NISA, you are authorized and directed to take over the control and management of the assets to Vicente T. Tan and of the following companies which are affiliated with, related to, associated with, owned and/or controlled by Vicente T. Tan:

Trans-World Investment Corporation Victan & Co., Inc. First International Investment Co., Inc. Far East Petroleum & Minerals Corporations Continental Land & Building Corporation Philcontrust International Corporation Continental Finance Corporation Overseas Insurance Corporation Pentagon Mines, Inc. Community Rural Bank of Panay City. Inc. Ortiga Development Corporation Cardinal Development Corporation Galleon Trader, Inc. Supreme Investment Corporation Community Foods, Inc.

at such times and under such circumstances as you may diem appropriate and necessary to safeguard the assets of Vicente T. Tan and of such companies.

You shall coordinate in this matter with the Secretary of National Defense and the Director-General of the National Intelligence and Security Authority.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of February in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
