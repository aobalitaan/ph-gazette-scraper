Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1092

WHEREAS, the President of the Philippines has issued Proclamation No. 2040 dated December 5, 1980, convening the Interim Batasang Pambansa (IBP) as a constituent body to consider proposals to amend the Constitution; and

WHEREAS, there is a need for sufficient funds to finance the Interim Batasang Pambansa in its assigned task;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby order:

1. The Minister of the Budget to set aside funds appropriated for the Interim Batasang Pambansa to be used by it as a constituent assembly to draft proposals for the amendment of the Constitution; and

2. The Chairman, Commission on Audit to pass upon in audit, subject to accounting and auditing rules, all expenditures of the Interim Batasang Pambansa as a constituent body.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
