MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 1252

In an earlier Letter of Instructions, I directed the Ministry of Agriculture and the Philippine Dairy Corporation to work together for the establishment of two Dairy Genetic Farms, one at the Malaybalay Stock Farm in Bukidnon and another at the Milagros Stock Farm in Masbate.

To supplement the above Dairy Genetic Farms, it is necessary to establish a third genetic farm at Los Baños which can be closely monitored and supervised by UPLB scientists and researchers.

I therefore direct the University of the Philippines at Los Baños, Laguna and the Philippine Dairy Corporation to coordinate and work closely in establishing a third dairy genetic farm within the UPLB experimental station.

UPLB will provide the technical expertise and assistance needed in the formulation and implementation of breeding schemes and genetic development programs. The Philippine Dairy Corporation shall be responsible for the actual management and operation of the dairy genetic farm and shall provide the necessary funds out of its captial outlay for the establishment and operation of the farm.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of June in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
