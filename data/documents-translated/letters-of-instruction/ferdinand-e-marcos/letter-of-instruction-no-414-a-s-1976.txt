MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 414-A

You are hereby directed to collect a security fee from every departing passenger on commercial aircraft, whether scheduled, non-scheduled or chartered, as follows: Ten (₱10.00) pesos on international flights and Three (₱3.00) pesos on domestic flights. Nonetheless, the Notional Action Committee on Anti-Hijacking (NACAH) for whose use the amounts collected are intended may provide exemptions in the collection of the fee from passengers departing from any domestic airport where the Civil Aeronautics Administration (CAA) does not maintain an adequate passenger terminal building.

The NACAH is hereby authorized to promulgate appropriate rules so that the collection of the security fee can be done expeditiously and efficiently.

The amounts collected, which shall be kept in a separate account by the CAA, are hereby automatically appropriated as a subsidy to the NACAH which may spend the same for the following purposes:

1. To provide infrastructures for itself and its components namely: the Secretariat, the Aviation Security Command (AVESCOM), and the Press Center;

2. To provide for the development and improvement of existing airports and airport terminal buildings ;

3. To provide ex oratia financial assistance to individual victims of hijacking; and

4. To provide for such other purposes for which the NACAH was established

All purchases made pursuant herewith shall be exempt from Presidential Memorandum Circular Nos. 518, 593 and 192, series of 1971, 1972 and 1974, respectively.

This Letter of Instructions supersedes Letter of Instructions No. 414.

Done in the City of Manila this 17th day of June in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy six .

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS
