MALACAÑANG Manila

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 649

In order to complete the upgrading program and the development of the national park for Barrios Suba and Nanguyudan along the concept defined under LOI No. 529 issued on April 5, 1977, the National Housing Authority is hereby directed to prepare the necessary master schedule of activities for this purpose.

There is allocated from the Special Activities Fund of the Office of the President and additional amount of P2.3 million for the completion of the development of Barrio Suba and P3.5 million for the upgrading of Barrio Nanguyudan which amounts shall be granted to the National Housing Authority. These amounts shall be in addition to the P2.0 million appropriated for upgrading of residential areas under said LOI 529.

The membership of PAOAY LAKE INTER-AGENCY COMMITTEE created under the said LOI 529 is hereby enlarged with the inclusion of the Barangay Chairman of each of the affected barrios as members. Said Committee shall, among others, see to the implementation of the plans and program for residential upgrading that the National Housing Authority shall design.

All directives, letter of instructions, or parts thereof, which are inconsistent with this Letter of Instructions are hereby suspended indefinitely.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 28th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
