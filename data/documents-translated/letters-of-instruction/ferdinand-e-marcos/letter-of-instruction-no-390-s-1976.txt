MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 390

TO : The Secretary, Department of Local Government & Community Development The Secretary, Department of Public Works & Transportation and Communications Director General, National Economic & Development Authority Chairman, Philippine National Oil Company Chairman, Oil Industry Commission

For reasons of national policy, it is essential that the fuel price support for certain designated transport and other industries be continued. Accordingly, the effectivity of Letter of Instruction No. 280 as extended by Letters of Instruction No. 306, 327 and 349 is hereby further extended for another period of three (3) months beginning April 1, 1976 and ending June 30, 1976.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 25th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Seventy-Six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines
