MALACAÑANG MANILA

LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 4

MOBILIZING POLITICAL, MORAL AND SECTORAL LEADERS AND LOCAL COMMUNITIES FOR POLITICAL REFORM

The Director General of the Presidential Management Staff shall see to it that the federalism roadmap committee shall draw up the schedule of discussions and consultations, including those with leaders and communities in the regions, as well as the Office of the Adviser on the Peace Process in anticipation of a peace agreement with the Moro Islamic Liberation Front incorporating a federal regional government.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

Manila, 23 November 2007

By the President of the Philippines: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
