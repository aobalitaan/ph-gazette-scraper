PROCLAMATION NO. 363

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM MAY 1 TO MAY 7 OF EACH YEAR AS “LABOR WEEK”

WHEREAS, the Republic of the Philippines is a country that exalts and values workers;

WHEREAS, Labor Day is an annual celebration and an important factor to recognize and highlight the remarkable accomplishments of workers in the economic field and in the advancement of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, the government firmly believes that the success of “Philippines 2000” rests in the hands of the workers;

WHEREAS, it is more than ever the time and aspiration of the government to devote a golden period for the people to commemorate and express our recognition of what workers have contributed and accomplished both here and abroad;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby designate the period from May 1 to May 7 of each year as “Labor Week”.

In furtherance hereof, I encourage all labor organizations to unite for a meaningful celebration of “Labor Week” now and in the future. I likewise urge investors to unite and help in this endeavor.

I direct the Department of Labor and Employment and other government agencies to cooperate and collaborate with labor organizations throughout the country to commemorate and bestow appropriate honors during the celebration of “Labor Week”.

I also encourage all members of the press to convey to the people, especially the youth, the importance of workers in the celebration of “Labor Week”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 29th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Four.

By the President of the Philippines:
(Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR.
Executive Secretary
