MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 28

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 31, 1992, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES

WHEREAS, Monday, August 31, 1992, marks the 85th birth anniversary of Ramon F. Magsaysay, the 3rd President of the Republic of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that at least the people of his birthplace be afforded full opportunity to celebrate his birthday with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book 1, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Revised Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, August 31, 1992, as a special day in the Province of Zambales.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) DIONISIO DE LA SERNA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
