MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 104

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE DON MARIANO MARCOS ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Don Mariano Marcos Elementary School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Poblacion, Municipality of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows, to wit:

A PARCEL OF LAND (Block 37 of the Subdivision Plan Ts-233-D) situated in the Poblacion, Municipality of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao.
