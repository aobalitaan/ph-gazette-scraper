MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 852

DECLARING TUESDAY, AUGUST 20, 1996, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN LUCENA CITY

WHEREAS, Tuesday, August 20, 1996, marks the foundation anniversary of Lucena City;

WHEREAS, it is only proper to give the people of Lucena City full opportunity to commemorate the occasion with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Tuesday, August 20, 1996, as a special day in Lucena City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety six.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
