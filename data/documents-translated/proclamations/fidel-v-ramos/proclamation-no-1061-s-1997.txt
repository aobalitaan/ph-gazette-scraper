MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1061

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE UDTONG ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY UDTONG, MUNICIPALITY OF LAMBAYONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Udtong Elementary School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land situated in Barangay Udtong, Municipality of Lambayong, Province of Sultan Kudarat, Island of Mindanao, which is more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 212, Pls-442

A parcel of land (Lot No. 212, Pls-442), situated in Barangay Udtong, Municipality of Lambayong, Province of Sultan Kudarat, Island of Mindanao.
