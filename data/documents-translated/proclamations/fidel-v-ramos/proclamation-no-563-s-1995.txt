MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 563

DECLARING THE PERIOD 12 APRIL 1995 TO 11 APRIL 1996 AS “CENTRAL LUZON STATE UNIVERSITY (CLSU) ALUMNI YEAR” AND AUTHORIZING THE CLSU AND THE CLSU ALUMNI ASSOCIATION, INC. (CLSUAAI) TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE FUND CAMPAIGN DURING SAID PERIOD

WHEREAS , the CLSU, has served the nation by providing relevant and high quality education since its establishment in 1907, and in the process, produced distinguished Filipinos working here and abroad;

WHEREAS , the CLSUAAI is engaged in a meritorious program to:

a. establish a multi-purpose CLSU Alumni Convention, Recreational and Service Center which shall provide facilities to help link up CLSU alumni and friends here and abroad;

b. generate and manage student scholarships and assistantships;

c. provide voluntary and free technical assistance and social services to CLSU alumni, and indigents in the area.

WHEREAS , the CLSUAAI needs the support of the general public and the citizenry in accomplishing said worthy endeavor.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period 12 April 1995 to 11 April 1996 as “Central Luzon State University Alumni Year” as well as authorize the CLSU and the CLSUAAI to undertake a nationwide fund campaign during said period.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12th day of April in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Five.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Executive Secretary
