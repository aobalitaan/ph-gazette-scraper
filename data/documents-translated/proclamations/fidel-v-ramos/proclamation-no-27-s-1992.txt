MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 27

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 1992 AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS, Thursday, September 3, 1992, marks the 24th Foundation Anniversary of the City of Iriga;

WHEREAS, the people of the City of Iriga should be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Revised Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Thursday, September 3, 1992, as a special day in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) DIONISIO DE LA SERNA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
