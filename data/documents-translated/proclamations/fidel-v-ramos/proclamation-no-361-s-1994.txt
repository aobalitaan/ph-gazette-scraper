MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 361

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 172, s. OF 1987, CERTAIN PORTIONS OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN SITUATED IN LOWER BICUTAN, TAGUIG, METRO MANILA, AND RESERVING THE SAME FOR MARITIME COMMUNICATIONS PROJECT SITE PURPOSES.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for Maritime Communications Project site purposes under the administration of the Department of Transportation and Communications, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following portions of the land in Barangay Lower Bicutan, Municipality of Taguig, Rizal, earmarked for public and quasi-public purposes and excluded from the operation of Proclamation No. 172, s. of 1987, more particularly described as follows:
