MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 733

RESERVING AS SCHOOL SITE A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANSALAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes, under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights if there be any, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Municipality of Bansalan, province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao, which is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 4419-B, Csd-11-010302-D

A PARCEL OF LAND, Lot 4419-B, Csd-11-010302-D equivalent to Lot 21561 (portion of Lot 4419, Cad-275), situated in the Barangay Anonang, Municipality of Bansalan, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2 by Lot 4419-A, Csd-11-010302-D; on the SE., along line 2-3 by Lot 4380, Cad-275 Sta. Cruz Cadastre; on the SW., along line 3-4 by Road; and on the NW., along line 4-1 by Lot 4418, cad-275. Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 4419-B, Csd-11-010302-D, being S. 52-23 E., 138.29 from B.L.L.M. 82, Cad-275; thence;

point of beginning. Containing an area of TWENTY FIVE THOUSAND (25,000) SQUARE METERS. All corners are marked on the ground as follows: point 1-2 by PS Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 40 cm. and the rest by Old BL Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cms.; Bearings true, date of Survey July 15, 1989 by Geodetic Engineer Jesus A. Brilleta and approved on August 25, 1989.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12 th day of February in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Six.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

(Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary
