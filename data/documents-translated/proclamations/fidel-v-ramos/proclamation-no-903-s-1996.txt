MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO 903

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE MASAYA NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL (FORMERLY MASAYA BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL), A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY MASAYA, MUNICIPALITY OF BAY, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Masaya, Municipality of Bay, Province of Laguna, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 1499 Cad-397-D, Bay Cadastre Psu-9409 Accepted)
