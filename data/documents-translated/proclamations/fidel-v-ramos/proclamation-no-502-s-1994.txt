MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 502

DECLARING THURSDAY, DECEMBER 8, 1994, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN ANGELES CITY

WHEREAS, Thursday, December 8, 1994, marks the foundation anniversary of Angeles City;

WHEREAS, the city residents wish to mark this memorable day with fitting activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Thursday, December 8, 1994, as a special day in Angeles City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety four.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President (Sgd.) TEOFISTO T. GUINGONA, JR. Exxecutive Secretary
