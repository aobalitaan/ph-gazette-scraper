MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 128

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 1, 1993, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN CAMARINES NORTE

WHEREAS, Monday, February 1, 1993, marks the 130th birthday of Jose Ma. Panganiban, whose ideals and principles in life are now the nation’s treasured legacy; and

WHEREAS, the people of Camarines Norte, his birthplace, desire to celebrate his natal day with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, February 1, 1993, as a special day in the Province of Camarines Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety three.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) ANTONIO T. CARPIO Chief Presidential Legal Counsel
