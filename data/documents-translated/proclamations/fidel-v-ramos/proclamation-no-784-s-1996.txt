MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 784

RESERVING FOR MILITARY USE PURPOSES OF THE PHILIPPINE AIR FORCE, DEPARTMENT OF NATIONAL DEFENSE, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF LAPU-LAPU, PROVINCE OF CEBU

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for military use purposes of the Philippine Air Force, Department of National Defense, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, situated in the City of Lapu-Lapu, Province of Cebu, which is particularly described as follows:

SK-07-000010 (Philippine Air Force)

A PARCEL OF LAND (SK-07-000010, Proposed DND-AFP Reservation), situated in the barrio of Canjulao, City of Lapu-lapu, Province of Cebu, Island of Cebu.
