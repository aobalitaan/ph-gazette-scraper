BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1041

DECLARING THE ANNUAL CELEBRATION FROM AUGUST 1-31 AS NATIONAL LANGUAGE MONTH

WHEREAS, the appreciation of a native national language has been proven by the existence of relevant provisions in the Constitutions of 1898, 1935, 1973, and 1987;

WHEREAS, a native general language is an important instrument for communication, understanding, unity and the development of the country;

WHEREAS, the native language which serves as the basis for the cultivated, developed and enriched national language Filipino, as stipulated in the 1987 Constitution, played an important role in the Revolution of 1896 towards the attainment of Independence whose 100th Anniversary is currently being celebrated and commemorated by the Filipino people;

WHEREAS, the late President Manuel Luis Quezon, considered the Father of the National Language, was born on August 19, 1878;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the annual celebration from August 1-31 as National Language Month, to be spearheaded by leaders and personnel in the government sector, leaders and teachers in the education sector, representatives of public relations, leaders and members of various linguistic, educational, cultural and civic organizations, and non-governmental organizations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 15th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Seven.

By the President:
(Sgd.) RUBEN D. TORRES
Executive Secretary
