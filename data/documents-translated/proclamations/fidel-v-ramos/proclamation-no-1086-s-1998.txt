MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1086

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE TAMBULIG NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAMBULIG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition and settlement, and reserve for school site purposes of the Tambulig National High School, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Municipality of Tambulig, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, Island of Mindanao, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 121, CSD-09-05-000622

A Parcel of Land, (Lot 121 of the subdivision plan, Csd-09-05-000622, being a portion of Lot 8164, Pls-61), situated in the Barrio of Lower Udiong, Municipality of Tambulig, Province of Zamboanga del Sur.
