MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 758

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 January 2025, Friday, the City of Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 85" Founding Anniversary and the
2025 Koronadal Hinugyaw Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
10 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of December in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
