MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 187

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2023, Wednesday, the City of Baliwag, Province of
Bulacan, will mark the celebration of the 160" birth anniversary of Mariano Ponce;

WHEREAS, Mariano Ponce, a native of Baliwag, Bulacan, was a prominent
member of the propaganda movement and one of the leading figures in the
Philippine reform movement during the Spanish colonization;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Baliwag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Baliwag,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
