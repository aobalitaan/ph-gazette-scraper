MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 928

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MALAPATAN, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Malapatan, Province of Sarangani will mark the
celebration of its 56 Founding Anniversary on 21 June 2025, which falls on a
Saturday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Malapatan to fully
participate in the occasion, the Sargguniang Bayan requested that 20 June 2025,
Friday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 21 June 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malapatan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
20 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Malapatan, Province
of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
