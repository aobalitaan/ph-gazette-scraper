MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 531

DECLARING THURSDAY, 09 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 09 May 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Charter Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 09 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof May _ , inthe year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
