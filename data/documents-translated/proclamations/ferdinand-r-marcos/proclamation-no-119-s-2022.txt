MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 119

DECLARING THURSDAY, 29 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN PEDRO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 29 December 2022, Thursday, the City of San Pedro,
Province of Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 9° Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pedro
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 29 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pedro,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
