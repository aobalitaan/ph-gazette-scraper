MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 46

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 16 September 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province, will mark the celebration of its 114" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bontoc be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
16 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
