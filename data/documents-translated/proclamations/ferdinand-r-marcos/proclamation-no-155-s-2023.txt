MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 155

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANGANTUCAN, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Pangantucan,
Province of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pangantucan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pangantucan, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7thday of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
