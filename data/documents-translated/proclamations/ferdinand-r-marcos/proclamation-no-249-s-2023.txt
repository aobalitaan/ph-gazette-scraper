MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 249

DECLARING MONDAY, 03 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAMAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Maramag,
Province of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 67" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Maramag to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 03 July 2023,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 01 July 2023 which
falls on a Saturday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maramag be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 03 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maramag,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof June _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
