MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 791

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 11 February 2025, Tuesday, the City of Talisay, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 27" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Talisay be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 11 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Talisay,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
