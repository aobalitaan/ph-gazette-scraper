MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 207

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT ROXAS, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 08 May 2023, Monday, the Municipality of President Roxas,
Province of Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 56" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

President Roxas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Monday, 08 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of President
Roxas, Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thday of April , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
