MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 446

DECLARING FRIDAY, 09 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAGBAYAN, PROVINCE OF BOHOL

WHEREAS, on 09 February 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Sagbayan,
Province of Bohol will mark the celebration of its 75" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sagbayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
09 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sagbayan,
Province of Bohol.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
