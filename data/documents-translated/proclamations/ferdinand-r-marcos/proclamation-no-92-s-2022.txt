MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 92

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 07 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIRAC, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, on 07 December 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of Virac,
Province of Catanduanes, will mark the celebration of the Kaaldawan nin Virac;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Viarc be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 07 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Virac, Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 thday of November in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
