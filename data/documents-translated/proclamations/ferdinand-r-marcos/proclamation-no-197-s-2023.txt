MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 197

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 24 October 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Pili, Province
of Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of the Cimmarones Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pili
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pili,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of March , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
