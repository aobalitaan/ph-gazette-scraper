MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 526

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOGPOG, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Mogpog,
Province of Marinduque will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mogpog be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mogpog, Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd dayof April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
