MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1028

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 08 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 08 October 2025, Wednesday, the City of Koronadal, Province
of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 25" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 08 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 thday of September, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

   

ATTY. LOVELY V. TOLENTINO-NAVA
