- MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 450

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY

IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Buug,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary and BOG Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Buug be given full opportunity to participate in the foregoing occasions and enjoy
their celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 26 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Buug,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
