MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1150

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 18 February 2026, Wednesday, the City of Bayawan, Province
of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of the Tawo-Tawo Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayawan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
