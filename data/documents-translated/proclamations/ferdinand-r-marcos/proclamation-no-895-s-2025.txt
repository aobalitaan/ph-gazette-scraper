MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 895

DECLARING MONDAY, 09 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 09 June 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Rosario, Province
of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rosario be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
09 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th dayof May _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
