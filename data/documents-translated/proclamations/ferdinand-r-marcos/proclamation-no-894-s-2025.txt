MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 894

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF BAMBAN, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 06 June 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Bamban, Province of
Tarlac will mark the celebration of Bamban Day in commemoration of the proclamation
of Bamban as the Revolutionary Capital of the Philippines by General Emilio
Aguinaldo;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bamban be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
06 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bamban, Province of
Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
