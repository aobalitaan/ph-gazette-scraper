MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1096

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIPLAHAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 19 December 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Diplahan,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Diplahan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
19 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Diplahan,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21s tday of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
