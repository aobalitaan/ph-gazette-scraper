MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1048

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGLE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 28 October 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Dingle, Province
of lloilo will commemorate the Cry of Lincud, the first declaration of revolution against
Spain in the province of Iloilo and island of Panay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dingle be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 28 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dingle,
Province of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30thday of Sept embeuin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
