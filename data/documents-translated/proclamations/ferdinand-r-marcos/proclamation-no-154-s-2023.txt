MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 154

DECLARING SATURDAY, 03 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF AYUNGON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 03 June 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Ayungon,
Province of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Ayungon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 03 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ayungon,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day ofFebruary , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
