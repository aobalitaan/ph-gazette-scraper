MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1136

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONSOLACION, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 06 February 2026, Friday, the Municipality of Consolacion,
Province of Cebu will mark the celebration of its 106 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Consolacion be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
06 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Consolacion,
Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15thday of January, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

/RECTO
YSecretary
