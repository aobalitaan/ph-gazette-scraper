MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 673

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIDDIG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 16 September 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Piddig,
Province of Ilocos Norte will commemorate the Basi Revolt of 1807;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Piddig be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
16 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Piddig, Province
of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Bane in the City of Manila, this 29th day of August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
