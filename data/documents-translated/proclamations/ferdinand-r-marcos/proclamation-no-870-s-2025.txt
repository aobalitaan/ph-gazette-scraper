MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 870

DECLARING 22 APRIL 2025 AS A DAY OF NATIONAL MOURNING
OVER THE PASSING OF NATIONAL ARTIST NORA CABALTERA
VILLAMAYOR

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 1390 (s. 2022) declared Nora Cabaltera
Villamayor as a National Artist in recognition of her works in the field of film and
broadcast arts;

WHEREAS, the passing of National Artist for Film and Broadcast Nora Cabaltera
Villamayor, also known as Nora Aunor, is a great loss to the Filipino people and to the
nation’s cultural and artistic community;

WHEREAS, her outstanding contribution to Philippine cinema, television, and
music has left a lasting legacy on the nation’s collective memory and identity;

WHEREAS, her artistry, depth, and dedication as a performer elevated the
standard of excellence in the fields of arts and culture, and inspired generations of
actors, filmmakers, and audiences, both in local and international stage;

WHEREAS, the nation mourns and joins the family and friends of National Artist
Nora Cabaltera Villamayor in this moment of grief and remembrance; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 27(k), Chapter 1 of the Implementing Rules and
Regulations of Republic Act No. 8491 or the “Flag and Heraldic Code of the
Philippines,” in relation to Proclamation No. 1001 (s. 1972), as amended, the flag shall
be flown at half-mast as a sign of mourning, on all buildings and places where it is
displayed on the day of the interment of the recipients of the National Artist award;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
do hereby declare 22 April 2025 as a Day of National Mourning over the passing of
National Artist Nora Cabaltera Villamayor.

As provided for by law, the national flag shall be flown at half-mast from sunrise

to sunset, on all government buildings and installations throughout the Philippines and
abroad, on 22 April 2025.

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 21st day of April ,in

the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.
