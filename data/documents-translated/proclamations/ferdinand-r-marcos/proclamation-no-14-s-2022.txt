MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 14

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2022, Monday, the Municipality of Compostela,
Province of Davao de Oro, will mark the celebration of its 74° Founding Anniversary
and the 16" Buganihan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Compostela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 01 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Compostela, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, i have hereunto set my hand and caused the seai
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
