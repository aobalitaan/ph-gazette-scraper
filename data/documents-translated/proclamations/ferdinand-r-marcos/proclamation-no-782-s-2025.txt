MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 782

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 24 February 2025, Monday, the Province of Zamboanga
Sibugay will mark the celebration of its 24" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy
the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
24 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga
Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

  

Executive Secretary
