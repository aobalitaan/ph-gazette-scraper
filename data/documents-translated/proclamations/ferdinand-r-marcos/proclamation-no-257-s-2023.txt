MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 257

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 04 August 2023, Friday, the City of Cabuyao, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its 11" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabuyao
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
04 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabuyao, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
