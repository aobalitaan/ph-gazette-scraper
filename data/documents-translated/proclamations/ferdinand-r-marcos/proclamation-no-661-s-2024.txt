MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 661

DECLARING FRIDAY, 27 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 27 September 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas, will mark the 159" birth anniversary of General Miguel Malvar,
one of the most recognized leaders of the Philippine Revolution and the Philippine-
American War who was born on 27 September 1865;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malvar be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
27 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 dayof August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
