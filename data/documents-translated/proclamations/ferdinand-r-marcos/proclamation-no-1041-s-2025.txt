MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1041

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 1022, S. 2025, BY DECLARING FRIDAY, 19
SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITIES OF PILI, OCAMPO, CANAMAN, CAMALIGAN, MAGARAO,
BOMBON, CALABANGA, GAINZA, LIBMANAN, MILAOR, MINALABAC,
PAMPLONA, PASACAO, AND SAN FERNANDO, ALL FROM THE PROVINCE
OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 654 dated 25 September 2013 which amended
Proclamation 776, s. 1991 declared every Friday immediately preceding the feast of Our
Lady of Pefiafrancia as a special day in the City of Naga;

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 1022, s. 2025 declared 19 September 2025, as a
special (non-working) day in the Municipalities of Pili, Ocampo, Canaman, Camaligan,
Magarao, Bombon, and Calabanga, all from the Province of Camarines Sur;

WHEREAS, considering the significant economic, cultural and historical ties
between Naga City and its neighboring municipalities, and the immense benefits that the
Pefiafrancia Festival would bring to their communities, there is a need to amend
Proclamation No. 1022, s. 2025 so as to include the following municipalities in its
coverage, namely: Gainza, Libmanan, Milaor, Minalabac, Pamplona, Pasacao, and San
Fernando, all from the Province of Camarines Sur.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by authority
of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday, 19
September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipalities of Pili, Ocampo,
Canaman, Camaligan, Magarao, Bombon, Calabanga, Gainza, Libmanan, Milaor,
Minalabac, Pamplona, Pasacao, and San Fernando, all from the Province of Camarines
Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the
Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of September, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
