MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 969

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KABACAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 18 August 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Kabacan,
Province of Cotabato will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kabacan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
18 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kabacan, Province
of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thgayof July |, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
