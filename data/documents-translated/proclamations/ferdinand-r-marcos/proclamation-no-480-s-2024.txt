MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 480

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SARRAT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Sarrat,
Province of Ilocos Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sarrat be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 28 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Sarrat, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
