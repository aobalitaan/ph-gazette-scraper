MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 139

DECLARING THURSDAY, 27 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONTALBAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 27 April 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Montalban,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of its 152" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Montalban be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 27 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Montalban, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
