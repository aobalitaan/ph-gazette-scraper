MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 227

DECLARING SATURDAY, 20 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GATTARAN, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 20 May 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Gattaran, Province
of Cagayan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gattaran be given full opportunity to enhance their cultural, historical and social
consciousness, and to enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 20 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gattaran,
Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
