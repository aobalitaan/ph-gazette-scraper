MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 128

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2023, Friday, the Province of Bulacan will
commemorate the birth anniversary of Blas F. Ople, a son of Bulacan, and a model
citizen and public officer to the Filipino people, especially to the “‘Bulakenyos”;

WHEREAS, Blas F. Ople served as Secretary of Foreign Affairs, Senate
President, and Secretary of Labor, devoting decades of his life to selfless and
dedicated service to our country and our people;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
03 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19thday of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
