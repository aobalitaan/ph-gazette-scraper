MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1110

DECLARING TUESDAY, 06 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF OROQUIETA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 06 January 2026, Tuesday, the City of Oroquieta, Province of
Misamis Occidental will mark the celebration of its 56" Charter Anniversary and its
96" Anniversary as the capital of the Province of Misamis Occidental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Oroquieta
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 06 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Oroquieta,
Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15ttday of December in the year of Our Lord,
