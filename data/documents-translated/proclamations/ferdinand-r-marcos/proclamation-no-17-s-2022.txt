MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 17

DECLARING THURSDAY, 04 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 04 August 2022, Thursday, the City of Cabuyao, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 10" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabuyao
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 04 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabuyao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25tn day of juiy —_, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
