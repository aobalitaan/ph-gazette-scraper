MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 575

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Rosario, Province of
Agusan del Sur will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Rosario and the Buyawan
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rosario be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
21 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd  dayof June _ , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
