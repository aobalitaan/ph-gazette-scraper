MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 756

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of San Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro will
commemorate the 80‘ Liberation Day of Mindoro Island from Japanese Occupation
on 15 December 2024, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of San Jose to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 16 December 2024,
Monday, be declared as a (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose in lieu of
15 December 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Jose be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
16 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Occidental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
