MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 4

DECLARING SATURDAY, 02 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, on 02 July 2022, Saturday, the City of Pasig will mark the
celebration of its 449'" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government,

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 02 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this ist dayof July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
