MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 814

DECLARING THURSDAY, 20 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 20 March 2025, Thursday, the City of San Fernando, Province
of La Union will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Fernando be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Thursday, 20 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Fernando,
Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of Februar yin the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
