MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 726

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BORONGAN, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, Eugenio S. Daza, born in Borongan, Eastern Samar on 15
November 1870 was the overall commander of the revolutionary forces in Eastern
Samar during the Philippine war against the United States, and represented the Third
District of Samar in the Philippine Assembly from 1907 to 1909;

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2024, Friday, the City of Borongan, Province of
Eastern Samar, will honor the 154" birth anniversary of Eugenio S. Daza;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Borongan
be given full opportunity to participate in the commemorative activities to foster unity
and reflect on the ideals that Daza championed;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Borongan, Province of
Eastern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th dayof October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

LUGASP. BERSAMIN
e-Secretary
