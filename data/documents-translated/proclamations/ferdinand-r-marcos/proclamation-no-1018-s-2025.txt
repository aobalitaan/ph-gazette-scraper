MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1018

DECLARING MONDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALICIA, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Alicia, Province of Isabela will mark the
celebration of its Founding Anniversary on 28 September 2025, which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Alicia to fully participate
in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 29 September 2025, Monday,
be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 28 September 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Alicia be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 29 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Alicia,
Province of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF. | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day ofSeptember jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
