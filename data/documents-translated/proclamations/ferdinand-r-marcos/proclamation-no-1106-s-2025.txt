MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1106

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 19 December 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Rizal, Province
of Laguna will mark the celebration of its 107 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rizal be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
19 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rizal, Province
of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
