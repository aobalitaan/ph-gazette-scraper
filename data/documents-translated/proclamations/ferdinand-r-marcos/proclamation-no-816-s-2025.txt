MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 816

DECLARING SATURDAY, 29 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAMBUSAO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ

WHEREAS, on 29 March 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Mambusao,
Province of Capiz will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mambusao be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 29 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mambusao, Province of Capiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
