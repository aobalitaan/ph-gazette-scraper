MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 322

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUPI, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 11 September 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Tupi,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 70" Founding
Anniversary and the 14" Agten Tufi Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tupi be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 11 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tupi,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th dayof August, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
