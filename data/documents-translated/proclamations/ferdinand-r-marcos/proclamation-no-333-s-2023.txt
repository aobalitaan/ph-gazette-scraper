MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 333

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 16 September 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Bontoc to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 15 September
2023, Friday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 16 September
2023 which falls on a Saturday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bontoc be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
