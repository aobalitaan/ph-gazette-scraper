MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 355

DECLARING THURSDAY, 12 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 12 October 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Pamplona,
Province of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of the Yamog Festival and its
annual fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pamplona be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 12 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pamplona, Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
