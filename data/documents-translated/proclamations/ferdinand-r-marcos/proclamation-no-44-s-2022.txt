MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 44

DECLARING FRIDAY, 09 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAIS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 09 September 2022, Friday, the City of Bais, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 54th Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bais be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
09 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bais.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25thday of August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

MALACANANG RECORDS OFFICE
