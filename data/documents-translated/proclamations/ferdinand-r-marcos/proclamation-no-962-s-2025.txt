MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 962

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ESPERANZA, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 22 August 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Esperanza,
Province of Masbate will mark the celebration of the Lilas Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Esperanza be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
22 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Esperanza,
Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8t? dayof July

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

, inthe year of Our Lord,
