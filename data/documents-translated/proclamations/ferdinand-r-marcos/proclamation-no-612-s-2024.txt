MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 612

DECLARING TUESDAY, 02 JULY 2024, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, on 02 July 2024, Tuesday, the City of Pasig will mark the
celebration of the 451s Araw ng Pasig;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 02 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
