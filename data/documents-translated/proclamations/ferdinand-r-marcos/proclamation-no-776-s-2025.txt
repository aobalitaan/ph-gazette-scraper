MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 776

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALAWIT, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 10 February 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Kalawit,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kalawit be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
10 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kalawit, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rdday of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
