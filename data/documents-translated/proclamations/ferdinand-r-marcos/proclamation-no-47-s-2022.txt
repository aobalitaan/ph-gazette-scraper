MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 47

DECLARING TUESDAY, 27 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 27 September 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas, will mark the 157" birth anniversary of General Miguel Malvar,
one of the most recognized leaders of the Philippine Revolution and the Philippine-
American War;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malvar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 27 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Malvar, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of August _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
