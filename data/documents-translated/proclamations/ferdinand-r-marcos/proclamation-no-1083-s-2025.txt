MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1083

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 11 December 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Jose be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 11 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Jose, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
