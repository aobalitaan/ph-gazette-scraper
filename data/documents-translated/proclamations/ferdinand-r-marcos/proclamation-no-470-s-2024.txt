MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 470

DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE ISLAND GARDEN CITY OF SAMAL, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2024, Thursday, the Island Garden City of Samal,
Province of Davao del Norte, will mark the celebration of its 26 Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Island Garden
City of Samal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 07 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Island Garden City of
Samal, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
