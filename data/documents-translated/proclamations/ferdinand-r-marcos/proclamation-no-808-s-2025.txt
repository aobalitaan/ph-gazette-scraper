MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 808

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF NORALA, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 March 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Norala, Province
of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Norala be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
10 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Norala, Province of
South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of*Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
