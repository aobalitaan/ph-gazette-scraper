MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. °&64

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALAUAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Calauan, Province
of Laguna will mark the celebration of the Calauan Pinya Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calauan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calauan,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of Apr#li jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
