MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 975

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Compostela, Province
of Cebu will mark the celebration of its town fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Compostela be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Compostela, Province
of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of

MALACANA
