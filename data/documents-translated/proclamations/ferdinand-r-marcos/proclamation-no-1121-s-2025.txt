MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.1121

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Carigara, Province of Leyte will mark the
celebration of its 455 Founding Anniversary on 25 January 2026, which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Carigara to fully
participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan requested that 26 January 2026,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 25 January 2026;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carigara be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
26 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Carigara, Province
of Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day ofpecember, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five
