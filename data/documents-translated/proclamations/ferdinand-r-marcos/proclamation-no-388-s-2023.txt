MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 388 i

DECLARING SATURDAY, 16 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SORSOGON, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON

WHEREAS, on 16 December 2023, Saturday, the City of Sorsogon, Province
of Sorsogon will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sorsogon
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 16 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sorsogon,
Province of Sorsogon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd — day of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
