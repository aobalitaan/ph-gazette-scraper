MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 595

DECLARING SATURDAY, 27 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GLAN, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 27 July 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Glan, Province of
Sarangani will mark the celebration of its 75 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Glan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 27 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Glan,
Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
