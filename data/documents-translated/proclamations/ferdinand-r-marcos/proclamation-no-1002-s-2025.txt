MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1002

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISULAN, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Isulan, Province of Sultan Kudarat will mark the
celebration of its 68'" Founding Anniversary and the 12‘* Hamungaya Festival on 30
August 2025, which falls on a Saturday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Isulan to fully participate
in the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan of Isulan requested that 29 August 2025,
Friday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 30 August 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Isulan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
29 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Isulan, Province of
Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

  

LY V. TOLENTINO-NAVA
DIRECTOR IV
