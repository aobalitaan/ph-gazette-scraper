MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

_ BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1118

DECLARING FRIDAY, 09 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, the City of Koronadal, Province of South Cotabato will mark the
celebration of its 86" Founding Anniversary and the 2026 Koronadal Hinugyaw
Festival on 10 January 2026, which falls on a Saturday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Koranadal to fully participate in
the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 09 January 2026, Friday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 10 January 2026;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
09 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato. :

IN WITNESS WHEREOEF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16thday of December in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five

By authority of the President:

    
 

RALPH G
tig Exephtye Secretary
