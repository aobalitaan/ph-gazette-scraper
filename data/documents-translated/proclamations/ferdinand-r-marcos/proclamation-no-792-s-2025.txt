MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 792

DECLARING THURSDAY, 13 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PARANAQUE

WHEREAS, on 13 February 2025, Thursday, the City of Parafiaque will mark
the celebration of its 27" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Parafiaque
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 13 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Parafiaque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
