MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 454

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE

WHEREAS, on 25 January 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Carigara,
Province of Leyte will mark the celebration of its 453" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carigara be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

, NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by

authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Carigara, Province of Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
_ Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
