MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 813

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 19 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Bongabong,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of the Sulyog Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bongabong be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 19 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bongabong.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

    

CERTIY!

ATTY. LOVELY V.
