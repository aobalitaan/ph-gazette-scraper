MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 317

DECLARING TUESDAY, 26 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 26 September 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Naval,
Province of Biliran will mark the celebration of its 163" Pueblo Day (Charter Day);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naval be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 26 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Naval, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
