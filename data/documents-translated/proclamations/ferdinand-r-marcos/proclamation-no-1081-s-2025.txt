MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1081

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF M’LANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on Friday, 05 December 2025, the Municipality of M’lang, Province
of Cotabato will celebrate the Kawayanan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
M'lang be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
05 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of M’lang, Province
of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
