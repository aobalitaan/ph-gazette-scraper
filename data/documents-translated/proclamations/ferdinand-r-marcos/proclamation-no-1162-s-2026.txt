MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.1162

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 20 March 2026, Friday, the City of San Fernando, Province of
La Union will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Fernando be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
20 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Fernando, Province of
La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day offebruaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:
