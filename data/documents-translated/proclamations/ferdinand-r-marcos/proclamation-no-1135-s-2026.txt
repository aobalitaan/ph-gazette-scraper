MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1135

DECLARING MONDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KATIPUNAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2026, Monday, the Municipality of Katipunan,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 112° Araw ng
Katipunan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that, the people of the Municipality of
Katipunan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 02 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Katipunan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15thday of January , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
