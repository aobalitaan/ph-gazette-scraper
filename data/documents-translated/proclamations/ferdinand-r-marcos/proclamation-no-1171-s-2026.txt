MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 1171

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TADIAN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 06 March 2026, Friday, the Municipality of Tadian, Mountain
Province, will mark the celebration of Ayyoweng Di Lambak Ed Tadian Festival;

-WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tadian be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
06 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tadian, Mountain
Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this20th day of February in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

President
