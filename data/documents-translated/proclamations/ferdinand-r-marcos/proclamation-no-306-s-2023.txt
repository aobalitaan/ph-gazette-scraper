MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 306

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 25 August 2023, Friday, the City of lloilo will mark the
celebration of its 86" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iloilo be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, i, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st dayof July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
