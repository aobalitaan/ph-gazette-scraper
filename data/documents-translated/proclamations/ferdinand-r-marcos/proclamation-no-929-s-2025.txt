MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 929

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN JUAN

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2025, Tuesday, the City of San Juan will mark the
celebration of the Wattah! Wattah! Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Juan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Juan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
