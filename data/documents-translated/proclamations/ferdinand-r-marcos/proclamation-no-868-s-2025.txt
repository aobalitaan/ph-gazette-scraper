MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 868

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 13 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TAYABAS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 13 August 2025, Wednesday, the City of Tayabas, Province of
Quezon will commemorate the anniversary of its independence from the Spanish rule;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tayabas be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 13 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tayabas,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21stgayof April | in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
