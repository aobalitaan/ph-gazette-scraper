MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 260

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF TABUK, PROVINCE OF KALINGA

WHEREAS, on 23 June 2023, Friday, the City of Tabuk, Province of Kalinga
will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabuk be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 23 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabuk, Province of
Kalinga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
