MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 243

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONCADA, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Moncada,
Province of Tarlac will mark the celebration of its 148"" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Moncada be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Moncada,
Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this31st dayof May, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
