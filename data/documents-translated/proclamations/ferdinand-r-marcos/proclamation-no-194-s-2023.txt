MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 194

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Rosario,
Province of Agusan del Sur will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Rosario and the
Buyawan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rosario be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Rosario.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of March _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
