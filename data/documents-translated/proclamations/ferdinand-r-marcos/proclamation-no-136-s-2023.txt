MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 136

DECLARING SATURDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 11 February 2023, Saturday, the City of lriga, Province of
Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of its Tinagba Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 11 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24thday of January in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
