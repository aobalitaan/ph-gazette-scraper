MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 947

DECLARING SATURDAY, 26 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 26 July 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Ipil, Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay will mark the celebration of its 76" Araw ng Ipil;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Ipil
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 26 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ipil, Province
of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rddayof June __, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
