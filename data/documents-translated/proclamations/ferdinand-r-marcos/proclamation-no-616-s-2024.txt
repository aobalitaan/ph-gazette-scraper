MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 616

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF KABANKALAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 02 August 2024, Friday, the City of Kabankalan, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 27" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Kabankalan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
02 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in. the City of Kabankalan, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this ist dayof july _, inthe year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
