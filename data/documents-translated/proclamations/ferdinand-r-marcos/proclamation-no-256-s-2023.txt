MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 256

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN PASCUAL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 04 August 2023, Friday, the Municipality of San Pascual,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its 54° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Pascual be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
04 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Pascual,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisi3th dayof June, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
