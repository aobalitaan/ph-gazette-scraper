MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 88

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 07 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro was
organized into a regular municipality through Act No. 3415 which was approved on
07 December 1927;

WHEREAS, on 07 December 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bongabong be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 07 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
