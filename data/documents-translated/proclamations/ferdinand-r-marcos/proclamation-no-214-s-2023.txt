MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 214

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Gingoog, Province of Misamis Oriental will mark the
celebration of its 63" Charter Day/44" Kaliga Festival on 23 July 2023;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Gingoog to celebrate and
participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod requested that 24 July
2023, Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 23 July 2023
which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 24 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philipines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
