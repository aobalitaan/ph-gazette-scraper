MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 449

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAGO, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 February 2024, Monday, the City of Bago, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 58" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bago be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 19 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bago,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
