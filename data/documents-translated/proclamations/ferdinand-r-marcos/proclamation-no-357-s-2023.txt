MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 357

DECLARING TUESDAY, 07 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUENCA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 07 November 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Cuenca,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its 147 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cuenca be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 07 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Cuenca, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
