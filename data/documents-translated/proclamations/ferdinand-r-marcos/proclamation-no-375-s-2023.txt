MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 375

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBLAY, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 22 November 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Tublay,
Province of Benguet will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tublay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, 1, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tublay, Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day ofOctober, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
