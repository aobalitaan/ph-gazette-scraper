MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 245

DECLARING SATURDAY, 08 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARMONA, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 11938 which was approved on 23 February
2023 provides for the conversion of the Municipality of Carmona in the Province of
Cavite into a component city to be known as the City of Carmona;

WHEREAS, Commission on Elections Resolution No. 10901 set the date of
the plebiscite to ratify the conversion of the Municipality of Carmona into a
component city on 08 July 2023;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Carmona the
opportunity to actively and fully participate in the plebiscite and exercise their right to
vote, it is but fitting and proper to declare 08 July 2023 as a special (non-working)
day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 08 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Carmona,
Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st dayof June _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
