MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 121

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JANUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 16 January 2023, Monday, the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of the 28" Coconut Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Monday, 16 January 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
