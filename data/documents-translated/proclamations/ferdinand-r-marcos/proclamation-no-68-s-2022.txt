MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 68

DECLARING THURSDAY, 20 OCTOBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIGCAWAYAN, PROVINCE OF NORTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 20 October 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Pigcawayan,
Province of North Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 69" Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pigcawayan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 20 October 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pigcawayan, Province of North Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

    

, FERROLTHO-BHAD
