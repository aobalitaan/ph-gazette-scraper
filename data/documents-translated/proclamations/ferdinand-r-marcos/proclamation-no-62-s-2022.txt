MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 62

DECLARING FRIDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Tanay,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of its 416" Founding Anniversary and the
12" Hane Festival;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
11 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tanay,
Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of September in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
