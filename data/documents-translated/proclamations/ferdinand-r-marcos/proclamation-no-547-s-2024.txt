MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 547

DECLARING THURSDAY, 06 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAMBAN, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 06 June 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Bamban,
Province of Tarlac will mark the celebration of Bamban Day in commemoration of
the proclamation of Bamban as the Revolutionary Capital of the Philippines by
General Emilio Aguinaldo;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bamban be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 06 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bamban,
Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
