MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 823

DECLARING MONDAY, 31 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PANABO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 31 March 2025, Monday, the City of Panabo, Province of Davao
del Norte, will mark the celebration of its 24° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Panabo be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
31 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Panabo, Province of Davao
del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
