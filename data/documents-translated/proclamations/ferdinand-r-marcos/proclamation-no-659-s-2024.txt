MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 659

DECLARING SATURDAY, 05 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA CRUZ, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 05 October 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Santa Cruz,
Province of Davao del Sur will mark the celebration of its 140 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santa Cruz be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 05 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santa
Cruz, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this O08 dayof August , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

CASP.B
Exé Secretary
