MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 570

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BARLIG, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Barlig, Mountain
Province will mark the celebration of its 58'" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Barlig be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 18 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Barlig,
Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd  dayof June _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
