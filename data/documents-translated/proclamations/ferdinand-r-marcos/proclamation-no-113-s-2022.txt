MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 113

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 JANUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 January 2023, Friday, the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of its Coffee Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
20 January 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two

By authority of the President:
