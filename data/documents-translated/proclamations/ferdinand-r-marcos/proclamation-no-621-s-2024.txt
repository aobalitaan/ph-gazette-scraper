MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 621

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, the City of Davao marks its annual celebration of Kadayawan
Festival and the Indigenous Peoples Day every third week of August;

WHEREAS, on 16 August 2024, Friday, the City of Davao will be celebrating
the 39" Kadayawan Festival and Indigenous Peoples Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
16 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof July _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
