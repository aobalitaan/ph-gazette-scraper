MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 918

DECLARING THURSDAY, 14 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTO TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 14 August 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Santo Tomas,
Province of Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of its 66" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santo Tomas be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 14 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santo
Tomas, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th dayof May __, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
