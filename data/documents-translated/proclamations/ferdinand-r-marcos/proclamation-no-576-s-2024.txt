MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 576

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MALAPATAN, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Malapatan, Province
of Sarangani will mark the celebration of its 55" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malapatan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration; :

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
21 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Malapatan, Province
of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof June _, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

 
   

WEN. ESPINO
DIRECTOR IV
