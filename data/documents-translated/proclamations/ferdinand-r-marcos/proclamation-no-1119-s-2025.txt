MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1119

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CURRIMAO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 21 January 2026, Wednesday, the Municipality of Currimao,
Province of Ilocos Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Currimao be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Currimao, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of Decembexin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five
