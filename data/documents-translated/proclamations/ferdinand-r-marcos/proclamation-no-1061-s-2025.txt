MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1061

DECLARING SATURDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF DINAGAT
ISLANDS

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Dinagat Islands will mark the celebration of its 36° Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Jose be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 15 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Jose, Province of Dinagat Islands.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

   
 

the President
