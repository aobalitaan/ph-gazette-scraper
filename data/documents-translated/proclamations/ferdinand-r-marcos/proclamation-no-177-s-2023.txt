MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 177

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 29 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAMBUSAO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ

WHEREAS, on 29 March 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Mambusao,
Province of Capiz will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mambusao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Wednesday, 29 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mambusao, Province of Capiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof March _ , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
