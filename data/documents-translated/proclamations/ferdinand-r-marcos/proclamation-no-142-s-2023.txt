MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 142

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 08 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIAONG, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, Claro M. Recto was born on 08 February 1890 in Tiaong,
Province of Quezon. A former Associate Justice of the Supreme Court and a former
Senator, he is also known to be the author of Republic Act No. 1425 or the Rizal
Law;

WHEREAS, on 08 February 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Tiaong,
Province of Quezon will commemorate the birth anniversary of Claro M. Recto;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tiaong be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 08 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tiaong, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
