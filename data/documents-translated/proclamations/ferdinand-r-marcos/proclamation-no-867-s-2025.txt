MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 867

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 22 May 2025, Thursday, the City of Gingoog, Province of
Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of the Lubi-Lubi Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog, Province
of Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19h day of AP*i! in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Executivé Secretary
