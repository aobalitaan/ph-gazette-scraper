MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 922

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF DUMALINAO, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Dumalinao, Province
of Zamboanga del Sur will mark the celebration of its 69" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dumalinao be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
16 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dumalinao, Province
of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
