MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 36

DECLARING MONDAY, 05 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, on 05 December 2022, Monday, the City of Palayan, Province of
Nueva Ecija, will mark the celebration of its 57" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government,

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 05 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan,
Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

LUCA B
