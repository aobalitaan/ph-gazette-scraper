MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 458

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CABADBARAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2024, Friday, the City of Cabadbaran, Province
of Agusan del Norte will mark the celebration of the Dagkot Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cabadbaran be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
02 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabadbaran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of january ; in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
