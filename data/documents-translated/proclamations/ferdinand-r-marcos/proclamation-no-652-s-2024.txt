MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.652

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 11 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUPI, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 11 September 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Tupi,
Province of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 71St Founding Anniversary
and the 15" Agten Tufi Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Tupi
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 11 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tupi, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 day of August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
