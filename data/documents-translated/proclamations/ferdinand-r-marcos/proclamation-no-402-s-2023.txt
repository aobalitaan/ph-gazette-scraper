MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 402

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HINOBA-AN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 20 November 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Hinoba-an,
Province of Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary
and the Huba-Huba Color Fest;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Hinoba-an be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 20 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Hinoba-an, Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
