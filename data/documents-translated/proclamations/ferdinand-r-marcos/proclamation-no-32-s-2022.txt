MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 32

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 OCTOBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LEMERY, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 25 October 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Lemery,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lemery be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 October 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Lemery, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisggenday of jyiy in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
