MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 903

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2025, Tuesday, the Province of Masbate will celebrate
the Bagat-Dagat Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th dayof May, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
