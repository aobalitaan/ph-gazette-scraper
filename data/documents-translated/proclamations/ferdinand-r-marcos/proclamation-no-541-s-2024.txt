MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 541

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TAYABAS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2024, Wednesday, the City of Tayabas, Province of
Quezon will mark the celebration of the Mayohan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tayabas
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tayabas,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
