MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 335

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONDRAGON, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN
SAMAR

WHEREAS, the formation of the pueblo of Mondragon from visitas of Guinay
and Marquivalo was approved through Royal Order No. 759 on 22 September 1883;

WHEREAS, on 22 September 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Mondragon,
Province of Northern Samar, will mark the celebration of its 140% Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mondragon be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
22 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mondragon,
Province of Northern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
