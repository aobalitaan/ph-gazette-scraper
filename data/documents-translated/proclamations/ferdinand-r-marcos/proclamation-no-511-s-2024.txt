BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 511

DECLARING THE 17™ DAY OF JULY OF EVERY YEAR AS
“NATIONAL CARDIOPULMONARY RESUSCITATION DAY”

WHEREAS, Section 15, Article Il of the Constitution mandates the State to
protect and promote the right to health of the people;

WHEREAS, under Section 30 of Republic Act (RA) No. 11223 or the “Universal
Health Care Act,” the Department of Health (DOH) shall strengthen national efforts in
providing a comprehensive and coordinated approach to health development with
emphasis on scaling up health promotion and preventive care;

WHEREAS, Section 2 of RA No. 10871 or the “Basic Life Support Training in
Schools Act,” provides that the State shall ensure that able-bodied citizens are
equipped with the necessary knowledge and basic skills to respond to certain health
emergencies;

WHEREAS, cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) is a lifesaving technique
useful in many emergencies, including heart attack or near drowning, in which an
individual's breathing or heartbeat has stopped;

WHEREAS, there is a need to instill and continuously promote health
consciousness among Filipinos by equipping them with the necessary information,
knowledge, behavior, and skills to respond to health emergencies, including bringing
CPR in every home as an emergency procedure; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all the executive departments, bureaus and offices and the
mandate to ensure the faithful execution of laws;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
do hereby declare the 17'" day of July of every year as “National Cardiopulmonary
Resuscitation Day.”

For this purpose, the DOH shall lead the planning, preparation, coordination,
organization, implementation, monitoring and evaluation of the initiatives and
