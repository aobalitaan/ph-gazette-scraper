MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 150

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 21 March 2023, Tuesday, the City of Calapan, Province of
Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its 25" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calapan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calapan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
