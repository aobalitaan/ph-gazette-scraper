MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 345

DECLARING SATURDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE
ORO

WHEREAS, on 25 November 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Maragusan,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maragusan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 25 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Maragusan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day ofSeptember, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
