MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 553

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAYOYAO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, on 01 June 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Mayoyao,
Province of Ifugao will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mayoyao be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mayoyao,
Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th dayof May , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
