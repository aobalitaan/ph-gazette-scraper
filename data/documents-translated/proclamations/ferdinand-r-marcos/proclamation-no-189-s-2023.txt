MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 189

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAMILING, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 08 May 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Camiling, Province
of Tarlac will mark the celebration of its town fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Camiling be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P, BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 08 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Camiling,
Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of March , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
