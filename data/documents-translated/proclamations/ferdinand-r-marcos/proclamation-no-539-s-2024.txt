MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 539

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PALAPAG, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 01 June 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Palapag,
Province of Northern Samar will commemorate the heroism and bravery of Agustin
Sumuroy, a native of Palapag who led the uprising against Spanish colonizers;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Palapag be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Palapag,
Province of Northern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
