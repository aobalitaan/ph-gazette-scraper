MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 38

DECLARING TUESDAY, 30 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MANDAUE, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 5519 which was approved on 21 June 1969
created the City of Mandaue;

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 586, series of 1969 designated 30 August 1969
as the formal organization of the government of the City of Mandaue;

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2022, Tuesday, the City of Mandaue will mark the
celebration of its 53° Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mandaue
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 30 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mandaue.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
