MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 696

DECLARING SATURDAY, 26 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ANGELES, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 26 October 2024, Saturday, the City of Angeles. Province of
Pampanga will mark the celebration of the Tigtigan Terakan Keng Dalan Festival, a
celebration of the city’s recovery from the Mt. Pinatubo eruption in June 1991;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Angeles be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 26 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Angeles,
Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
