MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 347

DECLARING THURSDAY, 05 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
SUR

WHEREAS, on 05 October 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Sta. Cruz,
Province of Davao del Sur will mark the celebration of its 139 Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Cruz be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 05 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta.
Cruz, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
