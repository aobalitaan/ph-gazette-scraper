MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1089

DECLARING SATURDAY, 29 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AURORA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 29 November 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Aurora,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur will mark the celebration of the Badak Festival or the
Adlaw sa Aurora;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aurora be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 29 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Aurora, Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisl2th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
