MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 274

DECLARING FRIDAY, 14 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF GUIHULNGAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 14 July 2023, Friday, the City of Guihulngan, Province of
Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Guihulngan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 14 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihulngan, Province
of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30thday of June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
