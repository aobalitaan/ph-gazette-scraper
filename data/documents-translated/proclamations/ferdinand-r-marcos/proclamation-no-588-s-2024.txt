MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 588

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Mauban, Province of
Quezon will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Mauban;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mauban be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
‘celebration; ‘

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,

15 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mauban, Province of
Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
