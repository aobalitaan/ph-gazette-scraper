MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 636

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAPITAN

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2024, Thursday, the City of Dapitan will celebrate its
annual fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dapitan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dapitan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

      

Executive Secretary
