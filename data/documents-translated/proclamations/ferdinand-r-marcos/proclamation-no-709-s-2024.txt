MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 709

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CULABA, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 16 October 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Culaba,
Province of Biliran will mark the celebration of its 71st Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Culaba be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Culaba, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th dayof October, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
(~_
USA BERSAMIN

Secretary
