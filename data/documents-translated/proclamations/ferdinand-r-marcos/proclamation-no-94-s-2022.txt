MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 94

DECLARING TUESDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF DINAGAT
ISLANDS

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Dinagat Islands will mark the celebration of its 334 Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 15 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Jose, Province of Dinagat Islands.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14tkiay of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
