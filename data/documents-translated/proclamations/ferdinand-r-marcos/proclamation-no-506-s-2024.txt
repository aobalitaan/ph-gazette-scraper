MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 506

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA CATALINA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Santa Catalina,
Province of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Town Fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santa Catalina be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santa
Catalina, Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nddayof Mach  , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
