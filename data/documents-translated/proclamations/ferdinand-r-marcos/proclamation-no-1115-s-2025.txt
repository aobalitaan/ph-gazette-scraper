MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1115

DECLARING SATURDAY, 31 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 31 January 2026, Saturday, the Municipality of Lian, Province
of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Liberation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Lian
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 31 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lian,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day ofDecember, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five .
