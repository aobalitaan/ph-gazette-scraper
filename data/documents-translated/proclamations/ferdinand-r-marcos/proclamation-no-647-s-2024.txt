MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. .647

DECLARING TUESDAY, 13 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGPET, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 13 August 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Magpet, Province
of Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 61St Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Magpet be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 13 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Magpet,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
