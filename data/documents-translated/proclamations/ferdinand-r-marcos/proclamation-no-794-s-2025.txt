MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 794

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ESCALANTE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2025, Friday, the City of Escalante, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 24" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Escalante
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
28 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Escalante, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of Febr,Yaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
