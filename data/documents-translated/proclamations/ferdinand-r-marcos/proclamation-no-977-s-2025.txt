MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 977

DECLARING MONDAY, 04 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN PASCUAL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 04 August 2025, Monday, the Municipality of San Pascual,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Pascual be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |; LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
04 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Pascual,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th dayof July, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
