MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 72

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN CARLOS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 04 November 2022, Friday, the City of San Carlos, Province
of Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its annual festival;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Carlos
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
04 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Carlos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14thday of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
