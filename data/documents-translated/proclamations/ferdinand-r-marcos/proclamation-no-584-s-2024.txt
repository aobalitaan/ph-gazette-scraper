MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 584

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MONCADA, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Moncada, Province
of Tarlac will mark the celebration of its 149 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Moncada be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,

01 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Moncada, Province of
Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof June _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
