MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 455

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)

DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 25 January 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Roxas,

Province of Oriental Mindoro will celebrate the SIPAG Festival and Feast of Patron
Sto. Nifio;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Roxas be given full opportunity to participate in the foregoing occasions and enjoy
their celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
, authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Roxas, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
