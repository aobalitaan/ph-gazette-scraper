MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 316

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, Josefa Llanes Escoda, founder of the Girl Scouts of ‘nS
Philippines was born in Dingras, Ilocos Norte on 20 September 1898;

WHEREAS, on 20 September 2023, Wednesday, the Province of Ilocos Norte
will commemorate the birth anniversary of Josefa Llanes Escoda;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ilocos
Norte be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
llocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
