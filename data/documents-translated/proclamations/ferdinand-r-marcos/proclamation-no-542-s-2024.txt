MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 542

DECLARING TUESDAY, 13 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TAYABAS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 13 August 2024, Tuesday, the City of Tayabas, Province of
Quezon will commemorate the anniversary of its independence from the Spanish
rule;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tayabas
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 13 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tayabas,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
