MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1021

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VICTORIA, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Victoria,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its 72"? Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Victoria
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by authority
of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Thursday, 18

September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Victoria, Province of
Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of September, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
