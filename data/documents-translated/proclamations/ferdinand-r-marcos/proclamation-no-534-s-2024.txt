MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 534

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CALACA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 23 May 2024, the City of Calaca, Province of Batangas will
mark the celebration of the birth anniversary of General Eleuterio Marasigan, a great
Calaquefio, patriot, and one of the leaders during the libertarian movement in the
Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calaca be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calaca, Province
of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
