MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 622

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF CALABANGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Calabanga, Province
of Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calabanga be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 15 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calabanga,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
