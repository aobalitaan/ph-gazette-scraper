MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 380

DECLARING TUESDAY, 05 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, on 05 December 2023, Tuesday, the City of Palayan, Province of
Nueva Ecija, will mark the celebration of its 58" Foundation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 05 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan,
Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
