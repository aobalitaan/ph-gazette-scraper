MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 535

DECLARING FRIDAY, 24 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF TRECE MARTIRES, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 24 May 2024, Friday, the City of Trece Martires, Province of
Cavite will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by.
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 24 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Trece Martires,
Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
