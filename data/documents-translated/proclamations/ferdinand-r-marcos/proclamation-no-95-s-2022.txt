MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 95

DECLARING THURSDAY, 17 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AROROY, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 17 November 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Aroroy,
Province of Masbate will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aroroy be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 17 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Aroroy, Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15tlay of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
