MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 950

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAPALONG, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Kapalong, Province
of Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of its 77! Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kapalong be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
01 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kapalong, Province
of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
