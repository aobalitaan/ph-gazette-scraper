MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDEN? OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 220

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 June 2023, Tuesday, the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of its 76 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be atfixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of May _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
