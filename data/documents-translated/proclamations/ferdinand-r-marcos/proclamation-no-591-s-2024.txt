MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 591

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 23 July 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Nabunturan,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 67 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Nabunturan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion 1 and enjoy the
- celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Tuesday, 23 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Nabunturan,
Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
