MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 554

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 11 June 2024, Tuesday, the Province of Rizal will mark the
celebration of its 1234 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Rizal
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 11 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
