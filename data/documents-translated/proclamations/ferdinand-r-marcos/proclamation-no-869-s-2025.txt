MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 869

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 23 July 2025, Wednesday, the City of Gingoog, Province of
Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of its 65" Charter Day/46" Kaliga Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 23 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set.my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st dayof April | inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

UCA BERSAMIN
eSecretary
