MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 70

DECLARING MONDAY, 07 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUENCA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 07 November 2022, Monday, the Municipality of Cuenca,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 146 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cuenca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 07 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Cuenca, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
