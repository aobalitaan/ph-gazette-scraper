MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 182

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAMALAYAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Pinamalayan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of the Bahaghari Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pinamalayan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Pinamalayan, Province of
Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th dayof March __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
