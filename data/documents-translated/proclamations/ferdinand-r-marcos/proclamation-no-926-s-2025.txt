MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 926

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SADANGA, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 25 June 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Sadanga,
Mountain Province will mark the celebration of its 62"4 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sadanga be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Sadanga, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
