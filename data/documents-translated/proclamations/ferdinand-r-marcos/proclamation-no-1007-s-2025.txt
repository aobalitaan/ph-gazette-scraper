MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1007

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CALBAYOG

WHEREAS, on 08 September 2025, Monday, the City of Calbayog will mark
the celebration of the Hadang Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calbayog
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 08 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calbayog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, the 3rd day ofSeptembein the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
