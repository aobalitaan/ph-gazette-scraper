MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1035

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 28 November 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Pifan, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 74" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pifan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
28 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pifan, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of Septemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
