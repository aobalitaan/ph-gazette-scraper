MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 915

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIM, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Maasim, Province
of Sarangani will mark the celebration of its 56° Founding Anniversary and the 18"
Kestebeng Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maasim be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maasim,
Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28thdayof May _ , inthe year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

CAS P. BERSAMIN
Executive Secretary
