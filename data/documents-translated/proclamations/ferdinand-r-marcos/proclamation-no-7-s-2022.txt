MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 07

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 19 July 2022, Tuesday, the City of Digos, Province of Davao
del Sur, will mark the celebration of the Padigosan Festival, the day Digos was
created as a Municipality;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Province of
Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
