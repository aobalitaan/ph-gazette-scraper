MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 60

DECLARING FRIDAY, 30 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MASBATE, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 30 September 2022, Friday, the City of Masbate, Provincg of
Masbate, will mark the celebration of its 22"¢ Cityhood Anniversary; .

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Masbate
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies; subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
30 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
