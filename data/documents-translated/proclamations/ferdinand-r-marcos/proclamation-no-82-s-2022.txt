MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 82

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2022, Wednesday. the Province of Benquet will
mark the celebration of its 122° Founding Anniversary,

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Benguet be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secratary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR.. do hereby declare
Wednesday, 23 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Dane in the City of Manila, this 20th day of October. in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
