MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 993

DECLARING TUESDAY, 09 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAIS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 09 September 2025, Tuesday, the City of Bais, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 57 Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bais be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration:

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 09 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bais.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
