MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1101

DECLARING MONDAY, 29 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN PEDRO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 29 December 2025, Monday, the City of San Pedro, Province
of Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 12" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pedro
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
29 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pedro, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this27th day of November jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
