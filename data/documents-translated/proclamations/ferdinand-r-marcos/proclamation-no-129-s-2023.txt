MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 129

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2023, Friday, the City of Bihan, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its 78'" Liberation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bifian be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
03 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bifian.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19thday of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
