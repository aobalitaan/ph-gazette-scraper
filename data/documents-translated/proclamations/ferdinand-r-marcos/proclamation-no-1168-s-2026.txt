MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1168

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGUILAR, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2026, Monday, the Municipality of Aguilar, Province
of Pangasinan will mark the celebration of the Ansakket Festival:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aguilar be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
16 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Aguilar, Province of
Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day offebruary, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
