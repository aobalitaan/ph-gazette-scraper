MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 497

DECLARING THURSDAY, 04 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 04 April 2024, Thursday, the City of Antipolo, Province of
Rizal will mark the celebration of its 26" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Antipolo
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 04 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Antipolo, Province
of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of Match , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
