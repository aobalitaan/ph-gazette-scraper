BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES .
PROCLAMATION NO. 775

DECLARING SATURDAY, 08 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIAONG, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, Claro M. Recto, a former Associate Justice of the Supreme Court,
former Senator and the author of the Rizal Law, was born on 08 February 1890 in
Tiaong, Quezon;

WHEREAS, on 08 February 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Tiaong,
Province of Quezon will commemorate the birth anniversary of Claro M. Recto;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tiaong be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 08 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tiaong,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
