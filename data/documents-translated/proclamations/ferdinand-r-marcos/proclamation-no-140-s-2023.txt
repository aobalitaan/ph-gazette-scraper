MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 140

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 11445, amending RA No. 9090, declared
February 1% of every year as Jose Maria Panganiban Day, in recognition of his
sacrifices and fight for humanitarian reforms for the country;

WHEREAS, on 01 February 2023, Wednesday, the Province of Camarines
Norte will mark the commemoration of the133" birth anniversary of Dr. Jose Maria
Panganiban;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Camarines Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 01 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Camarines Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
