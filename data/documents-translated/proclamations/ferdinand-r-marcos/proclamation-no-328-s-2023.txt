MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 328

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 30 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN JUAN

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2023, Wednesday, the City of San Juan will mark
the commemoration of Pinaglabanan Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Juan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 30 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Juan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
