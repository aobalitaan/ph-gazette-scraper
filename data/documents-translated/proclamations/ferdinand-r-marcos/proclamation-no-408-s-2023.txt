MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 408

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 20 December 2023, Wednesday, the City of Sipalay, Province
of Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 75" Founding Anniversary and
the Pasaway Handurawan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sipalay,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
