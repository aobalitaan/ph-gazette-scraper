MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 819

DECLARING FRIDAY, 14 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUKO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 14 March 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain
Province will mark the celebration of the Begnas di Bauko Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauko be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
14 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain
Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th — day of March, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
