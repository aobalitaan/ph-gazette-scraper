MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 510

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 03 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 03 April 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Rizal, Province
of Occidental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rizal be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 03 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rizal,
Province of Occidental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of March_, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
