BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 300

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCEL
OF LAND WHERE IT STANDS, LOCATED ALONG LACSON
STREET, BARANGAY MANDALAGAN, BACOLOD CITY, AS A
SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
CENTER), PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS
AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or
the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon
the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority (PEZA), |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the Republic of the
Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building, to be known as Lopue’s
Mandalagan IT Center, with a gross floor area of Five Thousand Seven Hundred
Eight square meters (5,708 m?), more or less, and the parcel of land where the
building stands, with an area of Three Thousand Nine Hundred Sixty-Three
square meters (3,963 m?), more or less, as a Special Economic Zone (Information
Technology Center), located along Lacson Street, Barangay Mandalagan, Bacolod
City, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules
and Regulations, PEZA Board of Directors Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as
amended by Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011) and 15-240 (s. 2015), and Resolution
No. 13-773 (s. 2013), as defined by the following technical description:

Lot 1424-B-2
Psd-06-085538
TCT No. 092-2015004452

1424-B, Psd-36833) situated in the Barangay of Mandalagan, City of
Bacolod, Island of Negros.

Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2 by Lot 1424-A, Psd-36833; on the
SE., along line 2-3 by Lot 1424-B-1 of the subdivision survey; on the
SW., along line 3-4 by Lot 1423; on the NW., along lines 4-5-6-1 by Lot
1459, both Bacolod Cad. 39.
