MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 739

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF DINAGAT ISLANDS

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2024, Friday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Dinagat Islands will mark the celebration of its 35" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Jose be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Dinagat Islands.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12thday of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
