MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 504

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF DOLORES, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 11 April 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Dolores, Province
of Quezon will mark the celebration of its 189" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dolores be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 11 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dolores,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
