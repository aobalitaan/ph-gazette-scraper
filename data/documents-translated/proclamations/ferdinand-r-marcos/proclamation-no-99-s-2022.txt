MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 99

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Malvar was founded by virtue of Executive
Order No. 56 which was issued on 16 December 1918;

WHEREAS, on 16 December 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malvar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
16 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
