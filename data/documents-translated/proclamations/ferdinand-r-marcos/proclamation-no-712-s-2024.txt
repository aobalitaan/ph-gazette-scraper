MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 712

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2024, Friday, the Province of Oriental Mindoro
will mark the celebration of its 74° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Oriental
Mindoro be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by

authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of Octobe, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

  
 
 

 
  

. BERSAMIN
ecutive Secretary
