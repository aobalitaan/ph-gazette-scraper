MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 308

DECLARING THE MONTH OF OCTOBER OF EVERY YEAR AS
“COMMUNICATIONS MONTH” AND THE 1174 OF OCTOBER OF
EVERY YEAR AS THE ANNIVERSARY OF THE PRESIDENTIAL
COMMUNICATIONS OFFICE

WHEREAS, Section 24, Article Il of the Constitution provides that the State
recognizes the vital role of communication and information in nation-building;

WHEREAS, on 11 October 1942, then President Manuel L. Quezon issued
Executive Order (EO) No. 5-W, creating the Office of Special Services (OSS), a
section under the Office of the President (OP), whose functions included the collection,
collation, organization, and dissemination of information about the Philippines and the
Commonwealth Government;

WHEREAS, through various Presidential issuances promulgated over the
years, the OSS has evolved to eventually become the Office of the Press Secretary
(OPS), tasked primarily to handle information dissemination and the communications
operations of the OP;

WHEREAS, EO No. 11 (s. 2022) renamed the OPS as the Presidential
Communications Office (PCO);

WHEREAS, under EO No. 11 and EO No. 16 (s. 2023), the PCO is responsible
for the crafting, formulation, development, enhancement, and coordination of the
messaging system of the Executive branch and the OP;

WHEREAS, the Administration aims to provide true, accurate, and relevant
information regarding its policies, priority programs, and projects to nurture a well-
informed and enlightened citizenry through appropriate media; and

WHEREAS, an effective communication mechanism is vital in the engagement
and involvement of the citizenry and the media industry in enriching the quality of
public discourse on all matters of governance;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
do hereby declare the month of October of every year as “Communications Month”
and the 11" of October of every year as the Anniversary of the PCO.

For this purpose, the PCO shall lead the observance of the Communications
Month and the Anniversary of the PCO, and identify the programs, projects and
activities for the yearly celebration thereof.

All other agencies and instrumentalities of the National Government, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations and state universities and colleges, are
hereby directed, and all local government units, non-government organizations, as
well as the private sector, are hereby encouraged, to actively participate in the
abovementioned observance and render the necessary support to the PCO for the
effective implementation of this Proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof August , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.
