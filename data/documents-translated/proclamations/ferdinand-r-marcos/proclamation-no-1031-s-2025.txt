MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1031

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 17 October 2025, Friday, the Province of Negros Oriental will
mark the celebration of the Buglasan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Negros
Oriental be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
17 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF-, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 1 thday of Septemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
