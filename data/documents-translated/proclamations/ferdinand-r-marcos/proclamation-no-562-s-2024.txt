MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 562

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIM, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Maasim, Province of
Sarangani will mark the celebration of its 55" Founding Anniversary and the 17%
Kestebeng Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maasim be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
21 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maasim, Province of
Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May , in the year of Our Lord, -
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
