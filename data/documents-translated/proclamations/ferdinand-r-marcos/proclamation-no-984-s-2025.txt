MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 984

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KUMALARANG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 29 August 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Kumalarang,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur will mark the celebration of the Padul-Os Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kumalarang be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
29 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kumalarang,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24thday of July _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
