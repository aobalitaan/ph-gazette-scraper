MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 183

DECLARING TUESDAY, 02 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 02 May 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Bauan, Province
of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Sublian Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 02 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauan,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
