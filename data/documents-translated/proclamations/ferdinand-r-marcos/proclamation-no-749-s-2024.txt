MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 749

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MEYCAUAYAN, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 10 December 2024, Tuesday, the City of Meycauayan,
Province of Bulacan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Meycauayan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 10 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Meycauayan,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

   
 

Executive Secretary
