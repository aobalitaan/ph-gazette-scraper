MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1085

DECLARING SATURDAY, 13 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NARVACAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on 13 December 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Narvacan,
Province of Ilocos Sur will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Narvacan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 13 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Narvacan, Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this7th day of November , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
