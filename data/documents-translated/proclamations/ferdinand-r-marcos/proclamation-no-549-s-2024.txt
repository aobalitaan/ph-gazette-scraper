MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 549

DECLARING THURSDAY, 30 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ESCALANTE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 30 May 2024, Thursday, the City of Escalante, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of the Manlambus Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Escalante
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 30 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Escalante,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
