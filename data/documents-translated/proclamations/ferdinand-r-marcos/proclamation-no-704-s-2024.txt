MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 704

DECLARING MONDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN CARLOS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 04 November 2024, Monday, the City of San Carlos, Province
of Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its annual festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Carlos
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
04 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Carlos, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this sth day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
