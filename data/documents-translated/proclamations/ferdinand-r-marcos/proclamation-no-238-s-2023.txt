MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 238

DECLARING TUESDAY, 06 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 06 June 2023, Tuesday, the Province of Zamboanga del
Norte will mark the celebration of its 71St Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Norte be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and
enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 06 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga
del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th dayof May _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
