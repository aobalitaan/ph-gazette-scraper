MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 461

DECLARING TUESDAY, 13 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PARANAQUE

WHEREAS, on 13 February 2024, Tuesday, the City of Parafiaque will mark
the celebration of its 26° Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Parafaque
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
* Tuesday, 13 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Parahaque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
