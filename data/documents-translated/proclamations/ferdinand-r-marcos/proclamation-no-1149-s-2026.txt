MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1149

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2026, Thursday, the City of Zamboanga will mark
the celebration of its 89" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Zamboanga
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rdday of January , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
