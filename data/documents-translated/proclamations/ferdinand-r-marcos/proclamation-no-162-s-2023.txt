MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 162

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYUG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 17 March 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Tayug, Province of
Pangasinan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tayug be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
17 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tayug, Province of
Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
