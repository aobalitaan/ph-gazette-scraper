MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 628

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 14 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAWAB, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 14 August 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Mawab,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mawab be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 14 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mawab, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thday of July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
