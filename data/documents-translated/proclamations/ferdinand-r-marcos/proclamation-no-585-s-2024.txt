MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 585

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MAGSAYSAY, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Magsaysay, Province
of Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Magsaysay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,

01 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Magsaysay, Province
of Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of June _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

 
     

P. BERSAMIN
ive Secretary
