MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIF'PINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 233

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, the Province of Tarlac will mark the commemoration of its
Founding Anniversary to be known as the Araw ng Lalawigan ng Tarlac on 28 May
2023;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Tarlac to fully participate
in the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlalawigan requested that 26 May 2023, Friday,
be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 28 May 2023 which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Tarlac be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
26 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

   

Executive Secretary

   

   
 

the President
