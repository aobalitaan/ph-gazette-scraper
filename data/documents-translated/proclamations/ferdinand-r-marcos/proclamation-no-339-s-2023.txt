MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 339

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IROSIN, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON

WHEREAS, on 29 September 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Irosin,
Province of Sorsogon will mark the celebration of the Paray Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
lrosin be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
29 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Irosin,
Province of Sorsogon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
