MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 580

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUIRINO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Quirino, Province
of Isabela will mark the celebration of its 57 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Quirino be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 17 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Quirino,
Province of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
