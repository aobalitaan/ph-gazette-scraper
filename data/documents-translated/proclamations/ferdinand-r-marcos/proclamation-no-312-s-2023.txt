MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 312

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 13 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVER, PROVINCE OF
SURIGAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 126 which was approved on 13 September
1955 created the Municipality of Claver;

WHEREAS, on 13 September 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Claver,
Province of Surigao del Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary
(Adlaw Nan Claver);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Claver be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 13 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Claver, Province of Surigao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of August _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
