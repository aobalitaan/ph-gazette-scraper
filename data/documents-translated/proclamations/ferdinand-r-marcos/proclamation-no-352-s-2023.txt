MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 352

DECLARING THURSDAY, 05 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DEL GALLEGO, PROVINCE OF
CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 05 October 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Del Gallego,
Province of Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Del Gallego be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 05 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Del
Gallego, Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day ofOc tober, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
