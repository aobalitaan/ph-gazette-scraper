MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 05

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Mauban, Province of
Quezon, will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Mauban;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mauban be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mauban, Province of
Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this gtnh day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
