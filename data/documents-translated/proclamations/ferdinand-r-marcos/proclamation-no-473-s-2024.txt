MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 473

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 10 March 2024, the City of Tanauan, Province of Batangas
will mark the celebration of its 23" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tanauan to fully participate in
the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 11 March 2024, Monday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 10 March 2024 which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanauan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 11 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanauan,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
