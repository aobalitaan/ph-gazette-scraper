MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1072

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAKE SEBU, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Lake Sebu,
Province of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 43°¢ Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Lake
Sebu be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 11 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lake
Sebu, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

 

he President
