MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 937

DECLARING TUESDAY, 15 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Mauban, Province
of Quezon will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Mauban;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mauban be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 15 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mauban,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
