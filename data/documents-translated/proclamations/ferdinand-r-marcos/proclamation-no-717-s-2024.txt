' MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 717

DECLARING SATURDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2024, Saturday, the Province of Benguet will
mark the celebration of its 124" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Benguet
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 23 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd dayof October , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
