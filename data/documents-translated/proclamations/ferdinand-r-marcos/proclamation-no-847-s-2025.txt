MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 847

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 21 April 2025, Monday, the City of Calamba, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its 24 Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
21 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba, Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25thday of March , jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
