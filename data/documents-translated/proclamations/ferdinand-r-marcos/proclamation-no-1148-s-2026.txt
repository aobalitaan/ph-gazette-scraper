MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1148

DECLARING SATURDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANZA, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2026, Saturday, the Municipality of Tanza,
Province of Cavite will mark the celebration of its 112" Araw ng Tanza;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanza be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 28 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tanza,
Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rday of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
