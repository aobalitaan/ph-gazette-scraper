MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 444

DECLARING SATURDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2024, Saturday, the City of Bifian, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its 79'" Liberation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bifian be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 03 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bifian.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
