MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1029

DECLARING TUESDAY, 14 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VICTORIA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 14 October 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Victoria,
Province of Laguna will mark the celebration of its 76 Founding Anniversary and the
24" Itik Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Victoria be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 14 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Victoria,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 thday of Septemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
