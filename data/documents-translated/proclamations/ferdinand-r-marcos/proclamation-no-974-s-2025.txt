MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 974

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGUILAR, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 16 July 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Aguilar, Province
of Pangasinan will mark the celebration of its 220'" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aguilar be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Aguilar,
Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th — day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
