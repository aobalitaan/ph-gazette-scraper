MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 476

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Bongabong,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of the Sulyog Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bongabong be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bongabong.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day ofFebruary, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
