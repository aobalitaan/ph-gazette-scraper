MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 175

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF NORALA, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 March 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Norala, Province of
South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Norala be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
10 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Norala, Province of
South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
