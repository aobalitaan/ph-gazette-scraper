MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 948

* DECLARING THURSDAY, 10 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 10 July 2025, Thursday, the City of Santa Rosa, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its 21st Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa Rosa
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 10 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29d dayof July _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
