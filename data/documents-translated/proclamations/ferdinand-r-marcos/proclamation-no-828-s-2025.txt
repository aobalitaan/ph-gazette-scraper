MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 828

DECLARING SATURDAY, 29 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINANGONAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 29 March 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Binangonan,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of its 125° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Binangonan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 29 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Binangonan, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this |= 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
