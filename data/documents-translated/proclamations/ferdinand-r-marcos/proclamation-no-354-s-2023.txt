MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 354

DECLARING MONDAY, 09 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, the City of Koronadal, Province of South Cotabato will mark the
celebration of its 23" Charter Anniversary on 08 October 2023;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Koronadal to fully participate in
the occasion, the Office of the Vice Mayor requested that 09 October 2023, Monday,
be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 08 October 2023 which falls on
a Sunday;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 09 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
