MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 457

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2024, Friday, the Province of Ilocos Norte will
mark the celebration of its 206"" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ilocos
Norte be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
02 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January ; in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
