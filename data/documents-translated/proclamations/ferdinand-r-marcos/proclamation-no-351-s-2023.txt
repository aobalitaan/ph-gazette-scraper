MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 351

DECLARING FRIDAY, 27 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MATI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Mati, Province of Davao Oriental celebrates the
Sambuokan Festival every last weekend of October in commemoration of its date of
establishment as a municipality before eventually becoming a city;

WHEREAS, to give their constituents the opportunity to participate in the
activities and celebrate as one, the Office of the Mayor requested that 27 October
2023, Friday, be declared a special (non-working) day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mati be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
27 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mati, Province of Davao
Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of September in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
