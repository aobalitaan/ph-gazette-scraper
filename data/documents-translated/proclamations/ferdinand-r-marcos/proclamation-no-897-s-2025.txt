MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 897

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2025, Tuesday, the City of Lapu-Lapu will mark the
celebration of its 64” Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lapu-Lapu
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 June 2028, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lapu-Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th dayof May , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
