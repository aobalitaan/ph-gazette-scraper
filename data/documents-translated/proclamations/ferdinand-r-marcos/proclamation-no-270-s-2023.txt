MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 270

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF CABADBARAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 28 July 2023, Friday, the City of Cabadbaran, Province of
Agusan del Norte will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cabadbaran be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
28 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabadbaran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of June __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
