MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 558

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2024, Monday, the City of Lapu-Lapu will mark the
celebration of its 63 Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lapu-Lapu
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the -President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 17 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lapu-Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
