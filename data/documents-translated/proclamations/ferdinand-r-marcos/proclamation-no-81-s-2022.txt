BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 31

DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ITOGON, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 22 Novernber 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of {togon,
Province of Benguet will mark the celebration of its 122° Founding Anniversary:

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
ltogon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government:

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 22 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
ltogon, Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th dayof october , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
