MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 2°

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINDOY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2025; Tuesday, the Municipality of Bindoy, Province of
Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bindoy be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bindoy,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th dayof May, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
