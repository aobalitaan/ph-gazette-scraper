MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 393

DECLARING SATURDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. NINO, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sto. Nifio, Province of South Cotabato will be
celebrating its 43 Founding Anniversary and the 24" Hinublag Festival on 23
December 2023;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sto. Nifio be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 23 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sto.
Nifio, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
