MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 361

DECLARING THURSDAY, 09 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 09 November 2023, Thursday, the Province of Eastern Samar
will mark the celebration of its 58" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Eastern Samar be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 09 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Eastern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
