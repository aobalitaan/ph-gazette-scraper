MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 169

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TANGUB, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2023, Tuesday, the City of Tangub, Province of
Misamis Occidental will mark the celebration of its 55" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 28 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tangub,
Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
