MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 1094

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. NINO, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Sto. Nifo,
Province of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 45'" Founding Anniversary
and the 26" Hinublag Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Sto.
Nifio be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 23 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sto.
Nifio, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12 thday of November, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
