MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 377

DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 07 December 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Bongabong,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bongabong be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 07 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
