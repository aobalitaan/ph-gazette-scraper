MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 596

DECLARING TUESDAY, 06 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MUNICIPALITY OF PILAR, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON

WHEREAS, on 06 August 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Pilar, Province of
Sorsogon, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pilar
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 06 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pilar,
Province of Sorsogon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
