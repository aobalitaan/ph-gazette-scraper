MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 439

DECLARING THURSDAY, 01 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 11445, amending RA No. 9090, declared
February 1% of every year as Jose Maria Panganiban Day, in recognition of his
sacrifices and fight for humanitarian reforms for the country;

WHEREAS, on 01 February 2024, Thursday, the Province of Camarines
Norte will mark the commemoration of Jose Maria Panganiban Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Camarines Norte be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy
the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 01 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Camarines Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
