MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 603

DECLARING SATURDAY, 10 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 10 August 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon will commemorate the birth of Lorenzo M. Tafiada Sr.;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and honor the memory
of Senator Lorenzo M. Tafiada Sr.;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 10 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thdayof June _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
