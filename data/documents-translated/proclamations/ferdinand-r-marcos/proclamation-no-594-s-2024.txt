MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 594

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 26 July 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Ipil, Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay will mark the celebration of its 75" Araw ng Ipil:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Ipil
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
26 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ipil, Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this13th dayof June , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
