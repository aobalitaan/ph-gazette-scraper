MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1091

DECLARING TUESDAY, 09 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BURGOS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 09 December 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Burgos,
Province of Ilocos Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Burgos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 09 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Burgos, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this12th day ofNovember, ijn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
