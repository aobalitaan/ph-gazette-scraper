MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.1176

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYUG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 17 March 2026, Tuesday, the Municipality of Tayug, Province
of Pangasinan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tayug be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tayug,
Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thday of February in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

  
   

  

Acting Exegutive Secretary

  
 
 

the President
