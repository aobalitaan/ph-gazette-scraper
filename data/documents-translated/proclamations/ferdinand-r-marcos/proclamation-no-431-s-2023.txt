MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 431

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2024, Friday, the Province of Ilocos Sur will mark
the celebration of its Founding Anniversary (Kannawidan Festival);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ilocos
Sur be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
02 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three .
