MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 389

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2023, Saturday, the City of Bayawan, Province
of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its 23" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Bayawan to fully participate in
the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Bayawan requested that 22
December 2023, Friday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 23
December 2023 which falls on a Saturday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayawan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
22 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan, Province of
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
