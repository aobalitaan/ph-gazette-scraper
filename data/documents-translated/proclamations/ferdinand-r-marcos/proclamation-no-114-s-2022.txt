MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 114

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 JANUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CADIZ, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 26 January 2023, Thursday, the City of Cadiz, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 49! Dinagsa Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 January 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two

By authority of the President:

CAS P. BERSAMIN
