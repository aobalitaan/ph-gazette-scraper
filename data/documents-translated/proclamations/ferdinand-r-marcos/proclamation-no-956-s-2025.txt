MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 956

DECLARING SATURDAY, 30 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ARAKAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Arakan, Province
of Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 34°" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Arakan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 30 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Arakan,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof July  , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
