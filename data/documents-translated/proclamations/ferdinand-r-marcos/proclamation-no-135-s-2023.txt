MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 135

DECLARING THURSDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2023, Thursday, the Province of llocos Norte will
mark the celebration of its 205" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ilocos
Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 02 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Ilocos
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
