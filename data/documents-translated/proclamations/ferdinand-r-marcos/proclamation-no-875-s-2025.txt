MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 875

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 26 May 2025, Monday, the City of Baliwag, Province of
Bulacan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Baliwag be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
26 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Baliwag, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
