MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 976

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF SANTIAGO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2025, Friday, the City of Santiago, Province of Isabela
will mark the celebration of the Aggaw na Naturales Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago, Province of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
