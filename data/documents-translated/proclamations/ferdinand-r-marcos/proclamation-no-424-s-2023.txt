MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 424

DECLARING SATURDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2024, Saturday, the City of Cabanatuan,
Province of Nueva Ecija will mark the celebration of its 74 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cabanatuan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 03 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of Decenibeu, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three .
