MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 293

DECLARING TUESDAY, 15 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIGA, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, on 15 August 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Viga, Province
of Catanduanes will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Viga;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Viga be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 15 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Viga,
Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18thdayof July , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
