MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 448

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 19 February 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Taytay,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Pasasalamat: HAMAKA
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taytay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 19 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Taytay, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th dayof January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
