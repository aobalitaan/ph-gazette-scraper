MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 329

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 08 September 2023, Friday, the City of Digos, Province of
Davao del Sur will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary (Araw ng Digos);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion.and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
08 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Province of
Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of August, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
