MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 843

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA CATALINA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Santa Catalina,
Province of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Town Fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santa Catalina be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santa Catalina,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this21st dayof March _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
