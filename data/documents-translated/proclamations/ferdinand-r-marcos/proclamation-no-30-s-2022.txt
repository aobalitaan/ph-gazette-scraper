MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 30

DECLARING SATURDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLOMOLOK, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 September 2022, Saturday, the Municipality of Polomolok,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 65" Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Polomolok be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 10 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Polomolok, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 53,, day of July ° in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
