MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 648

DECLARING FRIDAY, 30 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISULAN, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Isulan, Province of
Sultan Kudarat will mark the celebration of its 67" Founding Anniversary and the 11‘
Hamungaya Festival:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Isulan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
30 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Isulan, Province of
Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 day of August _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
