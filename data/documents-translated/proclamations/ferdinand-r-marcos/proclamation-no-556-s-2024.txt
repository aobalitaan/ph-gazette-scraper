MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 556

DECLARING SATURDAY, 15 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ANGELES, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, the volcanic eruption of Mt. Pinatubo on 15 June 1991, the
second-largest volcanic eruption in the 20 century, caused unprecedented
devastation and destruction in Angeles City and other parts of Pampanga;

WHEREAS, to enable Angelefios to commemorate the 33" anniversary of the
Mt. Pinatubo eruption and look back on how they rose from this adversity, the Office
of the Mayor requested that 15 June 2024 be declared a special (non-working) day
in the City of Angeles;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Angeles
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 15 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Angeles, Province
of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thday of May __, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
