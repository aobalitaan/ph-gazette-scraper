MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 438

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TARLAC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, the City of Tarlac, Province of Tarlac will mark the celebration of
the 8 Kaisa Festival on 20 January 2024;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tarlac to fully participate in the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 19 January 2024, Friday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 20 January which falls on a
Saturday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
19 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac, Province of
Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
