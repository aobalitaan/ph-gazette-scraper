MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 806

DECLARING FRIDAY, 07 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE ISLAND GARDEN CITY OF SAMAL, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2025, Friday, the Island Garden City of Samal,
Province of Davao del Norte, will mark the celebration of its 27" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Island Garden City
of Samal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
07 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Island Garden City of Samal,
Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
