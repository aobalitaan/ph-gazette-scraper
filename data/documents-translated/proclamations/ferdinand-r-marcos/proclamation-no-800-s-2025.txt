MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 800

DECLARING FRIDAY, 07 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2025, Friday, the City of Tagum, Province of Davao
del Norte will mark the celebration of the 27 Araw ng Tagum;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagum be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
07 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tagum, Province of Davao
del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day ofFebruary, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
