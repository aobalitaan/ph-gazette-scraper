MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 856

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 14 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PULILAN, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 14 May 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Pulilan, Province
of Bulacan will mark the celebration of the Kneeling Carabao Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pulilan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare 14 May
2025, Wednesday, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pulilan,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10thdayof April , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
