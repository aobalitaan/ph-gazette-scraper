MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 416

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 10 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 January 2024, Wednesday, the City Koronadal, Province
of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 84 Founding Anniversary and the
Hinugyaw Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 10 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day ofDecember , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Thitee.

By authority of the President:
