MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1177

DECLARING THURSDAY, 19 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWAYAN, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2026, Thursday, the Municipality of Kawayan,
Province of Biliran will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kawayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 19 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kawayan,
Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

   
 

President
