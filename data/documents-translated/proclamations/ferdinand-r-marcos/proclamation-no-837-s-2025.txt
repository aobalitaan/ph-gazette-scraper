MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 837

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF PINAMALAYAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Pinamalayan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of the Bahaghari Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pinamalayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pinamalayan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this20th dayofMarch _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
