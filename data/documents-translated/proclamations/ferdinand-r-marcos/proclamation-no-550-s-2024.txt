MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 550

DECLARING THURSDAY, 06 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 06 June 2024, Thursday, the Province of Zamboanga del
Norte will mark the celebration of the 72"4 Araw ng Zamboanga del Norte;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of

Zamboanga del Norte be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and
enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Thursday, 06 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga
del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st dayof May , inthe year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
