MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 607

DECLARING FRIDAY, 30 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ARAKAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Arakan, Province of
Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 33"¢ Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Arakan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
30 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Arakan, Province
of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th dayof June _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
