MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 941

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF MABALACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 21 July 2025, Monday, the City of Mabalacat, Province of
Pampanga will mark the celebration of its 13 Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabalacat
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
21 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat, Province of
Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17thday of June | in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
