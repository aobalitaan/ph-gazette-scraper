MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1040

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIBUTU, PROVINCE OF TAWI-TAWI

WHEREAS, on 21 October 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Sibutu, Province
of Tawi-Tawi will mark the celebration of its creation as a municipality;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sibutu be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sibutu,
Province of Tawi-Tawi.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this16 th day of September in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
