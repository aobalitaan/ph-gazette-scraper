MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 967

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 13 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGPET, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 13 August 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Magpet,
Province of Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 62"? Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Magpet be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 13 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Magpet, Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thdayof July _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
