MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 658

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 02 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOLANO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA

WHEREAS, on 02 October 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Solano,
Province of Nueva Vizcaya will mark the celebration of Solano Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Solano be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 02 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Solano, Province Nueva Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 06g dayof August  , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
