MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1016

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF TAWI-TAWI

WHEREAS, the Province of Tawi-Tawi will mark the celebration of its 52"¢
Anniversary on 27 September 2025, which falls on a Saturday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Tawi-Tawi to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Governor requested that 23 September
2025, Tuesday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 27 September
2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Tawi-
Tawi be given full opportunity to participate and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 23 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Tawi-
Tawi.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of Septembeip the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
