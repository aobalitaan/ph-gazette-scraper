MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1146

DECLARING SATURDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SARRAT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2026, Saturday, the Municipality of Sarrat,
Province of Ilocos Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sarrat be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 28 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sarrat,
Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

Officg of the President
MALACANAING RECORDS OFFICE
