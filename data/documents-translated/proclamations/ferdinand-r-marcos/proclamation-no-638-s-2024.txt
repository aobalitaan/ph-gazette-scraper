MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 638

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLOMOLOK, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 September 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Polomolok,
Province of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of the 21t Flomlok Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Polomolok be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 10 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Polomolok, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 g4,, day of July > in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
