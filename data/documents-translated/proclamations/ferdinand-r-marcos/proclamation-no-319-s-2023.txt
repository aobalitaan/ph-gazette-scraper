MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 319

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 30 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISULAN, PROVINCE OF SULTAN
KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Isulan,
Province of Sultan Kudarat, will mark the celebration of its 66" Founding Anniversary
and the 10 Hamungaya Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Isulan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 30 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Isulan, Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
