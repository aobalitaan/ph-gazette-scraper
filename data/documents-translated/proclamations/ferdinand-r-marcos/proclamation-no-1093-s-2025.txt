MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1093

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2025, Tuesday, the City of Bayawan, Province of
Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its 25"" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayawan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 23 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this!2th day ofNovember | jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
