MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 855

DECLARING SATURDAY, 26 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 26 April 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Rizal, Province of
Cagayan will mark the celebration of its Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rizal be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 26 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rizal,
Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

V. TOLENTINO-NAVA
DIRECTOR IV
