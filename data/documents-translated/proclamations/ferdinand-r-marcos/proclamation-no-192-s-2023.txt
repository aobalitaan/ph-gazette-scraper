MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 192

DECLARING THURSDAY, 01 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAYOYAO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, on 01 June 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Mayoyao,
Province of Ifugao will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mayoyao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 01 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mayoyao,
Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thday of March _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
