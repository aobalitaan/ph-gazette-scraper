MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 872

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MONCADA, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 02 May 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Moncada, Province of
Tarlac will mark the celebration of its 1518t Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Moncada be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
02 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Moncada, Province of
Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th — day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
