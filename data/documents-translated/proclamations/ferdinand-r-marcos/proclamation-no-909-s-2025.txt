MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 909

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FRANCISCO, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL
SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of San Francisco,
Province of Agusan del Sur will mark the celebration of the Araw ng San Francisco;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Francisco be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Francisco, Province of Agusan del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thdayof May _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
