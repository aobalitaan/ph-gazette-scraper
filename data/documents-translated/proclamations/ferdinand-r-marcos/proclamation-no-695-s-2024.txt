MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 695

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 October 2024, Friday, the Province of Negros Oriental will
mark the celebration of the Buglasan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Negros
Oriental be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of Septettbein the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
