MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1099

DECLARING FRIDAY, 12 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF GENERAL TRIAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 12 December 2025, Friday, the City of General Trias, Province
of Cavite will mark the celebration of its 10 Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Trias be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
12 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of General Trias, Province
of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this26th day of November in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
