MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 836

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PLARIDEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 23 April 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Plaridel,
Province of Bulacan will commemorate the “Battle of Quingua’”;

WHEREAS, the Battle of Quingua was fought on 23 April 1899 in Plaridel,
Bulacan, wherein the Filipino forces were under the command of General Gregorio del
Pilar, and was one of the most significant victories of the Filipinos in the Philippine-
American war;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Plaridel be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 23 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Plaridel,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of March ___, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
