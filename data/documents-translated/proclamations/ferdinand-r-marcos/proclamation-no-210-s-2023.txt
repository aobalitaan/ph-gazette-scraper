MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 210

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUCBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Lucban, Province
of Quezon will mark the celebration of its Pahiyas Festival 2023;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lucban be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 15 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lucban,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
