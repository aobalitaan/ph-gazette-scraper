MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 797

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADRE BURGOS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 17 February 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Padre Burgos,
Province of Quezon will mark the celebration of Padre Burgos Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Padre Burgos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
17 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Padre Burgos,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
