MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1167

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 18 March 2026, Wednesday, the Province of Masbate will mark
the celebration of its 125"" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 tpday of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
