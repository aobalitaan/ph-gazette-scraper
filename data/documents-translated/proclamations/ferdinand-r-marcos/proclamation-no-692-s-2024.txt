MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 692

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIGCAWAYAN, PROVINCE OF NORTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Pigcawayan, Province of North Cotabato will
mark the celebration of its 718 Founding Anniversary on 20 October 2024, which falls
on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Pigcawayan to fully
participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan of Pigcawayan requested that 21
October 2024, Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 20
October 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pigcawayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
21 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pigcawayan,
Province of North Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of september: in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

UGA
