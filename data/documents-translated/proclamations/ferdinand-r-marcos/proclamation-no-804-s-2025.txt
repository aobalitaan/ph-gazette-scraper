MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 804

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 March 2025, Monday, the City of Tabaco, Province of Albay
will mark the celebration of its Charter Day and its 24'" Cityhood Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
24 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco, Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF-, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this12th day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
