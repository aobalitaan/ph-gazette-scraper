MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 74

DECLARING SATURDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2022, Saturday, the City of Valenzuela will mark
the celebration of its 399" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Valenzuela
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
12 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
