MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 325

DECLARING THURSDAY, 21 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MATEO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 21 September 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of San Mateo,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of its 4518 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Mateo be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 21 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
San Mateo, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th dayof August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
