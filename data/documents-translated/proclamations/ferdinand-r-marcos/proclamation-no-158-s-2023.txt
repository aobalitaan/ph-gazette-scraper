MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 158

DECLARING THURSDAY, 02 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 02 March 2023, Thursday, the Province of La Union will mark
the celebration of its 173 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of La
Union be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 02 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
