MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 413

DECLARING SATURDAY, 06 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF OROQUIETA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 06 January 2024, Saturday, the City of Oroquieta, Province of
Misamis Occidental will mark the celebration of its 54"" Charter Anniversary and the
94th Anniversary as the capital of the Province of Misamis Occidental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Oroquieta
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 06 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Oroquieta,
Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day ofNovember , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
