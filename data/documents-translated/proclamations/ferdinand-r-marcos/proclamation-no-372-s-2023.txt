MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 372

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal will be celebrating its
417" Foundation Day and the 13" Hane Festival on 10 November 2023,

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
10 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tanay,

Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
