MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 518

DECLARING MONDAY, 06 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTIAGO

WHEREAS, on 05 May 2024, the City of Santiago will mark the celebration of
its 30" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Santiago to fully participate in
the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 06 May 2024, Monday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 05 May 2024 which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 06 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th dayof April, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
