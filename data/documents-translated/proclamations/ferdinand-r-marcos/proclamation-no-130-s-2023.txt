MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

“BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 130

DECLARING THURSDAY, 09 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAGBAYAN, PROVINCE OF BOHOL

WHEREAS, on 09 February 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Sagbayan,
Province of Bohol will mark the celebration of its 74" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sagbayan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 09 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Sagbayan, Province of Bohol.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thday of January , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

 

Office of the President
