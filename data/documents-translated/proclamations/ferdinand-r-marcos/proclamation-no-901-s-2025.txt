MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 901

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BAYUGAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2025, Saturday, the City of Bayugan, Province of
Agusan del Sur will mark the celebration of its 18" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayugan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayugan, Province
of Agusan del Sur. :

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th dayof May _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
