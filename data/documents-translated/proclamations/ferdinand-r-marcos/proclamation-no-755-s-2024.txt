MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 755

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 11 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PUGO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 11 December 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Pugo,
Province of La Union will mark the celebration of its 113" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pugo be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 11 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pugo, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
