MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 307

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, the City of Davao observes the celebration of the Kadayawan
Festival in the month of August;

WHEREAS, on 18 August 2023, Friday, the City of Davao will mark the
celebration of the Indigenous People’s Day in relation to the Kadayawan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 18 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
