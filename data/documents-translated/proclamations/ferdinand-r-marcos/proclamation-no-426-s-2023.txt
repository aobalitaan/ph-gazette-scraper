MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 426

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF RIZAL, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 19 December 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Rizal,
Province of Laguna will mark the celebration of its 105" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rizal be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Rizal, Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
