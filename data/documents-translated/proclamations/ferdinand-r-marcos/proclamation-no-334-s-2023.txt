MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 334

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2023, Monday, the City of Tacurong, Province
of Sultan Kudarat will mark the celebration of its 23 Citynood Anniversary and the
Talakudong Festival 2023;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 18 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
