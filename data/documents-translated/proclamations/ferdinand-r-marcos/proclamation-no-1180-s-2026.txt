MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1180

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUGUIAS, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 06 March 2026, Friday, the Municipality of Buguias, Province
of Benguet will mark the celebration of the Buguias Carrot Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Buguias be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
06 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Buguias, Province
of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
