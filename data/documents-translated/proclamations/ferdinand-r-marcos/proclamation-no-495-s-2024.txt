MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 495

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAYANTOC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 20 March 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Mayantoc,
Province of Tarlac will celebrate the Natan-Ok Mayantoc Kankanen Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mayantoc be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mayantoc, Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
