MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 924

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Lian, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of the Sambuhos Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Lian
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lian,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
