MALACANAN PALACE
~ MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 195

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CALACA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 23 May 2023, the City of Calaca, Province of Batangas will
mark the celebration of the birth anniversary of General Eleuterio Marasigan, a great
Calaquefo, patriot, and one of the leaders during the libertarian movement in the
Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calaca be -
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 23 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calaca, Province
of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th dayof March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
