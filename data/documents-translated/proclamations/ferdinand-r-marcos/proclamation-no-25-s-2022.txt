MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 25

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 11 August 2022, Thursday, the Province of Samar will
commemorate its declaration as a province;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Samar
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 11 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28ti dayof July _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
