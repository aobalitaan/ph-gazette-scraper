MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1027

DECLARING THURSDAY, 02 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF DINAGAT ISLANDS

WHEREAS, on 02 October 2025, Thursday, the Province of Dinagat Islands
will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Dinagat
Islands be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 02 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Dinagat
Islands.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this1 1th day of September, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
