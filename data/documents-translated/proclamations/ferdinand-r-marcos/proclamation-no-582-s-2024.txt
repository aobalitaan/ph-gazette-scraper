MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 582

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2024, Friday, the City of Pagadian, Province of
Zamboanga del Sur will mark the celebration of its 55" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by authority

of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday, 21 June

2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian, Province of Zamboanga del
Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof June _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
