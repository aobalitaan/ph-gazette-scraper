MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 563

DECLARING SATURDAY, 22 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA TERESITA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 22 June 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Santa Teresita,
Province of Cagayan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santa Teresita be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 22 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santa
Teresita, Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
