MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 627

DECLARING JULY 2024 AS NATIONAL CENSUS’ AND
COMMUNITY-BASED MONITORING SYSTEM MONTH

WHEREAS, Section 6 of Republic Act (RA) No. 10625 or the “Philippine Statistical
Act of 2013,” mandates the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) to prepare and conduct
periodic censuses on population, housing, agriculture, fisheries, business, industry and
other sectors of the economy;

WHEREAS, Sections 2 and 5 of RA No. 11315 or the “Community-Based
Monitoring System (CBMS) Act of 2019,” states that: ()) the State shall adopt a
community-baser! monitoring System which generates updeted and disaggregated data
necessary in targeting beneficiaries, conducting more comprehensive poverty analysis
and needs prioritization, designing appropriate policies and interventions, and monitoring
impact over time; and (ii) the Implementing Rules and Regulations of RA No. 11315 may
provide for a separate period for data collection depending on the needs of national
government agencies;

WHEREAS, Executive Order (EO) No. 352 (s. 1996) requires the conduct of the
following nationwide censuses and surveys and the maintenance/processing of
administrative-based records on a regular basis: (i) census of population (POPCEN)
every ten (10) years; and (ii) the mid-decade POPCEN every five (5) years;

WHEREAS, PSA Board Resolution No. 6 (s. 2024) recommends the conduct of
the 2024 POPCEN-CBMS from July to September 2024, with 1 July 2024 as the
reference date, and declares July 2024 as the “National Census and CBMS Month;”

WHEREAS, the POPCEN-CBMS shall be a nationwide government undertaking
that will involve the collection, compilation, evaluation, analysis, publication, and
dissemination of data on population count, demographic and socioeconomic
characteristics of the population, housing statistics, and other relevant data in each
barangay, municipality, city, province, and region in the country;

WHEREAS, the regular conduct of POPCEN-CBMS supports the decentralization
of functions to local government units, and equips government executives, policymakers
and planners with current and comprehensive population data to develop informed social
and economic development plans, policies, and programs; and

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all the executive departments, bureaus and offices and the
mandate to ensure the faithful execution of laws;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, do hereby declare July 2024 as the “National Census and Community-Based
Monitoring System Month.”

For this purpose, the National Economic and Development Authority (NEDA),
through the PSA, is hereby directed to lead, coordinate, and supervise the observance
of the National Census and CBMS Month.

All agencies and _ instrumentalities of the National Government, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations and state universities and colleges, are
hereby directed, and all local government units, non-government organizations,
professional associations, and the private sector, are hereby encouraged to render the
necessary support and assistance to NEDA and PSA, and to actively participate in the
observance of the National Census and CBMS Month.

All citizens and residents of the Philippines are encouraged to cooperate with the
PSA and its census enumerators in the conduct of the 2024 POPCEN-CBMS, by
providing truthful and complete information in response to census questions, subject to
existing laws, rules and regulations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 9th day of July , in the
year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.
