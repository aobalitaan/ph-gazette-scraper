MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 191

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 03 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 03 May 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Santa Cruz,
Province of Marinduque will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary known
as Santa Cruz Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santa Cruz be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 03 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santa
Cruz, Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thday of March , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
