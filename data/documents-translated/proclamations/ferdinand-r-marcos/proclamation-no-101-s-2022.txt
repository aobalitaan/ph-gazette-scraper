MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 101

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINDANGAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Sindangan,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 86'" Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sindangan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
23 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sindangan,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisist day of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
