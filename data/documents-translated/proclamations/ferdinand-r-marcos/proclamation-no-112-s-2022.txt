MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 112

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JANUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 18 January 2023, Wednesday, the City of Santa Rosa,
Province of Laguna will mark the celebration of its 231st Founding Anniversary and
the 24" Sikhayan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 January 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa
Rosa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two

By authority of the President:
