MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 679

DECLARING THURSDAY, 05 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 05 September 2024, Thursday, the City of General Santos,
Province of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of the 26" Tuna Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 05 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of General
Santos, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nq day of Septembets the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
