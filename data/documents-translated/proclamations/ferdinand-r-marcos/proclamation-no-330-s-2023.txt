PROCLAMATION NO. 330

DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND, LOCATED AT
BARANGAY KAMANGA, MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIM, PROVINCE OF
SARANGANI, FOR INCLUSION INTO THE EXISTING KAMANGA AGRO-
INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO.
7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise
known as the “Special.Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and
upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority (PEZA), |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the Philippines, do
hereby designate several parcels of land, located in Barangay Kamanga, Municipality of
Maasim, Province of Sarangani, for inclusion into the existing Kamanga Agro-Industrial
Economic Zone, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing
Rules and Regulations, PEZA Board of Directors Resolution No. 07-232 (s. 2007) and
Resolution No. 22-021 (s. 2022), with an aggregate area of Three Hundred Seventy
Thousand Seven Hundred Five square meters (370,705 m2), more or less, as defined
by the following technical description:
