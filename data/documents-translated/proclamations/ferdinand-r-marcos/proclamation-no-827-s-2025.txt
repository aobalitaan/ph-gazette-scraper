MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 827

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 19 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACO, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Baco, Province
of Oriental Mindoro will celebrate the Banana Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baco be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 19 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Baco,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Wee P. BERSAMIN
