MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 390

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUMINGAG, PROVINCE OF
ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 27 December 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Dumingag, Province of Zamboanga del Sur will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dumingag be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Dumingag, Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
