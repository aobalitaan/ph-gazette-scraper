MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 305

DECLARING MONDAY, 14 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAWAB, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 14 August 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Mawab,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mawab be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 14 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mawab,
Province of Davao de Cro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 34stday of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
