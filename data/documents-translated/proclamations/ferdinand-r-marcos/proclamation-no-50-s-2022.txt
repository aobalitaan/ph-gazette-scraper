MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 50

DECLARING TUESDAY, 13 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVER, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 126 which was approved on 13 September
1955 created the Municipality of Claver;

WHEREAS, on 13 September 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Claver,
Province of Surigao del Norte, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary
(Adlaw Nan Claver);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Claver be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 13 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Claver, Province of Surigao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of August _ ,in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
