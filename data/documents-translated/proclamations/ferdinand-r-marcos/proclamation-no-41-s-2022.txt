MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 41

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, the City of Tacurong, Province of Sultan Kudarat will mark the
celebration of its 22"¢ Cityhood Anniversary and the Talakudong Festival on 18
September 2022;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tacurong to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 19 September 2022, Monday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 18 September 2022, which falls on
a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 19 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th dayof August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
