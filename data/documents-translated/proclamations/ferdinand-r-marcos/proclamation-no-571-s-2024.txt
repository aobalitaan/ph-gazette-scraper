MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 571

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of San Fernando,
Province of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 58" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Fernando be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 18 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Fernando, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof June _ , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
