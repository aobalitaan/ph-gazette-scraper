MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 77

DECLARING SATURDAY, 26 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF DASMARINAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Section 1 of Republic Act (RA) No. 11461 dated 05 September
2019 declared November 26 of every year as a special working holiday to be known
as the Foundation Day of the City of Dasmarifias;

WHEREAS, RA No. 11461 further provides that upon proclamation of the
President, November 26 shall be a special nonworking holiday in the City of
Dasmarifas, Province of Cavite;

WHEREAS, on 26 November 2022, Saturday, the City of Dasmarifias,
Province of Cavite will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Dasmarifias be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 26 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dasmarifias.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of October __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
