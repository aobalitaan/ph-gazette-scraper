MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1024

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GLORIA, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 01 October 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Gloria,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary and
the Kawayanan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gloria be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 01 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gloria, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 thday of September, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
