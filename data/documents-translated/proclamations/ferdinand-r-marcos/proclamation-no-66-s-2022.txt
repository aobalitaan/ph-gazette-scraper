MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 66

DECLARING FRIDAY, 07 OCTOBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIPOS, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Guipos, Province of Zamboanga del Sur was
created by virtue of Republic Act No. 7159 dated 07 October 1991:

WHEREAS, on 07 October 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Guipos, Province
of Zamboanga del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 31st Araw ng Guipos;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Guipos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
07 October 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Guipos, Province
of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rq day of october, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
