MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1155

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADRE BURGOS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 17 February 2026, Tuesday, the Municipality of Padre Burgos,
Province of Quezon will mark the celebration of Padre Burgos Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Padre Burgos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Padre
Burgos, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thday of January , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
