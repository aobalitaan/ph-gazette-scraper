MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1005

DECLARING TUESDAY, 30 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MASBATE, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 30 September 2025, Tuesday, the City of Masbate, Province
of Masbate will mark the celebration of its 25" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Masbate be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 30 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF-, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28thday of August __, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
