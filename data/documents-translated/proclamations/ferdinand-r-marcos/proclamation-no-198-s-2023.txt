MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 198

DECLARING MONDAY, 03 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALANGIGA, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 03 April 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Balangiga,
Province of Eastern Samar will mark the celebration of its 169% Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Balangiga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 03 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Balangiga,
Province of Eastern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3istday of March_, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
