MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 232

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALANASAN, PROVINCE OF APAYAO

WHEREAS, on 25 June 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Calanasan,
Province of Apayao will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calanasan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Calanasan, Province of Apayao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
