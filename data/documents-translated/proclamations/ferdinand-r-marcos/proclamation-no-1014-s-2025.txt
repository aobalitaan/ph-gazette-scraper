MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1014

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 17 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 17 September 2025, Wednesday, the Province of Zamboanga
del Sur will mark the celebration of its 739 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy
the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 17 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day ofSeptemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
