MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1004

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 26 September 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Naval, Province
of Biliran will mark the celebration of its 165"" Pueblo Day (Charter Day);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naval be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
26 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Naval, Province
of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this28thday ofAugust __, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

     
     

  

ATTY, LOVELY V, TOLENTING-NAVA
