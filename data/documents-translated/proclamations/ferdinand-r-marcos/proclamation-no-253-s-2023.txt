MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 253

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2023, Tuesday, the City of Tanjay, Province of Negros
Oriental will mark the celebration of the Sinulog sa Tanjay Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanjay be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanjay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
