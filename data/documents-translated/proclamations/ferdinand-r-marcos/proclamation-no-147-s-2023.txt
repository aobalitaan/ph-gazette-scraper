MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 147

DECLARING FRIDAY, 24 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 24 February 2023, Friday, the Province of Zamboanga
Sibugay will mark the celebration of its 22°¢ Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is. but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
24 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga
Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
