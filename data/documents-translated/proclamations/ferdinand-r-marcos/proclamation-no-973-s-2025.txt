MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 973

DECLARING AS OFFICIAL THE 2024 POPULATION COUNT OF THE
PHILIPPINES BY PROVINCE, CITY/MUNICIPALITY, AND
BARANGAY BASED ON THE 2024 CENSUS OF POPULATION
CONDUCTED BY THE PHILIPPINE STATISTICS AUTHORITY

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 10625 or the “Philippine Statistical Act of
2013” provides that the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) shall be primarily
responsible for all national censuses and surveys, sectoral statistics, consolidation of
selected administrative recording systems, and compilation of national accounts;

WHEREAS, Section 6(b) of RA No. 10625 mandates the PSA to prepare and
conduct periodic censuses on population, housing, agriculture, fisheries, business,
industry, and other sectors of the economy;

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 627 (s. 2024) declared the month of July 2024
as the “National Census and Community-Based Monitoring System (CBMS) Month,”

WHEREAS, the 2024 Census of Population (POPCEN) and CBMS were
conducted by the PSA from July to September 2024, with 1 July 2024 as reference
date;

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 87 (s. 2025) directs the conduct of a
mid-decade census primarily to update the population count in all barangays
nationwide; and

WHEREAS, Section 8 of Batas Pambansa Blg. 72 (s. 1980) provides that the
final population count as determined from the processed census returns shall be
considered official for all purposes only upon proclamation by the President;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
do hereby declare as official for all purposes the results of the 2024 POPCEN, placing
the total population of the Philippines as of 1 July 2024 at 112,729,484, as shown in
the “2024 Census of Population: Population by Province, City/Municipality, and
Barangay,” attached hereto and made an integral part hereof.

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

The PSA, in coordination with the Presidential Communications Office, is
hereby directed to disseminate copies of the 2024 Census of Population: Population
by Province, City/Municipality, and Barangay to all concerned government agencies
and offices.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July , in the
year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.
