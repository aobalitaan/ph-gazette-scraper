MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 15

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2022, Monday, the Municipality of Kawit, Province
of Cavite, will mark the celebration of Kawit Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kawit be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 01 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kawit,
Province of Cavite.

iN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
