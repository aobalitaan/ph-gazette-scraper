MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 793

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANGUB, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2025, Friday, the City of Tangub, Province of
Misamis Occidental will mark the celebration of its 57 Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub be
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
28 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tangub, Province of
Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3-q day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
