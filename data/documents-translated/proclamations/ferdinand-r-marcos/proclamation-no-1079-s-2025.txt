MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1079

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BOCAUE, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Bocaue,
Province of Bulacan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bocaue be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 11 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bocaue, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
