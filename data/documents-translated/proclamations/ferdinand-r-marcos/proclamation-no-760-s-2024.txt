MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 760

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 16 January 2025, Thursday, the City of Batangas will mark its
Tourism and Cultural Day together with the celebration of its city fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batangas
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF ., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
