MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 64

DECLARING THURSDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BACOOR, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 29 September 2022, Thursday, the City of Bacoor will mark
the celebration of its 3515 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bacoor be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 29 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bacoor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
