MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1059

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2011, the Puerto Princesa Underground River
was chosen as one of the New Seven Wonders of Nature;

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 816 dated 03 July 2014 declared 11 November
of every year as Puerto Princesa Underground River Day;

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2025, Tuesday, the City of Puerto.Princesa will
mark the celebration of the Puerto Princesa Underground River Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Puerto
Princesa City be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Tuesday, 11 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Puerto
Princesa, Province of Palawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
