MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 348

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 06 October 2023, Friday, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of the Provincial Indigenous People’s Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Mountain Province be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
06 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of September in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

   

— CONCEPCION ZERY E. FERROLINO-ENAD
