MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 224

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ANGELES, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, the volcanic eruption of Mt. Pinatubo on 15 June 1991, the
second-largest volcanic eruption in the 20" century, caused massive destruction and
adversely affected the City of Angeles and the lives of its residents;

WHEREAS, in commemoration of the 32™ anniversary of the Mt. Pinatubo
eruption and the resilience of the Angelefios, the Office of the Mayor requested that
15 June 2023 be declared a special (non-working) day in Angeles City;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Angeles
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Angeles,
Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
