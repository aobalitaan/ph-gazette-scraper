MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 863

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Gumaca, Province
of Quezon will mark the celebration of the Araw ng mga Magsasaka at Arafia at
Baluarte Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of April | inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President

ECORDS OFFICE
