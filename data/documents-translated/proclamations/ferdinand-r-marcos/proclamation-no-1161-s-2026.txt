MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.1161

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2026, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IBAAN, PROVINCE OF
BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 14 February 2026, Wednesday, the Municipality of Ibaan,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
lbaan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 11 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
lbaan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

“R HG. R}
Acting Executive Secretary
