MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 484

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CANLAON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2024, Tuesday, the City of Canlaon, Province of
Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of the Pasayaw Festival 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Canlaon
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Canlaon,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rq day of February in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
