MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 181

DECLARING FRIDAY, 24 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 March 2023, Friday, the City of Tabaco, Province of Albay
will mark the celebration of its Charter Day and its 22"4 Cityhood Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
24 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco, Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
