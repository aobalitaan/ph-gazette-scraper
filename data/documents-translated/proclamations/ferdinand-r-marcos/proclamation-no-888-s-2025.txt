MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 888

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRESENTACION, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES
SUR

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Presentacion,
Province of Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of its 64" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Presentacion be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Presentacion, Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
