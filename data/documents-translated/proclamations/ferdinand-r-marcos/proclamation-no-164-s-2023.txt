MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 164

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2023, Wednesday, the City of Malaybalay, Province
of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 25" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Malaybalay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Malaybalay,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rdday of Fébruaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
