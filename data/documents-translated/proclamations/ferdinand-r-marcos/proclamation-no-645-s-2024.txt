BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 645

DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND, LOCATED AT
BARANGAY KAMANGA, MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIM, PROVINCE OF
SARANGANI, FOR INCLUSION INTO THE EXISTING KAMANGA
AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC
ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916,
otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No.
8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine
Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), 1, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, do hereby designate several parcels of land, located at Barangay Kamanga,
Municipality of Maasim, Province of Sarangani, for inclusion into the existing Kamanga
Agro-Industrial Economic Zone, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as
amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, PEZA Board of Directors Resolution
No. 18-468 (s. 2018), with an aggregate area of Three Hundred Twenty-Eight
Thousand Three Hundred Ninety-Three square meters (328,393 m?), more or less,
as defined by the following technical descriptions:
