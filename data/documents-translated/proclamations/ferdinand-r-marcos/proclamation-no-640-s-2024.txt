MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 640

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LUCENA

WHEREAS, on 20 August 2024, Tuesday, the City of Lucena will mark the
celebration of its 63" Charter Anniversary (Araw ng Lucena);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lucena be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lucena.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
