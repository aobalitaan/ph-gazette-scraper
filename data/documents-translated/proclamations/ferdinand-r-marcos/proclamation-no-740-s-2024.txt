MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 740

DECLARING MONDAY, 02 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAINTA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Cainta, Province of Rizal, will celebrate the
Sumbingtik Festival and its Founding Anniversary on 01 December 2024, which falls
on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, the Office of the Mayor requested that 02 December 2024,
Monday, be declared as a (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cainta in
celebration of the Sumbingtik Festival and its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cainta be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
02 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cainta, Province
of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 2¢ day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
