MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 825

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, on 17 March 2025, Monday, the City of Davao will mark the
celebration of its Araw ng Pasasalamat sa 88" Araw ng Davao;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by

authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
17 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
