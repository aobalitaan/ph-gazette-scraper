MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 583

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 26 June 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of San Isidro,
Province of Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of the 20 Araw ng San Isidro;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Isidro be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by authority
of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Wednesday, 26

June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Isidro, Province of
Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
