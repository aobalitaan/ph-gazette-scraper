MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 662

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 19 August 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Angono, Province
of Rizal will commemorate the signing of its independence as a municipality from the
Municipality of Binangonan, Rizal:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 19 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 42th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
