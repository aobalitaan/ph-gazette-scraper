MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 264

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SABANGAN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sabangan, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of its 61St Foundation Day on 25 June 2023;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Sabangan to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 23 June 2023,
Friday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 25 June 2023 which
falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sabangan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 23 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sabangan,
Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
