MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 386

DECLARING TUESDAY, 12 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 12 December 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of San Juan,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Juan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 12 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Juan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of Octoberin the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
