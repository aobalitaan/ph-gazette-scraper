MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 474

DECLARING THURSDAY, 14 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 14 March 2024, Thursday, the City of Passi, Province of lloilo
will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary as a component city;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Passi be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 14 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Passi, Province
of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this16th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
