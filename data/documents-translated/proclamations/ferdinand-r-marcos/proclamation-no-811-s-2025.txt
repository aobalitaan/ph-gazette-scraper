MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 811

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 18 March 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet will celebrate the Strawberry Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of La
Trinidad be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 18 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of La
Trinidad, Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this25th day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

       
   

CERTIR

OLENTINO-NAVA |
