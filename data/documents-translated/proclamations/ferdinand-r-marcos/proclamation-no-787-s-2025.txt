MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 787

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 11445, amending RA No. 9090, declared
February 1 of every year as Jose Maria E. Panganiban Day, in recognition of Dr.
Panganiban’s sacrifices and fight for humanitarian reforms for the country;

WHEREAS, on 01 February 2025, Saturday, the Province of Camarines Norte
will commemorate Jose Maria Panganiban Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Camarines Norte be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Camarines
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
