MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDEN7 OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 287

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Kawit, Province
of Cavite will mark the celebration of Kawit Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kawit be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kawit,
Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be aifixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
