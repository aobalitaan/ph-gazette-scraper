MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 131

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 10 February 2023, Friday, the City of Laoag, Province of
llocos Norte will mark the celebration of the Pamulinawen Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
10 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag, Province of
Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of January , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
