MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1175

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUGAIT, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2026, Monday, the Municipality of Lugait, Province of
Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of its Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lugait be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
16 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lugait, Province of
Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day offebruary, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
