MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 981

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIDDIG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 16 September 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Piddig,
Province of Ilocos Norte will commemorate the Basi Revolt of 1807;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Piddig be given full opportunity te participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 16 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Piddig, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th dayof July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
