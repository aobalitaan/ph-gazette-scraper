MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1003

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2025, Thursday, the City of Tacurong, Province
of Sultan Kudarat will mark the celebration of its 25" Cityhood Anniversary and the
Talakudong Festival 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 18 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this27tuday of August , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
