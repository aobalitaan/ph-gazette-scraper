MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 604

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 14 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTO TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 14 August 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Santo Tomas,
Province of Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of its 65" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santo Tomas be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 14 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santo
Tomas, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th dayof June, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

   
  

ERSAMIN
