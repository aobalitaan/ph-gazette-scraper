MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 229

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAUREL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Laurel,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its 54 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Laurel be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Wednesday, 21 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Laurel,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17thdayof May _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
