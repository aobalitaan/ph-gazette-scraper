MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 590

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 19 July 2024, Friday, the City of Digos, Province of Davao del
Sur will commemorate its creation as a municipality through the Padigosan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,

19 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Province of Davao del
Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13thdayof June , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
