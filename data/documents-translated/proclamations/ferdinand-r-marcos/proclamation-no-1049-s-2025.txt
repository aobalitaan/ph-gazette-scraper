MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1049

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 29 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MATI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Mati, Province of Davao Oriental celebrates the
Sambuokan Festival every last weekend of October in commemoration of its date of
establishment as a municipality before eventually becoming a city;

WHEREAS, on 29 October 2025, Wednesday, the City of Mati, Province of
Davao Oriental will mark the celebration of the Sambuokan Festival:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mati be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 29 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mati,
Province of Davao Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of Septembda the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
By authority of the President:

/LUCAS P. B SAMIN
Executive Secretary
