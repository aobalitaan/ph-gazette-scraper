MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 180

DECLARING TUESDAY, 14 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 14 March 2023, Tuesday, the City of Passi, Province of Iloilo
will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary as a component city;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Passi be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 14 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Passi, Province
of lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10thday of March __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
