MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1086

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 16 December 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malvar be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 16 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Malvar, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
