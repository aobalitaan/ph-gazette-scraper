MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1000

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUPI, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 11 September 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Tupi,
Province of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 72°? Founding Anniversary
and the 16" Agten Tufi Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Tupi
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 11 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tupi,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18thday of August __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
