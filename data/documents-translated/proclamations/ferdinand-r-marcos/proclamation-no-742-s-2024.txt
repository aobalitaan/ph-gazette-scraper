MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 742

DECLARING THURSDAY, 12 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 12 December 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of San Juan,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Juan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration; —

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 12 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Juan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
