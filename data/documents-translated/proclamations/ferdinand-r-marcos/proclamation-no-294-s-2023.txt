MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 294

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 18 August 2023, Friday, the City of Kidapawan, Province of
Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 76 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Kidapawan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
18 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Kidapawan, Province of
Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18thday of July _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
