MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 786

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANZA, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Tanza, Province
of Cavite will mark the celebration of its 111" Araw ng Tanza;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanza be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
28 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tanza, Province
of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
