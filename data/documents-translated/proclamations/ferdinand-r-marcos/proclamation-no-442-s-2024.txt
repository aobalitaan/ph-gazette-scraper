MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 442

DECLARING TUESDAY, 06 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONSOLACION, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 06 February 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Consolacion,
Province of Cebu will mark the celebration of its 104° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Consolacion be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 06 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Consolacion, Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

    

UCAS P. SAMI
Exécutive Segretary
