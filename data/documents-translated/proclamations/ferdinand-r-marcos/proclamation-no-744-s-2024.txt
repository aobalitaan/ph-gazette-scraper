MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 744

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 18 December 2024, Wednesday, the City of Tuguegarao,
Province of Cagayan will mark the celebration of its 25" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, itis but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tuguegarao
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Tuguegarao, Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this,2th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
