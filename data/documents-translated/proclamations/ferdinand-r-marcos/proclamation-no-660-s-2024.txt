MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 660

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HUNGDUAN, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, on 01 October 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Hungduan,
Province of Ifugao will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Hungduan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Hungduan, Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 day of August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
