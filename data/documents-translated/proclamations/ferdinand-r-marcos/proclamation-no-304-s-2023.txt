MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 304

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 02 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF KABANKALAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 02 August 2023, Wednesday, the City of Kabankalan,
Province of Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 26 Charter
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Kabankalan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 02 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Kabankalan,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this31st dayof July, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
