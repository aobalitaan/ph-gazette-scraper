MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 887

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALILANGAN, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Kalilangan,
Province of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 59" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kalilangan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by authority
of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Wednesday, 18
June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kalilangan, Province of
Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
