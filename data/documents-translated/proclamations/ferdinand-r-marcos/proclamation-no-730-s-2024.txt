MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 730

DECLARING MONDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 04 November 2024, the Province of Quezon will
commemorate the death anniversary of its local hero, Apolinario Dela Cruz, also
known as Hermano Puli;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Quezon be given full opportunity to honor the heroism and patriotism of Hermano
Puli through appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by

authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 04 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province. of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st dayof October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
