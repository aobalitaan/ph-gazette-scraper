MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 841

DECLARING THURSDAY, 03 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGSAYSAY, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 03 April 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Magsaysay,
Province of Occidental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Magsaysay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 03 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Magsaysay,
Province of Occidental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21stday of March __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
