MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 746

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 20 December 2024, Friday, the City of Sipalay, Province of
Negros Occidental will celebrate its 76" Founding Anniversary and the Pasaway
Handurawan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
20 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sipalay, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of Nedvember, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
