MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 278

DECLARING MONDAY, 14 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTO TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 14 August 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Santo Tomas,
Province of Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of its 64 Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santo Tomas be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 14 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santo
Tomas, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of June __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
