MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 55

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 OCTOBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGLE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 28 October 1898, the local revolutionaries manifested their
desire for independence from Spain in barrio Lincud, Iloilo and the act came to be
known as the Cry of Lincud;

WHEREAS, on 28 October 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Dingle, Province
of lloilo will mark the commemoration of the Cry of Lincud;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dingle be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
28 October 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dingle, Province
of lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. .

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of September in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
