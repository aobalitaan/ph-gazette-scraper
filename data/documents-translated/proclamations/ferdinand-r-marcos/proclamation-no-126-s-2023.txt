MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 126

DECLARING THURSDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MABALACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2023, Thursday, the City of Mabalacat, Province
of Pampanga will mark the celebration of its 311 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabalacat
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 02 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
