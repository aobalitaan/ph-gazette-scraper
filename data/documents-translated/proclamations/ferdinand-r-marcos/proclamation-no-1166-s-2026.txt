MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1166

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUKO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 13 March 2026, Friday, the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain
Province will mark the celebration of the Begnas di Bauko Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauko be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
13 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain
Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of February in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
