MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 97

DECLARING THURSDAY, 01 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAINTA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 01 December 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Cainta,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of the Sumbingtik Festival and its
Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cainta be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 01 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Cainta, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18tiday of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
