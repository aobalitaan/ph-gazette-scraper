MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.199

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TARLAC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, the City of Tarlac, Province of Tarlac will mark the celebration of
its 25" Charter Anniversary on 19 April 2023;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tarlac to celebrate and
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 21 April 2023,
Friday, as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 19 April 2023 which falls on a
Wednesday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
21 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof April , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

       

YE. FERROLINO-ENAD
