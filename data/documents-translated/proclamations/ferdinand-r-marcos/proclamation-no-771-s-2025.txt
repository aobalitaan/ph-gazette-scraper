MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 771

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 15 January 2025, Wednesday, the City of San Pablo, Province
of Laguna will celebrate the 30 Coconut Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
