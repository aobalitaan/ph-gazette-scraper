MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. g3

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 OCTOBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 28 October 2022, Friday, the Province of Negros Oriental will
mark the celebration of the Buglasan Festival;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Negros Oriental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
28 October 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
