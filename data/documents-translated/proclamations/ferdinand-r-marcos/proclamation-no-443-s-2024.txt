MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 443

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MABALACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2024, Friday, the City of Mabalacat, Province of
Pampanga will mark the celebration of its 312" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabalacat
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
02 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th dayof January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
