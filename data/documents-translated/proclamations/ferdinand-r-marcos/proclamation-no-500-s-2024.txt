MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 500

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 01 April 2024, Monday, the City of Tanjay, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanjay be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 01 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanjay, Province of
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
