MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 824

DECLARING MONDAY, 31 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF HIMAMAYLAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 31 March 2025, Monday, the City of Himamaylan, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary and the Himaya
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Himamaylan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
31 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Himamaylan, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
