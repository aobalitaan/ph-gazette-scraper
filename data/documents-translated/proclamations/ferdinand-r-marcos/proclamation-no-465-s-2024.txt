MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 465

DECLARING THURSDAY, 21 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 21 March 2024, Thursday, the City of Calapan, Province of
Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its 26th Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calapan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 21 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calapan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
