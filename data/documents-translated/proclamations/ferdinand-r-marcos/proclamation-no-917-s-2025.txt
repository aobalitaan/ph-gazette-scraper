MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 917

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 23 June 2025, Monday, the City of Batac, Province of Ilocos
Norte will mark the celebration of its 18" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
23 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac, Province of Ilocos
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th dayof May __ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
