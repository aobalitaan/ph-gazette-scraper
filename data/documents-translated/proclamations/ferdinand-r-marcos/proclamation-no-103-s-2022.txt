MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 103

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. NINO, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Sto. Nifo,
Province of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 42" Founding
Anniversary and the 23% Hinublag Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sto. Nifio be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
23 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sto. Nifio,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st dayof December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
