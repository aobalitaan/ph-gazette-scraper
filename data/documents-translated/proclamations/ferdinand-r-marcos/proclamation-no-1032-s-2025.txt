MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1032

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIGCAWAYAN, PROVINCE OF NORTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 20 October 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Pigcawayan,
Province of North Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 72°? Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pigcawayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
20 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pigcawayan,
Province of North Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 ttday ofSeptember, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
