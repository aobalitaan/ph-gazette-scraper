MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 687

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL WORKING DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARIA AURORA, PROVINCE OF AURORA

WHEREAS, Maria Aurora Aragon Quezon, the first daughter of President
Manuel L. Quezon and Aurora Aragon Quezon was born on 23 September 1919;

WHEREAS, on 23 September 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Maria Aurora
will commemorate the birth anniversary of Maria Aurora Aragon Quezon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maria Aurora be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 23 September 2024, a special working day in the Municipality of Maria
Aurora, Province of Aurora.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 42; ,day of September, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
