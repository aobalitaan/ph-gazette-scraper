MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1053

DECLARING THURSDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE
ORO

WHEREAS, on 13 November 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Pantukan,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 88" Founding Anniversary
and the 23 PASAKA Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pantukan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 13 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pantukan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3¢d day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
