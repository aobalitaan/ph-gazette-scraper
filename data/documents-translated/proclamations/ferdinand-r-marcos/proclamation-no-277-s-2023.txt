MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 277

DECLARING MONDAY, 14 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGPET, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Magpet, Province of Cotabato will mark the
celebration of its 60" Founding Anniversary on 13 August 2023;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Magpet to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 14 August 2023,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 13 August 2023 which
falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Magpet be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 14 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Magpet,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30thday of June __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
