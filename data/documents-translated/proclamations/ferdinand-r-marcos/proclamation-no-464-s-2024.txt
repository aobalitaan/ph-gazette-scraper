MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 464

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADRE BURGOS, PROVINCE OF
QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 17 February 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Padre
Burgos, Province of Quezon will mark the celebration of Padre Burgos Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Padre Burgos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 17 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Padre
Burgos, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
