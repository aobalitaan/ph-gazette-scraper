MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 898

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PATNANUNGAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Patnanungan,
Province of Quezon will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Patnanungan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Patnanungan, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th dayof May __, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
