MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 889

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF BOGO, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2025, Monday, the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu will
mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bogo be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
16 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu:

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May | inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
