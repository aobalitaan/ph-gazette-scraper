MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 807

DECLARING SATURDAY, 08 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 08 March 2025, Saturday, the Province of Davao de Oro will
mark the celebration of its 27 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Davao
de Oro be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 08 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Davao de
Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day ofFebruaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
