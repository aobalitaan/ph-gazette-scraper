MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 149

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, the Municipality of La Trinidad, Province of Benguet is set to
celebrate the Strawberry Festival for the whole month of March 2023, with the
Parade and Main Program set for 17 March 2023;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of La Trinidad to bond
and celebrate as one community, the Office of the Municipal Mayor requested that
Friday, 17 March 2023 be declared as a special non-working day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of La
Trinidad be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
17 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day ofFebruary in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
