MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 382

DECLARING FRIDAY, 27 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 27 October 2023, Friday, the Municipality of San Isidro,
Province of Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of the Sikwate Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Isidro be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
27 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Isidro,
Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

LUGA B
e-Secretary
