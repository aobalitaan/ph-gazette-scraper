MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

‘BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 611

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF SAN CARLOS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2024, Monday, the City of San Carlos, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Carlos
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
01 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Carlos, Province of Negros
Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF. | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
MALACANIANG RECORDS OFFICE
