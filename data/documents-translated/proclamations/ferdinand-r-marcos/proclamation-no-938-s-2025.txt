MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 938

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur will celebrate its
creation as a municipality through the Padigosan Festival on 19 July 2025, which falls
on a Saturday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Digos to fully participate in the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 18 July 2025, Friday, be declared as
a special (non-working) day in lieu of 19 July 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
18 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Province of Davao del
Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
