MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1022

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF PILI, OCAMPO, CANAMAN, CAMALIGAN,
MAGARAO, BOMBON, AND CALABANGA, ALL FROM THE PROVINCE OF
CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 654 dated 25 September 2013 which amended
Proclamation 776, s. 1991 declared every Friday immediately preceding the feast of
Our Lady of Pefiafrancia as a special day in the City of Naga;

WHEREAS, considering the significant economic, cultural and historical ties
between Naga City and its neighboring municipalities, the inclusion of the
Municipalities of Pili, Ocampo, Canaman, Camaligan, Magarao, Bombon, and
Calabanga, all from the Province of Camarines Sur in the coverage of the Pefafrancia
Festival would bring immense benefits to their communities.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
19 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipalities of Pili, Ocampo,
Canaman, Camaligan, Magarao, Bombon, and Calabanga, all from the Province of
Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of Septemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
