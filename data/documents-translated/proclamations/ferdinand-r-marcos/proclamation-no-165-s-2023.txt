MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.165

DECLARING FRIDAY, 31 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF HIMAMAYLAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 31 March 2023, Friday, the City of Himamaylan, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary and the
Himaya Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Himamaylan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
31 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Himamaylan, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day offébruaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
