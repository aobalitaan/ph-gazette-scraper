MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 364

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABUCGAYAN, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Cabucgayan, Province of Biliran will mark the celebration of its 74 Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cabucgayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Cabucgayan, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day ofOctober, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
