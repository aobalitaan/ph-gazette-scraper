MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 80

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 OCTOBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 24 October 2022, Monday, the Municipality of Pili, Province of
Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of the Cimarrones Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pili
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 24 October 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pili,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this19th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
