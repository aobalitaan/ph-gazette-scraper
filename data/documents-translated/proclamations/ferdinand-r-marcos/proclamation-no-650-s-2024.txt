MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.650

DECLARING TUESDAY, 03 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 03 September 2024, Tuesday, the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of its 56" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 03 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 day of August, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
