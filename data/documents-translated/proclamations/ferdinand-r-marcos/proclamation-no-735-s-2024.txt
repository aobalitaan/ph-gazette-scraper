MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 735

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NARVACAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on 13 December 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Narvacan,
Province of Ilocos Sur will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Narvacan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
13 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Narvacan,
Province of llocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
