MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 248

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 23 June 2023, Friday, the City of Batac, Province of Ilocos
Norte will mark the celebration of its 16" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
23 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac, Province of Ilocos
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof June, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
