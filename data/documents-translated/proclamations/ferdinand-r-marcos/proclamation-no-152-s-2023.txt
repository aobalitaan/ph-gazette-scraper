MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 152

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 26 April 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its 258"" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 26 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Jose, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day ofFebruaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

GAS PN ERSAMIN
