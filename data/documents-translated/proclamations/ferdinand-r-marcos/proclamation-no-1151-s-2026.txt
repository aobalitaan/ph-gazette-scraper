MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1151

DECLARING MONDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2026, Monday, the Province of Ilocos Sur will
mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ilocos
Sur be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,

02 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rdday of January , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:
