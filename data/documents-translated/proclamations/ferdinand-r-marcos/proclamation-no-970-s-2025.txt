MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 970

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 18 August 2025, Monday, the City of Kidapawan, Province of
Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 78'" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Kidapawan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
18 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Kidapawan, Province of
Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thist th day of July inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
