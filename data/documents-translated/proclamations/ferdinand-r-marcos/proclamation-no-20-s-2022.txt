MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 20

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 22 August 2022, Monday, the Municipality of Liloy, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte, will mark the celebration of its 71st Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Liloy be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 22 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Liloy,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26tadayof July, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
