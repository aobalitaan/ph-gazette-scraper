MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1090

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAINTA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 01 December 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Cainta,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of the Sumbingtik Festival and its Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cainta be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
01 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cainta, Province
of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
