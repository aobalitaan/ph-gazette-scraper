MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 565

DECLARING FRIDAY, 14 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, the Municipality of La Trinidad, Province of Benguet will mark the
celebration of its 74‘ Founding Anniversary on 16 June 2024, which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of La Trinidad to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 14 June 2024,
Friday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 16 June 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of La
Trinidad be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
14 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet. ,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th dayof May __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
