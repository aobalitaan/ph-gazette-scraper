MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 188

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 26 May 2023, Friday, the City of Baliwag, Province of
Bulacan will mark the celebration of its 290" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Baliwag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,

26 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Baliwag, Province of
Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
