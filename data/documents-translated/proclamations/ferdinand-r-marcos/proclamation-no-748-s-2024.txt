MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 748

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAGGAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 27 November 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Baggao,
Province of Cagayan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baggao be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Baggao, Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12thday of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
