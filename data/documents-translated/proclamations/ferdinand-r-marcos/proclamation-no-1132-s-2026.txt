MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1132

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2026, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KABASALAN, PROVINCE OF
ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 17 February 2026, Tuesday, the Municipality of Kabasalan,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Kabasalan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kabasalan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Kabasalan, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of January , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
