MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 137

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUGAIT, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Lugait, Province
of Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lugait be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lugait,
Province of Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24thday of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
