BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 893

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIAONG, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Tiaong, Province of
Quezon will mark the celebration of its town fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tiaong be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tiaong,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
