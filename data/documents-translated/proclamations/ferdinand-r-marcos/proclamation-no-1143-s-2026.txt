MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1143

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CADIZ, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 26 January 2026, Monday, the City of Cadiz, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 52"? Dinagsa Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
26 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz, Province of Negros
Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
