MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 778

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 17 February 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Taytay,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Pasasalamat: HAMAKA
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taytay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
17 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Taytay, Province
of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
