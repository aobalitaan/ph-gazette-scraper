MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 163

DECLARING SATURDAY, 18 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 18 March 2023, Saturday, the Province of Masbate will mark
the celebration of its 1224 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 18 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day ofFebruaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
