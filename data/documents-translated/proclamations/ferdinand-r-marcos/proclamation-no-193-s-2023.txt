MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 193

DECLARING FRIDAY, 09 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 09 June 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Rosario, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rosario be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,

09 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of March _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
