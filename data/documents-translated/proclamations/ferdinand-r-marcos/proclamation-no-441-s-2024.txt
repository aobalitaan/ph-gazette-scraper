MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 441

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 15 January 2024, Monday, the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of the 29" Coconut Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 15 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

    

Executive Secretary

Office of the President
