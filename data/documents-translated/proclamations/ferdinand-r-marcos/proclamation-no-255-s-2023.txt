MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 255

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAGUPAN

WHEREAS, on 20 June 2023, Tuesday, the City of Dagupan will culminate
the celebration of its 75" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dagupan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
