MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 686

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LEMERY, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 25 October 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Lemery, Province
of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lemery be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lemery, Province
of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of Septembéi, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
