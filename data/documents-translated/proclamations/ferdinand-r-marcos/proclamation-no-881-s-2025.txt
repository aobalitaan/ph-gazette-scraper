MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 881

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 06 June 2025, Friday, the Province of Zamboanga del Norte
will mark the celebration of the 73'¢ Araw ng Zamboanga del Norte;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Norte be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy
the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
06 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May __, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
