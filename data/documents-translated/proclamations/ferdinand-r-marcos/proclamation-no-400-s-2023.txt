MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 400

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of the Higantes Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Angono, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President

MALACANANG RECORDS OFFICE
