MAJ.ACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 338

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN RAFAEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 29 September 2023, Friday, the Municipality of San Rafael,
Province of Bulacan, will mark the celebration of its 273"? Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Rafael be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
29 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Rafael,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
