MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 751

DECLARING THURSDAY, 02 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONIFACIO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 02 January 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Bonifacio,
Province of Misamis Occidental will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bonifacio be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 02 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bonifacio, Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 418th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
