MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION.NO. 318

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MASBATE, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 30 September 2023, Saturday, the City of Masbate, Province
of Masbate will mark the celebration of its 23"? Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Masbate to fully participate in
the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 29 September 2023, Friday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 30 September 2023 which falls on
a Saturday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Masbate
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
29 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Masbate, Province of
Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

CAS BERSAMIN
