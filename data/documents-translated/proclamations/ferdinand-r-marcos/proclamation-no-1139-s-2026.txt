MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1139

DECLARING MONDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MABALACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2026, Monday, the City of Mabalacat, Province of
Pampanga will mark the celebration of its 314" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabalacat
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
02 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat, Province of
Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of January , inthe year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

Offife of the President
