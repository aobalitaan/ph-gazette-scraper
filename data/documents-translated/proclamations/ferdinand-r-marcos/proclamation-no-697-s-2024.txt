MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 697

DECLARING MONDAY, 28 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGLE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 28 October 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Dingle, Province
of Iloilo will commemorate the Cry of Lincud, the first declaration of revolution against
Spain in the province of Iloilo and island of Panay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dingle be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 28 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dingle,
Province of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of september in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
