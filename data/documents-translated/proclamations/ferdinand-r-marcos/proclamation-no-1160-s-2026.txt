MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.1160

DECLARING FRIDAY, 27 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS, on 27 February 2026, Friday, the City of General Santos will mark
the celebration of the Kalilangan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to participate in the foregoing occasions and enjoy
their celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
27 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of General Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this3rd day offebruary , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:
