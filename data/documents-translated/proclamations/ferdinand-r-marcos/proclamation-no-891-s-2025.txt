MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 891

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF DON CARLOS, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Don Carlos,
Province of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, itis but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Don
Carlos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Don
Carlos, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May | inthe year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
