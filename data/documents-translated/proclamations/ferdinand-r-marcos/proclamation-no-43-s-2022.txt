MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 43

DECLARING SATURDAY, 03 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 5261 which was approved on 15 June 1968
created the City of Iriga;

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 453, series of 1968 designated 03 September
1968 as the formal organization of the City of lriga;

WHEREAS, on 03 September 2022, Saturday, the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of its 54'" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 03 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of lriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of August, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
