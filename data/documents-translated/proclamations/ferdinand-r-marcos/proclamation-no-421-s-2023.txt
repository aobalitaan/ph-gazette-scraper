MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 421

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIPLAHAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA

SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 19 December 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Diplahan,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay will mark the celebration of its Founding

Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Diplahan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Diplahan, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
