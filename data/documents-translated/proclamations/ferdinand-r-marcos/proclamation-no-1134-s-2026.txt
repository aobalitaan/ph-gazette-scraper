MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1134

DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 MAY 2026, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 07 May 2026, Thursday, the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 07 May 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo, Province
of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14 thday of aie , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
