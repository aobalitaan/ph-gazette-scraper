MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 241

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2023, Saturday, the Province of Masbate will mark
the celebration of its Bagat-Dagat Festival;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Masbate to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Governor requested that 23 June 2023,
Friday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 24 June 2023 which
falls on a Saturday; .

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
23 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
