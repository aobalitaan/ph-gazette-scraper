MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 879

DECLARING MONDAY, 02 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MAYOYAO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Mayoyao, Province of Ifugao will mark the
celebration of its Founding Anniversary on 01 June 2025, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Mayoyao to fully
participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan requested that 02 June 2025,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 01 June 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mayoyao be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
02 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mayoyao, Province
of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
