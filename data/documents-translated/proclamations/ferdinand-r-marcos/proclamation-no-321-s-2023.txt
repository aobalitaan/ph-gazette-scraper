MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 321

DECLARING SATURDAY, 09 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAIS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 09 September 2023, Saturday, the City of Bais, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 55" Charter Anniversary,

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bais be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 09 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bais.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
