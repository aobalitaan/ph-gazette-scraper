MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 478

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANZA, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Tanza,
Province of Cavite will mark the celebration of its 110" Araw ng Tanza;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanza be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 28 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tanza, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
