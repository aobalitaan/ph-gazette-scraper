MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1179

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 March 2026, Tuesday, the City of Tabaco, Province of Albay
will mark the celebration of its Charter Day and its 25" Cityhood Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco, Province
of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

   

RECORDS OFFICE
