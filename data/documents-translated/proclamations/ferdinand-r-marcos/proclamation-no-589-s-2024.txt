MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 589

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2024, Monday, the Cordillera Administrative Region
(CAR) will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the CAR be given full
opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
15 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the CAR.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th dayof June _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
