MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 178

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 10 March 2023, Friday, the City of Tanauan, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of its 224 Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanauan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
10 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanauan, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
