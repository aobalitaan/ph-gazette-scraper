MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 215

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF APARRI, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 11 May 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Aparri, Province
of Cagayan will mark the celebration of its 343" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aparri be given full Opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 11 May 2023, a Special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Aparri,
Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
