MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 244

DECLARING THURSDAY, 01 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PALAPAG, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 01 June 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Palapag,
Province of Northern Samar will commemorate the heroism and bravery of Agustin
Sumuroy, a native of Palapag who lead the uprising against Spanish colonizers;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Palapag be given full opportunity to enhance their cultural, historical and social
consciousness, and to enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 01 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Palapag,
Province of Northern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of May _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
