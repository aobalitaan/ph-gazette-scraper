MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 557

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF BAYUGAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2024, Friday, the City of Bayugan, Province of
Agusan del Sur will mark the celebration of its 17" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayugan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
21 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayugan, Province of
Agusan del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
