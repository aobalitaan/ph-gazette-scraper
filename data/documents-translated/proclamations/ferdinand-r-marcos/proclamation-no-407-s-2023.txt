MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 407

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAINTA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 01 December 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Cainta,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of the Sumbingtik Festival and its
Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cainta be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, SERDINARE R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
01 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cainta,
Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
