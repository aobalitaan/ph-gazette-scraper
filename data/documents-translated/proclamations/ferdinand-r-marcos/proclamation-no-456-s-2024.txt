MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 456

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KATIPUNAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Katipunan,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 110% Araw ng
Katipunan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Katipunan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 02 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Katipunan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

     

UCAS P. BERSAMI
