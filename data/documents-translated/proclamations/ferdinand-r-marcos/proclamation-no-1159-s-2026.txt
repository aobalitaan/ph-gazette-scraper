MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1159

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 24 February 2026, Tuesday, the Province of Zamboanga
Sibugay will mark the celebration of its 25" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy
the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of February in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

ATTY. LOVELY V. TOLENTINO-NAVA
DIRECTOR IV F
