MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 716

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBLAY, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 22 November 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Tublay, Province
of Benguet will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tublay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
22 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tublay, Province
of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd dayof October, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
