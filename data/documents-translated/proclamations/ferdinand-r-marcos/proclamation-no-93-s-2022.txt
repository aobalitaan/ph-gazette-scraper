MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 93

DECLARING SATURDAY, 10 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MEYCAUAYAN, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 10 December 2022, Saturday, the City of Meycauayan,
Province of Bulacan will mark the celebration of its Araw ng Lungsod ng
Meycauayan;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Meycauayan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 10 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Meycauayan, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thday of Novemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

KSP. BERSAMIN
Axecutive Segretary
