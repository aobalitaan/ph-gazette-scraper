MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 629

DECLARING MONDAY, 09 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAIS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 09 September 2024, Monday, the City of Bais, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 56'" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bais be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
09 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bais.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 141th day of July, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
