MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 508

DECLARING TUESDAY, 30 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 30 April 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Calatagan,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Cultural Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calatagan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Tuesday, 30 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calatagan,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd dayof March _, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four. .
