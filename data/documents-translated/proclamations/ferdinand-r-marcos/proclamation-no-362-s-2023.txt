MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 362

DECLARING SATURDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYSAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Taysan,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its 105" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taysan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 11 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Taysan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
