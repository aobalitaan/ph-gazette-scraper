MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 754

DECLARING MONDAY, 02 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONIFACIO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 02 December 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Bonifacio,
Province of Misamis Occidental will celebrate the Kutsitsa Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bonifacio be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 02 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bonifacio, Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
