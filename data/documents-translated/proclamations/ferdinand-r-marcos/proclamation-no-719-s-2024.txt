MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 719

DECLARING TUESDAY, 26 NOVEMBER 2024, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF DASMARINAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 26 November 2024, Tuesday, the City of Dasmarifias, Province
of Cavite will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dasmarifias
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 26 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dasmarifias,
Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this22nd dayof October  , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
