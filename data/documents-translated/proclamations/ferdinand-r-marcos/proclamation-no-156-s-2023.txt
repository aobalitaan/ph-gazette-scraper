MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 156

DECLARING TUESDAY, 04 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 04 April 2023, Tuesday, the City of Antipolo, Province of
Rizal will mark the celebration of its 25"" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Antipolo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 04 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Antipolo, Province
of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day ofFebruaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
