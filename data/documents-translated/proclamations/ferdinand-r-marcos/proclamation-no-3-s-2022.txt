MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 03

DECLARING THURSDAY, 14 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GUIHULNGAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 14 July 2022, Thursday, the City of Guihulngan, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Guihulngan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 14 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihulngan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
