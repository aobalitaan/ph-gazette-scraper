MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 651

DECLARING MONDAY, 09 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur will mark the
celebration of the Araw ng Digos on 08 September 2024, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Digos to fully participate in the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 09 September 2024, Monday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 08 September 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
09 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Province of
Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 dayof August __, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
