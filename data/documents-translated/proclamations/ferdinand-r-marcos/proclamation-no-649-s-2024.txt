MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 649

DECLARING FRIDAY, 30 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN JUAN

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2024, Friday, the City of San Juan will commemorate
Pinaglabanan Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Juan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
30 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Juan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 dayof August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
