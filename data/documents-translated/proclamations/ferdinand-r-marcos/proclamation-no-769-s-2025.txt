MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 769

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 23 January 2025, Thursday, the Province of Bulacan will mark
the anniversary of the inauguration of the First Philippine Republic which took place at
the Barasoain Church, Malolos, Bulacan;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Bulacan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7¢, day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
