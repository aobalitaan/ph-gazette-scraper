MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 721

DECLARING THURSDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 28 November 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Pifian,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 73 Founding
Anniversary.

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pifan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 28 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pifan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 997,q dayof october , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
