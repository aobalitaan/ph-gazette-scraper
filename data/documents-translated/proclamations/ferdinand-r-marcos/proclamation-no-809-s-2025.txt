MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 809

DECLARING FRIDAY, 14 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 14 March 2025, Friday, the City of Passi, Province of Iloilo will
mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary as a component city;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Passi be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
14 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Passi, Province of lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day ofFebruary, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

ATTY. LOVELY (NTOLE!
