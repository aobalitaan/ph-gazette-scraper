MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 987

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LUCENA

WHEREAS, on 20 August 2025, Wednesday, the City of Lucena will mark the
celebration of its 64°" Charter Anniversary (Araw ng Lucena);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lucena be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lucena.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this4th day ofAugust , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
