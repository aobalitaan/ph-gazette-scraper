MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 267

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DIPOLOG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2023, Saturday, the City of Dipolog, Province of
Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 110" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dipolog be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dipolog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd dayof June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
