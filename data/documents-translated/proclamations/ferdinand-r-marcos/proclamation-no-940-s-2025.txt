MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 940

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MARIA AURORA, PROVINCE OF AURORA

WHEREAS, on 21 July 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Maria Aurora,
Province of Aurora will mark the celebration of its 76" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maria Aurora be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
21 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maria Aurora, Province
of Aurora.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17thday of June | jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
