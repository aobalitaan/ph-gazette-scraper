MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 799

DECLARING THURSDAY, 06 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUTALAC, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 06 March 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Gutalac, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 46" Araw ng Gutalac;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gutalac be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 06 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gutalac,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this1 2thday of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

   

  

ATTY. LOVELY

ARO.
