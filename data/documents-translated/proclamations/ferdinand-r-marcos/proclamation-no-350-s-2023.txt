MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 350

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 20 October 2023, Friday, the Province of Negros Oriental will
mark the celebration of the Buglasan Festival:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Negros Oriental be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
20 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
