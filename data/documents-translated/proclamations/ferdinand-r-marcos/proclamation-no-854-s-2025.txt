MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 854

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF UYUGAN, PROVINCE OF BATANES

WHEREAS, on 20 May 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Uyugan, Province
of Batanes will mark the celebration of its Foundation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Uyugan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Uyugan,
Province of Batanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof Aprilj}inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
