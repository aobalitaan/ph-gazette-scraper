MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1012

DECLARING FRIDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIQUIJOR, PROVINCE OF SIQUIJOR

WHEREAS, on 12 September 1972, President Ferdinand E. Marcos signed
Proclamation No. 1075 declaring the Municipality of Siquijor as the capital of the
Province of Siquijor;

WHEREAS, on 12 September 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Siquijor will
mark the celebration of its 53°? Anniversary as the capital of the Province of Siquijor;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Siquijor be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
12 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Siquijor,
Province of Siquijor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this4th day of September in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
