BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 851

DECLARING MONDAY, 05 MAY 2025, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAPANGAN, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 05 May 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Kapangan, Province
of Benguet will commemorate the birth anniversary of Governor Bado Dangwa;

WHEREAS, Bado Dangwa was born in Kapangan, Benguet on 05 May 1905,
served as First Lieutenant and Major in the Philippine Army during the Second World
War, and as governor of Benguet from 1953 to 1963;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kapangan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
05 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kapangan, Province
of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof April , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Five.
