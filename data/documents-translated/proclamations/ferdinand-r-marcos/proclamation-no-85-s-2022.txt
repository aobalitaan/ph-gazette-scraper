MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. :g5

DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, on 22 November 2022, Tuesday, the City of Palayan, Province of
Nueva Ecija, will mark the celebration of its Annual Thanksgiving Day for a bountiful
harvest;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 22 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan,
Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
