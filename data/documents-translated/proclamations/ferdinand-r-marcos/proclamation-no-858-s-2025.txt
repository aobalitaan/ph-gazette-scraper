MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 858

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF NATONIN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 21 April 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Natonin, Mountain
Province will celebrate the First Sas-alliwa Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Natonin be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
21 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Natonin, Mountain
Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
