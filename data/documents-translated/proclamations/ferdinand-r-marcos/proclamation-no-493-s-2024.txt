MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 493

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 218 (S. 2002) BY TRANSFERRING
THE OBSERVANCE OF THE “ACCOUNTANCY WEEK” FROM THE
THIRD WEEK OF JULY TO THE THIRD WEEK OF AUGUST OF
EVERY YEAR

WHEREAS, over the years, the accountancy profession in the Philippines has
consistently upheld and advanced the highest standards of professionalism and
ethics, and has significantly contributed to the nation's development, particularly in the
fields of business, finance, and trade;

WHEREAS, Act No, 3105 formally recognized the accountancy profession in
the Philippines on 17 March 1923;

WHEREAS, amending earlier issuances, Proclamation No. 218 (s. 2002) set
the Accountancy Week from the fourth week of July to the third week of July to provide
a common celebration for all CPAs from different sectoral organizations;

WHEREAS, there is a need to amend Proclamation No. 218 and move the
observance of the Accountancy Week to the third week of August of every year to
encourage broader engagement and ensure greater participation from all CPAs across
different sectors;

WHEREAS, Section 2 of Republic Act No 9298 or the “Philippine Accountancy
Act of 2004” declares it a State policy to develop and nurture competent, virtuous,
productive and well-rounded professional accountants whose standard of practice and
service shall be excellent, qualitative, world class and globally competitive through
inviolable, honest, effective, and credible licensure examinations, and through
regulatory measures, programs and activities that foster their professional growth and
development; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the

power of control over all the executive departments, bureaus and offices and the
mandate to ensure the faithful execution of laws;

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

NOW, THEREFORE, |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
do hereby declare the third week of August of every year as “Accountancy Week.”

For this purpose, the Board of Accountancy, under the Professional Regulation
Commission, together with relevant CPA organizations, shall lead the observance of
the Accountancy Week in the Philippines.

All other agencies and instrumentalities of the National Government, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations and state universities and colleges, are
hereby enjoined, and all local government units, non-government organizations,
professional associations, and the private sector, are hereby encouraged to actively
participate and render the necessary support and assistance to the Board of
Accountancy for the effective implementation of this Proclamation.

Proclamation No. 218 (s. 2002) is hereby repealed. All other issuances, orders,
rules and regulations, or parts thereof that are inconsistent with this Proclamation, are
hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 11th day of March , in the

year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.
