MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 624

DECLARING THURSDAY, 01 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GENERAL LUNA, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of General Luna,
Province of Surigao del Norte will mark the celebration of the Adlaw Nan Heneral
Luna;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
General Luna be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 01 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of General
Luna, Province of Surigao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 54, day of July _» in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
