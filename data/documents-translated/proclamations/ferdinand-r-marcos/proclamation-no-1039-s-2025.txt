MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1039

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUPI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 17 October 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Lupi, Province of
Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Lupi
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
17 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lupi, Province of
Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day ofSeptember , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
