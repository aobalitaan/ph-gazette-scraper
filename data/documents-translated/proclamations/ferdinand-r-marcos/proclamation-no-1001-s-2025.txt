MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1001

DECLARING THURSDAY, 28 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN AGUSTIN, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON

WHEREAS, on 28 August 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of San Agustin,
Province of Romblon will mark the celebration of the Kalipayan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Agustin be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 28 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Agustin, Province of Romblon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, the 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

       
     
    

LUCAS P-BERSAMIN
Executive Sécretary
