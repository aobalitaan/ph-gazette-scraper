MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1071

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, the Province of Eastern Samar will mark the celebration of its 60"
Founding Anniversary on 09 November 2025, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Eastern Samar to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Governor requested that 10 November
2025, Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 09 November
2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Eastern
Samar be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
10 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Eastern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th dayof October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
