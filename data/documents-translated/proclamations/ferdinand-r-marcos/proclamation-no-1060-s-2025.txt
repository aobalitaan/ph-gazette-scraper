MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1060

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Gumaca was established in 1582 under the
patronage of San Diego de Alcala whose feast day is on 12 November;

WHEREAS, 12 November is recognized as the municipality's founding date;

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon, will mark the celebration of its 443" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, i, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 12 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this13th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
