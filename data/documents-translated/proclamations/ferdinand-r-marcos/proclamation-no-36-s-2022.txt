MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 36

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 16 August 2022, Tuesday, the City of Tuguegarao, Province
of Cagayan, will mark the celebration of the Pavvurulun Afi Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 16 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao,
Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

MALACANANG RECORDS OFFICE
