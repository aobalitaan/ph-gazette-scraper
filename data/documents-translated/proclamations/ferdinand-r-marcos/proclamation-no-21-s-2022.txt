MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 21

DECLARING TUESDAY, 30 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISULAN, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Isulan, Province
of Sultan Kudarat, will mark the celebration of its 65" Founding Anniversary and the
9" Hamungaya Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Isulan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with

appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 30 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Isulan,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of July , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
