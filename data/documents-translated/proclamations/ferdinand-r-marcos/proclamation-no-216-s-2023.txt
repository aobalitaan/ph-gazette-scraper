MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 216

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ZAMBOANGUITA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Zamboanguita,
Province of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its town fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Zamboanguita be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 15 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Zamboanguita, Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of May, in the year of Our !.ord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
