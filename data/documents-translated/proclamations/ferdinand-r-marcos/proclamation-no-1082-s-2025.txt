MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1082

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBA, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 11 December 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Tuba,
Province of Benguet will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tuba be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 11 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tuba,
Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day ofNovember , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
