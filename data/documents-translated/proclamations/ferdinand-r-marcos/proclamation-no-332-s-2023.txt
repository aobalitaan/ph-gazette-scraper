MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 332

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF BOMBON, CANAMAN, CALABANGA,
CAMALIGAN, GAINZA, LIBMANAN, MAGARAO, MILAOR, MINALABAC,
OCAMPO, PASACAO, PAMPLONA, PILI, SAN FERNANDO, AND BULA

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 654 dated 25 September 2013 which amended
Proclamation 776, s. 1991 declared every Friday immediately preceding the feast of
Our Lady of Pefiafrancia as a special day in the City of Naga;

WHEREAS, considering the significant economic, cultural and historical ties
between Naga City and its neighboring municipalities, the inclusion of the
Municipalities of Bombon, Canaman, Calabanga, Camaligan, Gainza, Libmanan,
Magarao, Milaor, Minalabac, Ocampo, Pasacao, Pamplona, Pili, San Fernando, and
Bula in the coverage of the Pefiafrancia Festival would bring immense benefits to
their communities.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipalities of Bombon,
Canaman, Calabanga, Camaligan, Gainza, Libmanan, Magarao, Milaor, Minalabac,
Ocampo, Pasacao, Pamplona, Pili, San Fernando, and Bula.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
