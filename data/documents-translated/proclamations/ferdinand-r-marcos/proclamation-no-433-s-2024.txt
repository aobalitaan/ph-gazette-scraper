MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 433

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 31 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 31 January 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Lian,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its Liberation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lian be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 31 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Lian, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of January __, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four
