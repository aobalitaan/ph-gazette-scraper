MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 732

DECLARING FRIDAY, 27 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUMINGAG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
SUR

WHEREAS, on 27 December 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Dumingag,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur will celebrate the Araw ng Dumingag;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dumingag be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
27 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dumingag,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 54, day of oyvember ; in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
