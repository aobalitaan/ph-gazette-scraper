MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1144

DECLARING TUESDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2026, Tuesday, the City of Bifian, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its 815 Liberation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bilan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 03 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bihan, Province
of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19thday of January , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

ATTY. LOVELY V. TOLENTINO-NAVA
