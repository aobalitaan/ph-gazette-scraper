MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 213

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MACO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Maco, Province of
Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 56" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maco be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 17 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maco,
Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5thday of May , in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Three

By authority of the President:
