MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 102

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2022, Friday, the City of Bayawan, Province of
Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its 22"4 Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayawan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
23 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan, Province of
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of Decemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
