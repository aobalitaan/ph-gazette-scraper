MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 252

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 19 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 19 July 2023, Wednesday, the City of Digos, Province of
Davao del Sur will celebrate the Padigosan Festival, in commemoration of its
creation as a municipality;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 19 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Province
of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof June _, inthe year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
