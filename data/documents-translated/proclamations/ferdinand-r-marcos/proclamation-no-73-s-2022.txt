MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 73

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 04 November 2022, Friday, the Province of Quezon will mark
the commemoration of the death anniversary of its local hero, Apolinario dela Cruz,
also known as Hermano Puli;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
04 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
