MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1141

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 16 February 2026, Monday, the Municipality of Taytay,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Pasasalamat: _HAMAKA
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taytay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
16 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Taytay, Province
of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

NELY V. TOLENTINO-NAVA
DIRECTOR IV |
