MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 391

DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIRAC, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Virac, Province of Catanduanes will be
celebrating the Kaaldawan nin Virac on 07 December 2023.

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Virac be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 07 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Virac, Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
