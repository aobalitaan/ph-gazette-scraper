MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 829

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 01 April 2025, Tuesday, the City of Tanjay, Province of Negros
Oriental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanjay be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanjay, Province of
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

   
 
   
  

    

LUCAS P. BERSAMIN

Contive Seraary
