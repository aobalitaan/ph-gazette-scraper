MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 132

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SARRAT, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Sarrat,
Province of Ilocos Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sarrat be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 28 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Sarrat, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this20th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
