MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 555

DECLARING SATURDAY, 15 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, the volcanic eruption of Mt. Pinatubo on 15 June 1991, the
second-largest volcanic eruption in the 20' century, caused massive destruction and
adversely affected the Provinces of Zambales, Pampanga, Bataan, Tarlac, and
Nueva Ecija;

WHEREAS, in commemoration of the 33 anniversary of the Mt. Pinatubo
eruption and to ensure that the lessons and values learned from the volcanic
eruption live on and are never forgotten by the people of the Province of Pampanga,
the Office of the Governor requested that 15 June 2024 be declared a special (non-
working) day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Pampanga be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 15 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
