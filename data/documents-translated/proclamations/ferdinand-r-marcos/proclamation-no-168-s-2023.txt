MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 168

DECLARING SUNDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2023, Sunday, the City of Zamboanga will mark
the celebration of its 86" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Zamboanga
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Sunday,
26 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24thday of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
