MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 410

DECLARING THURSDAY, 04 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 04 January 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Naujan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its 119‘ Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naujan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 04 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
