MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1174

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2026, Monday, the City of Davao will mark the
celebration of its Araw ng Pasasalamat in relation to the 89th Araw ng Davao;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
146 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thday of February , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:
