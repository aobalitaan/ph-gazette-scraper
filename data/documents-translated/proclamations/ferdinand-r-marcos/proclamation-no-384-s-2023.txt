MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 384

DECLARING MONDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)

DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE
ORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Davao de Oro will be
celebrating its 86" Founding Anniversary and the 21st PASAKA Festival on 13
November 2023;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Pantukan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 13 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pantukan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
