MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 690

DECLARING SATURDAY, 12 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE. MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 12 October 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Pamplona,
Province of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of the Yamog Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pamplona be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 12 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pamplona, Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of Septembazin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
