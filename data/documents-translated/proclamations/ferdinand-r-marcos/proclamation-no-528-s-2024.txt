MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 528

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ

WHEREAS, the City of Roxas, Province of Capiz will mark the
commemoration of the date of the organization of its government or Roxas City Day
on 12 May 2024;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Roxas to fully participate in
the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod requested that 10 May 2024, Friday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 12 May 2024 which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Roxas be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 10 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Roxas, Province of
Capiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
