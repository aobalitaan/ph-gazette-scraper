MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 979

DECLARING TUESDAY, 12 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CANDONI, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 12 August 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Candoni,
Province of Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 64" Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Candoni be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 12 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Candoni,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18thdayof July _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
