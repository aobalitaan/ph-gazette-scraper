MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 745

DECLARING THURSDAY, 19 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIPLAHAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 19 December 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Diplahan,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Diplahan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 19 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Diplahan, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay. ,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
