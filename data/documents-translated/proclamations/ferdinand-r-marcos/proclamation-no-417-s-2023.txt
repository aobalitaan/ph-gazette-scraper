MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 417

DECLARING MONDAY, 29 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANTANGAN, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Tantangan was created through Executive
Order No. 415 which was signed on 27 January 1961;

WHEREAS, on 27 January 2024, the Municipality of Tantangan, Province of
South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 63 Founding Anniversary and the
14h Kulitangtang Festival;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Tantangan to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 29 January 2024,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 27 January 2024
which falls on a Saturday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tantangan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 29 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tantangan, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three .

By authority of the President:
