MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 892

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALETE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Balete, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of its 56'* Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Balete be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Balete,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May __, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
