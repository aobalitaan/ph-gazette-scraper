MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 882

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 11 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 11 June 2025, Wednesday, the Province of Rizal will mark the
celebration of its 124" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Rizal
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 11 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

the President
RECORDS OFFICE
