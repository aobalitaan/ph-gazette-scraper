MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 228

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MALABON

WHEREAS, the City of Malabon will mark the celebration of its 424th
Founding Anniversary on 21 May 2023;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Malabon to celebrate and
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 22 May 2023,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 21 May 2023 which
falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Malabon
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 22 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Malabon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

0
UeAS P. BERSAMI
ecutive Secretary
