MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 87

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF M’LANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 02 December 2022, Friday, the Municipality of M’lang,
Province of Cotabato will mark the celebration. of its annual festivity called the
Kawayanan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
M'lang be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
02 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of M’lang,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
