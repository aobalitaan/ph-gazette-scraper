MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 537

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SARIAYA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Sariaya,
Province of Quezon will mark the celebration of the Agawan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sariaya be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 May 2024, a special (non-working). day in the Municipality of
Sariaya, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
