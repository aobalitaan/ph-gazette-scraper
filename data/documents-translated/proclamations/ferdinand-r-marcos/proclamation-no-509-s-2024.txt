MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 509

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA CRUZ, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 01 April 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Santa Cruz,
Province of Occidental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its 75" Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santa Cruz be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 01 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santa
Cruz, Province of Occidental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
