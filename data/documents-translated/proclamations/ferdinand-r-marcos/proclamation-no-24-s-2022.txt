MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 24

‘>

DECLARING TUESDAY, 02 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF KABANKALAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 02 August 2022, Tuesday, the City of Kabankalan, Province
of Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 25" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Kabankalan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government; .

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 02 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Kabankalan,
Province of Negros Occidental. ;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th dayof July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
