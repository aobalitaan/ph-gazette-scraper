MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 711

DECLARING TUESDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2024, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2024, Tuesday, the City of Valenzuela will mark
the celebration of its 401st Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Valenzuela
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 12 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22ndday of3Ocxberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
