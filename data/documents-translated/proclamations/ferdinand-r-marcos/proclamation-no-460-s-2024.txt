MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 460

DECLARING THURSDAY, 08 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
5 DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TIAONG, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, Claro M. Recto, a former Associate Justice of the Supreme
Court, former Senator and the author of the Rizal Law, was born on 08 February
1890 in Tiaong, Province of Quezon;

WHEREAS, on 08 February 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Tiaong,
Province of Quezon will commemorate the birth anniversary of Claro M. Recto;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tiaong be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 08 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tiaong, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of January _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
