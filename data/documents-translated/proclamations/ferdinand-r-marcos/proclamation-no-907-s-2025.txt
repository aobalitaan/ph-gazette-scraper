MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 907

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of San Fernando,
Province of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 59'" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Fernando be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Fernando, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thgayof May, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
