MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 365

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ITOGON, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 22 November 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Itogon,
Province of Benguet will mark the celebration of its 123" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Itogon be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Itogon, Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
