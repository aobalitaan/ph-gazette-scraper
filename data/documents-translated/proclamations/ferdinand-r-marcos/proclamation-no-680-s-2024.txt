MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 680

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGRAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, Josefa Llanes-Escoda, a World-War II heroine and the founder of the
Girl Scouts of the Philippines was born on 20 September 1898 in Dingras, Ilocos Norte;

WHEREAS; on 20 September 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Dingras, Province
of Ilocos Norte, will commemorate the birth anniversary of Josefa Llanes-Escoda;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Dingras
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by authority
of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday, 20
September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dingras, Province of
Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of September, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
