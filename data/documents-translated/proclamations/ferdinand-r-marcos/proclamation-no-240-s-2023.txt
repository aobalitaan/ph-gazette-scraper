MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 240

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Pamplona, Province
of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its 73'4 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pamplona be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 16 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pamplona,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of May  ,in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
