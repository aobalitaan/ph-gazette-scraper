MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 545

DECLARING 15 MAY 2024 (WEDNESDAY), AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GIGMOTO, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Gigmoto,
Province of Catanduanes, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gigmoto be given full opportunity to participate in these occasions and enjoy the
celebrations;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 May 2024, as. special (non-working). day in the Municipality of
Gigmoto, Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14thday of Ma __, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
