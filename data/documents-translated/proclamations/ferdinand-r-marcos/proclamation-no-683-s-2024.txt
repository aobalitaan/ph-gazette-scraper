MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 683

DECLARING MONDAY, 07 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIPOS, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
SUR

WHEREAS, on 07 October 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Guipos, Province
of Zamboanga del Sur, will mark the celebration of the 33" Araw ng Guipos;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Guipos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
07 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Guipos, Province
of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 thday of Sept embg,in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
