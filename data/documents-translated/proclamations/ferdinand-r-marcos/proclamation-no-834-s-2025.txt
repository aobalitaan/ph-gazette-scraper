MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 834

DECLARING MONDAY, 07 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 07 April 2025, Monday, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Mountain Province be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
07 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this20th day of March , in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office pf the President
