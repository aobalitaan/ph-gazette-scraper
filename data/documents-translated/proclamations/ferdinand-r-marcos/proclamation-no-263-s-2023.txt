MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 263

DECLARING THURSDAY, 24 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CATBALOGAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 24 August 2023, Thursday, the City of Catbalogan, Province
of Samar will mark the celebration of the Manaragat Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Catbalogan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 24 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Catbalogan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
