MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 566

DECLARING FRIDAY, 14 JUNE 2024, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on Friday, 14 June 2024, the Province of Ilocos Sur will
commemorate the victory of the Filipinos over the Imperial Japanese Army in the
Battle of Bessang Pass during World War II;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ilocos
Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
14 June 2024, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of llocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof June __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

CAS P. BERSAMIN
