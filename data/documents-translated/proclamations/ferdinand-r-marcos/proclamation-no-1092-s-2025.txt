MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1092

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 18 December 2025, Thursday, the City of Tuguegarao,
Province of Cagayan will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tuguegarao
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 18 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao,
Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12thday of November in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
