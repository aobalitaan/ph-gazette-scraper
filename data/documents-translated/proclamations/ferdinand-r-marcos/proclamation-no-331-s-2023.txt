MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 331

DECLARING THURSDAY, 14 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE

ORO

WHEREAS, on 14 September 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Monkayo,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 69" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Monkayo be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 14 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality: of
Monkayo, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of Septemberin the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
