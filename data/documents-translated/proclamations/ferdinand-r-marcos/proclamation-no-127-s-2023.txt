MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 127

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECA

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2023, Friday, the City of Cabanatuan, Province
of Nueva Ecija will mark the celebration of its 73 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cabanatuan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
03 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19thday of January | jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
