MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 153

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 05 May 2023, Friday, the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its 83° Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
05 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
