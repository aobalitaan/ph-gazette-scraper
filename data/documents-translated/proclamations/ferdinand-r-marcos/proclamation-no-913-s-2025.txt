MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 913

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF NEW BATAAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of New Bataan,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of New
Bataan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of New
Bataan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this28th dayof May _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
