MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 170

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 08 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 08 March 2023, Wednesday, the Province of Davao de Oro
will mark the celebration of its 25'" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Davao de Oro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 08 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Davao
de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of Fébruarin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
