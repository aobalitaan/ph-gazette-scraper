MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1108

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 17 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 17 December 2025, Wednesday, the City of Baliwag, Province
of Bulacan will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Baliwag be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Wednesday, 17 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Baliwag,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12thday of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
