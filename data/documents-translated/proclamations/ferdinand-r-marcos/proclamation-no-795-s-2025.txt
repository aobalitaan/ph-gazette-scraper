MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 795

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 12 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 12 February 2025, Wednesday, the City of Kidapawan,
Province of Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 27" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Kidapawan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 12 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Kidapawan,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
