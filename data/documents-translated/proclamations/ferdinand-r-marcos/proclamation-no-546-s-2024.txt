BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 546

DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND, LOCATED AT BARANGAY
JAVALERA, CITY OF GENERAL TRIAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE,
FOR INCLUSION INTO THE EXISTING GATEWAY BUSINESS
PARK-SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC
ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or
the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon
the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority (PEZA), |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the Philippines, do
hereby designate a parcel of land, located at Barangay Javalera, City of General
Trias, Province of Cavite, for inclusion into the existing Gateway Business Park-
Special Economic Zone, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its
Implementing Rules and Regulations, and PEZA Board of Directors Resolution No.
23-078 (s. 2023), with an area of Five Thousand One Hundred Eleven square
meters (5,111 m2), more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
