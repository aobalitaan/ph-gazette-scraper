MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1036

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 03 October 2025, Friday, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of the Provincial Indigenous People’s Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Mountain Province be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
03 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
