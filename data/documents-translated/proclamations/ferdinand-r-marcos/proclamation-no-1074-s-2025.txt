MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1074

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AROROY, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 17 November 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Aroroy,
Province of Masbate will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aroroy be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
17 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Aroroy, Province
of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of November | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
