MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 691

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIBUTU, PROVINCE OF TAWI-TAWI

WHEREAS, on 21 October 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Sibutu, Province
of Tawi-Tawi will mark the celebration of its creation as a municipality;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sibutu be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
21 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sibutu, Province
of Tawi-Tawi.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of Septetber, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
