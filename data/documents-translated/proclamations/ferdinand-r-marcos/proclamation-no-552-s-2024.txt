MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 552

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF BINGAWAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Bingawan, Province
of lloilo will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary and the Pagnahi-an
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bingawan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
21 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bingawan, Province
of lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of May _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
