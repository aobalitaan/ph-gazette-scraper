MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 965

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Compostela,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 77 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Compostela be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
01 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Compostela,
Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thdayof July _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
