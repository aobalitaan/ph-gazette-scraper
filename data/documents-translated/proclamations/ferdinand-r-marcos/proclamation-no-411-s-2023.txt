MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 411

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUDIN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on 05 January 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Tagudin,
Province of Ilocos Sur will mark the celebration of its 438" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tagudin be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
05 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tagudin,
Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
