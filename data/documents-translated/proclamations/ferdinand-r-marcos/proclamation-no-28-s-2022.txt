MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 28

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 19 August 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Angono, Province
of Rizal, will mark the signing of its independence as a municipality from the
municipality of Binangonan, Rizal;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
19 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Angono, Province
of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28ctndayof july _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
