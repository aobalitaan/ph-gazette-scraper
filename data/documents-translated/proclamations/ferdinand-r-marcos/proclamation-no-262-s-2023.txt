MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 262

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAPALONG, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Kapalong,
Province of Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of its 75% Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kapalong be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Kapalong, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15thdayof June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
