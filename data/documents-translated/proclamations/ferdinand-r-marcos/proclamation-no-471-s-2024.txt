MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 471

DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2024, Thursday, the Province of Basilan will mark
the celebration of its 50 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Basilan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 07 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February . in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
