MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 845

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 30 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 30 April 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Calatagan,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Cultural Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calatagan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 30 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Calatagan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st dayof March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
