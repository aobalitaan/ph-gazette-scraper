MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1066

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 NOVEMBER 2025, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DASMARINAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 26 November 2025, Wednesday, the City of Dasmarifas,
Province of Cavite will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dasmarifias
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 26 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Dasmarifas, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of October | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
