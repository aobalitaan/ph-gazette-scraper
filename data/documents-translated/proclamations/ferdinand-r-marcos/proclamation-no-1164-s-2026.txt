MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1164

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 10 March 2026, Tuesday, the City of Tanauan, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of its 25" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanauan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 10 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanauan, Province
of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thday of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:
