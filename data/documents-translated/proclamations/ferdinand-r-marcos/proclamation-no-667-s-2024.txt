MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 667

DECLARING THURSDAY, 29 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KUMALARANG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 29 August 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Kumalarang,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur will mark the celebration of the Padul-Os Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Kumalarang be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 29 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Kumalarang, Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2145¢ day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
