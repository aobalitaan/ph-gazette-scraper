MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 296

DECLARING SATURDAY, 19 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 19 August 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal will mark the signing of its independence as a municipality from the
Municipality of Binangonan, Rizal;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 19 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Angono, Provirce of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of July _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
