MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 853

DECLARING FRIDAY, 09 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 09 May 2025, Friday, the Municipality of San Jose, Province of
Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Charter Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Jose be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
09 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose, Province
of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof April , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
MALACANANG RECORDS OFFICE
