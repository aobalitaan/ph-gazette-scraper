MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 654

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 16 September 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bontoc be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
16 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 dayof August __, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
