MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 61

DECLARING FRIDAY, 07 OCTOBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 07 October 2022, Friday, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of the Provincial Indigenous Peoples Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Mountain Province be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
07 October 2022, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
