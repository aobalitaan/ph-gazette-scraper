MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 990

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 05 September 2025, Friday, the City of General Santos,
Province of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of the 27" Tuna Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
05 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of General Santos,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
