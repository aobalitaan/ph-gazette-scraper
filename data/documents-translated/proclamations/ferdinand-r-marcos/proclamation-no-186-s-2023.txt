MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 186

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CANLAON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 20 March 2023, Monday, the City of Canlaon, Province of
Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Pasayaw Festival 2023;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Canlaon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 20 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Canlaon,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

UCAS P BERSAMIN,
