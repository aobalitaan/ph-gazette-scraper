MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1080

DECLARING THURSDAY, 04 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VINTAR, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 04 December 2025 Thursday, the Municipality of Vintar,
Province of Ilocos Norte will celebrate Vintar Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Vintar be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 04 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Vintar, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
