MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 862

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUCBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Lucban, Province
of Quezon will mark the celebration of the Pahiyas Festival 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lucban be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lucban,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15thdayof April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
