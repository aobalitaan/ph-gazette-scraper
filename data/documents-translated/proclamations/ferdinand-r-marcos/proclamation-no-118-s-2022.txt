MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 118

DECLARING THURSDAY, 12 JANUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENCIA, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 12 January 2023, Thursday, the City of Valencia, Province of
Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 22"? Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City Valencia be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 12 January 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valencia,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd _ day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two
