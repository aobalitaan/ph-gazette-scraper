MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 414

DECLARING FRIDAY, 12 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF VALENCIA, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 12 January 2024, Friday, the City of Valencia, Province of
Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 23" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City Valencia be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
12 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valencia, Province of
Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three .

By authority of the President:
