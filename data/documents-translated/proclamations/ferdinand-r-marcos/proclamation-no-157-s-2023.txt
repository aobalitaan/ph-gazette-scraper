MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 157

DECLARING MONDAY, 27 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS, on 27 February 2023, Monday, the City of General Santos will
mark the celebration of its 84" Founding Anniversary and the 34¢ Kalilangan
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 27 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of General
Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of Februaryn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
