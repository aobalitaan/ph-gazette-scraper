MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 865

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SUAL, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 20 May 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Sual, Province of
Pangasinan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Sual
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sual,
Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th dayof April | in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
