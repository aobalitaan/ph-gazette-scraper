MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 747

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. NINO, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sto. Nifio, Province of South Cotabato will
celebrate its 44° Founding Anniversary and the 25". Hinublag Festival on Monday, 23
December 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality, of Sto.
Nifio be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
23 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sto. Nifo,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
