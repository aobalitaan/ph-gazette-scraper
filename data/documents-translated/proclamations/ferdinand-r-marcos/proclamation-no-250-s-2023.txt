MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 250

DECLARING SATURDAY, 15 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2023, Saturday, the Cordillera Administrative Region
(CAR) will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the CAR be given full
opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 15 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the CAR.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5thdayof June _ , inthe year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
