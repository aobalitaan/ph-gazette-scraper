MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 27

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOLANA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 18 August 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Solana,
Province of Cagayan, will mark the celebration of its 1718t Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Solana be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 18 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Solana,
Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28taday of July _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two. ;
