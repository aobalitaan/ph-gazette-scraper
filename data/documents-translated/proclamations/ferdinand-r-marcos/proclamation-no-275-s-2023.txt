MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 275

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE
ORO

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Compostela,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 75" Founding Anniversary
and the 17" Buganihan Festival:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Compostela be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Compostela, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th dayof June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
