MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 261

DECLARING SATURDAY, 24 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MANILA

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2023, Saturday, the City of Manila will mark the
celebration of its 452" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Manila be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 24 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
