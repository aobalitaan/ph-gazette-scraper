MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 498

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 05 April 2024, Friday, the Province of Pangasinan will mark
the celebration of its 444'" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Pangasinan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
05 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of Maléh , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
