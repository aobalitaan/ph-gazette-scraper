MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 160

DECLARING FRIDAY, 31 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN

WHEREAS, on 31 March 2023, Friday, the City of Sipalay, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 22" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
31 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sipalay, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of Februayjn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
