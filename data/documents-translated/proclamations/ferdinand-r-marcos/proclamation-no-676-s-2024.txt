MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 676

DECLARING MONDAY, 30 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MASBATE, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 30 September 2024, Monday, the City of Masbate, Province of
Masbate will mark the celebration of its 24th Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Masbate be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
30 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th dayof August  , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

   

ucps’ PB R

Executive Secretary
