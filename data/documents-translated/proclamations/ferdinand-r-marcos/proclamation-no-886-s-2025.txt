MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 886

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUIRINO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Quirino, Province
of Isabela will mark the celebration of its 58" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Quirino be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Quirino,
Province of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of ™@y | in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
