MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 286

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTIAGO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2023, Tuesday, the City of Santiago, Province of
Isabela will mark the celebration of the Aggaw na Naturales Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago, Province
of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof July __, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
