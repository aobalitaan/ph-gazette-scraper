MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1054

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 25 November 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Maragusan,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Maragusan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maragusan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Maragusan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day ofOctober , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
