MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 31

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 26 September 2022, Monday, the Municipality of Naval,
Province of Biliran, will mark the celebration of its162"? Pueblo Day (Charter Day);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naval be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 26 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Naval, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23 dayof yu1y __. in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
