MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 605

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOLANA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Solana, Province of Cagayan will mark the
celebration of its 173 Founding Anniversary on 18 August 2024, which falls on a

Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Solana to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 16 August 2024,
Friday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 18 August 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Solana be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
16 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Solana, Province
of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thdayof June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
