MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 830

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 02 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BATAAN

WHEREAS, on 02 April 2025, Wednesday, the Province of Bataan will
commemorate the birth anniversary of Francisco “Balagtas” Baltazar, a prominent
Filipino poet and orator;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Bataan
be given full opportunity to remember the important contributions of Francisco
“Balagtas” Baltazar to the Filipino people;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 02 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bataan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
