MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 125

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 JANUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE

WHEREAS, on 25 January 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Carigara,
Province of Leyte will mark the celebration of its 452" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carigara be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 January 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Carigara, Province of Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

 

  
  

ATTY. CONCEPCIO FERROLINO-ENAD
