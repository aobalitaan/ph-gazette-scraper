MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 644

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GASAN, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 20 August 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Gasan, Province
of Marinduque will mark the celebration of its 415" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gasan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND. R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gasan,
Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of August, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
