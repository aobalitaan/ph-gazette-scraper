MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 212

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 22 May 2023, Monday, the City of Gingoog, Province of
Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of its Lubi-Lubi Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 22 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog, Province
of Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof May _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
