MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 789

DECLARING THURSDAY, 06 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONSOLACION, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 06 February 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Consolacion,
Province of Cebu will mark the celebration of its 105" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Consolacion be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 06 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Consolacion, Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
