MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESiDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 221

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALETE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Balete,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its 54" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Balete be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Balete,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th dayof May __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
