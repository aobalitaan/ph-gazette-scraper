MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 12

DECLARING TUESDAY, 26 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 26 July 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Ipil, Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its 73 Araw ng Ipil;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Ipil
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 26 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ipil,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13a day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
