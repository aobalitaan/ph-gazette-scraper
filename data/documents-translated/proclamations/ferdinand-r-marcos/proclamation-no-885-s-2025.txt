MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 885

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MACO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Maco, Province of
Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 58" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maco be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maco,
Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

   

the President
