MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 772

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian, Province of Zamboanga del Sur will celebrate
its annual fiesta dubbed as the Pasalamat Festival 2025 from 13-20 January 2025;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Pagadian to participate in the
occasion and enjoy the celebration, the Office of the Mayor requested that 20 January
2025, Monday, be declared a special (non-working) day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
20 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian, Province of
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
