MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 415

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN PEDRO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 29 December 2023, Friday, the City of San Pedro, Province
of Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 10" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pedro
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
29 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pedro, Province
of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day ofDecember _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
