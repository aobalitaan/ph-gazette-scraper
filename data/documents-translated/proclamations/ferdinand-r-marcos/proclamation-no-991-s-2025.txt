MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 991

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL

WHEREAS, on 08 September 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Loon,
Province of Bohol will celebrate the SidlaKasilak Festival or the Festival of Lights;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Loon
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
08 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Loon, Province
of Bohol.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
