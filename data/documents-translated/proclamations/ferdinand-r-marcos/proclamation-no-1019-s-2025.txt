MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1019

DECLARING MONDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN RAFAEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 29 September 2025, Monday, the Municipality of San Rafael,
Province of Bulacan, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Rafael be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
29 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Rafael,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of Septembezin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
