MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 729

DECLARING SUNDAY, 27 JULY 2025, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY

WHEREAS, on 27 July 2025, Sunday, the Iglesia ni Cristo will celebrate its
Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, in order to give the members of Iglesia ni Cristo the full opportunity
to participate in the occasion, 27 July 2025, Sunday, may be declared as a special
(non-working) day throughout the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Sunday,
27 July 2025, a special (non-working) day throughout the country.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
