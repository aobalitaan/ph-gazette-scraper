MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION No. 859

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF INFANTA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Infanta, Province of
Quezon will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Infanta be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Infanta, Province of
Quezon.

-IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
