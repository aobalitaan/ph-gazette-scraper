MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 1063

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Tanay,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of the 15"° Hane Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 12 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tanay, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th dayof October, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
