BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 37

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF NAGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, Jesse M. Robredo served as Secretary of the Department of the
Interior and Local Government from 2010 to 2012, and was instrumental in the
implementatién of far-reaching reforms in local governance;

WHEREAS, Secretary Robredo served with utmost dedication as Mayor of
Naga City from 1988 to 1998 and from 2001 to*2010;

WHEREAS, his outstanding performance as Mayor of Naga City earned him
numerous awards such as the Magsaysay Award for Government Service in 2000,
the first Filipino Mayor to be conferred such distinction;

WHEREAS, Secretary Robredo, considered one of the local heroes of Naga
City, passed away in a tragic plane accident on 18 August 2012;

WHEREAS, his many virtues as a public servant have permanently earned
him the gratitude of the City he led, and the Republic he served;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Naga be
given full opportunity to honor the memory of Secretary Robredo through appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 18 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Naga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
