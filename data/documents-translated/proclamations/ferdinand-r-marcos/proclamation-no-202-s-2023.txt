MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 202

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 21 April 2023, Friday, the City of Calamba, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its 224 Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
21 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
