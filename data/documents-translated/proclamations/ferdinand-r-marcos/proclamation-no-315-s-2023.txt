MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 315

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL

WHEREAS, on 08 September 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Loon,
Province of Bohol will celebrate the SidlaKasilak Festival or the festival of lights
displaying the rich and unique cultural heritage of the town;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Loon be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
08 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Loon,
Province of Bohol.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
