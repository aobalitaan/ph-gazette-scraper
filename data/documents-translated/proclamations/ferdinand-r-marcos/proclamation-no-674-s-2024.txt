MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 674

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 17 September 2024, Tuesday, the Province of Zamboanga del
Sur will mark the celebration of its 72"4 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy
the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of August _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
