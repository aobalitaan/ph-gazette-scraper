MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1107

DECLARING 7 DECEMBER 2025 AS A DAY OF NATIONAL
MOURNING OVER THE PASSING OF MANLILIKHA NG BAYAN
ALONZO AYATU SACLAG

WHEREAS, Alonzo Ayatu Saclag was declared a Manlilikha ng Bayan in 2000,
in recognition of his lifelong dedication to safeguarding, promoting, and enriching the
Kalinga cultural practices, including musical traditions, ritual movements, and dance
patterns intrinsic to the community's identity;

WHEREAS, Manlilikha ng Bayan Alonzo Ayatu Saclag passed away on 29
November 2025 at the age of 83, and his Necrological Services and State Funeral are
scheduled on 7 December 2025 at Awichon Cultural Village, Kalinga;

WHEREAS, the death of Manliliknha ng Bayan Alonzo Ayatu Saclag is a great
loss to our nation, and it is appropriate to honor his outstanding commitment as a
master of Kalinga music and dance, and his contributions in the preservation of
indigenous cultural expressions; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 27(k), Chapter 1, Rule 3 of the Implementing
Rules and Regulations of Republic Act No. 8491 or the “Flag and Heraldic Code of the
Philippines,” the Philippine flag shall be flown at half-mast as a sign of mourning on all
buildings and places where it is displayed, on the day of interment of recipients of
national orders and decorations;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
do hereby declare 7 December 2025 as a Day of National Mourning over the passing
of Manlilikha ng Bayan Alonzo Ayatu Saclag.

Consistent with existing laws, the national flag shall be flown at half-mast from
sunrise to sunset, on all government buildings and installations throughout the
Philippines and abroad, on 7 December 2025.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
