MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 703

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 25 October 2024, Friday, the Municipality of San Isidro,
Province of Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of the Sikwate Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Isidro be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Isidro,
Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this gth day of October, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
