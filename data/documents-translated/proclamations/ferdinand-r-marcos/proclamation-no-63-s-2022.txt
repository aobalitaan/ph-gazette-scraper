MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 63

DECLARING SATURDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Gumaca was established in 1582 under the
patronage of San Diego de Alcala whose feast day is on 12 November;

WHEREAS, 12 November is recognized as the municipality's founding date;

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2022, Saturday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon, will mark the celebration of its 440 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 12 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of Septemberin the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
