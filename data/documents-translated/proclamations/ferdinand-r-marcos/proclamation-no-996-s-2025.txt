MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 996

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 03 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 03 September 2025, Wednesday, the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of its 57" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of lriga be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 03 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this! 1th day of August , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
