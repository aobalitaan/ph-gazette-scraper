MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 766

DECLARING THURSDAY, 09 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MANILA

WHEREAS, the Feast of the Black Nazarene will be celebrated on 09 January
2025, Thursday;

WHEREAS, millions of devotees are expected to flock the Quiapo Church and
the City of Manila;

WHEREAS, in order to ensure orderly procession of devotees and to facilitate
the flow of traffic, the City of Manila requests that Thursday, 09 January 2025 be
declared a special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 09 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day ofJanuarin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five
