MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 35

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 25 August 2022, Thursday, the City of Iloilo will mark the
celebration of its 85'" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iloilo be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this gth dayof august, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

MALACANANG RECORDS OFFICE
