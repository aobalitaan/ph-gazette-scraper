MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 34

DECLARING SATURDAY, 20 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LUCENA

WHEREAS, the City of Lucena was created by virtue of Republic Act No.
3271 dated 17 June 1961;

WHEREAS, the City of Lucena was formally inaugurated as a Chartered City
on 20 August 1961;

WHEREAS, on 20 August 2022, Saturday, the City of Lucena will mark the
celebration of its 61St Charter Anniversary (Araw ng Lucena);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lucena be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 20 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lucena.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
