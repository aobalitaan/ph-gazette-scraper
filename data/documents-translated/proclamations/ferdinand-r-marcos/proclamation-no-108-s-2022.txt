MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 108

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 11929 which was approved on 30 July 2022
provides for the conversion of the Municipality of Baliwag in the Province of Bulacan
into a component city to be known as the City of Baliwag;

WHEREAS, Commission on Elections Resolution No. 1 0834, as amended by
Resolution No. 10849, set the date of the plebiscite to ratify the conversion of the
Municipality of Baliwag into a component city on 17 December 2022.

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Baliwag the
opportunity to actively and fully participate in the plebiscite and exercise their right to
vote, it is but fitting and proper to declare 17 December 2022 as a special (non-
working) day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 17 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Baliwag, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 42th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

MALACARANG RECORDS OFFICE
