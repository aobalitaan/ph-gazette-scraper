MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY-THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 707

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE
ORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Davao de Oro will be
celebrating its 87 Founding Anniversary and the 22" PASAKA Festival on 13
November 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pantukan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 13 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pantukan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
