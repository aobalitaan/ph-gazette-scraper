MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 369

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 20 October 2023, Friday, the City of Batac, Province of Ilocos
Norte, will mark the 157" birth anniversary of General Artemio Ricarte, a general
during the Philippine Revolution and Philippine-American War who was born in
Batac, Ilocos Norte on 20 October 1866;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
20 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac, Province of Ilocos
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
