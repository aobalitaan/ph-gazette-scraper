MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1070

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUNA, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 25 November 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Luna,
Province of La Union will mark the celebration of its town fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Luna
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Luna,
Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of October in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
