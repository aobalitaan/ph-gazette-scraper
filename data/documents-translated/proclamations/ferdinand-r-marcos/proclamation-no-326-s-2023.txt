MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 326

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LEMERY, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 25 October 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Lemery,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lemery be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Lemery, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of August, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
