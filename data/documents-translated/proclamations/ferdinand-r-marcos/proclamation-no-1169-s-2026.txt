MALACANAN PALACE 
MANILA 
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES 
PROCLAMATION NO. 116 9 
DECLARING SATURDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) 
DAY IN THE CITY OF TANGUB, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL 
WHEREAS, on 28 February 2026, Saturday, the City of Tangub, Province of 
Misamis Occidental will mark the celebration of its 58th Charter Anniversary; 
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub be 
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration; 
NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by 
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare 
Saturday, 28 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tangub, 
Province of Misamis Occidental. 
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of 
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. 
Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, 
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
