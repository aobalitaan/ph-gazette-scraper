MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 874

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF CALACA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 23 May 2025, Friday, the City of Calaca, Province of Batangas
will mark the celebration of the birth anniversary of General Eleuterio Marasigan, a
great Calaquefio, patriot, and one of the leaders during the libertarian movement in
the Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calaca be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
23 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calaca, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th dayof April | in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
