MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 852

DECLARING THURSDAY, 08 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT ROXAS, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 08 May 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of President Roxas,
Province of Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 58" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
President Roxas be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 08 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of President
Roxas, Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof April , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
MALACANANG BE
