MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 9g

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of the Higantes Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 23 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Angono, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this21st day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
