MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 507

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAMALAYAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Pinamalayan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of the Bahaghari Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pinamalayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pinamalayan, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
