MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 451

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACO, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 25 January 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Baco,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baco be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Baco,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
