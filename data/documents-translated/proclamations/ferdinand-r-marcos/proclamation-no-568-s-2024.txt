MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 568

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Molave, Province of Zamboanga del Sur will
mark the celebration of its 76'" Founding Anniversary on 16 June 2024, which falls
on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Molave to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 17 June 2024,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 16 June 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Molave be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 17 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Molave,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
