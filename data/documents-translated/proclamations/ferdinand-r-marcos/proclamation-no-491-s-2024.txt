MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 491

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 MARCH 2024, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2024, Friday, the City of Baliwag, Province of
Bulacan, will mark the celebration of the 161° birth anniversary of Mariano Ponce;

WHEREAS, Mariano Ponce, a native of Baliwag, Bulacan, was a prominent
member of the propaganda movement and one of the leading figures in the
Philippine reform movement during the Spanish colonization;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Baliwag be
given full-opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
22 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Baliwag, Province of
Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
