MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 577

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF LAUREL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Laurel, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of its 55"" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Laurel be. given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by .
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
21 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Laurel, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
