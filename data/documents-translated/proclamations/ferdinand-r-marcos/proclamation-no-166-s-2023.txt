MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 166

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 05 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 05 April 2023, Wednesday, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Mountain Province be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 05 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this23rd day of Februaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
