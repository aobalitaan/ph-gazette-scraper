MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA J

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 246

DECLARING TUESDAY, 06 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAMBAN, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 06 June 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Bamban,
Province of Tarlac will commemorate the proclamation of Bamban as the
Revolutionary Capital of the Philippines by General Emilio Aguinaldo;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bamban be given full opportunity to kindle a sense of history and awareness.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 06 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bamban,
Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
