MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 209

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 24 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TITAY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 24 May 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Titay, Province
of Zamboanga Sibugay will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Titay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 24 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Titay,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this27th day of April , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
