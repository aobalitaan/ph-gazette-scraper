MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 908

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF GUINAYANGAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 20 June 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Guinayangan,
Province of Quezon will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Guinayangan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
20 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Guinayangan,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thdayof May | inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

   

  

ATTY. LOVELY V. TOLENTINO-NAVA
