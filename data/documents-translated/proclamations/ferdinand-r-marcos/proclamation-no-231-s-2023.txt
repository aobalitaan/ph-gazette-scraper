MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIJENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCI.AMATION NO. 2314

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINGAWAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Bingawan,
Province of Iloilo will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary and its
Pagnahi-an Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Bingawan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bingawan, Province of lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nddayof May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
