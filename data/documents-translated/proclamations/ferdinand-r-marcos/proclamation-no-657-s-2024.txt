MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 657

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 26 September 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Naval,
Province of Biliran will mark the celebration of its 164" Pueblo Day (Charter Day);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naval be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Naval, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 0g dayof August , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
