MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 986

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 19 August 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Angono, Province
of Rizal will commemorate the signing of its independence as a municipality from the
Municipality of Binangonan, Rizal;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof August , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
