MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 564

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2024, Monday, the Province of Masbate will
celebrate the Bagat-Dagat Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 24 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th dayof May _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
