MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 934

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MARAMAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Maramag, Province
of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 69t Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maramag be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 July 2025, a special (non working) day in the Municipality of Maramay,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
