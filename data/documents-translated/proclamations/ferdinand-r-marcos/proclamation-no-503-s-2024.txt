MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 503

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 07 April 2024, Mountain Province will mark the celebration of
its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of Mountain Province to fully participate in
the occasion, the Office of the Governor requested that 08 April 2024, Monday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 07 April 2024 which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Mountain Province be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 08 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
