MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 445

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian, Province of Zamboanga del Sur will mark
the celebration of its annual fiesta dubbed as the Pasalamat Festival 2024 from 12-
22 January 2024;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Pagadian to participate in the
occasion and enjoy the celebration, the Office of the Mayor requested that 22
January 2024, Monday, be declared a special (non-working) day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 22 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four. :

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
