MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 422

DECLARING SATURDAY, 20 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 January 2024, Saturday, the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of the Coffee Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 20 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
