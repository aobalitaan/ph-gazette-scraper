MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 982

DECLARING MONDAY, 04 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 04 August 2025, Monday, the City of Cabuyao, Province of
Laguna will mark the celebration of its 13" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabuyao be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
04 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabuyao, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24thday of July , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
