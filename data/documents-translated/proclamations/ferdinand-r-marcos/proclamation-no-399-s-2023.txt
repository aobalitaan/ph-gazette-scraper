MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 399

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AROROY, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 17 November 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Aroroy,
Province of Masbate will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aroroy be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
17 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Aroroy,
Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
