MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 56

DECLARING THURSDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN RAFAEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 29 September 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of San
Rafael, Province of Bulacan will mark the celebration of its 272™4 Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Rafael be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 29 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
San Rafael, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
