MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 436

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 16 January 2024, Tuesday, the City of Batangas will mark the
celebration of its city fiesta, a day the residents come together to celebrate their
heritage and their shared identity as Batanguefios;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batangas
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 16 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
