BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 303

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCEL
OF LAND WHERE IT STANDS, LOCATED ALONG FLORES
AVENUE, BARANGAY PIAPI, DUMAGUETE CITY, NEGROS
ORIENTAL, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION
TECHNOLOGY CENTER), PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO.
7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or
the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon
the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority (PEZA), |, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the Philippines, do
hereby create and designate a building, to be known as Marina Town Dumaguete,
with a gross floor area of Eleven Thousand Two Hundred Forty-Six and 48/100
square meters (11,246.48 m?), more or less, and the parcel of land where the
building stands, with an area of Three Thousand Seven Hundred Eighty-Five
square meters (3,785 m2), more or less, as a Special Economic Zone (Information
Technology Center), located along Flores Avenue, Barangay Piapi, Dumaguete City,
Negros Oriental, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its
Implementing Rules and Regulations, PEZA Board of Directors Resolution No. 00-
411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011), 15-240 (s. 2015)
and 17-472 (s. 2017), and Resolution No. 17-272 (s. 2017), as defined by the
following technical description:

SK-07-000310

A parcel of land (SK-07-000310, portion of Lot 163, Dumaguete
Cadastre and Lot 171-B, Psd-07-096534), located at Barangay of Piapi,
City of Dumaguete, Province of Negros Oriental, Island of Negros.

Bounded on the SW., along line 1-2 by Portion of Lot 163, Dumaguete
Cadastre; on the SW., along line 2-3 by Lot 171-B, Psd-07-096534; on
the NW., along line 3-4 by Lot 171-A, Psd-07-096534; on the NE., along
lines 4-5-6 by Lot 171-C, Psd-07-096534 (Flores Avenue); on the SE.,
along line 6-7 by Lot 170, Dumaguete Cadastre; on the SE., along line 7-
1 by portion of Lot 163, Dumaguete Cadastre.
