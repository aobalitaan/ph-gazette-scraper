MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 398

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA BARBARA, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 17 November 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Santa Barbara,
Province of Iloilo will commemorate the anniversary of the Cry of Santa Barbara;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santa Barbara be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 17 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santa
Barbara, Province of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of Novembex in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
