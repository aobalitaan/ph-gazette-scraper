MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 58

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGRAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
NORTE

WHEREAS, Josefa Llanes-Escoda, a World-War II heroine and the founder of
the Girl Scouts of the Philippines was born on 20 September 1898 in Dingras, llocos
Norte;

WHEREAS, on 20 September 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Dingras,
Province of Ilocos Norte, will mark the commemoration of the birth anniversary of
Josefa Llanes-Escoda;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dingras be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Dingras, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
