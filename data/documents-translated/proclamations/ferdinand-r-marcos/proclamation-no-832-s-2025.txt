MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 832

DECLARING THURSDAY, 03 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALANGIGA, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 03 April 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Balangiga,
Province of Eastern Samar will mark the celebration of its 1718t Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Balangiga be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 03 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Balangiga,
Province of Eastern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th dayof March , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
