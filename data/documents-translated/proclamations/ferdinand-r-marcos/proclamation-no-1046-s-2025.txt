MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1046

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAPUYAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
SUR

WHEREAS, on 16 October 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Lapuyan,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur will mark the celebration of its 68‘ Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lapuyan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Lapuyan, Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this30th day of September , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
