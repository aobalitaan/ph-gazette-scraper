MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 631

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVER, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 13 September 2024 Friday, the Municipality of Claver, Province
of Surigao del Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary (Adlaw Nan
Claver);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Claver be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
13 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Claver,
Province of Surigao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4 4 4, day of July _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
