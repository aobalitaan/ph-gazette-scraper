MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 489

DECLARING SATURDAY, 16 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSELLER T. LIM, PROVINCE OF
ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Roseller T. Lim,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay will mark the celebration of its 42" Founding
Anniversary; °

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Roseller T. Lim be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 16 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Roseller
T. Lim, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
