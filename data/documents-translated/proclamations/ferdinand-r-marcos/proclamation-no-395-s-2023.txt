MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 395

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 18 December 2023, Monday, the City of Tuguegarao,
Province of Cagayan will mark the celebration of its 24" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 18 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao,
Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
