MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 208

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Gumaca, Province
of Quezon will mark the celebration of the Araw ng mga Magsasaka at Arafia at
Baluarte Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 15 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
