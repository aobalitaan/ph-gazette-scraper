MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. £3

DECLARING THURSDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL

WHEREAS, on 08 September 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Loon,
Province of Bohol, will mark the celebration of SidlaKasilak Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Loon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 08 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Loon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of August, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
