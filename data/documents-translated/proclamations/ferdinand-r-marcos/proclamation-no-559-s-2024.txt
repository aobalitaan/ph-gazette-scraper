MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 559

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MACO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Maco, Province of
Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 57 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maco be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 17 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maco,
Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thdayof May __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
