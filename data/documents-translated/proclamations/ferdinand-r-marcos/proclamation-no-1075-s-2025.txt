MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1075

DECLARING THURSDAY, 27 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DIPACULAO, PROVINCE OF AURORA

WHEREAS, on 27 November 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Dipaculao,
Province of Aurora will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dipaculao be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 27 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Dipaculao, Province of Aurora.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of Novembexin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
