MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1078

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAAL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Taal, Province
of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its traditional Parada ng mga Kabayo/Tiburin
Race;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Taal
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 11 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Taal,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
