MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 432

DECLARING SATURDAY, 16 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUGAIT, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Lugait, Province
of Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of its Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lugait be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 16 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lugait,
Province of Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three .

By authority of the President:

LUGAS P. BERSAMI
Executivé Secretary
