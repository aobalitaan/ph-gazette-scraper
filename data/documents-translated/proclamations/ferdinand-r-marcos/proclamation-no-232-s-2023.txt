MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 232

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALAPATAN, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Malapatan,
Province of Sarangani will mark the celebration of its 54‘ Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malapatan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Malapatan, Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22ndday of May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
