MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 105

DECLARING THURSDAY, 05 JANUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUDIN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on 05 January 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Tagudin,
Province of Ilocos Sur will mark the celebration of its 437 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tagudin be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 05 January 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
