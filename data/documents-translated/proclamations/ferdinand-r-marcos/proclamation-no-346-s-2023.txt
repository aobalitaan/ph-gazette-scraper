MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 346

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ILIGAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 29 September 2023, Friday, the City of Iligan will mark the
celebration of its city fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iligan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
29 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iligan, Province of
Lanao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
