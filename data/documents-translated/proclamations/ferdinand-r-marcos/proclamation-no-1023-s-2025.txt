MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1023

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONDRAGON, PROVINCE OF NORTHERN
SAMAR

WHEREAS, the formation of the pueblo of Mondragon from visitas of Guinay
and Marquivalo was approved through Royal Order No. 759 on 22 September 1883;

WHEREAS, on 22 September 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Mondragon,
Province of Northern Samar, will mark the celebration of its 142°¢ Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mondragon be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
22 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mondragon,
Province of Northern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
