MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 52

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 17 September 2022, Saturday, the Province of Zamboanga
del Sur will mark the celebration of its 70 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the
national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 17 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
