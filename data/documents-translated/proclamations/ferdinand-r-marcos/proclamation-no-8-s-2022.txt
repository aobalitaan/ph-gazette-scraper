MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 08

DECLARING THURSDAY, 21 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MABALACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 21 July 2022, Thursday, the City of Mabalacat, Province of
Pampanga, will mark the celebration of its Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabalacat
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 21 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat,
Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the yeax of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
