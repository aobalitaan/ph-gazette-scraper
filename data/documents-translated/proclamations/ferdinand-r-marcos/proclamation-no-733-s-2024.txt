MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 733

DECLARING SATURDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL.

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of the Higantes Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 23 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Angono, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
