MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1170

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 MARCH 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN.
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUTALAC, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 06 March 2026, Friday, the Municipality of Gutalac, Province of
Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 47 Araw ng Gutalac;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gutalac be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
06 March 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gutalac, Province
of Zamboanga de! Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day ofFebruary, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
