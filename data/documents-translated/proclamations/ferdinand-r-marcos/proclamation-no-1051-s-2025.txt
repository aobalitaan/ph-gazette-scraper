MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1051

DECLARING TUESDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 04 November 2025, Tuesday, the Province of Quezon will
commemorate the death anniversary of its local hero, Apolinario Dela Cruz, also
known as Hermano Puli;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Quezon
be given full opportunity to honor the heroism and patriotism of Hermano Puli through
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 04 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
