MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 810

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYUG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 17 March 2025, Monday, the Municipality of Tayug, Province
of Pangasinan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tayug be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
17 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tayug, Province of
Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25thday of February jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

MALACAN
