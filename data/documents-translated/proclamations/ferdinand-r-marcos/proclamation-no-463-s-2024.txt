MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 463

DECLARING MONDAY, 12 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 11 February 2024, the City of Talisay, Province of Negros
Occidental will mark the celebration of its 26" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Talisay to fully participate in
the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 12 February 2024, Monday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 11 February 2024 which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Talisay be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 12 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Talisay,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
