MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

~ BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1116

DECLARING TUESDAY, 27 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANTANGAN, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 27 January 2026, Tuesday, the Municipality of Tantangan,
Province of South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 65'" Founding Anniversary
and the 16" Kulitangtang Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tantangan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 27 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tantangan, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of Decembein the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
