MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 266

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN CARLOS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2023, Saturday, the City of San Carlos, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Carlos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Carlos,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21s¢ day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
