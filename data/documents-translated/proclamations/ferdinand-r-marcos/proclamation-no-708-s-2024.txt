MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 708

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABUCGAYAN, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Cabucgayan,
Province of Biliran will mark the celebration of its 75'"" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cabucgayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cabucgayan,
Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
