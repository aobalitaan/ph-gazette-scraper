MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 423

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 JANUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CADIZ, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 26 January 2024, Friday, the City of Cadiz, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its 50 Dinagsa Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
26 January 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
