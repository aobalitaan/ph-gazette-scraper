MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 574

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 19 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 19 June 2024, Wednesday, the City of Laoag, Province of
Ilocos Norte will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare .
Wednesday, 19 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag,
Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof June _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
