MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 340

DECLARING THURSDAY, 28 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALICIA, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Alicia, Province of Isabela was created
through Executive Order No. 268 which was signed on 28 September 1949;

WHEREAS, on 28 September 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Alicia,
Province of Isabela will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Alicia be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 28 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Alicia, Province of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
