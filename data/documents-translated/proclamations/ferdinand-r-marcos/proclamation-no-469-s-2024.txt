MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 469

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 06 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUTALAC, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 06 March 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Gutalac,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 45° Araw ng
Gutalac;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gutalac be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 06 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gutalac, Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
