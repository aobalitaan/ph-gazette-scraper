MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1129

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACOLOR, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 10 February 2026, Tuesday, the Municipality of Bacolor,
Province of Pampanga will mark the celebration of Bacolor Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bacolor be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 10 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bacolor, Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13thday of January in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

RECORDS OFFICE
