MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 798

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 21 February 2025, Friday, the Province of Marinduque will mark
the celebration of the Araw ng Marinduque;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Marinduque be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,

21 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
ORDS OFFICE
