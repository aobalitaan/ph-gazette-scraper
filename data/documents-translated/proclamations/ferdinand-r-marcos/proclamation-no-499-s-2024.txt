MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 499

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TARLAC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 19 April 2024, Friday, the City of Tarlac, Province of Tarlac
will mark the celebration of its 26" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
19 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th dayof March _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
