MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 161

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSELLER T. LIM, PROVINCE OF
ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Roseller T. Lim,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay will mark the celebration of its 41st Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Roseller T. Lim be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Roseller
T. Lim, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day ofFebruaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
