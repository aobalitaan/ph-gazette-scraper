MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 883

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF SILAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Silay, Province of Negros Occidental, will have its
annual fiesta celebration and cultural activities from 02 to 13 June 2025;

WHEREAS, on 13 June 2025, Friday, the City of Silay, Province of Negros
Occidental will mark the culmination of such momentous celebration;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Silay be
given full opportunity to participate and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
13 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Silay, Province of Negros
Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
