MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 151

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 29 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINANGONAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 29 March 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Binangonan,
Province of Rizal will mark the celebration of its 1234 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Binangonan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 29 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Binangonan, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day offebruaryin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
