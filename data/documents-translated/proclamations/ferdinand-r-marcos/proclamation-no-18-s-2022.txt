MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 18

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 10 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 10 August 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon, will mark the celebration of Lorenzo M. Tafiada Sr. Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 10 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, i have hereunto set my hand and caused the seai
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th dayof July _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
