MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 206

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF SANTIAGO

WHEREAS, on 05 May 2023, Friday, the City of Santiago will mark the
celebration of its 29" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
05 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thday of April __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
