MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 376

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 28 November 2023, Tuesday, the Province of Sarangani will
mark the celebration of its 318t Founding Anniversary and the 21st MunaTo Festival

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sarangani be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 28 November 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
