MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1117

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 16 January 2026, Friday, the City of Batangas will mark the
celebration of its city fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batangas
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
16 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16thday of December in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five .

By authority of the President:
