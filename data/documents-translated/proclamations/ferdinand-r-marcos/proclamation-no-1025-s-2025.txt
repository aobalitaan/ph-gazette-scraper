MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1025

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HUNGDUAN, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, on 01 October 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Hungduan,
Province of Ifugao will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Hungduan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 01 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Hungduan, Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
