MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 196

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF PILI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Pili, Province of
Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of its 68" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pili

be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,

16 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pili, Province of
Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of March , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
