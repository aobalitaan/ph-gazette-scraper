MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 242

DECLARING SATURDAY, 24 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2023, Saturday, the City of Tabaco, Province of
Albay will mark the celebration of its Tabak Festival, a showcase of the city’s rich
history, cultural diversity and beautiful tourist attractions;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 24 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco, Province
of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st dayof May _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
