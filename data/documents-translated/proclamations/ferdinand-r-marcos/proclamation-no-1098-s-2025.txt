MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1098

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 10 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MEYCAUAYAN, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 10 December 2025, Wednesday, the City of Meycauayan,
Province of Bulacan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Meycauayan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 10 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Meycauayan, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

ATTY. LOVELY V. TOLENTINO-NAVA
