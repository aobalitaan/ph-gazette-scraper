MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 172

DECLARING MONDAY, 06 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUTALAC, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 06 March 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Gutalac, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 44" Araw ng Gutalac;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gutalac be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 06 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gutalac,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of March , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
