MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 272

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 2023 AS THE NATIONAL AGRICULTURE
AND FISHERIES CENSUS MONTH

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 352 (s. 1996) mandates that the Census of
Agriculture and Fisheries (CAF) shall be conducted every ten (10) years, and designates
statistical activities that will generate critical data for decision-making of the government
and the private sector, and directs the maintenance/processing of administrative-based

records on a regular basis to allow for monitoring time series analysis and forecasting by
policy-makers;

WHEREAS, Section 6 of Republic Act No. 10625 or the “Philippine Statistical Act
of 2013" mandated the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) to prepare and conduct

periodic censuses on population, housing, agriculture, fisheries, business, industry and
other sectors of the economy;

WHEREAS, the 2022 CAF will be conducted in September 2023, with 01 January
to 31 December 2022 as census reference period;

WHEREAS, the 2022 CAF aims to come up with data on agricultural and fisheries
structures which will provide sampling frame for other statistical undertaking, and basic
data for use in national and local development planning; and

WHEREAS, recognizing the increasing importance of census data in meeting data
needs at the lowest geographic level, the usual assistance provided to the PSA, as in
previous censuses, will be instrumental to the success of the 2022 CAF;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, do hereby declare September 2023 as the “National Agriculture and Fisheries
Census Month.”

For this purpose, the PSA, under the National Economic and Development
Authority, shall lead the observance of the National Agriculture and Fisheries Census
Month.

All other agencies and instrumentalities of the National Government, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations and state universities and colleges, are
hereby directed, and all local government units, non-government organizations,
professional associations, and the private sector, are hereby encouraged to actively

participate and render the necessary support and assistance to the PSA for the effective
implementation of this Proclamation.

Covered persons and entities, including but not limited to individual citizens and
residents, government and private entities duly established and authorized to conduct
business in the Philippines, such as stock or non-stock corporations, partnerships and
cooperatives, among others, are hereby enjoined to cooperate with the PSA and its
census enumerators in the conduct of the census, by providing truthful and complete
information in response to census questions, subject to applicable laws, rules and
regulations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, ! have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.
