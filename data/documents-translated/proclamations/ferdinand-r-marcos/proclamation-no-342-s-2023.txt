MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 342

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 OCTOBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIGCAWAYAN, PROVINCE OF NORTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 20 October 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Pigcawayan,
Province of North Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 70 Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pigcawayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
20 October 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pigcawayan,
Province of North Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day ofSeptember, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

 

ATTY. CONCEPCION ZW E. PERROLINO-ERAD
