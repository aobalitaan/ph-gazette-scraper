MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1008

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUIRINO

WHEREAS, on 10 September 2025, Wednesday, the Province of Quirino will
mark the celebration of its 54" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Quirino
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 10 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Quirino.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day ofSeptember, jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
