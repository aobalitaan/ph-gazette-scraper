MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 9096

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JUNE 2025; A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BARLIG, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2025; Wednesday, the Municipality of Barlig, Mountain
Province will mark the celebration of its 59" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Barlig be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 June 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Barlig,
Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thdayof May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
