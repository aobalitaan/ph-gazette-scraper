MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 777

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 10 February 2025, Monday, the City of Laoag, Province of
llocos Norte will mark the celebration of the Pamulinawen Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
10 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag, Province of Ilocos
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
