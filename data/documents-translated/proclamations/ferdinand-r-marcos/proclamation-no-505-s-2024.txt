MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 505

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PLARIDEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 23'April 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Plaridel, Province
of Bulacan will mark the celebration of the “Battle of Quingua’”;

WHEREAS, the Battle of Quingua was fought on 23 April 1899 in Plaridel,
Bulacan, wherein the Filipino forces were under the command of General Gregorio
del Pilar, and was one of the most significant victories of the Filipinos in the
Philippine-American war;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Plaridel be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 23 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Plaridel,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th dayof March _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
