MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 581

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FRANCISCO, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2024, Friday, the Municipality of San Francisco, Province
of Agusan del Sur will mark the celebration of the Araw ng San Francisco;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Francisco be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by authority
of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday, 21 June
2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Francisco, Province of
Agusan del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
