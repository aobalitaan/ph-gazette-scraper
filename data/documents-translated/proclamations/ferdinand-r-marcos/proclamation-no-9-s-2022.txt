MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 09

DECLARING SATURDAY, 23 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 23 July 2022, Saturday, the City of Gingoog, Province of
Misamis Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 62"? Charter Day/43 Kaliga
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 23 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog, Province
of Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

DIRECTOR IV
