MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 688

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GLORIA, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 01 October 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of Gloria, Province
of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of the Kawayanan Festival:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gloria be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gloria,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 3tpday of September, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the Fresident:

Office of the President
