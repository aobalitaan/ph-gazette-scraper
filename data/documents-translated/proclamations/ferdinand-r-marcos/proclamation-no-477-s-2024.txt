MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 477

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LAS PINAS

WHEREAS, on 27 March 2024, Wednesday, the City of Las Pifias will mark
the celebration of its 117 Founding Anniversary and 27" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Las Pifias
be given full opportunity to participate in the foregoing occasions and enjoy their
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Las Pifias.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
