“MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 247

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOCORRO, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 22 June 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Socorro,
Province of Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Socorro be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Socorro,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
