MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 927

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JUNE 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CAGAYAN DE ORO

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 7670 declared 15 June of every year as
Cagayan de Oro City Charter Day and a special non-working holiday in the City of
Cagayan de Oro to commemorate the anniversary of the city;

WHEREAS, the City of Cagayan de Oro will mark the celebration of its
anniversary on 15 June 2025, which “alls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Cagayan de Oro to fully
participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 16 June 2025,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 15 June 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cagayan
de Oro be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 16 June 2025, a special (no1-working) day in the City of Cagayan de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.
