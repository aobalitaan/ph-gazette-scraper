MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 475

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 18 March 2024, Monday, the Province of Masbate will mark
the celebration of its 123" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 18 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
