MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 720

DECLARING THURSDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 28 November 2024, Thursday, the Province of Sarangani will
mark the celebration of its 32"? Foundation Anniversary and the 22" MunaTo Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sarangani be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 28 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
