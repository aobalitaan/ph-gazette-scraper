MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 91

DECLARING MONDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 28 November 2022, Monday, the Province of Sarangani will
mark the celebration of its 30° Founding Anniversary and the 20°" MunaTo Festival

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sarangani be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 28 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this11th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
