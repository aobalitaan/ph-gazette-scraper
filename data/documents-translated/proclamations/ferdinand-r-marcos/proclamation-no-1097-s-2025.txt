MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1097

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GATTARAN, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 25 November 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Gattaran,
Province of Cagayan will mark the celebration of the Panagsalukag Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gattaran be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gattaran, Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, the 21st day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
