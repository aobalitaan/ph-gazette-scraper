MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 392

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 15 December 2023, Friday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Occidental Mindoro will commemorate the 79" Liberation Day of
Mindoro Island from Japanese Occupation;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 15 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Jose, Province of Occidental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
