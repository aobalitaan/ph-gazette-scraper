MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 790

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CANDONI, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 11 February 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Candoni,
Province of Negros Occidental will celebrate the Dinagyaw sa Tablas Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Candoni be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 11 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Candoni, Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
MALACANANG RECORDS OFFICE
