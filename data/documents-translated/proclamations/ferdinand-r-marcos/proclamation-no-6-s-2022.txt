MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 06

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF CALABANGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Calabanga, Province
of Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calabanga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calabanga, Province
of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two. ®
