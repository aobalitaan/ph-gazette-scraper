MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 606

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 22 August 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Liloy, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 73’? Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Liloy
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Liloy,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th dayof June , in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
