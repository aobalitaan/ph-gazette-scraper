MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 783

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Buug,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary
and BOG Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Buug
be given full opportunity to participate in the foregoing occasions and enjoy their
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 26 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Buug, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23;qday of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
