MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1120

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 January 2026, Tuesday, the City of Lipa, Province of Batangas
will mark the celebration of the Coffee Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be given
full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Tuesday,
20 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day ofDecember, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five
