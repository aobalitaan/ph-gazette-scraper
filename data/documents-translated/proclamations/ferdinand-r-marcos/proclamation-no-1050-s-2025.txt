MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1050

DECLARING THURSDAY, 30 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 30 October 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of San Isidro,
Province of Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of the Sikwate Festival:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Isidro be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 30 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Isidro, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
