MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. i106

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 JANUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 January 2023, Tuesday, the City Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 83 Founding Anniversary and the
Hinugyaw Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 10 January 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two
