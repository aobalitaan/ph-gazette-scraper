MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1037

DECLARING TUESDAY, 07 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIPOS, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
SUR

WHEREAS, on 07 October 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Guipos,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur, will mark the celebration of the 34** Araw ng Guipos;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Guipos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 07 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Guipos,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of September , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
