MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 637

DECLARING MONDAY, 05 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CALBAYOG

WHEREAS, Jose Avelino, who was born on 05 August 1890 in Calbayog,
Western Samar, served as the first Senate President after the Second World War;

WHEREAS, on 05 August 2024, Monday, the City of Calbayog will
commemorate the birth anniversary of Jose Dira Avelino;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calbayog
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 05 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calbayog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1gth dayof july _, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
