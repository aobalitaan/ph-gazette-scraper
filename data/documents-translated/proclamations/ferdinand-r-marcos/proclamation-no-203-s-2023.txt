MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 203

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA CATALINA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Santa Catalina,
Province of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Town Fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santa Catalina be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santa
Catalina, Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
