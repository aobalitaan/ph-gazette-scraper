MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 487

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2024, Friday, the City of Malaybalay, Province of
Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 26" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Malaybalay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
22 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Malaybalay, Province of
Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th — day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
