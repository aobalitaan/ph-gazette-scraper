MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 524

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SUAL, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 20 May 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Sual, Province of
Pangasinan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sual be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 20 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sual,
Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this15th day of April, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
