MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1158

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PARANAQUE

WHEREAS, on 13 February 2026, Friday, the City of Parafiaque will mark the
celebration of its 28" Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Parafiaque
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
13 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Parafiaque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of February in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

  
 

Acting Execytive Secretary
