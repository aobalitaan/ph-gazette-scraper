MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 110

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 DECEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 20 December 2022, Tuesday, the City of Sipalay, Province of
Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of the Pasaway Handurawan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 December 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sipalay,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 3thday of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

  
     
   

CAS PSRERSAMIN
