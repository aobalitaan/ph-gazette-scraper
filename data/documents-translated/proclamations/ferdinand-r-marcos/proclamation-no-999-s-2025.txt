MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 999

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MINALIN, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 27 August 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Minalin,
Province of Pampanga will mark the celebration of its 411 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Minalin be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Minalin, Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
