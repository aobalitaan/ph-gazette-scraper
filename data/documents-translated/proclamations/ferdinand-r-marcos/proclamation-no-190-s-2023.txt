MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 190

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 MAY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF BUTUAN

WHEREAS, on 19 May 2023, Friday, the City of Butuan will mark the
celebration of its Balangay Festival:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Butuan be

given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
19 May 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Butuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of March __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
