MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 16

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 03 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 03 August 2022, Wednesday, the City of Tacurong, Province
of Sultan Kudarat, will mark the celebration of its 718t Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 03 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26ta dayof July __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
