MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 501

DECLARING THURSDAY, 04 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAAK, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 04 April 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Laak, Province of
Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 45"" Founding Anniversary,

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Laak be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 04 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Laak,
Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
