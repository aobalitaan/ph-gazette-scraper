MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 447

DECLARING SATURDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACOLOR, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 10 February 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Bacolor,
Province of Pampanga will mark the celebration of its Foundation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bacolor be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 10 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bacolor, Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th dayof January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
