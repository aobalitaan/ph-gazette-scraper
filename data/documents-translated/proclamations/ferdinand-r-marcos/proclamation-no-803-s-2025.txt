MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 803

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 21 March 2025, Friday, the City of Calapan, Province of
Oriental Mindoro will mark the celebration of its 27° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calapan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
21 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calapan, Province of
Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12ttday offebruary, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
