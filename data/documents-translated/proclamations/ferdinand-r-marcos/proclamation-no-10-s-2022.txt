MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 10

DECLARING MONDAY, 25 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2022, Monday, the City of Tanjay, Province of Negros
Oriental, will mark the celebration of the Sinulog sa Tanjay Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanjay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 25 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanjay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this gth day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
