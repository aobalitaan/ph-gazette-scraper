MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 682

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 04 October 2024, Friday, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of the Provincial Indigenous People’s Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Mountain Province be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
04 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of September , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
