MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 572

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF NEW BATAAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2024, Tuesday, the Municipality of New Bataan,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
New Bataan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by .
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 18 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of New
Bataan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of June _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
