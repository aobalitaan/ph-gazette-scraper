MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 838

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 30 APRIL 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN FRANCISCO, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 30 April 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of San Francisco,
Province of Quezon will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San
Francisco be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 30 April 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Francisco, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of March , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
