MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 289

DECLARING FRIDAY, 14 JULY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF TAYABAS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 14 July 2023, Friday, the City of Tayabas, Province of Quezon
will mark the celebration of its 16" Cityhood Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tayabas
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
14 July 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tayabas, Province of
Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
