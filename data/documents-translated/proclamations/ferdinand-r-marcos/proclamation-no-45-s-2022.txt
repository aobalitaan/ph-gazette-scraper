MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 45

DECLARING MONDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIQUIJOR, PROVINCE OF SIQUUOR

WHEREAS, on 12 September 1972, President Ferdinand E. Marcos signed
Proclamation No. 1075 declaring the Municipality of Siquijor as the capital of the
Province of Siquijor;

WHEREAS, on 12 September 2022 (Monday), the Municipality of Siquijor will
mark the celebration of the 50" Anniversary of this historical day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Siquijor be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 12 September 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Siquijor, Province of Siquijor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th dayof August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

MALACARANG RECORDS OFFICE
