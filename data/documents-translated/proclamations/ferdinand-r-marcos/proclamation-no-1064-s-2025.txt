MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1064

DECLARING THURSDAY, 20 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUPAX DEL SUR, PROVINCE OF NUEVA
VIZCAYA

WHEREAS, on 20 November 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Dupax del
Sur, Province of Nueva Vizcaya will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dupax del Sur be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 20 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Dupax del Sur, Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of October _ jn the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
