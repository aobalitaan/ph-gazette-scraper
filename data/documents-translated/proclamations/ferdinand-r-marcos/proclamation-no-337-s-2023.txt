MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 337

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOBO, PROVINCE OF
BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 27 September 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Lobo,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lobo be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Lobo, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day ofseptember, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
