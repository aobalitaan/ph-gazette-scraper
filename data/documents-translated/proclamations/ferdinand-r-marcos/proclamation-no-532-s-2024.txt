MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 532

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 22 May 2024, Wednesday, the City of Gingoog, Province of
Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of the Lubi-Lubi Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog,
Province of Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
