MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 857

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF BUTUAN

WHEREAS, on 19 May 2025, Monday, the City of Butuan will mark the
celebration of the Balangay Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Butuan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare 19 May
2025, Monday, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Butuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
