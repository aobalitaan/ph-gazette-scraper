MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRES!DENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 953

DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 07 August 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Rosario,
Province of La Union will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rosario be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 07 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario,
Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof July _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
