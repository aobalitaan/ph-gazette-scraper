MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 971

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 22 August 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Liloy, Province of
Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 74" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Liloy
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
22 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Liloy, Province of
Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th dayof July , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
