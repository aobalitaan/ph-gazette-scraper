MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.1138

DECLARING TUESDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2026, Tuesday, the City of Cabanatuan, Province
of Nueva Ecija will mark the celebration of its 76° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabanatuan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 03 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabanatuan,
Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15ttday of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
