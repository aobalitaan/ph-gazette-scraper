MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 284

DECLARING FRIDAY, 11 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 11 August 2023, Friday, the Province of Samar will
commemorate its declaration as a province;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of

Samar be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 11 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
