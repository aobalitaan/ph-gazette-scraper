MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 988

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF DANGCAGAN, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 29 August 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Dangcagan,
Province of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dangcagan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
29 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dangcagan,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof August , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

   

RSAMIN
ecretary
