MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 960

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Kawit, Province of
Cavite will mark the celebration of Kawit Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kawit be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
01 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kawit, Province of
Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
