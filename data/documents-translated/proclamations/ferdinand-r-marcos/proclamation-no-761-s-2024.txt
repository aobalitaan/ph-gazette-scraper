MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 761

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS, on 16 January 2025, Thursday, the City of Navotas will mark the
celebration of its 119" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Navotas be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of Decembein the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
MALACANANG RECORDS OFFICE
