MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 141

DECLARING MONDAY, 06 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONSOLACION, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 06 February 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Consolacion,
Province of Cebu will mark the celebration of its 103"? Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Consolacion be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 06 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Consolacion, Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January . in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
