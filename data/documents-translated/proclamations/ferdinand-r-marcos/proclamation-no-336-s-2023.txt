MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 336

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 SEPTEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, PROVINCE OF
BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 27 September 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas, will mark the 158" birth anniversary of General Miguel Malvar,
one of the most recognized leaders of the Philippine Revolution and the Philippine-
American War who was born in Sto. Tomas, Batangas on 27 September 1865;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malvar be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 September 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Malvar, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
