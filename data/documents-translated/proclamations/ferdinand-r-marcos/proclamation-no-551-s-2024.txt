MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 551

DECLARING THURSDAY, 13 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SILAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Silay, Province of Negros Occidental, will have its
annual fiesta celebration and cultural activities from 03 to 13 June 2024;

WHEREAS, on 13 June 2024, Thursday, the City of Silay, Province of Negros
Occidental will mark the culmination of such momentous celebration;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Silay be
given full opportunity to participate and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Thursday, 13 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Silay, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
