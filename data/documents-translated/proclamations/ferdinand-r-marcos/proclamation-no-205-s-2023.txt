MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 205

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2023, Tuesday, the City of Isabela, Province of
Basilan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Isabela be

given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Tuesday, 25 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Isabela, Province
of Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
