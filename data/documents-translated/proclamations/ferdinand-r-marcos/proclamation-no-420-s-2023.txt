MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 420

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBA, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 11 December 2023, Monday, the Municipality of Tuba,
Province of Benguet will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tuba be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the

celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 11 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tuba, Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
