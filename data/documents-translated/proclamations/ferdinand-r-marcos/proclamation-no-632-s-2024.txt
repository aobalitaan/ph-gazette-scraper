MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 632

DECLARING SATURDAY, 14 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE
ORO

WHEREAS, on 14 September 2024, Saturday, the Municipality of Monkayo,
Province of Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 70" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Monkayo be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 14 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Monkayo, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14 th day of July inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
