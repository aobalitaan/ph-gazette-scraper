MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 954

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 13 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BOMBON, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES
SUR

WHEREAS, on 13 August 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Bombon,
Province of Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bombon be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 13 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bombon, Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
