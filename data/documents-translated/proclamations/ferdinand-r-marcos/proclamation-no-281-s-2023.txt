MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 281

DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 22 August 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Liloy, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte will mark the celebration of its 72"4 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Liloy be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 22 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Liloy,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
