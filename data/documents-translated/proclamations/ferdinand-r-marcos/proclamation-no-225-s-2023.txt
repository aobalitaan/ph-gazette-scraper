MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 225

PROCLAMATION DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BOGO, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2023, Friday, the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu will
mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bogo be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
16 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16thday of May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
