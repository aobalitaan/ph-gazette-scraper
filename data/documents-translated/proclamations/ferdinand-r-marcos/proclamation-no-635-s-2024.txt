MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 635

DECLARING MONDAY, 02 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, the Province of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 110"
Founding Anniversary on 01 September 2024, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Bukidnon to fully participate
in the occasion, the Office of the Governor requested that 02 September 2024,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 01 September 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bukidnon be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
02 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th dayof July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
