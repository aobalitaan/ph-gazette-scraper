MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 522

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 MAY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon will mark the celebration of the Araw ng mga Magsasaka at
Arafia at Baluarte Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Gumaca be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 May 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of April, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
