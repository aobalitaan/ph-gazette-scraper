MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 78

DECLARING FRIDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYSAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Taysan,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 104! Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taysan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
11 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Taysan,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of october, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
