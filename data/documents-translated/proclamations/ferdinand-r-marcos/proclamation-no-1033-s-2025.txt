MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1033

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NARVACAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on 23 October 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Narvacan,
Province of Ilocos Sur will mark the celebration of the Aldaw Ti Wayawaya-lli A
Narvacan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Narvacan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Narvacan, Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 thday ofSeptember, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
