MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 174

DECLARING TUESDAY, 07 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2023, Tuesday, the City of Tagum, Province of
Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of the 25" Araw ng Tagum;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagum be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 07 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tagum, Province
of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of March _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
