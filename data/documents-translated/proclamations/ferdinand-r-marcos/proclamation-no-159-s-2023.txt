MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 159

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, on 03 March 2023, Friday, the City of Davao will mark the
celebration of its 86" Araw ng Dabaw;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
03 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13thday of Februaxiy the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

CERTIFJED COPY

ATTY. COMCEPCION ZERTY
DIRECTOR
