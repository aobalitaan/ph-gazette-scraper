MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 235

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF BARLIG, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Barlig, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of its 57" Founding Anniversary on 18 June 2023;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Barlig to fully
participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan requested that 16 June 2023,
Friday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 18 June 2023 which
falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Barlig be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 16 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Barlig,
Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of May _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
