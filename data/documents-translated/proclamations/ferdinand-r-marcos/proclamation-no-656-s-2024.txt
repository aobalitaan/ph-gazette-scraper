MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 656

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 SEPTEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF BOMBON, BULA, CANAMAN, CALABANGA,
CAMALIGAN, GAINZA, LIBMANAN, MAGARAO, MILAOR, MINALABAC,
OCAMPO, PASACAO, PAMPLONA, PILI, SAN FERNANDO, SIRUMA, ALL
FROM THE PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 654 dated 25 September 2013 which amended
Proclamation 776, s. 1991 declared every Friday immediately preceding the feast of
Our Lady of Pefafrancia as a special day in the City of Naga;

WHEREAS, considering the significant economic, cultural and historical ties
between Naga City and its neighboring municipalities, the inclusion of the
Municipalities of Bombon, Bula, Canaman, Calabanga, Camaligan, Gainza,
Libmanan, Magarao, Milaor, Minalabac, Ocampo, Pasacao, Pamplona, Pili, San
Fernando, Siruma, all from the Province of Camarines Sur in the coverage of the
Pefiafrancia Festival would bring immense benefits to their communities.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
20 September 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipalities of Bombon,
Bula, Canaman, Calabanga, Camaligan, Gainza, Libmanan, Magarao, Milaor,
Minalabac, Ocampo, Pasacao, Pamplona, Pili, San Fernando, Siruma, all from the
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 08 dayof August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
