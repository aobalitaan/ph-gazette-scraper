MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1100

DECLARING SATURDAY, 13 DECEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ARINGAY, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 13 December 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Aringay,
Province of La Union will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aringay be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 13 December 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Aringay, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of Novembetn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By autherity of the President:
