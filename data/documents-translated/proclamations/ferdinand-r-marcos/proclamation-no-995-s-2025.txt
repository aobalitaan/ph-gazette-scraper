MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 995

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANSALAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
SUR

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2025, Thursday, the Municipality of Bansalan,
Province of Davao del Sur will mark the celebration of its 73" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bansalan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 18 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bansalan, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof August , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
