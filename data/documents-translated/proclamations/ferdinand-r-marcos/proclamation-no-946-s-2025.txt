MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 946

DECLARING MONDAY, 14 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF GUIHULNGAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 14 July 2025, Monday, the City of Guihulngan, Province of
Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Guihulngan
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 14 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihulngan, Province
of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
