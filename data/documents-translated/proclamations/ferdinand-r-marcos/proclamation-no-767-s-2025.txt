MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 767

DECLARING FRIDAY, 24 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Carigara, Province of Leyte will mark the
celebration of its 454" Founding Anniversary on 25 January 2025, which falls on a
Saturday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Carigara to fully
participate in the occasion and to provide for a longer weekend, the Sangguniang
Bayan requested that 24 January 2025, Friday, be declared as a special (non-working)
day in lieu of 25 January 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carigara be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
24 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Carigara, Province
of Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rq day of January, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Five .

. By authority of the President:

Office President
