MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 578

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 JUNE 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, the City of Batac, Province of Ilocos Norte will mark the
celebration of its 17 Charter Anniversary on 23 June 2024, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Batac to fully participate in the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Batac requested that 24 June 2024,
Monday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 23 June 2024;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 24 June 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac, Province of
llocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. :

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd  dayof June _, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

CAS P. BERSAMIN
