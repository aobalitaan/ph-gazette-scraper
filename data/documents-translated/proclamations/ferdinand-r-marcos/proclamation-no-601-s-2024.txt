MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 601

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYOMBONG, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA

WHEREAS, on 02 August 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Bayombong,
Province of Nueva Vizcaya, will mark the celebration of its town fiesta;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bayombong be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
02 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bayombong,
Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thday of June _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
