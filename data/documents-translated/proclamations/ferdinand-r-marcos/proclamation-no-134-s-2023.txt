MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 134

DECLARING TUESDAY, 07 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE ISLAND GARDEN CITY OF SAMAL, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2023, Tuesday, the Island Garden City of Samal,
Province of Davao del Norte, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Island Garden
City of Samal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 07 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Island Garden City of
Samal, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thday of January , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
