MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1052

DECLARING TUESDAY, 14 OCTOBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANTIPAS, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 14 October 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Antipas,
Province of Cotabato will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Antipas be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 14 October 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Antipas,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd  dayof October , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
