MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 418

DECLARING SATURDAY, 09 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BURGOS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 09 December 2023, Saturday, the Municipality of Burgos,
Province of Ilocos Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Burgos be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 09 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Burgos, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
