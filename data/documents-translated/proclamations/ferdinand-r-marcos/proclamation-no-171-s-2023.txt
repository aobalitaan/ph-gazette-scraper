MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 171

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 20 March 2023, Monday, the City of San Fernando, Province
of La Union will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Fernando be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 20 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Fernando,
Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of Februaxyin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
