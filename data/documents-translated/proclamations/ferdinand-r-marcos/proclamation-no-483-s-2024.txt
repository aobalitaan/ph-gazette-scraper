MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 483

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2024, Monday, the City of Zamboanga will mark
the celebration of its 87" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Zamboanga be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 26 February 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
