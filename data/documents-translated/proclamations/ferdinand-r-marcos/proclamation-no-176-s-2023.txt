MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 176

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2023, Wednesday, the Province of Cavite will mark
the celebration of the birth anniversary of General Emilio Aguinaldo, the first
President of the Republic of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Cavite be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3¢d day of March _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
