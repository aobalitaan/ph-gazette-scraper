MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 283

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 30 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ARAKAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of Arakan,
Province of Cotabato will mark the celebration of its 32"¢ Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Arakan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 30 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Arakan, Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of June __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
