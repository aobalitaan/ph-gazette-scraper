MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 770

DECLARING FRIDAY, 31 JANUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 31 January 2025, Friday, the Municipality of Lian, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of its Liberation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Lian
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
31 January 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lian, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7+} day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

 
 

BERSAMIN
Secretary
