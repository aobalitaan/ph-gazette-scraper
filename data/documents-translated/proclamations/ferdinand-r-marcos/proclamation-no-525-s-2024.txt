MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 525

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 24 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAGAWE, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, on 24 April 2024, Wednesday, the Municipality of Lagawe,
Province of Ifugao will mark the celebration of the Kulpi ad Lagawe;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lagawe be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 24 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Lagawe, Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
