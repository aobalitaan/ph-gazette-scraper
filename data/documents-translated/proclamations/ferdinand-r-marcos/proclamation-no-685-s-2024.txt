MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 685

DECLARING THURSDAY, 24 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 24 October 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Pili, Province of
Camarines Sur will mark the celebration of the Cimarrones Festival:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pili
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 24 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pili,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thday of Septembein the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Be PO RSANIN

Office of the President
