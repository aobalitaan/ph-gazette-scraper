MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 502

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 APRIL 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAMUT, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, on 05 April 2024, Friday, the Municipality of Lamut, Province of
Ifugao will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lamut be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
05 April 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lamut, Province of
Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
