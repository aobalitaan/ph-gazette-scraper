MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 04

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2022, Friday, the Cordillera Administrative Region
(CAR) will mark the celebration of its 35° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the CAR be given full
opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies,
subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, VICTOR D. RODRIGUEZ, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the CAR.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two. 4
