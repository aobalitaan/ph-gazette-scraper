MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 184

DECLARING FRIDAY, 31 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PANABO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 31 March 2023, Friday, the City of Panabo, Province of Davao
del Norte will mark the celebration of its 22"4 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Panabo be

given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,

31 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Panabo, Province of
Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thist4th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
