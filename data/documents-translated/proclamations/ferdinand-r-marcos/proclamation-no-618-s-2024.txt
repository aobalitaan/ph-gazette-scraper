MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 618

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 AUGUST 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIGA, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, on 15 August 2024, Thursday, the Municipality of Viga, Province
of Catanduanes will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Viga;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Viga
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 August 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Viga,
Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st dayof July, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
