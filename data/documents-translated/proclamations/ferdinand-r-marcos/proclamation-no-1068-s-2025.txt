MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1068

DECLARING SATURDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, on 22 November 2025, Saturday, the City of Palayan, Province of
Nueva Ecija, will celebrate the annual festival of thanksgiving for a bountiful harvest;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 22 November 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan,
Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this16th dayof October | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
