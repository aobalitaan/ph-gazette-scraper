MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 714

DECLARING THURSDAY, 05 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, on 05 December 2024, Thursday, the City of Palayan, Province of
Nueva Ecija, will mark the celebration of its 59" Foundation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 05 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan,
Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22ndday of October , in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
