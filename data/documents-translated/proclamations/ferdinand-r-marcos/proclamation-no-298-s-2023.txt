MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 298

LIFTING THE STATE OF NATIONAL EMERGENCY ON ACCOUNT OF
LAWLESS VIOLENCE IN MINDANAO

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 55 dated 04 September 2016 (s. 2016) declared
a State of National Emergency in Mindanao in view of the lawless violence perpetrated
by private armies and local warlords, bandits, criminal syndicates, terrorist groups, and
religious extremists across the island group;

WHEREAS, through successful focused military and law enforcement
operations and programs that promote sustainable and inclusive peace, the
government has made significant gains in improving and restoring peace and order in
the region;

WHEREAS, the conditions then prevailing for which Proclamation No. 55 (s.
2016) was issued have been significantly mitigated or reduced;

WHEREAS, the lifting of Proclamation No. 55 (s. 2016) will boost economic
activity and hasten the recovery of the local economy;

NOW THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws,
do hereby lift the state of national emergency on account of lawless violence in
Mindanao, effective immediately.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 25th of July in the year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Twenty-Three.

By the President: .
