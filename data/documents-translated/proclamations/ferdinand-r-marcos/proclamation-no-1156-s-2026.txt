MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1156

DECLARING THURSDAY, 19 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAGO, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 February 2026, Thursday, the City of Bago, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 60" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bago be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 19 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bago, Province
of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thday of January , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.

By authority of the President:

  
 

CTOR IV
