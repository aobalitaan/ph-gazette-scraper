MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 663

DECLARING MONDAY, 14 OCTOBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VICTORIA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 14 October 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Victoria, Province
of Laguna will mark the celebration of its 75‘ Founding Anniversary and the 23 Itik
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Victoria be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
14 October 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Victoria, Province
of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
