MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 750

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAYANTOC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Mayantoc,
Province of Tarlac will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mayantoc be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
23 December 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mayantoc,
Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

_ Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.
