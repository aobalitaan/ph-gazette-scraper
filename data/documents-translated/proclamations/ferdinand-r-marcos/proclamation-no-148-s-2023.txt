MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 148

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ESCALANTE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2023, Tuesday, the City of Escalante, Province
of Negros Occidental will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Escalante
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 28 February 2023, a special (non-working) day in the City of Escalante,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day offebruary, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
