MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 242

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 JULY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2025, Friday, the City of Tanjay, Province of Negros
Oriental will celebrate the Sinulog sa Tanjay Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanjay be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
25 July 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanjay, Province of Negros
Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
