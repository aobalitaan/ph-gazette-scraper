MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 179

DECLARING TUESDAY, 04 APRIL 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAAK, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 04 April 2023, Tuesday, the Municipality of Laak, Province of
Davao de Oro will mark the celebration of its 44" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Laak be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 04 April 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Laak,
Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8thday of March , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
