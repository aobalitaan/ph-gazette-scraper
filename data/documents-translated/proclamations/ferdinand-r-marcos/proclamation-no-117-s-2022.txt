MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 117

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 JANUARY 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 23 January 2023, Monday, the Province of Bulacan will mark
the anniversary of the inauguration of the First Philippine Republic which took place
at the Barasoain Church, Malolos, Bulacan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 23 January 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two

By authority of the President:
