MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1114

DECLARING MONDAY, 05 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUANG, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 05 January 2026, Monday, the Municipality of Bauang,
Province of La Union will mark the celebration of its 440" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauang be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
05 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauang, Province
of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of Decemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five

By authority of the President:

   
 

Acting Exécufive Secretary
