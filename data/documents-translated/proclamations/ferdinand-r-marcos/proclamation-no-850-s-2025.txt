MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 850

DECLARING MONDAY, 05 MAY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF SANTIAGO

WHEREAS, on 05 May 2025, Monday, the City of Santiago will mark the
celebration of its 318 Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
05 May 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof April , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
