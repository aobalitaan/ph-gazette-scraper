MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 385

DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF M’LANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of M’lang, Province of Cotabato will be
celebrating its annual festivity called the Kawayanan Festival on Thursday, 07
December 2023.

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

M'lang be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 07 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
M'lang, Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd dayof October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
