MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 812

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 19 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWAYAN, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Kawayan,
Province of Biliran will mark the celebration of its 118° Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kawayan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 19 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Kawayan, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25thday of February jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

ENTINO-NAVA
