MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 951

DECLARING MONDAY, 04 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, the City of Tacurong, Province of Sultan Kudarat will mark the
celebration of its 74 Founding Anniversary on 03 August 2025, which falls on a
Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tacurong to fully participate in
the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 04 August 2025, Monday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 03 August 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fittirg and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Monday, 04 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
