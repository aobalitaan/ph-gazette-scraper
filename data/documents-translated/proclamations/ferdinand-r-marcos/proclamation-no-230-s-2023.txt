MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 230

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2023, Friday, the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet will mark the celebration of its 73" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of La
Trinidad be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
16 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this22nd dayof May __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
