MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 2961

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GASAN, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 20 August 2025, Wednesday, the Municipality of Gasan,
Province of Marinduque will mark the celebration of its 416 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gasan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gasan, Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof July _, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

MALACANAN

CERTI
