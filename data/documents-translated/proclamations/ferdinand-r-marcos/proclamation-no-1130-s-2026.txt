MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1130

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 FEBRUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 21 February 2026, Saturday, the Province of Marinduque will
mark the celebration of the Araw ng Marinduque;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Marinduque be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 February 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
