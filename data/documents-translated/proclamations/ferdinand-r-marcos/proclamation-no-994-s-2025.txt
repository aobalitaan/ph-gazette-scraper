MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 994

DECLARING SATURDAY, 13 SEPTEMBER 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVER, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 13 September 2025, Saturday, the Municipality of Claver,
Province of Surigao del Norte will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary
(Adlaw Nan Claver);

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Claver be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Saturday, 13 September 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Claver, Province of Surigao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof August , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
