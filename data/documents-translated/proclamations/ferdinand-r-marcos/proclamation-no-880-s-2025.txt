MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 880

DECLARING TUESDAY, 03 JUNE 2025, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF AYUNGON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 03 June 2025, Tuesday, the Municipality of Ayungon, Province
of Negros Oriental will mark the celebration of its 101s Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Ayungon be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 03 June 2025, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Ayungon, Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May __ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
