MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 173

DECLARING TUESDAY, 07 MARCH 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2023, Tuesday, the Province of Basilan will mark
the celebration of its 49° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Basilan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 07 March 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day ofMarch _,, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:

 

/ of the President
