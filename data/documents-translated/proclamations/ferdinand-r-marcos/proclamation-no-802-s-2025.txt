MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 802

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 18 March 2025, Tuesday, the Province of Masbate will mark
the celebration of its 124" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Tuesday, 18 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day ofFebruary, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:
