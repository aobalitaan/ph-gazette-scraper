MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 593

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JULY 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTIAGO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2024, Thursday, the City of Santiago, Province of
Isabela will mark the celebration of the Aggaw na Naturales Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 July 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago, Province
of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th dayof June, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

     

oH, ESPINO.
ACTING DIRECTOR IV
