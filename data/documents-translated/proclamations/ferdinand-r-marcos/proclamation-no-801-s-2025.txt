MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 801

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 MARCH 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 10 March 2025, Monday, the City of Tanauan, Province of
Batangas will mark the celebration of its 24'* Cityhood Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanauan be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
10 March 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanauan, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day ofFebruary, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.
