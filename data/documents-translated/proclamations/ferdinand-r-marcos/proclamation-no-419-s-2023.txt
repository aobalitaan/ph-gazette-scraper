MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 419

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SISON, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 15 December 2023, Friday, the Municipality of Sison,
Province of Pangasinan will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sison be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Friday, 15 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sison,
Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of December, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
