MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 784

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2025, Wednesday, the City of Zamboanga will
mark the celebration of its 88" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Zamboanga
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 26 February 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rdday of January, jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
