MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1131

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 JANUARY 2026, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUIGUINTO, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 22 January 2026, Thursday, the Municipality of Guiguinto,
Province of Bulacan will mark the celebration of the Halamanan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Guiguinto be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RALPH G. RECTO, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 January 2026, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Guiguinto, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this: 13th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Six.
