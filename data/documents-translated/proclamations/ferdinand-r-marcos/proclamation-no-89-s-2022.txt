MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 89

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 09 NOVEMBER 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 09 November 2022, Wednesday, the Province of Eastern
Samar will mark the celebration of its 57° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Eastern Samar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 09 November 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Eastern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
