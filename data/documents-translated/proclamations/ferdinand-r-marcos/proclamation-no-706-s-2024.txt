MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 706

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYSAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2024, Monday, the Municipality of Taysan,
Province of Batangas will mark the celebration of its 106"° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taysan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Monday,
11 November 2024, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Taysan,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
