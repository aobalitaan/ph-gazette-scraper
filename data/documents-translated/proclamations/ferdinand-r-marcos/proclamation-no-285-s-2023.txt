MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 285

DECLARING THURSDAY, 10 AUGUST 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 10 August 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon will commemorate the birth of Lorenzo M. Tafada Sr.;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and honor the
memory of Senator Lorenzo M. Tafada Sr.;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 10 August 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
