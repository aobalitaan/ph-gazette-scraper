MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 472

DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 MARCH 2024, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2024, Thursday, the City of Tagum, Province of
Davao del Norte will mark the celebration of the 26" Araw ng Tagum;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagum be
given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare

Thursday, 07 March 2024, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tagum,
Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16thday of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Four.

By authority of the President:
