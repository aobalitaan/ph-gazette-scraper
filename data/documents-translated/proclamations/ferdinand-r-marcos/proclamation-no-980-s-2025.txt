MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 989

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 AUGUST 2025, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, the City of Tuguegarao, Province of Cagayan will mark the
celebration of the Pavvurulun Afi Festival on 16 August 2025, which falls on a
Saturday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tugeugarao to fully participate
in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 15 August 2025, Friday, be
declared as a special (non-working) day in lieu of 16 August 2025;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tuguegarao
be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare Friday,
15 August 2025, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao, Province of
Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th dayof July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
