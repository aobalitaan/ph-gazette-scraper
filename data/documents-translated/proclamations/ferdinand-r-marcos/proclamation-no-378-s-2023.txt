MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 378

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 13 DECEMBER 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NARVACAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
SUR

WHEREAS, on 13 December 2023, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Narvacan, Province of Ilocos Sur will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Narvacan be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Wednesday, 13 December 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Narvacan, Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Three.

By authority of the President:
