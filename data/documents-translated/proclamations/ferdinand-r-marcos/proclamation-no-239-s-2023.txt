MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 239

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 JUNE 2023, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUGUEY, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 15 June 2023, Thursday, the Municipality of Buguey,
Province of Cagayan will mark the celebration of its 427" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Buguey be given full opportunity to participate in the occasion and enjoy the
celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LUCAS P. BERSAMIN, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, FERDINAND R. MARCOS, JR., do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 June 2023, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Buguey,
Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31s5¢ day of May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Three.
