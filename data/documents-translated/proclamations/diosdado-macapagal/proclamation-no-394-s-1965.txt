MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 394

EXTENDING THE SPECIAL SCHOOL HOLIDAYS FOR PUBLIC AND PRIVATE SCHOOLS IN THE CITY OF CEBU UP TO APRIL 30, 1965.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the special school holidays for public and private schools in the City of Cebu, authorized under Proclamation No. 391, dated April 21, 1965, up to April 30, 1965.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
