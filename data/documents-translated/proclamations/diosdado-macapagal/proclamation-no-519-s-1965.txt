MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 519

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF THE DAVAO PENAL COLONY RESERVATION A CERTAIN PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC LAND EMBRACED THEREIN, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. TOMAS (FORMERLY MUNICIPALITY OF PANABO), PROVINCE OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of the Davao Penal Colony Reservation a certain parcel of land embraced therein situated in the municipality of Sto. Tomas (formerly Municipality of Panabo), Province of Davao, Island of Mindanao, and declare the same open to disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act, said parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

A parcel of land (Portion of Lot 4710, Tagum Cad. 276 and segregated from the Davoa Penal Colony Reservation pursuant to Survey Authority No. Sgs-2374, series of 1962) situated in the Barrio of Dujali, Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao.
