MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 477

DECLARING MONDAY, OCTOBER 25, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN ILIGAN CITY

WHEREAS, the laying of the cornerstone of the Iligan Integrated Steel Mill will be on October 25, 1965; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary that the people of Iligan City be afforded ample opportunity to celebrate the memorable event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, October 25, 1965, as a special public holiday in Iligan City.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
