MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 395

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MAY 17 TO 23, 1965, AS “PHILIPPINE REALTORS WEEK.”

WHEREAS, a home lot has always been a source and basis of wealth, a haven of peace and contentment, and a fountain of family and personal happiness;

WHEREAS, the Realtor has become an indispensible medium in the wise utilization, proper development and wide distribution of land; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Association of Real Estate Boards has been organized to improve and elevate the standards of the real estate business as a cog in the wheel of economic development and progress;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby proclaim the period from May 17 to 23, 1965, as Philippine Realtors Week. I call upon all government entities and instrumentalities, as well as all private organizations and individuals concerned with real estate, to join in the proper observance of the week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
