MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 241

RESERVING FOR MARKET SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF CEBU, ISLAND OF CEBU.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for market site purposes, under the administration of the City of Cebu, subject to the private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the City of Cebu, Island of Cebu, and more particularly described as follows, to wit:

Lot 913 Cebu Cadastre, Cad. 12

“A parcel of land (Lot 913 of the cadastral survey of Cebu, L.H.C. Cad. Record No.     ), situated in Cebu City. Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2 by Calle N. Carmelo; on the SE., along line 2-3 by Lot 3010, Cebu Cadastre; on the SW., along line 3-4 by Calle Washington; and on the NW., along line 4-1 by Calle Progreso. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 88 deg. 33′, W., 671.61 m. from B.L.L.M. 1, Cebu Cadastre, Cad. 12,

of beginning; containing an area of FIVE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED EIGHTY FIVE (5,685) SQUARE METERS. All points referred to are marked on the ground by Old Corners; bearings true; dated of cadastral survey, Dec., 1910 to Feb., 1912.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

(SGD. )DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary
