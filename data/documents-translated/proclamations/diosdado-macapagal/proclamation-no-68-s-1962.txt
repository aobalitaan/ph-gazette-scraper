MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 68

RESERVING FOR OFFICE SITE, NURSERY DISTRIBUTING CENTER, AND DEMONSTRATION STATION PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF DAVAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and. reserve for office site, nursery distributing center, and demonstration station purposes, under the administration of the Bureau of Agricultural Extension, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the City of Davao, and more particularly-described as follows:

Lot 2106-B Csd-6067 (Bureau of Agricultural Extension)

“A parcel of land (Lot 2106-B of the subdivision plan Csd-6067, being a portion of Lot 2106, Davao Townsite, K-7, L.R.C. Record No. ——–), situated in the City of Davao. Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2, by Magallanes Street; on the SE., along lines 2-3-4, by lot 2105, Davao Townsite, K-7; on SW., along line 45, by lot 76, Davao Townsite, K-7; and on the NW., along lines 5-6-1, by lot 2106-A of the subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 13 deg. 14′ W., 142.89 m. from Mon. 24, Davao Townsite, K-7, thence S. 60° 14′ E., 19.58 meters to point 2; thence S. 33° 30′ W., 42.00 meters to point 3; thence S. 32° 33′ W., 13.49 meters to point 4; thence N. 59° 15′ W., 12.43 meters to point 5; thence N. 21° 11′ E., 18.91 meters to point 6; thence N. 27° 07′ E., 36.55 meters to the point of beginning; containing an area of 920 square meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: point 2 by Old Point, point 3 by Old P.L.S. Cyl. Conc. Mon., point 4 by Old P.L. S./B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mon. and the rest, by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons.; bearings true; date of the original survey, Feb. 1916—May 1920 and that of the subdivision survey, March 29, 1960 which was approved on Nov. 3, 1961.

NOTES Lot 2106-B—Lot 3663, Davao Townsite, K-7 (formerly Lot 75-B, Bsd-10397).

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty two.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Executive Secretary
