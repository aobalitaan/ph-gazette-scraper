MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 500

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 449, DATED OCTOBER 4, 1957 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE COMMUNAL FISHING GROUND RESERVATION FRONTING MANILA BAY ALONG THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN, ISLAND OF LUZON, CERTAIN PORTIONS OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 449, dated October 4, 1957, which established the Communal Fishing Ground Reservation fronting the Manila Bay along the Province of Bulacan, Island of Luzon, certain portions of the land embraced therein and declare the same open to desposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act, which portions are more particularly described as follows, to wit:
