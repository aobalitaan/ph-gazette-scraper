MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 382

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 477 DATED OCTOBER 29, 1939, AS AMENDED BY PROCLAMATION NO. 447 DATED DECEMBER 23, 1953 AND PROCLAMATION NO. 232 DATED APRIL 20, 1964, SITUATED IN DAVAO CITY, AND RESERVING THE PARCEL OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE CENTRAL BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 477, dated October 29, 1939, which established the Department of Agriculture and Natural Resoures building site reservation, as amended by Prolamation No. 447 dated December 23, 1953, and Proclamation No. 232 dated April 20, 1964, situated in Davao City and reserve the parcel of land embraced therein with an area of 8,144 square meters, more or less, for building site purposes under the administration of the Governor of the Central Bank of the Philippines, subject to private rights, if any there be.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
