MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 359

AUTHORIZING THE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM AND LIBRARY, INC. TO CONDUCT ITS EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM MARCH 15 TO APRIL 30, 1965.

WHEREAS, it is our vital concern to exert all-out efforts in promoting the well-being of our children to develop them into good and useful citizens and enlightened leaders of our country;

WHEREAS, the development of the latent capabilities of our youth depends on the proper facilities for their right upbringing; and

WHEREAS, the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc. has facilities and objectives fully geared to help achieve these ends;

NOW THEREFORE, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from March 15 to April 30, 1965, for the educational, membership and fund campaign of the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc.  I hereby authorize and exhort all government officials and employees, private entities, all citizens, and nationals to support this humani­tarian movement,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

(SGD.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
