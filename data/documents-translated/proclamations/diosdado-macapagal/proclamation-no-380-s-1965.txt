MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 380

DECLARING SATURDAY, APRIL 17, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, the sixteenth day (Good Friday) and the eighteenth day (Sunday) of April, 1965, being holidays, the seventeenth day (Saturday) may be declared a special public holiday without prejudice to the public interests;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby proclaim Saturday, April 17, 1965, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
