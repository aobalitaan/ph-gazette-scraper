MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 384

RESERVING FOR LABORATORY AND FUMIGATION CHAMBER SITE PURPOSES OF THE BUREAU OF PLANT INDUSTRY A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN ZAMBOANGA CITY.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 83, Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for laboratory and fumigation chamber site purposes under the administration of the Director of Plant Industry, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the East Reclaimed Area, Zamboanga City, more particularly described as follows:

Nr-177 (Plant Quarantine Service— Bureau of Plant Industry)

A parcel of land (as shown on plan Nr-177, L.R.C. Record No.     ), situated in the District of East Reclaimed Area, City of Zamboanga. Bounded on the SE., along line 1-2, by Road; on the SW. along line 2-3, by property of Bureau of Custom; on the NW,, along line 3-4, by property of Bureau of Plant Industry (Rlla-78-D); and on the NE., along line 4-1, by property of Bureau of Plant Industry (Lot 1, Rlla-V-78). Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being S. 30 deg. 12 min. W., 184.95 meters from Mon. 14, Zamboanga City, thence S. 29 deg. 30 min. W., 15.03 meters to point 2; thence N. 59 deg. 52 min. W., 38.02 meters to point 3; thence N. 28 deg. 58 min. E., 15.92 meters to point 4; thence S. 31 deg. 29 min. E., 38.19 meters to the point beginning; containing an area of 589 square meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: points 1 and 4 by Old B.L.Cyl. Conc. Mons. and the rest by B.L.Cyl. Conc. Mons.; bearings true; date of survey, Jan. 13, 1964 and approved on Feb. 9, 1965.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
