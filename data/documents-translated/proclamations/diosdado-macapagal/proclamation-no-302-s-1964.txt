MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 302

ESTABLISHING AS TUGAS FOREST RESERVE FOR WOOD PRODUCTION, WATERSHED, SOIL PROTECTION, AND OTHER FOREST USES, THE PARCELS OF PUBLIC DOMAIN KNOWN AS TIMBERLAND, SURIGAO (SURIGAO DEL NORTE) LAND CLASSIFICATION PROJECT NO. 36-B AND SURIGAO (SURIGAO DEL SUR) LAND CLASSIFICATION PROJECT NOS. 3-A AND 4-A, PER B.F. MAP L. C. 2423, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CLAVER, SURIGAO DEL NORTE, AND THE MUNICIPALITIES OF CANTILAN AND CARRASCAL, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR, PHILIPPINES

Pursuant to the provisions of section 1826 of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended by Republic Act No. 3092, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settle­ment, or other disposition and establish as Tugas Forest Reserve, subject to private right, if any there be, the parcels of public domain known as Timber Land, Surigao (Surigao del Norte) Land Classification Project No. 36-B and Surigao (Surigao del Sur) Land Classification Project Nos. 3-A and 4-A, situated in the Municipality of Claver, Surigao del Norte, and the Municipalities of Cantilan and Carrascal, Province of Surigao del Sur, Philippines, demarcated and established under Forestry Administrative Order No. 4-941, dated December 22, 1960, for wood production, watershed, soil protection, and ether forest uses under the administration and control of the Director of Forestry who shall have the authority to regulate the use and occupancy of said forest reserve and the cutting, collections, and removal of timber and other forest products therein in accordance with forest laws regulations.

The aforementioned parcels of public domain are shown and more particularly described in the attached Bureau of Forestry Map L. C. 2423, comprising the Timber Land of the aforementioned Surigao (Surigao del Norte) Land Classification Project No. 36-B and Surigao (Surigao del Sur) Land Classification Project Nos. 3-A and 4-A, containing an area of 17,609 hectares, more or less.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary
