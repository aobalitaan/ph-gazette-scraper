MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 385

RESERVING FOR ANIMAL BREEDING STATION SITE PURPOSES OF THE BUREAU OF ANIMAL INDUSTRY A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF SAN AGUSTIN, MUNICIPALITY OF MAGALANG, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for animal breeding station site purposes under the administration of the Director of Animal Industry, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future survey, a certain parcel of land of the private domain situated in the Barrio of San Agustin, Municipality of Magalang, Province of Pampanga, Island of Luzon, and more particularly described as follows:

Portion of Lot 418 Magalang Cadastre (Proposed Bureau of Animal Industry, Breeding Station)

“A parcel of land (portion of Lot 418 of the cadastral survey of Magalang), situated in the Barrio of San Agustin, Municipality of Magalang, Province of Pampanga. Bounded on the W., along lines 1-2-3, by 513, Magalang Cad.; on the N., N., SE. and SW., along lines 3-4-5-6-1, by reamining portion of Lot 418, Magalang Cad. Beginning at a point marked 1 on the attached sketch plan, thence N. 28 deg. 01 min. E., 99.16 meters to point 2; thence N. 26 deg. 35 min. E.,89.66 meters to point 3; thence S. 83 deg. 27 min. E., 603.38 meters to point 4; thence S. 50 deg. 30 min. E., 194.38 meters to point 5; thence S. 42 deg. 05 min. W., 500.00 meters to point 6; thence N. 51 deg. 42 min. W., 638.43 meters to the point of beginning; containing an approximate area of 262,251 square meters.

NOTE: All data are approximate and subject to change based on future surveys.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
