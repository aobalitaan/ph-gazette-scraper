MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 339

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 731, DATED FEBRUARY 2, 1961, BY DECLARING THE PARCELS OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN OPEN TO DISPOSITION EXCLUSIVELY UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 141, AS AMENDED, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE PUBLIC LAND ACT

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 731, dated February 2, 1961, which declared the parcels of land embraced therein open to disposition under the provisions of Republic Act No. 274, in relation to Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, and Act No. 3038, by declaring the same open to disposition exclusively under the provisions of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, otherwise known as the Public Land Act.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in Olongapo, Zambales, this 17th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Secretary of Justice concurrently Acting Executive Secretary
