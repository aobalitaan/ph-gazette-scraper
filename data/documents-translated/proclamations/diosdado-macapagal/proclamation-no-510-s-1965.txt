MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 510

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 702 DATED APRIL 15, 1941, WHICH RESERVED FOR THE USE OF THE BUREAU OF ANIMAL INDUSTRY TWO PARCELS OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN PANDACAN, CITY OF MANILA, AND RESERVING THE SAME FOR THE USE OF THE PHILIPPINE COLLEGE OF COMMERCE.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 702 dated April 15, 1941 reserved for the use of the Bureau of Animal Industry two parcels of the private domain situated in Pandacan, City of Manila;

WHEREAS, it has been decided to transfer the offices of the Bureau of Animal Industry from the site to Alabang, Rizal; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine College of Commerce has made a meritorious representation that said site be assigned for its use;

.NOW, THEREFORE, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 702 dated April 15, 1941, which reserved for the use of the Bureau of Animal Industry two parcels of the private domain situated in Pandacan, City of Manila, and reserve the same for the use of the Philippine College of Commerce upon its being vacated by said bureau.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

(SGD.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Museum and Library

Note: This issuance is followed by Proclamation No. 512, s. 1965.
