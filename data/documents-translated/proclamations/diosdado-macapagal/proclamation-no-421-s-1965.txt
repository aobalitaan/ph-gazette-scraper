MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 421

EXTENDING THE SEVENTEENTH ANNUAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST OF GREATER MANILA.

WHEREAS, the period from June 1 to July 15, 1965, has been set aside for the (Seventeenth annual fund campaign of the Community Chest of Greater Manila under Proclamation No. 401, dated May 19, 1965; and

WHEREAS, said organization needs more time for the successful prosecution of the campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period for the seventeenth annual fund campaign of the Community Chest of Greater Manila up to August 15, 1965.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
