MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 484

RESERVING FOR FRESHWATER DEMONSTRATION FISH FARM AND NURSERY SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAY, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for freshwater demonstration fish farm and nursery site purposes, under the administration of the Commissioner of Fisheries, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future survey, a parcel of land of the public domain situated in barrio Sto. Domingo, municipality of Bay, province of Laguna and more particularly described as follows:

Proposed Site of the Bay, Laguna, Freshwater Demonstration Fish Farm Nursery

A parcel of land (The Proposed Site of the Bay, Laguna Freshwater Demonstration Fish Farm and Nursery), situated in the Barrio of Sto. Domingo, Municipality of Bay, Province of Laguna. Bounded on the SW., by property of the Municipality of Bay and Laguna de Bay; on the NW., by Laguna de Bay; on the NE., by Laguna de Bay and property of the Municipality of Bay; and on the SE., by property of the Municipality of Bay. Beginning at a point marked 1 on the attached Sketch Plan,

thence N. 15 deg. 58 min. W., 32S.25 meters to point 2; thence N. 73 deg. 00 min. E., 280.00 meters to point, 3; thence S . 29 deg. 00 min. E., 285.00 meters to point 4; thence S. 55 deg. 00 min. W., 150.00 meters to point 5; thence S. 71 deg. 00 min. W., 33.00 meters to point 6; thence S. 83 deg. 30 min. W., 164.00 meters to the point of

beginning; containing an approximate area of ninety eight thousand six hundred twenty one (98,621) square meters.

Note: All data are approximate and subject to change based on future surveys;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
