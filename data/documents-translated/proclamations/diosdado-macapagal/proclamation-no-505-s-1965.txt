MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 505

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 71, SERIES OF 1927, WHICH RESERVED FOR WATERSHED PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF MONTALBAN, SAN JUAN DEL MONTE, NORZAGARAY, ANGAT, SAN RAFAEL, PENARANDA AND INFANTA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, BULACAN, NUEVA ECIJA AND QUEZON, BY TRANSFERRING THE ADMINISTRATION THEREOF TO THE NATIONAL POWER CORPORATION.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 71, series of 1927, which reserved for watershed purposes a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the municipalities of Montalban, San Juan del Monte, Norzagaray, Angat, San Rafael, Peñaranda and Infanta, Provinces of Rizal, Bulacan, Nueva Ecija and Quezon, by transferring the administration thereof to the National Power Corporation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
