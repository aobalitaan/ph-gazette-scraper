MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 503

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE NATIONAL SHRINES COMMISSION TO NOVEMBER 30, 1966.

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission was authorized under Proclamation No. 331 dated November 30, 1964, to conduct its fund campaign from December 1, 1964, to November 30, 1965; and

WHEREAS, it needs more time to realize its fund campaign goal this year to enable it to pursue its planned projects on Corregidor island and also to give ample time for the Fund Campaign Committee to pursue vigorously its fund drive abroad.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the period of the National Shrines Commission Fund Campaign up to November 30, 1966.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
