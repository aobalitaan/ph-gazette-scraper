MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 471

AUTHORIZING THE ORDER OF THE KNIGHTS OF RIZAL TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 30, 1965, TO JANUARY 31, 1966.

WHEREAS, the Order of the Knights of Rizal has for its objectives, the development and training of the minds of the youth along the ideals of Dr. Jose Rizal;

WHEREAS, the Order of the Knights of Rizal has resolved to wage a nation-wide and dynamic program to disseminate Rizal’s teachings and make research on, preserve and publish the hero’s works; and

WHEREAS, the Order of the Knights of Rizal, in order to implement its objectives and projects needs financial assistance from all sectors of the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Order of the Knights of Rizal to conduct a national educational and fund campaign during the period from November 30, 1965, to January 31, 1966. I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of race and creed, to support the campaign. I authorize government officials and employees, school authorities and teachers, to accept, for the Order of the Knights of Rizal, fund-raising responsibilities and to give it active support and leadership in their respective communities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
