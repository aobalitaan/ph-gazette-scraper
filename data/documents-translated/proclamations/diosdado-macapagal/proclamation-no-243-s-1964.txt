MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 243

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 1 TO JULY 15, 1964, FOR THE SIXTEENTH ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST.

To enable the Community Chest to carry on its community welfare services, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from June 1 to July 15, 1964, for its sixteenth annual educational and fund campaign.

I call upon all residents, firms and organizations in the City of Manila, Quezon City, Pasay City, Caloocan City and municipalities of Makati, Malabon, Mandaluyong, Navotas, Parahaque and San Juan, Province of Rizal, to support this campaign and to give generously to the Community Chest which units into one annual appeal the fund campaigns of the following welfare agencies:

I authorize and urge all government officials and employees, including school authorities and teachers, to accept, for the Community Chest, fund-raising responsibilities and to give it full support and dedicated leadership in their respective communities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

(SGD. ) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary
