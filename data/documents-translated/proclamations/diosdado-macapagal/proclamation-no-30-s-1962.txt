MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 30

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 693, SERIES OF 1960, WHICH REVOKED PROCLAMATION NO. 187, SERIES OF 1955, AND RESERVING THE PARCELS OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN, TOGETHER WITH THE IMPROVEMENTS EXISTING THEREON, FOR PROVINCIAL BUILDING SITES AND FOR GOVERNMENT OFFICES PURPOSES

By virtue of the powers vested in my by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 693, series of 1960, which revoked Proclamation No. 187, series of 1955, and reserve the parcels of land embraced therein, together with the improvements existing thereon, for provincial building sites and for government offices purposes under the administration of the Province of Romblon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixteenth.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

AMELITO R. MUTUC Executive Secretary
