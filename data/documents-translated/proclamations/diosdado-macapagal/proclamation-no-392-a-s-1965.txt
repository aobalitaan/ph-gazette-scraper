MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION No. 392-A

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 675, DATED MAY 25, 1960, WHICH RESERVED FOR CITY HIGH SCHOOL AND FIRE STATION SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PORTIONS OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF INTRAMUROS, CITY OF MANILA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 675, dated May 25, 1960, which reserved for city high school and fire station site purposes certain portions of the private domain in the District of Intramuros, City of Manila, by reserving Lot 1, Block 52, Manila Cadastre, for City University site, instead of for city high school site purposes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in Sabtang, Batanes, this 24th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
