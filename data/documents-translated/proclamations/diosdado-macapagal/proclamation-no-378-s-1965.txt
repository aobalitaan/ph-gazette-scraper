MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 378

DECLARING FRIDAY, APRIL 2, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCES OF BULACAN AND BATAAN.

WHEREAS, the second day of April is the birthday of Francisco Balagtas, Prince of Filipino Poets; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of Bulacan, his birthplace, and Bataan, where he lived and died, should be given full opportunity to celebrate his birthday;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Friday, April 2, 1965, as a special public holiday in the provinces of Bulacan and Bataan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines, to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of March, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
