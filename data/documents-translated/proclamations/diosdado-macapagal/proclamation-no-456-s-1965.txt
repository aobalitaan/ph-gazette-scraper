MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 456

DECLARING SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 25, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE, PROVINCE OF ALBAY.

WHEREAS, Saturday, September 25, 1965, marks the 62nd anniversary of the surrender of General Simeon Ola, one of the last Filipino generals to surrender during the Filipino-American War; and

WHEREAS, the people of his province should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the authority vested in, me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Saturday, September 25, 1965, as a special public holiday in the Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: SALVADOR L. MARIÑO Acting Executive Secretary
