MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 11

TRANSFERRING THIS YEAR’S CELEBRATION OF THE “PHILIPPINE INDUSTRY AND NEPA WEEK” TO MARCH 4-10, 1962

WHEREAS, the period comprising the last seven days of February of every year is designated as Philippine Industry and. Nepa Week under Proclamation No. 483, dated February 4, 1958;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Chamber of Industries requests that this year’s celebration of the week be transferred to March 4-10, 1962;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Maeapagal, President of the Philippines, .by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, do hereby transfer this year’s celebration of “Philippine Industry and Nepa Week” to March 4-10, 1962.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-two, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixteenth.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

AMELITO MUTUC Executive Secretary
