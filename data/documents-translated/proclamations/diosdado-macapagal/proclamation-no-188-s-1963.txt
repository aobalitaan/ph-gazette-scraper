MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 188

RESERVING FOR NATIONAL MUSEUM BUILDING PARCELS OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF INTRAMUROS, CITY OF MANILA

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw’ from sale or settlement and reserve for building site purposes, under the administration of the Director of National Museum, subject to private rights, if any there be, cerain parcels of land of the private domain of the National Government situated in the District of Intramuros, City of Manila, and more particularly described as follows, to wit; Lot 1, Block 17, Manila Cadastre

“A parcel of land (Lot 1, Block 17 of the cadastral survey of the City of Manila), situated in the District of Intramuros, City of Manila. Bounded on the SW., along line 1-2, by Calle Arzobispo; on the NW., along line 2-3, by Lot 2, Block 17, Manila Cad.; on the NE., along line 3-4, by Calle Gen. Luna; and on the SE., along line 4-1, by Calle Postigo. Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being S. 5° 57′ W., 80.42 meters from Fire Hyd. No. 2, City of Manila, thence N. 42° 34′ W., 80.02 meters to point 2; thence N. 47° 39′ E., 73.050 meters to point 3; thence S. 42° 35′ E., 79.79 meters to point 4; thence S. 47° 28′ W., 73.06 meters to the point of beginning; containing an area of 5,8387.8. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by Old Points; bearings true; date of the cadastral survey, Nov. 1915-Feb., 1916.

Lot 2, Block 17 Manila Cadastre

“A parcel of land (Lot 2, Block 17 of the cadastral survey of the City of Manila), situated in the District of Intramuros, City of Manila. Bounded on the NW., along line 1.-2, by Calle Aduana; on the NE., along line 2-3, by Calle Gen. Luna; and on the SE., along line 3-1, by Lot 1, Block 17, Manila Cad. Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being S. 60° 20′ E., 20.93 meters from Fire Hyd, No. 1, City of Manila: thence N. 47° 27′ E., 773.04 meters to point 2; thence S. 42° 35′ E., 0.25 meters to point 3; thence S. 47° 39′ W., 73.05 meters to the point of beginning; containing an area of 9.3. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by Old Points; bearings true; date of the cadastral survey, Nov., 1915-Feb. 1916.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-three.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RUFINO G. HECHANOVA Executive Secretary
