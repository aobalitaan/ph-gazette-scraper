MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 364

TRANSFERRING THE CELEBRATION OF “PHILIPPINE INDUSTRY AND NEPA WEEK” TO THE FIRST WEEK OF APRIL OF EVERY YEAR

WHEREAS, the period comprising the last seven days of February of every year is designated as Philippine Industry and NEPA Week under the Proclamation No. 11 dated February 22, 1962; and

WHEREAS, the National Economic Protectionism Association requests that the celebration of the Week be transferred to April 5-11 this year and to the first week of April every year thereafter;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the first week of April of every year as “Philippine Industry and NEPA Week.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
