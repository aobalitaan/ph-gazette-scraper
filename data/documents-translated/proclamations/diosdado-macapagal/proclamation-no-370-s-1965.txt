MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 370

DECLARING MARCH 1-7, 1965, AS “WORKMEN’S COMPENSATION WEEK”

WHEREAS, the period from March 1 to 7, 1965, coincides with the second National Conference on Workmen’s Compensation; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus attention on the beneficent social legislations designed to protect workers and to make management and labor compensation—conscious;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from March 1 to 7, 1965, as Workmen’s Compensation Week under the auspices of the Bureau of Workmen’s Compensation.

I urge all officials, citizens, unions, and residents to cooperate in making the Week a success.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President: RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
