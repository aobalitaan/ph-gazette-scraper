MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 392

DECLARING MONDAY, APRIL 26, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN SAN JOSE, BATANGAS.

WHEREAS, the bicentennial of the founding of the Municipality of San Jose, Batangas, falls on Monday, April 26, 1965; and

WHEREAS, the people of the said municipality should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their town’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, April 26, 1965, as a special public holiday in San Jose, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

RAMON A. DIAZ Executive Secretary
