MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 443

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 9, 1965, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CEBU PROVINCE AND IN THE CITIES OF CEBU, DANAO, LAPU-LAPU AND TOLEDO.

WHEREAS, Thursday, September 9, 1965, marks the birthday of the late President Sergio Osmeña Sr., second President of the Commonwealth, patriot, statesman and staunch advocate of nationalism and Philippine independence; and

WHEREAS, at least the people of his province should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Diosdado Macapagal, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thursday, September 9, 1965, as a special public holiday in Cebu Province and in the Cities of Danao, Cebu, Lapu-lapu and Toledo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-five.

DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN S. CANCIO Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
