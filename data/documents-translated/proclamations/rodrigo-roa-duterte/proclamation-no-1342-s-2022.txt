MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1342

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 MAY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TITAY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 24 May 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Titay, Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Titay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 May 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Titay,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof April , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
