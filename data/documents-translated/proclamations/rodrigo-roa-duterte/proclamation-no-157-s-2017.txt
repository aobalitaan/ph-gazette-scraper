MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 157

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 21 March 2017, marks the celebration of the 19%
Founding Anniversary of the City of Calapan, Oriental Mindoro;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calapan,
Oriental Mindoro, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO I. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby

declare Tuesday, 21 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Calapan, Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority ef the President:
