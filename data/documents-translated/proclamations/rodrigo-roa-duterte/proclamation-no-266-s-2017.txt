MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 266

.

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPQSTELA, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

th ,, WHEREAS, Tuesday, 01 August 2017, marks the 69" Foundation Day and
11" Buganihan Festival of the Municipality of Compostela, Compostela Valley; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Compostela, Compostela Valley, be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality - of
Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

to
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
