MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 107

DECLARING THURSDAY, 8 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-

WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, PROVINCE OF
LA UNION

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Agoo, La Union marks the celebration
of its 438" Foundation Anniversary on Thursday, 8 December 2016:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Agoo, La Union be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Thursday, 8 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day
in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this25th day of November, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President

a soci ON
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
