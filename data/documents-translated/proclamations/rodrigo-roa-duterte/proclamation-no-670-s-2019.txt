MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 670

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS, on 27 February 2019, Wednesday, the City of General Santos
will mark its 80"" Founding Anniversary and the 30" Kalilangan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of General
Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof Februaryin the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

.

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
