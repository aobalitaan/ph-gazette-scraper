MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 817

DECLARING MONDAY, 30 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MASBATE, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 30 September 2019, Monday, the City of Masbate, Province
of Masbate, will mark the celebration of its 49" Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Masbate,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 30 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Masbate,
Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority fffthe President:

Maivanos C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
