MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1358

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MACO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Maco, Province of
Davao de Oro, will mark the celebration of its 55" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maco be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
17 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maco, Province of
Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1otMayof way , in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
