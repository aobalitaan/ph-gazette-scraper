MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.435

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Thursday, 22 March 2018 marks the celebration of the 20"
Charter Anniversary of the City of Malaybalay, Bukidnon; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Malaybalay, Bukidnon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Malaybalay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21St day of Februaryin the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

.
