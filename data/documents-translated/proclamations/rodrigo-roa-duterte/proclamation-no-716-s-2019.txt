MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 716

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of Quezon,
Province of Palawan, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Quezon, Province of Palawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this gth day of May, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority/of the President:

ALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
