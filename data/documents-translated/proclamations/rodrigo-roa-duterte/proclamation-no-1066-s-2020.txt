MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1066

DECLARING MONDAY, 04 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 04 January 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Naujan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its 116 Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naujan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 04 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Naujan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day ofDecember _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
