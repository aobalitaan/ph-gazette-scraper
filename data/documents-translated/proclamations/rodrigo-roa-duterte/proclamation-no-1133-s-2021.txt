MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1133

DECLARING FRIDAY, 30 APRIL 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAGUPAN, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, the City of Dagupan, Province of Pangasinan, will mark the
celebration of the Bangus Festival during the month of April;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Dagupan to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 30 April 2021, Friday, be declared a
special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dagupan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,

30 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan, Province of
Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16thday of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
