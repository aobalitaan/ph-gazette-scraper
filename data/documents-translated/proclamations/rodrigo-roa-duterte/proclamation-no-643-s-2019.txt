MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 643

DECLARING SATURDAY, 12 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENCIA, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 12 January 2019, Saturday, the City of Valencia, Province of
Bukidnon, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Valencia
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 12 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valencia,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of January, jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
