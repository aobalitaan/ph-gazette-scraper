MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1287

DECLARING THURSDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2022, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2022, Thursday, the City of Bifian, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 77" Liberation Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bifian be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the Presidentt RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 03 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bifian,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
