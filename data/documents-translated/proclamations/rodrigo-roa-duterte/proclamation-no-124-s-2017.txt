MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 124

DECLARING THE MONTH OF JANUARY OF EVERY YEAR AS
NATIONAL BIBLE MONTH CULMINATING IN THE LAST WEEK
THEREOF AS NATIONAL BIBLE WEEK

WHEREAS, the State recognizes the religious nature of the Filipino people and
the elevating influence of religion in human society;

WHEREAS, while maintaining neutrality in its treatment of all religious
communities, the government is not precluded from pursuing valid objectives secular in
character even if it would have an incidental result affecting a particular religion or sect;

WHEREAS, the 1987 Constitution calls on the government to support efforts to
strengthen the ethical and spiritual values and to develop the moral character of the
Filipino people;

WHEREAS, history bears witness to the profound impact of the Bible on the life
of nations, and to how it has moved and inspired many people, including statesmen and
social reformers, to work for the betterment of their fellow human beings even at great
cost to themselves;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper, for the molding of the spiritual, moral and
social fiber of our citizenry, that national attention be focused on the importance of
reading and studying the Bible;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Philippines,
by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the month of January of
every year as National Bible Month, culminating in the last week of January as the
National Bible Week.

IN’WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof January , in the Year of
our Lord Two Thousand and Seventeen.
