MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1289

DECLARING THURSDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 10 February 2022, Thursday, the City of Laoag, Province of
Ilocos Norte, will mark the celebration of the Pamulinawen Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 10 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag,
Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rq day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
