MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.388

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO

WHEREAS, the City of San Pablo marks the celebration of the 23" Coconut
Festival and Street Dancing Competition (Mardi Gras) on Monday, 15 January 2018;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with

appropriate ceremonies; 2

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 15 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of December, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
