MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. ©75

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, on 18 March 2019, Monday, the City of Davao will mark the
celebration of its Araw ng Pasasalamat in relation to the 82™ Araw ng Dabaw, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 18 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

by
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
