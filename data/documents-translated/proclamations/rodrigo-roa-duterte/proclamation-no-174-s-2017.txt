MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 174

DECLARING TUESDAY, 7 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 7 March 2017, the City of Tagum marks the
celebration of the Araw ng Tagum;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagum, be

given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Tuesday, 7 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
By authority of the President:
wd Oe

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary. -
