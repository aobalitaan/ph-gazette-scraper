MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO./79

DECLARING MONDAY, 05 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, the City of Cabuyao, Province of Laguna, will mark the
celebration of its 7". cityhood anniversary on 04 August 2019;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Cabuyao to celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Cabuyao requested that Monday, 05
August 2019, be declared a special (non-working) day, in lieu of 04 August 2019,
which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabuyao,
Province of Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 05 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabuyao,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of July, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
