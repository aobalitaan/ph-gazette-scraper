MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 993

DECLARING FRIDAY, 14 AUGUST 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 14 August 2020, Friday, the Municipality of Sto. Tomas,
Province of Davao del Norte, will mark the celebration of its 61% Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sto. Tomas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
14 August 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sto. Tomas,
Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
