MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 133

DECLARING FRIDAY, 3 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN

WHEREAS. on Friday 3 February 2017, the City of Cabanatuan
marks the celebration of its 67" Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it 's but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cabanatuan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Fnday, 3 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the
City of Cabanatuan

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January jn the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
