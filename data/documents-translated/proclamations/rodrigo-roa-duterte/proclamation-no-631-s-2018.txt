MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.631

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 DECEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAINTA, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 01 December 2018, Saturday, the Municipality of Cainta,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of its Sumbingtik Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cainta be given full opportunity to participate in and celebrate the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies; ,

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 December 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Cainta, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
