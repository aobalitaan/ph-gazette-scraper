MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 245

DECLARING TUESDAY, 04 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CADIZ

es WHEREAS, the City of Cadiz, Negros Occidental, marks the celebration of its
50" Charter Day on Tuesday, 04 July 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 04 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thday of June __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

Ai
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
