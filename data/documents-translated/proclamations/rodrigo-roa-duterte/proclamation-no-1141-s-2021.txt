MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1141

DECLARING MONDAY, 03 MAY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 03 May 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Sta. Cruz,
Province of Marinduque, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary,
known as the Santa Cruz Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Cruz be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 03 May 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz,
Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2gciday of apri1_, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
