MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 194

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALACA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 23 May 2017, marks the birth anniversary of General
Eleuterio Marasigan, a great Calaquefio and a patriot of the libertarian struggle in
the Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calaca, Province of Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate
in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 23 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calaca,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st dayof April, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
