MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 919

DECLARING SATURDAY, 14 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 14 March 2020, Saturday, the City of Passi, Province of lloilo,
will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary as a component city; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Passi be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 14 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Passi, Province
of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof March , jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
