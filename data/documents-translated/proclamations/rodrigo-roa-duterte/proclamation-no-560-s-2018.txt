MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 560

DECLARING TUESDAY, 04 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 04 September 2018, a Tuesday, the Municipality of Monkayo,
Province of Compostela Valley will mark the celebration of its 64" founding
anniversary and the 23" Kariyawan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Monkayo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 04 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd dayof August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

awe
