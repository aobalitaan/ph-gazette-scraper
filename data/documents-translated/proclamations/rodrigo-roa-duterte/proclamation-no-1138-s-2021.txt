MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1138

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 APRIL 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 26 April 2021, Monday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 256° Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 26 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of April , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
