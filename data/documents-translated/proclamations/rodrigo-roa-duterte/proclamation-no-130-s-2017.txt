“ MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 130

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF
LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 18 January 2017, marks the 225" Foundation
Day of the City of Santa Rosa, Laguna;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa, Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Wednesday, 18 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City
of Santa Rosa, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

te.
SALWADOR C. MEDIALDEA
