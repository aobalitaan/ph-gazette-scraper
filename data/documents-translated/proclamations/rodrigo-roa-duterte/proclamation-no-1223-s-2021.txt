MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1223

DECLARING MONDAY, 27 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 27 September 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas, will mark the 156" birth anniversary of General Miguel Malvar;
and .

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malvar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 27 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality_of
Malvar, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
