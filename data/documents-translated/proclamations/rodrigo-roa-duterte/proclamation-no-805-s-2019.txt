MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 805

DECLARING MONDAY, 09 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAIS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 09 September 2019, Monday, the City of Bais, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 51°' Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bais be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 09 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bais,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of Septembarthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

: oS
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
Executive Secretary
