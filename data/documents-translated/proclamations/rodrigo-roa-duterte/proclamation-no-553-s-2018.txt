BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 553

IN THE CITY OF NAGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, Jesse M. Robredo, who was appointed Secretary of the
Department of the Interior and Local Government, and a champion of good
governance, was instrumental in the implementation of far-reaching reforms in local

governance;

WHEREAS, Secretary Robredo served with utmost dedication as Mayor of
Naga City from 1988 to 1998 and from 2001 to 2010;

WHEREAS, his outstanding performance as Mayor of Naga City earned him
numerous awards such as the Magsaysay Award for Government Service in 2000,
the first Filipino Mayor to be conferred such distinction;

WHEREAS, Secretary Robredo, considered one of the local heroes of Naga
City, passed away in a tragic plane accident on 18 August 2012; and

WHEREAS, his many virtues as a public servant have permanently earned
him the gratitude of the City he led, and the Republic he served;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 18 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Naga, Province
of Camarines Sur, in commemoration of the death anniversary of Secretary Jesse M.

Robredo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Eighteen.
