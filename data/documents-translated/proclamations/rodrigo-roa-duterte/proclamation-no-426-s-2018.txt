MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 426

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MALOLOS, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS. on Saturday, 17 February 2018, the City of Malolos, Bulacan,
marks the birth anniversary of Teodulo Capili Natividad; and

WHEREAS, it Is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Malolos,
Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies,

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Saturday,
17 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Malolos, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this1 5th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.
