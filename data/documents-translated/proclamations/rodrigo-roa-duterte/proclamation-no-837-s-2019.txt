MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 837

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAAL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Taal,

Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 438" Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary, .
by authority of the Presidentt RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Monday, 11 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Taal,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
