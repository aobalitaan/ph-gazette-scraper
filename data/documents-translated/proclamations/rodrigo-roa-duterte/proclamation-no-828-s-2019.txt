MALACANAN PALACE
* MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 828

DECLARING THURSDAY, 24 OCTOBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 24 October 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Pili, Province
of Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of the Cimarrones Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pili
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 24 October 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pili,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

“dat
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
