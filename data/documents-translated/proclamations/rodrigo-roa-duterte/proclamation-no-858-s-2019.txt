MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 858

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 DECEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 16 December 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 101° Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malvar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 16 December 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Malvar, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day ofNovember_. in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

an Wea
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
