BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.224

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LIPA

WHEREAS. the City of Lipa marks the celebration of its 70” Founding
Anniversary on Tuesday, 20 June 2017; and

WHEREAS. it !s but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President. RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby deciare
Tuesday. 20 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this lst dayof June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
