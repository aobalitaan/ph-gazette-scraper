MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 744

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian, Province of Zamboanga del Sur will mark
the celebration of its 50"" Charter Anniversary on 22 June 2019;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Pagadian to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Friday, 21 June 2019, be declared a
special (non-working) day in lieu of 22 June 2019, which falls on a Saturday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian, Province of
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
