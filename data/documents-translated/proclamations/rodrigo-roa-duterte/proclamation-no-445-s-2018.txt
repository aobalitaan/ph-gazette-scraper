MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 445

DECLARING SATURDAY, 24 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LIGAO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 24 March 2018, the City of Ligao, Albay, marks the
celebration of its Cityhood Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Ligao,
Albay, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 24 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Ligao,
Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
