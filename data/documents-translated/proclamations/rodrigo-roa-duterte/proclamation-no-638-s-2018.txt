MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 638

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 DECEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Bayawan, Province of Negros Oriental, will mark the
celebration of its 18" charter anniversary on 23 December 2018, Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Bayawan to celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Bayawan requested that Friday, 21
December 2018, be declared a special (non-working) day instead of 23 December
2018, which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayawan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 December 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan, Province of
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
