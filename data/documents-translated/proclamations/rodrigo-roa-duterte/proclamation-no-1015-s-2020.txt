MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1015

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANSALAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2018, Friday, the Municipality of Bansalan,
Province of Davao del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 68" Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bansalan be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bansalan,
Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of September , jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
