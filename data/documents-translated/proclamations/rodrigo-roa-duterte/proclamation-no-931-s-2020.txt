MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 931

DECLARING TUESDAY, 31 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 31 March 2020, Tuesday, the City of Sipalay, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 49" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 31 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sipalay,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority/of the President:
