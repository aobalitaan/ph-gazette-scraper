MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1119

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Baliwag,
Province of Bulacan, will mark the 158" birth anniversary of Mariano Ponce;

WHEREAS, Mariano Ponce, a native of Baliwag, Bulacan, was a prominent
member of the propaganda movement and one of the leading figures in the
Philippine reform movement during the Spanish colonization; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baliwag be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 22 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Baliwag,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
