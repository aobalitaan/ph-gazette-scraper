MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1309

DECLARING TUESDAY, 29 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAMBUSAO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ

WHEREAS, on 29 March 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Mambusao,
Province of Capiz, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mambusao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national

government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 29 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mambusao, Province of Capiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21stday of February , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
