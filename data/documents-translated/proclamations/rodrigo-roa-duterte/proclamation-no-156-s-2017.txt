MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 156

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYUG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, Friday, 17 March 2017, marks the Founding Anniversary of the
Municipality of Tayug, Pangasinan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tayug, Pangasinan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Friday, 17 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tayug, Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

we A
MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary.
