MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1355

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 MAY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SUAL, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 20 May 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Sual, Province of
Pangasinan, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sual be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
20 May 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sual, Province of
Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29¢Hay of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
