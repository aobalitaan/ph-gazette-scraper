MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 453

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 25 April 2018, marks the celebration of the 17"
Founding Anniversary of the City of Isabela, Basilan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Isabela,
Basilan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Isabela,
Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of March_, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

4"

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
Executive Secretary
