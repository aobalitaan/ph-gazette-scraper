MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1297

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 02 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 02 March 2022, Wednesday, the Province of La Union will
mark the celebration of its 172"¢ Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of La
Union be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 02 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of La
Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
