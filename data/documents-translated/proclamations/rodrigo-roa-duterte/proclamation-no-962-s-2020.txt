MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 962

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 17 JUNE 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2020, Wednesday, the City of Lapu-Lapu will mark
the celebration of its 59"" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lapu-Lapu
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 17 June 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lapu-Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

Wey
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
