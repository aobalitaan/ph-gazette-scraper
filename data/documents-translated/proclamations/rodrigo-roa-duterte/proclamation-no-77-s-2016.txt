MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 77

DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija will celebrate its annual
festival of thanksgiving for bountiful harvest on Tuesday, 22 November 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Palayan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Tuesday, 22 November 2016, as a special (non-working) day
in the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day Of October. in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
