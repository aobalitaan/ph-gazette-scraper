MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 401

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN

WHEREAS, on Monday, 22 January 2018, the City of Pagadian, Zamboanga
del Sur marks the celebration of the 78” Pasalamat Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the Presidenti RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 22 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
