MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 213

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, the Province of Ifugao marks the celebration of its Foundation
Day every 18" of June;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Ifugao celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Governor requested that Monday, 19 June 2017 be
declared as a special non-working day, instead of 18 June 2017, which falls on a

Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Ifugao, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of \fugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19thday of May __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
