MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 785.

DECLARING TUESDAY, 13 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAGPET, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 13 August 2019, Tuesday, the Municipality of Magpet,
Province of Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 56" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Magpet be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 13 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Magpet,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

és

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
_ Executive Secretary
