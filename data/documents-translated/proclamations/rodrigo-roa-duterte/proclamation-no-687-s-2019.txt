MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 687

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAMBUSAO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ

WHEREAS, on 29 March 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Mambusao,
Province of Capiz, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mambusao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
29 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mambusao,
Province of Capiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
