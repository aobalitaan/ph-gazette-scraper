MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 529

DECLARING SATURDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 08 September 2018, a Saturday, the City of Digos, Davao del
Sur, will mark its 4g Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 08 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof July | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

cone NEDALDE
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
