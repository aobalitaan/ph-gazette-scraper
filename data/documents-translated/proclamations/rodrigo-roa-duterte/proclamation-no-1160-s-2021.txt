MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 3160

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BAYUGAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2021, Monday, the City of Bayugan, Province of
Agusan del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 14" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayugan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayugan, Province
of Agusan del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
