MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT 0F THE  PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.1022
DECLARING     WEDNESDAY,     30     SEPTEMBER    2020,     A    SPECIAL     (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MASBATE,  PROVINCE 0F MASBATE
WHEREAS,   on   30   September   2020,   Wednesday,   the   City   of   Masbate,
Province of Masbate, will mark the celebration of its 20th Cityhood Anniversary; and
WHEREAS,  it  is  but fitting  and  proper that the  people  of the  City of Masbate
be given full opportunity to celebrate and  participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies,  subject  to  community  quarantine,  social  distancing,  and  other  public
health measures;
NOW,  THEREFORE,  I,  SALVADOR  C.  MEDIALDEA,  Executive  Secretary,
by   authority   of  the   President,   RODRIGO   ROA   DUTERTE,   do   hereby   declare
Wednesday,   30   September   2020,   a   special   (non-working)   day   in   the   City   of
Masbate, Province of Masbate.
IN  WITNESS WHEREOF,  I  have  hereunto  set  my  hand  and  caused  the  seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
Done in the City of Manila, this  25tlday off;eptember in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
By authority of the President:
