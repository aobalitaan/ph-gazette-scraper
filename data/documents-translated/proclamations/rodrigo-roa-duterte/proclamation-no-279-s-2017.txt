MAT ACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 279

DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL

NORTE

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 22 August 2017, the Municipality of Liloy,
Zamboanga del Norte, marks the celebration of its 66" Foundation Day; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 22 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Liloy,
Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

=>
SALWADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary A
