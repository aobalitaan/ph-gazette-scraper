MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 576

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 17 September 2018, a Monday, the Province of Zamboanga
del Sur will mark its 66'" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 17 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10thday of September jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
