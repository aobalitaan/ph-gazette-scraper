MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 540

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MATEO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 21 September 2018, a Friday, the Municipality of San Mateo,
Rizal, will mark its 447" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Mateo, Rizal, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Mateo,
Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this25th gay of | July | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
