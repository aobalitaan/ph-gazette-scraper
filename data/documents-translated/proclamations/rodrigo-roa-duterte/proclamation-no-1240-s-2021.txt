MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1240

DECLARING FRIDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2021, Friday, the City of Valenzuela will mark
the celebration of its 398" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Valenzuela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
12 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
