MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 238

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Carmen, Davao del Norte was created under
Republic Act No. 4745 dated 18 June 1966;

WHEREAS, on Monday, 19 June 2017, the Municipality of Carmen, Davao
del Norte will celebrate its Araw ng Carmen,

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Carmen, Davao del
Norte, celebrate the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan of Carmen, Davao del Norte
requested that Monday, 19 June 2017 be declared as a special non-working day,
instead of 18 June 2017, which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carmen, Davao del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Monday, 19 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Carmen, Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
