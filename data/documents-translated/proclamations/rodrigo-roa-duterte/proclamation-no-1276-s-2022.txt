BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1276

RESERVING A PARCEL OF PATRIMONIAL LAND WITHIN THE ALABANG STOCK
FARM, MUNTINLUPA CITY, FOR RESEARCH INSTITUTE FOR TROPICAL
MEDICINE (RITM) BUILDING SITE PURPOSES, AND TRANSFERRING
OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH - RITM

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, upon the recommendation of the Secretary
of Environment and Natural Resources, and taking into consideration the Deed of Lot
Exchange dated 14 October 2021 between the Republic of the Philippines and Filinvest
Alabang, Inc., , RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the Philippines,
hereby withdraw from entry, sale or other form of disposition a certain parcel of patrimonial
land within the Alabang Stock Farm, situated in Barangay Alabang, Muntinlupa City, Island of
Luzon, and reserve the same for the Research Institute for Tropical Medicine (RITM) Building
Site, and transfer ownership of said property in favor of the Department of Health (DOH) -
RITM, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
