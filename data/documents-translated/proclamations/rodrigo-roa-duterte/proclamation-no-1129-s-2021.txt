BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
- PROCLAMATION NO. 1129

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCEL OF LAND ON
WHICH IT STANDS, LOCATED ALONG GENERAL MAXILOM AVENUE,
CEBU CITY, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION
TECHNOLOGY CENTER) TO BE KNOWN AS CYBERGATE GALLERIA,
PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC
ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or the
“Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the
recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority
(PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the Philippines,
do hereby create and designate a building to be known as Cybergate Galleria, with a
gross floor area of Thirty-Two Thousand Six Hundred Twenty-One and 7/100
square meters (32,621.07 m2), more or less, and the parcel of land on which it stands,
located along General Maxilom Avenue, Cebu City, as a Special Economic Zone
(Information Technology Center), subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as
amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, PEZA Board of Directors
Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011),
15-240 (s. 2015) and 17-472 (s. 2017), and Resolution No. 17-675 (s. 2017), as
amended by Resolution No. 20-053 (s. 2020), with an area of Three Thousand Forty-
Six square meters (3,046 m7), more or less, as defined by the following technical
