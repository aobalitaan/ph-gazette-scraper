MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 869

DECLARING SATURDAY, 04 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 04 January 2020, Saturday, the Municipality of Naujan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its 115" Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naujan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 04 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and: caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd dayof December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
