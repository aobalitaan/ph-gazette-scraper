MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 443

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro, marks the
celebration of the “Sulyog Festival” on 17 to 19 March 2018;

WHEREAS. to enable the people of the Municipality of Bongabong, Oriental
Mindoro, to celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday,
19 March 2018, be declared as a special non-working day; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.
