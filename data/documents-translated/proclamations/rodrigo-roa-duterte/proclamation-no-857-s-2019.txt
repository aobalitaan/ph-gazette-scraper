MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 857

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 DECEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NARVACAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on 13 December 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Narvacan,
Province of Ilocos Sur, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Narvacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
13 December 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Narvacan,
Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

es
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
