MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 544

DECLARING SATURDAY, 04 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, 04 August 2018, a Saturday, will mark the 6” cityhood
anniversary of the City of Cabuyao, Laguna; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabuyao,
Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 04 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabuyao,
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th gay of July | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Hasse westioe
