MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 875

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 16 January 2020, Thursday, the City of Batangas will mark its
Tourism and Cultural Day together with the celebration of its city fiesta; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batangas

be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this ©th dayof January | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
