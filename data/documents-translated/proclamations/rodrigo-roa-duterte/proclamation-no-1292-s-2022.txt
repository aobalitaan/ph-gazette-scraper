MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1292

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS on 18 February 2022, Friday, the City of Bayawan, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of the Tawo-Tawo Festival;

WHEREAS, the Tawo-Tawo Festival reflects the culture of the City of
Bayawan being a rice granary of the southern part of the Province of Negros
Oriental; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayawan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan, Province of
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
