MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 870

DECLARING MONDAY, 06 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUANG, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bauang, Province of La Union will mark the
celebration of the Baggak Festival Bauang Founding Anniversary on 05 January
2020;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Bauang to celebrate
the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan requested that Monday, 06 January 2020, be
declared a special (non-working) day in lieu of 05 January 2020, which falls on a
Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauang be given full opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 06 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bauang, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of December , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
