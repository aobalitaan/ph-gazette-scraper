MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 826

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 OCTOBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 18 October 2019, Friday, the Province of Negros Oriental will
mark the celebration of the Buglasan Festival: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Negros Oriental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 October 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th dayof October  , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

en

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary 4
