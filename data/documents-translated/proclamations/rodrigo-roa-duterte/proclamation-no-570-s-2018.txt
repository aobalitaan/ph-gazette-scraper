MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 570

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 16 September 2018, a Sunday, the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Bontoc, to celebrate
the occasion, the Office of the Mayor and the Sangguniang Bayan of the Municipality
of Bontoc requested that 17 September 2018, a Monday, instead of 16 September
2018 which falls on a Sunday, be declared a special (non-working) day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bontoc be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 17 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bontoc, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authcrity of the President:
