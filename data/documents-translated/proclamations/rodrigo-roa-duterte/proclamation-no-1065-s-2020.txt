MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1065

DECLARING SATURDAY, 02 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONIFACIO, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 02 January 2021, Saturday, the Municipality of Bonifacio,
Province of Misamis Occidental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bonifacio be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 02 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bonifacio, Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
