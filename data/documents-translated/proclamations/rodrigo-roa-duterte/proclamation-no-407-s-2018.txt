MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 407

DECLARING THURSDAY, 08 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GODOD, ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, Thursday, 08 February 2018, marks the celebration of the
Founding Anniversary of the Municipality of Godod, Zamboanga del Norte; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Godod, Zamboanga del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate
in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 08 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Godod, Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this23rd day of January, jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
