MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

-BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
“PROCLAMATION NO. 1252

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DASMARINAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 26 November 2021, Friday, the City of Dasmarifias, Province
of Cavite, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Dasmarifias be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
26 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dasmarifias, Province
of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15h day of November, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

fo
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive, Secretary (“
