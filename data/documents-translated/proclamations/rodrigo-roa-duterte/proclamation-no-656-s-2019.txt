MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 656

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 06 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE (EXCEPT ILIGAN CITY),
AND THE MUNICIPALITIES OF ALEOSAN, CARMEN, KABACAN,
MIDSAYAP, PIKIT, PIGKAWAYAN, AND TULUNAN, ALL IN THE
PROVINCE OF NORTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections will conduct a plebiscite on 06
February 2019, Wednesday, in the Province of Lanao del Norte (except Iligan City),
and the Municipalities of Aleosan, Carmen, Kabacan, Midsayap, Pikit, Pigkawayan,
and Tulunan, all in the Province of North Cotabato, for the ratification of Republic Act
11054 dated 27 July 2018, otherwise known as the “Organic Law for the
Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao”; and

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Province of Lanao del Norte
(except Iligan City), and the Municipalities of Aleosan, Carmen, Kabacan, Midsayap,
Pikit, Pigkawayan, and Tulunan, all in the Province of North Cotabato, the
opportunity to actively and fully participate in the plebiscite and exercise their right to
vote, it is but fitting and proper to declare 06 February 2019 a special (non-working)
day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 06 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the above-mentioned
places.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st dayof January , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

eS
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
