MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 678

DECLARING SATURDAY, 02 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 02 March 2019, Saturday, the Province of La Union will mark
the celebration of its 169" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of La
Union be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 02 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of February jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
