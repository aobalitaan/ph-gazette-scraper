MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 739

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MACO, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Maco, Province of
Compostela Valley, will mark the celebration of its 52" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maco be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 17 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maco,
Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th dayof June, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

a
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary |
