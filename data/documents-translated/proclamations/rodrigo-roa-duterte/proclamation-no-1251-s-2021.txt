MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1251

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE
ORO

WHEREAS, on 25 November 2021, Thursday, the Municipality of Maragusan,
Province of Davao de Oro, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maragusan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures; ,

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Maragusan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15thday of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
