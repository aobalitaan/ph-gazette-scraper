MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 67

DECLARING TUESDAY, 4 OCTOBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INFANTA, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS , Tuesday, 4 October 2016, marks the Foundation Day of the Municipality of lnfanta, Province of Pangasinan;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of lnfanta, Pangasinan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW , THEREFORE , I , SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE , do hereby declare Tuesday, 4 October 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of lnfanta, Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
