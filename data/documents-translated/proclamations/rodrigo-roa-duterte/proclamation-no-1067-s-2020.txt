MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1067

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 06 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF OROQUIETA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 06 January 2021, Wednesday, the City of Oroquieta,
Province of Misamis Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 518* Charter
Anniversary and its 91St Anniversary as the capital of the Province of Misamis
Occidental; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Oroquieta
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 06 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Oroquieta,
Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of December, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authorit the President:

ae

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
