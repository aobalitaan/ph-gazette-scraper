MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 539

DECLARING THE YEAR 2018 AS THE “YEAR FOR THE
CELEBRATION OF THE CENTENNIAL ANNIVERSARY OF THE
UPSILON SIGMA PHI”

WHEREAS, the Upsilon Sigma Phi was established on 18 November 1918 in
the University of the Philippines, Manila;

WHEREAS, the Upsilon Sigma Phi marks the entire year of 2018 as the
period of celebrating its centennial anniversary; and

WHEREAS, the government recognizes the Upsilon Sigma Phi for its
significant contributions to numerous civic and humanitarian causes, as well as the
dedication and commitment of its members to public service and nation-building;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, do hereby declare the year 2018 as the “Year for the Celebration of the
Centennial Anniversary of the Upsilon Sigma Phi.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 25 tday of July _ in the year of our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
