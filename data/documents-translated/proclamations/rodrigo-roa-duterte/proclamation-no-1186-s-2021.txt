MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1186

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 JULY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 19 July 2021, Monday, the City of Digos, Province of Davao
del Sur, will mark the celebration of the Padigosan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 July 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Province of
Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my*hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9thdayof July _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
