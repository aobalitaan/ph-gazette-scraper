MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 668

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAGO, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 February 2019, Tuesday, the City of Bago, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 53 Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bago be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bago,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
