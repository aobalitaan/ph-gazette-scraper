MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 698

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 APRIL 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 07 April 2019, Sunday, Mountain Province will mark its
Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of Mountain Province to fully celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of Mountain Province requested that
Monday, 08 April 2019 (instead of 07 April 2019 which falls on a Sunday), be
declared a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Mountain
Province be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Monday,
08 April 2019, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of March _, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
