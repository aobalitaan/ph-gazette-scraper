MALACANAN PALACE *
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 387

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUANG, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bauang, Province of La Union will celebrate
the “Baggak Festival Bauang Founding Anniversary” on Friday, 05 January 2018;

and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauang, Province of La Union be given full opportunity to participate and celebrate
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
05 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauang,
Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

i_
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
