MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1032

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 NOVEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUENAVISTA, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 06 November 2020, Friday, the Municipality of Buenavista,
Province of Marinduque, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Buenavista be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
06 November 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Buenavista,
Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd dayof October __, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

¢

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
