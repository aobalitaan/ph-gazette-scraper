MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES:
PROCLAMATION NO. 141

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF
LEYTE

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 25 January 2017, marks the 446" Founding
Anniversary of the Municipality of Carigara, Leyte; «

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carigara, Leyte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Carigara, Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

ON

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
