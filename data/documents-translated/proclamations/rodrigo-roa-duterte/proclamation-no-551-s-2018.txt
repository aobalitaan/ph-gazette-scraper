MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 551

DECLARING 17 AUGUST 2018 (FRIDAY) AND 20 AUGUST 2018 (MONDAY)
SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAYS IN THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, the City of Davao observes the weeklong celebration of the
Kadayawan Festival in the month of August;

WHEREAS to enable the people of the City of Davao to celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod requested that 17 August 2018 (Friday) and
20 August 2018 (Monday) be declared special (non-working) days in celebration of
the Indigenous People’s Day and the Araw ng Pasasalamat, respectively, in relation
to the Kadayawan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare 17
August 2018 (Friday) and 20 August 2018 (Monday) special (non-working) days in
the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of July | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
