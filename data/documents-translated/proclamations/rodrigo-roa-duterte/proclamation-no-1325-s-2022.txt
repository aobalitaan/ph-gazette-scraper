MALACANAN PAL/ACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1325

DECLARING SATURDAY, 19 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CANLAON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2022, Saturday, the City of Canlaon, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of the Pasayaw Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Canlaon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 19 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Canlaon,
Province of Negros Oriental.

‘IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th | day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
