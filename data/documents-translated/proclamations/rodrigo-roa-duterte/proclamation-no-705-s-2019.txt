MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.705

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 APRIL 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 19 April 2019, the City of Tarlac will mark the celebration of its
218 Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tarlac to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the City Mayor of Tarlac requested that Friday, 26 April 2019,
instead of 19 April 2019 which falls on a Good Friday, be declared a special (non-
working) day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
26 April 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of April_, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

q
SALWADOR C. MEDIALDEA
