MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 344

DECLARING SATURDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS. on Saturday, 25 November 2017, the Municipality of Maragusan,
Compostela Valley marks the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maragusan, Compostela Valley, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate
in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 25 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Maragusan, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authsrity of the President:
