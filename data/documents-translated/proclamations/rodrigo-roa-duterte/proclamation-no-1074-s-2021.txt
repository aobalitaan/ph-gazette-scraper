PROCLAMATION NO. 1074

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 January 2021, Wednesday, the Coffee Festival will be
held in the City of Lipa, Province of Batangas, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to participate in the programs and activities lined up during the
said festival, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

Sen
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE

Executive Secretary
