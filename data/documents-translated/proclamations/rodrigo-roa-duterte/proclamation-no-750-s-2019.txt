MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 750

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN CARLOS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2019, Monday, the City of San Carlos, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Carlos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Monday, 01 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of San
Carlos, Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18tWay of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

Me
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE

Executive Secretary
