MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 195

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, Friday, 26 May 2017, marks the celebration of the Charter Day
of the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baliwag, Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
26 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of April __, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

ex
SALVADOR Cc. MEDIALDEA

A Executive Secretary -
