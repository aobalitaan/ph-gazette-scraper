MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 231

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Friday, 16 June 2017, marks the 67"" Founding Anniversary of
the Municipality of Pamplona, Negros Oriental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pamplona, Negros Oriental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
16 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pamplona,
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of June | jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
