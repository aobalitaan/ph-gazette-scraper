MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 747

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF BAYUGAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2019, Friday, the City of Bayugan, Province of
Agusan del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 12" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayugan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayugan, Province of
Agusan del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

NX
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE/A

Executive Secretary
