MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION  NO.318
DECLARING   FRIDAY,   20   OCTOBER   2017,   AS   A  SPECIAL   (NON-WORKING)
DAY     IN     THE     MUNICIPALITY     OF     PIGCAWAYAN,     PROVINCE     OF
COTABATO
WHEREAS,  Friday,  20 October 2017,  marks the 64th Foundation Anniversary
of the Municipality of Pigcawayan,  Cotabato;  and
WHEREAS,  it  is  but  fittlng  and  proper  that  the  people  of  the  Municipality  of
Pigcawayan,  Cotabato,  be  given  full  opportunity  to  celebrate  and  participate  in  the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.
NOW,  THEREFORE,  I,  SALVADOR  C.  MEDIALDEA,   Executive  Secretary,
by authority of the  President,  RODRIGO  ROA  DUTERTE,  do  hereby declare  Friday,
20  October 2017,  as  a  special  (non-working)  day  in  the  Municipality of Pigcawayan,
Cotabato.
IN  WITNESS  WHEREOF,  I  have  hereunto  set  my  hand  and  caused  the  seal
of the  Republlc of the  Philippines to be  affixed.
Done  in  the  Clty  of Manila,  this  [8thday of    september in the  year of Our
