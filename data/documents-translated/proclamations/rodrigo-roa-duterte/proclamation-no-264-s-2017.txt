MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 264 .

D
weet FRIDAY, 28 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL, (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
ITY OF CABADBARAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, Friday, 28 July 2017, marks the 40" Charter Day of the City of
Cabadbaran, Province of Agusan del Norte; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cabadbaran, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
28 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabadbaran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary /4
