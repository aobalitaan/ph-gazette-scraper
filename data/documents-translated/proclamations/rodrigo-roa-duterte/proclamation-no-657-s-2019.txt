MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 657

DECLARING SATURDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2019, Saturday, the Province of Ilocos Norte will
mark the closing of its Bicentennial Year celebration; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ilocos
Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 02 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Ilocos
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of January in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

Kove os
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
