MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 601

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF HINABANGAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 15 October 2018, a Monday, the Municipality of Hinabangan,
Province of Samar, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Hinabangan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 15 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Hinabangan, Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
