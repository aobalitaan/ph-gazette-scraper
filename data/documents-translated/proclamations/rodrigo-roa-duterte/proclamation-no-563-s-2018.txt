MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 563

DECLARING SATURDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL

WHEREAS, on 08 September-2018, a Saturday, the Municipality of Loon,
Province of Bohol, will mark the celebration of the Sidlakasilak Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Loon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |!, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 08 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Loon, Province of Bohol.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
