MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 603

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, on 26 October 2018, a Friday, the Province of Catanduanes will
mark the celebration of its founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Catanduanes be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
26 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th dayof October _, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

fe
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
