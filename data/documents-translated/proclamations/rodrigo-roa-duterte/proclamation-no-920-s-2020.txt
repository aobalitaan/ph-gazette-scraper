MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 920

DECLARING THURSDAY, 19 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CANLAON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2020, Thursday, the City of Canlaon, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of the Pasayaw Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Canlaon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 19 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Canlaon,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof March — , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
