MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 155

DECLARING TUESDAY, 7 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 7 March 2017, marks the celebration of the 43”
Founding Anniversary of the Province of Basilan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Basilan, be given full 6pportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MENARDO I. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby

declare Tuesday, 7 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of February in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
