MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 120

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS, the City of Navotas marks the celebration of its qi"
Founding Anniversary on Monday, 16 January 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Navotas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Monday, 16 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in
the City of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City,of Manila, this 14thday of December, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
