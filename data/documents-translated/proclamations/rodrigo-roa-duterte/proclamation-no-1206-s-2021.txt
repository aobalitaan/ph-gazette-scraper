MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA.

BY THE.PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1206

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 03 September 2021, Friday, the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of its 53 Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of lriga be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
03 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th dayof August __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

mee
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
