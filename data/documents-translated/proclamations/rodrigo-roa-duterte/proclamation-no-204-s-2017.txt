MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 204

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTIAGO

WHEREAS. under Republic Act No. 7720, the Municipality of Santiago was
converted into an independent component city on 5 May 1994;

WHEREAS. Friday, 05 May 2017, marks the celebration of the Foundation
Day of the City of Santiago,

WHEREAS. tt is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
05 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd _—_ day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
