MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION No. °°7

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 02 November 2018, a Friday, the Province of Misamis
Occidental, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Misamis Occidental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
02 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Misamis
Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this245* day of OCteber in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
