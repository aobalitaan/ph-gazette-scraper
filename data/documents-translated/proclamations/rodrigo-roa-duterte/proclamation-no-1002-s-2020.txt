MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1002

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 04 September 2020, Friday, the Municipality of Monkayo,
Province of Davao de Oro, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Monkayo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
04 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Monkayo,
Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this25th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
