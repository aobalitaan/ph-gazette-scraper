MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1122

DECLARING MONDAY, 29 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINANGONAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 29 March 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Binangonan,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of its 121st Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Binangonan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 29 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Binangonan, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of March , in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

os
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
