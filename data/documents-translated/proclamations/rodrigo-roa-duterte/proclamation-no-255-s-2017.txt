MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 255

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MABALACAT, PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on Friday, 21 July 2017, the City of Mabalacat, Pampanga,
marks the celebration of its Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabatacat,
Pampanga, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat, Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

Stig
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
