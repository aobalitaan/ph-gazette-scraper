MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 464

DECLARING 17 MARCH 2018 TO 16 MARCH 2019 AS THE YEAR
FOR THE CELEBRATION OF THE 95™ ANNIVERSARY OF THE
PROFESSIONAL REGULATORY BOARD OF ACCOUNTANCY

WHEREAS, the development goal of inequality-reducing transformation, by
accelerating human capital development and expanding economic opportunities in
industries and services, remains one of the overriding objectives of the government
under the Philippine Development Plan 2017-2022;

WHEREAS, under Republic Act (RA) No. 9298 or the Philippine Accountancy Act
of 2004, the State recognizes the importance of accountants in nation-building and
development, and shall develop and nurture competent, ethical, productive, and well-
rounded professional accountants whose standards of practice and service shall be
excellent, world class, and globally competitive through inviolable, honest, effective, and
credible licensure examinations and through regulatory measures, programs, and
activities that foster their growth and development:

WHEREAS, the Professional Regulatory Board (PRB) of Accountancy is
mandated under RA No. 9298 to, among others, prescribe and adopt the rules and
regulations necessary for carrying out the provisions of RA No. 9298, to supervise the
registration, licensure and practice of accountancy in the Philippines, and to monitor the
conditions affecting the practice of accountancy and adopt such measures, including
promulgation of accounting and auditing standards, rules and regulations and best
practices as may be deemed proper for the enhancement and maintenance of high
professional, ethical, accounting, and auditing standards;

WHEREAS, the PRB of Accountancy will be celebrating its 95" year since its
formal organization on 17 March 1923 under Act No. 3105 (s. 1923);

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of
the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the
