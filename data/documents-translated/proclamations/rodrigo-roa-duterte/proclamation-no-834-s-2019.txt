MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 834

DECLARING TUESDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2019, Tuesday, the City of Valenzuela will
mark the celebration of its 396" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Valenzuela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 12 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7t5 day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
