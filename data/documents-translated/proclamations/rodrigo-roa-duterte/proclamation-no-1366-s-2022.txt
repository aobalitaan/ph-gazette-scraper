MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1366

DECLARING SATURDAY, 18 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Carmen, Davao del Norte was created by
virtue of Republic Act No. 4745 dated 18 June 1966;

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2022, Saturday, the Municipality of Carmen, Davao
del Norte, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carmen be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 18 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Carmen,
Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two .

By authority of the President:

be
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,~
Executive Secretary
