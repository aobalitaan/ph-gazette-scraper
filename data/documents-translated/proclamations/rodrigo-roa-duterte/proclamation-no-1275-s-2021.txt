MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1275

DECLARING SATURDAY, 15 JANUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 15 January 2022, Saturday, the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of the 27"" Coconut Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 15 January 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
