MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1280

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 JANUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, will mark
the celebration of its Pasalamat Festival 2022, which features various
commemorative activities to be held from 07 to 17 January 2022;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Pagadian to celebrate the
occasion, the City Mayor requested that Monday, 17 January 2022, be declared a
special (non-working) day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 17 January 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
