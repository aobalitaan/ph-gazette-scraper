MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1050

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 DECEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF
BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 16 December 2020, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Calatagan, Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calatagan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 December 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Calatagan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th dayof November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

79 ts

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
