MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 61

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 SEPTEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS , Monday, 19 September 2016, marks the celebration of the 64th Anniversary of the Province of Zamboanga del Sur;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW , THEREFORE , I , SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE , do hereby declare Monday, 19 September 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
