MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 908

DECLARING THE YEAR 2020 AS THE “YEAR FOR THE
CELEBRATION OF THE QUADRICENTENNIAL ANNIVERSARY OF
THE COLEGIO DE SAN JUAN DE LETRAN”

WHEREAS, Section 23, Article Il of the 1987 Constitution provides that the
State shall encourage non-governmental, community-based or — sectoral
organizations that promote the welfare of the nation;

WHEREAS, the Colegio de San Juan de Letran in Intramuros, Manila, one of °
the oldest academic institutions in the country, will celebrate its quadricentennial
anniversary in 2020; and

WHEREAS, the State recognizes the Colegio de San Juan de Letran’s
contributions to our history and nation-building, through the formation of individuals
who have excelled in various fields, including national heroes, leaders and artists, as
well as through community services to the poor and needy;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, do hereby declare the year 2020 as the “Year for the Celebration of the
Quadricentennial Anniversary of the Colegio de San Juan de Letran.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this21st day of February jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
