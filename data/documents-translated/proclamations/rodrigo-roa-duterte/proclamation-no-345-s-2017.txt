MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 345

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, the Province of Sarangani will celebrate its 25" Founding
Anniversary on Tuesday, 28 November 2017; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sarangani, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with

appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE,. do hereby declare
Tuesday, 28 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of

Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

tw
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
