MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.803

DECLARING THURSDAY, 05 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 05 September 2019, Thursday, the City of General Santos,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 52"? Inauguration Day
and the 21*' Tuna Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 05 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of General
Santos, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29cnday Of august , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
