* MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 173

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 166 DATED 24 FEBRUARY 2017 BY
DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF DAVAO, IN CELEBRATION OF THE ARAW NG
PASASALAMAT INSTEAD OF THE 80" ARAW NG DABAW

WHEREAS, the City of Davao marks the celebration of its Araw ng Dabaw on
16 March 2017;

WHEREAS, Proclamation No, 166 dated 24 February 2017 declared Friday,
17 March 2017 as a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao;

WHEREAS, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Davao requested that Friday, 17
March 2017, be declared as a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao in
relation to the 80" Araw ng Dabaw 2017 celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby amend
Proclamation No. 166 dated 24 February 2017 by declaring Friday, 17 March 2017,
as a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao, in celebration of the Araw ng
Pasasalamat instead of the 80” Araw ng Dabaw.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rq day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
