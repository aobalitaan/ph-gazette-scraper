DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2022, Tuesday, the City of Malaybalay, Province of
Bukidnon, will mark the celebration of its 24'" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Malaybalay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Tuesday, 22 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Malaybalay,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

aie
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary,
