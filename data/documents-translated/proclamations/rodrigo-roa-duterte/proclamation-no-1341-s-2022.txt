PROCLAMATION NO. i341

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 MAY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MALABON

WHEREAS, on 21 May 2022, Saturday, the City of Malabon will mark the
celebration of its 423" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Malabon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 May 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Malabon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this gia day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
