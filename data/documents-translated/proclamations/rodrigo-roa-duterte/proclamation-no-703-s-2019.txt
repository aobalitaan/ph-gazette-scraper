MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 703

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 APRIL 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF HIMAMAYLAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2019, Thursday, the City of Himamaylan, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its annual fiesta, the Himaya-an
Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Himamaylan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 April 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Himamaylan,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof April, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

— J
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
