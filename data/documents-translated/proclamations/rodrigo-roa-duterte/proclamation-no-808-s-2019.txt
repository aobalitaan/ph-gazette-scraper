MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 808

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 11 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUPI, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 11 September 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of Tupi,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 66" Founding
Anniversary and the 10" Agten Tufi Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tupi be given full opportunity to celebrate in these occasions with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 11 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tupi, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of Septembej the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

w
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDES
