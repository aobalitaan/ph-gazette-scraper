MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 600

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 October 2018, a Friday, the Province of Negros Oriental
will mark the celebration of the Buglasan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of

Negros Oriental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
19 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thislOth day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

S ide,
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
