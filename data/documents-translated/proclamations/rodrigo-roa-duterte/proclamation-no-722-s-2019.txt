MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 722

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MALABON

WHEREAS, on 21 May 2019, Tuesday, the City of Malabon will mark its
420" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Malabon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Malabon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

S
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
