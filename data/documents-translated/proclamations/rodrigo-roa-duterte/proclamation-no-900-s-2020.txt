MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 900

DECLARING THURSDAY, 13 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PARANAQUE

WHEREAS, on 13 February 2020, Thursday, the City of Parafaque will mark
the celebration of its 22" Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Parahaque
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 13 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Parafhaque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31stday of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

by

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
