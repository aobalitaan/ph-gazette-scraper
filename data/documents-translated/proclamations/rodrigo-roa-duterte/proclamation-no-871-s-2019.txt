MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 871

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 January 2020, Friday, the City of Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 80" founding anniversary and 20"
Hinugyaw Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
10 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of December _, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
