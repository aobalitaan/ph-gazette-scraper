MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1318

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 18 March 2022, Friday, the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet, will mark the celebration of its 41st Strawberry Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the peopie of the Municipality of La
Trinidad be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2naq dayof march _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
