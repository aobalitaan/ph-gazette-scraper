MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 127

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO

WHEREAS, the City of San Pablo marks the celebration of the 20m
Coconut Festival and Street Dancing Competition (Mardi Gras) on Friday, 13
January 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Pablo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 13 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the
City of San Pablo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th — day of January, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

Yt
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
