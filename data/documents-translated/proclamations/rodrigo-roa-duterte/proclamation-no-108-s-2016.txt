BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 108

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Friday, 23 December 2016, marks the celebration of the
16" Charter Day of the City of Bayawan, Negros Oriental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Bayawan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion

with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 23 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day in
the City of Bayawan, Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of Novemberin the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
