MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 412

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS, Monday, 26 February 2018, marks the celebration of the 81°
Charter Anniversary of the City of Zamboanga; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Zamboanga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 26 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

7
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
