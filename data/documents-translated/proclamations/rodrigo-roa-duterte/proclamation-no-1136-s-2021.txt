MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1136

DECLARING TUESDAY, 27 APRIL 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MUNICIPALITY OF MONTALBAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 27 April 2021, Tuesday, the Municipality of Montalban,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of its 150" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Montalban be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 27 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Montalban, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thday of April , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
