MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1340

DECLARING SATURDAY, 30 APRIL 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAGUPAN, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, the City of Dagupan, Province of Pangasinan, will mark the
celebration of the Bangus Festival during the month of April;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Dagupan to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 30 April 2022, Saturday, be
declared a special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan; and

WHEREAS, it is ‘but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dagupan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 30 April 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan,
Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this ota day of April __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

et
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary

Haier
