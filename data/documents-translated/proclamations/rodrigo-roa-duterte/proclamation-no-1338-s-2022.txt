MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1338

DECLARING SATURDAY, 30 APRIL 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 30 April 2022, Saturday, the Municipality of Calatagan,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Cultural Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calatagan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 30 April 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Calatagan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this stn dayof April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
