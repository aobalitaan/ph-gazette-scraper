MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 208

DECLARING SATURDAY, 24 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MANILA

WHEREAS, Saturday, 24 June 2017, marks the celebration of the 446"
Foundation Day of the City of Manila;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Manila, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 24 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisl19thday of May __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
