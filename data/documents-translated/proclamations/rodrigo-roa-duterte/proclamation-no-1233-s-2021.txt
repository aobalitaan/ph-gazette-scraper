MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1233

DECLARING THURSDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 04 November 2021, Thursday, the Province of Quezon will
mark the death anniversary of its local hero Apolinario dela Cruz, also known as
Hermano Puli, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 04 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of October _, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

t,
TAY
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
