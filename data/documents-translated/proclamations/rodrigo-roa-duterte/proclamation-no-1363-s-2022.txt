MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1363

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 MAY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALACA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 23 May 2022, Monday, the Municipality of Calaca, Province of
Batangas, will mark the birth anniversary of General Eleuterio Marasigan, a great
Calaquefio, patriot, and one of the leaders during the libertarian movement in the
Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the Presidentt RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 23 May 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calaca,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
By authority of the President:
