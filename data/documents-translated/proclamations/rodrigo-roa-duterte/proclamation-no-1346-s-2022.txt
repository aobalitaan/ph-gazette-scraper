MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1346

DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 APRIL 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 07 April 2022, Thursday, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of its 55'" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Mountain Province be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 07 April 2022, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila,this 6ta day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
