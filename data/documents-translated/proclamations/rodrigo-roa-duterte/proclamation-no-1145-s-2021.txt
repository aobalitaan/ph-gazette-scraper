MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1145

DECLARING SATURDAY, 22 MAY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 22 May 2021, Saturday, the City of Gingoog, Province of
Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of the Lubi-Lubi Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 22 May 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14thday of May _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
