MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 355

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADRE GARCIA, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Friday, 01 December 2017, marks the 68" Founding
Anniversary of the Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Padre Garcia, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
01 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the-Municipality of Padre

Garcia, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
