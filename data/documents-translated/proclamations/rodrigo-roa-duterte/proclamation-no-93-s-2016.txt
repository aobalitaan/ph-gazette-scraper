MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 93

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 OCTOBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, the Province of Davao Occidental marks the celebration of
its 1st Founding Anniversary on Friday, 28 October 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Davao Occidental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 28 October 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the
Province of Davao Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26thday of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

wry

MENARDOT. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary
