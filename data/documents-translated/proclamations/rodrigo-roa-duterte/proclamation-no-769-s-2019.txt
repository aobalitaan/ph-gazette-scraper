MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 769

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTIAGO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2019, Thursday, the City of Santiago, Province of
Isabela, will mark the celebration of the Aggaw na Naturales Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago,
Province of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of July , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
