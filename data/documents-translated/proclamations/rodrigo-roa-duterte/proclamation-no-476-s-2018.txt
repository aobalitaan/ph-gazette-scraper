MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 476

DECLARING MONDAY, 30 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Monday, 30 April 2018, marks the celebration of the Tourism and
Cultural Day in the Municipality of Calatagan, Batangas; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calatagan, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 30 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Calatagan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.
