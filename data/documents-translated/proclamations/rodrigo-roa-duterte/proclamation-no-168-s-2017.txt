MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 168

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 22 March 2017 marks the celebration of the 19"
Charter Day of the City of Malaybalay, Bukidnon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Malaybalay, Bukidnon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Malaybalay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
