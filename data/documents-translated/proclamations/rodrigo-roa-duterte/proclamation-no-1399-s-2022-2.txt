MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1399

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINGAWAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Bingawan,
Province of Iloilo, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary and the
Pagnahi-an Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bingawan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bingawan,
Province of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,
