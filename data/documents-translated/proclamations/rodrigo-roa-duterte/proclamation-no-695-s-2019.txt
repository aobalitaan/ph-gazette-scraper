MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 695

DECLARING THURSDAY, 04 APRIL 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 04 April 2019, Thursday, the City of Antipolo, Province of
Rizal, will mark the celebration of its 21° Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Antipolo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 04 April 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Antipolo, Province
of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th dayof March __, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

oe
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
