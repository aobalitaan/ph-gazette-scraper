MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 704

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 APRIL 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAMALAYAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Pinamalayan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of the Bahaghari Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pinamalayan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 April 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pinamalayan, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof April, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
