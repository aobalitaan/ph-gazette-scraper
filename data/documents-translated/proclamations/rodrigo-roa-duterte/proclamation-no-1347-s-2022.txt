MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1347

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 APRIL 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 19 April 2022, Tuesday, the City of Tarlac will mark the
celebration of its 24'° Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 April 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manilas this 11ta day of April, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

.

Ky
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
