MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 206

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 02 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BUTUAN

WHEREAS, the City of Butuan marks the celebration of “Adiaw Hong Butuan”
on Wednesday, 02 August 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Butuan, be

given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 02 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Butuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
Vee

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary

WY,

Office of the President
