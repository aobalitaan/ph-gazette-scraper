MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1282

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JANUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CURRIMAO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 21 January 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Currimao,
Province of Ilocos Norte, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Currimao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 January 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Currimao,
Province of llocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12thday of January , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
