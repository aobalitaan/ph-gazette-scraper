MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1018

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN
PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 16 September 2020, Wednesday, the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province, will mark the celebration of its 112" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bontoc be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bontoc, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

“sa
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
