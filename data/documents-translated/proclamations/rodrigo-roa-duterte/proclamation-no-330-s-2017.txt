MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 330

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 OCTOBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CALBAYOG

WHEREAS, on Monday, 16 October 2017, the City of Calbayog marks the
celebration of its Charter Day; and

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calbayog,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 16 October 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Calbayog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
