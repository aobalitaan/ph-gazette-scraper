MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 602

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MATI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Mati, Province of Davao Oriental, celebrates the
Sambuokan Festival every last weekend of October in commemoration of its date of
establishment as a municipality before eventually becoming a city;

WHEREAS, on 26 October 2018, a Friday, the City of Mati, Province of
Davao Oriental, will mark the celebration of the 18° Sambuokan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mati, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of.the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
26 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mati, Province of Davao
Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
