MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 671

DECLARING THURSDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ESCALANTE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2019, Thursday, the City of Escalante, Province
of Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Escalante
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 28 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Escalante,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of February, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
