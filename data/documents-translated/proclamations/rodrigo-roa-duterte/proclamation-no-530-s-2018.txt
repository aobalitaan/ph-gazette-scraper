MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 530

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 JULY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DAPITAN

WHEREAS, the City of Dapitan will mark the celebration of the Kinabayo
Festival on 25 July 2018, a Wednesday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dapitan,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies; :

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 July 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dapitan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof July _, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
