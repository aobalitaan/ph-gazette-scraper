MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1273

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 JANUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 January 2022, Monday, the City of Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 82"4 Founding Anniversary and 22"
Hinugyaw Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 10 January 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof January __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
