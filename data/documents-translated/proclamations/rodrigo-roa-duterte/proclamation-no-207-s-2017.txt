MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 207

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on Monday, 15 May 2017, the Municipality of Gumaca, Quezon
marks the celebration of the Araw ng Magsasaka at Arajia at Baluarte Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca, Quezon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Monday, 15 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

we
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
