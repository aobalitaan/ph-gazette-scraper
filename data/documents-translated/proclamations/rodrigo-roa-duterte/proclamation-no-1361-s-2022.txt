MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1361

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOCORRO, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 22 June 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of Socorro,
Province of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Socorro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Socorro, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th dayof may _ , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
