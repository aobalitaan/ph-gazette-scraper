MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 951

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 JUNE 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAYOYAO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, on 01 June 2020, Monday, the Municipality of Mayoyao,
Province of Ifugao, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mayoyao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 01 June 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mayoyao,
Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
AGN

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary ¢
