MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 506

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 19 June 2018, a Tuesday, the City of Laoag, Ilocos Norte, will
mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag,
llocos Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag, Ilocos
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF-, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
