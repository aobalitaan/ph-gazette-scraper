MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1296

DECLARING THURSDAY, 24 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 24 February 2022, Thursday, the Province of Zamboanga
Sibugay will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Zamboanga Sibugay; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the
national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 24 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

aN
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA _,
