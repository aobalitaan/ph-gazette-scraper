MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 597

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALLACAPAN, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 15 October 2018, a Monday, the Municipality of Allacapan,
Province of Cagayan will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Allacapan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 15 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Allacapan, Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof October | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

LS re
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
