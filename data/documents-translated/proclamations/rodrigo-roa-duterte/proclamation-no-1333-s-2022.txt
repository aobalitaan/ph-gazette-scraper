MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1333

DECLARING 18 MARCH 2022 TO 17 MARCH 2023 AS THE CENTENARY
YEAR OF THE ACCOUNTANCY PROFESSION IN THE PHILIPPINES

WHEREAS, Section 2 of Republic Act No. 9298 or the “Philippine Accountancy
Act of 2004,” recognizes the importance of accountants in nation-building, and declares
it a State policy to develop and nurture competent, virtuous, productive and well-rounded
professional accountants, whose standard of practice and service shall be qualitative,
world class and globally competitive;

WHEREAS, on 17 March 1923, the Sixth Philippine Legislature passed Act No.
3105, which legally recognized the accountancy profession, and created the Board of
Accountancy with the authority to promulgate rules and regulations, set professional
standards for the practice of the profession, and issue certification to qualified public
accountants;

WHEREAS, the accountancy profession in the Philippines has promoted and
sustained the highest standards of professionalism and ethics, and produced thousands
of Certified Public Accountants (CPAs) of invaluable integrity and service, who have
contributed immensely to the development of our nation, especially in the fields of
business, finance and trade; and

WHEREAS, the Board of Accountancy will conduct a year-long celebration to
commemorate the centenary year of the accountancy profession in the Philippines, which
will begin on 18 March 2022 and culminate on 17 March 2023;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of
the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, do hereby declare 18 March 2022 to 17 March 2023 as the “Centenary Year of
the Accountancy Profession in the Philippines.”

For this purpose, the Board of Accountancy, under the Professional Regulation
Commission, shall lead the observance of the Centenary Year of the Accountancy
Profession in the Philippines.

government-owned or -controlled corporations and state universities and colleges, are
