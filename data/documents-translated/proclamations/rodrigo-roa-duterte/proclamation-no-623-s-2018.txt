MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 623

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2018, a Friday, the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of the Higantes Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,

23 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
