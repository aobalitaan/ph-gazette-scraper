MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 697

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2019, Friday, the City of Malaybalay, Province of
Bukidnon, will mark the celebration of its 21° Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Malaybalay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
22 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Malaybalay, Province of
Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

2 be

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
