MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1270

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 05 JANUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUANG, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 05 January 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of Bauang,
Province of La Union will mark the celebration of the Bauang Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauang be given full opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 05 January 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bauang, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One
