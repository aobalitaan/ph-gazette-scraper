MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1137

DECLARING FRIDAY, 30 APRIL 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 30 April 2021, Friday, the Municipality of Calatagan, Province
of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Cultural Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calatagan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
30 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calatagan, Province
of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
