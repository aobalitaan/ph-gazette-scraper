MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 863

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 DECEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN NICOLAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 26 December 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of San
Nicolas, Province of llocos Norte, will mark the celebration of the Damili Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Nicolas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 December 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Nicolas, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this22md_ day of November _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
