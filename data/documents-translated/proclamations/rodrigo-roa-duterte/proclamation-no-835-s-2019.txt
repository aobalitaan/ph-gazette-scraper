MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 835

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2019, Friday, the Province of Oriental Mindoro
will mark the celebration of its 69" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Oriental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
15 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

cs.
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
Executive Secretary
