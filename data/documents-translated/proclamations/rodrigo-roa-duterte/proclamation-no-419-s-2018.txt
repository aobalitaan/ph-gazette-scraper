MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 419

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Taytay, Rizal, will be celebrating the Araw ng
Pasasalamat: HAMAKA Festival on 19 February 2018;

WHEREAS, the HAMAKA Festival promotes the woodworks and garments
industry of Taytay; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taytay, Rizal, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Taytay, Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
