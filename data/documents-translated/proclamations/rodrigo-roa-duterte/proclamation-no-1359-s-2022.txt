MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1559

DECLARING SATURDAY, 18 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2022, Saturday, the Municipality of Sta. Maria,
Province of Davao Occidental, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Maria be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 18 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta.
Maria, Province of Davao Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this ioth dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
