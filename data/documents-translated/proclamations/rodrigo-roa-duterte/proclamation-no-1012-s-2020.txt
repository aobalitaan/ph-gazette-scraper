MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1012

DECLARING TUESDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL

WHEREAS, on 08 September 2020, Tuesday, the Municipality of Loon,
Province of Bohol, will mark the celebration of the Sid/akasilak Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Loon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 08 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Loon, Province of Bohol.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authomty of the President:

(Bi vans C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
