MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 420

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Bayawan will celebrate the Tawo-Tawo Festival on 18
February 2018 to 19 February 2018;

WHEREAS, the Tawo-Tawo Festival reflects the culture of the City of
Bayawan being a rice granary of the southern part of the Province of Negros
Oriental; and

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Bayawan to celebrate and
participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Bayawan requested that
Monday, 19 February 2018, be declared as a special non-working day, in celebration
of the Tawo-Tawo Festival;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Te

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
