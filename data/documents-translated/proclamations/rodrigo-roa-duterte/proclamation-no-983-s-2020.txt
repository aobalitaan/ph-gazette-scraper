MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 983

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21] JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MABALIACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 21 July 2020, Tuesday, the City of Mabalacat, Province of
Pampanga, will mark the celebration of its Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabalacat
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public

health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE,|1, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 July 2020, a| special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat,

Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority/of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
