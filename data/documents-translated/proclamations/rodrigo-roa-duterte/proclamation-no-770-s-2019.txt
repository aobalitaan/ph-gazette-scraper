MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 770

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAPITAN

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2019, Thursday, the City of Dapitan will mark the
celebration of the Kinabayo Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dapitan be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dapitan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

a
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
