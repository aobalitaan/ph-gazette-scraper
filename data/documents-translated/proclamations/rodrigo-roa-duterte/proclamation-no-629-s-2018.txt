MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.629

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 28 November 2018, Wednesday, the Municipality of Pifan,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte, will celebrate its founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pihan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 28 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pifian, Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

cy
SALVADOR CG. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary &
