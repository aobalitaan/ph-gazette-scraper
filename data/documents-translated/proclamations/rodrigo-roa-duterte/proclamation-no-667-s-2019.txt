MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 667

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Bayawan, Province of Negros Oriental, will celebrate
the Tawo-Tawo Festival on the fourth week of February;

WHEREAS, the Tawo-Tawo Festival reflects the culture of the City of
Bayawan being a rice granary of the southern part of the Province of Negros
Oriental;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Bayawan to celebrate and
participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Bayawan requested that
Monday, 18 February 2019, be declared a special (non-working) day, in celebration
of the Tawo-Tawo Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayawan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 18 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
