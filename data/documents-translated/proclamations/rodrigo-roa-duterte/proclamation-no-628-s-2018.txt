MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 628

DECLARING TUESDAY, 27 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BURGOS, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 27 November 2018, Tuesday, the Municipality of Burgos,
Province of La Union, will mark the celebration of its 93" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Burgos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 27 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Burgos, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27t® — day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

ne

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
