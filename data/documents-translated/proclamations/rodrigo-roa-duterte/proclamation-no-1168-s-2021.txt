MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1168

DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOCORRO, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 22 June 2021, Tuesday, the Municipality of Socorro, Province
of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its 58" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Socorro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Tuesday, 22 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Socorro,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this g2nq_ day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
