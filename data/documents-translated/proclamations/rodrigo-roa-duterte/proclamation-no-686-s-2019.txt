MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 686

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 20 March 2019, Wednesday, the City of San Fernando,
Province of La Union will mark the celebration of its Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Fernando be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of San
Fernando, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

eX

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE,
Executive Secretary
