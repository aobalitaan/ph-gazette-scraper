MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 734

DECLARING THURSDAY, 13 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SILAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Silay, Province of Negros Occidental, will celebrate its
Charter Anniversary, which features cultural activities to be held from 03 to 13 June
2019;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Silay to celebrate the
occasion, the City Mayor requested that Thursday, 13 June 2019, be declared a
special (non-working) day, in culmination of the said event; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Silay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 13 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Silay, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.
