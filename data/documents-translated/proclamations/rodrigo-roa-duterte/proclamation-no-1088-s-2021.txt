MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 1088

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 10 February 2021, Wednesday, the City of Laoag, Province
of Ilocos Norte, will mark the celebration of the Pamulinawen Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures; : ;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 10 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag,
Province of Ilocos Norte,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
| of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4t® day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
