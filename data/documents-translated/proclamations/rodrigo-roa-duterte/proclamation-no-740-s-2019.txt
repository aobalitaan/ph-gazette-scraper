MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 740

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALILANGAN, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

*

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2019, Tuesday, the Municipality of Kalilangan,
Province of Bukidnon, will mark the celebration of its 53" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Kalilangan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Tuesday, 18 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Kalilangan, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

ty
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
