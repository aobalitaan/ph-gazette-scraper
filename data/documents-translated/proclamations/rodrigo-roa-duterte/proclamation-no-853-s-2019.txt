MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 853

DECLARING MONDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 25 November 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Maragusan,
Province of Compostela Valley, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maragusan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 25 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Maragusan, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of November jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

ee
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
