MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 115

DECLARING THURSDAY, 5 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUANG, PROVINCE
OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bauang, Province of La Union will
celebrate the Baggak Festival Bauang Foundation Anniversary on Thursday,
5 January 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Bauang, Province of La Union be given full opportunity to
participate and celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Thursday, 5 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in
the Municipality of Bauang, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

\ Done in the City of Manila, this 12thday of December, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
