MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 738

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINDOY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Bindoy, Province
of Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 70" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bindoy be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 17 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bindoy,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
