MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 472

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SULOP, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 24 April 2018, the Municipality of Sulop, Davao del
Sur, will mark the celebration of its 60" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sulop, Davao del Sur, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Tuesday,
24 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sulop, Province
of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

.
