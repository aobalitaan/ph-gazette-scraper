MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 444

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 20 March 2018, marks the celebration of the Cityhood
Anniversary of the City of San Fernando, La Union; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Fernando, La Union, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of San
Fernando, La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.
