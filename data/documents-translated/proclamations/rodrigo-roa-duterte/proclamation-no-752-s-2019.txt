MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 752

DECLARING SATURDAY, 29 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 29 June 2019, Saturday, the City of Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of the Tree Growing Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 29 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

oN
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
