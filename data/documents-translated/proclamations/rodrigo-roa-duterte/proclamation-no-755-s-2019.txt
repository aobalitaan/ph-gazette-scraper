MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 755

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONCADA, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Moncada, Province
of Tarlac, will mark the celebration of its 144'" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Moncada be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 01 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Moncada,
Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
