MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1108

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, the City of Davao observes the celebration of the Kadayawan
Festival in the month of August;

WHEREAS to enable the people of the City of Davao to celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod requested that 20 August 2021, Friday, be
declared a special (non-working) day in celebration of its Indigenous Peoples Day in
relation to the Kadayawan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare 20
August 2021, Friday, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26thday of February in the year of:Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
