MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 198

DECLARING THURSDAY, 27 APRIL 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 27 April 2017, the City of Lapu-Lapu marks the
celebration of the Kadaugan sa Mactan,

WHEREAS, the Battle of Mactan is among the significant events in Philippine
history and serves as testimony to the rich cultural and historical heritage of the
Filipinos; and

WHEREAS, it Is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lapu-Lapu
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 27 April 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lapu-Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
