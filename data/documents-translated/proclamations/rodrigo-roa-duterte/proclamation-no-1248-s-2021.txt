MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1248

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ITOGON, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 22 November 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Itogon,
Province of Benguet, will mark the celebration of its 121s Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Itogon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 22 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Itogon, Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

an
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
