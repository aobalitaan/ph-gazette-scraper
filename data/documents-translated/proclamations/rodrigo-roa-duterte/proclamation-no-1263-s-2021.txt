MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1263

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. NINO, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2021, Thursday, the Municipality of Sto. Nifio,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 418 Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sto. Nifio be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by ‘authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 December 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sto.
Nifio, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this15th day of December | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
