MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 463

DECLARING 02 FEBRUARY 2018 TO 02 FEBRUARY 2019 AS THE PERIOD OF
THE CELEBRATION OF THE “BICENTENNIAL YEAR OF ILOCOS
NORTE” i

WHEREAS, Ilocos Norte was created as a province on 02 February 1818
during the term of Governor-General Mariano Fernandez de Folgueras;

WHEREAS, the Province of Ilocos Norte marks the period of the celebration
of the “Bicentennial Year of Ilocos Norte” on 02 February 2018 to 02 February 2019;
and

WHEREAS, this commemoration aims to create more awareness on the
preservation of the rich cultural heritage, practices and traditions of Ilocos Norte;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare 02
February 2018 to 02 February 2019 as the period of the celebration of the
“Bicentennial Year of Ilocos Norte.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this3rd day of April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
