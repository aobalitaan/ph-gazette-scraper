MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 635

DECLARING THURSDAY, 13 DECEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NARVACAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
SUR

WHEREAS, on 13 December 2018, Thursday, the Municipality of Narvacan,
Province of Ilocos Sur, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Narvacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 13 December 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Narvacan, Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authcrity of the President:
