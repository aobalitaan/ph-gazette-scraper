MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 152

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ESCALANTE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS

OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 28 February 2017, marks the celebration of the
Charter Day of the City of Escalante, Negros Occidental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Escalante,
Negros Occidental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Tuesday, 28 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Escalante, Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

Wary

MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary _
