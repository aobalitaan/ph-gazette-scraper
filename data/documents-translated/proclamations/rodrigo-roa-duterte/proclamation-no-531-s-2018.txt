MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 531

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 JULY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2018, a Wednesday, the City of Tanjay, Negros
Oriental, will mark the celebration of the Sinulog sa Tanjay Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanjay, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 July 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanjay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of July, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

C &e
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
