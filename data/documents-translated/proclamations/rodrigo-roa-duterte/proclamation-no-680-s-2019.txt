DECLARING THURSDAY, 07 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2019, Thursday, the Province of Basilan will mark
the celebration of its 45"° Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Basilan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 07 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
