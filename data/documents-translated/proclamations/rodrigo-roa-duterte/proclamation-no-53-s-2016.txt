MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 53

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 24 AUGUST 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CATBALOGAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 24 August 2016, marks the celebration of the City of Catbalogan’s quadricentennial anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Catbalogan, Samar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE , do hereby declare Wednesday, 24 August 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Catbalogan, Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of August , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
