MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 548

DECLARING TUESDAY, 14 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL

NORTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sto. Tomas, Davao del Norte will mark the
celebration of the Araw ng Sto. Tomas on 14 August 2018, a Tuesday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sto. Tomas, Davao del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 14 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sto.
Tomas, Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

4
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
