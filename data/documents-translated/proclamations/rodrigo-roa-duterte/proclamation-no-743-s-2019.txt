MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 743

DECLARING THURSDAY, 20 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAGUPAN, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 20 June 2019, Thursday, the City of Dagupan, Province of
Pangasinan, will mark the celebration of its 72™ Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dagupan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 20 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan,
Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

FOR
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
