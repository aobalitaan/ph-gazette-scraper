MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

“BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1314

DECLARING TUESDAY, 08 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 08 March 2022, Tuesday, the Province of Davao de Oro will
mark the celebration of its 24" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Davao de Oro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 08 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Davao de
Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
4

as
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,

Executive Secretary
Office of the President
