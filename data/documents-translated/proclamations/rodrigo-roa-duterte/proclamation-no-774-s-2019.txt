MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 774

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, the City of Davao observes the weeklong celebration of the
Kadayawan Festival in the month of August;

WHEREAS to enable the people of the City of Davao to celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod requested that Friday, 16 August 2019 be
declared a special (non-working) day in celebration of the Indigenous People’s Day,
in relation to the Kadayawan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
16 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th dayof July _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

A ‘
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary «
