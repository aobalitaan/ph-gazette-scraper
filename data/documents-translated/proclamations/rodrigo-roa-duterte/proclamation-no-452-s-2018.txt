MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 452

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Municipality of San Jose, Batangas, marks the celebration of
its 253° Foundation Anniversary on Thursday, 26 April 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies,

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Jose, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.
