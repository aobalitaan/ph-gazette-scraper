MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 398

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on Friday, 02 February 2018, the Province of Ilocos Sur marks
the celebration of the Kannawidan Ylocos Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ilocos
Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
02 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16thday of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

.

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
