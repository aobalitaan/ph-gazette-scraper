MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 737

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2019, Monday, the City of Lapu-Lapu will mark
the celebration of its 58" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lapu-
Lapu be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Monday, 17 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lapu-
Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisl11th day of | June _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
