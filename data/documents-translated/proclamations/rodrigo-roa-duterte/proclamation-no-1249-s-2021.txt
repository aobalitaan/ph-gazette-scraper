MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

-BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1249

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2021, Tuesday, the Province of Benguet will
mark the celebration of its 121st Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Benguet be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 23 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Benguet. — °

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th, day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
