MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 665

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 13 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PARANAQUE

WHEREAS, on 13 February 2019, Wednesday, the City of Parafiaque will
mark the celebration of its Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Parafiaque
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 13 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Parafiaque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of February, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary

Office of the President
