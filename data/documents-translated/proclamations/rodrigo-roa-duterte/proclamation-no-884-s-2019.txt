MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 884

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALIBO, PROVINCE OF AKLAN

WHEREAS, the Province of Aklan celebrates the Ati-Atihan Festival every
month of January to depict and commemorate the way of life during the period when
the ten (10) Bornean Datus landed on and purchased the Island of Panay to settle
thereon for a life of peace and freedom;

WHEREAS, the Ati-Atihan Festival highlights the rich cultural heritage of the
Filipino and the customs and traditions of Aklan as a province;

WHEREAS, the Ati-Atihan Festival has significantly contributed to the
promotion of our tourism industry; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kalibo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
17 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kalibo, Province
of Aklan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

dace
SALVADOR C. MEDIAL DE’

Executive Secretary
