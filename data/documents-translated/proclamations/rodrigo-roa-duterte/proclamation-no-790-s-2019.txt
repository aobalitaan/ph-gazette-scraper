MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 790

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 14 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAWAB, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA
VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 14 August 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of Mawab,
Province of Compostela Valley, will mark the celebration of its 60" Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mawab be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 14 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mawab, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.
