MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 478

DECLARING TUESDAY, 15 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 15 May 2018, the Province of Surigao del Sur
marks the commemoration of the Founding Anniversary of “One Surigao”; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Surigao del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 15 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Surigao
del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof May , In the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
