MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 324

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 OCTOBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the Province of Negros Oriental marks the celebration of the
“Buglasan Festival’ in the month of October of every year; and

WHEREAS, to allow the people of the Province of Negros Oriental to
celebrate and participate in the event, the Sangguniang Panlalawigan has requested
that 20 October 2017 be declared as a special non-working day in the Province of
Negros Oriental;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
20 October 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
