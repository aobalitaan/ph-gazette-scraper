MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 613

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2018, a Thursday, the Province of Occidental
Mindoro will mark the celebration of its 68" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Occidental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Occidental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of November | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
