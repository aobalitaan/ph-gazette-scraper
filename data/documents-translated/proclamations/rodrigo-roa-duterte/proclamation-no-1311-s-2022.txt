MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1311

DECLARING MONDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANZA, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2022, Monday, the Municipality of Tanza,
Province of Cavite, will mark the celebration of its 108" Araw ng Tanza; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanza be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 28 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tanza,
Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24tiday of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
