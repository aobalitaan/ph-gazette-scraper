MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 302

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOON, PROVINCE OF BOHOL

WHEREAS, on Friday, 08 September 2017, the Municipality of Loon, Bohol
marks the celebration of the Sidlakasilak Festival: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Loon, Bohol, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
08 September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Loon,
Province of Bohol

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this Sth day of Septembexn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
