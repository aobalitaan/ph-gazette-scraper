MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 328

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 OCTOBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS Tuesday, 24 October 2017, marks the celebration of the
Cimarrones Festival in the Municipality of Pili, Camarines Sur; and

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pili, Camarines Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies:

NOW, THEREFCRE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the resident. RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 Oclober 2017. as a Special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pili,
Camarines Sur

IN WITNESS WHEREOF. | have hereunto set my hand and Caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen

By authority of the President:

OR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
