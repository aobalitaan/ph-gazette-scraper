MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 1084

DECLARING TUESDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN CITY OF MABALACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2021, Tuesday, the City of Mabalacat, Province
of Pampanga, will mark the celebration of its 309'* Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabalacat
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures; . 5

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 02 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat,
Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

for §,
