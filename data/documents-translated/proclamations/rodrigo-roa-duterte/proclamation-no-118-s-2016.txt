MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 118

DECLARING THURSDAY, 12 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENCIA, PROVINCE OF
BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Thursday, 12 January 2017, marks the Foundation Day of
the City of Valencia, Bukidnon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Valencia, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Thursday, 12 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in
the City of Valencia, Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF. | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of December in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

A” savabon Cc. MEDIALDEA
