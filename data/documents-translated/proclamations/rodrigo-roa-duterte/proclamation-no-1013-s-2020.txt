MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1013

DECLARING TUESDAY, 15 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 15 September 2020, Tuesday, the Province of Bulacan will
mark the celebration of the 122°¢ anniversary of the inauguration of the Malolos
Congress; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 15 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day ofseptemberin the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty.
