MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 274

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF NAGA

WHEREAS. Jesse M. Robredo, who was appointed as the Secretary of the
Department of the Interior and Local Government and a champion of good
governance, was instrumental in the implementation of far-reaching reforms in local
governance,

WHEREAS, Secretary Robredo served with utmost dedication as Mayor of
Naga City from 1988 to 1998 and from 2001 to 2010;

WHEREAS, his outstanding performance as Mayor of Naga City earned him
numerous awards such as the Magsaysay Award for Government Service in 2000,
the first Filipino Mayor to be conferred such distinction:

WHEREAS, Secretary Robredo, considered as one of the local heroes of
Naga City, passed away in a tragic plane accident on 18 August 2012: and

WHEREAS, his many virtues as a public servant have permanently earned
him the gratitude of the City he led, and the Republic he served.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Naga, in
commemoration of the death anniversary of Secretary Jesse M. Robredo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
