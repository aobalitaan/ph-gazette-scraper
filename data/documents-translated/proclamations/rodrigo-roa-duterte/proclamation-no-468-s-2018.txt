MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 468

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MALABON

WHEREAS, Monday, 21 May 2018, marks the 419" Founding Anniversary of
the City of Malabon; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Malabon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Malabon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th dayof April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

ew

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE.
