MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 481

DECLARING TUESDAY, 08 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGONDON, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 08 May 2018, the Municipality of Maragondon,
Cavite, will mark the commemoration of the founding date of the Liberators Guerrilla;

WHEREAS, on 08 May 1942, Patrocinio Z. Gulapa established an
organization which later became known as the Liberators Guerrilla in Maragondon,
Cavite, on 08 May 1942;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Maragondon, Cavite,
to celebrate the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan of Maragondon requested that
Tuesday, 08 May 2018, be declared as a special working holiday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maragondon, Cavite, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Tuesday,
08 May 2018, as a special working holiday in the Municipality of Maragondon,
Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
