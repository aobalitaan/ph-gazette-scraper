MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 574

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIQUIJOR, PROVINCE OF
SIQUIJOR

WHEREAS, on 12 September 2018, a Wednesday, the Municipality of
Siquijor, Province of Siquijor, will mark the celebration of its 46" anniversary as the
capital of the Province of Siquijor; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Siquijor be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 12 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Siquijor, Province of Siquijor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of September in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen. )
