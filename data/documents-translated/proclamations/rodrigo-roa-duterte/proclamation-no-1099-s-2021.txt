MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.1099

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 24 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 24 February 2021, Wednesday, the Province of Zamboanga
Sibugay will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Zamboanga Sibugay; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social
distancing, and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 24 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16thday of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

us
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
