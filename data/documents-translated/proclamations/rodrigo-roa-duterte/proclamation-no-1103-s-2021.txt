MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.: 1103

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2021, Friday, the City of Zamboanga will mark
the celebration of its 84" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Zamboanga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
26 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this23rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
