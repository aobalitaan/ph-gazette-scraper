MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 841

DECLARING TUESDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2019, Tuesday, the .Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon, will mark its 437" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 12 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of November, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority/of the President:

(ri vavos C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
