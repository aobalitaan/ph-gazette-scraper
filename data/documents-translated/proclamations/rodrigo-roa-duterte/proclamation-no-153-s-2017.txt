MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 153

DECLARING THURSDAY, 2 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, Thursday, 2 March 2017, marks the celebration of the 167"
Founding Anniversary of the Province of La Union;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of La
Union, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO I. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Thursday, 2 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

wn) API

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary
