MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 996

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 19 AUGUST 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 19 August 2020, Wednesday, the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal, will mark the signing of its independence as a municipality from
the municipality of Binangonan, Rizal; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 19 August 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Angono, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this14thday ofAugust _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

SG
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
