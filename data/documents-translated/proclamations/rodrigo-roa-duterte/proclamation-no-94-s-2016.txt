MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 94

DECLARING SATURDAY, 29 OCTOBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 486 dated 13 October 2003, declaring
October 29 of every year as “National Indigenous Peoples Thanksgiving Day”,

WHEREAS, the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of Alabel, Sarangani
Province requested that 27-October 2016 be declared as a special non-
working day as Indigenous Peoples’ Day;

WHEREAS, the National Historical Commission of the Philippines
recommended that 29 October 2016, instead of 27 October 2016, be declared
as a special non-working day in the Province of Sarangani in celebration of
the Indigenous Peoples’ Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sarangani be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Saturday, 29 October 2016, as a special (non-working) day in
the Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of October, in the year of
_ Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

Loe
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
