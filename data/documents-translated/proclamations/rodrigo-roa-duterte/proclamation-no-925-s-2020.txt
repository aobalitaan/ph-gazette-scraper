MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 925

DECLARING THURSDAY, 19 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL

MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2020, Thursday, the Municipality of Bongabong,
Province of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of the “Su/yog Festival”, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bongabong be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 19 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of March in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

“ef
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDES

Executive Secretary
