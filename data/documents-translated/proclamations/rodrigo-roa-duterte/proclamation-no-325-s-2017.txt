MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.325

DECLARING FRIDAY. 27 OCTOBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MATI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL

WHEREAS the City of Mati celebrates the Sambuokan Festival every last
weekend of Octobe: in commemoration of its date of establishment as a municipality
before eventually becoming a city

WHEREAS the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Mati, Davao Oriental requested
that Friday. 27 October 2017, be declared as a special non-working day in the City of
Mati, in celebration of the 17" Sambuokan Festival: and

WHEREAS it Is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mati, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
27 October 2017 as a special (non-working) day in the City of Mati.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
