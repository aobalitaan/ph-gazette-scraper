MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 954

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 JUNE 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 11 June 2020, Thursday, the Province of Rizal will mark the
celebration of its 119"" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Rizal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 11 June 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

ee
SALWADOR C. MEPIALDES

Executive Secretary
