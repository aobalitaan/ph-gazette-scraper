MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 499

DECLARING SATURDAY, 16 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2018, a Saturday, the Municipality of Molave,
Zamboanga del Sur, will mark its 70" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Molave, Zamboanga del Sur, be given. full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Saturday,
46 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga
del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority/of she President:

rn

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
