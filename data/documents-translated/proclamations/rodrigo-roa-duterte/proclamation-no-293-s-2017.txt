MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 293

DECLARING MONDAY, 04 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Monkayo, Compostela Valley marks the
celebration of its 63 Foundation Day and the 22”” Kariyawan Festival on Monday,
04 September 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Monkayo, Compostela Valley, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
these occasions with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 04 September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of August, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
