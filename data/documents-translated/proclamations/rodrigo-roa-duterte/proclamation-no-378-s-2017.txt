MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.378

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Bayawan marks the celebration of its 17" Charter Day
on 23 December 2017:

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Bayawan to celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Bayawan requested that Friday, 22
December 2017, be declared as a special non-working day instead of 23 December
2017, which falls on a Saturday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayawan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies; st

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
22 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan, Negros
Onental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
