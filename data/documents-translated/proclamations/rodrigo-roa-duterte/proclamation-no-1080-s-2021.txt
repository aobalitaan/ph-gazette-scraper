MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1080

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2021, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2021, Wednesday, the City of Bifian, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 76" Liberation Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bifian be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 03 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bifan,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
