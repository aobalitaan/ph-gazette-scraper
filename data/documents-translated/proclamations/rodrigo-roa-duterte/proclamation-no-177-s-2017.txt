MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 177

DECLARING TUESDAY, 4 APRIL 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO

WHEREAS, the City of Antipolo marks the celebration of its 19" cityhood
anniversary on Tuesday, 4 April 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Antipolo,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Tuesday, 4 April 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Antipolo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th — day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
