MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1344

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BOGO, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2022, Thursday, the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu,
will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bogo be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 16 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bogo, Province of
Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
