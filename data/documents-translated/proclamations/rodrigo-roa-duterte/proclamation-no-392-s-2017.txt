MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 392

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN BARANGAY BATASAN, SAN JOSE, OCCIDENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections will conduct a plebiscite on Friday,
05 January 2018, to ratify the creation of Barangay Naibuan consisting of fifteen (15)
sitios, to be taken out of Barangay Batasan, San Jose, Occidental Mindoro; and

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of Barangay Batasan, San Jose,
Occidental Mindoro the opportunity to actively and fully participate in the plebiscite
and exercise their right to vote, it is but fitting and proper to declare 05 January 2018
as a special non-working day; \

NOW, THEREFORE, !, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Friday, 05 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in Barangay
Batasan, San Jose, Occidental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of Decemberin the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

aevi

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary
