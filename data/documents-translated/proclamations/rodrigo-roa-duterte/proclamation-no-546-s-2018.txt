MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 546

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, the City of Tacurong will mark its 67" Founding Anniversary on
03 August 2018, a Friday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
03 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st dayof July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
