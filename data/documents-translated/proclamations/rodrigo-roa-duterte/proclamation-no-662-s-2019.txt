MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 662

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 10 February 2019, Sunday, the City of Laoag, Province of
llocos Norte, will mark the celebration of the Pamulinawen Festival;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Laoag, to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 11 February 2019, Monday (instead
of 10 February 2019 which falls on a Sunday), be declared a special (non-working)
day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 11 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag,
Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this Geb day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

- Le
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDES

Executive Secretary
