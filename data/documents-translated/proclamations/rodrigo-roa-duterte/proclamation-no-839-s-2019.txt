MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 839

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 13 November 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley, will mark the celebration of its 8274
Founding Anniversary and the 17" PASAKA Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pantukan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 13 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

~

La

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
