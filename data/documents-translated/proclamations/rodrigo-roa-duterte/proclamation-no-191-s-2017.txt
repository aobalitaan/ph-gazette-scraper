MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 191

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT ROXAS, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 4869, approved on 08 May 1967, created the
Municipality of President Roxas, Cotabato;

WHEREAS, the Municipality of President Roxas, Cotabato marks its 50"
Foundation Anniversary on Monday, 08 May 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
President Roxas, Cotabato, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 08 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
President Roxas, Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

—— ee
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
