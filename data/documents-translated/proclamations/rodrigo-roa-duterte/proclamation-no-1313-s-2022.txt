MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1313

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, on 01 March 2022, Tuesday, the City of Davao will mark the
celebration of its Araw ng Dabaw; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

ts

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary /
