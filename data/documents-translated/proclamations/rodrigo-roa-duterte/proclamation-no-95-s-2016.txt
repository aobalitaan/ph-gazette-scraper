MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 95

DECLARING SATURDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 12 November 2016, the City of Valenzuela
marks its 393 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Valenzuela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Saturday, 12 November 2016, as a special (non-working) day
in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
