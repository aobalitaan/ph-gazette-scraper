MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 257

DECLARING MONDAY, 14 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sto. Tomas, Davao del Norte marks the
celebration of the 58” Araw ng Sto. Tomas on Monday, 14 August 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sto. Tomas, Davao del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 14 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sto.
Tomas, Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
