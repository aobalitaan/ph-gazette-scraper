MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1243

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2021, Monday, the Province of Oriental Mindoro
will mark the celebration of its 71st Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Oriental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing,
and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 15 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Oriental
Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3r4 dayof November | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
