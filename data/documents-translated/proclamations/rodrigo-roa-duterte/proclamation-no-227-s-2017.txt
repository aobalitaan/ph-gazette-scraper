MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 227

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYUGAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Bayugan, Agusan del Sur, marks the celebration of
its 10" Charter Day on Wednesday, 21 June 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayugan,
Agusan del Sur, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayugan,

Agusan del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof June , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

AO’
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
