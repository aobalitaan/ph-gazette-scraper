MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1304

DECLARING SATURDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2022, Saturday, the City of Zamboanga will mark
the celebration of its 85'" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Zamboanga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 26 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
