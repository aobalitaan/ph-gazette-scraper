MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.477

DECLARING THURSDAY, 03 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 03 May 2018, the Municipality of Sta. Cruz,
Marinduque, marks the celebration of its Founding Anniversary, to be known as
Santa Cruz Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Cruz, Marinduque, be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 03 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta.
Cruz, Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
