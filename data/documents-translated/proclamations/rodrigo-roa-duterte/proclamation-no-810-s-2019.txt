MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 810

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 16 September 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province, will mark the celebration of its 111" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bontoc be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 16 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bontoc, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1Othday of Septembexn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
