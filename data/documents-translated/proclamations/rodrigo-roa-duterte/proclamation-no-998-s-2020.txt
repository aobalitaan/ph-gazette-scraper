MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 998

DECLARING THURSDAY, 27 AUGUST 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MINALIN, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 27 August 2020, Thursday, the Municipality of Minalin,
Province of Pampanga, will mark the celebration of its 406" Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Minalin be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 27 August 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Minalin,
Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, 17th dayof Augustin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
