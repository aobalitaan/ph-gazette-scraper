MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 85

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE
OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Friday, 16 December 2016, marks the Founding Anniversary
of the Municipality of Calatagan, Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calatagan, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Friday, 16 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the
Municipality of Calatagan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
