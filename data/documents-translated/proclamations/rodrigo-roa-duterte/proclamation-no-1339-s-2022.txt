MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1339

DECLARING TUESDAY, 03 MAY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 03 May 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Santa Cruz,
Province of Marinduque, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary to be
known as Santa Cruz Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Santa Cruz be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 03 May 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Santa
Cruz, Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5t® dayof April | inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
