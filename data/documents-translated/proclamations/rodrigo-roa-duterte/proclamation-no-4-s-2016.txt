MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 4

DECLARING SATURDAY, 2 JULY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, the City of Pasig marks the celebration of its 443 rd Anniversary on Saturday, 2 July 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive secretary, by authority of His Excellency, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE , do hereby declare Saturday, 2 July 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
