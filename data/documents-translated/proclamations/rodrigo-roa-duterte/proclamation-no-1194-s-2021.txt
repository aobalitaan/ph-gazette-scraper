MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1194

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILAR, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON

WHEREAS, on 06 August 2021, Friday, the Municipality of Pilar, Province of
Sorsogon, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pilar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
06 August 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pilar, Province of
Sorsogon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thiszoriday of Juiy _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
