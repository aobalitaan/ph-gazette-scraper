MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1370

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BAYUGAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2022, Tuesday, the City of Bayugan, Province of
Agusan del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 15” Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayugan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayugan,
Province of Agusan del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed,

Done in the City of Manila, this25th dayof ay, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
