MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 587

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 05 October 2018, a Friday, the Municipality of Sta. Cruz,
Province of Davao del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 134" Araw ng Sta. Cruz;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Cruz be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
05 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz,
Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. ,

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of September | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA ,,

Executive Secretary “
