MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 247

DECLARING SATURDAY, 15 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

WHEREAS, Saturday, 15 July 2017, marks the 30" Foundation Day of the
Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR); and
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the CAR be given full

opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 15 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the CAR.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

day of July, in the year of Our Lord,

By authority of the President:

Ce
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
