MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 888

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 January 2020, Monday, the Barako Festival will be held in
the City of Lipa, Province of Batangas, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be

given full opportunity to participate in the programs and activities lined up during the
said festival;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 20 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa, Province
of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
