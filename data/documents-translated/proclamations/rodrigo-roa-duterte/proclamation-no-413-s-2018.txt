MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 413

DECLARING 01 JULY 2018 TO 30 JUNE 2019 AS THE “YEAR FOR THE
CELEBRATION OF THE CENTENNIAL ANNIVERSARY OF THE ROTARY
CLUB OF MANILA AND THE ROTARY MOVEMENT IN THE PHILIPPINES
AND IN ASIA”

WHEREAS, the Rotary Club of Manila, the first Rotary Club in Asia, was
established on 01 June 1919 and started the entire Rotary Movement in the country
and in Asia;

WHEREAS, the Rotary Club of Manila, and the Rotary Movement in the
Philippines and in Asia, are celebrating their centennial anniversary on 01 June
2019; and

WHEREAS, the government recognizes the Rotary Club of Manila and the
entire Rotary Movement in the Philippines and in Asia for all their contributions to
uplift the lives of the people in the various communities where the Rotary Clubs are
located;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the
period of 01 July 2018 to 30 June 2019 as the “Year for the Celebration of the
Centennial Anniversary of the Rotary Club of Manila and the Rotary Movement in the
Philippines and in Asia.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January , In the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
