MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1155

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2021, Wednesday, the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet, will mark the celebration of its 71st Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of La
Trinidad be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of La
Trinidad, Province of Benguet

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One. ,

By authority of the President:

&

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
