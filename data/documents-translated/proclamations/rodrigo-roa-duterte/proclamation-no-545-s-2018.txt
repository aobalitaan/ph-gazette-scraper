MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 545

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Gumaca, Province of Quezon, will mark the
celebration of Lorenzo Tafiada, Sr. Day on 10 August 2018, a Friday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca, Quezon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
10 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

J
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
