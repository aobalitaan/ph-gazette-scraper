MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 820

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 OCTOBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF INFANTA, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 04 October 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Infanta, Province
of Pangasinan, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Infanta be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
04 October 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Infanta, Province
of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
