MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1130

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 14 APRIL 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 14 April 2021, Wednesday, the City of Cebu will mark the
celebration of the 500" year of the arrival of Christianity in the Philippines; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cebu be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 14 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

‘Oe
SALVADOR C. MEDIALD
Executive Secretary
