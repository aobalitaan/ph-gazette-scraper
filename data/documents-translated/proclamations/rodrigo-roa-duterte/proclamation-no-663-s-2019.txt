MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 663

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 11 February 2019, Monday, the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of the Tinagba Festival, an ancient
Bicolano First-Harvest Offering; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 11 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga, Province
of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this6th dayof February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

he
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary

Office of the President
