MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1291

DECLARING THURSDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KABASALAN, PROVINCE OF
ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 17 February 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Kabasalan,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kabasalan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Thursday,
17 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kabasalan,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3;q day of Februasyn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
