MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 111

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON- :

WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 20 December 2016, the City of Sipalay marks
the celebration of the Handurawan Festival and Pasaway Festival which
showcases the glorious past of Sipalay as a copper producing locality of the
country;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Tuesday, 20 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City
of Sipalay.

%
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
