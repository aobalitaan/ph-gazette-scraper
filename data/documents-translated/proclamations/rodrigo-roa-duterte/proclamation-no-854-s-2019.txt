MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 854

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BURGOS, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 27 November 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of Burgos,
Province of La Union, will mark the celebration of its 94'" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Burgos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Burgos, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of November, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

cannes £4
