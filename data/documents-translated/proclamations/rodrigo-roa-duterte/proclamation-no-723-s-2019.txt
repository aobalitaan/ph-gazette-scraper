MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 723

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 22 May 2019, Wednesday, the City of Gingoog, Province of
Misamis Oriental will mark the celebration of the Lubi-Lubi Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th — day of May, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.
