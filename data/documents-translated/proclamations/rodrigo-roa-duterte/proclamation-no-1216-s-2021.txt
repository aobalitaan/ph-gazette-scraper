MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1216

DECLARING SATURDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2021, Saturday, the City of.Tacurong, Province
of Sultan Kudarat, will mark the celebration of its 21st Cityhood Anniversary and the
Talakudong Festival 2021; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 18 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done jn the City of Manila, this 10th day of September, jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
