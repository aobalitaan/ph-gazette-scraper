BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1029

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN UPPER
KLINAN, MUNICIPALITY OF POLOMOLOK, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO, AS AN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT
TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916,
otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA
No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine
Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of
RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, PEZA Board of
Directors Resolution No. 07-232 (s. 2007) and Resolution No. 09-091 (s. 2009), as
amended by Resolution No. 19-385 (s. 2019), a parcel of land located in Upper
Klinan, Municipality of Polomolok, South Cotabato, as an agro-industrial economic
zone, to be known as the SRC Upper Klinan Economic Development Zone, with
an area of One Hundred Seventeen Thousand Seven Hundred Sixty Two square
meters (117,762 m2), more or less, as defined by the following technical description:

Lot 2676, Rel-12-000083-AR
