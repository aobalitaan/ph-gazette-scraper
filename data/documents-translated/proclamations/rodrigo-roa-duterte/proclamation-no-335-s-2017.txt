MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 335

DECLARING SATURDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, the Province of Quezon marks the death anniversary of its local
hero Apolinario dela Cruz, also known as Hermano Puli, on Saturday, 04 November
2017: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Quezon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 04 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this30th day of October , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
