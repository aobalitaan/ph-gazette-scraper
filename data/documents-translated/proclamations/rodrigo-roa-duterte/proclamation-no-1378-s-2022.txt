MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1378

DECLARING FRIDAY, 24 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2022, Friday, the City of Tabaco, Province of Albay,
will mark the celebration of the Tabak Festival, a showcase of the city’s rich history,
cultural diversity, and beautiful tourist attractions;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
24 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco, Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30tn dayof ay , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
