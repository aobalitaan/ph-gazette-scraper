MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 184

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 APRIL 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Municipality of San Jose, Batangas, marks the celebration of
its 252" Foundation Anniversary on Wednesday, 26 April 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 26 April 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Jose, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of March | jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
