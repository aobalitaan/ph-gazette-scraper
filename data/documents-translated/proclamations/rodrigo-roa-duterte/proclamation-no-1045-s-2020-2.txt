MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1045

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 NOVEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ITOGON, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Itogon, Province of Benguet, will mark the
celebration of its 120" Founding Anniversary on 22 November 2020;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Itogon to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Friday, 20 November 2020, be
declared a special (non-working) day in lieu of 22 November 2020, which falls on a
Sunday, in the Municipality of Itogon; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Itogon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
20 November 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Itogon,
Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day ofNovember __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
