MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 119

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the City of Batangas will hold a Tourism and Cultural Day
together with the celebration of its city fiesta on Monday, 16 January 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Monday, 16 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in
the City of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City 9f Manila, this 14th day of December , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
