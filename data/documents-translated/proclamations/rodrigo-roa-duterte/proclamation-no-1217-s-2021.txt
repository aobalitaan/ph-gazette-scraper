MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1217

DECLARING SATURDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
"DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANSALAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
SUR -

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2021, Saturday, the Municipality of Bansalan,
Province of Davao del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 69" Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bansalan be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures; .

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 18 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bansalan, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

day of September

Done in the City of Manila, this , in the year of Our Lord,
