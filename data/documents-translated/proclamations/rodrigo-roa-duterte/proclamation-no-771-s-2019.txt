MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 771

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 26 July 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Ipil, Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its 70" Araw ng Ipil; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Ipil
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
26 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ipil, Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

“wa
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary “
