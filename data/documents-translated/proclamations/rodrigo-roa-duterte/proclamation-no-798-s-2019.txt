MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 798

DECLARING MONDAY, 09 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur will mark the
celebration of its 19" Araw ng Digos on 08 September 2019;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Digos to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, 09 September 2019, be
declared a special (non-working) day in lieu of 08 September 2019, which falls on a
Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 09 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos,
Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of August , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

ew
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA “
