MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1368

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MUNICIPALITY OF PANGANTUCAN, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Pangantucan,
Province of Bukidnon, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pangantucan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
01 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pangantucan,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24tilay of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two
