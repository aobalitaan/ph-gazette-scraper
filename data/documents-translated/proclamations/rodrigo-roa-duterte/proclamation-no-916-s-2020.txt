MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 916

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2020, Monday, the City of Davao will mark the
celebration of its Parada Dabawenyo in relation to the 2020 Araw ng Dabaw; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 16 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof March _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

Ge
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
