MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 595

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 26 OF EVERY YEAR AS “WORLD
ENVIRONMENTAL HEALTH DAY” IN THE PHILIPPINES

WHEREAS, the Constitution declares that the State shall protect and advance
the right of the Filipino people to a balanced and healthful ecology and shall protect
and promote their right to health and instill health consciousness among them;

WHEREAS, a study by the World Health Organization (WHO) revealed that
about one-fourth, or an estimated 12.6 million, of all global deaths have occurred due
to environmental risks such as air, water and soil pollution, chemical exposures,
climate change and ultraviolet radiation;

WHEREAS, according to the WHO, environmental health addresses all the
physical, chemical, and biological factors external to a person, and all the related
factors impacting human behavior;

WHEREAS, environmental health encompasses the assessment and control
of the said environmental factors that can potentially affect health and is targeted
towards preventing diseases and creating health-supportive environments;

WHEREAS, September 26 has been declared as "World Environmental
Health Day" by the International Federation of Environmental Health, to be
celebrated each year to promote awareness of pressing environmental health
issues, encourage cooperation between countries on areas where environmental
health risks and problems cross borders and transcend boundaries, and stimulate
action among stakeholders; and

WHEREAS, the celebration of World Environmental Health Day in the
Philippines is supportive of the objectives of the Philippine Development Plan 2017
to 2022, as it seeks to raise public consciousness on the relationship of health and
the environment, and promote activities and programs which ensure accountability
on health and environmental integrity;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, do hereby declare September 26 of every year as “World Environmental

Health Day” in the Philippines.

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

The Inter-Agency Committee on Environmental Health (IACEH), through its
lead agencies, the Department of Health and the Department of Environment and
Natural Resources, shall promote the observance of the World Environmental Health
Day in the Philippines, as well as determine the theme and identify the programs,
projects, and activities for the yearly celebration thereof.

All other agencies and instrumentalities of the National Government, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and all local
government units, as well as the private sector, including non-government
organizations, the academe, and environmental health professionals, are hereby
encouraged to cooperate with the [ACEH in advocating and implementing programs
and activities pursuant to this Proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this lst day of October , in the year of our
Lord, Two Thousand Eighteen.
