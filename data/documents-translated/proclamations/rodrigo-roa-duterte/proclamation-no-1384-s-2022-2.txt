MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1334

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF BINDOY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Bindoy, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 73™ Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bindoy be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
17 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bindoy, Province of
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
