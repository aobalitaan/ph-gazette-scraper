BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1393

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 155 (S. 2011), WHICH RESERVED CERTAIN
PARCELS OF LAND UNDERLYING THE NAGA THERMAL POWER PLANT
AND GRANTED OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE NATIONAL
POWER CORPORATION, BY AUTHORIZING THE SECRETARY OF THE
DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES TO
ISSUE SPECIAL PATENTS OVER THE PARCELS OF LAND COVERED
THEREIN AND CORRECTING THE TECHNICAL DESCRIPTIONS OF
CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND STATED THEREIN

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment
and Natural Resources (DENR) and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, |,
RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby
amend Proclamation No. 155 (s. 2011), which reserved certain parcels of land
underlying the Naga Thermal Power Plant and granted ownership thereof in favor of
the National Power Corporation (NPC), situated in Barangay Colon, City of Naga,
Province of Cebu, Island of Cebu, by authorizing the DENR Secretary to issue and
sign, on my behalf, the corresponding Special Patents over said parcels of land in
favor of the NPC, and correcting the technical descriptions of certain parcels of land
covered by the said Proclamation, more particularly described as follows:
