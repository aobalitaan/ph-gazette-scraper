MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1044

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 NOVEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF DASMARINAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 26 November 2020, Thursday, the City of Dasmarifias,
Province of Cavite, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Dasmarifias be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 November 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Dasmarifias, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
