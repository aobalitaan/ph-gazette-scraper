MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1195

DECLARING THURSDAY, 05 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANINI-Y, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE

WHEREAS, on 05 August 2021, Thursday, the Municipality of Anini-y,
Province of Antique, will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Anini-y, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Anini-y be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 05 August 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Anini-y,
Province of Antique.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

1%”
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
