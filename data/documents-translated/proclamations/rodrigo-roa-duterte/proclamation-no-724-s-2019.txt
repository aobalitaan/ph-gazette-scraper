MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 724

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALACA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 23 May 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Calaca, Province
of Batangas, will mark the birth anniversary of General Eleuterio Marasigan, a great
Calaquefio, patriot, and one of the leaders during the libertarian movement in the
Province of Batangas; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calaca,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14thday of May _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

Bug,
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
