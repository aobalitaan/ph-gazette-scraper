MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 742

DECLARING THURSDAY, 20 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

*

WHEREAS, on 20 June 2019, Thursday the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 72" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 20 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

‘ A
SALVADOR C. MEDIALD

Executive Secretary
