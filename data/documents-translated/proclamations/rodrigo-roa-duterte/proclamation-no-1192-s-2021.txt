MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1192

DECLARING TUESDAY, 03 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 03 August 2021, Tuesday, the City of Tacurong, Province of
Sultan Kudarat, will mark the celebration of its 70 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 03 August 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisggch dayof july. in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
