MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 137

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 8 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN, PROVINCE OF
COTABATO : .

WHEREAS, the City of Kidapawan marks the celebration of its 19"
Foundation Anniversary on 12 February 2017; *

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Kidapawan celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Wednesday, 8 February 2017, be
declared as a special (non-working) day instead of 12 February 2017, which falls on
a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Kidapawan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Wednesday, 8 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Kidapawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. -

Done in the City of Manila, this17thday of January in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
