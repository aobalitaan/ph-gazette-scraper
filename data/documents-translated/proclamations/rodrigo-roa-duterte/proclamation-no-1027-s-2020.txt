MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1027

CALLING THE CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO A SPECIAL
SESSION

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Section 15, Article VI of the
Constitution, | RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Philippines, do hereby
call the Congress of the Philippines to a special session on 13 October 2020 to 16
October 2020 in order to resume the congressional deliberations on the proposed
2021 national budget and to avoid any further delays on the prompt passage thereof
in accordance with the Constitution and applicable laws, rules, and regulations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of October in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
