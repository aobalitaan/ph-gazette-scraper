MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.82

DECLARING FRIDAY, 2 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASAY

WHEREAS, the City of Pasay marks the celebration of its 153%
Founding Anniversary on Friday, 2 December 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Pasay be given full opportunity to participate in and celebrate the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 2 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day in
the City of Pasay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF. | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
