MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1190

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 JULY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 26 July 2021, Monday, the Municipality of ipil, Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its 72"° Araw ng Ipil; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Ipil
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 26 July 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ipil,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
