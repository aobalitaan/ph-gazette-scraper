MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1281

DECLARING THURSDAY, 20 JANUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 January 2022, Thursday, the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas, marks the celebration of the Coffee Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 20 January 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa, Province
of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th dayof January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two

By authority of the President:

ee
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
