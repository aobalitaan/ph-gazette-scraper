MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 428

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 21 March 2018, marks the celebration of the 20"
Founding Anniversary of the City of Calapan, Oriental Mindoro; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calapan,
Oriental Mindoro, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Calapan,
Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of February in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
