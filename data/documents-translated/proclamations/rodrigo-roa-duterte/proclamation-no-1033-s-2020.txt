MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1033

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2020, Wednesday, the Municipality of Tanay,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of its 414" Founding Anniversary and the
10" Hane Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other
public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 11 November 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tanay, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23, q4day Of gctober ; in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
