MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1324

DECLARING THURSDAY, 17 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYUG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 17 March 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Tayug, Province
of Pangasinan, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tayug be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 17 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tayug,
Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th — day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

“as
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
