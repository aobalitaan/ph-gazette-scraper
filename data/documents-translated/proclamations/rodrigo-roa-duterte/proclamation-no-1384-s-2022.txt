MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1385

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, the City of Laoag, Province of Ilocos Norte, will mark the
celebration of its Charter Day on 19 June 2022;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Laoag to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 20 June 2022, Monday, be declared
a special (non-working) day in lieu of 19 June 2022, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 20 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag, Province of
Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3,q day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
