MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 400

DECLARING SATURDAY, 20 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LIPA

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 20 January 2018, the City of Lipa, Batangas marks
the celebration of the Kapeng Barako Festival 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 20 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Sanit
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE,

Executive Secretary
