MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 393

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, the City of Tarlac marks the celebration of the 2" Kaisa Festival
on Friday, 19 January 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
19 January 2018 as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
