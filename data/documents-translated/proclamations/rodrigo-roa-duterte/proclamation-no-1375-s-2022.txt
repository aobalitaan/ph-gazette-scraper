MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1375

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet, will mark the celebration of its 72"¢ Foundation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of La
Trinidad be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of La
Trinidad, Province of Benguet

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26thday of may , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

a
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEAy

Executive Secretary
