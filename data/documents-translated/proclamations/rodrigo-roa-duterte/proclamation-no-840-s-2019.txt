MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.840

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Tanay,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of its 413" Founding Anniversary and the
9" Hane Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 11 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tanay, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this gth day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authoygty of the President:

ALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
Executive Secretary
