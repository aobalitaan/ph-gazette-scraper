BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. ©45

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN
SITUATED IN BARANGAY POBLACION, CITY OF PASAY, AS SITE FOR
EDUCATIONAL, CULTURAL, AND SPORTS PROGRAMS AND
FACILITIES OF PASAY CITY, AND TRANSFERRING OWNERSHIP
THEREOF TO THE CITY GOVERNMENT OF PASAY

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment
and Natural Resources (DENR) and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I,
RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby
reserve a certain parcel of land of the private domain in Barangay Poblacion, City of
Pasay, Island of Luzon, as site for educational, cultural, and sports programs and
facilities of Pasay City, and transfer ownership thereof to the City Government of
Pasay, subject to existing rights, if any.

This parcel of land is particularly described as follows:
