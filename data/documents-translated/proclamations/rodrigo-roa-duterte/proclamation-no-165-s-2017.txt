PROCLAMATION NO.165

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING, WHICH STANDS ON TWO PARCELS
OF LAND, LOCATED AT TOPAZ AND RUBY ROADS, ORTIGAS CENTER,
PASIG CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO
REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise
known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748
and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Philippines, do hereby create
and designate a building with a gross floor area of 46,222.29 square meters, subject to the
provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and
Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (S. 2000) and Resolution No. 15-377 (S. 2015) of
the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, as an Information
Technology Center to be known as Cyberscape Gamma which stands on two parcels of
land located at Topaz and Ruby Roads, Ortigas Center, Pasig City containing an
aggregate area of One Thousand Nine Hundred Fifty Four square meters and Fifty
square decimeters (1,954.50), more or less, as defined by the following technical
