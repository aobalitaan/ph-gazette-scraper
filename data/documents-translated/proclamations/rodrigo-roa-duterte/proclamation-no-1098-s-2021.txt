MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1098

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BAGO, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 February 2021, Friday, the City of Bago, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 55" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bago be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
19 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bago, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

ee
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
