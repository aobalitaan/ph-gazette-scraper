MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 552

DECLARING THURSDAY, 30 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISULAN, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Isulan, Province of Sultan Kudarat, will mark
its 61% Founding Anniversary and the 7” Hamungaya Festival on 30 August 2018, a
Thursday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Isulan, Sultan Kudarat, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 30 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Isulan,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st qay of July in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Oh
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
