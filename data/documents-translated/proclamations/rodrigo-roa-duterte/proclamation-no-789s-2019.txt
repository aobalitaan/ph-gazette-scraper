MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 789

DECLARING MONDAY, 12 AUGUST 2019, A REGULAR HOLIDAY
THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY, IN OBSERVANCE OF EID’L ADHA
(FEAST OF SACRIFICE)

WHEREAS, Eid’ Adha or Feast of Sacrifice is one of the two greatest feasts
of Islam;

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 9849 provides that Eid’! Adha shall be observed
as a regular holiday; and

WHEREAS, the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos has recommended
that 12 August 2019, Monday, be declared a national holiday, in observance of Eid'/
Adha;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday,
12 August 2019, a regular holiday throughout the country, in observance of Ejid’/
Adha.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
