MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 18

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, the Municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet marks the celebration of
its Strawberry Festival every 18" day of March;
WHEREAS. to enable the people of the Municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet,

to fully celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, 20
March 2017 be declared as a special non-working day instead of 18 March 2017

which falls on a Saturday;
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of La
Trinidad, Benguet, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.
NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive

Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Monday, 20 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality

of La Trinidad, Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Seventeen.
