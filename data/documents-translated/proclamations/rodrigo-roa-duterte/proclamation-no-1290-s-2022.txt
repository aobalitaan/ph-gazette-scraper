MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1290

DECLARING THURSDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADRE BURGOS, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 17 February 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Padre Burgos,
Province of Quezon, will mark the celebration of Padre Burgos Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Padre Burgos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Thursday,
17 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Padre Burgos,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day offebruary in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

ine bh
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
