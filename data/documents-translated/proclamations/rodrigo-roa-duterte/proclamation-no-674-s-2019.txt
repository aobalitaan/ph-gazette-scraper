PROCLAMATION NO. 674

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCEL OF LAND,
UPON WHICH IT STANDS, LOCATED AT QUIRINO HIGHWAY CORNER
REGALADO AVENUE, BARANGAY PASONG PUTIK, NOVALICHES,
QUEZON CITY AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION

AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or
the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon
the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority (PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the
Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building to be known as SM City
Fairview — BPO Towers, with a gross floor area of One Hundred Nine Thousand
Two Hundred Ninety One and 62/100 square meters (109,291.62 m?), more or less,
and the parcel of land, upon which the building stands, located at Quirino Highway
corner Regalado Avenue, Barangay Pasong Putik, Novaliches, Quezon City, with an
area of Eighteen Thousand Six Hundred Fifty Nine square meters (18,659 m2), more
or less, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing
Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution
Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011), 15-240 (s. 2015) and 17-472 (s. 2017), and Resolution No.
17-674 (s. 2017), as amended by Resolution No. 18-613 (s. 2018), of the PEZA
Board of Directors, as a Special Economic Zone (Information Technology Center), as
defined by the following technical description:
