MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 222

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Monday, 19 June 2017, marks the 156" birth anniversary of Dr.
Jose P. Rizal, our national hero; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies in keeping with his ideals and teachings.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of June __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
