MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 956

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 JUNE 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2020, Tuesday, the Municipality of Molave, Province
of Zamboanga del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 72"? Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Molave be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 16 June 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Molave,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22d dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

C
SALVADOR C. MERIALDE

Executive Secretary
