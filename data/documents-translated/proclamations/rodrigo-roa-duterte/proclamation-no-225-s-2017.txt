MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 225

DECLARING THURSDAY, 29 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, Thursday, 29 June 2017, marks the celebration of the Tree
Growing Festival in the City of Koronadal; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Koronadal, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 29 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thislst dayof June __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

.

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
