MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 336

DECLARING MONDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF

COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on Monday, 13 November 2017, the Municipality of Pantukan,
Compostela Valley marks the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pantukan, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 13 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of

Pantukan, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By autherity of the President:
