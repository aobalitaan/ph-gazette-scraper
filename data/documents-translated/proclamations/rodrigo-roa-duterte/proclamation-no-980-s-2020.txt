MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 980

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 23 July 2020, Thursday, the City of Gingoog, Province of
Misamis Oriental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures,

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 July 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog, Province
of Misamis Oriental

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of July, in the year of Our Lord
Two Thousand and Twenty

By authority of the President
