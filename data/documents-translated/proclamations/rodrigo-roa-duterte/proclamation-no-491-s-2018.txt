MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 491

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALACA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 23 May 2018, will mark the birth anniversary:.of
General Eleuterio Marasigan, a great Calaquefio, patriot, and one of the leaders
during the libertarian struggle in the Province of Batangas; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calaca, Province of Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate
in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 23 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Calaca, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen. .

By authority of the President:

eZ

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
