MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHLIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 145

DECLARING SATURDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 11 February 2017, the City of Iriga, Camarines Sur,
marks the celebration of the Tinagba Festival, an ancient Bicolano First Harvest

Offering;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 11 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga,
Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
