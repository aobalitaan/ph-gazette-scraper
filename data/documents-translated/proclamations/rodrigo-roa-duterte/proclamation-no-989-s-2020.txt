MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 989

DECLARING TUESDAY, 04 AUGUST 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 04 August 2020, Tuesday, the City of Cabuyao, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 8!" Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabuyao
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA. Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 04 August 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabuyao,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

pF LY

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
