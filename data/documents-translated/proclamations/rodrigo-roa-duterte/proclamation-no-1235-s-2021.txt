MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1235

DECLARING MONDAY, 06 DECEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECHJA

WHEREAS, the City of Palayan, Province of Nueva Ecija, will mark the
celebration of its 56'" Founding Anniversary on 05 December 2021;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Palayan to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 06 December 2021, Monday, be
declared a special (non-working) day in lieu of 05 December 2021, which falls on a

Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 06 December 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan,
Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26thlay of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
