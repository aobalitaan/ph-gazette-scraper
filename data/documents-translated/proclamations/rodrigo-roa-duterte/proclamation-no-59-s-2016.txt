MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 59

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 5 OCTOBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON- WORKING)  DAY    IN    THE    MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREA S, on Wednesday, 5 October 2016, the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Davao del Sur marks the celebration of its 132nd Araw ng Sta. Cruz;

WHEREAS , it is but   fitting   and   proper   that   the   people of the Municipality of Sta.Cruz Davao del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies ..

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Wednesday, 5 October 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this   7th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
