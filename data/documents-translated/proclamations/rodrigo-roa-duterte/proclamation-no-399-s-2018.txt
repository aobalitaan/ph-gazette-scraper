MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.399

DECLARING SATURDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 03 February 2018, the Province of Bulacan marks
the birth anniversary of Blas F. Ople;

WHEREAS, Blas F. Ople, who served as Secretary of Foreign Affairs, Senate
President and Secretary of Labor, devoted decades of his life to selfless and
dedicated service to our country and our people;

WHEREAS, Blas F. Ople, a son of Bulacan, is a model citizen and public
officer to the Filipino people, especially to the “Bulakenyos”. and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 03 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

vu
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
