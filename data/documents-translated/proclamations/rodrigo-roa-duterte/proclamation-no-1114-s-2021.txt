MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1114

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSELLER T. LIM, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2021, Tuesday, the Municipality of Roseller T. Lim,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its 39th Founding
Anniversary; and

5

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Roseller T. Lim be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing,
and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 16 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Roseller
T. Lim, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

EL
SALVADOR C. Seeiy

Executive Secretary
