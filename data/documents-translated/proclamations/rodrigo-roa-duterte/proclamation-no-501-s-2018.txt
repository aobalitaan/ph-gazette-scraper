MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 591

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAGAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 11 June 2018, a Monday, the City of Sagay, Negros
Occidental, will mark its 22" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sagay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Monday,
11 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sagay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority gf the President:

MICHAEL P. ONG
