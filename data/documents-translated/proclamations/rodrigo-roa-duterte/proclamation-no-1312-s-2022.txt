MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1312

DECLARING MONDAY, 07 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2022, Monday, the City of Tagum, Province of
Davao del Norte, will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Tagum; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagum be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 07 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tagum, Province
of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of February , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
