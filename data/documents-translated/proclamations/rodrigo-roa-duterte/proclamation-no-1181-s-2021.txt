MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
_ PROCLAMATION NO. 1181

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 14 JULY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF GUIHULNGAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 14 July 2021, Wednesday, the City of Guihulngan, Province
of Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Guihulngan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, 1, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 14 July 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihulngan,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

——, by
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
