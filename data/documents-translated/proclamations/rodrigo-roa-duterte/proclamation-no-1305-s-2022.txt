MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1305

DECLARING SATURDAY, 06 AUGUST 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILAR, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON

WHEREAS, on 06 August 2022, Saturday, the Municipality of Pilar, Province
of Sorsogon, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pilar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 06 August 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pilar,
Province of Sorsogon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14ta day of fFesruary , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
