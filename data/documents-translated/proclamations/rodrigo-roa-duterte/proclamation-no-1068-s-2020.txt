MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1068

DECLARING TUESDAY, 05 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUANG, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 05 January 2021, Tuesday, the Municipality of Bauang,
Province of La Union will mark the celebration of the Bauang Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauang be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures; __

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 05 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bauang, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
