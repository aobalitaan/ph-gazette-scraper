MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1210

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-
* WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOON, PROVINCE OF

BOHOL -

_ WHEREAS, on 08 September 2021, Wednesday, the Municipality of Loon,
Province of Bohol, will mark the celebration of the Sidlakasilak Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Loon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 08 September 2021, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality
of Loon, Province of Bohol.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One. .

By authority of the President:

~~

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary ¢
