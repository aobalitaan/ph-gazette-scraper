MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 391

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN BARANGAYS NBBS, TANGOS AND TANZA, CITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections will conduct three (3) plebiscites on
Friday, 05 January 2018, to ratify the creation of certain barangays in the City of
Navotas; and

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of Barangays NBBS, Tangos and
Tanza, City of Navotas the opportunity to actively and fully participate in the
plebiscite and exercise their right to vote, it is but fitting and proper to declare 05
January 2018 as a special non-working day;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Friday, 05 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in Barangays
NBBS, Tangos and Tanza, City of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th gay of Decemberin the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

|Gaecee

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary
