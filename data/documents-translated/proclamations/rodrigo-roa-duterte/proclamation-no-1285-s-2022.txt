MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1285

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN CITY OF MABALACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 02 February 2022, Wednesday, the City of Mabalacat,
Province of Pampanga, will mark the celebration of its 310" Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabalacat
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 02 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat,
Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th dayof January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two

By authority of the President:

re
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
