MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1272

DECLARING THURSDAY, 06 JANUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF OROQUIETA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 06 January 2022, Thursday, the City of Oroquieta, Province
of Misamis Occidental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary and its
Anniversary as the capital of the Province of Misamis Occidental; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Oroquieta
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 06 January 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Oroquieta,
Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One

By authority of the President:

hodecaes
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
