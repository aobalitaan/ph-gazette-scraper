MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 758

DECLARING TUESDAY, 02 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, on 02 July 2019, Tuesday, the City of Pasig will mark the
celebration of its 446" Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 02 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

“Oa
