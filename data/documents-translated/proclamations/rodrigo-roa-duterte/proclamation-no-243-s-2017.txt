MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 243

DECLARING SATURDAY, 24 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, Saturday, 24 June 2017, the Province of Masbate marks the
celebration of the Kasadyaan sa Dagat Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 24 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
