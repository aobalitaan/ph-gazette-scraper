MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 644

DECLARING TUESDAY, 15 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 15 January 2019, Tuesday, the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of the 24" Coconut Festival and Street Dancing
Competition (Mardi Gras); and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 15 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

7th January

Done in the City of Manila, this day of , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
