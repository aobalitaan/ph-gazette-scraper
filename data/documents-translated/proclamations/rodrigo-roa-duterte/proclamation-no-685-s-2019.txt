MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 685

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro, will
mark the celebration of the “Sulyog Festival” on 17 to 19 March 2019;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Bongabong to
celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Tuesday, 19 March
2019, be declared a special non-working day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bongabong be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bongabong, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
