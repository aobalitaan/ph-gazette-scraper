MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 999

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 AUGUST 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 25 August 2020, Tuesday, the City of lloilo will mark the
celebration of its 83 Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of lloilo be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 August 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
