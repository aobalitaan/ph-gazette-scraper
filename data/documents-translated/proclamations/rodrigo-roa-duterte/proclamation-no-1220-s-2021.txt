MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1220

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
. DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGRAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
NORTE —

WHEREAS, Josefa Llanes Escoda, a World War II heroine and the founder of
the Girl Scouts of the Philippines, was born on 20 September 1898 in Dingras, !locos
Norte;

WHEREAS, on 20 September 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Dingras,
Province of Ilocos Norte, will mark the celebration of the birth anniversary of Josefa
Llanes Escoda; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dingras be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 20 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Dingras, Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal __
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
