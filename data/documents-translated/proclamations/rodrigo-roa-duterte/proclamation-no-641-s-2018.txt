MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 641

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 04 January 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Naujan, Province
of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its 114"" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naujan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
04 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Naujan, Province
of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
