MALACANAN PAI ACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 252

DECLARING SATURDAY, 22 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 22 July 2017, the City of General Santos marks the
celebration of its Indigenous Peoples 3" Blaan Day; and

WHEREAS. it ts but fitttng and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 22 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of General
Santos

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
