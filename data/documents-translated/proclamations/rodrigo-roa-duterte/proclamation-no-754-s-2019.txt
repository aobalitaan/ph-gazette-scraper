MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 754

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2019, Monday, the City of Tabaco, Province of Albay,
will mark the celebration of the Tabak Festival, a showcase of the city’s rich history,
cultural diversity, and beautiful tourist attractions; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 24 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco, Province
of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
