MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 379

DECLARING THURSDAY, 04 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Naujan, Oriental Mindoro marks the
celebration of its 113" Founding Anniversary on Thursday, 04 January’2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naujan, Oriental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 04 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Naujan, Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
