MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1209

DECLARING SATURDAY, 11 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL {(NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUPI, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 11 September 2021, Saturday, the Municipality of Tupi,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 68% Founding
Anniversary and the 12" Agten Tufi Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tupi be given full opportunity to celebrate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 11 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tupi, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of September, jin the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
