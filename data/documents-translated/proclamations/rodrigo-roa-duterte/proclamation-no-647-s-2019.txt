MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 647

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 16 January 2019, Wednesday, the City of Batangas will mark
its Tourism and Cultural Day together with the celebration of its city fiesta; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batangas
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
