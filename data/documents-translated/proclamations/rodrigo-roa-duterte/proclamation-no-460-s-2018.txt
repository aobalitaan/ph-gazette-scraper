MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 460

DECLARING MONDAY, 02 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Tanjay marks its 37" Charter Anniversary on 01 April
2018;

—..WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tanjay celebrate the occasion,

the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Tanjay requested that Monday, 02 April 2018,
instead of 01 April 2018 which falls on a Sunday, be declared as a special non-
working day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanjay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 02 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanjay, Negros
Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of March _ in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
