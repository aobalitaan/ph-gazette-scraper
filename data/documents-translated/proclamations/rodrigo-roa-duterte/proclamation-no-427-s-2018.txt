MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 427

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYUG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, Saturday, 17 March 2018, marks the Founding Anniversary of
the Municipality of Tayug, Pangasinan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tayug. Pangasinan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the

occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 17 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of

Tayug, Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
