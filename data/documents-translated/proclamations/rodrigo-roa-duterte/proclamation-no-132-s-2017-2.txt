MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 132

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CADIZ

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 26 January 2017, the City of Cadiz marks
the celebration of its 43° Dinagsa Festival,

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cadiz be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Thursday, 26 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in
the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
