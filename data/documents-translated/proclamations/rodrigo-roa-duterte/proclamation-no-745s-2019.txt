MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 745

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF BALETE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

*

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Balete, Province of
Batangas, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Balete be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Balete, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th dayof June __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

on
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
