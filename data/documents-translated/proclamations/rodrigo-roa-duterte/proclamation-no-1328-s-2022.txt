MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1328

DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2022, Tuesday, the Province of Cavite will mark the
celebration of the birth anniversary of General Emilio Aguinaldo, the first President of

the Republic of the Philippines; and
\

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Cavite be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national

government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 22 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this gth day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
