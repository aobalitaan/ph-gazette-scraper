MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

[BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
-PROCLAMATION NO. 1250

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2021, Tuesday, the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of the Higantes Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 23 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Angono, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
