MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1371

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MONCADA, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2022, Friday, the Municipality of Moncada, Province
of Tarlac, will mark the celebration of its 147" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Moncada be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,

01 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Moncada, Province
of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th dayof May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEAy
