MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 917

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF NORALA, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 March 2020, Tuesday, the Municipality of Norala, Province
of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Norala be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 10 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Norala,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of March _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
