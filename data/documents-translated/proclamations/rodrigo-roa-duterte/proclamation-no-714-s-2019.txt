MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 714

DECLARING TUESDAY, 07 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 07 May 2019, Tuesday, the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 79" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 07 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of May , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
