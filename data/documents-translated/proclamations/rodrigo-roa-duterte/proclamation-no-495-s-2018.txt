MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 495

DECLARING SATURDAY, 16 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BOGO, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2018, a Saturday, the City of Bogo, Province of
Cebu, will mark its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bogo,
Province of Cebu, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Saturday,
16 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF-, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of May __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen. 7

By authority e President:
