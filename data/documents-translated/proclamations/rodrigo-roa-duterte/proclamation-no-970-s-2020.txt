MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 970

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 24 JUNE 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2020, Wednesday, the City of Tabaco, Province of
Albay, will mark the celebration of the Tabak Festival, a showcase of the city’s rich
history, cultural diversity, and beautiful tourist attractions; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary, I.
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare :
Wednesday, 24 June 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco,
Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd _—_day of June,.in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
