MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 467

DECLARING TUESDAY, 08 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT ROXAS, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 4869, approved on 08 May 1967, created the
Municipality of President Roxas, Cotabato;

WHEREAS, the Municipality of President Roxas, Cotabato, marks its 51°
Founding Anniversary on Tuesday, 08 May 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
President Roxas, Cotabato, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 08 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
President Roxas, Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of April ___, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
