MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 508

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the City of Lipa, Batangas, marks the celebration of its 715
Founding Anniversary on 20 June 2018, a Wednesday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa,
Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of May __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Hoaninaitinh dO EK,
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary &
