BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 382

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCELS OF LAND
UPON WHICH IT STANDS, LOCATED ALONG AGUINALDO HIGHWAY
CORNER NUENO AVENUE, TANZANG LUMA 1, IMUS, CAVITE, AS
SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER),
PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC
ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916,
otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA
No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine
Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building to be known
as the Lumina Point Information Technology Center, with a gross floor area of
Eighteen Thousand Four Hundred Ninety-Five and 60/100 square meters (18,495.60
m*), more or less, and the parcels of land upon which the building stands, located
along Aguinaldo Highway corner Nueno Avenue, Tanzang Luma 1, Imus, Cavite,
with an aggregate area of Three Thousand One Hundred Thirty-One square meters
(3,131 m*), more or less, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its
Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended
by Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011), 15-240 (s. 2015), 17-472 (s. 2017), and
Resolution No. 16-136 (s. 2016), as amended, of the PEZA Board of Directors, as
Special Economic Zone (Information Technology Center), as defined by the following
technical descriptions:
