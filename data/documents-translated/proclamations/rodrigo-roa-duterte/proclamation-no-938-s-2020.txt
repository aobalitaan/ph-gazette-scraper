MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 938

DECLARING THURSDAY, 02 APRIL 2020, AS SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN
CELEBRATION OF FRANCISCO BALAGTAS DAY

WHEREAS, 02 April 2020, Thursday, will mark the commemoration of the
birth anniversary of Francisco “Balagtas” Baltazar, a prominent Filipino poet and
orator; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper to render homage to the great Filipino
poet;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the power vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday,
02 April 2020, a special working day in celebration of Francisco Balagtas Day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of april, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
