MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 327

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MUNTINLUPA

WHEREAS fuesday. 19 December 2017, marks the celebration of the
Centennial Year of Muntiniupa as an independent local government unit; and

WHEREAS. it I's but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Muntiniupa, be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Muntiniupa

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th — day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen
