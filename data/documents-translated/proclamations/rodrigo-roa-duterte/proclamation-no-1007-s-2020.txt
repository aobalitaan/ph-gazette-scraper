MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1007

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 09 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BAIS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS

ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 09 September 2020, Wednesday, the City of Bais, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 524 Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bais be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 09 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bais,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of Septembein the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority me the President:
