MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 784

DECLARING SATURDAY, 10 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 10 August 2019, Saturday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon, will mark the celebration of Lorenzo Tahada Sr. Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 10 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality. of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof August _, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

ty

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
