MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 583

DECLARING SATURDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN RAFAEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 29 September 2018, a Saturday, the Municipality of San
Rafael, Province of Bulacan, will mark its 268" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Rafael be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 29 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Rafael, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day ofSeptember , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

5
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
