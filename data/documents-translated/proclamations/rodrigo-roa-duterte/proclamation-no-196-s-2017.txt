MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 196

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 03 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF
MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 03 May 2017, the Municipality of Sta. Cruz,
Marinduque marks the celebration of its Foundation Day, to be known as Santa Cruz
Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Cruz, Marinduque, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 03 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta.
Cruz, Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

E pe:
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary .
