MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1048

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE
ORO

WHEREAS, on 25 November 2020, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Maragusan, Province of Davao de Oro, will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maragusan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 November 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Maragusan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
