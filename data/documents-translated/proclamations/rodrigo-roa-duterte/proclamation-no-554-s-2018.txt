MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 554

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LUCENA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on Monday, 20 August 2018, the City of Lucena, Province of
Quezon, will mark the celebration of its Charter Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lucena be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 20 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lucena,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th — day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
