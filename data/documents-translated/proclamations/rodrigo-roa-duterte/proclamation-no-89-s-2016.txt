MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 89

DECLARING THURSDAY, 1 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADRE GARCIA,
BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Thursday, 1 December 2016, marks the 67" Founding
Anniversary of the Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate
and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Thursday, 1 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day
in the Municipality of Padre Garcia, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of Octoberin the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
