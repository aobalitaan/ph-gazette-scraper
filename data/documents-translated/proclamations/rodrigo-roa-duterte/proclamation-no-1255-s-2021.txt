MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1255

DECLARING TUESDAY, 07 DECEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIRAC, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, on 07 December 2021, Tuesday, the Municipality of Virac,
Province of Catanduanes, will mark the celebration of the Kaaldawan nin Virac; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Virac be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 07 December 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Virac, Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
