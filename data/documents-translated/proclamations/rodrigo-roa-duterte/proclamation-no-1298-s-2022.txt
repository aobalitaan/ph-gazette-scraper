MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1298

DECLARING FRIDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 11 February 2022, Friday, the City of Talisay, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 24" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Talisay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
11 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Talisay, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
