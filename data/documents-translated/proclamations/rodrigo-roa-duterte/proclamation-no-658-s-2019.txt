MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 658

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 06 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONSOLACION, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 06 February 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Consolacion, Province of Cebu, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Consolacion be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 06 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Consolacion, Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
