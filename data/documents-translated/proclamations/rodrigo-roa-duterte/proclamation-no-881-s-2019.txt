MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 881

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 23 January 2020, Thursday, the Province of Bulacan will
mark the anniversary of the Inauguration of the First Philippine Republic, which took
place at the Barasoain Church, Malolos, Bulacan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

o uw
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
