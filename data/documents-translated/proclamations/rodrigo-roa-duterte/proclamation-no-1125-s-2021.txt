MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1125

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 31 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF HIMAMAYLAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 31 March 2021, Wednesday, the City of Himamaylan,
Province of Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary
and the Himaya Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Himamaylan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing,
and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 31 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Himamaylan,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

04
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE

Executive Secretary
