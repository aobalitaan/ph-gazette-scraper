MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 273

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS on Friday. 18 August 2017, the City of Davao marks the highlight
of the celebration of the Kadayawan Festival, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate

ceremonies
NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 August 2017. as a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen
