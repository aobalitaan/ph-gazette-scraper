MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 538

DECLARING SATURDAY, 25 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on 25 August 2018, a Saturday, the City of Iloilo will mark its g1°
Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of lloilo be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 25 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd dayof July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

oO
SALWADOR C. CEE

Executive Secretary
