MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 455

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 430 DATED 19 FEBRUARY 2018 BY
DECLARING MONDAY, 26 MARCH 2018, INSTEAD OF 29 MARCH 2018,
AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF
MAMBUSAO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 430 dated 19 February 2018 declared
Thursday, 29 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mambusao, Capiz:

WHEREAS, the Office of the Mayor of the Municipality of Mambusao, Capiz,
requested that Monday, 26 March 2018, instead of 29 March 2018 which falls on a
Maundy Thursday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mambusao, Capiz, in celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mambusao, Capiz, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 26 March 2018, instead of 29 March 2018 which falls on a Maundy
Thursday, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mambusao, Capiz.

Proclamation No. 430 dated 19 February 2018 is hereby amended
accordingly.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
