MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 791

DECLARING TUESDAY, 20 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LUCENA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 20 August 2019, Tuesday, the City of Lucena, Province of
Quezon, will mark the celebration of its Charter Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lucena be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies; ‘

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 20 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lucena,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

Em
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDED

Executive Secretary
