MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 974

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DIPOLOG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2020, Wednesday, the City of Dipolog, Province of
Zamboanga del Norte, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dipolog be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 01 July 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dipolog,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
