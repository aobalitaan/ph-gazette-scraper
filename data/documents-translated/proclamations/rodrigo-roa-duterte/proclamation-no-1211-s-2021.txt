MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1211

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
‘IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLOMOLOK, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 September 2021, Friday, the Municipality of Polomolok,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark its 64° Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Polomolok be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
10 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Polomolok,
Province of South,Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:”
