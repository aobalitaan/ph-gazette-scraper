MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 308

De WEDNESDAY, 27 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, BATANGAS

WHEREAS. the Municipality of Malvar, Batangas marks the 152™ birth
anniversary of General Miguel Maivar on Wednesday, 27 September 2017; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Malvar, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Wednesday, 27 September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality
of Malvar, Batangas

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
