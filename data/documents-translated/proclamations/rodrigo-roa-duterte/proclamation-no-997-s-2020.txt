MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 997

DECLARING THURSDAY, 20 AUGUST 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LUCENA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 20 August 2020, Thursday, the City of Lucena, Province of
Quezon, will mark the celebration of its Charter Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lucena be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 20 August 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lucena,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of August in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

ey
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
