MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1365

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAUREL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Laurel, Province
of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 53'4 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Laurel be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Laurel,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13tndayof may, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
By authority of the President:

be
