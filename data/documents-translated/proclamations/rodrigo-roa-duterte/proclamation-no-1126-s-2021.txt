MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1126

DECLARING MONDAY, 05 APRIL 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 05 April 2021, Monday, the Province of Pangasinan will mark
the celebration of its 4418t Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Pangasinan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 05 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this. 30tiday of March __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
