MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.86

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINDANGAN,
PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sindangan was organized into a
municipality on 23 December 1936 by virtue of Executive Order No. 77;

WHEREAS, Friday, 23 December 2016, marks the Foundation
Anniversary of the Municipality of Sindangan, Zamboanga del Norte;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Sindangan, Zamboanga del Norte be given full opportunity to
celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 23 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day in
the Municipality of Sindangan, Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this13th day of October , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

Ame

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
M™ Executive Secretary.
