MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1118

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 22 March 2021, Monday, the City of Malaybalay, Province of
Bukidnon, will mark the celebration of its 23" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Malaybalay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 22 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Malaybalay,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this17th day of March __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
