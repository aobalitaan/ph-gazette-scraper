MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1081

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN

1
WHEREAS, on 03 February 2021, Wednesday, the City of Cabanatuan will
mark the celebration of its 718 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cabanatuan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing,
and other public health measures; : &

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 03 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One..

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
