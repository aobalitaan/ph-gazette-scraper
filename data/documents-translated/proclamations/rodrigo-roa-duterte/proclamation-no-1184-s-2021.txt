MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
,, PROCLAMATION NO, 1184

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 JULY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALABANGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2021, Thursday, the Municipality of Calabanga,
Province of Camarines Sur, will mark the’ Celebration of its Founding Aneereery
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calabanga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 July 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Calabanga, Province of Camarines Sur. n

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One. “
