MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 815

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 26 September 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Naval,
Province of Biliran, will mark its 159" Pueblo Day (Charter Day); and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naval be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Naval, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19ttday ofSeptemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority/of the President:

bee

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
