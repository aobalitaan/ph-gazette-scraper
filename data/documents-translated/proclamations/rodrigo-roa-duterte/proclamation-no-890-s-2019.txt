MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 890

DECLARING SUNDAY, 01 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 01 March 2020, Sunday, the City of Davao, Province of
Davao del Sur, will mark the celebration of its Araw ng Dabaw; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Sunday, 01 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao, Province
of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
