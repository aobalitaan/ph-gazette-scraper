PROCLAMATION NO. 636

2

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 DECEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 18 December 2018, Tuesday, the City of Tuguegarao,
Province of Cagayan, will mark the celebration of its 19th charter anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 18 December 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao,
Province of Cagayan. .

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEAW
