MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 405

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE

WHEREAS, Thursday, 25 January 2018, marks the 447" Founding
Anniversary of the Municipality of Carigara, Leyte; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carigara, Leyte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 25 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Carigara, Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
