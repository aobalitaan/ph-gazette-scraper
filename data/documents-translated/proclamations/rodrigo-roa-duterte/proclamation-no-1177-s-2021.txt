MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1177

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 JULY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, on 02 July 2021, Friday, the City of Pasig will mark the celebration
of its 448" Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday, 02
July 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thiszgth day of june , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
