MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1020

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MATEO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 21 September 2020, Monday, the Municipality of San Mateo,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of its 449" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Mateo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Mateo, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16thday of Septeaherin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

~
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
