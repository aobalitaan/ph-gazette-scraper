MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 823

DECLARING MONDAY, 14 OCTOBER 2019 AND FRIDAY, 25 OCTOBER 2019,
AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAYS IN THE CITY OF ANGELES,
PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 14 October 2019 (Monday) and 25 October 2019 (Friday), the
City of Angeles, Province of Pampanga, will mark the opening day of the Kuliat
Festival (Fiestang Kuliat) and the Tigtigan Terakan Keng Dalan, a festival which
symbolizes the resilience of the people during the Mt. Pinatubo eruption,
respectively; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Angeles
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare 14
October 2019 and 25 October 2019 as special (non-working) days in the City of
Angeles, Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of October _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
