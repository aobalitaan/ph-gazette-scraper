MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 874

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS, on 16 January 2020, Thursday, the City of Navotas will mark the
celebration of its 114" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Navotas
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof January _ jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
