MALACANAN PALACE
MLANELA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 342

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS. the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecya will celebrate its Annual
Thanksgiving Day for a bountiful harvest on Wecnesday, 22 November 2017; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate

ceremonies,

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of

Palayan, Nueva Ecya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this6th day of November. in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
