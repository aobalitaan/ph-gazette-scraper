MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 188

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 19 APRIL 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 19 April 2017, marks the 49'" Charter Anniversary of
the City of Tarlac;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate

ceremonies.
NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 19 April 2017, asa special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of April . inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

“A
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
