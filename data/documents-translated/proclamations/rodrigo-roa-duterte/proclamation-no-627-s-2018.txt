MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 627

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 22 November 2018, Thursday, the Province of Sultan
Kudarat will mark the celebration of its 45" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sultan Kudarat, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these
occasions with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Sultan
Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
