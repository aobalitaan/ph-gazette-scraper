MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 249

DECLARING SATURDAY, 15 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 15 July 2017, the Municipality of Mauban, Province
of Quezon marks the celebration of the Araw ng Mauban; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mauban, Quezon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Saturday, 15 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mauban, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7thday of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

It

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary A
