MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 800

DECLARING TUESDAY, 03 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 03 September 2019, Tuesday, the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of its 518' Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of lriga be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 03 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority/of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary 4
