‘ MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 134

DECLARING FRIDAY, 3 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on Friday, 3 February 2017, the City of Bifan, Laguna
marks the celebration of its 72" Liberation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Bifian, Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 3 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the
City of Bifian, Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
