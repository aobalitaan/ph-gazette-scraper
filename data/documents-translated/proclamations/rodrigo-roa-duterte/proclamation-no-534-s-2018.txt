MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. °34

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 JULY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, 25 July 2018, a Wednesday, will mark the celebration of the
100" founding anniversary of the Municipality of Maasin, Iloilo; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maasin, lloilo, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies:

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 July 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maasin,
lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
