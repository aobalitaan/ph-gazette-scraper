MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 84

DECLARING FRIDAY, 9 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAAL, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on Friday, 9 December 2016, the Municipality of Taal,
Batangas marks the celebration of the Taal Trade and Cultural Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Taal, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 9 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day in
the Municipality of Taal, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
