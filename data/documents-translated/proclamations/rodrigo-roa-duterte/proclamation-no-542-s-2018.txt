MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES ~
PROCLAMATION NO. 542

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY iN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, 01 August 2018, a Wednesday, will mark the 70" Founding
Anniversary and the 12" Buganihan Festival of the Municipality of Compostela,
Compostela Valley; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Compostela, Compostela Valley, be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies; and

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 01 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th dayof July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority/offhe President:

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
