MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1335

DECLARING THURSDAY, 21 APRIL 2022, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 21 April 2022, Thursday, the City of Calamba, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 21st Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 21 April 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of march ; in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
