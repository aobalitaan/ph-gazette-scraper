MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.422

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, Friday, 02 March 2018, marks the celebration of the 168"
Founding Anniversary of the Province of La Union; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of La
Union, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
02 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
