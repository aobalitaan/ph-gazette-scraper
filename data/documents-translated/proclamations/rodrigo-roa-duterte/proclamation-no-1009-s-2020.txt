MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1009

DECLARING THURSDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLOMOLOK, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 September 2020, Thursday, the Municipality of
Polomolok, Province of South Cotabato, will mark its 63"? Founding Anniversary and
the 17" Fiomlok Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Polomolok be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 10 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Polomolok, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this2nd day ofSeptembeyin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
