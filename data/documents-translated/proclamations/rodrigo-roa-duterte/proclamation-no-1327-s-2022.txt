MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1327

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 APRIL 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN MUNICIPALITY OF MONTALBAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 27 April 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of Montalban,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of its 1518 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Montalban be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 April 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Montalban, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of march , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

ay
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary,
