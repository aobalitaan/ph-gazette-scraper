MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 573

DECLARING FRIDAY, 07 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CAVITE, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 07 September 2018, a Friday, the City of Cavite, Province of
Cavite, will mark its 78"" Charter Anniversary and 447" Foundation Year; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cavite be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
07 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cavite, Province of
Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of September , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority/ofAhe President:
