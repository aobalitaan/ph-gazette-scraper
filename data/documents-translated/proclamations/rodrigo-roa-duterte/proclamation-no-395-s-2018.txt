BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 395

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCEL OF LAND,
UPON WHICH IT STANDS, LOCATED AT BARANGAY ALANG-ALANG,
MANDAUE CITY, CEBU, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (MEDICAL
TOURISM CENTER), PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS
AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916,
otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA
No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine
Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), | RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building, to be known as the Maayo
Medical, with a gross floor area of Thirty-Two Thousand Seven Hundred One and
53/100 Square Meters (32,701.53 m7), more or less and the parcel of land upon
which the building stands, located at Barangay Alang-Alang, Mandaue City, Cebu,
containing an area of Four Thousand Seven Hundred Seventeen Square Meters
(4,717 m*), more or less, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended by
Resolution No. 17-566, and 16-190 (s. 2016) of the PEZA Board of Directors, as a
Special Economic Zone (Medical Tourism Center), as defined by the following
technical description:
