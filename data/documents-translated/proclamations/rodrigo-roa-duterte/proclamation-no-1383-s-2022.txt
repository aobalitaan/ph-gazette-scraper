MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1383

DECLARING MONDAY, 06 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 06 June 2022, Monday, the Province of Zamboanga del Norte
will mark its 70 Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the
national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 06 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga
del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3;g | day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two. ,
