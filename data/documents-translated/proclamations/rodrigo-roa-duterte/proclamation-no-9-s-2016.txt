MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 9

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 JULY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS , on Friday, 15 July 2016,   the Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon marks the celebration of the “Araw ng Mauban;”

WHEREAS ,  it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Mauban,  Quezon  be given  full  opportunity  to  celebrate  and  participate  in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary,  by authority  of the  President, RODRIGO  R. DUTERTE ,  do hereby declare Friday, 15 July 2016, as a special (non-working)  day in the Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
