MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 334

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 OCTOBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, Thursday, 26 October 2017, marks the celebration of the
Founding Anniversary of the Province of Catanduanes; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Catanduanes, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 October 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25+) day of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By autiority of the President:
