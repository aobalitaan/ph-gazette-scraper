MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 239

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOCORRO, ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, Thursday, 22 June 2017, marks the celebration of the Founding
Anniversary of the Municipality of Socorro, Oriental Mindoro; and ‘

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Socorro, Oriental Mindoro, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Socorro, Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
