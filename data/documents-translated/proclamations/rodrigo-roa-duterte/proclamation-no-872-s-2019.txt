MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 872

DECLARING MONDAY, 13 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF VALENCIA, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, the City of Valencia, Province of Bukidnon, will mark the
celebration of its Founding Anniversary on 12 January 2020;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Valencia to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, 13 January 2020, be
declared a special (non-working) day, in lieu of 12 January 2020, which falls on a
Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Valencia
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 13 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valencia,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of December in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
