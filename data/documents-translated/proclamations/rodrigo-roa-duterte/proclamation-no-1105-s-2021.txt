MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1105

DECLARING TUESDAY, 02 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 02 March 2021, Tuesday, the Province of La Union will mark
the celebration of its 1718t Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of La
Union be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 02 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty. One.

By authority of the President:

on
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
