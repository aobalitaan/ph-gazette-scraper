MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 775

DECLARING THURSDAY, 01 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA
VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Compostela,
Compostela Valley, will mark its 71°' Founding Anniversary and the 13" Buganihan
Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Compostela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies; and

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 01 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
