MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 708

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 APRIL 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 26 April 2019, Friday, the Municipality of San Jose, Province
of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 254" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
26 April 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose, Province
of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd _ day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
