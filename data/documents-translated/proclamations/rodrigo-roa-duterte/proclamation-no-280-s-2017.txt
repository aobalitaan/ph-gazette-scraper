MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.280

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 30 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISULAN, PROVINCE OF SULTAN

KUDARAT

WHEREAS. the Municipality of Isulan, Province of Sultan Kudarat marks its
60" Foundation Day and the 6” Hamungaya Festival on Wednesday, 30 August

2017, and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
tsulan, Suitan Kudarat, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday. 30 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Isulan, Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th — day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

.

, a
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary Ay
