MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 612

DECLARING MONDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2018, a Monday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon, will mark its 436" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 12 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
