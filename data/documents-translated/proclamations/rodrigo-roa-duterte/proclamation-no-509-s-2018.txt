MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 509

DECLARING THURSDAY, 21 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIM, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2018, a Thursday, the Municipality of Maasim,
Sarangani, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary and the Kestebeng
Festival, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maasim, Sarangani, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 21 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maasim,
Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof June _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
