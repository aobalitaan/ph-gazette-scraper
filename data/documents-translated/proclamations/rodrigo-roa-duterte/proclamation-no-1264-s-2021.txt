MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1264

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2021, Thursday, the City of Bayawan, Province
of Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its 21St Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayawan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 December 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
