MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 29

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOS BAÑOS, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Friday, 16 September 2016, marks the 401st Foundation Day Municipality of Los Baños, Laguna;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Los Baños, Laguna celebrate the occasion; the Office of the Mayor requested that Friday, 16 September 2016, be declared as a special non-working day instead of 17 September 2016, which falls on a Saturday;

NOW , THEREFORE , I , SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE , do hereby declare Friday, 16 September 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Los Baños, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 4th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
