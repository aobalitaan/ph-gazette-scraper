MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 163

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro marks the
celebration of the “Sulyog Festival” on March 17 — 19, 2017;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Bongabong, Oriental
Mindoro, celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Friday, 17
March 2017, be declared as a special non-working day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
17 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bongabong,
Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

a Sane
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

A Executive Secretary
