MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1262

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 DECEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 20 December 2021, Monday, the City of Sipalay, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of the Pasaway-Handurawan Festival;

and .

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 20 December 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sipalay,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
