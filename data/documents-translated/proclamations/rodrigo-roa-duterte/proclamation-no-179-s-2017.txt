MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.179

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 5 APRIL 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 5 April 2017, marks the celebration of the 437"
Foundation Day of the Province of Pangasinan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Pangasinan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Wednesday, 5 April 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
