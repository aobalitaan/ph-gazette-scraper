BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PH:LIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 297

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A REGULAR HOLIDAY
THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY IN OBSERVANCE OF EID’L ADHA
(FEAST OF SACRIFICE)

WHEREAS, Eid’ Adha or Feast of Sacrifice is one of the two greatest feasts
of Islam;

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 9849 provides that Eid’! Adha shall be
celebrated as a regular holiday; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to the final declaration of the Government of the
Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, the National Commission on Muslim Filipinos has
recommended 01 September 2017 as the day corresponding to the observance of
Eid’! Adha.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Friday,
01 September 2017, as a regular holiday throughout the country in observance of
Eid’! Adha (Feast of Sacrifice).

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of tne Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
