MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 899

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 11 February 2020, Tuesday, the City of Talisay, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 22"? Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Talisay be

given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Tuesday, 11 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Talisay,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

3
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
