MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 935

DECLARING TUESDAY, 24 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 March 2020, Tuesday, the City of Tabaco, Province of
Albay, will mark the celebration of its Charter Day and 19 Cityhood Founding
Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 24 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco,
Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
