MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 982

DECLARING TUESDAY, 14 JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GUIHULNGAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 14 July 2020, Tuesday, the City of Guihulngan, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Guihulngan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 14 July 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihulngan,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
