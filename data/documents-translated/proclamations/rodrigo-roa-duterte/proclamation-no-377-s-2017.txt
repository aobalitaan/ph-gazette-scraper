MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 377

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 20 December 2017, the City of Sipalay marks
the celebration of the Pasaway-Handurawan Festival. and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Sipalay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
