MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 474

DECLARING 25 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF MABINI, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 25 April 2018, the Municipality of Mabini,
Batangas, marks the culmination of its Centennial Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mabini, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mabini, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24h day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
