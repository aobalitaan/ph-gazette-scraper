MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1132

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 APRIL 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PLARIDEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 23 April 2021, Friday, the Municipality of Plaridel, Province of
Bulacan, will mark the celebration of the “Battle of Quingua’;

WHEREAS, the Battle of Quingua was fought on 23 April 1899 in Plaridel,
Bulacan, wherein the Filipino forces were under the command of General Gregorio
del Pilar, and was one of the most significant victories of the Filipinos in the
Philippine-American war; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Plaridel be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
23 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Plaridel, Province of
Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this12thday of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
