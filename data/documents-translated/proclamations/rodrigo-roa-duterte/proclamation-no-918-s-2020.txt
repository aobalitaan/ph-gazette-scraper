MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION No. 218

DECLARING FRIDAY, 13 MARCH 2020, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUKO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain Province, will mark the
celebration of the Begnas di Bauko Festival on 12 to 14 March 2020;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain
Province, to fully celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that
Friday, 13 March 2020, be declared as a special non-working day in the Municipality
of Bauko, Mountain Province; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauko be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
13 March 2020, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain
Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof March | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
