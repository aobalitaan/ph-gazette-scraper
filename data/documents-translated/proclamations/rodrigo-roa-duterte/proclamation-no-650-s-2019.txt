MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 650

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TARLAC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 18 January 2019, Friday, the City of Tarlac, Province of
Tarlac, will mark the celebration of the 3 Kaisa Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac, Province of
Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this!4th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.
