MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. ©84

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF TALAKAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2019, Tuesday, the Municipality of Talakag,
Province of Bukidnon, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Talakag be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Talakag,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

Pen
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
Executive Secretary
