MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 550

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO

WHEREAS, on 16 August 2018, a Thursday, the City of Tuguegarao will
mark the celebration of the Pavvurulun “Afi” Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st dayof July, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
