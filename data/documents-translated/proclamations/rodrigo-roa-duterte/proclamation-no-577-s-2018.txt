MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 577

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VICTORIA, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 18 September 2018, the Municipality of Victoria,
Province of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Victoria be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 18 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Victoria, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Te
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
