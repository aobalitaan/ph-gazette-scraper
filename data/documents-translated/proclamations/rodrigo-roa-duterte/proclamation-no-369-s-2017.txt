MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 369

DECLARING SATURDAY, 16 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF
BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Saturday, 16 December 2017, marks the Founding Anniversary
of the Municipality of Calatagan, Province of Batangas; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calatagan, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 16 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Calatagan, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

“ (A Cee
SALVADOR C. aecay

Executive Secretary
