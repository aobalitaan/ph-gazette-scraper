MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 246

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Monday, 10 July 2017, marks the 13!” anniversary of the
conversion of the Municipality of Santa Rosa, Laguna into a Component City of the
Province of Laguna; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa, Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 10 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa,
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this Sth day of July, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Seventeen.
