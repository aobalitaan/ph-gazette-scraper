MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 830

DECLARING MONDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN CARLOS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 04 November 2019, Monday, the City of San Carlos,
Province of Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its annual festival, a day
of thanksgiving for the many blessings the city has received; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Carlos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Monday, 04 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Carlos,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th dayof October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
