MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 241

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONCADA, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, Saturday, 01 July 2017, marks the 142" Foundation Day of the
Municipality of Moncada, Province of Tarlac; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Moncada, Tarlac, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Moncada, Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisi9th day of June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

St
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
