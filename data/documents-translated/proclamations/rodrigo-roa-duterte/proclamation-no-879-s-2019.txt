MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 879

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 21 February 2020, Friday, the Province of Marinduque, will
mark the centennial celebration of the Araw ng Marinduque; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Marinduque be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authcrity of the President:

. ee.
