MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 634

DECLARING FRIDAY, 07 DECEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIRAC, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, on 07 December 2018, Friday, the Municipality of Virac,
Province of Catanduanes, will mark the celebration of the Kaaldawan nin Virac; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Virac, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
07 December 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Virac,
Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
