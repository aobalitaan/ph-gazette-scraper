MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 513

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 29 June 2018, a Friday, the City of Koronadal will mark the
celebration of the Tree Growing Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
29 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this4th day of June _ inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

awie
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
