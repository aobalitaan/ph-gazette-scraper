BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 150

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON SEVERAL
PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT ARCHBISHOP REYES AVENUE,
CEBU CITY AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT
TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO.
8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise
known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No.
8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine
Economic Zone Authority, 1, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building with a gross floor area of
22,088 square meters, more or less, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No.
7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411
(s. 2000) and Resolution No. 16-104 (s. 2016) of the Board of Directors of the
Philippine Economic Zone Authority, as an Information Technology Center to be
known as Avenir which stands on several parcels of land located at Archbishop
Reyes Avenue, Cebu City containing an aggregate area of One Thousand Four
Hundred Three (1,403) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following
technical description:
