MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.616

DECLARING TUESDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 13 November 2018, a Tuesday, the Municipality of Pantukan,
Province of Compostela Valley, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pantukan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 13 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
