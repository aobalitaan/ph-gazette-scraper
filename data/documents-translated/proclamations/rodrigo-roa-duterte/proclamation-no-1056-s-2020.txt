MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE  PRESIDENT OF THE  PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION  NO.1o56
AMENDING    PROCLAMATION    NO.    1053    DATED    18    NOVEMBER    2020    BY
DECLARING  FRIDAY,  04  DECEIVIBER 2020,  A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY   IN   THE   PROVINCE   OF   CEBU,   AS   WELL   AS   IN   THE    HIGHLY
URBANIZED  CITIES OF  CEBU,  LAPU-LAPU, AND  IVIANDAUE
WHEREAS,  04  December 2020,  Friday,  will  mark the  commemoration  of the
104th birth anniversary of Governor Sergio Osmefia Jr.;
WHEREAS, Governor Osmefia Jr. served as Governor of Cebu Province which
still  covers then  the  cities of Cebu,  Mandaue,  and  Lapu-Lapu  from  1951  to  1959;  as
Mayor of Cebu City from  1959 to 1963; and was elected as senator from 1965 to 1971 ;
and
WHEREAS,  it is but fitting and  proper that the people of the  Province of Cebu
and the cities of Cebu, Lapu-Lapu, and Mandaue be given full opportunity to celebrate
and  participate  in  the  occasion  with  appropriate  ceremonies,  subject  to  community
quarantine,  social distancing,  and other public health  measures;
NOW, THEREFORE,I, SALVADOR C.  MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,  by
authority    of    the    President,    RODRIGO    ROA    DUTERTE,    do    hereby    amend
Proclamation  No.1053 dated  18  November 2020  and  declare  Friday,  04  December
2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Cebu, Cebu City,  Lapu-Lapu City,
and  Mandaue City.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF,I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the  Republic of the  Philippines to  be affixed.
DoneinthecityofManila,this    lst   dayof    December     ,  intheyearofour
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
By authority of the  President:
