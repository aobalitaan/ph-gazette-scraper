MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1073

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TARLAC, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 20 January 2021, Wednesday, the City of Tarlac, Province of
Tarlac, will mark the celebration of the 5th Kaisa Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures; 7

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac,
Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

bey
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
