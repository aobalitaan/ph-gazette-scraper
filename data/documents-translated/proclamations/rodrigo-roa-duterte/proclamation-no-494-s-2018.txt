MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.494

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF ALEGRIA, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 15 June 2018, a Friday, the Municipality of Alegria, Surigao
del Norte, will mark its 50" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Alegria, Surigao del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday, 15
June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Alegria, Surigao del
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of “May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen. 4 -

By authority of te President:

we

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
