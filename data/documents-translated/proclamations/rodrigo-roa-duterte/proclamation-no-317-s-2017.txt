MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 317

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 OCTOBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUINAPONDAN, EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 17 October 2017, the Municipality of Quinapondan,
Eastern Samar marks the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Quinapondan, Eastern Samar, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate
in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 October 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Quinapondan, Eastern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
