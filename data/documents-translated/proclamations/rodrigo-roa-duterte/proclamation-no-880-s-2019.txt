MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 880

DECLARING SATURDAY, 18 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 18 January 2020, Saturday, the City of Santa Rosa, Province
of Laguna, will mark its 228" Founding Anniversary and 21° Sikhayan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 18 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

a
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
