MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1332

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 MAY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 26 May 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Baliwag,
Province of Bulacan, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baliwag be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 May 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Baliwag,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

’
SALWADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
