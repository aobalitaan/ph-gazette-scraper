MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 777

DECLARING THURSDAY, 01 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Kawit,
Province of Cavite, will mark the celebration of Kawit Day; ‘and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Kawit be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 01 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kawit,
Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

tt

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
