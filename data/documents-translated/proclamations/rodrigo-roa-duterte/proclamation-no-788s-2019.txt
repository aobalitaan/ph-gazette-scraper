MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 788

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO

WHEREAS, on 16 August 2019, Friday, the City of Tuguegarao will mark the
celebration of the Afi Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
16 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof August in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

aX
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
