BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 699

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCEL OF LAND
UPON WHICH IT STANDS, LOCATED AT AURORA BOULEVARD
CORNER DONA HEMADY STREET, QUEZON CITY, AS A SPECIAL
ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER), PURSUANT
TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO.
8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or
the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon
the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority (PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the
Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building to be known as Robinsons
Cybergate Magnolia, with a gross floor area of Eighteen Thousand Three Hundred
Fourteen and Ten Hundredths square meters (18,314.10 m2), more or less, and the
parcel of land upon which the building stands, located at Aurora Boulevard corner
Dofha Hemady Street, Quezon City, with an area of Two Thousand Seventy Six and
Forty Three Hundredths square meters (2,076.43 m?), more or less, subject to the
provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations,
Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011),
15-240 (s. 2015) and 17-472 (s. 2017), and Resolution No. 17-101 (s. 2017), as
amended by Resolution No. 18-244 (s. 2018) of the PEZA Board of Directors, as a
Special Economic Zone (Information Technology Center), as defined by the following
technical description:
