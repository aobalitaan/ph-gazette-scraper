MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 281

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYOMBONG, PROVINCE OF NUEVA
VIZCAYA

WHEREAS, on Friday, 04 August 2017, the Municipality of Bayombong,
Nueva Vizcaya marks the celebration of the 1° Bayongyong Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
04 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bayombong,
Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of July, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
