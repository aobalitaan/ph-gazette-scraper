MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 706

DECLARING TUESDAY, 30 APRIL 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALATAGAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 30 April 2019, Tuesday, the Municipality of Calatagan,
Province of Batangas, will mark its Cultural Day together with the celebration of its
municipal fiesta; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calatagan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 30 April 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Calatagan,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof April | inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
