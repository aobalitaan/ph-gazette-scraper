BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 338

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Province of Oriental Mindoro marks its 67 Founding
Anniversary on Wednesday, 15 November 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Oriental Mindoro, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the

occasion with appropriate ceremonies;
NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of

Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
