MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1096

DECLARING SATURDAY, 13 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PARANAQUE

WHEREAS, on 13 February 2021, Saturday, the City of Parafiaque will mark
the celebration of its 23 Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Parafiaque
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 13 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Parafaque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

Ory
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
