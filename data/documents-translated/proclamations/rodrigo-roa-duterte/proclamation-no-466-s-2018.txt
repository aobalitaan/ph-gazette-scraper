MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 466

DECLARING MONDAY, 07 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, the City of San Pablo marks the celebration of its 78" Chafter
Anniversary on Monday, 07 May 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo

be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 07 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof April, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

ms

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
