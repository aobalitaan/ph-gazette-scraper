MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 649

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 18 January 2019, Friday, the City of Santa Rosa, Province of
Laguna, will mark its 227" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
