MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1079

 

DECLARING THURSDAY, 28 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY. OF PADADA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
SUR

1

WHEREAS, on 28 January 2021, Thursday, the Municipality of Padada,
Province of Davao del Sur, will mark:the celebration of the Paindigay sa Padada;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Padada be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 28 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Padada, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
