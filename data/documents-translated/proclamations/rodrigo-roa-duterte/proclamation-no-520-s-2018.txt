MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 520

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 27 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF EL SALVADOR, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of El Salvador, Misamis Oriental, will mark the
celebration of its Charter Anniversary on 27 June 2018, a Wednesday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of El
Salvador, Misamis Oriental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 27 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of El Salvador,
Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of thé President:

ro

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
