MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
, PROCLAMATION NO. 1183

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 JULY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2021, Thursday, the Cordillera Administrative Region
(CAR) will mark the celebration of its 34" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the CAR be given full
opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies,
subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public health
measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 July 2021, a special (non-working) day in the CAR.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my | hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One. .

By authority of the President:

K
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
