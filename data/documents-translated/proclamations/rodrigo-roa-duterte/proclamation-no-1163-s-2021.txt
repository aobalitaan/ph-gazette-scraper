MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1163

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 23 June 2021, Wednesday, the City of Batac, Province of
Ilocos Norte, will mark the celebration of its 14" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 23 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac,
Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June | in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
