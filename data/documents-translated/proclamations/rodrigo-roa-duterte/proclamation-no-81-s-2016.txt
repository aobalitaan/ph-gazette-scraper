MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 81

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE
OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Angono, Rizal marks the celebration of
the Higantes Festival on Wednesday, 23 November 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Angono, Rizal be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Wednesday, 23 November 2016, as a special (non-working)
day in the Municipality of Angono, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13thday of October , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
