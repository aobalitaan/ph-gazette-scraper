MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1153

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BOGO, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2021, Wednesday, the City of Bogo, Province of
Cebu, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bogo be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bogo, Province
of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
