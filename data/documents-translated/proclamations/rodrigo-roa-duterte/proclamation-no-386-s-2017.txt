MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 386

DECLARING FRIDAY, 12 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENCIA, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Friday, 12 January 2018, marks the Founding Anniversary of the
City of Valencia, Bukidnon; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and Proper that the people of the City of Valencia,
Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
12 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Valencia, Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

. at
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
