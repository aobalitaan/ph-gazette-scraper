MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 975

DECLARING THURSDAY, 02 JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, on 02 July 2020, Thursday, the City of Pasig will mark the
celebration of its 447th Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be
given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures:

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR c. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 02 July 2020, a Special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30thday of June, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
