MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 558

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUARTERO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 23 August 2018, the Municipality of Cuartero,
Province of Capiz, will mark the celebration of its 80" anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cuartero be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Cuartero, Province of Capiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

SALWADOR C. MEDIALDEA
