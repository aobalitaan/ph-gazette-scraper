MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 493

DECLARING MONDAY, 28 MAY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 28 May 2018, a Monday, the City of Imus, Cavite, will mark
the anniversary of the Battle of Alapan, otherwise known as the National Flag Day;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Imus be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Monday,
28 May 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Imus, Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this22nd — day of May, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority e President:

wT

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
