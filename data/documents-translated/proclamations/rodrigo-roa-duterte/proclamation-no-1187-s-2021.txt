MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
, PROCLAMATION NO. 1187

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 JULY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MABALACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 21 July 2021, Wednesday, the City of Mabalacat, Province of
Pampanga, will mark the celebration of its Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mabalacat
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 July 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mabalacat,
Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisoth dayof July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

.

YY
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
