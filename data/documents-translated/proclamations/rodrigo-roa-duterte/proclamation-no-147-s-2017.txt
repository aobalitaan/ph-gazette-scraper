MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 147

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 8 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GODOD, ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 8 February 2017, marks the celebration of the
Founding Anniversary of the Municipality of Godod, Zamboanga del Norte;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Godod, Zamboanga del Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 8 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Godod, Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

tr
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE

Executive Secretary
