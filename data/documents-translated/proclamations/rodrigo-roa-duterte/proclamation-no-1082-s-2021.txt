MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1082

DECLARING SATURDAY, 06 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CONSOLACION, PROVINCE OF CEBU
1

WHEREAS, on 06 February 2021, Saturday, the Municipality of Consolacion,
Province of Cebu, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Consolacion be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing,
and other public health measures; 5°

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 06 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Consolacion, Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One. ..
