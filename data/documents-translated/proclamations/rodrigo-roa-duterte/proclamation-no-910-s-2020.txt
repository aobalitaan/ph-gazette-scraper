MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 910

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TANGUB, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2020, Friday, the City of Tangub, Province of
Misamis Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 52°4 Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
28 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tangub, Province of
Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

* MW
SALVADOR C. GEES

Executive Secretary
