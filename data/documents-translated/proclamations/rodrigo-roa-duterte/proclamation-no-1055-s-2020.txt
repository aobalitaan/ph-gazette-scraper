MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE  PRESIDENT OF THE  PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION  NO.1055
DECLARING   TUESDAY,   01    DECEMBER   2020,   A   SPECIAL   (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE  MUNICIPALITY OF  CAINTA,  PROVINCE OF  RIZAL
WHEREAS,   on   01   December  2020,   Tuesday,   the   Municipality  of  Cainta,
Province of Rizal,  will  mark the celebration of its Sumb/.ngf;'A Festival;  and
WHEREAS,  it  is  but  fitting  and  proper  that  the  people  of  the  Municipality  of
Cainta   be  given  full  opportunity  to   participate  in  and   celebrate  the  occasion  with
appropriate  ceremonies,   subject  to  community  quarantine,   social  distancing,   and
other public health measures;
NOW,  THEREFORE,  I,  SALVADOR  C.  IVIEDIALDEA,  Executive  Secretary,
by   authority   of   the   President,   RODRIGO   ROA   DUTERTE,   do   hereby   declare
Tuesday,   01   December  2020,   a   special   (non-working)   day  in   the   Municipality  of
Cainta,  Province of Rizal.
IN  WITNESS WHEREOF,  I  have  hereunto  set  my  hand  and  caused  the  seal
of the  Republic of the  Philippines to be affixed.
Done  in  the  City of Manila,  this   25th      day of November,  in the  year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand  and Twenty.
By authority of the  President:
SAL
ADOR C.  IVIEDIALDEA,,
Executive Secretary
