MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 712

DECLARING MONDAY, 06 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTIAGO

WHEREAS, on 05 May 2019, Sunday, the City of Santiago will mark the
celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

. WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Santiago to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, 06 May 2019, be declared
a special (non-working) day in lieu of 05 May 2019, which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting ardsproper that the people of the City of Santiago
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 06 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th dayof April, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

Ww
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
