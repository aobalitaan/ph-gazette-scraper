MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1046

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2020, Monday, the Province of Benguet will
mark the celebration of its 120° Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Benguet be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 23 November 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
