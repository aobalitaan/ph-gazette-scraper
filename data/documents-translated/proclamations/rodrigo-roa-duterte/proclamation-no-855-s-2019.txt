MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 855

DECLARING FRIDAY, 06 DECEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF M’LANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 06 December 2019, Friday, the Municipality of M’lang,
Province of Cotabato, will mark the celebration of the Kawayanan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
M'lang, be given full opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
06 December 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of M’lang,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of November jin the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
