MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 768

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 25 July 2019, Thursday, the City of Tanjay, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of the Sinulog sa Tanjay Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanjay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanjay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th dayof July _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
