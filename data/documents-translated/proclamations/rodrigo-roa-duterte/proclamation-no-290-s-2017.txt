MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 290

DECLARING TUESDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIQUIJOR, PROVINCE OF
SIQUIJOR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Siquijor marks the celebration of its 45"
Anniversary as the capital of the Province of Siquijor on Tuesday, 12 September
2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Siquijor, Province of Siquijor, be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Tuesday, 12 September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the
Municipality of Siquijor, Province of Siquijor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary
