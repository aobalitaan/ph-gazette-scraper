MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1031

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN CARLOS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 04 November 2020, Wednesday, the City of San Carlos,
Province of Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its annual festival
commemorating the renaming of the locality from its former name, Nabinkalan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Carlos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 04 November 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of San
Carlos, Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

ew
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
