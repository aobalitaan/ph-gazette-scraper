MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. ©82

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 08 March 2019, Friday, the Province of Compostela Valley
will mark its 218' Founding Anniversary and the 12'" Bulawan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
08 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
