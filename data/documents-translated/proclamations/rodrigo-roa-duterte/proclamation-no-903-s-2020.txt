MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION No, 203

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 17 February 2020, Monday, the Municipality of Taytay,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Pasasalamat: HAMAKA
Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taytay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RO

DRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 17 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Taytay, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

. ae
SALVADOR C. MEDIALD

Executive Secretary
