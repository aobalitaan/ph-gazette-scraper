MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1286

DECLARING THURSDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2022, Thursday, the City of Cabanatuan will
mark the celebration of its 724 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cabanatuan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion

with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 03 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
