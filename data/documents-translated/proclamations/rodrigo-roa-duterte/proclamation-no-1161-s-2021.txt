MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1161

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAUREL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Laurel, Province
of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 52"4 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Laurel be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Laurel,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

aw
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,
