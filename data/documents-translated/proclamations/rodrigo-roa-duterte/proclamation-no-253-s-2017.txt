MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 253

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on Friday, 25 August 2017, the City of Iloilo marks its 80"
Charter Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iloilo, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR CG. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
25 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

.

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
