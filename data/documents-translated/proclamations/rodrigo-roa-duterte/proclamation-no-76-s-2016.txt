MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 76

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KABASALAN,
PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, Friday, 17 February 2017, marks the celebration of the
80" Foundation Anniversary of the Municipality of Kabasalan, Zamboanga
Sibugay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Kabasalan, Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to
celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 17 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in
the Municipality of Kabasalan, Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of October, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
