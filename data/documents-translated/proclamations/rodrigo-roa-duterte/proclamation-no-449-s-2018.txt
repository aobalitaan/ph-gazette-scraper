MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 449

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PANABO, DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the City of Panabo, Davao del Norte, marks the celebration of its
17" Founding Anniversary on 31 March 2018;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Panabo to celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Panabo requested that Monday, 26
March 2018 be declared as a special non-working day in lieu of 31 March 2018, the
actual anniversary day, which falls on a Black Saturday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Panabo,
Davao de! Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 26 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Panabo,
Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th — day of March, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Eighteen.
