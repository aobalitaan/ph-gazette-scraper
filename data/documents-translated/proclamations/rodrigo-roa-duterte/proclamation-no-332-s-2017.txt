BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.332

PROCLAMATION DECLARING 13 — 15 NOVEMBER 2017 AS SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAYS IN THE NATIONAL CAPITAL. REGION (NCR),
PROVINCE OF BULACAN AND PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, the Philippines will host the 31" Association of Southeast Asian
Nations (ASEAN) Summit and Related Summits on 13-15 November 2017;

__ WHEREAS, various activities directly connected with the 31°! ASEAN Summit
and Related Summits, to be attended by ASEAN Leaders and ASEAN Dialogue
Partners, will be held in the NCR and Clark Field, Pampanga;

WHEREAS, the ASEAN 2017 National Organizing Council has recommended
that 13 ~ 15 November 2017 be declared as special (non-working) days in the NCR
and the Provinces of Bulacan and Pampanga, as these areas will be directly affected
by the said activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare 13 - 15
November 2017 as special (non-working) days in the NCR and the Provinces of
Bulacan and Pampanga. ,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of . October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen
