MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 767

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS, on 22 July 2019, Monday, the City of General Santos will mark
the celebration of its Indigenous Peoples 5" Blaan Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 22 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of General Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
