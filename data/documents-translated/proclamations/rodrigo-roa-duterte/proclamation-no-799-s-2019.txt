MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 799

DECLARING MONDAY, 02 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, the Province of Bukidnon will mark the celebration of its 105"
Founding Anniversary on 01 September 2019;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Bukidnon to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Governor requested that Monday, 02 September 2019, be
declared a special (non-working) day in lieu of 01 September 2019, which falls on a
Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 02 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authorityof/the President:

Be
