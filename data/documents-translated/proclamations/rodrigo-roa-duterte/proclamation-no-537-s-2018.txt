MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 537

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 JULY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA
VALLEY

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley will mark the
celebration of its 618' Founding Anniversary on 23 July 2018, a Monday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Nabunturan, Compostela Valley, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate
in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 23 July 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Nabunturan, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.
