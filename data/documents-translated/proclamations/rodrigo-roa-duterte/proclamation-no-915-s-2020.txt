MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 915

DECLARING SATURDAY, 07 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE ISLAND GARDEN CITY OF SAMAL, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 07 March 2020, Saturday, the Island Garden City of Samal,
Province of Davao del Norte, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Island Garden
City of Samal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 07 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Island Garden City of
Samal, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

Komoun a.
SALVADOR C. nee

Executive Secretary
