MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.100

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL ‘(NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALUNGON,
SARANGAN! PROVINCE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Malungon, Sarangani Province marks
the celebration of its Tribal Day and Slang Festival on Friday, 18 November
2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Malungon, Sarangani Province be given full opportunity to
celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 18 November 2016, as a special (non-working) day in
the Municipality of Malungon, Sarangani Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of November, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

Bae
SALYADOR C. MEDIALDEA
