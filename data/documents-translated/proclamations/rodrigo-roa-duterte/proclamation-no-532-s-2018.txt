MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 532

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 JULY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 26 July 2018, a Thursday, the Municipality of Ipil, Zamboanga
Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its 69" Araw ng Ipil; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Ipil, Zamboanga Sibugay, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 26 July 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ipil,
Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

4 Ut
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
