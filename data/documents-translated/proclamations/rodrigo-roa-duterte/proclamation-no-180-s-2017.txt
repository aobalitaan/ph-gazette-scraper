MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 180

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE

OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 22 March 2017, the Municipality of
Baliwag, Bulacan marks the 154" birth anniversary of Mariano Ponce;

WHEREAS, Mariano Ponce, a native of Baliwag, Bulacan, was a
prominent member of the propaganda movement and one of the leading
figures in the Philippine reform movement during the Spanish colonization;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Wednesday, 22 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in

the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of March, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
