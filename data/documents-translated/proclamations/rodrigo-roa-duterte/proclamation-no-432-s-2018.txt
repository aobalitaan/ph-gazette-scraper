MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 432

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 07 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 07 March 2018, the City of Tagum marks the
celebration of the Araw ng Tagum, and

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagum be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 07 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tagum,
Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
