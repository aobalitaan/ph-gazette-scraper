MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1374

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAYOYAO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, on 01 June 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of Mayoyao,
Province of Ifugao, will mark the celebration of its Foundation Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mayoyao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 01 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mayoyao, Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25ti day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
