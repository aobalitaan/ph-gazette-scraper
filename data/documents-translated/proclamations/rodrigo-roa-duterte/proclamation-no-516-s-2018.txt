MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 516

DECLARING THURSDAY, 21 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALETE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Balete, Batangas, will mark the celebration of
its Founding Anniversary on 21 June 2018, a Thursday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Balete, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 21 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Balete,
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June __ , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
