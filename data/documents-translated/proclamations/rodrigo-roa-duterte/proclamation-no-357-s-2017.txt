MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 357

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO

WHEREAS, the City of Antipolo marks the celebration of its 426" Founding
Anniversary on Friday, 08 December 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Antipolo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
08 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Antipolo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this22nd day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
