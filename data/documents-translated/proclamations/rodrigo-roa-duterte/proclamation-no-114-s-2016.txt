MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 114

DECLARING THURSDAY, 5 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUDIN, PROVINCE
OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur will
celebrate its 431%' Foundation Anniversary on Thursday, 5 January 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur be given full opportunity to
participate and celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Thursday, 5 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in
the Municipality of Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of December, in the year of
“Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
