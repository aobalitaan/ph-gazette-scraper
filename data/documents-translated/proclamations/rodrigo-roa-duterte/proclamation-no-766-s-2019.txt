MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 766

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 14 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STO. TOMAS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 14 August 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of Sto. Tomas,
Province of Davao del Norte, will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Sto. Tomas;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sto. Tomas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 14 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sto.
Tomas, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15thday of July , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
