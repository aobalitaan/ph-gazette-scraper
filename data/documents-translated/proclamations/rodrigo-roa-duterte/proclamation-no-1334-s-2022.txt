BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1334

DECLARING TUESDAY, 05 APRIL 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 05 April 2022, Tuesday, the Province of Pangasinan will mark
the celebration of its 442°¢ Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Pangasinan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 05 April 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Pangasinan. ,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th dayof arch _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
