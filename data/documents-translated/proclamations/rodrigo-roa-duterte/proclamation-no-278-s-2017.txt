MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 278

DECLARING SATURDAY, 19 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS. on Saturday, 19 August 2017, the Municipality of Angono,
Province of Rizal marks the signing of its independence as a municipality from the
municipality of Binangonan, Rizal: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono, Rizal, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 19 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Angono, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
