MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1062

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 DECEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 18 December 2020, Friday, the City of Tuguegarao, Province
of Cagayan, will mark the celebration of its 21st Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 December 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao, Province
of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
