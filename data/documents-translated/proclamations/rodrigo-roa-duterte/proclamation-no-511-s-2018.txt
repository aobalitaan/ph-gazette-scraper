MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 511

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF STA. TERESITA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sta. Teresita, Cagayan, will mark the
celebration of its Founding Anniversary on 22 June 2018, a Friday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Teresita, Cagayan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
22 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Teresita,
Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of June __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
