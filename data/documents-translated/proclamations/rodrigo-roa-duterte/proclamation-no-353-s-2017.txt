MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.353

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Pifian, Zamboanga del Norte will celebrate its
Founding Anniversary on Tuesday, 28 November 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pifian, Zamboanga del Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 28 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality
Pian, Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
