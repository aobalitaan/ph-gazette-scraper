MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. ©11

DECLARING MONDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2018, a Monday, the Municipality of Tanay,
Province of Rizal, will mark its 412" founding anniversary which coincides with the
celebration of the 8" Hane Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 12 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tanay, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
