MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 801

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 04 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 04 September 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley, will mark the celebration of its 65"
Founding Anniversary and the 24" Kariyawan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Monkayo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 04 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisagthday of August , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

i

LVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary |
