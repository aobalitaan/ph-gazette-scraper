MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 341

DECLARING MONDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS. the Municipality of Gumaca, Quezon marks its 435" Founding
Anniversary on 12 November 2017:

WHEREAS. to enable the people of the Municipality of Gumaca, Quezon, to
celebrate the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan of Gumaca requested that Monday,
13 November 2017 be declared as a special non-working day, instead of 12
November 2017. which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca, Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate tn the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW. THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President. RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 13 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of November , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
