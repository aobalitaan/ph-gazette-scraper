MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

.

PROCLAMATION NO. 591

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 08 October 2018, a Monday, the City of Koronadal, Province
of South Cotabato, will mark its 18'° Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 08 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal,
Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
re

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,,V
Executive Secretary /
