MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 402

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on Friday, 02 February 2018, the Province of Ilocos Norte marks
the celebration of its 200" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ilocos
Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
02 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Cea sG
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
