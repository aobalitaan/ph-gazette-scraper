MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 700

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 MARCH 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINANGONAN, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 29 March 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Binangonan,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of its 119" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Binangonan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
29 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Binangonan,
Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
