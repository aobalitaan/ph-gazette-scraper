BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1301

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCEL OF LAND UPON
WHICH IT STANDS, LOCATED IN BARANGAY UNGKA II, MUNICIPALITY
OF PAVIA, PROVINCE OF ILOILO, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916,
otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended, and upon
the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority (PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the
Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building with a gross floor area of
Ninety-Four Thousand Nine Hundred Ninety-Eight and 7/100 square meters
(94,998.07 m2), more or less, and the parcel of land where the building stands, located
in Barangay Ungka II, Municipality of Pavia, Province of Iloilo, as an Information
Technology Center to be known as Cybergate Iloilo Towers, subject to the provisions
of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, PEZA Board
of Directors Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution Nos. 11-006
(s. 2011), 15-240 (s. 2015) and 17-472 (s. 2017), and Resolution No. 20-048 (s. 2020),
with an area of Eight Thousand Seven Hundred Twenty-Three square meters
(8,723 m2), more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
