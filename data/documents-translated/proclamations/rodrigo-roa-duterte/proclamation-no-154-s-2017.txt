MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 154

DECLARING TUESDAY, 7 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE ISLAND GARDEN CITY OF SAMAL

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 7 March 2017, marks the celebration of the Charter
Anniversary of the Island Garden City of Samal;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Island Garden
City of Samal, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MENARDO I. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Tuesday, 7 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Island Garden
City of Samal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

ne

MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary <
