MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. ©08

DECLARING TUESDAY, 06 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUENAVISTA, PROVINCE OF
MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 06 November 2018, a Tuesday, the Municipality of
Buenavista, Province of Marinduque, will mark the celebration of its centennial
founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Buenavista be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 06 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Buenavista, Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 34St day of October _ in the year of Our
Lord, Two-Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
