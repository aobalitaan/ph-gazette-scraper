MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 690

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 APRIL 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PANABO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the City of Panabo, Province of Davao del Norte, will mark the
celebration of its 18 Founding Anniversary on 31 March 2019;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Panabo to celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Panabo requested that Monday, 01 April
2019 be declared a special (non-working) day in lieu of 31 March 2019, which falls
on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Panabo be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 01 April 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Panabo, Province
of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of March _ , inthe year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
