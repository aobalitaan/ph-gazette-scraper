MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 128

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian marks the celebration of the “Pasalamat
Festival” on 15 January 2017;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Pagadian celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, 16 January 2017, be
declared as a special (non-working) day instead of 15 January 2017, which falls

on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Pagadian be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Monday, 16 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Pagadian.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

Foe DI

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
