MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1373

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 JULY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF SAN CARLOS, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2022, Friday, the City of San Carlos, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Carlos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
01 July 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Carlos, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
