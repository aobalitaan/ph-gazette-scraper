MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1221

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IROSIN, PROVINCE OF

SORSOGON

WHEREAS, on 29 September 2021, Wednesday, the Municipality of Irosin,
Province of Sorsogon, will mark the celebration of the Paray Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
lrosin be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 29 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
lrosin, Province of Sorsogon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

La
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
