MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 175

DECLARING TUESDAY, 14 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 14 March 2017, the City of Passi, lloilo, marks the
celebration of its Foundation Day as a component city;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Passi,
lloilo, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Tuesday, 14 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Passi,
Province of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
By authority pf the President:

VV me VIE

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary
