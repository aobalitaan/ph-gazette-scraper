MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 450

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 14 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 14 March 2018, the City of Passi, lloilo, marks
the celebration of its Founding Anniversary as a component city; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Passi,
lloilo, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 14 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Passi,
Province of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
