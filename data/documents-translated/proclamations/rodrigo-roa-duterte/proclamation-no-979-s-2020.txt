MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 979

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2020, Wednesday, the Municipality of Mauban,
Province of Quezon, will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Mauban; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mauban be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 July 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mauban, Province of Quezon

IN WITNESS WHEREOF. | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th — day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President
