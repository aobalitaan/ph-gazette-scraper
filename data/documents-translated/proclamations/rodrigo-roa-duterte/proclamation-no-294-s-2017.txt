MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 294

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUPI, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Tupi, South Cotabato marks the celebration
of its 64""Foundation Day and the 8” Agten Tufi Festival on Monday, 11 September
2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and Proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tupi, South Cotabato, be given full Opportunity to celebrate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies.

Now, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR c. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 11 September 2017, asa special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tupi, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
