MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 320

DECLARING THE LAST WEEK OF NOVEMBER OF EVERY YEAR AS “DIRECT
SELLING WEEK”

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 163 (s. 1999) declared the last week of August
of every year as “Direct Selling Week", under the auspices of the Direct Selling
Association of the Philippines (DSAP);

WHEREAS, in recent years, the DSAP has been holding its major programs
and events such as industry awards, management conferences and trade expos
throughout the month of November;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby amend
Proclamation No. 163 (s. 1999) to declare the last week of November of every year
as “Direct Selling Week.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29thday of September _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
