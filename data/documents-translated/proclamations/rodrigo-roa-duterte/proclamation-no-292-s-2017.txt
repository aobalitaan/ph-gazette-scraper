MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 292

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOS BANOS, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Los Bajos, Laguna marks its 402™
Foundation Day on 17 September 2017;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Los Bafios, Laguna
celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Friday, 15 September
2017, be declared as a special non-working day instead of 17 September 2017,
which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Los Bafios, Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday, 15

September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Los Banos,
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
