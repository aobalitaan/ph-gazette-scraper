MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.352

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, PROVINCE OF
RIZAL

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Angono, Rizal marks the celebration of the
Higantes Festival on Thursday, 23 November 2017: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Angono, Rizal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 November 2017, as a Special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Angono, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
