MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 977

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2020, Wednesday, the Cordillera Administrative
Region (CAR) will mark the celebration of its 33 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the CAR be given full
opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies,
subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public health
measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 July 2020, a special (non-working) day in the CAR.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of July in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
