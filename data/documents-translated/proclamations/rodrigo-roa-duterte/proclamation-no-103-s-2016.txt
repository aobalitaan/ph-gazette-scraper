MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 103

DECLARING THURSDAY, 8 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO

WHEREAS, the City of Antipolo marks the celebration of its 425"
Foundation Day on Thursday, 8 December 2016,

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Antipolo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

ona

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Thursday, 8 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City
of Antipolo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of November , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
