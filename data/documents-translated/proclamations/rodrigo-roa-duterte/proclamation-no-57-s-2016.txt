MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 57

DECLARING SATURDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLOMOLOK, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, Saturday, 10 September 2016, marks the F’lomlok Festival and Foundation Anniversary of the Municipality of Polomolok, South Cotabato;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Polomolok, South Cotabato be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE , do hereby declare Saturday, 10 September 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Polomolok, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of September in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
