MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 203

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF APARRI, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 11 May 2017, the Municipality of Aparri, Cagayan
marks the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aparri, Cagayan, be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 11 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Aparri,
Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

tet te.
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

/ Executive Secretary
