MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 112

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO

WHEREAS, the City of Tuguegarao marks the celebration of its 17"
Charter Day on Friday, 16 December 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 16 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day in
the City of Tuguegarao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

; Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of December, in the year of
‘Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

.

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
