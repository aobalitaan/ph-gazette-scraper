MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES 4
PROCLAMATION NO. 1352

DECLARING THURSDAY, 05 MAY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTIAGO

WHEREAS, on 05 May 2022, Thursday, the City of Santiago will mark the
celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 05 May 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26tndayof April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
