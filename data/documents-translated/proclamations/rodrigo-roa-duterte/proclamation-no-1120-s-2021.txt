MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1120

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 MAY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 26 May 2021, Wednesday, the Municipality of Baliwag,
Province of Bulacan, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baliwag be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 26 May 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Baliwag, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this17tiday of March_, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
