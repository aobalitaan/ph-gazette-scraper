MALACANAN PALACE
MANIDA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 304

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS. the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province marks the
celebration of its Foundation Day on 16 September 2017;

WHEREAS. to enable the people of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain
Province, to celebrate the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan of Bontoc requested
that Friday. 15 September 2017 be declared as a special non-working day, instead
of 16 September 2017. which falls on a Saturday; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bontoc. Mountain Province, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
15 September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5thday ofSeptemberin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
