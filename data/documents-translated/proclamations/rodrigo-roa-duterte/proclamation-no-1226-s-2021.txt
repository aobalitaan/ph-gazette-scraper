MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1226

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 OCTOBER 2021 AND FRIDAY, 29 OCTOBER 2021,
AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAYS IN THE CITY OF ANGELES,
PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 11 October 2021 (Monday) and 29 October 2021 (Friday), the
City of Angeles, Province of Pampanga, will mark the opening day of the Kuliat
Festival (Fiestang Kuliat) and the Tigtigan Terakan Keng Dalan, a festival which
symbolizes the resilience of the people during the Mt. Pinatubo eruption,
respectively; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Angeles
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare 11
October 2021 and 29 October 2021 as special (non-working) days in the City of
Angeles, Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of September , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
