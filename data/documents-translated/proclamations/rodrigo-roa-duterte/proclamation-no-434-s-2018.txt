MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 434

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS. the Municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet, marks the celebration of
its 37" Strawberry Festival on the whole month of March 2018, and its Strawberry
Festival Parade on 16 March 2018;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet,
to fully celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Municipal Mayor requested that
Friday, 16 March 2018, be declared as a special non-working day in the Municipality
of La Trinidad, Benguet: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of La
Trinidad, Benguet, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
16 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of La Trinidad,

Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, ! have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. .

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th dayof February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
