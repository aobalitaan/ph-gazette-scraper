MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 3509

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 NOVEMBER 2017, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Tanay, Rizal marks its Founding Anniversary,
which coincides with the celebration of the annual Hane Festival on 12 November

2017;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Tanay, Rizal to
celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Friday, 10 November
2017, be declared a special non-working day, instead of 12 November 2017, which
falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tanay, Rizal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
10 November 2017, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tanay,
Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of November, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

WF be

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEAy, Y
