MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES .
PROCLAMATION NO.139

DECLARING MONDAY, 27 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS, on Monday, 27 February 2017, the City of General Santos
marks its 78" Foundation Anniversary and the 28" Kalilangan Festival,

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 27 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of General
Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisl7th day of January | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority cf the President:
