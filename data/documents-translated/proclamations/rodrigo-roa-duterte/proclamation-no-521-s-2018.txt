MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 521

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DAGUPAN

WHEREAS, on 20 June 2018, a Wednesday, the City of Dagupan will mark
the celebration of its 718‘ Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dagupan,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF ., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen
