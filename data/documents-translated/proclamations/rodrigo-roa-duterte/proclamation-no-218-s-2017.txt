MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 218

DECLARING SATURDAY, 24 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 24 June 2017, the City of Tabaco, Albay marks the
celebration of the Tabak Festival, a showcase of the city’s rich history, cultural
diversity and beautiful tourist attractions;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco,

Albay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, 1, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Saturday, 24 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco,
Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of May __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
