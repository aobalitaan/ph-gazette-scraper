4 MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1166

DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 22 June 2021, Tuesday, the City of Pagadian, Province of
Zamboanga del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 524 Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 22 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

YA
SALVADOR C. eeetey te

Executive Secretary
