MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.  23
DECLARING    THURSDAY,    4   AUGUST    2016,    AS   A   SPECIAL   (NON-
WORKING)    DAY    IN    THE    MUNICIPALITY    OF    SAN    PASCUAL,
PROVINCE 0F BATANGAS
WHEREAS,  Thursday,  4  August  2016,  marks  the  Foundation  Day  of
the Municipality of San  Pascual,  Batangas;
WHEREAS,    it   is    but   fltting    and    proper   that   the    people   of   the
Municipality  of  San  Pascual,  Batangas  be  given  full  opportunity  to  celebrate
and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.
NOW,    THEREFORE,    I,    SALVADOR    C.    IVIEDIALDEA,    Executive
Secretary,  by  authority  of the  President,  RODRIGO  R.  DUTERTE,  do  hereby
declare  Thursday,   4  August  2016,   as  a   special   (non-working)   day   in   the
Municipality of Sam  Pascual,  Batangas.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF,  I  have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the  Philippines to be affixed.
DoneinthecityofManila,  this28thdayof    July      ,  intheyearofour
Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
By authority of the President:
SAL
