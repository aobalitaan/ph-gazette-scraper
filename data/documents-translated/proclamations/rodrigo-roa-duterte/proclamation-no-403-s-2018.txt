MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 403

DECLARING SATURDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 03 February 2018, the City of Bihan, Laguna marks
the celebration of its 73 Liberation Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Binan,
Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 03 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Biflan,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
