MALACANAN PALACE

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.1071

6

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 15 January 2021, Friday, the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of the_26" Coconut Festival and Street Dancing
Competition (Mardi Gras); and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
15 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
