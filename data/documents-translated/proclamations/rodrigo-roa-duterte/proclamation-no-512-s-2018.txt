MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION No. >12

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian, Zamboanga del Sur, will mark its 49"
Charter Anniversary on 22 June 2018, a Friday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian,
Zamboanga del Sur, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
22 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian, Zamboanga del
Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof June _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
