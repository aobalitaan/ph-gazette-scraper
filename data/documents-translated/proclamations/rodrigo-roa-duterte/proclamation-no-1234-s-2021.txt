MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1234

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, on 22 November 2021, Monday, the City of Palayan, Province of
Nueva Ecija, will mark the celebration of its Annual Thanksgiving Day for a bountiful
harvest; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 22 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan,
Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26thday of October in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
