MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. ; 1294

DECLARING SATURDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2022, Saturday, the Municipality of Buug,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Buug be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 26 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Buug,
Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of february , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

Cys
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,,
Executive Secretary
