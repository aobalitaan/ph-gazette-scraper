MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1353

DECLARING THURSDAY, 19 MAY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BUTUAN

WHEREAS, on 19 May 2022, Thursday, the City of Butuan will mark the
celebration of the Balangay Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Butuan be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 19 May 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Butuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28ti day of April, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
