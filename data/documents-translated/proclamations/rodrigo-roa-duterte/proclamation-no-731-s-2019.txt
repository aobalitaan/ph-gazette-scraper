MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 731

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MANILA ~*

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2019, Monday, the City of Manila will mark the
celebration of its 448"" Foundation Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Manila be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 24 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.
