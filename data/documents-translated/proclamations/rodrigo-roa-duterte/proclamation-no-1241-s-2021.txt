MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1241

DECLARING SATURDAY, 13 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE
ORO

WHEREAS, on 13 November 2021, Saturday, the Municipality of Pantukan,
Province of Davao de Oro, will mark the celebration of its 84" Founding Anniversary
and the 19" PASAKA Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pantukan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing,
and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 13 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pantukan, Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof November , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
