MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1115

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 17 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYUG, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 17 March 2021, Wednesday, the Municipality of Tayug,
Province of Pangasinan, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tayug be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 17 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tayug, Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

le aw
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
Executive Secretary
