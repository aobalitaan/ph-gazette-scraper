MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1360

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Rosario, Province
of Agusan del Sur, will mark the celebration of the Buyawan Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rosario be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario,
Province of Agusan del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10ta dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
