MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 326

DECLARING SATURDAY, 28 OCTOBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGLE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS the First Battle Cry of Revolution against Spain in the Province of
lloilo and the Istand of Panay. known as the “Cry of Lincud,” happened on 28
October 1898 in Barrio Lincud Dingle, lloilo; and

WHEREAS in order for the people of the Municipality of Dingle, Iloilo to
celebrate and participate in the event, the Office of the Sangguniang Bayan of Dingle
has requested that Saturday 28 October 2017 be declared as a special non-working
day:

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Saturday, 28 October 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipatity of
Dingle, Province of lloilo

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of October, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen

By authority of the President:
