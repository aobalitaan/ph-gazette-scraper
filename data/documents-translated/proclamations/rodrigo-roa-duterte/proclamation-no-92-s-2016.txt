MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 92

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 2 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 2 November 2016, the Province of
Misamis Occidental marks the celebration of its Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Misamis Occidental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Wednesday, 2 November 2016, as a special (non-working)
day in the Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF. | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of October, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

: DS tame

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary
