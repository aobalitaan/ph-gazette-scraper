MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1225

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 OCTOBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 08 October 2021, Friday, the City of Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 21st Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but-fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
08 October 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal, Province of
South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

we

SALVADOR C.MEDIALDEA .
Executive Secretary
