MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1111

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 18 March 2021, Thursday, the Province of Masbate will mark
the celebration of its 120° Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 18 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

Piva,
