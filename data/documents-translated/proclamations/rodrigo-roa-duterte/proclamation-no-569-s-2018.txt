MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 569

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOS BANOS, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 17 September 2018, a Monday, the Municipality of Los
Bajos, Province of Laguna, will mark its 403% founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Los Bajios be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 17 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Los
Bajos, Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

et «
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
