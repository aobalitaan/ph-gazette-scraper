MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 12

DECLARING MONDAY, 1 AUGUST 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS , Monday, 1 August 2016marks the 68th Foundation Day and 10th Buganihan Festival of the Municipality of Compostela, Compostela Valley;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Compostela, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Monday, 1 August 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal if the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
