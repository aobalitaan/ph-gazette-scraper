MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 868

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 DECEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 20 December 2019, Friday, the City of ‘Sipalay, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of the Pasaway-Handurawan Festival;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with apprognae
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
20 December 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sipalay, Province of
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19t day of December, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
