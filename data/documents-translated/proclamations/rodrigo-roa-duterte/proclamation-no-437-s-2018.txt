MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 437

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CANLAON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Canlaon, Negros Oriental, marks the culmination of
the week-long Pasayaw Festival on 19 March 2018;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Canlaon to fully celebrate the
occasion, the City Government of Canlaon requested that Monday, 19 March 2018,
be declared as a special non-working day in the City of Canlaon; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Canlaon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Canlaon,
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of February: in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
