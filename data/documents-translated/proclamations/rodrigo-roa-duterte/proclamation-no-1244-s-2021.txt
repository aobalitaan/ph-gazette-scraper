MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1244

DECLARING THURSDAY, 11 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAAL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 11 November 2021, Thursday, the Municipality of Taal,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 440'" Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 11 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Taal, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th | day of November, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
