MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 633

DECLARING FRIDAY, 07 DECEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF M’LANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 07 December 2018, Friday, the Municipality of M’lang,
Province of Cotabato, will mark the celebration of the Kawayanan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
M'lang, be given full opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
07 December 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of M'lang,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
