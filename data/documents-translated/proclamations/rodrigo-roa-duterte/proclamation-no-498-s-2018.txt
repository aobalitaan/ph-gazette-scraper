MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 498

DECLARING SATURDAY, 09 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Rosario, Batangas, will mark its Founding
Anniversary on 09 June 2018, a Saturday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rosario, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Saturday,
09 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this24th dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen. ~

By authority e President:

7

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
