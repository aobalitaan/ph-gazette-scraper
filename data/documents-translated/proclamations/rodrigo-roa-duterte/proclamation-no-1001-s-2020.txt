MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1001

DECLARING THURSDAY, 03 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 03 September 2020, Thursday, the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of its 52"! Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 03 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25thday of Agustin the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

&
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
