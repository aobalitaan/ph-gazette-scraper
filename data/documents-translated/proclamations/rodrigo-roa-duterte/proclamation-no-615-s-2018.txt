MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 615

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AROROY, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 17 November 2018, a Saturday, the Municipality of Aroroy,
Province of Masbate, will mark the celebration of its 115" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aroroy be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 17 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Aroroy, Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof November, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

——
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
