MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.289

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLOMOLOK, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Polomolok, South Cotabato marks its 60"
Foundation Anniversary and the 14°" Fiomlok Festival on 10 September 2017:

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Polomolok, South
Cotabato, to celebrate these occasions, the Sangguniang Bayan of Polomolok
requested that Friday, 08 September 2017 be declared as a special non-working
day, instead of 10 September 2017, which falls ona Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Polomolok, South Cotabato be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
these occasions with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO I. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Friday, 08 September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the
Municipality of Polomolok, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
By authority of the President:
oe

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary
