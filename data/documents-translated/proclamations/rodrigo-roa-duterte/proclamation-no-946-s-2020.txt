BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 946

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCELS OF LAND ON
WHICH IT STANDS, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION
TECHNOLOGY CENTER) TO BE KNOWN AS THE GLAS OFFICE
DEVELOPMENT, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or the
“Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the
recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority
(PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do
hereby create and designate a building to be known as the GLAS Office Development,
with a gross floor area of One Hundred Three Thousand Two Hundred Eighty-Three
square meters and Eighteen square decimeters (103,283.18 m fe), more or less, and the
parcels of land on which the building stands, located along Opal Road corner Topaz and
Ruby Roads, Barangay San Antonio, Pasig City, with an area of Two Thousand Nine
Hundred Fifty-Two square meters and Fifty square decimeters (2,952.50 m 2), more
or less, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules
and Regulations, PEZA Board of Directors Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended
by Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011), 15-240 (s. 2015) and 17-472 (s. 2017), and
Resolution No. 17-226 (s. 2017), as a Special Economic Zone (Information Technology
Center), as defined by the following technical descriptions:
