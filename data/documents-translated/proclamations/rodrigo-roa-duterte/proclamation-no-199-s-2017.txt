MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 199

DECLARING TUESDAY, 2 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS. the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas, marks the celebration of its
Sublian Festival on Tuesday, 2 May 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauan, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 2 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauan,
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

Se

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
