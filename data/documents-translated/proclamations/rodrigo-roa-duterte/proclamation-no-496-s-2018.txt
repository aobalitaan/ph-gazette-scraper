MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 496

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Carmen, Davao del Norte was created by
virtue of Republic Act No. 4745 dated 18 June 1966;

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2018, a Monday, the Municipality of Carmen, Davao
del Norte will mark its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carmen, Davao del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Monday,
18 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Carmen, Davao del
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. -

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of May __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
