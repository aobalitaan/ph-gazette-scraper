MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 158

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TANGUB

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 28 February 2017, marks the celebration of the
Charter Day of the City of Tangub;
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub,

be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 28 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tangub.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
