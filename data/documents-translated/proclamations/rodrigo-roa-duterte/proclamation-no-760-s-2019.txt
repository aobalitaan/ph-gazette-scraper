MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 760

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 10 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 10 July 2019, Wednesday, the City of Santa Rosa, Province
of Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 15 Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 10 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
