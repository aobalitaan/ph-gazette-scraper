MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 83

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 7 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIRAC, PROVINCE OF
CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Virac, Catanduanes marks the
celebration of the “Kaa/dawan nin Virac” on Wednesday, 7 December 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Virac, Catanduanes be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Wednesday, 7 December 2016, as a special (non-working)
day in the Municipality of Virac, Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13thday of October , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
