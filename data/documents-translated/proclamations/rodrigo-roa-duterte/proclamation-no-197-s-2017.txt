MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 197

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 APRIL 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
METRO MANILA

WHEREAS, the 30" Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) Summit
2017 is being hosted by the Philippines,

WHEREAS, activities directly connected with the 30" ASEAN Summit, to be
attended by various world leaders, will be held in Metro Manila on 26 to 30 April 2017;

WHEREAS, the ASEAN 2017 National Organizing Council — Office of the Director-
General for Operations and the Metro Manila Development Authority recommended the
suspension of work for the government and private sectors in Metro Manila on 28 April
2017; and

WHEREAS, Memorandum Circular No. 18 (s. 2017) issued by the Office of the
President suspended work in Metro Manila for the government and private sectors to
ensure the successful hosting of the 30" ASEAN Summit.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary. by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday, 28 April
2017, as a special (non-working) day in Metro Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the
Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Seventeen.
