MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 696

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 APRIL 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 05 April 2019, Friday, the Province of Pangasinan will mark
the celebration of its 439" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Pangasinan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
05 April 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th dayof March , inthe year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
