MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 829

DECLARING MONDAY, 28 OCTOBER 2019, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGLE, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, the First Battle Cry of Revolution against Spain in the Province of
lloilo and the Island of Panay, known as the “Cry of Lincud,” happened on 28
October 1898 in Barrio Lincud, Dingle, lloilo;

WHEREAS, on 28 October 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Dingle,
Province of Iloilo, will mark the commemoration of the “Cry of Lincud”; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dingle be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 28 October 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dingle,
Province of Iloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th dayof October, jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
