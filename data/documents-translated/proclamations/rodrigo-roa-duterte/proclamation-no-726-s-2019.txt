MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 726

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 11 June 2019, Tuesday, the Province of Rizal will mark its
18h Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Rizal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 11 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th dayof May , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

“eG
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
