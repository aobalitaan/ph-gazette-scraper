MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 549

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 15 August 2018, a Wednesday, the Province of Bulacan will
mark the celebration of its founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of July __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

‘ -
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDFA

Executive Secretary
