MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 138

DECLARING FRIDAY, 24 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, the Province of Zamboanga Sibugay marks the celebration of its
16" Araw ng Sibugay on Friday, 24 February 2017,

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
24 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga
Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. .

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day ofJanuary in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

KN
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

N Executive Secretary
