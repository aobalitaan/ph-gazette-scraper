MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1106

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 01 March 2021, Monday, the City of Davao, Province of
Davao del Sur, will mark the celebration of its Araw ng Dabaw; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 01 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao, Province
of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24+h day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
