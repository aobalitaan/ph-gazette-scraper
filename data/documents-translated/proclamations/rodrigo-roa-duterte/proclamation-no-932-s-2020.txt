MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 932

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 APRIL 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TANJAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 01 April 2020, Wednesday, the City of Tanjay, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanjay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 01 April 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tanjay,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF-, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20thday of March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
