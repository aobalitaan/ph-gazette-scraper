MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 339

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 23 November 2017, the Province of Benguet will
celebrate its 117" Foundation Anniversary; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Benguet, be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

gl races is
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, ~
Executive Secretary
