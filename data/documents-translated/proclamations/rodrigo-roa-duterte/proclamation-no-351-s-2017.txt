MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 351

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AROROY, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on Friday, 17 November 2017, the Municipality of Aroroy,
Masbate marks its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aroroy, Masbate be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
17 November 2017, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Aroroy,
Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
