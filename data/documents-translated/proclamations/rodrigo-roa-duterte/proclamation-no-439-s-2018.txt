MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 439

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, on Monday, 19 March 2018, the City of Davao marks the
celebration-of its Araw ng Pasasalamat in relation to the “81" Araw ng Dabaw’, and

WHEREAS it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with appropriate

ceremonies,

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this27th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

oN
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
