MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 480

DECLARING SATURDAY, 05 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SANTIAGO

WHEREAS, under Republic Act No. 7720, the Municipality of Santiago was
converted into an independent component city on 05 May 1994;

WHEREAS, Saturday, 05 May 2018, marks the celebration of the Founding
Anniversary of the City of Santiago; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santiago
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 05 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Santiago.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd dayof May __in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
By authority of the President:
