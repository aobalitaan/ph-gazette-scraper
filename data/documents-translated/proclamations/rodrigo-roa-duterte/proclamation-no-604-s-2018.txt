MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 604

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 24 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 24 October 2018, a Wednesday, the Municipality of Pili,
Province of Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of the Cimarrones Festival, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pili
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies; ‘

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 24 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pili,
Province of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary

Office of the President
