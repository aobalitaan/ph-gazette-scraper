MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 610

DECLARING FRIDAY, 09 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 09 November 2018, Friday, the Province of Eastern Samar
will mark the celebration of its 53 founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Eastern Samar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
09 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Eastern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 318t day of October in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

A-— be
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
