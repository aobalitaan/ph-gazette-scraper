MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1266

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 DECEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 17 December 2021, Friday, the City of Bifian, Province of
Laguna, will commemorate the completion of Dr. Jose P. Rizal's first formal
education; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bihan be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
17 December 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bifian, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
