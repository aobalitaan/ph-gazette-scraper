MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1396

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 22 June 2022, Wednesday, the City of Pagadian, Province of
Zamboanga del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 53° Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
