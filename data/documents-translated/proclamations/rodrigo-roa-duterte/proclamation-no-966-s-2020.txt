MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 966

DECLARING TUESDAY, 23 JUNE 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BATAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 23 June 2020, Tuesday, the City of Batac, Province of Ilocos
Norte, will mark the celebration of its 43" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 23 June 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac, Province of
Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

its
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
