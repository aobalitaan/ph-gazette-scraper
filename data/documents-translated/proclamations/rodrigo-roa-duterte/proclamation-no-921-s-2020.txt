MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 921

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LA TRINIDAD, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, the Municipality of La Trinidad, Benguet, will mark the celebration
of its 39" Strawberry Festival on the whole month of March 2020;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of La Trinidad, Province
of Benguet, to fully celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Municipal Mayor
requested that Friday, 20 March 2020, be declared a special (non-working) day in
the Municipality of La Trinidad, Province of Benguet; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of La
Trinidad be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
20 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of La Trinidad,
Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof March _, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
