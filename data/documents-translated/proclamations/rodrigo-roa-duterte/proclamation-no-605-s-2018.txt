MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 605

DECLARING MONDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2018, a Monday, the City of Valenzuela will
mark the celebration of its 395" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Valenzuela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 12 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
