MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1025

DECLARING THURSDAY, 08 OCTOBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTAB ATO

WHEREAS, on 08 October 2020, Thursday, the City of Koronadal, Province
of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 20” Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate

ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 08 October 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal,
Province of South Cotabato,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this lst dayof October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
