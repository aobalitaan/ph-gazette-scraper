MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 220

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on Friday, 23 June 2017, the City of Batac, Ilocos Norte, marks
the celebration of its 10" Charter Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac,

llocos Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies,

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
23 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac, Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this}0th day of May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
