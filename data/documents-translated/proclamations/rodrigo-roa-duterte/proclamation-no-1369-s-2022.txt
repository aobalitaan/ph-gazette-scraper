MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1369

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2022, Friday, the City of Lapu-Lapu will mark the
celebration of its 61st Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lapu-Lapu
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
17 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lapu-Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25thday of may , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
