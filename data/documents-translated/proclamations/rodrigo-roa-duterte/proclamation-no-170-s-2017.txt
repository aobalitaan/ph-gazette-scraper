BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.170

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 APRIL 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF HIMAMAYLAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 25 April 2017, marks the celebration of the Himaya-an
Festival of the City of Himamaylan, Negros Occidental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Himamaylan, Negros Occidental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 25 April 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Himamaylan,
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of February | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
