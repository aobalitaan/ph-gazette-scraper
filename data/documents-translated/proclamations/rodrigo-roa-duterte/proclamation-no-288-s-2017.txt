MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 288

DECLARING FRIDAY, 01 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JASAAN, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS
ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Friday, 01 September 2017, marks the Foundation Anniversary
of the Municipality of Jasaan, Misamis Oriental; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Jasaan, Misamis Oriental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO I. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Friday, 01 September 2017, as a Special (non-working) day in the
Municipality of Jasaan, Province of Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

on) a

MENARDO I. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary
