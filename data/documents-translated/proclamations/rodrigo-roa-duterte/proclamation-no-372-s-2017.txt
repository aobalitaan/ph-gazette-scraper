MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 372

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, Friday, 08 December 2017, marks the celebration of the
Founding Anniversary of the City of Batac; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
08 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of December, jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

1!
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
