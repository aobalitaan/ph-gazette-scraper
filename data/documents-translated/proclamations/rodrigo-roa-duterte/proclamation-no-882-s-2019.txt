MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 882

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALASIQUI, PROVINCE OF
PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 23 January 2020, Thursday, the Municipality of Malasiqui,
Province of Pangasinan, will mark the celebration of its 349" Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malasiqui be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Malasiqui, Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

\
SALVADOR C. HERE
