DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS, on 26 February 2020, Wednesday, the City of Zamboanga will
mark the celebration of its 83" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Zamboanga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 26 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 34 day of February in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty. .
