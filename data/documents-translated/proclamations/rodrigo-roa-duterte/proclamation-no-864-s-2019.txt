MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 864

DECLARING THURSDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PINAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 28 November 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Pinan,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte, will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pifian be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 28 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality Pinan,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

4 ae
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
