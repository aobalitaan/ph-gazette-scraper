MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 424

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 07 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 07 March 2018, marks the celebration of the 44"
Founding Anniversary of the Province of Basilan: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Basilan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 07 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
