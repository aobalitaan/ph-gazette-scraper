MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 11

DECLARING TUESDAY, 26 JULY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS ,   on Tuesday,   26 July 2016, the Municipality   of lpil, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay marks the celebration of its “67th Araw ng Ipil;”

WHEREAS ,    it  is  but   fitting   and   proper   that   the   people   of  the Municipality of Ipil, Zamboanga  Sibugay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE,  I, SALVADOR  C. MEDIALDEA ,  Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE , do hereby declare  Tuesday,   26  July  2016,   as  a  special  (non-working)   day  in  the Municipality of Ipil, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,  Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
