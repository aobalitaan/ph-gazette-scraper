MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 796

DECLARING FRIDAY, 30 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF MANDAUE, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2019, Friday, the City of Mandaue, Province of
Cebu, will mark the celebration of its 50" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mandaue
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
30 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mandaue, Province of
Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
