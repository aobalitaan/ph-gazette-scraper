MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.433

DECLARING THURSDAY, 08 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, Thursday, 08 March 2018, marks the 20" Founding Anniversary
and the 11°” Bulawan Festival of the Province of Compostela Valley; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 08 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authcrity of the President:

—

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
