MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 851

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, the Province of Benguet will mark the celebration of its 119"
Founding Anniversary on 23 November 2019;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Benguet to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Governor requested that Friday, 22 November 2019, be
declared a special (non-working) day in lieu of 23 November 2019, which falls on a
Saturday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Benguet be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
22 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of November | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
