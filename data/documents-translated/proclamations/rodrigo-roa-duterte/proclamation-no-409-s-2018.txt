MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 409

DECLARING TUESDAY, 13 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PARANAQUE

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 13 February 2018, marks the celebration of the 20"
Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Parafiaque; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Parahaque
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 13 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Parafiaque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
