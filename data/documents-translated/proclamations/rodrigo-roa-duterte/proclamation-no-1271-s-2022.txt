MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1271

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 05 JANUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUDIN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, on 05 January 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of Tagudin,
Province of Ilocos Sur, will mark the celebration of its 436'" Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tagudin be given full opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 05 January 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of December in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One
