MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1193

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 04 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 04 August 2021, Wednesday, the City of Cabuyao, Province
of Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 9 Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabuyao
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 04 August 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabuyao,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29thdayof July , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
