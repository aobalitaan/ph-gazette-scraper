MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 567

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUPI, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 11 September 2018, a Tuesday, the Municipality of Tupi,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 65" founding anniversary
and the 9" Agten Tufi Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tupi be given full opportunity to celebrate in these occasions with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 11 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tupi, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of August , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
