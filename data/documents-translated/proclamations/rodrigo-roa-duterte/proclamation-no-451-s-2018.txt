MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 451

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PLARIDEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on Monday, 23 April 2018, the Municipality of Plaridel, Bulacan,
marks the commemoration of the “Battle of Quingua”;

WHEREAS, the Battle of Quingua was fought on 23 April 1899 in Plaridel,
Bulacan, wherein the Filipino forces were under the command of General Gregorio
de! Pilar, and was one of the most significant victories of the Filipinos in the
Philippine-American war, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Plaridel, Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 23 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Plaridel,
Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

be
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary 4
