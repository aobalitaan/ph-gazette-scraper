MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 763

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

WHEREAS, on 15 July 2019, Monday, the Cordillera Administrative Region
(CAR) will mark the celebration of its 32°¢ Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the CAR be given full
opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 15 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the CAR.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof July , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

Dey
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary |
