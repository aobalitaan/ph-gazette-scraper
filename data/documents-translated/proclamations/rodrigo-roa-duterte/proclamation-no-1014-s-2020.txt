MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1014

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2020, Friday, the City of Tacurong, Province of
Sultan Kudarat, will mark the celebration of its 20" Cityhood Anniversary and the
Talakudong Festival 2020; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong, Province of
Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of September , jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
