MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1127

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 07 APRIL 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 07 April 2021, Wednesday, Mountain Province will mark the
celebration of its 54° Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Mountain
Province be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 07 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

| at
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary “
