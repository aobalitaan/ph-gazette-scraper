MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 957

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 JUNE 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BOGO, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2020, Tuesday, the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu,
will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bogo,
Province of Cebu, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social
distancing and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 16 June 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bogo, Province of
Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this gna dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

eg
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
