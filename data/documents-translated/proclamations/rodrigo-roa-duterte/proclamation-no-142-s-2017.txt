MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 142

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF LAOAG

WHEREAS, the City of Laoag marks the celebration of the Pamulinawen
Festival and the “Laoag City Day’ on Friday, 10 February 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
10 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24thday of January jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

we
SALWADOR C. MEDIALDEA
