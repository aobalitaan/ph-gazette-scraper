MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 131

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF LIPA

WHEREAS, the City of Lipa marks the celebration of the Barako
Festival 2017 on Friday, 20 January 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 20 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the
City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

(XN
SALWADOR C. MEDIALDEA
