MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 518

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF AMBAGUIO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2018, a Monday, the Municipality of Ambaguio,
Nueva Vizcaya, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Ambaguio, Nueva Vizcaya, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies,

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Monday,
18 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ambaguio, Nueva
Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen
