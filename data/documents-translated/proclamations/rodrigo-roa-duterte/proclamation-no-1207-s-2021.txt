MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1207

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 08 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
SUR

WHEREAS, on 08 September 2021, Wednesday, the City of Digos, Province
of Davao del Sur, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 08 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos,
Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
