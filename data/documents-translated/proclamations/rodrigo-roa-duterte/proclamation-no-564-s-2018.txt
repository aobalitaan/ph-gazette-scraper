BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION No. 564
DECLARING MONDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUIRINO
WHEREAS,
Republic Act No. 6394 constituted the Province of Quirino on 10 September 1971;
WHEREAS,
on 10 September 2018, a Monday, the Province of Quirino will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary; and
WHEREAS,
it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Quirino be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;
NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,
Executive Secretary, by the authority of the President,
RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE,
do hereby declare Monday, 10 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Quirino.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF,
I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen
By authority of the President:
(Sgd.)
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
