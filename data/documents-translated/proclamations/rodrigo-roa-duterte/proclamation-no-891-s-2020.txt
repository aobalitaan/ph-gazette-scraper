MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 891

DECLARING MONDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2020, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BINAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2020, Monday, the City of Bifian, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 75" Liberation Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bifian be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 03 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bifan,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
