MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 262

.

ati THURSDAY, 10 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
N THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Gumaca, Province of Quezon marks the
celebration of Lorenzo Tafiada, Sr. Day on Thursday, 10 August 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca, Quezon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby deciare
Thursday, 10 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon. =

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

eu
SALYADOR C. MEDIALDEA
