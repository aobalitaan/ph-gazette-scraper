MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1156

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL

SUR

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2021, Wednesday, the Municipality of Molave,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur will mark the celebration of its 73 Founding

Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Molave be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Molave, Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of JUne _ in the year of Our Lord, ~
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
