MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 97

DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 22 November 2016, marks the 43" Founding
Anniversary of the Province of Sultan Kudarat;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sultan Kudarat be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Tuesday, 22 November 2016, as a special (non-working) day
in the Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, ! have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
