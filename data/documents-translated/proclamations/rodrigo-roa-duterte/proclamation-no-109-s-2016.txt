MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 109

DECLARING MONDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARGARITA,
PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sta. Margarita, Samar marks the
celebration of its Foundation Day on Monday, 28 November 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Margarita, Samar be given full opportunity to participate in and celebrate the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Monday, 28 November 2016, as a special (non-working) day: in the
Municipality of Sta. Margarita, Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of November, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
