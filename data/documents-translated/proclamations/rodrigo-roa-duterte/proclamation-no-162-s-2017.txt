MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 162

DECLARING FRIDAY, 31 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PANABO, DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, Friday, 31 March 2017, marks the celebration of the 16"
Foundation Anniversary of the City of Panabo, Davao del Norte;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Panabo,
Davao del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
31 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Panabo, Davao del
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

ee
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

/S Executive Secretary .
