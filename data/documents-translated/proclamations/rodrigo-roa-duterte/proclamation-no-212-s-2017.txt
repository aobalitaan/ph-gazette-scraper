MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 212

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 17 June 2017, the Province of Agusan del Sur
marks the celebration of its Fiftieth (60") Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Agusan del Sur, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 17 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Agusan
del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of May _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

/———- 21
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
