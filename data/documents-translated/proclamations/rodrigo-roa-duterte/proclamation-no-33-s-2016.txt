MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 33

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS , Friday, 16 September 2016, marks the Foundation Day of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW , THEREFORE , I , SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE , do hereby declare Friday, 16 September 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
