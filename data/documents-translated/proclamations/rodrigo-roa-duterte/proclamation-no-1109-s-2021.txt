MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1109

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 08 March 2021, Monday, the Province of Davao de Oro will
mark the celebration of its 23" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Davao de Oro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing,
and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 08 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Davao de
Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
