MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 778

DECLARING SATURDAY, 03 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 03 August 2019, Saturday, the City of Tacurong, Province of
Sultan Kudarat, will mark the celebration of its 68" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong

be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Saturday, 03 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29thday of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

be

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
