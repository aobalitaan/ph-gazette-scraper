MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1095

DECLARING FRIDAY, 12 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 12 February 2021, Friday, the City of Kidapawan, Province of
Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 23"? Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Kidapawan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
12 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Kidapawan, Province of
Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
