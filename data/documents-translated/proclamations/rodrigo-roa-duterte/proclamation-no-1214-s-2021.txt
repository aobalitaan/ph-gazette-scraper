MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1214

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
* DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on 16 September 2021, Thursday, the Municipality of Bontoc,
Mountain Province, will mark the celebration of its 113" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bontoc be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bontoc, Mountain,Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10thday of September , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President?
