MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 652

DECLARING FRIDAY, 25 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE

WHEREAS, on 25 January 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Carigara,
Province of Leyte, will mark its 448" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carigara be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
25 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Carigara,
Province of Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
