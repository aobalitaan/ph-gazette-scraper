MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 831

DECLARING MONDAY, 04 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 04 November 2019, Monday, the Province of Quezon will

mark the death anniversary of its local hero Apolinario dela Cruz, also known as
Hermano Puli; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of

Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 04 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of October | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
