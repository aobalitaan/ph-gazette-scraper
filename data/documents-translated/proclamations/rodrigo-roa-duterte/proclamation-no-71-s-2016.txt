BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPRINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 71

DECLARING MONDAY, 7 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUENCA, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Monday, 7 November 2016, marks the 140" Foundation
Anniversary of the Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fifting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas be given full Opporiunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR Cc. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Monday, 7 November 2016, as a special (non-working) day in
the Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this seh day of October , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
