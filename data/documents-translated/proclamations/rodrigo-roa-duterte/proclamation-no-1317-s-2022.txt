MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1317

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSELLER T. LIM, PROVINCE OF
ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, on 16 March 2022, Wednesday, the Municipality of Roseller T.
Lim, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay, will mark the celebration of its 40'" Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Roseller T. Lim be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Roseller T. Lim, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2ng day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

6
HS
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
