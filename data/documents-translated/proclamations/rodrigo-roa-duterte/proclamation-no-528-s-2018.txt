MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.528

DECLARING THURSDAY, 19 JULY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 19 July 2018, a Thursday, the City of Digos, Davao del Sur,
will mark the celebration of the Padigosan Festival, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 19 July 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this Sth day of July, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
