MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 547

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYOMBONG, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA

WHEREAS, on 03 August 2018, a Friday, the Municipality of Bayombong,
Nueva Vizcaya, will mark the celebration of the 2"? Bayongyong Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
03 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bayombong,
Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of July __, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

a \
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
