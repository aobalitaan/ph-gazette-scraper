MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 894

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 11 February 2020, Tuesday, the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of the Tinagba Festival, an ancient
Bicolano First-Harvest Offering; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 11 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of lriga, Province
of Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27t® day of January | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
