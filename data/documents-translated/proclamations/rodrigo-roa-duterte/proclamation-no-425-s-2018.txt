MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 425

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 07 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE ISLAND GARDEN CITY OF SAMAL, DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 07 March 2018, marks the celebration of the 20"
Founding Anniversary of the Island Garden City of Samal, Province of Davao del
Norte, to be known as “Araw ng /sland Garden City of Samal”: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Island Garden
City of Samal, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 07 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Island Garden
City of Samal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13h day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

im
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary

Office of the President
