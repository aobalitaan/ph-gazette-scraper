MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. ©32

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 DECEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MEYCAUAYAN, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 10 December 2018, Monday, the City of Meycauayan,
Province of Bulacan, will mark the celebration of its Araw ng Lungsod ng
Meycauayan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Meycauayan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 10 December 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Meycauayan,
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this29th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
