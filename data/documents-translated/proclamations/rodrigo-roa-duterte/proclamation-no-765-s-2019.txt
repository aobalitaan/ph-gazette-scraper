MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 765

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 19 July 2019, Friday, the City of Digos, Province of Davao del
Sur, will mark the celebration of the Padigosan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Execulive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,

19 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Province of Davao del
Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

we
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Execulive Secretary ib
