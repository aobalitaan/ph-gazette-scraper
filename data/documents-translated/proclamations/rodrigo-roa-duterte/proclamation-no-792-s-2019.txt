MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 792

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 22 August 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Liloy, Province
of Zamboanga del Norte, will mark the celebration of its 68" Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it'is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Liloy be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Liloy,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this14th day of August , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
