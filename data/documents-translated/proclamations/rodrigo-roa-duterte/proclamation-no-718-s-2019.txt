MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 718

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon, will mark the celebration of the Araw ng mga Magsasaka at
Arana at Baluarte Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

KaLvapox C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
