MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 104

DECLARING THURSDAY, 8 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Province of Batangas marks the celebration of its
Foundation Day on Thursday, 8 December 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

ri

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Thursday, 8 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14thday of November , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
