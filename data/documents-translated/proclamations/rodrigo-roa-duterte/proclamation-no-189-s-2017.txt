MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 189

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 APRIL 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Friday, 21 April 2017, marks the 16" Cityhood Anniversary of
the City of Calamba, Laguna;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba,
Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 April 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this4th day of April , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
