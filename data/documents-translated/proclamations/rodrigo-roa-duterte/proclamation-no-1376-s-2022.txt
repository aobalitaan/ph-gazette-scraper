MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1376

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 June 2022, Monday, the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 75" Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Monday, 20 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th dayof jay, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
