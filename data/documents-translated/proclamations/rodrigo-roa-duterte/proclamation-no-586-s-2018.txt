MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 586

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CALBAYOG, PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 16 October 2018, a Tuesday, the City of Calbayog, Province
of Samar, will mark the celebration of its Charter Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calbayog
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 16 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calbayog,
Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of September in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

ee
SALWADOR C. MEDIALDEA, /
