MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 159

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 8 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 8 March 2017, marks the 19" Foundation
Anniversary and the 10” Bulawan Festival of the Province of Compostela Valley;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Compostela Valley, be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 8 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
