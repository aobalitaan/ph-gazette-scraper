MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 343

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 22 November 2017, the Province of Sultan
Kudarat marks its 44"" Founding Anniversary and the 19" Kalimudan Festival. and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sultan Kudarat. be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these
occasions with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Sultan Kudarat,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
