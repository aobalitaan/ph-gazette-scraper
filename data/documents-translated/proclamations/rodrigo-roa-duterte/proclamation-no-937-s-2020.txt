MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 937

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 MAY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF PALAWAN

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections will conduct a plebiscite on 11 May
2020, Monday, in the Province of Palawan, for the ratification of Republic Act No.
11259 dated 05 April 2019, otherwise known as the “Charter of the Provinces of
Palawan del Norte, Palawan Oriental, and Palawan del Sur’; and

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Palawan the opportunity to
actively and fully participate in the plebiscite and to exercise their right to vote, itis but
fitting and proper to declare 11 May 2020 a special (non-working) day;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Monday, 11
May 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Palawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 24th day of March , inthe
year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

A—— oy
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
