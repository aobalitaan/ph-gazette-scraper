MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 936

DECLARING THURSDAY, 02 APRIL 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF BATAAN

WHEREAS, 02 April 2020, Thursday, will mark the celebration of the birth
anniversary of Francisco “Balagtas” Baltazar, a prominent Filipino poet and orator;

WHEREAS, Francisco “Balagtas” Baltazar, a notable Tagalog writer, married
a maiden from Orion, Bataan, resided in the town of Orion, became productive in his
art form, and died in the same town in 1862; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bataan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 02 April 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bataan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th dayof March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

ao Le
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE

Executive Secretary
