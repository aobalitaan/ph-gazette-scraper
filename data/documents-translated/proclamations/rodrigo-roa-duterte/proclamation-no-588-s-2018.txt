MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 588

DECLARING FRIDAY, 12 OCTOBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILAR, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON

WHEREAS, on 12 October 2018, a Friday, the Municipality of Pilar, Province
of Sorsogon, will mark the celebration of its founding date; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pilar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
42 October 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pilar, Province of
Sorsogon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of September, jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

tN
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,

Executive Secretary
