BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 348

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 08 NOVEMBER 2017, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TACLOBAN

WHEREAS, on 08 November 2013, the City of Tacloban was struck by super
typhoon Yolanda, causing loss of lives and destruction of property;

WHEREAS, on 08 November 2017, the City of Tacloban will commemorate
the persons who perished and survived super typhoon Yolanda, and highlight the
resilience of its people; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacloban
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C, MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 08 November 2017, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Tacloban.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
