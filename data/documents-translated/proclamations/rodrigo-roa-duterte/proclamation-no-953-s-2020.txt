CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCELS OF LAND

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or
the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon
the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority (PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Philippines, do
hereby create and designate a building, to be known as ROBINSONS LUISITA 2,
with a gross floor area of Five Thousand Thirty Three and Thirty Five Hundredths
square meters (5,033.35 m2), more or less, and the parcels of land upon which the
building stands, located along McArthur Highway, San Miguel, Tarlac City, with an
aggregate area of Five Thousand One Hundred Seventy Five and Fifty Three
Hundredths square meters (5,175.53 m?), more or less, subject to the provisions of
RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No.
00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011), 15-240 (s. 2015)
and 17-472 (s. 2017), and Resolution No. 19-004 (s. 2019) of the PEZA Board of
