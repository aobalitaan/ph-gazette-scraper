MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 886

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, will mark
the celebration of its 20"" Pasalamat Festival, which features various commemorative
activities to be held from 10 to 20 January 2020;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Pagadian to celebrate the
occasion, the City Mayor requested that Monday, 20 January 2020, be declared a
special (non-working) day, in culmination of the said event; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 20 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
