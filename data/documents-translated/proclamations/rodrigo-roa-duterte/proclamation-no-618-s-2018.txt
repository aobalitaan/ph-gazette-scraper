MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 618

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, on 22 November 2018, a Thursday, the City of Palayan,
Province of Nueva Ecija, will mark the celebration of its Annual Thanksgiving Day for
a bountiful harvest; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan,
Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
