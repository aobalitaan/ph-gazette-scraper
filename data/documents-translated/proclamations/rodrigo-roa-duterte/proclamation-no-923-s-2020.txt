MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 923

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 18 March 2020, Wednesday, the Province of Masbate will
mark the celebration of its 119" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to celebrate’ and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9*® = dayof March | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
