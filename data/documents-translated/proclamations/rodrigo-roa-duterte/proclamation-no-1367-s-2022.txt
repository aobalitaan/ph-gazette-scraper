MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1367

DECLARING SATURDAY, 25 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAS NIEVES, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 25 June 2022, Saturday, the Municipality of Las Nieves,
Province of Agusan del Norte, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Las Nieves be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 25 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Las
Nieves, Province of Agusan del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24tia day of wiay , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two

By authority of the President:
