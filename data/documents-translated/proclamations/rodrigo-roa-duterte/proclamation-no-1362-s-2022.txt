MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1362

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 23 June 2022, Thursday, the City of Batac, Province of Ilocos
Norte, will mark the celebration of its 15" Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac, Province
of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
