MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 244

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SAN CARLOS, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, Saturday, 01 July 2017, marks the Charter Day of the City of
San Carlos, Negros Occidental; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Carlos, Negros Occidental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of San Carlos,
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
