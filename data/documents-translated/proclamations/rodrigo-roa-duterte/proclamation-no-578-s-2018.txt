MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 578

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2018, a Tuesday, the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat, will mark its 18” cityhood anniversary and the
Talakudong Festival 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 18 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this10th day of September , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

~
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
