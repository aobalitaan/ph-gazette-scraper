MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 751

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2019, Monday, the Province of Masbate will mark
the celebration of the Kasadyaan sa Dagat 2019 (Bagat Dagat Festival); and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Masbate be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies ;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 24 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18thday of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

eu

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
