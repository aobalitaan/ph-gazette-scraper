MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1400

DECLARING FRIDAY, 24 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF MANILA

WHEREAS, on 24 June 2022, Friday, the City of Manila will mark the
celebration of its 451s' Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Manila be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
24 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
