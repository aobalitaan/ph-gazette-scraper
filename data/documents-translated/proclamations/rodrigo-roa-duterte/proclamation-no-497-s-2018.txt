MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 497

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 06 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the Province of Zamboanga del Norte will mark its 66" Founding
Anniversary on 06 June 2018, a Wednesday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 06 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
