MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 166

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, on Friday, 17 March 2017, the City of Davao marks the
celebration of its “80"" Araw ng Dabaw’;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate

ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
17 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen. .
