MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 397

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Santa Rosa was politically emancipated as a municipality on 18
January 1792;

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 18 January 2018, the City of Santa Rosa, Laguna
marks its 226" Founding Anniversary as a municipality; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa, Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 18 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa
Rosa, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
