MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 637

DECLARING THURSDAY, 20 DECEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 20 December 2018, Thursday, the City of Sipalay, Province
of Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of the Pasaway-Handurawan

Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 20 December 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Sipalay,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th dayof December , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA ~~
