MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1265

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINDANGAN, PROVINCE OF
ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2021, Thursday, the Municipality of Sindangan,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte, ‘will mark the celebration of its 85 Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sindangan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 23 December 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15thlay of December, jn the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
