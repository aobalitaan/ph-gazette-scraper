MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 414

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CADIZ

WHEREAS, on Friday, 26 January 2018, the City of Cadiz, Negros
Occidental marks the celebration of its 44” Dinagsa Festival: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
26 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
