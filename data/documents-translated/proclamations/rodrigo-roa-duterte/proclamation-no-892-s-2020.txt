MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 892

DECLARING MONDAY, 03 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 03 February 2020, Monday, the Province of Bulacan will
mark the celebration of the birth anniversary of Blas F. Ople;

WHEREAS, Blas F. Ople, who served as Secretary of Foreign Affairs, Senate
President, and Secretary of Labor, devoted decades of his life to selfless and
dedicated service to our country and our people;

WHEREAS, Blas F. Ople, a son of Bulacan, is a model citizen and public
officer to the Filipino people, especially to the “Bulakenyos”; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 03 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January | in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
