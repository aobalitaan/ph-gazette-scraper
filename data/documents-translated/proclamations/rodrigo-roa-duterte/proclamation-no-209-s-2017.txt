MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 209

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Friday, 16 June 2017, marks the 69" Founding Anniversary of
the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Molave, Zamboanga del Sur, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
46 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Molave,
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, thisl9th day of May , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
