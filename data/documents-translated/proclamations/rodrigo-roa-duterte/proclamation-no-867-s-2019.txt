MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 867

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 DECEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on 18 December 2019, Wednesday, the City of Tuguegarao,
Province of Cagayan, will mark the celebration of its 20" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 December 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Tuguegarao, Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

"Le
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE

Executive Secretary
