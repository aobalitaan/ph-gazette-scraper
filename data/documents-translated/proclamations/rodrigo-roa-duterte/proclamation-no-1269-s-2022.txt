MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1269

DECLARING TUESDAY, 04 JANUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL
MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 04 January 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Naujan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its 117! Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naujan be given full opportunity to celebrate and Participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 04 January 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Naujan, Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

Seog une te
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE

Executive Secretary
