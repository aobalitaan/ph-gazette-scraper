BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1004

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCELS OF LAND ON
WHICH IT STANDS, LOCATED AT LACSON STREET CORNER B.S. AQUINO
DRIVE, BACOLOD CITY, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION
TECHNOLOGY CENTER), PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS
AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or the
“Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the
recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority
(PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and
designate a building to be known as the T.U. Building, with a gross floor area of Nine
Thousand Thirty-Eight and 13/100 square meters (9,038.13 m?), more or less, and the
parcels of land upon which the building stands, located at Lacson Street corner B.S.
Aquino Drive, Bacolod City, with an area of Two Thousand Fifty-Four square meters
(2,054 m2), more or less, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its
Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by
Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011), 15-240 (s. 2015) and 17-472 (s. 2017), and Resolution
No. 18-176 (s. 2018), as amended by Resolution No. 19-382 (s. 2019) of the PEZA Board
of Directors, as a Special Economic Zone (Information Technology Center), defined by
the following technical description:
