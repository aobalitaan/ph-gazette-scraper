MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 865

DECLARING SATURDAY, 07 DECEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 07 December 2019, Saturday, the Province of Compostela
Valley will conduct a plebiscite to ratify the renaming of the Province of Compostela
Valley to the Province of Davao de Oro, pursuant to Republic Act No. 11297; and

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Province of Compostela Valley
the opportunity to actively and fully participate in the plebiscite and exercise their
right to vote, it is but fitting and proper to declare 07 December 2019 a special (non-
working) day;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Saturday, 07 December 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th dayof Novemberin the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
