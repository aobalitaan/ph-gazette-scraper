MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1179

DECLARING AS OFFICIAL THE POPULATION COUNT OF THE
PHILIPPINES BY PROVINCE, CITY/MUNICIPALITY AND BARANGAY
BASED ON THE 2020 CENSUS OF POPULATION AND HOUSING
CONDUCTED BY THE PHILIPPINE STATISTICS AUTHORITY

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 10625 or the “Philippine Statistical Act of
2013,” provides that the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) shall be primarily
responsible for all national censuses and surveys, sectoral statistics, consolidation of
selected administrative recording systems, and compilation of national accounts;

WHEREAS, Section 6(b) of RA No. 10625 mandates the PSA to prepare and
conduct periodic censuses on population, housing, agriculture, fisheries, business,
industry and other sectors of the economy;

WHEREAS, Batas Pambansa (BP) Blg. 72 (s. 1980) provides that an integrated
national census of population and other related data shall be taken every ten (10)
years beginning the year 1980, with the first day of May of every regular census year
as the reference date;

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 352 (s. 1996) directs the conduct of a census
every ten (10) years primarily to update the population count at the national level down
to the barangay level;

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 928 (s. 2020) declared the month of May 2020
as “National Census Month’;

WHEREAS, the 2020 Census of Population and Housing was conducted by the
PSA in September 2020 with 01 May 2020 as reference date; and

WHEREAS, pursuant to Section 8 of BP Blg. 72, the final poulation count as
determined from the processed census returns shall be considered official for all
purposes only upon proclamation by the President;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, do hereby declare as official for all purposes the results of the 2020 Census of
Population, placing the total population of the Philippines as of 01 May 2020 at
109,035,343 persons, as shown in the “2020 Census of Population and Housing by
