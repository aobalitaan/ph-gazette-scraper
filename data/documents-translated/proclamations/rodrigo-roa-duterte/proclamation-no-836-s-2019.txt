MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 836

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF DINAGAT
ISLANDS

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2019, Friday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Dinagat Islands, will mark the celebration of its Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
15 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Dinagat Islands.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this7th day of November, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
