MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 423

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF ESCALANTE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 28 February 2018, marks the celebration of the
Charter Anniversary of the City of Escalante, Negros Occidental; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Escalante,
Negros Occidental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 28 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Escalante, Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
