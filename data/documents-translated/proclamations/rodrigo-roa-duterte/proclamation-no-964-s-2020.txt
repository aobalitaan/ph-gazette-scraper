MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 964

DECLARING SATURDAY, 20 JUNE 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 June 2020, Saturday the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 73" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 20 June 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. siacrmomar 9

Executive Secretary
