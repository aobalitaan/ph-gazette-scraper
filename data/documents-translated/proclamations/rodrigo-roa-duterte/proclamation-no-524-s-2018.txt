MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 524

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 04 JULY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CADIZ, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Cadiz, Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of
its 51°' Charter Anniversary on 04 July 2018, a Wednesday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Wednesday,
04 July 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd dayof June __, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority 6ffhe President:

AZ

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
