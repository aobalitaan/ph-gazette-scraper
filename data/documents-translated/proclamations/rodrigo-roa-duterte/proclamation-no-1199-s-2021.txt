MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1199

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO

WHEREAS, on 16 August 2021, Monday, the City of Tuguegarao will mark
the celebration of the Afi Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 16 August 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
