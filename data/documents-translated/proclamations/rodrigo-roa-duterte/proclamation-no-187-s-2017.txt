CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON A PARCEL
OF LAND LOCATED ALONG QUEZON AVENUE, BARANGAY
PALIGSAHAN, QUEZON CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY
CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY
REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise
known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by Republic Act
No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine
Economic Zone Authority, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building with a gross floor area of
Twelve Thousand square meters (12,000 m7), subject to the provisions of
Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations,
Resolution No. 00-411 (S. 2000) and Resolution No. 16-135 (S. 2016) of the Board
of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, as an Information

of land located along Quezon Avenue, Barangay Paligsahan, Quezon City,
