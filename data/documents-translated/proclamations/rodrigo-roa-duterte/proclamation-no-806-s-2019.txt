MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 806

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF POLOMOLOK, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 10 September 2019, Tuesday, the Municipality of Polomolok,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark its 62" Founding Anniversary and the 16"
F'lomlok Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Polomolok be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 10 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Polomolok, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this4th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
