MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 517

DECLARING THURSDAY, 21 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BINGAWAN, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bingawan, Iloilo, will mark the celebration of
its Founding Anniversary on 21 June 2018, a Thursday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bingawan, lloilo, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 21 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bingawan, lloilo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June ___, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
