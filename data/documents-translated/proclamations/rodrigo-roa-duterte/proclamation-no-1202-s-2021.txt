MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES *
PROCLAMATION NO. 1202

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LUCENA

WHEREAS, on 20 August 2021, Friday, the City of Lucena will mark the
celebration of its Charter Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lucena be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
20 August 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lucena.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th dayof August _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two' Thousand and Twenty One.
