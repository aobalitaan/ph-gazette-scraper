MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 473

DECLARING 25 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF PINAMALAYAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 25 April 2018, the Municipality of Pinamalayan,
Oriental Mindoro, marks the celebration of the Bahaghari Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pinamalayan, Oriental Mindoro, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate
in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 25 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pinamalayan, Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
