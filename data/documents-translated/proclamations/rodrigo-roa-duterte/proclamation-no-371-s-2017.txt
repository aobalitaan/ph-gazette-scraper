MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 371

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TACLOBAN

WHEREAS, the City of Tacloban marks the celebration of the anniversary of
its conversion into a highly urbanized city on Monday, 18 December 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacloban
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 18 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacloban.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

/“—— en
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
