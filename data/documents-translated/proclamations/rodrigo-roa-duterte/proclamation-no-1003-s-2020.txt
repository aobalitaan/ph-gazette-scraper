MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1003

DECLARING SATURDAY, 05 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS, PROVINCE OF SOUTH
COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 05 September 2020, Saturday, the City of General Santos,
Province of South Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 53 Inauguration Day
and the 22" Tuna Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 05 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of General
Santos, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of August , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
