MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 732

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 JUNE 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN

THE MUNICIPALITY OF~STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2019, Tuesday, the Municipality of Sia. Maria,

Province of Davao Occidental, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Maria be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies:

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Tuesday, 18 June 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta.
Maria, Province of Davao Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

“TA

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,
Executive Secretary
