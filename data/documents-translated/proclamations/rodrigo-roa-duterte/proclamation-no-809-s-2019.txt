MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 809

DECLARING THURSDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIQUIJOR, PROVINCE OF SIQUIJOR

WHEREAS, on 12 September 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Siquijor,
Province of Siquijor, will mark the celebration of its 47" anniversary as the capital of
the Province of Siquijor; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Siquijor be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, ‘Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 12 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Siquijor, Province of Siquijor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of Septembathe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
