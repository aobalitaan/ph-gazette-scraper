MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 69

DECLARING FRIDAY, 4 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN CARLOS, NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of San Carlos, Negros Occidental marks its
annual festival on Friday, 4 November 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San
Carlos, Negros Occidental be given full Opportunity fo celebrate and
Participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, Il, SALVADOR c, MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 4 November 2016, as a special (non-working) day in
the City of San Carlos, Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines fo be affixed,

Done in the City of Manila, this Sth day of October jn the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

ere
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
