MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 456

DECLARING SATURDAY, 05 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAPANGAN, PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 05 May 2018, the Municipality of Kapangan,
Benguet, marks the commemoration of the birth anniversary of Governor Bado
Dangwa;

WHEREAS, Bado Dangwa was born in Kapangan, Benguet on 05 May 1905,
served as First Lieutenant and Major in the Philippine Army during the Second World
War, and as governor of Benguet from 1953 to 1963;

WHEREAS, Bado Dangwa focused his crusade against corruption,
encouraged education through scholarships, and reinforced the identity of the
indigenous people of Benguet; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kapangan, Benguet, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Saturday,
05 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kapangan,
Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this27th day of March | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
