MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 430

DECLARING THURSDAY, 29 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAMBUSAO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Mambusao, Capiz will celebrate its Founding
Anniversary on Thursday, 29 March 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mambusao, Capiz, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 29 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mambusao, Capiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this19th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Cex
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary 4
