MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1075

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 18 January 2021, Monday, the City of Santa Rosa, Province
of Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 229" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 18 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
