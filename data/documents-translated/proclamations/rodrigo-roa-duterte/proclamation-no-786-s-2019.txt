MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 786

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF VIGA, PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, on 15 August 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Viga, Province
of Catanduanes, will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Viga; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Viga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 15 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Viga,
Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof Augustin the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.
