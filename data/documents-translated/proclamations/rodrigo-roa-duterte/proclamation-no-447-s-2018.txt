MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 447

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 04 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, the City of Antipolo marks the celebration of its 20"" Cityhood
Anniversary on Wednesday, 04 April 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Antipolo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 04 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Antipolo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
