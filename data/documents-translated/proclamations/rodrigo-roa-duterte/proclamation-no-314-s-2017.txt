MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 314

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, 17 September 2017 marks the 65" Provincial Anniversary of the
Province of Zamboanga del Sur;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Zamboanga del Sur, to
celebrate the occasion, the Provincial Government of Zamboanga del Sur requested
that Monday, 18 September 2017 be declared as a special non-working day, instead
of 17 September 2017, which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province
Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 18 September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thday of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
