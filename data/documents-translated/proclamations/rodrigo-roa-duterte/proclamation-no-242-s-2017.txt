MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 242

DECLARING SATURDAY, 01 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAMAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Saturday, 01 July 2017, marks the 61° Foundation Day of the
Municipality of Maramag, Province of Bukidnon; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maramag, Bukidnon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 01 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Maramag, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19¢hday of June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
