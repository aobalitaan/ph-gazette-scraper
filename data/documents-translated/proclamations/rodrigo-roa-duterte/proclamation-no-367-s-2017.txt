MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.367

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Province of Batangas marks the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary on Friday, 08 December 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
08 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this28th day of November _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
