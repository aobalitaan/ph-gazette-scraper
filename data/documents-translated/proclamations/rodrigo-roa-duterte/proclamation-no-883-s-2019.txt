MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 883

DECLARING SATURDAY, 25 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE

WHEREAS, on 25 January 2020, Saturday, the Municipality of Carigara,
Province of Leyte, will mark its 449" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Carigara be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 25 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Carigara, Province of Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
