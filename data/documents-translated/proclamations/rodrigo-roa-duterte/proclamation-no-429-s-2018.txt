MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 429

DECLARING -THURSDAY, 22 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 22 March 2018, the Municipality of Baliwag,
Bulacan marks the 155" birth anniversary of Mariano Ponce;

WHEREAS, Mariano Ponce, a native of Baliwag, Bulacan, was a prominent
member of the propaganda movement and one. of the leading figures in the
Philippine reform movement during the Spanish colonization; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baliwag, Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Baliwag, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of February jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
