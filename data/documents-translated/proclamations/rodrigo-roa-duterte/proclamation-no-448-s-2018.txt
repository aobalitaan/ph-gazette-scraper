MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 448

DECLARING THURSDAY, 05 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, Thursday, 05 April 2018, marks the celebration of the 438"
Founding Anniversary of the Province of Pangasinan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Pangasinan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, OS April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

.

forme te
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
