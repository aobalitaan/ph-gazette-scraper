MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 676

DECLARING THURSDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TANGUB, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 28 February 2019, Thursday, the City of Tangub, Province of
Misamis Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 51*' Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 28 February 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tangub,
Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
