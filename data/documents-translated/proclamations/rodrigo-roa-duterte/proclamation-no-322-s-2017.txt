MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 322

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 04 OCTOBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INFANTA, PROVINCE OF
PANGASINAN

WHEREAS. Wednesday, 04 October 2017, marks the Foundation Day of the
Municipality of Infanta, Pangasinan, and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Infanta, Pangasinan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 04 October 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Infanta, Pangasinan

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this3xrd_ day of October _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
