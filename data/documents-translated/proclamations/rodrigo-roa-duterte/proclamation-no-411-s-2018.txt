MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 411

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAGO, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on Monday, 19 February 2018, the City of Bago, Negros
Occidental, marks the celebration of its 52" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bago be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bago.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this23rd day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
