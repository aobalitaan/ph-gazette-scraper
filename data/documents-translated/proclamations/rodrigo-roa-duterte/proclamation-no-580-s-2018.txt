MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. °80

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF
BILIRAN

WHEREAS, on 26 September 2018, a Wednesday, the Municipality of Naval,
Province of Biliran, will mark its 158" Pueblo Day (Charter Day); and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Naval be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 26 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Naval, Province of Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this10th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

(im
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
