MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 337

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Province of Occidental Mindoro marks the celebration of its
67" Founding Anniversary on Wednesday, 15 November 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Occidental Mindoro, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies:

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Occidental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF-, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
