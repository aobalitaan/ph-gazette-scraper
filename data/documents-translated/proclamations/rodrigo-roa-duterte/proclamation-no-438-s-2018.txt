MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 438

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TANGUB

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 28 February 2018, marks the celebration of the 60"
Charter Anniversary of the City of Tangub, Misamis Occidental; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies:

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 28 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Tangub.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
