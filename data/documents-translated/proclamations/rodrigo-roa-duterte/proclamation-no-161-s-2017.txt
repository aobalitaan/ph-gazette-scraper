MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 161

DECLARING FRIDAY, 31 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE

WHEREAS, the first mass in the Philippines was celebrated in the Island of
Limasawa, Province of Southern Leyte on 31 March 1521;

WHEREAS, Friday, 31 March 2017, marks the commemoration of the First
Mass in the Philippines in Limasawa Island, Southern Leyte;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Southern Leyte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
31 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Southern Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
