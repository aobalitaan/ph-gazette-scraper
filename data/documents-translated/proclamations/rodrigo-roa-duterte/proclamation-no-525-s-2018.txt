MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 525

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 JULY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 10 July 2018, a Tuesday, the City of Santa Rosa, Laguna,
will mark the 14" anniversary of its conversion to a component city; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa, Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Tuesday,
10 July 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority e President:

Se

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
