MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 1144

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 MAY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF MALABON

WHEREAS, on 21 May 2021, Friday, the City of Malabon will mark the
celebration of its 4224 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Malabon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 May 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Malabon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14thdayof May: , inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
