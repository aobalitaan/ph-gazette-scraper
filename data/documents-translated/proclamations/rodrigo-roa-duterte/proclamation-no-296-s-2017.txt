MALACANAN PALACE

MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 296

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 30 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MANDAUE

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 30 August 2017, the City of Mandaue marks the
celebration of its 48" Charter Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mandaue,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 30 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Mandaue.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
