MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1116

DECLARING FRIDAY, 19 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CANLAON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2021, Friday, the City of Canlaon, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of the Pasayaw Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Canlaon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday, .
19 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Canlaon, Province of
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
