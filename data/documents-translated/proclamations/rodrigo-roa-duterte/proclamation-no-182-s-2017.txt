MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 182

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CANLAON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Canlaon, Negros Oriental, marks the annual
celebration of the “Pasayaw Festival” every 19" of March;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Canlaon, to fully celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Canlaon requested that Monday, 20
March 2017 be declared as a special non-working day instead of 19 March 2017

which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Canlaon,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 20 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Canlaon,

Negros Oriental.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
Done in the City of Manila, this 16th — day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
