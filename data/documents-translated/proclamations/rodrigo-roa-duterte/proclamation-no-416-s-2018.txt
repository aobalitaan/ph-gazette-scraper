MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 416

DECLARING SATURDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF LAOAG

WHEREAS. on Saturday, 10 February 2018, the City of Laoag, Ilocos Norte
marks the celebration of the Pamulinawen Festival. and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate

ceremonies,

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 10 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this2"4 day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
