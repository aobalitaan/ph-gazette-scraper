MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 202

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG

WHEREAS, the City of Gingoog marks the celebration of the 8” Lubi-Lubi
Festival on Monday, 22 May 2017:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog

be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 22 May 2017, asa special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed,

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of April , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen,

By authority of the President:

rae
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
