MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 756

DECLARING MONDAY, 01 JULY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAMAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Maramag,
Province of Bukidnon, will mark the celebration of its 63° Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maramag be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Monday, 01 July 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maramag,
Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
