MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 113

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 4 JANUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE
OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Naujan, Oriental Mindoro marks the
celebration of its 112"" Founding Anniversary on Wednesday, 4 January 2017;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Naujan, Oriental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate
and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Wednesday, 4 January 2017, as a special (non-working) day
in the Municipality of Naujan, Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
* Done in the City of Manila, this 12thday of December, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
