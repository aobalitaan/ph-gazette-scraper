MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1247

CONVERSION OF THE CITY OF PAGADIAN, ZAMBOANGA DEL
SUR INTO A HIGHLY-URBANIZED CITY

WHEREAS, Section 452 of Republic Act (RA) No. 7160 or the “Local
Government Code of 1991,” provides that cities with a minimum population of two
hundred thousand (200,000) inhabitants as certified by the Philippine Statistics
Authority (PSA), and with a latest annual income of at least PhP50 Million based
on 1991 constant prices, as certified by the City Treasurer, shall be classified as
highly-urbanized cities;

WHEREAS, Section 453 of RA No. 7160 and its Implementing Rules and
Regulations provide that it shall be the duty of the President to declare a city as
highly-urbanized within thirty (30) days after it shall have met the minimum
requirements prescribed in Section 452 of the same Code, upon proper application
therefor, and the Commission on Elections (COMELEC), within 120 days
thereafter, shall conduct a plebiscite in the city proposed to be converted, where
the qualified voters therein shall ratify the aforementioned declaration;

WHEREAS, the plebiscite shall be preceded by a comprehensive
information campaign to be conducted by the COMELEC with the assistance of
national and local government officials, media, non-governmental organizations
and other interested parties;

WHEREAS, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Pagadian, through Resolution
No. 2020-0586, requested the President to declare the City of Pagadian,
Zamboanga del Sur as a highly-urbanized city;

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian, Zamboanga del Sur, has met the
minimum requirements prescribed under Section 452 of RA No. 7160 for a city to
be classified as highly-urbanized, by having a population of 210,452 persons, as
certified by the PSA, and an income of PhP270.774 Million, based on 1991 constant
prices, as certified by the Department of Finance - Bureau of Local Government
Finance; and

WHEREAS, it is a declared policy of the government to support initiatives of

local governments to attain their fullest development as self-reliant communities,
and make them effective partners in the attainment of national goals;

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the
Constitution and existing laws, do hereby proclaim the City of Pagadian as a
highly-urbanized city, provided that this Proclamation shall take effect only upon
ratification in a plebiscite by the qualified voters therein, as provided for in Section
453 of RA No. 7160.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 8th day of November , in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-One.
