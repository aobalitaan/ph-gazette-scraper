MALACANANG
Manila

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 331

DECLARING TUESDAY, 07 NOVEMBER 2017,

AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUENC

A, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 07 November 2017, marks the 141° Founding
Anniversary of the Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas: and

- WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Cuenca, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Tuesday, 07 November 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Cuenca, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of October | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
