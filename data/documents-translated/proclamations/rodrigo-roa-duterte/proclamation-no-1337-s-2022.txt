PROCLAMATION NO. 1337

DECLARING TUESDAY, 26 APRIL 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 26 April 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 257 Founding Anniversary;
and

* WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government; ,

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 26 April 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5ta day of April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
