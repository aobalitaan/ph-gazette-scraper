MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
. PROCLAMATION NO. 151

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of Bayawan will be celebrating the Tawo-Tawo Festival
Week on the third week of February;

WHEREAS, the Tawo-Tawo Festival reflects the culture of the City of
Bayawan being a ricé granary of the southern part of the Province of Negros

Oriental;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Bayawan celebrate and
participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Bayawan requested that
17 February 2017 be declared as a special non-working day, in celebration of the
Tawo-Tawo Festival.

NOW, THEREFORE, 1, MENARDO I. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Friday, 17 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Bayawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
ans

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary .
