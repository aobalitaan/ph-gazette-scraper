MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 625

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF DASMARINAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, on 26 November 2018, a Monday, the City of Dasmarinas,
Province of Cavite, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Dasmarifias be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 26 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Dasmarinas,
Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this!3th day of November jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
