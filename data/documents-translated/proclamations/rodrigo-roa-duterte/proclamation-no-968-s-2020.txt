MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 968

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONCADA, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 01 July 2020, Wednesday, the Municipality of Moncada,
Province of Tarlac, will mark the celebration of its 145" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Moncada be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 01 July 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Moncada, Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th qayof June | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
