MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.384

DECLARING FRIDAY, 05 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUDIN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur will celebrate
its 432™ Founding Anniversary on Friday, 05 January 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur be given full opportunity to participate and celebrate
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
05 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tagudin,
Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

~_
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary /?
