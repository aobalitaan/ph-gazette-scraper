MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 990

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 AUGUST 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 10 August 2020, Monday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon, will mark the celebration of Lorenzo Tafada, Sr. Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca, Quezon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social
distancing, and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 10 August 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

lie ba

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
