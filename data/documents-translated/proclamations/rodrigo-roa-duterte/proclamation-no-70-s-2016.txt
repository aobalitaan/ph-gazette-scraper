MALACAIVAN PALACE 
MANILA 
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES 
PROCLAMATION NO. 70 
DECLARING FRIDAY, 4 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON 
WHEREAS, on Friday, 4 November 2016, the Province of Quezon 
marks the death anniversary of its local hero, Apolinario dela Cruz, also 
known as Hermano Puli; 
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of 
Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion 
with appropriate ceremonies. 
NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive 
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do 
hereby declare Friday, 4 November 2016, as a special (non-working) day in 
the Province of Quezon. 
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the 
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. 
Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of October, in the year of 
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen. 
By authority of the President; 
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA 
/ V Executive Secretary
