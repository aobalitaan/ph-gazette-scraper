MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE  PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.1o63
DECLARING WEDNESDAY,  23  DECEMBER 2020,  A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY     IN     THE      MUNICIPALITY     0F     SINDANGAN,      PROVINCE      OF
ZAMB0ANGA DEL NORTE
WHEREAS,    on    23    December    2020,    Wednesday,    the    Municipality    of
Sindangan,   Province   of  Zamboanga   del   Norte,   will   mark   the   celebration   of   its
Founding Anniversary; and
WHEREAS,  it  is  but  fitting  and  proper that the  people  of the  Municipality  of
Sindangan  be given full opportiinity to celebrate and  participate  in the occasion with
appropriate  ceremonies,  subject  to  community  quarantine,  social  distancing,  and
other public health measures;
NOW,  THEREFORE,  I,  SALVADOR  C.  MEDIALDEA,  Executive  Secretary,
by   authority  of  the   President,   RODRIGO   ROA   DUTERTE,   do   hereby   declare
Wednesday,  23  December 2020,  a  special  (non-working)  day  in  the  Municipality of
Sindangan,  Province of Zamboanga del Norte.
IN WITNESS WHEREOF,  I  have  hereunto set my hand  and  caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
Done  in  the  City of Manila,  this  21st    day of  December,  in  the  year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
By authority of the President:
