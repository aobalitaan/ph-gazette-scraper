MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 519

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF SOCORRO, ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 22 June 2018, a Friday, the Municipality of Socorro, Oriental
Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Socorro, Oriental Mindoro, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday, 22
June 2018, a special (non-working) day.in the Municipality of Socorro, Oriental
Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authcrity of the President:

nH

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
