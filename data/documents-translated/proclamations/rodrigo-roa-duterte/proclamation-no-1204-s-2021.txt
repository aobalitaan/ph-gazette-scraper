MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1204*

DECLARING FRIDAY, 27 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MINALIN, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 27 August 2021, Friday, the Municipality of Minalin, Province
of Pampanga, will mark the celebration of its 407" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Minalin be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
27 August 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Minalin, Province
of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, 23rd dayof August __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
