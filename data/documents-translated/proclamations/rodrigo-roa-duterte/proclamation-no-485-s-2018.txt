MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 485

DECLARING TUESDAY, 15 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 15 May 2018, the Municipality of Gumaca, Quezon,
will mark the celebration of the Araw ng mga Magsasaka at Arafia at Baluarte
Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca, Quezon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 15 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
