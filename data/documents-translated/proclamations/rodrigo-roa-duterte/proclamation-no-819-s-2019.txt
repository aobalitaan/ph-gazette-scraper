MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 819

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 02 OCTOBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOLANO, PROVINCE OF NUEVA
VIZCAYA

WHEREAS, on 02 October 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of Solano,
Province of Nueva Vizcaya, will mark the celebration of Solano Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Solano be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 02 October 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Solano, Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thday of September, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
