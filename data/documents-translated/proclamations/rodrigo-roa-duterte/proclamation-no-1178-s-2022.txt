MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. i178

DECLARING SATURDAY, 10 JULY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 10 July 2021, Saturday, the City of Santa Rosa, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 17° Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa
Rosa be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Saturday, 10 July 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
