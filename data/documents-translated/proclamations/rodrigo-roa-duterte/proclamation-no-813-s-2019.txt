MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 813

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN
KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 18 September 2019, Wednesday, the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat, will mark its 19‘ Cityhood Anniversary and the
Talakudong Festival 2019; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 18 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Tacurong, Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
