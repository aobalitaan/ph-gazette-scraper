MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1008

DECLARING THURSDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CULION, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN

WHEREAS, on 10 September 2020, Thursday, the Municipality of Culion,
Province of Palawan, will mark the commemoration of Father Javier M. Olazabal
Memorial Day;

WHEREAS, Father Javier M. Olazabal, in his eighteen (18) years as Chaplain
of Culion, has served leprosy patients in the former Culion Leper Colony and
contributed immensely to the development of the local community; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Culion be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 10 September 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Culion, Province of Palawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of September, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
By authority of the President:

ir, ADOR C. ee

Executive Secretary
