MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 898

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, on 10 February 2020, Monday, the City of Laoag, Province of
Ilocos Norte, will mark the celebration of the Pamulinawen Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 10 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag,
Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
