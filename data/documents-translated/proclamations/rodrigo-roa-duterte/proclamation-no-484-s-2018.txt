MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 484

DECLARING FRIDAY, 11 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF APARRI, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS, on Friday, 11 May 2018, the Municipality of Aparri, Cagayan, will
mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary, and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aparri, Cagayan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies,

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
11 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Aparri, Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Oe
SALWADOR C. MEDIALDEA
