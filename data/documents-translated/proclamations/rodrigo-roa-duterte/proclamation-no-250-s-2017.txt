MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 2 50

DECLARING FRIDAY, 14

JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GUIHULNGAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Friday, 14 July 2017, marks the Charter Day of the City of
Guihulngan, Negros Oriental: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of

Guihulngan, Negros Oriental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,

14 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihuingan, Negros
Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
