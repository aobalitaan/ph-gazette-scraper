MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 440

DECLARING FRIDAY, 09 MARCH 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUKO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain Province, marks the 12”
Begnas di Bauko Festival on 08 March 2018 to 10 March 2018;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain
Province, to fully celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that
Friday, 09 March 2018, be declared as a special non-working day in the Municipality
of Bauko, Mountain Province; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauko, Mountain Province, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday, 09
March 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauko, Mountain
Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of March _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Genonc Nee Ye
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE,

Executive Secretary
