MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1049

DECLARING THURSDAY, 10 DECEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MEYCAUAYAN, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 10 December 2020, Thursday, the City of Meycauayan,
Province of Bulacan, will mark the celebration of its Araw ng Lungsod ng
Meycauayan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Meycauayan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing,
and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 10 December 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of
Meycauayan, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17thday of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
By authority of the President:
