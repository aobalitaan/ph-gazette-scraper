MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 677
DECLARING 24 FEBRUARY 2019 AS A DAY OF NATIONAL MOURNING

WHEREAS, the passing of renowned National Artist for Architecture Francisco
“Bobby” Mafiosa is a great loss to our nation;

WHEREAS, his passion and dedication for the arts shall always be an
inspiration to the Filipino people; and

WHEREAS, the nation is in solidarity with and joins in the grieving of his family
and friends;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and laws, do
hereby proclaim 24 February 2019 as a day of national mourning for National Artist
Francisco “Bobby” Mafosa.

| instruct all government offices, schools and educational institutions, and military
installations in the Philippines to lower the Philippine flag to half-mast on 24 February
2019.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila this 22nday of February, in the Year of our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

 

By the President:

we

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
