MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 415

DECLARING FRIDAY, 02 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CABADBARAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on Friday, 02 February 2018, the City of Cabadbaran, Agusan
de! Norte, marks the celebration of its Dagkot Festival. and

WHEREAS, it ts but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Cabadbaran be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies:

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
02 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabadbaran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd = day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
