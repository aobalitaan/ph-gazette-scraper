MALACANAN PALACE

MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 287

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, the City of Kidapawan marks the celebration of its 70"
Foundation Day on Friday, 18 August 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Kidapawan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Friday, 18 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Kidapawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15thday of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

weitess 1. GUEVARRA

Acting Executive Secretary
