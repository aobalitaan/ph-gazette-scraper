MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 431

DECLARING SATURDAY, 26 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, Saturday, 26 May 2018, marks the celebration of the Charter
Anniversary of the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baliwag, Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 26 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Baliwag, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of February , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
