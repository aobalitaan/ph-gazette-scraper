MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 143

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PADRE BURGOS, PROVINCE OF
QUEZON

WHEREAS, on Friday, 17 February 2017, the Municipality of Padre Burgos,
Quezon marks the celebration of Padre Burgos Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Padre Burgos, Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
17 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Padre
Burgos, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
