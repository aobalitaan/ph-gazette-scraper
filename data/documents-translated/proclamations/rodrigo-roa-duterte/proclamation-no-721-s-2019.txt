MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 721

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF SUAL, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, on 20 May 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Sual, Province of
Pangasinan, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sual be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies ;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 20 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sual,
Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
