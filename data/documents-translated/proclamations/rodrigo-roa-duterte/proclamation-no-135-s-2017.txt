. MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 135

DECLARING FRIDAY, 3 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on Friday, 3 February 2017, the Province of Bulacan
marks the birth anniversary of Blas F. Ople;

WHEREAS, Blas F. Ople, who served as Secretary of Foreign Affairs,
Senate President and Secretary of Labor, devoted decades of his life to
selfless and dedicated service to our country and our people;

WHEREAS, Bias F. Ople, a son of Bulacan, is a model citizen and
public officer to the Filipino people, especially to the “Bulakenyos”,

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Friday, 3 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the
Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thday of January _, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
