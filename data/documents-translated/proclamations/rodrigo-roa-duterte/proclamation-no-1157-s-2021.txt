MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1157

DECLARING THURSDAY, 17 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MACO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DE ORO

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2021, Thursday, the Municipality of Maco, Province
of Davao de Oro, will mark the celebration of its 54" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maco be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 17 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maco,
Province of Davao de Oro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof June _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
