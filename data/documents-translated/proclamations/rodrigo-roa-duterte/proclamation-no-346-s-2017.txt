MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 346

DECLARING TUESDAY, 05 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS. on Tuesday, 05 December 2017, the City of Palayan, Nueva
Ecija, marks the celebration of its 52’ Foundation Day; and

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Palayan,
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, , SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 05 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan,
Nueva Ecija

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th gay of November | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
