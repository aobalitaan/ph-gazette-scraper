MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.°5?9

DECLARING THURSDAY, 30 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF MANDAUE, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2018, a Thursday, the City of Mandaue, Province
of Cebu, will mark the celebration of its 49" Charter Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mandaue
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 30 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Mandaue,
Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

c
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary 6G

Office of the President
