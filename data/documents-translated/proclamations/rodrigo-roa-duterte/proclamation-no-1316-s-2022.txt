MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1316

DECLARING THE THIRD WEEK OF MARCH OF EVERY YEAR AS
“OCCUPATIONAL MEDICINE WEEK”

WHEREAS, Section 18, Article |l of the Constitution declares that the State
affirms labor as a primary social economic force, and shall protect and promote the
rights of workers and promote their welfare;

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 11508 or the “Occupational Safety and Health
Standards Act,” provides that the State shall promote strict but dynamic, inclusive and
gender-sensitive measures in the formulation of policies and programs related to
occupational safety and health; ;

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 307 (s. 1987) established the Occupational
Safety and Health Center in the Employees’ Compensation Commission of the
Department of Labor and Employment, with the main function of planning, developing
and implementing programs in the field of occupational safety and health, to
significantly enhance the productivity of industries, and promote the general well-being
and quality of life of Filipino workers;

WHEREAS, Occupational Health, as defined by the World Health Organization,
is a multi-disciplinary activity aimed at the protection and promotion of workers by
preventing and controlling occupational diseases and accidents;

WHEREAS, Occupational Medicine, being one of the fundamental disciplines of
Occupational Health, deals with the assessment of workers’ health, working conditions
and processes, and management of individual cases in the context of working ability
and production;

WHEREAS, the latest available data from the Integrated Survey on Labor and
Employment of the Philippine Statistics Authority reflects a total of 38,235 occupational
accidents, 46,283 cases of occupational injuries, and 101,851 cases of occupational
diseases in 2017 alone; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to promote awareness of the strategies to prevent
and control occupational injuries and diseases to reduce work-related disabilities and
deaths in the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of
the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing
laws, do hereby declare the third week of March of every year as “Occupational
Medicine Week.”

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

The Occupational Safety and Health Center (OSHC) in the Employees’
Compensation Commission of the Department of Labor and Employment, in
coordination with relevant non-government organizations and __ professional
associations, ‘is directed to promote the observance of the Occupational Medicine
Week, and identify the programs, projects and activities for the yearly celebration
thereof.

All other agencies and instrumentalities of the National Government, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations and state universities and colleges, are
hereby enjoined, and all local government units, as well as the private sector, are
hereby encouraged, to actively participate and render the necessary support and
assistance to the OSHC for the effective implementation of this Proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof march , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Two.
