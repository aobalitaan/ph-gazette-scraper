MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.277

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN PASCUAL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Friday, 04 August 2017, marks the 48" Foundation Day of the
Municipality of San Pascual, Batangas; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Pascual, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
04 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Pascual,
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
