MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1222

DECLARING THURSDAY, 30 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MASBATE, PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, on 30 September 2021, Thursday, the City of Masbate, Province
of Masbate, will mark the celebration of its 21st Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Masbate
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 30 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Masbate,
Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
