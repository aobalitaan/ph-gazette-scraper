MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA.

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1140

DECLARING FRIDAY, 07 MAY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 07 May 2021, Friday, the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 818 Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
07 May 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28thday of April ,in the year of Our Lord, .
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

ou
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
