MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 648

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS, on 16 January 2019, Wednesday, the City of Navotas will mark
the celebration of its 113" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Navotas
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 16 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority gf te President:

KRzivaoor C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
