MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 859

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SINDANGAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA
DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on 23 December 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Sindangan,
Province of Zamboanga del Norte, will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sindangan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Monday, 23 December 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Sindangan, Province of Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19thday of Novembeyp the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
