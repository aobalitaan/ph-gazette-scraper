MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 584

DECLARING SATURDAY, 29 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IROSIN, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON

WHEREAS, on 29 September 2018, a Saturday, the Municipality of lrosin,
Province of Sorsogon, will mark the celebration of the Paray Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Irosin be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 29 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
lrosin, Province of Sorsogon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of September, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

as
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
