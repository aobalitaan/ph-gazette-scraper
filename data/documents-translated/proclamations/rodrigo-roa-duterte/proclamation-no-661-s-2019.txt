Done in the City of Manila, this 6th 
day of February, 
in the year of Our

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal 
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. 
NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary, 
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare 
Monday, 11 February 2019, a special 
(non-working) 
day in the City of Talisay, 
Province of Negros Occidental. 
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Talisay be 
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate 
ceremonies; 
WHEREAS, on 11 February 2019, Monday, the City of Talisay, Province of 
Negros Occidental, will mark its 2151 Charter Anniversary; and 
DECLARING MONDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY 
IN THE CITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL
