MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 172

DECLARING THE 38° WEEK OF FEBRUARY 2017 AS
“PHILIPPINE INNOVATION WEEK”

WHEREAS, a series of notable conferences, fora, and other events
which are scheduled on the third week of February 2017 have been organized
upon the initiative of the Benita and Catalino Yap Foundation, with the aim of
recognizing and stimulating worthy innovations in the areas of government
service, small and medium enterprises, technical-vocational education, and

agri-business,

WHEREAS, it is a policy of the government to give priority to research
and development, invention, innovation, and their utilization; to science and
technology education, training, and services; and to the support of indigenous,
appropriate, and self-reliant scientific and technological capabilities, and their
application to the country’s productive systems and national life;

WHEREAS, the 10-point socioeconomic agenda of this Administration
includes increasing competitiveness and the ease of doing business, and the
promotion of rural and value chain development towards increasing agricultural

and rural enterprise activity;

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus national attention on the significance
of innovation and its benefits to society;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby proclaim
the 3 week of February 2017 as “Philippine Innovation Week”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
