MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1237

DECLARING TUESDAY, 09 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 09 November 2021, Tuesday, the Province of Eastern Samar
will mark the celebration of its 56" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Eastern Samar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing,
and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the Presidenti RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 09 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Eastern
Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

be
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
