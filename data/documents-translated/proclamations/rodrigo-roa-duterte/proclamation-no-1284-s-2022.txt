MANILA.

DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED IN BARANGAYS
BULIHAN, POBLACION, SAN PEDRO EAST AND SAN PEDRO WEST,
MALVAR, BATANGAS, FOR INCLUSION INTO THE EXISTING LIGHT
INDUSTRY & SCIENCE PARK IV SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE,
PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT (RA) NO. 7916, AS AMENDED

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916,
otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended, and
upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone
Authority (PEZA), |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the
Philippines, do hereby designate several parcels of land located in Barangays
Bulihan, Poblacion, San Pedro East and San Pedro West, Malvar, Batangas, for
inclusion into the existing Light Industry & Science Park IV Special Economic Zone,
subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and
Regulations, and PEZA Board of Directors Resolution No. 20-125 (s. 2020), with an
aggregate area of Five Hundred Forty-Eight Thousand Seven Hundred Eighty-
Nine Square Meters (548,789 m7), more or less, as defined by the following
technical description:
