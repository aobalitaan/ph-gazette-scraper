MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

, PROCLAMATION NO. 1188

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 JULY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS .

WHEREAS, on 22 July 2021, Thursday, the City of General Santos will mark
the celebration of its Indigenous Peoples 6" Blaan Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 July 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of General Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

&
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary (*
