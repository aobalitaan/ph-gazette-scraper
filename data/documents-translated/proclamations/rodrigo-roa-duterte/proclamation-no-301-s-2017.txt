MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 301

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-

WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGRAS, PROVINCE OF
ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS. Josefa Lianes Escoda, a World War II Heroine and the Founder

of the Girl Scouts of the Philippines, was born on 20 September 1898 in Dingras,
Ilocos Norte;

WHEREAS. the Municipality of Dingras, Ilocos Norte marks the birth
anniversary of Josefa Llanes Escoda on Wednesday, 20 September 2017: and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dingras, Ilocos Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 20 September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality
of Dingras, Province of Ilocos Norte

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of Septembethe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
