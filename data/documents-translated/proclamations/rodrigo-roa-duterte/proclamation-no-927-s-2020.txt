MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 927

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 MARCH 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 21 March 2020, Saturday, the City of Calapan, Province of
Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its 22"? Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper, that the people of the City of Calapan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 March 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calapan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th dayof March __, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
