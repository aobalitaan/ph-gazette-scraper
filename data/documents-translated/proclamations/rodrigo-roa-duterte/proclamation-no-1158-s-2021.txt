MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1158

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
CITY OF LAOAG, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, the City of Laoag, Province of Ilocos Norte, will mark the
celebration of its Charter Anniversary on 19 June 2021:

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Laoag to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 18 June 2021, Friday, be declared a
special (non-working) day in lieu of 19 June 2021, which falls on a Saturday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Laoag be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
18 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Laoag, Province of Ilocos
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of June | in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
