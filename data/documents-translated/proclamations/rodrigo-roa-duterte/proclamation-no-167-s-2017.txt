MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 167

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF SURIGAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on Monday, 15 May 2017, the Province of Surigao del Norte
marks the celebration of its “Adlaw Nan Probinsya Nan Surigao Del Norte’,

WHEREAS. it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Surigao del Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 15 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Surigao del
Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this24th day of February _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.
