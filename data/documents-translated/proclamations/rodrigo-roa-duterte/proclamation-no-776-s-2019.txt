MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 776

DECLARING THURSDAY, 01 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAPALONG, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL
NORTE

WHEREAS, on 01 August 2019, Thursday, the Municipality of Kapalong,
Province of Davao del Norte, will mark the celebration of its 71%’ Founding
Anniversary; and :

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kapalong be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 01 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Kapalong, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

cn
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
