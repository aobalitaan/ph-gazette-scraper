PROCLAMATION NO. 110

 

DESIGNATING A. BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON SEVERAL PARCELS OF
LAND LOCATED IN BARANGAY ALANG-ALANG, MANDAUE CITY, CEBU
AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO
REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise
known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No.
8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine
Economic Zone Authority, |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by the virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby
designate a building with a gross floor area of 17,979.20 square meters, subject to
the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and
Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (S. 2000) and Resolution No. 14-485 (S. 2014)
of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, as an
Information Technology Center to be known as The North Park, which stands on
several parcels of land located at Barangay Alang-Alang, Mandaue City, Cebu
containing an aggregate area of Three Thousand Eight Hundred Fifty Four
(3,854) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical
descriptions:
