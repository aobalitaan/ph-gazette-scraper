MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 582

DECLARING THURSDAY, 27 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOBO, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 27 September 2018, a Thursday, the Municipality of Lobo,
Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 147" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Lobo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 27 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Lobo, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10thday ofSeptember , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
