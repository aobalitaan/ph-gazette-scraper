MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 811

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 17 September 2019, Tuesday, the Province of Zamboanga
del Sur will mark the celebration of its 67!" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 17 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1Othday ofSeptembein the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
