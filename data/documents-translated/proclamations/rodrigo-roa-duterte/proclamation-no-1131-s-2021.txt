MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1131

DECLARING MONDAY, 19 APRIL 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 19 April 2021, Monday, the City of Tarlac will mark the
celebration of its 23" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 19 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

~”
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
Executive Secretary
