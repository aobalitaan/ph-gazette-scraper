MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PPESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 271

DECLARING TUESDAY, 01 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAPALONG, DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS. Tuesday, 01 August 2017, marks the celebration of the 69"
Foundation Day of the Municipality of Kapalong, Davao del Norte; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kapalong, Davao del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in

the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.
NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 01 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of

Kapalong, Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

—<

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
