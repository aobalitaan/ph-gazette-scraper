MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1213

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-
_ WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, on 15 September 2021, Wednesday, the Province of Bulacan will
mark the celebration of the 123% anniversary of the inauguration of the Malolos
Congress; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Bulacan. ¢

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of September, jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
