MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 470

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Saturday, 21 April 2018, marks the 17" Cityhood Anniversary of
the City of Calamba, Laguna; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba,
Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City-of Calamba,
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th —_ day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
