MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 653

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 21 January 2019, Monday, the City of Pagadian, Province of
Zamboanga del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 19"" Pasalamat Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
