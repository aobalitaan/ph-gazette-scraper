PROCLAMATION NO. >43

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 AUGUST 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAPALONG, DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, 01 August 2018, a Wednesday, will mark the celebration of the
70" Founding Anniversary of the Municipality of Kapalong, Davao del Norte; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kapalong, Davao del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 01 August 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Kapalong, Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority o President:

a

MICHAEL P. ONG
