MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 214

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIM, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 21 June 2017, the Municipality of Maasim,
Sarangani marks the celebration of its 48" Founding Anniversary and 9" Kestebeng

Festival:
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of

Maasim, Sarangani, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Maasim, Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19thday of May _ , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

.
