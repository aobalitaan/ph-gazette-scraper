MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1256

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 DECEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF M’LANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 03 December 2021, Friday, the Municipality of M’lang,
Province of Cotabato, will mark the celebration of the Kawayanan Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
M'lang be given full opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
03 December 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of M'lang,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority pf the President:

‘Vriupolee Bu

ALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
