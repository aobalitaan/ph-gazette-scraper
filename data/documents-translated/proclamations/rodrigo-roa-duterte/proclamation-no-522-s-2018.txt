MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 522

DECLARING THURSDAY, 21 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BAYUGAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Bayugan, Agusan del Sur, will mark the celebration of
its 11" Charter Anniversary on 21 June 2018, a Thursday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bayugan,
Agusan del Sur, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Thursday,
21 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayugan, Agusan del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th — day of June, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of thé President:

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
