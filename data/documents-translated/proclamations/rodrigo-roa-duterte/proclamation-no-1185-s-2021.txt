MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1185
DECLARING 07 TO 12 JULY 2021 AS DAYS OF NATIONAL MOURNING

WHEREAS, on 04 July 2021, a Philippine Air Force C-130 aircraft crash landed in
Jolo Airport, Sitio Amman, Barangay Bangkal, Patikul, Sulu, causing the demise of several
military personnel and civilians;

WHEREAS, these deaths constitute a great tragedy to our nation, and during this
time of grief, a most solemn and respectful commemoration of the benevolent service and
heroic acts of our military personnel is called for;

WHEREAS, the nation mourns and joins the families and friends of our fallen brothers
and sisters in their moment of sorrow; and

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 8491, or the “Flag and Heraldic Code of the
Philippines,” provides that the Philippine Flag shall be flown at haif-mast as a sign of
mourning on all buildings and places where it is displayed, during tragedies of national
significance as ordeted by the Office of the President;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, do
hereby declare 07 to 12 July 2021 as Days of National Mourning, in solidarity with the
bereaved families of the military personnel and civilians who perished in the crash landing of
a Philippine Air Force C-130 aircraft in Sulu on 04 July 2021.

During the said period, the national flag shall be flown at half-mast on all government
buildings and military installations throughout the Philippines.

The entire nation is requested to offer prayers for the eternal repose of the souls of
the victims of this tragic incident.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the
Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof July, inthe year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Twenty-One.
