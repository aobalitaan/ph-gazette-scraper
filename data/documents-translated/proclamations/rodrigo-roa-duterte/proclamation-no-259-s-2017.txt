MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 259

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG

WHEREAS, the City of Gingoog marks the celebration of its Charter Day on
the 23” of July;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Gingoog celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of Gingoog, Misamis Oriental requested that
Monday, 24 July 2017 be declared as a special non-working day, instead of 23 July
2017, which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog,

be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 24 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
