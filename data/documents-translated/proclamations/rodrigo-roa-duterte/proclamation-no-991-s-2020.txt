MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.991

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 AUGUST 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, the City of Davao observes the weeklong celebration of the
Kadayawan Festival in the month of August;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Davao to celebrate the
occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod requested that 24 August 2020, Monday, be
declared a special (non-working) day in celebration of the Araw ng Pasasalamat in
relation to the Indigenous Peoples Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare 24
August 2020 (Monday), a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Two Thousand and Twenty.
