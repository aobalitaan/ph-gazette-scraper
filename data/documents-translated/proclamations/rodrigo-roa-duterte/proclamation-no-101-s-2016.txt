MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 101

DECLARING THURSDAY, 17 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AROROY, PROVINCE OF
MASBATE

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 17 November 2016, the Municipality of Aroroy,
Province of Masbate marks the celebration of its Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Aroroy, Province of Masbate, be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Thursday, 17 November 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the
Municipality of Aroroy, Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th — day of November, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

fo

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
