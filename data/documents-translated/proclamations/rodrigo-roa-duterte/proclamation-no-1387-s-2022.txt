MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1387

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS, on 15 June 2022, Wednesday, the City of General Santos, will
mark the celebration of its 54° Charter Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of General
Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

ey
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,

Executive Secretary
