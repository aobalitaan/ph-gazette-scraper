MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 832

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 06 NOVEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUENAVISTA, PROVINCE OF
MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 06 November 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of
Buenavista, Province of Marinduque, will mark the celebration of its 1018t Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Buenavista be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 06 November 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Buenavista, Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th dayof October jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

& aN
SALVADOR C. MEBIALDEA

Executive Secretary
