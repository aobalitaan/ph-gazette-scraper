MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 711

DECLARING FRIDAY, 03 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 03 May 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province
of Marinduque, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary, known as the

Santa Cruz Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people. of the Municipality.of
Sta. Cruz be given full opportunity to Gelebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
03 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Cruz, Province
of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

ft &
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
