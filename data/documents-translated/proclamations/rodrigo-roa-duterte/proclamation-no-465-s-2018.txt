MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 465

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 02 MAY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas, marks the celebration of its
Sublian Festival on Wednesday, 02 May 2018; and ~

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bauan, Batangas, be given full Opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 02 May 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Bauan, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof April | inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.
