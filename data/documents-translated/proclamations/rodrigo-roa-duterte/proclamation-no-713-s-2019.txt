MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 713

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 08 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT ROXAS, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 08 May 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of President
Roxas, Province of Cotabato, will mark its 52 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
President Roxas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion

with appropriate ceremonies; oe
aan

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 08 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
President Roxas; Province of Cotabato. ‘

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

Kono a
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
