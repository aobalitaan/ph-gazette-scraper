MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1100

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 FEBRUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 22 February 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Taytay,
Province of Rizal, will mark the celebration of the Araw ng Pasasalamat: HAMAKA
Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Taytay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 22 February 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Taytay, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

~
as

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE,
Executive Secretary
