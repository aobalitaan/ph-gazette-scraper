MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 193

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 APRIL 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 25 April 2017, marks the celebration of the 16”
Founding Anniversary of the City of Isabela, Basilan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Isabela, Basilan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Tuesday, 25 April 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Isabela, Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

- ¢
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
