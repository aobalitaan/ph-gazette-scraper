MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 795

DECLARING FRIDAY, 30 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISULAN, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 30 August 2019, Friday the Municipality of Isulan, Province of
Sultan Kudarat, will mark the celebration of its 62" Founding Anniversary and the 8”
Hamungaya Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Isulan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
30 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Isulan, Province
of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
