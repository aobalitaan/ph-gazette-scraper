MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHLIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 236

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS, on Saturday, 17 June 2017 marks the 56" Charter Day of the
City of Lapu-Lapu, Cebu; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lapu-
Lapu, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 17 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lapu-Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this16thday of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

Komemun te
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary A
