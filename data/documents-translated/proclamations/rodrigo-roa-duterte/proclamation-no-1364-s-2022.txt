MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1364

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 16 June 2022, Thursday, the Municipality of Molave, Province
of Zamboanga del Sur will mark the celebration of its 74° Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Molave be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 16 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Molave,
Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, ! have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17tadayof may , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
