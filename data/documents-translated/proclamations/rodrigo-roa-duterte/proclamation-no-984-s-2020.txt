BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 984

DECLARING WEDNESDAY} 22 JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS, on 22 July 2020, Wednesday, the City of General Santos will
mark the celebration of its Indigenous Peoples 6'" Blaan Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of General
Santos be given full opportlinity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures; -

NOW, THEREFORE,|!, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 July 2020,| a special (non-working) day in the City of General
Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.
