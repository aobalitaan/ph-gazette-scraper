MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1034

DECLARING THURSDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, on 12 November 2020, Thursday, the City of Valenzuela will
mark the celebration of its 397" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Valenzuela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 12 November 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
