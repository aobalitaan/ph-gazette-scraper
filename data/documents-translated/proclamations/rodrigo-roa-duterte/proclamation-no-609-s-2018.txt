MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 609

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 07 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUENCA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 07 November 2018, a Wednesday, the Municipality of
Cuenca, Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration of its 14274 founding
anniversary; and é

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Cuenca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 07 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Cuenca, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
