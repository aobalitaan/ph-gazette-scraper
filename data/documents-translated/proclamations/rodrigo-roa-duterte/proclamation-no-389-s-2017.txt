MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 389

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS, the City of Navotas marks the celebration of its 112" Founding
Anniversary on Tuesday, 16 January 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Navotas
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 16 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
