MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 230

DECLARING FRIDAY, 09 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Friday, 09 June 2017, marks the celebration of the Foundation
Day of the Municipality of Rosario, Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Rosario, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
09 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario,
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this6th dayof June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
