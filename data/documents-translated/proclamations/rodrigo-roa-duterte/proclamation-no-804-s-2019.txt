MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 804

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MATEO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 21 September 2019, Saturday, the Municipality of San Mateo,
Province of Rizal, will mark its 448" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Mateo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Mateo, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of august , in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority/ofthe President:

Mutvanor C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
