MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 942

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 MAY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BUTUAN

WHEREAS, on 19 May 2020, Tuesday, the City of Butuan will mark the
celebration of the Balanghai Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Butuan be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 May 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Butuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of May in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

gs
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
