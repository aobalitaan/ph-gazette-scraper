MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 228

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian, Zamboanga del Sur marks its 48" Charter
Day on Thursday, 22 June 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian,
Zamboanga del Sur, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the Presidentt RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 22 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian,
Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

are
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary /
