MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 359

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Agoo, La Union marks the celebration of its
439" Foundation Anniversary on Friday, 08 December 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Agoo, La Union be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
08 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Agoo,
Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day Of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

, SALYADOR C. MEDIALDEA
