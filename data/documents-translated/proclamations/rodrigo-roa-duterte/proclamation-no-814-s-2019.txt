MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 814

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINGRAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, Josefa Llanes Escoda, a World War II heroine and the founder of
the Girl Scouts of the Philippines, was born on 20 September 1898 in Dingras, Ilocos
Norte;

WHEREAS, on 20 September 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Dingras,
Province of Ilocos Norte, will mark the birth anniversary of Josefa Llanes Escoda;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Dingras be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
20 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dingras,
Province of Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of September, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
