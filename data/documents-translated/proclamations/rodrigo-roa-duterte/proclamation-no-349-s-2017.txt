Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (R.A.) No. 7916,
otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by R.A.
No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine
Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), | RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the
Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building to be known
as The Finance Centre, with a gross floor area of One Hundred Twenty-Three
Thousand Five Hundred Seventy-Seven and 33/100 square meters (123,577.33 m’),
more or less, and the parcels of land upon which the building stands, located at lots
1 and 2, Block 55, 26" St. corner 9" Avenue, Bonifacio South District, Bonifacio
Global City, Taguig City, with an aggregate area of Five Thousand One Hundred
Twenty-Three square meters (5,123 m?), more or less, subject to the provisions of
R.A. No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution
No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011), 15-240 (s.
2015), and 17-472 (s. 2017), and Resolution No. 16-188 (s. 2016) of the PEZA
Board of Directors, as Special Economic Zone (Information Technology Center), as
