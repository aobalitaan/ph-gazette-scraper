MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1152

DECLARING THURSDAY, 17 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LAPU-LAPU

WHEREAS, on 17 June 2021, Thursday, the City of Lapu-Lapu will mark the
celebration of its 60" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lapu-Lapu
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 17 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lapu-Lapu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be atfixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof June, inthe year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
