MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES -‘

PROCLAMATION NO. 1230

DECLARING SATURDAY, 23 OCTOBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NARVACAN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS
SUR

WHEREAS, on 23 October 2021, Saturday, the Municipality of Narvacan,
Province of locos Sur, will mark the celebration of the Aldaw Ti Wayawaya — Ili A
Narvacan (Day of Freedom—Town of Narvacan); and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Narvacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 23 October 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Narvacan, Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

SALYADOR C. MEDIALDEAY
