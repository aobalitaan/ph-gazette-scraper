MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

-

PROCLAMATION NO. 1343

DECLARING SATURDAY, 11 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, on 11 June 2022, Saturday, the Province of Rizal will mark the
celebration of its 1218 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Rizal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the Presidenti RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 11 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th dayof April , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
