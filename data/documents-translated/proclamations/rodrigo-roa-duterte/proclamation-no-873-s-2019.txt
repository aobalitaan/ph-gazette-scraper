MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 873

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 JANUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 15 January 2020, Wednesday, the City of San Pablo,
Province of Laguna, will mark the celebration of the 25" Coconut Festival and Street
Dancing Competition (Mardi Gras); and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 January 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26thdayof December , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
