MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 617

DECLARING FRIDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2018, a Friday, the Province of Benguet will
mark the celebration of its 118" founding anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Benguet be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
23 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th dayof November , inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
