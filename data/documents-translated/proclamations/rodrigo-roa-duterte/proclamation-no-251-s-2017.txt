MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 251

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA

VALLEY

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley marks the
celebration of its 60" Founding Anniversary on 23 July 2017;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Nabunturan,
Compostela Valley, to celebrate the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan of
Nabunturan, Compostela Valley requested that Friday, 21 July 2017 be declared as
a special non-working day, instead of 23 July 2017, which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Nabunturan, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate
in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
21 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day In the Municipality of Nabunturan,
Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
