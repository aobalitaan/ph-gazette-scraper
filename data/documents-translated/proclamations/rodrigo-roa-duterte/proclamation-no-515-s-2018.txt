MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 515

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 18 June 2018, a Monday, the Municipality of Sta. Maria,
Davao Occidental, will mark its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Maria, Davao Occidental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 18 June 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Maria,
Davao Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13thday of June __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
