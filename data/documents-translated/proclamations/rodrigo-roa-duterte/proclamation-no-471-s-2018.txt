MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO.471

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 JUNE 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 19 June 2018, marks the 157" birth anniversary of Dr.
Jose P. Rizal, the country’s national hero, and the culmination of the Buhayani
Festival (Buhay ng Bayani, Buhay na Bayani), a festival in honor of the life and
works of Dr. Rizal and the life of everyday heroes; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba,
Laguna, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies in keeping with his ideals and teachings;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Tuesday, 19 June 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba,
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th —_ day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

. AON
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
