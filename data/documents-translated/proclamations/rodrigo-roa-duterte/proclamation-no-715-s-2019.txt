MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 715

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 08 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAMILING, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, on 08 May 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of Camiling,
Province of Tarlac, will mark the celebration of its annual cultural festivities; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Camiling be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies to enhance their historical and cultural consciousness;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 08 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Camiling, Province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

4 &
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
