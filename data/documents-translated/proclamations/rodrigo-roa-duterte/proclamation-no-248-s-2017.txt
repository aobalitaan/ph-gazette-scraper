7 . MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 248

DECLARING SATURDAY, 15 JULY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALABANGA, CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, Saturday, 15 July 2017, marks the celebration of the Foundation
Day of the Municipality of Calabanga, Camarines Sur; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Calabanga, Camarines Sur, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Saturday, 15 July 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Calabanga, Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this Sth day of July | in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

Ves
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary A
