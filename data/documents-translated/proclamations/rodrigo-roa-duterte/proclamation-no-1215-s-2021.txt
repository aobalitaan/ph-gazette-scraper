MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1215

DECLARING FRIDAY, 17 SEPTEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 17 September 2021, Friday, the Province of Zamboanga del
Sur will mark the celebration of its 69" Founding Anniversary; and

.WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province
Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate, ceremonies subject to community quarantine, social
distancing, and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,

17 September 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga del
Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of September jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.
