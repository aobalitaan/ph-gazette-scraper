MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 797

DECLARING TUESDAY, 27 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MINALIN, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, on 27 August 2019, Tuesday, the Municipality of Minalin,
Province of Pampanga, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Minalin be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 27 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Minalin,
Province of Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, 23rd day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
