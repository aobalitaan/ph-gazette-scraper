MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 909

DECLARING SUNDAY, 16 FEBRUARY 2020 AS SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY
IN CELEBRATION OF THE CORREGIDOR D-DAY

WHEREAS, the Government aims to inculcate and strengthen patriotism and
nationalism among Filipinos;

WHEREAS, historical records show that on 16 February 1945, Filipinos with
American allies commenced the successful attack to retake the Island of Corregidor;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper to commemorate victorious moments in
Philippine history in remembrance of Filipino bravery and heroism;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President ‘of the
Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Sunday,
16 February 2020 a special working holiday, in celebration of the Corregidor D- Day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

 

By the President:

finn dex,
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
