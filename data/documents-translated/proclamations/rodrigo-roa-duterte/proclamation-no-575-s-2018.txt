MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 575

DECLARING THURSDAY, 13 SEPTEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION

WHEREAS, on 13 September 2018, a Thursday, the Cordillera
Administrative Region will mark the commemoration of the 1986 Sipat Peace Talks;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Cordillera
Administrative Region be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 13 September 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Cordillera
Administrative Region.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th dayof Septembe} the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

Koon ix
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
