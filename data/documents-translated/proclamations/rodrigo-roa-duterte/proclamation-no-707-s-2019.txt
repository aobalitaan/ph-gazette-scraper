MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 707

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 APRIL 2019, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS, on 25 April 2019, Thursday, the City of Isabela, Province of
Basilan, will mark the celebration of its 18th Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Isabela, be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 April 2019, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Isabela,
Province of Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
