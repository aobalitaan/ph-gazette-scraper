MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 176

DECLARING FRIDAY, 24 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LIGAO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on Friday, 24 March 2017, the City of Ligao, Albay marks the
celebration of its Citinood Founding Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Ligao,
Albay, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MENARDO |. GUEVARRA, Acting Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby
declare Friday, 24 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Ligao,
Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

| Yano

MENARDO |. GUEVARRA
Acting Executive Secretary «
