MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1295

DECLARING FRIDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF IRIGA, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, on 11 February 2022, Friday, the City of Iriga, Province of
Camarines Sur, will mark the celebration of the Tinagba Festival, an ancient
Bicolano First-Harvest Offering; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of lriga be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,

11 February 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of lriga, Province of
Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

* 4 &
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA ,

Executive Secretary
