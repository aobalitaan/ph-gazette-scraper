MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 39

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 AUGUST 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS , on Monday, 22 August 2016, the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte, marks the celebration of its Foundation Day;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA , Executive Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRGO ROA DUTERTE , do hereby declare Monday, 22 August 2016, as a special (non-working day in the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA Executive Secretary
