MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1053

DECLARING FRIDAY, 04 DECEMBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, on 04 December 2020, Friday, the Province of Cebu will mark
the celebration of the birth anniversary of Governor Sergio Osmejia Jr.;

WHEREAS, Governor Osmefia Jr. served as Governor of Cebu Province from
1951 to 1959; as Mayor of Cebu City from 1959 to 1963; and was elected as senator
from 1965 to 1971; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Cebu
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
04 December 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18thday of November , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
