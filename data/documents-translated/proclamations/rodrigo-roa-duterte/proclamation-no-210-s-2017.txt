MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 210

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 JUNE 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BOGO, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, Friday, 16 June 2017, marks the Charter Day of the City of
Bogo, Province of Cebu;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bogo,

Province of Cebu, be given full opportunity to celebrate and Participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
16 June 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this19thday of May

, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

es
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
