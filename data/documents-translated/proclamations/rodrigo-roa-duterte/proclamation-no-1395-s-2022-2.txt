MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1395

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 JUNE 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIM, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, on 21 June 2022, Tuesday, the Municipality of Maasim, Province
of Sarangani, will mark the celebration of its 53 Founding Anniversary and the 15"
Kestebeng Festival;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maasim be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 June 2022, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maasim,
Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused tne seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA,
