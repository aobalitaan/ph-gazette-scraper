MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1135

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 APRIL 2021, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 21 April 2021, Wednesday, the City of Calamba, Province of
Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 20 Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 21 April 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba,
Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
