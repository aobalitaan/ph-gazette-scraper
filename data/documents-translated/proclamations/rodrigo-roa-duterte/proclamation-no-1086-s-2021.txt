PROCLAMATION NO. 1086

 

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING AND THE PARCEL OF LAND
WHERE IT STANDS, LOCATED AT ARCHBISHOP REYES STREET
CORNER ACACIA STREET, BARANGAY CAMPUTHAW, CEBU CITY, AS A
SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE (INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER) TO
BE KNOWN AS THE JEG TOWER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO.
7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

 

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916 or the
“Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the
recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority
(PEZA), I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic of the Philippines,
do hereby create and designate a building to be known as the JEG Tower, with a
gross floor area of Twenty-Six Thousand Three Hundred Seventy-Three square
meters (26, 373 m?), more or less, and the parcel of land on which the building stands,
located at Archbishop Reyes Street corner Acacia Street, Barangay Camputhaw,
Cebu City, as a Special Economic Zone (Information Technology Center), subject to
the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations,
PEZA Board of Directors Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution
Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011), 15-240 (s. 2015) and 17-472 (s. 2017), and Resolution No. 17-
228 (s. 2017), as amended by Resolution Nos. 19-094 (s. 2019) and 20-232 (s. 2020),
with an area of One Thousand Six Hundred Ninety-Seven square meters

(1,697 m?), more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
