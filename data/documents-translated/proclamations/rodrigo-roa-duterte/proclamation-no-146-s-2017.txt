MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHLIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 146

DECLARING SATURDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, Saturday, 11 February 2017, marks the 19"° Charter Day of the
City of Talisay, Negros Occidental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Talisay,
Negros Occidental be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Saturday, 11 February 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Talisay,
Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thiss™4 day of February, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
