MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.376

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 DECEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO

WHEREAS, the City of Tuguegarao marks the celebration of its 18" Charter
Day on Monday, 18 December 2017; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 18 December 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Tuguegarao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

X
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
