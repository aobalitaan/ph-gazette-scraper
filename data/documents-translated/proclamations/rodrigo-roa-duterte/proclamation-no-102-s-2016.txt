MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 102

DECLARING THURSDAY, 1 DECEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAINTA, PROVINCE
OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Cainta, Rizal marks the celebration of
its Sumbingtik Festival on Thursday, 1 December 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the
Municipality of Cainta, Rizal be given full opportunity to participate in and
celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies. —e

NOW, THEREFORE, 1, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Thursday, 1 December 2016, as a special (non-working) day
in the Municipality of Cainta, Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of November, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

LL
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
