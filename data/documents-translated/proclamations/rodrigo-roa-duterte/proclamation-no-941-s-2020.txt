MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 941

DECLARING FRIDAY, 08 MAY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE
MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT ROXAS, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 08 May 2020, Friday, the Municipality of President Roxas,
Province of Cotabato, will mark the celebration of its 53" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
President Roxas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
08 May 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of President Roxas,
Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th dayof May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.
