MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 533

DECLARING SATURDAY, 14 JULY 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GUIHULNGAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, 14 July 2018, a Saturday, will mark the Charter Day of the City
of Guihulngan, Negros Oriental; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Guihulngan, Negros Oriental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 14 July 2018, a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihulngan,
Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
