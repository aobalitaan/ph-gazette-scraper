MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 887

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 19 FEBRUARY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAGO, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 February 2020, Wednesday, the City of Bago, Province of
Negros Occidental, will mark the celebration of its 54" Charter Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bago be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 19 February 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Bago,
Province of Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
