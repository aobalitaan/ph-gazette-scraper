MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 861

DECLARING SATURDAY, 28 DECEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUDELA, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS
OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, on 28 December 2019, Saturday, the Municipality of Tudela,
Province of Misamis Occidental, will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Tudela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 28 December 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Tudela, Province of Misamis Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19thday of Novembner | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
