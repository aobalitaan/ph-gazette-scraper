MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 410

DECLARING SATURDAY, 17 FEBRUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KABASALAN, PROVINCE OF
ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, Saturday, 17 February 2018, marks the celebration of the 81°!
Founding Anniversary of the Municipality of Kabasalan, Zamboanga Sibugay; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Kabasalan, Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to celebrate and
participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 17 February 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Kabasalan, Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
