MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1024

DECLARING MONDAY, 05 OCTOBER 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. CRUZ, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, on 05 October 2020, Monday, the Municipality of Sta. Cruz,
Province of Davao del Sur, will mark the celebration of its 136 Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Cruz be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 05 October 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta.
Cruz, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st dayof October , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:
