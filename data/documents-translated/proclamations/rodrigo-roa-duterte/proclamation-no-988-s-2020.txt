MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 938

DECLARING MONDAY, 03 AUGUST 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TACURONG, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, on 03 August 2020, Monday, the City of Tacurong, Province of
Sultan Kudarat, will mark the celebration of its 69! Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 03 AuguSt 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong,
Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

cw

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
