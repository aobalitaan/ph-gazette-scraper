MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1242

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BORONGAN, PROVINCE OF EASTERN SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 15 November 2021, Monday, the City of Borongan, Province
of Eastern Samar, will mark the celebration of the birth anniversary of Eugenio S.
Daza;

WHEREAS, Eugenio S. Daza, a native of Borongan, Eastern Samar, was the
overall commander of the revolutionary forces in Eastern Samar and represented the
Third District of Samar in the Philippine Assembly from 1907 to 1909; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Borongan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 15 November 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Borongan,
Province of Eastern Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of November, jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
