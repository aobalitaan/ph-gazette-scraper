MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 385

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 10 JANUARY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 10 January 2018, marks the 78" Founding
Anniversary and 18" Hinugyaw Festival of the City of Koronadal; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in these occasions with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the Presidentt, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 10 January 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Koronadal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
