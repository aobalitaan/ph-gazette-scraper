MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 654

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 JANUARY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 20 January 2019, Sunday, the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas, will mark the celebration of the MAKALANYOG Festival;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Lipa, to celebrate the
occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that 21 January 2019, Monday, instead
of 20 January 2019 which falls on a Sunday, be declared a special (non-working)
day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW,. THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 January 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa, Province
of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.
