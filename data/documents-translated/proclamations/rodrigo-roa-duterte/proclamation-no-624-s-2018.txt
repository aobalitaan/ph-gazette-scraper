MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. ©24

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN, PROVINCE OF
COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on 25 November 2018, Sunday, the Municipality of Maragusan,
Province of Compostela Valley, will mark the celebration of its 41°‘ founding
anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Maragusan, to
celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Sangguniang Bayan requested that 26
November 2018, Monday, instead of 25 November 2018 which falls on a Sunday, be
declared a special (non-working) day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Maragusan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 26 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Maragusan, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
