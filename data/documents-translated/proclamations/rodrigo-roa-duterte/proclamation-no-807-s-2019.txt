MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 807

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUIRINO

WHEREAS, on 10 September 2019, Tuesday, the Province of Quirino will
mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Quirino be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 10 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Quirino.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of Septembein the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
