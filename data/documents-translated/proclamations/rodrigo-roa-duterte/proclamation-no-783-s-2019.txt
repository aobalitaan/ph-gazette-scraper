MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 783

DECLARING FRIDAY, 09 AUGUST 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYOMBONG, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA

WHEREAS, on 09 August 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Bayombong,
Province of Nueva Vizcaya, will mark the celebration of the 3 Bayongyong Festival;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bayombong be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
09 August 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bayombong,
Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of August , inthe year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.
