MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1162

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 JUNE 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF LIPA, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the City of Lipa, Province of Batangas, will mark the celebration
of its 74" Founding Anniversary; 4

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Lipa to celebrate the occasion,
the Office of the Mayor requested that 21 June 2021, Monday, be declared a special
(non-working) day in lieu of 20 June 2021, which falls on a Sunday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 June 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa, Province of
Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th dayof June , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

en
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
