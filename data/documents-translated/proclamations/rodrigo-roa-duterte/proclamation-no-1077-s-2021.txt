MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

 

PROCLAMATION NO. 1077

 

DECLARING SATURDAY, 23 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)

DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN
i
WHEREAS, on 23 January 2021, Saturday, the Province of Bulacan will

mark the anniversary of the Inauguration of the First Philippine Republic, which took
place at the Barasoain Church, Malolos, Bulacan; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Saturday, 23 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of January | jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty One,
