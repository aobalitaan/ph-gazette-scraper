MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1078

 

 

DECLARING MONDAY, 25 JANUARY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACO, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 25 January 2021, Monday, the Municipality of Baco, Province
of Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its 100 Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Baco be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community ‘quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 25 January 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Baco,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
