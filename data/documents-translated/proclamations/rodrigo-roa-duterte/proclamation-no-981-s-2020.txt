MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 981

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 JULY 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE

CITY OF SANTA ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, on 10 July 2020, Friday, the City of Santa Rosa, Province of

Laguna, will mark the celebration of its 16"" Cityhood Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa

Rosa be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,

by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
10 July 2020, a special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa, Province of
Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal

of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

A—— us
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
