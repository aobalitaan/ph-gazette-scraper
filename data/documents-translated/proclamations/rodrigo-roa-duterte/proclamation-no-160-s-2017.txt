MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 160

DECLARING FRIDAY, 24 MARCH 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, Friday, 24 March 2017, marks the celebration of the Foundation
Anniversary of the City of Tabaco, Province of Albay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco,

be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,

24 March 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco, Province of
Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of February in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
