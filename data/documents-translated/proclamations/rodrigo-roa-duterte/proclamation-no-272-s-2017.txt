MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 272

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 AUGUST 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 16 August 2017, the City of Tuguegarao marks
the celebration of its 2017 Pavvurulun “Afi” Festival. and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of

Tuguegarao, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President. RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Wednesday, 16 August 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Tuguegarao

IN WITNESS WHEREOF., | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of July, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:

|
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary A
