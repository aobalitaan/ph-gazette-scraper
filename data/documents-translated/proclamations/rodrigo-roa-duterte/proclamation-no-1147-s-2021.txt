MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1147

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 19 MAY 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF BUTUAN

WHEREAS, on 19 May 2021, Wednesday, the City of Butuan will mark the
celebration of the Balangay (Balanghai) Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the peoplé of the City of Butuan be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 19 May 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Butuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th — day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
