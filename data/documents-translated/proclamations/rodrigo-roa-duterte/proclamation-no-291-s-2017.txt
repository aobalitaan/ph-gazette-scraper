MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 291

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 SEPTEMBER 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, Friday, 15 September 2017, marks the celebration of the 119"
anniversary of the inauguration of the Malolos Congress in the Province of Bulacan;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO R. DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday, 15
September 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By autnority of the President:
