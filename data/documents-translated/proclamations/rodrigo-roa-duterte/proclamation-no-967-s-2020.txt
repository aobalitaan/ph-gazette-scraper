MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 967

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JUNE 2020, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAGULIN, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, on 25 June 2020, Thursday, the Municipality of Bagulin, Province
of La Union, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Bagulin be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Thursday, 25 June 2020, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bagulin,
Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

4
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary (1)
