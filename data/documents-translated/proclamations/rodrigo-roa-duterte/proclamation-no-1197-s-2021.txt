MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1197

DECLARING TUESDAY, 10 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUMACA, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on 10 August 2021, Tuesday, the Municipality of Gumaca,
Province of Quezon, will mark the celebration of Lorenzo M. Tafiada Sr. Day; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Gumaca be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 10 August 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Gumaca, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5: | day of August, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:
