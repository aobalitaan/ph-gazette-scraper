MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1198

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 11 AUGUST 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 11 August 2021, Wednesday, the Province of Samar will
mark the commemoration of its declaration as a province independent of Leyte and
Cebu; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Samar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and
other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 11 August 2021, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6thday of August , in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
