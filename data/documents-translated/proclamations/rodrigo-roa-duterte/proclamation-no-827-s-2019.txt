MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 827

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 OCTOBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIGCAWAYAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, on 21 October 2019, Monday, the Municipality of Pigcawayan,
Province of Cotabato, will mark its 66" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pigcawayan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 October 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pigcawayan, Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18thday of | October jn the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.
