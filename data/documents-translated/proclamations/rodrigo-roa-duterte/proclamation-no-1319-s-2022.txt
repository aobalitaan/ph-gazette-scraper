MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1319

DECLARING THURSDAY, 24 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 March 2022, Thursday, the City of Tabaco, Province of
Albay, will mark the celebration of its Charter Day and the 21% Cityhood Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare

Thursday, 24 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco,
Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof march _, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.
