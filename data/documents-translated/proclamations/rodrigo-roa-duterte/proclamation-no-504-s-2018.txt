MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.504

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 JUNE 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, the Province of Ifugao will mark the celebration of its Founding
Anniversary on 18 June 2018, a Monday; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Ifugao be given full opportunity to ‘celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare 18 June
2018, a special (non-working) day in the Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of May in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority ie President:

67

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
