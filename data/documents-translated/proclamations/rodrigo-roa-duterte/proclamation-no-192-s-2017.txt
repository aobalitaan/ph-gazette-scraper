MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 192

DECLARING MONDAY, 08 MAY 2017, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAMILING, PROVINCE OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, Monday, 08 May 2017, marks the celebration of the annual
cultural festivities of the Municipality of Camiling, Tarlac;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Camiling, Tarlac, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies to enhance their historical and cultural consciousness.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 08 May 2017, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Camiling, Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7h day of April, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
