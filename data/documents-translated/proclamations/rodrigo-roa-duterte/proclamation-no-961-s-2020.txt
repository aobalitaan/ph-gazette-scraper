MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 961

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 JUNE 2020, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 16 June 2020, marks the 70% Founding Anniversary of
the Municipality of Pamplona, Negros Oriental; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Pamplona, Negros Oriental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social
distancing, and other public health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Tuesday, 16
June 2020, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pamplona, Negros
Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of
the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two
Thousand and Twenty.

By authority of the President:

ot we
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDE
Executive Secretary
