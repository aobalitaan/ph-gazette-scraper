MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1308

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 MARCH 2022, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF CALAPAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on 21 March 2022, Monday, the City of Calapan, Province of
Oriental Mindoro, will mark the celebration of its 24" Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calapan
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to the public health measures of the national government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 March 2022, a special (non-working) day in the City of Calapan,
Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of February, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty Two.

By authority of the President:
