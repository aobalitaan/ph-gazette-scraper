MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 816

DECLARING FRIDAY, 27 SEPTEMBER 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALVAR, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, on 27 September 2019, Friday, the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas, will mark the 154" birth anniversary of General Miguel Malvar;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Malvar be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
27 September 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Malvar,
Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19thyay of Septembe fy the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority pf the President:

ivan foc C. MEDIALDEA
