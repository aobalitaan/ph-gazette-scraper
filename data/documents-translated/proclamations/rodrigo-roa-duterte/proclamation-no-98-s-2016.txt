MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 98

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 23 November 2016, the Province of
Benguet will celebrate its 116" Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Benguet be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive
Secretary, by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do
hereby declare Wednesday, 23 November 2016, as a special (non-working)
day in the Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:
