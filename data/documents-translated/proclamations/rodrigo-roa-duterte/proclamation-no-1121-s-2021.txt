MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1121

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 24 MARCH 2021, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF TABACO, PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, on 24 March 2021, Wednesday, the City of Tabaco, Province of
Albay, will mark the celebration of its Charter Day and the 20 Cityhood Founding
Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tabaco be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies, subject to community quarantine, social distancing, and other public
health measures;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 24 March 2021, a special (non-working) day in the City of Tabaco,
Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17thday of March __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.

By authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
