MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 492

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 JULY 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF GINGOOG

WHEREAS, the City of Gingoog, Province of Misamis Oriental, will mark the
celebration of its Charter Anniversary on Monday, 23 July 2018; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies; .

NOW, THEREFORE, I, MICHAEL P. ONG, Acting Executive Secretary, by
authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Monday,
23 July 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority gf the President:

A

MICHAEL P. ONG
Acting Executive Secretary
