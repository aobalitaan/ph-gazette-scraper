MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 626

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2018, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARGARITA, PROVINCE OF
SAMAR

WHEREAS, on 28 November 2018, a Wednesday, the Municipality of Sta.
Margarita, Province of Samar, will mark the celebration of its founding anniversary;
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Margarita be given full opportunity to participate in and celebrate the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 28 November 2018, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Sta. Margarita, Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:

&
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary @
