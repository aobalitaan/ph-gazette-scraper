MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 693

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 MARCH 2019, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF CANLAON, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, on 19 March 2019, Tuesday, the City of Canlaon, Province of
Negros Oriental, will mark the celebration of the Pasayaw Festival; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Canlaon
be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, !, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 19 March 2019, a special (non-working) day in the City of Canlaon,
Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th dayof March __, inthe year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:

ae
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA

Executive Secretary
