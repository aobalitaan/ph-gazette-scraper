MALACANAN PALACE

MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 459

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 APRIL 2018, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE CITY OF TARLAC

WHEREAS, the City of Tarlac marks the celebration of its 20" Charter
Anniversary on 19 April 2018;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tarlac celebrate the occasion,
the Office of the City Mayor of Tarlac requested that Friday, 20 April 2018, instead of
19 April 2018 which falls on a Thursday, be declared as a special non-working day,
and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be
given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate
ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare Friday,
20 April 2018, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27thday of March _, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Eighteen.

By authority of the President:
