MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.720

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 MAY 2019, A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN
THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOGPOG, PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE

WHEREAS, on 15 May 2019, Wednesday, the Municipality of Mogpog,
Province of Marinduque, will mark the celebration of its Founding Anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Mogpog be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, |, SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA, Executive Secretary,
by authority of the President, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 15 May 2019, a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Mogpog, Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,

Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority gf yne President:

ts 7

LVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
