PROCLAMATION NO. 392

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 JUNE 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAGAY

WHEREAS, Monday, 11 June 2012 marks the 16 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Sagay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sagay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 11 June 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Sagay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 st day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
