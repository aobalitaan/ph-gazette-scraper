MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 299

DECLARING THURSDAY, 8 DECEMBER 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Agoo, La Union marks the celebration of its 433 rd Foundation Day anniversary on Thursday, 8 December 2011;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Agoo La Union be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 8 December 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Agoo, Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
