MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 98

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 11, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS, Friday, February 11, 2011, the City of Iriga marks the celebration of the Tinagba Festival and the feast of Our Lady of Lourdes;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Iriga full opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare February 11, 2011 as special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Friday, February 11, 2011 as special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21 st day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
