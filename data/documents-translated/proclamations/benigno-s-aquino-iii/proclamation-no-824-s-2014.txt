MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 824

DECLARING SATURDAY, 16 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KADINGILAN, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Kadingilan, Bukidnon was created by virtue of Republic Act No. 6368, approved on 16 August 1971;

WHEREAS, Saturday, 16 August 2014, marks the anniversary of the creation of the Municipality of Kadingilan, Province of Bukidnon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Kadingilan, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 16 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kadingilan, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. O CHOA JR. Executive Secretary
