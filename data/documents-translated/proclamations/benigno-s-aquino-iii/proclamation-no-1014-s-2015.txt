MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1014

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 MAY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Quezon, Province of Palawan, marks the commemoration of its Foundation Day anniversary on Friday, 15 May 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Quezon, Palawan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 15 May 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Quezon, Palawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
