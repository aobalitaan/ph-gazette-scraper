MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1187

DECLARING MONDAY, 11 JANUARY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, Monday, 11 January 2016, marks the 75 th Foundation Anniversary and the 16 th Hinugyaw Festival of the City of Koronadal;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 11 January 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
