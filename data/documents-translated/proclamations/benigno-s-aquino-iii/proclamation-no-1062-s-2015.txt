MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1062

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JUNE 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SADANGA, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 25 June 2015, the Municipality of Sadanga, Mountain Province marks the celebration of its Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Sadanga, Mountain Province be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 25 June 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sadanga, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
