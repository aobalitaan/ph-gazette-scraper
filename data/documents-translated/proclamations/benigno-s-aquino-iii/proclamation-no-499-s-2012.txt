MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 499

DECLARING THURSDAY, 15 NOVEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, on Thursday. 15 November 2012, the Province of Oriental Mindoro marks the commemoration of its 62 nd Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Oriental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 15 November 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
