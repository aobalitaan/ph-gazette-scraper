MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 707

DECLARING MONDAY, 20 JANUARY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF LIPA

WHEREAS , the City of Lipa marks the celebration of its City Fiesta on Monday, 20 January 2014;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Monday, 20 January 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
