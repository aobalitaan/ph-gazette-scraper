MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 195

DECLARING SATURDAY, 2 JULY 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, Saturday, 2 July 2011, marks the 438 th Anniversary of the City of Pasig;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 2 July 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the City ofPasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
