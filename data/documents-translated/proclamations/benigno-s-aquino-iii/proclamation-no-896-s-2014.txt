MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 896

DECLARING MONDAY, 24 NOVEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, on 23 November 2014, the Province of Benguet will celebrate its 114 th Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Benguet to celebrate and participate in the occasion, the Governor requested that Monday, 24 November 2014, instead of 23 November 2014 which falls on a Sunday, be declared as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 24 November 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
