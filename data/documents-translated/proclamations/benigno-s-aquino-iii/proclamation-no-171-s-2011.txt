MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 171

DECLARING NOVEMBER 20 TO 26, 2011 AS “ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING WEEK”

WHEREAS, the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers of the Philippines, Inc. (IIEE), will hold its 36th Annual National Convention on November 23 to 26, 2011 at the Philippine International Convention Center, CCP Complex, Pasay City;.and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus national attention on the importance of the electrical engineering profession and highlight its role in nation-building.

NOW, THEREFORE, I BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, by the virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare November 20 to 26, 2011 as “Electrical Engineering Week” under the auspices of the IIEE.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the city of Manila, this 19 th day of May in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
