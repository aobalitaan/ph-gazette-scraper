MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 87

DECLARING THURSDAY, DECEMBER 23, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN , PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Thursday, December 23, 2010, marks the 10 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Bayawan;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Bayawan participate and celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare December 23, 2010 as a non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, December 23, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the City of Bayawan, Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
