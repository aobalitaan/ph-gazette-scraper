PROCLAMATION NO. 216

DECLARING MONDAY, 22 AUGUST 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DIGOS, DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the City of Digos marks the 60 th Founding Anniversary of the Mary Mediatrix of All Grace Parish on Monday, 22 August 2011;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Digos be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 22 August 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary
