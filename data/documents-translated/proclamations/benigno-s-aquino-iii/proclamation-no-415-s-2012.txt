MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 415

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN LOCATED AT BARANGAY PAWING, PALO, LEYTE AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as an Information Technology Park, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution No.04-295 (s. 2004) and Resolution No. 11-095 (s. 2011) of the PEZA Board of Directors, the Leyte Mikyu Economic Zone, which shall be established on several parcels of land of the private domain located at Barangay Pawing, Palo, Leyte, containing an aggregate area of Two Hundred Twenty-Two Thousand Six Hundred Forty-Three (222,643) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
