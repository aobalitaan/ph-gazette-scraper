PROCLAMATION NO. 305

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING BEING CONSTRUCTED ON A PARCEL OF LAND, WITH AN AGGREGATE AREA OF 44,924 SQUARE METERS, MORE OR LESS, SITUATED AT BRGY. WACK-WACK, MADALUYONG CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution No. 11-066 (s. 2011) and Resolution No. 11-151 (2011) of the PEZA Board of Directors, the SM Megamall I.T. Center as an Information Technology Center being constructed on a lot located at Brgy. Wack-Wack, Mandaluyong City, containing an area of Forty Four Thousand Nine Hundred Twenty Four (44,924) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
