MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1016

DECLARING TUESDAY, 16 JUNE 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BOGO, PROVINCE OF CEBU

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 16 June 2015 marks the Charter Day anniversary of the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 16 June 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bogo, Province of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
