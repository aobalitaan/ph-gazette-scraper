MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 479

DECLARING FRIDAY, 21 SEPTEMBER 2012. AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MATEO, PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, Friday, 21 September 2012, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of San Mateo, Rizal;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of San Mateo, Rizal the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 21 September 2012, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 21 September 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Mateo, Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
