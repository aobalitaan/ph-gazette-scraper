MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 851

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 3 SEPTEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 3 September 2014, the City of Iriga marks the commemoration of its 46 th Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Iriga the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 3 September 2014, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 3 September 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
