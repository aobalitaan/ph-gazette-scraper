MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 761

DECLARING MONDAY, 9 JUNE 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Monday, 9 June 2014, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Rosario, Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Rosario, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 9 June 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N . OCHOAJR. Executive Secretary
