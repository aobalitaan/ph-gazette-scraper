MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 738

DECLARING SATURDAY, 22 MARCH 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, the Province of Cavite marks the commemoration of the birth
anniversary of General Emilio Aguinaldo, the first President of the Republic, on
Saturday, 22 March 2014;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Cavite be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Saturday, 22 March 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the -Province of
Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18thday of March, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

  
 

PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR.
