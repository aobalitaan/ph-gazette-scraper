MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 608

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 JULY 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION (CAR)

WHEREAS, the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) marks its Foundation Day anniversary on Monday, 15 July 2013, with the theme “Bridging One Green Cordillera;”

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of CAR be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 15 July 2013, as a special (non-working) day in CAR.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
