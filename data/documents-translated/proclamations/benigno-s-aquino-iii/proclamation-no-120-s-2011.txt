MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 120

DECLARING THURSDAY, MARCH 31, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS , Thursday, March 31, 2011, marks the 10 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Sipalay, Negros Occidental;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, March 31, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the City of Sipalay, Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8 th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
