DECLARING THURSDAY, MARCH 10, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Thursday, March  10, 2011,  marks the  Foundation  Day anniversary of the Province of Bukidnon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, March 10, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 01 day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
