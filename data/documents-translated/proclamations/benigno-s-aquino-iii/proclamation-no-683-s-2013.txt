MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.683

DECLARING THURSDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, the Province of Sarangani marks its 21° Foundation Day
anniversary and MunaTo Festival on 28 November 2013;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sarangani be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies,

NOW, THEREFORE, |, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Thursday, 28 November 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. ~

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen. .

By authority of the President:

 
  
  

PAQUITO'N. OCHOA JR.
