MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1093

DECLARING FRIDAY, 14 AUGUST 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAWAB, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Mawab, Compostela Valley marks the celebration of its Foundation Day on Friday, 14 August 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Mawab, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 14 August 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mawab, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
