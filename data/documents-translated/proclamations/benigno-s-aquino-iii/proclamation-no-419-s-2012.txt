MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 419

DECLARING 11-17 NOVEMBER 2012 AS “ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING WEEK”

WHEREAS, the Institute of Integrated Electrical Engineers of the Philippines, Inc. (IIEE), will hold its 37 th Annual National Convention on 11-17 November 2012 at the SMX Convention Center, Seashell Lane, Mall of Asia Complex, Pasay City; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus national attention on the importance of the electrical engineering profession and highlight its role in nation-building.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO, III, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare 11-17 November 2012 as “Electrical Engineering Week” under the auspices of the IIEE.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 4 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President: (Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
