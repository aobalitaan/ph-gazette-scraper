PROCLAMATION NO. 118

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED AT BARANGAY SAN VICENTE, DASOL, PANGASINAN, AS TOURISM ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 02-342 dated 10 October 2002 and Resolution No. 09-545 dated 28 October 2009 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, several parcels of land situated at Barangay San Vicente, Dasol, Pangasinan, as Tourism Economic Zone to be known as DASOLand: A Family Adventure and Leisure Park with an aggregate area of SIX HUNDRED TWENTY THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED EIGHTY THREE (620,483) SQUARE METERS, as defined by the following technical description:
