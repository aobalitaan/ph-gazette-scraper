PROCLAMATION NO. 307

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JANUARY 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the City of Batangas marks the feast day of its patron saint, Sto. Niño on Monday, 16 January 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 16 January 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary
