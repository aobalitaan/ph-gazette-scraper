PROCLAMATION NO. 345

DECLARING TUESDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TANGUB

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 28 February 2012, marks the Charter Day anniversary of the City of Tangub;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 28 February 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tangub.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23 rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
