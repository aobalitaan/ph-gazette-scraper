MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 583

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 JUNE 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 11 June 2013, marks the 112 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Rizal;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Rizal be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 11 June 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
