MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 5 20

CREATING AND DESIGNATING TWO PARCELS OF LAND, TO BE KNOWN AS IRASAN-ROXAS ZANORTE SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, LOCATED AT BARANGAY IRASAN, MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, AS AN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 , AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 07-232 (s. 2007) and Resolution No. 11-168 (s. 2011), as amended by Resolution Nos. 11-422 (s. 2011), 11-525 (s. 2011) and 11-717 (s. 2011), of the PEZA Board of Directors, two parcels of land, to be known as Irasan-Roxas Zanorte Special Economic Zone, as an Agro-Industrial Zone, located at Barangay Irasan, Municipality of President Manuel A. Roxas, Zamboanga del Norte, with an aggregate area of Sixty Thousand Five Hundred Fifty-Two (60,552) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
