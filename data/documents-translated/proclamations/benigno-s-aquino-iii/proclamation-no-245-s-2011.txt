MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 245

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THREE PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN LOCATED AT BANTAYAN, DUMAGUETE CITY, NEGROS ORIENTAL AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (IT) PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT (RA) 7916, AS AMENDED BY RA 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under RA 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as an Information Technology Park, subject to the provisions of RA 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 (s. 2004) and Resolution No. 11-094 (s. 2011) of the PEZA Board of Directors, the LinkSY IT Park which shall be established on three parcels of land of the private domain located at Bantayan, Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental, containing an aggregate area of Eleven Thousand One Hundred Sixty Four (11,164) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
