MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION No. 222

DECLARING FRIDAY, 4 APRIL 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE CITY OF ANTIPOLO

WHEREAS, the City of Antipolo marks the celebration of the 16" cityhood
anniversary on Friday, 4 April 2014;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Antipolo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Friday, 4 April 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Antipolo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
