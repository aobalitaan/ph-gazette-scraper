MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 191

DECLARING MONDAY, 4 JULY 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CADIZ

WHEREAS, Monday,   4   July   2011,   marks   the   44 th Charter   Day anniversary of the City of Cadiz;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 4 July 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
