PROCLAMATION NO. 405

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING, TO BE KNOWN AS SLC BUILDING, WHICH STANDS ON TWO PARCELS OF LAND, LOCATED AT 6797 AYALA AVENUE, MAKATI CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution No. 11-066 (s. 2011) and Resolution No. 11-237 (s. 2011) of the PEZA Board of Directors, a building, to be known as the SLC Building , as an Information Technology Center, which stands on two parcels of land, located at 6797 Ayala Avenue, Makati City, with an aggregate area of Two Thousand Nine Hundred Ninety-Seven (2,997) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
