PROCLAMATION NO. 157

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH SHALL STAND ON SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT R.N. PELAEZ BOULEVARD, KAUSWAGAN, CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I , BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and. Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000) and Resolution No. 05-192 (s. 2005) as amended by Resolution No. 11-054 (s. 2011) of the Board of Directors of PEZA, the Georgetown Cybermall as an Information Technology Center which shall stand on several parcels of land of the private domain located at R.N.
