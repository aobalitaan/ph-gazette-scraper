MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 447

DECLARING SATURDAY, 4 AUGUST 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections will conduct the plebiscite on Saturday, 4 August 2012, to convert the Municipality of Cabuyao into a component city to be known as the City of Cabuyao;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Cabuyao, Laguna the opportunity to actively and fully participate in the event, it is but fitting and proper to declare 4 August 2012 as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 4 August 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cabuyao, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2 nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
