MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 850

DECLARING FRIDAY, 29 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ISULAN, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Isulan, Province of Sultan Kudarat marks the 57 th Foundation Day anniversary and the 3 rd Hamungaya Festival on 30 August 2014;

WHEREAS, in order for the people of the Municipality of Isulan, Sultan Kudarat to participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Friday, 29 August 2014 instead of 30 August 2014, which falls on a Saturday, be declared as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 29 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality Isulan, Province of Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary

Proclamation No. 850, August 18, 2014
