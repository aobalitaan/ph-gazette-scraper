MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 425

DECLARING THURSDAY, 2 AUGUST 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF KABANKALAN, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, Thursday, 2 August 2012, marks the Charter Day anniversary of the City of Kabankalan, Negros Occidental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Kabankalan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 2 August 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Kabankalan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
