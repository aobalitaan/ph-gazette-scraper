MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 177

DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JUNE 2011 AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BOGO

WHEREAS, Thursday, 16 June 2011 marks the Charter Day anniversary of the City of Bogo;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bogo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 16 June 2011 as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bogo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
