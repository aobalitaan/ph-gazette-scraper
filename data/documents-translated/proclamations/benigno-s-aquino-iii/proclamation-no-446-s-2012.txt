PROCLAMATION NO. 446

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND, TO BE INCLUDED TO THE LAKESIDE EVOZONE – INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK, LOCATED AT BARANGAY STO. DOMINGO, STA. ROSA CITY, LAGUNA, AN EXISTING INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 (s. 2004) and Resolution No. 11-449 (s. 2011), of the PEZA Board of Directors, several parcels of land, to be included to the Lakeside Evozone – Information Technology Park, an existing Information Technology Park, located at Barangay Sto. Domingo, Sta.
