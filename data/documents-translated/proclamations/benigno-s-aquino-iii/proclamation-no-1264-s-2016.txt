M A L A C A N A N PALACE 
MANILA 
BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES 
PROCLAMATION NO. ^264 
DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JUNE 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) 
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR 
WHEREAS. Thursday, 16 June 2016, marks the 68*^ Foundation 
Anniversary of the Municipality of Molave. Zamboanga del Sur; 
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of 
Molave, Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate 
in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies. 
NOW, THEREFORE, I. PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, 
by authority of His Excellency. BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare 
Thursday, 16 June 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of 
Molave, Zamboanga del Sur. 
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the 
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. 
Done in the City of Manila, this 11 thday of 
May 
, in the year of Our 
Lord. Two Thousand and Sixteen.
