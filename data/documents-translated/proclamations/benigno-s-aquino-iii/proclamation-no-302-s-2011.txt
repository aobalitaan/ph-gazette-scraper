MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 302

DECLARING   TUESDAY,   20   DECEMBER   2011,   AS   A   SPECIAL   (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SIPALAY

WHEREAS, the City of Sipalay marks the celebration of its fiesta the “Handurawan Festival”;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sipalay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 20 December 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the City ofSipalay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19 th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
