MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 46 9

DECLARING FRIDAY, 26 OCTOBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, Friday, 26 October 2012, marks the commemoration of the Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Catanduanes;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Province of Catanduanes the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 26 October 2012, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 26 October 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
