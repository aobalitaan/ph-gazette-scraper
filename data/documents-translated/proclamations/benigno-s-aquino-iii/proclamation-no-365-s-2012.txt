PROCLAMATION NO. 365

DECLARING TUESDAY, 3 APRIL 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ALBAY

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 3 April 2012, marks the celebration of “Albay Day” in the Province of Albay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Albay, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 3 April 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
