PROCLAMATION NO. 327

DECLARING FRIDAY, 3 FEBRUARY 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN

WHEREAS, Friday, 3 February 2012, marks the commemoration of the 62 nd Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Cabanatuan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabanatuan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 3 February 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31 st day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
