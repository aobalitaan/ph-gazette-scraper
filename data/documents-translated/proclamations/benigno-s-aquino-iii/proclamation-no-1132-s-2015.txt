MALACAÑANG PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1132

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 OCTOBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CATANDUANES

WHEREAS, Monday, 26 October 2015, marks the celebration of the Foundation Anniversary of the Province of Catanduanes;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Catanduanes be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 26 October 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Catanduanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of September in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
