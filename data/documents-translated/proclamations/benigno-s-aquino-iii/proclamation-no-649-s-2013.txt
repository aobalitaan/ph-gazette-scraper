MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 649

DECLARING THURSDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIQUIJOR PROVINCE OF SIQUIJOR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Siquijor marks the commemoration of its 41 st year anniversary as the capital town of the Province of Siquijor on Thursday 12 September 2013;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Siquijor, Province of Siquijor the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion it is but fitting and proper to declare 12 September 2013, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 12 September 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Siquijor, Province of Siquijor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
