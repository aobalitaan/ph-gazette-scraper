MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 660

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 OCTOBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY [IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PIGCAWAYAN,
PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, Sunday, 20 October 2013, marks the 60" Foundation Day
anniversary of the Municipality of Pigcawayan, Cotabato:

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Pigcawayan,
Cotabato, to celebrate and participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan
requested that Monday, 21 October 2013 be declared as special (non-working)
day instead of 20 October 2013, which falls on a Thursday.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. GCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 October 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Pigcawayan, Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this7th day of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:
