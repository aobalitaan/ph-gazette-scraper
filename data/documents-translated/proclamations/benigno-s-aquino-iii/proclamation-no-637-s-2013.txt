MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 637

DECLARING TUESDAY, 3 SEPTEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS , Tuesday, 3 September 2013, marks the commemoration of the 45 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Iriga;

WHEREAS , in order to give the people of the City of Iriga the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 3 September 2013, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Tuesday, 3 September 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
