MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 619

DECLARING MONDAY, 15 JULY 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF GUIHULNGAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Sunday, 14 July 2013, marks the Charter Day anniversary of the City of Guihulngan, Negros Oriental;

WHEREAS, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, 15 July 2013 instead of 14 July 2013 which falls on a Sunday, be declared as a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihulngan, Negros Oriental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Guihulngan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 15 July 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Guihulngan, Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
