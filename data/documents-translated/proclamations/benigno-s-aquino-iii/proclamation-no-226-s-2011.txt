MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 226

DECLARING FRIDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOS BAÑOS, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Friday, 16 September 2011, marks the 395 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Los Baños, Laguna;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Los Baños, Laguna the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 16 September 2011, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 16 September 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Los Baños, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
