DECLARING TUESDAY, 22 MARCH 2016, AS A S P E C I A L 
(NON-WORKING) 
DAY IN THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON 
W H E R E A S , Tuesday, 22 March 2016 marks the celebration of the 18*" 
Charter Day of the City of Malaybalay, Bukidnon; 
W H E R E A S , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of 
Malaybalay, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and fSarticipate in the 
occasion with appropriate ceremonies. 
NOW, T H E R E F O R E , I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by 
authority of His Excellency. BENIGNO S . AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 
22 March 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Malaybalay. 
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal 
of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
