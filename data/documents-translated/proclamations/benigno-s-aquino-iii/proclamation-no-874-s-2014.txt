MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 874

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BANSALAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Thursday, 18 September 2014, marks the 62 nd Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Bansalan, Davao del Sur;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bansalan, Davao del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 18 September 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bansalan, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
