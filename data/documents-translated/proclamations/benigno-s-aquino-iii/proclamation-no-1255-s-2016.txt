MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1255

DECLARING MONDAY, 2 MAY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS , the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas, marks the celebration of its “Sublian Festival” on Monday, 2 May 2016;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR ., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Monday, 2 May 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
