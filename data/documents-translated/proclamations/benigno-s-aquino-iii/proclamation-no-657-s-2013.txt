MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 657

DECLARING MONDAY, 7 OCTOBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Monday, 7 October 2013, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Imus, Cavite;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Imus, Cavite, the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 7 October 2013, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 7 October 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Imus, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
