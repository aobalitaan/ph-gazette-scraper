MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 1257

DECLARING SATURDAY, 21 MAY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF MALABON

WHEREAS, Saturday, 21 May 2016, marks the Foundation Anniversary
of the City of Malabon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Malabon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO Ill, do hereby declare
Saturday, 21 May 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Malabon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

  

PAQUITO N, OCHOA, JR.
