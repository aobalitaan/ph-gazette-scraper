MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 676

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BENGUET

WHEREAS, the Province of Benguet will celebrate its Foundation Day
anniversary on Saturday, 23 November 2013;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of Benguet to celebrate
and participate in the occasion, the Governor requested that Friday, 22
November 2013 be declared as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Friday, 22 November 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Benguet.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunio set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
