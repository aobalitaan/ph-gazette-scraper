MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 280

DECLARING THURSDAY, 10 NOVEMBER 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DINAS, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Dinas, Zamboanga del Sur marks the commemoration of its 61 st Foundation Day anniversary on November 7 to 9, 2011;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Dinas, Zamboanga del Sur participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Bayan requested that 10 November 2011 be declared as a special (non-working) day.

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Dinas, Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 10 November 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dinas, Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
