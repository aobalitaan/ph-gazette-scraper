MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 755

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 21 MAY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MALABON

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 21 May 2014, marks the 415 th Foundation Day Anniversary of the City of Malabon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Malabon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 21 May 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Malabon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR, Executive Secretary
