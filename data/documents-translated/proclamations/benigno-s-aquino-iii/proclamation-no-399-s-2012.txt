MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 399

DECLARING TUESDAY, 19 JUNE 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA AND THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS , Tuesday, 19 June 2012 marks the 151 st birth anniversary of Dr. Jose P. Rizal, our national hero;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba and the Province of Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies in keeping with his ideals and teachings.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Tuesday, 19 June 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba and the Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
