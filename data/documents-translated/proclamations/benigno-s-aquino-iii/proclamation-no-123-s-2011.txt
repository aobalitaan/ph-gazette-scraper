MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 123

DECLARING TUESDAY, MARCH 22, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Tuesday, March 22, 2011, marks the birth anniversary of Gen. Emilio Aguinaldo, the First President of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Cavite be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Tuesday, March 22, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10 th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
