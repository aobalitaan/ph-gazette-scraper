MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 784

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JUNE 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Monday, 16 June 2014 marks the 66 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 16 June 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
