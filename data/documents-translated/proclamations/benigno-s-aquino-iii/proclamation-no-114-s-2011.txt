MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 114

DECLARING THURSDAY, MARCH 10, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TANAUAN, BATANGAS

WHEREAS , Thursday, March 10, 2011, marks the 10 th Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Tanauan, Batangas;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tanauan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW , therefore, I, Paquito N. Ochoa Jr., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, Benigno S. Aquino III, do hereby declare Thursday, March 10, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the City of Tanauan, Batangas.

IN WITNESS THEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18 day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
