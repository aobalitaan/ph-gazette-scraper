MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 250

DECLARING MONDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SIQUIJOR, PROVINCE OF SIQUIJOR

WHEREAS, Monday, 12 September 2011, marks the commemoration of the Municipality of Siquijor as the capital of the Province of Siquijor;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Siquijor, Province of Siquijor the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 12 September 2011, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S, AQUINO III , do hereby declare Monday, 12 September 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Siquijor, Province of Siquijor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
