MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 502

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 5 DECEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija marks the 47 th anniversary of its Foundation Day on December 3-5, 2012;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Palayan the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor has requested that 5 December 2012 be declared as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 5 December 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13 th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
