MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 169

RESERVING A PARCEL OF LAND IN SITIO BOBOWA, BARANGAY TABANGOHAY, MUNICIPALITY OF ALANG-ALANG, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, ISLAND OF LEYTE AS TRAINING, RESEARCH AND EDUCATIONAL CENTER UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE-AGRICULTURAL TRAINING INSTITUTE (DA-ATI)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale or settlement and reserve a certain parcel of land of the public domain for the DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE-AGRICULTURAL TRAINING INSTITUTE (DA-ATI) REGIONAL TRAINING CENTER, subject to final survey and private rights, if any there be, situated in Sitio Bobowa, Barangay Tabangohay, Municipality of Alang-alang, Province of Leyte, Island of Leyte and more particularly described as follows:

SK-08-000073, MBM No. 18, Cad-220, Tacloban Cadastre

AGRICULTURAL TRAINING INSTITUTE REGIONAL TRAINING CENTER

A PARCEL OF LAND (SK-08-000073, MBM No. 18, Cad-220) situated in Sitio Bobowa, Barangay Tabangohay, Municipality of Alang-alang, Province of Leyte, Island of Leyte.

Beginning from a point marked “1” on the plan being S. 81 deg. 04’W., 164.92 m. from MBM No. 18, Cad-220, Tacloban Cadastre, thence:

beginning. Containing an area of TWO HUNDRED EIGHTY ONE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED FIFTY NINE (281,259) SQUARE METERS, more or less.

The area within timberland shall revert to the mass of the public domain should the same no longer serve the purposes for which it was reserved.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16 th day of May, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
