PROCLAMATION NO. 373

DECLARING MONDAY, 28 MAY 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Monday, 28 May 2012, the Municipality of Imus, Cavite marks the anniversary of the Battle of Alapan, otherwise known as the National Flag Day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Imus, Cavite be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 28 May 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
