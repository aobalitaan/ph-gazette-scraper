MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1098

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 16 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON­-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 16 September 2015, marks the 107th Foundation Day of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 16 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
