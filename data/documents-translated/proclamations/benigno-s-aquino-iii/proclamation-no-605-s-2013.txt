MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 605

DECLARING MONDAY, 1 JULY 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAMAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Monday, 1 July 2013, marks the 56 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Maramag, Province of Bukidnon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Maramag, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 1 July 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maramag, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
