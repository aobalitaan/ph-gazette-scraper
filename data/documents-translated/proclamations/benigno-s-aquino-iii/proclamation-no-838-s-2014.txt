MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 838

DECLARING FRIDAY, 8 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAYOMBONG, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA

WHEREAS, on Friday, 8 August 2014, the Municipality of Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya marks the celebration of the “Araw ng Bayombong”;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 8 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bayombong, Province of Nueva Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, Ihave hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
