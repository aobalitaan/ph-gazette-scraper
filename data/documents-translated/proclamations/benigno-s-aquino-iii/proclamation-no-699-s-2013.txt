MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 699

DECLARING SATURDAY, 4 JANUARY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAUJAN, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS , the Municipality of Naujan, Oriental Mindoro marks the celebration of its 109 th Foundation Day anniversary on Saturday, 4 January 2014;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Naujan, Oriental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Saturday, 4 January 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Naujan, Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
