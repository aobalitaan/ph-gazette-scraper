MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 623

DECLARING THURSDAY, 25 JULY 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DAPITAN

WHEREAS, Thursday, 25 July 2013, marks the celebration of the feast of Saint James the Greater and the holding of the “Kinabayo Festival;”

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dapitan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 25 July 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Dapitan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
