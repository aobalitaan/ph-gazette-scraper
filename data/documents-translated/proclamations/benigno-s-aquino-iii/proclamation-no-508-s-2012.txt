MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 508

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, the Province of Sarangani marks its 19 th Foundation Day anniversary and the 10 th Munah’to Festival on November 28-30, 2012;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Province of Sarangani the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, the Office of the Governor has requested that 28 November 2012 be declared as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Wednesday, 28 November 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
