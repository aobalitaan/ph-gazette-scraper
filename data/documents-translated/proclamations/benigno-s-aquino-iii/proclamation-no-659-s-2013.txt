PROCLAMATION NO. 659

DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND, SITUATED AT BARANGAYS PANTAY BATA, LAUREL, ULANGO, TANAUAN CITY AND BARANGAY STA. ANASTACIA, STO. TOMAS, BATANGAS, AS INCLUSIONS TO THE EXISTING FIRST PHILIPPINE INDUSTRIAL PARK-SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7961, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916 , otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , President of the Philippines, do hereby designate several parcels of land, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 11-421 (s. 2011) of the PEZA Board of Directors, located at Barangays Pantay Bata, Laurel, Ulango, Tanauan City and Barangay Sta. Anastacia, Sto. Tomas, Batangas, as inclusions to the existing First Philippine Industrial Park-Special Economic Zone , with an aggregate area of One Hundred Nineteen Thousand Nine Hundred Fifty (119,950) Square Meters , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
