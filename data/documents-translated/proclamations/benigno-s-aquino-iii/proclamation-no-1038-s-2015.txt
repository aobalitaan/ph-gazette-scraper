MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1038

DECLARING TUESDAY, 26 MAY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BALIWAG, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 26 May 2015, marks the commemoration of the Charter Day anniversary of the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 26 May 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Baliwag, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
