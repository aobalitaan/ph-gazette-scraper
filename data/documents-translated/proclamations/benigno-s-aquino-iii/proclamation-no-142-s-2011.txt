MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 142

DECLARING MONDAY, MAY 2, 2011 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, April 27 -May 3, 2011 marks the week-long celebration of the town fiesta of the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas;

WHEREAS, it is. but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the . occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Monday, May 2, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
