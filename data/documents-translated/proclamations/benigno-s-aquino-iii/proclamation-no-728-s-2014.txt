MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 728

DECLARING FRIDAY, 7 MARCH 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS , Friday, 7 March 2014, marks the celebration of the Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Basilan;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Basilan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Friday, 7 March 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
