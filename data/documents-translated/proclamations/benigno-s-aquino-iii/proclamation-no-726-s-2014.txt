MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 726

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 MARCH 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 18 March 2014, marks the commemoration of the 113 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Masbate;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Masbate be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 18 March 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
