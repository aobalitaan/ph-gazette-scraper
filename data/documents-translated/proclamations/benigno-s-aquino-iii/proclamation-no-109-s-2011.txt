MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 109

DECLARING TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 22, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING DAY) IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN PEDRO, LAGUNA

WHEREAS , Tuesday, February 22, 2011, marks the celebration of the town fiesta of the Municipality of San Pedro, Laguna, in honor of its patron saint, St. Peter the Apostle;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San Pedro, Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Tuesday, February 22, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Pedro, Laguna.

IN WITNESS THEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9 th Day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
