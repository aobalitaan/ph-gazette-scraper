PROCLAMATION NO. 308

DECLARING MONDAY, 16 JANUARY 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN

WHEREAS, the City of Pagadian will celebrate its annual city fiesta clubbed as “Pasalamat Festival” on January 7 to 16, 2012;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Pagadian participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, 16 January 2012 be declared as special (non-working) day.

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pagadian be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 16 January 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Pagadian.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR.

Executive Secretary
