MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 450

DECLARING TUESDAY, 4 SEPTEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley, marks the celebration of its Foundation Day anniversary and the Kariyawan Festival on Tuesday, 4 September 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Monkayo, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 4 September 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
