PROCLAMATION NO. 1276

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING, LOCATED AT 7 th AVENUE CORNER 25 th ST., BONIFACIO GLOBAL CITY, FORT BONIFACIO, TAGUIG CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate a building, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolutions Nos. 11-066 (s. 2011) and 15-240 (s. 2015), and Resolution No. 15-163 (s. 2015) of the PEZA Board of Directors, as an Information Technology Center, to be known as the Twenty-five Seven McKinley, with a gross floor area of Thirty-Four Thousand Three Hundred Eighty-Two square meters and Forty-Four square decimeters (34,382.44), which stands on a parcel of land, located at 7 th Avenue cor. 25 th St., Bonifacio Global City, Fort Bonifacio, Taguig City, with a land area of One Thousand Six Hundred (1,600) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:

Lot 4, Block 7 Pcs-00-007928 TCT No. 164-2015000586
