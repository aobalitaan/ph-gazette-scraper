MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1049

DECLARING THURSDAY, 18 JUNE 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 18 June 2015, marks the Foundation Day of the Municipality of Sta. Maria, Davao del Sur;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Sta. Maria, Davao del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 18 June 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Maria, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
