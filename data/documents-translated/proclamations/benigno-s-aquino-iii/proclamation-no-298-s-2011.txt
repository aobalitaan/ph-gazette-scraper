MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 298

DECLARING THURSDAY, 8 DECEMBER 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TAGUIG

WHEREAS, the City of Taguig marks the celebration of its Foundation Day anniversary on Thursday, 8 December 2011;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Taguig be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 8 December 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Taguig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of December, in the year of Our Lord. Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
