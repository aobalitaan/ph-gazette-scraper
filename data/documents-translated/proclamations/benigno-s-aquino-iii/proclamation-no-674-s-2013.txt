MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 674

DECLARING MONDAY, 4 NOVEMBER 2013, 4S A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, Monday, 4 November 2013, marks the remembrance and
recognition of the heroism of Hermano Puli, the great man from Lukban, Quezon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Quezon, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO Ill, do hereby declare
Monday, 4 November 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18thday ofOctober , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authoiity of the President:

 
  
 

PAQUITO NV CCHOA JR.
