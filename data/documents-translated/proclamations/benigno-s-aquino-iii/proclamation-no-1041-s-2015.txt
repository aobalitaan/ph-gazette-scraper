MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1041

DECLARING SATURDAY, 27 JUNE 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF EL SALVADOR, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the City of El Salvador, Misamis Oriental, marks the commemoration of its Charter Day anniversary on Saturday, 27 June 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of El Salvador, Misamis Oriental, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 27 June 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of El Salvador, Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
