MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 741

DECLARING SATURDAY, 26 APRIL 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF
BATANGAS

WHEREAS, ihe Municipality of San Jose, Batangas, marks ithe
commemoration of its Foundation Day anniversary on Saturday, 26 April 2014;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
San Jose, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Saturday, 26 April 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San
Jose, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this18th day of March , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

  
  

PAQUITO N/OCHOA JR.
Execute Secretary
