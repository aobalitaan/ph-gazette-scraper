MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 68

DECLARING THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 25, 2010, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, the Province of Sarangani, will celebrate its 18th Foundation Day anniversary and the 8th Munah’to Festival on November 25 to 27, 2010;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Saragani be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, November 25, 2010 as special (non-working) day in the Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the city of Manila, this 12th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
