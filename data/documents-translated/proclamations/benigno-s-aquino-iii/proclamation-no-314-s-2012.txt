PROCLAMATION NO. 314

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT BRGY. 47, PEÑARANDA EXTENSION, LEGAZPI CITY, ALBAY AS AN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 07-232 (s. 2007) and Resolution No. 10-361 (s. 2010), of the PEZA Board of Directors, several parcels of land located at Brgy. 47, Peñaranda Extension, Legazpi City, Albay, as an Agro-Industrial Economic Zone to be known as CIIF Agro-Industrial Park – Legazpi, with an aggregate area of FIFTY THREE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED FORTY NINE (53,449) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
