MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1111

DECLARING THURSDAY, 3 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 3 September 2015, the City of Iriga marks the celebration of its 47 th Charter Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 3 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
