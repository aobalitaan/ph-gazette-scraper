MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 519

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED AT PUROK 3, TABLON, CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY, AS AN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 , AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 07-232 (s. 2007) and Resolution No. 11-097 (s. 2011), as amended by Resolution No. 11-627 (s. 2011), of the PEZA Board of Directors, several parcels of land located at Purok 3, Tablon, Cagayan de Oro City, as agro-industrial economic zone to be known as CIIF Agro-Industrial Park – Cagayan, with an aggregate area of Fifty-Four Thousand Seven Hundred Sixty-Three (54,763) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
