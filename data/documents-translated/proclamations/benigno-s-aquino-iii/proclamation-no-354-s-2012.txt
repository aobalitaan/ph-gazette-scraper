PROCLAMATION NO. 354

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 1746 (s. 2009) WHICH DECLARED A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY CENTRO WEST, MUNICIPALITY OF PALANAN, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, BY GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES-PHILIPPINE NATIONAL POLICE (RP-PNP)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 1746, which reserved certain parcels of public land of the public domain in Barangay Centro West, Municipality of Palanan, Province of Isabela, Island of Luzon by granting ownership thereof in favor of RP-PNP, which parcel of land is particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 545-A, Csd-af-02-002361-D

Equivalent to Lot 8653, Cad-939-D

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot No. 545-A, Csd-af-02-002361-D), being a portion of Lot No. 545, Cad-939-D equivalent toLot8653. Cad-939-D as surveyed for PNP situated in the Barangay Centro West,MunicipalityofPalanan,ProvinceofIsabela,IslandofLuzon. Bounded on the Northwest along line 1-2; on the Northeast along line 2-3; on the Southeast along line 3-4 all by Lot 545-B; on the Southwest along line 4-1 by Lot 545-C (Existing Road), all of the subdivision plan; Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S. 14 deg. 03′ E., 107.36 m. from BLLM 1, Cad-939-D, thence:

Containing an area of FIVE HUNDRED SIXTY-SEVEN (567) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by PS Cyl Conc. Mons. 15 x 40 cms. Bearings true; the subdivision survey was executed by Geodetic Engineer Rolly M. Abrogena on 19 October 2003 and was approved on 14 November 2003.

The DENR Secretary is hereby authorized to issue a Special Patent covering the area herein granted.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 20 th day of March, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
