MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1293

DECLARING THURSDAY, 23 JUNE 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BATAC, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE

WHEREAS, Thursday, 23 June 2016, marks the celebration of the Charter Day of the City of Batac, Ilocos Norte;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Batac, Ilocos Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declareThursday, 23 June 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Batac, Ilocos Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
