MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 1267

DECLARING SATURDAY, 18 JUNE 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO
DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Saturday, 18 June 2016, marks the Foundation Day of the
Municipality of Sta. Maria, Davao del Sur;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Sta. Maria, Davao del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S, AQUINO Ill, do hereby declare
Saturday, 18 June 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Sta. Maria, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this l1tiday of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.
