MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 908

DECLARING TUESDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 25 November 2014, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Maragusan, Compostela Valley;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Maragusan, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 25 November 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maragusan, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
