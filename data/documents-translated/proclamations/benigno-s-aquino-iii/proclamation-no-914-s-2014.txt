MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 914

DECLARING MONDAY, 8 DECEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TAGUIG

WHEREAS, the City of Taguig marks Monday, 8 December 2014, as its Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Taguig be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 8 December 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Taguig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
