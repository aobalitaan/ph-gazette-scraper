MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 122

DECLARING MONDAY, MARCH 14, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASSI, PROVINCE OF ILOILO

WHEREAS , Monday, March 14, 2011, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Passi, Province of Iloilo;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Passi be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Monday, March 14, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the City of Passi.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10 th day March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
