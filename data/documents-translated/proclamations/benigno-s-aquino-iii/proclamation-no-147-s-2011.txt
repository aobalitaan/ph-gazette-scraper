PROCLAMATION NO. 147

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PROPOSED BUILDING TO BE KNOWN AS ONE GLOBAL PLACE TO BE ESTABLISHED ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT LOT 1 BLOCK 9, 5 th AVENUE CORNER 25 th STREET, BONIFACIO SOUTH DISTRICT, BONIFACIO GLOBAL CITY, TAGUIG CITY, METRO MANILA, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 (s. 2004) and Resolution No. 08-613 (s. 2008) of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, One Global Place as an Information Technology Center, with a gross floor area of 34,554 square meters, more or less, to be established on a parcel of land located at Lot 1 Block 9, 5 th Avenue corner 25 th Street, Bonifacio South District, Bonifacio Global City, Taguig City, Metro Manila, containing an area of ONE THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED (1,800) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
