MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.1262

DECLARING MONDAY, 6 JUNE 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY
IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, Monday, 6 June 2016, marks the 64" Foundation
Anniversary of the Province of Zamboanga del Norte;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Zamboanga del Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, PAQUITO N. GCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO II, do hereby declare
Monday, 6 June 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Zamboanga del Norie.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11thday of May _ , in the year of Our
Lord, Twe Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

  
  

PAQUITO Iv OCHOA, JR.
Executive Secretary
