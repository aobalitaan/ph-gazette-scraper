BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES 
PROCLAMATION NO.i261 
DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 1 JUNE 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) 
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAYOYAO. PROVINCE OF IFUGAO 
WHEREAS, Wednesday, 1 June 2016, marks the Foundation Day of the 
Municipality of Mayoyao, Ifugao; 
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of 
Mayoyao, Ifugao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the 
occasion with appropriate ceremonies. 
NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR.. Executive Secretary, 
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III. do hereby declare 
Wednesday, 1 June 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of 
Mayoyao, Ifugao. 
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, t have hereunto set my hand and caused the 
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. 
11th 
Done in the City of Manila, this 
day of May. in the year of Our Lord.
