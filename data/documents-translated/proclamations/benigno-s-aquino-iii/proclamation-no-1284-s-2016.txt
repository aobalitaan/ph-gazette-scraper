MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1284

DECLARING FRIDAY, 1 JULY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAMAG, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, Friday, 1 July 2016, marks the 60 th Foundation Day of the Municipality of Maramag, Province of Bukidnon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Maramag, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 1 July 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maramag, Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
