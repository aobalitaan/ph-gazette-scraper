MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 911

DECLARING TUESDAY, 2 DECEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASAY

WHEREAS, the City of Pasay shall celebrate its 151 st Foundation Day anniversary on Tuesday, 2 December 2014;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasay be given full opportunity to participate in and celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 2 December 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Pasay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
