MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 422

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND, TO BE INCLUDED TO THE CARMELRAY INDUSTRIAL PARK I – SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, LOCATED AT CANLUBANG, CALAMBA CITY, LAGUNA, AN EXISTING SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 11-572 (s. 2011) of the PEZA Board of Directors, a parcel of land, to be included to the Carmelray Industrial Park I – Special Economic Zone, an existing Special Economic Zone, located at Canlubang, Calamba City, Laguna, containing an area of Eighty-Seven Thousand Forty-Four (87,044) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:

Lot 1, Pcs-04-010975 TCT No. T-186464
