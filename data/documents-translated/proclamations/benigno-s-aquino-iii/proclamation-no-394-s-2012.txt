MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 394

DECLARING MONDAY, 18 JUNE 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALILANGAN, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Kalilangan, Bukidnon marks its 46 th Foundation Day anniversary on Monday, 18 June 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Kalilangan, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 18 June 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kalilangan, Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
