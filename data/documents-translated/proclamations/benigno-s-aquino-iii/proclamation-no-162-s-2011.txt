MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 162

DECLARING THURSDAY, JUNE 16, 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Thursday, June 16, 2011, marks the 63 rd Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, June 16, 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
