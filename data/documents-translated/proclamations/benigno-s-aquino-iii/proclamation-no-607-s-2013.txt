MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 607

DECLARING TUESDAY, 2 JULY 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, the City of Pasig marks the celebration of its 440 th Anniversary on Tuesday, 2 July 2013;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 2 July 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
