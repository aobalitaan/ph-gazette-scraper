MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 855

DECLARING TUESDAY, 9 SEPTEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BAIS

WHEREAS , Tuesday, 9 September 2014, marks the 46 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Bais;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Bais the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 9 September 2014, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 9 September 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bais.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
