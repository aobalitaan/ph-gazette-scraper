MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 779

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 18 JUNE 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARIA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 18 June 2014, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Sta. Maria, Davao Del Sur;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Sta. Maria, Davao Del Sur, the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 18 June 2014, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 18 June 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Maria, Province of Davao Del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of May , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
