MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 650

DECLARING TUESDAY, 17 SEPTEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONTOC, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 17 September 2013, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province, the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 17 September 2013 as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 17 September 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bontoc, Mountain Province.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
