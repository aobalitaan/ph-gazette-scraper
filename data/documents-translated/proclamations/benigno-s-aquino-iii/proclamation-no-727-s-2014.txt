MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 727

DECLARING FRIDAY, 28 FEBRUARY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TANGUB

WHEREAS, Friday, 28 February 2014, marks the commemoration of the Charter Day anniversary of the City of Tangub;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 28 February 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tangub.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
