MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 744

DECLARING MONDAY, 31 MARCH 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PANABO

WHEREAS, Monday, 31 March 2014, marks the celebration of the 13 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Panabo;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Panabo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 31 March 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Panabo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
