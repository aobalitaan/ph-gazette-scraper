MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 424

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND, TO BE INCLUDED TO THE TECO-SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, LOCATED AT BARANGAYS BUNDAGUL, PARALAYUNAN AND MANGALIT, MUNICIPALITY OF MABALACAT, PROVINCE OF PAMPANGA, AN EXISTING SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution Nos. 11-714 (s. 2011) and 12-023 (s. 2012), as amended by Resolution No. 12-139 (s. 2012), of the PEZA Board of Directors, several parcels of land, to be included to the TECO-Special Economic Zone, an existing Special Economic Zone, located at Barangays Bundagul, Paralayunan and Mangalit, Mabalacat, Pampanga, with an aggregate area of Seven Hundred Nineteen Thousand Eighty-Four (719,084) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
