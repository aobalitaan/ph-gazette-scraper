MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 639

DECLARING THURSDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY LIPA

WHEREAS , the City of Lipa will host thousands of devotees for the National Pilgrimage, a day of prayer for peace and the sanctification of the clergy on Thursday, 12 September 2013;

WHEREAS , in order for the people of the City of Lipa to participate in the event, the Office of the Mayor requested that 12 September 2013 be declared as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, 12 September 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
