MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO, 1292

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 JUNE 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LAUREL, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Laurel, Batangas, marks the celebration
of its Foundation Anniversary on Tuesday, 21 June 2016;

‘WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Laurel, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO Ill, do hereby declare
Tuesday, 21 June 2076, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Laurel, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Twe Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

  
 

PAQUITO N., OCHOA, JR.
