MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 421

DECLARING A PARCEL OF RECLAIMED LAND LOCATED IN BARANGAY VICTORY VILLAGE, CITY OF LEGAZPI, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, ISLAND OF LUZON, AS ALIENABLE AND DISPOSABLE AND TO BE DISPOSED OF IN FAVOR OF THE PHILIPPINE RECLAMATION AUTHORITY, AMENDING FOR THE PURPOSE PROCLAMATION NO. 2102 (S. 2010)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare a parcel of reclaimed land as Alienable and Disposable (A&D) and no longer needed for public purpose, to be disposed of in favor of the Philippine Reclamation Authority, subject to private rights, if any there be, identified under Plan RI-05-000005-D, situated in Barangay Victory Village, City of Legazpi, Province of Albay, and more particularly described as follows:
