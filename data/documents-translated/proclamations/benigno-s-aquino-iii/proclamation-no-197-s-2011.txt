MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THEPHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION No. 197

DECLARING TUESDAY, 2 AUGUST 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BUTUAN

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 2 August 2011, marks the 61 st Charter Day anniversary of the City of Butuan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Butuan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 2 August 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Butuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
