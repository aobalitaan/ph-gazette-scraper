MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 797

DECLARING SATURDAY, 26 JULY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IPIL, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA SIBUGAY

WHEREAS, Saturday, 26 July 2014, the Municipality of Ipil, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay marks the celebration of the “64 Araw ng Ipil;”

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Ipil, Zamboanga Sibugay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 26 July 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Ipil, Province of Zamboanga Sibugay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
