MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1202

DECLARING TUESDAY, 26 JANUARY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON­-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CADIZ

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 26 January 2016, marks the celebration of the 42 nd Dinagsa Festival of the City of Cadiz;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cadiz be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 26 January 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cadiz.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
