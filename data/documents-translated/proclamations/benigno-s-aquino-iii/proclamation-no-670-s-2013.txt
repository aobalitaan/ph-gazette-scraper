MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO.670

DECLARING FRIDAY, 6 DECEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MLANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS , the Municipality of Mlang, Province of Cotabato will celebrate the 5th Kawayanan Festival during the first week of December;

WHEREAS , to enable the people of the Municipality of Mlang, Cotabato to celebrate and participate in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Friday, 6 December 2013 be declared as a special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Friday, 6 December 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mlang, Province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
