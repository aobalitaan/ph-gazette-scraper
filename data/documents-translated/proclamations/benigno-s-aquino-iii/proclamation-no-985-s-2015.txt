MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 985

DECLARING FRIDAY, 20 MARCH 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MALAYBALAY

WHEREAS, Friday, 20 March 2015 marks the celebration of the 17 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Malaybalay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Malaybalay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR ., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Friday, 20 March 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Malaybalay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
