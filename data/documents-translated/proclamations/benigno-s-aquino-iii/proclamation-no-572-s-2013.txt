MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 572

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING, LOCATED AT ABANAO CORNER ZANDUETA STREETS, BAGUIO CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 , AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution No. 11-066 (s. 2011), and Resolution No. 12-582 (s. 2012), of the PEZA Board of Directors, a building which stands on several parcels of land, with an aggregate area of One Thousand Three Hundred Fifty (1,350) Square Meters, more or less, located at Abanao Street corner Zandueta Streets, Baguio City, as an Information Technology Center, to be known as the Abanao Square.
