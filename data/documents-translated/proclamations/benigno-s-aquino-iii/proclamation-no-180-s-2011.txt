MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 180

DECLARING SATURDAY, 11 JUNE 2011 AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAGAY

WHEREAS, Saturday, 11 June 2011 marks the 15 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Sagay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sagay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 11 June 2011 as a special (non-working) day in the City of Sagay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
