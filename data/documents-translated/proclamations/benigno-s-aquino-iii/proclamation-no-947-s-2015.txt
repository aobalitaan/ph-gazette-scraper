MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 947

DECLARING TUESDAY, 3 FEBRUARY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 3 February 2015, the City of Cabanatuan marks the commemoration of its 65 th Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabanatuan, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 3 February 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
