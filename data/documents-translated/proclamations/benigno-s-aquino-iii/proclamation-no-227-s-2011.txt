MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 227

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 7 SEPTEMBER 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 7 September 2011, marks the 440 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Cavite;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Cavite the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 7 September 2011, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 7 September 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
