MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 264

DECLARING MONDAY, 10 OCTOBER 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWAYAN, BILIRAN

WHEREAS, Monday, 10 October 2011, marks the Charter Day anniversary of the Municipality of Kawayan, Biliran;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Kawayan, Biliran be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 10 October 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kawayan, Biliran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
