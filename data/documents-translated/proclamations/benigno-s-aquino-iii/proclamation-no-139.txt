PROCLAMATION NO. 139

DECLARING ALIWAGWAG AS PROTECTED AREA UNDER THE CATEGORY OF PROTECTED LANDSCAPE AND ITS PERIPHERAL AREAS AS BUFFER ZONE SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF BOSTON AND CATEEL, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL AND IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY AND IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF BOSTON AND CATEEL, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL PURSUANT TO R.A. 7586 (NIPAS ACT OF 1992)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested upon me by law, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby set aside and declare Aliwagwag, situated in the Municipality of Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley and in the Municipalities of Boston and Cateel, Province of Davao Oriental, as protected area and shall be known as Aliwagwag Protected Landscape (APL), and its peripheral areas as Buffer Zone, subject to private rights, and without prejudice to the rights of indigenous peoples, as provided for in R.A. No. 8371, DENR Administrative Order No. 93-02 and other related rules and regulations, if any there be, as described in DENR NIPAS Map No. RO-XI-4.

The Aliwagwag Protected Landscape and its Buffer Zone shall be subject to actual ground survey and delineation by the DENR.

Any contract, permit or license for the extraction or utilization of natural resources therein already existing prior to this proclamation shall, subject to national interest, be respected until its expiration. Areas covered by such contracts, permits or licenses which are found not viable for development after assessment and/or exploration shall automatically form part of the Aliwagwag Protected Landscape. Likewise, all property and private rights within the APL already existing and/or vested prior to this Proclamation shall be respected in accordance with existing laws.

The Aliwagwag Protected Landscape and its Buffer Zone shall be under the administrative jurisdiction of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and shall be managed in accordance with the provisions of the NIPAS Act of 1992 and its Implementing Rules and Regulations.
