PROCLAMATION NO. 223

CREATING AND DESIGNATING AN EXISTING BUILDING TO BE KNOWN AS RELIANCE I.T. CENTER ESTABLISHED ON A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT 99 E. RODRIGUEZ JR., BARRIO UGONG, PASIG CITY, METRO MANILA, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I , BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 10-476 (s. 2010) of the PEZA Board of Directors, the Reliance I.T. Center with a gross floor area of 19,824 square meters, more or less, established on a parcel of land located at 99 E. Rodriguez Jr., Barrio Ugong, Pasig City, Metro Manila, containing an area of FOUR THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY FOUR (4,184) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
