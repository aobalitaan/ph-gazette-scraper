MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 767

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND, LOCATED AT BARANGAY PALAMPAS, SAN CARLOS CITY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 , AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, or the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate several parcels of land, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 10-106 (s. 2010), as amended by Resolution Nos. 10-387 (s. 2010) and 11-494 (s. 2011), of the PEZA Board of Directors, as a Special Economic Zone, located at Barangay Palampas, San Carlos City, Negros Occidental, to be known as the San Carlos Ecozone II, with an aggregate area of Three Hundred Ninety-Three Thousand Five Hundred Seventy-Three (393,573) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:

Lot No. 775-A Psd-06-012754

A parcel of land (Lot 775-A, Psd-06-012754, being a portion of Lot 775, Cad-149, San Carlos Cadastre), situated in the Barrio of Palampas, City of San Carlos, Island of Negros.

Bounded on the SE., SW., NW., & NE., along lines 1-2-3-4-5-6-7-1 by Lot 775-B, of this subdivision survey.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on the plan being, N. 4 deg. 11′ E., 415.75 m. from BLLM No. 16, Cad-149, San Carlos Cadastre;

beginning; containing an area of Thirty-Five Thousand Five Hundred Sixty-Five (35,565) square meters, more or less.

All points referred to are indicated on the subdivision plan and are marked on the ground by P.S. cyl. conc. mons., bearings true, surveyed by Engr. Ernesto F. Ciriaco on 06 January 1988 and approved on 05 February 1988.

Lot No. 775-B-2-A Psd-06-067124

Beginning at a point “1” on plan, being S. 85 deg. 33′ E., 215.50 m. from BBM No. 16, San Carlos Cadastre 149;

beginning; containing an area of Two Hundred Fifty-Five Thousand Eight Hundred Eighteen (255,818) square meters, more or less.

All corners referred to are indicated on plan and are marked on the ground by P.S. cyl. conc. mons., 15×40 cm., bearings true, surveyed by Engr. Alexander G. Celis on 05 November 2006 and approved on 28 November 2006.
