MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 104

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 11, 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TALISAY, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, Friday, February 11, 2011, marks the Charter Day anniversary of the City of Talisay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Talisay, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Friday, February 11, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the City of Talisay, Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
