MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 531

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING, LOCATED AT SAPPHIRE AND GARNET ROADS, ORTIGAS CENTER, PASIG CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution Nos. 00-411 (s. 2000) and 11-665 (s. 2011) of the PEZA Board of Directors, a building, located at Sapphire and Garnet Roads, Ortigas Center, Pasig City, containing an area of Two Thousand Five Hundred (2,500) Square Meters, more or less, as an Information Technology Center, to be known as Cyberscape Alpha . The land is defined by the following technical description:

Lot 2-B, Psd-00-036193

A parcel of land [Lot 2-B of the subdivision plan Psd-00-036193, being a portion of Lot 2, Block 4 (LRC) Psd-144362, LRC Rec. Nos. 699, 875 & 917], situated in (Oranbo) San Antonio, City of Pasig, Metro Manila, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the SW., along line 1-2 by Road Lot 12 (LEC) Psd-105493 (Garnet St.); on the NW., along line 2-3 by Lot 2-A of the subdivision plan; on the NE., along line 3-4 by Road Lot 13 (LRC) Psd-105493 (Sapphire St.); and on the SE., along line 4-1 by Lot 2-C of the subdivision plan.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 80 deg. 24’E., 1020.70 m. from BLLM #1, Wack-Wack;

beginning; containing an area of Two Thousand Five Hundred Square Meters (2,500), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by PS cyl. conc. mons. 15 x 60 cms; bearings true; date of original survey 08 April 1920 – 21 February 1921 (Mandaluyong est.); and that of the subdivision survey, 02 June 1995 and was approved on 10 July 1995.

IN WITNESS THEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed hereto.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 4th day of January, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III
