PROCLAMATION NO. 491

CREATING AND DESIGNATING ELEVEN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN LOCATED AT ASEANA BUSINESS PARK, PARAÑAQUE CITY, AS A TOURISM ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 , AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 02-342 (s. 2002) and Resolution No. 10-204 (s. 2010), as amended, of the PEZA Board of Directors, eleven (11) parcels of land of the private domain as a Tourism Economic Zone located at Aseana Business Park, Paranaque City with an aggregate area of Sixty Nine Thousand Five Hundred Ten (69,510) Square Meters, more less, as defined by the following technical description:
