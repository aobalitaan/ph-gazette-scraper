MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1235

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 MARCH 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BONGABONG, ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro marks the celebration of the “Sulyog Festival” on March 17 – 19, 2016;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro, to celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Friday, 18 March 2016, be declared as a special (non-working) day;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 18 March 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bongabong, Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
