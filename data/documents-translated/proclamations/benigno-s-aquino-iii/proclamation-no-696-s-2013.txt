DECLARING THURSDAY, 16 JANUARY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-

WHEREAS, the City of Navotas marks the commemoration of its lOS**^ 
Foundation Day anniversary on Thursday, 16 January 2014; 
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of 
Navotas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, 
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare 
Thursday, 16 January 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of 
Navotas. 
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the 
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. 
Done in the City of Manila, this 17thday of December, in the year of Our 
Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.
