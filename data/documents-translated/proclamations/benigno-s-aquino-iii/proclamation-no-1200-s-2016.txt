MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO . 1200

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 JANUARY 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON­-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF LIPA

WHEREAS, the City of Lipa marks the celebration of the Barako Festival 2016 on Wednesday, 20 January 2016;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Lipa be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR, Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 20 January 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
