MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 500

RENAMING THE CATICLAN AIRPORT TO GODOFREDO P. RAMOS AIRPORT

WHEREAS, the “Father of Aklan,” Godofredo P. Ramos, a former Aklan Congressman and Governor and Justice of the Court of Appeals, authored Republic Act No. 1414, officially separating Aklan from Capiz; and

WHEREAS, the renaming of Caticlan Airport to Godofredo P. Ramos Airport is in recognition of his dedication to public service and unwavering commitment to the development of the Province of Aklan.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby rename the Caticlan Airport to Godofredo P. Ramos Airport.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 7th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
