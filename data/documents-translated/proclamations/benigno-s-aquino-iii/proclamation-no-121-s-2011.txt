MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 121

DECLARING THURSDAY, MARCH 31, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PANABO

WHEREAS , the City of Panabo marks its “10 th Araw ng Panabo City” on March 24 –31, 2011;

WHEREAS , in order to give the people of the City of Panabo full opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare March 31, 2011 as a non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, March 31, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the City of Panabo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8 th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
