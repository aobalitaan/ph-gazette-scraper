MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 866

DECLARING FRIDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY LIPA

WHEREAS, the City of Lipa will host thousands of devotees for the National Pilgrimage, a day of prayer for peace and the sanctification of the clergy on Friday, 12 September 2014;

WHEREAS, considering the volume of devotees who will participate in the event, the Office of the Mayor requested that 12 September 2014 be declared as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 12 September 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
