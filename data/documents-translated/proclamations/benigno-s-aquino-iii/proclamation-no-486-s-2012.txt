MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 486

DECLARING MONDAY, 26 NOVEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DASMARIÑAS

WHEREAS, the City of Dasmariñas marks the commemoration of its Foundation Day anniversary on Monday, 26 November 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dasmariñas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 26 November 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Dasmariñas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4 th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
