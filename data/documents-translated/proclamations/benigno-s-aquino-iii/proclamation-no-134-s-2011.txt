PROCLAMATION NO. 134

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN LOCATED AT BARANGAY KAMANGA, MUNICIPALITY OF MAASIM, PROVINCE OF SARANGANI, AS AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916 , otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I , BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 07-232 dated 16 May 2007 and Resolution No. 10-457 dated 22 September 2010, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at Barangay Kamanga, Municipality of Maasim, Province of Sarangani, containing an area of Five Hundred Forty Six Thousand and Thirteen (546,013) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
