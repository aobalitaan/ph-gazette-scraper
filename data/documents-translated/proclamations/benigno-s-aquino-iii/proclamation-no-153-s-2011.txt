MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 153

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, APRIL 27, 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA

WHEREAS, April 21, 2011, marks the 10 th Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Calamba;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Calamba to celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor of the said City requested that Wednesday, April 27, 2011, be declared as a special (non-working) day instead of April 21, 2011, which falls on a Maundy Thursday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, April 27, 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba.

WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
