MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 210

DECLARING MONDAY, 1 AUGUST 2011, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, Monday, 1 August 2011, marks the 63 rd Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Compostela, Compostela Valley;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Compostela, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 1 August 2011, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Compostela, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
