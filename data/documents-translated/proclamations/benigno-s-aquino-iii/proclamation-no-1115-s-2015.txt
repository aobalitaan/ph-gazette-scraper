MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1115

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 30 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-­WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MASBATE

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 30 September 2015, marks the 15th Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Masbate;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Masbate be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 30 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Masbate.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
