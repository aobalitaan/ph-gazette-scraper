MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 587

DECLARING MONDAY, 17 JUNE 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MOLAVE, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, 16 June 2013 marks the 65 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur to celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, 17 June 2013 be declared as special (non-working) day instead of 16 June 2013, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 17 June 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Molave, Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
