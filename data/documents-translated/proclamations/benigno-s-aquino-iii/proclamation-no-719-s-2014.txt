MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 719

DECLARING FRIDAY, 7 MARCH 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, Friday, 7 March 2014 marks the celebration of the 16 th Araw ng Tagum ;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagum be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 7 March 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
