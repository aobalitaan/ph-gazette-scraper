MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO.677

DECLARING THURSDAY, 31 OCTOBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PORAC, PROVINCE OF
PAMPANGA

»

WHEREAS, on Thursday, 31 October 2013, the Municipality of Porac,
Pampanga marks the celebration of its 419" Foundation Day anniversary:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Porac, Pampanga, be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Thursday, 31 October 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Porac, Pampanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of October, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

 
  
 

PAQUITO N/OCHOA JR.
