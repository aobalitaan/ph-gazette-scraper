MALACAÑANG PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1151

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 NOVEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija will celebrate its annual festival of thanksgiving for bountiful harvest on Monday, 23 November 2015;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Palayan to celebrate the occasion, the Office of the City Mayor has requested that 23 November 2015 be declared as a special (non-working) day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 23 November 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
