MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 678

DECLARING TUESDAY, 12 NOVEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 12 November 2013 the City of Valenzuela marks the celebration of the “Araw ng Valenzuela” in observance of its 390 th Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Valenzuela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 12 November 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
