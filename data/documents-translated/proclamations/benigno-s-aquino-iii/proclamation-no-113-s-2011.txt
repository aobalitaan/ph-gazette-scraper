MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 113

DECLARING TUESDAY, MARCH 8, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS , Tuesday, March 8, 2011, marks the 4 th Bulawan Festival: 13 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Compostela Valley, with a theme “Stronger, Luckier, Getting Better ComVal @ 13!”;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW , therefore, I, Paquito N. Ochoa Jr., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, Benigno S. Aquino III, do hereby declare Tuesday, March 8, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS THEREO F, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18 day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
