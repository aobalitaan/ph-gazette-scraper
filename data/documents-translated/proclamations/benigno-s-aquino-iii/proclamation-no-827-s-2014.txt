MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 827

DECLARING FRIDAY, 1 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KAWIT, CAVITE

WHEREAS, on Friday, 1 August 2014, the Municipality of Kawit, Cavite marks the celebration of “Kawit Day” ;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Kawit, Cavite be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 1 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kawit, Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
