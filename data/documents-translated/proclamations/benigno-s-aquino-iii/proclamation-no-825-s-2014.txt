MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 825

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 20 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF LUCENA

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 20 August 2014, marks the 53 rd Charter Day anniversary of the City of Lucena;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Lucena the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 20 August 2014, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 20 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lucena.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, Ihave hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
