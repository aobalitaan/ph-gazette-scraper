MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 515

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN, LOCATED AT COGON WEST, CARMEN, CEBU, AS AN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution Nos. 07-232 (s. 2007) and 11-520 (s. 2011), of the PEZA Board of Directors, several parcels of land, as an Agro-Industrial Economic Zone, located at Cogon West, Carmen, Cebu, to be known as the Carmen Cebu Gum Industrial Zone, with an aggregate area of Seventy-Five Thousand Nine Hundred Ninety-Two (75,992) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
