MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 675

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 NOVEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT

.

WHEREAS, Friday, 22 November 2013, marks the 40" Foundation Day
anniversary of the Province of Sultan Kudarat;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of
Sultan Kudarat be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the
occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO II, do hereby declare
Friday, 22 November 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Province of
Sultan Kudarat.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this29th gay ofOctober | in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:
