MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 417

DECLARING MONDAY, 2 JULY 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TAGBILARAN

WHEREAS, Section 107 of Republic Act No. 4660 provides the creation of the City of Tagbilaran as a Chartered City on 1 July 1966;

WHEREAS, the City of Tagbilaran marks the commemoration of its Charter Day anniversary on 1 July 2012;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Tagbilaran participate in the occasion, the Eleventh Sangguniang Panlungsod requested that Monday, 2 July 2012, be declared as special (non-working) day instead of 1 July 2012, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagbilaran be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 2 July 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tagbilaran.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
