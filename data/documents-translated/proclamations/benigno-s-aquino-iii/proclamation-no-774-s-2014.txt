MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 774

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 11 JUNE 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAGAY

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 11 June 2014 marks the 18 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Sagay;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sagay be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO;N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 11 June 2014, as a special (nori-working) day in the City of Sagay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of May , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
