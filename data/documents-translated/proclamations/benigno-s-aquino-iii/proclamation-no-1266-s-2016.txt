DECLARING SATURDAY, 18 JUNE 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) 
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON 
WHEREAS, Saturday, 18 June 2016, marks the 50"* Foundation Day of 
the Municipality of Quezon, Bukidnon; 
WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of 
Quezon, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the 
occasion with appropriate ceremonies. 
NOW, THEREFORE, I. PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, 
by authority of His Excellency. BEN.IGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare 
Saturday, 18 June 2016. as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of 
Quezon, Bukidnon. 
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the 
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.
