MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 998

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 APRIL 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 21 April 2015, marks the 14 th Cityhood anniversary of the City of Calamba, Laguna;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba, Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 21 April 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
