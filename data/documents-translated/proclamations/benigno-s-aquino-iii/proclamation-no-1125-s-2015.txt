MALACAÑANG PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1125

DECLARING SATURDAY, 12 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON­-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY LIPA

WHEREAS, the City of Lipa will host thousands of devotees for the National Pilgrimage, a day of prayer for peace and the sanctification of the clergy on Saturday, 12 September 2015;

WHEREAS, considering the volume of devotees who will participate in the event, the Office of the City Mayor has requested that 12 September 2015 be declared as a special non-working day in the City of Lipa.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 12 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Lipa.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
