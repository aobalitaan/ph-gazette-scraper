MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 599

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 JUNE 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KALILANGAN, PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Kalilangan, Bukidnon marks its 47 th Foundation Day anniversary on Tuesday, 18 June 2013;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Kalilangan, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 18 June 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Kalilangan, Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
