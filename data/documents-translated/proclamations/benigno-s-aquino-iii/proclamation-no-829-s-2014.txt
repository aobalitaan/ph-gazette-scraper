MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 829

DECLARING FRIDAY, 15 AUGUST 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DAVAO

WHEREAS, the City of Davao observes in August the week-long celebration of the “Kadayawan Festival”;

WHEREAS, on Friday, 15 August 2014, the City of Davao marks the celebration of the “Kadayawan Festival”;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Davao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 15 August 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Davao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, Ihave hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
