MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 694

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 DECEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Monday, 23 December 2013, marks the 13" Charter Day
anniversary of the City of Bayawan, Negros Oriental;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Bayawan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, |, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Monday, 23 December 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Bayawan, Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16thday of December, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.
