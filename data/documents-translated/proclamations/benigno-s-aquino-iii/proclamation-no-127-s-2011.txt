MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 127

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR MUNICIPAL SLAUGHTERHOUSE SITE PURPOSES, SITUATED IN BARANGAY BAGUMBAYAN, MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, ISLAND OF MINDORO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or disposition and reserve for Municipal Slaughterhouse purposes, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, subject to private rights, if any there be, situated in Barangay Bagumbayan, Municipality of Roxas, Province of Oriental Mindoro, Island of Mindoro, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 1081,Cad-506-D

Roxas Cadastre

A parcel of land (Lot No. 1081 of the Cadastral Survey Cad-506-D) situated in Barangay Bagumbayan, Municipality of Roxas, province of Oriental Mindoro, Island of Mindoro.

Bounded on the Southwest, along line 1-2 by Lot 176, Cad-506-D, Roxas Cadastre; on the Northwest, along line 2-3 by Road; on the Northeast, along line 3-4 by Uyao Creek/Legal Easement (3.00 m. wide); and on the Southeast, along line 4-1 by Lot 1080, Cad-506-D, Roxas Cadastre.

Beginning from a point marked “1” on the plan being S. 68 deg. 37’E., 427.36 m. from BLLM No. 1, Cad-506-D, Roxas Cadastre, thence:

beginning. Containing an area of THREE HUNDRED EIGHTY TWO (382) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on plan and marked on the ground by old P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cm. Bearings Grid.

This plan Cad-506-D was surveyed on November 15 to December 7, 1994 by Elpidio E. Atienza, Geodetic Engineer, in accordance with the law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder, and was approved on September 26, 1995.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 21st day of March in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
