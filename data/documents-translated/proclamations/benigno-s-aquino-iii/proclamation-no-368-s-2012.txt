PROCLAMATION NO. 368

DECLARING THURSDAY, 26 APRIL 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Municipality of San Jose, Batangas, marks the commemoration of its Foundation Day anniversary on Thursday, 26 April 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San Jose, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 26 April 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Jose, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
