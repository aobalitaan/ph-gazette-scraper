MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 935

DECLARING MONDAY, 5 JANUARY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUDIN, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Tagudin, Ilocos Sur marks the commemoration of its Foundation Day anniversary on Monday, 5 January 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Tagudin, Ilocos Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 5 January 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Tagudin, Province of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
