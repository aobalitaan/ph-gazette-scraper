MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 638

DECLARING THURSDAY, 5 SEPTEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL(NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS , Thursday, 5 September 2013, marks the celebration of the 15 th Tuna Festival in the City of General Santos;

WHEREAS , in order to give the people of the City of General Santos the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 5 September 2013 as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, 5 September 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of General Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
