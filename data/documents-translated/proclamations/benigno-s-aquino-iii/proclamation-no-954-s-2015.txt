MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 954

DECLARING THURSDAY, 5 FEBRUARY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BIÑAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS. Republic Act No. 9427 dated 10 April 2007 declared February 5 of every year a special working holiday in the Municipality of Biñan, Province of Laguna, to be known as Biñan Liberation Day;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Biñan to celebrate the occasion, the Mayor requested that 5 February 2015 be declared as a special non-working day in celebration of its 70 th Liberation Day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Thursday, 5 February 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Biñan, Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR . Executive Secretary
