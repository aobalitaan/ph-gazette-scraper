MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 88

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AS OFFICE SITE FOR THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES (DENR), REGIONAL OFFICE NO. 1,   SITUATED IN BARANGAY SEVILLA, CITY OF SAN FERNANDO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION, ISLAND OF LUZON, AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES – DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES (RP-DENR)

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law , I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, lease or settlement and reserve a certain parcel of land of the public domain as Regional Office Site for the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (RP-DENR), subject to private rights, if any there be, situated in Barangay Sevilla, City of San Fernando, Province of La Union, Island of Luzon, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 26699-F

Csd-01-011164-D

Department of Environment and Natural Resources
