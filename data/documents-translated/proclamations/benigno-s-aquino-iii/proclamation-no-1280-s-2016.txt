MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1280

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 1 JUNE 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF OLONGAPO

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 4645 otherwise known as “An Act Creating the City of Olongapo” was approved on 1 June 1966;

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 1 June 2016, marks the 50 th Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Olongapo;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Olongapo be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 1 June 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Olongapo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
