MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO, 773

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 JULY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF GINGOOG

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 23 July 2014 marks the Charter Day
anniversary of the City of Gingoog;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Gingoog be given full opportunity fo celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, !, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 23 July 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Fourteen. :

By authority of the President:

  
 

PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR.
