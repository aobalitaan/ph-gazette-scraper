MALACAÑANG PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1152

DECLARING MONDAY, 7 DECEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija celebrates Its Foundation Day every 5 th day of December;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Palayan to fully celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor has requested that 7 December 2015 (Monday) be declared as a special (non-working) day instead of 5 December 2015 which falls on a Saturday;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 7 December 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
