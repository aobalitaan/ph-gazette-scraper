MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1055

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 15 JULY 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 15 July 2015, the Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon marks the commemoration of the “Araw ng Mauban;”

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Mauban, Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 15 July 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
