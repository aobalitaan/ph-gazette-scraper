MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 444

DECLARING SATURDAY, 8 SEPTEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DIGOS

WHEREAS, Saturday, 8 September 2012, marks the 12 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Digos;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Digos the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 8 September 2012, as a special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 8 September 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City ofDigos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
