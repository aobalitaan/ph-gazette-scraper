MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 772

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 MAY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF GINGOOG

WHEREAS, the City of Gingoog marks the celebration of the Lubi-Lubi
Festival on Thursday, 22 May 2014;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Gingoog be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with
appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare
Thursday, 22 May 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Gingoog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of May, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Fouteen.

By authority of the President:

  
 

  

PAQUITO MN. OCHOA JR.
