MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 521

DECLARING SATURDAY, 8 DECEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Agoo, La Union marks the celebration of its 434 th Foundation Anniversary on Saturday, 8 December 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Agoo, La Union be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 8 December 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Agoo, La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
