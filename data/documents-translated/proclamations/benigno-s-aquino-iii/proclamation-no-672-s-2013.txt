MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 672

DECLARING THURSDAY, 7 NOVEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CUENCA, BATANGAS

WHEREAS , on Thursday, 7 November 2013, marks the 136 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, 7 November 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cuenca, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
