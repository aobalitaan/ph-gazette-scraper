MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 576

DECLARING TUESDAY, 21 MAY 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MALABON

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 21 May 2013, marks the 414 th Foundation Day Anniversary of the City of Malabon;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Malabon be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 21 May 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Malabon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
