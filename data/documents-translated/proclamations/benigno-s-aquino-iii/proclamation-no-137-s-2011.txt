MALACAÑAN PALACE Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 137

DECLARING MARCH 30, 2011 to APRIL 1, 2011 AS A THREE-DAY NATIONWIDE CELEBRATION FOR ALL HOG FARMERS

WHEREAS, the Hog subsector is one of the biggest and most consistent positive growth contributors to agricultural output;

WHEREAS, hog farmers need to be recognized for the role they play in accelerating the development of the livestock industry as well as their important contribution to the government’s effort, towards food sufficiency;

WHEREAS, there is an imperative need to rechannel the hog farmers’ energies toward excellence in animal production and vigilance in animal health in order to produce good quality animals, meat, and meat products for the Filipino consumer;

WHEREAS, the Pork Producers Federation of the Philippines, Inc. (PROPORK) and the National Federation of Hog Farmers, Inc. (NFHFI) are scheduled to hold their respective annual conventions and trade exhibits to enlist the support of ail their respective members in the administration’s food security program.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare March 30, 2011 to April 1, 2011 as a period of celebration for all Hog Farmers in the entire country;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 30 th of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
