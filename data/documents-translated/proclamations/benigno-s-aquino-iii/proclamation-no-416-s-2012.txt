MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 416

DECLARING THURSDAY, 21 JUNE 204 2,AS A SPECIAL: (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYUGAN

WHEREAS, Thursday, 21 June 2012 marks’the Charter Day anniversary
of the City of Bayugan:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of
Bayugan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion
with appropriate ceremonies. :

NOW, THEREFORE, |, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO IH, do hereby declare
Thursday, 21 June 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bayugan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2ist day of June, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twelve. :

By authority of the President:

   
 

PAQUITO N./OCHOA JR.
