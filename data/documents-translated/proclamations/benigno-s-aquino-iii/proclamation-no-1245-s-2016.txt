MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1245

DECLARING FRIDAY, 22 APRIL 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DUPAX DEL NORTE, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA

WHEREAS , Friday, 22 April 2016 marks the celebration of the Foundation Day of the Municipality of Dupax del Norte, Nueva Vizcaya;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Dupax del Norte, Nueva Vizcaya be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 22 April 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dupax del Norte, Nueva Vizcaya.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
