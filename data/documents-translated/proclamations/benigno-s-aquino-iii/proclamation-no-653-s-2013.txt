MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 653

DECLARING FRIDAY, 4 OCTOBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INFANTA, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN

WHEREAS, Friday, 4 October 2013, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Infanta, Province of Pangasinan;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Infanta, Pangasinan, the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 4 October 2013, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 4 October 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Infanta, Province of Pangasinan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
