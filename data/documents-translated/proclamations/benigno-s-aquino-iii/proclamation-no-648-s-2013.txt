MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 648

DECLARING MONDAY, 9 SEPTEMBER 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BAIS

WHEREAS, Monday, 9 September 2013, marks the 45 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Bais;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Bais the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 9 September 2013, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 9 September 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bais.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
