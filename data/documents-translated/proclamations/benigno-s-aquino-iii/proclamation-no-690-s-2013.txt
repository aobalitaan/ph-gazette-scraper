MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 690

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 406 (S. 2003) , WHICH RESERVED CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED IN LIWASANG BONIFACIO, DISTRICT OF ERMITA, CITY OF MANILA, ISLAND OF LUZON FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE PHILIPPINE POSTAL CORPORATION BY SEGREGATING A PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN, RESERVING THE SAME AS SITE OF THE HEAD OFFICE OF THE PHILIPPINE POSTAL SAVINGS BANK, INC. AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES-PHILIPPINE POSTAL SAVINGS BANK, INC.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 406 (s. 2003) which reserved certain parcels of land located in Liwasang Bonifacio, District of Ermita, City of Manila, Island of Luzon for building site purposes of the Philippine Postal Corporation, by segregating a portion of the land embraced therein and reserving the same as site of the head office of the Philippine Postal Savings Bank, Inc. and granting ownership thereof in favor of the Republic of the Philippines-Philippine Postal Savings Bank, Inc., which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 3, SGS-00-000015

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 3, Sgs-00-000015) situated in Liwasang Bonifacio, District of Ermita, City of Manila, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the NW., & NE., along lines 1-2-3-4-5 by Road; on the NE., along lines 5-6-7-8-9-10-11-12-13 by (portion) Lot 1, Block 301, Manila Cadastre; on the NE., & SE., along lines 13-14-15-16-17-18 by Road; and on the SW., along lines 18-19-20-21-22-23-1 by Jones Bridge.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 28 deg. 27′ W., 1,595.73 m. from BLLM No. 47, Manila Cadastre, thence:

containing an area of TWO THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED FORTY-EIGHT (2,848) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by Old Points. Bearings True; date of survey September 25-29, October 2-6, 23, 25 & 27, 2000: as surveyed by Engr. B.C. Monponbanua and L.L. Aromin and was approved on June 8, 2001.

The DENR Secretary is hereby authorized to issue the Special Patent in the name of the Republic of the Philippines-Philippine Postal Savings Bank, Inc.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 26th day of November, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

(Sgd.) BENIGNO S. AQUINO III

By the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
