MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1288

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 22 JUNE 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SOCORRO, ORIENTAL MINDORO

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 22 June 2016, marks the Foundation
Anniversary of the Municipality of Socorro, Oriental Mindoro;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of
Socorro, Oriental Mindoro be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in
the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO Ill, do hereby declare
Wednesday, 22 June 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of
Socorro, Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this30th day of May , in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the Preside
