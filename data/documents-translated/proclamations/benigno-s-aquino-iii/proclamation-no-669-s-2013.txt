BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 669

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND, LOCATED AT NORTH BONIFACIO DISTRICT, BONIFACIO GLOBAL CITY, TAGUIG CITY, METRO MANILA, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 , AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate several parcels of land, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 (s. 2000), as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 (s. 2004), and Resolution No. 11-377 (s. 2011), as amended, of the PEZA Board of Directors, as an IT Park , to be known as the Uptown Bonifacio , located at North Bonifacio District, Bonifacio Global City, Taguig City, Metro Manila, with an area of One Hundred Forty-Four Thousand Six Hundred Thirteen Square Meters and Fifty Eight Square Decimeters (144,613.58) , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
