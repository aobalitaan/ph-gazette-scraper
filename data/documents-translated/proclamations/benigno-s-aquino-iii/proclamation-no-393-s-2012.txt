MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 393

DECLARING SATURDAY, 16 JUNE 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PILI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Pili, Camarines Sur marks its Foundation Day anniversary on Saturday, 16 June 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pili, Camarines Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Saturday, 16 June 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pili, Camarines Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
