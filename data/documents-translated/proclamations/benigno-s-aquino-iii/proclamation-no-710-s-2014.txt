MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 710

DECLARING TUESDAY, 11 FEBRUARY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS, on Tuesday, 11 February 2014, the City of Iriga marks the celebration of the Tinagba Festival, an ancient Bicolano First-Harvest Offering;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 11 February 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUlTO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
