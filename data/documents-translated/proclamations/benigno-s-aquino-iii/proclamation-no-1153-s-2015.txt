MALACAÑANG PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1153

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 25 NOVEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MARAGUSAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, on Wednesday, 25 November 2015, the Municipality of Maragusan, Compostela Valley marks its Foundation Anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Maragusan, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 25 November 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Maragusan, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
