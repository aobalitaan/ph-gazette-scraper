MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 509

DECLARING FRIDAY, 7 DECEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MARIANO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, the Municipality of San Mariano, Isabela marks the celebration of the Aggau na San Mariano, highlighting the diverse culture of the towns people, on Friday, 7 December 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of San Mariano, Isabela be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Friday, 7 December 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of San Mariano, Province of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21 st day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
