MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION N O. 1223

DECLARING MONDAY, 7 MARCH 2016, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, on Monday, 7 March 2016, the City of Tagum marks the celebration of the Araw ng Tagum ;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tagum be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 7 March 2016, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Sixteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
