MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 108

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 28, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF ESCALANTE, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS , Monday, February 28, 2011 marks the Charter Day anniversary of the City of Escalante;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Escalante be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR ., Executive Secretary, by the authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Monday, February 28, 2011, as special (non-working) day in the City of Escalante.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8 th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
