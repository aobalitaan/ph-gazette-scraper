MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1073

DECLARING TUESDAY, 4 AUGUST 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CABUYAO, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Tuesday, 4 August 2015, marks the 3 rd Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Cabuyao, Laguna;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabuyao, Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 4 August 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cabuyao, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
