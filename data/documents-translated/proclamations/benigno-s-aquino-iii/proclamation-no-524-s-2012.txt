MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 524

DECLARING TUESDAY, 18 DECEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TUGUEGARAO

WHEREAS, the City of Tuguegarao marks the celebration of its 13 th Charter Day anniversary on Tuesday, 18 December 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tuguegarao be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 18 December 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tuguegarao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
