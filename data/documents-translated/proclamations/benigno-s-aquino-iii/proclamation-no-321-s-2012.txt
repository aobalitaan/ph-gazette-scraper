PROCLAMATION NO. 321

DECLARING FRIDAY, 10 FEBRUARY 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF KIDAPAWAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, 12 February 2012 marks the 14 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Kidapawan;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Kidapawan to fully participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod requested that Friday, 10 February 2012 instead of 12 February, 2012 which falls on a Sunday, be declared as a special non-working day:

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Kidapawan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 10 February 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Kidapawan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR . Executive Secretary
