MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 834

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 23 JULY 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley marks the commemoration of the “Araw ng Nabunturan” on Wednesday, 23 July 2014;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 23 July 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Nabunturan, Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
