MALACAÑAN Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 117

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 28, 2011, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TANGUB

WHEREAS, Monday, February 28, 2011, marks the Charter Day Anniversary of the City of Tangub;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tangub be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, February 28, 2011 as special (non-working) day in the City of Tangub.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eleven.

By authority of the President

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOSE AMOR M. AMORADO Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
