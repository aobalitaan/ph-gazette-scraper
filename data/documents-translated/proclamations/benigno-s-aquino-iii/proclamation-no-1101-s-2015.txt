MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1101

DECLARING FRIDAY, 18 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG

WHEREAS, the City of Tacurong marks its 15th Cityhood Anniversary and Talakudong Festival 2015 on Friday, 18 September 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR, Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 18 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Tacurong.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
