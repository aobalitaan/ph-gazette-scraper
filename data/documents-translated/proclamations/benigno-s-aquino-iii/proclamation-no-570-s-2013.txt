MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 570

DECLARING TUESDAY, 30 APRIL 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DAGUPAN

WHEREAS, the City of Dagupan marks the celebration of the 12 th Bangus Festival on April 20 – May 5, 2013;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the City of Dagupan participate in the event, the Office of the Mayor requested that Tuesday, 30 April 2013 be declared as a special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dagupan be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 30 April 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand arid Thirteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
