MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 804

DECLARING MONDAY, 23 JUNE 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BACOOR

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 10160 dated 10 April 2012, otherwise known as “An Act Converting the Municipality of Bacoor, Province of Cavite into a Component City to be known as the City of Bacoor”;

WHEREAS , in order to give the people of the City of Bacoor the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 23 June 2014, as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, l , PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 23 June 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Bacoor.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
