MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1117

DECLARING TUESDAY, 1 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF GENERAL SANTOS

WHEREAS, Saturday, 5 September 2015, marks the 47th Charter Day and 17th Tuna Festival of the City of General Santos;

WHEREAS, the City of General Santos will hold weeklong festivities on September 1-6, 2015 in celebration of this occasion;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of General Santos the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlungsod has requested that 1 September 2015, instead of 5 September 2015 which falls on a Saturday, be declared as a special non-working day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Tuesday, 1 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of General Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
