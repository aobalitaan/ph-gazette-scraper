MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1096

DECLARING FRIDAY, 4 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONKAYO, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley, marks the celebration of its 61st Foundation Day and 20th Kariyawan Festival on Friday, 4 September 2015;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Monkayo, Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Friday, 4 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Monkayo, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
