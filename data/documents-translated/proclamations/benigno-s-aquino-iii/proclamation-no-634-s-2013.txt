MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 634

DECLARING THURSDAY, 22 AUGUST 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS , Thursday, 22 August 2013, marks the commemoration of the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte;

WHEREAS , in order to give the people of the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte, the opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare 22 August 2013, as a special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III , do hereby declare Thursday, 22 August 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Thirteen.

By the authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
