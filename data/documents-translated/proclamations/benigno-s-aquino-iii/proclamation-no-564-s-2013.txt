MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 564

CREATING AND DESIGNATING TWO PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT BARANGAY TAMBLER, GENERAL SANTOS CITY, AS AN AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 , AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act (RA) No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by RA No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of RA No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution Nos. 07-232 (s. 2007) and 11-317 (s. 2011), of the PEZA Board of Directors, two parcels of land, as an Agro-Industrial Economic Zone, to be known as Foodport Processing Zone, located at Barangay Tambler, General Santos City, with an aggregate area of Eighty-Two Thousand and Sixteen (82,016) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
