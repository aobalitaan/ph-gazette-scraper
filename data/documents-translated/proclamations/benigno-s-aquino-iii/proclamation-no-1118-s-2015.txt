MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1118

DECLARING MONDAY, 7 SEPTEMBER 2015, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Monday, 7 September 2015, marks the 75th Charter Anniversary and 444th Foundation Year of the City of Cavite;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cavite be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Monday, 7 September 2015, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fifteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary
