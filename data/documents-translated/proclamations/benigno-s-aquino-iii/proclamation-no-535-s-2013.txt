MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 535

DECLARING MONDAY, 21 JANUARY 2013, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING)
DAY IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN

WHEREAS, ihe City of Pagadian marks the celebration of the “Pasalamat
Festival 2013” on January 10-21, 2013:

WHEREAS, io enable the peaple of the City of Pagadian to celebrate and
pariicipaie in the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, 21
January 2013 be declared as a special (non-working) day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary,
by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO Ill, do hereby declare
Monday, 21 January 2013, as a special (non-working) day in the City of
Pagadian.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, | have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, thisl4th day of January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Thirieen.

By authority of the President:
