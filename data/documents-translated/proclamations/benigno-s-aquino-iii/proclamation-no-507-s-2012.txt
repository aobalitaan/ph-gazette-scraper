MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 507

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 28 NOVEMBER 2012, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARGARITA, PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Sta. Margarita, Samar marks the celebration of its Foundation Day anniversary on Wednesday, 28 November 2012;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Sta. Margarita, Samar be given full opportunity to participate in and celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. , Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 28 November 2012, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Margarita, Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twelve.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary
