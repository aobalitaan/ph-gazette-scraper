MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 909

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 26 NOVEMBER 2014, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DASMARIÑAS

WHEREAS, the City of Dasmariñas marks Wednesday, 26 November 2014 as the commemoration of its Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dasmariñas be given full opportunity to celebrate and participate in the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR., Executive Secretary, by authority of His Excellency, BENIGNO S. AQUINO III, do hereby declare Wednesday, 26 November 2014, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Dasmariñas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Fourteen.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA JR. Executive Secretary

Proclamation No. 909, November 13, 2014
