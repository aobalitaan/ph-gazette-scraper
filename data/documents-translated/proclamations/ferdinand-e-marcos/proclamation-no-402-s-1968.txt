MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 402

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT SITUATED IN BARRIO CABID-AN, MUNICIPALITY OF SORSOGON, PROVINCE OF SORSOGON.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Director of Public Schools, a parcel of land of the private domain of the government, situated in Barrio Cabid-an, Municipality of Sorsogon, Province of Sorsogon, which a portion of the land covered by T. C. T. No. 12 of the Register of Deeds of described as follows:

“A parcel of land (portion of Lot No. 4095 of the Cadastral Survey of Sorsogon) situated in the Barrio of Cabid-an, Municipality of Sorsogon, Sorsogon. Bounded on the N. by Road; on the E., by the Sorsogon Dairy Farm; on the S., by the Sorsogon Dairy Farm and in the W., by the Sorsogon Diary Farm, containing an area of TEN THOUSAND (10,000) SQUARE METERS. The boundaries are indicated on the ground by concrete cement posts.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
