MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 109

DECLARING THURSDAY, OCTOBER 20, 1966, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCES OF CAVITE AND ILOCOS NORTE AND IN THE CITIES OF LAOAG, CAVITE, TAGAYTAY AND TRECE MARTIRES.

WHEREAS, October 20, 1966, marks the birthday centenary of General Artemio Ricarte, one of our outstanding heroes who chose exile from his country rather than take an oath of allegiance to a foreign flag; and

WHEREAS, such exemplary patriotism and love of country is worthy of emulation;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare October 20, 1966, as a special public holiday in the provinces of Cavite and Ilocos Norte and in the cities of Laoag, Cavite, Tagaytay and Trece Martires. I enjoin all citizens to cooperate in making the celebration a success.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
