MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 539

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND DRIVE OF THE CIVIC ASSEMBLY OF WOMEN OF THE PHILIPPINES.

Whereas, the Civic Assembly of Women of the Philippines has been authorized under Proclamation No. 491 dated December 2, 1968, to conduct a national educational and fund drive from January 31 to March 15, 1969; and

WHEREAS, said organization needs additional time for the conduct of its drive to enable it to effectively realize its objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested upon me by law, do hereby extend the national educational and fund drive of the Civic Assembly of Women of the Philippines from March 15 to May 15, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
