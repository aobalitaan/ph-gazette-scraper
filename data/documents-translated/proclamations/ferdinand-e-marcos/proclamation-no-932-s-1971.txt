TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 932

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 8, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANES.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, December 8, 1971, marks the charter anniversary of the province of Batanes during which occasion the province desires to honor its exemplary citizens; and

WHEREAS, the residents should be afforded the opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Wednesday, December 8, 1971, as special public holiday in the province of Batanes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
