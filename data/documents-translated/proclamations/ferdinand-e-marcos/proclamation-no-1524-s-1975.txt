MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1524

EXTENDING THE AUTHORITY OF PEFTOK VETERANS ASSOCIATION, INCORPORATED TO CONDUCT A CAMPAIGN TO RAISE FUNDS UP TO JUNE 30, 1976.

WHEREAS, the PEFTOK Veterans Association, Inc. has been authorized to undertake a fund-raising campaign under Presidential Proclamation No. 1457 dated July 7, 1975, for the welfare and benefit of its duly registered members of about twelve thousand (12, 000) military retirees, widows and orphans, through the holding of benefit movie premiers, live musical shows, raffles and cockfights; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs additional time to realize its fund campaign goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS; President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the PEFTOK Veterans Association, Inc. to extend its fund-raising campaign for another six months, up to June 30, 1976.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of December in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) J. C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
