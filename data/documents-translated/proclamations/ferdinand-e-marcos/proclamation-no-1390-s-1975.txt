TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1390

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE HEART ASSOCIATION, INC. TO CONDUCT AN EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM FEBRUARY 1 TO JUNE 30, 1975.

WHEREAS, the heart is the seat of life, and the growing prevalence of heart cases among our people should arouse national awareness to this serious health problem; and

WHEREAS, the true wealth of a nation is the health of its people, and a nation is strong and healthy only as its people are strong and healthy;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Philippine Heart Association, Inc. to conduct an educational and fund campaign from February 1 to June 30, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary
