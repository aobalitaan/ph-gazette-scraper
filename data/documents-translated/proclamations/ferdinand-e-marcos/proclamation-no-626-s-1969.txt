MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 626

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 625 DATED NOVEMBER 5, 1969

Proclamation No. 625 dated November 5, 1969, is hereby amended so as to include November 8, 1969, as a special public holiday to give personnel of certain government offices and private firms opportunity to go home to their respective provinces and exercise their right of suffrage.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
