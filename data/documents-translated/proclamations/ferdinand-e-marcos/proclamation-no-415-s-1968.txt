MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO.415

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 405 DATED JUNE 5, 1968, BY DECLARING JUNE 19, 1968 AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, AND IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO.

Proclamation No. 405 dated June 5, 1968, is hereby amended by declaring June 19, 1968 as a special public holiday in the Province of Laguna and in the City of San Pablo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set may hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.)FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.)RAFAEL M.SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
