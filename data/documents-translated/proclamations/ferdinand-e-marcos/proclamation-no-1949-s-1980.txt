MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1949

RESERVING AS BIRD AND FISH SANCTUARY LAKE MALIMANGA SITUATED AT BARANGAY MALIMANGA, MUNICIPALITY OF CANDELARIA, PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES, ISLAND OF LUZON.

By virtue of the authority vested in me by law, I, Ferdinand E. Marcos, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for bird and fish sanctuary Lake Malimanga situated at Barangay Malimanga, Municipality of Candelaria, Province of Zambales, Island of Luzon, subject to private rights, if any there be, and more particularly described as follows:

containing an area of 127,485 square meters.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Presidential Staff Director
