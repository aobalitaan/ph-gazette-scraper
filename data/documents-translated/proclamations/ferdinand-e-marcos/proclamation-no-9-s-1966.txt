MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 9

EXTENDING THE EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE KNIGHTS OF RIZAL UP TO JUNE 19, 1966.

WHEREAS, the Order of the Knights of Rizal was authorized to conduct an educational and fund campaign from November 30, 1965, to January 31, 1966; and

WHEREAS, the Order of the Knights of Rizal, in order to implement its objectives and projects, needs additional time to conduct its campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, do hereby extend the campaign of the Order of the Knights of Rizal up to June 19, 1966.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
