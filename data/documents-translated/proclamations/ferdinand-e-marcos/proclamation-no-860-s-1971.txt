TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 860

DECLARING TUESDAY, JUNE 15, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, June 15, 1971, marks the twenty-first anniversary of the charter of Cagayan de Oro City; and

WHEREAS, the residents should be given the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, June 15, 1971, as a special public holiday in Cagayan de Oro City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
