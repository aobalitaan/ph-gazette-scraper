MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 112

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, OCTOBER 26, 1966, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY FOR THE MUNICIPALITY OF LOS BAÑOS, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA.

WHEREAS, the President of the United States, Lyndon B. Johnson, will visit the International Rice Research Institute, U.P., College of Agriculture and the U.P., College of Forestry located in Los Baños, Laguna on October 26, 1966;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of Los Baños be given full opportunity to welcome President Johnson;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Wednesday, October 26, 1966, as a special public holiday in the Municipality of Los Baños, Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
