MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 349

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 464 DATED DECEMBER 5, 1957, AND PROCLAMATION NO. 188 DATED DECEMBER 27, 1954 WHICH RESERVED CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN IN THE DISTRICT OF INTRAMUROS, CITY OF MANILA, FOR NATIONAL MUSEUM BUILDING SITE PURPOSES, RESPECTIVELY, AND OPENING THE LANDS COVERED THEREBY TO DISPOSITION UNDER REPUBLIC ACT NO. 5169.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 454 dated December 5, 1957, as Proclamation No. 186 dated December 27, 1965, which reserved certain parcels of land of the private domain in Intramuros, Manila, for National Economic Council and National Museum building site purposes, and open the lands covered thereby to disposition under the provisions of Republic Act No. 5169.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
