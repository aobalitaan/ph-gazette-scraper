MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 32

DECLARING MONDAY, MAY 2, 1966, AS ABAD SANTOS MEMORIAL DAY.

WHEREAS, Monday, May 2, 1966, marks the twenty-fourth anniversary of the martyrdom of the late Chief Justice Jose Abad Santos; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper to render honor and homage to the late Chief Justice whose exemplary patriotism is a source of inspiration to our people;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, May 2, 1966, as “Abad Santos Memorial Day”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
