MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 591

DECLARING FRIDAY, AUGUST 29, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF COTABATO.

WHEREAS, Friday, August 29, 1969, marks the 10th anniversary of the charter of Cotabato City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Cotabato City should be afforded an opportunity to commemorate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, August 29, 1969, as a special public holiday in the City of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
