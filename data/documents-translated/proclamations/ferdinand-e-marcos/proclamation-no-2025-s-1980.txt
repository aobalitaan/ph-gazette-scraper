MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2025

DECLARING FRIDAY, OCTOBER 31, 1980, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY.

WHEREAS, one of the most popular religious traditions in the country is paying homage to departed relatives and friends on the first of November;

WHEREAS, Letter of Instructions No. 814 classified the first of November as a national special public holiday; and

WHEREAS, Friday, October 31, 1980 may be declared a special public holiday to give the people full opportunity to celebrate the tradition with all its religious fervor;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Friday, October 31, 1980 as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Presidential Assistant
