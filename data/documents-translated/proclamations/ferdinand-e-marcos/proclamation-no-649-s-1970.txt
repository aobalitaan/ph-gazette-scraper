MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 649

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 423, DATED JULY 12, 1957, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE FORT ANDRES BONIFACIO MILITARY RESERVATION SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF PASIG, TAGUIG AND PARAÑAQUE, PROVINCE OF RIZAL AND PASAY CITY, A CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF BICUTAN, MUNICIPALITY OF TAGUIG AND RESERVING THE SAME AS MANPOWER SKILLS CENTER UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE NATIONAL MANPOWER & YOUTH COUNCIL.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 423 dated July 12, 1957, which established the Fort Andres Bonifacio Military Reservation, situated in the municipalities of Pasig, Taguig and Parañaque, Province of Rizal, and Pasay City, a certain portion of the land embraced therein situated in the Barrio of Bicutan, Municipality of Taguig, and reserve the same for Manpower Skills Development Center purposes under the administration of the National Manpower & Youth Council, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future survey, which portion is more particularly described as follows:

Proposed Site of NATIONAL MANPOWER & YOUTH COUNCIL (Lot 3-B-1-B-1)

A parcel of land (Lot 3-B-1-B-1) of the subdivision plan (LRC) Psd-__________, being a portion of Lot 3-B-1-B of Plan Bsd-11810), situated in the Barrio of Bicutan, Municipality of Taguig, Province of Rizal. Bounded on the SW, along lines 1-2-3, by Lot 3-B-1-A of plan Bsd-11810; on the NW, and NE, along lines 3-4-5, by Lot 3-B-1-B-2 of plan (LRC) Psd-_______: and on the S., along line 5-1, by Lot 3-B-1-A of plan Bsd-11810. Beginning at a point marked “1” on the attached Sketch Plan, being S. 51 deg. 31 min. E., 7010.96 meters from B.L.L.M. 1, Maricaban, Pasay,

thence N. 16 deg. 05 min. W., 278.44 meters to point 2; thence N. 16 deg. 07 min. W., 41.84 meters to point 3; thence N. 72 deg. 51 min. E., 267.75 meters to point 4; thence S. 16 deg. 07 min. E., 426.56 meters to point 5; thence N. 85 deg. 22 min. W., 286.45 meters to point of

beginning; containing an approximate area of one hundred thousand and one (100,001) square meters.

NOTE: Subject to change based on future survey.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
