MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 17

DECLARING THURSDAY, MARCH 10, 1966, AS MACARIO ADRIATICO DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO.

WHEREAS, Thursday, March 10, 1966, marks the birthday anniversary of Macario Adriatico, statesman and man of letters; and

WHEREAS, the people of Oriental Mindoro, his birthplace, should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, March 10, 1966, as Macario Adriatico Day in the Province of Oriental Mindoro.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
