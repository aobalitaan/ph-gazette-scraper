TANGGAPAN NG.PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 675

AUTHORIZING THE NATIONAL GERIATRIC CIVIC ASSOCIATION, INCORPORATED TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM APRIL 1 TO SEPTEMBER 30, 1970.

WHEREAS, the National Geriatric Civic Association, Inc. is engaged in various geriatric projects for the rehabilitation and care of the aged;

WHEREAS, there are now more than three and one-half million Filipino citizens of fifty years of age and above whom the National Geriatric Civic Association, Inc. has pledged to serve through its medical care, scientific research and socio-economic welfare program; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs funds for the establishment, operation and maintenance of the RESEARCH FOUNDATION CENTER FOR AGING DISEASES & NATIONAL GERIATRIC SANATORIA OF THE PHILIPPINES and other geriatric projects as it may establish;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the National Geriatric Civic Association, Inc. to conduct a national educational, membership and fund campaign during the period from April 1 to September 30, 1970. I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of race or creed, to assist in this drive in order that the Association may attain its objectives.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of March in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
