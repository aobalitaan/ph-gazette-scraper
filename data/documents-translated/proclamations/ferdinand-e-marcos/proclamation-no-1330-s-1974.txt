TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1330

DECLARING OCTOBER 9 TO 13, 1974, AS JAYCEES WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine Jaycees is a civic and non-profit organization active in youth and community development projects; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to acknowledge the contribution of Philippine Jaycees in nation-building;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare October 9 to 13, 1974, as Jaycee Week under the auspices of the Philippine Jaycees.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
