MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 530-A

EXTENDING THE SAVINGS AND THRIFT YEAR CAMPAIGN TO DECEMBER 31, 1969.

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 354 dated February 22, 1968, the period from March 1968 to Match 1969 has been declared as Savings and Thrift Year; and

WHEREAS, for reasons of public interest there is need to extend the savings and thrift year campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the savings and thrift year campaign to December 31, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) GILBERTO M. DUAVIT Acting Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
