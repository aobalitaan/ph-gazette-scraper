TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 910

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM AND LIBRARY, INC. EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND DRIVE TO DECEMBER 31, 1971.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 819, dated March 1, 1971, authorized the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc. to conduct its 1971 Educational , Membership and Fund Campaign during the period from March 1 to September 30, 1971;

WHEREAS, a good part of the campaign period falls within the period of the electoral ban on fund raising for which reason the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc. needs more time for the conduct of its fund drive for youth welfare and development projects;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the 1971 Educational, Membership and Fund Campaign of the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc. up to December 31, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
