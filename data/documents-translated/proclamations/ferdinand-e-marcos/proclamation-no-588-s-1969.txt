MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 588

DECLARING SATURDAY, AUGUST 16, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF PASAY.

WHEREAS, Saturday, August 16, 1969, marks the 22nd anniversary of the Charter of Pasay City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Pasay City should be afforded an opportunity to commemorate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, August 16, 1969, as a special public holiday in the City of Pasay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
