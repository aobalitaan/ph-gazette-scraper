TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1107

POSTPONING THE CELEBRATION OF PHILIPPINE AVIATION WEEK 1973 TO THE FIRST WEEK OF MAY, 1973, UNDER THE AUSPICES OF THE AEROSPACE ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, IN COOPERATION AND COORDINATION WITH THE CIVIL AERONAUTICS ADMINISTRATION AND OTHER ORGANIZATIONS.

By order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, do hereby postpone to the first week of May, 1973, this year’s celebration of Philippine Aviation Week.

The celebration of the week shall be under the auspices of the Aerospace Association of the Philippines, in cooperation and coordination with the Civil Aeronautics Administration and other organizations involved. Like last year’s, a provincial oriented celebration shall be observed with such air shows and other activities as may be organized in cities as Laoag, Legaspi, Manila, Davao, Bacolod/Iloilo, Mactan, and, Zamboanga.

Proclamations Nos. 774 and 985, dated November 10, 1970 and March 10, 1972, respectively, are modified accordingly.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
