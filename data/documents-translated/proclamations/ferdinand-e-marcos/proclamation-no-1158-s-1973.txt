Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1158

FURTHER EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1973.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 1095 dated January 9, 1973, to extend its educational and fund campaign up to June 30, 1973;

WHEREAS, the association needs additional time to raise more funds to achieve its goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association up to December 31, 1973.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
