MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1697

DECLARING THURSDAY, DECEMBER 15, 1977 AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN NAGA CITY

WHEREAS, the City of Naga will celebrate its 29th Charter anniversary on December 15, 1977;

WHEREAS, the residents of said City desire to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with fitting programs and ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, December 15, 1977 as a special public holiday in Naga City.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
