MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 12

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 500 DATED NOVEMBER 20, 1965, WHICH EXCLUDED FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 449 DATED OCTOBER 4, 1957, CERTAIN AREAS FROM THE COMMUNAL FISHING GROUNDS RESERVATION IN BULACAN AND REVERTING THE AREAS SO EXCLUDED AS COMMUNAL FISHING GROUND RESERVATION OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BULACAN, BULACAN.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 500 dated November 20, 1965, which excluded from the operation of Proclamation No. 449 dated October 4, 1957, certain areas from the communal fishing grounds reservation in Bulacan, Bulacan, and restore the areas so excluded to their original status as communal fishing grounds of the municipality of Bulacan, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
