TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1588

DECLARING TUESDAY, OCTOBER 12, 1976, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN QUEZON CITY.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, October 12, 1976, marks the 37th anniversary of the foundation of Quezon City; and

WHEREAS, the people of said city desire to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, October 12, 1976, as a special public holiday in Quezon City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
