MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 35

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 507-A DATED DECEMBER 16, 1965, WHICH EXCLUDED CERTAIN PORTIONS OF LAND FROM THE MT. APO NATIONAL PARK AND RESTORING THE SAME AREAS AS PART OF THE MT. APO NATIONAL PARK.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 507-A dated December 16, 1965, which excluded certain portions of land from the Mt. Apo National Park and declared the same as public forest within the purview of Republic Act No. 3092, and restore the same area to its original status as part of the Mt. Apo National Park closed to timber exploitation except when necessary in order to control the attacks of insects or diseases or conserve or enhance the beauty of the park.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
