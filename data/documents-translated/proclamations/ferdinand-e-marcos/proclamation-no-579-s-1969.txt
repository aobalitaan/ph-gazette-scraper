MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 579

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE ELKS CEREBRAL PALSY PROJECT, INC.

WHEREAS, the Elks Cerebral Palsy Project, Inc. was authorized under Proclamation No. 520, dated February 12, 1969, to conduct a national educational and fund campaign from May 1 to June 30, 1969; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs additional time for the conduct of its campaign to realize its campaign goal for the operation of its clinics;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national educational and fund campaign of the Cerebral Palsy Project, Inc. up to September 30, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
