MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 620

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 1, 1969 TO OCTOBER 1, 1970, AS MEMBERSHIP PROMOTIONAL DRIVE YEAR OF THE GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES FUNDS AND INVESTMENT CORPORATION (GEFIC).

WHEREAS, there is paramount need to inculcate the habit of savings in every income-earning Filipino for his own interest and that of the country as a whole;

WHEREAS, the Government Employees Funds and Investment Corporation (GEFIC), an investment project sponsored by the Kapisanan Ng Mga Kawani ng Pamahalaan, has been organized in response to that paramount need with the avowed purpose and objective of harnessing the savings of public workers and channeling them into productive enterprises and investments for their own benefit and for the country’s further economic development; and

WHEREAS, the GEFIC is to be operated on the basis of sound business principles with the involvement of the Development Bank of the Philippines and other private agencies with ample expertise and experienced in investment fields;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from October 1, 1969, to October 1, 1970 as Membership Promotional Drive Year of the Government Employees Funds and Investment (GEFIC) Promotional Drive. The drive shall be under the auspices of the Development Bank of the Philippines and the Kapisanan Ng Mga Kawani ng Pamahalaan, the Philippine Public School Teachers Association, the Philippine Government Employees Association, Confederation of Unions in Government Corporations, the Confederation of Government Employees Organizations, the Enlisted men and Personnel Association of the Armed Forces of the Philippines. I call upon all public officials and employees, and the Filipino people, including government entities, to extend their unstinted support and cooperation to the Membership Promotional Drive so that the novel objectives of this project may be a success.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
