MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1534

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 1 TO 7, 1976, AS OFFICE MACHINE WEEK.

WHEREAS, it is necessary to focus attention on the important role of modern office equipment as tools of economic progress and development; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Office Machine Distributors Association, Inc., an organization of leading machine importers and distributors in the country, has been instrumental in promoting modern business methods relevant to the country’s present development progress;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from March 1 to 7, 1976, as Office Machine Week under the auspices of the Philippine Office Machine Distributors Association, Inc.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
