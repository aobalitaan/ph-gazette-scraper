TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 882

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 9, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN ILOCOS NORTE AND LAOAG CITY.

WHEREAS, the ninth day of August, is the birthday of the late Governor Roque B. Ablan, an outstanding resistance leader of the occupation; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of Ilocos Norte, his birthplace, should be given full opportunity to celebrate his birthday;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, August 9, 1971, as a special public holiday in the province of Ilocos Norte and in the City of Laoag.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MECHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
