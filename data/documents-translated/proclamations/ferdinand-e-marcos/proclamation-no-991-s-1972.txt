TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 991

AUTHORIZING THE MORAL RE-ARMAMENT FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT ITS EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM MARCH 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1972.

WHEREAS, it is a vital concern of society to promote the moral well-being of our children and youth and their development into good and useful citizens and enlightened leaders of our country;

WHEREAS, civic movements designed to promote the moral upbringing of young people and to provide proper facilities for such enhancement should be encouraged and maintained; and

WHEREAS, the MORAL RE-ARMAMENT FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES carries out a program with objectives geared to these ends;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Moral Re-Armament Foundation of the Philippines to conduct an educational, membership and fund campaign from March 1 to December 31, 1972. I enjoin all citizens and other nationals to support this humanitarian movement.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
