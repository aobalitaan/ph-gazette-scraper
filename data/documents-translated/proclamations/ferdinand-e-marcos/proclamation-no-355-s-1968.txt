MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 355

DECLARING SATURDAY, FEBRUARY 24, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CEBU CITY.

WHEREAS, Saturday, February 24, 1968, marks the thirty-first anniversary of the founding of Cebu City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of the City should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, February 24, 1968, as a special public holiday Cebu City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
