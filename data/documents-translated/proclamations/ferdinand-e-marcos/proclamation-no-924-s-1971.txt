TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 924

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF ALL COMMUNITY CHESTS AND COUNCILS IN THE PHILIPPINES UP TO DECEMBER 30, 1971.

WHEREAS, all Community Chests and Councils in the Philippines were authorized under Proclamation No. 838 dated April 14, 1971, to conduct fund campaigns from June 1 to September 30, 1971, to support the services of their respective welfare agencies; and

WHEREAS, it has been found necessary to extend the period of the campaign to afford these organizations sufficient time to accomplish their goals and objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the Annual Educational and Fund Campaigns of all Community Chests and Councils in the Philippines to December 31, 1971. Proclamation No. 908 dated September 17, 1971, is hereby amended accordingly.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
