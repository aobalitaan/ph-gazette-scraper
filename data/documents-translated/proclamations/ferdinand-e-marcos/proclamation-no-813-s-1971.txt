Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 813

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JANUARY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1971, AS “PHILIPPINE MEDICAL EDUCATION CENTENNIAL YEAR”

WHEREAS, the year 1971 marks the centenary of medical education in the Philippines; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus attention on the invaluable contributions that Medicine has made towards national health and progress;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from January 1 to December 31, 1971, as “Philippine Medical Education Centennial Year.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
