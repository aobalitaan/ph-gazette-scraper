MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1721

AUTHORIZING THE NARCOTICS FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC. TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM JANUARY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1978.

WHEREAS, the Narcotics Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., a private non-profit organization, supplements the program of the Government in the treatment, rehabilitation and after-care services of drug abusers, who are mostly school boys and girls;

WHEREAS, the Foundation will expand its Rehabilitation Center by erecting additional buildings, as part of the continuing implementation of a comprehensive program against drug abuse; and

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs the support of the public to realize its laudable objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Narcotics Foundation of the Philippines, Inc. to conduct a nationwide educational and fund campaign during the period from January 1 to December 31, 1978.

I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality or creed, to give generously and participate actively in this campaign and thus help in rekindling the hopes and aspirations of the youth who are victims of drug abuse to play their role in our society as useful citizens.

All donations and contributions to this educational and fund campaign shall be exempt from income and gift taxes, and the same shall constitute allowable deductions from the income of the donors and contributors for income tax purposes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
