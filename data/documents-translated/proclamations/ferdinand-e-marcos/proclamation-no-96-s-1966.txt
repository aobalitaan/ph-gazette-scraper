MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 96

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 2, 1966, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN GUINOBATAN, ALBAY.

WHEREAS, Friday, September 2, 1966, marks the 101st birthday anniversary of General Simeon Ola y Arboleda, one of the last Filipinos to surrender during the Filipino-American war; and

WHEREAS, the people of Guinobatan, Albay, request that they be afforded an opportunity to render homage to the honored general;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Friday, September 2, 1966, as a special public holiday in Guinobatan, Albay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
