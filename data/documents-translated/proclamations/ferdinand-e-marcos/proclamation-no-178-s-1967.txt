MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 178

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 160 DATED SEPTEMBER 16, 1963, WHICH RESERVED FOR THE PUBLIC SERVICE COMMISSION CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT SITUATED IN QUEZON CITY, AND RESERVING THE SAME PARCELS OF LAND FOR THE LAND TRANSPORTATION COMMISSION.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 160 dated September 16, 1963, which reserved for the Public Service Commission certain parcels of land of the private domain in Quezon City, Island of Luzon, and reserve the same parcels of land for the Land Transportation Commission.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
