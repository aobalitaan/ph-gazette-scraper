TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1117

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 15 TO 21, 1973, AS NATIONAL PET WEEK.

WHEREAS, animal pets have brought joy and consolation to man; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to inculcate in the minds and hearts of our youth the virtues of kindness and mercy to our pets which have delighted humanity from time immemorial;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from March 15 to 21, 1973, as National Pet Week under the auspices of the Philippine Canine Club, Inc., the Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals and other organization involved.

Proclamation No. 955 dated January 27, 1972, is hereby modified accordingly.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
