MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 565

DECLARING FRIDAY, MAY 30, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BOHOL AND TAGBILARAN CITY.

WHEREAS, Friday, May 30, 1969, marks the height of the revolt of Dagohoy against Spanish tyranny in Bohol; and

WHEREAS, the people of Bohol should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate the occasion with fitting ceremonies and to render tribute to Dagohoy’s exemplary love of country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Friday, May 30, 1969, as a special public holiday in the Province of Bohol and Tagbilaran City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOSE L. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
