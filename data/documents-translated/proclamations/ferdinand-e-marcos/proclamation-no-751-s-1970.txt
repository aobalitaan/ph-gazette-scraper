MALACAñANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 751

CALLING THE CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO A THIRD SPECIAL SESSION.

WHEREAS, the public interest requires that the Congress of the Philippines be convened in a third special session in order to consider other legislative measures of urgent nature;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby call the Congress of the Philippines to a third special session for such number of days as may be necessary not exceeding thirty (30) days commencing on September 7, 1970 at 10:00 o’clock in the morning, to consider, among others the members may consider wise to act on, the following bills:

Further. I hereby certify, pursuant to the provisions of Article VI, Section 9 and 21(2) of the Constitution, to the necessity of the immediate enactment of the above-entitled bills.

All persons entitled to sit as members of the Congress of the Philippines are requested to take notice of this Proclamation.

In WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
