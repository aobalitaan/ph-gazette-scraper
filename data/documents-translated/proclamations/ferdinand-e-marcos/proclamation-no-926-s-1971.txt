TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 926

DECLARING SUNDAY, OCTOBER 24, 1971, AS AN INTERNATIONAL HOLIDAY.

WHEREAS, October 24 of every year has been declared United Nations Day under Proclamation No. 138, s. 1949;

WHEREAS, the Philippines finds it desirable to declare United Nations Day this year as an International Holiday for an increased awareness and appreciation of the purposes, objectives and significant accomplishments of the world organization;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare October 24, 1971, as an international holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
