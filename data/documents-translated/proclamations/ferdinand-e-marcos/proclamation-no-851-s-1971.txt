TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 851

DECLARING THE LAST WEEK OF MAY AS MARKETING WEEK.

WHEREAS, it is one of the avowed policies of the Government to promote our economy through expanded foreign trade;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Marketing Association, the Chamber of Commerce of the Philippines, the Philippine Chamber of Industries, the Department of Commerce and Industry and the Board of Investments are co-sponsoring “Export Marketing Conference ’71” on May 26 to 28, 1971, at the Penthouse Social Hall of the Development Bank of the Philippines; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus public attention on the importance on marketing in business and industry;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from May 24 to 30, 1971, and the last week of May thereafter as Marketing Week in the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
