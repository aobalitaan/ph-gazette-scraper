TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 639

DECLARING TUESDAY, JANUARY 6, 1970 AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF TOLEDO.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, January 6, 1970 marks the anniversary of the City Charter of Toledo City; and

WHEREAS, the people of said City should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ERNESTO M. MACEDA, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, January 6, 1970, as a special public holiday in Toledo City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
