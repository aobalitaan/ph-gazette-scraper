OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 20 TO 26, 1974, AS “BROADCAST WEEK”

WHEREAS, the Association of Broadcasters of the Philippines will celebrate its 50th year of broadcasting this month; and

WHEREAS, there is a necessity to focus public attention on the important role the Association plays in radio broadcasting service during national emergencies, disasters and calamities, and in promoting and fostering friendly international relations;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from October 20 to 26, 1974, as “Broadcast Week” under the auspices of the Association of Broadcasters of the Philippines and other broadcasting associations.

By the Acting Executive Secretary:
