TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 912

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 1, 1971, TO MARCH 3, 1972, FOR THE NATIONAL MEMBERSHIP, EDUCATIONAL AND FUND DRIVE OF THE AEROSPACE ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC.

WHEREAS, aviation and aerospace technology are careers which should be encouraged among the youth of the land; and

WHEREAS, the educational, technological and recreational activities of the Aerospace Cadets of the Philippines need the support of the Filipino people;

NOW, THEREFORE, I ROBERTO V. REYES, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from October 1, 1971 to March 3, 1972, for the national membership, educational and fund drive of the Aerospace Association of the Philippines, Inc.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
