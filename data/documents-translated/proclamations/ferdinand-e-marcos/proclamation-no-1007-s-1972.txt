TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1007

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 18 TO 24, 1972 AS “NATIONAL SALESMEN’S WEEK.”

WHEREAS, Salesmen play an important role in stimulating business and industry;

WHEREAS, Salesmen have the obligation to maintain high ethical standards for the protection of the public and consumers; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus attention on this dual role of Salesmen at least once a year;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do: hereby declare the period from June 18 to 24, 1972, as National Salesmen’s Week under the auspices of Salesmen and Marketing Associations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
