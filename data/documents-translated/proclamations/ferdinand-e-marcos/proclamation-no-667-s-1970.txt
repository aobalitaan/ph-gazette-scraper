MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 667

DECLARING SATURDAY, MARCH 28, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, Saturday, March 28, 1970, may be declared as a special public holiday without detriment to the public service:

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS,President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, March 28, 1970, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary
