MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 144

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 8, 1967, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN TIAONG, QUEZON, AND IN LIPA CITY.

WHEREAS, the seventy-seventh birthday anniversary of Don Claro M. Recto falls on Wednesday, February 8, 1967;

WHEREAS, during his lifetime by sheer force of character brilliance of mind and greatness of heart he gave honor, dignity and sense of pride to our country and people, fought for freedom by championing the cause of nationalism and contributed much in the adoption of our Constitution; and

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper to afford the residents of Tiaong, Quezon, his birthplace, an ample opportunity to celebrate the event with an appropriate ceremony.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Wednesday, February 8, 1967, as a special public holiday in Tiaong, Quezon, and Lipa City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
