MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 311

ESTABLISHING AS CIVIL RESERVATION FOR ADDITIONAL TOWNSITE OF REAL, QUEZON A CERTAIN PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF REAL, PROVINCE OF QUEZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, and pursuant to Chapter XI of the Public Land Act, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale or settlement or other disposition and reserve as civil reservation for additional townsite of Real, Quezon a certain parcel of the public domain situated in the Municipality of Real, Province of Quezon, under the administration of the Municipality of Real, subject to private rights, if any there be, and under the condition that the cutting, collection and removal of timber and other forest products therein shall be in accordance with forest laws and regulations.

The aforementioned parcel of public domain is more particularly described as follows:

A PARCEL OF LAND beginning at point 1 which is identical to Corner 1 of Cn. F. Par. I, Real, Quezon;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
