MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 733

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 17, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF PASAY.

In order to afford the residents of Pasay City the opportunity to celebrate the City’s charter anniversary which falls on August 16, 1970, with appropriate ceremonies, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, August 17, 1970, as a special public holiday in the City of Pasay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source: University of the Philippines, College of Law Library
