TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 976

DECLARING THE SECOND WEEK OF APRIL 1972 AS ELECTRONIC DATA PROCESSING WEEK

WHEREAS, electronic data processing and computer programing are increasingly v playing important roles in government, business and industry by modernizing systems, methods and programming techniques; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus attention on the importance of electronic data processing in economic development;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the second week of April 1972 as Electronic Data Processing Week under the auspices of the Electronic Data Processing Society of the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of February in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
