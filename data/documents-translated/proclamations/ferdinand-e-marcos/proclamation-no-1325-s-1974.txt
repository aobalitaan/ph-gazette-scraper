TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1325

DECLARING OCTOBER 8 OF EVERY YEAR AS PHILIPPINE LIONS SERVICE DAY

WHEREAS, October 8 has been declared as World Lions Service Day during which millions of Lions Clubs members all over the world will rededicate themselves towards rendering humanitarian service to their fellow men; and

WHEREAS, the Lions Clubs of the Philippines desire to commemorate World Lions Service Day by making all their members throughout the country and the public as well aware of the need to work hand in hand in the great task of nation building, and to give support to the country’s efforts towards economic and social progress;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare October 8 of every year as Philippine Lions Service Day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
