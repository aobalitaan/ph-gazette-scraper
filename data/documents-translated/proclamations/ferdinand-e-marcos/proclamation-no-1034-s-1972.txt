RESERVING FOR THE USE OF THE AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTIVITY COMMISSION, THE BUREAU OF SOILS, THE BUREAU OF PLANT INDUSTRY, THE BUREAU OF LANDS AND THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT OF IPIL, ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE SAID MUNICIPALITY.
