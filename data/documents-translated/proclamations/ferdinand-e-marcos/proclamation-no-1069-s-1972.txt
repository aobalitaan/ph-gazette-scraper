TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1069

CALLING THE CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO A NINTH SPECIAL SESSION.

WHEREAS, the public interest requires that the Congress of the Philippines be convened anew in another special session in order to consider legislative measures of urgent nature;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby call the Congress of the Philippines to a Ninth Special Session for such number of days as may be necessary not exceeding thirty (30) days commencing immediately upon adjournment of the current Eighth Special Session, to consider such subjects as the members may deem and/or such subjects as I may certify as urgent for enactment, to include the following:

Further, I hereby certify, pursuant to the provisions of Article VI, Sections 9 and 21(2) of the Constitution, to the necessity of the immediate enactment of the above-entitled bills.

All persons entitled to sit as members of the Congress of the Philippines are requested to take notice of this proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary and concurrently Legislative Secretary
