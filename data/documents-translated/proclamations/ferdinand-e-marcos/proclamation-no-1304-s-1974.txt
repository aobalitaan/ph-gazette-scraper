TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1304

TRANSFERRING THE CELEBRATION OF “HISTORY WEEK” TO SEPTEMBER 15 TO 21 OF EVERY YEAR

Upon recommendation of the National Historical Commission, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby transfer the annual celebration of “History Week” from September 17 to 23 to September 15 to 21 of every year.

Proclamation No. 1105 dated January 24, 1973, is modified accordingly.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
