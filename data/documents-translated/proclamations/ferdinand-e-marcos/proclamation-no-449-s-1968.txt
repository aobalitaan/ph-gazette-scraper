MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 449

DECLARING SATURDAY, AUGUST 31, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN NOVELETA, CAVITE.

WHEREAS, Saturday, August 31, 1968, marks the seventieth anniversary of the revolt of the people of Noveleta, Cavite against Spanish tyranny; and

WHEREAS, the people of Noveleta should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this event in our people’s struggle for freedom;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Saturday, August 31, 1968, as a special public holiday in Noveleta, Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
