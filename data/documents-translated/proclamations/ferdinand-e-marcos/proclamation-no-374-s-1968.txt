MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 374

DECLARING MAY 1968 TO APRIL 1969 AS NEPA YEAR.

WHEREAS, the National Economic Protectionism Association (NEPA) has been organized for the protection and promotion of local products and industries;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Government has launched a more extensive and vigorous program of economic development; and

WHEREAS, the NEPA has contributed in a large measure towards economic development through its espousal of Philippine-made products and goods;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from May 1968 to April 1969 as “NEPA Year.” During this period, the NEPA is authorized to initiate and carry out projects for the promotion of economic development, particularly through patronage of Philippine-made goods. I call upon the mass media, including theater owners, to support NEPA projects.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
