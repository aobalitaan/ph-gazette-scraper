TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 690

DECLARING TUESDAY, MAY 12, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN ROXAS CITY

WHEREAS, Tuesday, May 12, 1970, marks the anniversary of the foundation of the City of Roxas; and

WHEREAS, the people of that city should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, May 12, 1970 as a special public holiday in Roxas City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
