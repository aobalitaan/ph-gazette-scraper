MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 299

NAMING THE NATIONAL PARK IN THE DISTRICT OF ERMITA, CITY OF MANILA, AS RIZAL PARK.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby name the National Park in the District of Ermita, City of Manila, the metes and bounds of which are delimited in Proclamation No. 273 dated October 4, 1967, as Rizal Park.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
