MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2432

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE ZARZUELA FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC.

WHEREAS, the Zarzuela Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 2360, dated May 15, 1984, to conduct a fund campaign from May 1, 1984 to April 30, 1985;

WHEREAS, due to the unfavorable economic climate prevailing in 1984, the foundation was not able to generate satisfactory results, thus rendering constraints on the implementation of its schedule projects; and

WHEREAS, the foundation needs additional time to raise more funds for its laudable goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Zarzuela Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., up to December 31, 1985.

This amends Proclamation No. 2360, dated May 15, 1984.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
