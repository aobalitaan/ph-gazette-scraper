MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2190

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE WORLD SAFETY ORGANIZATION UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1982.

WHEREAS, the World Safety Organization was authorized under Proclamation No. 2036 dated November 20, 1980 to conduct an educational, membership and fund campaign from January 1 to December 31, 1981; and

WHEREAS, the Organization needs additional time to raise more funds to achieve its goal.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the World Safety Organization up to December 31, 1982.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
