MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 150

ESTABLISHING AS FOREST RESERVE FOR WATERSHED, SOIL PROTECTION, TIMBER PRODUCTION AND OTHER FOREST USES, THE PARCEL OF PUBLIC DOMAIN KNOWN AS TIMBER LAND, COTABATO AND CLASSIFICATION PROJECT NO. 42-Y, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF KIDAPAWAN, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, PHILIPPINES.

Pursuant to the provisions of Section 1826 of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended by the Republic Act No. 3092, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or other disposition, and establish as FOREST RESERVE, subject to private rights, if any there be, the parcel of public domain known as Timber Land, Cotabato Land Classification Project No. 42-Y, situated in the Municipality of Kidapawan, Province of Cotabato, for watershed, soil protection, timber production and other forest purposes under the administration and control of the Director of Forestry who shall have the authority to regulate the use and occupancy of said Forest Reserve and the cutting, collection and removal of timber and other forest products therein in accordance with forest laws and regulations.

The aforementioned parcel of public domain is shown in the attached Bureau of Forestry Map L . C . -2467, comprising the Timber Land of the aforementioned Cotabato Land Classification Project No. 42-Y, containing an area of TEN THOUSAND AND FORTY-TWO (10,042) HECTARES, more or less.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
