TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 804

DECLARING THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 18, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF NORTHERN SAMAR.

WHEREAS, the charter anniversary of the province of Northern Samar falls on February 18, 1971; and

WHEREAS, the people of the province should be given opportunity to observe the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thursday, February 18, 1971, as a special public holiday in the province of Northern Samar.

IN WITNESS THEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) PONCIANO G.A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
