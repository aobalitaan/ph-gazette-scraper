MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 324

RESERVING FOR NATIONAL SHRINE PURPOSES TO BE KNOWN AS THE “MABINI SHRINE” – CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF PANDACAN, CITY OF MANILA.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for national shrine purposes to be known as the “Mabini Shrine” under the administration of the National Historical Commission, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future survey, a certain parcel of land of the private domain situated in the District of Pandacan, City of Manila, more particularly described as follows:

The Proposed Site for the MABINI SHRINE

A PARCEL OF LAND ( the proposed Site for the MABINI SHRINE), situated at the former Bureau of Animal Industry Compound, Pandacan, City of Manila. Bounded on the NE., along lines 1-2-3-4-5-6-7-8-9, by Pasig River; on the E., along line 9-10, by Proposed Mabini Bridge; on the SW., along line 10-11, by former Bureau of Animal Industry Compound and Existing Road. Beginning at  appoint marked “1” on the attached Sketch Plan,

thence S. 87 deg. 23’E., 5.02 m. to point 2; thence S. 80 deg. 50’E., 11.77 m. to point 3 thence S. 68 deg. 34’E., 8.90 m. to point 4; thence S. 11 deg. 39’E., 10.66 m. to point 5; thence S. 50 deg. 44’E., 5.80 m. to point 6; thence S. 44 deg. 07’E., 8.85 m. to point 7; thence S. 50 deg. 36’E., 32.12 m. to point 8; thence S. 50 deg. 18’E., 29.00 m. to point 9; thence N. 62 deg. 57’W ., 100.38 m. to point 10; thence N. 7 deg. 29’W ., 40.83 m. to the point of beginning; containing an approximate area of THREE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED TWENTY SQUARE METERS AND NINETY SQUARE DECIMETERS (3,520.90)

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2 nd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty- eight.

SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
