MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2091

DECLARING FRIDAY, JUNE 19, 1981, AS DR. JOSE P. RIZAL’S DAY IN LAGUNA.

WHEREAS, the nineteenth of June marks the 120th birth anniversary of Dr. Jose P. Rizal, our national hero; and

WHEREAS, it is most fitting and proper to give the people of Laguna full opportunity to celebrate his natal day with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare June 19, 1981 as Dr. Jose P. Rizal Day in Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
