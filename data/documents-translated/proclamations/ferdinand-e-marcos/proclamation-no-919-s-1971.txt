TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 919

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 18 TO 24, 1971, AS MECHANICAL AND ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING WEEK.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Association of Mechanical and Electrical Engineers holds its 41st Annual Convention on October 18-24, 1971; and

WHEREAS, it is necessary to underscore the important role the mechanical and electrical engineering professions play in our country’s economic development;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from October 18 to 24, 1971, as Mechanical and Electrical Engineering Week under the auspices of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering Associations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
