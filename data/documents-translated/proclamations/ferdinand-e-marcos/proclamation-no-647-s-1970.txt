TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 647

DECLARING JANUARY 31, 1970, AS SPECIAL HOLIDAY IN NASUGBU, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, January 31, 1970, marks the 25th anniversary of the airborne landing of liberating forces in Nasugbu, Batangas; and

WHEREAS, the people of that municipality should be given opportunity to celebrate this historical event with appropriate, ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ERNESTO M. MACEDA, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare January 31, 1970, as a special public holiday in the municipality of Nasugbu, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
