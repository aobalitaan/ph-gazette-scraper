MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 26

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM APRIL 28 TO MAY 15, 1966, AS ASIAN YOUTH FORTNIGHT.

WHEREAS, the Philippines will be host to the 8th Asian Youth Football Tournament in which fourteen Asian nations will participate; and

WHEREAS, such international tournaments promote better understanding and relations among countries and their peoples, besides fostering character development and physical fitness among the youth;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from April 28 to May 15, 1966, as Asian Youth Fortnight under the auspices of 8th Asian Youth Football Tournament Committee. I enjoin public officials, employees and citizens to cooperate in making this event a success.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
