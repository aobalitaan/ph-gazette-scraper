MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1470

FURTHER EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION TO DECEMBER 31, 1975

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 1383 dated February 11, 1975, to extend its educational and fund campaign up to June 30, 1975; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs additional time to raise more funds in preparation for the 1976 Montreal World Olympics;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby further extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association up to December 30, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
