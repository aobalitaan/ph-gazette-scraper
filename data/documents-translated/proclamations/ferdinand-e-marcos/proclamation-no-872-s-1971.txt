TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 872

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 20 TO AUGUST 1, 1971, AS “NATIONAL ADVERTISING WEEK.”

WHEREAS, advertising plays a vital role in the nation’s economy by maximizing consumers’ benefits through ethical practices; and

WHEREAS, consumers should be made to realize the benefits they derive through responsible advertising;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from July 26 to August 1, 1971, as “National Advertising Week” under the auspices of the Philippine Association of National Advertisers and other advertising associations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
