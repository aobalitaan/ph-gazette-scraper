MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 668

RESERVING FOR ICE AND COLD STORAGE PLANT AND LABORATORY SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED AT JOLO, SULU, ISLAND OF JOLO, UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE FISHERIES COMMISSION.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 63 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for Ice and Cold Storage Plant and Laboratory Building site purposes, under the administration of the Philippine Fisheries Commission, subject to private rights, if any there be, a parcel of land of the public domain, situated in Port Area, Jolo, Sulu, more particularly described in Bureau of Lands Plan NR-240, as follows:

A parcel of land (as shown on plan Nr-240), situated in the District of Port Area, Municipality of Jolo, Province of Sulu, Island of Jolo, bounded on the NW., along line 1-2, by Reclamation Area (under water) and Proposed Boulevard (Reclamation Area, under water); on the SE. and SW., along lines 2-3-4, by Reclamation Area (under water); and on the NW. and N. along lines 4-5-6-1, by Proposed Road (Reclamation Area, under water). Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 12 deg. 31 min. W., 517.55 meters from B.L.L.M. 2, Mt. Dajo Cadastre,

of beginning; containing an area of one thousand nine hundred sixteen (1,916) square meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: points 3, point under water; and the rest, are Intersections of Conc. Wall; bearing true; date of survey, March 1-29, 1968 and approved on July 22, 1969.

NOTE: This survey is within Sulu Sea and is outside Mt. Dajo Cadastre.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary
