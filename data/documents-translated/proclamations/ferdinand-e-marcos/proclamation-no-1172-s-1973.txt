TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1172

AUTHORIZING THE MORAL RE-ARMAMENT FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT AN EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM AUGUST 1 TO JANUARY 31, 1974.

WHEREAS, the promoting of moral values in the country and the development of our youth into good and useful citizens and enlightened leaders of our country are vital concern of society and all efforts should be exerted in that direction;

WHEREAS, projects for the development of the youth towards this end and the provision of proper facilities for such development should be encouraged; and

WHEREAS, the Moral Re-Armament Foundation of the Philippines is carrying out a program with objectives geared to these ends;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Moral Re-Armament Foundation of the Philippines to conduct an educational and fund campaign from August 1 to January 31, 1974. I enjoin all residents, business firms, civic, educational, religious and government institutions to support this humanitarian movement.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
