MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2300

AUTHORIZING THE NATIONAL LEAGUE OF PUERICULTURE AND FAMILY PLANNING CENTERS (NLPFC), INC. TO CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 15, 1983 TO JULY 15, 1984.

WHEREAS, the NLPFC was authorized under Proclamation No. 1855 to conduct a fund campaign from May 1, 1979 to May 1, 1980 to enable it to update medical facilities and supplies by launching “Operation Medical Equipment and Supplies for Puericulture Centers”;

WHEREAS, said fund campaign was extended by Proclamation No. 1970 to May 1, 1981; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs more time to realize its fund campaign goals.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law do hereby authorize the National League of Puericulture and Family Planning Centers to conduct a fund campaign during the period from July 15, 1983 to July 15, 1984.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-three.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
