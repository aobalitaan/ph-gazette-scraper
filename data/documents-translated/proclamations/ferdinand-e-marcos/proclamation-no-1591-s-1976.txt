TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1591

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF VOTING FOR THE REFERENDUM-PLEBISCITE UP TO BUT NOT BEYOND NOON OF OCTOBER 17, 1976.

WHEREAS, due to the increase in the number of registered barangay voters throughout the country, the Commission on Elections has submitted the view that the voting in the referendum-plebiscite may not be completed in one day;

WHEREAS, it is imperative that the barangay voters be afforded ample opportunity to participate in the referendum-plebiscite;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers in me vested by the Constitution do hereby extend the period of voting for the referendum-plebiscite throughout the country from October 16 up to but not beyond noon of October 17, 1976; Provided , however , That the Commission on Elections may authorize the voting in any barangay referendum-plebiscite center to continue unabated in places where the voting may be completed in the same day without causing undue inconvenience to the voters.

IN WITNESS HEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and cause the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 15th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
