MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1680

EXTENDING THE PERIOD FOR THE REGISTRATION OF KABATAANG BARANGAY MEMBERS UP TO OCTOBER 31, 1977

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 1674 dated October 4, 1977, declared the period from October 8 to 15, 1977, as registration days for Kabataang Barangay Members; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to give sufficient time for our youth to register with their Barangays;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the period of registration for Kabataang Barangay members up to October 31, 1977.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
