TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1332

DECLARING NOVEMBER 26, 1974, AS “LIBERATION DAY” OF ISABELA, NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS, November 26, 1974, marks the anniversary of the liberation of Isabela, Negros Occidental, from the Imperial Japanese forces thirty years ago; and

WHEREAS, the Veterans Federation of the Philippines, 3rd District Negros Occidental, Isabela Branch, wish to commemorate the occasion in honor of the unknown heroes who died side by side with the Allied Forces;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare November 26, 1974, as “Liberation Day” of Isabela, Negros Occidental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
