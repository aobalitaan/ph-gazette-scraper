Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1212

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL MEMBERSHIP, EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE MORAL RE-ARMAMENT FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES TO DECEMBER 31, 1974.

WHEREAS, the Moral Re-Armament Foundation of the Philippines was authorized under Proclamation No. 1172 dated October 19, 1973, to conduct a national fund campaign from August 1 to January 31, 1974; and

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs additional time to enable it to realize its fund campaign goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the national fund campaign of the Moral Re-Armament Foundation of the Philippines to December 31, 1974.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
