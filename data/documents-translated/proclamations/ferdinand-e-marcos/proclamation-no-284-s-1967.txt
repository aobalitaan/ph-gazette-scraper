MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 284

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION UP TO DECEMBER 30, 1967.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 204, dated May 22, 1967, to conduct a fund drive for the construction of public swimming pools all over the country; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs additional time for the conduct of its drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund drive of the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association up to December 30, 1967.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
