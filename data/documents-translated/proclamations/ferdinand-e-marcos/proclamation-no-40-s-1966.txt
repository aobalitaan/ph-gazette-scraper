MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 40

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 1 TO AUGUST 15, 1966, FOR THE EIGHTEENTH ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST.

To enable the Community Chest to carry on its community welfare services, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from June 1 to August 15, 1966, for its eighteenth annual educational and fund campaign.

I call upon all residents, firms and organizations in the City of Manila, Quezon City, Pasay City, Caloocan City and municipalities of Makati, Malabon, Mandaluyong, Navotas, Parañaque and San Juan, Province of Rizal, to support this campaign and to give generously to the Community Chest which unites into one annual appeal the fund campaigns of the following welfare agencies:

Abiertas House of Friendship American Philippine Guardian Association de Damas Filipinas Settlement House Asilo de San Vicente de Paul Catholic Youth Organization Free Legal Aid Clinic Free Medical Clinics (Patronatos) St. Joseph’s Patronage of the Sick St. Philomena’s Patronage of the Sick Poor Patronato de Nuestra Sra. de Lourdes Patronato de Santa Luisa de Marillac Patronato de San Francisco Javier Girl Scouts of the Philippines Quezon City Girl Scouts Council Pasay City Girl Scouts Council Manila Girl Scouts Council Rizal Girl Scouts Council Caloocan City Girl Scouts Council Good Shepherd Convent La Proteccion de la Infancia Manila Children’s Hospital Gota de Leche National Federation of Women’s Clubs Nursery Classes Philippine Association of the Deaf Philippine Band of Mercy Philippine Mental Health Association YMCA of the Philippines Manila YMCA Downtown YMCA Quezon City YMCA YWCA of the Philippines Manila YWCA General Assembly of the Blind Family Life Workshop of the Philippines Young Christian Workers

I authorize and urge all government officials and employees, including school authorities and teachers, to accept, for the Community Chest, fund-raising responsibilities and to give it full support and dedicated leadership in their respective communities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
