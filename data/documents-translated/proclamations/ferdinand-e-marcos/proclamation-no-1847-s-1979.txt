MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1847

DECLARING MONDAY, MAY 7, 1979, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO.

WHEREAS, the 39th foundation day of the City of San Pablo falls on May 7, 1979; and

WHEREAS, the people therein desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this milestone in the history of their city;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare May 7, 1979, as a special public holiday in the City of San Pablo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
