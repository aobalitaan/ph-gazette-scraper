TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1303

AUTHORIZING THE ZONTA CLUB OF MAKATI, PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT A FUND DRIVE FROM AUGUST 15, 1974 TO OCTOBER 15, 1974

WHEREAS, the Zonta Club of Makati, Philippines, a non-profit civic organization, is engaged in several social action projects, among which are the Silahis Drug Center and the Nutrition Center and Self-Help Project at Carmona, Cavite; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs the support and cooperation of the public to enable it to carry on with its projects;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize said association to conduct a fund drive from August 15, 1974 to October 15, 1974.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
