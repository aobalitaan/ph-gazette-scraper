MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1423

DECLARING FRIDAY, APRIL 25, 1975, AS PHILIPPINE AUSTRALIAN ASSISTANCE DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM DAY

WHEREAS, Friday, April 25, 1975, marks the anniversary of the signing of the memorandum of Understanding of the Philippine Australian Assistance Development Program; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper to focus public attention on the significance of said Program in Philippine-Australian relations;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, April 25, 1975, as Philippine Australian Assistance Development Program Day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
