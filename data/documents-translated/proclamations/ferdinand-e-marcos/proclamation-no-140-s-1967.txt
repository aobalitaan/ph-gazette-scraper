MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 140

DECLARING FRIDAY, JANUARY 27, 1967, AND EVERY LAST FRIDAY OF JANUARY OF EACH YEAR AS TEACHERS DAY.

WHEREAS, teachers play a vital role in imparting knowledge to, and instilling character among, our youth; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting that a day be set aside to honor them for their services to the country and to the people;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Friday, January 27, 1967, and every last Friday of January of each year thereafter as Teachers Day under the auspices of the Department of Education.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
