TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 989

DECLARING SATURDAY, APRIL 1, 1972, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, the 31st day (Good Friday) of March and the 2nd day (Sunday) of April, nineteen hundred and seventy-two, being public holidays, the 1st day (Saturday) of April, nineteen hundred and seventy-two, may be declared as a special public holiday without any prejudice to the public interest;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, April 1, 1972, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Source: Malacañang Records Office
