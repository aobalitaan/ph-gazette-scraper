MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1099

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 23, 1973, AS “MARINDUQUE PROCLAMATION DAY” IN THE PROVINCE OF MARINDUQUE.

WHEREAS, Friday, February 23, 1973, marks the 53rd anniversary of the establishment of Marinduque as an independent province; and

WHEREAS, to the residents of Marinduque the day is a milestone in their Province’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, February 23, 1973, as “Marinduque Proclamation Day” in the Province of Marinduque.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
