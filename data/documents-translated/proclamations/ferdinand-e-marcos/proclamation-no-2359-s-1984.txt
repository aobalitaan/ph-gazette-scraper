MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2359

RESERVING FOR A PLAYGROUND AND SCHOOL SITES PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARRIO BULIGAY, MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE’S POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN.

Upon recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and reserve for playground and school sites purposes under the administrator of the Director of Elementary Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barrio Buligay, Municipality of Brooke’s Point, Province of Palawan, Island of Palawan, more particularly described as follows:

Ccn-04-000034

A parcel of land (as shown in the consolidation plan Ccn-04-000034, being the consolidation of Lots 10, 11, 12, 13, 106, 107, 108 and 152, Ts-1027, Buligay Townsite, L.R.C. Cad. Record No. situated in the Barrio Buligay, Municipality of Brooke’s Point, Province of Palawan. Bounded on the NE., along lines 1-2-3-4-5 by Lot 247, Pls-96, Brooke’s Point Public Land Subdivision; on the SE., along lines 5-6-7-8 by Road (2.00 m. wide); on the SW., along lines 8-9-10-11 by Foreshore Land of Sulu Sea; and on the NW., along lines 11-12-13-1 by Road 2.00 m. wide). Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 72 deg. 20’W., 796.19 m. from BLLM No. 1, Pls-96, Brooke’s Point Public Land Subd.;

thence S. 69 deg. 58′ E., 11.12 m. to point 2; S. 69 deg. 58′ E., 12.23 m. to point 3; S. 69 deg. 58′ E., 12.50 m. to point 4; S. 69 deg. 58′ E., 10.49 m. to point 5; S. 18 deg. 26′ W., 49.43 m. to point 6; S. 15 deg. 43′ W., 2.07 m. to point 7; S. 18 deg. 21′ W., 15.59 m. to point 8; N. 64 deg. 46′ W., 10.02 m. to point 9; N. 66 deg. 01′ W., 12.50 m. to point 10; N. 75 deg. 06′ W., 23.97 m. to point 11; N. 18 deg. 05′ E., 16.73 m. to point 12; N. 17 deg. 19′ E., 1.95 m. to point 13; N. 18 deg. 26′ E., 48.79 m. to point of beginning, containing an area of three thousand seventy (3,070) square meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and area marked on the ground by Old. B.L.Z. Cyl. Conc. Mons. (15 x 60 cm) Bearings true, date of the original survey, December 26, 1963 – January 20, 1964 and March 1-20, 1964 and the consolidation was prepared on March 19, 1984. Date approved, March 20, 1984.

NOTE: This is lot 153, Ts-1027, Buligay Townsite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-four.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
