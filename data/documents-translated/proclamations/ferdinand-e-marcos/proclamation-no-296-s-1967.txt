MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 296

RESERVING FOR MARKET SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MABINI, PROVINCE OF DAVAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of General Services, and pursuant to the provisions of Section 64 (E) of the Revised Administrative Code in relation to Section 1 of Republic Act No. 477, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for market site purposes under the administration of the Municipality of Mabini, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of land of the private domain, situated in the Municipality of Mabini, Province of Davao, and more particularly described as follows:
