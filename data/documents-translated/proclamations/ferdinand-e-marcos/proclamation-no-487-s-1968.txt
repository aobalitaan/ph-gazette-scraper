MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 487

TRANSFERRING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE NATIONAL LEAGUE OF PUERICULTURE CENTERS (NLPC) TO THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 15 TO DECEMBER 31, 1968.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 394 dated May 10, 1968, which authorized the National League of Puericulture Centers to conduct a national educational and fund campaign from May 16 to June 15, 1968, by postponing said campaign to the period covering from November 15 to December 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
