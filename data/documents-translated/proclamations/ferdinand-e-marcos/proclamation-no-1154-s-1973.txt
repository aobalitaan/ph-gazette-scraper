TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1154

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 8 TO 14, 1973, AS PHILIPPINE INDUSTRY WEEK

WHEREAS, the 20th National Convention of Manufacturers and Producers will be held during the second week of July 1973, with the end in view of discussing ways and means to aid or assist in the industrialization and socio-economic development of the country; and

WHEREAS, there is need to focus public attention on the New Society’s goal of attaining a stable and progressive economy;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from July 8 to 14, 1973, as Philippine Industry Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
