TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1454

AUTHORIZING THE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM AND LIBRARY, INC. FOUNDATION TO CONDUCT AN EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND DRIVE FROM JULY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1975.

WHEREAS, the promotion of the well-being of our children and their development into good and useful citizens and enlightened leaders of our country are of vital concern to society and all efforts should be exerted in that direction;

WHEREAS, civic projects for the development of the latent capabilities of our youth and the provision of proper facilities for such development should be encouraged; and

WHEREAS, the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc., a non-stock, non-profit civic foundation, is carrying out a program with objectives geared to these ends;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc. to conduct an educational, membership and fund drive from July 1 to December 31, 1975. I enjoin all residents, business firms, civic, religious, educational, government and other institutions to support this humanitarian movement.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
