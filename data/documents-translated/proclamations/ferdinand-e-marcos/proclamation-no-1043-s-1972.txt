TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1043

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND DRIVE OF THE PHILIPPINE CANCER SOCIETY, INC.

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 958, dated January 31, 1972, the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc. was authorized to conduct a national educational, membership and fund drive during the period from February 1 to June 30, 1972; and

WHEREAS, the Society needs additional time to raise more funds to achieve its goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the national educational, membership and fund drive of the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc., up to December 31, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
