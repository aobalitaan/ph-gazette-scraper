TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1037

DECLARING SATURDAY, JUNE 24, 1972, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF MANILA

WHEREAS, Saturday, June 24, 1972, marks the 101st anniversary of the foundation of Manila as a Christian capital city; and

WHEREAS, the residents should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, June 24, 1972, as a special public holiday in the City of Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
