TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 895

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA.

WHEREAS, Friday, September 3, 1971, marks the charter anniversary of the City of Iriga; and

WHEREAS, the residents of the city should be afforded the opportunity to commemorate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, September 3, 1971, as a special public holiday in Iriga City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MECHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
