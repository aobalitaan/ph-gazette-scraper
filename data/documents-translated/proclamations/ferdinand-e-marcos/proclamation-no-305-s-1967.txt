MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 305

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE 1967 NATIONAL ANTI-TB EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE TUBERCULOSIS SOCIETY, INC. UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1967.

WHEREAS, the period for the 1967 National Anti-TB Educational and Fund Campaign of the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. has been set from August 19 to October 31, 1967 under Proclamation No. 213 dated June 16, 1967; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc., needs more time to realize its fund campaign goal of P600,000.00 for the maintenance of its vital medical, social and educational services to the people;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the Anti-TB Educational and Fund Campaign of the Philippine Tuberculosis Society up to December 31, 1967.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
