MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1970

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE NATIONAL LEAGUE OF PUERICULTURE AND FAMILY PLANNING CENTERS (NLPFPC), INC.

WHEREAS, the NLPFPC was authorized under Proclamation No. 1855 to conduct a national fund campaign from May 1, 1979 to May 1, 1980 to enable it to update its medical facilities and supplies by launching “Operation Medical Equipment and Supplies for Puericulture Centers”; and

WHEREAS, the organization need more time to realize its fund campaign goals;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the National League of Puericulture and Family Planning Centers, Inc. to May 1, 1981.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
