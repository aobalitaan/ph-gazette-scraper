MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 202

RESERVING FOR HEALTH CENTER AND MARKET SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for health center and market site purposes under the administration of the Municipal Government of Calamba, Laguna, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to the provisions of Executive Order No. 113, series of 1955, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the municipality of Calamba, province of Laguna, which is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 369

Calamba Public Land Subdivision

Pls-709

(Proposed Health Center and Market Site)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 369, of Calamba Public Land Subdivision, Pls-709), situated in the Barrio; of Palingon, Municipality of Calamba, Province of Laguna.

Bounded on the NW. and NE., along lines 1-2-3, by Road; on the SE., along line 3-4, by Road; and on the SW., along line 4-1, by Road.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 26 deg. 05′ E., 3016.11 m. from M.B.M. 5, Calamba, Laguna,

thence N. 59 deg. 50′ E., 43.98 m. to point 2;

thence S. 79 deg. 02′ E., 28.11 m. to point 3;

thence S. 19 deg. 04′ W., 36.29 m. to point 4;

thence N. 71 deg. 55′ W., 56.55 m. to the point of

beginning; containing an area of ONE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED THIRTY THREE (1,433) Square Meters.

NOTE:

Verification of Calamba Public Land Subdivision, Pls-709, is still in progress in the Public Land Survey Section and therefore the technical description appearing herein is still subject to change.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
