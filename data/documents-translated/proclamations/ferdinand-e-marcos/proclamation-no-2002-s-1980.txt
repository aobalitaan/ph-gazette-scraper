MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2002

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 1980, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN IRIGA CITY.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 3, 1980, marks the 12th Charter Anniversary of the City of Iriga; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Iriga should be afforded the opportunity to commemorate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law do hereby declare September 3, 1980, as a special public holiday in Iriga City, but the day will be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Senior Presidential Assistant
