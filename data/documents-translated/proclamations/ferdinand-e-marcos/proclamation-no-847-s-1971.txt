MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 847

DECLARING THE YEAR 1971 AS GENERAL MAKABULOS CENTENNIAL YEAR IN THE PROVINCE OF TARLAC.

WHEREAS, September 17, 1971, marks the centenary of the birth of the late General Francisco Makabulos, one of the country’s foremost revolutionary leaders; and

WHEREAS, there is need to perpetuate his gallantry and patriotism to give inspiration to the people of our generation, especially the people of Tarlac, the province of his birth;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the year 1971 as General Makabulos Centennial Year in the province of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
