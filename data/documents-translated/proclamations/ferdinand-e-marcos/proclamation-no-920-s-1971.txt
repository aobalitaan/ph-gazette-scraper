TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 920

DECLARING TUESDAY, OCTOBER 19, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF BACOLOD.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, October 19, 1971, marks the 33rd Charter Anniversary of the City of Bacolod; and

WHEREAS, the residents of the City should be given the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, October 19, 1971, as a special public holiday in Bacolod City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
