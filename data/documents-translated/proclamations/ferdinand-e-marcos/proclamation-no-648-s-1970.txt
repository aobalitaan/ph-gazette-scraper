TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 648

DECLARING FEBRUARY 16 OF EVERY YEAR AS CALOOCAN DAY

WHEREAS, February 16, 1970 marks the anniversary of the foundation of the City of Caloocan; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus public attention on the accomplishments and progress of the City so as to stimulate the interest, cooperation and participation of the residents therein;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ERNESTO M. MACEDA, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare February 16 of every year as “Caloocan Day.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
