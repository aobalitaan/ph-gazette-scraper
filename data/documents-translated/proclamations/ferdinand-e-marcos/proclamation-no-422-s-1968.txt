MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 422

FURTHER EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE BARRIO BOOK FOUNDATION, INC. UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1968.

WHEREAS, the Barrio Book Foundation, Inc. was authorized under Proclamation No. 337 dated February 5, 1968, to conduct its campaign for book fund for barrio high schools throughout the Philippines; and

WHEREAS, the Foundation has not been successful in attaining its original goal of ₱500,000.00 needed to carry on its mission;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Barrio Book Foundation, Inc., up to December 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
