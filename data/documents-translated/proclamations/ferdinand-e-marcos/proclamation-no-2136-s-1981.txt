MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2136

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 23 TO 28, 1981 AS “REAL ESTATE AND HOUSING WEEK”.

WHEREAS, the Chamber of Real Estate and Builders’ Association Inc., the Real Estate Brokers Association and the Subdivision Owners Association of the Philippines will hold a national conference on November 24 to 26, 1981; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper to focus national attention on the vital role played by said organizations in the development of our country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from November 23 to 28, 1981 as “Real Estate and Housing Week”.

IN WITNESS, WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
