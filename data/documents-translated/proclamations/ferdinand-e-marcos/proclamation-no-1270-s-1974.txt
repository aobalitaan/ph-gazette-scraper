Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1270

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE DR. JOSE P. RIZAL MEMORIAL FOUNDATION.

WHEREAS, the Dr. Jose P. Rizal Memorial Foundation was authorized under Proclamation No. 1231 dated February 26, 1974, to conduct a fund drive from February 23 to June 19, 1974, for its Dr. Jose P. Rizal Medical Research Center in the resettlement community at Dasmariñas, Cavite; and

WHEREAS, the foundation needs more time for the conduct of its drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the Dr. Jose P. Rizal Memorial Foundation up to December 31, 1974.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
