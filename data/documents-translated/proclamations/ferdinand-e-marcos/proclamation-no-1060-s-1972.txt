TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1060

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 1046 DATED JULY 12, 1972 DECLARING A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN CERTAIN PROVINCES.

Proclamation No. 1046 dated July 12, 1972, is hereby amended so as to include the following provinces of Leyte, Bohol, Capiz, Cotabato, Cotabato South, Davao Norte, Lanao del Sur, Iloilo and Surigao Norte, which have long been under a prolonged period of drought as calamity areas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
