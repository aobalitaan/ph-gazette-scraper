TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 884

DECLARING TUESDAY, AUGUST 31, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NOVELETA, CAVITE.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, August 31, 1971, marks the anniversary of the revolt of the people of Noveleta, Cavite, against Spanish tyranny; and

WHEREAS, the people of Noveleta should be afforded the opportunity to celebrate this historic event in our people’s struggle for freedom and democracy;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, August 31, 1971, as a special public holiday in the municipality of Noveleta, province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MECHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
