TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1277

DECLARING MONDAY, JULY 1, 1974, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN SOUTHERN LEYTE

WHEREAS, Monday, July 1, 1974, marks the Charter anniversary of the Province of Southern Leyte; and

WHEREAS, the people of the province should be given opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their Province’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, July 1, 1974, as a special public holiday in Southern Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
