MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 489

PROCLAMATION EXTENDING THE 1968 NATIONAL MEMBERSHIP AND EDUCATIONAL CAMPAIGN OF PHILIPPINE MENTAL HEALTH ASSOCIATION, INC. UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1969.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, hereby orders that the designated period from August 15 to November 15 under Proclamation No. 428 dated July 10, 1968 for the 1968 National Membership and Educational Campaign of the Philippine Mental Health Association, Inc., be extended up to December 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
