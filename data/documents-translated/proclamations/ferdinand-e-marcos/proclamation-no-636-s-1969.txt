TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 636

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE GIRL SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO FEBRUARY 28, 1970.

WHEREAS, the “Girl Scouts of the Philippines” was authorized under Proclamation No. 583 to conduct its national fund campaign from August 1 to December 31, 1969; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs more time for the conduct of an effective fund campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ERNESTO M. MACEDA, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the national fund campaign of the Girl Scouts of the Philippines to February 28, 1970.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
