TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 873

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE CANCER SOCIETY, INC., UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1971.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 781 dated December 8, 1970, to conduct a fund drive for its cancer detection, diagnostic and research programs up to June 30, 1971; and

WHEREAS, the Society needs more time for the conduct of its drive to attain its fund goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc., up to December 31, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
