MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 598

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST OF CEBU, INC. TO DECEMBER 31, 1969.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 370 dated June 26, 1969 authorized the Community Chest of Cebu, Inc. to conduct its first annual educational and fund campaign from July 1 to August 15, 1969; and

WHEREAS, said association needs additional time for the conduct of its campaign in order to meet its goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Community Chest of Cebu, Inc. to December 31, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
