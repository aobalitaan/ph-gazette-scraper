MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2409

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 2152, DATED DECEMBER 29, 1981, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE MANGROVE SWAMP FOREST RESERVES, AND DECLARING AS FOREST LAND FOR FISHPOND DEVELOPMENT CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF UBAY, PROVINCE OF BOHOL, ISLAND OF VISAYAS.

Upon recommendation of the Ministers of Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 2152, dated December 29, 1981, which established the Mangrove Swamp forest Reserves, and declare the said portion so excluded as forest lands suitable for fishpond development, subject to private rights, if any there be, as well as to the conditions that the utilization of timber and other forest products found therein shall be subject to Forest and Internal Revenue Laws and Regulations, the parcel of land of the public domain containing an approximate area of 37.0 hectares situated in the Municipality of Ubay, Province of Bohol, Island of Visayas, which is shown in BFD Map LC-3065 as Timberland Block-A and more particularly described as follows:

Beginning from corner 1, which is N 55º 59’W, 10,307.62 meters from BLLM No. 1 Cad. 554-D, Ubay, Bohol, being N 88º 37’E, about 181.05 meters to corner 2;

Thence, S 40º 04E, about 32. 23 meters to corner 3; Thence, S 41º 00’W, about 85 meters to corner 4; Thence, S 40º 59’E, about 184.6 meters to corner 5; Thence, S 54º 21’E, about 97.02 meters to corner 6; Thence, S 51º 37’W, about 90.18 meters to corner 7; Thence, S 23º 08’E, about 52.50 meters to corner 8; Thence, N 8º 30’E, about 94.93 meters to corner 9; Thence, S 6º 21’W, about 69.90 meters to corner 10; Thence, S 34º 02’E, about 121.55 meters to corner 11; Thence, N 79º 02’E, about 62.69 meters to corner 12; Thence, S 12º 12’E, about 104.55 meters to corner 13; Thence, N 60º 50’W, about 94.68 meters to corner 14; Thence, N 43º 20’W, about 171.91 meters to corner 15; Thence, N 87º 12’W, about 66.78 meters to corner 16; Thence, S 40º 51’W, about 98.19 meters to corner 17; Thence, N 61º 16’W, about 168.99 meters to corner 18; Thence, S 25º 51’E, about 84.17 meters to corner 19; Thence, S 56º 09’E, about 240.17 meters to corner 20; Thence, N 59º 19’W, about 112.19 meters to corner 21; Thence, N 89º 58’W, about 419.80 meters to corner 22; Thence, N 5º 15’W, about 364.23 meters to corner 23; Thence, N 6º 41’E, about 506.01 meters to corner 24; Thence, S 51º 44’E, about 120.57 meters to corner 1.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.
