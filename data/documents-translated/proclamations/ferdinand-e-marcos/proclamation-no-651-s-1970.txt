TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 651

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 648 DATED JANUARY 24. 1970, ENTITLED “DECLARING FEBRUARY SIXTEEN OF EVERY YEAR AS CALOOCAN DAY” BY DECLARING FEBRUARY SIXTEEN AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CALOOCAN CITY.

Proclamation No. 648 dated January 24, 1970, is hereby amended by declaring February 16, 1970, as a Special Public Holiday in Caloocan City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
