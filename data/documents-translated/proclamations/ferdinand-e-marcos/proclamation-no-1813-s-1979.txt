MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1813

RELEASING FROM THE OPERATION OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 34 DATED APRIL 11, 1917, OF THE GOVERNOR-GENERAL OF THE PHILIPPINE ISLANDS THE PARCEL OF LAND DESCRIBED THEREIN, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIMAY, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, ISLAND OF LUZON, PHILIPPINES

WHEREAS, a petition has been filed for the release of the tract of land situated in Barrio Kitang, Limay, Bataan, covered by the Forest Station Site Reservation established under Executive Order No. 34 dated April 11, 1917;

WHEREAS, after a thorough investigation made by the Bureau of Forest Development, the area in question was found fully occupied by residents therein for residential purposes; and

WHEREAS, the Bureau of Forest Development does not need the said reservation and may find an alternate area;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law and upon the recommendation of the Director of Forest Development and concurred in by the Minister of Natural Resources, do hereby release from the operation of Executive Order No. 34 dated April 11, 1917, and declare reverted to the public domain the parcel of land described therein situated in Limay, Bataan, to be certified for disposition under the Public Land Act.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
