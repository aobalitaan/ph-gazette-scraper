MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1815

SETTING FEBRUARY 4, 1979, AS THE DATE FOR THE HOLDING OF THE PLEBISCITE IN CERTAIN BARANGAYS IN GANDARA, SAMAR, PURSUANT TO BATAS PAMBANSA BLG. 11

WHEREAS, Batas Pambansa Blg. 11 approved on December 6, 1978, provides that a plebiscite shall be held in specific barangays of the municipality of Gandara, province of Samar, to decide whether the said barangays shall become a new municipality, to be known as the Municipality of San Jorge, which shall be conducted within a period of sixty days after the approval of said Act;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law and pursuant to Batas Pambansa Blg. 11, do hereby set February 4, 1979, as the date for the holding of the plebiscite in the barangays of the municipality of Gandara, province of Samar, listed below, to decide whether the said barangays shall be constituted into a new municipality to be known as San Jorge:

San Jorge, Sinit-an, Guadalupe, Hernandez, Bungliw, Janipon, Quezon, New San Isidro, Cantaguio, Ranera, Tomogbong, Rosalim, Aurora, Canyake, Sapinit, Bulao, Buenavista, Himay, Blanca Aurora, Calundan, Lincoro, Lapaz, Erenas, Mobo-ob, Cag-toto-og, Matalud, Libertad, Gayondato and Pohagan.

The Commission on Elections shall promulgate the rules and regulations necessary to implement this Proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
