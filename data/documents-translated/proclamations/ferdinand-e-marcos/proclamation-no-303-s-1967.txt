MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 303

DECLARING SUNDAY, NOVEMBER 5, 1967, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BAGO CITY.

WHEREAS, November 5, 1967, is an important date in the history of Bago City as it marks its liberation from Spanish rule under the leadership of General Juan Araneta in November 5, 1898; and

WHEREAS, the people of Bago City wish to celebrate this important event in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Sunday, November 5, 1967, as a special public holiday in Bago City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
