MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2056

DECLARING THE WEEK FROM FEBRUARY 8 TO 15, 1981, AS CHEMICAL ENGINEERING WEEK.

WHEREAS, the Asian Pacific Confederation of Chemical Engineering (APCCHE), a confederation of chemical engineering organizations in the Asian Pacific Region will hold its 2nd Congress on February 8 to 12, 1981, here in Manila; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable that a week be set aside to focus attention to chemical engineering and its role in industrial progress as well as energy development and conservation;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from February 8 to 12, 1981, as “Chemical Engineering Week” under the auspices of the Asian Pacific Confederation of Chemical Engineering (Philippines).

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
