MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 854

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 223 DATED MARCH 25, 1964, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE REGIONAL BUILDING SITE RESERVATION OF THE CENTRAL BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES AT THE CITY OF CEBU.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 223 dated March 25, 1964, which reserved for regional building site purposes of the Central Bank of the Philippines a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the City of Cebu, Island of Cebu, and reserve the land embraced therein for shrine purposes under the administration of the National Shrines Commission.

IN WITNESS THEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
