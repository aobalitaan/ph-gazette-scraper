TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1266

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE RESPONSIBLE PARENTHOOD COUNCIL UP TO MAY 31, 1975.

WHEREAS, the Responsible Parenthood Council was authorized under Proclamation No. 1031 dated June 14, 1972, to conduct a fund drive for its responsible parenthood program; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs additional time for the conduct of its campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the Responsible Parenthood Council up to May 31, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
