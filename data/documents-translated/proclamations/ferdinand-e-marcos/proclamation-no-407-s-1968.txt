MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 407

CREATING A PHILIPPINE COMMITTEE TO UNDERTAKE PREPARATIONS FOR THE PROPER OBSERVANCE OF THE TWENTIETH ANNIVERSARY OF THE WORLD HEALTH ORGANIZATION.

WHEREAS, the World Health Organization is celebrating its twentieth anniversary this year; and

WHERE, the Philippines has always been one of the active and militant supporters of the said organization since its creating;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create a committee to undertake a preparations for the proper observance of the twentieth anniversary of the World Health Organization:

The Committee shall sponsor activities to commemorate the twentieth anniversary of WHO; disseminate information about the objectives and work of the Organization; and call on public and private entities, particularly medical association and schools, to participate in the proper observance thereof.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set may hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
