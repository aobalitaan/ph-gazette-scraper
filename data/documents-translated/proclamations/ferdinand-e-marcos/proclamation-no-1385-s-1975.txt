PROCLAMATION NO. 1385

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM FEBRUARY 14 TO 20, 1975, AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER, AS “RETARDED CHILDREN’S WEEK”

By order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS , President of the Philippines, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR , Executive Secretary, do hereby designate the period from February 14 to 20, 1975, and every year thereafter, as “Retarded Children’s Week” under the auspices of the National Coordinating Committee for Retarded Children’s Week Celebration and other concerned entities.

Proclamation Nos. 479, s. 1968 and 1349, s. 1974, are hereby revoked.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Seventy-Five.

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Executive Secretary
