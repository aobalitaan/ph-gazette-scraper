MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 389

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 272 DATED MARCH 6, 1956, WHICH RESERVED A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN IN DAVAO CITY FOR CITY GOVERNMENT OFFICE PURPOSES, AND RESERVING THE SAME AREA COVERED THEREBY FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES.

Pursuant to Section 64 (c) of the Revised Administrative Code, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation N. 272 dated March 6, 1956, and reserve the area covered thereby for school site purposes under the administration of the Director of Public Schools.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
