MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 413

EXTENDING THE SOS CHILDREN’S VILLAGE PHILIPPINES, INC. FUND CAMPAIGN.

WHEREAS, the SOS Children’s Village Philippine, Inc. was authorized to conduct an educational, membership and fund campaign under Proclamation No. 325 dated January 2, 1968; and

WHEREAS, the said association needs additional time for the conduct of its drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the SOS Children’s Village Philippines, Inc. fund drive up to August 15, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
