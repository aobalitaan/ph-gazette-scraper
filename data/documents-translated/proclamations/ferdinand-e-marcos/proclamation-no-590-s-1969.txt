MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 590

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 132 DATED DECEMBER 6, 1966, ENTITLED “ESTABLISHING A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN, AS A CIVIL RESERVATION FOR THE HIGA-ONON TRIBESMEN OF MINDANAO.”

Proclamation No. 132 dated December 6, 1966, establishing a parcel of land of the public domain situated in the municipality of Carmen, Province of Agusan, as a civil reservation for the Higa-onon tribesmen of Mindanao, is hereby amended so as to transfer the administration over the civil reservation from the Department of National Defense to the Commission on National Integration and the Provincial Government of Agusan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
