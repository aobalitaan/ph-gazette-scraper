MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2223

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 453 DATED DECEMBER 23, 1953 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE AMAI PAKPAK (FORMERLY CAMP KEITHLEY) MILITARY RESERVATION A CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN SITUATED IN MATAMPAY, CITY OF MARAWI, ISLAND OF MINDANAO AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT.

Upon the recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 453 dated December 23, 1953 which established the Amai Pakpak (former Camp Keithley) military reservation, a certain portion of the land embraced therein situated in Matampay, City of Marawi, Island of Mindanao, containing an area of 29,995 square meters and declare the same open to disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act, which portion is more particularly described as follows:

Beginning at a point marked “1”, on plan N. 50 deg. 12’W., 1,506.59 meters from BLLM No. 1, Q-124, Dan Cadastre;

of beginning; containing an area of Twenty-nine Thousand Nine Hundred Ninety-five (29,995) Square Meters more or less.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of July in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
