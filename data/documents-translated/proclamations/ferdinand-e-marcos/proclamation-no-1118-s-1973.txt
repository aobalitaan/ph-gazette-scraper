MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1118

DECLARING THE YEAR 1973 AS PUERICULTURE CENTER MOVEMENT YEAR

WHEREAS, the puericulture center movement will mark this year its DIAMOND (60th) ANNIVERSARY;

WHEREAS, the movement — the only one of its kind in the whole world — has played an important role in lowering infant and maternal mortality in the country;

WHEREAS, in its six decades of existence it has expanded its services to include general medical cases and it has rendered great service to the poor and indigents in the rural areas; and

WHEREAS, in cooperation with the Government’s program of population control, the movement has further expanded its services and set a goal of “Family planning as a way of life in the New Society.”

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the year 1973 as Puericulture Center Movement Year.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-three.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
