MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 477

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE TUBERCULOSIS SOCIETY, INC. UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1968.

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 434 dated July 25, 1968, the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. was authorized to conduct a national educational and fund campaign form August 19 to October 31, 1968; and

WHEREAS, the Society needs additional time to realize its goal of ₱600,000.00 in order to maintain its existing health services and activities throughout the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national educational and fund campaign of the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. up to December 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
