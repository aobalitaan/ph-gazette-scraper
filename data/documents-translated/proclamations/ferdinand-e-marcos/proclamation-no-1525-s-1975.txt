MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1525

FURTHER EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION TO JUNE 30, 1976.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 1470 dated July 31, 1975, to extend its educational and fund campaign up to December 30, 1975; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs additional time to raise more funds in preparation for the 1976 Montreal World Olympics;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby further extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association up to June 30, 1976.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of December in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
