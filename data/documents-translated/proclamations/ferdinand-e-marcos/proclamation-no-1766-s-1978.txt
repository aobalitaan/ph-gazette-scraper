MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1766

AUTHORIZING THE METRO MANILA COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT FOUNDATION, INCORPORATED TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN.

WHEREAS, the Metro Manila Community Development Foundation, Inc., a non-stock and non-profit corporation, is engaged in a broad range of projects, programs and activities to enhance community living in Metropolitan Manila;

WHEREAS, one of the initial projects of the Foundation is to alleviate the plight of Barangay Captains of Metro Manila as a fitting recognition to their untiring efforts, dedication and devotion to duty and the community; and

WHEREAS, the foundation needs the moral and financial support of the public and private sectors to realize its laudable objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Metro Manila Community Development Foundation, Inc. to conduct a national fund campaign for a period of one year from the issuance of this proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-eight.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
