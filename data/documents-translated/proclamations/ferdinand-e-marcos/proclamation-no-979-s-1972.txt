TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 979

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM APRIL 3 TO 9, 1972, AS FREEDOM WEEK.

WHEREAS, April 9, 1972, marks the 30th anniversary of the Fall of Bataan, a historic event in our people’s struggle for freedom and democracy; and

WHEREAS, every freedom loving citizen of this country should reflect on the sacrifices of those who fought in the night of the crucial struggle to preserve our way of life and to pass on the torch of a glorious dawn of peace, happiness and freedom for our beloved country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from April 3 to 9, 1972, as Freedom Week under the auspices of the committee created under Administrative Order No. 315 dated December 6, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 3rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
