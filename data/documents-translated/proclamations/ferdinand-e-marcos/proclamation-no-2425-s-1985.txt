MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2425

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JUNE 19, 1985, AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA AND IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO.

WHEREAS, the 124th Birth Anniversary of our national hero Dr. Jose P. Rizal, who was born in Calamba, Laguna, falls on Wednesday, June 19, 1985; and

WHEREAS, the people therein desire to celebrate the birth anniversary of our national hero with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Wednesday, June 19, 1985, as a non-working special public holiday in the Province of Laguna and in the City of San Pablo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
