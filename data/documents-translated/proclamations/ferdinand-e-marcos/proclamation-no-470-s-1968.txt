MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 470

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 8 TO 14, 1968, AS RECREATION WEEK.

WHEREAS, recreation is deeply ingrained in the makeup of man and his physical and mental health depends on it;

WHEREAS, man does not live on the technology and economic prosperity of a civilization alone but requires occasions for self-awareness and for human engagements;

WHEREAS, the proper pursuit of healthful recreational activities leads to the conservation and improvement of national parks and grounds and recreational facilities; and

WHEREAS, a week should be dedicated in arousing greater concern for the people’s welfare through worthwhile leisure activities for all age;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from December 8 to 14, 1968, as Recreation Week under the auspices of all entities involved.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
