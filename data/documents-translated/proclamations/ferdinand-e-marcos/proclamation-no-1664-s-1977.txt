MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1664

DECLARING TUESDAY, AUGUST 30, 1977, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, August 30, 1977, marks the birth anniversary of Marcelo H. del Pilar, one of the most outstanding Filipino heroes in the country’s struggle for freedom and democracy; and

WHEREAS, the people of Bulacan, the province of his birth desire to celebrate his birthday with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, August 30, 1977, as a special public holiday in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
