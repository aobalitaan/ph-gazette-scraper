MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1691

DECLARING MONDAY, NOVEMBER 7, 1977, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN METROPOLITAN MANILA

WHEREAS, the second anniversary of the creation of the Metropolitan Manila Commission falls on Monday, November 7, 1977; and

WHEREAS, the people of Metropolitan Manila should be afforded the opportunity to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this historical event;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, November 7, 1977, as a special public holiday in Metropolitan Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
