MALACANANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1860

DECLARING FRIDAY, JUNE 1, 1979, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN MAKATI, METROPOLITAN MANILA.

WHEREAS, Makati, Metropolitan Manila celebrates “Araw ng Makati” on June 1, 1979, marking its 408th foundation anniversary; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the residents of Makati, Metropolitan Manila be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their town’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Friday, June 1, 1979, as a special public holiday in Makati, Metropolitan Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
