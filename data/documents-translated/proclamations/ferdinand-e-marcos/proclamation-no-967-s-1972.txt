TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 967

DECLARING THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 24, 1972, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CEBU CITY.

WHEREAS, Thursday, February 24, 1972, marks the charter anniversary of the City of Cebu; and

WHEREAS, the residents of the City should be given the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thursday, February 24, 1972, as a special public holiday in the City of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
