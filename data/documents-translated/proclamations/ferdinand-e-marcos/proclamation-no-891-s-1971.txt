TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 891

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 30, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN SAN JUAN, RIZAL.

WHEREAS, the 30th of August marks the 75th anniversary of the Battle of Pinaglabanan where Filipinos First clashed with American soldiers, sparkling the struggle for freedom from American domination;

WHEREAS, the people of San Juan, Rizal, should be given full opportunity to celebrate this occasion with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, August 30, 1971, as a special public holiday in San Juan, Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MECHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
