MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION No. 1254

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, APRIL 10, 1974, AND SATURDAY, APRIL 13, 1974 AS SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAYS.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, April 10, 1974 and Saturday, April 13, 1974 may be declared as special public holidays without detriment to the public service;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me under Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Wednesday, April 10, 1974, and Saturday, April 13, 1974, as special public holidays.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
