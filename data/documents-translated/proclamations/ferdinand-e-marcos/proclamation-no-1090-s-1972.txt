TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1090

DECLARING NOVEMBER 19 TO 26, 1972, AS AMATEUR RADIO WEEK

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Radio Association celebrates its 40th anniversary on November 26, 1972; and

WHEREAS, there is a necessity to focus public attention on the important role the association plays in radio broadcasting service;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare November 19 to 26, 1972, as Amateur Radio Week.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
