MALACAṄANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 718

FURTHER AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 132 DATED DECEMBER 6. 1966, AS AMENDED BY PROCLAMATION NO. 590 DATED AUGUST 7, 1969, BY PLACING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE COMMISSION ON NATIONAL INTEGRATION.

Proclamation No. 132 dated December 6, 1966, as amended by Proclamation No. 590 dated August 7, 1969, is hereby further amended by placing the land embraced therein under the administration of the Commissioner on National Integration.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
