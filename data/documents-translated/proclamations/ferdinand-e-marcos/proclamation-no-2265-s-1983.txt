MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2265

EXTENDING THE NATIONWIDE FUND DRIVE OF THE DR. JOSE P. RIZAL MEMORIAL FOUNDATION, (PHIL.) INC. TO DECEMBER 31, 1983

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 2153 dated January 9, 1982, authorized the Dr. Jose P. Rizal Memorial Foundation, (Phil.) Inc. to conduct a nationwide fund drive during the period from January 1 to December 31, 1982; and

WHEREAS, said Foundation needs more time to achieve its fund campaign goals.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Dr. Jose P. Rizal Memorial Foundation, (Phil.) Inc. to December 31, 1983.

I hereby enjoin all citizens and residents of this country to give generously of their means to enable the Foundation to succeed in all its humanitarian endeavors.

All donations or contributions to the Foundation shall be exempted from all forms of taxes and shall constitute allowable deductions in full for income tax purposes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen hundred and eighty-three.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) MANUEL M. LAZARO Presidential Assistant for Legal Affairs
