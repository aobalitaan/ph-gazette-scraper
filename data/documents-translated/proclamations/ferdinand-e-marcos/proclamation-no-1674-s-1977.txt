MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1674

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 8 TO 15, 1977 AS REGISTRATION DAYS FOR KABATAANG BARANGAY MEMBERS.

WHEREAS, there is a need to gather and computerize all pertinent data regarding Kabataang Barangay members all over the country in order to hasten the implementation and expansion of the KB out-of-school youth and other KB development-oriented programs;

WHEREAS, to accomplish these objectives, all Kabataang Barangay members all over the country are required to register in accordance with a set of guidelines promulgated for the purpose;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from October 8 to 15, 1977, as registration days for Kabataang Barangay members.

I hereby enjoin all Kabataang Barangay members all over the country to register in accordance with the guidelines prescribed for the purpose.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 4th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-seven.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
