MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 53

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 510 DATED DECEMBER 20, 1965, WHICH RESERVED PARCELS OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN IN PANDACAN, MANILA, FOR THE PHILIPPINE COLLEGE OF COMMERCE, AND RESERVING THE AREA EMBRACED THEREIN FOR THE USE OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 510 dated December 20, 1965, which reserved for the use of the Philippine College of Commerce the parcels of land reserved for the Bureau of Animal Industry in Pandacan, Manila, and reserve the same for the use of the national government.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
