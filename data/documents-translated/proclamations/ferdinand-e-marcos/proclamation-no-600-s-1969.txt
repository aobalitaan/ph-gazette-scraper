MALACAÑANG MANILA RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 600

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 3, 1969, marks the first anniversary of the formal organization of the Government of the City of Iriga.

WHEREAS, the people of Iriga City should be afforded an opportunity to commemorate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 3, 1969, as a special public holiday in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th, day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
