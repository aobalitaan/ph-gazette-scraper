MALACAñANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 760

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 6 TO 12, 1970, AS GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES WEEK.

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus attention on the legion of honest and efficient government workers for their contribution to nation building; and

WHEREAS, recognition of their service is an incentive to greater dedication and efficiency in public office;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from December 6 to 12, 1970, as Government Employees’ Week. I call upon all citizens to turn their thoughts to the honest and efficient government employee in grateful appreciation for his services. The Week should be devoted to the attain­ment of maximum service efficiency and the highest degree of harmony and cooperation between the public and the government employee.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
