MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 206

DECLARING MAY 28, 1967, AS “DIEGO SILANG DAY”.

WHEREAS, the 204th death anniversary of Diego Silang, famed leader of the Ilocos Revolt, falls on May 28, 1967;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare May 28, 1967, as “Diego Silang Day” in La Union, his birthplace.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Assistant Executive Secretary
