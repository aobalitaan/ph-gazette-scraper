MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 442

EXTENDING THE ELKS CEREBRAL PALSY PROJECT, INC., FUND DRIVE.

WHEREAS, the Elks Cerebral Palsy Project, Inc., was authorized by Proclamation No. 344 conduct a national educational and fund campaign during the period from May 1 to June 30, 1962;

WHEREAS, the Elks Cerebral Palsy Project, Inc., was not able to solicit enough funds from the public during its campaign period and would need additional time to realize its quote of ₱300,000.00 to continue its activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national educational and fund campaign of the Elks Cerebral Palsy Project, Inc., up to August 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
