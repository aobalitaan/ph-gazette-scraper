TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1314

DECLARING NOVEMBER 22, 1974, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF MAGUINDANAO

WHEREAS, Friday, November 22, 1974, marks the 1st charter anniversary of the Province of Maguindanao; and

WHEREAS, the residents of the province should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their province’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of his Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, November 22, 1974, as a special public holiday in the Province of Maguindanao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
