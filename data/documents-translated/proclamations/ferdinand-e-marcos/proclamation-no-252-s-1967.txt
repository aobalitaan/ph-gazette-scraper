MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 252

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE 1967 NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE BOY SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO SEPTEMBER 30, 1967.

WHEREAS, the Boy Scouts of the Philippines was authorized under Proclamation No. 211 dated June 13, 1967, to conduct a fund campaign from July 1 to August 15, 1967, to attain its avowed aims and purposes; and

WHEREAS, it has been found necessary to extend the campaign period in order to insure that all elements of our local communities may be afforded the opportunity to contribute to, and give tangible expression of their support for, the Scouting Movement;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the 1967 national fund campaign of the Boy Scouts of the Philippines to September 30, 1967.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
