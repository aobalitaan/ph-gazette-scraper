MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 41

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 12 TO 19, 1966, AS FLAG WEEK.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Flag is the symbol of our country and the purest ideals and aspirations of our people; and

WHEREAS, the Flag is also the emblem of national sovereignty, national unity, and freedom and liberty;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that a week be set aside to ponder on the Flag and inspire our citizenry to greater acts of sacrifice and patriotism for the country to make it truly great;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from June 12 to 19, 1966, as Flag Week. I enjoin all government officials and employees, the public and civic organizations to cooperate in making the Week a success.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
