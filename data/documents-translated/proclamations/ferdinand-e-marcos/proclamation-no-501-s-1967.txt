MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 501

RESERVING FOR MOTOR POOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE BUREAU OF PUBLIC HIGHWAYS A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JOLO, PROVINCE OF SULU, ISLAND OF SULU.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to do authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for motor pool site purposes under the administration of the Commissioner of Public Highways. subject to private rights, if any there be a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the municipality of Jolo, province of Sulu, Island of Sulu more particularly described as follows:

Lot No.2744, Sgs-2637 is a portion of Lot 2561-C-4 Psd-44205

A parcel of land (Lot 2744 of the cadastral survey of Mt Dajo and shown on plan Sgs-2637), situated in the Poblacion, Municipality of Jolo, Province of Sulu, Island of Jolo.

Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by Lot 2561-B of plan Psd-29591 (Municipal Building Site); on the N., E., N. and E., along lines 2-3-4-5-G-7, by Lot 2561-C-4 (portion) of plan Psd-44205: on the S., along line 7-8, by Road to Airport (Lot 2561-C-4 (Portion) of plan Psd-44205; on the SW., and NW., along lines 8-9-1, by Jolo Tabibi Road.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 35 deg. 34 min. E., 190.91 meters from B.L.L.M. 2, Mt. Dajo Cad.

beginning: containing an area of four thousand nine hundred fifty one (4,951) square meters.

All points referred to indicated on the plan and marked on the ground as follows: point 1, by Old B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mon.; and rest by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons: bearings true: date of survey, Jan. 3, 1964.

NOTE: Lot 2741 is a portion of Lot 2561-C-4, Psd 44205.

This plan was prepared under Sgs-2637, which was approved on Jan. 16, 1966.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
