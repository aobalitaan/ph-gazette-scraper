TANGGAPANG NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1429

DECLARING MAY 12, 1975, AS PAKIL FOUNDATION DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LAGUNA.

WHEREAS, May 12, 1975, marks the foundation of Pakil, Laguna, as an independent and separate municipality; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper to call attention on this milestone of the town’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare May 12, 1975, as Pakil Foundation Day in the province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
