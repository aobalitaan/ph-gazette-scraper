MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 282

RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED IN MT. LUAY, TOLEDO, PROVINCE OF CEBU.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for military purposes under the administration of the Chief of Staff, Armed Forces of the Philippines, a certain parcel of the public domain situated in Mt. Luay, Toledo, Cebu, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Beginning at a point marked “1” being S. 71 deg. 07′ E., 15078.97 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 4, Municipality of Toledo, Cebu;

thence N. 79 deg. 39′ E., 119.08 m. to point 2;

thence S. 9 deg. 15′ W., 65.54 m. to point 3;

thence S. 51 deg. 10′ E., 42.63 m. to point 4;

thence S. 47 deg. 00′ W., 147.01 m. to point 5;

thence S. 68 deg. 00′ W., 172.20 m. to point 6;

thence N. 21 deg. 51′ W., 85.75 m. to point 7;

thence N. 40 deg. 28′ E., 101.20 m. to point 8;

thence N. 50 deg. 12′ E., 122.44 m. to the point of

beginning, containing an area of THIRTY NINE THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED SEVENTEEN (39,717) SQUARE METERS, more or less.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
