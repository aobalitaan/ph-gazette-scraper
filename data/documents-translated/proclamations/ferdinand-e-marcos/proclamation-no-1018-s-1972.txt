TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1018

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1972

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 928 dated November 17, 1971, to extend its educational and fund campaign up to June 30, 1972; and

WHEREAS, the association needs additional time to raise more funds to achieve its goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association up to December 31, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set by hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
