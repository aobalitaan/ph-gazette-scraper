PROCLAMATION NO. 1173

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 26 TO DECEMBER 2, 1973, AS NATIONAL MUSIC WEEK FOR YOUNG ARTISTS

WHEREAS , Filipino music is the expression of the soul of the Nation;

WHEREAS, in recognition of the Filipino’s innate love for music, a fitting celebration to encourage young artists to excel in music composition and performance is most opportune; and

WHEREAS, there is an imperative need to preserve, develop and promote Philippine music as an art and as a handmaid of cultural development;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from November 26 to December 2, 1973, and henceforth every last week of November of each year, as “NATIONAL MUSIC WEEK FOR YOUNG ARTISTS.” I urge all citizens of this country as well as all associations and organizations to celebrate this week appropriately to encourage Filipinos to appreciate Filipino music and make it form part of their cultural life.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 7th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Seventy-Three.

President
