MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 570

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 1 TO AUGUST 15, 1969 FOR THE FIRST ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST OF CEBU, INC.

To enable the Community Chest of Cebu. Inc. to carry out its community welfare services, I, FERDINAN E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from July 1 to August 15, 1969, for its first annual educational and fund campaign.

I call upon all residents, firms and organizations especially in the City of Cebu, Lapulapu City, Toledo City, Danao City and the municipalities of Cebu, to support this campaign and to give generously to the Community Chest of Cebu, Inc. which unites into one annual appeal the 18 fund campaigns of the following welfare agencies:

Asilo de la Medalla Milagrosa Cebu Boys’ Center, Inc. Cebu Girls’ Center Cebu Society for Crippled Children Children’s Community Kitchen Elks Celebral Palsy Clinic Franciscan Missionaries of Mary Family Life Workshop of the Philippines Friends of the Lepers (Cebu) Association Good Shepherd Convent Philippine Educating Service for the Blind Philippine Mental Health Association St. Martin de Porres School for Retarded Children St. Theresa’s Social and Medical Center Young Christian Workers (Girls) of Cebu Young Men’s Christian Association Young Women’s Christian Association Young Ladies Association of Charity

I urge all residents to give the drive full support and cooperation.

Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Thursday, July 1, 1969, as a special public holiday in Southern. Leyte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred. and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
