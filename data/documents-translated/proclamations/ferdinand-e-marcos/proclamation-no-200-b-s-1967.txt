MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 200-B

RESERVING FOR NATIONAL IMPROVEMENT PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE FORESHORE OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANDAUE, CEBU, ISLAND OF CEBU.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for National Improvement purposes, under the administration of the Province of Cebu, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the foreshore of the Municipality of Mandaue, Cebu, Island of Cebu, and more particularly described as follows, to wit:

Pls- _______

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
