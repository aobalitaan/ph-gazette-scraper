TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 866

DECLARING THURSDAY, JULY 1, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN SAN CARLOS CITY (NEGROS OCCIDENTAL) .

WHEREAS, Thursday, July 1, 1971, marks the charter anniversary of the City of San Carlos; and

WHEREAS, the residents of the City should be given the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Thursday, July 1, 1971, as a special public holiday in the City of San Carlos (Negros Occidental).

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) FLORES BAYOT Assistant Executive Secretary
