TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 844

DECLARING FRIDAY, MAY 7, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN SAN PABLO CITY.

WHEREAS, Friday, May 7, 1971, marks the 31st charter anniversary of the City of San Pablo; and

WHEREAS, the residents of said city should be given the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, May 7, 1971, as a special public holiday in San Pablo City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
