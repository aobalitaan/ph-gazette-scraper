MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 306

RESERVING FOR CEMETERY SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE SITIO OF CANAAM, MUNICIPALITY OF VINTAR, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE.

Upon the recommendation of the Director of Forestry, approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 64 (e) of the Revised Administrative Code, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for cemetery site purposes, under the administration of the Municipal Government of Vintar, Ilocos Norte, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Sitio of Canaam, Barrio No. 15, Municipality of Vintar, Province of Ilocos Norte, Philippines.

The aforementioned parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

“Proposed Cemetery Site (C. R.-58)

A parcel of land beginning at point which is S. 52° W. 435 m. from FZ-4 of Block X, A, or D., Project No. 9-A,

thence Due S 100 m. to point 2; thence Due W 100 m. to point 3; thence Due N 100 m. to point 4; thence Due E 100 m. to point of beginning,

containing an area of ONE (1) HECTARE more or less.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
