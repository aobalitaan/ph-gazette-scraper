MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 507

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE NATIONAL SHRINES COMMISSION FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF CORREGIDOR AND MT. SAMAT NATIONAL SHRINES TO DECEMBER 31, 1969.

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission was authorized under Proclamation No. 310 dated November 29, 1967 to conduct its fund campaign from December 31, 1967 to December 31, 1968 for the development of Corregidor and Mt. Samat National Shrines;

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission needs more time to realize its fund campaign goals to pursue its planned projects on Corregidor and Mt. Samat National Shrines;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the period of the National Shrines Commission Fund Campaign up to December 31, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
