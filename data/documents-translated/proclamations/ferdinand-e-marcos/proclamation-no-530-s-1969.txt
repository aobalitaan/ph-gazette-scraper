MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 530

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 26, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN ZAMBOANGA CITY.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, February 26, 1969, marks the thirty-second anniversary of the charter of Zamboanga City; and

WHEREAS, the people of that city should be given full opportunity to celebrate the milestone in their City’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Wednesday, February 26, 1969, as a special public holiday in Zamboanga City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
