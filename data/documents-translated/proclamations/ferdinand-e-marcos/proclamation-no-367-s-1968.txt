MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 367

EXTENDING UP TO MARCH 31, 1968, THE PERIOD WITHIN WHICH THE RESEARCH FOUNDATION IN PHILIPPINE ANTHROPOLOGY & ARCHEOLOGY, INC., MAY CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 320 dated December 20, 1967, authorized the Research Foundation in Philippine Anthropology & Archeology, Inc., to conduct a national fund campaign from November 1, 1967 to January 31, 1968;

WHEREAS, by reason of unavoidable circumstances, the said Foundation was able to start the campaign only on January 20, 1968;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign period of the Research Foundation in Philippine Anthropology &Archeology, Inc., up to March 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source : Presidential Management Staff
