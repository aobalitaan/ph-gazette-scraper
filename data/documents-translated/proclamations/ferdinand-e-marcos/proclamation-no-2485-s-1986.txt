Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

PROCLAMATION NO. 2485

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE DASMARIÑAS BAGONG BAYAN, DASMARIÑAS, CAVITE, FOR HOUSING AND RESETTLEMENT PROJECT

WHEREAS, the social and economic upliftment of the dislocated families relocated from the Greater Manila area to resettlement projects is the concern of the Government;

WHEREAS, the National Housing Authority is the owner and administrator of various housing and resettlement projects, one of which is Dasmariñas Bagong Bayan Resettlement Project located at Dasmariñas, Cavite;

WHEREAS, the recent survey determined that there is a parcel of public land with an area of THIRTY FOUR THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED SEVENTY TWO (34,372) square meters, more or less, situated within the Dasmariñas Bagong Bayan Resettlement Project which is necessary for the project’s comprehensive and integrated development.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby reserve for housing and resettlement project a certain parcel of public land situated in Dasmariñas Bagong Bayan Dasmariñas, Province of Cavite, subject to private rights, if any, which parcel of public land is more particularly described as follows:
