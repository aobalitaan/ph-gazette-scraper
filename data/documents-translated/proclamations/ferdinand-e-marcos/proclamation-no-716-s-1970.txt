TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 716

DECLARING TUESDAY, JULY 7, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CUENCA, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Tuesday, July 7, 1970, marks the death anniversary of the late Col. Pedro Pasia, educator, resistance leader, four-term mayor and exemplary public servant of Cuenca, Batangas; and

WHEREAS, the residents of said town should be afforded the opportunity to render homage to their former chief executive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, July 7, 1970, as a special public holiday in Cuenca, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and. seventy.

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A MATHAY Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
