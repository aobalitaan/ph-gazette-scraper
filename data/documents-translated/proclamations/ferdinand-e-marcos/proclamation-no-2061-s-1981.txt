MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2061

AUTHORIZING THE DR. JOSE P. RIZAL MEMORIAL FOUNDATION, INC. TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE FUND DRIVE FROM FEBRUARY 2 TO DECEMBER 31, 1981.

WHEREAS, the Dr. Jose P. Rizal Memorial Foundation, Inc. is operating a Medical Research Center at Bagong Bayan, Dasmariñas, Cavite, for the less privileged members of our society; and

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs the continued support of the public to enable it to accomplish its humanitarian undertakings;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorized the Dr. Jose P. Rizal Memorial Foundation, Inc. to conduct a nationwide fund drive from February 1 to December 31, 1981.

I hereby enjoin all citizens and residents of this country to give generously of their means to enable the Foundation to succeed in all its humanitarian endeavors.

All donations or contributions to the Foundation shall be exempted from all forms of taxes and shall constitute allowable deductions in full for income tax purposes.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
