MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2221

DECLARING AS PALAWAN FLORA, FAUNA AND WATERSHED RESERVE A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, ISLAND OF PALAWAN, AMENDING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 67 DATED AUGUST 21, 1912.

Upon recommendation of the Director of Forest Development, duly concurred in by the Minister of Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby set aside as Palawan Flora, Fauna and Watershed Reserve under the administration of the Bureau of Forest Development, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to final survey, that parcel of land of the public domain situated in the City of Puerto Princesa, Island of Palawan, more particularly described as follows:

Beginning at Corner 1 a point marked “1” on the map being N. 17° W, 310 meters from the intersection of the Road from Puerto Princesa to Iwahig and Balsahan River,

Thence, following Balsahan River upstream in a general Northwest, Northeast, direction about 2,320 meters straight (direct) distance to point “2”;

Thence, following Iratag River downstream in a general Southeasterly direction to 3,000 meters straight (direct) distance to point “11”;

beginning, containing an area of Four Thousand Seven Hundred Seventy-six (4,776) hectares more or less, as shown on the attached BFC map which forms part of this Proclamation.

The Ministry of Natural Resources, through the Bureau of Forest Development, may, by itself or in joint venture with the City Government of Puerto Princesa and/or other appropriate agencies, undertake the development of the Reserve in the manner most feasible to preserve the flora and fauna of Palawan, including the rehabilitation of the watershed of Puerto Princesa City.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of July in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Eighty-Two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
