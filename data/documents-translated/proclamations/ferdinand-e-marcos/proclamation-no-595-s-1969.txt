MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 595

DESIGNATING AUGUST 10, 1969, AS THE DATE FOR THE FORMAL ORGANIZATION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF THE CITY OF SAN JOSE.

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 6051, approved on August 4, 1969, created the City of San Jose in Nueva Ecija; and

WHEREAS, Section 93 of said Act provides that the government of the City of San Jose shall be organized on such date as may be fixed by the President of the Philippines:

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, Pursuant to Section 93 of Republic Act No. 6051, do hereby proclaim that the formal organization of the government of the City of San Jose shall take place on August 10, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and cause the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
