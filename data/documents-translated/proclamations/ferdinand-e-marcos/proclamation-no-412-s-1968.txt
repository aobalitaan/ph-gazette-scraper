MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 412

EXTENDING THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION FUND CAMPAIGN.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association was authorized under Proclamation No. 334 dated January 25, 1968 to extend its fund campaign up to June 30, 1968; and

WHEREAS, the Association need additional time for a more effective and productive fund campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund of the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association up to December 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
