MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 215

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 19, 1967, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CALAMBA, LAGUNA.

WHEREAS, June 19, 1967; marks the one hundred sixth birthday of Jose Rizal, our national hero; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the residents of Calamba, Laguna, his birthplace, be given full opportunity to celebrate the day with appropriate ceremonies in keeping with his ideals and teachings;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Monday, June 19, 1967, as a special public holiday in Calamba, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
