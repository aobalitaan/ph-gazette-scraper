MALACANANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 95

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 92 DATED AUGUST 21, 1966.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 92 dated August 21, 1966, by including the City of Olongapo within the scope of the proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
