MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1514

EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE NATIONAL NATIONAL SCHOOL FOR THE BLIND FOUNDATION, INC. TO JUNE 15, 1976

WHEREAS, the PNSB Foundation, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 1498 dated August 26, 1975 to conduct an educational, membership and fund campaign from August 15 to December 31, 1975; and

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs a longer time to conduct its educational, membership and fund campaign to enable it to carry on its laudable activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the educational, membership and fund campaign of the PNSB Foundation, Inc. to June 15, 1976.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES

By the Acting Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
