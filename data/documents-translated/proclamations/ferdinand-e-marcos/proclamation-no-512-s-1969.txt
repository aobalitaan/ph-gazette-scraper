MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 512

DECLARING TUESDAY, JANUARY 28, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAVITE AND IN THE CITIES OF CAVITE, TAGAYTAY AND TRECE MARTICES.

In commemoration of the 108th birthday anniversary of Julian Felipe, composer of the Philippine National Anthem, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Tuesday, January 28, 1969, as a special public holiday in the Province of Cavite and in the Cities of Cavite, Tagaytay and Trece Martires.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
