TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 908

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHESTS AND COUNCILS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO NOVEMBER 30, 1971.

WHEREAS, the Community Chests and Councils of the Philippines were authorized under Proclamation No. 838 dated April 14, 1971, to conduct fund campaigns from June 1 to September 30, 1971, to support the services of their respective welfare agencies; and

WHEREAS, it has been found necessary to extend the campaign in view of the election ban this year;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the Annual Educational and Fund Campaign of the Community Chests and Councils of the Philippines to November 30, 1971.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
