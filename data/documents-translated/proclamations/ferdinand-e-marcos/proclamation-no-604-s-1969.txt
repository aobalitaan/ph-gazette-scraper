MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 604

EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE 1969 NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE BOY SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO DECEMBER 31, 1969.

WHEREAS, the Boy Scouts of the Philippines was authorized under Proclamation No. 574, dated June 26, 1969, to conduct a fund campaign from July 1 to August 15, 1969, to attain its avowed aims and purposes; and

WHEREAS, it has been found necessary to extend the campaign period in order to insure that all elements of our communities may be afforded the opportunity to contribute to, and give tangible expression of their support for, the Scouting Movement;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the 1969 national fund campaign of the Boy Scouts of the Philippines to December 31, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
