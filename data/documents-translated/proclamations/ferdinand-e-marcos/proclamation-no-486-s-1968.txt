MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 486

EXTENDING UP TO JANUARY 31, 1969, THE PERIOD WITHIN WHICH THE PHILIPPINE BADMINTON ASSOCIATION, INC., MAY CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 411, dated June 12, 1968, authorizes the Philippine Ameteur Athletic Federation (PAAF) and the national sports association affiliated with it, to conduct a national educational and fund campaign from June 15 to October 31, 1968; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Badminton Association Inc., an affiliate of the PAAF needs more time to realize its fund campaign goal of ₱500,000.00 to carry out its meritorious and noble objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the fund campaign of the Philippine Badminton Association Inc., up to January 31, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
