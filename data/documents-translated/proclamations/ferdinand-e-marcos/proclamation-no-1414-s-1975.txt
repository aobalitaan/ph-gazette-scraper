TANGGAPANG NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1414

AUTHORIZING THE CALOOCAN BANTAYOG JAYCEES TO CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM APRIL 5 TO MAY 15, 1975.

WHEREAS, the Caloocan Bantayog Jaycees will sponsor the 13th Metropolitan Regional Jaycee Conference in Caloocan City on May 17-18, 1975; and

WHEREAS, the Caloocan Bantayog Jaycees needs the support of its sympathizers and the public in general in order to carry out its civic and social activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Caloocan Bantayog Jaycees to conduct a fund campaign during the period from April 5 to May 15, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I, have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
