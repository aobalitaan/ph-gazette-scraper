TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 833

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 829 DATED MARCH 17, 1971, ENTITLED “DECLARING FRIDAY, MARCH 26, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CEBU AND IN THE CITIES OF CEBU, DANAO, LAPU-LAPU AND TOLEDO.”

By order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, Proclamation No. 829 dated March 17, 1971, is hereby amended by including Mandaue City among the Cities covered by said proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
