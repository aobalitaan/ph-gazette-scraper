MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 453

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 3, 1968, AS THE DATE FOR THE FORMAL ORGANIZATION OF THE CITY OF IRIGA.

WHEREAS, Republic Act. No. 5261 approved on June 15, 1968, created the City of Iriga; and

WHEREAS, Section 95 for the said Act provides that the City of Iriga shall be organized on such date as may be fixed by the President of the Philippines;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the provisions of Section 96 of Republic Act No. 5261 do hereby proclaim that the formal organization of the City of Iriga shall take place on September 3, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippine to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
