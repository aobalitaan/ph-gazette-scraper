MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1524-A

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 22 TO 28, 1975, AS GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES WEEK.

WHEREAS, efficient government administration depends greatly on the performance and dedication of government officials and employees;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper to focus public attention on the role played by state workers in the social, economic and political development effort under the New Society;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from December 22 to 28, 1975, as Government Employees Week under the auspices of the Philippine Government Employees Association and other government employees organizations. The week should be devoted to serious soul-searching reflection towards developing a dynamic and responsive civil service.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
