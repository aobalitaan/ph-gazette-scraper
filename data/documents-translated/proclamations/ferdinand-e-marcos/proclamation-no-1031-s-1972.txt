TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1031

DECLARING JUNE 1972 TO MAY 1973, AS FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE RESPONSIBLE PARENTHOOD COUNCIL

WHEREAS, the Responsible Parenthood Council is a non-stock and non-profit organization whose object is to disseminate information and techniques on family planning all over the country; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs the support of the public to realize its laudable objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare June 1972 to May 1973, as fund campaign period of the Responsible Parenthood Council.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
