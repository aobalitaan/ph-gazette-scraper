TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 938

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 15, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN NAGA CITY

WHEREAS, Wednesday, December 15, 1971, marks the 23rd charter anniversary of the City of Naga; and

WHEREAS, the people of Naga City should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history and to bring the residents closer together and inspire greater efforts towards rehabilitation from the effects of the recent typhoons;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Wednesday, December 15, 1971, as a special public holiday in Naga City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
