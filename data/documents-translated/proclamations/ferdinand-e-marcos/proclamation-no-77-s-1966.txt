MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 77

ESTABLISHING AS KAPAI FOREST RESERVE FOR WOOD PRODUCTION WATERSHED, SOIL PROTECTION AND OTHER FOREST USES, THE PARCEL OF PUBLIC DOMAIN KNOWN AS TIMBERLAND, LANAO (LANAO DEL SUR) LAND CLASSIFICATION PROJECT NO. 13-E, PER B.F. MAP L . C . -2250, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF KAPAI, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL SUR, PHILIPPINES.

Upon recommendation of the Director of Forestry, approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 1826 of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended by Republic Act No. 3092, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or other disposition and establish as KAPAI FOREST RESERVE, subject to private rights, if any there be, the parcel of public domain known as Timberland, Lanao (Lanao del Sur) Land Classification Project No. 13-E, situated in the Municipal District of Kapai , Province of Lanao del Sur, Philippines, demarcated and established under Forestry Administrative Order No. 4-768, dated December 20, 1958, for wood production, watershed, soil protection and other forest purposes under the administration and control of the Director of Forestry who shall have the authority to regulate the use and occupancy of said forest reserve and the cutting, collection and removal of timber and other forest products therein in accordance with forest laws and regulations.

The aforementioned parcel of public domain is shown and more particularly described in the attached Bureau of Forestry Map L . C . -2250, comprising the Timberland of the aforementioned Lanao (Lanao del Sur) Land Classification Project No. 13-E, containing an area of FOUR THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY EIGHT (4,188) HECTARES, more or less.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
