MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 537

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE SAN JOSE PUBLIC HIGH SCHOOL A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, INCLUDING THE GOVERNMENT BUILDINGS EXISTING THEREON, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SAN JOSE, PROVINCE OF OCCIDENTAL MINDORO, ISLAND OF MINDORO.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the San Jose Public High School under the administration of the Director of Public Schools, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to the condition that the Bureau of Lands shall continue to occupy the government buildings existing thereon until another building shall have been constructed on a new site earmarked and to be reserved for the use of said Bureau, a parcel of land of the public domain, including the aforesaid buildings existing thereon, situated in the municipality of San Jose, province of Occidental Mindoro, island of Mindoro, which is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 2 Swo-24201

A parcel of land (Lot 2 of plan Swo-24201), situated in the Barrio of Pandurucan, Municipality of San Jose, Province of Mindoro. Bounded on the SE., SW., NW. and NE., by Proposed Road. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 73 deg. 52 min. W., 113.79 meters from B.L.L.M. 1, Mp. of San Jose, Mindoro,

55.98 meters to point 2;

2.88 meters to point 3;

108.22 meters to point 4;

29.95 meters to point 5;

30.02 meters to point 6;

107.90 meters to point 7;

3.23 meters to the point of beginning

containing an area of Six thousand six hundred eleven (6,611) Square Meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons.; bearing true; date of survey, Sept. 27, 1949 and approved on Nov. 19, 1949.

NOTE: Lot 2 = Lot 2502 of Pandurucan Townsite, Ts-99.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
