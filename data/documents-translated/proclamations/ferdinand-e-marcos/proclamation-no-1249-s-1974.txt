{"translation":"Office of the President of the Philippines

PROCLAMATION NO. 1249

PROVIDING FOR THE ANNUAL CELEBRATION OF BALAGTAS DAY ON APRIL 2.

WHEREAS, Francisco (Balagtas) Baltazar, principal Filipino poet and one of the great heroes of the race, was born on April 2, 1788; and

WHEREAS, through his Florante at Laura and other works, he preceded many national heroes who paved the way for Filipino nationalism;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby proclaim and decree the annual national celebration of Balagtas Day on April 2. This day is declared a special non-working day in the province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this first day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

By the Executive Secretary:"}
