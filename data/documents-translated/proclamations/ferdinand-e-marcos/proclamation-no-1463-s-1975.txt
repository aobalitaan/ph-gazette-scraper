TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1463

AUTHORIZING THE YOUTH AND STUDENT TRAVEL ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP, AND FUND CAMPAIGN FROM JULY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1975.

WHEREAS, the Youth and Student Travel Association of the Philippines, is a non-profit civic organization whose objective is to promote tourism among the youth and students of the country; and

WHEREAS, to carry out its objectives it needs the moral and financial support of the public;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Youth and Student Travel Association of the Philippines to conduct a nationwide educational, membership and fund campaign during the period from July 1 to December 31, 1975.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-five.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
