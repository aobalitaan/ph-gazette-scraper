PROCLAMATION NO. 2137

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 23 TO 27, 1981 AS “PRODUCTIVITY CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK.”

WHEREAS, productivity consciousness is one of the primary concern of government; and

WHEREAS, the National Productivity Commission, the government agency tasked in the pursuit of this goal, has decided on the institutionalization of a productivity week celebration as a strategy to promote productivity consciousness;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from November 23 to 27, 1981 as “Productivity Consciousness Week.”

IN WITNESS, WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Acting Presidential Executive Assistant
