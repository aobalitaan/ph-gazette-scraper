MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2449

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE FOUNDATION FOR THE REHABILITATION OF THE DISABLED, INC. TO CONDUCT A FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 1, 1985 TO JUNE 30, 1986.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Foundation for the Rehabilitation of the Disabled, Inc. is a non-profit organization which provides assistance to the disabled; and

WHEREAS, the said foundation needs public support to enable it realize its laudable objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Philippine Foundation for the Rehabilitation of Disabled, Inc. to conduct a fund drive during the period from July 1, 1985 to June 30, 1986.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
