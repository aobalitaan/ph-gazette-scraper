TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 703

DECLARING THURSDAY, JUNE 18, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN GINGOOG CITY

In order to give the people of Gingoog City full opportunity to celebrate the 10th charter anniversary of their city, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare June 18, 1970, as a special public holiday in Gingoog City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
