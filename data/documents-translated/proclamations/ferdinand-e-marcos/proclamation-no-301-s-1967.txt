MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 301

DECLARING MONDAY, NOVEMBER 13, 1967, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY.

Pursuant to the authority vested in me under Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, November 13, 1967, as a special public holiday to give personnel of government and private firms opportunity to go home to their respective provinces and exercise their right of suffrage.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
