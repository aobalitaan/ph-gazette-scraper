TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 694

AUTHORIZING THE KIWANIS CLUB OF MAKATI TO CONDUCT A FUND DRIVE FROM JUNE 1 TO OCTOBER 31, 1970.

WHEREAS, the Kiwanis Club of Makati is involved in various projects for the retarded and physically handicapped wards of Elsie Gaches Village in Alabang, Rizal; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs the moral and financial support of the public to enable it to carry out its mission;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Kiwanis Club of Makati to conduct a fund drive for the purpose of undertaking the building of several cottages in and providing for essential needs of the wards of Elsie Gaches Village in Alabang, Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
