TANGAPANG NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1327

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 8 TO 14 OF EVERY YEAR AS STANDARDS WEEK

WHEREAS, the International Organization for Standardization (IOS), of which the Philippines is an active member, has declared October 14 of every year as “World Standards Day;”

WHEREAS, standardization has greatly helped in accelerating the economic growth of nations through the formulation of commodity standards that encourage a more profitable exchange of goods in both global and local markets; and

WHEREAS, there exists a continuing need to instill standards-consciousness among producers and especially consumers in order to hasten the attainment of national development goals;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from October 8 to 14 of each year as Standards Week. I enjoin all residents, business firms and manufacturers, as well as civic, educational, religious and government institutions to celebrate the Week appropriately.

I authorize the Philippines Bureau of Standards of the Department of Trade to take charge of the celebration of Standards Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
