MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 642

DECLARING THE WEEK FROM JANUARY 24 TO 31, 1970, AS NATIONAL SECURITIES-INVESTMENT CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK.

WHEREAS, investment in securities is a practical means of saving for economic development;

WHEREAS, stock exchanges and investment houses have played a vital role in providing the facilities for securities investment to the general public; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable that a week be set aside to encourage greater interest and participation in the country’s economic development through investments;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from January 24 to 31, 1970, as “National Securities-Investment Consciousness Week” under the auspices of the stock exchanges and investment houses involved;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary
