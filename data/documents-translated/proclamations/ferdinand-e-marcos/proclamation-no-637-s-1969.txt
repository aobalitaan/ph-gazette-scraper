PROCLAMATION NO. 637

DECLARING FRIDAY, JANUARY 2, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN LA CARLOTA CITY.

WHEREAS, Friday, January 2, 1970, marks the anniversary of the charter of La Corlota City; and

WHEREAS, the people of that city should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s his­tory;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ERNESTO M. MACEDA, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, January 2, 1970, as a special public holiday in La Corlota City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
