TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 956

AUTHORIZING THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR VOLLEYBALL ASSOCIATION TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND MEMBERSHIP FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM FEBRUARY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1972.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Volleyball Association, one of the nineteen (19) national sports affiliates of the Philippine Amateur Athletic Federation (PAAF), is committed to develop not only healthy citizens but also future participants in volleyball Olympics, through a national sports development program;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Amateur Volleyball Association is riot in a position to finance its long range national and regional programs without public support;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Philippine Amateur Volleyball Association to conduct a national, educational and membership fund campaign during the period from February 1 to December 31, 1972. I call upon all citizens and residents of the Philippines, irrespective of nationality or creed, to assist in this drive by giving generously of their means.

IN WITNESS, WHEREOF, I have hereunto set by hand and; caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred .and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) Alejandro Melchor Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) Roberto V. Reyes Assistant Executive Secretary
