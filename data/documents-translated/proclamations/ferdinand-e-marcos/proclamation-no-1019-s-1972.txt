TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1019

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 997 DATED APRIL 13, 1972, ENTITLED “AUTHORIZING THE NARCOTICS FOUNDATION OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC., TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM MAY 1 TO DECEMBER 31, 1972”

The third paragraph of Proclamation No. 997 dated April 13, 1972, is hereby amended to read as follows:

“NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Narcotics Foundation of the Philippines, Inc., to conduct a nationwide educational, membership and fund drive during the period from May 1 to December 31, 1972, and to further declare the week of July 24 to July 30 as “ANTI-DRUG DEPENDENCE WEEK” in order to dramatize the vital importance of our common fight against the insidious evils of drug addiction.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
