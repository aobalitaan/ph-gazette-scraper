MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 485

DECLARING TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 12, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN DAPITAN CITY.

WHEREAS, November 12, 1968, marks the fifth anniversary of the organization of the City of Dapitan; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Dapitan City should be afforded the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, November 12, 1968, as a special public holiday in the City of Dapitan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
