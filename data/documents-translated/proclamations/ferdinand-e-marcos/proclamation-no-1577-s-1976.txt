TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1577

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 1, 1976, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN BAGUIO CITY.

WHEREAS, the first day of September marks the foundation of the City of Baguio; and

WHEREAS, the people of Baguio City should be afforded full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E, MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 1, 1976, as a special public holiday in Baguio City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
