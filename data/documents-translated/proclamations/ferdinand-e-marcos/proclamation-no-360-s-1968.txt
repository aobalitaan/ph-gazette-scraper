MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 360

DECLARING SATURDAY, MARCH 16, 1968, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN DAVAO CITY.

WHEREAS, Saturday, March 16, 1968, marks the thirty-first anniversary of the founding of Davao City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of the City should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Saturday, March 15, 1968, as a special public holiday in Davao City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source : Presidential Management Staff
