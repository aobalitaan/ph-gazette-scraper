TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1271

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 16 TO 22, 1974, AS NATIONAL SALESMEN’S WEEK.

WHEREAS, the orderly distribution of the country’s agricultural and industrial products is vital to the continuing efficient operations of our farms and factories as instruments of economic growth and prosperity;

WHEREAS, to the salesmen we owe this vital service not only as fulfiller of our wants and needs, but also as pillars in the economic stability of the country; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to call the attention of the public to the important task society has assigned to the salesmen;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from June 16 to 22, 1974, as National Salesmen’s Week under the auspices of the Sales and Marketing Executives of the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
