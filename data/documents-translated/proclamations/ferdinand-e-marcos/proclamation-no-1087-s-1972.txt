TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1087

DECLARING NOVEMBER 22 TO 26, 1972, AS “CARDIO-THORACIC WEEK.”

WHEREAS, the Philippine Association of Thoracic Surgeons will host the First Asian Congress of Thoracic and Cardiovascular Surgery on November 22 to 26, 1972, at the Manila Hilton with the end in view of discussing ideas, methods and techniques, as well as adopting available facilities for the needs of developing countries in the field of surgery; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to call the attention of the public to the importance of this occasion;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare November 22 to 26, 1972, as “Cardio-thoracic Week.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
