TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1286

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM AUGUST 1 TO 7, 1974, AS NATIONAL ELECTRIFICATION WEEK

WHEREAS, it is the declared national policy of the government to foster the electrification program of the entire country; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting to focus public attention on the accomplishments of the government towards electrification;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from August 1 to 7, 1974, as National Electrification Week under the auspices of the National Electrification Administration and other involved entities and organizations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
