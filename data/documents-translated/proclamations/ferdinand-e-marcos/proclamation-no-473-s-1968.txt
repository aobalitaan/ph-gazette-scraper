MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 473

RESERVING FOR PLANT QUARANTINE BUILDING, BODEGA, AND FUMIGATION CHAMBER SITES PURPOSES ON CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority bested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale of settlement and reserve or plan quarantine building, bodega and fumigation chamber site purposes under the administration of the Director of Plant Industry, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated in the City of Davao, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot 18 Gss-299, Sheet 1

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 18 of plan Gss-229 Sheet 1), situated in the City of Davao. Bounded on the S., along line 1-2, by Lot 20; on the SW., along line 2-3, by Lot 25, both of plan Gss-229; on the W. and N., along lines 3-4-5-6, by Lot 39 (Road); and on the E., along line 6-1, by Lot 17, both of plan Gss-299. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 0 deg. 43’ E., 1819.45 m. from B.L.L.M. 5, Davao Cad.,

thence N. 83 deg. 02’ W., 27.97 m. to point 2; thence N. 11 deg. 37’ W., 8.75 m. to point 3; thence N. 8 deg. 32’ E., 16.98 m. to point 4; thence N. 57 deg. 32’ E., 3.26 m. to point 5; thence S. 82 deg. 14’ E., 27.74 m. to point 6; thence S. 6 deg. 54’ W., 26.95 m. to point of; beginning; containing an approximate area of EIGHT HUNDRED SIXTEEN (816) Square Meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons.; bearings true; date of Survey, July 23-24, 1964, Jan. 21-23 & Feb. 7-12, 1955 and approved on June 7, 1956.

Lot 20 Gss-299, Sheet 1

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 20 of plan Gss-229 Sheet 1), situated in the City of Davao. Bounded on the E., along line 1-2, by Lot 19; on the S., along line 2-3, by Lot 21 (Road); on the SW., along line 3-4, by Lot 25; and on the N., along line 4-1, by Lot 18, all of plan Gss-229. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 0 deg. 43’ E., 1819.45 m. from B.L.L.M. 5, Davao Cad.,

thence S. 6 deg. 52’ W., 27.19 m. to point 2; thence N. 83 deg. 16’ W., 18.85 m. to point 3; thence N. 11 deg. 37’ W., 28.77 m. to point 4; thence S. 83 deg. 02’ E., 27.97 m. to point of;

beginning; containing an approximate area of SIX HUNDRED THIRTY EIGHT (638) Square Meters. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and marked on the ground by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons.; bearings true; date of Survey, July 23-24, 1964, Jan. 21-23 & Feb. 7-12, 1955 and approved on June 7, 1956.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippine

By the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO JR. Assistant Executive Secretary
