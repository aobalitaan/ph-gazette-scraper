OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

DECLARING MAY 4, 1975, AS DON MONICO MERCADO DAY IN SEXMOAN, PAMPANGA

WHEREAS, May 4, 1975, marks the birthday centenary of Don Monico R. Mercado, who was a former Congressman, poet, writer and educator;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare May 4, 1975, as Don Monico Mercado Day in Sexmoan, Pampanga.

By the Executive Secretary:
(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA
Assistant Executive Secretary
