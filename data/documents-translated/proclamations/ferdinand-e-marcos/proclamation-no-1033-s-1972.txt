TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1033

DECLARING AS A CITY EMERGENCY HOSPITAL SITE RESERVATION THE PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AS SHOWN AND DESCRIBED ON THE BUREAU OF FORESTRY MAP CR-110, SITUATED IN THE CITY OF DIPOLOG, ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, PHILIPPINES

Upon recommendation of the Director of Forestry, approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 83, Chapter XIII of the Public Land Act, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by the powers vested in me by law, do hereby set aside and declare as a City Emergency Hospital Site Reservation for the City of Dipolog the parcel of land containing an aggregate area of approximately Two (2) hectares situated in the City of Dipolog, Zamboanga del Norte, subject to valid private rights, if any there be and place the same under the administrative jurisdiction of the Director of the City Hospital, in accordance with existing laws and regulations, which parcel of land is more particularly described and indicated on the Bureau of Forestry Map CR-110.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
