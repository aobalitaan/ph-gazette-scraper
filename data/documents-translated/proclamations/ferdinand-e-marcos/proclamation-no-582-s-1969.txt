MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 582

DECLARING MONDAY, JULY 21, 1969, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, Monday, July 21, 1969, marks Man’s first attempt to land on the moon;

WHEREAS, the people of the Philippines share with the rest of the world the jubilation of man’s historic achievement;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Monday, July 21, 1969, as a special public holiday in the Philippines, to enable the people to watch the historic landing of “Apollo 11” and Man’s first steps on the moon via television.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
