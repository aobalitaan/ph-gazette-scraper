MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 418

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 201, SERIES OF 1928, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE SCHOOL SITE RESERVATION SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF JOLO, PROVINCE OF SULU, ISLAND OF JOLO, BY ENLARGING THE AREA AND CHANGING THE TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 201, series of 1928, which established the school site reservation situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Jolo, province of Sulu, island of Jolo, by enlarging the area and changing the technical description of the land embraced therein so as to read as follows, to wit:
