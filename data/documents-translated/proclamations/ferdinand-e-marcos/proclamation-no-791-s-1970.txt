MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 791

DECLARING SATURDAY, DECEMBER 26, 1970; THURSDAY, DECEMBER 31, 1970, AND SATURDAY, JANUARY 2, 1971, AS SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAYS.

WHEREAS, Saturday, December 26, 1970; Thursday, December 31, 1970, and Saturday. January 2, 1971, may be declared special public holidays without detriment to the public service;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E, MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me under Section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Saturday, December 26, 1970; Thursday, December 31, 1970, and Saturday, January 2, 1971, as special public holidays.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
