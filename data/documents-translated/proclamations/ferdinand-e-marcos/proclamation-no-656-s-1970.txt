MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 656

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 29 TO APRIL 4, 1970, AS NATIONAL CREDIT CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from March 29 to April 4, 1970, as “Credit Consciousness Week” under the auspices of the Credit Management Association of the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary
