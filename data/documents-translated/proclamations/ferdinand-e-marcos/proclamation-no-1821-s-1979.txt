MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1821

DECLARING FEBRUARY 16, 1979, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF CALOOCAN.

WHEREAS, the 17th Foundation Day of the City of Caloocan falls on February 16, 1979; and

WHEREAS, the people of said city should be afforded full opportunity to celebrate, with fitting ceremony, this milestone in the history of their city although the day will be considered as a regular working and school day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare February 16, 1979, as a special public holiday in the City of Caloocan.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
