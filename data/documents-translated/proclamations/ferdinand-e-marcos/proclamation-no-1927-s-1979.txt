MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1927

DECLARING NOVEMBER 16TH OF EVERY YEAR AS SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ILOCOS SUR.

WHEREAS, November 16th marks the birth anniversary of the late President Elpidio Quirino, one of the great sons of Ilocos Sur and a dedicated leader during the formative years of our young Republic;

WHEREAS, his able stewardship of the Government and his personal virtues has developed among the Filipinos in general and Ilocanos in particular a feeling of pride for the Filipino race and inspire in them a sense of patriotism; and

WHEREAS, the people of Ilocos Sur should be given full opportunity to celebrate his birth anniversary with fitting ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare November 16th of every year as a special holiday of Ilocos Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen hundred and seventy-nine.

(SGD . )FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant
