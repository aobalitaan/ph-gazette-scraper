TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 820

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 1 TO 7, 1971, AS “NATIONAL PRESS WEEK” FOR 1971.

WHEREAS, National Press Week is celebrated during the second week of February of every year pursuant to Proclamation No. 191 dated January 9, 1964; and

WHEREAS, the Press has requested the transfer of the celebration of the Week this year to the period from March 1 to 7, 1971;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from March 1 to 7, 1971, as “National Press Week”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
