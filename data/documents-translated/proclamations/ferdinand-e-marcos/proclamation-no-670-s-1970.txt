MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 670

AUTHORIZING THE CITIZENS’ LEGAL AID SOCIETY OF THE PHILIPPINES TO CONDUCT A NATIONWIDE FUND CAMPAIGN DURING THE PERIOD FROM MAY 1 TO AUGUST 31, 1970.

WHEREAS, the Citizens’ Legal Aid Society of the Philippines (CLASP) is a civic organization committed to, among other things, the giving of free legal services to those who; on account of their meager resources, could not afford the expenses of litigation for the redress of legitimate grievances or defense of individual rights; and

WHEREAS, in order to effectively carry out its noble objectives, the Society needs the financial and moral support of the public;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby authorize the Citizens’ Legal Aid Society of the Philippines to conduct a nationwide fund campaign during the period from May 1 to August 31, 1970. I call upon all citizens and residents of the country, irrespective of nationality, race or creed, to assist in this endeavor by giving generously of their means and to actively support the campaign. I further call upon all lawyers and bar associations of the country to assist the CLASP in its noble objectives.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary
