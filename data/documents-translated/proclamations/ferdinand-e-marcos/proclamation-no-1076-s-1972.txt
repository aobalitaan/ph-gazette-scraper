TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1076

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 3 TO 9, 1972, AS GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES’ WEEK.

WHEREAS, there are legions of unsung heroes in the service of the government whose existence remain unnoticed;

WHEREAS, the public must be made to realize the existence of these loyal and efficient state workers;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from December 3 to 9, 1972, as Government Employees’ Week. I call upon all citizens to give a thought to the dedicated and persevering government employee in grateful appreciation for his services. The week should be devoted to maximizing service efficiency and the promotion of better relations between the public and the government employee.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
