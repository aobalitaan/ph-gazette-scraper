MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2187

SETTING THE DATE FOR THE HOLDING OF ELECTIONS FOR THE POSITIONS OF REPRESENTATIVES TO THE SANGGUNIANG PAMPOOK OF REGIONS NINE AND TWELVE.

WHEREAS, the three-year term of office of the representatives to the Sangguniang Pampook of Regions Nine and Twelve expires on May 8, 1982; and

WHEREAS, Batas Pambansa Blg. 20 approved on March 23, 1979, authorized the President to fix the date of the election of representatives to the Sangguniang Pampook of Regions Nine and Twelve.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby set the date of the elections, the campaign period and the last day for the filing of certificates of candidacy by candidates to the positions of representatives to the Sangguniang Pampook of Regions Nine and Twelve, as follows:

1. The election of representatives to the Sangguniang Pampook of Regions Nine and Twelve shall be held on June 7, 1982.

2. The campaign period of candidates shall not be more than fifteen (15) days starting on May 21, 1982 and ending on June 5, 1982.

3. No person shall be elected representatives to the Sangguniang Pampook of Region Nine or Twelve unless he files in triplicate with the Commission on Elections, not later than May 11, 1982, a sworn certificate of candidacy in the form prescribed by the Commission on Elections.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant
