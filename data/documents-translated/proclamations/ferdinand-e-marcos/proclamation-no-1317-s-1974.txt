TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1317

DECLARING SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 28, 1974, AS WENCESLAO Q. VINZONS DAY IN CAMARINES NORTE

WHEREAS, the birth anniversary of Wenceslao Q. Vinzons, Sr., outstanding hero and martyr of World War II and a native son of Camarines Norte falls on Saturday, September 28, 1974; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting to commemorate his birthday and recall to mind his exemplary love of country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare September 28, 1974, as Wenceslao Q. Vinzons Day in Camarines Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
