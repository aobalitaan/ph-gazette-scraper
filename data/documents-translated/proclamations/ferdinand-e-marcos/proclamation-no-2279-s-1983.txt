MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2279

RESERVING FOR TRAINING CENTER SITE OF THE NATIONAL MANPOWER AND YOUTH COUNCIL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY PROPER, CITY OF ILOILO, ISLAND OF PANAY

Upon recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources and by virtue of the power vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and reserve for training center site purposes of the National Manpower and Youth Council, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the city proper, City of Iloilo, Island of Panay, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 1063-C Bsd-06-000016

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 1063-C, Bsd-06000016) situated in Iloilo City, Island of Panay. Bounded on the SW., along line 1-2 by Lot 1063-B. Swo-9997, covered by Proclamation No. 86, Series of 1927 (SCHOOL SITE); along line 2-3 Lot 1063-A, Swo-3388 covered by Executive Order No. 61, series of 1917 (SCHOOL SITE); on the NW., along line 3-4 by CALLE ZAMORA (20.00 m. wide); on the NE., along line 4-5 by IN-149, covered by Executive Order No. 17, series of 1917 (WIRELESS TELEGRAPH SITE); and on the SE., along line 5-1 by CALLE DURAN (20.00 m. wide). Beginning at a point marked “1” on the plan, being S. 84-18’E., 991.37 m. from B.L.L.M. #1, Iloilo Cadastre, thence

N. 53-12’W.,   92.27 m. to point 2; N. 44-51’W.,  185.00 m. to point 3; N. 48-15’W.,  82.86 m. to point 4; S. 38-45’E.,   266.98 m. to point 5; S. 36-09’W.,  43.12 m. to point of beginning, containing an area of EIGHTEEN THOUSAND AND FIFTY NINE (19,059) SQUARE METERS. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by Old points. Bearing true. This lot was surveyed in accordance with law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder by Jose S. Umadhay, Sr., Geodetic Engineer on May 10, 1982 and approved by the Regional Director on August 4, 1982.

This proclamation is subject to the condition that the occupants of the land shall be compensated for the value of their improvements.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen hundred and eighty-three.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) MANUEL M. LAZARO Presidential Assistant for Legal Affairs
