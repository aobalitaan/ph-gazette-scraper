TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1044

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL SHRINES COMMISSION FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF CORREGIDOR AND MT. SAMAT NATIONAL SHRINES UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1972

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission was authorized under Proclamation No. 880 dated July 26, 1971, to extend its fund campaign for the development of Corregidor and Mt. Samat national shrines up to June 30, 1972; and

WHEREAS, the National Shrines Commission needs more time to realize its fund campaign goals to pursue its ongoing projects on Corregidor and Mt. Samat National Shrines;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby further extend the National Shrines Commission fund campaign period up to December 31, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
