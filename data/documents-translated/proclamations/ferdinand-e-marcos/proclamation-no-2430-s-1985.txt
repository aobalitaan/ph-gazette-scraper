MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2430

DECLARING JUNE 29, 1985 AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN.

WHEREAS, the 402nd anniversary of the establishment of the civil government of the Province of Cagayan falls on June 29, 1985; and

WHEREAS, the people therein desire to commemorate this milestone in the history of their province and every year thereafter with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare June 29, 1985 and every year thereafter as a non-working special public holiday in the Province of Cagayan as “Aggao Na Cagayan”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-five.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
