MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2363

DECLARING THURSDAY JUNE 21, 1984, AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN

WHEREAS, the Charter Day of the City of Pagadian falls on Thursday, June 21, 1984;

WHEREAS, the people of the said city desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies their Charter Day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, June 21, 1984, as a non-working special public holiday in the City of Pagadian.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-four.

(Sgd . ) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
