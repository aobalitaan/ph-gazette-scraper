MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 81

ESTABLISHING AS BUUG WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE THE PARCEL OF PUBLIC DOMAIN PER B.F. MAP NO. WR-1, SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BUUG, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR, PHILIPPINES.

Upon recommendation of the Director of Forestry, approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of Section 1826 of the Revised Administrative Code, as amended by Republic Act No. 3092, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or other disposition and establish and proclaim as BUUG WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE, subject to private rights, if any there be, the parcel of the public domain located in the Municipality of Buug, Province of Zamboanga del Sur, under the administration and control of the Director of Forestry who shall have the authority to regulate the use and occupancy of said watershed forest reservation and the cutting, collection and removal of timber and other forest products therein in accordance with forest laws and regulations.

The aforementioned parcel of public domain is more particularly described as follows:

Beginning at point marked “1” on the B.F. Map No. WR-1, a Narig tree, 45 cm. in diameter which is identical to Corner 40, Alienable and Disposable Block I, Zamboanga del Sur Land Classification Project No. 19-B;

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines
