MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 581

DESIGNATING JULY 23, .1969, AS THE DATE FOR THE FORMAL ORGANIZATION OF THE GOVERNMENT OF BATANGAS CITY.

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 5495, approved on June 21, 1969, created Batangas City; and

WHEREAS, Section 93 of said Act provides that the government of Batangas City shall be organized on such a date as may be fixed by the President of the Philippines;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, pursuant to the provision of Section 93 of Republic Act No. 5495, do hereby proclaim that the formal organization of the government of Batangas City shall take place on July 23, 1969.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, on this 16th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-nine.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
