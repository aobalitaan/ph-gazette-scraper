MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 2060

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 27, 1981, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN GENERAL SANTOS CITY.

WHEREAS, the residents of General Santos City celebrates their City’s 42nd Foundation Anniversary on Friday, February 27, 1981;

WHEREAS, said residents desire to celebrate the event with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Friday, February 27, 1981, as a special public holiday in General Santos City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN T. VENUS, JR. Deputy Presidential Executive Assistant
