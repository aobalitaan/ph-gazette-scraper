MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 3

MAKING PUBLIC THE CONVENTION OF PARIS FOR THE PROTECTION OF INDUSTRIAL PROPERTY OF 20TH MARCH, 1883, REVISED AT BRUSSELS ON 14TH DECEMBER, 1900, AT WASHINGTON ON 2ND JUNE, 1911, AT THE HAGUE ON 6TH NOVEMBER, 1925, AT LONDON ON 2ND JUNE, 1934, AND AT LISBON ON 31ST OCTOBER, 1958.

WHEREAS, the representatives to the Diplomatic Conference which took place at Lisbon from 6th to 31st October, 1958, made certain modifications and additions to the International Convention of 20th March, 1883, establishing the International Union for the Protection of Industrial Property, revised at Brussels on 14th December, 1900, at Washington on 2nd June, 1911, at The Hague on 6th November, 1925, and at London on 2nd June, 1934;

WHEREAS, the Senate of the Philippines, by its Resolution No. 69, adopted on May 10, 1965, concurred in the adherence to the Convention and its modifications and additions in accordance with the Constitution of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, the Instrument of Adherence by the Philippines to the Convention was deposited with the Swiss Government on August 11, 1965; and

WHEREAS, the Philippines became a party to the Convention on September 27, 1965;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare that said Convention be made public to the end that the same and every article and clause thereof may be observed and fulfilled with good faith by the Republic of the Philippines and citizen thereof.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
