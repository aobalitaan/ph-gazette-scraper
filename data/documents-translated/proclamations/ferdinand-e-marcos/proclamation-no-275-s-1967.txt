MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 275

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 1967 TO OCTOBER 1968 AS “CONSCIOUSNESS YEAR FOR FILIPINO GREATNESS”.

WHEREAS, it is the civic obligation and patriotic duty of every Filipino to participate actively in the achievement of national progress through more positive thinking and action; and

WHEREAS, to achieve this end, a concerted and long-term program by all civic or religious organizations, schools and the press for instilling among people a greater consciousness of the heroic and noble deeds and sacrifices of Filipinos, living and dead, and of their dignity and honor as true Filipinos, in all fields of endeavor, would be very timely and give national direction to our efforts;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from October 1967 to October 1968 as “Consciousness Year for Filipino Greatness” under the auspices of the United Crusade for Greatness, Inc., civic organizations, the schools and the press.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
