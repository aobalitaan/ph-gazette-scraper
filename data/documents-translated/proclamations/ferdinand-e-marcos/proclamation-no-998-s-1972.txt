TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 998

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 1 TO OCTOBER 30, 1972, FOR THE ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGNS OF COMMUNITY CHESTS AND COUNCIL ORGANIZATIONS IN THE PHILIPPINES.

To enable Community Chests and Council organizations in the Philippines to carry on the funding of Community health and welfare services in their respective areas, I, ROBERTO V. REYES, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from June 1 to October 30, 1972, for the Annual Educational Fund Campaign of the following Community Chest and Council organizations and designating their respective areas of solicitation:

I call upon residents, firms and organizations in the area of their respective operation to support these campaigns and to give generously to the Community Chest and Council organizations which unite into one annual appeal the health and welfare needs of the various Red Feather Agencies in their respective areas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
