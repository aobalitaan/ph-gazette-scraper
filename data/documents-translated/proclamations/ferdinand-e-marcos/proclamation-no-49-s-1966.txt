MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 49

EXTENDING THE MEMBERSHIP AND FUND DRIVE OF THE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM AND LIBRARY, INC.

WHEREAS, the membership and fund drive of the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc., expired on June 15, 1966; and

WHEREAS, the organization needs additional time to enable it to raise its fund goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the membership and fund drive of the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc., up to August 31, 1966.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-six.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
