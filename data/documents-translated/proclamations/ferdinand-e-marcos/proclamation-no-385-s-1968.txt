MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 385

EXTENDING UP TO MAY 31, 1968, THE PERIOD WITHIN WHICH THE PHILIPPINE CANCER SOCIETY, INC., MAY CONDUCT A NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 314, dated December 15, 1967, authorized the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc., to conduct a national educational membership and fund campaign from January 15 to April 15, 1968; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Cancer Society Inc., needs more time to realize its fund campaign goal of ₱350,000.00 to help carry out its free cancer detection program;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the period of the national educational, membership and fund campaign of the Philippine Cancer Society, Inc. up to May 31, 1968.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Source : Presidential Management Staff
