TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 1318

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM OCTOBER 21 TO 27, 1974, AS SENIOR CITIZENS’ WEEK

WHEREAS, Senior Citizens have contributed their share to nation-building; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable to focus public attention on model senior citizens who continue serving their country and fellow-men and the need for uplifting the handicapped and the aged among them;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from October 21 to 27, 1974, as Senior Citizen Week under the auspices of the Executive Committee for Senior Citizen Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Assistant Executive Secretary
