TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 928

FURTHER EXTENDING THE EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE PHILIPPINE AMATEUR SWIMMING ASSOCIATION.

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 852 dated May 14, 1971, the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association was authorized to extend its educational and fund campaign up to December 31, 1971; and

WHEREAS, the Association needs more time for the conduct of its drive;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the fund campaign of the Philippine Amateur Swimming Association up to June 30, 1972.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary
