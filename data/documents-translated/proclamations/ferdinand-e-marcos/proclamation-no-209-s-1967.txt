MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 209

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 1 TO AUGUST 15, 1967, FOR THE NINETEENTH ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST.

To enable the Community Chest to carry on its community welfare services, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate period from June 1 to August 15, 1967, for its Nineteenth Annual educational and fund campaign.

I call upon all residents, firms and organizations especially in the City of Manila, Quezon City; Pasay City, Caloocan City and municipalities of Makati, Malabon, Mandaluyong, Navotas, Parañaque and San Juan, Province of Rizal, to support this campaign and to give generously to the Community Chest which unites into one annual appeal the 33 fund campaigns of the following welfare agencies:

Abiertas House of Friendship

American Philippine Guardian

Asociacion de Damas de Felipinas Settlement house

Asilo de San Vicente de Paul

Patronato de Nuestra Sra. de Lourdes

Patronato de Santa Luisa de Marillao

Patronato de San Francisco Javier

Girl Scouts of the Philippines

Quezon City Girl Scouts Council

Pasay City Girl Scouts Council

Manila Girl Scouts Council

Rizal Girl Scouts Council

Caloocan City Girl Scouts Council

Good Shepherd Convent

La Proteccion de la Infanta

Manila Children’s Hospital

Gota de Leche

National Federation of Women’s Clubs

Nursery Classes

Philippine Association of the Deaf

Philippine Band of Mercy

YMCA of the Philippines

Manila YMCA

Downtown YMCA

Quezon City YMCA

YWCA of the Philippines

Manila YWCA

General Assembly of the Blind

Family Life Workshop of the Philippines

Young Christian Workers

I authorize and urge all government officials and employees, including all school authorities and teachers, to accept, for the Community Chest, fund-raising responsibilities and to give it full support and dedicated leadership in their respective communities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1st day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-seven.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
