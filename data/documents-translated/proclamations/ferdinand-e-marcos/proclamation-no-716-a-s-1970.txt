TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 716-A

DECLARING TUESDAY, JULY 14, 1970, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, July 14, 1970, marks the twentieth anniversary of the foundation of the City of Cabanatuan; and

WHEREAS, the people of said city should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, PONCIANO G. A. MATHAY, Acting Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, July 14, 1970, as a special public holiday in the City of Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) PONCIANO C. A. MATHAY Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
