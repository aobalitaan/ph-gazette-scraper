TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

PROCLAMATION NO. 799

DECLARING TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 16, 1971, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CALOOCAN CITY.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, February 16, 1971, marks the 9th foundation anniversary of Caloocan City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Caloocan City should be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALEJANDRO MELCHOR, Executive Secretary, by order of His Excellency, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President-of the Philippines, do hereby declare Tuesday, February 16, 1971, as a special public holiday in Caloocan City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary
