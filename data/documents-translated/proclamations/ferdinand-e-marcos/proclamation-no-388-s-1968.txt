MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 388

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MALABON, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Director of Public Schools, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the municipality of Malabon, province of Rizal, Island of Luzon, more particularly described as follow:

Block 8, Swo-40720 Proposed Proclamation for SCHOOL SITE

A PARCEL OF LAND (the proposed SCHOOL SITE), situated in the barrio of Tañong, Municipality of Malabon, Province of Rizal. Bounded on the SW., NW. and NE., along lines 1-2-3-4-5-6, by Proposed Road, and on the SE., along line 6-1, by Malabon River. Beginning at a point marked “1” on the attacked Sketch Plan, being S. 24 deg. 37′ E., 657.57 m. from B.L.L.M. 1, Mp. of Malabon, Rizal,

thence N. 34 deg. 15′ W., 39.48 m. to point 2; thence N. 9 deg. 41′ E., 2.14 m. to point 3; thence N. 48 deg. 22′ E., 64.20 m. to point 4; thence S. 68 deg. 01′ E., 1.12 m. to point 5; thence S. 32 deg. 57′ E., 57.84 m. to point 6; thence S. 64 deg. 07′ W., 65.15 to the point of

beginning; containing an area of THREE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED FORTY FOUR (3,244) Square Meters. All points referred to are indicated on the attached Sketch Plan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-eight.

(SGD.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
