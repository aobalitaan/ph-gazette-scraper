MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 348

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 2, 2000, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF KABANKALAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, August 2, 2000, marks the 3rd Charter Anniversary of the City of Kabankalan.

WHEREAS, in order for the people of this city to actively participate and give meaning to the celebration, the Sangguniang Panlungsod of the City of Kabankalan has requested that the aforesaid date be declared as a special non-working holiday for the City of Kabankalan.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, August 2, 2000, as a special day in the City of Kabankalan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
