MALACAÑANG Manila BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 186

AUTHORIZING THE WORLD CHILDREN’S FUND PHILIPPINES, INC. TO CONDUCT A NATIONAL FUND RAISING CAMPAIGN FOR A PERIOD OF ONE (1) YEAR

WHEREAS, the World Children’s Fund Philippines, Inc. (“WCF Philippines”) is a non-stock and non-profit charitable and civic organization dedicated to the promotion and aid of needy and suffering children around the world by providing relief for the distressed, abandoned, needy or orphaned children in the community;

WHEREAS, WCF Philippines provides relief and aid not only to the disadvantaged children and their families, but also to children who are victims of natural disasters;

WHEREAS, WCF Philippines is likewise involved in community projects and programs that improve and uplift the quality of life, moral, mental, physical and psychological status of children in the community;

WHEREAS, to ensure and ascertain the continuous, progressive and consistent funding and financial support for programs and services in promotion of its avowed objectives and mission, WCF Philippines shall solicit funds, public contributions and donations, whether in cash or in kind, from individual and corporate donors and contributors nationwide on a year-round basis;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the World Children’s Fund Philippines, Inc. to conduct a national fund raising campaign for a period of one (1) year effective upon the promulgation of this Proclamation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 ST day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
