MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 114

DECLARING THURSDAY, JUNE 17, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, Thursday, June 17, 1999, marks the 32 nd Charter Day Anniversary of the Province of Agusan del Norte;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Agusan del Norte be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their province’s history with appropriate ceremony;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book 1, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Thursday, June 17, 1999 as a special day in the Province of Agusan del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16 th day of JUNE, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
