MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 170

DECLARING TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 28, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALICIA, PROVINCE OF ISABELA

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Alicia will be celebrating its 50 th Golden Anniversary on Tuesday, September 28, 1999;

WHEREAS, it was on such a date that former President Elpidio Quirino signed Executive Order No. 268 which made Alicia a Municipality;

WHEREAS, the residents of the Municipality of Alicia desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this event in their town’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Administrative Code of 1987 do hereby declare Tuesday, September 28, 1999, as a special day in the Municipality of Alicia, Province of Isabela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
