MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 176

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM SEPTEMBER 13 TO 19, 1999, AS “PRODUCTION MANAGEMENT WEEK”

WHEREAS, the Production Management Association of the Philippines (PROMAP), a non-partisan, and non-stock organization was established to promote industrial development through better and more efficient management of the production system;

WHEREAS, PROMAP will be holding the 12th National Convention to be held at the Manila Midtown Hotel, Ermita, Manila on September 16 to 17, 1999, with the theme: “PRODUCTION STRATEGIES TO FAST TRACK ECONOMIC RECOVERY”;

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus national attention on the important role production executives play in the development of our economy as well as in the manufacturing sector;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from September 13 to 19, 1999, as “Production Management Week” under the auspices of the Production Management Association of the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
