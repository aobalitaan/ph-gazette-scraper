MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 335

DECLARING JULY 18 OF EVERY YEAR AS “SOUTH COTABATO DAY”, AND TO FURTHER DECLARE TUESDAY, JULY 18, 2000 AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS, Tuesday, July 18, 2000, marks the Foundation Day Anniversary of the Province of South Cotabato;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of South Cotabato be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their province’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare July 18 of every year as “South Cotabato Day”, and to further declare Tuesday, July 18, 2000 as a special day in the Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 st day of July, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
