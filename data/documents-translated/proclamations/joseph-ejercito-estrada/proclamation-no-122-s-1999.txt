MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 122

DECLARING FRIDAY, JULY 2, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, Friday, July 2, 1999, marks the 426th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Pasig;

WHEREAS, the people of the City of Pasig wish to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Friday, July 2, 1999, as a special day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
