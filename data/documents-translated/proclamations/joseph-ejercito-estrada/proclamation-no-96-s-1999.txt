MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 96

REAFFIRMING COMMITMENT OF SUPPORT TO THE PHILIPPINE JAYCEES AS NATIONAL PARTNER IN THE OBSERVANCE OF THE THIRD WEEK OF APRIL AS THE NATIONAL JAYCEE WEEK CELEBRATION

WHEREAS, the third week of April every year has been declared as National Jaycee Week by Presidential Proclamation No. 160, Series of 1993;

WHEREAS, it is significant to reiterate the importance of the Junior Chamber of the Philippines’ contribution to the nation;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby reaffirm the government’s commitment of support to the Philippine Jaycees as National Partner in the observance of every third week of April each year as the National Jaycee Week Celebration.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 08 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RAMON B. CARDENAS Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
