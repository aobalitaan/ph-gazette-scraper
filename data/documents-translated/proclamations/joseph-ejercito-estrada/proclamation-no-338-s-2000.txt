MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 338

DECLARING THE 7 TH DAY OF JULY 2000 AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER AS “ARAW NG PAMBANSANG KASAGANAAN”

WHEREAS , the 7 th day of July marks the founding anniversary of Kataastaasan Kagalanggalangan Katipunan (KKK), whose primary aim was to achieve independence;

WHEREAS , the Government, in commemorating the founding anniversary of KKK, will launch pro-poor programs and projects aim towards eradicating poverty, with the National Anti-Poverty Commission (NAPC) as the lead agency;

WHEREAS , in the forthcoming 108 th funding anniversary of KKK, the government will launch the National Anti-Poverty Action Agenda (NAAA), formulated with multi-stakeholder participation by the NAPC, in partnership with various government agencies;

WHEREAS , there is a pressing need to keep alive the spirit of unity exemplified by our forefathers, no longer for independence, but to fight poverty besetting our nation;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the 7 th day of July 7, 2000 and every year thereafter as “Araw ng Pambansang Kasaganaan”.

I call upon the public and private sectors to give the observance of the day their full support.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 th day of JuLy, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
