MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 260

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED AT THE FILINVEST CORPORATE CITY IN ALABANG, MUNTINLUPA CITY, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by RA 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 99-167, dated 30 June 1999, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at Filinvest Corporate City, Barangay Alabang, Muntinlupa City, Metro Manila, to be known as the NORTHGATE CYBERZONE , consisting of ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY SEVEN THOUSAND SIXTY EIGHT (187,068) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
