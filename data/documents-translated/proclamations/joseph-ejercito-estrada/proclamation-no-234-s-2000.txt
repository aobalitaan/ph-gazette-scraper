MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 234

DECLARING MONDAY, JANUARY 31, 2000 AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CABADBARAN, AGUSAN DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, Monday, January 31, 2000, marks the foundation anniversary of the Municipality of Cabadbaran, Agusan del Norte;

WHEREAS, in order to rekindle the momentum of the town’s independence from its mother place, and considering the series of activities that will be conducted in line with the celebration, the Sangguniang Bayan of Cabadbaran, Agusan del Norte has requested that the aforesaid date be declared as a non-working holiday.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, January 31, 2000, as a special day in the Municipality of Cabadbaran, Agusan del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
