MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 6

DECLARING THE FIRST WEEK OF SEPTEMBER 1998 AS “DIRECT SELLING WEEK”

WHEREAS, it is one of the avowed policies of the government to advance economic growth by promoting expanded trade and commerce;

WHEREAS, it is necessary to recognize the important role production executives play in the development of our economy;

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus national attention on the contribution of direct selling industry in boosting economic growth,

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the first week of September 1998 as “Direct Selling Week” in the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7 day of Aug, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
