MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 11

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 2, 1998, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 2, 1998, dubbed as Victory Day in the Province of Ifugao to commemorate the triumph of democracy by the Filipino-American soldiers during the World War II;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ifugao be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book 1, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 2, 1998 as a special day in the Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 st day of SEPTEMBER, 1998 in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
