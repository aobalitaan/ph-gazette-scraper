PROCLAMATION NO. 320

DECLARING THE MAINIT HOTSPRINGS NATIONAL PARK SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NABUNTURAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE AS A PROTECTED AREA AND ITS PERIPHERAL AREAS AS BUFFER ZONE PURSUANT TO RA 7586 (NIPAS ACT OF 1992), AND SHALL BE KNOWN AS MAINIT HOTSPRINGS PROTECTED LANDSCAPE
