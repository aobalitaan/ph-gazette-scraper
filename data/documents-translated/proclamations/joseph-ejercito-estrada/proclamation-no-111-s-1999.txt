MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 111

CALLING THE CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO A SPECIAL SESSION

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by the Constitution, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA , President of the Philippines, do hereby call the Congress of the Philippines to a Special Session on July 12 to 16, 1999 to urgently consider and enact the following vital legislative measures on:

¨ Organic Act for the New Autonomous Region in Southern Philippines;

¨ Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao (ARMM) Elections;

¨ Omnibus Power Industry Bill; and

¨ Home Insurance and Guarantee Corporation (HIGC) bill,

and such other bills certified as to the necessity of their immediate enactments pursuant to the provisions of Section 26 (2), Article VI of the Constitution.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 2nd day of June in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
