MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 116

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 21, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF PAGADIAN

WHEREAS, Monday, June 21 1999, marks the 30th Charter Anniversary of the City of Pagadian;

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 590 dated June 20, 1990, declares June 21, 1990 as “Pagadian City Day”;

WHEREAS, the residents of the City of Pagadian desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this event in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Monday, June 21, 1999, as a special day in the City of Pagadian.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
