MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 370

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 8, 2000 AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER AS “BUEN SUCESO DAY” IN THE CITY OF PARAÑAQUE

WHEREAS, the City Council of the City of Parañaque adopted and approved Ordinance No. 00-12 dated July 18, 2000, declaring September 8 as “Buen Suceso Day”;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper to give the people of the City of Parañaque full opportunity to participate and join in this historic occasion.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare September 8, 2000 and every year thereafter as “Buen Suceso Day” in the City of Parañaque. The day, however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
