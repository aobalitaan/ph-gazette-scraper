MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 78

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF PRESIDENT QUIRINO CENTRAL SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT QUIRINO, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition, or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the President Quirino Central Elementary School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land situated in Poblacion, Municipality of President Quirino, Province of Sultan Kudarat, Island of Mindanao, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 10695, PLS-73 (IDENTIFICATION TO LOT 2, CCS-12-000052-D)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 10695, Pls-73, identical to Lot 2, Ccs-12-000052-D, being a portion of Lot 784, 7819 and Road, Pls-73, Buluan Public Land Subdivision) situated in Poblacion, Municipality of President Quirino, Province of Sultan Kudarat. Bounded on the SE., along line 1-2 by Road, 15.00 m wide; on the SE., and SW., along lines 2-3-4-5 by Lot 6899, Pls-73; on the W., along line 5-6 by Lot 1 of the Consolidation Subdivision Plan; Ccs-12-000052-D; on the NW., along line 6-7 by Lot 783, Pls-73; on the N., along line 7-8 by Lot 3 (National Highway 60.00 m. wide of the Consolidation-Subdivision Plan Ccs-12-000052-D identical to Lot 10696, Pls-73); along line 8-9 by National Highway 60.00 m. wide; along line 9-10 by Lot 5, (National Highway 60.00 m. wide of the Consolidated Subdivision Plan, Ccs-12-000052-D, identical to Lot 10698, Pls-73); and on the SE., along lines 10-11-1 by Road 15.00 m. wide.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S. 70 deg. 35 E., 870.24 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 7, Pls-73; thence,

beginning, containing an area of TWENTY SIX THOUSAND SEVENTY TWO (26,072) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground. Corners 1, 2, 3, 4 and 11 by Old, B.L. Cyl. 15 x 60 cms. and the rest are PS Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cms. Bearing true. Date of Original Survey on October 1946 – February 1952 and Consolidation-Subdivision Survey by Santiago C. Alhambra on December 10–14, 1984 and approved on July 8, 1985.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24 th day of FEBRUARY, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
