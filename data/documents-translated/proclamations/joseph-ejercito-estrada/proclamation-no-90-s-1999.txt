MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 90

DECLARING SATURDAY, APRIL 3, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY

WHEREAS, April 3, 1999, falls on a Saturday, between Good Friday and Easter Sunday;

WHEREAS, the said day, April 3, 1999, may be declared a special day without detriment to public service to allow our people uninterrupted opportunity to observe and ponder on the significance of lent.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, April 3, 1999, a special day throughout the country.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13 th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
