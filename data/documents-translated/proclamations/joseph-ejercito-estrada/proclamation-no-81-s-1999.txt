MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 81

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE MANGELEN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF MANGELEN, MUNICIPALITY OF PRESIDENT QUIRINO, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Mangelen Elementary School a parcel of land situated in the Barrio of Mangelen, Municipality of President Quirino, Province of Sultan Kudarat, Island of Mindanao, which is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 6999, Pls-73

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 6999, Pls-73) situated in the barrio of Mangelen, Municipality of President Quirino, Province of Sultan Kudarat, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the SE., SW., and NW., along lines 1-2-3-4 by Lot 6846, on the NW., and NE., along lines 4-5-6 by Lot 6847, all of Pls-73, on the NE., along line 6-7 by Road; and on the NE., and SE., along lines 7-8-1 by Lot 7787, Pls-73. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 00 deg. 28′ E., 918.10 m. from B.B.M. No. 23, Pls-73, thence;

beginning, containing an area of FORTY THOUSAND AND FORTY SEVEN (40,047) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cms. Bearing true. Date of surveyed October, 1946 – February 20, 1952 and surveyed by P. Apostol and was approved on May 21, 1955.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24 th day of FEBRUARY, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
