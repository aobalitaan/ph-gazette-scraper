MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 73

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 3, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, February 3, 1999, marks the 49th Foundation Anniversary of the City of Cabanatuan;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the City of Cabanatuan be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, February 3, 1999, as a special day in Cabanatuan City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 03 rd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
