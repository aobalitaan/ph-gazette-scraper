MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 70

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE CONCEPCION ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY CONCEPCION, MUNICIPALITY OF MACO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Concepcion Elementary School under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Concepcion, Municipality of Maco, Province of Davao, Island of Mindanao, which is more particularly described as follows:

Lot 7040-A, Csd-11-010886-D (Identical to Lot 14710, Cad-276) CONCEPCION ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 7040-A, Csd-11-010886-D, portion of Lot 7040, Cad-276), situated in the Barrio of Concepcion, Municipality of Maco, Province of Davao, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the N., along line 3-4 by Lot 7039 (Lot 2766 K, Csd-11227); on the E., along lines 4-5-1 by lot 5083 (Lot 111, Bsd-20041-D); both of Cad-276; on the S., along line 1-2 by lot 7040-B (Lot 14711, Cad-276); on the W., along line 2-3 by lot 7040-E (Road – Lot 14714, Cad-276) both of Csd-11-010886-D.
