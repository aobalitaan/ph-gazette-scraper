MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 168

DECLARING FRIDAY, SEPTEMBER 17, 1999, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Friday, September 17, 1999, marks the 47 th Foundation Anniversary of the Province of Zamboanga del Sur;

WHEREAS, the residents of the Province of Zamboanga del Sur should be given full opportunity to celebrate the event with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Friday, September 17, 1999, as a special day in the Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety nine.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
