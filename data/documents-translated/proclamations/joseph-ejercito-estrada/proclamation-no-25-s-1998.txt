MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 25

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 23 TO 29, 1998 AND EVERY LAST WEEK OF NOVEMBER OF EACH YEAR AS NATIONAL MUSIC WEEK FOR YOUNG ARTISTS

WHEREAS, Filipino music is the expression of the soul of this nation;

WHEREAS, in recognition of the Filipino’s inmate (sic) love for music, a fitting celebration to encourage young artists to excel in music composition and performance is most opportune; and

WHEREAS, there is an imperative need to preserve, develop and promote Philippine Music as an art and as a handmaid of cultural development;

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 1173 dated August 7, 1973, the National Music Competitions for Young Artists Foundation, Inc. has effectively undertaken the discovery and development of young talents in music by holding annual competitions and festivals in celebration of National Music Week;

NOW THEREFORE, I, JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from November 23 to November 29, 1998 and every last week of November of each year as “NATIONAL MUSIC WEEK FOR YOUNG ARTISTS”.

I urge all citizens of this country as well as all associations and organizations to declare this week appropriately to encourage Filipinos to appreciate Filipino music and make it form part of their cultural life.

Done in the city of Manila, this 30 th day of SEPT., in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety eight.

(Sgd.) JOSEPH EJERCITO ESTRADA

By the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary
