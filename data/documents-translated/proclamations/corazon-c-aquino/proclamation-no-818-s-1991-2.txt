PROCLAMATION NO. 818

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 92 DATED JUNE 1, 1922 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE PIKIT TOWNSITE RESERVATION SITUATED IN THE BARANGAY OF BATULAWAN, MUNICIPALITY OF PIKIT, PROVINCE OF NORTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, BY EXCLUDING THEREFROM CERTAIN PORTIONS OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND RESERVING THE SAME FOR THE NATIONAL IRRIGATION ADMINISTRATION-MALITUBOG-MARIDAGAO IRRIGATION PROJECT OFFICE BUILDING SITE PURPOSES

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 92 dated June 1, 1922 which established the Pikit Townsite Reservation situated in the Barangay of Batulawan, Municipality of Pikit, Province of North Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, by excluding therefrom contain portions of the land embraced therein and reserving the same for the National Irrigation Administration-Malitubog-Maridagao Irrigation Project office building site purposes, which parcels are more particularly described as follows:

Lot-A, CCS-12-000106 (National Irrigation Administration)
