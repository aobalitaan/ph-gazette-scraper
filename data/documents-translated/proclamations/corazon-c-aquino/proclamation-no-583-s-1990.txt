MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 583

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 3 TO 9, 1990, AS SALESMAN’S WEEK

WHEREAS, the Sales and Marketing Executives International-Philippines (SMEI) will be commemorating its 25th Anniversary in the month of June;

WHEREAS, the occasion will be highlighted by the awarding of The Outstanding Philippine Salesmen;

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus national attention on the Contribution of salesmen to the continued economic growth of our country;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from June 3 to 9, 1990, as Salesman’s Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 4th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
