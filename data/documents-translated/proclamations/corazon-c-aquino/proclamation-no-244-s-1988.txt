MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 244

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE GIRL SCOUTS OF THE PHILIPPINES UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1988.

WHEREAS, the Girl Scouts of the Philippines was authorized under Proclamation No. 68 dated February 20, 1987, to conduct a national fund campaign up to October 17, 1987; and

WHEREAS, the Girl Scouts of the Philippines needs additional time to raise more funds for its laudable goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national fund campaign period of the Girl Scouts of the Philippines up to December 31, 1988.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila this 12th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
