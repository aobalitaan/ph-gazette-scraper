MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 559

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE UNITED WAY PHILIPPINES, INC., UP TO MARCH 31, 1991

WHEREAS, the United Way Philippines, Inc., formerly Community Chests and Councils of the Philippines, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 395, dated March 31, 1989, to conduct a national fund campaign during the period from March 31, 1989 to March 31, 1990;

WHEREAS, the United Way Philippines, Inc., needs additional time to raise more funds for its laudable objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national fund campaign period of the United Way Philippines, Inc., up to March 31, 1991.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of April , in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
