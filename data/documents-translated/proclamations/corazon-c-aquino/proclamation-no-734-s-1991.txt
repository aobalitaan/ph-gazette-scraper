MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 734

ESTABLISHING AS ANDANAN RIVER WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE FOR PURPOSES OF PROTECTING, MAINTAINING, OR IMPROVING ITS WATER YIELD AND PROVIDING RESTRAINING MECHANISM AGAINST INAPPROPRIATE FOREST EXPLOITATION AND DISRUPTIVE LAND-USE, A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF SIBAGAT AND BAYUGAN, PROVINCE OF AGUSAN DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition and settlement and set aside as Andanan River Watershed Forest Reservation, subject to Private rights, if any there be, and to the operation of previous proclamations reserving any portion thereof for specific purposes and also to the implementation of socially-oriented projects and other forestry development programs consistent with the objectives of the proclamation as may be determined by the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, a parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Municipalities of Sibagat and Bayugan, Province of Agusan del Sur, Island of Mindanao as shown and described in the Forest Management Bureau Map No. WR 88 with the following descriptions:
