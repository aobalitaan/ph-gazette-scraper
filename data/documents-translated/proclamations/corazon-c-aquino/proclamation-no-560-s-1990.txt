MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 560

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MAY 7 TO 13, 1990, AS MANUFACTURING ENGINEERING WEEK

WHEREAS, the manufacturing engineering profession plays a key role in organizing the production facilities, processes and equipment in order to manufacture the highest quality products consistently and efficiently;

WHEREAS, in pursuance of these objectives, the 1990 National Convention Manufacturing Engineering will be held on May 10 and 11, 1990;

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus attention to the protection of manufacturing engineering as a key function in planning for the industrialization of the Philippines;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from May 7 to 13, 1990, as Manufacturing Engineering Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I, have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
