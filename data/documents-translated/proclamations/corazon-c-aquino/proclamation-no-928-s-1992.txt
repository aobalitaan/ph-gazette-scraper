MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 928

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JULY 1, 1992, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF DIPOLOG

WHEREAS, Wednesday, July 1, 1992, marks the 79th Charter Anniversary of Dipolog City;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of Dipolog City be given the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, July 1, 1992, as a special day in Dipolog City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
