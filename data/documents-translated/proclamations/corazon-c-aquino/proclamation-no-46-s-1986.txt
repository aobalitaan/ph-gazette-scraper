M A L A C A Ñ A N G MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 46

DECLARING TUESDAY, DECEMBER 2, 1986, AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF PASAY.

WHEREAS, Tuesday, December 2, 1986, marks the 39th Foundation Anniversary of the City of Pasay; and

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the residents of Pasay City be given full opportunity to celebrate the day with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, December 2, 1986, as a non-working special public holiday in the City of Pasay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-six.

(SGD) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By authority of the President:

(SGD) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
