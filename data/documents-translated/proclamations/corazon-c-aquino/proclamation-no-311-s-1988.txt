MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 311

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM SEPTEMBER 4 TO 10, 1988, AND THEREAFTER THE SECOND WEEK OF SEPTEMBER OF EVERY YEAR AS THE HOTEL AND RESTAURANT INDUSTRY WEEK AND CREATING A COMMITTEE TO IMPLEMENT THE PROGRAM INCIDENT THERETO.

WHEREAS, the government recognizes the important role played by the hotel and restaurant industry in the development and economic well-being of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, for this industry’s contribution to national economic development – the employment it generates, the income derived from this industry and its significance to the overall cultural and social upliftment of our people – it is imperative that its vital role be fully understood, appreciated and supported by everyone in all segments of our society; and

WHEREAS, there is also need to provide recognition to hoteliers and restaurateurs as they strive to be more competitive in the local and world markets and place the Philippines in the forefront of hospitality in Asia and the world;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from September 4 to 10, 1988 and thereafter the second week of September of every year as the Hotel and Restaurant Industry Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
