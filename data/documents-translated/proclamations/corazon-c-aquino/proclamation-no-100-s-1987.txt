MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 100

AUTHORIZING THE REHABILITATION AND PLACEMENT FOUNDATION FOR THE DISABLED, INC. TO CONTINUE ITS NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN UP TO JUNE 30, 1987

WHEREAS , the Rehabilitation and Placement Foundation for the Disabled, Inc. is a non-profit and charitable organization established to assist the disabled;

WHEREAS , the Rehabilitation and Placement Foundation for the Disabled, Inc. needs funds to support its various medical, financial and food and material assistance and other programs;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby authorize the Rehabilitation and Placement Foundation for the Disabled, Inc. to continue its fund-raising campaign up to June 30, 1987.

I call upon all citizens and residents of the country to give generously of their means, actively support the campaign and assist the Foundation in its laudable objectives.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have set hereunto my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of May in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
