MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 452

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY KATIPUNAN, MUNICIPALITY OF STO. NIÑO, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if there be any, certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Katipunan, Municipality of Sto. Niño, Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao and more particularly describe as follows:

Lot 4991, Cad. 409 identical to Lot No. 56, Ts-322 (KATIPUNAN BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL)

A parcel of land, Lot 4991, Cad. 409 identical to Lot 56, Ts-322 situated in Barangay Katipunan, Municipality of Sto. Niño, Province of South Cotabato, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the North along lines 1-2, 2-3, 3-4, by road; on the East along line 4-5 by road; on the South along lines 5-6, 6-7, 7-8 by road; on the West along line 8-1 by road. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S. 32 deg. 13’E., 6,119.96 m. from BLLM No. 1 Cad. 409.

beginning, containing an area of FIFTEEN THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED NINETY (15,490) SQUARE METERS more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by B.L. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cm. bearings true; date of survey January 6, 1966, by Geodetic Engineer Feliciano T. Juarez and was approved on 11 July 1983.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
