MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 556

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 16, 1991, AS SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVOTAS, METROPOLITAN MANILA

WHEREAS, on January 16, 1906, the Municipality of Navotas was created as a separate town;

WHEREAS, it is fitting to set aside Wednesday, January 16, 1991, as a special day in the Municipality of Navotas in order to enable the entire citizenry of the said Municipality to commemorate and celebrate an important page in their town’s history;

WHEREAS, the residents of the Municipality of Navotas wish to mark this momentous day with fitting activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Wednesday, January 16, 1991, as a special day in the Municipality of Navotas, Metropolitan Manila. The day however shall be a working day.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
