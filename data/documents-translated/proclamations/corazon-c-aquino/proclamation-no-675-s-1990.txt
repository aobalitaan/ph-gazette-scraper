MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 675

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE TOLOSA BARANGAY HIGH SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY DOÑA BRIGIDA, MUNICIPALITY OF TOLOSA, PROVINCE OF LEYTE, ISLAND OF LEYTE

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserves for school site purposes of the Tolosa Barangay High School, under the administration of the Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Doña Brigida, Municipality of Tolosa, Province of Leyte, Island of Leyte, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 3712, Cad-822-D

A parcel of land surveyed as Lot No. 3712, Cad-822-D, Tolosa Cadastre, located at Barangay Doña Brigida, Municipality of Tolosa, Province of Leyte, Island of Leyte. Bounded on the Northeast (NE), along line 1-2 by Guinarona River; on the Southeast (SE), along line 2-3 by Lot No. 260; along line 3-4 by Lot No. 258; along line 4-5 by Lot No. 256; on the Southwest (SW), along line 5-6 by Guinarona River; along line 6-7 by Lot No. 255; on the Northwest (NW), along line 7-8 by Lot No. 257; and along line 8-1 by Lot No. 259, all of Cad-822-D, Tolosa Cadastre. Beginning at a point marked “1” on the plan being S. 14 deg. 28 min. E., 441.25 m. from BLLM No. 1 of Cad-822-D. Tolosa Cadastre thence:

S. 69 deg. 14 min. E., 40.06 m. to point 2;

S. 11 deg. 53 min. W., 46.32 m. to point 3;

S. 16 deg. 55 min. W., 46.62 m. to point 4;

S. 12 deg. 18 min. W., 86.54 m. to point 5;

N. 37 deg. 33 min. W., 17.62 m. to point 6;

N. 60 deg. 34 min. W., 31.87 m. to point 7;

N. 14 deg. 02 min. E., 111.43 m. to point 8;

N. 17 deg. 00 min. E., 53.26 m. to point of beginning.

Containing an area of SEVEN THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED AND FORTY SIX (7,246) SQUARE METERS, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the Plan and marked on the ground by PS. cyl. conc. mons. Bearings Grid, Surveyed by Silvino R. Pineda, Cadastral Surveyor on January 24 to February 14, 1984 and approved on September 10, 1984;

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) MARIANO SARMIENTO II DeputyExecutive Secretary
