MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 763

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JULY 24 TO 31, 1991, AS “ENTREPRENEURS WEEK”

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 617, the period from July 24 to 31, 1990, was declared “Entrepreneurs Week” under the auspices of the Entrepreneurs Society of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, the aforesaid Society will hold its 1st National Convention of Entrepreneurs on July 26, 1991 under the theme “Entrepreneurship: The Gateway To The Challenging World of Business”;

WHEREAS, it is but proper and fitting to refocus public attention and awareness to the invaluable contribution of our entrepreneurs to the country’s economic development;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from July 24 to 31, 1991, as “Entrepreneurs Week” under the auspices of the Entrepreneurs Society of the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
