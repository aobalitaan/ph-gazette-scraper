MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 148

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 7, 1987 AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, September 7, 1987 marks the 47th Charter Anniversary of the City of Cavite;

WHEREAS, the residents of Cavite City should be given the opportunity to commemorate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare September 7, 1987 as a non-working special day in the City of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
