MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 630

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE FILIPINO WAR VETERANS FOUNDATION (FILVETS), INC., UP TO SEPTEMBER 14, 1991

WHEREAS, the Filipino War Veterans Foundation (FILVETS), Inc. was authorized under Proclamation No. 467, dated September 14, 1989, to conduct a national fund campaign during the period from September 14, 1989 to September 14, 1990;

WHEREAS, the Filipino War Veterans Foundation (FILVETS), Inc., needs additional time to raise more funds for its laudable objectives;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national fund campaign period of the Filipino War Veterans Foundation (FILVETS), Inc., up to September 14, 1991.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 17th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
