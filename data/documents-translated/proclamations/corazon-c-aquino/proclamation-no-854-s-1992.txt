MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 854

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 22 TO 28, 1992, AS PHILIPPINE ROTARY WEEK

WHEREAS, the Rotary International District 3810 – Philippines will hold its Annual District Conference in Manila on March 26 to 28, 1992;

WHEREAS, Rotary International District 3810 will also host the 4th Rotary Goodwill Congress in Asia, which will be attended by Rotarians from the neighbor countries of the Philippines on March 24 to 25, 1992;

WHEREAS, it is desirable to set aside a week to focus national attention to the various programs and projects of the Rotary for the poor, including its thrusts to promote and initiate various peace and environmental projects;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from March 22 to 28, 1992, as Philippine Rotary Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to the affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
