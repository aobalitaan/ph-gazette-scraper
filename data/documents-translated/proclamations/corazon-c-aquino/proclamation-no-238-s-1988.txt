MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 238

DECLARING MAY 17-23 OF EVERY YEAR AS “NATIONAL ELECTRONICS AND TELECOMMUNICATIONS WEEK”

WHEREAS, telecommunications plays a vital role in the development and growth of the nation’s economy, thereby contributing to the overall socio-economic and political development of the country;

WHEREAS, there is a need to solidify the gains realized under a truly democratic society through effective and efficient means of processing and transferring information to the greatest number of our people; and

WHEREAS, there is also a need to focus public attention on the significant role of telecommunications in the national development program of the Government;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from May 17-23 of every year, as “National Electronics and Telecommunications Week”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
