MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 157

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF LIBERTAD ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF LIBERTAD, MUNICIPALITY OF KOLAMBUGAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Libertad Elementary School under the administration of the Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Barrio of Libertad, Municipality of Kolambugan, Province of Lanao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 7167, PLS-89
