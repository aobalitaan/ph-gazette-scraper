PROCLAMATION NO. 136

ESTABLISHING AS CASECNAN RIVER WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE THE PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE PROVINCES OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, QUIRINO, AND AURORA, ISLAND OF LUZON, PHILIPPINES.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C.
