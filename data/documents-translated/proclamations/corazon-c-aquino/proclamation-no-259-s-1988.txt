MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 259

DECLARING THURSDAY, MAY 12, 1988, AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY IN ROXAS CITY.

WHEREAS, Thursday, May 12, 1988 marks the 37th anniversary of the Charter of Roxas City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Roxas City should be given full opportunity to celebrate this occasion with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, May 12, 1988, as a special holiday in Roxas City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
