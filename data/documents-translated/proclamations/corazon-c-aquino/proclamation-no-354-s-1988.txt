MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 354

CALLING THE CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO A SPECIAL SESSION TO CONSIDER LEGISLATIONS FOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF ARTICLE X, SECTIONS 18 AND 19, OF THE CONSTITUTION.

I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby call the Congress of the Philippines to a special session from January 9 to January 13, 1989 to consider and pass the organic acts for the autonomous regions in Mindanao and the Cordilleras in compliance with Article X, Sections 18 and 19, of the Constitution.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 14th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
