MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 740

FURTHER AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 713 DATED APRIL 17, 1991, AS AMENDED, DECLARING THE EXISTENCE OF A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN CERTAIN MUNICIPALITIES IN BATANGAS, ZAMBALES AND TARLAC IN VIEW OF THE PRESENT ACTIVITIES OF THE TAAL VOLCANO AND MT. PINATUBO TO INCLUDE THEREIN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF MABALACAT, FLORIDABLANCA, PORAC AND MAGALANG OF PAMPANGA PROVINCE, ANGELES CITY AND OLONGAPO CITY

WHEREAS, the Municipalities of Mabalacat, Floridablanca, Porac and Magalang of Pampanga province, Angeles City and Olongapo City must be declared as additional areas under a state of public calamity to facilitate the on-going rescue and relief efforts by both the local Disaster Coordinating Councils and the relief agencies due to the continuous volcanic eruption of Mt. Pinatubo.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby further amend Proclamation No. 713 dated April 17, 1991, as amended, declaring the existence of a state of public calamity in certain municipalities in the provinces of Batangas, Zambales and Tarlac in view of the present activities of the Taal Volcano and Mt. Pinatubo to include therein the Municipalities of Mabalacat, Floridablanca, Porac and Magalang of Pampanga province, Angeles City and Olongapo City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) OSCAR M. ORBOS Executive Secretary
