MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 77

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 27, 1987, AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN GENERAL SANTOS CITY.

WHEREAS, Friday, February 27, 1987, marks the 48th Foundation Anniversary of General Santos City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of General Santos City should be afforded full opportunity to fittingly observe the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Friday, February 27, 1987, as a non-working special public holiday in the City of General Santos.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of February in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary
