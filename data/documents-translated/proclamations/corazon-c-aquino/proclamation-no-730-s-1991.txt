MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 730

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 6 TO 12, 1991 AS “NATIONALISM WEEK”

WHEREAS, there is a continuing need to strengthen patriotism and nationalism in the consciousness and activities of every Filipino;

WHEREAS, the importance of Filipino nationalism can effectively be underscored by focusing national attention on patriotism and nationalism, particularly on June 12;

WHEREAS, to achieve this end, a concerted and unified effort from sectors of society must be pursued so as to inspire in everyone a sense of pride in his heritage as a Filipino;

NOW, THEREFORE, I CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from June 6 to 12, 1991 as “Nationalism Week”. All sectors of society and government shall join hands in pursuing projects and activities geared toward inspiring nationalism among our people.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) OSCAR M. ORBOS Executive Secretary
