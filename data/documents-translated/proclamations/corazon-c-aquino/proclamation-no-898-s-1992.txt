MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 898

DECLARING TUESDAY, 12 MAY 1992, AS A NATIONWIDE SPECIAL DAY

WHEREAS, the elections for the President, Vice-President, Senators, Members of the House of Representatives, and local officials shall be held on Monday, 11 May 1992;

WHEREAS, qualified voters who shall exercise their right of suffrage in places other than their places of work or temporary residence should be given sufficient time to return to their respective places of work or temporary residence;

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections as well as the different Boards of Canvassers should be given ample opportunity to thoroughly perform their functions consistent with the requirements of free, orderly and honest elections even after election day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, 12 May 1992, as a nationwide special non-working day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 5th day of May , in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
