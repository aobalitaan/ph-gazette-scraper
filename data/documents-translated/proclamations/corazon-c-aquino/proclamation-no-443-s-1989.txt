MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 443

EXTENDING THE NATIONAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE PHILIPPINE TUBERCULOSIS SOCIETY, INC., UP TO DECEMBER 31, 1990.

WHEREAS, the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc., was authorized under Proclamation No. 271, s. of 1986, to conduct a national educational and fund campaign up to December 31, 1989; and

WHEREAS, it has been found necessary to extend the campaign period of the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc. in order to maintain its humanitarian services to victims of tuberculosis and to protect the community from the White Plague;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue vested in me by law, do hereby extend the national educational and fund campaign period of the Philippine Tuberculosis Society, Inc., up to December 31, 1990.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
