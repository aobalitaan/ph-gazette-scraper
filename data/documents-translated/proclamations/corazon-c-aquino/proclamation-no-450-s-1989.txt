MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 450

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT THE BARRIO OF RUPARAN, MUNICIPALITY OF DIGOS, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Ruparan Elementary School, under the administration of the Secretary of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at the Barrio of Ruparan, Municipality of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao, which is more particularly described as follows:   casia

Lot No. 4083, Cad-275 (Ruparan Elementary School Site)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot No. 4083, Cad-275) situated in the Barrio of Ruparan, Municipality of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur. Bounded on the SW., along lines 1-2-3 by Road; on the NW., along line 3-4 by Lot 1857, Cad-275, on the NE and SE., along lines 4-5-6-1 by Lot 1803, Cad-275, Sta. Cruz Cadastre. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being S. 30-26’W., 462.84 M from BLLM-23, Cad-275, Sta. Cruz Cadastre.

beginning, containing an area of FIFTY THREE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED EIGHTY THREE (53,483) SQUARE METERS. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the grounds by Old Points. Bearings true; date of survey November 1938 – February 1940, which was approved on April 30, 1941.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
