MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 910

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 1, 1992, AS A SPECIAL WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MAKATI, METRO MANILA.

WHEREAS, Monday, June 1, 1992, marks the 322nd founding anniversary of the Municipality of Makati;

WHEREAS, the people of the Municipality of Makati should be given full opportunity to commemorate this event with fitting ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, June 1, 1992, as a special working holiday in the Municipality of Makati, Metro Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
