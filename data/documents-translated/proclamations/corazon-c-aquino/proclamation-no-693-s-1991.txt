MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 693

DECLARING THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 28, 1991, AS “TANGUB CITY DAY”

WHEREAS, Thursday, February 28, 1991, marks the 23rd city charter anniversary of Tangub City;

WHEREAS, the residents of Tangub City desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, February 28, 1991, as “Tangub City Day”. The day, however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) OSCAR M. ORBOS Executive Secretary
