MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 203

MODIFYING PROCLAMATION NO. 191 DATED NOVEMBER 27, 1987 DECLARING THE EXISTENCE OF A STATE OF PUBLIC CALAMITY IN CERTAIN PROVINCES IN THE COUNTRY WHICH WERE ALL HIT BY TYPHOON “SISANG”.

I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby modify Proclamation No. 191 dated November 27, 1987 declaring the existence of a state of public calamity in certain provinces in the country which were all hit by typhoon “Sisang” by including in said proclamation provinces of Catanduanes and Camarines Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 28th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
