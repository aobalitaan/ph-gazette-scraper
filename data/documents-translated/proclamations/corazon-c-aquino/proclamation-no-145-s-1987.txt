MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 145

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 31, 1987, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN LIPA CITY.

WHEREAS, Monday, August 31, 1987, marks the 40th anniversary of the creation of Lipa City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Lipa City should be afforded an opportunity to participate in the ceremonies on that historic occasion;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, August 31, 1987, as a special public holiday in Lipa City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
