MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 816

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL HEALTH CENTER SITE PURPOSES OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SULOP A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF SULOP, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for health center site purposes of the Municipality of Sulop under the administration of the Department of Health, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Poblacion, Municipality of Sulop, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao, and which is more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 9126-A, Csd-11-010160-D, equivalent to Lot 148, Ts-231 Poblacion, Sulop, Davao del Sur (MUNICIPAL HEALTH CENTER OF SULOP)
