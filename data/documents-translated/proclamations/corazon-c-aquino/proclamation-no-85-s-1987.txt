MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 85

DECLARING MONDAY, MARCH 16, 1987, AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN DAVAO CITY.

WHEREAS , Monday, March 16, 1987, marks the Golden Anniversary of the founding of Davao City; and

WHEREAS , the residents of the City should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO , President of the Philippines, do hereby declare Monday, March 16, 1987, as a non-working special public holiday in Davao City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary
