MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 802

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 207, SERIES OF 1950, WHICH RESERVED FOR PRESIDENCIA SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTIAGO, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, ISLAND OF LUZON, AND RESERVING THE LOT EMBRACED THEREIN FOR MARKET EXPANSION AND OTHER COMMERCIAL SITE PURPOSES OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF SANTIAGO, ISABELA

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 207, series of 1950, which reserved for presidencia site purposes a certain parcel of land situated in Santiago, Isabela, and reserved the land embraced therein for market expansion and other commercial site purposes under the administration of the Municipal Government of Santiago, Isabela, subject to private rights, if any there be.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
