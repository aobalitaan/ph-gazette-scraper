MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 674

PROCLAIMING MONDAY DECEMBER 24, 1990 A SPECIAL DAY

WHEREAS, Christmas Day Celebration is one of the most cherished traditions of Filipinos;

WHEREAS, in order to afford the people full opportunity to celebrate the same with all religious fervor, December 24, 1990, a Monday, may well be declared a Special Day;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, December 24, 1990, a Special Day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 7th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
