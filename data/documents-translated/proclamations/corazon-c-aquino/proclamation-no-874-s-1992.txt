MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 874

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 650 DATED OCTOBER 5, 1990 INSTITUTIONALIZING THE KABISIG PEOPLE’S MOVEMENT (KABISIG) PROGRAM TO FACILITATE, COORDINATE AND MONITOR THE IMPLEMENTATION OF GOVERNMENT PROGRAMS AND PROJECTS, AND PROVIDING LINKAGES BETWEEN AND AMONG THE PRIVATE SECTOR GROUPS AND THE GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES FOR THE PURPOSE.

I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 650 dated October 5, 1990 institutionalizing the Kabisig People’s Movement (KABISIG) Program to facilitate, coordinate and monitor the implementation of government programs and projects, and providing linkages between and among the private sector groups and the government agencies and instrumentalities for the purpose, so as to designate Governor BREN Z. GUIAO of Pampanga as Director General of the Kabisig People’s Movement Program, Vice Governor Roberto Pagdanganan of Bulacan.

All other provisions of Proclamation No. 650 shall remain in force and in effect.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
