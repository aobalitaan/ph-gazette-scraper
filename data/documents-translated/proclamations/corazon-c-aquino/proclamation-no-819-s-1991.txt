MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 819

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 2005, DATED SEPTEMBER 5, 1980, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE RESERVATION FOR UNSPECIFIED PUBLIC PURPOSES OF BARANGAY MADIANGAT A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN SAID BARANGAY, MUNICIPALITY OF QUEZON, PROVINCE OF NUEVA VIZCAYA, ISLAND OF LUZON, BY EXCLUDING FROM ITS OPERATION THE RESIDENTIAL AREAS EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE EXCLUDED PORTION OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT; AND RESERVING THE REMAINING AREA SPECIFICALLY FOR SCHOOL SITE AND PUBLIC PLAZA PURPOSES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested, in me by law. I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 2005, dated September 5, 1980 which established the reservation for public purposes of Barangay Madiangat of a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in said Barangay, Municipality of Quezon Province of Nueva Vizcaya, Island of Luzon, by excluding from its operation the residential areas embraced therein and declaring the excluded portions open to disposition under the provisions of the Public Land Act; as amended; and reserving the remaining parcels of land specifically for school site and public plaza purposes, as hereunder indicated and more particularly described as follows:
