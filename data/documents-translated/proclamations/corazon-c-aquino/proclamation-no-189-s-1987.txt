EXTENDING THE PERIOD OF THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE BANTAYOG NG MGA BAYANI FOUNDATION, INC.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 43 dated November 21, 1986 authorized the Bantayog ng mga Bayani Foundation, Inc. to conduct a fund campaign for a period of one year to accomplish its various programs and projects;

WHEREAS, the Bantayog ng mga Bayani Foundation, Inc. needs additional time to enable it to raise more funds to achieve its laudable goals;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby extend for a period of six months the fund campaign of the Bantayog ng mga Bayani Foundation, Inc.

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Acting Executive Secretary
