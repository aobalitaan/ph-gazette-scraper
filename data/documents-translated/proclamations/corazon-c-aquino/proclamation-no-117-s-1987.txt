MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 117

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 15, 1987, AS SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY.

WHEREAS, Monday, June 15, 1987, marks the 37th anniversary of the signing of the Charter of Cagayan de Oro City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Cagayan de Oro City should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their City’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, June 15, 1987, as a special public holiday in Cagayan de Oro City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 10th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(SGD) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
