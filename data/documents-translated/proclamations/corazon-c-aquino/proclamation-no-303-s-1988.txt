MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 303

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 1, 1988, AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY IN BAGUIO CITY.

WHEREAS, Thursday, September 1, 1988, marks the 79th anniversary of the charter of Baguio City; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Baguio City should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Thursday, September 1, 1988, as a special holiday in Baguio City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of August in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
