MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 629

DECLARING FRIDAY, AUGUST 31, 1990, AS “PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON DAY” IN THE PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS, the foundation anniversary of the Province of Bukidnon falls on September 1;

WHEREAS, the residents of the Province of Bukidnon desire to celebrate the foundation anniversary of their province on August 31, 1990, to coincide with the Kaamulan Festival;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Friday, August 31, 1990, as “Province of Bukidnon Day” in the province of Bukidnon. The Day, however, shall be a regular working and school day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
