MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 446

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 7 TO SEPTEMBER 20, 1989 AS “PEACE TOYS WEEK”

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 320, Declaring September 7 to September 20, 1988 as “Peace Toys Week” was issued to “[h]eighten public awareness against the glorification of violence and the propagation of war which war toys promote” and “[t]o introduce children to peace toys and encourage the use thereof for their well-being”, objectives which deserved the support of the Government;

WHEREAS, finding a request for renewal of Proclamation No. 320 “[i]n order to make the observance of Peace Toys Week a huge success like last year” justified and cognizant of the objectives which led to the issuance of Proclamation No. 320;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare September 7 to September 20, 1989 as “Peace Toys Week” under the auspices of the National Federation of Women’s Clubs of the Philippines.

I call upon all public and private offices, institutions and organizations to extend their assistance to make the observance of “Peace Toys Week” meaningful and a success.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
