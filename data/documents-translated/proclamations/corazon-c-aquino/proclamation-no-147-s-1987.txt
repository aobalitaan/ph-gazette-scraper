MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 147

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 2, 1987, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 2, 1987, marks the anniversary of the foundation of the Province of Nueva Ecija; and

WHEREAS, the residents of the Province of Nueva Ecija should be afforded an opportunity to celebrate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 2, 1987, as a special public holiday in the Province of Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
