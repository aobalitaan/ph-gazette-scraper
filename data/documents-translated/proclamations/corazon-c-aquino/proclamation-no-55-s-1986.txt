M A L A C A Ñ A N G MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 55

DECLARING MONDAY, FEBRUARY 2, 1987 AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY THROUGHOUT THE PHILIPPINES

WHEREAS, on Monday, February 2, 1987 a plebiscite will be conducted on the proposed Constitution of the Republic of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, it is necessary that the people should be given the fullest opportunity to participate and vote in this important national political exercise;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, February 2, 1987 as a non-working special public holiday throughout the Philippines.

IN VIEW WHEREOF, I have set hereunto my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 19th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-six.

(SGD) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
