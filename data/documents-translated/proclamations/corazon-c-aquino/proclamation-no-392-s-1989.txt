MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 392

MODIFYING PROCLAMATION NO. 390, SERIES OF 1989, ENTITLED “SETTING THE DATE FOR THE HOLDING OF PLEBISCITE TO DECIDE THE CREATION OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF LIMASAWA, PROVINCE OF SOUTHERN LEYTE”

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 390, dated March 17, 1989, directs that: “[O]n March 28, 1989, a plebiscite shall be held in all barangays and sitios in the island of Limasawa in the Municipality of Padre Burgos, Province of Southern Leyte, to determine whether the said barangays shall become a new municipality to be known as the Municipality of Limasawa, Province of Southern Leyte;”

WHEREAS, the Department of Local Government has requested that the aforesaid plebiscite be held on a date to be determined by the Commission on Elections but not later than May 31, 1989 and clarified the extent of the units directly affected by the creation of the Municipality of Limasawa;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order that a plebiscite shall be held in all the barangays and sitios in the Municipality of Padre Burgos, Province of Southern Leyte to determine whether all the barangays and sitios comprising the Island of Limasawa in the Municipality of Padre Burgos shall become a new municipality to be known as the Municipality of Limasawa, Province of Southern Leyte.

The Commission on Elections shall determine the date of the plebiscite which shall not be later than May 31, 1989, and shall supervise the conduct thereof.

Proclamation No. 390, s. of 1989, is hereby modified accordingly.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 21st day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
