M A L A C A Ñ A N G MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 47

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 1 TO 7, 1986 AS “NATIONAL FURNITURE WEEK”.

WHEREAS, it is but proper that the government recognizes the role of Filipino furniture entrepreneurs and furniture craftsmen in national development;

WHEREAS, the holding of the National Convention of the Chamber of Furniture Industries of the Philippines on December 5-6, 1986 in Cebu City provides an opportune occasion for paying such recognition;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare December 1 to 7, 1986 as “National Furniture Week” in conjunction with the holding of the National Convention of the Chamber of Furniture Industries of the Philippines on December 5-6, 1986.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of November in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-six.

(SGD) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
