MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 353

DECLARING FRIDAY, DECEMBER 23, 1988, AS “MUNICIPALITY OF RODRIGUEZ (FORMERLY MONTALBAN) DAY”.

WHEREAS, Friday, December 23, 1988 marks the foundation anniversary of the Municipality of Rodriguez (formerly Montalban), Province of Rizal; and

WHEREAS, the residents of Rodriguez should be afforded the opportunity to commemorate the event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Friday, December 23, 1988, as “Municipality of Rodriguez Day”. The day, however, shall be a regular working day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 13th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
