MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 465

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 2268 DATED FEBRUARY 14, 1983 ENTITLED “RESERVING A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CURRIMAO, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, ISLAND OF LUZON, FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF A KKK REGIONAL PROCESSING CENTER WHICH SHALL PROVIDE INDUSTRIAL SITES AND SERVICES, RAW MATERIAL PROCUREMENT AND STORAGE FACILITIES, COMMON-SERVICE FACILITIES, RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT, AND SKILLS TRAINING FOR SMALL AND MEDIUM SCALE ENTERPRISES AND MANAGED BY THE KKK PROCESSING CENTER AUTHORITY” FOR THE PURPOSE OF REVERTING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN TO ITS ORIGINAL STATUS AS FOREST LANDS UNDER THE JURISDICTION OF THE FOREST MANAGEMENT BUREAU OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES

WHEREAS, the purposes for which Proclamation No. 2268 dated February 14, 1983 was issued no longer exist;

WHEREAS, the government agency which was given the authority to manage the KKK Regional Processing Center Reservation certified that the land covered by the said reservation is no longer needed by it;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, upon recommendation of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, do hereby order the revocation of Proclamation No. 2268 dated February 14, 1983 which established KKK Regional Processing Center Reservation at Currimao, Ilocos Norte and the reversion of the land embraced therein to its original status as forest lands under the jurisdiction and administration of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources through its Forest Management Bureau. It is likewise ordered that all improvements introduced therein shall be turned over to the said agency for its proper use and maintenance.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 13th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
