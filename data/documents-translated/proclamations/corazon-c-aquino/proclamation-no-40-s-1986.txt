M A L A C A Ñ A N G MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 40

DECLARING THE THIRTY FIRST OF OCTOBER NINETEEN HUNDRED EIGHTY-SIX AND EVERY YEAR THEREAFTER AS “EVELIO B. JAVIER DAY” AND A SPECIAL NON-WORKING PUBLIC HOLIDAY FOR THE PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE AND ROXAS CITY THEREIN.

WHEREAS, on Friday, October 31, 1986, marks the birthday of Evelio B. Javier, a noble son of Antique who in his lifetime pursued the dream of someday seeing freedom, justice and truth reign in his hometown and whose death contributed to the people’s protest against violence and terrorism;

WHEREAS, the people of Antique, his birthplace, desire to celebrate his natal day with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the thirty-first day of October, 1986, and every year thereafter as “Evelio B. Javier Day” and a special non-working public holiday in the Province of Antique and Roxas City therein.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-six.

(SGD) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
