MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 349

DECLARING FRIDAY, DECEMBER 2, 1988, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF PASAY.

WHEREAS, Friday, December 2, 1988, marks the 41st Foundation Anniversary of the City of Pasay; and

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus public attention on the accomplishments and progress of the city so as to stimulate the interest, cooperation, and participation of the residents therein;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority in me vested by Section 2 of Executive Order No. 203, dated June 30, 1987, do hereby declare Friday, December 2, 1988, as a special day in the City of Pasay.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed. cd

DONE in the City of Manila, this 25th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
