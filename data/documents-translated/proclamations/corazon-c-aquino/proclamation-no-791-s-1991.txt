MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 791

FURTHER EXTENDING THE NATIONAL FUND CAMPAIGN PERIOD OF THE FILIPINO WAR VETERANS FOUNDATION (FILVETS), INC. UP TO SEPTEMBER 14, 1992

WHEREAS, the Filipino War Veterans Foundation (FILVETS), Inc. was authorized under Proclamation No. 467 dated September 14, 1989 to conduct a national fund campaign during the period from September 14, 1989 to September 14, 1990 and extended under Proclamation No. 630 dated August 17, 1990 up to September 14, 1991;

WHEREAS, the noble and laudable purposes of the Filipino War Veterans Foundation (FILVETS), Inc. deserve the full support of every Filipino and resident in the country, and every government office and agency, to include the private sector; and

WHEREAS, the Filipino War Veterans Foundation (FILVETS), Inc. needs additional time to enable it to raise more funds to attain its laudable goal;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby further extend the national fund campaign period of the Filipino War Veterans Foundation (FILVETS), Inc. up to September 14, 1992.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 4th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
