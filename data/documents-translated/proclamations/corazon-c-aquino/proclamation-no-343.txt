PROCLAMATION NO. 343

AMENDING THE COMPOSITION OF THE SPECIAL NATIONAL COMMITTEE CREATED UNDER PROCLAMATION NO. 1173, S. 1973 , TO UNDERTAKE PREPARATIONS FOR THE PROPER OBSERVANCE OF THE NATIONAL MUSIC WEEK FOR YOUNG ARTISTS

Proclamation No. 1173, dated August 7, 1973, creating the Special National Committee to undertake preparations for the proper observance of the National Music Week for Young Artists, is hereby amended by changing its composition to read as follows:

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 10th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Eighty-Eight.

President of the Philippines

By the President:

Executive Secretary
