FURTHER EXTENDING THE FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE BANTAYOG NG MGA BAYANI FOUNDATION, INC.

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 43, dated November 21, 1986, authorized the Bantayog Ng Mga Bayani Foundation, Inc. to conduct a fund campaign for a period of one year from November 21, 1986, which was extended by Proclamation No. 189, dated November 23, 1987, for six months or up to May 23, 1988; and

WHEREAS, the Foundation needs more time to conduct its drive to achieve its funding goals;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby further extend the fund campaign of the Bantayog Ng Mga Bayani Foundation, Inc., up to December 31, 1988.

By the President:
(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR.
Executive Secretary
