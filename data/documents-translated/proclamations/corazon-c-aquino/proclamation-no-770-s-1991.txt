MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 770

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 1991 AS “WORLD QUIZ BEE MONTH”

WHEREAS, the country will host the Second World Quiz Bee from September 1 to 9, 1991 under theme “Quiz For World Peace” to emphasize the Children’s role in the achievement of world peace and international understanding;

WHEREAS, the World Quiz Bee dubbed as “The Summit of Young Geniuses” will be participated in by gifted children from various countries who will engage themselves in a friendly contest of wits and brains in the field of science, mathematics and world history and culture;

WHEREAS, there is need to focus public attention and awareness, especially among the youth, to the significance and importance of the said event;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare September 1991 as “World Quiz Bee Month”.

I call upon all residents, regardless of nationality, race, creed, as well as government and non-government agencies, offices and especially educational institutions to lend their full support to ensure the success of the Second World Quiz Bee.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
