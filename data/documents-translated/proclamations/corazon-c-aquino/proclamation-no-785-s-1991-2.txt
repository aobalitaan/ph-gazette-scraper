MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 785

DECLARING TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 1991, AS “IRIGA CITY DAY” IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS, 23rd Charter Anniversary of Iriga City falls on September 3, 1991;

WHEREAS, the residents of Iriga City would like to celebrate the occasion with appropriate programs and activities;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday September 3, 1991, as “Iriga City Day” in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-one.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
