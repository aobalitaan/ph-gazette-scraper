MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 900

DECLARING MONDAY, MAY 11, 1992, AS A SPECIAL DAY THROUGHOUT THE PHILIPPINES

WHEREAS, the national elections shall be held on Monday, May 11, 1992;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, May 11, 1992, as a special day throughout the country.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
