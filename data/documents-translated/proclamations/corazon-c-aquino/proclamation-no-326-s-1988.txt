MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 326

DECLARING MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 19, 1988, AS “DANAO CITY DAY”.

WHEREAS, Monday, September 19, 1988, marks the 27th Charter Anniversary of Danao City; and

WHEREAS, the people of Danao City should be given full opportunity to celebrate the day with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Monday, September 19, 1988, as “Danao City Day”, a regular working day.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 15th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
