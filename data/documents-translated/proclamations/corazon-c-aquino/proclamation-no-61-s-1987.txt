MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 61

DECLARING TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 24, 1987, AS A NON-WORKING SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE CITY OF CEBU.

WHEREAS , Tuesday, February 24, 1987, marks the Golden Charter Anniversary of the City of Cebu; and

WHEREAS , the people of the City of Cebu should be afforded the opportunity to commemorate the event with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I CORAZON C. AQUINO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, February 24, 1987, as a non-working special public holiday in Cebu City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary
