{"translation":"PROCLAMATION NO. 19

DECLARING THE OBSERVANCE OF NATIONAL LANGUAGE WEEK FROM AUGUST 13-19 ANNUALLY.

WHEREAS, a common native language is the bond of national unity, a symbol of racial pride, and an effective instrument for the development of an independent and free nation; and

WHEREAS, the National Language Filipino has played a very important role in the revolution sparked by the People Power which led to a new government.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare that annually, the period from August 13 to 19, the birthdate of the late President Manuel L. Quezon, considered the Father of the National Language, shall be observed as National Filipino Language Week, to be celebrated by all citizens throughout the country, led by those in government and schools, as well as leaders in various fields of life.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, nineteen hundred and eighty-six; and of the independence of the Philippines, the forty-eighth.

By the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary"}
