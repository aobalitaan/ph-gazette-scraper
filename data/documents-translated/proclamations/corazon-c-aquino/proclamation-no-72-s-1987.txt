MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 72

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE LA UNION ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF LA UNION MUNICIPALITY OF SAN ISIDRO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ORIENTAL, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Minister of Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes under the administration of the Minister of Education, Culture and Sports, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barrio La Union, Municipality of San Isidro, Province of Davao Oriental, Island of Mindanao, more particularly described as follows:

Lot 2, II-11485

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 2, II-11485), situated in Barrio La Union, Municipality of San Isidro, Province of Davao Oriental, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the W., along line 1-2 by Hilario Sumulan; on the NE., along line 2-3 by Public Land; on the E., along line 3-4 by C.H. Sawyer; and on the SE., along line 4-1 by unimproved road. Beginning at a point marked “1” of Lot 2, II-11485, being N. 66-05’E., 545.09 m. from B.L.B.M. No. 1, Bo. of La Union, Municipality of San Isidro.

beginning. Containing an area of FIFTY ONE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED SIXTY NINE (51.569) SQUARE METERS. All points referred to are indicated and are marked on the ground. Bearings true. This lot was surveyed in accordance with law and existing regulations on January 15, 1916, and was approved on June 14, 1916.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-seven.

(SGD.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(SGD.) FULGENCIO S. FACTORAN, JR. Deputy Executive Secretary
