MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 435

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM AUGUST 1 TO 6, 1989 AS “RURAL ELECTRIFICATION WEEK”

WHEREAS, the total electrification of the country is one of the fundamental programs of the government and is vital to the general welfare of the people and indispensable to the progress of the nation;

WHEREAS, it is the policy of this administration to pursue this objective vigorously; and

WHEREAS, it is essential that the Filipino people be made aware of the efforts undertaken by the National Electrification Administration and take full cognizance of the benefits of electrification not only in the urban centers but also in the rural areas;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from August 1 to 6, 1989 as “Rural Electrification Week”.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-nine.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
