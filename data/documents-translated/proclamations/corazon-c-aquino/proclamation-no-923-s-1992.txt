MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 923

DECLARING SATURDAY, JUNE 20, 1992, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF COTABATO

WHEREAS, Saturday, June 20, 1992, marks the 33rd Foundation Anniversary of the City of Cotabato; and

WHEREAS, the people of the City of Cotabato should be given full opportunity to celebrate the day with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Saturday, June 20, 1992, as a special day in the City of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
