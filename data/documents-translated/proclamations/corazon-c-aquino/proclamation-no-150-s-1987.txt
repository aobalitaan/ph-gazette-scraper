MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 150

WITHDRAWING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 88 DATED AUGUST 9, 1966 WHICH ESTABLISHED THE MANSALAY FOREST RESERVE CERTAIN IN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANSALAY, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, ISLAND OF LUZON, AND OPENING THE SAME FOR MINING PURPOSES

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from the operation of Proclamation No. 88, dated August 9, 1966 establishing the Mansalay Forest Reserve, certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at the Municipality of Mansalay, Province of Oriental Mindoro, Island of Luzon, and declare the area so withdrawn open for Mining purposes, subject to existing private rights, if any there be, and existing laws, policies, rules and regulations pertinent thereto, as shown in the Bureau of Forest of Development Map No. FR.-175, with the following technical description:

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Eighty Seven.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO
