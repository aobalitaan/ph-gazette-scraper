MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 268

DECLARING FRIDAY, JUNE 10, 1988, AS A SPECIAL HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ABUCAY, BATAAN.

WHEREAS, Friday, June 10, 1988, marks the 400th anniversary of the Christianization of the Province of Bataan, initially in Abucay;

WHEREAS, the people of Abucay should be given full opportunity to commemorate this historic event with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare June 10, 1988, as a special holiday in the Municipality of Abucay, Bataan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9th day of June in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
