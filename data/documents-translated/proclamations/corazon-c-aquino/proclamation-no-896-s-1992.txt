MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 896

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM APRIL 27 TO MAY 3, 1992, AS NATIONAL PRESS WEEK

WHEREAS, the National Press Club of the Philippines (NPC) traditionally holds National Press Week starting the last Monday of April until the first Sunday of May;

WHEREAS, the NPC desires to hold the National Press Week during the period from April 27 to May 3, 1992;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from April 27, to May 3, 1992, as National Press Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
