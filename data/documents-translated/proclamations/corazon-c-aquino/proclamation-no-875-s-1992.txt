MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 875

DECLARING THE MONTH OF MARCH 1992 AS NATIONAL SOFTBALL MONTH IN THE PHILIPPINES

WHEREAS, the Amateur Softball Association of the Philippines will host the International Softball Federation 8th Men’s World Softball Championship from March 20 to 29, 1992;

WHEREAS, there is a need to focus national attention on softball, a sport in which Filipinos can excel and attain world class caliber and recognition;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the month of March 1992 as National Softball Month in the Philippines under the auspices of the Amateur Softball Federation of the Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety two.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO

By the President: (Sgd.) FRANKLIN M. DRILON Executive Secretary
