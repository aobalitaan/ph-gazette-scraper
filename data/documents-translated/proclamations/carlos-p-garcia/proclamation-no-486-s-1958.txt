PROCLAMATION NO. 486

RESERVING FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE CENTRAL BANK OF THE PHILIPPINES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF ERMITA, CITY OF MANILA
