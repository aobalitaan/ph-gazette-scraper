PROCLAMATION NO. 423

RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF PASIG, TAGUIG, PARAÑAQUE, PROVINCE OF RIZAL AND PASAY CITY

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and National Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, CARLOS P. GARCIA, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for military purposes, under the administration of the Chief of Staff of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, subject to private rights, if any there be, and other conditions stated hereunder the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the Municipalities of Pasig, Taguig, Parañaque, Province of Rizal and Pasay City, Island of Luzon, and more particularly described as follows:

PARCEL NO. 2 (Portion)

Beginning at a point marked “54” in plan Psu-2031,

the point of beginning.

Containing an area of THREE MILLION SIXTEEN THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED EIGHTY (3,016,380) square meters, more or less.

Portions of the above described parcel are occupied by the Civil Aeronautics Administration which has the following improvements existing thereon; communication center; weather forecasting center; overseas and foreign air communication system (OFACS); international terminal building; central tower; and maintenance and engineering building. The Civil Aeronautics Administration will continue to make use and occupy the said portions while it has need for them.

PARCEL 3 — A PARCEL OF LAND (Parcel 3 as shown on plan Psu. 2031) situated in Fort Wm McKinley, Rizal, bounded on the N. by San Pedro Makati Estate, Municipality of Pasay and Parcel 4, Psu. 2031; on the E. by Municipality of Taguig and Laguna de Bay Lake; on the S. by Maculim Creek and on the W. by Municipality of Taguig and the Manila Railroad Tracks. Beginning at point marked “1” on the plan, being S. 89 deg. 36′ E; 6226 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 1, Maricaban,

Containing an area of FIFTEEN MILLION EIGHT HUNDRED TWELVE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED EIGHTY FOUR (15,812,684) SQUARE METERS, more or less.

PARCEL 4 — A parcel of land (Parcel 4 as shown on the plan Psu 2031) situated in Fort Wm McKinley, bounded on the N. by Guadalupe Estate and Pasig River; on the E. by Pateros River; on the S. by Parcel 3, Psu 2031. Beginning at point marked “1” on the plan, beginning S. 36 deg. 32′ E., 5629 meters from B.L.L.M. No. 1, Maricaban,

the point of beginning.

Containing an area of SEVEN MILLION SIX HUNDRED SIXTY THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED TWENTY EIGHT (7,660,128) SQUARE METERS, more or less.

From the above parcel 4 the following lots are excluded:

A. American Battle Monument Cemetery.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N 78º 35′ E, 4821.22 meters from B.L.B.M. No. 1, Maricaban;

point of beginning.

Containing an area of 606,985 square meters, more or less.

B. Traffic Circle:

Beginning at a point marked “A” on plan, being S 16 deg. 21′ W., 93.07 meters from point 4, (Note: This line coincide with the line from point 3 to point 4; Parcel 4-A, designated as the American Battle Monument Cemetery); thence N 23 deg. 01′ 09″ E., 57 meters to point “C” (Center of Traffic Circle). With point “C” as center and radius CA (57 meters length) an arc is described from “A” to “B”, a point on the line from point 4 to point 5, Parcel 4-A with an equivalent arc length of 288.52 meters. Containing an area of SEVEN THOUSAND NINETY THREE (7,093) square meters.

C. Diplomatic and Consular Area consisting of 10 has., more or less, and particularly described and indicated in Annex 2, U.S. Diplomatic Note No. 0634, dated December 5, 1956 to the Department of Foreign Affairs, Philippines.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Fifty-Seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twelfth.
