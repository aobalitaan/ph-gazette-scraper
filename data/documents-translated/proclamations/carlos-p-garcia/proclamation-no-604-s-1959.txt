MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PHILIPPINES OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 604

EXTENDING THE ELEVENTH ANNUAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST OF GREATER MANILA.

WHEREAS, the period from July 1 to July 15, 1959, has been set aside for the eleventh annual fund of the Community Chest for Greater Manila under Proclamation No. 586, dated May 15, 1959; and

WHEREAS, said organization needs additional time for the successful prosecution of its campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CARLOS P. GARCIA, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the eleventh annual fund campaign for the Community Chest of Greater Manila up to July 31, 1959.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of July in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fourteenth.

(SGD.) CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
