RESERVING FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE AND INDUSTRY CERTAIN PARCELS OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT, SITUATED IN THE CITY OF MANILA

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of section 64( e ) of the Revised Administrative Code, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for building site purposes of the Department of Commerce and Industry, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the private domain, of the Government, comprising lots Nos. 8 and 9, block No. 2135, situated in the City of Manila, and more particularly described as follows:

Transfer Certificate of Title No: 46987 (Lot No. 8)

A piece of land (lot No. 8 of block No. 2135 of the cadastral survey of the City of Manila), with the buildings erected on it, situated on the line of Isla de Romero Street, Santa Cruz District. Bounded on the NE with lot No. 9 of block No. 2135; on the SE with Isla de Romero Street; on the SO with lot No. 1 of block No. 2135; and on the NW with Helios Street. Starting from a point marked 1 on the plan, whose point is located S. 15° 59′ E., 78.40 meters from fire hydrant No. 491; and S. 66° 42′ W., 119.49 meters from fire hydrant No. 394; and from said point 1 N. 14° 23′ E., 22.56 meters to point 2; from here S. 75° 54′ E., 27.23 meters to point 3; from here S. 15° 36′ E., 25 meters to point 4; from here W. 70° 42′ O., 26.82 meters to the point of origin; measuring a surface area of 641 square meters 70 decimeters, more or less. All the points named are marked on the plan and on the ground, points 1, 2 and 3 are determined by galvanized iron stakes and point 4 by an old large stake; the orientation followed is true; and the date of the measurement, August 10, 1916-June 14, 1917.

Transfer Certificate of Title No. 46986 (Lot No. 9)

Cruz District. Bounded on the NE with Echague Street and with lot No. 11 of block No. 2135; on the SE with Isla de Romero Street; on the SO with Isla de Romero Street, lot No. 8 of block No. 2135 and Helios Street; and on the NW with Helios Street and with lot No. 10 of block No. 2135. Starting from a point marked 1 on the plan whose point is located S. 26° 23′ E., 59.54 meters from fire hydrant No. 491; and S. 76° 28′ W., 107.86 meters from fire hydrant No. 394; and from said point 1 N. 12° 32′ E., 26.64 meters to point 2; from here N. 55° 21′ 30″ E., 4 meters to point 3; from here S. 81°. 49′ E., 22.91 meters to point 4; from here S. 34° 39′ E., 4 meters to point 5; from here S. 12° 31′ W., 29.35 meters to point 6; from here N. 77° 36′ W., 0.51 meters to point 7; from here N. 75° 54′ W., 27.23 meters to point 8; from here N. 76° 30′ W., 0.77 meters to the point of origin, measuring a surface area of 870 square meters 50 decimeters, more or less. All the points named are marked on the plan and on the ground, points 1, 6, 7 and 8 are determined by galvanized iron stakes; the orientation followed is true; and the date of the measurement August 10, 1916-June 14, 1917.
