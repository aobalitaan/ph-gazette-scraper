MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PHILIPPINES OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 689

EXTENDING THE TWELFTH ANNUAL FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST OF GREATER MANILA

WHEREAS, the period from June 1 to July 15, 1960, has been set aside for the twelfth annual fund campaign of the Community Chest of Greater Manila under Proclamation No. 670, dated April 29, 1960; and

WHEREAS, said organization needs additional time for the successful prosecution of its campaign;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby extend the twelfth annual fund campaign of the Community Chest of Greater Manila up to August 15, 1960.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

NATALIO P. CASTILLO Executive Secretary
