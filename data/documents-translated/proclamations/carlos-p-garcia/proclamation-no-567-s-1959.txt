MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 567

DECLARING SATURDAY, MARCH 28, 1959, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY

WHEREAS, the 26th day (Holy Thursday) and the 27th day (Good Friday) of March, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, being public holidays, the 28th day (Saturday) of March, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, may be declared a special public holiday without any prejudice to the public interest;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, and there being sufficient reasons therefor, do hereby declare Saturday, March 28, 1959, as a special public holiday.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
