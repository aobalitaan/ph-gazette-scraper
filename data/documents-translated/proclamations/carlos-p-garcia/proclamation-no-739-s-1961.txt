MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 739

DECLARING FRIDAY, MARCH 17, 1961, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBALES AND IN THE CITY OF CEBU

Whereas, the fourth anniversary of the sudden and untimely death in Cebu City of the late President Ramon Magsaysay falls on March 17, 1961; and

Whereas, the people of his native province of Zambales and the City of Cebu request that they be given full opportunity to observe the said anniversary with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code do hereby declare Friday, March 17, 1961, as a special public holiday in the province of Zambales and in the City of Cebu.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

NATALIO P. CASTILLO Executive Secretary
