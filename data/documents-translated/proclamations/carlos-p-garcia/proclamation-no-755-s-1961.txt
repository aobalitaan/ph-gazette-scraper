MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 755

DESIGNATING THE PERIOD FROM JUNE 1 TO JULY 15, 1961, FOR THE THIRTEENTH ANNUAL EDUCATIONAL AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE COMMUNITY CHEST

To enable the Community Chest to carry on its community welfare services, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate the period from June 1 to July 15, 1961, for its thirteenth annual educational and fund camp;

I call upon all residents, firms, and organizations in the City of Manila, Quezon City, Pasay City, and the municipalities of Caloocan, Makati, Malabon, Mandaluyong, Navotas, Parañaque, and San Juan to support this fund campaign and to give generously to the Community Chest which unites into one annual appeal the fund campaigns of the following:

I authorize and urge all government officials and employees, including school authorities and teachers, to accept, for the Community Chest, fund-raising responsibilities and give it full support and dedicated leadership in their respective communities.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

NATALIO P. CASTILLO Executive Secretary
