MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 479

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 101, SERIES OF 1954, WHICH RESERVED FOR EDUCATIONAL CENTER SITE PURPOSES SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF ERMITA, CITY OF MANILA, A PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND RESERVING THE LAND FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE WORLD HEALTH ORGANIZATION (WHO)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 101, series of 1954, which reserved for Educational Center site purposes situated in the District of Ermita, City of Manila, a portion of the land embraced therein and reserve the same for building site purposes of the World Health Organization (WHO) under the administration of the Secretary of Health, subject to private rights, if any there be, which portion of land is mere particularly described as follows, to wit:

World Health Organization Building Proposed Site

A parcel of land (the proposed site of World Health Organization Building and shown on the attached sketch plan), situated in the District of Ermita, City of Manila. Bounded on the NE., along line l-2by Taft Avenue; along lines 2-3 and 3—4 by remaining portion of this lot; and along line 4-1 by Isaac Peral Street. Beginning at a point marked-1 on the attached sketch plan, which is at the intersection of Isaac Peral Street and Taft Avenue, thence S. 28° 43′ E., 100 meters to point 2; thence S. 61° 22′ W., 100 meters to point 3; thence N, 28° 43′ W., 100 meters to point 4; thence N. 61° 22′ E., 100 meters to point 1, point of beginning; containing an approximate area of 10,000 square meters.

NOTE: All data are approximate and subject to future surveys.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-eight, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twelfth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
