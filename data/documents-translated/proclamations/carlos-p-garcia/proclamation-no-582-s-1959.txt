MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 582

RESERVING FOR PUBLIC PLAZA SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DANAO, PROVINCE AND ISLAND OF CEBU

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of section 83 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for public plaza site purposes under the administration of the municipality of Danao, subject to private rights if any there be, and to future survey a certain parcel of the public domain situated in the municipality of Danao, province and Island of Cebu, and more particularly described as follows:

Proposed Reservation for the Municipality of Danao

“A parcel of land (the proposed reservation for the municipality of Danao), situated in the municipality of Danao, province of Cebu. Bounded on the NW., along line 1-2 by Nicolas del Carmen Road; on the E. and SE., along lines 2-3-4-5 by national highway; and on the SW., along line 5-1 by Marcelo del Pilar Road. Beginning at a point marked 1 oh the attached sketch plan, being N. 30° 17′ E., 169.90 meters from B.L.L.M. 1, Municipality of Danao, thence N. 28° 42′ E., 55.01 meters to point 2; thence S. 1° 38′ E., 22.87 meters to point. 3; thence S, 10° 08′ W., 18.86 meters to point 4; thence S. 20° 11′ W., 14.57 meters to point 5; thence N. 69° 54′ W., 19.93 meters to point 1, point of beginning; containing an approximate area of 672 square meters.”

NOTE: Data are approximate and subject to change based on future survey.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-nine, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the thirteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President: JUAN C. PAJO Executive Secretary
