MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 788

RESERVING FOR THE SITE OF THE BONIFACIO MEMORIAL STADIUM A PORTION OF THE TONDO FORESHORE LAND SITUATED ALONG DEWEY BOULEVARD EXTENSION, DISTRICT OF TONDO, CITY OF MANILA

Upon the recommendation of the Chairman-Administrator of the Land Tenure Administration and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve, under the administration of the City of Manila, for the site of the Bonifacio Memorial Stadium a portion of the “Tondo Foreshore Land” situated along Dewey Boulevard Extension, District of Tondo, City of Manila, which portion is more particularly described as follows:

A parcel of land (portion of the reclaimed land) situated in the District of Tondo, City of Manila. Bounded on the N., by a canal; on the NE., by Dewey Boulevard Extension; on the SE., by Proposed Street; and on the SW. and NW., by reclaimed land (Tondo Foreshore Land). Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan, being N. 31° 55′ W., 778.40 meters from BLLM. No. 8, Manila Cadastre; thence S. 85° 33′ W., 172.00 meters to point 2; thence N. 5° 27′ W., 43.92 meters to point 3; thence N. 7° 29′ W., 43.91 meters to point 4; thence N. 9° 27′ W., 43.92 meters to point 5; thence N. 11° 29′ W., 43.93 meters to point 6; thence N. 13° 30′ W., 43.91 meters to point 7; thence N. 15° 29′ W., 43.92 meters to point 8; thence N. 17° 31′ W., 43.91 meters to point 9; thence N. 19° 31′ W., 43.92 meters to point 10; thence N. 21° 31′ W., 43.91 meters to point 11; thence N. 23° 32′ W., 2.34 meters to point 12; thence N. 57° 17′ W., 244.83 meters to point 13; thence S. 74° 26′ E., 20.17 meters to point 14; thence S. 4° 27′ E., 500.00 meters to the point of beginning containing an area of 90,000 snare meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan, bearings true; date of survey, December 8-15, 1960.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the sixteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

EDILBERTO B. GALLARES Assistant Executive Secretary
