MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 730

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 22, DATED MARCH 10, 1947, ENTITLED “EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 305, SERIES OF 1938, AND RESERVING FOR WAREHOUSE PURPOSES, BLOCK NO. 166, RECLAMATION NO. 1, MANILA SOUTH HARBOR, PORT AREA, CITY OF MANILA

Pursuant to the provisions of section 83 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 22, dated March 10, 1947, by, authorizing the Philippine National Red Cross to use Block No. 166, Reclamation No. 1, Manila South Harbor, Port Area, City of Manila, for warehouse and other purposes, and to make constructions thereon to carry out its purposes as set forth in Republic Act No. 95, as amended by Republic Act No. 855.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

NATALIO P. CASTILLO Executive Secretary
