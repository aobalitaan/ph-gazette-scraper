MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 449

RESERVING FOR COMMUNAL FISHING GROUND PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN COMPRISING A PORTION OF THE FORESHORE FRONTING THE MANILA BAY ALONG THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Seccretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for communal fishing ground purposes, subject to private rights as well as those of occupants who are the holders of fishpond leases or public land applications, if any there be, and to future survey, a certain parcel of the public domain comprising a portion of the foreshore fronting the Manila Bay along the province of Bulacan, Island of Luzon, which is particularly described as follows:

Bounded on the North by the claim and improved fishponds of Margarita Ignacio, et al. (Psu-9717), Perfecto and Isabel Sanchez (Rs-339), Maria L. de Lorenzo (Psu-140323) and (Psu-140622), Felipe Asuncion (Psu-118984), Guillermo V. Enriquez (Fla-227), Pastor Morales (Psu-ll4098 Lot 1), Felicisimo Cruz and Pastor Lalita (Psu-144037), Monica Santos (Psu-1144097 Lot 1), Felicidad Alvendia (Fla-1284), Anastacia F. Torres (Fla-1282), and Victoria Roxas (Psu-135369); on the East by the Bulacan River; on the South by Manila Bay; and on the West by Manila Bay, containing an approximate area of two thousand nine hundred fifty five (2,955) hectares.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this 4th day of October, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and fifty-seven and of the Independence of the Philippines, the twelfth.

CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
