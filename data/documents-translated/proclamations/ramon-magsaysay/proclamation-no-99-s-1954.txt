MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 99

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM DECEMBER 26 TO 31, 1954, AS PHILATELIC WEEK

In order to commemorate the centenary of the first postage stamp issued in the Philippines in 1854, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from December 26 to 31, 1954, as Philatelic Week.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-four, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the ninth.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
