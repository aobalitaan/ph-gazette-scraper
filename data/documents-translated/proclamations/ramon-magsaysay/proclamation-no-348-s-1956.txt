MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 348

DECLARING SATURDAY, SEPTEMBER 29, 1956, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LANAO AND IN THE CITIES OF ILIGAN AND MARAWI

WHEREAS, an agricultural exposition will be held in Iligan City beginning Saturday, September 29, 1956; and

WHEREAS, it is desirable that the people of the province of Lanao and of the cities of Iligan and Marawi be given an opportunity to witness the opening of said exposition;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Saturday, September 29 1956, as a special public holiday in the province of Lanao and in the cities of Iligan and Marawi.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
