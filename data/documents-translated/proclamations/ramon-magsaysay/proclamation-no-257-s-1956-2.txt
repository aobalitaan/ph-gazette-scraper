MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 257

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 798 DATED APRIL 29, 1935, A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITIES OF SILAY AND TALISAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL AND RESERVING THE SAME AS THE TUBERCULOSIS REST SETTLEMENT RESERVATION

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the provisions of section 1826 of the Revised Administrative Code and section 83 of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, I hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation No. 798 dated April 29, 1935, a parcel of the land embraced therein situated in the municipalities of Silay and Talisay, province of Negros Occidental, and reserve the same as the Tuberculosis Rest Settlement Reservation under the administration of the Secretary of Health, subject to private rights, if any there be, and final survey, and that the utilization and removal or disposal of the timber and other forest products therein shall be made in accordance with the Forest or Internal Revenue Laws or Regulations, which parcel of land is shown in the Bureau of Forestry Map CR-32, and technically described, to wit:

Beginning from corner 1, a miscellaneous specie tree 30 centimeters in diameter which is S. 45° E., and about 1,800 meters from barrio San Juan, Silay, Negros Occidental; thence S. 75° E., 370 meters to corner 2, a white lauan tree 30 centimeters in diameter; thence S. 45° E., 450 meters to corner 3. a bignay tree 20 centimeters in diameter; thence S. 31° E., 290 meters to corner 4, a guisok tree 30 centimeters in diameter; thence due east, 220 meters to corner 5, a white lauan tree 30 centimeters in diameter; thence N. 39° E., 180 meters to corner 6, a lanete tree 20 centimeters in diameter; thence N. 43° E,, 370 meters to comer 7, a tangili tree 30 centimeters in diameter; thence Ni. 21° E., 270 meters to corner 8, an almon tree 25 centimeters in diameter; thence following a creek downstream in a northeasterly direction about 130 meters to corner 9, a white lauan tree 30 centimeters in diameter, on the south bank of dry creek; thence folowing creek downstream in a southeasterly direction about 80 meters to corner 10, a point at the junction of the creek and Coyong Creek; thence following Coyong Creek upstream in a southeasterly, direction about 560 meters to corner 11, a white lauan tree .20 centimeters in diameter on the west bank of Coyong Creek; thence following Coyong Creek in a southeasterly direction about 300 meters to corner 12, a waterfall tree 25 centimeters in diameter at the junction of the creeks; thence following the Coyong Creek upstream in a southerly and southwesterly direction about 520 meters to corner 13, a loctob tree 50 centimeters in diameter; thence following Coyong Creek upstream in a southerly direction about 480 meters to corner 14, a waterfall tree 20 centimeters in diameter at the junction with a creek; thence following Coyong Creek upstream in southerly direction about 420 meters to corner 15, a loctob tree 15 centimeters in diameter on west bank of Dumalobdab Creek; thence S. 71° W., 100 meters to corner 16, a canariumi specie 40 centimeters in diameter; thence S. 75° W., 150 meters; to corner 17, a canarium 50 centimeters in diameter, at the junction of two trails; thence S. 67° W., 140 meters to comer 18, a red lauan tree 15 centimetres in diameter, at the junction of Malisbog River and a creek; thence following Malisbog River downstream in a northwesterly direction about 1,120 meters to corner 19, a eugenia specie 15 centimeters in diameter, at the junction of Malisbog River and Pulang-tubig Creek; thence following Malisbog River downstream in a northwesterly direction about 780 meters to corner 20, a hui tree 20 centimeters in diameter, on east bank of, Malisbog River; thence following Malisbog River downstream in a northwesterly direction about 820 meters containing an area of 201 hectares.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the tenth.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Acting Executive Secretary
