MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

RESERVING FOR THE USE OF THE PRICE STABILIZATION CORPORATION A CERTAIN PARCEL OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE GOVERNMENT, SITUATED IN THE CITY OF DAVAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the provisions of section 64 ( e ) of the Revised Administrative Code in relation to Republic Act No. 477, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for the use of the Price Stabilization Corporation, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of the private domain of the Government, situated in the City of Davao, and more, particularly described as follows:

“A land encompassed within Civil Reserve No. 111 of Davao, situated on the NE side of Magallanes Street, Townsite of Davao, District of Davao. Bounded on the NE by the property of Antonio Dy Chiao; on the SW by Magallanes Street; and on the NW by the property of Felipe Sandig. Starting from a point marked 1 on the plan, whose point is located N. 84° 18′ E., and forty-six meters with twenty-eight centimeters (46.28) from Mojon No. 10, Davao; and from said point 1 S. 52° E. twenty-eight meters with eighteen centimeters (28.18) to point 2; from this point S. 42° 03′ 0., thirty meters with ninety-nine centimeters (30.99) to point 3; from this point NW. 47° 52′ 0., twenty-seven meters with fifty-eight centimeters with ninety-six centimeters (28.96) to the point of origin; measuring a superficial area of 835 square meters. All points named are marked on the plan; the second orientation is true; the magnetic declination being 2° 34′ E.; and the date of the measurement June 1908.

By the President: (SGD.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
