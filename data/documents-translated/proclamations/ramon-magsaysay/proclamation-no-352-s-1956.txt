MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 352

DECLARING TUESDAY, OCTOBER 16, 1966, AS A SPECIAL PUBLIC HOLIDAY IN THE PROVINCES OF BUKIDNON, COTABATO, DAVAO, LANAO, PALAWAN, SULU, ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, AND ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR AND IN THE CITIES OF BASILAN, DAVAO, ILIGAN, MARAWI, AND ZAMBOANGA

On the occasion of the Muslim national feast of Mauledin Nabi, one of the most important holidays for Muslims, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the authority vested in me by section 30 of the Revised Administrative Code, do hereby declare Tuesday, October 16, 1956, as a special public holiday in the provinces of Bukidnon, Cotabato, Davao, Lanao, Palawan, Sulu, Zamboanga del Norte, and Zamboanga del Sur and in the cities of Basilan, Davao, Iligan, Marawi, and Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, find of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
