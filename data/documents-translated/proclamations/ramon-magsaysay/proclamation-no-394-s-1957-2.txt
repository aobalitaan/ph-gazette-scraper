MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 394

REVOKING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 20 DATED JUNE 2, 1918, WHICH RESERVED AND EXEMPTED FROM LEASE, SALE, AND OTHER DISPOSITION THE LOTS OF THE BANILAD FRIAR LANDS ESTATE, SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF MABOLO, MUNICIPALITY OF CEBU, NOW CITY OF CEBU, AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF THE FRIAR LANDS ACT

By virtue of the power vested in me by law, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Executive Order No. 20 dated June 2, 1918, and declare the lots embraced therein open to disposition under the provisions of the Friar Lands Act, Act 1120, and other applicable laws,

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
