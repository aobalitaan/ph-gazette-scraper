MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 357

DECLARING THE SECOND WEEK OF NOVEMBER OF EVERY YEAR AS PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION WEEK

In order to achieve further efficiency in the public service, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the second week of November of every year as Public Administration Week and designate the following committee to take charge of, and coordinate, all activities in the celebration of said week:

I hereby enjoin all offices in the national, provincial, city, and municipal governments, as well as all universities, colleges, and schools throughout the country, particularly the Institute of Public Administration of the University of the Philippines, to participate actively in the celebration by holding practical projects, programs, demonstrations, or similar activities with efficient, honest, and democratic public administration as the theme.

Emphasis should be laid on the rendition by government officials and employees of effective services to the public, without delay and without any discrimination whatsoever. Special attention should be focused on the improvement of government services to the masses of our people, particularly those in rural communities, in order to enhance their living conditions.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
