MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 355

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM NOVEMBER 19 TO 25, 1956, AS SPECIAL SCIENCE WEEK AND CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE OBSERVANCE THEREOF

In order to give impetus to scientific endeavors and thereby accelerate the full economic development of the country, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare the period from November 19 to 25, 1956, as National Science Week, to be observed throughout the country, particularly in schools, colleges, universities, scientific and technical institutions, and societies with appropriate ceremonies.

The following are hereby designated to compose a committee which is hereby created to take charge of the observance of National Science Week:

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-six, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
