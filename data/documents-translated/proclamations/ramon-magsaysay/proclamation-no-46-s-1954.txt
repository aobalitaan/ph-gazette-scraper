MALACAÑANG RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 46

RESERVING FOR THE USE OF THE CITY OF MANILA AS SITES FOR THE MEISIC POLICE STATION; THE JOSE ABAD SANTOS HIGH SCHOOL; THE MEISIC HEALTH CENTER; AND THE DISTRICT NUMBER TWO, DIVISION OF STREETS AND BRIDGES, OFFICE OF THE CITY ENGINEER, THE PROPERTY OTHERWISE KNOWN AS “CUARTEL MEISIC” SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF BINONDO, CITY OF MANILA.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, hereby excluded from sale or settlement and reserve for the use of the City of Manila as sites for the Meisic Police Station; the Jose Abad Santos High School; the Meisic Health Center; and the District Number Two, Division of Streets and Bridges, Office of the City Engineer, subject to private rights, if any there be, the property otherwise known as “Cuartel Meisic”, situated in the District of Binondo, City of Manila.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-four, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the ninth.

(SGD.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: (SGD.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
