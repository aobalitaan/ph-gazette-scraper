MALACAÑANG

RESIDENCE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 181

FIXING SEPTEMBER 5, 1955, FOR THE ORGANIZATION OF THE CITY OF TRECE MARTIRES

Pursuant to the authority conferred upon me by section 42 of Republic Act No. 981, creating the City of Trece Martires, I, Ramon Magsaysay, President of the Philippines, do hereby fix September 5, 1955, for the organization of the Government of the City of Trece Martires.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-five, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the tenth.

RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President: FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
