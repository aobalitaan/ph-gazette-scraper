MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 351

RESERVING FOR FOREST STATION SITE PURPOSES AND DESIGNATING AS LALAWIGAN FOREST STATION SITE RESERVATION THE PARCEL OF PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DAET, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of .Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, known as “The Public Land Act,” I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, hereby reserve for forest station site purposes, and withdraw from ‘ sale, settlement, or other disposition, subject to private rights, if any there be, that portion of the public domain, situated in the municipality of Daet, Province of Camarines Norte, Island of Luzon, and described in the Bureau of Forestry map F. R.-88, to wit:

Beginning at corner marked 1 on Bureau of Forestry map No. F. W.-88, a camagon tree, 38 cm. diameter, marked 12/1 at edge of swamp, which is S. 76° W., about 540 meters, more or less, from barrio of Lalawigan, municipality of Daet, Province of Camarines Norte; thence S. 63° W., 205 meters to corner 2, malaikmo tree, 38 cm. diameter, marked 12/2 on east bank on dry creek; thence N. 57° W., 78 meters to corner 3, a stump of white lauan tree, 90 cm. diameter, marked 12/3, identical to corner 9 of H. No. 146630; thence N. 8° E., 74 meters to corner 4, a white lauan tree, 40 cm. in diameter, marked 12/4, identical to corner 1 of H. No. 146630; thence N. 52° E., 206 meters to corner 5, a bagabolo tree, 30 cm. diameter marked 12/5, identical to corner 2, of H. No. 466630; thence following a dry creek in a general southeasterly direction, about 134 meters to corner 6, a lauan tree, 90 cm. diameter, marked F. Z. 12/7, identical to corner 6, block IV, alienable and disposable, Camarines Norte project 7-B; and thence S. 11° W., 54 meters to corner 1, the point of beginning.

Corners 1 to 5 were marked with the Bureau of Forestry official marking hatchet No. 485.

Containing an area of approximately 3.6 hectares.

The said parcel of land shall be known as the Lalawigan Forest Station Site Reservation of the Bureau of Forestry.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twelfth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
