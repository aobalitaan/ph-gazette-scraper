MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 575

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF ANTATET, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipal district of Antatet, Province of Isabela, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16487 (Psu-96845), to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on the Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16487, S. 74° 19′ E, 63.79 m. from Post. No. 6, Isabela Estate, thence N. 0° 01′ E., 100.00 m. to point 2; thence S. 89° 59′ E., 200.00 m. to point 3; thence . 0 W„ 100.00 m. to point 4; thence S. 0° 01′ W, 100 00 m. to point 5; thence N. 89° 59′ W., 200.00 m. to point 6; thence N. 0° 01′ E., 100.00 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 40,000 square meters.

All corners are P. L. S. concrete monuments

Bounded on the north by property of Custodio Labog, on the east, by properties of Custodio Labog and Emilio Cabauatan; on the south, by property of Emilio Cabauatan and on the west, by properties of Emilio Cabauatan and Custodio Labog.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 20′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16487 (Psu-96845).

Surveyed: January 19, 1933.

Approved: June 13, 1933.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighth day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
