MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 421

RESERVING FOR PLANT PROPAGATION STATION PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF DIGOS, MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA CRUZ, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for plaint propagation station purposes, under the administration of the Director of Plant Industry, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Digos, municipality of Santa Cruz, Province of Davao, Island of Mindanao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Ir-458, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-458, S. 77° 14′ W. 1,258.89 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 62, Santa Cruz cadastre No. 275, thence N. 42° 57′ E. 251.16 m. to point 2; N. 23° 55′ E. 7.84 m. to point 3; N. 42° 59′ E. 247.68 m. to point 4; N. 41° 41′ E. 58.74 m. to point 5; N. 42° 45′ E. 449.98 m. to point 6; S. 47° 06′ E. 400.68 m. to point 7; S. 42° 24′ W. 2521.10 m. to point 8; S. 42° 32′ W. 249.84 m. to point 9; S. 42° 20′ W. 250.48 m. to point 10; S. 41° 48′ W. 262.51 m. to point 11; N. 46° 26′ W. 205.67 m. to point 12; N. 47° 49′ W. 201.98 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 408,687 square meters.

Point 5, galvanized iron spike on tree; and the rest are B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast by Davao-Cotabato national highway; on the southeast, by road; on the southwest, by lots Nos. 2384 and 2383; and on the northwest, by lot No. 2399, road and lots Nos. 2411 and 2491.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 50′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-458.

Surveyed: March 31, April 5 and 7, and May 27, 1937.

Approved: April 25, 1939.

NOTE.—This is advance plan of lot No. 2400 of Santa Cruz cadastre No. 275.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-fourth day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
