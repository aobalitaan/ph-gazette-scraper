MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 526

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES THREE PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF OSORIO, SUMARAY AND PANPANAN, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN REMIGIO, PROVINCE OF ANTIQUE, ISLAND OF PANAY.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Osorio, Sumaray and Panpanan, municipality of San Remigio, Province of Antique, island of Panay, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-407, to wit:

Lot No. 1 (Bo. of Osorio). —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-407, S. 88° 57′ E. 7,928.17 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Mp. of San Remigio. thence N. 55° 38′ E. 122.30 m. to point 2; S. 34° 37′ E. 247.71 m. to point 3; S. 49° 23′ W. 98.97 m. to point 4; N. 39° 54′ W. 259.74 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 28,003 square meters.

All corners are B. L. concrete monuments 15 x 60 cm.

Bounded on the northeast by property of Telesforo Mariano; on the southeast, by property of Gregorio Rafael; on the southwest, by property of the municipal government of San Remigio; and on the northwest, by property of Juan Alejandro.

Bearings true. Declination 1° 36′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-407, sheet No. 1

Surveyed: February 20, 1939.

Approved: April 17, 1939.

The reservation of the sites in the barrio3 of Sumaray and Panpanan is subject further to the condition that any time that the said sites are abandoned for school purposes, the same shall revert to the category of public forest.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twelfth day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
