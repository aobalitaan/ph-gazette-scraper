PROCLAMATION NO. 271

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 217, SERIES OF 1929, A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN THE BARRIO OF TALNAG, MUNICIPALITY OF ITOGON, SUBPROVINCE OF BENGUET, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section fourteen of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred thirty-seven, I hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation Numbered Two hundred seventeen, series of nineteen hundred and twenty-nine, the following-described parcel of land situated in the barrio of Talnag, municipality of Itogon, subprovince of Benguet, Mountain Province, to wit:

“A parcel of land with the buildings and improvements thereon situated in the municipality of Itogon; bounded on the N. by the Tuban Mining Co.; on the E. by the Old Times Group; on the S. and W. by the Banao Group of C. P. Hann, Beginning at a point marked “1” on the plan, being S. 41° 24’ E.; 14,444 m. more or less, from Santo Tomas Triangulation Station; thence due E. 600 m. to point “2”; thence due N. 270.89 m. to point “3”; thence N. 28° 00’ E. 372.74 m. to point ‘4’; thence due E. 1,025.00 m. to point ‘5’; thence due S. 1,200 m. to point ‘6’; thence due W. 1,800 m. to point ‘7’; thence due N. 600 m. to point of beginning; containing an area of one million seven hundred seventy-one thousand two hundred three (1,771,203) sq. m., more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and the ground, points ‘1’ to ‘7’ inclusive, are marked by concrete monuments each 15 by 15 cm. on each side and 60 cm. long; bearings true, declination 0° 45’ E.; date of survey, April 26-30, 1937, by Demetrio Resurreccion.”

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this nineteenth day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonweatlh of the Philippines, the third.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines

By the President:

Secretary to the President
