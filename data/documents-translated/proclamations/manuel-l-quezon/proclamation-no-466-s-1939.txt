MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 466

RESERVING FOR RAILROAD PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CATABANGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF RAGAY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for railroad purposes, under the administration of the Manila Railroad Company, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Catabangan, municipality of Ragay, Province of Camarines Sur, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-104315, to wit:

Lot No. 1. —Beginning at a point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-104315, being N. 13° 15′ E„ 2,872.75 meters, more or less, from B. L. B. M. No., 1, barrio of Catabangan, municipality of Ragay, thence S. 21″ 39′ E., 249.50 m. to point “2;” S. 68° 22′ W., 15.00 m. to point “3;” N. 21° 40′ W., 249.52 m. to point “4;” N. 21° 38′ W., 249.49 m. to point “5;” N. 22° 24′ W-, 11.21 m. to point “6;” N. 24° 08′ W., 18.70 m. to point “7;” N. 64° 40′ E., 15.00 m. to point “8;” S. 24° 08′ E., 19.32 m. to point “9;” S. 22° 21′ E., 11.57 m. to point “10;” S. 21° 39′ E., 249.50 m. to point “1,” point of beginning.

Containing an area of seven thousand nine hundred fifty-two (7,952) square meters, more or less.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, point “1” is marked by old P. L. S. marked on stone, 60 by 25 cm., 20 cm. above ground; points “2” and “10)” by old P. L. S./M. E. concrete monuments, 18 cm. diameter- by 72 cm., points “3” to “7,” inclusive, by P. L. S./M. R. concrete monuments 15 cm. diameter by 60 cm.; and points “8” and “9” by old stakes.

Bounded on the NE., by lot No. 3-New of plan Psu-92760-Amd.; and on the SE., SW., and NW., by property of Juan Lagao, S. A. No. 13396 (not yet surveyed).

Lot No. 2. —Beginning at a point marked “1” on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-104315, being N. 13° 45′ E., 2,890.20 meters, more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of Catabangan, municipality of Ragay, thence N. 21° 39′ W., 249.50 m. to point “2;” N. 22° 23′ W., 12.29 m. to point “3;” N. 24° 08′ W., 20.52 m. to point “4;” N. 64° 43′ E., 15.01 m. to point “5;” S. 24° 06′ E., 21.13 m. to point “6;” S. 22° 23′ E., 12.63 m. to point “7;” S. 21° 39′ E-, 249.51 m. to point “8;” S. 21° 39′ E., 249.49 m. to point “9;” S. 68° 22′ W., 15.00 m. to point “10;” N. 21′ 39′ W., 249.50 m. to point “1,” point of beginning.

Containing an area of seven thousand nine hundred eighty-two (7,982) square meters, more or less.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “1,” “2,” and “10” are marked by old P. L. S./M. R. concrete monuments, 18 cm. diameter by 72 cm.; points “3” and “4” by old stakes; and points “5” to “9,” inclusive, by P. L. S./M. R. concrete monuments, 15 cm. diameter by 60 cm.

Bounded on the NE., SE., and NW., by property of Juan Lagao, S. A. No. 13396 (not yet surveyed); and on the SW., by lot No. 3-New of plan Psu-92760-Amd.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 54′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-104315.

Surveyed: March 17-18 and October 1, 1937.

Approved: December 27, 1938.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this ninth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
