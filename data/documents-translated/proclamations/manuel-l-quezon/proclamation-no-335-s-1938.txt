MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 335

RESERVING FOR PLAZA PURPOSES THREE PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF ILOILO, ISLAND OF PANAY.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three or Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred

and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for plaza purposes, under the administration of the City of Iloilo, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcels of the public domain, situated in the City of Iloilo, Island of Panay, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plans Mr-295 and Swo-15270, to wit:

Lot No. 1. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-295, S. 75° 56′ E. 528.89 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Iloilo cadastre, thence N. 44° 31′ W.

122.22 m. to point 2; N. 45° 50′ E. 87.24 m. to point 3; S. 40° 29′ E. 124.52 m. to point 4; S. 47° 17′ W. 78.51 m to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1.0215 hectares.

Bounded on the northeast, southeast and southwest, by road (no name); and on the northwest, by Calle Progreso.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 25′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-295, sheet No. 1

Surveyed: October, 1911-August, 1912.

Approved: November 22, 1933.

NOTE.—This is lot No. 1079 of Iloilo cadastre No. 23, case No. 4

All corners are B. L. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cms.

Lot No. 1356. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15270, N. 24° 57′ W. 88.89 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Iloilo, thence N. 62° 47’ E. 72.94 m. to point 2; S. 26° 18′ E. 76.74 m. to point 3; S. 55° 25′ W. 20.47 m. to point 4; S. 55° 25′ W. 43.30 m to point 5; N. 32° 55′ W. 85.35 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 5,519 square meters.

All corners are stakes. Bounded on the northeast by Calle San Vicente; on the southeast, by lots Nos. 1887 and 1355; on the southwest, by Calle Antigua; and on the northwest, by Calle San Marcos.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 40’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15270.

Surveyed: November, 1911-June, 1912.

Approved: February 3, 193

NOTE:—This survey was formerly in the barrio of Molo, municipality of Iloilo.

No. 1, Agonoa cadastre, thence S. 34° 05’ W. 141.33 m. to point 2; N. 44° 56′ W. 59.58 m. to point 3; N. 41° 39′ E. 137.35 m. to point 4; S. 47° 13′ E. 40.88 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 0.6940 hectare.

Point 1, B. L. concrete monument; point 2, B. L. L. M

No. 1; points 3 and 4, stakes.

Bounded on the northeast by Calle Rizal; on the southeast, by Calle Cementerio; on the southwest, by Calle Guzman; and on the northwest, by road (no name).

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 25

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-295, sheet No. 3

Surveyed: During 1911 to 1912.

Approved: November 22, 1933.

NOTE.—This is lot No. 2574 of Agonoa cadastre No. 8, case No. 1

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighteenth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines
