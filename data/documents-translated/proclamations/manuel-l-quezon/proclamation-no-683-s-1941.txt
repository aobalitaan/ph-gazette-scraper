MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 683

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF IMPIG, MUNICIPALITY OF SIPOCOT, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Impig, municipality of Sipocot, Province of Camarines Sur, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-440, to wit:

Mr-440 .—Beginning at point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-440, N. 5° 37′ E. 1,215.31 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Sipocot, thence S. 75° 46′ E. 212.34 m. to point 2; S. 7° 53′ W. 73.32 m. to point 3; S. 29° 08′ W. 45.66 m. to point 4; S. 56° 16′ W. 43.66 m. to point 5; S. 48° 23′ W. 70.36 m. to point 6; S. 41° 56′ W. 56.96 m. to point 7; N. 59° 53′ W. 21.88 m. to point 8; N. 62° 34′ W. 19.40 m. to point 9; N. 65° 27’ W. 22.09 m. to point 10; N. 69 ° 25′ W. 20.90 m. to point 11: N. 72° 37′ W. 21.22 m. to point 12; N. 76° 2, W. 22.53 m. to point 13; N. 17° 09′ E. 238.84 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of forty-six thousand nine hundred and sixty-three (46,963) square meters, more or less.

Points 1 and 3, B. L. concrete monuments; points 2, 4, 5 and 6, B. L. stone monuments; and the rest are old stakes.

Bounded on the northeast and southeast by public land; on the southwest, by lot No. 4 of plan Psu-61444; and on the northwest, by public land.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 33′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-440.

Surveyed: April 3, 1940.

Approved: October 31, 1940.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this seventeenth day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
