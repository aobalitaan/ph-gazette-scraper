PROCLAMATION NO. 22

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL BUILDING SITE A PARCEL OF LAND IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GONZAGA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-one of Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and seventy-four, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for municipal council of Gonzaga, Cagayan, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the Municipality of Gonzaga, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon, and more particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-12999, to wit:

Lot No. 2.—Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo—12999, S. 19ْ 14’ E. 30.11 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Gonzaga, Cagayan, thence N. 72ْ 04’ E. 68.77 m. to point 2; S. 23ْ 40’ E. 139.84 m. to point 3; S. 66ْ 06’ W. 67.16 m. to point 4; N. 24ْ 10’ W. 146.99 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 9,720 square meters.

Point 1, 2, 3, and 4, B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast, by Calle Bonifacio; on the southeast, by property of Mariano Enong; on the southwest, by Calle Mabini; and on the northwest, by lot No. 1.

Bearing true. Declination, 0ْ 12’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-12999.

Surveyed, November 24, 1920. Approved, January 23, 1933.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this tenth day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
