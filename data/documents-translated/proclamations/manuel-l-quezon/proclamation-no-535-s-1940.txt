MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 535

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 769, DATED FEBRUARY 9, 1935

By virtue of the powers conferred upon me by law, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, hereby revoke Proclamation Numbered Seven hundred and sixty-nine, dated February ninth, nineteen hundred and thirty-five.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed

Done at the City of Manila, this first day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
