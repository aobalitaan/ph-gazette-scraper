PROCLAMATION NO. 48

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF LAND IN THE BARRIO OF BATO-BATO, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BALIMBING, PROVINCE OF SULU, ISLAND OF TAWI-TAWI.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-one of Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and seventy-four, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcels of the public domain, situated in the Barrio of Bato-bato, Municipal District of Balimbing, Province of Sulu, Island of Tawi-Tawi, and more particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Pr-214, to wit:

Lot No. 1.—Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-214, S. 35ْ 07’ W. 7,054 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 2, municipal district of Balimbing, thence N. 88ْ 11’ W. 24.71 m. to point 2; N. 6ْ 05’ E. 25.09 m. to point 3; S. 88ْ 10’ E. 23.06 m. to point 4; S. 2ْ 19’ W. 25.01 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 598 square meters.

Bounded on the north, by a street; on the east, by lot No. 1; on the south, by lot No. 4 of plan Ts-66; and on the west, by lot No. 7 of plan Ts-66.

Bearings true. Declination, 2ْ 15’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-214.

Surveyed, September 13, 1930.

Approved, November 11, 1935.

NOTE.—Lot No. 1 = Lot No. 5 of Bato-bato townsite, Ts-66.

Lot No. 2 = Lot No. 6 of Bato-bato townsite, Ts-66.

All corners are P. L. S. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 centimeters.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fifth day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
