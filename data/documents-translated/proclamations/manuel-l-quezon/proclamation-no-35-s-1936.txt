PROCLAMATION NO. 35

DECLARING OPEN TO DISPOSITION ALL LANDS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN COVERED BY PUBLIC LAND APPLICATIONS RECEIVED AND ACCEPTED IN THE BUREAU OF LANDS ON OR BEFORE DECEMBER 31, 1935.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of sections seven and eight of the Public Land Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and seventy-four, as amended, I hereby declare, subject to future delimitation and survey and to private rights, if any there be, open to concession or disposition through homestead, sale, and lease, all lands of the public domain covered by public land applications received and accepted in the Bureau of Lands on or before December thirty-first, nineteen hundred and thirty-five.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fifth day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines

By the President:

Secretary of the Interior
