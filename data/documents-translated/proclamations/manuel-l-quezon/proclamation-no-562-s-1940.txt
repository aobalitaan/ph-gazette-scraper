MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 562

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL AND MUNICIPAL BUILDING SITE PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BAKUN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.
