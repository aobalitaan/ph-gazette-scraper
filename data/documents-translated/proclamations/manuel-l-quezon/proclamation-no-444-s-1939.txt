MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 444

RESERVING FOR CEMETERY PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CULAO, MUNICIPALITY OF DINGRAS, PROVINCE OF ILOCOS NORTE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for cemetery purposes, under the administration of the Director of Health, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Culao, municipality of Dingras, Province of Ilocos Norte, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-377, to wit:

Lot No. 1. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-377, S. 18° 12′ E. 7,192.33 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of Lanas, municipality of Dingras, thence N. 73° 38′ W. 100.00 m. to point 2; N. 16° 22′ E. 100.00 m. to point 3; S. 73° 38′ E. 100.00 m. to point 4; S. 16° 22′ W. 100.00 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 10,001 square meters.

All corners are B. L. stone monuments.

Bounded on the northeast, southeast, southwest, and northwest, by lot No. 2.

Lot No. 2. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-377, S. 18″ 18′ E. 7,261.67 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of Lanas, municipality of Dingras, thence N. 73° 38′ W. 200.00 m. to point 2; N. 16° 22′ E. 200.00 m. to point 3; S. 73° 38′ E. 200.00 m. to point 4; S. 61° 22′ W. 70.71 m. to point 5; N. 73° 38′ W. 100.00 m. to point 6; S. 16° 22′ W. 100.00 m. to point 7; S. 73° 38′ E. 100.00 m. to point 8; N. 16° 22′ E. 100.00 m. to point 9; N. 61° 22′ E. 70.71 m. to point 10; S. 16° 22′ W. 200.00 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 30,002 square meters.

Points 1, 2, 3, 4, and 10, crosses on stone monuments; points 5, 6, 7, 8, and 9, B. L. stone monuments.

Bounded on the northeast, southeast, southwest, and northwest, by property of Fabian Bayudan. Lot No. 1 is entirely surrounded by this lot.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 13′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-377.

Surveyed: January 11, 1939.

Approved: June 28, 1939.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this thirtieth day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
