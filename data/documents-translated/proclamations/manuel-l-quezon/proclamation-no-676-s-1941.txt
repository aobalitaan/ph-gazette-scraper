RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE COMMONWEALTH OF THE PHILIPPINES SITUATED AT PALOMAR PARK, CITY OF MANILA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Pursuant to the provision of section sixty-four, paragraph (e), of Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and eleven, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site and purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the private domain situated at Palomar Park, City of Manila, Island of Luzon, and particularly described as follows:

Parcel of land (City Stable No. 2), without the buildings existing therein belonging to the City of Manila, situated on the SE line of Calle Reina Regente, corner Calle Azcarraga, District of Tondo. Bounded on the NE by property of Haussermann, Cohn and Fisher; on the SE by the Madalena stream; on the SW by Calle Azcarraga; and on the NW by Calle Reina Regente. Starting from a point marked 1 on the plan, whose point is located at S. 87° 25’ E., and eight hundred fifty-one meters and fifty centimeters (851.50) from the dome of the Tondo Church; and from said point 1 S. 68° 16’ E. 186.53 meters to point 6; from here S. 65° 32’ W., 41.06 meters to point 7; from here S. 75° 20’ W., 4.90 meters to point 8; from here N. 70° 13’ W., 13.00 meters to point 9; from here N. 49° 22’ W., 33.34 meters to point 10; from here N. 65° 26’ W., 44.83 meters to point 11; from here N. 82° 52’ W., 1.61 meters to point 12; from here S. 36° 16’ W., 3.96 meters to point 13; from here N. 52° 25’ E., 3.34 meters to point 14; from here S. 64° 03’ E., 43.32 meters to point 15; from here S. 50° 20’ E., 29.74 meters to point 16; from here S. 13° 44’ E., 5.64 meters to point 17; from here S. 20° 34’ W., 30.79 meters to point 18; from here N. 63° 26’ W., 6.17 meters to point 19; from here N. 57° 48’ W., 28.15 meters to point 20; from here N. 30° 41’ E., 0.69 meters to point 21; from here N. 58° 30’ W., 2.03 meters to point 22; from here N. 52° 21’ W., 6.14 meters to point 23; from here N. 67° 57’ W., 56.41 meters to point 24; from here N. 49° 11’ W., 1.16 meters to point 25; from here N. 7° 15’ W., 1.19 meters to point 26; from here N. 18° 09’ E., 71.49 meters to point 27; from here N. 18° 20’ E., 89.89 meters to the point of beginning; measuring a superficial area of twenty-four thousand three hundred seventy-eight square meters (24,378.00), more or less. Points 1, 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 18, 19, 22, 23, 24, 25 and 26 are determined by nails and points 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 15, 16 and 17 by stakes; the orientation followed is true, the magnetic declination being 0° 51’ E.; and the date of the measurement, July 17, 1911.

Done at the City of Manila, this thirty-first day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON
President of the Philippines
