MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 336

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES THREE PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF CENTRAL, TUBIG, AND PANACAN, MUNICIPALITY OF ABORLAN, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Central, Tubig, and Panacan, municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, Island of Palawan, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psm-492, to wit:

Lot No. 1 (barrio of Central). —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psm-492, N. 35° 46′ W. 472.09 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Aborlan, Palawan, thence S. 6° 41′ E. 27.78 m. to point 2; S. 6° 20′ E. 31.31 m. to point 3; S. 7° 58′ E. 43.89 m. to point 4; S. 75° 29′ W. 18.20 m. to point 5; S. 11° 58′ W. 22.87 m. to point 6; S. 5° 12′ E. 28.05 m. to point 7; S. 85° 58′ W. 78.84 m. to point 8; N. 85° 52′ W. 6.25 m. to point 9; N. 0° 32′ E. 67.97 m. to point 10; N. 13° 50′ E. 43 01 m. to point 11; S. 25° 25′ E. 53.49 m. to point 12; S. 85° 25′ E. 8.88 m. to point 13; N. 84° 26′ E. 49.44 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 13,292 square meters.

Points 9, 10, and 11, stakes; point 12, tree.

Bounded on the north by property of Palwas (Tagbanua), on the east, by properties of Masiad (Tagbanua), Licwas (Tagbanua), Opsa (Tagbanua), Buyon (Tagbanua), and Alice; on the south, by property of Leonora Badilla; and on the west and northwest, by Tagbuaya Creek.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 39′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psm-492, sheet No. 1.

Surveyed: February 19-20, 1930.

Approved: March 3, 1931.

NOTE.—All corners not otherwise described are B. L. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cms.

Lot No. 2 (barrio of Tubig). — Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psm-492, N. 44° 50’ E. 3,220.31 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Aborlan, Palawan, thence N. 48° 43′ W. 100.05 m. to point 2; N. 41° 17′ S. 149.99 m. to point 3; S. 48° 43′ E. 100.02 m. to point 4; S. 41° 17′ W. 150.02 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 15,005 square meters.

Points 1, 2, 3, and 4, B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on all sides by public land.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 39’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psm-492, sheet No. 2

Surveyed: February 21, 24, and 25, 1930.

Approved: March 3, 1931.

Lot No. S (barrio of Panacan). —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psm-492, which is identical with B. L. B. M. No. 2, barrio of Panacan, thence S. 1° 24′ W. 18.47 m. to point 2; S. 3° 07′ W. 85.19 m. to point 3: S. 86° 11′ W. 104.99 m. to point 4; N, 2° 44’ E. 108.46 m. to point 5; N. 88° 49′ E. 104.71 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 11,093 square meters. Points 2, 3, 4, and 5, B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north by property of Esteban Ramos; on the east, by properties of Felix Vitin and Julian Sedores; on the south, by barrio street; and on the west, by property of Marcelo Magbanua.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 39′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psm-492, sheet No. 3

Surveyed: February 26, 1930 Approved: March 3, 1931.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighteenth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
