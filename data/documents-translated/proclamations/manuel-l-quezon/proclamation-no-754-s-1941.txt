MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 754

ESTABLISHING AS EUCALYPTUS FOREST RESERVE FOR FOREST PROTECTION AND CONSERVATION OF AMAMANIT TREES ( Eucalyptus deglupta, Blume), A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF LEBAK, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, PHILIPPINES.

Upon recommendation of the Director of Forestry, approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, and pursuant to the provisions of section eighteen hundred and twenty-six of Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and eleven, known as the Revised Administrative Code, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, hereby establish for forest protection and conservation of amamanit trees (Eucalyptus deglupta, Blume), the Eucalyptus Forest Reserve and place it under the administration and control of the Bureau of Forestry, which shall have the authority to regulate the use and occupancy of this forest reserve and the cutting, collection, and removal of amamanit trees (Eucalyptus deglupta, Blume) and other forest products therein in accordance with the Forest Law and Regulations.

I, therefore, withdraw from entry, sale or settlement, and hereby declare and proclaim to be the Eucalyptus Forest Reserve, subject to private rights, if any there be, that portion of the public domain situated in the municipal district of Lebak, Province of Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, Philippines, and described on Bureau of Forestry map No. F.
