MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 657

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF ARELLANO, MUNICIPALITY OF SURIGAO, PROVINCE OF SURIGAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school site purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Arellano, municipality of Surigao, Province of Surigao, Island of Mindanao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16697, to wit:

Lot No. 2449. — Beginning at point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16697, N. 55° 50’ E. 640.78 m, more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 116, Surigao Cad. No. 234, thence N. 73° 33′ W. 51.04 m. to point 2; N. 19° 27′ E. 92.20 m. to point 3; S. 67° 26′ E. 58.14 m. to point 4; S. 43° 42 E. 6.24 m. to to point 5; S. 1° 00′ W. 6.90 m. to point 6; S. 30° 21’ W. 78.43 m. to point 1; point of beginning.

Containing an area of five thousand two hundred fifty-two (5,252) square meters.

Point 5, tree; and the rest are B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast by lot No. 2450; on the east and southeast, by lot No. 2447; on the southwest, by lot No. 2448; and on the northwest, by lot No. 2453.

Bearings true. Declination 1° 50’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16697.

Surveyed: October 7, 1928.

Approved: October 12, 1931.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-third day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
