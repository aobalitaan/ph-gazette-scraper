MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 524

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 266, DATED OCTOBER 11, 1929, AND RESERVING THE PARCELS OF LAND EMBRACED THEREIN FOR THE USE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE AND COMMERCE.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby revoke Proclamation Numbered Two hundred and sixty-six dated October eleven, nineteen hundred and twenty-nine, and reserve the parcels of land embraced therein for the use of the Department of Agriculture and Commerce.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twelfth day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
