MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 412

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 352, DATED DECEMBER 12, 1938, RESERVING FOR WAREHOUSE PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF DANSALAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, Proclamation Numbered Three hundred fifty-two, dated December twelfth, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, reserving for warehouse purposes two parcels of the public domain situated in the barrio of Poblacion, municipality of Dansalan, Province of Lanao, Island of Mindanao, is hereby amended by transferring the administration of said reservation from the National Rice and Corn Corporation to the National Warehousing Corporation.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-third day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
