MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 420

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 274, DATED NOVEMBER 22, 1929, AND RESERVING THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby revoke Proclamation Numbered Two hundred and seventy-four, dated November twenty-second, nineteen hundred and twenty-nine, and reserve the land embraced therein for school purposes under the administration of the Director of Education.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-fourth day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
