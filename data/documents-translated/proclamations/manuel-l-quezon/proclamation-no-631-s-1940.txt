MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 631

RESERVING FOR RAILROAD PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF SAN RAFAEL, MUNICIPALITY OF ATIMONAN, PROVINCE OF TAYABAS, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for railroad purposes, under the administration of the Manila Railroad Company, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of San Rafael, municipality of Atimonan, Province of Tayabas, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psd-16050, to wit:

Lot No. 8816-A. —A parcel of land (lot No. 8816-A of the subdivision plan Psd-16050, being a portion of lot No. 8816 of the cadastral survey of Atimonan, G. L. R. O. Cad. Record No. 1019), situated in the barrio of San Rafael, municipality of Atimonan, Province of Tayabas. Bounded on the NE. and E., by lot No. 8816-B of the subdivision plan; on the SE., by lot No. 8813-A of the subdivision plan; on the SW., by lot No. 9563, creek and lot No. 9564; and on the W., and NW., by lot No. 9564. No. 14 of Atimonan cadastre; thence S. 1° 30′ W., 17.64 m. to point “2”; thence S. 0° 42′ E., 19.46 m. to point “3”; thence S. 2° 58′ E., 19.33 m. to point “4”; thence S. 5° 16′ E., 19.47 m. to point “5”; thence S. 7° 33′ E., 19.33 m. to point “6”; thence S. 9° 51′ E., 19.46 m. to point “7”; thence S. 12° 07′ E., 19.33 m. to point “8”; thence S. 14° 35. In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
