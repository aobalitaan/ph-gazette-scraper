MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 651

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO (2) PARCELS OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN OF THE COMMONWEALTH OF THE PHILIPPINES SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF PACO, CITY OF MANILA, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section sixty-four paragraph ( e ) of Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and eleven, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the private domain, situated in the District of Paco, City of Manila, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16600, to wit:

Lot No. 3. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16600, N. 59° 41′ W. 43.92 m from F. Hyd. No. 224, City of Manila and N. 36° 52′ W. 113.32 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 44, Manila Cad. No. 13, thence N. 4° 36′ W. 7.29 m. to point 2; N. 86° 10′ E. 1.79 m. to point 3; S. 6° 32′ E. 7.65 m. to point 4; S. 85° 32′ W. 2.06 m. to point 5; N. 4° 38′ W. 0.37 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 14.7 square meters.

Points 1 and 5, G. I. S. on trees; points 2, 3 and 4, P. L. S./B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north by lot No. 7; on the east, by lot No. 11; on the south, by lots Nos. 11 and 2; and on the west, by lot No. 6.

Lot No. 7. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16600, N. 52° 35′ W. 48.46 m. from F. Hyd. No. 224, City of Manila and N. 35° 00′ W. 119.55 m„ more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 44, Manila Cad. No. 13, thence N. 4° 36′ W. 11.27 m. to point 2; N. 82° 12′ E. 0.96 m. to point 3; N. 67° 07′ E. 0.49 m. to point 4; S. 6° 30′ E. 11.50 m. to point 5; S. 86° 10′ W. 1.79 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 18.2 square meters.

Points 1 and 5, P. L. S./B. L. concrete monuments; point 2, G. I. S. on face of Pltr.; points 3 and 4, stakes.

Bounded on the north, by lot No. 4; on the east, by lot No. 12; on the south, by lot No. 3; and on the west, by lot No. 6.

Bearings true. Declination variable.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-16600.

Surveyed: July 28-September 21, 1918. Approved: March 29, 1921.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fourth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
