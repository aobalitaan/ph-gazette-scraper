MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 371

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF NAGA, MUNICIPALITY OF KABASALAN, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Naga, municipality of Kabasalan, Province of Zamboanga, Island of Mindanao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14197, to wit:

A parcel of land (the original plain Psu-87187), situated in the barrio of Naga, municipal district of Kabasalan, Province of Zamboanga. Bounded on the N. by property of Jose M. Savillo; on the E., by property of Ismael (Moro); on the SE., by the seashore of Sibuguey Bay; and on the W., by property of Imam Arani (Moro) and the proposed municipal government site. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being identical with B. L. M. No. 1, barrio of Naga, municipal district of Kabasalan, thence N. 6° 00′ E., 61.08 m. to point “2;” thence S. 82° 02′ E., 186.29 m. to point “3;” thence S. 88° 45′ E., 293.00 m. to point “4;” thence S. 9° 07′ E., 350.64 m. to point “5;” thence S. 12° 55′ E., 151.18 m. to point “6;” thence S. 71° 04′ W., 197.48 m. to point “7;” thence S. 22° 36′ W., 129.63 m. to point “8;” thence S. 63° 22′ W., 150.08 m. to point “9;” thence N. 21° 55′ W., 253.72 m. to point “10;” thence N. 10° 39′ “W., 379.12 m. to point “11;” thence N. 19° 10′ W., 114.46 m. to the point, of beginning; containing an area of two hundred ninety thousand one hundred thirty-nine (290,139) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground point “1” is marked by B. L. M. No. 1; point “2” by B. L. M. No. 2; point “3” by P. L. S. cylindrical concrete monument; points “4,” “5,” “7,” “8,” “10,” and “11” by galvanized iron spikes in crosses cut on trees; and points “6” and “9” by P. L. S. cylindrical concrete monuments; bearings true; declination, 1″ 47′ E.; date of the original survey, January 29-31, 1931, and that of the approval, July 23, 1931.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-third day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
