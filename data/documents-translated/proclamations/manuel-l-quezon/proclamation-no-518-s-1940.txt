MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 518

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF CONNER, SUBPROVINCE OF APAYAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipal district of Conner, subprovince ofApayao, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-106225, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-106225, N. 86° 37′ E. 12,331.11 m., more or less, from Triangulation Station, Mt. Calauag, B. L. Mp. of Conner, thence N. 22° 51′ E. 50.77 m. to point 2; S. 72° 18’ E. 249.66 m. to point 3; S. 20° 09’ W. 10.33 m. to point 4; S. 71° 68′ E. 105.20 m. to point 5; S. 2° 45′ E. 116.86 m. to point 6; S. 70° 18′ E. 46.42 m. to point 7; S. 44° 00′ W. 155.36 m. to point 8; N. 42° 42′ W. 154.84 m. to point 9; N. 48° 13′ E. 56.92 m. to point 10; N. 47° 14′ W. 111.06 m. to point 11; N. 59° 43′ W. 175.25 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 52, 275 square meters.

Points 1 and 9, P. L. S. stone monuments; point 10, P. L. S. stone; and the rest are nails on crosses on trees.

Bounded on the northeast by properties of Juan Sagatan and Domingo Sabatan (joint owners) and Narciso Litawa; on the east, by property of Narciso Litawa; on the southeast, by property of Roman Catholic Church (mission site); and on the southwest and northwest, by barrio road.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 15’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-106225.

Surveyed: June 16-21, 1938.

Approved: December 28, 1938.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this first day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
