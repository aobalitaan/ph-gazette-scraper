MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 704

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES THREE PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF MARAG, ANAO AND BOLIWONG, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF LUNA AND BURNAY, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes under the administration of the of the Director of Education, subject to private rights if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Marag, Anao and Boliwong, municipal districts of Luna and Burnay, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-105364 and Psu-105318, to wit:

(PSU-105364). ‑A parcel of land (the original plan Psu-105364, G. L. R. O. record No.     ), situated in the barrio of Marag, municipal district of Luna, Mountain Province (subprovince of Apayao).
