PROCLAMATION NO. 108

RESERVING FOR PARK AND GARDEN PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA, PHILIPPINES.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-one of Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and seventy-four, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for park and garden purposes, under the administration of the City of Zamboanga, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14674, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14674, N. 16ْ 09’ W. 834.26 m. from NE. corner at the Nueva and del Fuerte Streets crossing at Isabela of Basilan, thence S. 8ْ 24’ E. 25.87 m. to point 2; N. 48ْ 17’ E. 196.77 m. to point 3; N. 75ْ 59’ W. 164.50 m. to point 4; N. 11ْ 23’ W. 91.25 m. to point 5; S. 74ْ 08’ W. 106.53 m. to point 6; S. 7ْ 47’ W. 77.49 m. to point 7; S. 61ْ 05’ E. 57.00 m. to point 8; S. 47ْ 47’ E. 56.65 m. to point 9; S. 37ْ 20’ E. 79.36 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 31,210 square meters.

Points 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7, M. R. U. S. concrete monuments; points 8 and 9, stakes.

Bounded on the northeast, by road and property of Simplicio de Guzman; on the southeast, by public land; on the southwest, by road; and on the west and northwest, by property of Pablo Mirasol.

Bearings true. Declination, 2ْ 54’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14674.

Surveyed: June 13-15, 1911. Approved: October 3, 1936.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this nineteenth day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the second.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
