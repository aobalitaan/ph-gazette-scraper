MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 563

RESERVING FOR RAILROAD PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF NAGLABRAHAN, MUNICIPALITY OF GUIMBA, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, ISLAND OF LUZON .

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for railroad purposes, under the administration of the Manila Railroad Company, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Naglabrahan, municipality of Guimba, Province of Nueva Ecija, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plans Psd-4950 and Psd-4951, to wit:

Lot No. 2875-A. —A parcel of land (lot No. 2875-A of the subdivision plan Psd-4950, being a portion of Lot No. 2875 of the cadastral survey of Guimba, G. L. R. O. Cad. Record No. 399), situated in the barrio of Naglabrahan, municipality of Guimba, Province of Nueva Ecija. Bounded on the NE., by lot No. 2874-A of plan Psd-4952; on the SE., by Lot No. 2875-B of the subdivision plan; on the SW,. by Lot No. 2876-A of plan Psd-4949; and on the NW., by Lot No. 2875-C of the subdivision plan and Lot No. 500-A of plan Psd-4951. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 74° 27′ W., 1,123.21 meters, more or less, from B. B. M. No. 4 of Guimba Cadastre; thence N. 54° 59’ E., 15.73 m. to point “2”; thence N. 81° 07′ E., 26.02 m. to point “3”; thence S. 13° 01′ E., 20.03 m. to point “4”; thence S. 54° 69′ W., 43.48 m. to point “5”; thence N. 13° 25′ W, 32.31 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of One thousand one hundred forty-nine square meters (1,149), more or less.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “1”, “2”, “4” and “5” are marked by P. L. S./M. R. concrete monuments 18 cm. diam. x 72 cm.; and point “3” by intersection; bearings true; declination 0° 42′ E.; date of the original survey, November, 1919—February, 1922; and that of the subdivision survey, April 10—July 31, 1928.

Lot No. 500-A. —A parcel of land (lot No. 500-A of the subdivision plan Psd—4951, being a portion of Lot No. 500 of the cadastral survey of Guimba, G. L. R. O. Cad. Record No. 399), situated in the barrio of Naglabrahan, municipality of Guimba, Province of Nueva Ecija. Bounded on the NE. by Lot No. 499-A of plan Psd-4953; and on the SE., by Lot No. 2875-A of plan Psd-4950; and on the NW. by Lot No. 500-B of the subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 27° 57′ W., 1,044.83 meters, more or less, from M. B. M. No. 7 of Guimba Cadastre; thence S. 13° 01′ E., 12.37 m. to point “2”; thence S. 81° 07′ W., 26.02 m. to point “3”; thence N. 54° 59′ E., 27.99 m. to the point of beginning, containing an area of one hundred sixty square meters (160), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground, points “1” and “3” are marked by P. L. S./M. R. concrete monuments 18 cm. diam. x 72 cm.; and point “2” by intersection; bearings true; declination 0° 42′ E.; date of the original survey, November, 1919—February, 1922; and that of the subdivision survey, April 10—July 31, 1928.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the teal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-second day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
