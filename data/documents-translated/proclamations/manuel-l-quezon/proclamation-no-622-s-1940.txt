MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 622

RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF ROMBLON, PROVINCE OF ROMBLON, ISLAND OF ROMBLON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for military purposes, under the administration of the Chief of Staff of the Philippine Army, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Romblon, Province of Romblon, island of Romblon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Ir-474, to wit:

Beginning at point marked 1 and plan Ir-474, S. 83° 13′ W. 308.71 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Municipality of Romblon, thence S. 39° 08′ W. 54.91 m. to point 2; S. 45° 54′ W. 30.00 m. to point 3; N. 30° 06′ W. 50.02 m. to point 4; N. 41° 13′ E., 84.71 m. to point 5; S. 30° 19′ E. 50.43 m. to Point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 4,135 square meters.

Points 1, 2, 3 and 4, B. L. concrete monuments; point 5, temporary pole under water with a depth of about 50 ft.

Bounded on the northeast by Romblon Bay; on the southeast, by provincial road; and on the southwest and northwest, by Romblon Bay.

Bearings true. Declination 1° 33′ E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Ir-474.

Surveyed: October 13, 1939.

Approved: May 16, 1940.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-sixth day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
