MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 598

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF LUNA, SUBPROVINCE OF APAYAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipal district of Luna, subprovince of Apayao, Mountain Province, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-107498, to wit:

Lot No. 1, school site —A parcel of land (lot No. 1 of plan Psu-107498, G. L. R. O. Rec. No. ), situated in the Poblacion, municipal district of Luna, Mountain Province, (subprovince of Apayao), island of Luzon. Bounded on the NE., SE., and SW., by the property of Teresa Sagun (H-101938-D); and on the NW, by lot No. 2. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 76° 42′ W., 5,757.00 meters, more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of Dabba, municipality of Abulug, Cagayan, thence S. 33° 44′ W., 101.54 m. to point “2”; thence N. 44° 10′ W., 137.13 m. to point “3”; thence N. 12° 60′ E., 115.76 m. to point “4”; thence S. 44° 51′ E., 178.86 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of fifteen thousand five hundred fifty-two square meters (15,552) more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and, on the ground, are marked by P. L. S. concrete monuments. 15 by 60 cms.; bearings true; declination 0° 43′ E.; date of survey, July 12-13 and November 10, 1938; and that of the approval, April 15, 1939

Lot No. 2 school site. —A parcel of land (lot No. 2 of plan Psu-107498, G. L. R. O. Record No. ), situated in the Poblacion, municipal district of Luna, Mountain Province, (subprovince of Apayao), island of Luzon. Bounded on the NE. by the property of Maria Sagun (Psu-99871); on the SE., by lot No. 1; on the SW., by the property of Maria Sagun (Psu-99871); and on the NW., by Calog River (10 meters wide). Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 78° 13′ W., 6,970.38 m., more or less, than B. L. L. M. No. 1, barrio of Dabba, municipality of Abulug, Cagayan, thence N. 53° 04′ E., 97.25 m. to point “2”; thence S. 44° 51′ E., 53.92 m. to point “3”; thence S. 12° 50′ W., 115.76 m. to point “4”; thence N. 44° 10′ W., 129.21 m. to the point of beginning; containing an area of eight thousand eight hundred seventy-one square meters (8,871), more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and on the ground; point “1” is marked by old P. L. S. concrete monument, 15 by 60 cms.; and points “2”, “3”, and “4”, by P. L. S. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cms.; bearings true; declination 0° 43′ E.; date of survey, July 12-13 and November 10, 1938; and that of the approval, April 15, 1939.

Note:

Lot No. 1 is a portion of H-101938-D as surveyed for Teresa Sagun.

Lot No. 2 is a portion of Psu-99871 as surveyed for Maria Sagun.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fifth day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
