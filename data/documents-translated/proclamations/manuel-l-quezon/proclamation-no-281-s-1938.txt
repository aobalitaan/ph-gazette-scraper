PROCLAMATION NO. 281

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CALOMINTAO, MUNICIPALITY OF MAMBURAO, PROVINCE OF MINDORO, ISLAND OF MINDORO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Calomintao, municipality of Mamburao, Province of Mindoro, Island of Mindoro, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-264, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-264, S. 68° 59’ 16,973.85 m. , more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 2, municipality of Maburao, thence N. 70° 24’ W. 170.34 m. to point 2; N. 15° 23’ E. 283.58 m. to point 3; N. 16° 23’ E. 142.36 m. to point 4; S. 59° 48’ E. 106.68 m. to point 5; S. 62° 49’ E. 216.46 m. to point 6; S. 15° 50’ W. 179.79 m. to point 7; S. 16° 21’ W. 129.77 m. to point 8; N. 77° 32’ W. 144.20 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 10.9207 hectares.

Points 1, 2, and 8, old B. L. cylindrical concrete monuments; points 3, cross marked on stone; points 4 and 6, stones marked B. L.; points 5 and 7, crosses on trees; point 2, on bank of Pagbanan River.

Bounded on the northeast and southeast, by property claimed by the Government of the Philippines Islands (Mangyan Reservation); on the southwest, by property of Lorenzo Bernardo; and on the northwest, by Pagbanan River and property claimed by the Government of the Philippine Islands (Mangyan Reservation).

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 11’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-264.

Surveyed, March 15, 1933. Approved, July 31, 1933.

NOTE.—This survey is portion of lot No. 2, Ir-341, as surveyed for the Government of the Philippine Islands. (Mangyan Tribes.)

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this nineteenth day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
