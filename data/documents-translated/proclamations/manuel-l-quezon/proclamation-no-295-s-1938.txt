MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 295

ESTABLISHING AS TIBIANG-DOMAGONDONG WATERSHED FOREST RESERVE FOR FOREST PROTECTION AND TIMBER PRODUCTION THE PARCEL OF PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GUINAYANGAN, PROVINCE OF TAYABAS, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Director of Forestry, approved by the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, and pursuant to the provisions of section eighteen hundred and twenty-six of Act Numbered Twenty-seven and eleven, known as the Revised Administrative Code 1, I Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, hereby reserve for forest protection and timber production, and withdraw from entry, sale, or settlement, subject to private rights if any there be, the following parcel of land situated in the municipality of Guinayangan, province of Tayabas, Island of Luzon, and described in Bureau of Forestry map No. F. R.-81, to wit:

Containing an area of approximately 280 hectares.

The said parcel of land shall be known as the “Tibiang-Domagondong Watershed Forest Reserve” and shall remain under the administration of the Bureau of Forestry, which shall have authority to regulate the use and occupancy of this reserve, and the cutting, collection, and removal timber and other forest products therein in accordance with the Forest Law and Regulations.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-first day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
