MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 715

RESERVING FOR USE OF THE MANILA RAILROAD COMPANY TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF LEGASPI, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon, the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for use of the Manila Railroad Company, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Legaspi, Province of Albay, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-106139, to wit:

(PSU-106139)

Bounded on the N. by public land; on the E., by Albay Gulf; on the S., by lot No. 1124 (Property of the Manila Railroad Company); and on the W., by lot No. 1421 (property of the Manila Railroad Company).
