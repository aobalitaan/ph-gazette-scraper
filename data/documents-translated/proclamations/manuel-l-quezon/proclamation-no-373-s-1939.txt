MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 373

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CABUGBUGAN, MUNICIPALITY OF SANTA IGNACIA, PROVINCE OF TARLAC, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Cabugbugan, municipality of Santa Ignacia, Province of Tarlac, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psm-953, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psm-953, S. 77° 27′ E. 5,239.75 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, barrio of San Bartolome, municipality of Mayantoc, thence S. 79° 02′ E. 100.00 m. to point 2; S. 19° 52′ W. 100.00 m. to point 3; N. 79° 02′ W. 100.00 m. to point 4; N. 19° 52′ E. 100.00 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 9,880 square meters.

Points 1, 2, 3, and 4, B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast, southeast, and southwest, by public land; and on the northwest, by barrio road.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 07′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psm-953.

Surveyed: September 18, 1937.

Approved: February 3, 1938.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-seventh day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
