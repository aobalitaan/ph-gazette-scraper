MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 627

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO (2) PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF PAGSANGHAN AND OESTE, MUNICIPALITY OF TARANGNAN, PROVINCE OF SAMAR.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Pagsanghan and Oeste, municipality of Tarangnan, Province of Samar, islands of Samar and Libucan, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-414, to wit:

Lot No. 1. (school site) —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-414, S. 83″ 52′ E. 2,733.00 m., more or less, from U. S. C. and G. S. Triangulation Station “Gandara,” thence N. 88° 25′ E. 71.89 m. to point 2; S. 3° 02′ W. 114.23 m. to point 3; S. 89° 32′ W. 87.32 m. to point 4; N. 1°52′ W. 64.73 m. to point 5; N. 25° 56′ E. 53.49 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 9,806 square meters.

Points 1, 2 and 3, B. L. concrete monuments; point 4, old stake; and point 5, Old P. L. S./B. L. concrete monument.

Bounded on the north by proposed barrio road; on the east, by public land; on the south, by proposed barrio road; on the west, by lot No. 3 of plan 11-2246; and on the northwest, by property of the municipal government of Tarangnan (school site.).

Bearings true. Declination 0° 27′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-414, sheet No. 1.

Surveyed: March 27, 1940.

Approved: June 29, 1940.

Lot No. 2 (school site) —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-414, N. 49° 35′ W. 1,809.38 m., more or less, from U. S. C. and G. S. Triangulation Station, “Libucan,” thence N. 15° 59′ W. 78.02 m. to point 2; N. 89° 35′ E. 70.79 m to point 3; S. 15° 28′ E. 79.10 m. to point 4; N. 89° 24′ W. 70.40 m. to point 1, point of beginning. Containing an area of 5,336 square meters. Points 1 and 2, B. L. concrete monuments; and points 3 and 4, old rock capped with concrete and marked by P. L. S. Bounded on the north by property of Basilio Labian; on the northeast, by property of the municipal government of Tarangnan; on the south, by barrio road (proposed extensions; and on the southwest, by property of Jesus Cataros. Bearings true. Declination 0° 27′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-414, sheet No. 2.

Surveyed: March 28, 1940. Approved: June 29, 1940.

The reservation of the school site in the barrio of Oeste is subject to the condition that at any time the said site is abandoned for school purposes, the land shall revert to the category of, and shall form part of, public forest.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand arid caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eighth day of October, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
