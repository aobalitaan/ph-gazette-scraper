PROCLAMATION NO. 43

RESERVING FOR GOVERNMENT CENTER SITE A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BANAUE, SUBPROVINCE OF IFUGAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-four, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for Government center site, under the administration of the provincial board of Mountain Province, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to future survey that may be made, the following-described parcel of the public domain situated in the Municipal District of Banaue, Subprovince of Ifugao, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14298, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Swo-14298, being S. 58ْ 25’ E. 561.23 m. to point 2; 65ْ 02’ E. 39.95 m. to point 3; N. 80ْ 05’ E. 14.80 m. to point 4; N. 73ْ 12’ E. 34.93 m. to point 5; N. 69ْ 40’ E. 18.42 m. to point 6; N. 6ْ 17’ E. 238.00 m. to point 7; N. 26ْ 30’ W. 66.00 m. to point 8; N. 1ْ 40’ E. 106.44 m. to point 9; N. 79ْ 32’ W. 79.75 m. to point 10; N. 86ْ 03’ W. 51.17 m. to point 11; N. 53ْ 23’ W. 90.34 m. to point 12; S. 69ْ 11’ W. 81.81 m. to point 13; N. 11ْ 25’ E. 61.50 m. to point 14; N. 79 ْ 53’ W. 67.68 m. to point 15; S. 65ْ 29’ W. 73.40 m. to point 16; S. 48ْ 13’ W. 75.30 m. to point 17; S. 45ْ 37’ W. 132.00 m. to point 18; S. 8ْ 14’ E. 47.30 to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an approximate area of 192,150 square meters.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fifth day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the first.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines
