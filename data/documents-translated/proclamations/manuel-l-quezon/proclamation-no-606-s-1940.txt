MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINE

PROCLAMATION NO. 606

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 208 DATED OCTOBER 5, 1937, AND OPENING TO DISPOSITION UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 137 A PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CAMP 7, MUNICIPALITY OF MINGLANILLA, PROVINCE OF CEBU, ISLAND OF CEBU.

Whereas a petition has been filed for the release of certain mining claim located in the barrio of Camp 7, municipality of Minglanilla, Province of Cebu, island of Cebu, from the Talisay-Minglanilla Estate Forest Reserve established under Proclamation Numbered Two hundred eight, dated October fifth, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven;

Whereas, according to the findings of the Director of Mines and the Director of Forestry, concurred in by the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, the land embraced in the said mining claim now included in the Talisay-Minglanilla Estate Forest Reserve appears to be mineralized; and

Whereas, under the provisions of section fourteen of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and thirty-seven, otherwise known as the Mining Act, lands within reservations for purposes other than mining, which, after such reservation is made, are found to be more valuable for their mineral contents than for the purpose for which the reservation was made, may be withdrawn from such reservations by the President of the Philippines only with the concurrence of the National Assembly;

Now, therefore, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation Numbered Two hundred eight, dated October fifth, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, the following parcel of land situated in the barrio of Camp 7, municipality of Minglanilla Province of Cebu, island of Cebu, and described on plan No. Crp-43, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan Crp-43, being N. 0° 47′ E., 73.80 meters, more or less, from M. B. M. No. 13, Minglanilla-Toledo, Cebu, thence due N. 200.00 meters to point “2”; thence due E. 200.00 meters to point “3”; thence due S. 200.00 meters to point “4”; and thence due W. 200.00 meters to point “1”, to the point of beginning.

Containing an area of 4.000 hectares.

Bounded on the north, east, south and west by the Talisay-Minglanilla Estate Forest Reserve established under Proclamation No. 208, dated October 5, 1937.

Bearings: True. Declination: 1° 23′ E.

Date of survey: May 23-June 9, 1939, by Victorino Gonzales, mineral land surveyor.

The cutting, removal and disposal of the timber and other forest products shall be under the supervision and control of the Bureau of Forestry and at any time the said parcel of land shall be abandoned for mining purposes, the same shall revert to as part of the Talisay-Minglanilla Estate Forest Reserve.

This proclamation shall not take effect until it is concurred in by a resolution of the National Assembly duly adopted for the purpose.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this ninth day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
