MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 356

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 751, DATED DECEMBER 6, 1934, AND DECLARING THE LAND RESERVED THEREBY OPEN TO DISPOSITION.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby revoke Proclamation Numbered Seven hundred and fifty-one, dated December sixth, nineteen hundred and thirty-four, and declared the land reserved thereby open to disposition.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this seventeenth day of December, in the year of Our-Lord, nineteen, hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President

Source: University of the Philippines, College of Law Library
