MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 352

RESERVING FOR WAREHOUSE PURPOSES TWO PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF DANSALAN, PROVINCE OF LANAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or resettlement and reserve for warehouse purposes, under the administration of the National Rice and Corn Corporation, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Poblacion, municipality of Dansalan, Provinde of Lanao, Island of Mindanao and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Ir-456, to wit:

Lot No. 1 .—Beginning a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-456, S. 40° 30’ W. 119.14 m. more or less, from monument No. 7, Dansalan townsite, K-3, thence S. 45° 06’ E. 39.70 m. to point 2; S. 44° 54’ W. 45.10 m. to point 3; S. 26° 12’ E. 20.60 m. to point 4; S. 44° 54’ W. 18.13 m. to point 5; N. 39° 40’ W. 26.94 m. to point 6; N. 66° 44’ W. 34.79 m. to point 7; N. 44° 54’ E. 80.27 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 3,286 square meters.

Points 1, 2, and 7, B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the northeast, by street and lot No. 2; on the southeast, by lot No. 2 and block No. 59 of Dansalan townsite; on the southeast, by Lake Lanao; and on the northwest, by Fourth Avenue.

Lot No. 2 .—Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-456, S. 23° 06’ W. 183.80 m., more or less, from monument No. 7, Dansalan-townsite, K-3, thence N. 26° 12’ W. 20.60 m. to point 2; N. 44° 54’ E. 45.10 m. to point 3; S. 45° 06’ E. 1950 m. to point 4; S. 44° 54’ W. 51.80 to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 944 square meters.

Point 3, B. L. concrete meters.

Bounded on the northeast, by street; on the southeast and northwest, by lot No. 1

Bearing true. Declination, 2-10 E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-456.

Surveyed: During 1907 and November 1-18, 1915.

Approved: October 6, 1938.

NOTE.—Lot No. 1, Pn-8, as surveyed for the provincial government of Lanao.

Lot No. 2—Block No. 3-A of Dansalan townsite, K-3.

All corners not otherwise described are stakes.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twelfth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
