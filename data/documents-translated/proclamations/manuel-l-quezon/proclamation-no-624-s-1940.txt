MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 624

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF KAUB, MUNICIPALITY OF ABORLAN, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Kaub, municipality of Aborlan, Province of Palawan, island of Palawan, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-429, to wit:

Beginning at point marked 1 on plan Mr-429, N. 16° 14′ W. 2,135.30 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 1, Aborlan Pls-111, thence N. 47° 12′ E. 222.24 m. to point 2; S. 29° 17 E. 378.69 m. to point 3; S. 86° 40′ W. 341.45 m. to point 4; N. 2° 08′ W. 199.24 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 7.4926 hectares.

Bounded on the northeast by property of Iwahig Penal Colony; on the south and west, by road; and on the northwest, by national highway.

Bearings true. Declination 1° 45′ E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Mr-429.

Surveyed: May 11—September 13, 1939.

Approved: July 11, 1940.

NOTE:—This is lot No. 112 of Aborlan Public Lands Subdivision Pls-111.

All corners are B. L. concrete monuments.

In witness whereof. I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-sixth day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
