MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 750

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL BUILDING SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF MANGALI, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF TANUDAN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for municipal building site purposes, under the administration of the municipal district of Tanudan, Mountain Province, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Mañgali, muilcipal district of Tanudan, Mountain Province, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Psu-108266, to wit:

Psu-108266

Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan Psu-108266, S. 5.54-53’E. 17312.80m. more or less from B.L.L.M. No. 1, Mpl. Dlst. of Lubuagn, thence

S.22-08’E. 19.59m. to point 2;

S. 4-35’W. 50.51m. to point 3;

N. 62-02’W. 82.80m. to point 4;

N.26-00’E. 15.37m. to point 5;

N. 7-33’E. 13.33m. to point 6;

N. 36-58’E. 8.35m to point 7;

S.85-55’E. 56.41m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of THREE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED FORTY FOUR SQUARE METERS (3,644), more or less.

Points 2 and 3, Nails on Crosses on Trees; and the rest are P.L.S. Stone Mons.

Bounded on the Northeast and North, by property of Banawag and Agagon (Joint Owners); on the East, by property of Dongadong; on the Southwest, by property of Banawag and Dongadong (Joint Owners); and on the northwest, by property of Ul-lalim.

Bearings true. Declination 0-22’E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Psu-108266.

Surveyed: May 18-31, 1938 & May 2-4, 1939.

Approved: July 26, 1939.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this 12 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L QUEZON

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
