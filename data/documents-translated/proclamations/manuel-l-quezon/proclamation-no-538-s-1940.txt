MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 538

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BALBALAN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the municipal district of Balbalan, Mountain Province, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-105902, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-105902, N. 13° 57′ E. 5,403.11 m., more or less, from B. L. B M. No. 1, Barrio of Ableg, Municipal District of Balbalan, thence N. 20° 28′ E. 57.61 m. to point 2; S. 86 07′ E. 54.60 m. to point 3; S. 6° 33′ E. 190.50 m. to point 4; N. 68″ 14′ W. 99.40 m. to point 5; N. 2° 17′ W. 102.19 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 13,753 square meters.

Points 1, 3, 4 and 5, stones marked P. L. S.; point 2. P. L. S. concrete monument.

Bounded on the north and east by property of the municipal district government of Balbalan Townsite; on the southwest, by provincial road; on the west, by property of the municipal district government of Balbalan Townsite; and on the northwest, by the municipal road.

Bearings true. Declination 0° 12’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-105902.

Surveyed: May 4-6 and July 23, 1938.

Approved: February b28, 1939.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fifteenth day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
