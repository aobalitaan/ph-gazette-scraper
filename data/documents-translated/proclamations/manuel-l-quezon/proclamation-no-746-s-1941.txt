MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 746

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES ONE PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF TADIAN, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF KAYAN, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Tadian, municipal district of Kayan, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in plan Psu-107232, to wit:

Psu-107232. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan Psu-107232, N. 26° 48′ W. 2,114.52 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipal district of Kayan, thence N. 56° 00′ E. 56.03 m. to point 2; S. 29° 01′ E. 31.57 m. to point 3; S. 16° 38′ E. 52,34 m. to point 4; S. 76° 35′ W. 20.87 m. to point 5; S. 54° 13′ W. 33.06 m. to point 6; S. 68° 34′ W. 52.67 m. to point 7; N. 8° 51′ W. 18.00 m. to point 8; N. 1° 12′ E. 29.17 m. to point 9; N. 15° 32′ E. 18.71 m. to point 10; N. 33° 40′ E. 29.87 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of seven thousand forty-nine (7,049) square meters, more or less.

Points 1, 2, 4, 5, 9, and 10, P. L. S. stones; points 3 and 6, nails on crosses on trees; point 7, cross on stone wall; point 8, stone in place.

Bounded on the northeast by trail; on the southeast, by properties of Pacpaco, Paguigui, and Kayacay; and on the west and northwest, by barrio road.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 29′ E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Psu-107232.

Surveyed: November 21, 1938.

Approved: March 8, 1939.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this ninth day of September, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
