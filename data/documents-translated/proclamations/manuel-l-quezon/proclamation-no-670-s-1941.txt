MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 670

RESERVING FOR MUNICIPAL BUILDING SITE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF LAGAWI, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF BURNAY, SUBPROVINCE OF IFUGAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for municipal building site purposes, under the administration of the municipal district of Burnay, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio Lagawi, municipal district of Burnay, subprovince of Ifugao, Mountain Province, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-107233, to wit:

Beginning at point marked “1” on plan Psu-107233, N. 55° 37’ E. 5,716.88 m. from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipal district of Kiangan, thence N. 58° 46’ E. 78.16 m. to point 2; S. 77° 16’ E. 25.23 m. to point 3; S. 39° 40’ W. 46.99 m. to point 4; S. 9° 31’ E. 39.07 m. to point 5; N. 76° 07’ W. 32.60 m. to point 6; S. 61° 28’ W. 42.86 m. to point 7; N. 21° 01’ W. 12.66 m. to point 8; N. 8° 19’ E. 41.00 m. to point 1, point of beginning

Containing an area of four thousand four hundred fifty six square meters (4,456), more or less.

Points 1 and 2, P. L. S. concrete monuments; and the rest are P. L. S. stone monuments.

Bounded on the north by barrio road; on the east and southeast, by property of Angel and Puguan; on the south, by properties of Guinamay and Bogati and Dumawal and Buliio; on the west, by property of Ambognan Dagadag; and on the northwest, by property of Dango Uyame.

Bearings true.

Declination 0° 40′ E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Psu-107233.

Surveyed: December 23, 1938.

Approved: May 13, 1939.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-eighth day of January, in the year of Our Lord nineteen hundred and forty-one and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
