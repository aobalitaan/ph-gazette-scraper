MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 397

RESERVING FOR MILITARY PURPOSES SIX PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section seventy-six of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for military purposes, under the administration of the Chief of Staff of the Philippine Army, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the City of Baguio, Island of Luzon, and particularly described im Bureau of Lands plan Bsd-5688, to wit:

Lot No. 37-C. —A parcel of land (lot No. 37-C of the subdivision plan Bsd-5689, being a portion of lot No. 37 of Baguio townsite, G. L. R. 0. Record No.     ), situated in the residence section H, City of Baguio, Mountain Province. Bounded on the SE, by lot No. 88; on the S., by Lucban Road; and on the NW., by lot No. 36-B-3 of the subdivision plan.
