MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 743

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF SAN NICOLAS, MUNICIPALITY OF TALISAY, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES NORTE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrio of San Nicolas, municipality of Talisay, Province of Camarines Norte, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in plan Psu-108820, to wit:

Psu-108820. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on plan Psu-108820, N. 9° 1,9′ E. 4,061.09 m. more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of Daet, Camarines Norte, thence N. 10° 45′ ,E. 99.94 m. to point 2; S. 79° 20′ E. 103.57 m. to point 3; S. 12″ 51′ W. 99.73 m. to point 4; N. 79° 30′ W. 99.93 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of ten thousand on hundred fifty-four (10,154) square meters, more or less.

Point 1, B. L. concrete monument; points 2, 3 and 4, P. L. S. concrete monuments.

Bounded on ,all sides by public land.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 15′ E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Psu-108820.

Surveyed: March 27, 1939.

Approved: September 13, 1939.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eleventh day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the sixth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
