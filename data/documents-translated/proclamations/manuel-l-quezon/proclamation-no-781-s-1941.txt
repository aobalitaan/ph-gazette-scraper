MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 781

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES ONE PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF PALINA MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF PUGO, PROVINCE OF LA UNION, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of Section Eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Palina, municipal district of Pugo, province of La Union, island of Luzon, and particularly described on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-483, to wit:

Mr-483.

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-483, N.26-38’E. 1490.90 m. more or less from B.L.B.M. No. 1, Bo. of Palina, Mpl. Dist. of Pugo, thence

N. 84-54’E. 90.54 m. to point 2;

S. 7-48’B. 125.44 m. to point 3;

S. 82-45’W. 156.87 m. to point 4;

N. 19-35’E. 144.38 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of FIFTEEN THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED SEVENTY SEVEN SQUARE LETERS (15,777), more or less.

All corners are B.L. Conc. Mons.

Bounded on the North, by property of Feliciano Cataras; and on the East, South and Northwest, by Public Land.

Bearings true. Declination 0-42’E.

Points referred to are marked on plan Mr-483.

Surveyed: November 26, 1940.

Approved: September 11, 1941.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty-one, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the seventh.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L QUEZON

By the President: (Sgd.) JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
