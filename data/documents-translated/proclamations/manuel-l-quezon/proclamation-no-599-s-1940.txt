MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 599

RESERVING FOR WAREHOUSE PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF LEGASPI, PROVINCE OF ALBAY, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for warehouse purposes, under the administration of the National Rice and Corn Corporation, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Legaspi, Province of Albay, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Ir-481, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-481, S. 43° 35′ E. 326.48 m. more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Legaspi Cad. No. 47, thence S. 40° 48′ E. 42.29 m. to point 2; S. 53° 59′ E. 10.00 m. to point 3; S. 53° 59’ E. 15.07 m. to point 4; S. 42° 43′ E. 0.18 m. to point 5; S. 82° 43′ W. 73.31 m. to point 6; N. 52° 37′ W. 38.02 m. to point 7; N. 7° 16′ W. 57.88 m. to point 8; S. 86° 51’ E. 14.01 m. to point 9; S. 86° 51′ E. 25.96 m. to point 10; S. 45° 54′ E. 27.39 m. to point 11; S. 29° 26′ E.0.45 m . to point 12; S. 42° 18′ E. 3.61 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 5,060 square meters.

Point 1, old corner stone wall; points 2, 4 and 12, old B. L. concrete monuments; points 5, 6, 7 and 8, B. L. concrete monument; point 9, old concrete monument post; and the rest are old P. L. S. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north by lots Nos. 9778-A-5 and 9778-A-6 of plan Psd-10839; on the NE., by lot No. 1631, Alley, lot No. 1777 and lots Nos. 1776-A and 1776-B of plan Psd-13189; on the southeast, by public land; on the southwest, by lot No. 1115; and on the west, by proposed street.

Bearing’s true. Declination 0° 03′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-481.

Surveyed: December 9 and 19, 1939.

Approved: April 10, 1940.

NOTE:

This is outside of Legaspi Cad. No. 47, formerly a portion of Tibu River.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this fifth day of August, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
