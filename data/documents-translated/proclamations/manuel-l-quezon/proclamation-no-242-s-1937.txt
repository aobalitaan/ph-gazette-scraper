MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 242

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 423, DATED OCTOBER 17, 1931

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby exclude from the operation of Proclamation Numbered Four hundred and twenty-three, dated October seventeenth, nineteen hundred and thirty-one, and declare open to disposition and settlement, a parcel of land, situated in the municipality of Montalban, Province of Rizal, and particularly described in the Bureau of Lands plan H-83762, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan H-83762, N. 70° 59′ E. 12,170.83 m., more or less, from monument No. 142, Tala Estate, thence N. 56° 49′ E. 23.65 m. to point 2; S. 59° 12′ E. 68.86 m. to point 3; S. 56° 17′ E. 222.82 m. to point 4; S. 32° 46′ E. 51.70 m. to point 5; S. 19° 50′ W. 40.99 m. to point 6; N. 70° 49′ W. 59.26 m. to point 7; N. 47° 39′ W. 103.01 m. to point 8; N. 46° 25′ W. 201.90 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1.8096 hectares.

Points 1, 2, 4, 5, 6, and 7, galvanized iron spikes on “X” on trees; and points 3 and 8, stakes.

Bounded on all sides by Puray River.

Bearings true. Declination, 0° 13′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan H-83762.

Surveyed under the authority of sections 12—21, Commonwealth Act No. 141, and in accordance with existing regulations of the Bureau of Lands, by Cornelio R. Domingo, Junior Surveyor, under the supervision of Isabelo del Carmen, public land surveyor, on October 24-28, 1936, and approved on June 26, 1937.

NOTE.—This is lot No. 33, portion of lot No. 24, Swo-9033, as surveyed for the Bureau of Non-Christian Tribes (Ipd-80, lot No. 24). In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-fourth day of December, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-seven, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: ELPIDIO QUIRINO Secretary of the Interior
