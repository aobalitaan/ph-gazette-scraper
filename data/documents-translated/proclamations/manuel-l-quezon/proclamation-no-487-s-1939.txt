MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 487

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF CASAMBALANGAN AND CABIRAOAN, MUNICIPALITY OF GONZAGA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numberd One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the barrios of Casambalangan and Cabi-raoan, Municipality of Gonzaga, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-400, to wit:

Lot No. 2 (Barrio of Casambalangan). —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-400, N. 46-46′ E. 18,904.35 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1,. Mp. of Gonzaga, thence N. 50-01′ W. 100.00 m. to point 2; N. 33-19′ E. 100.00 m. to point 3; S. 50-01′ E. 100.00 m. to point 4; S. 33-19′ W. 100.00 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1.0000 hectare.

All corners are B. L. concrete monuments 15 X 60 cm.

Bounded on the northeast by public land; on the southeast, by provincial road; and on the southwest and northwest, by public land.

Bearings true. Declination 0-01′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-400, sheet No. 2.

Surveyed: July 15, 1939.

Approved: October 3, 1939.

Lot No. 3 {Barrio of Cabiraoan). —Beginning at a. point-marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-400, N. 53-36′ E. 11,863.51 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, Mp. of Gonzaga, thence S. 77-57′ E. 53.81 m. to point 2; S. 78-35′ E. 47.55 m . to point 3; S. 3-26′ W. 101.60 m. to point 4; N. 76-52′ W. 24.27 m. to point 5; N. 76-49′ W. 85.99 m. to point 6; N. 8-20′ E. 97.97 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1.0463 hectares.

Point 5, old B. L. concrete monument; and the rest are B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the north by barrio road; on the east, by-public land; on the south, by property of Isidoro Garma: and on the west, by public land.

Bearings true. Declination 0-32′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-400. sheet No. 3.

Surveyed: July 19, 1939.

Approved: October 3, 1939.

In witness, whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-ninth day of November, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
