PROCLAMATION NO. 262

ESTABLISHING AS CAMAGON-ANAHAU FOREST RESERVE FOR FOREST PROTECTION AND CONSERVATION OF CAMAGON AND ANAHAU TREES, THE PARCEL OF PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF INFANTA, PROVINCE OF TAYABAS, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, and pursuant to the provisions of section eighteen hundred and twenty-six of Act Numbered Twenty-seven hundred and eleven, known as the Revised Administrative Code, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, hereby establish for forest protection and conservation of camagon and anahau trees, the “Camagon-Anahau Forest Reserve” and place it under the administration and control of the Bureau of Forestry, which shall have authority to regulate the use and occupany of this reserve and the cutting, collection, and removal of camagon and anahau trees and other forest products therein in accordance with the Forest Law and Regulation.

I, therefore, withdraw from entry, sale, or settlement, and hereby declare and proclaim to be the Camagon-Anahau Forest Reserve, subject to private rights, if any there be, the parcel of public domain situated in the municipality of Infanta, province of Tayabas, Island of Luzon, and described in the Bureau of Forestry map No. F. R-77, to wit:
