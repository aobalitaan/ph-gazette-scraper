MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 560

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CALAWAG, MUNICIPALITY OF TAYTAY, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Calawag, municipality of Taytay, Province of Palawan, Island of Palawan, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-424, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-424, M. 25° 10′ E. 343.64 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 2, barrio of Calawag, municipality of Taytay, thence N. 70° 19′ W. 436.11 m. to point 2; N 31° 57E 121.64 m. to point 3; S. 68° 04′ E. 415.00m. to point 4, S. 22° 08′ W. 102.70 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 4.7228 hectares.

All corners are B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on all sides by public land.

Bearings true; declination 1°34’E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan

Surveyed: November 17-18, 1938.

Approved: February 8, 1940.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-second day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and forty, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fifth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
