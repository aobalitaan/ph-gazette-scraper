MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 360

ALLOTTING FREQUENCIES AND WAVELENGTHS TO THE UNITED PRESS ASSOCIATIONS AND ISSUING A LICENSE FOR THE USE THEREOF.

By virtue of the powers granted me by section five of Commonwealth Act Numbered Three hundred eighty-seven, I, Manuel L. Quezon, President of the Philippines, do hereby allot to the United Press Associations the following frequencies and wavelengths, and license it to use such frequencies and wavelengths for the wireless transmission of wordage, picture, or other matter intended for publication, either in the public press or by means of broadcast radionews to points designated:

7,485 Kcs. (38.24 m) Hongkong 9,895 Kcs. (30.32 m) Shanghai 14,800 Kcs (20.27 m) Singapore 15,700 Kcs. (19.10 m) San Francisco, U. S. A.

It is understood that the authority granted herein does not authorize the United Press Associations to establish and/or operate radio stations under. Act Numbered Three thousand eight hundred forty-six, as amended by Commonwealth Act Numbered Three hundred sixty-five.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of; the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this twenty-fourth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred, and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President

Source: University of the Philippines, College of Law Library
