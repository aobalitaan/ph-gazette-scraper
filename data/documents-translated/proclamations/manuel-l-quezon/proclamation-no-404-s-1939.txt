MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 404

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF LABUNGAN, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF AWANG, PROVINCE OF COTABATO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Labungan, municipal district of Awang, Province of Cotabato, Island of Mindanao, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Psu-101293, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-101293, S. 75° 15′ E. 761.96 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, barrio of Labungan, municipal district of Awang, thence N. 88° 52′ E. 99.72 m. to point 2; S. 1° 19′ E. 95.82 m. to point 3; S. 78° 06′ W. 97.00 m. to point

4; N. 3° 31′ W. 114.04 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 10,250 square meters.

All corners are P. L. S. concrete monuments, 15 by 60 cm.

Bounded on the north, east, and south, by public land; and on the west, by road.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 52′ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Psu-101293, sheet No. 2.

Surveyed: December 20, 1935.

Approved: April 9, 1937.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this eleventh day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-nine, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines

By the President: JORGE B. VARGAS Secretary to the President
