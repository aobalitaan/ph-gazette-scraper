PROCLAMATION NO. 282

RESERVING FOR SETTLEMENT FARM SCHOOL PRUPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF GIBATO, MUNICIPALITY OF DUMARAO, PROVINCE OF CAPIZ, ISLAND OF PANAY.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section, eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sail or settlement and reserve for settlement for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Gilbato, municipality of Dumarao, Province of Capiz, Island of Panay, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Pr-223, to wit:

Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-223, S. 84° 11’ W. 13,383.44 m., more or less, from B. L. B. M. No. 2, barrio of Lemery, municipality of Sara, thence N. 19° 08’ E. 449.61 m. to point 2; N. 18° 48’ E. 271.76 m. to point 3; N. 34° 33’ E. 309.09 m. to point 4; S. 7° 33’ W. 38.63 m. to point 5; N. 58° 04’ E. 232.32 m. to point 6; S. 35° 01’ W. 280.02 m. to point 7; s. 16° 58’ E. 240.57 m. to point 8; S. 57° 22’ W. 221.67 m. to point 9; S. 10° 37’ W. 355.73 m. to point 10; N. 75° 09’ W. 1945.62 m. to point 11; N. 76° 30’ W. 126.39 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 27.7283 hectares.

Points 1, 3, and 11, old P. L. S./B. L. concrete monuments; points 2 and 6, old B. L. concrete monuments; points 4, 5, and 7, old galvanized iron spikes on crosses on trees; points 8, 9 and 10, B. L. concrete monuments

Bounded on the north, by property of Victorino de la Cruz; on the east, by property of Francisco Aguilar and public land; on the south by property of Potenciano Palma; and on the west, by properties of Felipe Vergara and Victorino de la Cruz.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 42’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Pr-223.

Surveyed: July 19, 1937. Approved: December 24, 1937.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this nineteenth day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

[SEAL]

President of the Philippines

By the President:

Secretary to the President
