MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 571

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES FIVE (5) PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIOS OF HALIAP, JULONGAN, ANTIPOLO, MUNGAYAN AND NAGACADAN, MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF KIANGAN, SUBPROVINCE OF IFUGAO, MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, as amended, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of public domain, situated in the barrios of Haliap, Julongan, Antipolo, Mungayan and Nagacadan, municipal district of Kiangan, subprovince of Ifugao, Mountain Province, island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plans Psu-105485, Psu-105484, Psu-105270, and Psa-105317, to wit:
