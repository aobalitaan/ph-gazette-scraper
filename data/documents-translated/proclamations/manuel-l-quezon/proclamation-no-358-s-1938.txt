MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 358

RESERVING FOR WORKING-MEN’S VILLAGE PURPOSES TWO PARCELS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF BAGUIO, CONTAINING A TOTAL AREA OF 31.8084 HECTARES.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section. seventy-six of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for a working-men’s village, to be administered by the City of Baguio in accordance with regulations that shall be promulgated by the city government with the approval of the Chief Executive, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcels of the public domain, situated in the residence section “H,” City of Baguio, Island of Luzon, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Ir-450-A, to wit:

Lot No. 1. —Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-450-A, S. 89° 47′ E. 1,402.85 m„ more or less, from triangulation station skyline, Baguio, Mountain Province,: S. 74° 22′ E. 205.80 m. to point 2; S. 60° 00′ W. 64.00 m. to. point 3; N. 13° 30′ E. 116.89 m. to point 4; N. 88° 45′ W. 99.26 m. to point 5; N. 18° 29′ W. 76.16 m. to point 6; N. 30° 02’ E. 58.112 m. to point 7; N. 11° 15’ W. 81.20 m. to point 8; N. 74° 59’ E. 127.34 m. to point 9; N. 38° 02’ E. 65.69 m. to point 10; N. 2° 42’ W. 160.93 m. to point 11; N. 48° 18 E. 95.54 m. to point 12; N. 11° 02’ E. 38.13 m. to point 13; S. 56° 09’ E. 40.81 m. to point 14; S. 63° 28’ E. 90.12 m. to point 15; S. 59° 34’ E. 93.57 m. to point 16; N. 5° 49’ E. 60.27 m. to point 17; N. 19° 59’ W. 73.37 m. to point 18; N. 26° 08’ W. 78.76 m. to point 19; N. 11° 16’ W. 31.60 m to point 20; N. 19° 10’ W. 32.13 m. to point 21; S. 81° 22’ E. 187.75 m. to point 22; S. 16° 46’ W. 43.88 m. to point 23; S 12° 52’ W. 466.94 m. to point 24; S. 12° 38 E. 205.40 m. to point 25; S. 89° 50’ W. 253.99 m. to point 1; point of beginning.

Containing an area of 30.4670 hectares.

Points 4 and 6, old crosses on trees; point 8, old galvanize iron spike on cross on tree; point 13, intersection; point 20, old cross on rock; points 21 and 22, B. L. concreter monuments.

Bounded on the north by public; on the east, by Baguio Water Supply Reservation and lot No. 203; on the south, by lot No. 36-C and lot No. 2 and public land; and on the rest, by public land, lot No. 29-B of plan Bsd-2802, lot No. 35, lot No. 34-A of plan Bsd-1207, property of Ynso and public land.

Lot No. 2 .—Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-450-A, S. 89° 47’ E. 1,402.85 m., more or less, from triangulation station Skyline, Baguio, Mountain Province, thence S. 50° 53’ W. 210.97 m. to point 2; S. 87° 04’ W. 100.44 m. to point 3; N. 14° 16’ E. 41.75 m. to point 4; N. 60° 00’ W. 64.00 m. to point 5; N. 74° 22′ E. 205.80 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

Containing an area of 1.3414 hectares.

Point 2, old cross on tree; and the rest are old B. L. concrete monuments.

Bounded on the southeast and south, by lot No. 88; on the west, by lot No. 35-A; and on the northwest, by lot No. 1.

Bearings true. Declination, 1° 30’ E.

Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Ir-450-A.

Surveyed: June 3, 1938.

Approved: November 29, 1938.

NOTE.—Lot No. 1=Additional lot No. 89 of Baguio townsite, residence section “H.”

Lot No. 2=Lot No. 82 of Baguio townsite, residence section “H.”

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this seventeenth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.

MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines
