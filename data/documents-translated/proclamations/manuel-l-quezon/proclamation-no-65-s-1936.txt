PROCLAMATION NO. 65

EXCLUDING FROM THE OPERATION OF PROCLAMATION NO. 217, SERIES OF 1929, AND RESERVING, SETTING APART, AND DESIGNATING AS BAGUIO-BONTOC SCENIC NATIONAL PARK FOR THE BENEFIT AND ENJOYMENT OF THE PEOPLE OF THE PHILIPPINES THE PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPAL DISTRICTS OF TUBLAY, ATOK, BOKOD, KABAYAN, KIBUNGAN, BUGUIAS, AND AMPUSUNGAN, SUBPROVINCE OF BENGUET; MUNICIPAL DISTRICT OF KIANGAN, SUBPROVINCE OF IFUGAO; AND MUNICIPAL DISTRICTS OF BAUKO, BANAAO, AND SABANGAN, SUBPROVINCE OF BONTOC; MOUNTAIN PROVINCE, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Quezon, President of the Philippines, hereby exclude from the. operation of the provisions of Proclamation Numbered Two hundred and seventeen, dated February sixteenth, nineteen hundred and. twenty-nine, establishing the Central Cordillera Forest Reserve, the parcel of land situated in the Municipal Districts of Tublay, Atok, Bokod, Kabayan, Kibungan, Buguias, and Ampusungan, Sub-province of Benguet; Municipal District of Kiangan, Sub-province of Ifugao; and Municipal Districts of Bauko, Banaao and Sabangan, Subprovince of Bontoc; Mountain Province, Island of Luzon; and pursuant to the provisions “of section one of Act Numbered Thirty-nine hundred and fifteen, entitled “An Act providing for the establishment of national parks, declaring such parks as game refuges, and for other purposes,” I hereby reserve, set apart and designate as “Baguio—Bontoc Scenic National Park” under the administration of the Bureau of Forestry, subject to the general executive control and supervision of the said Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce for park purposes for the benefit and enjoyment of the people of the Philippines and withdraw from sale, settlement, or other disposition, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the municipal districts .of Tublay, Atok, Bokod, Kabayan, Kibungan, Buguias, and Ampusungan, subprovince of Benguet; municipal district of Kiangan, subprovince of Ifugao; and municipal districts of Bauko, Banaao, and!
