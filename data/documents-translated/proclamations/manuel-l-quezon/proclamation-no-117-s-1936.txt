PROCLAMATION NO. 117

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF CARABALAN, MUNICIPALITY OF HIMAMAYLAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL, ISLAND OF NEGROS.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-one of Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and sschool purposes, under the administration of the Director of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the Barrio of Carabalan, Municipality of Himamaylan, Province of Negros Occidental, Island of Negros, and particularly described in Bureau of Lands plan Bsd-5233, to wit:

Lot No. 2297-A. —A parcel of land (lot No. 2297-A of the subdivision plan Bsd-5233, being a portion of lot No. 2297 of the cadastral survey of Himamaylan, G. L. R. O. Cadastral Record No. 168), situated in the barrio of southern Negros Forest Zone, municipality of Himamaylan, Province of Negros Occidental. Bounded on all sides by lot No. 2297-B of the subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 79 54’ E., 3,817.33 m., more or less, from B. B. M. No. 24, Himamaylan Cadastre; thence S. 83 22’ W., 99.99 m. to point “2;” thence N. 6 39’ W., 99.99 m. to point “3;” thence N. 83 21’ E., 100.00 m. to point “4;” thence S. 6 38’ E., 100.00 m. to point of beginning; containing an area of ten thousand (10,000) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan; and on the ground are marked by B. L. concrete monuments 15 by 60 cm.; bearings true; declination, 1 30’ E.; date of the original survey, October, 1915-September, 1916, and that of the subdivision survey, April 30, 1931.

NOTE.—Lot No. 2297-A=LotNo. 2332 of Himamaylan Cadastre No. 82, case No. 1.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this seventh day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-six, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the second.

President of the Philippines
