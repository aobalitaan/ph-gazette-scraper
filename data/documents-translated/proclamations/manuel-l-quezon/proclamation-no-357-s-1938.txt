MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 367

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL PURPOSES A PARCEL OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATION IN THE BARRIO OF LUYANG, MUNICIPALITY OF CARMEN, PROVINCE OF CEBU, ISLAND OF CEBU.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce and pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-three of Commonwealth Act Numbered One hundred and-forty-one, I hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for school purposes, under the administration of the Bureau of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, the following parcel of the public domain, situated in the barrio of Luyang, municipality, of Carmen, Province of Cebu, Island of Cebu, and particularly described in the Bureau of Lands plan Swo-15383, to wit:

Lot No. 4-C. —A parcel of land (lot No. 4-C of the subdivision plan Bsd-1047, being a portion ,of lot No. 4 described on the original plan 11-5044; G. L. R. O. Record No. situated in the barrio of Luyang, municipality of Carmen, Province of Cebu.—Bounded on the N., by property of Maximina Bonhok; on the E., by properties of Ignacio Porsada and Victorino Pepito; on the SE., by lot No. 4-B of the subdivision plan; on the SW., by property of Tomas Osmeña; and on, the NW., by road. In witness whereof, I have Hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this seventeenth day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the fourth.
