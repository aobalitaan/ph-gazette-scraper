PROCLAMATION NO. 275

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 569, DATED APRIL 6, 1933

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Commerce, I hereby amend Proclamation Numbered Five hundred and sixty-nine, dated April sixth, nineteen hundred and thirty-three, to read as follows:

“Pursuant to the provisions of section eighty-one of Act Numbered Twenty-eight hundred and seventy-four, as amended, I hereby reserve, under the administration of the Director of Education, for school purposes. Subject to private rights if any there be, and withdraw from sale or settlement the following-described parcel of the public domain, situated in the Poblacion, municipality of Rizal, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon, and described in Bureau of Lands plan Mr-238-Amd, to wit:

“Beginning at a point marked 1 on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-238-Amd., N. 7° 58’ W. 155.21 m., more or less, from B. L. L. M. No. 1, municipality of rizal, thence s. 61° 27’ W. 91.31 m. to point 2; N. 32° 13’ w. 69.67 m. to point 3; N. 65° 49’ E. 142.74 m. to point 3a; N. 26° 56’ w. 46.46 m. to point 3b; S. 68° 12’ W. 89.86 m. to point 3c; N. 26° 53’ W. 1.21 m. to point 5; N. 35° 06’ W. 85.58 m. to point 6; N. 40° 26’ E. 119.98 m. to point 7; N. 57° 59’ E. 59.90 m. to point 8; S. 18° 15’ E. 194.54 m. to point 9; S. 60° 24’ W. 84.04 m. to point 10; S. 26° 34’ E. 55.68 m. to point 1, point of beginning.

“Containing an area of 27,345 square meters.

“Point 3, a pyramidical shaped concrete monument; points 3a and 3b, old P. L. S. concrete monuments; point 3c, intersection; points 5 and 10, P. L. S. concrete monuments; and the rest are B. L. concrete monuments.

“Bounded on the northeast, by properties of Candido Gandan and Alfonso Palmera; on the southeast by property of Alfonso Palmera, municipal street, and lot No. 4 of plan Psu-14359; on the southwest, by municipal street, lot No. 4 of plan Psu-14359, and property of Candido Blaquera; and on the northwest, by lot No. 4 of plan Psu-14359 and public land.

“Bearings true. Declination 0° 02’ E.

“Points referred to are marked on Bureau of Lands plan Mr-238-Amd.

“Surveyed: February 13, 1932.

“Approved: September 25, 1937.

“In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Government of the Philippine Islands to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this sixth day of April, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-three.

“[SEAL]                                                                                  JOHN H. HOLIDAY

“Acting Governor-General”

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Commonwealth of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done at the City of Manila, this nineteenth day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.
