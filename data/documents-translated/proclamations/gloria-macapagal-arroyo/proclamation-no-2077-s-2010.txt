MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2077

DECLARING FRIDAY, JUNE 18, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF IFUGAO

WHEREAS , Friday, June 18, 2010, the Province of Ifugao marks the celebration of the annual “Gotad Ad Ifugao”, its Foundation Day anniversary;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ifugao be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LEANDRO R. MENDOZA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, June 18, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEANDRO R. MENDOZA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) AGUSTIN S. DIZON Deputy Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs
