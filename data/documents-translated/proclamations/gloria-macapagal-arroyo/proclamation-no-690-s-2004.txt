MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 690

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 1, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF JASAAN, MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS , Wednesday, September 1, 2004, marks the 56 th anniversary of the Municipality of Jasaan, Misamis Oriental; and

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Jasaan, Misamis Oriental be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, September 1, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Jasaan, Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
