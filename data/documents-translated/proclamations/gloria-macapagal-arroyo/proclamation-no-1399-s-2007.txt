MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1399

CREATING AND DESIGNATING TWO (2) PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY LOMA, BIÑAN, LAGUNA, AS AN INCLUSION TO THE EXISTING LAGUNA TECHNOPARK III, SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the “Special Economic Zone Act of 1995,” as amended by Republic Act No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 07-147 dated 18 April 2007 of the PEZA Board of Directors, an area located at Barangay Loma, Biñan, Laguna, as an inclusion to the existing Laguna Technopark III Special Economic Zone, consisting of TWENTY SEVEN THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED THREE (27,703) Square Meters , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
