BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NO. 1726

RESERVING AS MUNICIPAL TOURISM SITE A CERTAIN PARCEL OF
FORESHORE LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN

BARANGAYS 4, 10 AND 11, MUNICIPALITY OF BALAYAN,
PROVINCE OF BATANGAS, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of
Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by
law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the
Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserve for
municipal tourism site purposes under the administration, supervision and control °
of the Municipality of Balayan, Batangas, subject to private rights, if any there
be, a certain parcel of foreshore land of the public domain situated at Barangays
4, 10 and 11, Municipality of Balayan, Province of Batangas, Island of Luzon, and
more particularly described as follows:
