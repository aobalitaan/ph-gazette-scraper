MALACAÑANG Manila BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

PROCLAMATION NO. 401

DECLARING THURSDAY, JUNE 12, 2003, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN TACLOBAN CITY

WHEREAS, “Independence Day” which is observed as regular holiday on June 12 of each year recognizes the patriotism and heroism of our countrymen, who struggled for freedom and democracy;

WHEREAS, the commemoration of “Independence Day” falls on a Thursday this year;

WHEREAS, by virtue of Proclamation No. 373, series of 2003, June 12, 2003 was declared a regular working day;

WHEREAS, June 12, 2003 marks the 50 th Charter Day anniversary in Tacloban City;

WHEREAS, The City Government of Tacloban has lined up activities in connection with its 50 th Charter Day anniversary to enliven the spirits of its constituents:

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of Tacloban City be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, June 12, 2003, as a special (non-working) day in Tacloban City, provided that the historical significance of “Independence Day” is maintained.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
