PROCLAMATION NO. 1497

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JUNE 11, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF RIZAL

WHEREAS, Wednesday, June 11, 2008, marks the 107 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Rizal;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Rizal be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THERFORE I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, June 11, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have here unto set my hand and caused seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
