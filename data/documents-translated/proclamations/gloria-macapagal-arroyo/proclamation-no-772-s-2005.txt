MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 772

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 483 DATED OCTOBER 28, 1968, WHICH RESERVED A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN POBLACION, TAGUM CITY FOR CHILDREN’S PARK AND PLAYGROUND PURPOSES BY GRANTING OWNERSHIP OF THE SAME IN FAVOR OF THE CITY GOVERNMENT OF TAGUM

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by the virtue of the powers vested in me by the law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 483 dated October 28, 1968, which reserved a parcel of land located in Poblacion, Tagum City for children’s park and playground purposes by granting ownership of the same in favor of the City Government of Tagum, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 1330, CAD 276

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 1330, Cad 276, Tagum Cadastre), located in Poblacion, Tagum City, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao.
