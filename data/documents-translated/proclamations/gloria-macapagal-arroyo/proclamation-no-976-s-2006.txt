MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 976

DECLARING FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 3, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN

WHEREAS , Friday, February 3, 2006, marks the 56 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Cabanatuan;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Cabanatuan be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, February 3, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the City of Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
