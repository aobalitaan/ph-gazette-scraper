MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 673

DECLARING MONDAY, AUGUST 2, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF KABANKALAN

WHEREAS , Monday, August 2, 2004, marks the 7 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Kabankalan;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Kabankalan be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, August 2, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of Kabankalan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
