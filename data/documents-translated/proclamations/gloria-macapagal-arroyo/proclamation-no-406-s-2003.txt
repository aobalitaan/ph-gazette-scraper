MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 406

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND, CONTAINING AN AGGREGATE AREA OF NINE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED FIFTEEN (9,115) SQUARE METERS, FOR BUILDING SITE PURPOSES OF THE PHILIPPINE POSTAL CORPORATION (PPC), SITUATED IN THE DISTRICT OF ERMITA, CITY OF MANILA, IN ACCORDANCE WITH SECTION 83, TITLE V, CHAPTER XII OF COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 141, OTHERWISE KNOWN AS THE “PUBLIC LAND ACT”

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve certain parcels of land, containing an aggregate area of NINE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED FIFTEEN (9,115) SQUARE METERS, for building site purposes of the Philippine Postal Corporation (PPC), situated in the District of Ermita, City of Manila, in accordance with Section 83, Title V, Chapter XII of Commonwealth Act No. 141, otherwise known as the “Public Land Act”, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot 1, Sgs-00-000016 Republic of the Philippines (Philippine Postal Corporation)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 1 of the segregation survey, as described on plan Sgs-00-000016), situated in District of Ermita, City of Manila, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the SE., along lines 1 to 7 by Road (Sta. Cruz Bridge), on the SE. & SW., along lines 7 to 14 by existing road; and on the NW., along line 14 to 18-1 by Lot 1, Block 301, Manila Cadastre, {Philippine Postal Corporation (Portion)}

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 19 deg. 00″ W., 1537.38 meters from BLLM No. 47, Cad. 13, Manila Cadastre; thence

beginning, containing an area of FIVE THOUSAND SIX HUNDRED NINETY SIX (5,696) Square Meters , more or less. All points referred to are indicated on plan and marked on the ground by G.I. Spike on conc. Wall/pavement except pt. 14 to 1 by old points.

Bearings true; date of original survey That of the segregation; date of survey, September 25-29, October 2-6, 23, 25 & 27, 2000 and approved on June 8, 2001.
