MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1299

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 18, 2007, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MATI, DAVAO ORIENTAL

WHEREAS , Commission on Elections will conduct the plebiscite on Monday, June 18, 2007, to convert the Municipality of Mati, Davao Oriental into a component city to be known as the City of Mati;

WHEREAS , in order to give the people of the Municipality of Mati ample time to exercise their right to vote, there is a need to declare Monday, June 18, 2007 as special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, June 18, 2007, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Mati, Davao Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
