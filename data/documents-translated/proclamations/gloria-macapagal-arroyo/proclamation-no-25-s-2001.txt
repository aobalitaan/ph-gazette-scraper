MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 25

DECLARING SATURDAY, APRIL 14, 2001, AS A SPECIAL DAY THROUGHOUT THE COUNTRY

WHEREAS , April 14, 2001, falls on a Saturday, between Good Friday and Easter Sunday;

WHEREAS , the said day, April 14, 2001, may be declared a special day without detriment to public service to allow our people uninterrupted opportunity to observe and ponder on the significance of Lent.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Saturday, April 14, 2001, as a special day throughout the country.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and one.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) RENATO S. DE VILLA Executive Secretary
