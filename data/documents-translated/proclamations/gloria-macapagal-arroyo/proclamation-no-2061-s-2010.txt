MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2061

DECLARING FRIDAY, MAY 21, 2010 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN GINGOOG CITY

WHEREAS , Friday, May 21, 2010, the City of Gingoog marks the celebration of the feast of its patron saint, Sta. Rita de Casia;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Gingoog be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LEANDRO R. MENDOZA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL‑ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, May 21, 2010, as special (non-working) day in Gingoog City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of May , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEANDRO R. MENDOZA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) AGUSTIN S. DIZON Deputy Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs
