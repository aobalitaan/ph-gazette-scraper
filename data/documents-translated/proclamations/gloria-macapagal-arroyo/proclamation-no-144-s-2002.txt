MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 144

SEGREGATING CERTAIN AREA FROM THE PARCEL OF LAND COVERED BY TRANSFER CERTIFICATE OF TITLE NO. 6735 UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF MANILA INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT AUTHORITY BY VIRTUE OF EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 903 s. 1983 SITUATED IN BARRIO BALAGBAG, SAN ROQUE AND MARICABAN, CITY OF PASAY AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN TO DISPOSITION TO QUALIFIED APPLICANTS IN ACCORDANCE WITH ACT NO. 3038, IN RELATION TO THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT, COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 141, AS AMENDED

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby segregate certain areas from the parcel of land covered by Transfer Certificate of Title No. 6735 now under the administration of Manila International Airport Authority by virtue of Executive Order No. 903 promulgated on July 21, 1983 and declaring the same open to disposition to qualified applicants in accordance with Act No. 3038, in relation to the provision of the Public Land Act, Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended. The specific portions herein segregated subject to final actual survey, are described as follows:

Such part or parts of the area herein declared open for disposition under Act 3038, in relation to the provisions of the Public Land Act, as amended, which are being used or earmarked for public or quasi-public purposes shall be excluded from such disposition except in favor of the government or any of each branches or agencies. All lands disposed of under this proclamation shall not be subject to alienation and encumbrances for a term of ten (10) years from the issuance of the title in case of sale, or execution of contract in case of lease, nor shall they become liable to the satisfaction of any debt contracted prior to the expiration of said period; but the improvements on the land may be mortgaged to qualified persons, associations or corporations.

Existing roads or proposed roads within the area shall conform with the provisions of Executive Order No. 113, series of 1995.

The Department of Environment and Natural Resources, National Capital Region shall conduct or supervise the actual boundary survey and subdivision survey of the proclaimed area. In the execution of the subdivision survey of the proclaimed area, open spaces for parks, playgrounds, barangay hall, school site and sufficient roads and alleys for the convenience of the residents thereat shall be strictly provided.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of January , in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
