PROCLAMATION NO. 1905

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AS MUNICIPAL POWER GENERATOR SITE PURPOSES, SITUATED IN BARANGAY BAGUMBAYAN, MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF ORIENTAL MINDORO, ISLAND OF MINDORO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or disposition and thereby reserve as municipal power generator site purposes, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, under the administration of the Municipal Government of Roxas, containing an area of Four Hundred Thirteen (413) Square Meters, situated in Barangay Bagumbayan, Municipality of Roxas, Province of Oriental Mindoro, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

“Lot No. 514, (Psu-0136217, Lot 2, Amd. Port.) Cad-506-D

Roxas Cadastre

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot No. 514, of the Cadastral Survey Cad 506-D), situated in Barangay Bagumbayan, Municipality of Roxas, Province of Oriental Mindoro, Province of Mindoro, Island of Mindoro. Bounded on the Southwest along line 1-2 by Lot 526, Psu-136217, (Lot 3, Amd.) (Acc); and on the Northwest and Northeast, Southeast along lines 2-3-4-1 by Lot 513, Psu, 136217 (Lot 2 Amd.) (Port.)

This plan Cad-506-D was surveyed on November 15 to December 07, 1994 by Elipidio E. Atienza, Geodetic Engineer, in accordance with the law and existing regulations promulgated thereunder, and was approved on September 26, 1995.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 30 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
