MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 951

DECLARING THURSDAY, DECEMBER 8, 2005 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TAGUIG

WHEREAS , Thursday, December 8, 2005, marks the 1 st Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Taguig;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Taguig be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, December 8, 2005 as special (non-working) day in the City of Taguig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Five.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
