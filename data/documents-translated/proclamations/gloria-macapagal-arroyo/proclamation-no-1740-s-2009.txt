PROCLAMATION NO. 1740

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 303 DATED SEPTEMBER 30, 1991 WHICH RESERVED CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN IN POBLACION, COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE, DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO FOR OFFICE, WAREHOUSE, DRIER, AND OTHER FACILITIES SITE PURPOSES OF THE NATIONAL FOOD AUTHORITY, BY GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF NFA

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by the virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 803 dated September 30, 1991 which reserved certain parcels of land of the public domain located in Poblacion, Compostela Valley Province, Davao del Norte of Mindanao, for office, warehouse, drier, and other facilities site purposes of the National Food Authority (NFA), by granting ownership thereof in favor of NFA subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
