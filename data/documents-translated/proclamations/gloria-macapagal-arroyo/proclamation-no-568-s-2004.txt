MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 568

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, MARCH 10, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BUKIDNON

WHEREAS , Wednesday, March 10, 2004, marks the 87 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Bukidnon;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their province’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, March 10, 2004,. as special (non-working) day in the Province of Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 5th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
