MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 142

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 16, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING HOLIDAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS , Wednesday, January 16, 2002, marks the 96th year Founding Anniversary of the Municipality of Navotas;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of Navotas be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Administrative  code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, January 16, 2002, as a special non-working holiday in the Municipality of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16 th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
