MALACAÑANG Manila

BY THE PRESIDENT

PROCLAMATION NO. 910

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 12, 2005, AS A NATIONAL DAY OF PRAYER, CONVERSION, REPARATION, AND CONSECRATION FOR OUR NATION

WHEREAS , the promotion of peace and unity among the Filipino people is one of the highest goals of the Government;

WHEREAS , this goal has all too often lost its relevance in our nation, which is beset by hostility, bitterness, constant conflict, and too much politics;

WHEREAS , our country is facing several challenges, which require the steadfast devotion of every Filipino to work for the common good and for the betterment of our country;

WHEREAS , particularly in these trying times, it is time to make a reaffirmation of our commitment to this goal, through personal reflection, change in attitudes, and solidarity for the public welfare and the development of our country;

WHEREAS , a day of prayer, conversion, reparation, and consecration may serve to promote the attainment of our national goals;

NOW, THEREFORE I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare September 12, 2005 as a National Day of Prayer, Conversion, Reparation, and Consecration for our Nation.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

City of Manila, 05 September 2005

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
