MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 495

RESERVING FOR THE BUREAU OF FISHERIES AND AQUATIC RESOURCES AS SITE FOR THE FRESHWATER DEMONSTRATION FISH FARM PURPOSES UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, LOCATED AT LIWAN WEST, RIZAL, KALINGA, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve for the Bureau of Fisheries and Aquatic Resources (BFAR) under the administration of Department of Agriculture (DA) as site for Freshwater Demonstration Fish Farm purposes, subject to actual survey, and withdraw the same from occupation, entry, sale, lease or other disposition unless further declared as alienable and disposable by the President which parcel of land is more particularly describes as follows:

LOT NO. 5595-B, Csd-2-05-003852 (Equivalent to Lot No. 8330, Pls-245)

A parcel of land (Lot-5595-B, Csd-2-05-003852) situated at Liwan West, Rizal, Kalinga, Island of Luzon.
