PROCLAMATION NO. 1480

DECLARING FRIDAY, JULY 25, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PLARIDEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Plaridel, Bulacan marks the celebration of the feast day of its Patron Saint St. John, the Apostle, the foundation day of the old parish and the foundation day of the Municipality on Friday, July 25, 2008;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Plaridel, Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, IGNACIO R. BUNYE, Acting Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, July 25, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Plaridel, Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set may hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
