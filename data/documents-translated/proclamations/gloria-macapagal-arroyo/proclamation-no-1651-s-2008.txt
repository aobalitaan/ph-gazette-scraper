PROCLAMATION NO. 1651

RESERVING AS SCHOOL SITE FOR ASUNCION CONSUNJI ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED IN BARANGAY IMELDA, MUNICIPALITY OF SAMAL, PROVINCE OF BATAAN, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of Asuncion Consunji Elementary School, under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain subject to private rights, if any there be, situated in Barangay Imelda, Municipality of Samal, Province of Bataan, Island of Luzon, and more particularly described as follows to wit:
