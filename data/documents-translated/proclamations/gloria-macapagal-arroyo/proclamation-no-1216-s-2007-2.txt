MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1216

DECLARING SATURDAY, JANUARY 20, 2007, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TARLAC

WHEREAS , Saturday, January 20, 2007, marks the 5th MALATARLAK FESTIVAL of the City of Tarlac that will showcase their City’s unique beauty and splendor;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tarlac be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Saturday, January 20, 2007, as special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
