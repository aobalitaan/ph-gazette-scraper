PROCLAMATION NO. 779

EXCLUDING CERTAIN AREAS COVERED BY EXISTING MINING TENEMENTS FROM THE QUIRINO PROTECTED LANDSCAPE PROCLAIMED UNDER PROCLAMATION NO. 548, DATED FEBRUARY 9, 2004

WHEREAS , Proclamation No. 548 entitled “Declaring the Municipalities of Diffun, Cabarroguis, Aglipay, Maddela and Nagtipunan, all in the Province of Quirino, as a Protected Area pursuant to R. A. No. 7586, also known as the National Integrated Protected Areas System (NIPAS) Act of 1992, which shall be known as the Quirino Protected Landscape,” was issued on February 9, 2004;

WHEREAS , the said Proclamation provides that the declaration of the subject parcel of land as protected area is subject to private rights;

WHEREAS , the Quirino Protected Landscape, (QPL) as per technical description, is occupied by valid and existing mining tenements;

NOW, THEREFORE , upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) and pursuant to the powers vested upon me by law I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby exclude from the coverage of the QPL the areas covered by valid and existing mining tenements, and consequently amend the boundaries and technical description of the QPL, which shall now be described as follows:
