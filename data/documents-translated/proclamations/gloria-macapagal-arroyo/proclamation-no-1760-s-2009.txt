PROCLAMATION NO. 1760

DECLARING SATURDAY, MAY 2, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, BATANGAS

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas celebrates its annual fiesta on May 2 & 3, 2008;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, CERGE M. REMONDE, Acting Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL–ARROYO, do hereby declare Saturday, May 2, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bauan, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
