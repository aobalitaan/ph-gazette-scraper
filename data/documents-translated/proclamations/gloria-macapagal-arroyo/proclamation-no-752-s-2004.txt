MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES PROCLAMATION NO. 752 CALLING THE CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINES TO A SPECIAL SESSION

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by the Constitution, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby call the Congress of the Philippines to a Special Session from January 5 to 7, 2005 to urgently consider the passage of the bill providing for optimum performance in revenue collection through the grant of special rewards for exemplary service as well as lateral attrition to revenue-generating agencies of government, and such other revenue-mobilization measures prioritized by the Legislative Executive Development Advisory Council (LEDAC) towards improving the country’s fiscal position and/or certified by this Office pursuant to the provisions of Section 26(2), Article VI of the Constitution.

In WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Baguio, this 29 th day of December in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
