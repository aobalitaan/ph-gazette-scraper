MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 225

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 18, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING DAY IN THE CITY OF TACURONG

WHEREAS , Wednesday, September 18, 2002, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Tacurong;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Tacurong be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 18, 2002, as a special non-working day in the City of Tacurong.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 29 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
