MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1150

DECLARING MONDAY, OCTOBER 9, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, PROVINCE OF SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS , Republic Act No. 8803 dated August 16, 2000, provided for the conversion of the Municipality of Koronadal into a component city to be known as the City of Koronadal and was ratified on October 8, 2000;

WHEREAS , Sunday, October 8, 2006, marks the 6 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Koronadal;

WHEREAS , to enable the people of the City of Koronadal to celebrate the occasion, the Office of the Mayor requested that Monday, October 9, 2006 be declared as special (non-working) day instead of October 8, 2006, which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal, Province of South Cotabato be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, October 9, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the City of Koronadal, Province of South Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
