PROCLAMATION NO. 1734

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR OFFICE SITE PURPOSES OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES (DENR) SITUATED IN THE CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF DENR

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserved for DENR-PENR Office and CENR Office Site purposes of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of the public domain situated in Tagum City, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows:
