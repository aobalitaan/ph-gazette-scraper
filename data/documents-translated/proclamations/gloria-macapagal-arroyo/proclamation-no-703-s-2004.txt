Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

PROCLAMATION NO. 703

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 23, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVAL, PROVINCE OF BILIRAN

WHEREAS , Thursday, September 23, 2004, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Naval, Biliran; and

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Naval, Biliran, be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their Municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Acting Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, September 23, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Naval, Province of Biliran, to be known as “Pueblo Day.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
