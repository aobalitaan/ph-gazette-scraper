PROCLAMATION NO. 1856

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 19, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ANGONO, RIZAL

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Angono, Rizal will celebrate its 71 st year of independence as a municipality on Wednesday, August 19, 2009;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Angono, Rizal, the opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies, it is but fitting to declare August 19, 2009 as special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, August 19, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Angono, Rizal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
