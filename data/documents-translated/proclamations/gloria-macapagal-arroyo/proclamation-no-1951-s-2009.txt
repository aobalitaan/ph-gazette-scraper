PROCLAMATION NO. 1951

DECLARING FRIDAY, DECEMBER 4, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PALAYAN, NUEVA ECIJA

WHEREAS, December 1 to 5, 2009, marks the 44 th Foundation Anniversary of the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija the opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare December 4, 2009 as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, December 4, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the City of Palayan, Nueva Ecija.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
