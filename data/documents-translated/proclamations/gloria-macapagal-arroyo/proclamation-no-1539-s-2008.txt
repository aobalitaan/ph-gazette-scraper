PROCLAMATION NO. 1539

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THE ST. LUKE’S MEDICAL CENTER BONIFACIO GLOBAL CITY, LOCATED ALONG RIZAL DRIVE CORNER 5 TH AVENUE AND 32 ND STREET, FORT BONIFACIO GLOBAL CITY, TAGUIG CITY, METRO MANILA, AS MEDICAL TOURISM CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Medical Tourism Center, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolutions Nos. 06-512 and 07-146 dated 14 November 2006 and 18 April 2007, respectively, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), the St. Luke’s Medical Center Bonifacio Global City, to be established on a lot located along Rizal Drive corner 5th Avenue and 32nd Street, Fort Bonifacio Global City, Taguig City, Metro Manila, containing an area of SIXTEEN THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED FORTY ONE (16,741) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
