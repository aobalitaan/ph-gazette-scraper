MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 235

DECLARING TUESDAY, AUGUST 20, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING DAY IN THE CITY OF LUCENA

WHEREAS , Tuesday, August 20, 2002, marks the 41 st Charter Day anniversary of the City of Lucena;

WHEREAS , it is only proper to give the people of the City of Lucena full opportunity to commemorate the occasion with fitting ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Tuesday, August 20, 2002, as a special non-working day in the City of Lucena.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
