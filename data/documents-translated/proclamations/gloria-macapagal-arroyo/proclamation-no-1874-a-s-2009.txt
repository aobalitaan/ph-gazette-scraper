PROCLAMATION NO. 1874-A

DECLARING SEPTEMBER 7, 2009, THE DAY OF INTERMENT OF KA ERAÑO G. MANALO AS A DAY OF NATIONAL MOURNING

WHEREAS, on September 3. 2009, as a consequence of the death of Ka Eraño “Erdy” Manalo, the President signed Proclamation No.  1874 ”Declaring a Period of National Prayer For and Remembrance of the Late Ka Eraño G. Manalo, Executive Minister of lglesia ni Cristo “;

WHEREAS, the Iglesia ni Cristo has determined that September 7, 2009, Monday, is the date of interment of the late Ka Erdy Manalo;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the power vested in me by law, do hereby proclaim September 7, 2009, Monday, the day of interment of the late Ka Eraño Manalo, as a day of national mourning. On this day, flags of all government buildings and installations shall be flown at half-mast from sunrise to sunset.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4 th day of September 2009, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:
