MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1030

DECLARING FRIDAY, JUNE 9, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS , Friday, June 9, 2006, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, June 9, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
