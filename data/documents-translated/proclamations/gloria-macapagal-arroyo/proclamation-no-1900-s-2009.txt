PROCLAMATION NO. 1900

RESERVING FOR HOSPITAL SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AS OSPITAL NG PALAWAN SITUATED IN BARANGAY SAN MIGUEL, PUERTO PRINCESA CITY, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserved for Ospital ng Palawan, a certain parcel of land of the public domain, situated in Barangay San Miguel, Puerto Princesa City, Province of Palawan, Island of Palawan, under the administration of the Department of Health, containing an area of SIXTEEN THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED NINETY NINE (16,799) SQUARE METERS, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
