MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 742

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 8, 2004 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, LA UNION

WHEREAS , Wednesday, December 8, 2004, marks the 426 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Agoo, La Union;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Agoo, La Union be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, December 8, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Agoo, La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By the President: ( Sgd .) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
