MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 961

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THE SANTA ROSA COMMERCIAL IT PARK SITUATED IN THE BARANGAY OF DON JOSE, CITY OF STA. ROSA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, ISLAND OF LUZON, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Park , subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolutions Nos. 00-411 and 05-191 dated 29 December 2000 and 29 June 2005 respectively, of the PEZA Board of Directors, the Santa Rosa Commercial IT Park located at Barangay Don Jose, Sta. Rosa City, Laguna, consisting an area of TEN THOUSAND NINE HUNDRED FORTY (10,940) SQUARE METERS, MORE OR LESS, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
