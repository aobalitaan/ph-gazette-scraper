PROCLAMATION NO. 1848

DECLARING SATURDAY, AUGUST 1, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF COMPOSTELA, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS, Saturday, August 1, 2009, marks the 61 st Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GABRIEL S. CLAUDIO, Acting Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Saturday, August 1, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Compostela, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31 st day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
