MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 271

DECLARING TUESDAY, OCTOBER 8, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING DAY IN THE CITY OF KORONADAL, SOUTH COTABATO

WHEREAS , Republic Act No. 8803 dated August 16, 2000, provides for the conversion of the Municipality of Koronadal into a component city to be known as the City of Koronadal and was ratified on October 8, 2000;

WHEREAS , Tuesday, October 8, 2002, marks the 2 nd Charter Day anniversary of the City of Koronadal;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Koronadal be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Tuesday, October 8, 2002, as a special non-working day in the City of Koronadal.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 ST day of October, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
