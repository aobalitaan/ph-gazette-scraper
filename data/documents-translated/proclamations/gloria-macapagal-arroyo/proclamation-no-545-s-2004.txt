MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 545

DECLARING TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 3, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS , Tuesday, February 3, 2004, marks the birth anniversary of the late Blas F. Ople ;

WHEREAS , Blas F. Ople , Secretary of Foreign Affairs, former Senate President and Secretary of Labor, had devoted decades of his life to selfless, dedicated and sincere service to our country and our people;

WHEREAS , the late Blas F. Ople , a son of Bulacan, has been a model to the Filipino people especially to the “Bulakenyos”;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that at the people of the Province of Bulacan be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Tuesday, February 3, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 30th day of January , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: ( Sgd .) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
