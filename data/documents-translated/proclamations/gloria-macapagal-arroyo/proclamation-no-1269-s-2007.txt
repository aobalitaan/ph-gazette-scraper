PROCLAMATION NO. 1269

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED AT FORT BONIFACIO GLOBAL CITY, TAGUIG, METRO MANILA, AS “INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK” PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748
