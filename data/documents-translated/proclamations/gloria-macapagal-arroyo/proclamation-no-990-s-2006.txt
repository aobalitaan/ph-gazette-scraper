PROCLAMATION NO . 990

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND SITUATED AT E. RODRIGUEZ, JR. AVENUE, BARANGAY UGONG NORTE, QUEZON CITY, METRO MANILA, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Park, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004 and Resolution No. 05-190 dated 29 June 2005, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), a parcel of land located at E. Rodriguez, Jr. Avenue, Barangay Ugong Norte, Quezon City, Metro Manila, containing an area of TEN THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY SQUARE METERS AND SEVENTY SQUARE DECIMETERS (10,180.70) more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
