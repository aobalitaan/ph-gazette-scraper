MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 322

DECLARING TUESDAY, JULY 1, 2003, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MONCADA, TARLAC

WHEREAS, Tuesday, July 1, 2003, marks the Foundation Day Anniversary of the Municipality of Moncada, Tarlac;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the Municipality of Moncada, Tarlac be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Tuesday, July 1, 2003, as a special day in the Municipality of Moncada, Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
