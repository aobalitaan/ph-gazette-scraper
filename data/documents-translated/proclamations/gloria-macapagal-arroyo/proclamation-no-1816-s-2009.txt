PROCLAMATION NO. 1816

RESERVING AS SCHOOL SITE FOR PEÑABLANCA EAST NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY LAGUM, MUNICIPALITY OF PEÑABLANCA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, ISLAND OF LUZON AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES – DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL – ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of Peñablanca East National High School and grant ownership thereof in favor of the Republic of the Philippines – Department of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain, situated in Barangay Lagum, Municipality of Peñablanca, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon, and more particularly described as follows:
