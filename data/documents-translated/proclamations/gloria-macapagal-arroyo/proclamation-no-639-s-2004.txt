MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 639

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE PADADA CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED IN POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF PADADA, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Padada Central Elementary School under the administration of the Department of Education, certain parcels of land of the public domain located in Poblacion, Municipality of Padada, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao, subject to private rights, if there be any, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
