Malacañang Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 925

RESERVING AS A SCHOOL SITE FOR PURPOSES OF CONSTRUCTION OF SCHOOL BUILDINGS, TEACHERS’ DORMITORY, AND OTHER PUBLIC PURPOSES, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF GOVERNMENT LAND LOCATED AT THE CORNER OF NUMANCIA AND URBIZTONDO STREETS, BINONDO, MANILA

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve and declare as a school site for purposes of construction of school buildings, teachers’ dormitory, and for other public purposes, in accordance with the provisions of the Public Land Act (C.A. 141), as amended, and the Administrative Code of 1987 a certain parcel of government land located at the corner of Numancia and Urbiztondo Streets, Binondo, Manila, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to actual ground survey, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

All corners not otherwise described are old points; date of subdivision; survey June 12-14, 1978 and approved on October 1978.

The DENR-NCR shall supervise the conduct of the boundary survey of the proclaimed area.

The area proclaimed herein shall be under the administration of the City Government of Manila; Provided, That the administration of the school buildings and teachers’ dormitory constructed therein shall be done in coordination with the Department of Education.

An inter-agency committee is hereby created, with the Mayor of the City Government of Manila as Chair, and representatives from the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and Department of Education as members, to formulate the implementing rules and regulations of this proclamation.

IN WITNESS THEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 4th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Five.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
