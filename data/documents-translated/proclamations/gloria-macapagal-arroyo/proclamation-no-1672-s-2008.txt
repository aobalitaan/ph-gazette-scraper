PROCLAMATION NO. 1672

DECLARING TUESDAY, DECEMBER 2, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN PASAY CITY

WHEREAS, Tuesday, December 2, 2008, marks the 145 th Foundation Day anniversary of Pasay City;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of Pasay City be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPACAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Tuesday, December 2, 2008, as special (non-working) day in Pasay City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21 st day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
