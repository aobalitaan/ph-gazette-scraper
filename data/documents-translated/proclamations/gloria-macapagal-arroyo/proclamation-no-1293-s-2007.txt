MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1293

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JULY 25, 2007, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PLARIDEL, PROVINCE OF BULACAN

WHEREAS , Wednesday, July 25, 2007, marks the foundation day anniversary of the Municipality of Plaridel, Province of Bulacan and the feast day of St. John, the Apostle, its patron saint;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Plaridel, Province of Bulacan, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, July 25, 2007, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Plaridel, Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of June , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
