MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 617

RESERVING FOR MULTI-PURPOSE PLAZA SITE PURPOSES A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN CAGANGANAN, BANAYBANAY, DAVAO ORIENTAL

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from the sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for Plaza site purposes under the administration of the Local Government Unit, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at Caganganan, Banaybanay, Davao Oriental, Island of Mindanao, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 56, TS-349 (Caganganan Multi-Purpose Plaza)

A parcel of land Lot No. 56, TS-349, situated in the Municipality of Banaybanay, Province of Davao Oriental. Bounded on the North East; along lines 8-1-2 by Road Lot 4, on the South East; along lines 2-3-4 by Road 5, on the South West; along lines 4-5-6 by Road Lot 20, on the North West; along lines 6-7-8 by Road Lot 6 all of TS-349. Beginning from point marked “1” on plan being S51-52W., 7399.96 meters from B.L.L.M. No. 1, Cad 353-D, Lupon Cadastre. Thence . . . .

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of April , in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.
