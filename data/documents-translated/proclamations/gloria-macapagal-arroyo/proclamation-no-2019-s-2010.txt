PROCLAMATION NO. 2019

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED ALONG BAY CITY, PARAÑAQUE CITY, AS TOURISM ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748
