PROCLAMATION NO. 2040

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, APRIL 21, 2010 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA

WHEREAS, Wednesday, April 21, 2010, marks the 9 th year anniversary of the City of Calamba;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the residents of the City of Calamba be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LEANDRO R. MENDOZA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, April 21, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19 th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Deputy Executive Secretary
