PROCLAMATION NO. 1945

DECLARING SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 28, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF STA. MARGARITA, PROVINCE OF SAMAR

WHEREAS, Saturday, November 28, 2009, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Sta. Margarita, Province of Samar;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Sta. Margarita, Province of Samar, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Saturday, November 28, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Sta. Margarita, Province of Samar.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 23 rd day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
