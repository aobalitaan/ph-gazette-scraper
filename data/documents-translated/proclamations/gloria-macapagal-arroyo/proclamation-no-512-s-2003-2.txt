MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 512

DECLARING MONDAY, DECEMBER 8, 2003, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, LA UNION

WHEREAS , Monday, December 8, 2003, marks the 425 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Agoo, La Union;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the residents of the Municipality of Agoo, La Union, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, December 8, 2003, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Agoo, La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 5 th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
