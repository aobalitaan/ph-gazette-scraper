MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 307

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN DIPOLOG CITY AS CIVIL RESERVATION FOR FORESHORE DEVELOPMENT PURPOSES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby reserve as civil reservation for foreshore development purposes under the administration and management of the City Government of Dipolog, subject to actual survey, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Central Barangay and Barangay Miputak, Dipolog City, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

“A PARCEL OF LAND, described on plan Mr-09-00027, situated in Central Barangay and Barangay Miputak, City of Dipolog.
