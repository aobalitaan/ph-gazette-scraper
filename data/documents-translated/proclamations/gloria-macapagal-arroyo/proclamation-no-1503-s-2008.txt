PROCLAMATION NO. 1503

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING WHICH SHALL STAND ON TWO PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT ZUELLIG AVENUE, MANDAUE CITY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 07-467 dated 10 October 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Robinland Business Center as an Information Technology Center which shall stand on two parcels of land of the private domain located at Zuellig Avenue, Mandaue City Province of Cebu, containing an aggregate area of Two Thousand Five Hundred (2,500) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
