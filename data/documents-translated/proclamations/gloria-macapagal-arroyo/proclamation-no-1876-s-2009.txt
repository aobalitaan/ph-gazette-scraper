PROCLAMATION NO. 1876

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 9, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BAIS

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 9, 2009, the City of Bais marks its Charter Day anniversary;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Bais, the opportunity to join in the celebration of this momentous event in their city’s political history, it is but fitting and proper to declare September 9, 2009 as special (non-working) day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 9, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the City of Bais.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
