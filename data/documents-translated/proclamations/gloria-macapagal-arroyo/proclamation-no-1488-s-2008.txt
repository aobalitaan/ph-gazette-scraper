PROCLAMATION NO. 1488

DECLARING MONDAY, APRIL 21, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Monday, April 21, 2008, marks the 7 th year anniversary of the City of Calamba, Province of Laguna;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Calamba, Province of Laguna be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, IGNACIO R. BUNYE, Acting Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, April 21, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba, Province of Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
