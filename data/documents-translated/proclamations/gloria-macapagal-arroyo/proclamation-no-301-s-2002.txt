MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 301

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF PANABO CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY SAN FRANCISCO, MUNICIPALITY OF PANABO, PROVINCE OF DAVAO, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law , I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of Panabo Central Elementary School, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the Barangay of San Francisco, Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao, Island of Mindanao, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 207, Csd-60302 (PANABO CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot No. 207, Psd-60302, being a portion of Block 71, Psd-39001) situated in Barangay San Francisco, Municipality of Panabo, Province of Davao, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the NW., along lines 2-3-4 by Lot 204, Psd 60302; along lines 4-5 by Lot 2511, Cad-276; on the NE., along lines 5-6 by National Highway (60.00 m. wide); on the SE., along lines 6-7 by road Lot 9; and on the SW., along lines 7-8-1 by Lot 208; and along lines 1-2 by Lot 206, all of Psd-60302.
