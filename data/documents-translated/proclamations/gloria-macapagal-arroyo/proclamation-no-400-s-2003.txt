MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 400

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JULY 2, 2003, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF PASIG

WHEREAS, Wednesday, July 2, 2003 marks the 430 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Pasig;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Pasig be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, July 2, 2003, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Pasig.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
