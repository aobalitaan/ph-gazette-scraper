BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
PROCLAMATION NGC, 774

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 2, 2005 AS SPECIAL (NON-
WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CANDELARIA,
PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS, Wednesday, February 2, 2005 marks the centennial
anniversary of the arrival of Wuestra Sefiora De La Candelaria;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality
of Candelaria, Province of Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate this
milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by
authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby
declare Wednesday, February 2, 2005 as special (non-working) day in the
Municipality of Candelaria, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the
seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of January, in the year of Our
Lord, Two Thousand and Five.
