PROCLAMATION NO. 1870

DECLARING TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 29, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BACOOR, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Tuesday, September 29, 2009, marks the annual celebration of the “Araw ng Bacoor” of the Municipality of Bacoor, Province of Cavite;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Municipality of Bacoor, Province of Cavite, the opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare September 29, 2000 as special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Tuesday, September 29, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Bacoor, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 1 st day of September in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
