MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1146

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PROPOSED BUILDING WHICH SHALL BE ESTABLISHED ON TWO PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED ALONG LOPEZ JAENA STREET, SUBANGDAKU, MANDAUE CITY, PROVINCE OF CEBU, AS AN EXPANSION OF THE EXISTING KRC IT ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as an expansion of the existing KRC IT Zone, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolutions Nos. 00-411 and 05-417 dated 29 December 2000 and 28 November 2005, respectively, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), a proposed building which shall be established on two parcels of land located along Lopez Jaena Street, Subangdaku, Mandaue City, Province of Cebu, with an aggregate area of ONE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED NINE (1,109) SQUARE METERS , as defined by the following technical descriptions:
