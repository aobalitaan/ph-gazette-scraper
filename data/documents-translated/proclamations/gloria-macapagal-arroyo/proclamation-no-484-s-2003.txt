MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 484

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 416 DATED JUNE 29, 1994, WHICH ESTABLISHED THE PEÑABLANCA PROTECTED LANDSCAPE, BY EXPANDING ITS COVERAGE TO INCLUDE CERTAIN PARCELS OF LANDS OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED WITHIN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PEÑABLANCA, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, AND RE-NAMING THE SAME AS THE “PEÑABLANCA PROTECTED LANDSCAPE AND SEASCAPE”

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Proclamation No. 416 dated 29 June 1994 which established the “Peñablanca Protected Landscape” by expanding its coverage to include certain parcels of lands of the public domain situated in the Municipality of Peñablanca, Province of Cagayan, embracing the forest, agricultural, caves and coastal ecosystems therein and renaming the same as “Peñablanca Protected Landscape and Seascape” , subject to existing, recognized, and valid private rights and without prejudice to the rights of the indigenous people as provided for in RA 8371, DENR Administrative Order No. 93-02, and other relevant rules and regulations, if any there be, more particularly described in DENR NIPAS Map RO-02-05:

The subject protected area shall henceforth be known as the “Peñablanca Protected Landscape and Seascape” and shall be under the administrative jurisdiction of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources. The protected area shall likewise be managed in accordance with the provisions of Republic Act No. 7586 or the NIPAS Act of 1992.

All contracts, permits or licenses for the extraction or utilization of natural resources therein already prior to this Proclamation shall, subject to national interest, be respected until expiration.
