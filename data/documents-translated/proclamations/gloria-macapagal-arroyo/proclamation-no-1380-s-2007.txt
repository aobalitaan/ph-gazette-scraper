PROCLAMATION NO. 1380

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 27, 2007, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Thursday, September 27 2007, marks the 55 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Zamboanga del Sur;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Zamboanga del Sur be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Thursday, September 27, 7007, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Zamboanga del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
