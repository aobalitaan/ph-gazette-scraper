MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 651

RESERVING FOR USE AS SCHOOL SITE OF THE COLACLING HIGH SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY COLACLING, MUNICIPALITY OF LUPI, PROVINCE OF CAMARINES SUR, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdrawn from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for use as school site of Colacling High School under the administration of Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Colacling, Municipality of Lupi, Province of Camarines Sur, Island of Luzon, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:
