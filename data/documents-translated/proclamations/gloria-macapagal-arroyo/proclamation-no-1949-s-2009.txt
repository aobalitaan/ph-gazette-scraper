PROCLAMATION NO. 1949

DECLARING FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 27, 2009 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF SARANGANI

WHEREAS, the Province of Sarangani will celebrate its 17 th Foundation Day anniversary and 7 th Mulato Festival on November 23 to 28, 2009;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Province of Sarangani, the opportunity to celebrate the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlalawigan requested that November 27, 2009 be declared as special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, November 27, 2009 as special (non-working) day in the Province of Sarangani.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
