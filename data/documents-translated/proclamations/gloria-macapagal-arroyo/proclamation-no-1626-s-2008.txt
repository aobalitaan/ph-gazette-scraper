PROCLAMATION NO. 1626

CREATING AND DESIGNATING ETON CYBERPOD CORINTHIAN, SITUATED AT CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN LOCATED AT ORTIGAS AVENUE CORNER EPIFANIO DELOS SANTOS AVENUE, BARANGAY UGONG NORTE, QUEZON CITY, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create and designate the Eton Cyberpod Corinthian as Information Technology Park, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 08-127 dated 28 February 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, which shall be established on certain parcels of land of the private domain located at Ortigas Avenue corner Epifanio Delos Santos Avenue, Barangay Ugong Norte Quezon City, containing an aggregate area of Ten Thousand Eight Hundred Forty Six (10,846) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
