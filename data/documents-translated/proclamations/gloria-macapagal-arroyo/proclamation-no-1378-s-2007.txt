MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1378

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY FATIMA, MUNICIPALITY OF PANTABANGAN, PROVINCE OF NUEVA ECIJA, ISLAND OF LUZON FOR THE USE OF THE NATIONAL POWER CORPORATION (NPC)
