MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 612

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF SAN JUAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY SAN JUAN, MUNICIPALITY OF CABAGAN, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement, and reserve for school site purposes of San Juan Elementary School, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay San Juan, Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, Island of Luzon, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT NO. 973, PLS-834-D

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot No. 973, Pls-834-D) as surveyed for San Juan School Site, situated in Barangay San Juan, Municipality of Cabagan, Province of Isabela, Island of Luzon.
