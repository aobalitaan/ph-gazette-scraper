MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 469

DECLARING MONDAY, DECEMBER 8, 2003, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF TAAL, BATANGAS

WHEREAS , Monday, December 8, 2003, marks the 400 th Anniversary of Our Lady of Caysasay, the patroness of the Municipality of Taal; and

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the residents of the Municipality of Taal, Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, December 8, 2003, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Taal, Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 15 th day of Sept., in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
