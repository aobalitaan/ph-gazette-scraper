MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2008

DECLARING MONDAY, MARCH 8, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS , Monday, March 8, 2010, marks the “Bulawan Festival 2010”: 12 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Compostela Valley;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, March 8, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
