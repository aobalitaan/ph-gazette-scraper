PROCLAMATION NO. 2014

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AS OFFICE SITE PURPOSES FOR THE NATIONAL IRRIGATION ADMINISTRATION, SITUATED IN LAPU-LAPU ST., ZONE III, DIGOS CITY, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserved as office site purposes under the administration of the National Irrigation Administration (NIA), subject to private rights, if any there be certain parcel of land of the public domain, situated in Lapu-Lapu St., Zone III, Digos City, Province of Davao del Sur, Island of Mindanao, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follow, to wit:
