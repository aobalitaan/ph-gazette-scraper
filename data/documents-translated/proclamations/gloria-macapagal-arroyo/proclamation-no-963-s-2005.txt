MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 963

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THE ROBINSONS METRO BACOLOD BUILDING SITUATED ALONG ARANETA STREET, BARANGAY OF SINGCANG, CITY OF BACOLOD, ISLAND OF NEGROS, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY BUILDING PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO ; President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue, of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Building , subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolutions Nos. 00-411 and 05-391 dated 29 December 2000 and 26 October 2005 respectively, of the PEZA Board of Directors, the Robinsons Metro Bacolod Building, which stands on a 10-square meter lot, more or less, located along Araneta Street, Singcang, Bacolod City, as defined by the following technical descriptions:

“LOT 452-C (LRC) PSD-13171 T-276408

A parcel of land (Lot 452-C of the subdivision plan (LRC) Psd-13171, being a portion of Lot 452 of the Cadastral Survey of Bacolod, LRC (GLRO) Cad. Record No. 55, situated in the Barangay of Singcang (formerly within the territorial boundary of Barangay Tangub), City of Bacolod, Island of Negros. Bounded on the NE., SE., and SW., points 2 to 4 to 1 by Lot 452-D of the subdivision plan and on the NW., points 1 to 2 by Lot 452-B (Service Road) of the subdivision plan. Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 28 deg. 43′ W., 469.07 m. from B.L.L.M. 10, Bacolod Cadastre; thence

beginning; containing an area of TEN THOUSAND (10,000) SQUARE METERS , more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by P.S. cyl. conc. mons. 15×60 cm., declination 1 deg. 20′ E., date of the original survey, March 1913 – March 1914 and that of the subdivision survey, June 7 to June 11, 1960.”

IN WITNESS THEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed hereto.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 27th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Five.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
