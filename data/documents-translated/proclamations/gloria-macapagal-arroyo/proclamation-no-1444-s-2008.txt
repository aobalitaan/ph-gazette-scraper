MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1444

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 16, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF NAVOTAS

WHEREAS , Wednesday, January 16, 2008, marks the 102 nd Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Navotas;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Navotas be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, January 16, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the City of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
