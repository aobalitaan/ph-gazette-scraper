PROCLAMATION NO. 1682

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND IN BARANGAY BAGONG SILANG, CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, AS SITE FOR THE MEMORIAL MONUMENT OF THE LATE SPEAKER RAMON V. MITRA, JR. BY EXCLUDING THE SAME FROM A PARCEL OF LAND RESERVED AS LIGHTHOUSE STATION OF THE PHILIPPINE COAST GUARD PER EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 42 DATED 29 OCTOBER 1907

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserved a parcel of land situated in Bagong Silang, Puerto Princesa City, Province of Palawan, as site for the Memorial Monument of the late Speaker Ramon V. Mitra, Jr. by excluding the same from a parcel of land reserved as lighthouse station of the Philippine Coast Guard per Executive Order No. 42 dated 29 October 1907 subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 2032-A, Csd-04-025388

(equivalent to Lot No. 30978, Cad. 800-D, Puerto Princesa Cadastre)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot No. 30978, Cad. 800-D, Puerto Princesa Cadastre), situated in Barangay Bagong Silang, Puerto Princesa City, Island of Palawan. Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2 by Lot No. 2032-B, Csd-04-025388; on the SE., SW., and NW., along lines 2-3-4-5 by Puerto Princesa Bay, and on the NW., along line 5-1 by Lot No. 2032-B Csd-04-025388. The parcel of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease or other mode of disposition until again declared as alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of November the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
