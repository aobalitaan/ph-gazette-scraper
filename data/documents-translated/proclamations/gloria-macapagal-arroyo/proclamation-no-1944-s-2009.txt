PROCLAMATION NO. 1944

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 25, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF DASMARIÑAS, CAVITE

WHEREAS, Republic Act No. 9723, otherwise known as the “Charter of Dasmariñas” transformed the Municipality of Dasmariñas, Cavite into a component city, to be known as the City of Dasmariñas;

WHEREAS, the Commission on Elections will conduct the plebiscite on Wednesday, November 25, 2009;

WHEREAS, in order to fully involve and empower the constituents of Dasmariñas to participate and cast their votes in the said plebiscite, there is a need to declare Wednesday, November 25, 2009 as a special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, November 25, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Dasmariñas, Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 19 th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
