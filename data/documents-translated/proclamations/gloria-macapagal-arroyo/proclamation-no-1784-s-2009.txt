MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1784

RESERVING FOR AIRPORT SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND IN BARANGAY BANCAO-BANCAO, CITY OF PUERTO PRINCESA, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN, AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES – DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION AND COMMUNICATION (DOTC)

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ‑ ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or disposition and reserve for airport site purposes certain parcels of land of the public domain, situated in Bancao-Bancao, Puerto Princesa City, Province of Palawan and granting ownership thereof in favor of the Republic of the Philippines-Department of Transportation and Communication (RP-DOTC), containing an aggregate area of NINETEEN THOUSAND TWENTY FIVE (19,025) SQUARE METERS, more or less, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:

The Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) is hereby authorized to issue the corresponding Special Patent in favor of the Republic of the Philippines – Department of Transportation and Communications .

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 25 th day of MAY, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.
