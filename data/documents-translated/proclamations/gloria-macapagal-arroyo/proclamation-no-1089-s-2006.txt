MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1089

DECLARING TUESDAY, JUNE 20, 2006 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DAGUPAN

WHEREAS , Tuesday, June 20, 2006, marks the 59 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Dagupan;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Dagupan be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA , Acting Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Tuesday, June 20, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the City of Dagupan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) EDWIN R. ENRILE Undersecretary
