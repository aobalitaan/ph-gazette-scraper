PROCLAMATION NO. 1948

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR TOWN PLAZA AND MARKET SITES PURPOSES, RESPECTIVELY, FOR THE MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE’S POINT, PALAWAN, SITUATED IN BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE’S POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN, ISLAND OF PALAWAN

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or disposition and reserve as town plaza and market sites purposes for the Municipality of Brooke’s Point, Palawan certain parcels of land of the public domain, situated in Barangay Poblacion II, Municipality of Brooke’s Point, containing an aggregate area of NINETEEN THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED ELEVEN (19,411) SQUARE METERS, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
