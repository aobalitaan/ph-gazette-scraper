MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 414

DECLARING FRIDAY, JULY 11, 2003, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF VALENZUELA

WHEREAS, Friday, July 11, 2003, marks the 134 th birth anniversary of the City’s most illustrious son, the late Dr. Pio Valenzuela;

WHEREAS, it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Valenzuela, be given full opportunity to celebrate this event with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, July 11, 2003 as a special (non-working) day in the City of Valenzuela.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
