MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 152

DECLARING TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 26, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING DAY IN THE CITY OF ZAMBOANGA

WHEREAS , Tuesday, February 26, 2002, marks the 65th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Zamboanga;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of the City of Zamboanga be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Tuesday, February 26, 2002, as a special non-working day in the City of Zamboanga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
