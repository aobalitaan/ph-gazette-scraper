MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 566

DECLARING FRIDAY, MAY 7, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN PABLO

WHEREAS , Friday, May 7, 2004, marks the 64 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of San Pablo;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Pablo be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, May 7, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of San Pablo.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 4th day of March , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
