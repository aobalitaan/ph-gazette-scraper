MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 536

DECLARING TUESDAY, JANUARY 20, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF TARLAC

WHEREAS , Tuesday January 20, 2004, marks the 2 nd MALATARLAK FESTIVAL of the City of Tarlac that will showcase their City’s unique beauty and splendor;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of the City of Tarlac be given full opportunity to participate in the celebration.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Tuesday, January 20, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of Tarlac.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 19th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
