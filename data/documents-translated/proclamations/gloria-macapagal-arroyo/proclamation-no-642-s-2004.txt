MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 642

DECLARING FRIDAY, JUNE 11, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAGAY, PROVINCE OF NEGROS OCCIDENTAL

WHEREAS , Republic Act No. 8192 converting the Municipality of Sagay into a component city of the Province of Negros Occidental, was signed on June 11, 1996;

WHEREAS , Friday, June 11, 2004, marks the 8 th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Sagay;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Sagay be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, June 11, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of Sagay, Province of Negros Occidental.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
