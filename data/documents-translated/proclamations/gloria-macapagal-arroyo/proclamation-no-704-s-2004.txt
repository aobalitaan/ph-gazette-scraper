MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 704

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 30, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MASBATE

WHEREAS , Thursday, September 30, 2004, marks the 4 th Charter Day anniversary of the Cityhood of Masbate;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Masbate, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Acting Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, September 30, 2004, as special (non-working) day in Masbate City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of September , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
