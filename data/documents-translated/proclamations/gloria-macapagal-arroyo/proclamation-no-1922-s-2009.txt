PROCLAMATION NO. 1922

DECLARING TUESDAY, DECEMBER 8, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS, Tuesday, December 8, 2009, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS, to give the people of the Province of Batangas the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in the history of their province, it is but fitting and proper to declare December 8, 2009 as special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Tuesday, December 8, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
