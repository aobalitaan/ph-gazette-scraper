MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 466

DECLARING FRIDAY, OCTOBER 3, 2003, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, CAVITE

WHEREAS , Friday, October 3, 2003, marks the 208 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Imus, Cavite; and

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the residents of the Municipality of Imus, Cavite, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies to remind them of the importance and significance of this day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, October 3, 2003, as a special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Imus, Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be attached.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
