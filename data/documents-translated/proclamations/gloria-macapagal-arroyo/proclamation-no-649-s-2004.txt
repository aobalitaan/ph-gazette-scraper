MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 649

DECLARING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN BARANGAY SAN LORENZO, MUNICIPALITY OF MAUBAN, PROVINCE OF QUEZON, ISLAND OF LUZON, FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE SAN LORENZO PRIMARY SCHOOL

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve, a certain parcel of public domain situated in Sitio Aluhin, Barangay of San Lorenzo, Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon for school site purposes of the San Lorenzo Primary School under the administration of the Department of Education, containing an area of Two Thousand One Hundred Forty Four (2,144) square meters, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

PLAN RL-04-000100-D

A parcel of land, (shown plan RL-04-000100-D), situated in Sitio Aluhin, Barangay San Lorenzo, Municipality of Mauban, Province of Quezon, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2-3-4 by Lot ‑2762, Cad-245; on the SE., along lines 4-5 by public land (Dried Aluhin River Bed); on the SW. & NW., along lines 5-6-7 by legal easement (3.00 m. wide), Aluhin river; on the N. along line 7-1 by Provincial Road.

Beginning at a point marked “1” RL-04-000-100-D being N. 65° 02′ E. 200.94 m. from B.B.M. # 24 Cad-245, Mauban Cadastre, thence:

IN WITNESS THEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16th day of June in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
