MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 627

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 518, SERIES OF 1990 BY EXCLUDING CERTAIN PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY COMEMBO, MAKATI CITY AND RESERVING THE SAME FOR AFP OFF-BASE HOUSING PURPOSES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the power vested in me by the law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 518, S. of 1990, by excluding from the operation thereof, certain portion of the land embraced therein located in Barangay Comembo, Municipality of Makati, and declaring the same as site for the OFF-BASE HOUSING of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, to be disposed and administered by the National Housing Authority in coordination of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows;

“A PARCEL OF LAND (as shown on plan, Swo-00-001300 as surveyed for Bases Conversion Development Authority), situated in Barangay of Comembo, Municipality of Makati, Province of Metro Manila, Island of Luzon.
