MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 858

DECLARING THURSDAY, JUNE 16, 2005, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PAMPLONA, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS , Thursday, June 16, 2005, marks the 55 th Charter Day anniversary of the Municipality of Pamplona, Province of Negros Oriental;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pamplona, Province of Negros Oriental be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, June 16, 2005, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pamplona, Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Five.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
