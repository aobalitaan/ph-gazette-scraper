PROCLAMATION NO. 109

DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF BIÑAN, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, TO BE ANNEXED INTO THE LAGUNA TECHNOPARK SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916 otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended, and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby designate as part of the Laguna Technopark Special Economic Zone, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 01-193 dated 13 July 2001 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at the Municipality of Biñan, Province of Laguna, consisting of THIRTY ONE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED EIGHTY SIX (31,286) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
