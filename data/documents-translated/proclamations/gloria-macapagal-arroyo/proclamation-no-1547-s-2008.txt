MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1547

DECLARING THURSDAY, JULY 10, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SANTA ROSA, LAGUNA

WHEREAS , Thursday, July 10, 2008, marks the 4 th Cityhood Anniversary of the City of Santa Rosa, Laguna;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Santa Rosa be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, July 10, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the City of Santa Rosa, Laguna.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
