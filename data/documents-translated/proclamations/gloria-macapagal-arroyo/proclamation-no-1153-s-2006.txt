MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1153

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 13, 2006 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF GENERAL TRIAS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS , Wednesday, December 13, 2006, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of General Trias, Province of Cavite;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of General Trias, Province of Cavite be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, December 13, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of General Trias, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 9th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
