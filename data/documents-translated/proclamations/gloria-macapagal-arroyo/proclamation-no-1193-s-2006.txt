MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1193

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 848 DATED JANUARY 14, 1992, WHICH TRANSFERRED TO THE NATIONAL HOUSING AUTHORITY THE ADMINISTRATION AND DISPOSITION OF THE LAND LOCATED IN PUNTA, STA. ANA, MANILA RESERVED FOR RELOCATION SITE PURPOSES UNDER PROCLAMATION NO. 687 SERIES OF 1960, BY TRANSFERRING THE MANAGEMENT AND ADMINISTRATION OF A PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN TO THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES (DENR)

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 848 dated January 14, 1992 which transferred to the National Housing Authority the administration and disposition of the land located in Punta, Sta. Ana., Manila reserved for relocation site purposes under Proclamation No. 687 Series of 1960, by transferring the management and administration of a portion of the land embraced therein to the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR), subject to actual survey, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION
