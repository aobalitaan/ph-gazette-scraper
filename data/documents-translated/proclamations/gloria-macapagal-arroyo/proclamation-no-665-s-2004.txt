MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 665

DECLARING FRIDAY, JULY 23, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN BATANGAS CITY

WHEREAS , Friday, July 23, 2004, marks the 35 th Foundation Day anniversary of Batangas City;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of Batangas City be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO . Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, July 23, 2004, as special (non-working) day in Batangas City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 12th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Source : CDAsia

Note: This issuance is followed by Proclamation No. 667, s. 2004.
