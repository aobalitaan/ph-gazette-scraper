MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 425

DECLARING THE THIRD WEEK OF SEPTEMBER EACH YEAR AS “NATIONAL RADIATION PROTECTION WEEK”

WHEREAS , the Philippine Association for Radiation Protection, Inc., (PARP) will be holding its 10 th Biennial National Convention on September 26 to 27, in Cebu City;

WHEREAS , the PARP is a professional society of workers occupationally exposed to radiation from the various fields of radiation application, namely, in health and medical sector;

WHEREAS , the PARP serves as a venue for the exchange and sharing of information and experiences among its members, thus updating their knowledge and skills in radiation protection; and

WHEREAS , there is a need to draw attention to the positive uses of radiation and accompanying safety and protective measures related thereto.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the third week of September each year as “National Radiation Protection Week” .

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22 nd day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
