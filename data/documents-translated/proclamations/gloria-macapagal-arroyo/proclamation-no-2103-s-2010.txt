MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2103

PROPOSED PROCLAMATION RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY BANGAO, BUGUIAS, BENGUET, FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF SINIPSIP NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL-BANGAO EXTENSION

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve a certain parcel of land of the public domain for school site purposes of Sinipsip National High School – Bangao Extension, under the administration and supervision of the Department of Education (DepEd) situated in Barangay Bangao, Municipality of Buguias, Province of Benguet, Island of Luzon, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of forest land is more particularly described as follows:

“A PARCEL OF LAND (SWO-CAR-000718) , situated in Barangay Bangao, Municipality of Buguias, Province of Benguet, Island of Luzon.
