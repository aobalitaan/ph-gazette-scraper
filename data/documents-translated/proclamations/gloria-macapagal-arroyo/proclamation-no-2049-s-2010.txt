PROCLAMATION NO. 2049

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED AT BARANGAY BAGBAG, MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF CAVITE, FOR INCLUSION TO THE EXISTING CAVITE ECONOMIC ZONE II ‑ SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 07-518 dated 09 November 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, a parcel of land of the private domain located at Rosario, Cavite, containing an area of Nine Thousand Eight Hundred Seventy Eight (9,878) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
