MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 327

DECLARING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED IN BARANGAY BASPER, TACLOBAN CITY AS SITE OF THE CEMETERY OF TACLOBAN CITY UNDER THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE CITY GOVERNMENT OF TACLOBAN

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby declare certain parcels of land covered by Transfer Certificate of Title No. T-465 containing an area of 100,443 square meters situated in Barangay Basper, Tacloban City, registered in accordance with the provisions of the Land Registration Act, in the name of the Republic of the Philippines as cemetery site under the administration of the City Government of Tacloban City, which parcels are more particularly described as follows:
