MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1405

DECLARING TUESDAY, NOVEMBER 13, 2007, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS , Tuesday, November 13, 2007, marks the foundation day anniversary of the Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, IGNACIO R. BUNYE , Acting Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Tuesday, November 13, 2007, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of October , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seven.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) IGNACIO R. BUNYE Acting Executive Secretary

By the Acting Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
