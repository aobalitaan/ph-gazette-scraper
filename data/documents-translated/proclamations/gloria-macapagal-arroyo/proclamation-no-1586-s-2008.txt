PROCLAMATION NO. 1586

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF IRIGA

WHEREAS, Wednesday, September 3, 2008, marks the 40 th Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Iriga;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Iriga, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, September 3, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the City of Iriga.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
