PROCLAMATION NO. 1919

DECLARING FRIDAY, OCTOBER 23, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, the Province of Negros Oriental will be celebrating the Buglasan Festival on October 16 – 25, 2009;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the Province of Negros Oriental the opportunity to participate and celebrate the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlalawigan requested that October 23, 2009 be declared as special non-working day in the Province of Negros Oriental.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, October 23, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16 th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
