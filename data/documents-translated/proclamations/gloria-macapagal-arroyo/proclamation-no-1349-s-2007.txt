MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1349

RESERVING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF QUIRINO STATE COLLEGE SITUATED AT BARANGAY ANDRES BONIFACIO, MUNICIPALITY OF DIFFUN, PROVINCE OF QUIRINO, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserve for School Site Purposes of Quirino State College, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at Barangay Andres Bonifacio, Municipality of Diffun, Province of Quirino and more particularly described as follows:

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by Old P.S. cyl. conc. mos. 15 x 60 cms. in diameter and 20 x 20 cms. conc. posts. Bearings true. The consolidation subdivision survey was executed by Geodetic Engineer Raymundo C. Orpia on August 8-9, 2000 and was approved on October 13, 2000.

The area covered by the Proclamation shall be under the administrative jurisdiction, supervision and control of the Commission on Higher Education, which shall be responsible in maintaining the usefulness of the area.

The tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease or other disposition until again declared as alienable by Proclamation of the President.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of August in the year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Seven.
