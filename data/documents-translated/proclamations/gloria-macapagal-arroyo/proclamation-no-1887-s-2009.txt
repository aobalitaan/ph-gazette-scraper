PROCLAMATION NO. 1887

CREATING AND DESIGNATING PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT BARANGAY LOOC, SIBULAN, NEGROS ORIENTAL, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000, as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004 and Resolution No. 06-572 dated 18 December 2006 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at Barangay Looc, Sibulan, Negros Oriental as Information Technology Park, consisting of FORTY FIVE THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED SIX (45,806) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
