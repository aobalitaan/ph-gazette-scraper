MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1099

RESERVING FOR LAGUINDINGAN RELOCATION SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PORTION OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF TUBAJON, MUNICIPALITY OF LAGUINDINGAN, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL ISLAND OF MINDANAO

By virtue of the powers vested in me by Law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines and upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, do hereby set aside and proclaim, subject to private rights, if there be any, certain parcel of the public domain of Laguindingan, Misamis Oriental for relocation site purposes for the inhabitants of Laguindingan, Misamis Oriental who were displaced by the establishment or creation of the Laguidingan International Airport as shown and described in C.R. Map No. FMB-00 which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

“Lot No. : LOT 155-A GSS-880 Tubajon Laguindingan, Misamis Oriental Island of Mindanao

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 155-A, GSS-880, Cad-237, Cagayan Cadastre) situated in Barangay Tubajon, Municipality of Laguidingan, Province of Misamis Oriental, Island of Mindanao. Bounded on the NE., along lines 13-14-15 by Lot Sgs-10-000939-D, along lines 15-16-17 by Cadastre, on the SE., along line 18-19 by Lot 20-21 by Lot 112, along lines 21-22 by Lot 113, both of Gss-880, along line 22-23 by Lot 12507, along lines 23-24-25 by Lot 12506, both of Cad-237, Cagayan Cadastre, along line 25-26 by Road along lines 26-27-28 by Lot 110, Gss-880, along line 28-29 by the same Lot, along lines 29-30-31-32 by Lot 111, Cad-237, Cagayan Cadastre, along lines 32-33-34-35-36-37 by Lot Sgs-10-000938, along line 37-38 by Lot 111, Cad 237, Cagayan Cadastre, along 38-39 of the same Lot, on the S., along line 39-40 by Lot 109, along lines 40-41-1-2-3 by Lot 153, along lines 3-4-5 by Lot 105, along line 5-6 by Lot 104, all of Gss-880, on the SW., along line 6-7 by Lot 12519, Cad-237, Cagayan Cadastre, on the W., along lines 7-8-9 by 153, Gss-880, and on the NW., along line 9-10 by Lot 12516, along lines 10-11-12 by Lot 12515, both of Cad-237, Cagayan Cadastre and along line 12-13 by Lot Sgs-10-000874.

Beginning from a point marked “1” on the plan being S., 53 deg., 40W., 790.06 M. from BLLM No. 107, Cad-237, Cagayan Cadastre

THENCE:

To the point of beginning; containing an area of THREE HUNDRED FORTY FOUR THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED NINETEEN (344,219) SQUARE METERS more or less. Subject to final ground survey.”

The administration and management of the area covered by the reservation shall be under the Local Government Unit of Laguindingan, Misamis Oriental in coordination with the Department of Environment and Natural Resources; and any development activity therein shall have minimal effect to the ecosystem. For this purpose a five-year development plan shall be submitted to the Office of the President of the Republic of the Philippines, within one (1) year from approval hereof, furnishing the Department of Environment and Natural Resources through the Forest Management Bureau a copy thereof.

The cutting and utilization of timber and other forest products remaining in the area shall be subject to Forestry and Revenue Laws and Regulations.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 14th day of July in the year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Six.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
