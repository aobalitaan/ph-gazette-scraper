PROCLAMATION NO. 2025

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THREE (3) PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BAKANGAY CANLUBANG, CALAMBA CITY, LAGUNA, AS AN INCLUSION TO THE EXISTING CARMELRAY INDUSTRIAL PARK I – SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations and Resolution No. 09-210 dated 13 May 2009 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, three (3) parcels of land located at Barangay Canlubang, Calamba City, Laguna for inclusion to the existing Carmelray Industrial Park I – Special Economic Zone with an aggregate area of FORTY SIX THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED SEVENTY THREE (46,573) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
