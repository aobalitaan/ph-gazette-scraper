PROCLAMATION NO. 1793

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN MERALCO CENTER, BARANGAY UGONG, ORTIGAS AVENUE, PASIG CITY AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its-Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 08-125 dated 15 February 2008, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, a parcel of land located in Meralco Center, Barangay Ugong, Ortigas Avenue, Pasig City, as Information Technology Park, to be known as Rockwell-Meralco Business Center with an area of Thirteen Thousand Seven Hundred Eight Five (13,785) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
