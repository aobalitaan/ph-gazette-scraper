PROCLAMATION NO. 1509

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THE ROBINSONS CAGAYAN DE ORO BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON SEVERAL PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT ROSARIO CRESCENT CORNER FLORENTINO STREET, LIMKETKAI CENTER, CAGAYAN DE ORO CITY, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution No. 07-465 dated 10 October 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Robinsons Cagayan de Oro Building as an Information Technology Center located at Rosario Crescent corner Florentino Street, Limketkai Center, Cagayan de Oro City, Province of Misamis Oriental, which stands on several parcels of land of the private domain with an area of Ten Thousand Seven Hundred Seventy Three (10,773) square meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
