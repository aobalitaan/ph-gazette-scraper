MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 269

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE BALET ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED AT BARANGAY BALET, BABAK, ISLAND GARDEN CITY OF SAMAL, DAVAO DEL NORTE

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve Lot 377-B, Csd-11-010967-D for school site purposes of the Balet Elementary School, under the administration of the Department of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, situated at Barangay Balet, Babak, Island Garden City of Samal, Davao del Norte, and more particularly described as follows:

Lot 377-B, Csd-11-010967-D, identical to Lot 1399, Pls-566-D (Balet Elementary School)

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 377-B, Csd-11-010967-D), situated in the Municipality of Babak, Island Garden City of Samal, Davao del Norte.

Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2 by Lot 377-A, Csd-11-010967-D, on the SW., along line 2-3 by Lot 378, Pls-566-D, on the SW., along line 3-4 by Lot 376, Pls-566-D, and on the NW., along line 4-1 by Lot 376, Pls-566-D.

Beginning from point marked “1” on plan being N. 71-11’E., 5991.07 m. from BLLM No. 1, Pls-566-D, Babak, Public Land Subdivision, thence,

point of beginning.

Containing an area of THIRTY SEVEN THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED THIRTY SIX (37,136) SQUARE METERS . Points 1 & 2 are marked on the plan as P.S. Cyl. Conc. Mon. 15 x 60 cm. Bearings true. Date of Original Survey December 10, 1956 to June 18, 1957. Date of Subdivision Survey October 31, 1991 by Geodetic Engineer Gersacio Magno and was approved on November 8, 1991.

The tract of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease, or other disposition until again declared alienable by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
