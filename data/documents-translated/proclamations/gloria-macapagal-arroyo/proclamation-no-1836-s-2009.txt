PROCLAMATION NO. 1836

DECLARING FRIDAY, JULY 17, 2009, AS A SPECIAL WORKING DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF BULACAN, IN COMMEMORATION OF THE BIRTHDAY OF GEN. ALEJO S. SANTOS

WHEREAS, July 17, 2009 is the birthday of Gen. Alejo S. Santos, a native of Bulacan, a bemedalled soldier, a well-loved politician and an exemplary public servant;

WHEREAS, a hero to his townmates, it is but fitting that a special day be declared in appreciation and in memory of his accomplishments and his heroism.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by the power of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Friday, July 17, 2009, as a special working day in the Province of Bulacan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 16 th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Acting Deputy Executive Secretary
