MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 541

DECLARING THE PERIOD FROM MARCH 1 TO 5, 2004 AS PURCHASING AND SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT WEEK

WHEREAS , the Philippine Institute for Supply Management (PISM), is a non-stock, non profit organization of purchasing and materials managers of the country’s top corporations;

WHEREAS , purchasing and materials managers and PISM help upgrade the quality and competitiveness of Philippine goods and services both the domestic and foreign markets;

WHEREAS , it is desirable that the important role played by the purchasing and materials managers in commerce and industry be brought to the attention of the people;

WHEREAS , the PISM will celebrate its 12 th National Convention and Exhibit this year called SUPPLYLINK 2004 with the theme “…Strengthening Alliances…Building Linkages…Renewing Relationship”.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the period from March 1 to 5, 2004 as Purchasing and Supply Chain Management Week .

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 20TH day of Jan, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

( Sgd .) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: ( Sgd .) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
