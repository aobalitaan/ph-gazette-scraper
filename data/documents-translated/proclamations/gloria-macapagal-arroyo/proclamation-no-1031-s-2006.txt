MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1031

DECLARING THURSDAY, MARCH 16, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LUGAIT, MISAMIS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS , Thursday, March 16, 2006, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Lugait, Misamis Oriental;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Lugait, Misamis Oriental, be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive, Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, March 16, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Lugait, Misamis Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
