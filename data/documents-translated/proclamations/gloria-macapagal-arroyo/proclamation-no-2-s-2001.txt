MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2

DECLARING SATURDAY, FEBRUARY 3, 2001, AS SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF CABANATUAN

WHEREAS , Saturday, February 3, 2001, marks the 51st Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Cabanatuan:

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of the City of Cabanatuan be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Saturday, February 3, 2001, as a special day in the City of Cabanatuan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of February in the year of Our Lord, two thousand one.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) RENATO S. DE VILLA Executive Secretary
