MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1050

DECLARING MONDAY, MAY 15, 2006 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ALABAT, PROVINCE OF QUEZON

WHEREAS , Monday, May 15, 2006, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Alabat, Province of Quezon;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Alabat, Province of Quezon be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, May 15, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Alabat, Province of Quezon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
