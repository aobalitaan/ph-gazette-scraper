MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 685

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES THE HALIAP NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL—MAIN CAMPUS, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ASIPULO, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or any other form of disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes the Haliap National High School – Main Campus, under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain containing an area of TWENTY THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED TWENTY TWO (20,322) SQUARE METERS , situated in the Municipality of Asipulo, Province of Ifugao, Island of Luzon, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

PLAN SWO-CAR-000688

A PARCEL OF LAND (SWO-CAR-000688), situated in the Municipality of Asipulo, Province of Ifugao, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the West along line 1-2 by Haliap Elementary School; on the Northeast, along lines 2-3-4-5 by Peter Ngabit; on the Southeast, along line 5-6 by Carlos Catama; and on the West, along line 6-1 by Haliap Elementary School.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 83 deg. 35′ E., 2,579.37 m. from BLLM No. 1 Cad-1195-D, Asipulo Cadastre, thence:

115.47 m. to point 2;

123.56 m. to point 3;

52.33 m. to point 4;

82.65 m. to point 5;

125.56 m. to point 6;

165.35 m. to point of beginning,

containing an area of TWENTY THOUSAND THREE HUNDRED TWENTY TWO (20,322) SQUARE METERS , more or less.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground as follows. Corner 4 by Conc. Mons. Rock Boulder and the rest by G.S. Cyl. Conc. Mons. 15 x 60 cms. Bearings Grid: Date of subdivision survey: February 26 – March 1, 2001 executed by Engr. Honesto D. Kimayong and was approved on June 17, 2002.

This proclamation shall be deemed automatically revoked or cancelled in the event that the reservation no longer serves the purpose for which it was originally established and/or has been abandoned by the said reservee and the area covered thereby be forthwith reverted to the mass of forestlands under the administrative jurisdiction of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources through its appropriate agency.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done, in the City of Manila this 9th day of August in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
