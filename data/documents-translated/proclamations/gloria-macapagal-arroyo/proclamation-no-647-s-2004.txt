PROCLAMATION NO. 647

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING, TO BE KNOWN AS INNOVE IT PLAZA, WHICH STANDS ON A 2,014 SQUARE METER LOT LOCATED AT SAMAR LOOP CORNER PANAY ROAD, CEBU BUSINESS PARK AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY BUILDING PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916 otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended by Republic Act No. 8748, and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Building , subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolutions Nos. 00-411 and 04-001 dated 29 December 2001 and 14 January 2004, respectively, of the PEZA Board of Directors, the Innove IT Plaza , which stands on a lot located at Samar Loop corner Panay Road, Cebu Business Park, Cebu City, with an area of TWO THOUSAND FOURTEEN (2,014) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
