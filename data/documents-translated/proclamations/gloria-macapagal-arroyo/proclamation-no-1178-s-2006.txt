MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO.1178

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THE RIVERBANKS ARCADE AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER TO BE ANNEXED TO THE EXISTING RIVERBANKS CENTER ICT BUILDING I LOCATED AT RIVERBANKS CENTER, A. BONIFACIO AVENUE, MARIKINA CITY, PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Center annexed to the existing Riverbanks Center ICT Building I, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolutions Nos. 00-411 and 06-279 dated 29 December 2000 and 27 June 2006, respectively, of the PEZA Board of Directors, a building, also known as the Riverbanks Arcade, which stands on a 3,577-square meter lot, more or less, located at Riverbanks Center, A. Bonifacio Avenue, Marikina City, as defined by the following technical descriptions:

LOT 2 Pcs-00-011941

A parcel of land (Lot 2, of the consolidation subdivision plan, Pcs-00-011941, being a portion of consolidated Lot 1-B, Psd-00-055453 & Lot 2, (LRA) Pcs-31486, L.R.C. Record No. 7672), situated in the Barangay of Barranca, City of Marikina, Province of Metro Manila, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the NE., along line 1-2 by Lot 1 of the consolidation subdivision plan; on the SE., along lines 2-3-4 by Lot 3-A, Psd-00-066714; and on the SW., & NW., along lines 4-5-1 by Lot 1 of the consolidation subdivision plan.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being S. 75 deg. 47′ W., 1380.49 m. from BLLM No. 2, Municipality of Marikina, thence;

beginning, containing an area of THREE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED SEVENTY SEVEN (3,577) square meters, more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by PS cyl. conc. mons. 15 x 40 cm., except point 3 by Old PS cyl. conc. mons. 15 x 60 cm.; bearings grid; date of original survey, and that of the consolidation subdivision survey, June 2, 2006. Approved on September 11, 2006.

IN WITNESS THEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed hereto.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30th day of November, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
