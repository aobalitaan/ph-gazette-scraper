MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 847

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF BAGUINGE ELEMENTARY SCHOOL CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY BAGUINGE, MUNICIPALITY OF KIANGAN, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of Baguinge Elementary School, under the administration of the Department of Education, certain parcels of land of the public domain located in Barangay Baguinge, Municipality of Kiangan, Province of Ifugao, Island of Luzon, containing an aggregate area of Six Thousand Forty One (6,041) square meters , subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
