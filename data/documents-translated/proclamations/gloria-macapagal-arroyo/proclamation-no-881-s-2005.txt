MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 881

DECLARING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE CITY OF BAIS, NEGROS ORIENTAL AS CIVIL RESERVATION FOR SOCIALIZED HOUSING PURPOSES

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by Law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby declare as civil reservation for socialized housing purposes under the administration of the City Government of Bais, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in the City of Bais, Province of Negros Oriental, containing an area of ONE HUNDRED FIFTY SIX THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED EIGHTY SEVEN (156,587) square meters, more or less, subject to private rights, if there be any and to actual survey, which parcel is more particularly described as follows:

Any mangrove/swampy areas found inside the area herein reserved is hereby segregated from the coverage of this proclamation and should not be disturbed nor alienated.

The area herein reserved as Civil Reservation for resettlement and agricultural site purposes shall not be used for any other purpose. However, beneficiaries may be awarded with special forest landuse agreements in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 705 otherwise known as the Forestry Code of the Philippines and related rules and regulations.

A Local Inter-Agency Committee (LIAC) is hereby created with the Housing and Urban Development Coordinating Council (HUDCC), as Chair, the City Government of Bais as Co-Chair, the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR), the Presidential Commission for the Urban Poor (PCUP), and a representative of the People’s Organization (PO) in the area as members. The LIAC shall formulate the implementing rules and regulations of this proclamation.

The cutting, gathering and utilization of all natural resources within the proclaimed site shall be subject to the existing DENR rules and regulations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 28th day of July in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Five.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
