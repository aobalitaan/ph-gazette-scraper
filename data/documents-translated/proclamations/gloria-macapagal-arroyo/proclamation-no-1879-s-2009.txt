PROCLAMATION NO. 1879

DECLARING MONDAY, SEPTEMBER 7, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Monday, September 7, 2009, marks the 438 th Foundation Day anniversary and 69th Charter Day anniversary of the City of Cavite;

WHEREAS, in order to give the people of the City of Cavite, the opportunity to participate in the various ceremonies and activities designed to give meaning and significance to the occasion, it is but fitting and proper to declare September 7, 2009 as special non-working day.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, September 7, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the City of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 4 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
