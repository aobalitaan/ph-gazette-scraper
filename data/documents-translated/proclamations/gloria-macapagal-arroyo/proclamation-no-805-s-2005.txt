MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 805

DECLARING A STATE OF CALAMITY IN MUNICIPALITY OF MABINI, PROVINCE OF BOHOL

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, do hereby declare a state of calamity in Municipality of Mabini, Province of Bohol, in view of the mass food poisoning incident that took the lives of twenty seven (27) elementary school children and hospitalization of more than one hundred (100) victims in said locality.

All departments and other government agencies concerned are hereby directed to extend relief and medical assistance to those affected in accordance with existing operational plans and directives and orders issued for the purpose.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila this 14h day of March, in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Five.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
