PROCLAMATION NO. 1576

DECLARING SATURDAY, AUGUST 30, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF MANDAUE

WHEREAS, Saturday, August 30, 2008, marks the Charter Day anniversary of the City of Mandaue;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Mandaue, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Saturday, August 30, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the City of Mandaue.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6 th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

(Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary

Source: Presidential Management Staff
