PROCLAMATION NO. 1608

RESERVING AS SITE FOR WASTE AND GARBAGE DISPOSAL AREA OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE’S POINT, PALAWAN, A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY MAASIN, MUNICIPALITY OF BROOKE’S POINT, PROVINCE OF PALAWAN

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve as site for waste and garbage disposal area of the Municipality of Brooke’s Point, Palawan, a parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barangay Maasin, Municipality of Brooke’s Point, Province of Palawan, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
