MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 494

DECLARING FRIDAY, JANUARY 16, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVOTAS, METRO MANILA

WHEREAS , Friday, January 16, 2004, marks the 98 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Navotas, Metro Manila;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of the Municipality of Navotas be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, President GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Friday, January 16, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 03 day of NOVEMBER, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: ( Sgd .) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
