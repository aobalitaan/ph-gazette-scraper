MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 82

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 22, 2001, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF LILOY, ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE

WHEREAS, the Municipality of Liloy will be celebrating its 50 th Foundation Day anniversary on Wednesday, August 22, 2001;

WHEREAS, it was on such a date that former President Elpidio Quirino signed Executive Order No. 469, s. 1951, which made Liloy a Municipality;

WHEREAS, the residents of the Municipality of Liloy desire to celebrate with fitting ceremonies this event in their town’s history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27 of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Wednesday, August 22, 2001, as a special non-working day in the Municipality of Liloy, Zamboanga del Norte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 14 th day of AUGUST, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and one.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
