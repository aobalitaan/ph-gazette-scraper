MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 834

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF ENRILE VOCATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED IN BRGY. MADDARULUG, MUNICIPALITY OF ENRILE, PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of Enrile Vocational High School under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain located in Brgy. Maddarulug, Municipality of Enrile, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon, containing an area of Fifty thousand (50,000) square meters , subject to private rights if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT NO. 1, Ccs-2-01-000161-D

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 1, Ccs-2-01-000161-D), being a portion of the consolidation/subdivision of Lots 20, 21, and 22 situated in Brgy. Maddarulug, Municipality of Enrile, Province of Cagayan, Island of Luzon.

Bounded on the SE., and NE., along lines 1-2-3-4-5-6 by National Road (60.00 m. wide); on the NE., SE., and SW., along lines 6-7-8-9 by Lot 2; on the SW., along line 9-10 by Lot 3; and on the SW., and NW., along lines 10-11-1 by Lot 4, all of Plan Ccs-2-01-000161-D.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of April in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Five.
