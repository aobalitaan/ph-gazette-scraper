PROCLAMATION NO. 1800

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, 01 JULY 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF DIPOLOG

WHEREAS, Wednesday, 01 July 2009 marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the City of Dipolog;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the City of Dipolog be given the opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their history;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, 01 July 2009, as special (non-working) day in the City of Dipolog.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Acting Deputy Executive Secretary
