PROCLAMATION NO. 1812

RESERVING AS MARKET SITE FOR THE MUNICIPALITY INFANTA, PANGASINAN, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF INFANTA, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, ISLAND OF LUZON.

Upon the recommendation of the secretary of the Department of the Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President, of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, disposition or settlement and reserve as market site purposes for Infanta, Pangasinan certain parcels of land of the public domain, subject to prior rights, if any there be, situated in Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of Infanta, Province of Pangasinan, Island of Luzon, and more particularly described as follows to wit:

The administration and management of the area covered by the reservation shall be under the Local Government Unit of Infanta, Pangasinan.

The tract or tracts of land reserved under the provisions of Section Eighty-Eight shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease or other disposition until again declared alienable under the provisions of this Act or by proclamation of the President.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 23 rd day of June in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.
