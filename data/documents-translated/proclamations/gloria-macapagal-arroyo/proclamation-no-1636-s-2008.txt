PROCLAMATION NO.1636

DECLARING TUESDAY, OCTOBER 7, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF IMUS, PROVINCE OF CAVITE

WHEREAS, Tuesday, October 7, 2008, marks the 233 rd Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Tuesday, October 7, 2008 as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Imus, Province of Cavite.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6 th day of October, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
