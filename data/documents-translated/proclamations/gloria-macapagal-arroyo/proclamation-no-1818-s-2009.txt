PROCLAMATION NO. 1818

DECLARING TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 8, 2009 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN DIGOS CITY, DAVAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Tuesday, September 8, 2009, marks the founding day anniversary of the City of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur;

WHEREAS, the commemoration of Araw ng Digos City is an opportune time for Digoseños to learn about their heritage, the uniqueness of their culture and enjoy the pleasures of belonging to a community;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Tuesday, September 8, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the City of Digos, Province of Davao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 30 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Acting Deputy Executive Secretary
