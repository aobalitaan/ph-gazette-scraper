MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2080

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 21, 2010, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF MANOLO FORTICH, BUKIDNON

WHEREAS , Monday, June 21, 2010, marks the celebration of the 94 th Charter Day anniversary of the Municipality of Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, LEANDRO R. MENDOZA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, June 21, 2010, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Manolo Fortich, Bukidnon.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Ten.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) LEANDRO R. MENDOZA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) AGUSTIN S. DIZON Deputy Executive Secretary for Legal Affairs
