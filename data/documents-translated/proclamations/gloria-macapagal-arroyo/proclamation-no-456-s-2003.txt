MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 456

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 10, 2003, AS A SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF SAN JOSE DEL MONTE

WHEREAS , Republic Act No. 8797 provided for the conversion of the Municipality of San Jose del Monte, Bulacan, into a component city to be known as the City of San Jose del Monte ;

WHEREAS , Wednesday, September 10, 2003, marks the 3 rd Charter Day anniversary of the City of San Jose del Monte; and

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of San Jose del Monte be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, September 10, 2003, as a special (non-working) day in the City of San Jose del Monte.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 3 rd day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
