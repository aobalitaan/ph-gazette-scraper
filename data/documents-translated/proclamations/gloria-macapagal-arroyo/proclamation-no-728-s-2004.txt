MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 728

DECLARING SATURDAY, NOVEMBER 13, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF PANTUKAN, PROVINCE OF COMPOSTELA VALLEY

WHEREAS , Saturday, November 13, 2004, marks the foundation day anniversary of the Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley; and

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Saturday, November 13, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Pantukan, Province of Compostela Valley.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 11th day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By authority of the President: ( Sgd .) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
