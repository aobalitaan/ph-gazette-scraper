PROCLAMATION NO. 1885

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY MARIA CRISTINA, MUNICIPALITY OF BALO-I, PROVINCE OF LANAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, AS AGRO-INDUSTRIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 07-232 dated 16 May 2007 and Resolution No. 08-470 dated 10 September 2008 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, a parcel of land located at Barangay Maria Cristina, Balo-I, Lanao del Norte as agro-industrial economic zone to be known as Balo-I Agro-Industrial Economic Zone, with an area of One Hundred Thirty Nine Thousand Twelve (139,012) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
