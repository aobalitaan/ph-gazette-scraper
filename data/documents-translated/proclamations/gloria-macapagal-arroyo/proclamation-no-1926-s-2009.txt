PROCLAMATION NO. 1926

DECLARING A CERTAIN PARCEL OF RECLAIMED LAND SITUATED IN BARANGAY MADAUM, CITY OF TAGUM, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO, AS ALIENABLE AND DISPOSABLE AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF PHILIPPINE RECLAMATION AUTHORITY (PRA),

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby declare a certain parcel of reclaimed land of the public domain situated in Barangay Madaum, City of Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, as alienable and disposable (A & D) lands and granting ownership thereof in favor of the Philippine Reclamation Authority (PRA), and more particularly described as follows:
