MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1544

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JULY 2, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF CAGAYAN

WHEREAS , Wednesday, July 2, 2008, marks the observance of the feast day of Our Lady of Piat in the Province of Cagayan;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Cagayan be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Wednesday, July 2, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Cagayan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of June , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
