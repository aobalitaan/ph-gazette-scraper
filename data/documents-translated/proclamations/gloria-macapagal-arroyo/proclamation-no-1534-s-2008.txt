PROCLAMATION NO. 1534

DECLARING TUESDAY, JUNE 24, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF CAGWAIT, SURIGAO DEL SUR

WHEREAS, Tuesday, June 24, 2008, marks the celebration of Kaliguan Festival in the Municipality of Cagwait, Surigao del Sur;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Cagwait, Surigao del Sur, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Tuesday, June 24, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Cagwait, Surigao del Sur.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 16 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
