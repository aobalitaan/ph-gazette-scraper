MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 821

DECLARING AS CIVIL RESERVATION FOR PUBLIC MARKET SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF PUBLIC DOMAIN LOCATED IN BRGY. POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF LAGAWE, PROVINCE OF IFUGAO, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I , GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby declare as civil reservation for public market site purposes certain parcels of land of public domain located in Brgy. Poblacion, Municipality of Lagawe, Province of Ifugao, Island of Luzon, containing an aggregate area of Five Thousand Four Hundred Seventy Seven (5,477) square meters, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
