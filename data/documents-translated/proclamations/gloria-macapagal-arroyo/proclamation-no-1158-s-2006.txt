MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO.1158

RESERVING A PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED IN BARANGAY CUYAMBAY, MUNICIPALITY OF TANAY, PROVINCE OF RIZAL, ISLAND OF LUZON, AS NEW SITE OF THE NEW BILIBID PRISON AND FIELD OFFICE SITE OF THE DENR-CALABARZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby reserve a certain portion of the titled property of the Republic of the Philippines located in Barangay Cuyambay, Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, Island of Luzon, with an area of Two Hundred Seventy (270) hectares as new site of the New Bilibid Prison under the Department of Justice and Thirty (30) hectares as field office site of the DENR-Calabarzon, which portions are subject to private rights if any there be and future subdivision survey, the complete technical descriptions of the parcel of land are as follows:

Lot 10, Psu-103291-Amd

O. C. T. No. 3556 Republic of the Philippines

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 10, plan Psu-103291-Amd., L.R. Case No. 1328, L.R.C. Record No. 52645), situated in the Barrio of Cuyambay, Municipality of Tanay, Province of Rizal, Island of Luzon. Bounded on the NE., by property of Lupo Dumandan and Lots 9 and 8; on the SE., by Public Land; on the SW., and W., by the Mariquina Watershed; and on the NW., by Property of Lupo Dumandan.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan being N. 24 deg. 04′ W., 6868.51 m. from B.L.L.M. No. 12, Tanay Public Land Subdivision, PLS-39, thence;

of beginning, containing an area of THREE MILLION (3,000,000) SQ. M., more or less. All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground; bearings true; date of original survey, April 15-May 6, 1937, and that of the amendment survey, February 1-15, 1959.

The area of THIRTY (30) hectares will be the site of the Provincial Environment and Natural Resources Office (PENRO), the Community Environment and Natural Resources Office (CENRO) and a Forest Tree Nursery Site of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, Province of Rizal, Calabarzon Region.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 8th day of September in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
