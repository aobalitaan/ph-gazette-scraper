PROCLAMATION NO. 1892

RESRVING FOR SCHOOL SITES PURPOSES FOR BALET NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT BARANGAY BALET, MUNICIPALITY OF BABAK, ISLAND GARDEN CITY OF SAMAL, PROVINCE OF DAVAO ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the power vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition and settlement and reserve for school site purposes for Balet National High School, under the administration of the Department of Education, subject to private rights, if any there be, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated at Barangay Balet, Municipality of Babak, Island Garden City of Samal, Province of Davao, Island of Mindanao, which is more particularly described as follows:

“Lot No. 377-A, Csd-11-010967-D

The tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale, lease or other disposition until again declared alienable by proclamation of the President.”

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done the in the City of Manila, this 24 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.
