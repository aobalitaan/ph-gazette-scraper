PROCLAMATION NO. 1510

RESERVING A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN FOR PUBLIC PURPOSES OF THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT OF ROXAS, ISABELA, SITUATED IN POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF ROXAS, PROVINCE OF ISABELA, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from any disposition, either through sale, lease or settlement and reserved a parcel of land of the public domain for public purposes of the Municipal Government of Roxas, Isabela, situated in Poblacion, Municipality of Roxas, Province of Isabela, Island of Luzon, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:
