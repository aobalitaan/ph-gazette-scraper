MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 657

DECLARING THE YEAR 2000-2010 AS THE “BONE AND JOINT DECADE”

WHEREAS , musculoskeletal conditions caused by trauma and accidents ranked 6 th as a leading caused of mortality and morbidity in the 1989 Health Intelligence Survey Service of the Department of Health, and is projected to be number 3 leading cause of mortality by the year 2020;

WHEREAS , the year 2000-2010 has been declared the Bone and Joint Decade endorsed by the United Nations and the World Health Organization, and by more than 80 governments to date;

WHEREAS , the objectives of the Bone and Joint Decade are as follows: a) Raising awareness of the growing burden of musculoskeletal disorders on society; b) Empowering patients to participate in their own care; c) Promoting cost effective prevention and treatment; and d) Advancing understanding of musculoskeletal disorders through research to improve prevention and treatment.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare the year 2000-2010 as the “Bone and Joint Decade” to be observed in the country.

IN WITNESS THEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 5th day of July , in the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
