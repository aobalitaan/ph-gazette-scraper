MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 523

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 430, DATED DECEMBER 29, 2000, PROCLAMATION 423 DATED JULY 12, 1957 BY EXCLUDING PORTION OF THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN AND DECLARING THE SAME OPEN FOR DISPOSITION TO THE BONAFIDE AND ACTUAL RESIDENTS THEREOF UNDER THE PROVISIONS OF REPUBLIC ACT NO. 730 IN RELATION TO THE PROVISIONS OF THE PUBLIC LAND ACT (COMMONWEALTH ACT NO. 141, AS AMENDED)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 430 dated December 29, 2000 and Proclamation 423 dated July 12, 1957, over certain parcels of land embraced therein and declaring the same open for disposition to the bonafide and actual residents thereof under the provisions of the Act No. 730, in relation to the provisions of Commonwealth Act No. 141, as amended, under the administration of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources, subject to private rights, if any there be, and to final survey which parcels of land are particularly described as follows:
