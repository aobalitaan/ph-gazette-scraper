MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1329

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN LOCATED AT BARANGAYS BOLO AND SAN MIGUEL, MUNICIPALITY OF BAUAN, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as a special economic zone, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 06-390 dated 25 August 2006, of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, an area located at Barangays Bolo and San Miguel, Municipality of Bauan, Province of Batangas, with an area of TWO HUNDRED TWENTY NINE THOUSAND ONE HUNDRED EIGHTY SIX (229,186) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
