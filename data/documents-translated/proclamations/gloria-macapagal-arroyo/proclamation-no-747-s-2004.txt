MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 747

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE NEW ALBAY ELEMENTARY SCHOOL A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN NEW ALBAY, MARAGUSAN, COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I , GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserved for school site purposes of the New Albay Elementary School, under the administration of the Department of Education, (DepEd), a certain parcel of land of public domain situated in New Albay, Maragusan, Compostela Valley Province, Island of Mindanao, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land more particularly described as follows:

Lot No. 574-A, Csd-11-006073-D
