PROCLAMATION NO. 1654

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND LOCATED ALONG MCARTHUR HIGHWAY, ABANGAN NORTE, MARILAO, BULACAN AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY PARK PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000, as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004, and Resolution No. 06-018 dated 26 January 2006 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the First Bulacan information Technology Park located along McArthur Highway, Abangan Norte, Marilao, Bulacan, as Information Technology Park, with an area of ELEVEN THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED EIGHTY SIX (11,486) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
