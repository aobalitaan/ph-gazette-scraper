MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 724

AMENDING PROCLAMATION NO. 42 DATED JULY 5, 1954 AND THE SERIES OF PERTINENT SUBSEQUENT PROCLAMATIONS, BY TRANSFERRING THE ADMINISTRATION AND DEVELOPMENT OF PORTIONS OF QUEZON PARK RESERVATION, SPECIFICALLY PORTIONS OF R.P. LOT NOS. 3-B-4-B-1-B-4-D AND 3-B-3-A-1-B-3-B-2 FROM THE PARKS AND WILDLIFE BUREAU (FORMERLY PARKS AND WILDLIFE OFFICE), DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES, TO THE NATIONAL HOUSING AUTHORITY, RESERVING THE SAME FOR GOVERNMENT OFFICE SITES.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby amend Proclamation No. 42 dated July 5, 1954 and the series of pertinent subsequent proclamations, by transferring the administrative jurisdiction, control, management, development and disposition of portions of R.P. Lot Nos. 3-B-4-B-1-B-4-D and 3-B-3-A-1-B-3-B-2, which form part of the Quezon Memorial Park Reservation at North Triangle Subdivision and East Triangle Subdivision, respectively, from the Parks and Wildlife Bureau (formerly Parks and Wildlife Office), Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR) to the National Housing Authority (NHA), which portions are, subject to final survey, more or less generally identified as follows:

(Portion of Lot R.P. 3-B-4-B-1-B-4-D, Bsd-22019) TCT NO. RT-121839 (314361) Titled to: Peoples Homesite and Housing Corporation

A parcel of land being a portion of Lot RP-3-B-4-B-1-B-4-B-1-B-4-D, Bsd-22019 situated in North Triangle Subdivision, Diliman, Quezon City, bounded on the SE., by DENR-NCR Nursery Site (remaining portion of Lot RP-3-B-4-B-1-B-4-D, Bsd-22019), on the SW., by Philippine Science High School Compound; on the NW., by Agham Road and on the NE., by North Avenue, containing an area of ELEVEN THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED SEVENTY ONE (11,471) square meters, more or less, subject to final survey.

(Portion Lot RP-3-B-3-A-1-B-3-B-2, Bsd-22015) TCT NO. 305409 Titled to: National Housing Authority

A parcel of land being a portion of Lot RP-3-B-3-A-1-B-3-B-2, Bsd-22015 situated in East Triangle Subdivision, Diliman, Quezon City. Bounded on the SW., by BIR Road; on the NW., by Quezon Avenue; on the NE., by Lung Center of the Philippines and on the SE., by remaining portion of Lot RP-3-B-3-A-1-B-3-B-2, Bsd-22015 (DENR-NCR and LLDA Office site) and PAGASA Compound containing an area of FIFTY THOUSAND (50,000) square meters more or less, subject to final survey.

The same shall be reserved for government office sites, of which the proceeds from the development and disposition thereof shall finance the much needed revenue for the socialized housing program of the government.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 25th day of October in the Year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Acting Executive Secretary
