PROCLAMATION NO. 1686

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING KNOWN AS WALTERMART-NORTH EDSA ESTABLISHED ON PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT EPIFANIO DELOS SANTOS AVENUE (EDSA), BARANGAY VETERANS VILLAGE, QUEZON CITY AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000, as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004 and Resolution No. 07-466 dated 10 October 2007 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Waltermart – North EDSA with a gross floor area of 54,000 square meters, more or less, established on parcels of land located at Epifanio debs Santos Avenue (EDSA), Barangay Veterans Village, Quezon City, containing an area of TWELVE THOUSAND FIVE (12,005) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
