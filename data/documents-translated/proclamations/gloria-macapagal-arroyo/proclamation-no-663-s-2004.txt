MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 663

RESERVING FOR GOVERNMENT CENTER SITE PURPOSES OF THE MUNICIPAL GOVERNMENT OF MARASUGAN, COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE, CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN, SITUATED AT POBLACION, SAN MARIANO (NOW MARAGUSAN), COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and pursuant to the authority vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, entry, disposition or settlement and reserve for government center site purposes of the Municipal Government of Maragusan, Compostela Valley Province, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated at Poblacion, San Mariano (now Maragusan), Compostela Valley Province, Island of Mindanao containing an aggregate area of Eighty One Thousand Nine Hundred Forty Seven (81,947) square meters , subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:
