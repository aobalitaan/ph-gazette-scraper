MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 19

CREATING AND DESIGNATING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND SITUATED IN BARRIO LUZ BANZON & SOLANA, MUNICIPALITY OF JASAAN, PROVINCE OF MISAMIS ORIENTAL, AS A SPECIAL ECONOMIC ZONE PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, as a special economic zone, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 00-228 dated 27 July 2000 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, a certain parcel of land located in Barrio Luz Banzon and Solana, Municipality of Jasaan, Province of Misamis Oriental, to be known as JASAAN MISAMIS ORIENTAL ECOZONE, consisting of TWO HUNDRED FIFTY TWO THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED (252,500) SQUARE METERS, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
