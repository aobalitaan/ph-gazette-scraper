MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO . 510

CREATING AND DESIGNATING SIX (6) PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PRIVATE DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARANGAY OF CANLUBANG, CITY OF CALAMBA, PROVINCE OF LAGUNA, ISLAND OF LUZON TO BE ANNEXED INTO THE CARMELRAY INDUSTRIAL PARK I PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995, as amended, and, upon the recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate as part of Carmelray Industrial Park I, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916, as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolution No. 03-224 dated 09 September 2003 of the PEZA Board of Directors, six (6) parcels of land, all located in the Barangay of Canlubang, City of Calamba, Province of Laguna, consisting an area of ONE HUNDRED ONE THOUSAND TWENTY SIX (101,026) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical descriptions:
