MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1009

RESERVING CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT TUBURAN, MAWAB, COMPOSTELA VALLEY PROVINCE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO FOR MILITARY, TRAINING SITE AND CAMP SITES PURPOSES OF THE 701 ST INFANTRY (KAUGNAY) BRIGADE, PHILIPPINE ARMY

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Department of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale and settlement and reserve for Military, Training and Camp sites purposes of the 701st Infantry (Kaugnay) Brigade, Philippine Army, under the administration of the Department of National Defense, subject to future ground survey and private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain containing a total land area of FOUR HUNDRED FIFTY SEVEN THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED SIXTY TWO (457,462) SQUARE METERS , more or less, situated in Tuburan, Mawab, Compostela Valley Province, Island of Mindanao, which parcels are more particularly described as follows:

PARCEL I

A parcel of land, (Parcel I, Campsite I, L.C. Map No. 1887) situated in Tuburan, Mawab, Compostela Valley Province, Island of Mindanao, bounded on North-East along line 18-19-20-21-22, by Timberland; on the South-East along lines 1-2-3-4-5 by Timberland; on the South-West along lines 5-6-7-8-9-10-11-12-13 by Timberland; and on the North-West along lines 13-14-15-16-17-18 by Timberland.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 84 deg. 56’W., 3201.04 m. from BLLM No. 33, Cad. 276, Tagum Cadastre:

beginning, containing an area of ONE HUNDRED FORTY FIVE THOUSAND FOUR HUNDRED SIXTY SIX (145,466) SQUARE METERS , more or less.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by stakes. The perimeter survey was undertaken by Land Management Sector Team under Geodetic Engineer Andres C. Gumapac per L.C. Map No. 1887 Certified on August 27, 1995.

PARCEL II

A parcel of land, (Parcel II, Training Site, L.C. Map No. 1887) situated in Tuburan, Mawab, Compostela Valley Province, Island of Mindanao, bounded on North-East along line 9-10 by Timberland; on the South-East along lines 10-11-12-13-1 and on the South-West along lines 1-2-3 and on the North-West along lines 4-5-6-7-8-9 all by Timberland.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 80 deg. 46’W., 3972.74 m. from BLLM No. 33, Cad. 276, Tagum Cadastre:

Beginning, containing an area of TWO HUNDRED SIXTY FIVE THOUSAND TWO HUNDRED SIXTY SIX (265,266) SQUARE METERS , more or less.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by stakes. The perimeter survey was undertaken by Land Management Sector Team under Geodetic Engineer Andres C. Gumapac per L.C. Map. No. 1887 Certified on August 27, 1995.

PARCEL III

A parcel of land, (Parcel III, Campsite II, L.C. Map No. 1887) situated in Tuburan, Mawab, Compostela Valley Province, Island of Mindanao, bounded on North along line 2-3-4; on the North East along lines 4-5; on the South East along line 5-6; on the South along lines 6-7-8; and on the South West along lines 8-1-2; all of Timberland.

Beginning at a point marked “1” on plan, being N. 74 deg. 54’W., 3288.02 m. from BLLM No. 33, Cad. 276, Tagum Cadastre:

beginning, containing an area of FORTY SIX THOUSAND SEVEN HUNDRED THIRTY (46,730) SQUARE METERS , more or less.

All points referred to are indicated on the plan and are marked on the ground by stakes. The perimeter survey was undertaken by Land Management Sector Team under Geodetic Engineer Andres C. Gumapac per L.C. Map No. 1887 Certified on August 27, 1995.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 20th day of February in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary
