MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 308

DECLARING MONDAY, DECEMBER 23, 2002, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE CITY OF BAYAWAN, PROVINCE OF NEGROS ORIENTAL

WHEREAS, Monday, December 23, 2002, marks the 2 nd Charter Day anniversary of the City of Bayawan.

WHEREAS, it is only proper to give the people of the City of Bayawan full opportunity to commemorate the occasion with fitting ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, ALBERTO G. ROMULO, Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, December 23, 2002 as a special day in the City of Bayawan, Province of Negros Oriental.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this, 20th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

By order of the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
