MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1145

CREATING AND DESIGNATING THE JY SQUARE IT CENTER III SITUATED AT SALINAS DRIVE, BARANGAY LAHUG, CEBU CITY, ISLAND OF CEBU, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA), I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby create and designate as Information Technology Center , subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and Resolutions Nos. 00-411 and 06-001 dated 29 December 2000 and 10 January 2006, respectively, of the PEZA Board of Directors, the JY Square IT Center III, which stands on a 5,300-square meter lot, more or less, located at Salinas Drive, Lahug, Cebu City, as defined by the following technical descriptions:

LOT 2-C-2-A TCT NO. T-70836
