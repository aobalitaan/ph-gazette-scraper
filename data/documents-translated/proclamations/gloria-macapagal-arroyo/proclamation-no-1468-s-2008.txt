PROCLAMATION NO. 1468

DECLARING MONDAY, MARCH 3, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF LA UNION

WHEREAS, March 2, 2008, marks the 158 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of La Union which falls on a Sunday;

WHEREAS, to enable the people of the Province of La Union celebrate the occasion, the Sangguniang Panlalawigan of La Union requested that Monday, March 3, 2008 be declared as a special (non-working) day instead;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of La Union be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, March 3, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Province of La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 28 th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
