MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 1122

DECLARING THURSDAY, AUGUST 10, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN SAN JOSE CITY

WHEREAS , Thursday, August 10, 2006, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of San Jose City;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of San Jose City, be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, August 10, 2006, as a special (non-working) day in San Jose City.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9th day of August, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
