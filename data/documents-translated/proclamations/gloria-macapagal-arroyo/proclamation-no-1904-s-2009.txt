PROCLAMATION NO. 1904

RESERVING PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN AS MUNICIPAL PUBLIC MARKET SITE, MUNICIPAL TOWN HALL SITE, MUNICIPAL PUBLIC AUDITORIUM SITE, AND MUNICIPAL PARK SITE PURPOSES FOR THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNIT OF SAN MANUEL, PANGASINAN, SITUATED IN BARANGAY POBLACION, MUNICIPALITY OF SAN MANUEL, PROVINCE OF PANGASINAN, ISLAND OF LUZON

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of the Department of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from entry, sale, settlement or disposition and reserve certain parcels of land as Municipal Public Market Site, Municipal Town Hall Site, Municipal Public Auditorium site, and Municipal Park Site purposes for the Local Government Unit of San Manuel, Pangasinan, subject to private rights, if any there be, situated in Barangay Poblacion, Municipality of San Miguel, Province of Pangasinan, Island of Luzon, which parcels of land are more particularly described as follows:

The tract or tracts of land herein reserved shall be non-alienable and shall not be subject to occupation, entry, sale lease or other mode of disposition until again declared as alienable by proclamation of the President.

The areas covered by this proclamation shall be under the administrative jurisdiction, supervision, and control of the Local Government Unit of San Manuel, Pangasinan.

WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 30 th day of September, in the year of Our Lord Two Thousand and Nine.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO
