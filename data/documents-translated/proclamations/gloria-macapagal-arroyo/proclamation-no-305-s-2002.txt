MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 305

DECLARING THURSDAY, JANUARY 16, 2003, AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF NAVOTAS, METRO MANILA

WHEREAS, Thursday, January 16, 2003, marks the 97 th year Founding Anniversary of the Municipality of Navotas;

WHEREAS, it is fitting that the people of the Municipality of Navotas be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Thursday, January 16, 2003, as a special day in the Municipality of Navotas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13 th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
