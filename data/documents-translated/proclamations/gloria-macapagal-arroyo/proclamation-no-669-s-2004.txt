MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 669

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE IHAN NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL, A CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED AT IHAN, KIBLAWAN, PROVINCE OF DAVAO DEL SUR, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or any other form of disposition or settlement and reserve for school site purposes of the Ihan National High School, under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain containing an area of FIFTEEN THOUSAND (15,000) SQUARE METERS , situated at Ihan, Kiblawan, Province of Davao del Sur, subject to private rights, if there be any, and to final survey, which portion of land is more particularly described as follows:

A PARCEL OF LAND (Lot 1075-A, Csd-112406-011440), situated in Barangay Ihan, Municipality of Kiblawan, Davao del Sur.
