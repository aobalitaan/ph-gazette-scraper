MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 312

RESERVING FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES CERTAIN PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN THE BARRIO OF PURIKAY, MUNICIPALITY OF LEBAK, PROVINCE OF SULTAN KUDARAT, ISLAND OF MINDANAO

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, President of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale or settlement and reserved for school site purposes of the Purikay Central Elementary School, subject to private rights, if any there be, certain parcels of land of the public domain situated at Barrio Purikay, Municipality of Lebak, Province of Sultan Kudarat, Island of Mindanao, and which parcel of land is more particularly bounded and described as follows:

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 6th day of January , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Three.
