MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 784

DECLARING AS CIVIL RESERVATION FOR SCHOOL SITE PURPOSES OF THE BARRA ELEMENTARY AND NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOLS, CERTAIN PARCEL OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN BARRA, DIPOLOG CITY, PROVINCE OF ZAMBOANGA DEL NORTE, ISLAND OF MINDANAO.

Upon recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL ARROYO, President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby declare as civil reservation for school site purposes of the Barra Elementary and High Schools under the administration of the Department of Education, a certain parcel of land of the public domain situated in Barra, Dipolog City, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, Island of Mindanao, subject to private rights, if any there be, which parcel of land is more particularly described as follows:

LOT 1, SGS-09-000639

A PARCEL OF LAND, (Lot 1, Sgs-09-000639, being a portion of Lot 914, Cad-85, situated in Barra, Dipolog City, Province of Zamboanga del Norte, Island of Mindanao.
