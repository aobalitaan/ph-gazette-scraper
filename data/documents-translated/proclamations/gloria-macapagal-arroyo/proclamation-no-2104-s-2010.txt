MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2104

PROPOSED PROCLAMATION RESERVING PARCELS OF LAND OF THE PUBLIC DOMAIN SITUATED IN KM. 48, CANOCOTAN, TAGUM CITY, DAVAO DEL NORTE, AS OFFICE SITE PURPOSES FOR THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS AND HIGHWAYS (DPWH) AND BUREAU OF EQUIPMENT (BOE), AND GRANTING OWNERSHIP THEREOF IN FAVOR OF THE REPUBLIC OF THE PHILIPPINES-DPWH (RP-DPWH)

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Environment and Natural Resources and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Republic of the Philippines, do hereby withdraw from sale, lease settlement and reserve certain parcel of land of the public domain as office site purposes and grant ownership thereof in favor of the Republic of the Philippines – Department of Public Works and Highways (RP-DPWH), subject to private rights, if any there be, situated in Km-48, Canocotan, City of Tagum, Province of Davao del Norte, Island of Mindanao, which parcels of land are particularly described as follows:
