PROCLAMATION NO. 1817

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JULY 8, 2009 AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN ISABELA CITY, BASILAN

WHEREAS, Wednesday, July 8, 2009, celebrates the town fiesta of the City of Isabela, Province of Basilan;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the City of Isabela be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by the power of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, July 8, 2009, as a special (non-working) day in the City of Isabela, Province of Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila this 30 th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Acting Deputy Executive Secretary

for General Administration
