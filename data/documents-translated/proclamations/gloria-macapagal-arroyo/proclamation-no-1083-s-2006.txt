MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1083

DECLARING MONDAY, JUNE 19, 2006, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF CALAMBA

WHEREAS , Monday, June 19, 2006 marks the 145 th birth anniversary of Dr. Jose P. Rizal, our national hero;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the residents of the City of Calamba, his birthplace, should be given full opportunity to celebrate the day with appropriate ceremonies in keeping with his ideals and teachings.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of bier Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Monday, June 19, 2006, as special (non-working) day in the City of Calamba.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 7th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Six.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: (Sgd.) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
