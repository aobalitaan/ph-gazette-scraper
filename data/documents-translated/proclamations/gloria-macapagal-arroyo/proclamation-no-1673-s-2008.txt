PROCLAMATION NO. 1673

DECLARING MONDAY, DECEMBER 8, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF AGOO, LA UNION

WHEREAS, Monday, December 8, 2008, marks the 430 th Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Agoo, La Union;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Agoo, La Union be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Monday, December 8, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Agoo, La Union.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21 st day of November, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
