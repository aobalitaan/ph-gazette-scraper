MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1100

CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING TO BE KNOWN AS DPC PLACE BUILDING WHICH STANDS ON TWO PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT 2322 CHINO ROCES AVENUE, MAKATI CITY, AS INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916, AS AMENDED, BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000 and Resolution. No. 04-081 dated 26 March 2004 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the DPC Place Building as an Information Technology Center which stands on two parcels of land of the private domain located at 2322 Chino Roces Avenue, Makati City, containing an aggregate area of Two Thousand Nine Hundred Seventy Seven (2,977) Square Meters, more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
