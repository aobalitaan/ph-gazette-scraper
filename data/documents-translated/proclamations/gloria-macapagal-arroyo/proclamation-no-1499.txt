PROCLAMATION NO. 1499

DECLARING WEDNESDAY, JUNE 18, 2008, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF IFUGAO.

WHEREAS, Wednesday, June 18, 2008, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Province of Ifugao;

WHEREAS, it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Province of Ifugao be given full opportunity to celebrate the occasion with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THERFORE I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA, Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO, do hereby declare Wednesday, June 18, 2008, as special (non-working) day in the Province of Ifugao.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, I have hereunto set my hand and caused seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 9 th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Eight.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary:

Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
