MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 1770

DECLARING TUESDAY, JUNE 9, 2009, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE MUNICIPALITY OF ROSARIO, PROVINCE OF BATANGAS

WHEREAS , Tuesday, June 9, 2009, marks the Foundation Day anniversary of the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas;

WHEREAS , it is but fitting and proper that the people of the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas, be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their municipality’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Executive Secretary, by authority of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Tuesday, June 9, 2009, as special (non-working) day in the Municipality of Rosario, Province of Batangas.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of May , in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Nine.

By authority of the President: ( Sgd .) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
