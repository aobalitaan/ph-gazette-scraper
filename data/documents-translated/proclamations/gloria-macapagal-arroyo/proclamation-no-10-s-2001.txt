MALACAÑANG MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 10

DECLARING TUESDAY, 27 FEBRUARY 2001 AS A SPECIAL DAY IN THE PROVINCE OF COTABATO

WHEREAS , the Province of Cotabato will hold a two-day activity on the occasion of the culmination of the Jubilee Celebration on 26-27 February 2001;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of Cotabato be given full opportunity to participate in this once in a lifetime celebration;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby declare Tuesday, 27 February 2001 as a special day in the province of Cotabato.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 26 th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand One.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) RENATO S. DE VILLA Executive Secretary
