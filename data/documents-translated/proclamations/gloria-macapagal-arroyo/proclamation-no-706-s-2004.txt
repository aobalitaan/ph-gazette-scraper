MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 706

DECLARING THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 9, 2004, AS SPECIAL (NON-WORKING) DAY IN THE CITY OF BAIS

WHEREAS , Thursday, September 9, 2004, the City of Bais marks its Charter Day anniversary;

WHEREAS , it is fitting and proper that the people of the City of Bais be given full opportunity to participate in these momentous events.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, EDUARDO R. ERMITA , Acting Executive Secretary, by order of Her Excellency, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , do hereby declare Thursday, September 9, 2004, as special (non-working) day in the City of Bais.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 7th day of September, in the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Four.

By order of the President: ( Sgd .) EDUARDO R. ERMITA Acting Executive Secretary

By the Executive Secretary: ( Sgd .) JOAQUIN C. LAGONERA Senior Deputy Executive Secretary
