MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 189

DECLARING THURSDAY, APRIL 25, 2002, AS A SPECIAL NON-WORKING DAY IN THE CITY OF ISABELA, PROVINCE OF BASILAN

WHEREAS , Republic Act No. 9023 converting the Municipality of Isabela into a component city of the Province of Basilan, was signed into law on April 25, 2001;

WHEREAS , Thursday, April 25, 2002, marks the 1st Charter Day anniversary of the City of Isabela;

WHEREAS , it is fitting that the people of the City of Isabela be given full opportunity to celebrate this milestone in their city’s history with appropriate ceremonies.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me in Book I, Chapter 7, Section 27, of the Administrative Code of 1987, do hereby declare Thursday, April 25, 2002, as a special non-working day in the City of Isabela, Province of Basilan.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF , I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 25th day of April, in the year of Our Lord, two thousand and two.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President: (Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary
