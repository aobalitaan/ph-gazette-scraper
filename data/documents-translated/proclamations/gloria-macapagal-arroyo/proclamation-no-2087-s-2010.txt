MALACAÑANG Manila

PROCLAMATION NO. 2087

PROPOSED PROCLAMATION CREATING AND DESIGNATING A BUILDING TO BE KNOWN AS PLAZUELA DE ILOILO WHICH SHALL STAND ON PARCELS OF LAND LOCATED AT BENIGNO AQUINO AVENUE, BARANGAY SAN RAFAEL, MANDURRIAO, ILOILO CITY, AS AN INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CENTER PURSUANT TO REPUBLIC ACT NO. 7916 AS AMENDED BY REPUBLIC ACT NO. 8748

Pursuant to the powers vested in me under Republic Act No. 7916, otherwise known as the Special Economic Zone Act of 1995 as amended by Republic Act No. 8748 and upon recommendation of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, I, GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO , President of the Philippines, do hereby create and designate, subject to the provisions of Republic Act No. 7916 as amended, its Implementing Rules and Regulations, Resolution No. 00-411 dated 29 December 2000, as amended by Resolution No. 04-295 dated 20 September 2004 and Resolution No. 10-104 dated 18 March 2010 of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Economic Zone Authority, the Plazuela de Iloilo with a gross floor area of 8,528 square meters, more or less, which shall stand on parcels of land located at Benigno Aquino Avenue, Barangay San Rafael, Mandurriao, Iloilo City, containing an aggregate area of TWELVE THOUSAND FIVE HUNDRED THIRTY THREE (12,533) SQUARE METERS , more or less, as defined by the following technical description:
