MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

PROCLAMATION NO. 429

REVOKING PROCLAMATION NO. 243 DATED MARCH 27, 1951, AND DECLARING OPEN TO DISPOSITION THE LAND EMBRACED THEREIN SITUATED IN PASAY CITY.

Upon the recommendation of the Secretary of Agriculture and Natural Resources, and by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, Elpidio Quirino, President of the Philippines, do hereby revoke Proclamation No. 243, dated March 27, 1951, and declare open to disposition the parcel of land embraced therein situated in Pasay City.

In witness whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the seal of the Republic of the Philippines to be affixed.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-three, and of the Indepedence of the Philippines, the eighth.

ELPIDIO QUIRINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

MARCIANO ROQUE Acting Executive Secretary
