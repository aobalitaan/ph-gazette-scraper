MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO 58

RECONSTITUTING THE CABINET CRISIS COMMITTEE

The Cabinet Crisis Committee created to attend to the matter of preventing a recurrence of the January 26th, 1987 attempted coup and how to meet a similar situation is hereby reconstituted as follows:

This Administrative Order takes effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 16th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-eight.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
