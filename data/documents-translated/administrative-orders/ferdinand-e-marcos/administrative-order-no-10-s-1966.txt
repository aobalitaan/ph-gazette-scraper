MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 10

CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE CELEBRATION OF PHILIPPINE INDEPENDENCE DAY ON JUNE 12, 1966

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby create a National Committee to take charge of the celebration of Philippine Independence Day on June 12, 1966.

The Committee shall be composed of the following:

The Committee shall meet at the call of the Chairman and, for the purpose of discharging its functions, may create such-committees as may be necessary.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred sixty-six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
