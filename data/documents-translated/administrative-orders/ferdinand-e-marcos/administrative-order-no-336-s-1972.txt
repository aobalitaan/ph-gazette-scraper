MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 336

FURTHER AMENDING ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 228 DATED JULY 27, 1970

Administrative Order No. 228 dated July 27, 1970, reconstituting the Executive Committee of the Central Luzon Development Program, is hereby further amended so as to designate Secretary ARTURO R. TANCO, JR. as Chairman of the Committee vice Undersecretary Jose M. Crisol.

Done in the City of Manila, this 5th day of Sept., in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
