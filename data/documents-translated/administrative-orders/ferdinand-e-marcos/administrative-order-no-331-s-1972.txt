MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 331

MODIFYING ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 248 DATED NOVEMBER 17, 1970, BY CONSIDERING MR. PEDRO R. RABAGO, FORMER MUNICIPAL JUDGE OF CAGWAIT, SURIGAO DEL SUR, AS RETIRED FROM THE SERVICE

Upon petition for reconsideration filed by former Municipal Judge Pedro R. Rabago of Cagwait, Surigao del Sur, who was removed from office under Administrative Order No. 248 dated November 17, 1970, and after a restudy of the facts and the circumstances of the case, coupled with his length of service (more than 24 years) in the Government, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby modify Administrative Order No. 248 dated November 17, 1970, by considering him as retired from the service, effective on his last day of service with pay.

Done in the City of Manila, this 31st day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-two.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
