December 26, 1972

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 463

In the interest of the administration of justice and pursuant to the provisions of Section 51 of Republic Act No. 206, as amended, the Honorable EUFROCINIO DELA MERCED, District Judge of Palawan and Puerto Princesa, City Branch III at Brooke’s Point, is hereby authorized to hold court in Masbate, for a period of not more than three (3) months beginning January 2, 1973, or as soon thereafter as practicable, for the purpose of trying all kinds of cases and to enter judgments therein.

(Sgd.) VICENTE ABAD SANTOS Secretary of Justice
