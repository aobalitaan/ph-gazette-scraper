MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 204

AMENDING ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 175 DATED JULY 14, 1969, ENTITLED “CREATING A COMMITTEE TO INVESTIGATE THE CHARGES OF THE PROVINCIAL GOVERNOR OF PANGASINAN AGAINST LICERIO P. SENDAYDIEGO, PROVINCIAL TREASURER OF THE SAID PROVINCE

Administrative Order No. 175, dated July 14, 1969, is hereby amended by empowering the same committee created therein to investigate the administrative charges files by the Provincial Governor of Camarines Norte against Provincial Treasurer Esteban Enchano of said province.

Done in the City of Manila, this 21st day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary
