December 14, 1972

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 447

In the interest of the administration of justice and considering that there is no certainty as to when District Judges may be appointed to preside over Branches II and VII of the Court of First Instance of Iloilo, all the cases pending therein are hereby distributed by raffle among all the other Branches of said Court in such a manner that all Branches will have more or less an even number of cases after the distribution.

This cancels Administrative Order No. 380, dated October 31, 1972, of this Department, authorizing Judge Valerio Rovira to take cognizance of all cases pertaining to Branch II.

(Sgd.) VICENTE ABAD SANTOS Secretary of Justice
