December 18, 1972

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 456

In the interest of the public service, Mr. FLORENCIO V. SERRA, First Assistant City Fiscal, Legaspi City, is hereby detailed with the Prosecution Staff, this Department, effective immediately and to continue until December 31, 1973.

Fiscal Serra shall report to the Chief. State Prosecutor for assignment and during this detail he shall not be entitled to per diems while in the City of Manila.

This is a renewal of Administrative Order No. 187, series of 1972, also of this Department.

(Sgd.) VICENTE ABAD SANTOS Secretary of Justice
