MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 5

The membership of the Philippine Panel to the RP-US Joint Veterans Commission created under Administrative Order No. 125 dated May 12, 1965, as reconstituted under Administrative Order No. 4 dated February 21, 1966, is hereby amended to read as follows:

Done in the City of Manila, this 24th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred sixty-six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary
