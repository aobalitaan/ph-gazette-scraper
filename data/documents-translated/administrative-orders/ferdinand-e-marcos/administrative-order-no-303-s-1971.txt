MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 303

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO INVESTIGATE THE COMPLAINTS OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF CARIGARA AND THE CARIGARA FARMERS COOPERATIVE ASSOCIATION AGAINST THE LOGGING OPERATIONS OF THE NORTHERN LEYTE CONSOLIDATED TIMBER CORPORATION WITHIN THE PROPOSED CARIGARA WATERSHED RESERVATION

By virtue of the powers vested in me, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, do hereby create a committee to investigate the complaints of the Municipal Council of Carigara, Leyte, and the Carigara Farmers Cooperative Association against the logging operations of the Northern Leyte Consolidated Timber Corporation within the proposed Carigara Watershed Reservation to be composed of the following:

The Committee may ask any department, bureau, office, agency or instrumentality of the government for such assistance as it may need in the performance of its functions.

The Committee shall submit its report and recommendation to the President as soon as practicable, together with a draft of proclamation establishing the Carigara Watershed Reservation.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of July, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-one.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
