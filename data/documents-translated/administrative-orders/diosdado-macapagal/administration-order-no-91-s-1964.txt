MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 91

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF ALL ARRANGEMENTS CONNECTED WITH THE 9TH COUNCIL OF MINISTERS AND 10TH MILITARY ADVISERS MEETINGS OF THE SOUTH EAST ASIA TREATY ORGANIZATION TO BE HELD ON APRIL 9 TO 15, 1964.

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL, President of the Philippines, do hereby create a Committee to take charge of all arrangements in connection with the meetings of the Council of Ministers and Military Advisers of the South East Asia Treaty Organization to be held on April 9 to 15, 1964.

The Committee shall be composed of the following:

The Committee shall be responsible for the planning, coordination, and smooth execution of all arrangements that will be made in connection with the meetings.

The Committee is authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency, or instrumentality of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in discharging its functions.

Done in the City of Manila, this 13th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-four.

(Sgd.) DIOSDADO MACAPAGAL President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) CALIXTO O. ZALDIVAR Acting Executive Secretary
