MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 357

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF ALL ARRANGEMENTS CONNECTED WITH THE FORTHCOMING VISIT OF GENERAL OF THE ARMY DOUGLAS MACARTHUR

By virtue of the power vested in me by law, I, Carlos P. Garcia, President of the Philippines, do hereby create a committee to take charge of all arrangements connected with the forthcoming visit of General of the Army Douglas MacArthur, including his reception, stay, and departure. The committee shall be composed of the following:

The committee shall be responsible for the planning, coordination, and smooth execution of all arrangements that will be made in connection with the said visit.

The committee is hereby authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency, or instrumentality of the government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in discharging its duties and functions.

Done in the City of Manila, this 26th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and sixty-one, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the fifteenth.

(Sgd.) CARLOS P. GARCIA President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) NATALIO P. CASTILLO Executive Secretary
