MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 304

There is hereby created a Fact-Finding Committee to look into and investigate the allegations contained in the privilege speech of Senate President Ernesto M. Maceda delivered on November 29, 1996.
