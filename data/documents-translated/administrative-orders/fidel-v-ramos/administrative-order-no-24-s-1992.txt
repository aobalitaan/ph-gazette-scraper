MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 24

RECONSTITUTING THE MEMBERSHIP OF THE RIZAL DAY NATIONAL COMMITTEE IN CONNECTION WITH THE OBSERVANCE OF THE 96TH DEATH ANNIVERSARY OF DR. JOSE P. RIZAL ON DECEMBER 30, 1992

I, FIDEL V. RAMOS, President of the Republic of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby reconstitute the membership of the Rizal Day National Committee in connection with the observance of the 96th Death Anniversary of Dr. Jose P. Rizal; on December 30, 1992, as follows:

This Administrative Order shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 17th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and ninety-two.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) EDELMIRO A. AMANTE, SR. Executive Secretary
