MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 39

AUTHORIZING THE GRANT OF A ONE-TIME FINANCIAL
ASSISTANCE OF 20,000.00 TO EMPLOYEES’ COMPENSATION
PENSIONERS

WHEREAS, Article 177(e) of Presidential Decree (PD) No. 626 (s. 1974),
entitled “Further Amending Certain Articles of Presidential Decree No. 442 entitled
‘Labor Code of the Philippines’,” as amended, empowers the Employees’
Compensation Commission (ECC) to (i) make the necessary actuarial studies and
calculations concerning the grant of constant help and income benefits for permanent
disability or death, and the rationalization of benefits for permanent disability and
death; and (ii) upgrade benefits and add new ones, subject to approval of the
President, provided that the actuarial stability of the Social Insurance Fund (SIF) shall
be guaranteed and such increases in benefits shall not require any increase in
contribution;

WHEREAS, the grant of a one-time financial assistance of *20,000.00 to
Employees’ Compensation (EC) pensioners will benefit more or less 31,000
pensioners of the Social Security System (SSS) and Government Service Insurance
System (GSIS);

WHEREAS, the results of the actuarial studies of the SSS and GSIS show that
the SIF can finance the one-time grant without affecting its stability, and without
requiring additional contributions;

WHEREAS, through Board Resolution Nos. 20-07-14 (s. 2020) and 20-08-17
(s. 2020), the ECC approved the proposed grant of a one-time financial assistance,
based on the observation that pensioners experience undue financial hardships
because of their vulnerabilities, and in anticipation of the prolonged economic impact
of the pandemic on their lives; and

WHEREAS, the adverse effects of the pandemic on the economy,
supplementary health necessities for the battle against the virus, and restrictions
imposed on our mobility and social interaction, have increased the financial and health
burdens experienced by the EC pensioners;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by law, do hereby order:

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

Section 1. Grant of Financial Assistance. The ECC is hereby authorized to
grant a one-time financial assistance of 20,000.00 to EC pensioners for permanent
partial disability, permanent total disability and survivorship, in the private and public
sectors, subject to the availability of funds and pertinent laws, rules and regulations.

Section 2. Appropriation and Release from the SIF. The ECC, SSS and
GSIS are hereby directed to appropriate and release the amounts necessary to cover
the one-time financial assistance from the reserves of the relevant SIF that the SSS
and GSIS administer under the EC Program.

Section 3. Implementing Rules and Regulations. The ECC, in coordination
with the SSS and GSIS, shall issue the implementing rules and regulations necessary
for the effective implementation of this Order.

Section 4. Effectivity. This Order shall take effect immediately following its
publication in.the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.

DONE in the City of Manila this 19thtay of April inthe Year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty One.
