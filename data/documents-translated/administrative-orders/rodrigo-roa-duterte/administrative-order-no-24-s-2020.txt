BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 24

MANDATING ALL NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES, GOVERNMENT -OWNED OR

~-CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AND STATE UNIVERSITIES AND COLLEGES
TO STRICTLY IMPLEMENT WATER CONSERVATION MEASURES

WHEREAS, Section 16, Article Il of the Constitution enshrines the policy of the State

to protect and advance the right of the people to a balanced and healthful ecology in accord
with the rhythm and harmony of nature;

WHEREAS, Executive Order (EO) No. 16 (s. 2001) created a Presidential Task

Force to enhance agency coordination in the formulation and implementation of measures
that will mitigate the effects of the El Nifio and La Nifia phenomena;

WHEREAS, Memorandum Order No. 38 (s. 2019) reactivated the El Nifo Task Force
to implement, among others, short-term solutions to ensure water security;

WHEREAS, based on the projections of the National Water Resources Board
(NWRB), the water level in Angat Dam will be below its normal high operating level of 210

meters from the months of January to March 2020 and below its minimum operating level of
180 meters from the months of April to May 2020;

WHEREAS, considering the above projections, the NWRB has reduced the water
allocation for the Metropolitan Waterworks and Sewerage System (MWSS) from its normal
demand of 44 cubic meter per second (m*/s) to 40 m*/s for January 2020, and for the National
Irrigation Administration (NIA) to 20 m%/s or fifty percent (50%) of the total allocation

requirement of the NIA for the dry cropping season in agricultural areas served by the Angat
Dam, such as the provinces of Bulacan and Pampanga;

WHEREAS, there is an urgent need to implement water conservation measures to
avert a looming water crisis and in order to attain at least a twenty percent (20%) reduction in

water consumption especially within the areas served by the Angat Dam, including Metro
Manila and parts of Cavite, Rizal and Bulacan provinces;

WHEREAS, under Republic Act (RA) No. 7160 or the “Local Government Code of
1991,” as amended, local government units (LGUs) are mandated to discharge the functions
and responsibilities of national agencies and offices devolved to them, which includes

enactment of ordinances that shall protect the quantity of water supply within their respective
jurisdictions;

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution confers to the President the

WHEREAS, under Section 4, Article X of the Constitution, the President exercises
general supervision over local governments;

; NOW, THEREFORE, I, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Philippines, by
virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and existing laws, do hereby order:

Section 1. All national government agencies, government-owned or —controlled
corporations and state universities and colleges, especially those located within areas served
by the Angat Dam, are directed to urgently implement water conservation measures in
coordination with the El Nifio Task Force and the NWRB.

Section 2. The El Nifio Task Force, in coordination with the NWRB, MWSS, Local
Water Utilities Administration, Department of Human Settlements and Urban Development,
Department of Education, Commission on Higher Education and the Presidential
Communications Operations Office, shall undertake a massive information campaign and
various advocacy programs on water conservation in traditional and social media platforms,
schools, subdivisions and government offices.

Section 3. In accordance with RA No. 7160, as amended, all LGUs, especially those
within the areas served by the Angat Dam, shall issue advisories and enact ordinances that
will protect water supply within their respective jurisdictions. The Department of the Interior
and Local Government shall monitor compliance of concerned LGUs with this directive.

Section 4. Failure to comply with this Order shall result in the filing of appropriate
administrative cases pursuant to the Civil Service Commission Rules on Administrative Cases
in the Civil Service, RA No. 7160, as amended, and other relevant laws, rules and regulations.

Section 5. If any part or provision of this order is held unconstitutional or invalid,
other parts not affected thereby shall continue to be in full force and effect.

Section 6. This Order shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 18th day of March in the year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand Twenty.
