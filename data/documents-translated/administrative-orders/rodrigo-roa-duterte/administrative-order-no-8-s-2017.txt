MALACANAN PALACE
MANILA

BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO.8

RATIONALIZING THE COMPOSITION OF THE NATIONAL
ECONOMIC AND DEVELOPMENT AUTHORITY (NEDA) BOARD,
THE NEDA BOARD EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE (EXCOM), AND THE
INVESTMENT COORDINATION COMMITTEE (ICC)

WHEREAS, Executive Order (EO) No. 230 (s. 1987) defines the composition
of the NEDA Board, its powers and functions, and the composition, powers and
functions of its inter-agency committees;

WHEREAS, under Section 4 of EO No. 230, the President may revise the
membership of the NEDA Board whenever the same is deemed necessary for the
effective performance of the Board’s functions through an administrative or
memorandum order;

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 7653 or the New Central Bank Act, RA
No. 7638 or the Department of Energy Act of 1992, and RA No. 9996 or the
Mindanao Development Act of 2010 provide for ex-officio members of the NEDA
Board, namely: the Deputy Governor designated by the Governor of the Bangko
Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP), the Secretary of Energy, and the Chairperson of the
Mindanao Development Authority (MinDA);

WHEREAS, Memorandum Order (MO) No. 222 (s. 1994), as amended,
reactivated the NEDA Board ExCom constituted by virtue of NEDA Board Resolution
No. 17 (s. 1973), as amended, to facilitate action on matters requiring the decision of
the NEDA Board;

WHEREAS, EO No. 158 (s. 2002) reactivated and reorganized the NEDA
Board ExCom;

WHEREAS, Section 4 of EO No. 99 (s. 2012) provides that the Cabinet
Secretary shall be a member of the NEDA Board ExCom and the ICC;

WHEREAS, it is the policy of the Administration to streamline the decision-

making process in the bureaucracy, including the |CC and NEDA Board, for a more
effective and speedy disposition of matters presented for their approval;

THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

NOW, THEREFORE |, RODRIGO ROA DUTERTE, President of the Republic
of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution and
existing laws, do hereby order:

reconstituted and shall be composed of the following members:

Governor of the BSP

SECTION 4. Responsibilities of the NEDA Board ExCom. The NEDA Board
ExCom shall have the following powers and functions:

(a) Provide policy direction and resolve policy issues involving few
agencies or a specific socio-economic sector, without the necessity of
convening the entire NEDA Board, and in accordance with existing
laws, rules and regulations;

(b) Approve development plans and programs consistent with the policies
set by the President of the Philippines; and

(c) Confirm ICC-approved projects which are classified as extremely
urgent by the ICC.

The NEDA Board Secretariat shall submit, for approval of the NEDA Board,
guidelines and procedures governing ExCom meetings, within sixty (60) days from
the issuance of this Order.

SECTION 5. Separability. If any section or part of this Order is held unconstitutional
or invalid, the other sections or provisions not otherwise affected shall remain in full
force or effect.

SECTION 6. Repealing Clause. All orders, proclamations, rules, regulations or parts
thereof, which are inconsistent with this Order are hereby revoked, amended or
modified accordingly.

SECTION 7. Effectivity. This Order shall take effect immediately.
