MALACAÑAN PALACE MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 58

APPOINTING RAMON J. FERNANDEZ AS CHAIRMAN OF THE COMMITTEE TO STUDY THE PROPOSED ESTABLISHMENT OF A FOREIGN TRADE ZONE AT THE PORT OF MANILA

Mr. Ramon J. Fernandez is hereby appointed, vice Jose Paez, Chairman of the Committee, created by Administrative Order No. 52, dated November 19, 1397, to study the proposed establishment of a foreign trade zone at the port of Manila.

Done at the City of Manila, this 13th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and thirty-eight, and of the Commonwealth of the Philippines, the third.

(Sgd.) MANUEL L. QUEZON President of the Philippines
