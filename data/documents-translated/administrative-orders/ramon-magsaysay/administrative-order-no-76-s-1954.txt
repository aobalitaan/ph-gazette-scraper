MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 76

FURTHER AMENDING ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 37, SERIES OF 1954, AS AMENDED BY ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 62, SAME SERIES, ENTITLED “CREATING A COMMITTEE TO STUDY AND RECOMMEND IMPROVEMENTS OF THE LAW NATIONALIZING THE RETAIL TRADE”

By virtue of the powers vested in me by law, I, RAMON MAGSAYSAY, President of the Philippines, do hereby further amend Administrative Order No. 37, series of 1954, as amended by Administrative Order No. 62, same series, so as to include Atty. Efren V. Mendoza as additional member of the Committee created therein.

Done in the City of Manila, this 15th day of November, in the year of our Lord, nineteen hundred and fifty-four, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the ninth.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FRED RUIZ CASTRO Executive Secretary
