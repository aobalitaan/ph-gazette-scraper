MALACAÑANG MANILA

ADMINISTRATIVE ORDER NO. 233

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE CELEBRATION OF TRAFFIC SAFETY WEEK AND SAFE-DRIVING DAY

A committee is hereby created to take charge of all arrangements and activities for the fitting celebration this year of Traffic Safety Week and Safe-Driving Day under Proclamation No. 242 dated January 9, 1956, as amended. The committee shall be composed of the following:

The committee is hereby authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency, or instrumentality of the Government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, for such assistance as it may need in the celebration of Traffic Safety Week and Safety Driving Day.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of February, in the year of Our Lard, nineteen hundred and fifty-seven, and of the Independence of the Philippines, the eleventh.

(Sgd.) RAMON MAGSAYSAY President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) FORTUNATO DE LEON Executive Secretary
