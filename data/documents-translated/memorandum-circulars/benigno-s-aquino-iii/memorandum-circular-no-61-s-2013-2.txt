BY THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 61

CREATING AN INTER-AGENCY COMMITTEE FOR THE PHILIPPINE HOSTING OF
THE ASIA EUROPE MEETING (ASEM) CONFERENCE ON TECHNOLOGY
AND INNOVATION FOR DISASTER RISK REDUCTION AND MANAGEMENT
AND CLIMATE CHANGE ADAPTATION

There is hereby created an Inter-Agency Committee (IAC) to organize and
implement activities for the hosting of the Asia Europe Meeting (ASEM) Conference on
Technolegy and Innovation for Disaster Risk Reduction and Management and Climate
Change Adaptation on 08-09 September 2014. The IAC shall be composed of
representatives, with ranks not lower than Assistant Secretary, from the following
government agencies:

Co-Chairs:

Department of National Defense (DND)
Office of Civil Defense (OCD)
National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (NDRRMC)

Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA)
Office of European Affairs (OEA)

Members:

Department of Science and Technology (DOST)
Department of the Interior and Local Government (DILG)
Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSVVD)
National Economic and Development Authority (NEDA)
Department of Trade and Industry (DTI)

Climate Change Commission (CCC)

The IAC, supported by a Secretariat composed of DND-OCD and DFA-OEA,
shall closely coordinate with and call upon the heads of other relevant agencies for
assistance including the establishment of Technical Working Groups (TWGs), as may

be necessary.

The IAC shall perform the following tasks and functions:

a) Formulate a work program and budget plan for the hosting of the said
Conference; . .
