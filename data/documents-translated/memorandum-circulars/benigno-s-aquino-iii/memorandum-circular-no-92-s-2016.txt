Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 92

CREATING AN INTER-AGENCY TASK FORCE TO EXPEDITE THE ORGANIZATION OF THE PHILIPPINE COMPETITION COMMISSION

WHEREAS, Republic Act (RA) No. 10667 , or the “Philippine Competition Act,” established the Philippine Competition Commission to implement the national competition policy and the objectives and purposes under the law;

WHEREAS, the timely performance by the Commission of some of its crucial functions, such as the promulgation of the Implementing Rules and Regulations within 180 days from the effectivity of RA 10667, the implementation of a 2-year advocacy program, and the organization and mobilization of resources, necessitates the immediate organization and operation of the Commission;

WHEREAS, such initial organization may be hampered by the various prohibitions of certain activities during the forthcoming elections; and

WHEREAS, there is an urgent need to provide a temporary office space and other logistical support for the Chairperson and Members of the Commission.

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

SECTION 1. There is hereby established an Inter-Agency Task Force (IATF) composed of representatives, with a rank of not lower than Undersecretary, from the Office of the President, Department of Budget and Management, Development Academy of the Philippines, and the Commission. The IATF shall be headed by the Commission Chairperson or his duly designated representative.

The heads of the agencies comprising the IATF shall designate their respective representatives and thereafter, the IATF shall convene immediately upon call by the Commission Chairperson.
