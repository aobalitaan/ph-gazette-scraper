Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 63

ENJOINING ALL OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN ALL DEPARTMENTS, AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR –CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AS WELL AS ENCOURAGING LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO ACTIVELY PARTICIPATE IN THE OBSERVANCE OF THE 28 TH YEAR ANNIVERSARY OF THE EDSA PEOPLE POWER REVOLUTION OF 1986

WHEREAS , Executive Order (EO) No. 82 (s. 1999) , as amended by EO 17 (s. 2010) , created the EDSA People Power Commission (EPPC) to plan, organize, and implement the yearly commemoration of the 1986 EDSA People Power Revolution every 25 th of February; and

WHEREAS , the celebration of the 28 th year Anniversary of the EDSA People Power Revolution on 25 February 2014 will enable Filipinos all over the country to recall and rekindle the unity and principles expressed by the said historical event.

NOW, THEREFORE , all heads of departments, agencies, and instrumentalities of the national government, including Government-Owned or –Controlled Corporations (GOCCs) are hereby directed to extend their support and participate, while Local Government Units (LGUs) are hereby encouraged to extend their support and participate, in the programs and activities to be held for this purpose, including the following activities recommended by the EPPC:

This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) PAQUITO N. OCHOA, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, Philippines, 17 February 2014
