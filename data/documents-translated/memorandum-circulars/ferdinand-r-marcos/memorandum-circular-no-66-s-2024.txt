MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 66

The Philippines, in partnership with the United Nations Office for Disaster Risk
Reduction, will host the 10" Session of the Asia-Pacific Ministerial Conference on
Disaster Risk Reduction (APMCDRR) on 14 to 18 October 2024 at the Philippine
International Convention Center (PICC) in Manila, with the theme, “Surge to 2030:
Enhancing Ambition in Asia-Pacific to Accelerate Disaster Risk Reduction.”

In view of the numerous participants expected to travel to and within the Cities
of Manila and Pasay on 14 and 15 October 2024 for the opening of APMCDRR in
PICC, and to allow for the organized conduct of the APMCDRR, work in government
offices and classes at all levels in the Cities of Manila and Pasay shall be suspended
on 14 and 15 October 2024.

However, those agencies whose functions involve the delivery of basic and
health services, preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or the
performance of other vital services shall continue their operations and render the
necessary services.

The suspension of work for private companies and offices is left to the discretion
of their respective heads.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.
