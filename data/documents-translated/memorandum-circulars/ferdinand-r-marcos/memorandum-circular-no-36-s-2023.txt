MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 36

DIRECTING RELEVANT NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES TO
RENDER FULL SUPPORT AND ASSISTANCE TO THE CONGRESS
OF THE PHILIPPINES IN ITS HOSTING OF THE 31ST ANNUAL
MEETING OF THE ASIA PACIFIC PARLIAMENTARY FORUM IN
NOVEMBER 2023

WHEREAS, the Asia Pacific Parliamentary Forum (APPF) is the premier and
broadest parliamentary forum in the region, which serves as an avenue to discuss
pressing issues of common concern, address common challenges, identify possible
solutions and broaden areas of cooperation in the Asia Pacific;

WHEREAS, the APPF provides the parliamentary dimension to the Asia Pacific
Economic Cooperation and a dialogue mechanism to strengthen regional cooperation;

WHEREAS, the Philippines, through the Senate and the House of
Representatives, has accepted the hosting of the 31st Annual Meeting of the APPF
from 23 to 26 November 2023;

WHEREAS, the 31st APPF Annual Meeting will provide an excellent opportunity
to demonstrate our country’s readiness to engage with the global community, and _ will
provide an avenue to exhibit its support for multilateral efforts to achieve a peaceful,
stable and prosperous community of nations in the Asia Pacific;

WHEREAS, to ensure the successful preparations and hosting of the 31%
Annual Meeting of the APPF, unequivocal support and assistance from the relevant
national government agencies (NGAs) is warranted; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all the Executive departments, bureaus and offices;

NOW, THEREFORE, to ensure the successful preparations and hosting of the
315' Annual Meeting of the APPF, the following are hereby ordered:

Section 1. Directive to NGAs. The Secretaries or Heads of the following
NGAs are hereby directed to closely coordinate with, and assist the Congress in, the
preparation for the hosting of the 31st Annual Meeting of the APPF:

Department of Foreign Affairs;
Department of Justice;
Department of Transportation;
Department of Tourism;
Department of Finance;

Section 2. Support from Other Agencies. As may be necessary, Congress
may enlist the support and assistance of other relevant departments, bureaus, offices,
agencies or instrumentalities of the government, consistent with their respective
mandates.

Section 3. Funding. The funds necessary for the implementation of this
Circular may be sourced from available appropriations of the concerned agencies,
subject to applicable budgeting, accounting and auditing laws, rules and regulations.

Section 4. Venues. The Philippine International Convention Center (PICC)
and the Cultural Center of the Philippines (CCP), as conference venues for the
meetings and sessions of the 31%t Annual Meeting of the APPF, shall ensure
availability of their grounds and facilities and, shall undertake the necessary
improvements thereof as may be suitable for the event.

Section 5. Effectivity. This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 1ith day of October , in the
