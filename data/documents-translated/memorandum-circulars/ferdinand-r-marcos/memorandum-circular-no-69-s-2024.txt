MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 69

In view of the continuous rainfall brought about by Tropical Storm “Kristine,” and
to ensure the safety of the general public, work in government offices and classes at
all levels in Region V (Bicol Region) are hereby suspended on 24 October 2024.

However, those agencies whose functions involve the delivery of basic and
health services, preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or the
performance of other vital services shall continue with their operations and render the
necessary services.

The suspension of work for private companies and offices is left to the discretion
of their respective heads.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

 

Manila, 24 October 2024
