MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 85

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES TO SUPPORT THE PHILIPPINES’
HOSTING OF THE 25° INTERNATIONAL COMPETITION
NETWORK ANNUAL CONFERENCE

WHEREAS, Section 12 of Republic Act No. 10667 or the “Philippine Competition
Act,” mandates the Philippine Competition Commission (PCC) to, among others, act as
the official representative of the Philippine government in international competition
matters, promote capacity building and the sharing of best practices with other
competition-related bodies, and advocate pro-competitive policies of the government;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Development Plan 2023-2028 emphasizes the
importance of sustaining research and development efforts to identify competition-related
issues in priority sectors;

WHEREAS, the International Competition Network (ICN) Annual Conference is a
global conference platform that brings together competition experts from over 140
countries to engage in high-level plenary and breakout sessions, share the latest
developments in competition enforcement and policy, and collaborate on the creation of
innovative tools and strategies to strengthen global competition frameworks;

WHEREAS, the Philippines, through the PCC, has been selected by ICN to host
the 25" ICN Annual Conference in May 2026;

WHEREAS, the hosting of the said conference aims to reaffirm the Philippines’
commitment to competitive business environment, promote inclusive and sustainable
economic growth, and set the country at the forefront of global policy dialogue in
competition enforcement;

WHEREAS, the success of the hosting of the 25t ICN Annual Conference requires
the active participation of all concerned government agencies and instrumentalities, as
well as the support of local government units (LGUs) and the private sector; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the

power of control over all the executive departments, bureaus and offices, and the
mandate to ensure the faithful execution of laws;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

The PCC shall lead the hosting of the 25t ICN Annual Conference. Further,
consistent with their respective mandates, the following agencies shall coordinate and

Philippines’ hosting of the 25' ICN Annual Conference:

Department of Foreign Affairs;

Department of Tourism;

National Commission for Culture and the Arts;
Bureau of Immigration;

Bureau of Customs;

Philippine National Police; and

Metro Manila Development Authority.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.
