MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 55

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, AND ENCOURAGING LOCAL GOVERNMENT
UNITS, TO SUPPORT THE PHILIPPINES’ HOSTING OF THE 22"4 ASIAN
KARATE YOUTH CHAMPIONSHIPS

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article II of the Constitution provides that the State shall
give priority to education, science and technology, arts, culture, and sports to foster
patriotism and nationalism, accelerate social progress, and promote total human
liberation and development;

WHEREAS, Section 19, Article XIV of the Constitution provides that the State shall
promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and
amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline,
teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

WHEREAS, the Asian Karate Federation Championships is a highly-anticipated
tournament of Karate in Asia, which aims to bring together the best young Karate athletes
from across the region;

WHEREAS, the Philippines, through the Philippine Sports Commission, in
collaboration with the Karate Pilipinas Sports Federation, Inc., will host the 22"¢ Asian
Karate Federation Cadets, Juniors, and U21 Championships, which will be participated
in by 38 countries and about 900 athletes;

WHEREAS, the hosting of said event in the Philippines will not only promote the
sport of Karate in the country, but will also serve as an opportunity for training of Filipino
athletes;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

All government agencies and instrumentalities, including government-owned
or -controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and all local government units are
encouraged, to extend full support and cooperation to the Philippines’ hosting of the 22"
Asian Karate Youth Championships which will be held on 22 to 25 August 2024 at the
Philsports Multi-purpose Arena, Pasig, City.

The Presidential Communications Office is hereby directed to implement effective
measures to inform and encourage the public to support the said event.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 464, day of July , in the year of
our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.
