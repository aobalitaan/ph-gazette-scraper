MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 95

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS,
AND THE PRIVATE SECTOR, TO SUPPORT THE 2025
PHILIPPINE CREATIVE INDUSTRIES MONTH

WHEREAS, Section 2 of Republic Act (RA) No. 11904 or the “Philippine
Creative Industries Development Act” declares it a policy of the State to promote
and support the development of Philippine creative industries by protecting and
strengthening the rights and capacities of creative firms, artists, artisans, creators,
workers, indigenous cultural communities, content providers, and stakeholders in
the creative industries;

WHEREAS, Section 4 of RA No. 11904 created the Philippine Creative
Industries Development Council (PCIDC) to spearhead the development and
promotion of the creative industries of the Philippines;

WHEREAS, Section 24 of RA No. 11904 declared the month of September
of every year as the “Philippine Creative Industries Month” to promote the goods,
products and services of the Philippine creative industries, and to raise awareness
of their role in nation building and socioeconomic advancement;

WHEREAS, Section 24 of RA No. 11904 also mandates the Department of
Trade and Industry (DTI), as Secretariat of the PCIDC, in partnership with the
private sector and the academe, to ensure the successful celebration of the
Philippine Creative Industries Month, and to determine the mechanisms, programs,
and activities for the celebration thereof;

WHEREAS, Section 24 of RA No. 11904 directs all government agencies
and instrumentalities of the National Government, including government-owned or
-controlled corporations (GOCCs), local government units (LGUs), and the private
sector, to extend full support to and actively participate in the programs and
activities of the Philippine Creative Industries Month; and

WHEREAS, according to DTI, the 2025 Philippine Creative Industries Month,
bearing the theme, “We are a Creative Nation,” shall feature a variety of programs
and activities intended to showcase the Filipino people’s rich cultural heritage and
diverse artistic expression, with the end in view of propelling the Philippines towards
becoming Asia’s Premier Creative Hub by 2030;

BAGONG PILIPINAS

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

Pursuant to Section 24 of RA No. 11904, the PCIDC, through the DTI, shall
lead the observance of the 2025 Philippine Creative Industries Month.

All departments, agencies, and _ instrumentalities of the National
Government, including GOCCs, LGUs, and the private sector, are hereby directed
to extend full support to and actively participate in the programs and activities for
the celebration of the 2025 Philippine Creative Industries Month.

The funding requirements for the implementation of this Circular shall be
charged against the available appropriations of the concerned agencies, subject to
pertinent budgeting, accounting, and auditing laws, rules, and regulations.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 2nd dayof September , inthe
year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.
