MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 39

DIRECTING ALL NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED
OR -CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, GOVERNMENT FINANCIAL
INSTITUTIONS, AND STATE UNIVERSITIES AND COLLEGES, AND
ENCOURAGING ALL LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, TO ATTEND,
PARTICIPATE AND PROVIDE FULL SUPPORT TO THE BAGONG
PILIPINAS OFFICIAL CAMPAIGN KICK-OFF RALLY

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 14 (s. 2023) approved and adopted the
Philippine Development Plan 2023-2028, which seeks to steer the economy back
to a high-growth path, towards building an environment that provides equal
opportunities to all Filipinos, and equipping them with skills to participate fully in an
innovative and globally competitive economy;

WHEREAS, the Administration, through Memorandum Circular (MC) No. 24
(s. 2023) launched the Bagong Pilipinas campaign as its brand of governance and
leadership towards attaining a promising future, focused on implementing an all-
inclusive plan for deep and fundamental social and economic transformations in all
sectors of society and government, and to foster the State’s commitment towards
the attainment of comprehensive policy reforms and full economic recovery;

WHEREAS, MC No. 24 (s. 2023) directed all national government agencies
and instrumentalities, including government-owned or -controlled corporations
(GOCCs), and state universities and colleges (SUCs), to be guided by the
principles, strategies, and objectives of the Bagong Pilipinas brand of governance
and leadership in planning their programs, activities and projects, as well as in their
branding and communications strategy; and

WHEREAS, the success of the Bagong Pilipinas brand of leadership,
characterized by a principled, accountable and dependable government and
reinforced by unified institutions of society, would require the dynamic participation
of all national government agencies and Local Government Units (LGUs);

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

The Presidential Communications Office (PCO) is hereby directed to lead
and organize the conduct of the Bagong Pilipinas Official Campaign Kick-Off Rally.

All national government agencies and instrumentalities, including GOCCs,
government financial institutions, and SUCs are hereby directed, and all LGUs are

encouraged, to extend full support for and cooperation with the PCO in the conduct
of the Bagong Pilipinas Official Campaign Kick-Off Rally, and to provide the PCO
with the necessary assistance in spearheading the call for deep and fundamental
transformations in all sectors of society and government towards the attainment of
comprehensive policy reforms and full economic recovery.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 9th dayof November _, in the year
of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.
