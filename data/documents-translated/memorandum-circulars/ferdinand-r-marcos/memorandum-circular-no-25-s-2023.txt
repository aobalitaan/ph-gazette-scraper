MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 25

In view of the forecasted inclement weather brought about by Typhoon “Egay” and
the scheduled seventy-two (72)-hour transport strike in Metro Manila, work in government
offices and classes in public schools at all levels in the National Capital Region are
hereby suspended on 24 July 2023.

However, those agencies whose functions involve the delivery of basic and health
services, preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or the performance of

other vital services shall continue with their operations and render the necessary
services.

The suspension of work for private companies and classes in private schools is
left to the discretion of their respective heads.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

Manila, 21 July 2023
