MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 112

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES TO SUPPORT THE CALL FOR
NOMINATIONS FOR THE 2026 PRESIDENTIAL AWARDS
FOR FILIPINO INDIVIDUALS AND ORGANIZATIONS
OVERSEAS (PAFIOO)

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 498 (s. 1991) institutionalized the PAFIOO as
a biennial award conferred by the President of the Republic of the Philippines to
outstanding overseas Filipinos, as well as foreign individuals and organizations, in
recognition of their exceptional achievements, humanitarian and _ philanthropic
initiatives, and significant contributions to Philippine development and the
advancement of the interests of the Filipino diaspora;

WHEREAS, since its establishment in 1991, the PAFIOO has been conferred
by seven (7) Presidents to 555 individuals and organizations from 55 countries and
territories, emphasizing the enduring excellence, selfless service, and deep
commitment of overseas Filipinos and international partners to nation-building;

WHEREAS, Section 3(d) of Batas Pambansa Blg. 72 (s. 1980) or “An Act
Creating the Commission on Filipinos Overseas (CFO) and for Other Purposes,”
mandates the CFO to initiate and directly undertake the implementation of special
projects and programs, whenever necessary, to promote the welfare of Filipinos
overseas with respect to their interests and activities in the Philippines;

WHEREAS, the CFO has opened the call for nominations for the 2026 PAFIOO
from 11 December 2025 to 29 May 2026, covering the following award categories:
Lingkod sa Kapwa Pilipino Award, Banaag Award, Pamana ng Pilipino Award, and
Kaanib ng Bayan Award, after which the nominations shall undergo a multi-stage
evaluation process leading to the conferment of the awards in December 2026; and

WHEREAS, the successful conduct of the 2026 PAFIOO requires the
dissemination of information and the active participation of government agencies and
instrumentalities, as well as the general public in identifying and endorsing qualified
nominees;

BAGONG PILIPINAS

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

All government agencies and instrumentalities, including government-owned
or -controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and all local government units,
non-government organizations, and the private sector, are hereby encouraged, to
extend full support to the CFO in the conduct of the 2026 PAFIOO.

Further, the Presidential Communications Office is also directed to implement
effective measures to inform and encourage the public to nominate qualified
individuals in the 2026 PAFIOO.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 15th dayof January , in the
year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Six.
