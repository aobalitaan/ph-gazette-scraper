MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 86

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE 2025 NATIONAL
INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY (ICT)
SUMMIT

The Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT), in
partnership with the Chief Information Officers Forum, Inc. (CIOF) and the CIOF
Foundation, Inc., (CIOFF), will hold the 2025 National ICT Summit from 19 to 20 June
2025 at the Manila Hotel, Ermita, Manila, with the theme, “Transforming Together: Building
an Inclusive Digital Nation.”

In this regard, all departments, agencies and instrumentalities of the National
Government, including government-owned or -controlled corporations, government
financial institutions, state universities and colleges, are hereby directed, and all local
government units and the private sector are hereby encouraged, to support the DICT in the
conduct of the 2025 National ICT Summit.

Government officials and employees who are: (i) chief information officers (CIOs);
(ii) other executives designated as ClOs; (iii) members of CIOF and CIOFF; (iv) technical
staff; (v) academics; and (vi) key personnel from the government are also authorized to
attend the 2025 National ICT Summit, on official time. Their attendance shall be subject to
the issuance of pertinent clearances or authorities by their respective heads of offices, and
their participation in the said Summit must not adversely affect agency operations, as well
as the delivery of services to the publlc.

Expenses to be incurred by the attendees shall be allowed at prescribed rates in
accordance with the existing policies of the Department of Budget and Management,
Commission on Audit and the Civil Service Commission, chargeable against the funds of
their respective offices/agencies, and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules
and regulations.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 18th day of June , in the
year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.

By authority of the President:
