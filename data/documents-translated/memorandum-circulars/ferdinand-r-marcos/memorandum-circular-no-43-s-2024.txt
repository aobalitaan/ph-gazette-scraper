MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 43

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR
CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AND ENCOURAGING THE PRIVATE
SECTOR, TO SUPPORT THE 11™ ASIAN AGE GROUP
CHAMPIONSHIPS

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article II of the Constitution provides that the State shall
give priority to education, science and technology, arts, culture, and sports to foster
patriotism and nationalism, accelerate social progress, and promote total human
liberation and development;

WHEREAS, Section 19, Article XIV of the Constitution provides that the State shall
promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and
amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline,
teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

WHEREAS, the 11‘ Asian Age Group Championships is a highly anticipated and
prestigious swimming competition that brings together young swimmers from across Asia
and showcases the talent and potential of the region’s emerging swimmers;

WHEREAS, Philippine Aquatics, Inc., the national sports association for aquatic
sports in the Philippines, will host the 11° Asian Age Group Championships from 26
February to 9 March 2024 at the New Clark City Aquatics Center, Capas City, Tarlac; and

WHEREAS, the hosting of said championship event in the Philippines will bring
international prestige, publicity, goodwill, and economic benefits to the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, all government agencies and instrumentalities, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and the private
sector is encouraged, to extend full support and necessary assistance towards the
successful hosting of the 11" Asian Age Group Championships in the Philippines.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 17th dayof January ° , in the year of
our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.

By authority of the President:
