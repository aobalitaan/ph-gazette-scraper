MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 17

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR
-CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AND ENCOURAGING LOCAL
GOVERNMENT UNITS, TO SUPPORT THE 2023 FEDERATION
INTERNATIONALE DE VOLLEYBALL (FIVB) VOLLEYBALL NATIONS
LEAGUE AND THE 2023 VOLLEYBALL WORLD BEACH PRO TOUR -
CHALLENGE

WHEREAS, Section 19, Article XIV of the Constitution provides that the State shall
promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and
amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline,
teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

WHEREAS, the Philippine National Volleyball Federation, Inc. (PNVF) will host the
third week of the 2023 FIVB Volleyball Nations League for Men, which will be held on 04
to 09 July 2023 in Pasay City, and the 2023 Volleyball World Beach Pro Tour - Challenge,
to be held on 30 November to 03 December 2023 in Taguig City; and

WHEREAS, the hosting of said sporting events in the Philippines will bring
international prestige, publicity, goodwill, and economic benefits to the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, all government agencies and instrumentalities, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and all local
government units are encouraged, to extend full support to the 2023 FIVB Volleyball
Nations League and the 2023 Volleyball World Beach Pro Tour - Challenge.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 4th dayof April _, in the year of our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.

By authority of the President:

    

Executive Secretary
