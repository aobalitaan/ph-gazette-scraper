MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 107

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION OF CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE
(CES) OFFICERS, ELIGIBLES AND ON-STREAM CANDIDATES FOR
CES ELIGIBILITY IN THE 528° FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY AND
2025 LIFELONG LEARNING FOR LEADERSHIP CONGRESS
(CESCON)

The Career Executive Service Board (CESB) will celebrate its 52° Founding
Anniversary and hold the 2025 CESCON from 12 to 13 November 2025 at the Manila
Hotel, Ermita, Manila, with the theme “Leading for Results: Driving Execution with
Integrity in Service.”

In view thereof, and based on the representations of the CESB, all CES officers,
eligibles, and on-stream candidates for CES eligibility are hereby authorized to attend
the abovementioned events on official time, provided that this will not hinder the
operations of their respective offices as well as the delivery of essential services to the
public.

Expenses to be incurred by the participants shall be allowed at prescribed rates,
in accordance with the existing policies of the Department of Budget and Management
and the Commission on Audit.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 11thday of November __, in the
Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.
