MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 31

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 17 (S. 2023) BY
TRANSFERRING THE VENUE OF THE 2023 VOLLEYBALL WORLD

BEACH PRO TOUR - CHALLENGE FROM TAGUIG CITY TO SANTA
ROSA CITY, LAGUNA

WHEREAS, Memorandum Circular (MC) No. 17 (s. 2023) designated Taguig City
as the official venue for the 2023 Volleyball World Beach Pro Tour - Challenge, which will
be held from 30 November until 03 December 2023; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine National Volleyball Federation, Inc. recommended the

change of venue of the 2023 Volleyball World Beach Pro Tour - Challenge from Taguig
City to Santa Rosa City, Laguna;

NOW, THEREFORE, all national government agencies and instrumentalities,
including government-owned or -controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and all local
government units are hereby encouraged, to extend full support to the 2023 Volleyball
World Beach Pro Tour - Challenge which will be held from 30 November until 03
December 2023 in Santa Rosa City, Laguna.

The unaffected provisions of MC No. 17 shall remain in full force and effect.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 19thday of September, in the year of our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.

By authority of the President:
