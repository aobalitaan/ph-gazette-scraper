MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 49

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES TO SUPPORT THE PHILIPPINES’ HOSTING OF
FIVE (5) INTERNATIONAL VOLLEYBALL AND BEACH VOLLEYBALL
EVENTS IN 2024

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article Il of the Constitution provides that the State shall
give priority to education, science and technology, arts, culture, and sports to foster
patriotism and nationalism, accelerate social progress, and promote total human
liberation and development;

WHEREAS, Section 19, Article XIV of the Constitution provides that the State shall
promote physical education and encourage sports programs, league competitions, and
amateur sports, including training for international competitions, to foster self-discipline,
teamwork, and excellence for the development of a healthy and alert citizenry;

WHEREAS, the Philippines, through the Philippine National Volleyball Federation,
Inc., will host various volleyball and beach volleyball events in 2024; and

WHEREAS, the hosting of said sporting events in the Philippines aims to promote
volleyball and other sports within the Philippine landscape, encourage sports participation
among the youth, uplift the country’s prestige in the global sporting community, and boost
tourism and economy through sports;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

All government agencies and instrumentalities, including government-owned
or -controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and all local government units are
encouraged, to extend full support to the Philippines’ hosting of the following volleyball
and beach volleyball events in 2024:
