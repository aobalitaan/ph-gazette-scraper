MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 60

ADOPTING THE PHILIPPINE STATISTICAL DEVELOPMENT
PROGRAM 2023-2029, AND DIRECTING THE IMPLEMENTATION
THEREOF

WHEREAS, Section 5 of Republic Act (RA) No. 10625 or the “Philippine
Statistical Act of 2013” created the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) as the primary
government agency responsible for all national censuses and surveys, sectoral
statistics, consolidation of selected administrative recording systems, and compilation
of national accounts;

WHEREAS, Section 7 of RA No. 10625 created the PSA Board as the highest
policy-making body on statistical matters, and mandated the same to, among others:
(i) establish appropriate mechanisms for efficient and effective statistical systems in
the government; (ii) formulate policies on matters relating to government statistical
operations; and (iii) approve the Philippine Statistical Development Plan (PSDP),
among others;

WHEREAS, Section 6(|) of RA No. 10625 mandates the PSA, in consultation
with the PSA Board, to prepare a PSDP, which shall serve as the primary mechanism
for setting the direction, strategies, and priorities of the Philippine Statistical System
(PSS), the government-wide system responsible for providing official statistics in the
country; and provide the vital information requirements of the Philippine Development
Plan (PDP) 2023-2028;

WHEREAS, under the PDP 2023-2028, strengthening collection, analysis, and
use of data are among the key strategies to promoting good governance,
transparency, accountability, and evidence-based decision making;

WHEREAS, on 29 January 2024, the PSA Board, through Resolution No. 2
(s. 2024), approved the PSDP 2023-2029 and resolved to endorse the same to the
Office of the President;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

The PSDP 2023-2029 is hereby adopted. The PSDP shall serve as the
statistical support framework to the PDP 2023-2028, sectoral and local development
plans, Sustainable Development Goals, and other development plans and
international commitments.

 

BAGONG PILIPINAS

As the overall coordinator of the PSS, the PSA shall monitor and evaluate the
implementation of the programs and activities specified in the PSDP 2023-2029, and
apprise the PSDP Steering Committee on the outcomes thereof. The PSDP
2023-2029 Steering Committee shall provide recommendations to address issues on
the implementation of the PSDP. Results of the monitoring and evaluation, as well as
recommendations by the Steering Committee, shall be submitted to the PSA Board
every first quarter of the year.

In accordance with their respective mandates, all concerned agencies and
instrumentalities of the National Government, including GOCCs, are hereby directed,
and all LGUs and the private sector are hereby encouraged, to undertake efforts in
support of the implementation of the PSDP 2023-2029.

The PSA, in coordination with the Presidential Communications Office, is
hereby directed to disseminate an official copy of the PSDP 2023-2029 to all
concerned government agencies and offices.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 13th day of August , in the

year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.
