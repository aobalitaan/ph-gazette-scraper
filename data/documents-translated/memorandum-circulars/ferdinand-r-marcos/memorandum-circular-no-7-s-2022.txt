MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 07

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION IN THE 21ST ANNUAL NATIONAL
UNION OF CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE OFFICERS (NUCESO)
CONFERENCE

The National Union of Career Executive Service Officers, Inc. will hold the 21%
Annual NUCESO Conference from 19 to 21 October 2022 at the Masbate Coliseum,
Masbate City, with the theme, “Transformational Leadership: Redefining the Role of
Career Executive Service Officers as Agents of Change.”

In this regard, all Career Executive Service (CES) Officers, CES eligibles,
Career Service Executive eligibles, and other third level officials, are encouraged to
participate, and those interested may attend on official time, provided that they are
issued the pertinent clearances or authorities by their respective heads of office,
consistent with Civil Service Commission Memorandum Circular No. 43 (s. 1993) and
National Budget Circular No. 563 (s. 2016), and their participation in the said
Conference will not adversely affect the operations of their offices, as well as the
delivery of services to the public.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 6th dayof October in
the Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Two.
