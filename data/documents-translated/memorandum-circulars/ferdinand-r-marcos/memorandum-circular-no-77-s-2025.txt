MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 77

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, AND ENCOURAGING LOCAL
GOVERNMENT UNITS AND THE PRIVATE SECTOR, TO SUPPORT
THE CELEBRATION OF THE 127° ANNIVERSARY OF THE
PROCLAMATION OF PHILIPPINE INDEPENDENCE

WHEREAS, the country will commemorate the 127" Anniversary of the
Proclamation of Philippine Independence from 28 May 2025 to 12 June 2025 to
celebrate the freedom fought for by our forefathers, the future that they dreamt for the
nation, and the history we aim to remember and honor;

WHEREAS, Section 15, Article XIV of the Constitution provides that the State
shall conserve, promote and popularize the nation’s cultural and historical heritage
and resources;

WHEREAS, Section 2 of Republic Act No. 10086 or the “Strengthening
Peoples’ Nationalism Through Philippine History Act,” declares it a policy of the State
to adopt measures that will strengthen the people’s nationalism, love of country, and
respect for its heroes and pride for the people's accomplishments, by reinforcing the
importance of Philippine national and local history in daily life, with the end in view of
raising social consciousness;

WHEREAS, Administrative Order (AO) No. 23 (s. 2011) established a Steering
Committee on National Observances and designated the National Historical
Commission of the Philippines (NHCP) as the lead agency mandated to plan, organize
and undertake the commemoration of select historical events, including the Philippine
Independence Day; and

WHEREAS, AO No. 08 (s. 2023) constituted an inter-agency committee chaired
by the NHCP as the lead agency to facilitate the planning and execution of programs
and projects for the 125" Anniversary of the Proclamation Philippine Independence
from 2023 to 2026;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

All government agencies and instrumentalities, including government-owned or
-controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and all local government units and the
private sector are encouraged, to extend full support and cooperation to the conduct
of programs and activities in relation to the commemoration of the 127" Philippine
Independence which will be held from 28 May 2025 to 12 June 2025.

BAGONG PILIPINAS

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 13th day of February , in
the year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.
