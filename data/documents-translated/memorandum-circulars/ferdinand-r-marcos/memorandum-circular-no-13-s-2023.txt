MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 13

ENCOURAGING ALL GOVERNMENT LAWYERS IN THE SOUTHERN
LUZON REGION TO PARTICIPATE IN THE SOUTHERN LUZON
REGIONAL CONVENTION OF THE INTEGRATED BAR OF THE
PHILIPPINES

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines will hold the Southern Luzon Regional
Convention on 20 January 2023 at the Bellevue Hotel Manila in Alabang, Muntinlupa
City, with the theme, “Revolutionizing the Legal System in the Country: Digitization of
the Law and Legal Processes.”

In this regard, all lawyers presently employed in departments, agencies, and
offices of the government, including government-owned or -controlled corporations,
government financial institutions, as well as state universities and colleges, in the
Southern Luzon region, are encouraged to participate, and those interested may
attend, on official time, provided that they are issued the pertinent clearances or
authorities by their respective heads of office, consistent with Civil Service Commission
Memorandum Circular No. 43 (s. 1993) and National Budget Circular No. 563 (s. 2016),
and their participation in the said Convention will not adversely affect the operations of
their offices, as well as the delivery of services to the public.

This Circular shall take effect immediately,

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 18thday of January in
the Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.
