MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 42

DIRECTING ALL NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR -CONTROLLED
CORPORATIONS, AND STATE UNIVERSITIES AND COLLEGES, TO ATTEND,
PARTICIPATE AND PROVIDE FULL SUPPORT TO THE BAGONG PILIPINAS
OFFICIAL CAMPAIGN KICK-OFF RALLY

WHEREAS, the Administration, through Memorandum Circular (MC) No. 24 (s. 2023)
launched the Bagong Pilipinas campaign as its brand of governance and leadership towards
attaining a promising future, focused on implementing an all-inclusive plan for deep and
fundamental social and economic transformations in all sectors of society and government, and to
foster the State’s commitment towards the attainment of comprehensive policy reforms and full
economic recovery;

WHEREAS, MC No. 24 also directed all national government agencies (NGAs) and
instrumentalities, including government-owned or -controlled corporations (GOCCs), and state
universities and colleges (SUCs), to be guided by the principles, strategies, and objectives of the
Bagong Pilipinas brand of governance and leadership in planning their programs, activities and
projects, as well as in their branding and communications strategy;

WHEREAS, MC No. 39 (s. 2023) designated the Presidential Communications Office
(PCO), as the lead agency for the Bagong Pilipinas Kick Off Rally; and

WHEREAS, the success of this campaign and call to action, requires the dynamic support
of all government agencies and instrumentalities;

NOW, THEREFORE, all NGAs and instrumentalities, including GOCCs and SUCs, are
hereby directed to participate in and extend full support for the Bagong Pilipinas Kick Off Rally on
28 January 2024 at the Quirino Grandstand, Manila.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 16th day of January , in the year of our Lord,
Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.

By authority of the President:
