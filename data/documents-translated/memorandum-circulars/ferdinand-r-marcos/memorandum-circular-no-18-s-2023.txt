MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 18

IMPOSING A MORATORIUM ON THE TRANSFER OF NATIONAL
GOVERNMENT REGIONAL OFFICES FROM ZAMBOANGA CITY TO
PAGADIAN CITY PURSUANT TO MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR (MC)
NO. 78 (S. 2020)

WHEREAS, Executive Order No. 429 (s. 1990) reorganized the administrative
regions in Mindanao to guarantee the effective delivery of the field services of
government agencies, taking into consideration the formation of the Autonomous
Region in Muslim Mindanao;

WHEREAS, Section 1 of EO No. 429 reorganized Region IX and designated
Pagadian City as the regional center and Zamboanga City as the commercial and
industrial center of Western Mindanao;

WHEREAS, by virtue of MC No. 75 (s. 2004), all regional offices of departments
were directed to transfer from Zamboanga City to Pagadian City as soon as
practicable, except the Department of Trade and Industry, Department of Labor and
Employment, and Department of Tourism, which shall remain in Zamboanga City but
shall establish a presence in Pagadian City;

WHEREAS, MC No. 11 (s. 2010) directed a moratorium on the transfer of
regional offices of all departments and agencies from Zamboanga City to Pagadian
City;

WHEREAS, MC No. 78 (s. 2020) repealed MC No. 11, and lifted the moratorium
on the transfer of regional offices from Zamboanga City to Pagadian City; and

WHEREAS, relative to the ongoing devolution of national government services,
as well as the high economic and social costs brought about by the transfer, there is

a need to re-evaluate the foregoing policy of implementing the full transfer of regional
offices to Pagadian City;

NOW, THEREFORE, to provide an opportunity to study and re-assess the
justifications for, as well as the implications of the transfer of regional offices to
Pagadian City, the following are hereby ordered:

SECTION 1. All departments, bureaus and other government agencies are
enjoined to suspend the transfer of their regional offices in Region |X from Zamboanga
City to Pagadian City pending the study of the political, economic, and social
implications of said transfer, and consultations with relevant stakeholders to be jointly

conducted by the National Economic and Development Authority, Department of the
Interior and Local Government, and Department of Budget and Management.

The Regional Offices that are already in Pagadian City shall continue to operate
thereat.

SECTION 2. The abovementioned departments tasked to conduct the study on
the matter shall submit a comprehensive report on their findings together with their
recommendations to the President, through the Office of the Executive Secretary.

SECTION 3. This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 19th dayof April in the Year of
our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.
