MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 33

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION OF CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE
(CES) OFFICERS, ELIGIBLES, AND ON-STREAM CANDIDATES FOR
CES ELIGIBILITY IN THE 50™ FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY OF THE
CES BOARD (CESB) AND THE 2023 CES LIFELONG LEARNING
FOR LEADERSHIP CONGRESS

The CESB will hold its 50" Founding Anniversary and the 2023 CES Lifelong
Learning for Leadership Congress from 28 to 29 November 2023 at the Fiesta
Pavilion, Manila Hotel, One Rizal Park, Ermita, Manila, with the theme “Celebrating a
Stronger Career Executive Service — 50 Years and Beyond.”

In view thereof, all CES officers, eligibles, and on-stream candidates for CES
eligibility are authorized to attend the 50" Founding Anniversary and CES Congress
on official time, to the extent that will not impair the operations of their respective
offices as well as the delivery of essential services to the public.

Expenses to be incurred by the participants shall be allowed at prescribed rates,
in accordance with the existing policies of the Department of Budget and Management
and the Commission on Audit.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this26thday of September in
the Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Three.
