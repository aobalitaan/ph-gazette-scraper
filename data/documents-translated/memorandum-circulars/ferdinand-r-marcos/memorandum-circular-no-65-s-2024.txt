MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 65

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION OF CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE
(CES) OFFICERS, ELIGIBLES AND ON-STREAM CANDIDATES FOR
CES ELIGIBILITY IN THE 5157 FOUNDING ANNIVERSARY AND 2024
LIFELONG LEARNING FOR LEADERSHIP CONGRESS (CESCON)

The Career Executive Service Board will hold the 51t Founding Anniversary
and 2024 CESCON from 5 to 6 November 2024 at the Philippine International
Convention Center, with the theme “Pag-ibig sa Bayan, CESerbisyong may Dangal.”

In view thereof, all CES officers, eligibles, and on-stream candidates for CES
eligibility are authorized to attend the 51st Founding Anniversary and 2024 CESCON
on official time, provided that this will not hinder the operations of their respective
offices as well as the delivery of essential services to the public.

Expenses to be incurred by the participants shall be allowed at prescribed rates,
in accordance with the existing policies of the Department of Budget and Management
and the Commission on Audit.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 4th day of October in the
Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.

By authority of the President:
