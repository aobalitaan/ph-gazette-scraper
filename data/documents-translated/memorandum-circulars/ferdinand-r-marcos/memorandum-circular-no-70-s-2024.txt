MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO.70

Upon the recommendation of the National Disaster Risk Reduction and
Management Council relative to the forecasted continuous heavy rainfall due to
Severe Tropical Storm “Kristine,” and to aid in the rescue and relief operations of the
government, work in government offices and classes at all levels in Luzon are hereby
suspended on 24 October 2024.

Agencies involved in the delivery of basic and health services,
preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or the performance of other
vital services are hereby directed to continue their operations and render the
necessary services.

Further thereto, the localized cancellation or suspension of classes and/or work
in government offices in other areas may be implemented by their respective Local
Chief Executives, pursuant to relevant laws, rules and regulations.

The suspension of work for private companies and offices is left to the discretion
of their respective heads.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.
