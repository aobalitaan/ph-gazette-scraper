MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 110

DIRECTING ALL NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED
OR -CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS AND STATE UNIVERSITIES
AND COLLEGES, AND ENCOURAGING ALL LOCAL
GOVERNMENT UNITS, TO OBSERVE AUSTERITY IN THE
CELEBRATION OF THE CHRISTMAS SEASON

WHEREAS, Section 1, Article XI of the Constitution mandates public officers
and employees to serve the people with utmost responsibility, integrity, loyalty, and
efficiency;

WHEREAS, Section 1, Chapter 1, Subtitle B, Title 1, Book V of Executive Order
No. 292 or the “Administrative Code of 1987” provides that all government resources
shall be managed, expended, or utilized in accordance with relevant laws, rules, and
regulations, and safeguarded against loss or wastage through illegal or improper
disposition to ensure efficiency, economy, and effectiveness in the operations of
government;

WHEREAS, Administrative Order No. 6 (s. 2017) mandates all national
government agencies (NGAs) and instrumentalities to observe judicious and prudent
use of government funds, in accordance with relevant laws, rules, and regulations;

WHEREAS, the Administration is committed to promoting fiscal discipline and
the judicious use of government resources, consistent with the country’s
socio-economic priorities and ongoing expenditure management reforms; and

WHEREAS, in light of recent natural disasters and calamities in the country, it
is necessary to adopt austerity measures during the Christmas season to ensure
greater efficiency in public spending, prevent the misuse of government resources,
promote a culture of simplicity in the bureaucracy, and support the government's
efforts to prioritize essential programs and services;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

All NGAs and instrumentalities, including government-owned or -controlled
corporations and state universities and colleges, are hereby directed, and local
government units are hereby encouraged, to observe austerity in the celebration of
the Christmas season, and to keep Christmas and year-end celebrations simple and
meaningful, in solidarity will all the Filipinos still recovering from recent disasters.

BAGONG PILIPINAS

All heads of agencies are hereby directed to ensure the strict implementation
and observance of all relevant laws, rules, and regulations governing the judicious and
prudent use of government funds.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 15th day of

December , inthe
year of Our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.
