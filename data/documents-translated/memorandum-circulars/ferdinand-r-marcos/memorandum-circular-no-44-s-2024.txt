MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO, 44

CONDUCT OF PROGRAMS AND ACTIVITIES FOR | THE
CELEBRATION OF THE 2024 “NATIONAL INNOVATION DAY”

WHEREAS, Section 2 of Republic Act (RA) No. 11293 or the “Philippine
Innovation Act” declares it a policy of the State to foster innovation in the country as a
vital component of national development and sustainable economic growth;

WHEREAS, Section 27 of RA No. 11293 declares April 21 of every year as
“National Innovation Day” and mandates the National Innovation Council (NIC), in
coordination with concerned government agencies, to plan, design and implement
programs and activities in celebration thereof;

WHEREAS, according to the NIC, this year's celebration of “National
Innovation Day,” with the theme “Unboxing the Filipinnovation Mindset,” aims to
increase public awareness on innovation programs and activities of the public and
private sectors, cultivate among all Filipinos innovative culture and mindset, and
promote Filipinnovation, a whole-of-nation call and commitment to accelerate the
country’s progress through innovation;

WHEREAS, the celebration of the “National Innovation Day” will highlight
innovation in all areas, consistent with the transformation agenda outlined in the
Philippine Development Plan 2023-2028 and National Innovation Agenda and
Strategy Document 2023-2032, particularly on establishing a dynamic innovation
ecosystem; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all the executive departments, bureaus, and offices and the
mandate to ensure faithful execution of laws;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered.

The NIC, in close coordination with the Department of Science and Technology,
Department of Trade and Industry, Department of Education, Commission on Higher
Education, the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority and such other
concerned agencies, shall spearhead the conduct of activities and programs during
the annual celebration of the “National Innovation Day.”

All national government agencies and_ instrumentalities, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and local
government units and the private sector are hereby encouraged, to extend full support

and cooperation in the conduct of programs and _ activities during the “National
Innovation Day.”

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 12th dayof March
the year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.
