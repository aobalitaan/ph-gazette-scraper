MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 92

DIRECTING STRONGER PARTICIPATION AND
INVOLVEMENT OF ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES IN THE OBSERVANCE OF THE
ASSOCIATION OF SOUTHEAST ASIAN NATIONS (ASEAN)
MONTH

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 282 (s. 2017) declared the month of August of
every year as the “ASEAN Month” and enjoined all government agencies, especially
the Department of Foreign Affairs, Department of Education, Department of the Interior
and Local Government, Department of Tourism, Department of Trade and Industry,
and the Commission on Higher Education, to initiate and implement programs and
activities to help generate consciousness and public support in promoting awareness
of the ASEAN;

WHEREAS, during the 43'°* ASEAN Summit in September 2023, the ASEAN
leaders agreed that the Philippines will assume the Chairpersonship of ASEAN and
host the ASEAN Summit in 2026 (ASEAN 2026);

WHEREAS, Administrative Order No. 17 (s. 2024) constituted the ASEAN
National Organizing Council (NOC) to facilitate the planning, implementation, and
monitoring of programs, activities, and projects for the Philippines’ hosting of ASEAN

WHEREAS, there is a need to further promote and heighten awareness of the
ASEAN, provide preparatory activities for the public in anticipation of the Philippines’
hosting of ASEAN in 2026, and highlight the importance of ASEAN and regional
partnerships; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all the executive departments, bureaus and offices, and the
mandate to ensure the faithful execution of laws;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

The ASEAN NOC member-agencies are hereby directed to conduct enhanced
programs and activities for the commemoration of the ASEAN Month in August 2025.
The programs and activities shall be aligned with the theme “Inclusivity and
Sustainability,” to reflect Malaysia’s ASEAN Chairpersonship theme for 2025 and
ASEAN’s shared commitment to regional peace, security, stability, and prosperity
within an inclusive and sustainable framework.

 

BAGONG PILIPINAS

In this regard, the ASEAN NOC member-agencies, through the Office of the
Director General for Operations, are hereby directed to submit a report on the
programs and activities to be conducted during the 2025 ASEAN Month, within fifteen
(15) days from the issuance of this Circular. Further, the Presidential Communications
Office is also directed to implement effective measures to inform and encourage public
support, and to ensure that both the public and the private sectors are made aware of
said programs and activities.

All national government agencies and_ instrumentalities, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations, government financial institutions, and
state universities and colleges, are hereby directed to render full support and
participation in the programs and activities during the 2025 ASEAN Month, including
the recital of the ASEAN Anthem during flag-raising ceremonies for the entire month
of August 2025. For this purpose, the heads of all national government agencies and
instrumentalities shall ensure that the ASEAN Anthem, which is annexed to this
Circular, is properly disseminated within their respective offices.

Local government units, non-government organizations, and the private sector
are hereby encouraged to engage in appropriate initiatives in support of the programs
and activities for the 2025 ASEAN Month.

The funding requirements for the implementation of this Circular shall be
charged against the available appropriations of the concerned agencies, subject to
pertinent budgeting, accounting, and auditing laws, rules, and regulations.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 24th day of July , in the
year of our Lord, Two Thousand and Twenty-Five.

By authority of the President:

Office of the President
