MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 41

DIRECTING ALL NATIONAL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, AND ENCOURAGING ALL LOCAL
GOVERNMENT UNITS, TO SUPPORT THE CONDUCT OF
PROGRAMS AND ACTIVITIES IN CELEBRATION OF THE
COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT WEEK AND COMMUNITY
DEVELOPMENT DAY

WHEREAS, Section 23 Article II of the Constitution declares it a policy of the
State to encourage non-governmental, community-based, or sectoral organizations
that promote the welfare of the nation;

WHEREAS, Proclamation No. 316 (s. 1994) declared the first week of January
and January 6" of every year as Community Development Week and Community
Development Day, respectively, in order to uphold and instill in the consciousness of
Filipinos the Bayanihan Spirit and the value of community cooperation, as well as to
recognize the invaluable contributions and concerted efforts of both government and
non-government organizations in the development of local communities, people
empowerment and community mobilization;

WHEREAS, under Proclamation No. 316, the Department of the Interior and
Local Government is designated as the lead agency in the annual celebration of the
Community Development Week and Community Development Day, in coordination
with non-government and people’s organizations; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all executive departments, bureaus, and offices;

NOW, THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

The Department of the Interior and Local Government, in partnership with
non-government and people's organizations, shall spearhead the conduct of activities
and programs during the annual celebration of the Community Development Week
and Community Development Day.

All national government agencies and instrumentalities, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations, government financial institutions, and
state universities and colleges, are hereby directed, and all local government units are
encouraged, to extend full support for, and cooperation in, the conduct of relevant

activities and programs during the annual celebration of the Community Development
Week and Community Development Day.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 3rd day of January in the year
of our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-Four.

By authority of the President:
