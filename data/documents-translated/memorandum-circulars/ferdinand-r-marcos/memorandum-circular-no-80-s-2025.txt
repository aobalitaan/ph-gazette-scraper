MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 80

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, AND ENCOURAGING LOCAL
GOVERNMENT UNITS AND THE PRIVATE SECTOR, TO SUPPORT
THE CELEBRATION OF THE 2025 NATIONAL INNOVATION DAY

WHEREAS, Section 2 of Republic Act (RA) No. 11293 or the “Philippine
Innovation Act” declares it a policy of the State to foster innovation in the country as a
vital component of national development and sustainable economic growth;

WHEREAS, Section 27 of RA No. 11293 declares April 21 of every year as
“National Innovation Day,” and mandates the National Innovation Council (NIC), in
coordination with concerned government agencies, to plan, design, and implement
programs and activities in celebration thereof;

WHEREAS, the celebration of the 2025 National Innovation Day will be held on
28 to 30 April 2025 with the theme “FLIP: Forging Leaders of Innovation in the
Philippines;”

WHEREAS, the 2025 National Innovation Day aims to: (i) inspire the youth by
engaging them in an intensified “Huddle, Analyze, Build and Innovation” thinking
workshop; (ii) elevate public awareness on Filipino ingenuity and creativity through the
holding of the National and Regional Innovation Awards or the “Filipinnovation
Awards;” and (iii) honor Filipino innovators or “Filipinnovators” who have made
significant contributions in promoting and advancing innovative solutions in their
respective fields;

WHEREAS, the celebration of the 2025 National Innovation Day is aligned with
one of the key strategies identified in the Philippine Development Plan 2023-2028,
particularly the whole-of-government approach to establishing and strengthening a
dynamic innovation system in the development of technology and innovation-based
strategies for the agriculture, industry, and service sectors;

WHEREAS, the celebration of the 2025 National Innovation Day aims to
highlight five (5) priority areas identified in the National Innovation Agenda and
Strategy Document 2023-2032, namely: (i) health and well-being; (ii) food and
agribusiness; (iii) transportation and logistics; (iv) public administration; and
(v) learning and education;

BAGONG PILIPINAS

WHEREAS, the success of the celebration of the 2025 National Innovation Day
requires the active participation of all concerned government agencies and
instrumentalities, as well as the support of local government units (LGUs) and the
private sector; and

WHEREAS, Section 17, Article VII of the Constitution vests in the President the
power of control over all executive departments, bureaus, and offices;

NOW THEREFORE, the following are hereby ordered:

Consistent with Section 27 of RA No. 11293, the NIC, in close coordination with
the Department of Science and Technology, Department of Trade and Industry,
Department of Education, Commission on Higher Education, and Technical Education
and Skills Development Authority, shall spearhead the conduct of programs and
activities for this year’s celebration of the National Innovation Day, which is scheduled
on 28 to 30 April 2025.

All government agencies and instrumentalities, including government-owned or
-controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and all LGUs and the private sector are
hereby encouraged, to extend full support and cooperation in the conduct of programs
and activities in the celebration of the 2025 National Innovation Day.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.
