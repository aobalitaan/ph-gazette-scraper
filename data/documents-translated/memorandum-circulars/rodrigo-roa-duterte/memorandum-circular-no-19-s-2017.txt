MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 19

ENCOURAGING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR
-CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS,
TO EXTEND SUPPORT AND PARTICIPATION TO THE KABISIG
PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT EXPO AND TRADE FAIR 2017

WHEREAS, Presidential Proclamation’No. 650 (s. 1990) created and institutionalized
the Kabisig People’s Movement (KABISIG) with the mandate of facilitating, coordinating and
monitoring the implementation of projects and programs of the government, as well as
providing linkages between the private and government sectors;

WHEREAS, in the pursuit of such mandate, KABISIG is conducting the KABISIG
Philippine Government Expo and Trade Fair 2017, which showcases the profiles, service
facilities, programs, projects and accomplishments of government agencies and
instrumentalities, including government-owned or -controlled corporations (GOCCs), thereby
providing the government an opportunity to promote public awareness of its accomplishments
and activities, as well as allowing the general public to participate in the development of the
country;

NOW, THEREFORE, all heads of departments, agencies and instrumentalities of the
National Government, including GOCCs and the local government units, are hereby
encouraged to extend support and participate in the above-stated Expo and Trade Fair,
subject to the availability of funds and applicable budgetary, accounting and auditing laws,
tules, regulations and procedures.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, thi? 7t ay of April , in the Year of our Lord
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
