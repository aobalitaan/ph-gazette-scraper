MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 09

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNIMENT-OWNED OR —
CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AND ENCOURAGING LOCAL
GOVERNMENT UNITS, TO EXTEND SUPPORT TO THE 2016
FEDERATION INTERNATIONALE DE VOLLEYBALL WOMEN’S
CLUB WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP

WHEREAS, the Philippine Super Liga (PSL) will host the 2016 Federation
Infernationale de Volleyball (FIVB) Women’s Club World Championship (VVCWC) to
be held on 718 to 23 October 2016 in Metro Manila; and

WHEREAS, ihis will be an. opportunity for the further recognition of ihe
Philippines as a world-class venue for international sporting evenis.

NOW, THEREFORE, all national government agencies and instrumenialities,
including government-owned or —controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and
local government units are encouraged, to extend full support to the 2016 FIVB
WCWC, subject to applicable laws, rules and regulations.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 4th day of October, in the year of our Lord
Two Thousand and Sixteen.
