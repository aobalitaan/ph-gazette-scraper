MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 14

DIRECTING GOVERNMENT DEPARTMENTS, BUREAUS,
OFFICES, AGENCIES, AND INSTRUMENTALITIES TO GIVE
PREFERENCE TO THE PHILIPPINE INTERNATIONAL
CONVENTION CENTER AS VENUE FOR THEIR OFFICIAL
EVENTS

WHEREAS, the Philippine International Convention Center (PICC) is a
government-owned institution, constructed pursuant to Presidential Decree No.
520 (s. 1974), as amended, as an international conference center suitable for
the holding of international conferences, meetings, and the like;

WHEREAS, the PICC possesses world-class facilities and equipment
which meet international standards, and is operated by an experienced and
professional staff who can meet the requirements of sophisticated domestic and
foreign clienteles;

WHEREAS, while Administrative Order No. 136 (s. 2005) encourages all
government departments, bureaus, offices, agencies, and instrumentalities to
hold their meetings, workshops, seminars, conferences and other special
events at the PICC, it has been noted that the number of government-organized
and -sponsored events held at the PICC has continued to decline through the
years vis-a-vis the number of such events held at privately-owned
establishments;

WHEREAS, pursuant to the 2016 Revised Implementing Rules and
Regulations (IRR) of Republic Act 9184 or the Government Procurement
Reform Act, it is preferred that government agencies lease publicly-owned real
property or conference venues from other government agencies;

WHEREAS, the same IRR provides that in the event the procuring
authority would resort to privately-owned real property or conference venue, the
end-user shall justify that the same is more efficient and economical to the
government;

NOW, THEREFORE, it is hereby directed that:
SECTION 1. All departments, bureaus, offices, agencies and

instrumentalities of the government, including government-owned or -controlled
corporations, government financial institutions and other government corporate

entities, and state universities and colleges, shall give preference to the
facilities of the PICC in holding their local, regional, national and international
conventions, conferences, meetings, seminars, workshops, symposiums,
exhibitions, commencement exercises and other similar official activities and
events.

SECTION 2. The PICC shall develop and offer special packages,
discounts, and other incentives for such official and/or governmental activities
and events, which should be competitive but not detrimental to the financial
viability of the PICC.

SECTION 3. This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 14thday of Februaryin the year of our
Lord Two Thousand and Seventeen.
