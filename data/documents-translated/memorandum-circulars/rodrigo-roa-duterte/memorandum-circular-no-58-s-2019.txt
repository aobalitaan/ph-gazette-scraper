MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 58

ENCOURAGING THE PARTICIPATION OF ALL LAWYERS IN THE
OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT AND ITS ATTACHED AGENCIES,
INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR -CONTROLLED
CORPORATIONS, IN THE 177 INTEGRATED BAR OF THE
PHILIPPINES NATIONAL CONVENTION OF LAWYERS

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines (IBP) will hold its 17th National Convention
of Lawyers on 27-31 March 2019 at the lloilo Convention Center, Iloilo City, with the
theme “Upholding the Rule of Law in Periods of Great Change.”

The IBP aims to gather a maximum of 2,500 lawyers, including those in the
government service, to discuss important issues and concerns affecting the legal
profession.

The Civil Service Commission has issued Announcement No. 54 (s. 2018),
enjoining all concerned government employees to attend the event.

In this regard, all lawyers in the Office of the President and its attached
agencies, including government-owned or -controlled corporations, are encouraged to
participate in the 47" IBP National Convention of Lawyers, and those interested may
attend on official time, provided that they are issued the necessary
clearances/authorities by their respective heads of office, and their participation in the
said Convention will not adversely affect the operations of their offices, as well as the
delivery of services to the public.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.
DONE, in the City of Manila, this15th day of February, in the year of Our

Lord, Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By authority of the President:
