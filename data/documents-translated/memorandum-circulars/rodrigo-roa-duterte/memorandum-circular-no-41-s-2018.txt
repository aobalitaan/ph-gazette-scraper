MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 41

In view of the organized transport strike, and in order to minimize the
inconvenience to the riding public, classes in public and private schools at all levels in
Metro Manila, are hereby suspended effective 2:00 PM today, 19 March 2018.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.
