MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 40

ENJOINING PUBLIC INFORMATION OFFICERS OF GOVERNMENT AGENCIES
AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS TO ATTEND AND PARTICIPATE IN THE
NATIONAL INFORMATION CONVENTION TO BE CONDUCTED ON 19-21

FEBRUARY 2018

WHEREAS. the Presidential Communications Operations Office, through the
Philippine Information Agency, will hold the National Information Convention, a three-
day event to be held at the SMX Convention Center, SM Lanang, Davao on 19-21
February 2018 that will gather government public information officers, members of
the national and regional media, communications and technology industry leaders

and members of the academe;

WHEREAS, the Administration aims to invest in the development of human
capital, particularly among its own personnel in the public sector, to promote public
awareness, ensure public access to information, and institutionalize response and
feedback mechanisms to fully engage and empower the citizenry;

NOW, THEREFORE, all public information officers or those performing similar
functions in national government agencies and local government units are enjoined
to attend and participate in the National Information Convention, on official time with
expenses such as transportation, per diems, registration fee, and other necessary
fees/expenses at rates prescribed in accordance with existing policies of the
Department of Budget and Management, the Commission on Audit and the Civil
Service Commission, chargeable against the funds of their respective
agencies/offices, and subject to the usual accounting and auditing rules and

regulations.
This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.
