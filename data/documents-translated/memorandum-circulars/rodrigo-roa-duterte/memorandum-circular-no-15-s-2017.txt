MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 15

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION IN THE 67" PHILIPPINE SOCIETY OF
AGRICULTURAL ENGINEERS (PSAE) ANNUAL NATIONAL CONVENTION AND
aan INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE

In celebration of the 28" Philippine Agricultural Engineering Week, the PSAE,
inc. will hold the 67" PSAE Annual National Convention and 14" International
Conference and Exhibition on 23 to 29 April 2017 in Legazpi City, Albay.

This year’s convention theme is “Agricultural and Biosystems Engineers:
Prime Movers for the Development and Management of Appropriate
Technologies for Food Security.”

In view thereof, the participation of government-employed agricultural
engineers and agricultural and biosystems engineers, on official time, is hereby
authorized, subject to applicable laws, rules and regulations.

The head of agencies or offices concerned shall determine the number of
participants to the convention, provided that there will be no interruption of normal
office services or activities due to their attendance to the conference.

Manila, Philippines, 30 March 2017
