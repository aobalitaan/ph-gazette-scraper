MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 23

AUTHORIZING THE PARTICIPATION OF GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED
CORPORATIONS, AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS IN THE KALIKASAN GREEN
PRODUCTIVITY, GREEN PURCHASING TOWARDS GREEN PHILIPPINES EXPO
CONFERENCE 2017

WHEREAS, it is a policy of the State to protect and advance the right of the
people to a balanced and healthful ecology in accord with the rhythm and harmony of
nature;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Development Plan 2017-2022 seeks to advance the
development and implementation of sustainable consumption and production policies
and initiatives, particularly practices and teachings that will facilitate the attainment of
both economic goals and environmental standards;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Center for Environmental Protection and Sustainable
Development, Inc. (PCEPSDI), the Department of Trade and Industry, the Department of
Environment and Natural Resources and the Department of Tourism jointly organized
the Kalikasan Green Productivity, Green Purchasing Towards Green Philippines (KGP3)
Expo Conference 2017 to be held on 19-23 September 2017 at the SMX Convention
Center, SM Mall of Asia, with the purpose of providing an essential platform for
information dissemination, identification and presentation of best practices, and sharing
of knowledge and experiences on approaches and strategies for a green economy;

NOW, THEREFORE, ail heads of departments, agencies and instrumentalities of
the National Government, including government-owned and -controlled corporations,
and local government units, are authorized to participate in the KGP3.

The attendance of at least one (1) key official or employee per agency in charge
of the formulation or implementation of development plans, programs and projects on
environment and natural resources, tourism, engineering and supply and procurement
services is hereby allowed, on official time with entitlement to registration fee of not more
than Php 2,000, chargeable against the concerned agency's budget, subject to the
availability of funds and applicable budgetary, accounting and auditing laws, rules and
regulations.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, thi®th day of September , in the Year
of our Lord Two Thousand and Seventeen.
