MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 68

AUTHORIZING THE PARTICIPATION OF GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR
-CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, IN
THE KALIKASAN GREEN PRODUCTIVITY, GREEN PURCHASING
TOWARDS GREEN PHILIPPINES CONFERENCE 2019

WHEREAS, Article Il, Section 16 of the Constitution declares it a policy of the State to
protect and advance the right of the people to a balanced and healthful ecology in accord with
the rhythm and harmony of nature;

WHEREAS, the Philippine Development Plan 2017-2022 seeks to advance the
development and implementation of sustainable consumption and production policies and
initiatives, particularly, practices and teachings that will facilitate the attainment of both
economic goals and environmental standards; and

WHEREAS, the Philippine Center for Environmental Protection and Sustainable
Development, Inc. (PCEPSDI), the Department of Trade and Industry, the Department of
Environment and Natural Resources and the Climate Change Commission jointly organized the
Kalikasan Green Productivity, Green Purchasing towards Green Philippines (KGP3)
Conference 2019 to be held on 5-6 November 2019 at Novotel Manila, Araneta Center, Cubao,
Quezon City, with the purpose of providing essential platform for information dissemination,
identification and presentation of best practices, and sharing of knowledge and experience on
strategies towards achieving a green economy;

NOW, THEREFORE, all heads of departments, agencies and instrumentalities of the
National Government, including government-owned or -controlled “corporations and_ local
government units, are authorized to participate in the KGP3. \

The attendance of an official or employee per agency in charge of the formulation or
implementation of development plans, programs and projects on environment and natural
resources, engineering, and supply and procurement services, to the extent that will not impair
the delivery of services in their respective offices,sis hereby allowed on official time, with
entitlement to registration fee of not more than 22,500.00, chargeable against the concerned
agency’s budget, subject to the availability of funds and applicable budgetary, accounting and
auditing laws, rules and regulations.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this28tHay of October in the Year of
our Lord Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By Authority of the President:

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDES

Executive Secretary
