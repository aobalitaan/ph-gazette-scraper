MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 71

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION IN THE 18TH ANNUAL NATIONAL
UNION OF CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE OFFICERS, INC.
(NUCESO) CONFERENCE

The National Union of Career Executive Service Officers, Inc. will hold the 18th
Annual NUCESO Conference on 13-15 November 2019 at the Rizal Park Hotel,
Ermita, Manila.

This year’s theme is “Malinis, Mabilis na Serbisyo, Alay ng NUCESO sa
Sambayanang Pilipino.”

In this regard, all Career Executive Service (CES) officers, CES eligibles,
Career Service Executive eligibles and other third level officials, are encouraged to
participate, and those interested may attend on official time, provided that they are
issued the necessary clearances/authorities by their respective heads of office, and
their participation in the said Convention will not adversely affect the operations of their
offices, as well as the delivery of services to the public.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this7th day of November jn the
Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Nineteen.
