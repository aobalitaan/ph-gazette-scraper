MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 65

Pursuant to Proclamation No. 60 (s. 1992) which declared the last week of
September of every year as Family Week, and in order to afford government workers
more time to spend with their families on this, the first day of the 27" National Family
Week Celebration, work in government offices in the Executive Branch on 23 September
2019 is hereby suspended from 3:00 o'clock in the afternoon onwards.

However, those agencies whose functions involve the delivery of basic and health
services, preparedness/response to disasters and calamities, and/or the performance of
other vital services shall continue with their operations and render the necessary
services.

The suspension of work in other branches of government and in independent
commissions or bodies is left to the sound discretion of their respective
heads/management.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority/of the President:

Ms a

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
