MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 20

DIRECTING ALL GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR -
CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AND ENCOURAGING_ LOCAL
GOVERNMENT UNITS, TO EXTEND SUPPORT TO THE 19" ASIAN
SENIOR WOMEN’S VOLLEYBALL CHAMPIONSHIP

WHEREAS, the Larong Volleyball sa Pilipinas, Inc. will host the 19" Asian Senior
Women’s Volleyball Championship 2017, which will be held from 09 to 17 August 2017
at Bifian, Laguna; and

WHEREAS, ithe hosting of said sporting event in the Philippines will bring
international prestige, publicity, goodwill, and economic benefits to the country;

NOW, THEREFORE, all government agencies and instrumentalities, including
government-owned or -controlled corporations, are hereby directed, and all local
government units are encouraged, to extend full support to the 19" Asian Senior
Women’s Volleyball Championship 2017.

This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this6th dayof July __, in the year of Our Lord,
Two Thousand and Seventeen.
