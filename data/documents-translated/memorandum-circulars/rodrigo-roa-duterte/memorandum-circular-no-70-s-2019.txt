MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 709

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION IN THE CAREER EXECUTIVE
SERVICE (CES) CONGRESS

The Career Executive Service Board will hold the CES Lifelong Learning for
Leadership Congress on 20-22 November 2019 at the Taal Vista Hotel, Tagaytay
City.

This year's theme is “GREATful Leadership: Performance, Positivity and
Possibilities towards a Resilient Society.”

In view thereof, all CES officers, eligibles and eligibility on-stream candidates
are hereby authorized to attend the Congress on official time, to the extent that will not
impair the operations of their respective offices, as well as the delivery of services to
the public.

Expenses to be incurred by the participants shall be allowed at prescribed rates,
in accordance with the existing policies of the Department of Budget and Management
and the Commission on Audit.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this7th day of November in the
Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Nineteen.

By Authority of the President:

A K
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
