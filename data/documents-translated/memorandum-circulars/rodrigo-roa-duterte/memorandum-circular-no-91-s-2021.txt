MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 91

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION IN THE CAREER EXECUTIVE
SERVICE (CES) CONGRESS

The Career Executive Service Board will conduct the CES Lifelong Learning
for Leadership Congress through virtual platform on 09-12 November 2021.

This year’s theme is “Resilience 4.0: Courage, Agility and Future-Ready
Leadership.”

In view thereof, all CES officers, eligibles and eligibility on-stream candidates
are authorized to attend the CES Congress on official time, to the extent that will not
impair the operations of their respective offices in the delivery of essential services to
the public.

Expenses to be incurred by the participants shall be allowed at prescribed rates,
in accordance with the existing policies of the Department of Budget and Management
and the Commission on Audit.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 22nday of October in
the Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-One.
