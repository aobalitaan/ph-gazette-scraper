MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 92

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION IN THE 20™ ANNUAL NATIONAL
UNION OF CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE OFFICERS, INC. (NUCESO)
CONFERENCE

The National Union of Career Executive Service Officers, Inc. will hold the 20%
Annual NUCESO Conference on 27-29 October 2021 via Zoom platform.

This year’s theme is “Leading Beyond: Reimagining Public Governance.”

In this regard, all Career Executive Service (CES) Officers, CES eligibles, Career
Service Executive eligibles, and other third level officials, are encouraged to participate,
and those interested may attend on official time, provided that they are issued the
necessary clearances/authorities by their respective heads of office, and their
participation in the said Conference will not adversely affect the operations of their offices,
as well as the delivery of services to the public.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, Philippines, this 26thdayof October in the
Year of our Lord Two Thousand and Twenty-One.

By authority of the President:

Ny
SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary
