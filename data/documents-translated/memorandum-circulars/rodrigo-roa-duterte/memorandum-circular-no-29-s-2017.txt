Office of the President
of the Philippines
Malacaiang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 29

In view of the continuing transport strike, coupled with possible inclement
weather, work in government, both national and local, and classes at all levels, both
public and private, including state universities and colleges, are hereby suspended on
Tuesday, 17 October 2017.

The suspension of work in the other branches of government, as well as in
constitutional commissions and other independent bodies, is left to the discretion of their
respective heads.

The suspension of work in private companies and offices is likewise left to the
sound discretion of their responsible executive officers, taking into account the safety
and convenience of their employees.

For your information and guidance.

By Authority of the President:

ee aya

SALVADOR C. MEDIALDEA
Executive Secretary

Manila, 16 October 2017
