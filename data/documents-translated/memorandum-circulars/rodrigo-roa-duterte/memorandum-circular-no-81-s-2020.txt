MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 81

AUTHORIZING PARTICIPATION IN THE CAREER EXECUTIVE
SERVICE (CES) CONGRESS

The Career Executive Service Board will conduct the CES Lifelong Learning
for Leadership Congress through virtual platform on 24-27 November 2020.

This year’s theme is “ Thriving Leadership: Spirit-Led Stewardship towards the
Better Normal.”

In view thereof, all CES officers, eligibles and eligibility on-stream candidates
are hereby authorized to attend the Congress on official time, to the extent that will not
impair the operations of their respective offices and the delivery of essential services
to the public.

Expenses to be incurred by the participants shall be allowed at prescribed rates,
in accordance with the existing policies of the Department of Budget and Management
and the Commission on Audit.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

Manila, Philippines, 09 November 2020 |
