MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 33

ENJOINING ALL DEPARTMENTS, AGENCIES, AND
INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT,
INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR -CONTROLLED
CORPORATIONS, LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, AND STATE
UNIVERSITIES AND COLLEGES, TO SUPPORT THE INTRAMUROS
REGIONAL FAIRS OF THE DEPARTMENT OF TOURISM AND THE
INTRAMUROS ADMINISTRATION

WHEREAS, the Philippine Development Plan (PDP) 2017-2022 is geared
towards the promotion of Filipino culture and values as it acknowledges the
empowering nature of culture in the enhancement of the social fabric toward a high-
trust society;

WHEREAS, the Government aims to expand inclusive cultural structures as
civic spaces for dialogue and cultural exchange by utilizing cultural infrastructure
and facilities as venues for fostering social cohesion and valuing cultural diversity;

WHEREAS, consistent with the PDP cultural agenda, the Department of
Tourism and the Intramuros Administration will organize regional fairs at the historic
district of Intramuros, beginning March 2018, to highlight the history and culture of
the Filipino people through lectures, workshops, performances, fashion exhibits,
culinary festivals, tourism destination products and trade exhibits;

NOW, THEREFORE, all departments, agencies, and instrumentalities of the
national government, including government-owned or -controlled corporations, local
government units, and state universities and colleges, are enjoined to lend full
support in the conduct of the Intramuros regional fairs and to encourage active
participation in all its programs and activities.

This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE, in the City of Manila, this 16th dayof November __, inthe year of
Our Lord, Two Thousand and Seventeen.

By authority of the President:
