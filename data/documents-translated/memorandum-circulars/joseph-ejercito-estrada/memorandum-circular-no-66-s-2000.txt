Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 66

AUTHORIZING THE ATTENDANCE OF LAWYERS EMPLOYED IN GOVERNMENT OFFICES AND AGENCIES IN THE 8 th NATIONAL CONVENTION OF LAWYERS ON APRIL 26-28, 2001 AT WATERFRONT CEBU CITY HOTEL, CEBU CITY

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines will hold the 8 th National Convention of Lawyers on April 26-28, 2001 at the Waterfront Cebu City Hotel, Cebu City.

In this connection, lawyers employed in government offices and agencies are hereby authorized to attend the Convention on official time.

Participants shall submit to their respective units a complete report of their attendance or participation in the said convention.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RONALDO B. ZAMORA Executive Secretary

Manila, September 15, 2000
