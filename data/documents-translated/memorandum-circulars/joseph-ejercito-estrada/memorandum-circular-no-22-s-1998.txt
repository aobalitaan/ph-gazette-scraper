All officials and employees of the government, including government owned and
controlled corporations, may take the afternoon off effective 12:01 p.m. of December 24,
1998, to enable them to celebrate the traditional Christmas Eve with their families and love
ones. Government agencies performing vital functions shall, however, maintain a skeletal
work force.

Government offices shall resume normal operations on December 28, 1998.

This Circular takes effect immediately.
