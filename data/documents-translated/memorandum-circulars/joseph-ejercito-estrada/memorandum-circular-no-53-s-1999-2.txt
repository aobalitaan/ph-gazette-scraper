MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. _5_3__ 
All officials and employees of the government, including government owned and 
controlled corporations, may take the afternoon off effective 12:01 p.m. of December 24, 
1999, to enable them to celebrate the traditional Christmas Eve with their families and 
loved ones. Government agencies performing vital functions shall, however, maintain a 
skeletal work force. 
Government offices shall resume nonnal operations on December 27, 1999. 
This Circular takes effect immediately. 
By authority of the President: 
RONALDO B. ZAMORA 
Executive Secretary 
Manila, December 23, 1999
