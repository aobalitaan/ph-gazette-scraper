OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 159

PROHIBITING THE DETAIL OF NATIONAL GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL FROM THE PROVINCES TO NATIONAL GOVERNMENT OFFICES IN MANILA.

In line with the administration’s campaign to reduce to the minimum the administrative expenses of the national government in order to conserve public funds for the much-needed projects, the detail of national government personnel with station in the provinces to national government offices in Manila is hereby prohibited, as such detail constitutes a big drain in government funds payable in the form of per diems and allowances incident thereto.

This Order shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, February 19, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
