OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 359

ANNOUNCING THE ESTABLISHMENT OF AN AREA OFFICE IN MANILA OF THE INTERNATIONAL LABOR ORGANIZATION (ILO).

The Director General of the International Labor Organization (ILO), in a letter to the Secretary of Foreign Affairs, advised the Philippines Government of the establishment of an ILO area office in Manila to be headed by Mr. Hugo Lane as Director. In view thereof, all communications concerning the ILO programme in the Philippines should henceforth be addressed to the Director of the ILO Area Office in Manila, as follows:

The Director, ILO Area Office, Ramon Magsaysay Centre, Manila (Philippines)

This Circular is issued for the information and guidance of all government offices concerned.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, May 29, 1970
