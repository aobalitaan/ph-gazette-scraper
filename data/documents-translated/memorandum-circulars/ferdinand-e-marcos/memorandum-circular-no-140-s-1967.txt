OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 140

URGING ATTENDANCE OF CERTAIN GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES IN THE SECOND NATIONAL CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE ASSOCIATION OF OCCUPATIONAL MEDICINE.

The Philippine Association of Occupational Medicine will hold its Second National Convention in Manila on February 2-4, 1968, with the objective of encouraging more effective occupational health activities through the active participation of representatives of management, labor, and government agencies engaged in occupational health work.

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including local governments and government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to allow their employees who are engaged directly or indirectly in occupational health work to attend the convention provided attendance shall be on official tine only and that not more than five (5) delegates shall come from any one agency

The delegates shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance in the convention.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, December 21, 1967

Source: Presidential Management Staff
