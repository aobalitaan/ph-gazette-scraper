MALACANANG Manila

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 963

AUTHORIZING MEMBERSHIP IN THE GOVERNMENT PUBLIC RELATIONS ORGANIZATION OF THE PHILIPPINE, INC. (PROP).

The Government Public Relations Organization of the Philippines, Inc. (PROP), an organization of government officials and. employees performing public relations and information work, was, organized with the following objectives:

1. To foster a stronger and more cohesive fellowship and cooperation among those involved in public relations and information services of the government;

2. To develop dedication, discipline and professionalism, in their work of bringing the government closer to the people;

3. To raise the status of government public relations officers to a position of high responsibility and importance as will enable them to actively participate in policy decisions of their respective agencies; and

4. To assist in every way possible in the national endeavor of achieving reforms.

In line with our efforts to upgrade and improve the public information activities of the government, all public information, public relations or public affairs officers of the government are hereby authorized to become members of the above-mentioned organization.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, March 4, 1977

Source: Presidential Management Staff
