TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1145

TO : ALL HEADS OF MINISTRIES, AGENCIES, LOCAL UNITS INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS

SUBJECT: ATTENDANCE IN THE BRIEFING ON CAREER SERVICE EXAMINATION OF BUKLOD MEMBERS ON OFFICIAL TIME

The Civil Service Commission as the central personnel agency of the government is in charge of the conduct of selection processes and examinations to ensure the eligibility of competent manpower in the public service. In the conduct of the Career Service Examinations for both- the Professional and Sub-Professional level on June 17, 1979, the members of the Buklod ng mga Kabataang Kawani are tapped to serve as Procters in the different testing centers in the country.

In this connection, all Buklod members who shall serve as Proctors are authorized to attend on official time the Briefing of Examiners and Proctors in their respective testing centers.

Please be guided accordingly.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, June 1, 1979

Source: Presidential Management Staff
