OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 424

CREATING AN INTERDEPARTMENTAL TASK FORCE FOR A CONTINUING EVALUATION AND UPDATING OF THE FOUR-YEAR DEVELOPMENT PLAN OF THE PHILIPPINES

There is hereby created an inter-departmental task force to be headed by the Chairman of the National Economic Council to undertake:

1. A continuing evaluation of the progress of implementation of the Four-Year Development Plan for the Philippines.

2. The updating of the Plan; and

3. The preparation of a Perspective Plan.

The task force shall be composed of the following
