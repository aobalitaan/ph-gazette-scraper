TANGGAPAN NG PAGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 800

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 730 DATED MAY 20, 1974 OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT, AS AMENDED OF MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 747, SERIES OF 1974.

Memorandum Circular No. 739 dated May 20, 1974 of this Office urging attendance in the Seminar on Supply Management to be conducted by the Bureau of Supply Coordination is hereby further amended so as to include Iloilo City as the seminar site for the Western Visayas Region.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, February 7, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
