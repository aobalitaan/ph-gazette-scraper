TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 934

URGING ATTENDANCE OF BUREAU DIRECTORS AND ASSISTANT DIRECTORS IN THE ONE-DAY SEMINAR ON “PERCEPTIONS OF PHILIPPINE REALITIES.”

The Philippine Council for Policy Science, a non-stock, non-profit scholarly organization, will hold a one-day seminar on ”Perceptions of Philippine Realities” at the Magsaysay Hall, SSS Building, Quezon City, on September 30, 1976.

Various aspects of the policy sciences, such as problem and prospects of social policy implementation in the Philippine context, nature of social policy, and the social policy and Muslims will be discussed during the seminar with the end in view of acquainting government administrators and the private sector with practical principles relevant to the task of decision-making at the managerial level, and to correct studies of clerical groups regarding the Philippine situation.

In line with the policy of the government encouraging government administrators to improve their performance capabilities at managerial level, all bureau directors and assistant directors within the Greater Manila area are hereby urged to attend the seminar on official time only.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, September 23, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
