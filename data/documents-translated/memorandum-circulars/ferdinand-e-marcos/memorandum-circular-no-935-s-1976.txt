OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 935

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 927 DATED AUGUST 20, 1976, AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE ON THE SEMINAR FOR ACTION OFFICERS, THE CHAIRMEN AND MEMBERS OF SPECIAL ACTION COMMITTEES OF ALL GOVERNMENT OFFICES IN THE METROPOLITAN MANILA AREA.

Memorandum Circular No. 927 dated August 20, 1976, authorizing attendance in the seminar for Action Officers, the Chairman and Members of Special Action Committee of all government offices in the Metropolitan Manila Area, is hereby further amended so as to indicate the duration of the seminar from November 8 to November 11, 1976, instead of September 27 to October 1, 1976.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, September 27, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
