TANGGAPAN NG PAGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 807

AMENDING MEMORANDUM ORDER NO. 470 DATED NOVEMBER 29, 1974, CREATING A STEERING COMMITTEE TO FORMULATE PLANS AND PROGRAMS FOR THE ESTABLISHMENT OF AN ILONGOT VILLAGE.

Memorandum Order No. 470 of this Office dated November 29, 1974, is hereby amended so as to include the duly-designated representative of the Department of Tourism as member of the Steering Committee to formulate plans and programs for the establishment of an Ilongot Village, and to entitle the members thereof to collect reasonable transportation expenses per meeting for actual attendance in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344 dated November 26, 1973, as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127 dated November 27, 1973.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, February 17, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
