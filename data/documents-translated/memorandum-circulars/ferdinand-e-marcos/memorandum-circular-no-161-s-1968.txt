OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 161

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE ANNUAL GENERAL MEMBERSHIP MEETING OF THE MANAGEMENT PRACTITIONERS’ ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES.

The Management Practitioners’ Association of the Philippines (MaPAP), a non-profit technical and professional association of government and private management specialists, will hold its Annual General Membership Meeting on Thursday, February 29, 1968, at the GSIS Social Hall, Arroceros Street, Manila.

The meeting would complement the administration’s efforts at improving management work in the government.

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to allow the attendance in the meeting on official time of their employees who are members of the Association.

The participants shall summit to their respective agency heads a report of their attendance in the meeting.

Further information regarding this matter may be secured from the MaPAP Secretariat, Management Service, Budget Commission.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, February 26, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
