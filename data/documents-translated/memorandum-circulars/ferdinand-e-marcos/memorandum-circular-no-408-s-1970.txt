OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 408

GRANTING HEADS OF DEPARTMENTS AND CHIEFS OF BUREAUS AND OFFICES IN THE GREATER MANILA AREA THE DISCRETION TO EXCUSE THEIR PERSONNEL FROM OFFICE TODAY.

In view of typhoon “Yoling”, heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, in the Greater Manila area, except those engaged in relief and rescue operations, transportation and communication, are hereby given discretion to excuse their personnel from office today, November 19, 1970, provided that a skeleton force shall remain in office to attend to official business transactions.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, November 19, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
