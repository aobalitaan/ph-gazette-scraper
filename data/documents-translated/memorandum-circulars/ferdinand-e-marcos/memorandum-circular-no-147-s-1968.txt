OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 147

ENJOINING ATTENDANCE IN THE THIRD ANNUAL NATIONAL CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE FEDERATION OF NUTRITION COUNCILS AND RELATED AGENCIES FOR THE PROMOTION OF NUTRITION CONSCIOUSNESS AMONG THE MASSES.

The Philippine Federation of Nutrition Councils and Related Agencies will hold its third Annual National Convention for the promotion of nutrition consciousness among our people curing Nutrition Week on March 1 and 2, 1968, in the City of Manila.

Departments, bureaus, and offices, as well as city, provincial and municipal governments, including government-owned or controlled corporations, which have nutrition councils or nutrition projects, are hereby authorized to send not more than four members from each agency to the convention. Their attendance shall be on official time and their expenses for transportation, subsistence, lodging and convention fees in connection therewith shall be chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and usual accounting and auditing requirements. Each agency may send delegates in excess of four but the attendance of those in excess of four shall be on official time only.

Delegates shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance and participation in the convention.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, January 22, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
