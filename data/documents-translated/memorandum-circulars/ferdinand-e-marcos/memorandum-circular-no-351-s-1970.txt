OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 351

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE REGULAR MONTHLY MEETING AND OTHER OFFICIAL ACTIVITIES OF THE RURAL BROADCASTERS’ COUNCIL.

The Rural Broadcasters’ Council, as independent organization of rural broadcasters and farm writers and other public information officers, has been coordinating with the government in disseminating valuable information on modern agricultural techniques and family planning.

This Council holds a regular monthly meeting at the Bureau of Plant Industry and undertakes information campaign by going directly to the farmers in rural areas.

In view of the importance of this Council’s contribution to the government’s crash food production program, all heads of Departments, bureaus and offices indicated in the attached list whose personnel are active members of the Council are hereby urged to allow said personnel to attend on official time the regular monthly meeting and other official activities of the Rural Broadcasters’s Council.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, May 5, 1970

Source : Presidential Management Staff
