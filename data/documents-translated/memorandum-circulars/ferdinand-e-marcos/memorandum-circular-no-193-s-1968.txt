OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 193

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINARS ON THE CIVIL SERVICE LAW AND RULES SPONSORED BY THE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION.

In pursuance of its leadership function in the developed all government personnel at all levels, the Civil Service Commission will conduct a series of seminars on the Service Law and Rules in cooperation with the local governments, as hereunder indicated:

These seminars are aimed to securing understanding, enlightened appreciation, and proper implementation of the Civil Service Law and Rules by all officials and employees of the government w called upon “to insure .and promote the constitutional mandate regarding appointments only according to merit and fitness, and to provide within the public service a progressive system of personnel administration to insure the maintenance of an honest, efficient., progressive and courteous civil service” in the Philippines.

In view of the importance of the objectives of these seminars, all local government subdivisions and national government agencies and units within the territorial limits of the region whore the training program will he conducted hereby authorized to send not more than three (3) participants each to the seminar whose participation shall be considered official business, with expenses for transportation, lodging, subsistence, and seminar fees, chargeable against appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a report of their attendance in the seminar.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, June 19, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
