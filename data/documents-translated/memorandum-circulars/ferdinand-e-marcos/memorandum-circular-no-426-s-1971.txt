OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 426

EXCUSING FROM OFFICE GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL ON CERTAIN DAYS.

In view of the continued disruption of public transportation services in the Greater Manila Area due to the strike staged by jeepney drivers, government officials and employees holding office within the said area are hereby excused from work starting from the time indicated opposite the following dates:

January 11, 1971 – 4:00 P.M. January 12, 1971 – 3:00 P.M. January 13, 1971 – 11:00 P.M. January 14, 1971 – 3:00 P.M

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, January 15, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
