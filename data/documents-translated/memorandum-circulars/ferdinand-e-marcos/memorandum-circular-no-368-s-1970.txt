{"translation":"MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 368

ORDERING THE CONDUCT OF PROGRAMS IN CELEBRATION OF NATIONAL LANGUAGE WEEK.

Proclamation No. 186, series of 1995, stipulates that the annual celebration of National Language Week is from August 13-19.

The national language is an important component of true patriotic spirit, and it is needed to inspire citizens towards the achieved national transformation.

Therefore, all heads of departments, bureaus, offices, and other branches of the national and local government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are ordered to conduct programs in Filipino, even for just thirty (30) minutes, on any day within the celebration week, related to the theme “Unite for Change.” The leaders and personnel of the government are required to lead the conduct of short programs in the offices they serve.

By order of the President: (SGD.) PONCIANO G.A. MATHAY Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, July 2, 1970"}
