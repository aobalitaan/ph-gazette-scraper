Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1254

In line with the policy of the State to develop and uplift economically depressed areas in all regions of the country to ensure political stability, and pursuant to Executive Order No. 852 which provides for effective coordination between national development and security operations, Ticao Island, Masbate, a depressed and politically unstable area, has been chosen as a tri-sectoral development pilot project.

To achieve the above-mentioned goal, a task force is hereby created to be known as the Ticao Island Development Task Force (TIDETAF) which shall be composed of the following:

TIDETAF shall perform the following functions:

1. To formulate plans and programs for Ticao Island and supervise the implementation of such development plans and programs;

2. To coordinate with all agencies involved and recommend possible courses of action to effectively implement the programs;

3. To submit monthly status reports to the Office of the President.

TIDETAF may call on other agencies whenever it deems necessary to fully implement its programs and projects.

MANILA, March 7, 1983

By Authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA
