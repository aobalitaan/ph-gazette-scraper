OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 222

REVOKING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NOS. 107 AND 107-A SERIES OF 1967

Memorandum Circular No, 107 dated March 50, 1967, requiring submission of government contracts involving; private financing; schemes to the Investments Office of the Presidential Economic Staff for evaluation and feasibility 1 study and Memorandum Circular No. 107-A dated June 29, 1967 clarifying the provisions of said Memorandum Circular No. 107 are hereby revoked effective today.

Henceforth, all such government contracts shall be submitted directly to the Financial Policy Committee created under Executive Order No. 97, series of 1967.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, December 6, 1968

Source : Presidential Management Staff
