TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1045

SUSPENDING THE RURAL SERVICE FOR THE FIRST HALF OF APRIL, 1978, UNDER THE PAGLILINGKOD: BAGONG LIPUNAN PROGRAM.

In view of the election on April 7, 1978, for members of the Interim Batasang Pambansa, the rural service under the Paglilingkod: Bagong Lipunan Program for the period April 1 – 15, 1978 required of government officials and employees pursuant to the provisions of Letter of Instructions No. 559 dated June 23, 1977, is hereby suspended.

The employees scheduled for rural service on the period April 1 – 15, 1978 shall be distributed among the other periods left so that all shall have rendered service as of September 15, 1978.

Please be guided accordingly.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, February 21, 1978

Source: Presidential Management Staff
