OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 311

TERMINATING ALL EXISTING DETAILS OF GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL IN THE VARIOUS DEPARTMENTS, BUREAUS, OFFICES, AGENCIES AND INSTRUMENTALITIES OF THE GOVERNMENT INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS.

Effective upon notice and/or receipt hereof, all existing details of government personnel in the various Departments, bureaus, offices, agencies and instrumentalities of the government, including governments-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby terminated, and the person or persons concerned shall accordingly be returned to their respective mother offices: Provided, However, that hereafter heads of offices may request for and the detail shall be extended only under following conditions, to wit:

(a) The services of the official or employee, whose detail is requested, are deemed essential;

(b) The detail shall not exceed a period of six (6) months; and

(c) The detail shall not involve payment of any additional emolument or compensation, per diem, transportation or representation allowance or any other form financial benefits whatsoever.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, January 12, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
