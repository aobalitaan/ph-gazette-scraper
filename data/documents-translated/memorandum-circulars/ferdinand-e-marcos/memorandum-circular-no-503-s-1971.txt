OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 503

REITERATING THE POLICY PROHIBITING THE PURCHASE OR IMPORTATION HIGH-POWERED FIREARMS.

The recent incidents wherein lawless elements have created state of lawlessness and disorder affecting public safety and the security of the State clearly show the compelling need to control the possession firearms by our countrymen. Accordingly, the directive of the President of the Philippines dated October 16, 1970, to the Acting Chief of Constabulary not to issue clearances for the purchase or importation of firearms without the President’s own written approval is hereby reiterated.

Strict compliance with the provisions of this Circular is desired.

This Circular takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, September 7, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
