OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 290

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE ANNUAL CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE ASSOCIATION OF MECHANICAL AND ELECTRICAL ENGINEERS.

The Philippine Association of Mechanical and Electrical Engineers (PAMEE) will hold its Thirty-Ninth Annual Convention in Davao City on October 24-26, 1969, on the theme: “Rural Electrification, The Key to National Progress.”

In view of the importance of this convention to the rural electrification efforts of the Administration, all heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to send one of their mechanical or electrical engineers to the said convention on official business, that is, with official time and expenses for transportation, subsistence and lodging, and convention fees, if any, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

The delegates shall submit to their agency heads a report on their attendance in the convention within 30 days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ERNESTO M. MACEDA Executive Secretary

Manila, September 25, 1969

Source : Presidential Management Staff
