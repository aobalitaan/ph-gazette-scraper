Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1191

CURRICULUM DEVELOPMENT WORKSHOP FOR LINGAP NG PANGULO SA BARANGAY (SCHOOL-ON-THE-AIR-PROGRAM) (LOI 561)

TO: The Chairman and Members of the Lingap ng Pangulo Sub-Cabinet Committee:

Political Deputy Minister Felicita G. Bernardino, MEC Deputy Minister Orlando Sacay, MA Deputy Minister Sylvia Montes, MSSD Deputy Minister Jesus Azurin, MOH Deputy Minister Rosendo Marquez, MLGCD Assistant Secretary Angelina R. Muñoz, MAR Assistant Secretary Jose A. Janolo, MNR Director Gregorio S. Cendaña, NMPC and OIC, MPI

1. A curriculum development workshop for Lingap ng Pangulo sa Barangay School-on-the-Air Program will be held on 14-31 July 1900 at the San Jose del Monte Elementary School, San Jose del Monte, Bulacan.

2. The specific objectives of the workshop are:

a) Acquaint the participants on the problems regarding the existing Lingap teaching guides (TG’s);

b) Review the existing TG’s to determine their relevance and adaptability to the needs of the target clientele;

c) Produce revised manuscripts of the TG’s;

d) Determine possible topics for additional TG’s; and

e) Produce additional sets of teaching guides based on recommendations and felt needs in the regions.

3. Each agency mentioned above shall send two representatives, to this curriculum workshop, preferably, those who participated in the preparation of the original teaching guides.

4. The Office of Nonformal Education, Ministry of Education and Culture, in cooperation with the above mentioned agencies, will sponsor the said workshop.

5. Traveling expenses of the participants to and from, the workshop venue will be charged against their respective ministries.

6. Enclosed is the tentative program of activities.

7. Participation to this workshop of the above agencies is enjoined.

By Authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

June 25, 1980
