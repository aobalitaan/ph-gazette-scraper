TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 607

TO: All Heads of Departments

The First Lady, through her Social Secretary, requests all department heads to invite all officials and employees in their respective departments to the presentation of the “BAGONG ANYO ’72,” at five o’clock in the afternoon.

All guests have to pay an entrance fee of ₱1.00 at the Nayong Filipino gate to entitle them to see the presentation for free.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, November 7, 1972

Source: Presidential Museum and Library
