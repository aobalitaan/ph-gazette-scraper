TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 882-A

AMENDING THE LAST PARAGRAPH OF MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 606 DATED OCTOBER 21, 1972, ENTITLED “MAKING CERTAIN CLARIFICATIONS CONCERNING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 20 DATED OCTOBER 12, 1972.”

The last paragraph of Memorandum Circular No. 606 dated October 21, 1972, is hereby amended to read as follows:

The prohibition against increase in rental contained in Presidential Decree No. 20, however, shall not apply to a case where a dwelling unit or land is vacated during the effectivity of the decree and a new tenant occupies or leases the same. The owner of such dwelling unit or land and the prospective lessee may determine the terms and conditions of their contract provide, however, that the rental which may be agreed upon shall not exceed ₱300 a month.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, November 27, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
