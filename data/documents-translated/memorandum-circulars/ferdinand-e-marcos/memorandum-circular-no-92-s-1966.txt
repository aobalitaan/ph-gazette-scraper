OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 92

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINAR ON THE PREVENTION OF CRIME AND TREATMENT OF OFFENDERS.

The National Bureau of Investigation and the Bureau of Prisons will hold a three-day symposium-forum en the Prevention of Crime and Treatment of Offenders in the City or Manila on January 4-6, 1967.

The purpose of the seminar is to crystallize public opinion and seek ways and means of effectively combatting crime in all its forms.

Considering the timeliness and significance of the seminar, all heads of national government agencies engaged in law enforcement, investigation, probation, parole, institutional treatment, after-care and social welfare services of offenders, as well as of nearby provinces and cities, are hereby authorized to send not more than three participants each to this seminar. The participants’ attendance shall be on official tine and their expenses for transportation, subsistence, lodging and seminar fees in connection therewith shall be chargeable against the Impropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing r requirements.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a report of their attendance and participation in the seminar.

By authority of the President:

SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, December 29, 1966

Source: Presidential Management Staff
