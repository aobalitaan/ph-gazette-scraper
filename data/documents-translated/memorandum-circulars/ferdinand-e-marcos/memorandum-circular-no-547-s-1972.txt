TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 547

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 539 DATED FEBRUARY 4, 1972, ENTITLED “ENJOINING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINAR TO BE CONDUCTED BY THE COMMUNITY CHESTS AND COUNCILS OF THE PHILIPPINES.

Memorandum Circular No. 539 dated February 4, 1972 entitled “Enjoining Attendance in the Seminar to be conducted by the Community Chests and Councils of the Philippines,” is hereby amended to include the provincial heads of the Bureau of Forestry to promote greater community involvement therein, and to change the place of the Seminar to the DBP Auditorium, Makati, Rizal, instead of the National Federation of Women’s Clubs Social Hall, 2nd floor, National Federation of Women’s Club Building, 1056, J. Llanes Escoda Street, corner San Marcelino Street, Ermita, Manila.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 1, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
