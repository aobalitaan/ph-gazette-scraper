OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 363

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE PHILIPPINE MANAGEMENT CONGRESS OF THE PHILIPPINE COUNCIL OF MANAGEMENT.

The Philippine Council of Managenent (PHILICOMAN), with the cooperation of the Asian Association of Management Organizations of the Conseil International pour 1” Organisation Scientifique (AAMOCICS) will sponsor the Philippine Management Congress to be held on Juno 24-27, 1970, at the new building of the Asian Institute of Management (AIM), Makati, Rizal. The theme of the Congress is “Management in the 70’s.”

Management experts, practitioners, and educators representing eleven Asian-Pacific nations will participate as speakers, discussants, and delegates. They will pool ideal and try to seek solutions to some challenging management problems covered by such subject areas as the Environment of Management, the Special Problems of Management and the Improvement of Management.

The Congress would afford participants the opportunity to get an insight into the modern trends and developments in business management and public administration.

In view hereof, all heads of departments, and chief of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to allow attendance in the Congress of select participants on official time.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a report of the attendance, with their observations and comments on deliberations in the meeting.

Further information regarding this matter may be secured from the Congress Secretariat, c/o the Management Service, Budget Commission.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, June 11, 1970
