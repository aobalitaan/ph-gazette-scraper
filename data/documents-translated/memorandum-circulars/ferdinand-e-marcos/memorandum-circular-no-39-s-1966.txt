OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 39

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 28 DATED MARCH 25, 1966 URGING THE ATTENDANCE OF CERTAIN MUNICIPAL MAYORS IN THE FOURTH EXECUTIVE DEVELOPMENT COURSE TO BE CONDUCTED BY THE PACD.

Memorandum Circular No. 28 dated March 25, 1966, urging the attendance of Municipal Mayors who have been invited by the PACD in the Fourth Executive Development Course to be held at the University of San Agustin, City of Iloilo, from March 23 to April 2, 1966, is hereby amended so as to change the date of the course to June 11-16, 1966, instead of from March 28 to April, 1966, as therein originally indicated.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, May 9, 1966

Source : Presidential Management Staff
