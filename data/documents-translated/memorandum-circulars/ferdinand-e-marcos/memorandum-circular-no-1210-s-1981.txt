Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1210

ENJOINING ALL HEADS OF GOVERNMENT OFFICES TO CONDUCT SEMINARS ON THE PROPOSED CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENTS IN THEIR RESPECTIVE OFFICES.

In view of the importance of the coming plebisite and considering the need to enlighten our people of the significance of this political exercise, all heads of government offices and agencies, including government-owned or controlled corporations are hereby enjoined to conduct seminars on the proposed constitutional amendments in their respective offices.

The officers and members of the Alumni Association of the Career Executive Service Development Program which has launched a nation-wide information campaign on the proposed amendments may be asked to assist and coordinate the holding of the seminars.

Report of the implementation of this Circular shall be submitted to this Office within ten (10) days from receipt hereof.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, 18 March 1981

Source: Presidential Management Staff
