TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 849

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE REFRESHER COURSE FOR GRADUATES OF THE PHILIPPINE EXECUTIVE ACADEMY

The Philippine Executive Academy (PEA) of the University of the Philippines, in conjunction with its Alumni Association (APEX), will conduct its Third Refresher Course from December 2 to 6, 1975 at Baguio City.

This year’s course is being held for the purpose of upgrading Philippine Executive Academy graduates on the latest developments and especially problems in executive management in the context of the new administrative environment and what, individually and collectively, the alumni can do to contribute to national unity and development.

In line with the Administration’s objectives of reinforcing the capabilities of our executives from the government and private business and industry in the promotion of economic and social development, encouraging the free exchange of ideas and work experiences among themselves with a view to further appreciating vital elements of leadership in government and business, and arousing interest in contemporary issues that impinge upon development management, this office hereby authorizes government executives who aregraduates of the Philippine Executive Academy to attend the course on official business with expenses for transportation, subsistence, lodging and course fees chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 dated August 8, 1972.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance in the course within thirty (30) days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Deputy Executive Secretary

Manila, June 27, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
