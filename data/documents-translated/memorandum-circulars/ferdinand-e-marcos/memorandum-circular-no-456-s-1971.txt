OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 456

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 449 URGING THE ATTENDANCE OF MUNICIPAL HEALTH OFFICERS IN THE NATIONAL CONVENTION OF THE ASSOCIATION OF MUNICIPAL HEALTH OFFICERS OF THE PHILIPPINES (AMHOP).

Memorandum Circular No. 449 urging the attendance of Municipal Health Officers in the National Convention of the Association of Municipal Health Officers of the Philippines (AMHOP) is hereby amended so as to authorize the participants in the said Convention transportation expenses chargeable against the funds of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, April 13, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
