TANGGAPAN NG PAGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 779

Participants from provincial government and other government agencies who will attend the Field Workshop on Real Property Tax Administration at Palawan and the Data Gathering Course in Manila of the Provincial Development Assistance Project on 2 to 6 December and 9 to 11 December, respectively are hereby exempted from the provisions under Memo Circular of the President, dated 8 August 1972 regarding attendance at meetings, seminars, workshops, conventions or the like.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, December 2, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
