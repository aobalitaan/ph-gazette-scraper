TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 512

EXCUSING FROM OFFICE GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL BEGINNING 2:30 O’CLOCK P.M., OCTOBER 12, 1971.

In view of the inclement weather caused by typhoon “Dadang” which may pass by Manila, sometime this evening, government officials and employees holding office in the Greater Manila Area are hereby excused from work this afternoon only, beginning 2:20 o’clock, except those engaged in relief and rescue operations and the necessary personnel of the Weather Bureau.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, October 12, 1971
