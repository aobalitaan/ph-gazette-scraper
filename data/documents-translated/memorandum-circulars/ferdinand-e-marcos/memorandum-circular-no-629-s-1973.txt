TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 629

URGING ALL CONCERNED TO COMMEMORATE THE 31 ST ANNIVERSARY OF THE FALL OF BATAAN AND CORRIGIDOR WITH APPROPRIATE CEREMONIES AND/OR ACTIVITIES SYMBOLIC OF THE REBIRTH OF A NEW NATION.

WHEREAS, the forthcoming anniversary of the Fall of Bataan and Corrigidor shall be celebrated in a manner that shall reflect the rebirth of a new nation;

WHEREAS, to emphasize the overriding significance of the occasion, the school children shall wear the New Society pin which shall be distributed by the Department of Education;

WHEREFORE, al heads of departments, bureaus and offices, local governments, including government-owned or controlled corporations and instrumentalities of the government are hereby urged to carry out the objective of this Circular.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, April 3, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
