OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 36

AUTHORIZING THE ATTENDANCE OF GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN A FORUM TO BE SPONSORED BY THE WOMEN LAWYERS’ ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES INC. (WLAP) AND THE CIVIC ASSEMBLY ON WOMEN OF THE PHILIPPINES (CAWP).

The Women Lawyers’ Association of the Philippines Inc. (WLAP), and the Civic Assembly or Women of the Philippines (CAWP), are jointly sponsoring a forum on current issues of vital interest not only to people in business and industry but also to government employees at the Girl Scouts Auditorium, Padre Faura Street, Manila, at 2:30 p.m. on May 4, 1966.

All departments, bureaus and offices of the national government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, located in Manila and suburbs, are hereby authorized to send not more than two of their officials and employees to the forum on official time.

Those authorized to attend shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance and participation in the forum.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, May 3, 1966

Source : Presidential Management Staff
