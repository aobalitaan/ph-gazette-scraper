OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 394

EXCUSING GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL, EXCEPT THOSE ENGAGED IN RELIEF AND RESCUE OPERATIONS LIKE THE ARMED FORCES OF THE PHILIPPINES, SOCIAL WELFARE ADMINISTRATION, DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC WORKS FROM OFFICE ON SEPTEMBER 2 AND 3, 1970.

In view of the continuous rains which inundated the Greater Manila area and most of the Central and Southern Luzon and disrupted all means of land transportation in said places on September 2 and 3, 1970, government officials and employees in Central Luzon, the Greater Manila area and Southern Luzon,except those engaged in relief, and rescue operations, like the Armed Forces of the Philippines, the Social Welfare Administration, the Department of Health and the Department of Public Works, are excused from office on September 2 and 3, 1970.

It is hereby provided that daily paid and emergency casual employees outside the exceptions shall not suffer a reduction in pay for their failure to report for duty on the said dates.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, September 11, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
