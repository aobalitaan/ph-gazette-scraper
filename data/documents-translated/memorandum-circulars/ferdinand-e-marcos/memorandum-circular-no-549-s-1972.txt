TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 549

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 539 DATED FEBRUARY 4 1972, AS AMENDED BY MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 547 DATED MARCH 1, 1972, ENTITLED “ENJOINING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINAR TO BE CONDUCTED BY THE COMMUNITY CHESTS AND COUNCILS OF THE PHILIPPINES.”

Memorandum Circular No. 539 dated February 4, 1972, as amended by Memorandum Circular No. 547 dated March 1, 1972, entitled “Enjoining Attendance in the Seminar to be conducted by the Community Chests and Councils of the Philippines,” is hereby further amended so as to include a representative each of the Provincial Governors and City Mayors concerned as among those authorized to attend the seminar.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, March 8, 1972

Source : Presidential Management Staff
