OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 476

PROVIDING INFORMATION CONCERNING THE TERRITORY COMPRISING THE CITY OF GREATER MANILA AREA.

The President of the Philippines has issued Proclamation No. 869 dated June 19, 1971, declaring Thursday, June 24, 1971, as a Special Public Holiday in the Greater Manila area to afford the residents of the City the opportunity to properly and fully celebrate the 400th anniversary of the formal organization of the City of Manila as Christian capital city.

For the information and guidance of all concerned in the implementation of the said Proclamation, the City of Greater Manila, as defined in Executive Order No. 400 dated January 1, 1942, includes the City of Manila, Caloocan City, Quezon City and Pasay City, and the municipalities of Malabon, Navotas, San Juan, Mandaluyong, Parañaque and Makati.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, June 19, 1971

Source: Presidential Management Staff
