TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 909

EXCUSING FROM OFFICE GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL IN METROPOLITAN MANILA, QUEZON PROVINCE AND BICOL REGION ON MAY 20 AND 21, 1976 DUE TO TYPHOON “DIDANG.”

The announcement issued by this Office through the radio, television and telephone, excusing from work officials and employees, whether regular, casual or contractual, of government offices within the Metropolitan Manila, Quezon Province, the Bicol Region and other areas affected by typhoon “Didang”, except those engaged in relief, rehabilitation and rescue operations, as well as communications and public utility services including the necessary personnel of the “PAGASA”, from May 20 to 21, 1976, is hereby confirmed.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, May 21, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
