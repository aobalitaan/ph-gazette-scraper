TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1184

URGING GOVERNMENT AGENCIES CONCERNED TO GIVE MAXIMUM SUPPORT AND ASSISTANCE TO THE NATIONAL BOARD FOR TEACHERS IN CONNECTION WITH CONDUCT OF THE THIRD PROFESSIONAL BOARD EXAMINATION FOR TEACHERS

WHEREAS, the National Board for Teachers, through the Ministry of Education and Culture (MEC) and the Civil Service Commission (CSC), is charged with the duty of administering the Third Professional Board Examination for Teachers required of all persons who shall engage in teaching, whether in the public or private elementary or secondary schools to pass the said examination, as provided under Presidential Decree No. 1006 dated September 22, 1976;

WHEREAS, the Third Professional Board Examination for Teachers is scheduled to be given on May 25, 1980 in at least 25 examination centers throughout the country to about 110,000 examinees;

WHEREFORE, the Ministry of National Defense (MND), the Ministry of Finance (MOF), the Armed, Forces of the Philippines (AFP), the National Intelligence and Security Authority (NISA), the Integrated National Police (INP), the Ministry of Transportation and Communication the Philippine National Railways, and all offices under those agencies, are hereby tasked to extend maximum support and assistance to the MEC and CSC to ensure full security, availability of air, sea, and land transportation of examination materials, personnel, and smooth administration of the Third Professional Board Examination for Teachers all over the country.

The Ministry of Public Information (MPI), the National Media Production Center (MPC), and the National Computer Center (NCC) are likewise enjoined to extend maximum support and assistance relative to the dissemination of essential information on the holding of the examination, the utilization of communication facilities throughout the country, and the computer processing of the test results, respectively.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

March 6, 1980

Source: Presidential Management Staff
