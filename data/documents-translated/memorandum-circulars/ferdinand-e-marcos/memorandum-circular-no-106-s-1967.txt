OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 106

URGING PARTICIPATION IN THE TRAINING PROGRAM IN DEVELOPMENT ECONOMICS CONDUCTED BY THE UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES

The School of Economies of the University of the Philippines, with the assistance of the University of Wisconsin will conduct its Third Training Program in Development Economics from July 1967 to April 1968.

This Training Program is designed specifically to improve the skills of persons engaged in substantive planning work along the specialized techniques and procedures of planning and programming for economic development.

Offices and agencies of the government involved in planning and programming, including government-owned and/or controlled corporations, regional planning authorities an entities with an overall planning and coordinating perspective and local government, are hereby urged to send their technical personnel who meet the qualification requirements to attend the Training Program. Participation in this Program may be authorized by the Department Head concerned to be on official time. Transportation and seal allowances will be provided each participant by the U.P.School of Economics.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads monthly report on their attendance and participation in the training program.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, March 29, 1967
