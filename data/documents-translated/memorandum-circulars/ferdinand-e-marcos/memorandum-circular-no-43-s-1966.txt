OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 43

REQUIRING ALL DEPARTMENTS, BUREAUS, AND OFFICES OF THE NATIONAL GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AND OFFICES OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENTS TO SUBMIT, IN DUPLICATE, ALL REPLIES TO MEMORANDA, ORDERS OR LETTERS SIGNED PERSONALLY BY THE PRESIDENT.

All agencies herein named are hereby re-quested, whenever they receive any memorandum, order or letter signed personally by the President, to submit their replies thereto in duplicate.

One copy of the reply shall be coursed through the Office of the Executive Secretary for proper evaluation and/or action. The other copy shall be sent directly to the President.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, May 19, 1966

Source : Presidential Management Staff
