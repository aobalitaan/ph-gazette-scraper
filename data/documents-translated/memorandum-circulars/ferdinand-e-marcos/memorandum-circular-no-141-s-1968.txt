OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 141

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE FOURTH ANNIVERSARY CELEBRATION OF THE GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES WORLD WAR VETERANS LEAGUE, INC.

The Government Employees World War Veterans League, Inc., will celebrate its fourth anniversary on January 8, 1968, in Quezon City. The occasion will be highlighted by a Thanksgiving Mass at the “Libingan Ng Mga Bayani.”

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to allow their employees who are members of the League to attend the celebration on official time.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, January 2, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
