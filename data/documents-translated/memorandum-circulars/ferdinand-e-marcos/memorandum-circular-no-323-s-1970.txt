OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 323

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 322, DATED FEBRUARY 26, 1970, “CREATING AN INTER-DEPARTMENTAL TASK FORCE FOR THE REPARATION OF THE FOUR-YEAR DEVELOPMENT PLAN.”

In addition to the committee constituting the Inter-departmental Task Force created under Memorandum Circular No.322, dated February 26, 1970, there is hereby constituted a Committee on Monetary Policy and Balance of Payments which shall be composed of the following:

The Committee on Other Social Services shall have as member Mrs. Leonora de Guzman of the National Economic Council.

This Circular shall take effect today.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, February 27, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
