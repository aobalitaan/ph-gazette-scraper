TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 958

RESETTING THE SEMINAR SCHEDULE OF THE PERSONNEL OFFICERS ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES (POAP) FOR THE AREAS COVERED BY ADMINISTRATIVE REGION NO. 9

In view of the plebiscite called in 13 southern provinces on February 21, 1977, the seminar on “The New Orientation” to cover Administrative Region No 9 in Zamboanga City sponsored by the Personnel Officers Association of the Philippines (POAP) with the cooperation and technical assistance of the Civil Service Commission is hereby reset from February 21-25, 1977 to February 28-March 4, 1977 to afford participants who are residents of the region to cast their votes.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, February 16, 1977

Source: Presidential Management Staff
