TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 883

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINAR-WORKSHOP ON DEVELOPMENT AND COORDINATION OF THE NATIONAL HOUSING AUTHORITY, TONDO FORESHORE LAND-DAGAT-DAGATAN URBAN DEVELOPMENT PROJECT.

The National Housing Authority, Tondo Foreshore-Dagat-Dagatan Project will sponsor a seminar-workshop on “Development and Coordination” on December 10-12, 1975, at the Sierra Grande Resort, Tagaytay City.

The objective of the seminar aims to coordinate the agencies concerned to avert mismanagement, confusion and needless delay in the implementation of the project.

The agencies listed hereunder are hereby directed to send one (1) representative each (regional director/officer in-charge or their assistants) to the seminar-workshop on official time with reasonable expenses for transportation and per diems, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344 dated November 26, 1973, as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127 dated November 27, 1973, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 193 of this Office dated August 8, 1972.

Department of Social Welfare, Region IV Department of Public Information, Region IV Department of Local Government and Community Development, Region IV Department of Health, Region IV Manila Health Department, 1st District Bureau of Public Works National Media Production Center Planning and Project development Office, Department of Public Works, Transportation and Communication Department of Labor, Region IV Manila Action Center City Engineers Office, Manila Nutrition Center of the Philippines Philippine Coast Guard Tondo General Hospital Bureau of Customs Metropolitan Waterworks and Sewerage System Western Police District, Metropolitan Police Force Ports Authority Manila Electric Company Bureau of Coast and Geodetic Survey Project Tulungan Department of Public Highways National Food and Agricultural Council Population Commission Department of Agrarian Reform Office of the Mayor, City of Manila Office of the Mayor, City of Caloocan Office of the Mayor, Municipality of Malabon Office of the Mayor, Municipality of Navotas Tondo Inter-Agency Action Center Department of Public Services, Manila Don Bosco Youth Center, Tondo Department of Education and Culture, Division of City Schools National Economic Development Authority Bureau of Lands

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance in the seminar-worksop within thirty (30) days after the termination thereof.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, December 10, 1975
