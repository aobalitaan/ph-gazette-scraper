OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 50

AUTHORIZING THE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION TO HOLD REGIONAL SEMINARS ON PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION.

In order to effectively carry out the provisions of Section 25 of Republic Act No. 2260, the Civil Service Commission, through its Regional Offices and with the cooperation of the Personnel Officers Association of the Philippines, Inc., hereby authorized to conduct regional seminars on public administration as hereunder indicated:

Each seminar will be geared to the theme:”Developing Executive Skills for Dynamic Management,” and will feature, among other things, discussions on current problems of the executive and the difficulties that he must meet in the faces of changes that are now taking place in the reshaping of the national economy.

All departments, bureaus and offices of the national government, including government-owned or controlled corporations, with branches or units in the regions affected, are hereby authorized to send not more than two (2) participants from such branches or units to the seminar to be held in their respective regions. Provincial, city and municipal governments are likewise authorized to send not more than two participants each to the corresponding regional seminar. Their attendance shall be on official time with expenses for transportation, lodging, subsistence, and seminar fees, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency needs a report of their attendance in the seminar.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, June 15, 1966

Source : Presidential Management Staff
