TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 824

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE 7TH NATIONAL MARKETING CONFERENCE.

The Philippine Marketing Association will sponsor the 7th National Marketing Conference on May 28-30, 1975 at the Hyatt Regency-Manila, on the theme, “Marketing in Action: Turning Problems of the 70’s into Opportunities for the 80’s.”

Marketing experts, practitioners and educators; leaders of business in practically all industrial sectors will participate as speakers, discussants and delegates. The Conference will focus on the urgency of marketing innovations in the light of drastic and rapid changes in the economic, social and political environment, particularly in the Philippines and all over the world, in general.

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned of controlled corporations are hereby authorized to send their selected participants to the said Conference on official time only.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, April 23, 1975

Source: Presidential Management Staff
