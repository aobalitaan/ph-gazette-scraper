Office of the President of the Philippines

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 111

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE SECOND NATIONAL CONFERENCE OF THE PHILIPPINE FOA FELLOWS ASSOCIATION.

The Philippine FOA Fellows Association, with the technical assistance of the National Economic Council, the Civil Service Commission, and the Agency for International Development, USOM/Manila, will hold its Second National Conference at the Fiesta Pavilion, Manila Hotel, on August 9-11, 1967, on the theme: “Effective Public Administration: Imperative to National Progress.”

The conference aims to encourage a more active participation of recipients of training grants under the NEC/AID technical assistance program in the implementation of government programs for social and economic advancement. The conference theme is significant as it will focus attention on the need for an efficient administrative machinery as an effective arm in carrying out such programs.

National and local offices including government-owned or controlled corporations are hereby authorized to send their officials and employees who have undergone training abroad under NEC/AID technical assistance program to attend this conference. Their attendance shall be on official time and their expenses for transportation, subsistence, lodging and conference fees in connection therewith shall be chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements.

Delegates shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance and participation in the conference.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, April 7, 1967
