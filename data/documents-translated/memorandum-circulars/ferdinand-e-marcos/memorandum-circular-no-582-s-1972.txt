TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS

(OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 582

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 575 DATED MAY 29, 1972, ENTITLED “AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE 2ND NATIONAL CONFERENCE OF THE ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICERS LEAGUE OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC. (AOLP).”

Memorandum Circular No. 575 dated May 29, 1972, authorizing attendance in the 2 nd National Conference of the Administrative Officers League of the Philippines, Inc. (AOLP), is hereby amended so as to authorize the said organization to hold the conference at Legazpi City on July 19-21, 1972, instead of June 14-16, 1972 as originally scheduled.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, June 17, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
