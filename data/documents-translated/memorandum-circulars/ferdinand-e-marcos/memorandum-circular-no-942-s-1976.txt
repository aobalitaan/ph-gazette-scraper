Tanggapan ng Pangulo ng Pilipinas (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 942

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 936, ENTITLED “AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE SEMINAR ON LOSS CONTROL MANAGEMENT SPONSORED BY THE WORLD SAFETY ORGANIZATION.”

Memorandum Circular No. 936 of this Office dated September 27, 1976, authorizing attendance in the seminar on Loss Control Management sponsored by the World Safety Organization to be held on September 27-30, 1976, at the Development Academy of the Philippines (DAP), Tagaytay City, is hereby amended 80 as to reschedule the seminar on February 22-25, 1977.

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, November 2, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
