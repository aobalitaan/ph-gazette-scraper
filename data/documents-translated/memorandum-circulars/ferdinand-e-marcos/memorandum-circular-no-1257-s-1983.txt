Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1257

URGING GOVERNMENT AGENCIES CONCERNED TO GIVE MAXIMUM SUPPORT AND ASSISTANCE TO THE CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION IN THE CONDUCT OF THE 1983 CAREER SERVICE (PROFESSIONAL AND SUBPROFESSIONAL LEVELS) EXAMINATIONS

WHEREAS, the Civil Service Commission is charged with the duty of administering the Career Service (Professional and Subprofessional levels) examinations;

WHEREAS, the Career Service (Professional and Subprofessional levels) examinations are scheduled on July 17, 1983 in at least 51 examination centers throughout the country;

WHEREFORE, the Central Bank is commissioned to print security forms for the examinations;

The Ministry of National Defense, through the Office of the Deputy Minister for Civilian Relations, the Philippine Constabulary, the Philippine Air Force, the Philippine Navy, the Integrated National Police, the National Intelligence and Security Authority, and the Ministry of Education, Culture and Sports are requested to extend maximum support and assistance to the Civil Service Commission to ensure full security and smooth administration of the 1983 Career Service (Professional and Subprofessional levels) examinations;

The Office of Media Affairs, the National Media Production Center, the Philippine Airlines, the Ministry of Transportation and Communications, the Ministry of Public Works and Highways, the Philippine National Railways, and the Electronic Data Processing Systems Office of the Philippine Sugar Commission are likewise enjoined to extend maximum support and assistance relative to the dissemination of essential information on theholding of the examinations, the utilization of communication facilities throughout the country the transport of examination materials and personnel, and the computer processing of the results, respectively;

The Deputy Minister of National Defense, the Commanding General of the Aviation Security Command, and the Commissioner of Customs are requested to exempt from inspection all classified materials pertinent to the Career Service(Professional and Subprofessional levels) examinations;

All heads of government agencies whose representatives may be designated by the Chairman/Commissioners or the Regional Directors of the Civil Service Commission,to compose the Regional Examination committees and the Local Examination Committees to be established in provincial/city capitals where the examination; will be held, are requested to extend maximum support and assistance in all matters relative to the conduct of the examinations.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila,March 16, 1983

Source: Presidential Management Staff
