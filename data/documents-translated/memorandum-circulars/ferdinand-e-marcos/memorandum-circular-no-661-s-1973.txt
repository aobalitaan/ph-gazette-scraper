TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 661

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE LIBRARY ASSOCIATION.

The Philippine Library Association will hold a convention in commemoration of its Golden Jubilee Anniversary in Manila October 25-29, 1973, on the theme “Libraries and Infrastructure Development”

All government librarians from national and local offices, as well as government-owned or controlled corporations are hereby authorized to attend the convention on official time with reimbursement for convention fee only of not higher than ₱30.00 as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593, series of 1972.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance in the convention within thirty (30) days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, September 3, 1973

Source: Presidential Management Staff
