TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 581

CREATING A COMMITTEE TO CODIFY ALL EXECUTIVE ORDERS, MEMORANDUM CIRCULARS, MEMORANDUM ORDERS AND OTHER ISSUANCES PERTAINING TO PROCUREMENT OF SUPPLIES, MATERIALS AND EQUIPMENT AND NON-PERSONAL SERVICES.

There is hereby created a Committee which shall undertake the codification of all Executive Orders, Memorandum Circulars, Memorandum Orders and other issuances pertaining to procurement of supplies, materials and equipment and non-personal services.

It shall be composed of the following:

The Committee is hereby empowered to create a sub-committee to assist it in its work and to call upon any department, bureau or office concerned for such assistance as it may need in the accomplishment of its mission. The Committee shall accomplish its work within a period of sixty (60) days from the date hereof.

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, June 15, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
