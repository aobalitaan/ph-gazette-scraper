TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 551

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE SIXTH ANNUAL CONVENTION OF THE ASSOCIATION OF GOVERNMENT MECHANICAL AND ELECTRICAL ENGINEERS OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC.

The Association of Government Mechanical and Electrical Engineers of the Philippines, Inc. will held its 6th Annual Convention on May 11-12, 1972, at the Dr. Paulino Garcia Hall, NSDB Building, Herran Street, Manila.

All heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations, are hereby authorized to sent their mechanical and electrical engineers to the convention on official business, that is, on official time and with expenses for transportation, subsistence and lodging, as well as conference fees, all chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements. It is understood that the delegates stationed in the Greater Manila Area shall be entitled to expenses for transportation and conference fees only.

The delegates shall submit to their agency heads a report on their attendance in the convention within thirty (30) days after termination thereof.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, March, 9, 1972

Source : Presidential Management Staff
