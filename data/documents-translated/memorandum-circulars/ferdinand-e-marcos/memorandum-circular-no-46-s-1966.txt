OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 46

FURTHER AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 131 DATED JULY 9, 1965, AS AMENDED BY MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 134 DATED JULY 30, 1965, URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE INSTITUTES ON HOW TO USE SIMPLE TECHNICS AND TOOLS IN ASSESSING AND UNDERSTANDING BEHAVIORAL CHANGES IN CHILDREN.

Memorandum Circular No. 131 dated July 9, 1965 as amended by Memorandum Circular No. 134 dated July 30, 1965, urging attendance in the Institutes on How To Use Simple Technics and Tools in Assessing and Understanding Behavioral Changes in Children, is hereby further amended so as to authorize the National coordinating Center for the Study and Development of Filipino Children and Youth to conduct regularly two such institutes every year at four months duration each, subject to the applicable terms and conditions set forth in the aforesaid Memorandum Circular No. 131.

The Director of the Center shall submit to this Office a report on each Institution within 30 days after the termination thereof.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, May 31, 1966

Source : Presidential Management Staff
