OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 47

ENJOINING ALL OFFICES AND AGENCIES UNDER THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT TO GIVE FULL SUPPORT TO THE EDUCATIONAL, MEMBERSHIP AND FUND CAMPAIGN OF THE CHILDREN’S MUSEUM AND LIBRARY, INC.

Under Proclamation No. 30 dated April 28, 1966, of the President, the Children’s Museum and Library, Inc. (CMLI), is authorized to conduct an educational, membership and fund campaign from May 1 to June 15, 1966.

The campaign is designed to assist the CMLI in its projects for the promotion of the well-being of children, the development of their latent capabilities and the provision of proper facilities necessary for their growth as good and useful citizens.

In order to help the CMLI attain its objectives, all offices and agencies under the Office of the President are hereby enjoined to give full support to the campaign. To this end, it is earnestly expected that offices or agencies concerned shall exert every effort towards the subscription of their assigned quotas and shall extend the usual courtesies to authorized representatives of the CMLI who will call on them for the purpose.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, June 6, 1966

Source : Presidential Management Staff
