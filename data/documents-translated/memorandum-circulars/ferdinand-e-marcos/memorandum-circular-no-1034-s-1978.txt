TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1034

ESTABLISHING A FUTURE LEADERS INTERNSHIP PROGRAM TO IMPLEMENT THE PROVISIONS OF LETTER OF INSTRUCTIONS NO. 628.

Letter of Instructions No. 628 issued by the President on November 18, 1977 provides that the upper 50% of the graduates of the First Future Leaders Program will be assigned to Career Executive Service Officers at both national and regional levels as part of their continuing training for leadership.

To implement this requirement, the Future Leaders Internship Program herewith attached is instituted. All heads of departments, bureaus, and agencies including; local governments and government-owned and controlled corporations are hereby urged to assist and support this program.

This Program shall embrace future graduates of the continuing series of the Future Leaders Program sponsored by the Civil Service Commission to provide incentive to the young people in the government to develop careers and realize their potential for leadership.

This Memorandum-Circular shall take effect immediately.

By Authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

January 17, 1978

Source: Presidential Management Staff
