TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1006

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1005 REQUIRING ALL HEADS OF DEPARTMENTS, BUREAUS, AGENCIES OF THE GOVERNMENT, INCLUDING GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS, AND LOCAL GOVERNMENTS TO MAKE AN INVENTORY OF THEIR RESPECTIVE PERSONNEL TO DETERMINE WHO AMONG THEM BE ASSIGNED FOR EXTENSION WORK.

Memorandum Circular No. 1005 is hereby amended by adding a new paragraph after the penultimate paragraph thereof to read as follows:

“The Office of the President and the Civil Service Commission shall coordinate in collating the inventory of officials and employees required in Letter of Instructions No. 599.”

This circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, September 26, 1977

Source: Presidential Management Staff
