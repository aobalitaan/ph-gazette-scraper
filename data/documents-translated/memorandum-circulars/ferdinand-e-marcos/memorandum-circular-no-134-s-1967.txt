OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 134

URGING ATTENDANCE IN THE 20TH ANNUAL CONVENTION AND SYMPOSIUM OF THE PHILIPPINE GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES’ ASSOCIATION.

In conjunction with the observance of Government Employees Week, the Philippine Government Employees’ Association (PGEA) will hold its 20th Annual Convention on December 6-8, 1967, in Manila, on the theme: “A PROGRESSIVE NATIONAL ECONOMY WITH A A SOUND WAGE STRUCTURE IS THE FOUNTAINHEAD OF A DYNAMIC AND EFFICIENT CIVIL SERVICE.”

Departments, bureaus and offices, as well as city, provincial and municipal government, and other government agencies which have PGEA Chapters, are hereby authorized to send not more than five members from each chapter to the convention. Their attendance shall be on official time only.

The delegates shall submit to their respective agency heads a report of their attendance and participation in the convention.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, October 24, 1967

Source: Presidential Management Staff
