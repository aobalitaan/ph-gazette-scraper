TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 579

AUTHORIZING CHIEFS OF GOVERNMENT OFFICES IN THE GREATER MANILA AREA TO EXCUSE THEIR PERSONNEL TODAY, JUNE 7, 1972, BEGINNING 9:30 A.M.

In view of the inclement weather characterized by heavy rains and flood, chiefs of government offices in the Greater Manila Area are hereby given discretion to excuse the employees under them today beginning 9:30 A.M., provided that a skeleton force is required to stay in office to maintain essential services.

This Circular shall not apply to those engaged in relief and rescue operations as well as communications and public utility services and necessary personnel of the Weather Bureau.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, June 7, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
