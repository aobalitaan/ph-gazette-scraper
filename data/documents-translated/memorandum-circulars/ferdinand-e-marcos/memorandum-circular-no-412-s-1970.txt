OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 412

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR 409, DATED NOVEMBER 20, 1970, BY ADDING GOVERNMENT CORPORATIONS WITH EMPLOYEES COVERED BY COLLECTIVE BARGAINING AGREEMENTS TO THOSE OFFICES NOT AFFECTED BY THE CHANGE IN WORKING HOURS.

Memorandum Circular No. 409 dated November 20, 1970, is hereby amended by adding to those offices not affected by the change in working hours all government corporations whose employees are covered by collective bargaining agreements. Such corporations are therefore directed to maintain regular working hours of 8:00 A. M. to 5:00 P. M. from Monday to Friday.

By authority of the President

(SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, November 25, 1970

Source: Presidential Management Staff
