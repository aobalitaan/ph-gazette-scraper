Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1236

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1229 DATED JANUARY 4, 1982 CREATING AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE PREPARATION AND ARRANGEMENTS FOR THE HOLDING OF THE 15TH ANNUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS, ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK.

Memorandum Circular No. 1229 of this Office dated January 4, 1982 creating an Executive Committee to take charge of the preparation and arrangement for the holding of the 15th annual meeting of the Board of Governors, Asian Development Bank, to be held in Manila on April 28-30. 1982, is hereby amended to include the representative of the Prime Minister as member of the Committee.

This Circular takes effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, March 4, 1982

Source: Presidential Management Staff
