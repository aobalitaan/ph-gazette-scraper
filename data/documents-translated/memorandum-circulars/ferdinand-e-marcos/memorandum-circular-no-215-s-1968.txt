OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 215

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE OF CERTAIN GOVERNMENT OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES IN THE SYMPOSIUM TO BE CONDUCTED BY THE COMMISSION ON NATIONAL INTEGRATION.

The Commission on National Integration will hold a three—day symposium on December 20-22, 1968, at the Asian Labor Educational Center, University of the Philippines, on the those: “National Integration for a Meaningful Filipinism.”

In view of the significance of this activity, heads of departments and chiefs of bureaus and offices which perform work concerning the minorities or which could contribute to the government’ s integration program are hereby authorized to send to the symposium not more than throe (3) delegates each on official time.

Those authorized to attend shall submit to their respective agency heads a report on their attendance and participation in the symposium.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) RAFAEL M. SALAS Executive Secretary

Manila, November 21, 1968

Source : Presidential Management Staff
