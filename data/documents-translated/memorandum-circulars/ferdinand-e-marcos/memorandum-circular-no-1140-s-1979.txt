TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1140

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE CONFERENCE OF THE PHILIPPINE MEDICAL WOMEN’S ASSOCIATION, INC.

The Philippine Medical Women’s Association, Inc. will hold its conference in Manila on May 4-5, 1979, on the theme: “EFFECTIVE CHILD CARE OUTREACH” in connection with their 30th Anniversary Celebration.

Heads of Ministries, chiefs of bureaus and offices, including government-owned or controlled corporations within the Metropolitan Manila Area are hereby authorized to send their women physicians, who are members of the Association, to the conference on official time only without expenditure of government funds of whatever nature in connection therewith.

The participants shall submit to their respective office heads a complete report on their attendance or participation in the conference as proscribed under Memorandum Circular No. 987 dated June 10, 1977.

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, May 4, 1979

Source: Presidential Management Staff
