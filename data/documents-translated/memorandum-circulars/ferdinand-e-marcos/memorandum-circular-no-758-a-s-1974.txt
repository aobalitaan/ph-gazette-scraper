TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 758-A

EXCUSING FROM OFFICE GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL IN THE GREATER MANILA AREA AT 4:00 P.M., AUGUST 16, 1974.

Due to inclement weather characterized by continuously rising floodwaters, chiefs of government offices within the Greater Manila Area are hereby given the discretion to excuse from work their respective employees at 4:00 o’clock this afternoon.

This Circular shall not apply to employees engaged in relief and rescue operations, as well as communications, public utility services and weather forecasting.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) ROBERTO V. REYES Assistant Executive Secretary

Manila, August 16, 1974

Source: Presidential Management Staff
