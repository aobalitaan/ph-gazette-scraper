TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1083

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE FOURTH NATIONAL CONFERENCE OF THE NATIONAL ASSOCIATION FOR FILIPINO PSYCHOLOGY.

The National Association for Filipino Psychology will hold its 4th National Conference at the University of the Philippines, Los Baños, Laguna, on October 25-28, 1978, on the theme: “ANG KABATAAN PILIPINO AT ANG IKATLONG DAIGDIG.”

The conference aims to discuss and analysis the problems of the Filipino youth in relation to the state of the third world societies.

Heads of ministries, bureaus and offices, are hereby authorized to send nor more two (2) participants each, who are members of the Association or those directly involved in the work of Filipinos psychology, to the conference on official time with reasonable expenses for transportation, per diems and a conference fee of ₱300.00 per participants chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344 dated November 26, 1973, as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127, series of 1973, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 of this Office dated August 8, 1972. Participants residing or stationed within the site of the conference shall be entitled to conference fees only. Meals covered by the conference fee shall be deducted from per diems.

Each participant shall submit to his agency head a complete report on his attendance or participation in the conferences as prescribed under Memorandum Circular No. 987 dated June 10, 1977. Likewise, the Association shall submit to this Office a report and evaluation of the conference, a copy furnished the Ministry of the Budget their respective offices.

JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, September 29, 1978

Source: House of Representatives, Legislative Library
