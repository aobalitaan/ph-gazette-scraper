OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 181

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR 177 DATED APRIL 17, 1968 SO AS TO CHANGE THE DATE OF THE HOLDING OF THE ANNUAL NATIONAL CONFERENCE OF THE GOVERNMENT PROSECUTORS’ LEAGUE OF THE PHILIPPINES, INC.

In view of unforseen circumstances, the Government Prosecutors’ League of the Philippines, Inc., has not been able to make final preparations for the holding of its annual national conference on April 24-28, 1968 as originally scheduled and as indicated in Memorandum Circular No. 177 of this Office dated April 17, 1968. The said Circular is hereby amended so as to indicate that the annual conference will be held on May 6-9, 1968, in Manila, instead of on April 24-28, 1968 in Quezon City.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, April 26, 1968

Source: Presidential Management Staff
