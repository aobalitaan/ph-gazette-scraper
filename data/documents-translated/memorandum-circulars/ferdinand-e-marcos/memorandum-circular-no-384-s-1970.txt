MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 384

DESIGNATION OF PERSONNEL TO OVERSEE ALL COMMUNICATIONS IN THE FILIPINO LANGUAGE IN ALL DEPARTMENTS, BUREAUS, OFFICES AND OTHER BRANCHES OF THE GOVERNMENT AND GOVERNMENT-OWNED OR CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS

Executive Order No. 187 dated August 6, 1969 aims to further promote the dissemination of the national language by using the Filipino language as much as possible in all government communications and transactions. In accordance with the provisions of said executive order, all departments, bureaus, offices, and other branches of the government, and corporations owned or controlled by the government are hereby ordered to designate the appropriate personnel to oversee all communications and transactions in the Filipino language.

This circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, August 17, 1970
