TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 573

CONSTITUTING THE TECHNICAL PANEL TO UNDERTAKE THE FEASIBILITY STUDY OF A LARGE SALT-MAKING PLANT IN TIWI, ALBAY UTILIZING GEOTHERMAL ENERGY.

The following are hereby designated to form a technical panel to undertake a feasibility study of a large salt-making plant in Tiwi, Albay that will make use of geothermal energy:

The panel is hereby instructed to meet as soon as possible and to render its findings to the National Power Corporation Board for evaluation, authorization and financing.

By authority of the President: (SGD.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Manila, May 18, 1972

Source: Presidential Management Staff
