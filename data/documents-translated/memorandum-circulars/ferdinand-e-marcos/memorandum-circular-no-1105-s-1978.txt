TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1105

EXCUSING FROM OFFICE GOVERNMENT PERSONNEL IN SPECIFIED AREAS IN THE COUNTRY AFFECTED BY TYPHOONS “WELING”, “YANING”, AND “KADING”.

The announcements issued by this Office, through radio, television and telephone, excusing from work officials and employees, whether regular, casual or contractual, of government offices within the typhoon-affected areas are hereby confirmed on the dates and the hours indicated below:

Southern Luzon including Metropolitan Manila

Not covered by this circular are employees engaged in relief, rehabilitation and rescue operations, as well as communications and public utility services including the necessary personnel of “PAGASA”. The discretion to excuse employees of offices outside of the places specified above not directly affected by the aforecited typhoons but which also experienced inclement weather and floods is left to their respective heads

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, December 4, 1978

Source: Presidential Management Staff
