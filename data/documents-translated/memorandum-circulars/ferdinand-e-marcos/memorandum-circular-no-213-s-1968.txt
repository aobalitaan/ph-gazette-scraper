OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 213

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 209 OF THIS OFFICE DATED OCTOBER 16, 1968, URGING ATTENDEE IN THE CONVENTION-SEMINAR OF THE ASSOCIATION OF GOVERNMENT INTERNAL AUDITORS (AGIA).

Memorandum Circular No. 209 of this Office dated October 16, 1968, urging attendance in the convention-seminar of the Association of Government Internal Auditors (AGIA) to be hold in Baguio City, is hereby amended so as to indicate therein the change of the date of the holding thereof from November 20-22, 1968, to November 18-20, 1968.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JOSE J. LEIDO, JR. Acting Executive Secretary

Manila, November 5, 1968

Source : Presidential Management Staff
