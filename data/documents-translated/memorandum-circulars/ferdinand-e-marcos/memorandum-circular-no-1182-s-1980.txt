TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1182

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1180 DATED JANUARY 16, 1980 CREATING AN EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE PREPARATION AND ARRANGEMENT FOR THE HOLDING OF THE 13TH ANNUAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF GOVERNORS, ASIAN DEVELOPMENT BANK, TO BE HELD IN MANILA FROM APRIL 30, TO MAY 2, 1980.

Amending memorandum circular no. 1180 dated January 16, 1980 creating an executive committee to take charge of the preparation and arrangement for the holding of the 13th annual meeting of the board of governors, Asian development bank, to be held in manila from April 30, to May 2, 1980, is hereby amended to include the Director, Bureau of Quarantine, as Member of the Executive Committee.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JUAN C. TUVERA Presidential Assistant

Manila, February 6, 1980

Source: Presidential Management Staff
