TANGGAPAN NG PANGULO NG PILIPINAS (OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES)

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 923

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE IN THE NATIONAL CONGRESS SPONSORED BY THE COUNCIL OF TECHNOLOGY AND ALLIED SCIENCES OF THE PHILIPPINES (COTASP) ON JULY 30 TO AUGUST 1, 1976.

The Council of technology and Allied Sciences of the Philippines (COTASP) will hold a National Congress on July 30-31 and August 1, 19761 on the theme, “SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY IN THE PHILIPPINES TODAY.”

In view of the relevance of the theme of the Congress to our present development efforts especially in the field of science and technology, the following government agencies are hereby authorized to send not more than two (2) representatives to the Congress on official time with expenses for transportation, per diems and convention fees, chargeable against the appropriations of their respective offices, in accordance with the provisions of Presidential Decree No. 344 dated November 26, 1973 as implemented by COA General Circular No. 127 dated November 27, 1973, subject to the availability of funds and the usual accounting and auditing requirements, as an exception to Memorandum Circular No. 593 of this Office dated August 8, 1972:

1. Department of Public Works, Transportation & Communication; 2. Department of Education & Culture 3. Department of Local Government & Community Development 4. Bureau of Public Works 5. Bureau of Mines 6. Government Service Insurance System 7. Social Security System – Housing and Real Estate 8. Development Bank of the Philippines 9. National Housing Authority 10. National Power Corporation 11. National Economic Development Authority 12. National Science Development Board 13. National Pollution Control Commission 14. University of the Philippines – Diliman 15. University of the Philippines – Los Baños 16. Maritime Industry Administration 17. Metropolitan Waterworks Si Sewerage System 18. City Engineer’s Office, Manila 19. City Engineer’s Office, Quezon City 20. City Engineer’s Office, Coloocan City 21. City Engineer’s Office, Pasay City 22. Radio Control Board 23.Human Settlement Commission

This Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

(SGD.) JACOBO C. CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Manila, July 27, 1976

Source: Presidential Management Staff
