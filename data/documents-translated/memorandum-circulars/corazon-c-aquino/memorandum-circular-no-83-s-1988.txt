MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 83

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 62 CREATING AN AD-HOC PRESIDENTIAL SCREENING PANEL TO ASSIST IN THE SELECTION OF THE MEMBERS OF THE CORDILLERA REGIONAL CONSULTATIVE COMMISSION.

Memorandum Circular No. 62 creating an ad-hoc Presidential Screening Panel to Assist in the Selection of the Members of the Cordillera Regional Consultative Commission, is hereby amended to include Atty. Myra Carmelita Cruz and Mr. Clemente Cancio as Members of the said Committee.

This Memorandum Circular takes effect as of May 20, 1988.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, October 17, 1988

Source: Malacañang Records Office
