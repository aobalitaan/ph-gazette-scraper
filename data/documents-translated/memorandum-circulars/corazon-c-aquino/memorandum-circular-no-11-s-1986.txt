MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 11

CREATING A NATIONAL COMMITTEE TO TAKE CHARGE OF THE CELEBRATION OF PHILIPPINE INDEPENDENCE DAY ON JUNE 12, 1986.

Pursuant to the powers vested in me by law, I, CORAZON C. AQUINO, President of the Philippines, do hereby create a national committee to take charge of the celebration of Philippine Independence Day on June 12, 1986.

The Committee shall be composed of the following:

The Committee shall meet at the call of the Chairman and for the purpose of discharging its functions, may create its sub-committees as may be necessary.

The Committee is hereby authorized to call upon any department, bureau, office, agency or instrumentality of the government including government-owned or controlled corporation, for such assistance as it may need in the discharge of its duties and functions.

Done in the City of Manila, this 22nd day of May, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and eighty-six.

(Sgd.) CORAZON C. AQUINO President of the Philippines

By the President: (Sgd.) JOKER P. ARROYO Executive Secretary
