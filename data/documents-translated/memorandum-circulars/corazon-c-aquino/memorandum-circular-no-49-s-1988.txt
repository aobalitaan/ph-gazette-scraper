Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 49

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 45, DATED JANUARY 6, 1988.

Memorandum Circular No. 45, dated January 6, 1988, “Creating an Executive Committee to Take Charge of the Preparation and Arrangement for the Holding of the 21st Annual Meeting of the Board of Governors, Asian Development Bank, to be Held in Manila from April 28 to 30, 1988,” is hereby amended so as to include therein the Chief, National Quarantine Office, as member.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary

Manila, January 27, 1988

Source: Malacañang Records Office
