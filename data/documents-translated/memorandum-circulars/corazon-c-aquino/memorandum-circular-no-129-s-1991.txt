Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 129

ESTABLISHING THE PORT INTEGRATED CLEARANCE OFFICE (PICO) IN ALL MAJOR PORTS OF THE COUNTRY

There is hereby established a Port lntegrated Clearance Office (PICO) in all major ports of the country for the purpose of expediting processing of entrance and departure clearances for domestic vessels and cargoes.

The PICO shall involve the following agencies:

Philippine Ports Authority

Bureau of Customs

Bureau of Quarantine

Bureau of Animal Industry

Bureau of PIant Industry

Forest Managerent Bureau

Postal Services Office

National Telecommunications Commission

Philippine Coast Guard

Philippine National Police

The Heads of the aforementioned agencies are hereby directed to designate one (1) permanent and one (1) alternate representative to the PICO ftrom their present personnels. The operationalization of the PICO shall be charged against the respective appropriations for CY 1991 of the said agencies.

The Philippine Ports Authority (PPA) representative to the PICO shall act as officer-in-charge and shall coordinate the day-to-day operations of the PICO. The PPA shall provide the necessary office spaces and staff support for the operations of the PICO.

This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.

By authority of the President: (Sgd.) OSCAR M. ORBOS Executive Secretary

Manila, April 16, 1991
