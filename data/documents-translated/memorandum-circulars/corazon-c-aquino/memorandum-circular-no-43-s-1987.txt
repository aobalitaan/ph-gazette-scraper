Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 43

AMENDING MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 40 DATED NOVEMBER 4, 1987 CREATING AN INTER-AGENCY COMMITTEE ON SUGAR TO COORDINATE PLANNING AND POLICY FORMULATION ON THE SUGAR INDUSTRY.

Memorandum Circular No. 40 dated November 4, 1987 creating an Inter-Agency Committee on Sugar to coordinate planning and policy formulation on the sugar industry is hereby amended to include the representative from the Department of Labor and Employment as Member of the said Committee.

This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.

Manila, December 1, 1987.

By authority of the President:

(Sgd.) CATALINO MACARAIG, JR. Executive Secretary
