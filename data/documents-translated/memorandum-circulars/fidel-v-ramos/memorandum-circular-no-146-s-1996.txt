Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 146

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE BY GOVERNMENT LAWYERS IN THE GENERAL LAW PRACTICE SEMINAR FOR LAWYERS TO BE HELD ON OCTOBER 10 AND 11, 1996, AT THE AMIGO TERRACE HOTEL, ILOILO CITY.

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines, Iloilo Chapter, is co-sponsoring with the U.P. Law Center Institute of Judicial Administration a General Law Practice seminar for lawyers of all IBP Chapters in the provinces of Aklan, Antique, Capiz and Iloilo to be held on October 10 and 11, 1996, at the Amigo Terrace Hotel, Iloilo City.

The participation on official time and business in said seminar of all government lawyers in the above-mentioned provinces under the jurisdiction of the Executive Department is hereby authorized, to enable them to update their knowledge of law and keep them in step with current procedure, provided there will be no disruption of normal office activities/services in their respective offices by reason thereof.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a complete report on their attendance or participation in the seminar. Likewise, the IBP is requested to submit to this Office and the CSC a general report and evaluation of the seminar, including a list by offices of participants therein.

RUBEN D. TORRES Executive Secretary

Manila, August 12, 1996.
