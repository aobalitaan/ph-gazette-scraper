Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 37

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE OF GOVERNMENT LIBRARIANS IN THE SEMINAR-WORKSHOP TO BE CONDUCTED ENTITLED “THE LIBRARY IN THE YEAR 2000-VISION, NEW TRENDS, NEW IMAGE” TO BE HELD AT THE DEVELOPMENT ACADEMY OF THE PHILIPPINES, ON MARCH 18, 1993.

The Association of Special Libraries of the Philippines (ASLP) will conduct a one-day seminar-workshop entitled “The Library in the Year 2000-Vision: New Trends, New Image,” on March 18, 1993, from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. to be held at the DAP, Pasig, Metro Manila.

The participation by government librarians is hereby authorized. Heads of agencies/offices concerned shall determine the number of participants, provided there will be no disruption of normal office services/activities.

The participants shall submit to their respective agency heads a complete report on their attendance or participation in the seminar-workshop. Likewise, ASLP is requested to submit to this Office and the Civil Service Commission a general report and evaluation of the seminar-workshop, including a list by offices of participants from the government.

(Sgd.) EDELMIRO A. AMANTE, SR Executive Secretary

11 MAR 1993
