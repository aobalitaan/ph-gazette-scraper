MALACANANG MANILA

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 27

DESIGNATING THE MEMBERS OF THE BIPARTISAN EXECUTIVE LEGISLATIVE ADVISORY COUNCIL ON THE SABAH ISSUE

WHEREAS, consonant with Senate Resolution No. 13 (1992) and House Resolution No. 19 (1992), Executive Order No. 46 (1993) constituted a Bipartisan Executive-Legislative Advisory Council on the Sabah Issue to serve as an advisory body to the President with respect to the Philippine claim on Sabah;

WHEREAS, the Council shall be composed of eight (8) representatives from the Executive Branch, eight (8) members of the Senate including a member of the minority party and eight (8) members of the House of Representatives including a member of the minority party;

WHEREAS, the President of the Senate and the Speaker of the House have respectively transmitted to this Office the members of the Legislative Branch designated to be members of the Council;

WHEREFORE, the following representatives of the executive and legislative branches of government are hereby designated members of the Council:

From the Executive Branch:

a. Sec. Roberto R. Romulo b. Sec. Renato S. de Villa c. Sec. Franklin M. Drilon d. Sec. Rafael M. Alunan III e. Sec. Rizalino S. Navarro f. Sec. Nieves R. Confesor g. Sec. Angel C. Alcala h. National Security Adviser Jose T. Almonte

From the Legislative Branch:

2.1. Senate Representatives

a. Sen. Teofisto T. Guingona, Jr. b. Sen. Orlando S. Mercado c. Sen. Blas F. Ople d. Sen. Santanina T. Rasul e. Sen. Leticia Ramos-Shahani f. Sen. Francisco S. Tatad g. Sen. Arturo M. Tolentino h. Senate Minority Party Representative

2.2. House Representatives

Speaker Jose C. de Venecia, Jr. (with two alternates: Rep. Simeon Datumanong and Rep. Macabangkit Lanto) j. Rep. Eduardo Ermita k. Rep. Nur Jaafar l. Rep. Jaime Lopez m. Rep. Michael Mastura n. Rep. Juan Ponce Enrile o. Rep. Asani Tammang p. Rep. Bensaudi Tulawie

The Council shall be chaired by Secretary Roberto R. Romulo.

This Memorandum Circular shall take effect immediately.

DONE in the City of Manila, this 18th day of January in the year of Our Lord, Nineteen Hundred and Ninety-Three.

(Sgd.) FIDEL V. RAMOS

By the President: (Sgd.) ANTONIO T. CARPIO Chief Presidential Legal Counsel
