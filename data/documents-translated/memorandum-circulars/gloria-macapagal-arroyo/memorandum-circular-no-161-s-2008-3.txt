MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 161

ENJOINING ALL CABINET MEMBERS AND HEADS OF GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS TO OBSERVE THE INTERNATIONAL HUMANITARIAN LAW DAY ON AUGUST 12, 2008 AND ACTIVELY SUPPORT AND PARTICIPATE IN THE CONDUCT OF ACTIVITIES RELATIVE THERETO

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 134 dated July 31, 1999, declaring August 12 as International Humanitarian Law (IHL) Day, all cabinet members and heads of government-owned and controlled corporations are enjoined and encouraged to support the initiatives lined-up for the observance of the International Humanitarian Day on August 12, 2008. The theme for this year’s celebration is “Together for Humanity, Dignity and Peace”, in line with the Government’s thrust, among others, to sustain institutional and popular support and participation of all sectors of the society in building a culture of peace in the country.

Further, you are also encouraged to provide other support that may be deemed necessary to make the celebration more meaningful.

The Department of National Defense (DND) and the Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA), in cooperation with PNRC and ICRC shall spearhead the implementation of the activities lined-up for the celebration and shall carry out the necessary coordination among the participating government agencies, bureaus and government-owned and controlled corporations.

As the Chairman for this year’s celebration, the Secretary of National Defense shall submit a report to the President, through the Executive Secretary copy furnished the Presidential Chief of Staff, on the conduct of the IHL Day, including but not limited to, the participation of government agencies, bureaus and government-owned and controlled corporations.

SO ORDERED.

Manila, Philippines, August 04, 2008
