MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 1

DIRECTING ALL NON-CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE OFFICERS [CESO] OCCUPYING CAREER EXECUTIVE SERVICE [CES] POSITIONS TO CONTINUE, IN A HOLD-OVER CAPACITY, TO PERFORM THEIR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

Pursuant to existing laws and jurisprudence, all non-Career Executive Service Officers [CESO] occupying career executive service [CES] positions shall continue, in a hold-over capacity, to perform their duties and discharge their responsibilities until their successors have been appointed and have qualified.

All official actions of said non-CES officials while in such hold-over capacity are deemed legitimate.

This directive shall lake effect immediately.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, 30 January 2001
