MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 119
Upon the recommendation 
of the National Disaster Coordinating
Council 
(NDCC), work in government offices in the National 
Capital
Region is hereby suspended on Friday, 29 September 2006.
However, those agencies whose functions 
involve the delivery of
health services, response to disasters and calamities, emergency services
and security, and/ or the performance of other vital public services shall
continue with their operations and render the necessary services.
This Circular shall take effect immediately.
By authority of the President:
R. ERMITA
Executive Secretary
Manila,
28 September 2006
