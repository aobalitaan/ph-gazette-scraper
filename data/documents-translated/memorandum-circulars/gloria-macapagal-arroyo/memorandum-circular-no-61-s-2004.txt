MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 61

DIRECTING ALL CABINET MEMBERS, HEADS OF OFFICES AND AMBASSADORS TO CONTINUE, IN A HOLD-OVER CAPACITY, TO PERFORM THEIR DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

To avoid disruption of public service, all Cabinet members, heads of offices and ambassadors shall remain in office and continue, in a hold-over capacity, to perform their duties and discharge their responsibilities until their resignation is accepted, or their respective replacements are appointed or designated and shall have qualified, or they shall have been reappointed.

Done in the City of Manila, this Circular shall take effect this 30 th day of June 2004.

(Sgd.) GLORIA MACAPAGAL-ARROYO

By the President:

Executive Secretary
