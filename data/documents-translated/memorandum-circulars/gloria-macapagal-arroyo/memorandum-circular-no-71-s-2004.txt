MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 71

AUTHORIZING THE PARTICIPATION ON OFFICIAL TIME OF MEMBERS OF THE SOCIETY OF PHILIPPINE ACCREDITED CONSULTANTS, INC. (SPAC) IN VARIOUS GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS IN THE NATIONAL SYMPOSIUM TO BE HOSTED BY SPAC

The Society of Philippine Accredited Consultants, Inc. (SPAC), will hold its National Symposium on October 15, 2004 (half-day) at the National Center for Transport Studies, U.P. Campus, Diliman, Quezon City.

In this connection, members of the SPAC in various government agencies and local government units are hereby authorized to attend the above-mentioned Symposium on official time only, at no cost to the government except payment of their salaries, provided that such attendance will not adversely affect the functions of their respective offices as well as the delivery of services to the public.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, OCT 18 2004
