Office of the President of the Philippines Malacañang

MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 18

TO : All Department Secretaries, Heads of Agencies, Bureau and Offices

In order to expedite the processing of requests for Presidential issuances and/or approvals, such as executive orders, proclamations, administrative orders, memorandum orders, memorandum circulars and similar directives, the requesting agency shall submit, as complete staff work, the basic papers and all documents related thereto and a Brief not exceeding two pages containing the information specified in Annex “A”.

(Sgd.) ALBERTO G. ROMULO Executive Secretary

Manila, 23 November 2001

Reference: Checklist on Requests For EOs, AOs And Other Presidential Issuances Or Approvals
