MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO._78

In view of super-typhoon “Yoyong” (International name “Nanmado”),
work in all government offices nationwide, including government-owned and
controlled corporations, is hereby suspended today. Government agencies
shall maintain skeletal work force fo maintain government service. Those
agencies whose functions are the delivery of basic services and response to
disaster and calamities shail continue to be in their place of work.

This Circular shail take effect immediately.
