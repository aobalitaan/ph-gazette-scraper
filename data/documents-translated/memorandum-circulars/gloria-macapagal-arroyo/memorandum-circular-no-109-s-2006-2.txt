MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 109

AUTHORIZING ATTENDANCE TO THE 43 RD FOUNDATION DAY AND ALUMNI CONVENTION AND HOMECOMING FELLOWSHIP OF THE NATIONAL DEFENSE COLLEGE OF THE PHILIPPINES

The National Defense College of the Philippines (NDCP) will celebrate its 43 rd Foundation Day Anniversary and Alumni Convention and Homecoming on August 10 to 11, 2006 at the NDCP Honor Hall.

The NDCP Symposium and Convention will be a good opportunity for the alumni to enhance their expertise in governance and decision-making.

In this connection, alumni of NDCP who are working in the government sector are authorized to attend, on official time , the 43 rd Foundation Anniversary and Alumni Convention and Homecoming to be held on August 11, 2006 from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. at the NDCP Honor Hall, and a Symposium to be held on same date from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, August 08, 2006
