MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 80

ENJOINING ALL GOVERNMENT OFFICES TO UNDERTAKE APPROPRIATE ACTIVITIES IN THE OBSERVANCE OF THE NATIONAL HUMAN RIGHTS CONSCIOUSNESS WEEK

Pursuant to Republic Act No. 9201 dated July 2, 2002 which declared December 4 to 10 as National Human Rights Consciousness Week in the country and for other purposes, all heads of departments, bureaus and agencies of the national government, including government-owned and/or controlled corporations, government financial institutions as well as state colleges and universities, are hereby enjoined to undertake appropriate activities during the week-long celebration. These activities should aim at propagating a human rights culture in the country, as provided by law.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, December 3, 2004
