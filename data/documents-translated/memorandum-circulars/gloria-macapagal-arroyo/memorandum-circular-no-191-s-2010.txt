MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 191

AUTHORIZING THE PARTICIPATION ON OFFICIAL TIME OF MECHANICAL ENGINEERS IN VARIOUS GOVERNMENT AGENCIES AND LOCAL GOVERNMENT UNITS, STATE COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES AND GOVERNMENT-OWNED AND CONTROLLED CORPORATIONS IN THE 9 th NATIONAL MID-YEAR CONVENTION OF THE PHILIPPINE SOCIETY OF MECHANICAL ENGINEERS

The Philippine Society of Mechanical Engineers (PSME), will hold its 9 th National Mid-Year National Convention on April 29 to May 1, 2010 at Naga, Camarines Sur.

In this connection, Mechanical Engineers in various government agencies and local government units, state colleges and universities and government-owned and controlled corporations are hereby authorized to attend the above-mentioned Convention on official time only, at no cost to the government except payment of their salaries, provided such attendance will not adversely affect the functions of their respective offices as well as the delivery of services to the public.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, April 19, 2010
