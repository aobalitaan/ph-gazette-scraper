MEMORANDUM CIRCULAR NO. 38

AUTHORIZING THE ATTENDANCE OF LAWYERS EMPLOYED IN GOVERNMENT OFFICES AND AGENCIES IN THE 9 th NATIONAL CONVENTION OF LAWYERS ON MAY 21-24, 2003 AT THE PHILIPPINE INTERNATIONAL CONVENTION CENTER, PASAY CITY

The Integrated Bar of the Philippines will hold the 9 th National Convention of Lawyers on May 21-24, 2003 at the Philippine International Convention Center, Pasay City.

In this connection, lawyers employed in government offices and agencies are hereby authorized to attend the Convention on official time, subject to the discretion of the office head to determine the number of attendees so as not to disrupt the operations of their offices.

Participants shall submit to their respective unit/heads a complete report of their attendance or participation in the said convention.

By authority of the President:

Executive Secretary

Manila, May 15, 2003
