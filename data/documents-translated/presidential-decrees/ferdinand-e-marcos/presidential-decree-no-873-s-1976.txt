PRESIDENTIAL DECREE No. 873

AMENDING PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 342 BY DESIGNATING A REPRESENTATIVE OF THE OFFICE OF THE PRESIDENT, IN LIEU OF THE ABOLISHED POSITION OF THE EXECUTIVE SECRETARY, AS ONE OF THE MEMBERS OF THE PHILIPPINE CENTER FOR ADVANCED STUDIES BOARD OF TRUSTEES

Presidential Decree No. 342 is hereby amended by designating a “Representative of the Office of the President,” in lieu of the abolished position of the Executive Secretary, as one of the members of the PCAS Board of Trustees. The representative shall be appointed by the President.

Done in the City of Manila, this 12th day of January, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS

By the President:

(Sgd.) JACOBO CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant
