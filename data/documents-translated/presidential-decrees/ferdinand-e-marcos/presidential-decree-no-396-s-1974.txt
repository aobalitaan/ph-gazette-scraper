PRESIDENTIAL DECREE No. 396

CONSTRUCTION OF A CONVENTION CENTER IN BAGUIO CITY

WHEREAS, since the proclamation of Martial Law, the country has experienced an unprecedented expansion in all aspects of its tourist trade due to the heavy and continuing influx of tourists from all parts of the world;

WHEREAS, as a result of such an upsurge in tourism, the need for new tourist infrastructure facilities has correspondingly expanded;

WHEREAS, as part of the aforementioned tourist trade expansion, an increasing number of the international conferences and/or conventions are being held in Manila and other places in the Philippines;

WHEREAS, the City of Baguio has become one of the most attractive venues for local, regional and international conferences and/or conventions because of its pleasant climate and attractive scenery;

WHEREAS, the City ofBaguiocan best serve the requirements of local, regional, and international conferences and/or conventions by establishing a complete convention center equipped with all the necessary facilities for servicing large gatherings;

WHEREAS, a suitable place to construct a convention center in the City of Baguio are on parcels of land more particularly described as follows:

WHEREAS, said properties belong to the National Government;

WHEREAS, the National Government is contributing the aforesaid parcels of land to the Government Service Insurance System to augment its retirement funds;

WHEREAS, the National Government desires to convey, transfer and cede said parcels of land in the name of the Government Service Insurance System on condition that the GSIS shall construct a convention center within said parcels of land for its own account;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution as Commander-in-Chief of all the Armed Forces of the Philippines and pursuant to Proclamation No. 1081, dated September 21, 1972, as amended by Proclamation No. 1104, dated 17 January 1973, and General Order No. 1, dated 22 September 1972, as amended, in order to effect the transfer of ownership of the parcels of land above described to the GSIS as additional contribution of the National Government to the System’s retirement funds, and to provide a convention center within the City of Baguio, I hereby order and decree:

Section 1. The above-described parcels of land located in Baguio City valued at Seven Million One hundred forty-seven thousand four hundred forty pesos (P7,147,440.00) are hereby conveyed, transferred and ceded to the Government Service Insurance System as additional contribution of the National Government to augment the actuarial solvency of the System’s Retirement Insurance Fund, on the condition that the GSIS shall for its own account construct a convention center equipped with the essential facilities and amenities necessary to service local, regional, and international conferences.

Section 2. Any provision of law, executive order, rules and regulations inconsistent with this Decree is hereby repealed or modified accordingly.

Section 3. This Decree shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of February, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President Republic of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
