PRESIDENTIAL DECREE No. 611

RATIFYING THE EMPLOYMENT POLICY CONVENTION, 1964, ADOPTED BY THE INTERNATIONAL LABOR ORGANIZATION.

I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby decree and order that the Employment Policy Convention, 1964 (Convention No. 122), adopted by the International Labor Organization on July 9, 1964, at its Forty-eight session held at Geneva, Switzerland, shall be, as it is, hereby ratified.

Let a copy of this Decree be transmitted to the Director-General, International Labor Office pursuant to its rules.

Done in the City of Manila, this 18th day of December, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary

Source: Malacañang Records Office

Presidential Decree No. 611 December 18, 1974
