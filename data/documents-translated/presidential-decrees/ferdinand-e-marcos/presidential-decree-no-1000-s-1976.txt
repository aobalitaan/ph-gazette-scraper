{"translation":"AMENDING SECTION 4 OF PRESIDENTIAL DECREE NO. 925, ENTITLED “ORGANIZING THE PAMPOOK NA KATIPUNAN NG MGA SANGGUNIAN”

“Sec. 4. Executive Committee. There is hereby created an Executive Committee to be elected by and from the members of the Local Chapter of the Association of Councils which is authorized to act for and in behalf of said Local Chapter of the Association of Councils when not in session.

“The Executive Committee shall be composed of 7 members, to wit:

a) one governor or city mayor;

b) one barangay federation president;

c) one youth council federation president; and

d) four sectoral representatives.

“In addition to the above members of the Executive Committee, the President may appoint another member at large from each region.”

This Decree shall take effect immediately."}
