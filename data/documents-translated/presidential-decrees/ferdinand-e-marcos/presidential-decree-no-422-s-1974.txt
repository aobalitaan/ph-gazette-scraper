PRESIDENTIAL DECREE No. 422

NAMING THE BRIDGE ACROSS THE ABRA RIVER LINKING THE MUNICIPALITIES OF TAYUM AND DOLORES, ABRA, AS “DON MARIANO MARCOS BRIDGE”

WHEREAS, the Provincial Board and the Mayor’s League of Abra in a meeting held on February 8, 1974, adopted a resolution, the full text of which follows:

“WHEREAS, the bridge that is being built across the Abra River at Barrio Bumageat, Tayum and Barrio Mudiit, Dolores is about to be completed;

“WHEREAS, this bridge, one of the longest in the Philippines, is a vital link in the Abra-Kalinga-CagayanValley national highway;

“WHEREAS, this bridge plays an important role in the promotion of trade and commerce between Kalinga-Apayao and the Cagayan Valley on one hand and the Ilocos provinces on the other;

“WHEREAS, this bridge symbolizes the unprecedented progress attained by the infrastructure program of His Excellency, President Ferdinand E. Marcos;

“WHEREAS, the late Assemblyman Don Mariano Marcos of Ilocos Norte, while heading the pacification campaign in the Ilocos provinces during the early days of the Japanese Occupation won the hearts and admiration of the people of Abra through his timely intercession, his convincing pleas for unity in the face of political crisis, and his pragmatic approach in extending the much needed material assistance to a down-trodden people, thereby fortifying their faith and bolstering their morale;

“NOW, THEREFORE, be it resolved, as it is hereby resolved by the Provincial Board and the Mayors’ League of Abra in a joint meeting held at the Capitol Building, Bangued, Abra this eight day of February, 1974 and acting in behalf of the people of this province, to petition His Excellency, President Ferdinand E. Marcos, to name the new bridge, DON MARIANO MARCOS BRIDGE, in memory of the man, who, during those critical days of the Japanese Occupation saved a vanquished people from the mire of despair into which they were arbitrarily cast by their victors.”

WHEREAS, it will be more in compliance with the desire and sentiment of the provincial and local officials of the province of Abra that said request be accommodated.

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers in me vested by the Constitution, do hereby decree and order that the bridge span across the Abra River linking the municipalities of Tayum and Dolores, Abra, be named the “DON MARIANO MARCOS BRIDGE.”

This Decree shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 27th day of March, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-four.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) ALEJANDRO MELCHOR Executive Secretary
