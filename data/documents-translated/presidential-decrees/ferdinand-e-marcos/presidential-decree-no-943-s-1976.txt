PRESIDENTIAL DECREE No. 943

CHANGING THE NAME OF THE STO. TOMAS-BATANGAS CITY-NASUGBU ROAD TO “PRESIDENT LAUREL HIGHWAY”

WHEREAS, among the illustrious leaders of the country whose name is revered by all Filipinos is the late President Jose P. Laurel, Sr., the beloved son of the province of Batangas; and

WHEREAS, it has been the tradition of the Filipino people to name important buildings, bridges and highways after illustrious leaders of the country to perpetuate their names and memories;

NOW, THEREFORE, I, FERDINAND E. MARCOS, President of the Philippines, by virtue of the powers vested in me by the Constitution, do hereby order and decree:

Section 1. The name of the present road traversing Sto. Tomas, Batangas City, and Nasugbu, now called the Sto.Tomas-Batangas City-Nasugbu Road, is hereby changed to “PRESIDENT LAUREL HIGHWAY.”

Section 2. This Decree shall take effect immediately.

Done in the City of Manila, this 10th day of June, in the year of Our Lord, nineteen hundred and seventy-six.

(Sgd.) FERDINAND E. MARCOS President of the Philippines

By the President:

(Sgd.) JACOBO CLAVE Presidential Executive Assistant

Source: Malacañang Records Office
