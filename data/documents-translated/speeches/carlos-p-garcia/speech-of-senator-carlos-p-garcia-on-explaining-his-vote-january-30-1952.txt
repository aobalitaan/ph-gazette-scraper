Speech of Honorable Carlos P. Garcia Senator of the Philippines On explaining his vote

[Released on January 30, 1952]

EL SEN. GARCÍA EXPLICA SU VOTO

Senator GARCÍA. Mr. President, I have just been furnished with a copy of this measure. During the last session—that was six months ago—I had, however, a chance to glance over this bill. Frankly speaking, I am not very prepared to emit my vote on this measure, although at first blush it looks like a good bill. In order to be honest with myself, I would rather abstain.
