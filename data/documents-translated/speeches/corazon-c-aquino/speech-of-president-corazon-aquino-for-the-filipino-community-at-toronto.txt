A CALL FOR UNITY

Sa gitna ng mga pagsubok na kinakaharap ng ating bansa, mahalaga ang pagkakaisa. Ang ating mga pagkakaiba-iba sa paniniwala, kultura, at pinanggalingan ay dapat maging pundasyon ng ating lakas, hindi ng ating pagkakahati-hati. Kailangan nating magtulungan upang malampasan ang mga hamon at makamit ang isang mas magandang kinabukasan para sa lahat ng Pilipino.

We must remember that our shared identity as Filipinos transcends any division. Let us prioritize dialogue, understanding, and cooperation. Let us work together to build a nation where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Ang pagkakaisa ay hindi lamang isang hangarin, ito ay isang pangangailangan. Sama-sama nating itaguyod ang kapayapaan, pag-asa, at pag-unlad para sa ating mahal na Pilipinas. Let us all commit to being agents of positive change and building a brighter future for generations to come.

The President expressed the belief that every Filipino possesses a sense of patriotism, regardless of their location or social standing.

I would like to express my gratitude for the warm welcome you have extended to me and my delegation. I am here to share a special moment of remembrance with you, my fellow citizens, whose prayers and expressions of support sustained our struggle and helped us solidify our victories.

You Filipinos residing abroad have a role to play in our future. An important chapter of our history is unfolding. The participation of our beloved countrymen is expected – countrymen who are once again hoping for a bright future.

I ask you today. Are you still part of our nation that experienced the rebirth of freedom and honor? Do you still unite with all Filipino citizens to achieve the ideal guided by the principles of democracy? In these questions, I am confident that your responses will be in agreement, because I believe that the patriotism of every Filipino still runs in your veins and cannot be suppressed by the temporary comforts offered by the country you now reside in.

In this mass offered for the aspirations of our countrymen, I earnestly prayed that every Filipino may open their eyes so that true unity may once again exist in our country. I also asked the Almighty to allow all our countrymen to discover their inherent abilities and importance towards the democratic principles of our nation. You possess the ability to help and participate. God has given you intelligence and diligence. Our beloved countrymen await the realization of your desire to unite towards the prosperity and progress of our nation. Thank you very much.
