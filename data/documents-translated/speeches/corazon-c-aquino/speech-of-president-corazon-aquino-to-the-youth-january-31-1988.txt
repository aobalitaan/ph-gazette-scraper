YOUTH AS THE FUTURE

Ang kabataan ang pag-asa ng bayan. Ito ay isang kasabihang lagi nating naririnig, lalo na sa mga programa at aktibidad na may kinalaman sa pag-unlad ng ating bansa. Ngunit ano nga ba ang ibig sabihin nito? At paano natin masisiguro na ang ating kabataan ay magiging tunay na pag-asa ng bayan?

The youth are the hope of the nation. This is a saying we often hear, especially in programs and activities related to the development of our country. But what does it really mean? And how can we ensure that our youth will truly be the hope of the nation?

Sa kasalukuyan, maraming kabataan ang nahaharap sa iba't ibang hamon. Poverty, lack of access to quality education, unemployment, at climate change are just some of the issues that affect their lives. These challenges can hinder their potential and prevent them from contributing to the progress of our society. Kailangan nating tugunan ang mga problemang ito upang mabigyan natin ng magandang kinabukasan ang ating kabataan.

Currently, many young people face various challenges. Poverty, lack of access to quality education, unemployment, and climate change are just some of the issues that affect their lives. These challenges can hinder their potential and prevent them from contributing to the progress of our society. We need to address these problems so that we can give our youth a bright future.

Kaya naman, mahalaga na magkaroon tayo ng mga programa at polisiya na susuporta sa pag-unlad ng ating kabataan. Dapat nating bigyan sila ng access sa de-kalidad na edukasyon, pagkakataon sa trabaho, at sapat na suporta upang mapalakas ang kanilang kakayahan at potensyal. We must also empower them to participate in decision-making processes and to become active citizens of our country. Sa pamamagitan ng pagbibigay-halaga sa ating kabataan, masisiguro natin na sila ang magiging tunay na pag-asa ng bayan.

Therefore, it is important that we have programs and policies that support the development of our youth. We must give them access to quality education, job opportunities, and sufficient support to strengthen their skills and potential. We must also empower them to participate in decision-making processes and to become active citizens of our country. By valuing our youth, we can ensure that they will truly be the hope of the nation.

The President pays tribute to the youth, their idealism, and uncompromising attitudes on matters of right and commitment to the rebuilding of our nation.

The usual thing is to say that the youth are the future of the country.

That is true of course, because many of you will still be around when many of us will be gone.

But that saying makes it seem like the youth are important only tomorrow, when they are already adults. And not today while they are young.

Now that is not true.

It is not true because the youth constitute the bulk of the Filipino nation today.

It is not true because the youth, with their idealism and uncompromising attitudes on matters of right, made our new country what it is today.

It is not true because without the commitment of our youth to the rebuilding of our nation we shall have no tomorrow.

I am honored to be here for the centennial celebration of St. John Bosco’s death: the friend and patron of youth. He was a man who felt that every young person was of infinite value and that no life should be wasted. The waste of a life through poverty, unemployment, and uselessness was, for him, a kind of sacrilege, a desecration of God. For he took literally what the Bible says, That each person is made in God’s image.

I said that the youth are not only the future of our nation, but also that, without the commitment of our youth, we shall have no future. I believe this because only the youth can supply the energy, the time, the intelligence and the patriotism that are needed to make this country strong and progressive.

You are the future, indeed. You are our country, today and tomorrow.

As you will be what you make of yourselves, this country will be what you make of it.

I believe in you, as the saint we honor today believed in you. And I know that you will do us proud.

Thank you and good day.
