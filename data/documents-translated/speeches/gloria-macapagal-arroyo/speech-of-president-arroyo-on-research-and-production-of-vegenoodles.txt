Speech of Her Excellency Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo President of the Philippines On Research and Production of Vegenoodles

[Delivered at Brgy. Betag, La Trinidad, March 22, 2008]

I wish to take advantage of my visit to instruct the study of manufacturing of vegenoodles whose raw materials can be sourced from the salad bowl of Cordillera and the pinakbet bowl of Pangasinan (Villasis et al). The vegenoodles can be part of Tindahan Natin stock. The program is given a substantial allocation in this year’s national budget. Vegetable-fortified noodles will make them more nutritious and delicious and less costly to manufacture in this era of soaring wheat prices. They will improve the income of farmers, and solve storage problems of what by nature are quickly perishable items.

Benguet State University can partner with vegetable processors and noodle makers, which include family of Mr. GoNegosyo, Joey Concepcion, with respect to the technological aspect of commercial production, DA to ensure supply through technical assistance to growers of these high-value crops, PMS to ensure credit assistance, and DTI to support marketing concerns.

Filipino companies, notably the Chan family of Oishi fame, have been producing veggie-based and veggie-added snacks and noodle. These patriots, I know, will join in any national project that will feed our children, help our farmers and give our consumers more and better nutritional choices.

I am giving a check of P10 million to Benguet State University with the instruction for them to concentrate on vegenoodles as their research and development mandate.
