Speech of the Honorable Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo, President of the Philippines, at the Launching of the National Supplemental Feeding Program

A strong and healthy child is equivalent to a strong and healthy nation. Every child needs a healthy start, or a healthy beginning to life.

Therefore, Assistant Secretary Santos, what we will call this program is “Malusog na Simula” or “Healthy Start.”

This healthy start nutrition program aims to fill our children with hope as well as a nutritious breakfast.

We must nourish the hearts and minds of our children through the gift of education. However, before this can happen, they first need full stomachs. Our children should start each day happy, healthy, and full of hope. They should only look forward to gaining knowledge and not to the basic need of having food on the table for breakfast.

Providing nutritious breakfast to children, who would otherwise go hungry, is the most important thing our country can do. It is a matter of decency, social necessity, and economic well-being.

[MOCK TRANSLATION] Ang pagkakaloob ng masustansiyang almusal sa mga bata, na kung hindi ay gugutumin, ang pinakamahalagang bagay na magagawa ng ating bansa. Ito ay kagandahang asal, kinakailangang panlipunan at kagalingang pangkabuhayan.
