Speech of the Honorable Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo, President of the Philippines, at the Barangay Anti-Drug Abuse Council (BADAC) Congress of the National Capital Region

Thank you very much Secretary Joey Lina, Secretary of the DILG and current Chairman of the Dangerous Drugs Board.

To our host mayor, Mayor Sonny Belmonte; to our president of the Mayors’ League of Metro Manila, Mayor Lito Atienza; Mayor of Caloocan, Rey Malonzo; the new Mayor of Navotas – oh, actually not new, he’s a former mayor – Toby Tiangco; Mayor of Pateros, Endo Capco; and the new Mayor of Malabon, Jayjay Yambao.

I would also like to greet the vice mayors who are here, who gave us statements of best practices: Vice Mayor of Makati, Vice Mayor Mercado; Vice Mayor of Navotas, Vice Mayor Cruz; Vice Mayor Herbert Bautista of Quezon City; Vice Mayor Calixto of Pasay.

Let us also greet our officials who are helping your barangays in the fight against drugs, the officials of the Dangerous Drugs Board — Efren Fernandez is here; Jun Avenido; the head of the police, Jun Ebdane; the head of the PNP task force against drugs, Egay Aglipay; the head of the police of Metro Manila, Rey Velasco; James Marty Lim, president of the association of barangays; chairmen and members of BADAC (Barangay Anti-Drug Abuse Council).

Long live the barangays!

[MOCK TRANSLATION] Mabuhay ang mga barangay!
