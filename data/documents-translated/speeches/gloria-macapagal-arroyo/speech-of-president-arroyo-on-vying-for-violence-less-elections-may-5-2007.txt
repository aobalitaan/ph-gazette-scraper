The political process is just as important as the political outcome. It will truly be… And we can ensure that the election will truly be democratic and we will see, it will reveal the will of the people. Let us all do everything we can to help our democracy overcome the trials we face. Every election is a test of democracy. In this way, we will further strengthen the foundation for a good future for our country, economy and society. Together, we can show the world that Filipinos thrive in a democratic system based on free choice. The value of a strong democracy to strengthen the economy and improve the lives of the people is undeniable.

Together, our efforts must be directed at reducing the violence. That is why I ask those participating in this meeting to focus on how security agencies can help ensure the order of the upcoming election.

I hope you can tell all the security guards who are part of your organization and all those training security guards that our future is at stake in the next election. What is at stake also includes the continued reform of our economy, the cleaning of the environment, the improvement of hospitals, investment in education, and the creation of good jobs.

I hope you will inform or declare or repeat that “reforms build; revolt destroys.” And because reforms build, that is why I do not lose focus on my work.
