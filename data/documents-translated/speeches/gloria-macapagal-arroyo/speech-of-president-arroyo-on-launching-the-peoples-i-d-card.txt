LAUNCHING THE PEOPLE’S I.D. CARD

I’d like to let you know that we’re launching the People’s I.D. card. The purpose is to strengthen good governance, improve transparency, empower our citizenry, and give every Filipino faster access to dependable government services.

This card is part of the unified multi-purpose I.D. system or MU-ID forged by various government agencies from concept to implementation for the convenience and security of the public. We are now pilot testing the system to make it foolproof and remove any bugs in it.

This system fills the last mile in the gap between the people’s needs and a streamlined government that serves those needs. As we continue to make government operations, system and procedures move faster and better, we shall integrate and harmonize existing I.D. systems in the different government agencies and government-owned-and-controlled corporations.

Isn't it better if you only use one ID for all government transactions, rather than having your wallet bursting with the number of IDs you need? That’s the whole purpose of that.
