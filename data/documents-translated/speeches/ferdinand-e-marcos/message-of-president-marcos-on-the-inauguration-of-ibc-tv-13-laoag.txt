MESSAGE OF HIS EXCELLENCY FERDINAND E. MARCOS
PRESIDENT OF THE PHILIPPINES
On the Inauguration of IBC TV – 13 Laoag

My countrymen of Ilocos Norte, ladies and gentlemen:

It is with a deep sense of fulfillment that I join you today in the inauguration of IBC TV – 13 Laoag. This occasion marks not only the expansion of our national television network but also a significant step forward in our efforts to bring the benefits of communication and information to every corner of our beloved Philippines.

Television, as we all know, is a powerful medium. It is a window to the world, a source of education, and a catalyst for progress. It can unite us as a nation, inspire us to greater heights, and empower us to shape our own destiny.  *Kaya naman* we have always considered the development of a truly national television network as a vital component of our national development program.

Ilocos Norte has always been close to my heart. It is the land of my birth, the home of my people, and the cradle of my political convictions. I have always believed in the potential of this province and its people.  *At naniniwala ako* that with the advent of IBC TV – 13 Laoag, this potential will be further unlocked.

This television station will serve as a vital link between the government and the people of Ilocos Norte. It will provide a platform for the dissemination of information on government programs and policies, as well as a forum for the discussion of issues that affect the lives of our citizens.  It will also showcase the rich culture and heritage of Ilocos Norte, and promote its economic development.

I urge the management and staff of IBC TV – 13 Laoag to uphold the highest standards of journalistic integrity and professionalism.  *Siguraduhin ninyo* that your programs are informative, educational, and entertaining.  Let your station be a beacon of truth and a source of inspiration for all.

Let us all work together to build a strong, prosperous, and united Philippines.  *Mabuhay ang Pilipinas!*  Mabuhay ang Ilocos Norte!  Mabuhay ang IBC TV – 13 Laoag!

Today, as we open the broadcast of IBC TV-13 Laoag, on this occasion, the First Lady and I express our joy to all our Ilokano brothers and sisters with heartfelt greetings. This event in the media would not have been possible, but through the dedicated public service of the Intercontinental Broadcasting Corporation, it deserves praise and recognition.

We hope that in the coming months, the value of the service this communication provides will be strengthened for the provinces and the people of the region. Aside from the benefits offered by the programs, we hope that through this station, an effective instrument will also be provided so that the people will know the various activities in the region and in national development. Truly, this facility will be a great help not only in improving the needs of our people in Ilocos but also in service to national unity.

Only three days have passed, during that dark period of suffering that took lives and harmed our fellow citizens, I propose a call to action to hasten the recovery of those affected and to restore what we have lost. We began this. Today we will take another important step in rebuilding our community.

Thank you and good day to you.

Marcos, F. E. (1983). Speeches by President Ferdinand E. Marcos. [Manila] : Presidential Library.
